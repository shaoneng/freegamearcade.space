Golf Orbit

Launch your golf ball into space, using planetary gravity to reach the next hole!

How to Play Golf Orbit

Welcome to Golf Orbit, where the fairway is the final frontier! This isn't your average game of golf. Instead of greens and sand traps, you'll be navigating the gravitational pull of planets to sink your ball into the next hole.

Core Objective

Your goal is to launch the golf ball from one planet and have it land perfectly in the hole of the next one. Itâ€™s a game of timing, precision, and understanding physics.

Controls

  • Aiming: Click (or tap on mobile) and hold down your mouse button. An arrow will appear, showing the projected path of your ball.
  • Shooting: Drag the cursor to adjust the direction and power of your shot. Release the mouse button to launch the ball.

Gameplay Tips

  • Use Gravity: Each planet has its own gravitational field. Use it to your advantage to bend your shots around obstacles or to slow your ball down as it approaches the target.
  • Watch the Orbit Line: Pay close attention to the white dotted line that shows the ball's orbit. This is key to lining up the perfect shot.
  • Patience is Key: Wait for the perfect alignment. Sometimes it's better to let the planets rotate into a more favorable position before taking your shot.

Ready to tee off into the cosmos? Good luck, astronaut!
