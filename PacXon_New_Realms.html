Pac-Xon New Realms

Fill the empty space and capture the ghosts!

How to Play Pac-Xon New Realms

Welcome to Pac-Xon New Realms, a fantastic arcade game that combines the classic gameplay of Pac-Man and Xonix. Your mission is to fill the empty space and trap the ghosts by building walls.

Game Objective:

  • Fill the Board: Your goal is to fill at least 80% of the game area to pass the level. The percentage is shown at the top of the screen.
  • Build Walls: Move out from the edge to create a wall. Once you return to a solid area, the space you've enclosed will be filled.
  • Avoid Ghosts: Do not let the ghosts touch you or your unfinished wall. If they do, you will lose a life!

Controls:

  • Use the Arrow Keys (or WASD) to control your Pac-Xon character.

Power-Ups:

Keep an eye out for helpful power-ups that appear on the board:

  • Cherries: Makes your Pac-Xon move faster.
  • Orange: Makes the ghosts move slower.
  • Ice Cubes: Temporarily freezes the ghosts in place.
  • Power Pellets: The large flashing dots turn you into a ghost-chomping powerhouse for a short time!
