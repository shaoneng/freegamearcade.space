Ultimate Moto

Experience high-speed motorcycle racing action!

How to Play Ultimate Moto

Feel the rush of the race in Ultimate Moto! Your goal is to outrace your opponents, master the tracks, and finish in first place to become the ultimate motorcycle champion.

Game Objective:

  • Race to Win: Compete against a field of skilled riders and fight your way to the front of the pack.
  • Unlock New Content: Earn cash from winning races to unlock faster bikes and new, more challenging circuits.
  • Master the Controls: Smooth handling and strategic use of your boost are key to dominating the competition.

Controls:

Keyboard:
  • W or Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • S or Down Arrow: Brake
  • A or Left Arrow: Steer Left
  • D or Right Arrow: Steer Right
  • Shift: Nitro / Boost

Navigate sharp turns, find the best racing line, and hit the nitro on straightaways to leave your rivals in the dust!
