12 MiniBattles

How to Play 12 MiniBattles

Welcome to 12 MiniBattles, the ultimate game for settling scores and challenging your friends! The game is a frantic collection of different two-player mini-games, chosen at random for you to compete in.

The controls are incredibly simple. Each player uses just one button to control their character. The first player uses the "A" key, and the second player uses the "L" key. The function of your button changes with each mini-game, so pay close attention to the on-screen objective!

Your goal is to win more rounds than your opponent. Each mini-game presents a unique challenge, from soccer shootouts and axe throwing to helicopter battles and cowboy duels. Adapt quickly, master the timing, and press your button at the right moment to claim victory in each round. The first player to win the required number of battles wins the match. May the best player win!
