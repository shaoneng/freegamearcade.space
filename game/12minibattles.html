12 MiniBattles

How to Play 12 MiniBattles

Welcome to the ultimate tie-breaker! 12 MiniBattles is a frantic and fun 2-player game designed for quick, head-to-head competition. The game pits you and a friend against each other in a series of randomly selected mini-games. Each battle is unique, testing everything from your reaction time and accuracy to pure button-mashing speed.

The controls are incredibly simple, making it accessible for everyone. Each player uses only ONE button to control their character. Player 1 typically uses the "A" key, and Player 2 uses the "L" key. On mobile devices, each player taps their side of the screen. The objective for each mini-game will appear on screen, so pay close attention!

The first player to win the required number of rounds is crowned the champion. With a wide variety of games, from soccer duels to wild west shootouts, you never know what's coming next. It's the perfect way to settle any argument or just have a laugh with a friend. Good luck!
