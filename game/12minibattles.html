How to Play 12 MiniBattles

Welcome to 12 MiniBattles, the perfect game to settle any score with a friend! This is a fast-paced, 2-player game where you compete in a series of random, quirky mini-games. The beauty of 12 MiniBattles lies in its simplicityâ€”every single game is controlled with just one button per player.

The controls are as straightforward as it gets. Player 1 uses the "A" key, and Player 2 uses the "L" key. That's it! Whether you're flying a helicopter, shooting a cowboy, or playing soccer, your single button is all you need to control the action.

When the game starts, you and your opponent will be thrown into a random battle. The objective of each battle is different, so you must adapt quickly. The first player to win a set number of these mini-battles wins the entire match. It's a true test of timing, reflexes, and quick thinking. Can you master them all and claim victory?
