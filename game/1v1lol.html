1v1.LOL

How to Play 1v1.LOL

1v1.LOL is an action-packed online game that combines third-person shooting with strategic building mechanics. Your primary goal is to eliminate your opponents and be the last player standing. To succeed, you'll need to master both your aim and your ability to build structures for defense and tactical advantage.

Controls:

  • Movement: Use the WASD keys to move your character.
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar.
  • Crouch: Use the Left Shift key.
  • Aim/Shoot: Use the Right Mouse Button to aim down sights and the Left Mouse Button to fire your weapon.
  • Building: Use the Z, X, C, V, and Y keys to select and place different building pieces like walls, floors, and ramps.
  • Reload: Press the R key.

Jump into the action and switch between your weapons (including shotguns, sniper rifles, and axes) and your building tools to outmaneuver your rivals. You can build protective forts, create high ground, or block enemy fire. The game supports various modes, including 1v1 duels, Battle Royale, and a practice mode to hone your skills. You can even connect a PlayStation or Xbox controller for a different gameplay experience!
