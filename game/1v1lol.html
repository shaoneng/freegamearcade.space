1v1.LOL

How to Play 1v1.LOL

Your main objective in 1v1.LOL is to eliminate your opponent and be the last player standing. This game uniquely combines fast-paced third-person shooting with strategic building mechanics, similar to popular battle royale titles.

Use your mouse to aim and shoot, and your keyboard to move and build. You have the ability to instantly construct walls, floors, ramps, and roofs to create cover or gain a height advantage. Cycle through your build options and your weapons (which include axes, shotguns, and sniper rifles) to adapt to any situation.

Jump straight into a 1v1 match, or hone your skills in the practice mode to get comfortable with the controls. The key to victory is a blend of quick reflexes for shooting and smart, rapid building to outplay your opponent. You can even connect a PlayStation or Xbox controller for a different gameplay experience!
