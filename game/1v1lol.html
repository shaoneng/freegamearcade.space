1v1.LOL

How to Play 1v1.LOL

Welcome to the fast-paced world of 1v1.LOL, where your shooting and building skills are put to the ultimate test! The goal is to outbuild, outwit, and outgun your opponent to be the last one standing. This game uniquely combines third-person shooting with on-the-fly construction, creating a dynamic and strategic combat experience.

Controls: Use your mouse and keyboard to control your character. The standard WASD keys are for movement, the mouse is for aiming, and the left-click is for shooting. Use the right-click to aim down sights with scoped weapons like the sniper rifle. Your building keys (Z, X, C, V) are for quickly placing walls, floors, ramps, and roofs.

Building for Victory: Building is just as important as shooting. You can instantly create structures around you to block enemy fire, gain a height advantage, or trap your opponents. Master the art of quick editing and building to create a fortress and control the battlefield.

Weapons and Combat: Arm yourself with a powerful arsenal including shotguns for close-quarters combat, axes for melee and resource gathering, and sniper rifles for long-range takedowns. Switch between your weapons and building mode seamlessly to adapt to any situation and secure your victory!

You can also play 1v1.LOL with a PlayStation or Xbox controller. Simply connect your controller to your device, and the game will automatically detect it, allowing you to customize your layout in the settings menu.
