1v1.LOL

How to Play 1v1.LOL

1v1.LOL is an electrifying third-person shooter and building simulator where your goal is to eliminate your opponent and be the last player standing. The game combines fast-paced combat with on-the-fly strategic construction, much like popular battle royale games.

Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate, build, and fight. The core mechanic involves switching between your weapons and your building tool to create structures like walls, floors, and ramps. Use these structures to defend against enemy attacks, gain a high-ground advantage, or push aggressively towards your opponent.

  • WASD: Move your character.
  • Mouse: Aim your weapon or building tool.
  • Left Mouse Button: Shoot or build.
  • Right Mouse Button: Aim down sights with your weapon.
  • Z, X, C, V, Y: Quickly select different building pieces.
  • R: Reload your weapon.

You can jump into various game modes, including a 1v1 battle, a practice mode to hone your skills, or team up with friends in party mode. The game also supports PlayStation and Xbox controllers for an alternative way to play. Master the blend of building and shooting to dominate the arena!
