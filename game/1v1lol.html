How to Play 1v1.LOL

1v1.LOL is an intense third-person shooter and building simulator where your primary goal is to eliminate your opponent and be the last player standing. The core gameplay combines shooting accuracy with quick building skills to create cover and gain a strategic advantage.

Use your mouse to aim and the left-click button to fire your weapon. Right-click to aim down sights, which is especially useful with the sniper rifle. You can switch between your weapons (shotguns, sniper rifles, axes) and your building tools to adapt to any situation.

Building is crucial for survival. You can instantly construct walls, floors, ramps, and roofs to defend against incoming fire or to reach higher ground. Master the keybinds for each building piece to build complex structures on the fly and outmaneuver your rivals. If you're new, hop into the practice mode to get a feel for the controls before diving into a real match. You can also connect a PlayStation or Xbox controller for a different gameplay experience!
