1v1.LOL

How to Play 1v1.LOL

1v1.LOL is a unique third-person shooter where tactical building is just as important as your aim. The core objective is to eliminate your opponents and be the last player standing. You can instantly build walls, floors, ramps, and roofs to defend yourself, block incoming fire, or gain a height advantage.

Mastering the controls is key to victory. Use your mouse to aim and shoot (Left-click) and to aim down sights with scoped weapons (Right-click). Use the WASD keys for movement. The building keys are crucial: Z for a wall, X for a floor, C for a ramp, and V for a roof. You can switch between your weapons and building axe using the number keys.

The game features several modes, including 1v1, Box Fight, and a Battle Royale mode where you fight to be the last one standing. Arm yourself with a powerful shotgun for close-quarters combat, a precise sniper rifle for long-range engagements, and your trusty axe for both building and close-up attacks.

For those who prefer a console experience, 1v1.LOL supports PlayStation and Xbox controllers. Simply connect your controller to your device via Bluetooth or USB, head to the in-game settings, and choose the controller option to view and customize your layout.
