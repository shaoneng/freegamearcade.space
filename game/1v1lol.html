1v1.LOL

How to Play 1v1.LOL

Welcome to 1v1.LOL, an intense and fast-paced third-person shooter that blends combat with strategic building. Your objective is simple: eliminate your opponent in a head-to-head battle to be the last one standing. The game challenges you to think on your feet, combining sharp shooting skills with the ability to construct defenses in an instant.

Mastering the controls is key to survival. Use your mouse to aim and the left-click button to shoot. Right-click allows you to zoom in with scoped weapons like the sniper rifle. Building is just as crucial as combat; use dedicated keys to quickly place walls, floors, ramps, and roofs to create cover, block your opponent's shots, or gain a tactical height advantage.

Your arsenal includes a variety of weapons suited for different situations, such as close-quarters shotguns, long-range sniper rifles, and a trusty axe for breaking down structures. Switch between your building tools and weapons seamlessly to adapt to the flow of the battle. For newcomers, a practice mode is available to help you get a feel for the controls and game mechanics before diving into real matches.

You can also play 1v1.LOL with a controller! Simply connect your PlayStation or Xbox controller to your device, head to the in-game settings, and select the controller option to view and customize your button layout.
