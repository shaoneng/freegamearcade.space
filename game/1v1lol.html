How to Play 1v1.LOL

Welcome to 1v1.LOL, an exciting blend of a third-person shooter and a fast-paced building simulator. Your main objective is simple: eliminate your opponents and be the last player standing. To succeed, you'll need to master both your aim and your ability to build structures on the fly.

Use your mouse to aim and the left-click button to shoot your weapons. Cycle through your arsenal of shotguns, sniper rifles, and axes to find the right tool for the job. Right-click to aim down sights with your rifle for precision shots. The key to survival, however, is building. Quickly construct walls, floors, and ramps to defend against incoming fire, gain high ground, and outmaneuver your rivals.

The game can be played with a keyboard and mouse. While the specific keybinds can be customized in the settings, the standard controls are WASD for movement, Spacebar to jump, and number keys or mouse wheel to switch between weapons and building pieces. You can also connect an Xbox or PlayStation controller for a different gameplay experience. Jump into Practice Mode to get a feel for the controls before heading into a real match!
