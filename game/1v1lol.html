1v1.LOL

How to Play 1v1.LOL

1v1.LOL is a high-octane blend of a third-person shooter and a fast-paced building simulator. The objective is simple: eliminate your opponents and be the last player remaining on the battlefield. To succeed, you must master both shooting accuracy and strategic construction.

Control your character using your keyboard and mouse. Use the WASD keys to move, the Space Bar to jump, and your Mouse to aim. The Left Mouse Button fires your weapon, while the Right Mouse Button aims down the sights for better accuracy.

Building is crucial for defense and creating tactical advantages. Quickly construct walls, ramps, and floors to outmaneuver your foes. Use the following keys to build:

  • Z: Build a wall
  • X: Build a floor
  • C: Build a ramp
  • V: Build a roof

You have access to a variety of weapons, including shotguns for close-quarters combat and sniper rifles for long-range engagements. Use the number keys (1, 2) to switch between your weapons strategically. Press the F key to interact with items. You can also play with a PlayStation or Xbox controller by connecting it and selecting the controller option in the game's settings menu.
