1v1.LOL

How to Play 1v1.LOL

Welcome to 1v1.LOL, the ultimate test of your shooting and building skills! The objective is simple: be the last person standing. This game uniquely combines fast-paced third-person shooting with strategic, on-the-fly building mechanics, much like in the popular game Fortnite. You'll need to out-build and out-gun your opponents to claim victory.

Use your keyboard and mouse for precise control. Move your character with the WASD keys and aim with your mouse. Use the Left Mouse Button to fire your weapon and the Right Mouse Button to zoom in and aim down sights. To build structures, use the Z, X, C, V, and Y keys to quickly place walls, floors, ramps, and other defensive pieces. Mastering the switch between combat and building is the key to success.

Your arsenal includes powerful weapons like shotguns for close-quarters combat, sniper rifles for long-range engagements, and an axe for breaking down enemy structures and close-up attacks. Choose the right tool for the job to gain an advantage in any situation. If you're new, jump into the Practice Mode to get a feel for the controls and mechanics before heading into a real match.

1v1.LOL also offers full controller support! You can connect your PlayStation or Xbox controller to your device via Bluetooth or a cable. Once connected, head to the in-game settings menu and select the controller option to customize your layout or play with the default settings.
