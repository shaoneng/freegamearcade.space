1v1.LOL

How to Play 1v1.LOL

1v1.LOL is an action-packed third-person shooter and building simulator where your survival depends on both your aim and your architectural speed. The main objective is simple: be the last player standing in a fast-paced arena. You'll need to strategically build structures to defend yourself while simultaneously hunting down your opponent.

Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the world and engage in combat. The game features intuitive controls similar to other popular battle royale games. If you're new, it's a great idea to jump into the Practice Mode to get a feel for the controls and mechanics before diving into a real match.

Building is a core mechanic. You can instantly construct walls, floors, and ramps to create cover, block enemy fire, or gain a high-ground advantage. Switching between your weapons and your building tools quickly is the key to outplaying your rivals.

Your arsenal includes a variety of weapons, such as shotguns for close-quarters combat, sniper rifles for long-range engagements, and an axe for breaking down structures. Right-click your mouse to aim down the sights with your rifle, and left-click to fire your weapon or swing your axe.

For those who prefer a different setup, 1v1.LOL also supports PlayStation and Xbox controllers. Simply connect your controller to your device via Bluetooth or a cable, and you can customize your bindings in the game's settings menu.
