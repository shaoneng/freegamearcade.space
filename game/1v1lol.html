How to Play 1v1.LOL

Welcome to 1v1.LOL, where your shooting skills and building speed are put to the ultimate test! The core objective is simple: be the last person standing. In this fast-paced third-person shooter, you will engage in intense duels while simultaneously building structures like walls, floors, and ramps to defend yourself and gain a strategic high ground over your opponent.

Mastering the controls is key to victory. Use your keyboard and mouse for precise movement, aiming, and shooting. The game offers a helpful practice mode to get you acquainted with the mechanics before you jump into a real match. For those who prefer a different setup, 1v1.LOL also supports PlayStation and Xbox controllers. Simply connect your controller to your device via Bluetooth or a cable, head to the in-game settings, and select the controller option to customize your layout.

Your arsenal includes a variety of weapons suited for different combat scenarios, such as powerful shotguns for close-quarters battles, axes for melee encounters, and sniper rifles for long-range precision. Remember to use the right mouse button to aim down your sights for better accuracy. Combine smart building with sharp shooting to outplay your opponents and claim victory!
