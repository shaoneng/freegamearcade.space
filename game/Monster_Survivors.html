How to Play Monster Survivors

Welcome to Monster Survivors! Your mission is to withstand relentless waves of creatures and fight for your survival as long as you can.

Core Gameplay:

  • Movement: Use WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character around the map.
  • Combat: Your character will often attack automatically. Focus on maneuvering to avoid enemies and position yourself strategically.
  • Experience & Upgrades: Defeated monsters drop experience gems. Collect them to level up.
  • Level Up Choices: Each time you level up, you'll be presented with a choice of new abilities or upgrades to existing ones. Choose wisely to build a powerful character!
  • Survival: The longer you survive, the more challenging the waves of monsters become.

Tips for Survival:

  • Keep Moving: Don't stand still for too long, or you'll get overwhelmed.
  • Prioritize Threats: Some monsters are more dangerous than others. Learn their patterns.
  • Synergize Abilities: Pick upgrades that work well together to maximize your damage and survivability.
  • Collect Wisely: Sometimes it's safer to clear an area before rushing to collect all experience gems.

This game challenges your reflexes, strategic thinking, and endurance. Good luck, survivor!
