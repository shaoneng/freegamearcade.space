American Truck Car Driving

How to Play American Truck Car Driving

Your primary goal in American Truck Car Driving is to transport important cargo across vast highways to its final destination. Use your driving skills to navigate through traffic-filled roads, ensuring your precious delivery arrives safely and on time.

Control your powerful truck using the Arrow Keys for steering, acceleration, and braking. Keep an eye on your fuel and damage levels. If you're running low on gas or have taken some damage, look for gas stations along the way to refuel and repair your vehicle.

Make your truck your own! The game allows you to customize various aspects of your vehicle, including its body color, window tint, and even the color of the smoke from your exhaust. Use the Mouse to navigate the customization menus and other in-game options.
