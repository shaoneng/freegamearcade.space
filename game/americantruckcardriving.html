American Truck Car Driving

How to Play American Truck Car Driving

Your primary mission in American Truck Car Driving is to navigate the stunning American highways, delivering essential cargo to its final destination. Use the on-screen arrows to guide your truck and stay on the right path to successfully complete your jobs.

Master control of your rig using the arrow keys for movement and the mouse for menu interactions. The open road is filled with other vehicles, so be careful! Avoid colliding with traffic to prevent damage to your truck and cargo, which could end your run prematurely.

Keep a close watch on your fuel and damage gauges. On your way, you'll encounter gas stations where you can stop to refuel and repair your truck. You can also customize your vehicle's appearance by changing the car color, window tint, and even the smoke color to make your truck uniquely yours.
