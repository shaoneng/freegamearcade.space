American Truck Car Driving

How to Play American Truck Car Driving

Your primary objective in American Truck Car Driving is to safely transport valuable cargo across vast American highways. Navigate your powerful truck from the starting point to the final destination, following the on-screen guides to stay on the right path.

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to steer your truck, accelerate, and brake. The mouse can also be used for certain interactions and camera controls. Master these controls to maneuver through traffic and tight spots.

Keep an eye on the road and avoid colliding with other vehicles, as damage can affect your truck's performance. You'll also need to manage your fuel level. Look for gas stations along your route to refuel or repair any damage your truck has sustained. Don't forget to visit the customization shop to personalize your truck's color, window tint, and even the color of its smoke!
