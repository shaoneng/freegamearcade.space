American Truck Car Driving

How to Play American Truck Car Driving

Your primary goal in American Truck Car Driving is to become a master of the open road. You'll take on missions to transport valuable cargo across stunning highways, ensuring it reaches its destination safely and on time. Follow the on-screen arrows to navigate your route and guide your big rig to the drop-off point.

Driving a large truck requires skill and awareness. Keep a sharp eye on the traffic around you to avoid collisions that could damage your vehicle and your cargo. Along your journey, you'll find gas stations where you can stop to refuel your tank or repair any damage your truck has sustained.

Make your truck your own with the customization options. You can change the main color of your truck, the tint of your windows, and even the color of the exhaust smoke to create a unique look for your vehicle.

Controls: Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to steer, accelerate, and brake. Use the mouse to interact with game menus and customization options.
