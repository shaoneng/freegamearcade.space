American Truck Car Driving

How to Play American Truck Car Driving

Your primary mission in American Truck Car Driving is to navigate the amazing highways, hauling important cargo safely to the final destination. On-screen arrows will guide you, helping you steer your truck toward specific areas and complete your delivery routes.

Use the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your truck. Press the up arrow to accelerate, the down arrow to brake or reverse, and the left and right arrows to steer. You can also use the Mouse for certain in-game interactions and to look around.

Drive carefully to avoid hitting other cars in traffic, as collisions will damage your truck. Along your route, you'll find gas stations where you can stop to refuel or repair any damage sustained. You can also visit the garage to customize your truck's appearance, changing its body color, window tint, and even the color of the exhaust smoke to make your rig truly unique!
