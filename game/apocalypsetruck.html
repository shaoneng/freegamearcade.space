Apocalypse Truck

How to Play Apocalypse Truck

In Apocalypse Truck, your main goal is to drive your customized monster truck through post-apocalyptic city ruins, crushing hordes of zombies and racing to the finish line.

Use the **Arrow Keys** or **WASD** to control your truck: accelerate, brake, and balance your vehicle over rough terrain. Smash through wrecked cars and other obstacles to maintain momentum.

Collect coins as you drive to unlock upgrades for your truck, making it even more powerful and resilient against the undead. Perform mid-air flips off ramps for extra points and style. Your mission is to survive each level and escape the city! Fuel up, slam the pedal, and show those zombies who's boss!
