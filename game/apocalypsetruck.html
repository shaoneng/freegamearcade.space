How to Play Apocalypse Truck

Your mission in Apocalypse Truck is to survive the zombie-infested wasteland and escape the ruined city. You'll take control of a powerful, beat-up monster truck, your last hope in this desolate world. Use your keyboard to control the truck, accelerating to build momentum and tilting to maintain balance.
