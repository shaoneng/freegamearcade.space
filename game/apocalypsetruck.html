Apocalypse Truck

How to Play Apocalypse Truck

Your mission is simple: survive. You are behind the wheel of a powerful, modified truck in a city overrun by zombies. Your goal is to drive to the finish line in each level, crushing as many undead as possible along the way.

Use the arrow keys or W/A/S/D to control your truck. Press the up arrow or 'W' to accelerate, and the down arrow or 'S' to brake or reverse. Use the left and right arrow keys (or 'A' and 'D') to balance your truck in mid-air to land your jumps perfectly and perform awesome flips.

Watch your fuel and health! Smash through obstacles and zombies, launch off ramps, and collect coins scattered throughout the levels. Use these coins to upgrade your truck and improve your chances of survival in the apocalypse.
