Apocalypse Truck

How to Play Apocalypse Truck

Your mission in Apocalypse Truck is to navigate your heavily armored monster truck through a zombie-infested wasteland and reach the finish line in one piece. The goal is to survive, crush as many zombies as possible, and earn coins along the way.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your vehicle. Press the Up Arrow or 'W' key to accelerate and the Down Arrow or 'S' key to brake or reverse. While airborne, use the Left and Right Arrow keys or 'A' and 'D' keys to balance your truck and perform stylish flips for extra points.

Smash through waves of the undead, launch your truck off ramps made from wrecked vehicles, and keep an eye on your fuel. Collect coins scattered throughout the level to upgrade your truck for even more destructive power. Can you escape the city ruins?
