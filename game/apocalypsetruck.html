How to Play Apocalypse Truck

Your mission in Apocalypse Truck is to navigate a zombie-infested, post-apocalyptic city and reach the finish line in one piece. Your souped-up monster truck is your only tool for survival.

Use the arrow keys or on-screen controls to accelerate, brake, and balance your truck. Drive forward to smash through waves of zombies and obstacles. As you launch off ramps and wrecked vehicles, use your controls to perform mid-air flips for extra points and style.
