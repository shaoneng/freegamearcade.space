Apocalypse Truck

How to Play Apocalypse Truck

Your goal in Apocalypse Truck is to drive through a zombie-infested city and reach the finish line to escape. The world has fallen, but your powerful monster truck is your ticket to freedom.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your truck. Press the up arrow or 'W' to accelerate and the down arrow or 'S' to brake or reverse. Use the left and right arrows ('A' and 'D') to balance your truck in mid-air to land perfectly after a jump or a flip.

As you drive, you'll encounter hordes of zombies. Don't hesitateâ€”run them over! You'll also find ramps and wrecked cars. Use them to launch into the air, perform flips for extra points, and collect coins scattered throughout the levels.

Keep an eye on your fuel and health. Crashing or taking too much damage can end your run. Use the coins you collect to upgrade your truck between levels, improving its engine, armor, and weapons to better handle the apocalyptic chaos.
