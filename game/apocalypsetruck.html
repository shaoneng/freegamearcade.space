Apocalypse Truck

How to Play Apocalypse Truck

The apocalypse is here, and your only way out is through a city swarming with the undead. In Apocalypse Truck, you're behind the wheel of a powerful monster truck, your last hope for survival.

Your mission is to drive through the chaotic, ruined cityscape, smashing everything in your path. Use your powerful vehicle to run over waves of zombies, clearing your way to the finish line. It's not just about brute force; you'll need to navigate treacherous terrain, launching off wrecked cars and other ramps.

While airborne, show off your skills by performing mid-air flips for extra points. Don't forget to grab the coins scattered throughout the levels to upgrade your ride. Fuel up, hit the gas, and see if you have what it takes to survive the zombie-infested streets in this action-packed 2D driving game.

Apocalypse Truck is developed by FreezeNova, known for their fast and fun browser games. If you enjoyed this, try a different kind of undead challenge with Zombie Sniper!
