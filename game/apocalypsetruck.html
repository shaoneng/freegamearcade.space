Apocalypse Truck

How to Play Apocalypse Truck

The world has fallen to a zombie virus, and you're one of the last survivors. Your only way out of the ruined city is a powerful, armored monster truck. It's time to hit the gas and smash some zombies!

In this 2D side-scrolling driving game, your primary goal is to reach the finish line of each level. Along the way, you'll need to navigate a post-apocalyptic landscape filled with the undead. Use your truck's momentum and spiked armor to run over zombies and clear your path.

Look out for ramps made of wrecked cars! Launch your truck into the air to perform awesome mid-air flips for bonus points. Don't forget to grab all the coins you see scattered throughout the levels. These can be used for crucial upgrades to improve your chances of survival. Keep an eye on your fuel, hit the pedal, and drive for your life!
