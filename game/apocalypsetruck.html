Apocalypse Truck

How to Play Apocalypse Truck

Your goal in Apocalypse Truck is to drive your powerful monster truck through a zombie-infested wasteland and reach the finish line at the end of each level. The city is in ruins, and the undead are everywhere, but your spiked and plated truck is the perfect tool for the job!

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to control your truck. Press the up arrow or 'W' key to accelerate forward, and the down arrow or 'S' key to brake or reverse. Use the left and right arrow keys (or 'A' and 'D') to balance your truck in mid-air, which is crucial for landing safely after big jumps and performing stylish flips.

As you drive, don't hesitate to run over any zombies that get in your way! You'll also find coins scattered throughout the levels. Collect as many as you can to potentially upgrade your vehicle. Use ramps made of wrecked cars to launch into the air, perform flips for extra points, and smash your way to victory.
