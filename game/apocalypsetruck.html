Apocalypse Truck

How to Play Apocalypse Truck

Your mission in Apocalypse Truck is to survive the zombie-infested city ruins by driving your monster truck to the finish line of each level. The objective is simple: go forward and don't let the undead horde, treacherous terrain, or an empty fuel tank stop you!

Mastering your truck is key to survival. Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your vehicle:

  • Up Arrow / W Key: Accelerate forward.
  • Down Arrow / S Key: Brake or reverse.
  • Left Arrow / A Key: Tilt your truck backward (essential for climbing hills and landing flips).
  • Right Arrow / D Key: Tilt your truck forward.

As you speed through the levels, aim to run over as many zombies as possible for points. Use wrecked cars and ramps to launch into the air, and control your tilt mid-air to perform flips and earn extra coins. Keep a close eye on your fuel gauge and grab fuel canisters along the way to make it to the end.
