Apocalypse Truck

How to Play Apocalypse Truck

Your mission in Apocalypse Truck is to survive the zombie-infested city and race to the finish line in your powerful, beat-up monster truck. The world has fallen, but your wheels can still roll!

The controls are straightforward. Use the W key or Up Arrow to accelerate and push forward through obstacles and hordes of the undead. Use the S key or Down Arrow to brake or reverse when you get stuck. While you're airborne from launching off wrecked cars, use the A/D keys or Left/Right Arrows to balance your truck, perform incredible flips for extra points, and ensure a safe landing.

As you drive, keep an eye out for coins to collect, which can help you on your journey. Your goal is not just to destroy zombies but to successfully navigate each level and escape the post-apocalyptic ruins. It's a high-octane blend of driving, destruction, and survival!
