Apocalypse Truck

How to Play Apocalypse Truck

The world has fallen to a zombie virus, but you're not giving up. Your only hope is a beat-up monster truck. In Apocalypse Truck, your goal is simple: slam the gas pedal and drive through the undead hordes to escape the city ruins.

This thrilling 2D side-scrolling survival game challenges you to navigate a post-apocalyptic wasteland. Use your truck to run over zombies, launch off wrecked cars for spectacular air time, perform mid-air flips, and grab coins along the way. Use your earnings to upgrade your vehicle and increase your chances of survival.

Apocalypse Truck is developed by FreezeNova, a studio known for making fast, fun, and action-packed browser games. If you enjoy monster trucks, destruction, and flattening zombies, you're in for a treat.

Once you've mastered the road, why not try a different approach to zombie survival? Give Zombie Sniper a try, where you'll trade your truck for a sniper rifle and take out the undead with precision from a rooftop vantage point.
