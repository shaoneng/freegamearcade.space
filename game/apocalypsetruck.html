Apocalypse Truck

How to Play Apocalypse Truck

Your goal is to drive your monster truck to the end of each level, navigating a post-apocalyptic landscape filled with zombies and obstacles. Use your driving skills to survive and escape the city ruins.

Use the arrow keys or W, A, S, D keys to control your truck. Press the Up Arrow or 'W' key to accelerate forward, and the Down Arrow or 'S' key to brake or reverse. Use the Left/Right arrow keys or 'A'/'D' to balance your truck in mid-air, allowing you to perform flips for extra points and land safely.

Smash through zombies to clear your path, but be careful not to get stuck or flip your vehicle. Collect coins scattered throughout the levels to boost your score. Use wrecked cars and ramps to launch into the air and show off your stunt skills. Reach the finish line to complete the level and continue your escape!
