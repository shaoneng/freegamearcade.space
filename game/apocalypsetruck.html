How to Play Apocalypse Truck

Your mission in Apocalypse Truck is to survive the zombie-infested city and reach the finish line. Get behind the wheel of your powerful monster truck and put the pedal to the metal!

Use your keyboard controls to accelerate, brake, and balance your truck as you navigate the treacherous, ruin-filled landscape. Your primary goal is to smash through waves of zombies that stand in your way. Don't hesitateâ€”running them over is the key to clearing a path.

Keep an eye out for ramps made of wrecked cars and other debris. Launching off these will send you airborne, allowing you to perform stylish mid-air flips. Successfully landing these stunts will reward you. While driving, make sure to collect as many coins as you can to boost your score. Manage your fuel, crush the undead, and race to safety!
