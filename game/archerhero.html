Archer Hero

How to Play Archer Hero

Welcome to the mystical world of Archer Hero! Your mission is to vanquish hordes of enemies using your expert archery skills. Navigate through various chapters and stages, defeating every foe that stands in your way.

The controls are simple and intuitive: tap and hold the screen, then drag your finger to aim your bow. When you release your finger, your hero will automatically shoot an arrow at the target. Your character will automatically move to a new position when you are not aiming, so focus on dodging enemy attacks while preparing your next shot.

As you defeat enemies, you'll earn coins and experience. After clearing each stage, you'll be presented with a choice of new abilities. Choose wisely, as these skills will define your strategy and power for the rest of your run. Can you create the perfect build to conquer all 50 stages?
