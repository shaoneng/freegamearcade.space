ArmedForces.io

How to Play ArmedForces.io

ArmedForces.io is an action-packed 3D first-person shooter that drops you right into the heart of battle. Your goal is to outmaneuver and outgun your opponents across various maps and game modes. Before deploying, you can customize your loadout, choosing from eight unique weapons and enhancing them with attachments like silencers and sights as you gain experience.

The game offers several exciting modes to test your combat skills:

  • Team Deathmatch: Join a squad and work together to eliminate the opposing team. The team with the most kills at the end of the round wins.
  • Free For All: It's every soldier for themselves! Your objective is to be the last one standing in a chaotic battlefield where everyone is a target.
  • Capture the Flag: A classic team-based objective mode. Infiltrate the enemy's base, steal their flag, and bring it back to your own base to score a point.

Use standard FPS controls to navigate the battlefield: WASD keys to move, your Mouse to aim and shoot, and other keys for actions like reloading and switching weapons. Don't forget to visit the profile section to save your experience using a load code, allowing you to keep your progress between sessions.
