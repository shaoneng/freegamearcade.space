ArmedForces.io

How to Play ArmedForces.io

Welcome to the battlefield of ArmedForces.io! This is a 3D first-person shooter where your objective is to eliminate enemy forces and dominate the match. Before deploying, you can arrange your loadout from eight distinct weapons, and as you progress, customize them with attachments like silencers and sights for a tactical advantage.

The game features multiple modes to test your skills. In Team Deathmatch, collaborate with your teammates to achieve a higher score than the enemy team. For a true solo challenge, enter the Free For All mode, where it's every soldier for themselves. In Capture the Flag, your mission is to infiltrate the enemy's base, grab their flag, and return it to your own base while defending yours.

Use your keyboard and mouse to move, aim, and shoot. You can save your experience points via a load/save code in your profile, allowing you to maintain your progress across sessions. Choose your mode, perfect your loadout, and prove your mettle in the arena!
