ArmedForces.io

How to Play ArmedForces.io

Welcome to the battlefield of ArmedForces.io, a thrilling 3D first-person shooter! Your main objective is to eliminate enemy combatants using a variety of weapons and tactics. Before you deploy, you can customize your soldier's loadout, choosing from eight distinct weapons and adding attachments like silencers and weapon sights as you level up and gain experience.

The game features several exciting modes, each with a unique objective:

Team Deathmatch: Join a squad and work together to achieve a higher score than the opposing team by eliminating their players.

Free For All: It's every soldier for themselves in this chaotic mode. Your goal is to be the last one standing by taking out every other player on the map.

Capture the Flag: A classic team-based objective mode. You must infiltrate the enemy's territory, steal their flag, and bring it back to your base while defending your own flag from being captured.

Master the controls, choose the right mode for your playstyle, and dominate the arena. You can save your progress (experience only) via a load code in your profile, allowing you to pick up where you left off in a future session.
