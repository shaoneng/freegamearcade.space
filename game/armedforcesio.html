ArmedForces.io

How to Play ArmedForces.io

Welcome to ArmedForces.io, a dynamic 3D first-person shooter where your goal is to dominate the battlefield. Before diving into the action, head to your profile to set your nickname and customize your loadout. You can choose from eight unique weapons and add attachments like sights or silencers to gain a tactical advantage.

ArmedForces.io features several exciting game modes, each with a unique objective:

- Team Deathmatch: Join a squad and work together to achieve a higher score than the opposing team by eliminating its members.
- Free For All: It's every soldier for themselves! The objective is to survive and get the most kills to be the last one standing.
- Capture the Flag: Infiltrate the enemy base, steal their flag, and bring it back to your own base to score a point, while also defending your team's flag from being captured.

As you play, you'll gain experience which can be saved via a unique code in your profile. Explore each mode, master your weapons, and climb the ranks to become an elite soldier!
