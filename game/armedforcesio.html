How to Play ArmedForces.io

Welcome to the battlefield of ArmedForces.io! Your primary goal is to outsmart and outgun your opponents in fast-paced, 3D first-person shooter combat. Work with your team or fight solo to dominate the match and climb the leaderboard.

The game offers several exciting modes to test your skills. In Team Deathmatch, collaborate with your allies to achieve a higher score than the enemy team. For a true test of individual skill, jump into Free For All, where it's every soldier for themselves. In Capture the Flag, your objective is to infiltrate the enemy base, steal their flag, and return it to your own base while defending yours.

Before each battle, you can customize your loadout. Choose from eight different primary weapons and equip them with attachments like silencers and scopes to suit your playstyle. As you gain experience, you can save your progress and continue to build your ultimate soldier.

Use the WASD keys to move, the mouse to aim, and the left mouse button to shoot. Press R to reload your weapon and the spacebar to jump. Master these controls to effectively navigate the maps and engage enemy players.
