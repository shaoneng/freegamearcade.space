Army Combat

How to Play Army Combat

Welcome to the battlefield, soldier! In Army Combat, your first choice is your strategy: will you be the attacker, seeking out the enemy, or the defender, holding your ground? You will face a special forces team of highly trained soldiers in a vast, forested environment.

Use the terrain to your advantage. The forest is filled with trees, bushes, and abandoned vehicles that provide excellent cover. Move cautiously and try to take down enemies one by one. Rushing in is a quick way to get overwhelmed, as you are always outnumbered.

Select the right tool for the job. Your arsenal includes everything from shotguns for close-quarters combat to powerful sniper rifles. When playing as an attacker, use your sniper to eliminate targets from a safe distance. Keep an eye on the red arrows above enemies to track their positions. Good luck!
