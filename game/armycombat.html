Army Combat

How to Play Army Combat

Welcome to the battlefield, soldier! In Army Combat, you dive into a realistic 3D world where you must face off against highly trained enemy soldiers. Your first choice is critical: will you play as the Attacker, infiltrating enemy lines, or the Defender, holding your ground against an onslaught?

The key to survival is strategy. This isn't a run-and-gun free-for-all. Your opponents are smart and will work together. Use the vast forested environment to your advantage. Hide behind trees, vehicles, and bushes to gain the element of surprise. Keep an eye out for the red arrows above your enemies to track their positions and plan your attack or defense carefully. Try to isolate and eliminate them one by one.

You have a full arsenal at your disposal. Switch between weapons like shotguns for close-quarters combat and a powerful sniper rifle for taking out targets from a safe distance. Choosing the right weapon for the situation is crucial to completing your mission. Good luck, soldier!
