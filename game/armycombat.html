Army Combat

How to Play Army Combat

Welcome to the intense battlefield of Army Combat! Your mission is to engage and eliminate highly trained enemy soldiers in a vast, forested area. Before you begin, you can choose to play as either an Attacker or a Defender, each offering a unique tactical experience.

As an Attacker, your goal is to infiltrate and neutralize the enemy. Use your sniper rifle to your advantage, picking off soldiers from a distance to avoid close-quarters combat. Move stealthily, using the dense foliage and abandoned vehicles for cover. Take your time and eliminate threats one by one.

As a Defender, you must hold your ground against incoming enemy waves. The key to survival is using the environment. Hide behind trees and bushes, and wait for the perfect moment to strike. Keep an eye out for the red arrow above your opponents to track their positions. Don't rush; let them come to you and pick them off individually. Switch between your available weapons, from shotguns for close encounters to rifles for medium-range fights, to adapt to any situation.
