Army Combat

How to Play Army Combat

Welcome to the intense battlefield of Army Combat! Your mission is to engage and neutralize a team of elite soldiers. You can choose to play in two distinct modes: Attacker or Defender.

In this 3D action game, you'll be fighting against highly trained soldiers in a large, forested area. Use the environment to your advantage. Hide behind trees, bushes, and military vehicles to stay out of sight and plan your attack.

As the Attacker, your goal is to infiltrate and eliminate the enemy force. Make strategic use of your sniper rifle to take out targets from a safe distance before they even know you're there.

As the Defender, you are outnumbered and must survive waves of attacks. Don't rush into a fight; be patient. Track enemy soldiers using the red arrow indicator above them and try to eliminate them one by one. Switch between a variety of weapons, from shotguns for close-quarters combat to rifles for medium-range engagements, to adapt to any threat.
