Army Combat

How to Play Army Combat

Welcome to Army Combat, a 3D action shooter where you face off against a team of highly trained soldiers. Your mission is to survive and eliminate the enemy in a large, forested battlefield. You can choose to play as either the attacker or the defender, each with its own strategic approach.

As an attacker, your goal is to infiltrate and eliminate the enemy forces. Use the environment to your advantage. Hide behind trees, bushes, and army vehicles to approach your targets stealthily. The sniper rifle is your best friend in this mode, allowing you to take down soldiers from a safe distance before they even know you're there.

In defender mode, you must hold your position against incoming waves of enemies. They will outnumber you, so direct confrontation is risky. Find a good defensive spot, use cover, and pick them off one by one. Keep an eye out for the red arrows above the soldiers to track their positions and avoid being surrounded.

A variety of weapons are scattered throughout the map. Swap between shotguns for close-quarters combat and rifles for long-range engagements to adapt to any situation. Stay alert, be patient, and use tactics to overcome the superior numbers of the enemy.
