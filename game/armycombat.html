Army Combat

How to Play Army Combat

Step onto the battlefield in Army Combat, a thrilling 3D action game where you face off against elite soldiers. Your first decision is crucial: choose to be the attacker, taking the fight to the enemy, or the defender, holding your position against all odds.

The key to survival is strategy and stealth. You are outnumbered, so use the vast forested environment to your advantage. Hide behind trees, bushes, and military vehicles to ambush your opponents. Track enemy soldiers by watching for the red arrow above their heads, allowing you to plan your attack and take them down one by one.

Your arsenal is diverse. When attacking, use the sniper rifle to eliminate threats from a distance. For close-quarters combat, switch to a shotgun or assault rifle. The right weapon for the right moment can turn the tide of battle.

Controls:

  • Move: W, A, S, D keys
  • Aim: Mouse
  • Shoot: Left Mouse Button
  • Switch Weapons: Number Keys
  • Reload: R key
  • Crouch: C key
