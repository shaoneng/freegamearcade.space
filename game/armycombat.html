Army Combat

How to Play Army Combat

Welcome to the battlefield of Army Combat! In this 3D action game, you'll go head-to-head with elite soldiers. Your first choice is crucial: will you be the attacker, or will you defend your position? Each role offers a unique tactical experience.

The battle takes place in a large forest filled with trees, bushes, and abandoned army vehicles. Use this environment to your advantage! Take cover to avoid enemy fire and plan your next move. Your opponents are skilled and numerous, so rushing in is not advised.

If you choose to be the Attacker, stealth is your best friend. Use the terrain to get close to your targets without being seen. Your sniper rifle is an excellent tool for this mode, allowing you to eliminate enemies from a safe distance before they even know you're there.

As a Defender, you'll face waves of attackers. They won't come alone, so stay hidden and be patient. Keep an eye out for the red arrow above each enemy to track their position. Try to isolate and eliminate them one by one to even the odds. You have access to a variety of weapons, so switch between them to find the best tool for the situation.
