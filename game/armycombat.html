Army Combat

How to Play Army Combat

Welcome to the intense battlefield of Army Combat! In this 3D action game, you'll face off against highly trained soldiers. Your first choice is crucial: will you be on the offense, attacking the enemy base, or on the defense, protecting your own territory? Your opponents are elite and will use advanced tactics, so be prepared for a serious fight.

The battle takes place in a large, dense forest filled with trees, bushes, and military vehicles. Use these objects as cover to your advantage, especially when outnumbered. A red arrow will appear above enemies, helping you track their positions. Plan your movements carefully, take them down one by one, and avoid rushing into open combat.

Your arsenal is varied to suit your playstyle. If you choose to be an attacker, the sniper rifle is your best friend, allowing you to eliminate targets from a safe distance. For defensive or close-quarters situations, switch to weapons like the shotgun for maximum impact. Choose the right tool for the job and dominate the battlefield!
