Army Combat

How to Play Army Combat

Welcome to Army Combat, an intense 3D action game where you'll face elite soldiers. Before each mission, you must choose your role: will you be the attacker, pushing into enemy territory, or the defender, holding your ground against an onslaught?

Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the battlefield. Move through the large, forested area and aim your weapon to engage the enemy. Your opponents are skilled and will use tactics against you, so stay alert and keep moving.

Strategy is key to survival. If you're attacking, use your sniper rifle to eliminate targets from a safe distance. If you're defending, use the trees, bushes, and abandoned vehicles as cover. Move slowly and try to take down enemies one by one. Watch for the red arrow above their heads to track their positions.

You'll have access to a full arsenal, from shotguns to assault rifles. Switch between weapons to adapt to different combat situations. Your mission is to eliminate all enemy soldiers on the map to win. Good luck, soldier!
