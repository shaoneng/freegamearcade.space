Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Step onto the pitch and lead your nation to glory in Asian Cup Soccer! Your first task is to choose one of the 12 available national teams. Once selected, you'll enter the knockout stage of the tournament, where every match is a tense penalty shootout.

Taking Your Shot: When it's your turn to kick, an arrow will appear on the field. The controls are simple but require timing. Click once to lock in the horizontal direction. Click a second time to set the height of your shot. A final click allows you to add a curve, bending the ball around the wall of defenders. Master this three-click system to score spectacular goals!

Defending Your Goal: When the opponent is shooting, you become the goalkeeper. Pay close attention to the screen! A pair of goalkeeper gloves will briefly appear, indicating where the ball is heading. Click on the gloves as quickly as you can to make a diving save and deny the opposition.

The objective is straightforward: score more goals than your opponent in the shootout to win the match and advance to the next round. Good luck, and may the best team win the cup!
