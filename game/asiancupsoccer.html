Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Your journey begins by selecting one of Asia's top national teams to represent in a major knockout tournament. The objective is to score more goals than your opponent in a penalty shootout to advance to the next round and ultimately win the championship.

When Kicking: Taking a shot is a simple three-step process. An arrow will appear on the field. Click once to lock in the direction of your kick. Click a second time to set the height. A final click determines the curve of the ball, allowing you to swerve it around defenders and fool the goalkeeper.

When Defending: When it's your opponent's turn to shoot, you become the goalie. Pay close attention to the pair of goalkeeper gloves that appear on the screen, indicating where the ball is headed. Click on them quickly to make a save and block the shot!

Each match is a tense showdown. The player with the most goals at the end wins and moves on. Good luck!
