Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Get ready to feel the roar of the crowd in Asian Cup Soccer! Your journey begins by selecting your favorite national team from a roster of 12 Asian powerhouses. You'll enter a high-stakes knockout tournament, where every match is a must-win to advance towards the final championship.

Taking a Shot: When it's your turn to take a free kick, an arrow will appear on the field. The controls are simple but require good timing. Click your mouse once to lock in the direction of your shot. Click a second time to set the height. A final, third click will determine the curve, allowing you to bend the ball around the wall of defenders. Master this three-click system to score stunning goals!

Playing as the Goalkeeper: When your opponent is shooting, you become the last line of defense. A pair of goalkeeper gloves will briefly appear on the screen, indicating where the ball is headed. You must react quickly and click on the gloves to make a crucial save and deny the other team a goal.

Each match is a penalty shootout. The player who scores the most goals wins the game and moves on to the next round. Can you handle the pressure and lead your team to glory?
