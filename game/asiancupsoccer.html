Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Welcome to the electrifying atmosphere of the Asian Cup! Your goal is to lead your chosen national team through the knockout stages and win the championship. Each match is a tense penalty shootout where you'll alternate between taking kicks and defending your goal.

Taking Your Shot: When it's your turn to kick, an arrow will appear on the field. The process is a simple three-click system to aim and shoot:

  • Click 1: Sets the horizontal direction of your shot.
  • Click 2: Sets the height of the ball.
  • Click 3: Adds curve to your kick, perfect for bending it around the wall of defenders.

Defending the Goal: When you're playing as the goalkeeper, watch for a pair of gloves to appear on the screen just before the opponent shoots. This indicates where the ball is heading. Click on the gloves as quickly as you can to make a spectacular save!

Each match is a best-of-five shootout. Score more goals than your opponent to win the match and advance to the next round. Good luck!
