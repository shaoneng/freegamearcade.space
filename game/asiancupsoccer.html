Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Welcome to the electrifying atmosphere of Asian Cup Soccer! Your first step is to choose your favorite national team from a selection of Asia's best. You'll then enter a knockout tournament, where every match is a do-or-die penalty shootout. The goal is simple: outscore your opponent to advance to the next round and claim the championship trophy!

Taking Your Shot: When it's your turn to kick, an arrow will appear on the field. The three-click system makes scoring easy to learn but hard to master. Click once to set the direction of your shot. Click a second time to determine the height. A final, quick click will add a curve to the ball, helping you bend it around the wall of defenders and past the goalkeeper.

Defending the Goal: When your opponent is shooting, you become the goalkeeper. Keep your eyes on the goal! A pair of goalie gloves will appear just before the shot, indicating where the ball is headed. Click on the gloves as quickly as you can to make a heroic save and deny your opponent a goal.

Each match is a test of nerves. The player with the most goals at the end of the shootout wins and moves closer to the final. Good luck!
