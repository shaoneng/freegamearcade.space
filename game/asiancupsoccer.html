Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Step onto the pitch and feel the roar of the crowd in Asian Cup Soccer! Your journey begins by selecting one of Asia's top national teams to represent in a high-pressure knockout tournament. Your goal is to advance through each round and claim the championship trophy.

Shooting: When it's your turn to take a free kick, an arrow will appear on the field. The controls are a simple three-click process. Click once to set the horizontal direction of your shot. Click a second time to set the height. A final click determines the curve, allowing you to bend the ball around the wall of defenders. Master this timing to score incredible goals!

Goalkeeping: When your opponent is shooting, you become the last line of defense. A pair of goalkeeper gloves will appear on screen just before the shot, indicating where the ball is heading. Click on the gloves quickly to make a diving save and deny the other team a goal.

Each match is a penalty shootout showdown. The team that scores the most goals wins the match and moves on to the next round. Good luck!
