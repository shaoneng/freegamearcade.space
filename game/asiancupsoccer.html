Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Step into the boots of a soccer star and lead your nation to glory in the Asian Cup! The roar of the crowd, the intense pressure—it all comes down to your skill in this thrilling free-kick showdown.

Your journey begins by choosing one of 12 top national teams from Asia. Once you've picked your side, you'll enter the knockout stage, where every match is a must-win to advance towards the championship.

Taking the Kick

When it's your turn to shoot, an arrow will appear on the field to guide your aim. The controls are simple but require precise timing:

  • Click once to set the direction of your shot.
  • Click a second time to determine the height.
  • Click a third time to add a curve, bending the ball around the defenders.

Playing as the Goalkeeper

When you're defending the goal, stay alert! A pair of goalkeeper gloves will appear to show you where the opponent is aiming. Click on the gloves as quickly as you can to make a heroic save and deny them a goal.

Each match is a showdown where the team with the most goals wins and moves on. Can you handle the pressure, win cool trophies, and lift the cup for your country?
