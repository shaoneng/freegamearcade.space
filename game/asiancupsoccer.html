Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Score a game-winning goal with the whole stadium watching! In Asian Cup Soccer, you'll compete in a high-pressure free-kick tournament. The goal is simple: outscore your opponent and advance through the knockout rounds to become the champion.

First, select your favorite national team from a lineup of 12, including powerhouses like Japan, South Korea, India, and Australia. You'll begin your journey in the Round of 16.

When it's your turn to kick, an arrow will appear on the field. The controls are a simple three-click process: click once to set the direction, a second time to determine the height of your shot, and a final time to add a curve, helping you bend the ball around the defensive wall.

When you're on defense, it's your chance to be the hero goalkeeper. A pair of gloves will appear on the screen indicating where the opponent is aiming. Click on the gloves quickly to make a crucial save and deny them a goal.

Each match is a penalty shootout showdown. The player with the most goals at the end wins and moves on to the next round. Good luck on your path to winning the cup!
