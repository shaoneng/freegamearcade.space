Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Step onto the pitch and feel the roar of the crowd in Asian Cup Soccer! This thrilling free-kick game puts you in the heart of a major tournament. Choose your favorite national team from Asia's best and lead them through the intense knockout rounds to championship glory.

To begin, select your squad from a lineup of 12 top national teams, including powerhouses like Japan, South Korea, India, and Australia. Each match is a high-stakes penalty shootout where every goal counts.

Kicking: When it's your turn to take a shot, an arrow will appear on the field. Timing is everything! Click once to set the direction of your kick, a second time to determine the height, and a final click to add a curve, bending the ball around the defenders. Master the three-click system to score unforgettable goals.

Defending: When you're the goalkeeper, your reflexes are put to the test. As the opponent shoots, a pair of goalkeeper gloves will flash on the screen, indicating the ball's trajectory. Click on the gloves quickly to make a crucial save and keep your team in the game.

Each match is a head-to-head battle. The player with the most goals at the end of the shootout wins and advances to the next round. Can you handle the pressure and lift the trophy?
