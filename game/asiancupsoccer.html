Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Your journey begins by selecting one of 12 top Asian national teams. Your goal is to battle through the knockout stages of the tournament, from the Round of 16 all the way to the final, aiming to become the champion.

When it's your turn to take a free kick, an arrow will appear on the field. The controls are simple but require precision. Click once to set the direction of your shot. Click a second time to set the height. A final click allows you to add curve to the ball, helping you bend it around the wall of defenders and past the goalkeeper.

When your opponent is shooting, you take on the role of the goalkeeper. Pay close attention to the screen! A pair of goalie gloves will briefly appear, indicating where the ball is headed. You must click on that spot quickly to make a crucial save and keep the ball out of your net. The player with the most goals at the end of the shootout wins the match and advances to the next round.
