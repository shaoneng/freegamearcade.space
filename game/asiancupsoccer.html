Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Welcome to the stadium! Asian Cup Soccer is a fast-paced penalty shootout game with simple controls that are easy to master. Your goal is to outscore your opponent and lead your team to victory in the tournament.

Choosing Your Team: Start by selecting one of the 12 top national teams from Asia. Represent your favorite country as you enter the knockout stages.

Taking the Shot: When it's your turn to kick, an arrow will appear. Your timing is key:

  • Click once to set the horizontal direction of your shot.
  • Click a second time to set the height.
  • Click a final time to add a curve, bending the ball around the defenders.

Playing as Goalkeeper: When your opponent is shooting, it's your chance to be the hero. A pair of goalkeeper gloves will briefly appear where the ball is heading. Click on that spot quickly to make the save!

Each match is a test of skill and nerves. Score more goals than your opponent to win the round and advance closer to the championship trophy!
