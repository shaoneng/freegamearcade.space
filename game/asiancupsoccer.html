Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Welcome to the big leagues! In Asian Cup Soccer, your goal is to win the tournament by outscoring your opponents in a thrilling penalty shootout. Begin by selecting your favorite national team from a roster of 12 Asian powerhouses.

To Kick: When it's your turn to shoot, an arrow will appear. Your shot is controlled by three simple clicks. The first click sets the horizontal direction of your shot. The second click determines the height. The third and final click adds a curve to the ball, helping you bend it around the defenders and into the net!

To Defend: When you're the goalkeeper, your reflexes are key. A pair of goalie gloves will appear on the screen just before the shot, indicating where the ball is heading. Click on the gloves as quickly as you can to make a spectacular save and deny the opposition a goal.

Each match is a best-of-five shootout. Win your match to advance through the knockout rounds, from the Round of 16 all the way to the final. Can you lift the trophy?
