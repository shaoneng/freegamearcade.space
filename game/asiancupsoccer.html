Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Welcome to the pitch! Your goal is to win the Asian Cup by outscoring your opponent in a tense penalty shootout. First, choose your favorite national team and enter the knockout stage of the tournament.

Taking a Shot: When it's your turn to kick, an arrow will appear on the field. You'll need to click your mouse or tap the screen three times to set your shot perfectly. The first click determines the horizontal direction, the second sets the height, and the third adds a curve to bend the ball around the defenders. Master the timing to score amazing goals!

Playing as Goalkeeper: When your opponent is shooting, it's your time to shine as the goalie. Watch closely for a pair of gloves to appear on the screen—this indicates where the ball is going. Click or tap on the gloves as quickly as you can to make a heroic save and deny them a goal.

Each match is a showdown to see who can score the most goals. Win your matches to advance through the rounds and fight for your chance to lift the championship trophy!
