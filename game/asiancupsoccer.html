Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Your journey begins by selecting one of Asia's top 12 national teams to represent. You'll compete in a tense knockout tournament where every match is a penalty shootout. The goal is simple: score more goals than your opponent to advance to the next round and ultimately win the championship trophy!

When it's your turn to take a shot, a moving arrow will appear on the field. The controls are a simple three-click process. Click once to lock in the direction of your kick. Click a second time to set the height. A final click determines the amount of curve you want to put on the ball, perfect for getting around the defensive wall.

When you're the goalkeeper, your reflexes are key. As the opponent shoots, a pair of goalie gloves will appear on screen, indicating where the ball is headed. Click on the gloves as quickly as you can to make a dramatic save and deny your opponent a goal.
