Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Welcome to the roar of the stadium! Your goal is to lead your chosen national team through the knockout stages and win the championship. Each match is a penalty shootout, where you'll take turns shooting and defending.

Taking a Kick: When it's your turn to shoot, an arrow will appear on the field. The controls are simple: click once to set the direction of your kick. Click a second time to set the height. Click a final time to add curve to your shot, bending it around the wall of defenders. Timing is everything to score that perfect goal!

Playing as Goalkeeper: When the opposing team takes their shot, you become the goalkeeper. Watch closely for a pair of goalie gloves to appear on screen, indicating where the ball is headed. Click on that spot quickly to make a diving save and block the shot.

Winning the Match: Each match is a test of nerves. The team with the most goals at the end of the shootout wins and advances to the next round. Keep your focus, score more than your opponent, and you could become the champion of Asia!
