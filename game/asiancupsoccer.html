Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Welcome to the knockout stages of the Asian Cup! Your goal is to lead your chosen nation to victory in a series of tense penalty shootouts. Select your favorite from 12 top national teams and prepare for the high-stakes action.

When it's your turn to take a kick, the controls are simple yet skillful. You'll see an arrow on the field. The first click sets the direction of your shot. The second click determines the height. A final, third click will add a curve to the ball, helping you bend it around the wall of defenders. Master this three-click system to score unforgettable goals!

When your opponent is shooting, you'll switch roles and become the goalkeeper. Pay close attention to the screen! A pair of goalie gloves will appear, indicating where the ball is headed. Click on them quickly to make a spectacular save and deny your opponent a goal.

Each match is a head-to-head battle. The player who scores the most goals wins the shootout and advances to the next round. Can you hold your nerve and become the champion?
