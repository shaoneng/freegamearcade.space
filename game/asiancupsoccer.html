Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Your goal is to lead your chosen national team to victory in a knockout-style tournament. Each match is a penalty shootout where you alternate between taking free kicks and defending your goal.

First, pick your favorite team from a lineup of 12 top Asian nations. You'll start in the Round of 16 and must win each match to advance to the final.

Kicking: When it's your turn to shoot, an arrow will appear on the field. Click your mouse or tap the screen once to set the horizontal direction. Click a second time to set the ball's height. A final click allows you to add a curve to your shot, helping you bend it around the wall of defenders.

Goalkeeping: When the other team is shooting, it's your time to defend. A pair of goalkeeper gloves will appear on the screen, showing where the ball is heading. You must click or tap on the gloves as quickly as possible to make a crucial save!

The player with the most goals at the end of the shootout wins the match and moves on to the next round. Good luck!
