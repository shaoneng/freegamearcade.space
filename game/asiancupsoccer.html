Asian Cup Soccer

How to Play Asian Cup Soccer

Begin your journey to glory by selecting your favorite national team from a roster of 12 Asian powerhouses. You will then enter the high-stakes knockout tournament, where every match is a must-win to advance.

When it's your turn to take a free kick, the controls are simple yet skillful. An arrow will appear on the field. The first click sets the direction of your shot. The second click determines the height, and a final, third click adds a curve to bend the ball around the defenders. Master this three-click system to score spectacular goals!

When your opponent is shooting, you become the goalkeeper. Pay close attention to the screen as a pair of gloves will appear, indicating where the ball is headed. Click quickly on the gloves to make a crucial save and protect your lead. The player with the most goals at the end of the shootout wins the match and moves one step closer to the championship!
