A Small World Cup

How to Play A Small World Cup

Welcome to the wacky world of A Small World Cup, where the rules of soccer are thrown out the window! Your objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent. However, instead of kicking the ball, you'll be launching your entire player at it like a human cannonball.

The controls are incredibly intuitive. Simply click and hold (or tap and hold on mobile) on your player. Drag back to set the power and angle of your launch, just like a slingshot. Release your click or tap to send your player flying towards the ball. The further you pull back, the more powerful the launch will be.

Timing and aiming are everything! Try to hit the ball at the right angle to send it into the opponent's goal. You can also use your player to block incoming shots or even knock the opposing player out of the way.
