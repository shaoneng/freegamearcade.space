A Small World Cup

How to Play A Small World Cup

Welcome to the most unconventional soccer game you'll ever play! The goal in A Small World Cup is simple: score more goals than your opponent. However, the method is anything but ordinary. Instead of controlling players to kick the ball, you'll be launching your entire player head-first at it!

To play, simply click (or tap and hold on mobile) on your player. Drag your cursor or finger back to aim and build power, just like a slingshot. A guiding line will show you the trajectory of your launch. When you're ready, release the click or your finger to send your ragdoll player flying towards the ball. The key is to time your launches and aim precisely to knock the ball into the opponent's goal.

You can choose your favorite national team and compete in various tournaments, including the World Cup and Euro Cup 2024. The game can be played solo against the computer or in a 2-player mode on the same device for some hilarious head-to-head action. Master the art of the ragdoll dive, anticipate the chaotic bounces, and lead your team to victory!
