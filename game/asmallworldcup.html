A Small World Cup

How to Play A Small World Cup

Welcome to the most unconventional soccer match you'll ever play! In A Small World Cup, the rules are simple but the results are hilariously unpredictable. Instead of kicking the ball, you launch your entire player at it!

Controls for Desktop & Mobile:

  • Aim: Move your mouse or drag your finger on the screen to aim your player. A directional arrow will show you the path.
  • Launch: Click and hold the left mouse button (or tap and hold on your screen) to build power. The longer you hold, the harder you'll launch.
  • Release: Let go of the button or your finger to send your player flying towards the ball!

Your goal is to score more goals than your opponent within the time limit. You can choose to compete against the computer in various tournaments or challenge a friend in the 2-player mode. Embrace the chaos, master the physics, and lead your team to victory!
