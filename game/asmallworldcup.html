A Small World Cup

How to Play A Small World Cup

A Small World Cup is an hilariously unconventional 2-player soccer game where the players themselves become the projectiles! Forget traditional kicking; in this game, it's all about launching your ragdoll footballers.

To play, you'll aim your ragdoll player, pull back to set the power and direction, and then release to send them diving headfirst towards the ball. The goal is to propel the ball into the opponent's net using your airborne players. The physics-based gameplay means every shot is unpredictable and often leads to laugh-out-loud moments, including accidental self-goals!

Choose your national team and challenge a friend in exciting 1v1 matches. Compete for glory in various tournaments like the Euro Cup 2024, World Cup, and America Cup. Master the unique ragdoll launch mechanics to score fantastic goals and win the cup!
