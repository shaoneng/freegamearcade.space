A Small World Cup

How to Play A Small World Cup

Welcome to the most unconventional soccer match you'll ever play! In A Small World Cup, the goal is simple: get the ball into your opponent's net. The method, however, is anything but ordinary. Instead of kicking the ball, you'll be flinging your entire player at it!

To play, simply click (or tap) and drag on your ragdoll player to aim your shot. Pull back to build power, and then release to launch your player headfirst towards the ball. It's a game of physics and timing, where a well-aimed dive can lead to a glorious goal, or a hilarious own-goal!

You can choose your favorite national team and compete against the computer or challenge a friend in the 2-player mode. Compete in various tournaments and see if you have what it takes to become the champion of this chaotic and funny soccer game.
