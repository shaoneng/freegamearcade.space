A Small World Cup

How to Play A Small World Cup

Welcome to the most unconventional soccer match you'll ever play! In A Small World Cup, the rules are simple but the results are hilarious. Instead of kicking the ball, you'll be launching your entire player at it like a human cannonball.

The Goal: Score more goals than your opponent before time runs out. Simple, right?

Controls: Use your mouse or touch screen to aim your ragdoll player. Click and drag (or tap and hold) to pull back and build power. Release to send your player flying towards the ball. The angle and power of your launch are key to hitting the ball just right and sending it into the net.

Game Modes: You can choose your favorite national team and compete against the computer in various tournaments, or you can challenge a friend in the exciting 2-player mode on the same device. Prepare for chaotic fun, surprising goals, and maybe even scoring on yourself a few times!
