A Small World Cup

How to Play A Small World Cup

Welcome to the most chaotic soccer game ever! In A Small World Cup, you don't control players to kick the ball. Instead, you launch the entire player, ragdoll-style, directly at the ball. The objective is simple: get the ball into your opponent's goal by any means necessary.

To play, simply click (or tap) and drag your player. This action aims your shot and builds power. The further you drag, the more powerful the launch will be. Release the mouse button (or your finger) to send your footballer flying across the field. It's all about timing and trajectory!

Mastering the physics is key. Sometimes a gentle nudge is better than a full-speed dive. You can even use your opponent's players to ricochet the ball into the net. Choose your national team and compete in various cup tournaments. Be prepared for hilarious moments, surprising goals, and maybe even a few own goals along the way!
