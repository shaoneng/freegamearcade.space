A Small World Cup

How to Play A Small World Cup

Welcome to the most unconventional soccer match you'll ever play! The goal in A Small World Cup is simple: score more goals than your opponent. However, the method is anything but ordinary. Instead of controlling players who kick a ball, you'll be launching the players themselves!

To play, simply click (or tap) and drag your ragdoll player. Aim your trajectory towards the soccer ball, pull back to build power, and then release to send your player flying across the field. The further you pull back, the more powerful the launch will be.

Mastering the physics is key. Use your player to block shots, knock opponents out of the way, and, of course, slam into the ball to send it into the goal. The game can be played solo against the computer or with a friend in 2-player mode on the same device. Choose your team and get ready for some hilarious, ragdoll-fueled soccer chaos!
