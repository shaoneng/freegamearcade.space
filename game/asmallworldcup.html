A Small World Cup

How to Play A Small World Cup

The objective in A Small World Cup is simple: score more goals than your opponent. However, the method is anything but ordinary! Forget traditional soccer controls; this game is all about chaotic, physics-based fun.

Instead of kicking the ball, you launch your ragdoll player directly at it. To play, use your mouse or finger to click and drag on your footballer. This action sets the power and trajectory of your dive. Release the button to send your player flying across the pitch.

Mastering the angle and force is key to outmaneuvering your opponent and scoring spectacular (and often accidental) goals. Choose your national team, compete in various cups, and enjoy the hilarious, unpredictable action. You can even challenge a friend in the 2-player mode!
