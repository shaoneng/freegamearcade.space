A Small World Cup

How to Play A Small World Cup

The goal in A Small World Cup is simple: score more goals than your opponent. However, the gameplay is anything but traditional! Instead of controlling your player's movement, you will launch them like a projectile towards the soccer ball.

To play, simply click (or tap on mobile) and drag your player away from the ball. This action sets the power and angle of your launch. Release the mouse button or your finger to send your ragdoll footballer flying. The further you pull back, the more powerful your launch will be. Master the physics to score spectacular goals!

You can choose to represent your favorite national team and compete in various cup tournaments against the computer, or you can challenge a friend directly in the exciting 2-player mode. Master the chaos and aim for victory!
