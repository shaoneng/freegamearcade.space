A Small World Cup

How to Play A Small World Cup

A Small World Cup turns soccer on its head! Instead of kicking the ball, your goal is to launch your ragdoll player directly at it. Aim your player, pull back to build power, and release to send them flying across the field. It's a game of chaotic physics where strategy meets slapstick comedy.

You can play solo against the computer or challenge a friend in local 2-player mode. Select your favorite national team and compete in various tournaments, including the World Cup, America Cup, and Euro Cup 2024, to prove your ragdoll-flinging skills and bring home the trophy.

The controls are simple: click and drag your player to aim and set the power of your launch. Release the mouse button to dive. Master the angle and force of your launches to outsmart your opponent, block their shots, and score a spectacular goal!
