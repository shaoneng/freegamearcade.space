Aventador Vice Crime City

How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Welcome to the sprawling metropolis of Aventador Vice Crime City! Your goal is to become the king of the streets by completing missions, performing insane stunts, and earning cash (CR). Take your loyal dog companion and get ready for an adrenaline-fueled adventure. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your car. Hit the Spacebar for the handbrake to execute sharp turns and drifts. For a burst of speed, press Left Shift to use your nitro. You can also change your perspective with the C key and activate slow motion with the G key.

The city is your playground, and there are multiple ways to play. The game features four distinct modes: Drift, where you rack up points with stylish slides; Stunt, where you perform death-defying jumps and tricks; Police, a high-stakes escape from the law; and Kill, where you can earn quick cash (but attract police attention). You'll start with a 5,000 CR gift. Earn more by drifting, speeding, completing missions, or... less savory activities. Use your CR to unlock the different game modes and take on bigger challenges for greater rewards.

Make your ride truly your own in the garage. You can change your vehicle's body and rim color, and even add flashy neon lights to stand out. To get a real edge on the competition, invest your CR in performance upgrades. Boost your Fuel capacity, increase your Nitro duration, improve your Drift Factor, and enhance your Engine for more power. Don't forget to keep an eye on your fuel gauge and visit gas stations when needed!
