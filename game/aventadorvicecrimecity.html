Aventador Vice Crime City

How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Take control of your high-performance Aventador using the WASD or Arrow Keys for driving. Use the Spacebar for the handbrake to execute sharp turns and drifts. For a burst of speed, hit Left Shift to activate your nitro. You can also cycle through different camera views with the C key and activate a cool slow-motion effect with the G key.

Your adventure in Vice Crime City begins with a starting bonus of 5,000 CR. The goal is to earn more cash by pushing your driving to the limit. Gain CR by drifting, hitting top speeds, performing tricks on stunt ramps, and completing missions. The game features four distinct modes to test your abilities: Drift, Stunt, Police, and Kill. Each mode offers unique challenges and bigger rewards as you progress.

Make your car truly your own! Head to the garage to customize your vehicle's body and rim color, and add flashy neon lights to stand out. To gain a competitive edge, invest your CR in performance upgrades. Boost your Fuel capacity, Nitro power, Drift Factor, and Engine performance to dominate every challenge the city throws at you.

Keep an eye on your fuel gauge and visit gas stations when you're running low. If you crash, you can repair your car for free at any time. Don't forget to log in daily to claim your free CR rewards and keep the action going!
