Aventador Vice Crime City

How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Welcome to the sprawling metropolis of Aventador Vice Crime City! This isn't just a driving game; it's a full-blown urban adventure. Hop into your high-performance car with your loyal dog companion and explore a realistic open world filled with wild missions and high-speed action. Your goal is to earn currency (CR) to unlock new modes, customize your ride, and become the king of the streets.

Controls:
  • Drive: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Handbrake: Spacebar
  • Nitro Boost: Left Shift
  • Change Camera View: C
  • Slow Motion: G

You start with a 5,000 CR welcome bonus. Earn more by drifting, hitting top speeds, landing incredible stunt jumps, and completing missions. You can also earn cash by causing a little chaos, but be warned: attract too much attention, and the police will be on your tail!

The game features four exciting modes to test your skills: Drift (score points by sliding sideways), Stunt (perform death-defying jumps and tricks), Police (escape relentless police chases), and Kill (earn cash from pedestrians, but watch out for the cops!).

Make your car your own by changing its body and rim colors or adding flashy neon lights. You can also head to the garage to upgrade key performance stats like your Engine, Nitro, Fuel, and Drift Factor to gain an edge on the competition.
