Aventador Vice Crime City

How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Welcome to the high-stakes world of Aventador Vice Crime City! Your goal is to explore the open city, take on thrilling missions, and earn CR (in-game currency) to become the ultimate driver. You start with a 5,000 CR welcome gift to get you going.

Master the controls to dominate the streets:

  • Drive: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Handbrake: Spacebar
  • Nitro Boost: Left Shift
  • Change Camera View: C
  • Slow Motion: G

Earn more CR by performing insane drifts, hitting top speeds, completing missions, or even causing a little chaos by hitting pedestrians (but be warned, the police will come after you!). Use your CR to unlock four distinct game modes: Drift, Stunt, Police (escape chases), and Kill (pedestrian challenges).

Make your car your own by visiting the garage. Customize its body color, rims, and add flashy neon lights. You can also spend your hard-earned CR to upgrade key performance stats like your engine, nitro, and fuel capacity to stay ahead of the competition.
