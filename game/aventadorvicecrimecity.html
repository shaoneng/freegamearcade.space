Aventador Vice Crime City

How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Welcome to the sprawling metropolis of Aventador Vice Crime City! Your goal is to become the king of the streets in this open-world driving adventure. Start with a welcome gift of 5,000 CR and earn more by drifting, speeding, completing missions, or performing daring stunts. Use your earnings to unlock new game modes, customize your ride with fresh paint and neon lights, and upgrade its performance to dominate the competition.

Mastering the controls is key to your success:

  • Drive: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Handbrake: Spacebar
  • Nitro Boost: Left Shift
  • Change Camera View: C
  • Slow Motion: G

The game features four thrilling modes to test your skills. In Drift mode, you'll rack up points with insane slides. Stunt mode challenges you to perform death-defying tricks. In Police mode, you must escape relentless police chases after breaking the law. Finally, Kill mode has you earning cash by hitting pedestrians, but be warned—the cops will be on your tail after just two hits!

Keep an eye on your fuel gauge and head to a Gas Station when you're running low. Don't forget to log in daily for rewards, and if your car gets wrecked, you can repair it for free at any time. Now, get in your car, hit the gas, and own the city!
