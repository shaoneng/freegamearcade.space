Aventador Vice Crime City

How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Welcome to the sun-drenched, high-octane streets of Aventador Vice Crime City! Your goal is to become the king of the road by exploring the city, completing thrilling missions, and earning currency (CR) to upgrade your ride. You start with a 5,000 CR welcome bonus to get you going.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your car. Hit the Spacebar for the handbrake to execute sharp turns and drifts. For a burst of speed, press Left Shift to activate your nitro. You can also cycle through different camera views with the C key and activate a slow-motion effect with the G key.

The game features four distinct modes to test your skills, each costing 250 CR to unlock initially. In Drift Mode, you'll score points by sliding sideways. Stunt Mode challenges you to perform daredevil tricks. In Police Mode, you must escape from the cops after breaking the law. Finally, Kill Mode involves causing mayhem, but be warnedâ€”the police will be on your tail!

Spend your hard-earned CR in the garage to customize your vehicle. Change the body and rim colors, and add stylish neon lights. You can also upgrade essential stats like Fuel, Nitro, Drift Factor, and Engine power to dominate the streets.
