Aventador Vice Crime City

How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Welcome to the high-octane streets of Aventador Vice Crime City! Your goal is to become the ultimate urban driver by exploring the city, completing missions, and earning CR (cash). You'll start your journey with a welcome gift of 5,000 CR to get you going.

Controls:

  • Drive: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Handbrake: Spacebar
  • Nitro Boost: Left Shift
  • Change Camera View: C Key
  • Slow Motion: G Key

Game Modes: The city offers four distinct challenges, each unlocked with CR. Master them all to maximize your rewards!

  • Drift: Slide around corners to rack up points before time expires.
  • Stunt: Perform death-defying jumps and tricks on specially designed ramps.
  • Police: You've broken the law! Evade the relentless police pursuit and escape.
  • Kill: Earn quick cash by causing chaos, but be warned—the cops will be on your tail after two hits.

Customization and Upgrades: Make your Aventador truly yours. Head to the garage to change its body and rim color, and add awesome neon lights. You can also spend your hard-earned CR to upgrade key stats like Fuel, Nitro, Drift Factor, and the Engine for superior performance. Keep an eye on your fuel gauge and visit gas stations to refuel when needed. If you crash, don't worry—you can repair your car for free at any time!
