Aventador Vice Crime City

How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Welcome to the sprawling, sun-drenched streets of Aventador Vice Crime City! Your goal is to become the ultimate driver by exploring the city, completing missions, and earning cash (CR) to upgrade your ride. Jump in your car with your loyal dog and get ready for some high-octane action.

Master the controls to dominate the streets:
• Drive: Use WASD or the Arrow Keys.
• Handbrake: Press the Spacebar for sharp turns and drifts.
• Nitro Boost: Hit Left Shift for a burst of speed.
• Change Camera: Press C to switch your view.
• Slow Motion: Press G for epic, cinematic moments.

The city offers four thrilling game modes to test your skills, each costing CR to enter. The entry fee and rewards increase as you level up:
• Drift: Slide around corners and rack up points by performing the craziest drifts before time runs out.
• Stunt: Take on dangerous ramps and obstacles, pushing your car to its limits to perform incredible stunts.
• Police: You've broken the law! Evade relentless police chases and prove you're the best escape artist.
• Kill: Earn quick cash by hitting pedestrians, but be warned—after two hits, the cops will be on your tail!

Make your car truly your own. Head to the garage to change your vehicle's body and rim color, and add flashy neon lights. You can also spend your hard-earned CR to upgrade key performance stats like Fuel, Nitro, Drift Factor, and the Engine to gain a competitive edge. Keep an eye on your fuel, log in for daily rewards, and become a legend of Vice Crime City!
