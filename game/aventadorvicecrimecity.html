How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Welcome to the sun-drenched, action-packed streets of Aventador Vice Crime City! Your adventure begins with a cool 5,000 CR in your pocket and your loyal dog companion by your side. The goal is to explore the vast city, complete thrilling missions, and earn cash to become the ultimate street legend. Master your driving skills to unlock new challenges and upgrade your ride.

Take control of your powerful vehicle using the WASD or Arrow Keys for driving. Use the Spacebar for a sharp handbrake turn, and hit Left Shift to unleash a powerful nitro boost. You can cycle through different camera angles with the C key and activate a cinematic slow-motion effect with the G key.

The game features four distinct modes, each offering a unique challenge. Use your CR to unlock them and take on new objectives. In Drift mode, you'll slide through corners to rack up points against the clock. Stunt mode challenges you to perform death-defying jumps and tricks. Make your Aventador truly yours. Head to the garage to customize your car's body and rim colors, and add stylish neon lights. You can also spend your hard-earned CR to upgrade key performance stats like your engine, nitro capacity, and drift factor. Keep an eye on your fuel gauge and visit gas stations to refuel. Log in daily for bonus rewards, repair your car when it gets damaged, and dominate the city streets!
