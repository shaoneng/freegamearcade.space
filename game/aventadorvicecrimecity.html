Aventador Vice Crime City

How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Welcome to the sprawling, sun-soaked streets of Aventador Vice Crime City! Your goal is to become the ultimate driver by exploring the city, completing missions, and earning cash (CR). Jump into your high-performance Aventador with your trusty dog companion and get ready for an adrenaline-fueled urban adventure.

Mastering your vehicle is key to ruling the city. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive. Pull off sharp turns with the Spacebar for the handbrake, and get a burst of speed with Nitro by pressing Left Shift. You can change your perspective with the C key for the perfect camera view and trigger epic slow-motion moments with the G key.

You start with a 5,000 CR welcome bonus. Earn more by drifting, speeding, landing incredible stunts, and completing missions. The game features four distinct modes to test your skills: Drift, Stunt, Police (escape the cops), and Kill (cause chaos for cash). Each mode has an entry fee, but the rewards grow as you progress!

Make your ride truly your own in the garage. Customize your car's body and rim colors, and add flashy neon lights to stand out. Don't forget to upgrade your car's performance by boosting its Fuel, Nitro, Drift Factor, and Engine power. Keep an eye on your fuel gauge and head to a Gas Station when you're running low.
