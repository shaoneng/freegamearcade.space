Aventador Vice Crime City

How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Get behind the wheel and explore the sprawling metropolis of Vice Crime City! Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your high-performance Aventador. Use the Spacebar for the handbrake to execute sharp turns, hit Left Shift for a nitro boost, and press C to change your camera view. The core of the game is to earn CR (the in-game currency) by performing drifts, hitting high speeds, completing missions, and pulling off insane stunts.

The city offers four distinct and thrilling game modes to test your driving prowess. In Drift mode, you'll need to slide your way to a high score before time runs out. Stunt mode challenges you to perform death-defying tricks. Break the law and you'll find yourself in Police mode, trying to escape relentless pursuers. Finally, in Kill mode, you can earn extra cash by causing mayhem, but be warnedâ€”the police will be on your tail quickly!

Make your car truly your own. Head to the garage to change your vehicle's body and rim colors, and even add flashy neon lights. To gain a competitive edge, you can also upgrade key performance stats like your engine, fuel capacity, nitro, and drift factor. Don't forget to keep an eye on your fuel gauge and visit a gas station when you're running low!
