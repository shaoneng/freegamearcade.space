Aventador Vice Crime City

How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Welcome to the high-octane world of Aventador Vice Crime City! Your goal is to become the king of the streets in this open-world adventure. You'll start with a 5,000 CR welcome gift to kickstart your career. Earn more CR by drifting around corners, hitting insane top speeds, pulling off impossible tricks on stunt ramps, and completing thrilling missions.

Master the controls to dominate the city:

  • Drive:WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Handbrake / Drift:Spacebar
  • Nitro Boost:Left Shift
  • Change Camera:C
  • Slow Motion:G

The game features four distinct modes, each unlocked for 250 CR. As you level up, the entry fee increases, but so do the potential rewards!

  • Drift: Make the craziest drifts to earn maximum points before time runs out.
  • Stunt: Push your speed and skill to the limit with dangerous, high-flying stunts.
  • Police: You've broken the law! Evade the relentless police force in high-speed chases.
  • Kill: Cause mayhem by hitting pedestrians for cash, but bewareâ€”after 2 hits, the cops are coming for you!

Make your car stand out by customizing its body and rim color, and add eye-catching neon lights. To get a real performance edge, upgrade key stats like Fuel, Nitro, Drift Factor, and Engine power. Keep an eye on your fuel gauge and refill at gas stations when you see the icon appear on screen.
