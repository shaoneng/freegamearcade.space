Aventador Vice Crime City

How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Welcome to the streets of Aventador Vice Crime City! Your goal is to become the king of the urban jungle by earning cash (CR) and mastering your driving skills. You start your adventure with a 5,000 CR welcome bonus. From there, you can earn more by drifting, hitting top speeds, completing missions, or even taking out pedestrians (but be ready for the police!).

Controls:

  • Drive: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Handbrake: Spacebar
  • Nitro Boost: Left Shift
  • Change Camera View: C
  • Slow Motion: G

The game features four exciting modes to test your abilities, each costing 250 CR to unlock. Compete in Drift challenges to rack up points with stylish slides, take on dangerous jumps in Stunt mode, or try to outrun the law in the intense Police chase mode. In Kill mode, you can earn cash by hitting pedestrians, but be warnedâ€”the cops will be on your tail after just two hits!

Use your hard-earned CR to customize your ride with new paint jobs, rim colors, and eye-catching neon lights. You can also head to the garage to upgrade your car's Fuel, Nitro, Drift Factor, and Engine for a serious performance boost. Keep an eye on your fuel gauge, log in for daily rewards, and remember you can repair your car for free at any time!
