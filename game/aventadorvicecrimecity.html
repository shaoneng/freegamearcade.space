Aventador Vice Crime City

How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Master the streets using your keyboard. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your car. When you need to stop on a dime or initiate a drift, hit the Spacebar for the handbrake. For a burst of speed, activate your nitro with the Left Shift key. You can also cycle through different camera views with the C key and enter slow-motion with the G key for those epic moments.

Your goal is to become the king of the city. You'll start with a welcome gift of 5,000 CR. Earn more CR by performing insane drifts, hitting top speeds, completing missions, or even causing a little chaos by taking out pedestrians (but be warned, the police will come after you!).

The game features four distinct modes to test your skills: Drift, where you rack up points with stylish slides; Stunt, where you push your car to the limit on dangerous ramps; Police, a high-octane escape mode from relentless cops; and Kill, where you earn cash by hitting pedestrians. Each mode has an entry fee that increases as you level up, but so do the rewards!

Make your ride your own by customizing its body and rim color, and even add neon lights. You can also upgrade key stats like Fuel, Nitro, and Engine power. Don't forget to refuel at gas stations, collect daily rewards for free CR, and repair your car for free whenever it gets too damaged.
