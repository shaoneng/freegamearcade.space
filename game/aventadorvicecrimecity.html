Aventador Vice Crime City

How to Play Aventador Vice Crime City

Get ready for an adrenaline-fueled urban adventure! Your main goal is to explore the open-world city, complete missions, and earn CR (cash) to become the ultimate street king. You start with a 5,000 CR bonus to kick things off.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your powerful car. Pull off sharp turns with the Spacebar (Handbrake) and get a burst of speed by pressing Left Shift (Nitro). You can switch camera angles with the C key and activate a cool slow-motion effect with the G key.

The game features four exciting modes: Drift, where you score points for stylish slides; Stunt, for pulling off daredevil tricks; Police, where you must escape relentless cops; and Kill, where you earn cash by hitting pedestrians (but this will attract police attention!).

Use your CR to customize your ride with new body and rim colors, and add flashy neon lights. You can also upgrade your car's Fuel, Nitro, Drift Factor, and Engine for better performance on the streets. Keep an eye on your fuel gauge and find a gas station when it gets low!
