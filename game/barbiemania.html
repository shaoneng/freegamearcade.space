How to Play Barbiemania

Welcome to Barbiemania! Your goal is to become Ellie's personal stylist and create three unique looks for her. You'll need to design outfits and apply makeup for three different events: a casual everyday look, a professional work style, and a glamorous party ensemble.

Use your mouse to browse through the extensive collection of clothing, accessories, hairstyles, and makeup options. Click on items to apply them to Ellie. Pay attention to her subtle reactionsâ€”she might give you a hint about her favorites!

Once you are satisfied with all three makeovers, it's time for the grand finale! Send your creations to the photoshoot event to capture stunning pictures of Ellie's new styles. You can save the photos to your device to share your fashion masterpieces with friends.
