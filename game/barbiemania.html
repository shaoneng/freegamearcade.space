Barbiemania

How to Play Barbiemania

Your mission in Barbiemania is to become the ultimate fashion stylist for the lovely Ellie. The game is controlled entirely with your mouse or by tapping on the screen.

You will be tasked with creating three unique looks for Ellie, each for a different event: a casual everyday style, a professional work outfit, and a glamorous party look. Start by selecting one of the occasions.

Use your creative eye to browse through a vast collection of makeup, hairstyles, clothing, and accessories. Click on the items you want to apply to Ellie. Pay close attention to her subtle movements and expressions—she might give you a hint about her favorite choices!

Once you have perfected all three styles, you can send your creations to a special photoshoot event. Capture stunning pictures of your work to save to your device or share with friends to showcase your fashion skills!
