Barbiemania

How to Play Barbiemania

Welcome to Barbiemania, where your fashion expertise is needed! Your main goal is to give the iconic doll, Ellie, a complete makeover for three different events: her everyday look, her professional office attire, and a glamorous party outfit. Use your creativity to mix and match styles and make her shine.

Playing is simple and intuitive. Use your mouse to click through the extensive options for makeup, hairstyles, clothing, and accessories. As you browse, pay attention to Ellie! Her lifelike animations, like glancing at items she likes, will give you subtle clues to help you create the perfect ensemble.

Once you are satisfied with all three distinct looks, it's time for the grand finale. Send your creations to the photoshoot event to capture your amazing work. You can save the stunning pictures to your device or share them with friends to show off your styling skills!
