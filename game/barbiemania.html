Barbiemania

How to Play Barbiemania

Welcome to Barbiemania! Your goal is to become Ellie's personal stylist and create three stunning looks for different occasions: a casual everyday outfit, a professional look for work, and a glamorous party ensemble.

Use your mouse to interact with the game. Click on the various makeup palettes, hairstyles, clothing items, and accessories to apply them to Ellie. Experiment with different combinations to find the perfect style for each theme.

Pay close attention to Ellie! She's not just a static doll; she'll react to your choices with subtle animations, giving you hints about her favorite items. Let her guide you to create looks she truly loves.

Once you have perfected all three styles, it's time for the final step. Take your creations to the photoshoot event to capture your amazing work. You can then save the pictures to your device or share them with your friends!
