Barbiemania

How to Play Barbiemania

Your goal in Barbiemania is to become the ultimate stylist for Ellie, a beautiful and iconic doll. You are tasked with creating three distinct and fabulous looks for her: a chic everyday outfit, a professional look for work, and a glamorous ensemble for a party.

Using your mouse or touch screen, navigate through the extensive makeup and wardrobe options. Start with the makeup, applying eyeshadow, lipstick, blush, and more to enhance Ellie's features. Then, move on to the closet to mix and match tops, bottoms, dresses, and accessories to assemble the perfect outfit for each occasion.

Pay attention to Ellie's subtle reactions; she'll give you hints about what she likes! Once you are satisfied with all three makeovers, it's time for the final step. Send your creations to the photoshoot event to capture stunning pictures of your work. You can save these images to your device or share them with friends to show off your fashion sense!
