How to Play Barbiemania

Barbiemania is your chance to become a top fashion stylist! Your goal is to create three unique and stunning looks for Ellie: one for everyday wear, one for work, and one for a party. Use your mouse to click and select from a wide array of makeup, hairstyles, and clothing options.

For each look, explore the different categoriesâ€”from eyeshadow and lipstick to dresses and accessories. Experiment with various combinations to find the perfect style that suits each occasion. Pay attention to Ellie's subtle reactions; she might give you clues about her favorite choices!

Once you have perfected all three outfits, it's time for the grand finale! Send your creations to the photoshoot event. You can then capture beautiful pictures of your work to save to your device or share with your friends to show off your incredible styling skills.
