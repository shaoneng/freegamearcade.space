How to Play Barbiemania

Welcome to Barbiemania! Your goal is to become Ellie's personal stylist and create three distinct and fabulous looks for her. You'll be designing outfits and applying makeup for three different occasions: a casual everyday look, a professional work ensemble, and a dazzling party outfit.

All interactions in the game are done with your mouse. Simply click on the makeup palettes, brushes, hairstyles, and clothing items to apply them to Ellie. Experiment with different combinations of cosmetics, accessories, and apparel to find the perfect style for each event.
