Barbiemania

How to Play Barbiemania

Welcome to Barbiemania! Your mission is to become the ultimate stylist for the stunning doll, Ellie. You will be creating three distinct and fabulous looks for her: one for everyday wear, one for the office, and one for a glamorous party.

Use your mouse to click on the various makeup options, hairstyles, and clothing items. Experiment with different combinations to find the perfect style for each occasion. Watch Ellie closely; her subtle reactions might give you a hint about her favorite choices!

Once you are satisfied with all three makeovers, it's time for the grand finale! Send your creations to the photoshoot event. You can then capture pictures of your amazing work to save on your device or share with your friends. Pick up your virtual makeup brushes and let your creativity shine!
