How to Play Barbiemania

Welcome to the glamorous world of Barbiemania! Your goal is to become the ultimate stylist for the stunningly beautiful Ellie. You'll be creating three unique looks for her, each for a different occasion: a casual everyday style, a professional work outfit, and a dazzling party ensemble.

Use your mouse (or tap on a touch screen) to browse through the extensive collection of makeup, hairstyles, clothes, and accessories. Click on items to apply them to Ellie and see how they look. Pay attention to her subtle reactionsâ€”she might give you a hint about her favorites!

Once you are satisfied with all three makeovers, it's time for the grand finale! Send your creations to the photoshoot event. Here, you can capture professional-quality pictures of your designs. Save the images to your device to keep a portfolio of your work or share them with your friends to show off your styling skills.
