Barbiemania

How to Play Barbiemania

Welcome to the fabulous world of Barbiemania! Your goal is to become Ellie's personal stylist and create three stunning looks for different events: a casual everyday outfit, a professional look for work, and a glamorous party ensemble.

Use your mouse to click or tap on the various makeup options, hairstyles, clothing, and accessories. Experiment with different combinations to find the perfect style for each occasion. The game features incredibly realistic graphics, making the experience truly immersive.

As you work your magic, pay close attention to Ellie. She'll give you subtle hints about her preferences through her expressions and glances. Choose the items she seems to love the most to create a look that truly shines.

Once you've finalized all three distinct styles, it's time for the grand finale! Send your creations to the photoshoot event to capture your amazing work. You can save the pictures to your device or share them with friends to show off your styling skills.
