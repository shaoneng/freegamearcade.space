Barbiemania

How to Play Barbiemania

Your goal in Barbiemania is to become Ellie's personal stylist! You'll be tasked with creating three distinct and fabulous looks for different occasions: a casual everyday style, a professional look for work, and a dazzling outfit for a party.

Use your mouse or tap the screen to interact with the extensive wardrobe and makeup palette. Select different hairstyles, tops, bottoms, dresses, and accessories to assemble the perfect ensemble. Apply makeup like eyeshadow, lipstick, and blush to complete her transformation.

Once you are satisfied with all three makeovers, send your creations to the photoshoot event. You can then capture pictures of your stunning designs to save to your device or share with friends!
