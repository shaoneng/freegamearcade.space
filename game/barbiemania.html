Barbiemania

How to Play Barbiemania

Welcome to the ultimate fashion and makeup experience! Your goal in Barbiemania is to help Ellie create three unique and stunning looks for different occasions: one for every day, one for work, and one for a party.

Use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with the game. Click through the extensive wardrobe and makeup palettes to make your selections. Choose from a variety of hairstyles, makeup options like eyeshadow and lipstick, and fashionable outfits and accessories.

As you make your choices, pay attention to Ellie! Her lifelike animations and subtle glances might give you a hint about what she likes best. Experiment with different combinations to find the perfect style for each event.

Once you have finalized all three looks, don't forget the final step! Send your creations to a virtual photoshoot to capture the moment. You can then save the beautiful pictures to your device or share them with friends to show off your amazing styling skills.
