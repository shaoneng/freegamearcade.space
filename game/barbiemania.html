Barbiemania

How to Play Barbiemania

Welcome to the fabulous world of Barbiemania! Your mission is to become the ultimate stylist for Ellie, a stunningly beautiful doll. You'll be creating three unique looks for her, each for a different occasion: every day, work, and a glamorous party.

Use your mouse to interact with the vast wardrobe and makeup kit. Select from a wide array of clothing, accessories, hairstyles, and makeup options to craft the perfect ensemble for each event. Pay close attention to Ellie; she'll offer subtle hints and reactions, like looking at her favorite items, to guide your styling choices.

Once you are satisfied with all three makeovers, it's time for the grand finale! Send your creations to the photoshoot event. Capture the perfect pictures of Ellie's new looks, which you can then save to your device or share with your friends to show off your incredible fashion sense.
