Barbiemania

How to Play Barbiemania

Your mission in Barbiemania is to become the ultimate stylist for the iconic doll, Ellie. The game challenges you to create three unique and stunning looks for different occasions: a casual everyday outfit, a sophisticated professional ensemble for work, and a glamorous look for a party.

Use your mouse or touch screen to navigate through the extensive makeup and wardrobe options. Start by selecting a makeup style, from eyeshadow and lipstick to blush and mascara. Pay attention to Ellie's subtle reactions; she might give you clues about her favorite choices!

Once the makeup is perfect, move on to the wardrobe. Mix and match tops, bottoms, dresses, and accessories to assemble the perfect outfit for each theme. After you've completed all three makeovers, you can send Ellie to a special photoshoot event to capture your creations. Save the pictures to your device or share your styling skills with friends!
