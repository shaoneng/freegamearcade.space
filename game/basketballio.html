Basketball io

How to Play Basketball io

The goal in Basketball io is to outscore your opponent in fast-paced matches. The controls are designed to be simple and intuitive, allowing you to focus on the action, from stealing the ball to executing incredible slam dunks.

As you win matches, you'll earn stars and diamonds. Use these rewards in the in-game shop to unlock a huge variety of cosmetic items. You can customize your entire team's appearance, choosing from classic players, cheerleaders, monsters, or even basketball-playing bears! You can also unlock unique basketballs, like a rainbow ball or a fireball, by earning diamonds or completing specific in-game tasks.

Challenge yourself across four different courts, including a traditional indoor arena, a streetball court, and a relaxing beachside location. Each environment offers a fresh visual style for your matches. Win five matches to get ranked and show everyone who's the king of the court!
