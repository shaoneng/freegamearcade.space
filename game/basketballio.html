Basketball io

How to Play Basketball io

Jump straight into the action in Basketball io! This game is designed for quick and fun matches with straightforward controls, so you don't need to be a pro to start playing. The main goal is to outscore your opponent in fast-paced, 3D basketball matches.

As you play and win games, you'll earn stars and diamonds. These rewards are your key to unlocking awesome content from the shop. You can customize your team's appearance with a wide variety of styles, from professional players to wacky characters like skeletons, monsters, and even bears!

You can also use your earnings to unlock new basketballs, each with a unique look. Complete in-game tasks, like stealing the ball 50 times, to get special balls like the rainbow ball or a fireball. Choose your team, pick your favorite court, and start dunking on the competition!
