Basketball io

How to Play Basketball io

Welcome to the court! The goal in Basketball io is to outscore your opponent in fast-paced matches. The game features intuitive, simple controls, allowing you to jump right into the action without a steep learning curve. Move your players around the court, steal the ball, and aim for the hoop to score points and secure victory.

Winning matches is key to progression. For every five victories, you'll earn stars and diamonds which can be used in the shop. The shop is filled with exciting customization options! You can change your team's appearance to be anything from classic NBA players to fun characters like skeletons, monsters, or even basketball-playing bears.

Beyond your team, you can also customize your ball and the court you play on. Unlock up to 10 different types of basketballs, some by earning diamonds and others by completing specific tasks, like stealing the ball 50 times to get the rainbow ball. You can also choose from four different fields, including a classic indoor court, a streetball court, or a sunny beach arena. Mix and match to find your perfect style and dominate the game!
