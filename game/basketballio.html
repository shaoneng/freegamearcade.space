How to Play Basketball io

Welcome to the court! Basketball io is designed for fun, fast-paced action with incredibly simple controls. The main objective is to outscore your opponent in thrilling basketball matches.

Use your mouse or keyboard to move your players around the court. The game handles the complex moves for you, so you can focus on strategy, positioning, and timing your shots and slam dunks. Work with your team to steal the ball from your opponents and sink it in the hoop.

Winning matches is key to progression. Each victory rewards you with stars and diamonds. You can use these currencies in the shop to unlock a huge variety of cosmetic items.
