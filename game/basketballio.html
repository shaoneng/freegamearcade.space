Basketball io

How to Play Basketball io

Welcome to Basketball io, where the action is fast and the controls are simple! The goal is to outscore your opponent in exciting 3D matches. Use your mouse or keyboard to move your player, steal the ball from the opposing team, and make your way to the basket. When you're in position, take the shot and aim for a spectacular slam dunk!

Winning matches is key to your success. Each victory earns you stars and diamonds, which are your currency for upgrading your team. Head to the shop to unlock a huge range of unique playersâ€”you can build a team of pro athletes, skeletons, monsters, or even bears! You can also purchase new basketballs with special effects, like the Rainbow Ball or the Fireball, by earning diamonds or completing specific in-game tasks.

Don't forget to customize your arena! Choose from four different courts, including a classic grandstand, a streetball court, or a sunny beach. Master the simple controls, win five matches to climb the ranks, and build the most stylish and formidable team on the court!
