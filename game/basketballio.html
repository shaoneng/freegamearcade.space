How to Play Basketball io

Jump into the action with Basketball io, an exciting sports game featuring awesome 3D graphics and incredibly simple controls. The main objective is to outscore your opponent in fast-paced matches. Use your skills to dribble, steal the ball, and perform amazing slam dunks!

As you win matches, you'll earn stars and diamonds. These are your tickets to customizing your game. Head over to the shop to unlock unique teamsâ€”play as classic NBA-style players, cheerleader girls, spooky skeletons, monsters, or even a team of basketball-loving bears!

You can also customize the ball you play with. Unlock 10 different types of balls by spending diamonds or completing in-game challenges, such as stealing the ball 50 times to get the cool rainbow ball. You can even play with a fireball! Choose from four different courts, including a classic arena, an outdoor street court, and a sunny beach court, to vary your gameplay experience.
