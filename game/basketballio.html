Basketball io

How to Play Basketball io

Welcome to Basketball io, where the action is fast and the controls are simple! The goal is to outscore your opponent in thrilling basketball matches. Use your mouse or keyboard to move your players, steal the ball, and shoot for the hoop. The intuitive controls make it easy for anyone to pick up and play, while the stunning 3D visuals bring the courts to life.

Winning is key to progression. Secure victory in five matches to earn valuable stars and diamonds. These rewards are your ticket to unlocking a world of customization. The more you win, the more you can enhance your team and show off your style on the court.

Head to the shop to spend your hard-earned currency. You can completely change your team's look, swapping out standard players for cheerleaders, skeletons, monsters, or even a team of basketball-playing bears! You can also purchase over 10 different types of balls and unlock four unique courts, from a classic arena to a street court or a sunny beach.

Some items are unlocked by completing specific challenges. For example, prove your defensive skills by stealing the ball 50 times to get the coveted rainbow ball. Keep playing, complete tasks, and build the wackiest, most skilled basketball team imaginable!
