Basketball io

How to Play Basketball io

Dive into Basketball io, a fun and accessible 3D basketball game designed for everyone. The controls are incredibly simple, allowing you to focus on the action. Move your players around the court, get close to the opponent to automatically steal the ball, and approach the net to perform spectacular slam dunks. The gameplay is fast-paced and intuitive, making it easy to pick up and play.

Your main objective is to win five matches to earn rewards like stars and diamonds. Use these to customize your experience! Head to the shop to unlock a huge variety of teams. You can play as classic NBA-style athletes, cheerleaders, spooky skeletons, or even a team of basketball-playing bears! The choice is yours.

Further personalize your game by unlocking over 10 different basketballs, each with its own style. Some are unlocked by earning diamonds, while others require completing specific tasks, like stealing the ball 50 times to get the cool rainbow ball. You can also choose from four different courts, including a professional arena, an outdoor street court, and a relaxing beachside court. Find your favorite combination and dominate the game!
