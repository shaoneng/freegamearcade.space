How to Play Basketball io

Welcome to Basketball io, where the action is fast and the controls are simple! The main goal is to outscore your opponent in exciting online matches. Use your mouse or keyboard to control your player's movement around the court.

When you're on defense, get close to the opponent with the ball to automatically steal it. Once you have possession, move towards the opponent's basket to shoot. Get close enough, and your player will automatically perform an epic slam dunk or a jump shot to score points!
