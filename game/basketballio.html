Basketball io

How to Play Basketball io

Welcome to the court! Basketball io is designed for easy pick-up-and-play fun. The controls are simple and intuitive, allowing you to focus on the action. Your main goal is to outscore your opponent and win the match.

Before you start, you can customize your team's look. As you win matches, you'll earn stars and diamonds. Use these rewards in the shop to unlock a huge variety of player stylesâ€”from professional athletes to fun characters like monsters and bears! You can also unlock new basketballs with unique designs and play on one of four different courts.

During the game, move your players to get the ball, dribble towards the opponent's hoop, and go for the shot. Master the art of the steal to take possession from the other team and set up your own spectacular slam dunks. Win five matches to earn special rewards and climb the ranks!
