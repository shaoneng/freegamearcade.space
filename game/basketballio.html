Basketball io

How to Play Basketball io

Welcome to the court! Basketball io is an exciting 3D basketball game designed for fast-paced fun with super simple controls. Your main goal is to outscore your opponent and win the match.

Before you start, you can customize your experience. Head to the shop to choose your team's style – you can play as classic NBA-style players, cheerleaders, skeletons, monsters, or even bears! You can also select from four different courts, from a professional arena to a streetball court or a sunny beach.

Winning five matches earns you stars and diamonds, which are your keys to unlocking more cool stuff. Use your earnings in the shop to buy new team styles and over 10 different types of basketballs. Some items can also be unlocked by completing specific tasks, such as stealing the ball 50 times to get the special rainbow ball. Jump in, score some epic slam dunks, and dominate the court!
