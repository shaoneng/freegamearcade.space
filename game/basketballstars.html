Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Welcome to the court! In Basketball Stars, your objective is to outscore your opponent in thrilling one-on-one matches. You can compete in a quick match, enter a challenging tournament, or team up with a friend for two-player action. Dribble past your opponent, block their shots, and score amazing baskets to claim victory.

Player 1 Controls: Use A/D keys to move, W key to jump, and the B key to shoot or steal the ball. To perform a powerful dash, double-tap A or D. Use the V key for a supershot when it's charged.

Player 2 Controls: Use the Arrow Keys to move, the Up Arrow to jump, and the L key to shoot or steal. Dash by double-tapping the left or right arrow key. Use the K key for a supershot.

To dunk, run towards the hoop and press the jump key, then press the shoot key while airborne and close to the basket. Master these moves to become a true basketball star!
