Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Basketball Stars offers exciting gameplay for both single and two-player modes. You can choose to jump into a quick match to hone your skills, or test your mettle in a challenging tournament against tough opponents.

The basic controls are intuitive: use the **arrow keys** or **WASD** to move your player around the court. To perform actions like shooting or passing, you'll use specific keys depending on whether you're Player 1 or Player 2.

Mastering the dunk is key to dominating the court! To execute a dunk, control your player to run towards the hoop. When you are close enough, jump by pressing **W** (for Player 1) or **Up Arrow** (for Player 2). While your player is in the air, quickly press **X** (for Player 1) or **L** (for Player 2) to shoot and complete that epic dunk!

Practice your shooting, dribbling, and defensive strategies to outscore your opponent and rise to become a true basketball legend in this thrilling game!
