Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Step onto the court in Basketball Stars, a free online basketball game you can enjoy right in your browser. Start strong, show off your skills with three-pointers, and dominate the boards with rebounds. Don't give your opponent any breathing room and become a legend on the court!

Basketball Stars allows you to enjoy the game on your own or with friends. You have the choice to join a thrilling tournament where the competition is intense, or you can simply pick your team and jump into a fast and exciting match.

Controls & Dunks

To perform a spectacular dunk, take the ball and run to the hoop. When you are close enough, jump by pressing W or the Up Arrow, then press X or L to shoot while you're in the air. Master these moves to leave your opponent in the dust!

Basketball Stars was created by MadPuffers. If you enjoy sports games, be sure to check out other titles like Head Basketball.
