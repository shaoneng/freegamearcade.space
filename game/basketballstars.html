Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Welcome to the court! In Basketball Stars, your objective is to outscore your opponent in fast-paced one-on-one matches. Dribble, shoot, and play tough defense to claim victory. You can compete against the computer in a tournament or challenge a friend in the 2-player mode. Master the controls to block shots, steal the ball, and unleash powerful dunks and supershots!

Player 1 Controls: Use the A/D keys to move left and right, and the W key to jump. Press the B key to shoot the ball or to steal it from your opponent. Use the S key to perform a pump fake or to block a shot. Double-tap A or D to dash, and press the V key to use your supershot when it's charged!

Player 2 Controls: Use the Left and Right Arrow keys to move, and the Up Arrow key to jump. Press the L key to shoot or steal the ball. Use the Down Arrow key to pump fake or block. Double-tap the Left or Right Arrow key to dash, and press the K key for your supershot.
