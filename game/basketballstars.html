Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Step onto the court and prove you're a legend! In Basketball Stars, your objective is to outscore your opponent in a fast-paced one-on-one match. Dribble, shoot, and defend your way to victory. You can play solo in a tournament or jump into a quick match against a friend.

Player 1 Controls

  • Move: WASD Keys
  • Shoot / Steal: X Key
  • Dash: Press A or D twice

Player 2 Controls

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Shoot / Steal: L Key
  • Dash: Press Left or Right Arrow twice

How to Dunk

To perform a show-stopping dunk, run with the ball towards the hoop. When you are close enough to the basket, jump (W key for Player 1, Up Arrow for Player 2) and press the shoot key (X for Player 1, L for Player 2) while in mid-air.
