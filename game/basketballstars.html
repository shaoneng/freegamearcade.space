Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Welcome to the court! The objective in Basketball Stars is to outscore your opponent in a fast-paced basketball match. You can play solo against an AI opponent or challenge a friend in the exciting 2-player mode. Choose between entering a competitive tournament or jumping straight into a quick match.

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: WASD keys
  • Shoot / Block: B key
  • Supershot: V key
  • Dunk: Run towards the hoop and press B while in the air.

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Shoot / Block: L key
  • Supershot: K key
  • Dunk: Run towards the hoop and press L while in the air.
