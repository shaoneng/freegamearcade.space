Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Step onto the court and prove your skills in Basketball Stars! The goal is simple: score more points than your opponent before the time runs out. Dribble, shoot, and block your way to victory in various game modes, including tournaments and quick matches. You can play solo or challenge a friend in exciting 2-player action.

Controls for Player 1

  • Move: A/D Keys
  • Jump: W Key
  • Shoot / Steal: X Key
  • Dash: Press A or D twice quickly
  • Super Shot: Z Key

Controls for Player 2

  • Move: Left / Right Arrow Keys
  • Jump: Up Arrow Key
  • Shoot / Steal: L Key
  • Dash: Press Left or Right Arrow twice quickly
  • Super Shot: K Key

Tip for Dunking: To slam dunk, run towards the hoop while holding the ball. When you are close to the basket, jump and press the shoot key while in the air to execute a powerful dunk!
