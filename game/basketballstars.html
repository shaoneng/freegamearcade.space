Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Get ready to hit the court in Basketball Stars! The objective is simple: outscore your opponent before time runs out. You can play against the AI in a tournament or jump into a quick match against a friend.

Player 1 Controls: Use the A/D keys to move left and right. Press the W key to jump, and use the X key to shoot the ball or steal it from your opponent.

Player 2 Controls: Use the Left and Right Arrow keys to move. Press the Up Arrow key to jump, and use the L key to shoot or steal.

To perform a spectacular dunk, run towards the hoop, jump when you're close, and press the shoot button while in the air. Master these controls to block, steal, and score your way to victory!
