Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Step onto the virtual court and prove your skills in Basketball Stars! The objective is simple: outscore your opponent before the timer runs out. You can play solo against the computer or challenge a friend in 2-player mode.

Controls for Player 1:

  • Move: W, A, S, D keys
  • Shoot / Block: B key
  • Pump Fake / Steal: S key
  • Supershot: V key

Controls for Player 2:

  • Move: Arrow keys
  • Shoot / Block: L key
  • Pump Fake / Steal: K key
  • Supershot: J key

To perform a slam dunk, run towards the hoop with the ball. When you're close, jump (W or Up Arrow) and then press the shoot key (B or L) while airborne. Master your timing for shots, get rebounds, and play tight defense to become a true basketball star!
