Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Get ready to hit the court in Basketball Stars! You can play solo against the computer or challenge a friend in exciting 2-player mode. Choose to enter a competitive tournament to prove you're the best, or simply select your team and dive into a quick match for instant action.

Player 1 Controls: Use the A/D keys to move left and right, and the W key to jump. When you have the ball, press the X key to shoot or block.

Player 2 Controls: Use the Arrow Keys to move, and the Up Arrow to jump. Press the L key to shoot or block.

To perform a spectacular dunk, run towards the opponent's hoop. When you get close, jump (W or Up Arrow) and then press the shoot button (X or L) while in the air to slam the ball home! Master your defense, snatch rebounds, and time your shots perfectly to become a legend on the court.
