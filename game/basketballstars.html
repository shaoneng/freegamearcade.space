Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Step onto the court and prove you're the best in Basketball Stars! This game offers intense 1-on-1 matches where you can play against a friend or challenge the computer in a tournament. The objective is simple: score more points than your opponent before time runs out. Use your skills to dribble, shoot, block, and steal the ball to dominate the game.

Master the controls to become a true basketball legend:

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: WASD Keys
  • Shoot / Steal: B Key
  • Pump Fake / Block: S Key
  • Dash: Press A or D key twice quickly
  • Super Shot: V Key

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Shoot / Steal: L Key
  • Pump Fake / Block: Down Arrow Key
  • Dash: Press Left or Right Arrow key twice quickly
  • Super Shot: K Key

To perform a spectacular dunk, run towards the opponent's hoop with the ball. When you get close, jump and then press the shoot key while in the air to slam it home!
