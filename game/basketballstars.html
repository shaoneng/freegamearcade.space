Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Get ready to hit the court in Basketball Stars! The objective is simple: score more points than your opponent before the timer runs out. You can play against the computer or challenge a friend in exciting 1-on-1 or 2-on-2 modes. Choose to enter a tournament for a series of challenging matches, or jump into a quick random game for instant action.

Master the controls to dominate the game:

Player 1 Controls:
  • Move: A/D Keys
  • Jump: W Key
  • Shoot / Steal: B Key
  • Dash: Press A/D twice
  • Super Shot: V Key
Player 2 Controls:
  • Move: Left / Right Arrow Keys
  • Jump: Up Arrow Key
  • Shoot / Steal: L Key
  • Dash: Press Left/Right Arrow twice
  • Super Shot: K Key

To perform an epic dunk, dribble the ball towards the hoop. Once you are close, jump towards the basket and press the shoot key while you are in the air to slam the ball home! Master your blocks, rebounds, and shots to become a legend on the court.
