Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Basketball Stars is a free online basketball game that you can enjoy on your browser. Start strong, and show your skills with three-pointers. Get the rebounds, and do not give your opponent any breathing room. Be a legend on the court!

Basketball Stars allows you to enjoy the game on your own or with friends in two-player mode. You have the choice to join a thrilling tournament where the competition is intense, or you can simply pick your team and jump into a fast and exciting match.

Controls

This game can be played by one or two players. The key to victory is mastering your moves!

  • How to Dunk: To perform an epic dunk, take the ball and run towards the hoop. When you are close enough, jump by pressing the W key (Player 1) or the Up Arrow key (Player 2). While in the air, press the X key (Player 1) or the L key (Player 2) to shoot and complete the dunk.

Basketball Stars was created by MadPuffers. If you are into sports games, you might also enjoy playing Head Basketball.
