Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Welcome to the court! The goal in Basketball Stars is to outscore your opponent in fast-paced, one-on-one matches. You can play in tournament mode against the AI or challenge a friend in two-player mode. Use your skills to dribble, shoot, block, and steal the ball to secure victory.

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: WASD Keys
  • Shoot / Steal: B Key
  • Pump Fake: S Key
  • Dash: A/D Key twice
  • Supershot: V Key

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Shoot / Steal: L Key
  • Pump Fake: Down Arrow Key
  • Dash: Left/Right Arrow twice
  • Supershot: K Key

To perform a dunk, run towards the hoop and press the shoot key (B or L) when you are close to the basket. Time your supershot carefully by charging the power bar for a guaranteed point!
