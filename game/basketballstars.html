Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Get ready to dominate the court! Basketball Stars lets you play solo against the computer or challenge a friend in exciting 2-player mode on the same device. Choose between a competitive tournament or a quick match to get right into the action.

The controls are simple to learn but take skill to master. Your objective is to outscore your opponent by shooting, dunking, and playing tough defense.

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: A / D Keys
  • Jump: W Key
  • Shoot / Steal: B Key
  • Dunk: Run to the hoop, jump with W, then press B in mid-air.

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: Left / Right Arrow Keys
  • Jump: Up Arrow Key
  • Shoot / Steal: L Key
  • Dunk: Run to the hoop, jump with the Up Arrow, then press L in mid-air.
