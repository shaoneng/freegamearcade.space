Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Get ready to dominate the court in Basketball Stars! The goal is simple: outscore your opponent by shooting hoops. Dribble past the defender, go for a long-range three-pointer, or drive to the basket for a powerful dunk. Play smart on defense by blocking shots and stealing the ball to create your own scoring opportunities.

The game can be enjoyed solo in tournament mode or against a friend in a quick match. The controls are intuitive and easy to master:

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: W, A, S, D Keys
  • Shoot / Steal: B Key
  • Pump Fake: S Key
  • Dash: Press A or D key twice quickly
  • Super Shot: V Key

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Shoot / Steal: L Key
  • Pump Fake: Down Arrow Key
  • Dash: Press Left or Right Arrow key twice quickly
  • Super Shot: K Key

To perform a dunk, run towards the hoop with the ball. When you are close enough, press the shoot key to jump and score in style!
