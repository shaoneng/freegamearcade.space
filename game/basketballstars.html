Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Get ready to hit the court in Basketball Stars! Your objective is to outscore your opponent by sinking incredible shots. You can play solo in a tournament or quick match, or challenge a friend in the 2-player mode. Master your movement, shooting, and defensive skills to dominate the game.

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: WASD Keys
  • Shoot / Steal: B Key
  • Pump / Block: S Key
  • Dash: Double-tap A or D

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Shoot / Steal: L Key
  • Pump / Block: Down Arrow Key
  • Dash: Double-tap Left or Right Arrow

How to Dunk:

To execute a powerful dunk, run towards the opponent's hoop with the ball. When you are close enough to the net, press your shoot key (B for Player 1, L for Player 2) to jump and slam it in for a spectacular score!
