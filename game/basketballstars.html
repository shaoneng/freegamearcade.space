Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Welcome to the court! Basketball Stars offers multiple ways to play. You can dive into a challenging single-player tournament and battle your way to the championship, or you can grab a friend for a competitive 2-player quick match to see who is the true basketball legend.

The controls are straightforward, allowing you to focus on the action:

  • Player 1: Use the WASD keys to move. Press B to shoot or steal the ball.
  • Player 2: Use the Arrow Keys to move. Press L to shoot or steal the ball.

To perform a spectacular dunk, run towards the hoop with the ball. When you are close, jump (W for Player 1, Up Arrow for Player 2) and press the shoot button (B for Player 1, L for Player 2) while airborne. Master your shots, get rebounds, and don't give your opponent any room to breathe!
