Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Get ready to hit the court in Basketball Stars! Your goal is to outplay your opponent in fast-paced matches. You can play solo, team up with a friend for 2v2 action, or go head-to-head in 1v1 mode. Master the basic moves to control the game, from shooting long-range three-pointers to executing powerful dunks.

The controls are simple and designed for both single-player and two-player fun on the same keyboard:

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: A/D Keys
  • Jump: W Key
  • Shoot/Steal: B Key
  • Dunk/Block: V Key

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: Left/Right Arrow Keys
  • Jump: Up Arrow Key
  • Shoot/Steal: L Key
  • Dunk/Block: K Key

To perform an epic dunk, dribble the ball towards the hoop. Once you're in range, jump and then press the shoot key while airborne to slam it home! Use your defensive skills to block shots and steal the ball to keep the pressure on your opponent.
