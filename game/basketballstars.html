Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Step onto the virtual court and become a legend in Basketball Stars! This exciting basketball game lets you play solo or challenge a friend right in your browser. Choose between an intense tournament mode or jump straight into a quick match.

Show off your skills with killer three-pointers, grab every rebound, and play tight defense to keep your opponent from scoring. Dominate the game and prove you're the best on the court.

Controls & Dunks

To perform an epic dunk, dribble the ball and run towards the hoop. When you get close, press the jump key (W or Up Arrow) and then immediately press the shoot key (X or L) while in the air to slam it home!

  • Player 1 Controls: Use WASD keys to move, X to shoot/steal.
  • Player 2 Controls: Use Arrow keys to move, L to shoot/steal.
