Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Step onto the court and show off your skills in Basketball Stars, a thrilling basketball game you can play by yourself or with a friend. Choose between a competitive tournament mode or jump straight into a quick, action-packed match.

The goal is to outscore your opponent. Use your agility to get rebounds, block shots, and sink incredible three-pointers. Don't give your opponent any breathing room and dominate the game to become a legend of the court!

To perform a dunk, run towards the hoop with the ball. When you're close, jump by pressing 'W' (for Player 1) or the Up Arrow (for Player 2), and then press 'X' or 'L' while in the air to shoot. Master these moves to secure your victory.
