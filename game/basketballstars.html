Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Welcome to the court! In Basketball Stars, your objective is to outscore your opponent in a fast-paced, one-on-one matchup. Use your skills to dribble, shoot, block, and perform epic dunks to secure victory. You can play against the computer or challenge a friend in local 2-player mode.

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: WASD Keys
  • Shoot/Dunk: B Key
  • Pump Fake/Block: S Key
  • Dash: Double-tap A or D Key

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Shoot/Dunk: L Key
  • Pump Fake/Block: Down Arrow Key
  • Dash: Double-tap Left or Right Arrow Key

Choose your team and decide whether to jump into a quick match or compete in a full tournament. Time your shots, play strong defense, and don't give your opponent any room to breathe. Master these controls to become a true basketball legend!
