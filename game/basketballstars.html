Basketball Stars

How to Play Basketball Stars

Step onto the court and get ready for some fast-paced basketball action! You can play solo or challenge a friend in two-player mode. Choose between entering a competitive tournament or jumping straight into a quick match.

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: W, A, S, D keys
  • Shoot / Steal: X key
  • Pump / Block: S key

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: Arrow keys
  • Shoot / Steal: L key
  • Pump / Block: Down Arrow key

To perform a spectacular dunk, run towards your opponent's hoop with the ball. When you get close, jump (W or Up Arrow) and then press the shoot key (X or L) while you're in the air to slam it home!
