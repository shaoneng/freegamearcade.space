Basket Hoop

How to Play Basket Hoop

Welcome to Basket Hoop, where the gameplay is simple to learn but challenging to master! Your objective is to score as many baskets as possible within the time limit. Gravity is your main opponent, so you'll need perfect timing for each shot to succeed.

The controls are incredibly straightforward. On a computer, simply use your mouse to aim and click to shoot the ball. If you're playing on a mobile device, just tap the screen to send the ball flying towards the hoop.

As you play, you'll earn coins for each successful basket. Use these coins in the Ball Shop to unlock 27 different and exciting balls! From tennis balls to golf balls, each one offers a unique feel and can even provide faster gameplay. Collect them all and find your favorite to maximize your score!
