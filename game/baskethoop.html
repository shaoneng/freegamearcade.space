Basket Hoop

How to Play Basket Hoop

Welcome to Basket Hoop, where the challenge is simple but addictive! Your objective is to score as many baskets as you can within the time limit. The charm of this game lies in its one-button control system.

On a computer, simply use your mouse to click and shoot the ball. If you're playing on a mobile device, just tap the screen to shoot. Timing is key! Watch the hoop's movement carefully and tap at the perfect moment to make the ball go in.

For every successful shot, you'll earn coins. You can use these coins in the Ball Shop to unlock 27 different and fun ball designs. Customize your game and aim for the highest score!
