Basket Hoop

How to Play Basket Hoop

The goal in Basket Hoop is straightforward: score as many baskets as you can before the time runs out. The gameplay is deceptively simple, relying on a single action. Whether you're on a computer or a mobile device, your timing is key to success.

To shoot the ball, simply use your mouse to click on the screen if you're on a computer. If you're playing on a mobile phone or tablet, just tap the screen. The ball will launch towards the hoopâ€”mastering the timing of your tap or click is crucial to making the shot and achieving a high score.

As you score points, you'll earn in-game coins. Use these coins in the Ball Shop (accessible via the shop icon) to unlock 27 different balls, including unique designs like tennis balls, volleyballs, and more! These alternative balls can even offer a faster gameplay experience, allowing you to score more quickly.
