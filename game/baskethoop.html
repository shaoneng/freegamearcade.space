Basket Hoop

How to Play Basket Hoop

Getting started with Basket Hoop is incredibly easy, making it a perfect game for players of all ages. The controls are designed for simplicity, allowing you to focus purely on the fun of shooting hoops!

On a Computer: Use your mouse to shoot the ball. The key is to time your click perfectly to launch the ball at the right angle and power to sink it into the hoop.

On a Mobile Device: Simply tap the screen to shoot the ball. The intuitive one-touch control makes playing on your phone or tablet a seamless experience.

Your objective is to score as many baskets as you can within the time limit. Earn coins with each successful shot and use them in the Ball Shop to unlock over 27 different ball designs for a fresh look and feel!
