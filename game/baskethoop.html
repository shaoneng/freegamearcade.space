Basket Hoop

How to Play Basket Hoop

Welcome to Basket Hoop, where the gameplay is simple but the challenge is addictive! Your goal is to score as many baskets as you can within the time limit. Gravity is your main obstacle, and perfect timing is your only tool for success.

The controls are incredibly intuitive. If you're playing on a computer, use your mouse to click anywhere on the screen to shoot the ball. If you're on a mobile device, simply tap the screen to send the ball flying towards the hoop. The key is to time your tap or click just right to overcome the pull of gravity and swish it through the net.

As you play, you'll earn in-game coins for every successful basket. Save up your coins and head to the Ball Shop! There you can unlock 27 unique and zany balls, from a classic volleyball to a fan-favorite Pokéball. Each ball can change the feel of the game, so try to collect them all!
