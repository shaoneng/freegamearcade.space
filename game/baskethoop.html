Basket Hoop

How to Play Basket Hoop

Welcome to Basket Hoop, where the gameplay is simple to learn but challenging to master! Your main goal is to score as many baskets as possible within the time limit. Gravity is your biggest opponent, so you'll need perfect timing to succeed.

The controls are incredibly straightforward. If you're playing on a computer, use your mouse to click and shoot the ball. For mobile players on iOS or Android, simply tap the screen to send the ball flying towards the hoop. The key is to tap at the right moment to get the perfect arc and score.

As you sink more shots, you'll earn in-game coins. Don't forget to visit the Ball Shop! You can use your coins to unlock 27 different and fun ball designs. Experiment with various balls, as some might even feel faster than the standard basketball, adding a new twist to your gameplay. Compete against the clock and yourself to set a new high score!
