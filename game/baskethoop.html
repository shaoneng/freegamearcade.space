Basket Hoop

How to Play Basket Hoop

The gameplay in Basket Hoop is beautifully simple. Your objective is to score as many baskets as you can within the time limit. The ball bounces automatically, and your only control is to make it jump higher. Time your tap or click perfectly to launch the ball into the hoop.

The controls are intuitive and work seamlessly on any device:
Computer: Use your mouse to click anywhere on the screen to shoot the ball.
Mobile: Simply tap the screen to shoot the ball.

As you score points, you'll earn coins. Be sure to visit the Ball Shop by tapping the shop icon. You can spend your coins to unlock 27 different balls! Each new ball, from a tennis ball to a golf ball, can change up the gameplay and add a fresh challenge. See if you can collect them all!
