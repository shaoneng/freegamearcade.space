Basket Hoop

How to Play Basket Hoop

The gameplay in Basket Hoop is brilliantly simple yet incredibly addictive. Your main goal is to score as many points as you can by shooting the ball into the hoop before the timer runs out. Gravity is your only challenge, and a well-timed tap is your only move.

To play, simply click your mouse (on a computer) or tap your screen (on a mobile device) to launch the ball. The key is to get the timing just right to arc the ball perfectly into the net. Each successful shot earns you coins.

Don't forget to visit the Ball Shop! You can use the coins you earn to purchase and unlock 27 different balls, including everything from a tennis ball to a Pokéball. These new balls not only change your game's look but can also offer a different feel. Aim for the high score and complete your ball collection!
