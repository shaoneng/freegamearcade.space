Basket Hoop

How to Play Basket Hoop

Your goal in Basket Hoop is simple: score as many points as possible before the timer runs out. The only thing standing in your way is gravity! The gameplay relies on a single action, making it easy to learn but challenging to master.

To shoot the ball, simply use your mouse to click on the screen. If you're playing on a mobile device, just tap the screen. The key is to time your tap or click perfectly to send the ball flying into the hoop.

As you score baskets, you'll earn coins. Use these coins in the Ball Shop to unlock 27 different balls, including tennis balls, volleyballs, and even more exotic choices. Each new ball can change the feel of the game. Try to collect them all!
