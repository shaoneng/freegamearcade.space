Basket Hoop

How to Play Basket Hoop

The goal in Basket Hoop is simple: score as many baskets as possible before the timer runs out. Gravity is your main opponent, and your only tool is a single tap or click. It's a true test of timing and precision!

Controls:

  • Computer: Use your mouse to click anywhere on the screen to shoot the ball.
  • Mobile: Tap the screen to shoot the ball.

Timing is everything. Watch the ball's movement and shoot at the perfect moment to send it soaring through the hoop. As you play, you'll earn coins which you can use in the Ball Shop to unlock 27 different balls, each with a unique look!
