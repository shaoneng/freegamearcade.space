Basket Hoop

How to Play Basket Hoop

In Basket Hoop, your goal is simple: make the ball jump and send it inside the hoop. Gravity is your main challenge, and tapping is your only weapon. Score as many baskets as you can within a limited amount of time to achieve a high score.

For computer players, use your mouse to click and make the ball jump. For mobile players, simply tap on your screen to shoot the ball. It's a one-button game that's easy to pick up but challenging to master!

Don't forget to explore the Ball Shop! You'll find 27 unique unlockable balls, ranging from tennis and volleyballs to golf balls and Pokeballs. Use your in-game coins to purchase these options, many of which offer faster gameplay than the default ball, enhancing your experience and helping you complete your collection.
