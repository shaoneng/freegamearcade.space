Basket Hoop

How to Play Basket Hoop

The goal in Basket Hoop is to score as many baskets as you can before the timer runs out. It's a simple, one-button game where timing is everything!

On a computer: Use your mouse to click anywhere on the screen. This will cause the ball to jump. Time your click perfectly to arc the ball into the hoop.

On a mobile device: Simply tap the screen to shoot the ball. The controls are designed for a seamless mobile experience.

With every successful basket, you earn in-game coins. You can use these coins to visit the Ball Shop and unlock 27 unique balls, giving you fun new ways to play and complete your collection.
