Basket Hoop

How to Play Basket Hoop

Welcome to Basket Hoop, where your timing is everything! The objective is simple: score as many baskets as you can before the clock runs out. In this game, gravity is your main opponent, and a well-timed click or tap is your only tool. Aim carefully and time your shot to send the ball swishing through the net.

The controls are incredibly straightforward, making it easy for anyone to pick up and play. If you're on a computer, simply use your mouse to click and shoot the ball. For mobile players on iOS or Android devices, a single tap on the screen is all it takes to launch the ball towards the hoop.

Don't forget to customize your experience! As you score baskets, you'll earn in-game coins. Use these coins in the Ball Shop (accessible via the shop icon) to unlock 27 unique and fun ball designs. From classic sports balls like tennis and volleyball to more exotic choices, each new ball can offer a slightly different gameplay feel. Collect them all and find your favorite!
