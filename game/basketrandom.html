Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the unpredictable courts of Basket Random! This is a wacky, physics-based basketball game where you'll compete against the CPU or a friend in chaotic matches. The goal is simple: be the first player to score 5 points by dunking the ball into your opponent's hoop.

The controls are deceptively simple, as you control your floppy players with just one key each. Timing your jumps is everything!

  • Player 1: Press the 'W' key to jump and shoot.
  • Player 2: Press the 'UP ARROW' key to jump and shoot.

The "Random" in the title is the core of the fun. After every point is scored, the game changes! You might find yourself playing on a snowy field or a sandy beach, with players suddenly wearing different outfits. The ball itself can change from a standard basketball to a heavy iron ball or a super-light one, drastically altering the physics of each shot. Adapt to the chaos, master the one-button controls, and outsmart your opponent to claim victory!
