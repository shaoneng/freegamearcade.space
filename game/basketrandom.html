Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to Basket Random, the most unpredictable basketball game you'll ever play! The goal is simple: be the first team to score 5 points. However, the gameplay is anything but ordinary. You'll control a team of two floppy, physics-driven players with just a single key. Timing your jumps is crucial to grab the ball, shoot, and defend your hoop in this chaotic and hilarious arcade game.

The controls are incredibly simple to learn but challenging to master. In 1-Player mode, you'll use one key, and in 2-Player mode, each player gets their own key:

  • Player 1: Press the 'W' key.
  • Player 2: Press the 'UP ARROW' key.

Get ready for constant surprises! After every point scored, the game changes. You might find yourself playing on a snowy field or a sandy beach, with players in different outfits. The ball itself can change—from a heavy iron ball to a super light one—completely altering the physics. Even the players' arms and the hoops can randomly grow or shrink, ensuring no two matches are ever the same. Adapt to the chaos to win!
