Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the wild and unpredictable courts of Basket Random! The goal is simple: score more baskets than your opponent to win. The controls are even simpler—each player uses just one button to make their two characters jump and flail towards the ball. Time your jumps perfectly to snatch the ball, shoot, and dunk your way to victory.

You can take on the challenge in two different modes. In 1 Player mode, you'll face a surprisingly tough computer opponent that will test your skills. For even more fun, grab a friend and dive into 2 Player mode, where you can compete against each other on the same keyboard for ultimate bragging rights.

True to its name, this game is all about randomness! After every single point is scored, the game changes. You might find yourself playing on a frozen court, a sunny beach, or with players in wacky outfits. The ball itself can change from a normal basketball to a heavy iron ball or a super-light one, and even the players' arms and the hoops can randomly change size. Adapt to the chaos to win!
