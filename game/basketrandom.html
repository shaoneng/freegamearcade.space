Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the wild and unpredictable courts of Basket Random! The goal is simple: score more baskets than your opponent. However, the gameplay is anything but ordinary. You control your two ragdoll players with just a single key, making them jump and flail towards the hoop. Timing your jumps is crucial to both score baskets and defend your own.

The game offers two exciting modes. In 1 Player mode, you'll face a surprisingly skilled CPU opponent. In 2 Player mode, you can challenge a friend on the same keyboard for some hilarious head-to-head action.

The "Random" in the title is no joke! After every single point is scored, the entire game can change. You might find yourself playing on an icy winter court, a sandy beach, or with players in wacky outfits. The ball can change from a normal basketball to a heavy iron ball or a floaty light one. Even the players and hoops can randomly get longer or shorter, keeping you on your toes at all times!

Controls:

  • Player 1: Press the 'W' key to jump.
  • Player 2: Press the 'Up Arrow' key to jump.

The first team to score 5 points wins the match. Adapt to the chaos and dunk your way to victory!
