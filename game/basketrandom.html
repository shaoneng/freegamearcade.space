Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the zany world of Basket Random! The goal is simple: score more baskets than your opponent. The catch? Everything is random! Control your team of two floppy players with just one button. You can challenge the computer in 1-Player mode or go head-to-head with a friend in 2-Player mode. The first team to score 5 points wins the match.

Each time a basket is scored, the game changes dramatically. You might find yourself playing on a snowy field, a sunny beach, or anywhere in between. The players' outfits, the shape and weight of the ball, and even the length of the players' arms can change in an instant, creating hilarious and unpredictable gameplay.

Controls

  • Player 1: Press the 'W' key to jump and shoot.
  • Player 2: Press the 'Up Arrow' key to jump and shoot.

Time your jumps perfectly to snatch the ball, block shots, and score incredible dunks. Adapt to the new random conditions after every point and lead your team to victory!
