Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the wacky and unpredictable world of Basket Random! The goal is simple: be the first player to score 5 points. The gameplay, however, is anything but simple. This is a physics-based game where control is part of the fun and challenge.

You can play against the computer in 1-Player mode or challenge a friend in 2-Player mode. The controls are incredibly easy to learn: one button is all you need! Player 1 uses the 'W' key, and Player 2 uses the 'UP ARROW' key. Press the key to make your characters jump and flail towards the ball to shoot.

The "Random" in the title is what makes this game special. After every point is scored, the game changes! You might find yourselves on a snowy court, a sunny beach, or somewhere else entirely. The players' uniforms will change, and even the basketball itself might transform into a heavy iron ball or a super lightweight one. Keep an eye out for randomly shrinking hoops or lengthening arms that add to the chaos!
