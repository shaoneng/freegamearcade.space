Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the unpredictable world of Basket Random! The goal is simple: be the first team to score 5 points. The catch? Everything is random!

Controls: This is a one-button game! Use the "W" key for Player 1 and the "UP ARROW" key for Player 2. Press the key to make your players jump, flop, and shoot the ball towards the hoop.

Gameplay Modes: You can challenge the computer in 1-PLAYER mode or grab a friend for a hilarious head-to-head match in 2-PLAYER mode.

The Random Factor: After every point is scored, the game changes! You might find yourselves playing on a beach or a frozen court. The players' outfits will change, and even the ball will transform, from a heavy iron ball to an uncontrollably light one. Adapt to the new physics and try to master the chaos to win!
