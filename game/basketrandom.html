Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the wacky world of Basket Random! The goal is simple: be the first player to score 5 points. The controls are just as straightforward. If you're playing solo against the computer, use the 'W' key to control your players. For two-player mode, Player 1 uses 'W' and Player 2 uses the 'UP ARROW' key. One button controls your entire team!

Each press of your key will make your two players jump and flail. Your characters have limited mobility and move in a floppy, unpredictable way, so timing is everything! You'll need to skillfully maneuver your players to grab the ball and send it flying towards your opponent's hoop. Each match is a hilarious test of physics-based controls and a bit of luck.

True to its name, the game changes after every single basket! You might find yourself playing on a snowy court, a sandy beach, or with players in different outfits. The ball itself can change from a heavy iron ball to a super light one, altering the physics completely. Even the players' arms can randomly get longer or shorter, making every round a new and unpredictable challenge!
