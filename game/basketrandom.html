Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the chaotic world of Basket Random! This isn't your typical basketball game. In this pixelated, physics-based arcade game, you control your players with just one key. Your goal is simple: score more dunks than your opponent to win, but expect the unexpected at every turn!

Each team has two players that you move simultaneously: a dunker positioned to score and a defender to guard your hoop. Use your single control key to make them jump and flail towards the ball. Timing your jumps is crucial to snatch the ball, block opponents, and sink that perfect, wacky dunk.

Challenge the computer in 1 Player mode or grab a friend for a hilarious head-to-head match in 2 Player mode. The controls are simple, but the random nature of the game makes every match a unique test of skill and luck.

The "Random" in the title is no joke! After every point, the game changes. You might find yourself playing on a snowy field or a sandy beach, with players wearing different outfits. The ball can change from a heavy iron ball to an uncontrollably light one. Even the players' arms and the hoops themselves can randomly lengthen or shorten, leading to unpredictable and funny gameplay moments. Adapt to the chaos and dunk your way to victory!
