Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the hilariously unpredictable world of Basket Random! The goal is simple: be the first team to score 5 points. The gameplay, however, is anything but. This is a one-button game where you'll control your two floppy, physics-based players to dunk on your opponent.

Choose your mode: battle the computer in 1P mode or challenge a friend in 2P mode for local multiplayer madness. The controls are as straightforward as they get. For Player 1, use the 'W' key. For Player 2, use the 'Up Arrow' key. Pressing the key makes your players jump and flail their arms to shoot, block, and move around the court.

The real fun begins after each score. The game is "random" for a reason! The court might change to a snowy field or a sandy beach. The players might suddenly wear different outfits. Most importantly, the ball itself can change, from a heavy iron ball that's hard to throw to a super light one that flies out of control. Adapt to the chaos and dunk your way to victory!
