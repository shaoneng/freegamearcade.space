Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the hilarious and unpredictable world of Basket Random! The objective is simple: be the first to score 5 points. However, achieving this is anything but straightforward in this physics-based game of chaos.

You control both of your ragdoll players with a single button. Press the button to make them jump and flail towards the ball. Master the timing of your jumps to grab the ball, outmaneuver your opponent, and score a basket. The controls are the same whether you're playing against the computer or a friend.

The "Random" in the title is the core of the fun. After every point is scored, the entire game changes! You might find yourselves on a winter court or a sandy beach. The basketball might turn into a heavy iron ball or a super-light bouncy ball. Even the players' arms and the hoops can randomly lengthen or shorten. You must adapt to these constant changes to win!

Choose between 1 Player mode to challenge the crafty AI, or 2 Player mode to go head-to-head with a friend on the same computer. Good luck, and embrace the randomness!
