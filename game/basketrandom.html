Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Basket Random is a wildly fun and unpredictable pixelated basketball game. The beauty is in its simplicity and chaos—control your entire team with just a single key! Your goal is to perform the most dunks and outscore your opponent.

Each team has two players: a dunker positioned to attack and a defender to guard your hoop. With each key press, both of your players will move. Time your jumps perfectly to snatch the ball and send it flying into the basket. You can take on the surprisingly challenging computer in 1-Player mode or challenge a friend in 2-Player mode for some hilarious local multiplayer action.

The "Random" in the title is the core of the fun. After every basket scored, the game changes dramatically. You might be transported from a regular court to a snowy field or a sandy beach. The players' outfits, the shape of the ball, and even the length of the players' arms and the height of the hoops can change randomly, ensuring no two matches are ever the same!
