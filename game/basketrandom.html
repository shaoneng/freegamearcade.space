Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Basket Random is an engaging pixelated arcade basketball game controlled with just one key, making it easy to pick up but challenging to master!

Each team consists of two players: a dunker who focuses on scoring and stealing the ball, and a defender positioned behind to guard your hoop. Both players move simultaneously with your single input. Your primary goal is to perform successful dunks into the opponent's hoop while preventing them from scoring on yours.

You can choose between two exciting game modes: '1 Player' to test your skills against a formidable CPU opponent, or '2 Player' to challenge a friend for some competitive local multiplayer action.

What makes Basket Random truly unique is its dynamic nature. With every point scored, the game introduces random changes: the playing environment might switch from a snowy court to a sandy beach, players' uniforms will transform, and even the ball's properties can change (e.g., a heavy iron ball or a light, unpredictable one). Keep an eye out, as players' arm lengths and hoops can also randomly adjust, adding to the delightful chaos!
