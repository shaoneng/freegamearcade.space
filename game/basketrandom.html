Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the wacky world of Basket Random! The goal is simple: be the first player to score 5 points. The catch? You control your two ragdoll players with just a single button! Press the button to make your players jump and flail towards the ball. Time your jumps perfectly to snatch the ball, dribble (in a fashion), and dunk it into your opponent's hoop.

You can take on the surprisingly skilled computer AI in 1-Player mode or challenge a friend in the hilarious 2-Player mode. The controls are as simple as the concept:
- Player 1: Press the 'W' key.
- Player 2: Press the 'UP ARROW' key.
That's it! Master the timing of your one-button command to outplay your rival.

True to its name, Basket Random is filled with unpredictability. After every single point is scored, the game changes! You might find yourself playing on a frozen court with players in winter coats, or on a sandy beach. The players' outfits, their arm length, and even the ball itself will randomly change. One round you might be playing with a light, bouncy ball, and the next with a heavy iron ball that barely moves. Adapt to the chaos to secure your victory!
