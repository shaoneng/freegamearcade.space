Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the unpredictable world of Basket Random! The goal is simple: be the first to score 5 points. The controls are just as simple, with each player using only one button to jump and flop their characters around the court.

Controls:

  • Player 1: Press the 'W' key.
  • Player 2: Press the 'Up Arrow' key.

Press your key to make your two players jump and flail. Time your jumps to intercept the ball, move towards the opponent's hoop, and score a basket. The challenge comes from controlling two ragdoll players at once with a single input!

After every point, the game changes! You might find yourself playing on a snowy field, a beach, or a classic court. The players' outfits, the ball's size and weight, and even the physics can change, ensuring no two matches are ever the same. Adapt to the randomness and lead your team to victory in either 1-player mode against the computer or in a hilarious 2-player showdown with a friend.
