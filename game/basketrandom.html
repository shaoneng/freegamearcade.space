Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the wild and unpredictable courts of Basket Random! The goal is simple: score more points than your opponent. The catch? You control your two ragdoll players with just a single button! Press the button to make your players jump and flail towards the ball and the hoop. Timing is everything in this physics-based game.

You can take on the challenge solo in 1 Player mode against a surprisingly tough computer opponent. Or, for even more chaotic fun, grab a friend and go head-to-head in the 2 Player mode on the same computer. It's the perfect game for a quick, hilarious competition.

True to its name, every round is different. After each basket, the game changes everything! You might find yourself playing on a snowy field or a sunny beach, with players in different outfits, and even with different types of basketballs. From heavy iron balls to bouncy light ones, you'll need to adapt to the randomness to win!

The controls are as simple as it gets: Player 1 uses the 'W' key and Player 2 uses the 'Up Arrow' key. Press your key to jump and shoot. The first team to score 5 points wins the match!
