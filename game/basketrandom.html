Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the wonderfully wacky world of Basket Random! The goal is simple: be the first to score five points. The controls are even simplerâ€”you only need one key to make your players jump and flail towards the basket!

Each team has two players, and you control both of them simultaneously with a single button press. Time your jumps carefully to launch the ball towards your opponent's hoop while also trying to defend your own. The first team to sink five baskets wins the match.

What makes Basket Random truly unique is the 'random' element. After every point scored, the game changes! You might find yourself playing on a snowy field, a sunny beach, or with players in bizarre outfits. The ball can change too, from a heavy iron ball to an unpredictably light one. Adapt to the new physics and environment with each round to stay ahead!

You can challenge the computer in 1-Player mode or grab a friend for a hilarious head-to-head match in 2-Player mode. Good luck, and embrace the chaos!
