Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Get ready for the most unpredictable basketball game ever! In Basket Random, your objective is to be the first to score 5 points. The controls are incredibly simple—you only need to use a single key to control your ragdoll players!

Press your designated key to make your players jump and shoot. You can play solo against the CPU in 1 Player mode or challenge a friend on the same keyboard in the exciting 2 Player mode.

The real fun of Basket Random is the "random" element. After every point is scored, the players, the court, and even the ball can change! One moment you're on a standard court, the next you're playing on the beach with a volleyball, or dealing with an extra-bouncy ball. Embrace the chaos, adapt your strategy on the fly, and jump your way to victory!

Controls: Player 1 uses the 'W' key, and Player 2 uses the 'Up Arrow' key.
