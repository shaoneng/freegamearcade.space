Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the zany court of Basket Random! The goal is simple: be the first to score 5 baskets to win the match. The controls are just as simple, but mastering the chaotic physics is the real challenge!

This game uses a one-button control scheme. Just press one key to make your two players jump and flail their arms. Timing your jump is crucial to shooting the ball, blocking your opponent, and getting into position.

Controls:

  • Player 1: Press the 'W' key.
  • Player 2: Press the 'Up Arrow' key.

Each time a basket is scored, the round changes! You might find yourself on a snowy field, a sunny beach, or with different player outfits. The ball can change too, from a heavy iron ball to a super-light one, and even the players' arms can randomly get longer or shorter. Adapt to the randomness to secure your victory!
