Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the unpredictable world of Basket Random! The goal is simple: be the first to score five points. The catch? Everything is completely random!

The controls are minimalist and fun. You only need one key to control your two players, making them jump and flail towards the ball.

  • Player 1: Press the 'W' key.
  • Player 2: Press the 'UP ARROW' key.

You can play solo against the computer (1P mode) or challenge a friend on the same keyboard (2P mode). After every point is scored, the game throws a new random element at you. You might find yourself playing on a snowy field, a sunny beach, with different player outfits, or even with a new ball that has unique physics. Adapt to the changing conditions, time your jumps perfectly, and lead your team to a wacky victory!
