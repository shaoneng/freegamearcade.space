Basket Random

How to Play Basket Random

Welcome to the wild world of Basket Random! The goal is simple: be the first player to score 5 points. But how you get there is anything but ordinary. This physics-based game is all about chaotic fun, whether you're playing solo against the computer or challenging a friend in two-player mode.

Controlling your team is incredibly easy—it only takes one button! For Player 1, press the 'W' key. For Player 2, use the 'Up Arrow' key. Pressing your key makes both of your players jump and flail. Time your jumps perfectly to snatch the ball from your opponent, defend your hoop, and sink incredible shots.

What makes Basket Random truly special is the 'random' factor. After every point scored, the game changes completely! You'll be transported to a new court—from a snowy winter wonderland to a sunny beach. The players' outfits will change, and even the basketball itself might be a heavy iron ball, a bouncy light ball, or something else entirely. Adapt to the new conditions to keep your winning streak alive!
