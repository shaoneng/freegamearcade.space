Block Blast

How to Play Block Blast

The goal in Block Blast is simple yet captivating: to score as many points as possible by clearing blocks from an 8x8 grid. At the bottom of the screen, you will be presented with three block shapes at a time.

Simply click and drag these shapes onto the grid. Your objective is to place them strategically to form complete horizontal or vertical lines. When you successfully fill a full line, it will disappear from the grid, freeing up space and earning you points.

Unlike traditional block puzzle games, Block Blast has no time limit, allowing you to think through your moves and plan your strategy without pressure. The game ends when there are no possible moves left for the given block shapes. Think ahead and manage your space wisely to achieve the highest score!
