Block Blast

How to Play Block Blast

Block Blast offers a relaxing yet strategic twist on classic block puzzle games. The objective is simple: score as many points as possible by clearing lines on the 8x8 grid.

At the bottom of the screen, you will be presented with three block shapes. Your task is to drag and drop these shapes onto the grid. Try to place them in a way that fills a complete horizontal row or a vertical column.

Once a row or column is completely filled with blocks, it will be cleared from the grid, earning you points and freeing up valuable space. Unlike classic Tetris, there are no time limits, so you can take your time to think about the best placement for each piece. The game ends when there are no possible moves left for any of the three available block shapes. Plan ahead and keep the board clear to achieve the highest score!
