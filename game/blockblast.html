Block Blast

How to Play Block Blast

Your goal in Block Blast is simple: score as many points as possible by clearing lines of blocks. Unlike traditional Tetris, there are no time limits, allowing you to play at your own relaxed pace.

At the bottom of the screen, you will be given a set of three different block shapes. Click and drag these shapes onto the 8x8 grid. Your objective is to place them strategically to form complete horizontal or vertical lines.

When you successfully fill an entire row or column from edge to edge, that line will be cleared from the grid, earning you points and making space for new blocks. The game ends when you can no longer fit any of the given block shapes onto the board.
