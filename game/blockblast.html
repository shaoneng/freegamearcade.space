Block Blast

How to Play Block Blast

Welcome to Block Blast, the chill puzzle game where you can take your time and strategize. The objective is simple: place the given blocks onto the 8x8 grid to create complete lines, either horizontally or vertically.

To play, simply click and drag a block from the selection below the grid and drop it into an empty space on the board. When you successfully fill an entire row or column, it will be cleared, earning you points and freeing up valuable space.

Unlike fast-paced block games, Block Blast has no time limit, so you can think through each move carefully. The game ends when there are no possible moves left for the blocks you have. Plan ahead to maximize your score and keep the board clear for as long as possible!
