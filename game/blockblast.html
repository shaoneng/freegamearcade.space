Block Blast

How to Play Block Blast

The goal of Block Blast is to score as many points as possible by strategically placing blocks on an 8x8 grid. It's a test of spatial awareness and forward-thinking, all at a relaxing pace.

You will be presented with three block shapes at a time. Click and drag a block from the selection and place it anywhere on the grid. When you successfully fill a complete row or a complete column with blocks, that line will be cleared from the grid, freeing up space and earning you points.

Unlike many other puzzle games, there are no time limits in Block Blast. Take your time to think about the best placement for each piece to maximize your score and keep the game going. The game ends when there is no more space on the grid to place any of the three available block shapes. Plan ahead and keep the board clear for as long as you can!
