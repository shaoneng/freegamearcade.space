Block Blast

How to Play Block Blast

Block Blast offers a relaxing twist on the classic block puzzle genre. The objective is simple: score as many points as possible by clearing blocks from the 8x8 grid.

At the bottom of the screen, you will be presented with three block shapes at a time. Click and drag a block from the selection onto the grid. Place it anywhere you see fit – there are no time limits, so you can plan your moves carefully!

To clear blocks and free up space, you must complete a full line, either vertically (a column) or horizontally (a row). When a line is complete, all blocks in that line will disappear, and you'll earn points. The game ends when there is no more space on the grid to place any of the three available block shapes.

Think ahead and manage your space wisely to achieve a new high score in this endless puzzle challenge!
