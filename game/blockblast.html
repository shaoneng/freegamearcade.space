Block Blast

How to Play Block Blast

Welcome to Block Blast, the chill puzzle game that combines classic block-fitting strategy with a stress-free experience. The goal is simple: keep the 8x8 grid from filling up for as long as possible by strategically placing the blocks you're given.

To play, simply use your mouse to drag the three block shapes provided at the bottom of the screen and drop them onto the grid. Your objective is to create a solid line of blocks, either horizontally or vertically, across the entire grid. Once a line is complete, it will be cleared, freeing up space and earning you points.

Unlike other fast-paced block games, Block Blast has no time limit. You can take as much time as you need to decide on the perfect spot for each piece. The game ends only when there are no more possible moves for the current set of blocks. Plan ahead, aim for clearing multiple lines at once (a combo!), and chase that high score!
