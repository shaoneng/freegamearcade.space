Blocky Combat Swat

How to Play Blocky Combat Swat

Welcome to the intense, blocky world of Blocky Combat Swat! Your mission is simple but challenging: survive the relentless onslaught of pixelated zombies. These undead foes will come at you from every direction, sometimes creeping, sometimes sprinting. Stay alert and keep your trigger finger ready!

Use your mouse to aim and the left-click button to shoot. Navigate the battlefield using the standard WASD keys for movement. When the action gets heavy, you'll need to switch weapons. You start with a handgun, rifle, SMG, sniper, and shotgun. Use the number keys (1-5) to quickly swap between them in the heat of battle.

You can choose to face the horde alone in single-player mode or team up with others in online multiplayer. As you defeat zombies and progress, your character will level up, unlocking more powerful weapons from a total arsenal of eighteen. Eliminate every last zombie, level up your gear, and dominate the blocky battlefield!
