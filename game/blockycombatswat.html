Blocky Combat Swat

How to Play Blocky Combat Swat

Your mission in Blocky Combat Swat is to survive against hordes of relentless, pixelated zombies. These undead foes will come at you from all directions, so stay alert and keep moving. You can choose to face the challenge alone in single-player mode or team up with others in online multiplayer.

Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot. The zombies in this world are surprisingly resilient; even after being shot down, some may continue to crawl towards you, so make sure they're completely eliminated. Be prepared for zombies that creep, sprint, or sneak up on you.

You begin the game equipped with five starting weapons: a Handgun, Rifle, SMG, Sniper, and Shotgun. You can easily switch between them by pressing the number keys (1-5). As you defeat zombies and level up your character, you will unlock even more powerful weapons to add to your arsenal.
