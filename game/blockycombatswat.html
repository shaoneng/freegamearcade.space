Blocky Combat Swat

How to Play Blocky Combat Swat

Welcome to the blocky battlefield! Your mission in Blocky Combat Swat is simple: survive the zombie apocalypse. Zombies will spawn around you from all directions. Your job is to eliminate them before they overwhelm you. Be warned, these zombies are tough and unpredictable; they can creep, sprint, or even crawl towards you after being knocked down.

Use standard first-person shooter controls (WASD keys to move, mouse to aim and fire) to navigate the map and defend your position. You begin the game with a versatile loadout of five weapons: a Handgun, Rifle, SMG, Sniper, and Shotgun. You can easily switch between them using the number keys (1-5).

As you defeat zombies and level up your character, you will unlock more powerful firearms from a total arsenal of eighteen weapons. You can choose to face the horde alone in single-player mode or team up with other players in online multiplayer for a more dynamic and cooperative experience. Stay sharp, aim true, and clear the area of the blocky undead!
