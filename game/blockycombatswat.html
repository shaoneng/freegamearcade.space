Blocky Combat Swat

How to Play Blocky Combat Swat

Welcome to the blocky battlefield! Your mission in Blocky Combat Swat is to survive against hordes of pixelated zombies. These undead enemies will attack from all sides, so stay alert and be ready to shoot. They can creep, sprint, or even crawl towards you after being shot, so make sure they're completely eliminated!

Use standard first-person shooter controls to navigate the world. Press the **W, A, S, and D keys** to move your character, and use your **mouse** to aim and fire your weapon. You can jump over obstacles with the **spacebar** and use the **Shift key** to sprint when you need to make a quick escape.

You begin the game with a versatile loadout of five different weapons. Cycle through your Handgun, Rifle, SMG, Sniper, and Shotgun using the **number keys 1 through 5**. As you defeat zombies and level up your character, you will unlock an impressive arsenal of up to 18 unique weapons. Choose to fight alone in single-player or join forces with others in online multiplayer modes. Good luck, soldier!
