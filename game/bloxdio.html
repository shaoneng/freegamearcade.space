Bloxd io

How to Play Bloxd io

Welcome to Bloxd io, the ultimate online block-building and adventure game! This game offers a wide variety of modes, each with its own unique objective. Whether you're in the mood for creative construction, intense PvP combat, or agile parkour, Bloxd io has a world waiting for you.

Use the WASD keys to move your character around the world. Press the Spacebar to jump. Use your mouse to look around and click the left mouse button to place or break blocks, depending on the game mode. The number keys (1-9) can be used to select items from your hotbar.

To start, simply choose a game mode from the main menu. In 'Survival,' you'll need to gather resources and fend off other players. In 'Creative,' you have unlimited resources to build anything you can imagine. 'DoodleCube' challenges you to build based on a theme, while 'BloxdHop' tests your parkour skills. Explore each mode to discover its unique rules and objectives. You can play solo or join servers with friends for a multiplayer experience!
