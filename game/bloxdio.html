Bloxd io

How to Play Bloxd io

Getting started in Bloxd io is simple! Use the standard W, A, S, and D keys to move your character around the blocky world, and press the Spacebar to jump. To interact with the world, use your mouse: left-click to destroy blocks and right-click (or Shift) to place them.

The core of Bloxd io is its diverse range of game modes, each with a unique objective. When you join the game, you can choose the experience you want. Jump into "Survival" to gather resources and fend off enemies, unleash your creativity in "Peaceful" mode with unlimited blocks, or test your combat skills in fast-paced PvP modes like "Bed Wars" or "Sky Wars".

There are also modes for every type of player, including "DoodleCube" where you build based on a theme, and various parkour maps to challenge your platforming skills. Simply select a game mode from the main menu, join a server, and have fun playing solo or with friends online!
