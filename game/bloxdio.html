About Bloxd io

Welcome to Bloxd io, the ultimate online Minecraft-inspired game offering a wide variety of game modes. Whether you're in the mood for peaceful building or prefer intense Player-vs-Player battles, Bloxd io has an adventure waiting for you.

Dive into diverse modes, each with its unique objective. Test your resilience in Survival, unleash your creativity in building modes, prove your strength in combat, or even solve thrilling murder mysteries. You can embark on these adventures solo or team up with friends online for even more fun.

Created by Arthur Baker, Bloxd io delivers a rich and engaging block-world experience.
