How to Play Burnout City

Welcome to the neon-soaked streets of Burnout City! Your first step is to choose from eleven incredible cars and head straight to the customization menu. Here, you can change everything from paint jobs to body kits and neon underglows. Don't worry about the cost â€“ this game features an unlimited money cheat! If you run low, just head back to the main menu and click the "add money" button to top up your funds.

Once your ride is perfect, hit the streets and explore the city. You'll find glowing circles scattered across the map, each representing a different challenge. Simply drive into a circle to start a game mode. Choose from five thrilling options: outpace your rivals in Race Mode, chase down targets in Pursuit Mode, hit cones with precision in Trailblazer, beat the clock in Checkpoints, or just enjoy the freedom of the city in Free Ride mode.

Use the standard keyboard controls (WASD or Arrow Keys) to drive your car. Master drifting, find hidden ramps, and dominate every challenge Burnout City throws at you!
