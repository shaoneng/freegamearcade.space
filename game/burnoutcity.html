Burnout City

How to Play Burnout City

Your first stop is the garage. Choose from one of eleven incredible cars and head to the customization menu. Here, you can personalize your ride with unique paint jobs, body kits, and neon underglow. Don't worry about the costâ€”you have an unlimited supply of in-game currency! If you ever run low, just return to the main menu and click the "add money" button to top up your wallet.

Once your car is tricked out, hit the streets of Burnout City. The open-world map features several glowing circles, each representing a different challenge. Simply drive your car into a circle to begin that game mode.

Explore five distinct game modes: challenge AI opponents in Race Mode, evade the police in high-octane Pursuit Mode, test your precision in Trailblazer, beat the clock in Checkpoints, or enjoy the freedom to perform stunts and explore the city at your own pace in Free Ride.
