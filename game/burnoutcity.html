How to Play Burnout City

Get ready to rule the night in Burnout City! Your first step is to choose your ride from a selection of eleven awesome cars. Head straight to the customization menu to personalize your vehicle with unique paint jobs, neon underglows, and performance upgrades. Don't worry about the costâ€”you have an unlimited supply of in-game money! If you ever run low, just pop back to the main menu and click the 'add money' button to refuel your funds.

Once your car is tricked out, it's time to hit the streets. Drive into the main city map where you'll find several glowing circles. Each circle represents a different challenge or game mode. To start playing, simply drive your car over the circle of the mode you want to play.

Burnout City features five exciting game modes to test your skills: challenge AI opponents in Race Mode, chase down targets in Pursuit Mode, navigate a cone course with precision in Trailblazer, race against the clock in Checkpoints, or simply explore the city, perform stunts, and cause chaos in Free Ride Mode.
