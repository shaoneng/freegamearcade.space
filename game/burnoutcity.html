Burnout City

How to Play Burnout City

Your adventure begins on the nocturnal streets of Burnout City, a vast urban playground designed for high-speed thrills. Your primary goal is to master your vehicle, pull off incredible drifts, and conquer the various challenges scattered throughout the city.

First, choose your wheels from a selection of eleven awesome cars. Head to the customization menu to personalize your ride. From slick paint jobs and body kits to vibrant neon underglow, you have full creative control. Don't worry about the cost – you have an unlimited supply of in-game currency! If you ever run low, just pop back to the main menu and click the "add money" button to top up your wallet.

Once your car is ready, hit the streets and explore the map. You will find several glowing circles, each representing a different game mode. To start a challenge, simply drive your car into the circle of your choice.

Burnout City offers five distinct game modes to test your skills:

  • Race Mode: Compete against AI opponents and fight for a first-place finish.
  • Pursuit Mode: Turn the tables and chase down a target vehicle in a high-stakes pursuit.
  • Trailblazer: Test your precision driving by hitting a series of cones before time runs out.
  • Checkpoints: Race against the clock, hitting all the checkpoints to extend your time.
  • Free Ride: Explore the city at your own pace, with no missions or timers. Perfect for practicing stunts and discovering hidden areas.
