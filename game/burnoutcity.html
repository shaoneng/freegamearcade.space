Burnout City

How to Play Burnout City

Your adventure in Burnout City begins in the garage. First, select your ride from a collection of eleven unique cars. Once you've picked your vehicle, head over to the customization menu to give it a personal touch. With a huge selection of paint jobs, neon underglows, and other upgrades, you can design the perfect car. Best of all, you have an unlimited supply of in-game money—just click the "add money" button in the main menu if you run low!

After your car is tuned to perfection, it's time to hit the streets. Drive into the main city map where you'll find several colored circles scattered around. Each circle represents a different game mode or challenge. To start playing, simply drive your car over the circle of the mode you want to try.

Burnout City offers five distinct game modes to test your driving skills:

  • Race Mode: Compete against skilled AI drivers and prove you're the fastest on the streets.
  • Pursuit Mode: Engage in intense, mafia-style chases and try to take down your target.
  • Trailblazer: Test your precision by hitting a series of cones before time runs out.
  • Checkpoints: Race against the clock, hitting all the checkpoints to complete the challenge.
  • Free Ride: Explore the city at your own pace, pull off incredible stunts, and enjoy the freedom of the open road without any missions.
