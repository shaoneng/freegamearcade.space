Burnout City

How to Play Burnout City

Welcome to the perpetual night of Burnout City! Your main goal is to become the king of these streets. Start by choosing your ride from a selection of eleven high-performance cars. Each car has its own unique feel, so don't be afraid to try them all.

Once you've picked your car, head over to the customization menu. Here, you can truly make the car your own with a wide array of options, including new paint, rims, spoilers, and even a neon underglow. Don't worry about the cost—you have an unlimited supply of in-game currency! If you ever run low, just pop back to the main menu and click the 'add money' button to instantly refill your wallet.

With your car tuned to perfection, it's time to hit the streets. Drive around the open-world map and look for glowing circles. Each circle represents a different game mode. Simply drive into a circle to begin the challenge. You can test your speed against AI in Race Mode, chase down targets in Pursuit Mode, master precision driving in Trailblazer, beat the clock in Checkpoints, or simply cruise and perform stunts in the relaxing Free Ride mode.
