Burnout City

How to Play Burnout City

Burnout City offers an exhilarating car gaming experience with plenty of action. First, select one of the eleven available cars. Head to the customization menu to personalize your ride with slick paint jobs, neon underglow, and more. Don't worry about the budget – you have an unlimited supply of in-game currency! If you need more, simply return to the main menu and click the "add money" button.

Once your car is ready, enter the map. You'll find multiple circles representing different game modes. Drive over a circle to start a new challenge. Choose from Race Mode to compete against AI, Pursuit Mode for exciting chases, Trailblazer for precision cone hitting, Checkpoints for timed challenges, or Free Ride for pure exploration and stunt-filled fun.
