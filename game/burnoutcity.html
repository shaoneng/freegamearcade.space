Burnout City

How to Play Burnout City

Welcome to the perpetually night-covered streets of Burnout City! Your first step is to choose your ride from a selection of eleven awesome cars. Head straight to the customization menu to personalize your vehicle. With an unlimited supply of in-game currency (just click the "add money" button in the main menu if you run low), you can freely upgrade everything from slick paint jobs and body kits to neon underglow, making your car a true reflection of your style.

Once your car is tuned to perfection, it's time to hit the streets. Drive around the open-world map and you will see several glowing circles on the ground. Each circle represents a different game mode. To start a challenge, simply drive your car over the circle of your choice.

Burnout City offers five distinct game modes to test your skills. Choose Race Mode to compete against AI opponents, Pursuit Mode for intense police chases, Trailblazer to test your precision by hitting cones, or Checkpoints for a thrilling race against the clock. If you just want to explore, perform stunts, and enjoy the city, select Free Ride for a mission-free experience.
