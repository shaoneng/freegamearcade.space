Burnout City

How to Play Burnout City

Welcome to the neon-soaked streets of Burnout City! Your first step is to choose your ride from a selection of eleven awesome cars. Head over to the customization menu to personalize it. With an unlimited supply of in-game cash (just click the "add money" button in the main menu if you run low), you can go wild with paint jobs, neon underglow, and performance upgrades to make your car a true standout.

Once your car is tuned to perfection, drive out into the city map. You'll see several glowing circles, each representing a different challenge. To start a game mode, simply drive your car into the corresponding circle.

Master all five game modes:

  • Race Mode: Compete against AI opponents and prove you're the fastest on the streets.
  • Pursuit Mode: Engage in epic, high-speed chases and take down your target.
  • Trailblazer: Test your precision by hitting a series of cones before time runs out.
  • Checkpoints: Race against the clock, hitting all the checkpoints to win.
  • Free Ride: Explore the city at your own pace, discover secrets, and pull off insane stunts with no objectives.
