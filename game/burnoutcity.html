Burnout City

How to Play Burnout City

Welcome to the perpetual night of Burnout City! Your first stop is the garage, where you'll choose from eleven incredible cars. Head to the customization menu to personalize your ride with everything from slick paint jobs to vibrant neon underglow. Best of all, you have an unlimited supply of in-game cash! If you ever run low, just pop back to the main menu and hit the 'add money' button to instantly top up your wallet.

Once your car is tuned to perfection, drive out into the city map. You'll see several glowing circles, each representing a unique challenge. Simply drive into a circle to start a game mode. Test your speed against AI opponents in Race Mode, chase down targets in Pursuit Mode, navigate a precise cone course in Trailblazer, or beat the clock in Checkpoints. If you just want to explore and pull off stunts without any pressure, dive into the Free Ride mode.

The goal is to master the streets, complete challenges, and build the ultimate custom car. Explore every corner of the city, from high-speed highways to tricky drift-friendly alleys. Have fun and burn some rubber!
