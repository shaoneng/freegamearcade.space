Cake Match Puzzle

How to Play Cake Match Puzzle

Welcome to Cake Match Puzzle! Your goal is to become the ultimate bakery chef by merging cake slices to create complete, beautiful cakes. The game is played on a 4x4 tray, and your objective is to score as high as you can by making as many cakes as possible before you run out of moves or space.

Use your mouse to drag and drop cake slices from the queue onto the board. When you place slices of the same type next to each other, they will automatically merge. Continue merging slices until you form a complete cake, which will then be cleared from the board, freeing up valuable space.

Think strategically about where you place each slice to set up future matches and avoid getting stuck. If the tray fills up, the game is over! If you find yourself in a tight spot, you can use special boosters to help you clear the board and keep baking.
