Cake Match Puzzle

How to Play Cake Match Puzzle

Welcome to the sweet world of Cake Match Puzzle! Your goal is to become a master pastry chef by merging cake slices to create beautiful, complete cakes. The game is played on a 4x4 tray where new cake slices will appear.

Simply use your mouse to click and drag a cake slice onto an identical slice. When you merge enough matching slices, they will combine to form a full cake, clearing up valuable space on your tray and earning you points. Be strategic with your placements, as the tray will fill up quickly! If you run out of available moves or space, the game is over.
