Cake Match Puzzle

How to Play Cake Match Puzzle

Welcome to your very own virtual bakery! In Cake Match Puzzle, your goal is to become a master chef by assembling complete cakes. You'll be working on a 4x4 tray where cake slices appear. Your task is to strategically merge these slices to form full, delicious cakes.

Use your mouse to click and place the incoming cake slices onto the board. When you place slices of the same type next to each other, they will merge. Keep merging until you create a complete cake, which will then be cleared from the tray, freeing up valuable space.

The challenge is to keep the board from filling up completely. Plan your moves carefully to create combos and clear space efficiently. The game ends when you run out of available moves. Try to create as many cakes as you can to get a high score!

If you find yourself in a tricky situation, don't worry! You can use special boosters to help you clear the board and continue your baking spree.
