Cake Match Puzzle

How to Play Cake Match Puzzle

Welcome to the delightful world of Cake Match Puzzle! Your mission is to step into the shoes of a bakery chef and create as many complete cakes as possible. The game is played on a 4x4 tray where you'll be placing various cake slices.

The core mechanic is simple: use your mouse to select and place incoming cake slices onto the grid. When you arrange four identical slices to form a complete 2x2 square, they will automatically merge into a full cake, clearing those four spots and earning you points.

Strategy is key! You must think ahead and place your slices wisely to set up merges and keep the board from filling up. The game ends when there are no more available spaces on the tray, so manage your space carefully to achieve the highest score.

If you find yourself in a tricky situation, don't worry! You can use special boosters like "Freeze" to help you out of a jam. Keep merging, keep scoring, and enjoy the sweet satisfaction of baking beautiful cakes!
