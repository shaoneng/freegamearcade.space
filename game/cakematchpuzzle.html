Cake Match Puzzle

How to Play Cake Match Puzzle

Welcome to the bakery! In Cake Match Puzzle, your goal is to become a master pastry chef by creating as many complete cakes as possible. The game is played on a 4x4 tray, and your objective is to merge individual cake slices until you form a whole cake.

Using your mouse, you will place incoming cake slices onto the board. When you place a slice next to an identical one, they will merge into a larger piece. Continue merging pieces until a full cake is formed, which then clears up space and earns you points. Be strategic with your placements to keep the tray from filling up!

The game ends when there are no more available moves. Keep an eye out for special boosters that can help you when you're in a tight spot. Plan your moves carefully, manage your space, and aim for a high score by baking a delightful variety of cakes!
