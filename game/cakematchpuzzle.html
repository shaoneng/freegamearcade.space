Cake Match Puzzle

How to Play Cake Match Puzzle

Welcome to your very own virtual bakery! In Cake Match Puzzle, your goal is to become a master cake chef by assembling full cakes from individual slices. The game is played on a 4x4 tray where you'll be placing different cake pieces.

Use your mouse to drag and drop the cake slices that appear onto an empty spot on the tray. When you place slices of the same type next to each other, they will automatically merge. Continue merging slices until you form a complete, round cake!

Creating a full cake is a sweet victory! Not only does it score you points, but it also clears up valuable space on your tray, allowing you to continue playing. The key to a high score is to manage your space wisely and plan your moves ahead.

Be careful! If the entire 4x4 tray fills up with slices and you have no more moves left, the game is over. Think strategically about where you place each piece to maximize your merging opportunities and keep the board clear for as long as possible.
