Cake Match Puzzle

How to Play Cake Match Puzzle

Welcome to the delightful world of Cake Match Puzzle! Your goal is to become a master pastry chef by merging individual cake slices to create whole, delicious cakes. The game is played on a 4x4 tray where new slices will appear.

Use your mouse to click on an empty space on the tray to place the upcoming cake slice. When you place a slice next to another identical one, they will merge into a larger piece. Continue merging until you have a complete, round cake, which will then clear itself from the board and earn you points.

The challenge lies in managing your space. The board will fill up quickly, so you need to think strategically about where you place each slice. Plan your moves carefully to create as many full cakes as you can before you run out of available spaces. If the tray fills up completely, the game is over!
