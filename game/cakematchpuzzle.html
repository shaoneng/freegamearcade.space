Cake Match Puzzle

How to Play Cake Match Puzzle

Welcome to your virtual bakery! In Cake Match Puzzle, your goal is to become a master pastry chef by merging cake slices into complete cakes. You'll be presented with slices on a 4x4 grid. Simply use your mouse to drag and drop new slices from the side onto the board.

When you place slices of the same type next to each other, they will automatically merge. Keep merging them until you've formed a full, delicious cake! Completing a cake clears up valuable space on your tray, allowing you to continue playing.

The challenge lies in managing your space effectively. If the 4x4 tray fills up completely and you have no more valid moves, the game is over. Think strategically about where you place each slice to set up future merges and keep the board clear for as long as possible. See how many full cakes you can create!

Feeling stuck? Don't worry! The game provides helpful boosters to get you out of tight spots. Use them wisely to clear space and extend your gameplay.
