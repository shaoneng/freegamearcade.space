Cake Match Puzzle

How to Play Cake Match Puzzle

Welcome to the sweet world of Cake Match Puzzle! As a bakery chef, your goal is to merge slices to create full cakes on a 4x4 tray.

Use your mouse to drag and drop the incoming cake slices onto the board. When you successfully group four identical slices together, they will automatically merge into a complete cake, freeing up valuable space on your tray.

The key is strategy! Plan your moves carefully to keep the tray from filling up. The game ends when there are no more possible moves. Challenge yourself to bake as many cakes as possible and achieve a high score!

If you get stuck, you can use powerful boosters like Freeze and Nova to help clear the board and keep the fun going.
