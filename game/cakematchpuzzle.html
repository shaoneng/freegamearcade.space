Cake Match Puzzle

How to Play Cake Match Puzzle

Welcome to your virtual bakery! The objective in Cake Match Puzzle is to become a master chef by merging individual cake slices into complete, delicious cakes. You'll be working on a 4x4 grid, and your goal is to create as many full cakes as you can to rack up a high score before the tray fills up and you run out of space.

Using your mouse, you will drag and drop new cake slices onto the board. When you place a slice next to one or more matching slices, they will automatically merge together. Keep merging until you have a full cake, which will then clear itself from the board, freeing up valuable space for more creations. Think ahead and plan your moves carefully to create combos and keep the puzzle going!

Feeling stuck? Don't worry! The game provides helpful boosters to get you out of a tight spot. Use these special power-ups to clear unwanted slices or rearrange the board, giving you a second chance to continue your cake-making spree. Use them wisely to maximize your score!
