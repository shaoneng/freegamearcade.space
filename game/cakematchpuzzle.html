Cake Match Puzzle

How to Play Cake Match Puzzle

Welcome to your own personal bakery! In Cake Match Puzzle, your goal is to become a master pastry chef by merging cake slices to create beautiful, full cakes.

You will play on a 4x4 tray. Slices of different cakes will appear, and you must use your mouse to place them strategically on the board. When you place slices of the same cake next to each other, they will merge. Keep merging until you have a complete, round cake!

The challenge is to manage your space wisely. If the 4x4 tray fills up completely and you have no more valid moves, the game is over. Think ahead and plan your placements to keep clearing space and making room for new slices. Complete as many cakes as you can to achieve a high score!

Stuck? Don't worry! The game offers helpful boosters to get you out of a tight spot. Use them wisely to continue your cake-making adventure.
