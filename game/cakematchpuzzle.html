Cake Match Puzzle

How to Play Cake Match Puzzle

Welcome to your virtual bakery! In Cake Match Puzzle, your goal is to become the ultimate cake master. The game is played on a 4x4 tray where you'll be given different slices of cake. Your task is to merge identical slices to create a full, delicious cake.

Use your mouse to drag and drop cake slices onto the board. When you place slices of the same type next to each other, they will merge. Once you've combined enough slices to form a complete circle, the cake is finished and clears up space, earning you points!

The challenge is to manage the limited space on your tray. Plan your placements carefully to create combos and clear cakes efficiently. The game ends when the tray is completely full and you can no longer make any moves. Aim to create as many cakes as you can to achieve the highest score!
