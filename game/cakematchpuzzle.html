Cake Match Puzzle

How to Play Cake Match Puzzle

Welcome to your very own virtual bakery! In Cake Match Puzzle, your goal is to become the ultimate cake chef by merging slices into complete cakes. The game is played on a 4x4 tray where cake slices will appear.

Use your mouse to drag and drop the incoming cake slices onto the board. When you place slices of the same cake next to each other, they will automatically merge. Continue merging until you have a complete, round cake. A completed cake will clear itself from the board, giving you points and more space to play.

The challenge is to keep making matches and clearing cakes before the entire 4x4 tray fills up. If you run out of valid moves, the game is over. Think ahead and plan your placements to create chain reactions and keep your bakery running smoothly!

If you get stuck, don't worry! You can use special boosters like the Freeze to help you clear the board and continue your delightful baking adventure.
