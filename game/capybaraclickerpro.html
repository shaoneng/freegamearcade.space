Capybara Clicker Pro

How to Play Capybara Clicker Pro

The goal in Capybara Clicker Pro is simple and satisfying: click on the main capybara to generate coins. Each tap adds to your total, starting your journey to becoming a capybara mogul.

To accelerate your earnings, invest your coins in the upgrades listed on the right side of the screen. You can boost the number of coins you get per click or increase your passive income, which generates coins automatically every second. Strategic purchasing is key to rapid growth!

As your coin count soars, you can unlock fun cosmetic items. Click the t-shirt icon at the top to browse and equip a variety of adorable new capybara characters. You can also change the scenery by clicking the cloud icon to switch between different weather conditions like sun, rain, and snow.

Keep an eye on the pink progress bar in the bottom-left corner. As you click, this bar fills up. Once it's full, you'll activate a temporary boost, doubling the coins you earn for a short period!
