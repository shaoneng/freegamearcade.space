Capybara Clicker Pro

How to Play Capybara Clicker Pro

Welcome to the charming world of Capybara Clicker Pro! The goal is simple: click on the adorable capybara at the center of your screen to generate coins. Every click adds to your treasury, starting your journey to becoming a capybara tycoon.

As you accumulate coins, you can invest them in powerful upgrades. Look to the right side of the screen for various boosters. These allow you to increase the number of coins you earn per click and also generate passive income every second. Strategically purchasing these upgrades is key to maximizing your earnings.

The fun doesn't stop with coins! You can unlock a variety of cool new looks for your capybara by clicking the t-shirt icon at the top of the screen. Want to change the scenery? Click the cloud icon to switch between different weather conditions like sunny, rainy, snowy, or even a starry night.

Keep an eye on the pink bar in the bottom-left corner. As you click continuously, this bar will fill up. Once it's full, you'll activate a bonus mode, doubling the coins you earn for a short period. Click fast to fill the bar and boost your income!
