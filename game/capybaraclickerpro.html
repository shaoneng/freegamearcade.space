Capybara Clicker Pro

How to Play Capybara Clicker Pro

The gameplay is simple and incredibly addictive. Your primary goal is to click on the cute capybara at the center of the screen to generate coins. Every tap adds to your total coin count, setting you on the path to becoming a capybara tycoon.

To accelerate your coin collection, invest in upgrades available on the right side of the screen. These boosters will increase the number of coins you earn per click and also provide a steady stream of passive income every second. Strategically purchasing these upgrades is key to advancing quickly.

As you accumulate wealth, you can unlock fun cosmetic items to customize your experience. Click the t-shirt icon at the top of the screen to discover and switch between a variety of adorable capybara characters. You can also change the game's ambiance by clicking the cloud icon to cycle through different weather conditions like sunny, rainy, snowy, or a starry night.

Keep an eye on the pink progress bar in the lower-left corner. As you click, this bar will fill up. Once it's completely full, you'll activate a bonus mode that temporarily doubles the coins you earn from every click!
