Capybara Clicker Pro

How to Play Capybara Clicker Pro

Welcome to Capybara Clicker Pro, an engaging idle game where the goal is to generate as many coins as possible. The gameplay is straightforward: simply click on the adorable capybara at the center of your screen to earn coins with every tap.

To boost your earnings, look to the right side of the screen. Here you'll find a list of upgrades you can purchase with your coins. These upgrades will increase the number of coins you generate per click and automatically per second. Strategically investing in these boosters is the key to achieving massive coin totals.

As you accumulate wealth, you'll unlock fun cosmetic options. Click the t-shirt icon at the top of the screen to browse and equip different cute capybara skins. You can also change the background by clicking the cloud icon, allowing you to switch between sunny, rainy, snowy, and starry night weather conditions for a customized vibe.

Pay attention to the pink bar in the bottom-left corner. As you click, this bar fills up. Once full, it activates a special boost, temporarily doubling the coins you earn. Click away and become the ultimate capybara tycoon!
