Capybara Clicker Pro

How to Play Capybara Clicker Pro

The objective in Capybara Clicker Pro is charmingly straightforward: click the main capybara on the screen to generate coins. Every single tap adds to your growing fortune. The more you click, the more coins you'll accumulate!

To speed up your coin production, invest your earnings in the upgrades listed on the right side of the screen. These boosters will increase the number of coins you get per click and also provide a passive income, generating coins automatically every second. This allows you to build your empire even when you're taking a break from clicking.

As your wealth grows, you can unlock fun cosmetic customizations. Click the t-shirt icon at the top to browse and purchase new, adorable capybara characters. You can also change the background scenery by clicking the cloud icon, allowing you to switch the weather from sunny to rainy, snowy, or even a starry night!

Pay attention to the pink progress bar in the lower-left corner. As you continuously click, this bar fills up. Once it's completely full, you'll activate a temporary boost that doubles all the coins you earn for a short time, so click away!
