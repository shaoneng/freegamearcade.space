Capybara Clicker Pro

How to Play Capybara Clicker Pro

The gameplay in Capybara Clicker Pro is delightfully simple. Your main objective is to click on the adorable capybara at the center of the screen to generate coins. Every click adds to your total, starting you on your path to becoming a capybara tycoon.

To accelerate your coin production, you can purchase upgrades from the menu on the right. These boosters will increase the number of coins you earn per click and also provide automatic income per second, allowing your wealth to grow even when you're not actively clicking.

As your coin count soars, you'll unlock new cosmetic items. Click the t-shirt icon at the top to browse and equip different capybara characters, each one cuter than the last. You can also change the background weather by clicking the cloud icon, cycling through sunny, rainy, snowy, or starry nights to set the perfect mood.

Keep an eye on the pink progress bar in the bottom left corner. As you click, this bar will fill up. Once it's completely full, you'll activate a bonus mode, temporarily doubling your coin earnings for a massive boost!
