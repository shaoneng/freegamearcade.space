Capybara Clicker Pro

How to Play Capybara Clicker Pro

Welcome to the charming and addictive world of Capybara Clicker Pro! The gameplay is wonderfully simple: just click on the main capybara on your screen to start earning coins. The more you click, the more coins you'll accumulate.

To accelerate your coin collection, use your earnings to buy upgrades from the menu on the right. You can invest in boosts that increase the number of coins you get with every single click, as well as automatic upgrades that generate coins for you every second, even when you're not clicking!

As your capybara empire grows, you can unlock fun cosmetic items. Click the t-shirt icon at the top to browse and unlock a variety of adorable new capybara skins. You can also change the scenery by clicking the cloud icon to switch between sunny, rainy, snowy, and starry night weather conditions.

Keep an eye on the pink progress bar in the bottom-left corner. As you click, this bar will fill up. Once it's full, you'll activate a special boost, doubling your coin earnings for a short period. Keep clicking, upgrading, and unlocking to become the ultimate capybara tycoon!
