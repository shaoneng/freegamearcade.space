Capybara Clicker Pro

How to Play Capybara Clicker Pro

Welcome to the charming world of Capybara Clicker Pro! The goal is simple and addictive: click on the cute capybara to generate coins. The more you click, the more you earn, setting you on a path to becoming a capybara tycoon.

To accelerate your coin production, invest in the boosters available on the right side of the screen. These upgrades will increase the number of coins you get per click and even generate coins for you automatically every second. Strategic investment in these boosters is key to progressing quickly.

As your fortune grows, you can unlock a variety of delightful new outfits for your capybara by clicking the t-shirt icon at the top. You can also change the scenery by clicking the cloud icon, unlocking different weather conditions like rain, snow, or a starry night to customize your clicking experience.

Keep an eye on the pink bar in the bottom left corner. As you click rapidly, this bar will fill up. Once it's completely full, you'll activate a bonus mode, doubling the coins you earn for a short period!
