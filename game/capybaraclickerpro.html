Capybara Clicker Pro

How to Play Capybara Clicker Pro

Welcome to the charming and addictive world of Capybara Clicker Pro! Your main goal is simple: click on the adorable capybara at the center of the screen to generate coins. Every tap adds to your wealth!

Look to the right side of the screen to find a list of powerful boosters. Spend your hard-earned coins on these upgrades to automatically increase the number of coins you generate per second and per click. Investing wisely in these boosters is the key to accelerating your progress and reaching astronomical coin counts.

As your coin empire grows, you can unlock fun cosmetic items. Click the t-shirt icon at the top to discover and switch between a variety of cute capybara characters. You can also change the scenery by clicking the cloud icon, which lets you unlock and set different weather conditions like rain, snow, or a starry night.

Keep an eye on the pink bar in the bottom-left corner. As you click, this bar will fill up. Once it's full, you'll activate a temporary boost, doubling the number of coins you earn for a short period!
