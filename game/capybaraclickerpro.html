Capybara Clicker Pro

How to Play Capybara Clicker Pro

Tap the adorable Capybara to earn Capybara coins! The primary goal of Capybara Clicker Pro is to collect as many coins as possible by repeatedly clicking on the main character.

To significantly boost your coin earnings per second and per click, purchase upgrades and boosters located on the right side of the game screen. Invest your hard-earned Capybara coins wisely to advance faster.

As you accumulate more coins, you'll unlock a variety of new, charming Capybara characters. Discover these wonderful critters by clicking on the t-shirt icon at the top of the game screen.

Beyond characters, you can also change the weather conditions! Click the cloud icon at the top to explore and unlock different atmospheric effects like rain, snow, sun, and even starry nights.

Keep an eye on the pink bar in the lower-left corner. Continuously clicking on the Capybara fills this bar, and once complete, you'll activate a special boost that doubles your coin earnings for a short period!
