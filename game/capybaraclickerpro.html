Capybara Clicker Pro

How to Play Capybara Clicker Pro

The objective in Capybara Clicker Pro is delightfully simple: click on the cute capybara to earn coins. Every tap adds to your total, so get clicking!

On the right side of the screen, you'll find a list of boosters and upgrades. Spend your hard-earned coins on these to increase the number of coins you get per click and automatically per second. Investing in these upgrades is key to progressing faster.

As you accumulate more coins, you can unlock new cosmetic items. Click the t-shirt icon at the top to discover and equip a variety of adorable new capybara characters. You can also change the scenery by clicking the cloud icon to switch between sunny, rainy, snowy, and even starry night weather conditions.

Keep an eye on the pink bar in the bottom-left corner. As you click, this bar fills up. Once it's full, you'll activate a temporary boost, earning double the coins for a short period!
