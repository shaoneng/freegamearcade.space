Capybara Clicker Pro

How to Play Capybara Clicker Pro

The gameplay is charmingly simple: click on the giant capybara at the center of the screen to generate coins. Your primary goal is to accumulate as many coins as possible to become the ultimate capybara tycoon.

To accelerate your coin production, you must invest in upgrades. On the right side of the screen, you will find boosters that increase your coins per click and your passive income per second. Strategically purchasing these upgrades is the key to progressing quickly and achieving enormous coin totals.

As you reach new milestones, you'll unlock fun cosmetic options. Click the t-shirt icon at the top to browse and unlock new, even cuter capybara characters. You can also change the scenery by clicking the cloud icon to switch between sunny, rainy, snowy, and even starry night weather conditions.

Keep an eye on the pink progress bar in the lower-left corner. As you click, this bar will fill up. Once it's completely full, you'll activate a special bonus mode that temporarily doubles the number of coins you earn!
