Car Football

How to Play Car Football

Welcome to the chaotic and fun-filled arena of Car Football! The objective is simple: drive your powerful monster truck and smash a giant soccer ball into your opponent's goal. Each match lasts for two minutes, and the player with the most goals at the end wins. You can challenge the computer through 30 challenging levels or take on a friend in an exciting 2-player mode on the same device.

Mastering your vehicle is key to victory. Use your controls to accelerate, brake, and turn. The most important move is the jump, which allows you to launch your truck into the air to hit high balls or make incredible aerial shots. Don't forget to play defense! You can strategically block your opponent's path and ram them to disrupt their attacks.

Winning isn't just for glory; it's for gold! You'll earn 10 gold coins for every goal you score, but only if you win the match. Save up your coins to unlock a fleet of six different monster trucks, each with its own style. When playing with a friend, you can also customize the experience by choosing from five unique game environments. Get behind the wheel and start scoring!
