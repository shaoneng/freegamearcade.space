Car Football

How to Play Car Football

Welcome to the high-octane world of Car Football! The objective is simple: drive your powerful monster truck and smash the giant soccer ball into your opponent's goal. Each match is a fast-paced two-minute showdown. Score more goals than your opponent before time runs out to claim victory!

Winning isn't just for glory! For every goal you score, you'll earn 10 gold coins. Rack up these coins to unlock a fleet of new, more powerful vehicles from the garage. Remember, you only get to keep your coins if you win the match, so every goal counts!

Mastering control is key. Use your vehicle's speed to outmaneuver your rival and time your jumps perfectly to intercept the ball mid-air. Don't be afraid to get aggressive! Block your opponent's path and use your truck's bulk to create scoring opportunities. The game can be played solo or with a friend on the same device for an even more exciting challenge across five different environments.
