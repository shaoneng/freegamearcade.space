Car Football

How to Play Car Football

Welcome to Car Football, where monster trucks meet the soccer pitch! The goal is to drive your vehicle and smash the giant ball into your opponent's net. Each match is a fast-paced two-minute showdown, and the player with the most goals at the final whistle is the champion.

Maneuver your truck, boost for speed, and jump to perform epic aerial shots. A key part of the strategy is using your vehicle's bulk to block your opponent and defend your goal. You can play solo against the AI through 30 challenging levels or grab a friend for an exciting 2-player match on the same computer. When playing with a friend, you can even select from five different arenas!

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use the WASD keys to drive.
  • Player 2: Use the Arrow Keys to drive.

Winning isn't just for glory—it's for gold! You'll earn 10 coins for every goal scored in a winning match. Use these coins to unlock a fleet of awesome new monster trucks and dominate the field in style.
