Car Football

How to Play Car Football

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled arena of Car Football! This game mashes up the high-speed chaos of monster trucks with the classic thrill of soccer. Your objective is simple: drive your car and knock the giant soccer ball into your opponent's goal before the time runs out. Each match is a fast-paced two-minute showdown where every goal counts!

Master your vehicle's controls to outmaneuver your rival. You can accelerate, reverse, and turn, but the key to victory lies in a well-timed jump or a strategic boost of speed. Use your jump to perform aerial hits and block shots, and use your boost to close the distance to the ball or deliver a powerful strike. You can play solo against the computer or challenge a friend in an exciting 2-player mode on the same device!

Controls

  • Player 1: Use WASD keys to drive and jump.
  • Player 2: Use the Arrow Keys to drive and jump.

For every goal you score, you'll earn 10 gold coins, but only if you win the match! Collect these coins to unlock a variety of awesome new monster trucks from the garage, each with its own unique look. You can also customize your experience by choosing from five different game environments when playing with a friend. Now, hit the field and score some goals!
