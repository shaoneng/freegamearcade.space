Car Football

How to Play Car Football

Welcome to the high-octane arena of Car Football! The goal is simple: drive your monster truck and push the giant soccer ball into your opponent's net. Each match lasts for two minutes, and the player with the most goals at the end wins!

Control your vehicle's speed and direction to outmaneuver your rival. A well-timed jump can be the key to hitting the ball in mid-air or blocking a shot. In 2-player mode, you and a friend can compete on the same device, choosing from five different arenas to battle in.

Winning isn't just for glory! For every goal you score, you'll earn 10 gold coins. Save up your coins to unlock a total of 6 different powerful cars, each with its own style. Remember, you only get coins if you win the match, so play to win!

You can also hone your skills in single-player mode, which features 30 challenging levels to conquer. Good luck, and may the best driver win!
