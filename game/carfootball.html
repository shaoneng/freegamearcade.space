Car Football

How to Play Car Football

Welcome to the high-octane arena of Car Football, where monster trucks replace players in a frantic battle for soccer supremacy! The objective is simple: drive your powerful vehicle and smash the giant soccer ball into your opponent's goal. Each match lasts for two minutes, and the player with the most goals at the end wins.

Car Football is best enjoyed with a friend in its exciting 2-player mode, played on the same device. You can unlock new, more powerful cars by earning coins from your victories. For every goal you score in a winning match, you'll earn 10 gold coins. Use these coins to purchase from a selection of six different monster trucks. You can also choose from five unique stadiums when challenging a friend. Master your vehicle's speed and use the jump feature to perform incredible aerial shots and block your opponent's path to victory!

Controls

Player 1 Controls:
  • Move: A/D or Left/Right Arrow Keys
  • Jump: W or Up Arrow Key
Player 2 Controls:
  • Move: J/L Keys
  • Jump: I Key
