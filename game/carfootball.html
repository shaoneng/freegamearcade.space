Car Football

How to Play Car Football

Welcome to Car Football, where soccer meets monster trucks! The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent within the two-minute time limit. Drive your powerful car and smash the giant ball into the opposing net.

For every goal you score, you'll earn 10 gold coins. Collect these coins by winning matches to unlock new and better vehicles. There are six unique cars to choose from, but you'll need to prove your skills to get them all! Remember, you only keep the coins if you win the game.

You can play solo against the AI or challenge a friend in the exciting 2-player mode on the same device. Choose from five different arenas to keep the action fresh. Master controlling your car's speed and use the jump ability strategically to hit the ball out of the air or to block your opponent's path. Good luck!
