Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel offers a diverse driving experience that you can enjoy solo or with a friend in a local two-player, split-screen mode. The core objective is to complete missions across various game modes to earn coins. These coins can then be used in the garage to purchase and unlock new, faster sports cars.

The game is divided into several modes, each with its own unique challenge. In Race Mode, you'll compete against your friend and AI drivers on city tracks. Parking and Challenge Modes test your precision and control as you navigate tight spaces and park perfectly before time runs out. For those who love destruction, Crash Mode rewards you for smashing into as many cars as possible. You can also weave through traffic in Traffic Mode, master cornering in Drift Mode, or just explore the open city without any rules in Free Ride Mode.

Controls for each player are displayed on screen. Typically, Player 1 uses the WASD keys to drive and Player 2 uses the Arrow Keys. Follow the on-screen prompts for actions like using NOS boosts to gain an edge in races.
