Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel offers various exciting game modes for solo play or two players on a split screen, all within a dynamic 3D open-world city. Earn coins by completing tasks or finding money bags scattered around the map, then head to the garage to unlock new, faster cars.

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: W (Forward), S (Brake/Reverse), A (Left), D (Right)
  • NOS Boost: Left Shift

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: Up Arrow (Forward), Down Arrow (Brake/Reverse), Left Arrow (Left), Right Arrow (Right)
  • NOS Boost: M Key

Game Modes:

  • Race Mode: Compete against a friend and two CPU cars on 10 unique tracks. Use your NOS boost strategically and master cornering to cross the finish line first.
  • Parking Precision: Test your accuracy in 20 timed parking scenarios. Maneuver carefully, avoid barriers, and complete the mission before the clock runs out, as every bump damages your car.
  • Checkpoint Parking: Drive through specific small checkpoints and park perfectly in highlighted spots. This mode requires precise control and patience, with levels becoming more complex and time limits shrinking.
  • Crash Mode: Your mission is to cause maximum destruction. Hit as many cars as possible to earn points, watching them explode. The more cars you smash, the more coins you collect at the end of the round.
  • Traffic Mode: Speed through busy city lanes, skillfully overtaking civilian cars without crashing. Each successful overtake adds to your score, allowing you to drive better sports cars in subsequent runs.
  • Drift Mode: Achieve speeds over 80 km/h and steer sharply to pull off long drifts around corners. Maintain tight control and your angle to rack up a high score and prove your drifting skills.
  • Free Roam: No goals, no pressure. Just you and the city. Explore ramps, jump off rooftops, take in the scenery, practice tricks, or simply collect money bags scattered on the streets. It's perfect for experimenting or relaxing between challenges.
