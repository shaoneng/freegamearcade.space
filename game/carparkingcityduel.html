Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a comprehensive driving game that you can enjoy solo or with a friend on a split screen. The game offers a variety of modes set in a large, open-world city. Your primary goal across all modes is to earn coins, which you can use in the garage to unlock faster and better cars.

Choose from several game modes, each with a unique objective:

Parking & Precision Parking: Test your driving accuracy. Navigate through tight spaces and avoid obstacles to park your car perfectly in the designated area before the timer runs out. Your car has a health bar, so be gentle with your maneuvers!

Race Mode: Compete against a friend and two AI cars on 10 different city tracks. Use your NOS boost wisely and master the corners to finish first.

Crash Mode: Unleash chaos! Your mission is to crash into as many cars as you can within the time limit. Every car you hit explodes, earning you points and coins.

Drift Mode: Show off your control by drifting around corners. The longer and smoother your drift, the more points you'll score.

Free Roam: No rules, no objectives. Simply drive around the city, explore, find hidden ramps, jump off buildings, or collect money bags scattered throughout the map. It's the perfect mode to relax or practice your skills.
