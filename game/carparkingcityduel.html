Car Parking City Duel

About Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a free online driving game where you can play solo or on split-screen with a friend. It features a 3D open-world city and combines realistic driving physics with a variety of fun game modes, so you're not limited to just parking. This game also lets you race through traffic, drift, crash into cars, complete timed missions, and freely explore the map.

You earn coins by completing tasks or collecting money bags scattered on the map. Head to the garage to unlock better, faster cars with your coins. Whether you prefer solo or local two-player, mission-based challenges or free roam, Car Parking City Duel offers something for every kind of driving fan.

Game Modes

Race Mode: Go head-to-head against a friend and two AI cars on 10 unique tracks. Use your NOS boost wisely and master tight corners to be the first to cross the finish line.

Parking Mode: Test your parking precision across 20 different scenarios. Maneuver carefully to park without damaging your car before time runs out.

Checkpoint Mode: A true test of control where you must drive through small checkpoints and park perfectly in highlighted spots, all under a shrinking time limit.

Crash Mode: Your goal is to cause chaos! Hit as many cars as possible to earn points. The more cars you explode, the more coins you'll get.

Traffic Run Mode: Skillfully speed through busy traffic, overtaking civilian cars without crashing to rack up a high score and unlock better sports cars.

Drift Mode: Speed over 80 km/h and steer sharply to execute long, stylish drifts around corners. Maintain control to earn a high drift score.

Free Roam: No goals, no rules, no pressure. Just you and the city. Explore, practice stunts, or simply relax and drive around.
