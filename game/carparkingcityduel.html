Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to the ultimate driving sandbox! Car Parking City Duel offers a massive open-world city where you can play solo or challenge a friend on the same screen. The goal is to earn coins by completing various missions to unlock faster and better cars from the garage.

Controls

  • Player 1: Use W, A, S, D to drive, Spacebar for the handbrake, and F for NOS boost.
  • Player 2: Use the Arrow Keys to drive, K for the handbrake, and L for NOS boost.

Game Modes

Choose from a variety of exciting game modes:

  • Race Mode: Compete against a friend and AI opponents on 10 different tracks. Use your NOS boost strategically to get ahead.
  • Parking & Precision Mode: Test your driving accuracy in timed challenges. Navigate tight spaces and park perfectly without damaging your vehicle.
  • Crash Mode: The objective is simple: cause as much chaos as possible! Smash into other cars to make them explode and rack up points.
  • Traffic Run: Weave through busy traffic lanes at high speed. Overtake cars closely without crashing to maximize your score.
  • Drift Mode: Show off your control by executing long, smooth drifts around corners. The longer the drift, the more points you earn.
  • Free Roam: Explore the city with no rules or objectives. Practice your skills, find hidden ramps, or just enjoy a relaxing drive.
