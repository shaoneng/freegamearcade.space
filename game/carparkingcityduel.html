Car Parking City Duel

About Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a comprehensive free online driving game that you can enjoy solo or with a friend on a split screen. Dive into a massive 3D open-world city where realistic driving physics meet a wide array of exciting game modes. Forget just parking; here you can race through traffic, execute perfect drifts, cause mayhem in crash mode, take on timed missions, or just explore the city at your own pace.

Earn coins by completing challenges or finding money bags hidden throughout the city. Use your earnings in the garage to unlock a fleet of faster, more powerful cars. Whether you're playing alone or challenging a friend locally, Car Parking City Duel offers a customized driving experience for every fan of the genre.

Game Modes

Race Mode: Go head-to-head against a friend and two AI opponents across 10 unique tracks. Navigate tight corners, weave through city traffic, and time your NOS boosts perfectly to claim victory.

Parking Mode: Test your precision in 20 realistic parking scenarios. Maneuver carefully around obstacles and park before the timer runs out. Keep an eye on your car's health—every bump costs you!

Precision Parking Mode: This mode takes parking to the next level. Drive through narrow checkpoints and park flawlessly in designated spots against an ever-shrinking time limit. It's a true test of vehicle control and patience.

Crash Mode: Unleash your destructive side! Your goal is to crash into as many cars as possible. Every collision results in an explosion and earns you points and coins. It's pure, chaotic fun.

Traffic Mode: Skillfully navigate busy highways, overtaking civilian cars at high speed without crashing. Each successful overtake boosts your score, helping you unlock better sports cars for your next run.

Drift Mode: Get sideways and rack up drift points. Speed past 80 km/h, then steer sharply into corners to maintain long, stylish drifts. Smooth control is key to achieving a high score.

Free Roam: No rules, no objectives, no pressure. Simply explore the vast city, discover hidden ramps, practice your stunts, or just enjoy a relaxing drive. It's the perfect way to unwind between challenges.
