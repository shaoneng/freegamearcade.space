Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a versatile driving game offering modes for both solo and two-player split-screen action. The game features realistic driving physics across a large, open-world city. Complete missions or collect money bags to earn coins, which you can use to unlock faster and better cars in the garage.

Player 1 Controls: Use WASD to drive, the Spacebar for the handbrake/drift, and F for NOS (nitro boost).

Player 2 Controls: Use the Arrow Keys to drive, K for the handbrake/drift, and L for NOS (nitro boost).

Game Modes:

Race Mode: Compete against a friend and AI opponents on various city tracks. Use your driving skill and timely NOS boosts to be the first to cross the finish line.

Parking & Precision Parking: Test your control by carefully maneuvering your car into designated spots before the timer runs out. Avoid hitting obstacles, as each bump damages your vehicle and reduces your health.

Crash Mode: Your goal is destruction! Smash into as many other cars as you can to earn points and coins. The more chaos you cause, the higher your score.

Free Roam: Explore the open-world city with no rules or objectives. It's the perfect mode to practice your driving, find hidden ramps and secrets, or just enjoy the scenery with a friend.
