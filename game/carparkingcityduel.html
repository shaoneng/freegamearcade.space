Car Parking City Duel

About Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a free online driving game where you can play solo or on split-screen with a friend. It features a vast 3D open-world city and combines realistic driving physics with a variety of fun game modes. You're not limited to just parking; this game also lets you race through traffic, drift, crash into cars, complete timed missions, and freely explore the map without rules.

What makes this car game stand out is how it wraps up different driving games into one big package. Whether you want to show off your drifting skills, roam the city, or cause mayhem in crash mode, there's something for you. You earn coins by completing tasks or finding money bags scattered on the map, which you can use in the garage to unlock better, faster cars. Your rules, your game.

Game Modes

Race Mode: Go head-to-head against a friend and two AI opponents across 10 unique tracks. Navigate tight corners, city traffic, and open stretches. Use your NOS boost wisely and perfect your cornering to claim victory.

Parking Mode: Test your precision in 20 realistic, timed parking scenarios. Maneuver carefully around obstacles to park before time runs out. Keep an eye on your health bar—every bump causes damage!

Extreme Parking Mode: This mode takes precision to the next level. Drive through small checkpoints and park perfectly in highlighted spots. Levels get more complex and time limits shrink, demanding patience and smooth control.

Crash Mode: Your mission is simple: hit as many cars as possible. Every crash earns points, and other cars explode on impact, like driving a tank. The more destruction you cause, the more coins you'll earn.

Traffic Mode: Speed through busy traffic lanes and overtake civilian cars without crashing. Each successful overtake adds to your score, helping you unlock better sports cars for your next run.

Drift Mode: Speed over 80 km/h and steer sharply to execute long drifts around corners. Rack up a high score with smooth entries, tight control, and maintaining your drift angle without hitting walls.

Free Roam: No goals, no pressure. Just you and the city. Drive wherever you want, explore ramps, jump off rooftops, or simply practice your skills. It's the perfect mode to relax between intense challenges or hunt for money bags.
