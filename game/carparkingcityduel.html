Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a versatile online driving game that you can enjoy solo or on a split-screen with a friend. Dive into a massive 3D open-world city where realistic driving physics meet a wide array of game modes. This isn't just about parking—you can race, drift, crash, and explore to your heart's content.

Complete missions or find money bags scattered across the map to earn coins. Use your earnings in the garage to unlock faster and better cars. Whether you prefer mission-based challenges or a lawless free-roam experience, Car Parking City Duel lets you set the rules.

Game Modes

Race Mode: Compete against a friend and AI opponents on 10 unique tracks. Master tight corners and use your NOS boost strategically to claim victory.

Parking Mode: Test your precision in 20 timed scenarios. Navigate tight spaces and park perfectly before the clock runs out, but be careful—every bump damages your car!

Precision Parking: A true test of control. Drive through small checkpoints and park with ultimate accuracy in challenging, time-sensitive levels.

Crash Mode: Unleash chaos! Your goal is to cause as much destruction as possible by crashing into other cars. The more you smash, the more coins you earn.

Traffic Mode: Weave through busy traffic at high speeds. Each successful overtake adds to your score, allowing you to unlock even faster sports cars.

Drift Mode: Hit speeds over 80 km/h and slide around corners to rack up drift points. Smooth control is key to achieving a high score.

Free Roam: No rules, no goals. Just you, your car, and a vast city to explore. Practice stunts, find hidden ramps, or simply enjoy a relaxing drive.
