Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to Car Parking City Duel! This game offers a vast open-world city and multiple game modes for you to enjoy either solo or with a friend in split-screen mode. The primary goal across most modes is to complete challenges, earn coins, and use them to unlock faster and better cars in the garage.

The game is divided into several exciting modes:

  • Race Mode: Compete against your friend and AI opponents on 10 different tracks. Use your NOS boost strategically and master the corners to finish first.
  • Parking Mode: Test your precision by parking your car in designated spots under a time limit. Avoid hitting obstacles, as damage will reduce your health bar.
  • Crash Mode: The goal is simple: cause as much destruction as possible! Smash into other cars to make them explode and rack up points.
  • Drift Mode: Show off your driving skills by executing long, smooth drifts around corners to earn drift points.
  • Free Roam: Explore the city with no rules or objectives. Discover hidden ramps, practice your stunts, or just enjoy a relaxing drive.

You can choose to play by yourself or challenge a friend in local two-player mode. Navigate the menus to select your preferred game mode and car. Keep an eye out for money bags scattered around the city in free roam to boost your earnings!
