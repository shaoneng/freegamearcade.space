Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to Car Parking City Duel, an expansive driving game that offers much more than just parking! Whether you're playing solo or with a friend on split-screen, you can choose from a variety of exciting game modes set in a vast 3D city. The objective varies by mode, but the core goal is to have fun, complete challenges, and earn coins to upgrade your ride.

The controls are simple and designed for both single-player and two-player action:

  • Player 1: Use WASD to drive, Left Shift for NOS (boost), and R to reset your car's position.
  • Player 2: Use the Arrow Keys to drive, M for NOS, and O to reset.

Explore the different modes to find your favorite way to play:

  • Free Drive: No rules, no time limits. Freely roam the city, practice your driving, find ramps, and collect hidden money bags scattered across the map.
  • Racing: Compete against your friend and AI opponents on 10 different tracks. Use your NOS strategically to get ahead and be the first to cross the finish line.
  • Parking: Test your precision in timed parking challenges. Navigate tight spaces and park perfectly without damaging your vehicle.
  • Crash: Your mission is simple: cause as much destruction as possible by crashing into other cars. The more you smash, the more points you earn!
  • Drifting: Show off your control by sliding around corners. Rack up a high score by executing long, smooth drifts.

Complete missions and collect cash to earn coins, which you can spend in the garage to unlock faster and better sports cars. Your rules, your game—dive in and dominate the streets!
