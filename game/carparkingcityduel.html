Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to the sprawling metropolis of Car Parking City Duel! This game offers a complete driving experience that you can enjoy solo or with a friend in split-screen mode. The goal changes depending on the mode you select, from precision parking to chaotic destruction. Earn coins by completing challenges and finding money bags to unlock faster, better cars in the garage.

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use WASD to drive the car. Press Left Shift for a nitro boost and R to reset your car's position.
  • Player 2: Use the Arrow Keys to drive. Press Right Shift for a nitro boost and P to reset your car's position.

Game Modes:

  • Race: Compete against your friend and AI opponents on various city tracks.
  • Parking: Test your maneuvering skills by parking perfectly in designated spots before time runs out.
  • Drift: Score points by executing long, smooth drifts around corners.
  • Crash: Cause as much destruction as possible by smashing into other vehicles.
  • Free Roam: Explore the city at your own pace with no rules or objectives.
