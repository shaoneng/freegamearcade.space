Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to Car Parking City Duel! This game offers a massive open-world city filled with various driving challenges that you can tackle solo or with a friend in split-screen mode. The main goal is to complete missions across different game modes—like Racing, Parking, Drifting, and Crashing—to earn coins. Use these coins in the garage to unlock faster and better cars for your adventures.

The controls are straightforward. For Player 1, use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive, 'F' for NOS/boost, and 'R' to reset your car's position. For Player 2 in split-screen mode, use the Arrow Keys to drive, 'K' for NOS/boost, and 'P' to reset.

Explore the different game modes from the main menu. In Race Mode, compete to be the first across the finish line. In Parking Mode, carefully maneuver your vehicle into designated spots without taking damage. Drift Mode challenges you to rack up points by sliding around corners, while Crash Mode rewards you for causing maximum destruction. If you just want to explore, select Free Roam to drive anywhere without rules or objectives.
