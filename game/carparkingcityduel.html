How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to Car Parking City Duel! This game offers a variety of driving challenges you can enjoy solo or with a friend on the same screen. The controls are intuitive: Player 1 uses the WASD keys to drive, and Player 2 uses the Arrow Keys.

Choose from several game modes, each with a unique goal. In Free Roam, there are no rulesâ€”just drive around the open city, explore, and collect money bags to earn coins. Use these coins in the garage to unlock new and better cars!

Test your skills in Parking Mode, where you must navigate tight spaces and park your car perfectly in designated spots before the timer runs out. Be careful, as every bump damages your car! For a head-to-head challenge, try Race Mode against your friend and AI opponents. Use your NOS boost wisely to get ahead.

If you prefer destruction, jump into Crash Mode and earn points by smashing into other vehicles. Or, showcase your control in Drift Mode by sliding around corners to rack up a high score. No matter which mode you choose, the goal is to have fun and master the road!
