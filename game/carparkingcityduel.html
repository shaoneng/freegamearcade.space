Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to Car Parking City Duel, a versatile driving game that you can enjoy solo or with a friend in split-screen mode. The goal is to complete missions across various modes to earn coins, which you can use in the garage to unlock faster and better cars.

Player 1 Controls:
Move: W, A, S, D
NOS/Boost: Left Shift
Brake: Spacebar

Player 2 Controls:
Move: Arrow Keys
NOS/Boost: Right Shift
Brake: Down Arrow

Choose from several exciting game modes:

  • Race Mode: Compete against a friend and AI opponents on 10 different tracks. Use your NOS boost strategically and master the corners to finish first.
  • Parking Mode: Test your precision by parking your car in designated spots against the clock. Avoid hitting obstacles, as every bump damages your car.
  • Drift Mode: Score points by executing long, smooth drifts around corners. Control your speed and angle to rack up a high score.
  • Crash Mode: Your objective is destruction! Hit as many cars as you can to earn points. Each crash causes an explosion, adding to the chaos and your coin total.
  • Free Roam: Explore the open-world city with no rules or pressure. Drive around, discover ramps and secrets, practice your skills, or collect money bags scattered across the map.
