Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to the sprawling metropolis of Car Parking City Duel! This game offers a variety of modes you can enjoy either solo or with a friend in split-screen co-op. The controls for each player are displayed on-screen, typically using WASD or Arrow Keys for movement, along with keys for nitro and braking.

Choose your preferred game mode from the main menu. In Race Mode, compete against your friend and AI drivers to be the first to cross the finish line. In Parking Mode, you must carefully maneuver your vehicle into designated spots without taking damage before the timer runs out. For more action, try Crash Mode, where the goal is to cause as much destruction as possible by ramming into other cars. You can also test your skills in Drift, Traffic Run, or simply explore in Free Roam mode.

As you play, you'll earn coins by completing missions, winning races, or finding money bags scattered throughout the city. Head over to the garage to spend your hard-earned coins on unlocking faster, more powerful sports cars to enhance your driving experience.
