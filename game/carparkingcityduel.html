Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to Car Parking City Duel! This isn't just a parking game; it's a complete driving experience. You can play by yourself or challenge a friend in two-player split-screen mode. The goal is to complete various missions across the city—such as racing, parking, and drifting—to earn coins. Use your coins in the garage to unlock faster and better cars.

The controls are straightforward and designed for both solo and two-player action. For Player 1, use WASD to drive, Left Shift for NOS (boost), and the Spacebar for the handbrake. For Player 2, use the Arrow Keys to drive, N for NOS, and K for the handbrake. If you get stuck, press R to reset your car's position.

Choose from several exciting game modes! In Race Mode, compete against your friend and AI drivers. In Parking Mode, test your precision by parking perfectly within the time limit without taking damage. Go on a rampage in Crash Mode by smashing into as many cars as possible to earn points. Or, simply explore the city at your own pace in Free Roam Mode, discovering secrets and collecting money bags.
