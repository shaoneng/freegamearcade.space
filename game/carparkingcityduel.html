Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a free online driving game where you can play solo or on split-screen with a friend. It features a 3D open-world city and combines realistic driving physics with a variety of fun game modes. You're not just limited to parking; you can also race through traffic, drift, crash into cars, complete timed missions, and freely explore the map.

Earn coins by completing tasks or by collecting money bags scattered around the city. Head to the garage to use your coins to unlock better, faster cars. Whether you prefer playing solo or with a friend, mission-based challenges or free-roaming exploration, this game has something for every type of driving fan.

Race Mode

Go head-to-head against a friend and two CPU-controlled cars across 10 unique tracks. You'll need to navigate tight corners, city traffic, and long straightaways. The first to cross the finish line wins, but catching up is tough if you fall behind. Use your NOS boost wisely and perfect your cornering to gain an edge.

Parking & Precision Mode

Test your parking skills in 20 realistic, timed scenarios. Maneuver through tight spaces and avoid barriers to complete missions before the clock runs out. Every bump damages your car, so be careful! For a tougher challenge, Precision Mode requires you to drive through small checkpoints and park perfectly in highlighted spots, with increasingly difficult levels and shorter time limits.

Crash Mode

Your mission is simple: cause as much destruction as possible by hitting other cars. Every crash earns you points, and even a slight bump will make other cars explode. The more cars you destroy, the more coins you'll earn at the end of the round.

Traffic Mode

Weave through busy traffic lanes, overtaking civilian cars without crashing. Each successful overtake adds to your score, which helps you unlock better sports cars for your next run. It's one of the most rewarding modes in the game.

Drift Mode

Get your speed above 80 km/h and hit the corners hard to pull off long drifts and rack up drift points. This mode demands smooth entries, tight control, and the ability to maintain your angle without hitting a wall.

Free Roam

No goals, no pressure. Just you and the open city. Drive wherever you want, find ramps, jump off rooftops, or just practice your driving skills. This mode is perfect for relaxing between the more intense challenges.

Car Parking City Duel was developed by RHM Interactive, known for making fun competitive and co-op browser games. They've mastered the split-screen formula, making every mode feel perfectly suited for both solo and two-player action.
