Car Parking City Duel

About Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a versatile free online driving game that you can enjoy solo or on a split-screen with a friend. Set in a sprawling 3D open-world city, it blends realistic driving physics with an array of exciting game modes, so you're never just limited to parking. You can race through traffic, execute perfect drifts, cause mayhem in crash mode, take on timed missions, or simply explore the map with complete freedom.

Earn coins by successfully completing challenges or by discovering money bags scattered throughout the city. Use your earnings in the garage to unlock a selection of faster, more powerful cars to dominate the streets.

Game Modes

Race Mode

Challenge a friend or CPU opponents on 10 unique tracks. Use your NOS boost wisely and master tight corners to cross the finish line first.

Parking Mode

Test your precision in 20 timed parking scenarios. Navigate tight spaces and avoid obstacles to park flawlessly before time runs out. Watch your health bar—every bump counts!

Extreme Parking Mode

This mode demands ultimate control. Drive through narrow checkpoints and park in precise locations against a shrinking time limit. It’s a true test of patience and skill.

Crash Mode

Unleash your destructive side! The goal is to crash into as many cars as possible. Each collision earns you points and coins, turning the city into your personal demolition derby.

Traffic Run Mode

Weave through busy traffic lanes at high speed. Overtake other cars without crashing to rack up a high score and unlock better sports cars for your next run.

Drift Mode

Show off your control by pulling off long, stylish drifts around corners. Maintain your angle and speed to accumulate drift points and prove you're the king of the slide.

Free Roam

No rules, no missions, no pressure. Just you, your car, and the open city. Explore, find ramps, practice stunts, or just enjoy a relaxing drive. The city is your playground.

Developed by RHM Interactive, Car Parking City Duel excels in providing a polished co-op and solo browser gaming experience. If you love the freedom of city driving, check out other traffic and parking games on our site for more urban adventures!
