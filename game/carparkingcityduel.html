Car Parking City Duel

About Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a versatile free online driving game that you can enjoy solo or with a friend on a split-screen. Dive into a massive 3D open-world city and experience realistic driving physics across a wide variety of game modes. You're not just limited to parking—this game lets you race through traffic, drift around corners, cause mayhem in crash mode, complete timed missions, or simply explore the city at your own pace.

The standout feature is how it combines multiple driving experiences into one package. Earn coins by completing challenges or by finding money bags scattered throughout the city. Use your earnings in the garage to unlock faster, more powerful cars. Whether you prefer mission-based gameplay or a free-roam sandbox, Car Parking City Duel has something for every type of driving enthusiast.

Game Modes

Race Mode: Compete against a friend and two AI drivers on 10 unique tracks. Master tight corners, navigate city traffic, and time your NOS boost perfectly to claim victory. Falling behind early makes it tough to catch up, so a good start is crucial!

Parking Mode: Test your precision in 20 realistic parking scenarios. Navigate tight spaces and avoid obstacles to park your car before the timer runs out. Keep an eye on your health bar—every bump causes damage!

Precision Parking Mode: This mode takes parking to the next level. Drive through a series of small checkpoints and park perfectly in designated spots. It's a true test of control and patience, with increasingly difficult levels and shorter time limits.

Crash Mode: Unleash your destructive side! Your mission is to smash into as many cars as possible, causing them to explode. Each crash earns points and coins. It's not about speed, but strategic destruction.

Traffic Mode: Weave through busy traffic lanes at high speed, overtaking civilian cars without crashing. Each successful overtake boosts your score, allowing you to unlock better sports cars for your next run.

Drift Mode: Get sideways and rack up drift points. Speed over 80 km/h and execute long, smooth drifts around corners. Maintain control and avoid hitting walls to achieve a high score and prove your drifting prowess.

Free Roam: No rules, no missions, no pressure. Just you, your car, and a vast city to explore. Discover ramps, jump off buildings, practice your stunts, or simply cruise around and enjoy the scenery.
