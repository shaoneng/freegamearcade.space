Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a versatile driving game that you can enjoy solo or with a friend on a split-screen. It drops you into a massive 3D open-world city, blending realistic driving physics with a huge variety of game modes. Whether you want to race, drift, park with precision, or just cause chaos, this game has you covered.

Earn coins by completing missions or by finding money bags hidden around the city. Use your earnings in the garage to unlock faster and better cars. The game offers a wide array of modes to test your skills:

Race Mode: Go head-to-head against a friend and two AI drivers on 10 different tracks. Master tight corners and use your NOS boost wisely to get ahead.

Parking Mode: Test your precision in 20 timed parking challenges. Navigate tight spaces and avoid obstacles before the clock runs out. Watch your health bar—every bump causes damage!

Extreme Parking Mode: A true test of control. Drive through small checkpoints and park perfectly in highlighted zones against a shrinking time limit.

Crash Mode: Your goal is simple: hit as many cars as you can. Every collision causes other cars to explode, earning you points and coins. It's all about destructive fun!

Traffic Mode: Skillfully weave through busy traffic, overtaking civilian cars without crashing to rack up a high score and unlock better sports cars.

Drift Mode: Get your speed over 80 km/h and execute long, smooth drifts around corners to collect points. It requires finesse and control to avoid hitting walls.

Free Roam: No rules, no goals. Just you and the open city. Explore, find ramps, practice stunts, or simply cruise around and collect money bags at your own pace.
