Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to Car Parking City Duel! The core of the game is to drive, explore, and master various challenges in a massive open-world city. You can play by yourself or challenge a friend in a local two-player split-screen mode. Your main goal is to complete missions and collect money bags scattered around the city to earn coins. These coins can be used in the garage to unlock faster and better cars.

The game offers a wide variety of modes to choose from:

Race Mode: Compete against a friend and AI opponents on 10 different tracks. Use your NOS boost wisely and master the corners to finish first.

Parking & Precision Parking: Test your driving accuracy with timed parking challenges. Navigate tight spaces and park perfectly without damaging your vehicle.

Crash Mode: Unleash chaos! The objective is to crash into and destroy as many cars as possible to earn points and coins.

Drift Mode: Show off your skills by executing long, smooth drifts around corners to rack up a high score.

Free Roam: No rules, no objectives. Simply drive around the city, discover hidden ramps, practice your stunts, or just enjoy the scenery.

Choose your favorite mode, jump into the driver's seat, and prove you're the king of the city streets!
