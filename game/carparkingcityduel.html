Car Parking City Duel

About Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a versatile online driving game that you can enjoy solo or with a friend on a split-screen. Dive into a massive 3D open-world city where realistic driving physics meet a wide array of exciting game modes. Forget just parking—here you can race through city traffic, execute perfect drifts, cause mayhem in crash mode, complete timed missions, or simply explore the map with no rules.

You'll earn coins by tackling missions or finding money bags hidden throughout the city. Use these coins in the garage to unlock a fleet of faster, better-performing cars. Whether you prefer mission-based challenges or free-roaming fun, Car Parking City Duel puts you in the driver's seat.

Game Modes

Race Mode: Challenge a friend or AI opponents on 10 unique tracks. Navigate tight corners, weave through traffic, and master the open stretches. Use your NOS boost wisely and perfect your cornering to claim victory!

Parking Mode: Test your precision in 20 realistic parking scenarios. This isn't your average park-and-go; you'll need to maneuver carefully around obstacles and park perfectly before the timer runs out. Keep an eye on your health bar—every bump causes damage!

Extreme Parking Mode: Take your skills to the next level. Drive through narrow checkpoints and park in precisely highlighted spots against an ever-shrinking time limit. This mode demands ultimate control and patience.

Crash Mode: Unleash your destructive side! The goal is simple: hit as many cars as you can. Every collision results in an explosion and earns you points. It's not about speed, but about creating maximum chaos and racking up a high score.

Traffic Mode: Weave through busy traffic lanes at high speeds, overtaking civilian cars without a single scratch. Each successful overtake boosts your score, allowing you to unlock better sports cars for your next run.

Drift Mode: Hit speeds over 80 km/h and slide around corners to collect drift points. Master your entry, control your angle, and avoid the walls to prove you're a drift king. Smooth, sustained drifts are the key to a high score.

Free Roam Mode: No rules, no missions, no pressure. Just you, your car, and a vast city to explore. Discover hidden ramps, jump off rooftops, practice your stunts, or simply enjoy a relaxing cruise. It's the perfect way to unwind between challenges.
