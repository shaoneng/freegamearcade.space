Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a comprehensive driving game that you can enjoy solo or with a friend on a split-screen. Dive into a massive 3D open-world city where you're not just limited to parking. This game offers a variety of modes including racing, drifting, and even causing chaotic crashes! Complete missions or find money bags scattered around the city to earn coins, which you can use in the garage to unlock faster and better cars.

Game Modes

Race Mode: Challenge a friend and two AI opponents across 10 unique tracks. You'll need to master tight corners, navigate city traffic, and strategically use your NOS boost to cross the finish line first.

Parking Mode: Put your precision to the test in 20 timed parking scenarios. Navigate tight spaces and avoid barriers to park your car before time runs out. Keep an eye on your health bar—every bump causes damage!

Challenge Mode: This mode demands ultimate control. Drive through a series of small checkpoints and park perfectly in designated spots against a shrinking time limit. Patience and smooth handling are key.

Crash Mode: Unleash chaos! Your goal is to crash into as many cars as possible to earn points and coins. Every car you hit explodes, making for a satisfying destructive spree.

Traffic Mode: Skillfully weave through busy traffic, overtaking civilian cars as closely as possible without crashing. Each successful overtake boosts your score, helping you unlock better sports cars.

Drift Mode: Show off your style by executing long, smooth drifts around corners. Maintain speed and control to rack up a high drift score and prove you're the king of the slide.

Free Roam: No rules, no goals, no pressure. Simply explore the vast city at your own pace. Discover ramps, jump off rooftops, practice your skills, or just enjoy a relaxing drive.

Controls

Player 1:
Move: W, A, S, D
NOS: L-Shift
Brake: Spacebar
Restart: R
Look Back: T

Player 2:
Move: Arrow Keys
NOS: R-Shift
Brake: P
Restart: O
Look Back: L
