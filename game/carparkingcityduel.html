Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to Car Parking City Duel, a versatile driving game that you can enjoy solo or with a friend in split-screen mode. The game features a huge city to explore and a variety of game modes to test your skills. Whether you want to race, park, drift, or just cause chaos, there's a mode for you.

Controls (Player 1):
- Drive: W, A, S, D
- NOS/Nitro: Left Shift
- Reset Car: R
- Change Camera: C

Controls (Player 2):
- Drive: Arrow Keys
- NOS/Nitro: N
- Reset Car: L
- Change Camera: U

Navigate the menu to choose your preferred game mode. In 'Parking' and 'Challenge' modes, your goal is to carefully maneuver your car into the designated parking spot without crashing. 'Race' mode pits you against a friend and AI opponents. In 'Crash' mode, the objective is to smash into as many cars as possible, while 'Drift' mode rewards you for stylish slides. If you just want to drive without rules, select 'Free Roam' and explore the city at your own pace!
