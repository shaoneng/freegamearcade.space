Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to Car Parking City Duel! This game is an all-in-one driving experience that you can enjoy solo or with a friend in split-screen mode. The core of the game is completing missions across various modes to earn coins, which you can use in the garage to unlock faster and better cars.

The game features several exciting modes. In Race Mode, compete against your friend and AI drivers on 10 unique city tracks. In Parking Mode, your precision is tested as you navigate tight spaces to park perfectly before the timer runs out. For chaotic fun, jump into Crash Mode, where the goal is to destroy as many cars as possible. If you're a fan of style, Drift Mode lets you slide around corners to rack up points.

If you just want to chill, Free Roam lets you explore the open-world city with no rules or pressure. Use your keyboard to control your vehicle. For two-player mode, one player typically uses the WASD keys while the other uses the Arrow keys. Follow the on-screen prompts for specific controls like using NOS. Good luck on the streets!
