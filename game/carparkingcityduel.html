Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to the driver's seat of Car Parking City Duel! This game offers a complete urban driving experience that you can enjoy solo or with a friend in split-screen mode. The goal changes depending on which mode you select, offering a huge variety of challenges.

Use your keyboard to control your vehicle. Navigate the menus to choose from modes like Racing, Precision Parking, Crash Derby, Drifting, and a relaxing Free Roam mode. In race mode, your objective is to be the first across the finish line. In parking mode, you must carefully maneuver your car into designated spots without taking too much damage. For crash mode, let loose and cause as much destruction as possible to earn points!

As you complete missions and explore the city, you'll earn coins. Save up these coins to visit the garage and unlock a fleet of faster and more powerful sports cars to dominate the streets. Whether you want to compete, practice, or just explore, your urban adventure awaits!
