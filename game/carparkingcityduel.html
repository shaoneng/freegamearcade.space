Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to Car Parking City Duel! This game offers a complete driving experience in a massive 3D city, playable either solo or with a friend in split-screen mode. The goal is to master various driving challenges. You'll earn coins by completing tasks or finding them scattered around the map, which you can use in the garage to unlock faster and better cars.

Choose from several exciting game modes. In Race Mode, compete against your friend and AI drivers to be the first to cross the finish line. In Parking Mode, test your precision by carefully maneuvering your car into tight spots before the timer runs out. For pure chaos, jump into Crash Mode and earn points by smashing into other vehicles. If you'd rather perfect your technique, Drift Mode challenges you to rack up points with stylish slides. Or, simply select Free Roam to explore the vast city at your own pace without any rules or objectives.
