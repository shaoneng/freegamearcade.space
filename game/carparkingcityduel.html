Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to Car Parking City Duel, a versatile driving game set in a sprawling 3D city. You can enjoy this game by yourself or challenge a friend in split-screen two-player mode. The game is packed with different modes, so you're never limited to just one activity.

Choose from seven unique game modes, each offering a different challenge. In Race Mode, compete against a friend and AI cars on intense city circuits. Test your control in Parking Mode and Precision Parking, where you must perfectly park your vehicle under a time limit. Unleash chaos in Crash Mode by smashing as many cars as possible for points. Weave through traffic in Overtake Mode, or master stylish cornering in Drift Mode. For a chill session, jump into Free Roam to explore the city without any rules.

As you play and complete challenges, you'll earn coins. These can also be found scattered around the map in Free Roam mode. Save up your earnings and head to the garage to unlock faster, more powerful sports cars to enhance your driving experience. Your rules, your game—pick a mode and hit the streets!
