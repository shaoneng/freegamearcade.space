Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to Car Parking City Duel! This game offers a massive open-world city with multiple game modes you can enjoy alone or with a friend in a split-screen two-player mode. The objective changes depending on the mode you select, from precision parking to chaotic racing and drifting.

Player 1 Controls:
- Drive: W, A, S, D
- NOS/Boost: Left Shift
- Reset Car Position: R
- Change Camera: C

Player 2 Controls:
- Drive: Arrow Keys
- NOS/Boost: M
- Reset Car Position: O
- Change Camera: P

Explore the different game modes like **Race**, where you compete to cross the finish line first; **Parking**, where you test your precision against the clock; **Crash**, where your mission is to cause destruction; or **Free Roam**, where you can explore the city, find collectibles, and practice your driving without any rules. Earn coins from challenges to unlock new and better cars in the garage!
