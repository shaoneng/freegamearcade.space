Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to Car Parking City Duel, an expansive driving game set in a vibrant 3D city. You can play solo or challenge a friend in local split-screen mode. The core of the game is completing missions to earn coins, which you can then use in the garage to unlock faster and more powerful cars.

The game is packed with different modes to suit any mood. In Race Mode, compete against your friend and AI drivers on 10 unique tracks. For a test of precision, try the Parking and Precision Parking modes, where you must carefully maneuver your vehicle into tight spots against the clock without taking damage.

If you prefer action, jump into Crash Mode to cause as much destruction as possible, or test your reflexes in Traffic Mode by weaving through cars. Show off your style in Drift Mode by pulling off long drifts, or simply unwind in Free Roam, exploring the city with no rules or objectives. Choose your mode and hit the streets!
