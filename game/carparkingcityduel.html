Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a free online driving game where you can play solo or on split-screen with a friend. It has a 3D open-world city and combines realistic driving physics with a variety of fun game modes. So, you're not limited to just parking. This game also lets you race through traffic, drift, crash into cars, complete timed missions, and freely explore the map without rules.

What makes this car game stand out is how it wraps up different driving challenges into one big package. You earn coins by completing tasks or collecting money bags randomly scattered on the map. Head to the garage to unlock better, faster cars with your coins. Your rules, your game. Car Parking City Duel offers something for every kind of driving fan.

Race Mode

Put your skills to the test against a friend and two CPU-controlled cars. With 10 unique tracks featuring tight corners, city traffic, and open stretches, every race is a new challenge. Timing your NOS boost and mastering corners can make all the difference between victory and defeat.

Parking Mode

Test your parking precision with timed goals in 20 different realistic parking scenarios. You'll need to maneuver around tight corners and avoid barriers before the clock runs out. Be careful—every bump damages your car, and your health bar is always watching.

Precision Parking

This mode takes parking to the next level. Drive through small checkpoints and park perfectly in highlighted spots. It's a true test of control where each level gets more complex and the time limit shrinks.

Crash Mode

Unleash your destructive side! Your mission is to hit and explode as many cars as possible. Every crash earns you points and coins. It’s not about speed; it's about strategic destruction.

Traffic Run

Speed through busy traffic lanes and overtake civilian cars without crashing. Each successful overtake adds to your score, allowing you to unlock better sports cars for your next run. It's one of the most rewarding modes of all.

Drift Mode

Get your speed over 80 km/h and steer sharply to pull off long drifts around corners, collecting points along the way. Rack up a high score and prove your drifting skills with smooth entries and tight control.

Free Roam

No goals, no pressure. Just you and the city. Drive wherever you want, explore ramps, jump off rooftops, or practice your tricks. This mode is perfect for relaxing between intense challenges or finding hidden money bags.

Developed by RHM Interactive, Car Parking City Duel masters the split-screen formula, making every mode enjoyable for both solo and two-player fun. If you enjoy this game, check out other driving and parking games available for free online.
