Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Welcome to the sprawling city of Car Parking City Duel! Whether you're playing solo or challenging a friend in split-screen mode, your goal is to master the streets. Start by selecting a game mode from the main menu. You can race, park with precision, drift around corners, cause destruction, or simply explore the open world.

In Race Mode, compete against a friend and AI drivers. Use your NOS boost wisely to get ahead. In Parking and Precision Parking Modes, carefully maneuver your vehicle into designated spots without taking too much damage before the timer runs out. For pure chaos, jump into Crash Mode and earn points by causing as much destruction as possible. Or, test your skills in Drift Mode and Traffic Run Mode.

Use the WASD keys or Arrow Keys to control your vehicle. For two-player mode, one player typically uses WASD while the other uses the Arrow Keys. Look for on-screen prompts for specific actions like handbrakes or nitro boosts.

As you complete missions and explore, you'll earn coins by accomplishing tasks or finding money bags scattered around the city. Use these coins in the garage to unlock faster and more powerful cars to dominate every challenge!
