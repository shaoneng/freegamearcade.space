Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a versatile driving game that you can enjoy solo or with a friend in split-screen mode. The goal is to complete challenges across various game modes to earn coins, which you can use to unlock new and better cars in the garage.

Controls (Player 1):

  • Move: W, A, S, D keys
  • NOS/Boost: Shift key
  • Brake: Spacebar
  • Camera View: C key
  • Reset Car Position: R key

Controls (Player 2):

  • Move: Arrow keys
  • NOS/Boost: M key
  • Brake: K key
  • Camera View: P key
  • Reset Car Position: O key

Game Modes Explained: Choose from a variety of exciting modes!

  • Race: Compete against your friend and AI cars on 10 different tracks. Use your NOS wisely to get ahead!
  • Parking: Test your precision by parking your car in designated spots before time runs out. Be careful, as every bump damages your vehicle.
  • Crash: The goal is simple: cause as much destruction as possible by crashing into other cars to earn points.
  • Overtake: Weave through traffic and overtake other cars without crashing to rack up a high score.
  • Drift: Show off your control by sliding around corners to earn drift points.
  • Free Roam: Explore the city without any rules or objectives. Practice your skills, find hidden ramps, or just cruise around.
