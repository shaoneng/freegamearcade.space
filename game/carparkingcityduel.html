Car Parking City Duel

How to Play Car Parking City Duel

Car Parking City Duel is a versatile driving game that you can enjoy solo or with a friend on a split-screen. Set in a 3D open-world city, it combines realistic driving physics with a wide array of game modes, so you're never just stuck with parking. You can race through traffic, drift around corners, complete timed missions, or simply explore the city at your own pace.

Complete tasks or collect money bags scattered around the map to earn coins. Use these coins in the garage to unlock faster and better cars. Whether you prefer mission-based challenges or free-roaming fun, this game has something for every type of driving enthusiast.

Race Mode

Go head-to-head against a friend and two AI cars on 10 unique tracks. You'll navigate tight corners, city traffic, and long straights. The first to cross the finish line wins, but timing your NOS boosts and mastering your cornering is key to victory.

Parking Mode

Test your precision in 20 realistic, timed parking challenges. Maneuver carefully around obstacles to park your car before the clock runs out. Be careful—every bump damages your car, and your health bar is always visible.

Extreme Parking

This mode demands ultimate control. Drive through small checkpoints and park perfectly in designated spots. Each level increases in complexity as the time limit shrinks, requiring patience and smooth handling.

Crash Mode

Unleash chaos and earn points by crashing into as many cars as possible. Every collision causes the other car to explode, earning you coins. It's not about speed, but about destructive and strategic targeting.

Traffic Mode

Weave through busy traffic lanes at high speed, overtaking civilian cars without crashing. Each successful overtake adds to your score, allowing you to unlock better sports cars for your next run.

Drift Mode

Get your speed above 80 km/h and steer sharply to execute long drifts around corners, racking up drift points. Smooth entries and tight control are essential to avoid hitting walls and to achieve a high score.

Free Roam

No rules, no goals, no pressure. Just you, the city, and your car. Explore ramps, jump off rooftops, practice tricks, or simply cruise around and enjoy the scenery. It's the perfect way to relax between challenges.
