Car Stunt King

How to Play Car Stunt King

Welcome to the high-flying world of Car Stunt King! Your main goal is to navigate your vehicle through challenging obstacle courses, performing jaw-dropping stunts along the way. As you drive, be sure to collect the gold coins scattered throughout the maps. These coins, along with a passive income system, are your key to building the ultimate stunt machine.

The game offers three exciting modes to test your skills. In Solo Race, you'll tackle the courses on your own. In Police Chase, you must evade the law in a thrilling pursuit. Finally, Pair Racing pits you against another car in a head-to-head competition across seven unique racetracks.

Head to the garage to spend your earnings! You can unlock eleven different vehicles, including a Dodge, a school bus, and even a monster truck.
