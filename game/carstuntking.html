Car Stunt King

How to Play Car Stunt King

Get ready to burn rubber in Car Stunt King! The main objective is to navigate your vehicle through thrilling obstacle courses, perform jaw-dropping stunts, and compete in various high-speed challenges. Whether you're racing against the clock or escaping the police, your driving skills will be put to the ultimate test.

Explore three exciting game modes: take on challenging tracks by yourself in Solo Race, outrun law enforcement in Police Chase, or go head-to-head in Pair Racing. Each mode offers a unique way to experience the five detailed maps, from the deserts of Arizona to the iconic Golden Gate.

As you play, you'll earn gold coins and dollars. Use this currency in the garage to unlock a diverse fleet of 11 vehicles, including sports cars, a school bus, and even a monster truck! Don't just buy new cars—tune them! Upgrade parts up to level 20 to maximize performance and become the undisputed king of stunts.
