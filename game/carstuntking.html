Car Stunt King

How to Play Car Stunt King

Welcome to the driver's seat of Car Stunt King! Your main goal is to master the art of driving and stunting across a variety of thrilling obstacle courses. The game offers several exciting modes to test your skills: engage in a solo race against the clock, escape the law in a high-stakes police chase, or compete head-to-head in pair racing mode.

As you drive, be sure to collect coins scattered throughout the maps. You'll also earn a passive income of both gold coins and dollars, which are crucial for upgrading your ride. Use your earnings in the garage to unlock a diverse fleet of eleven vehicles, ranging from a powerful Dodge muscle car to a massive monster truck and even a school bus!

Customization is key to becoming the king. Spend your currency to buy new car parts and tune your vehicle's performance up to 20 levels. Explore five unique maps, including Arizona, the Port, and the iconic Golden Gate, each offering a different landscape and challenge. Choose your mode, pick your car, and get ready to perform some mind-blowing stunts!
