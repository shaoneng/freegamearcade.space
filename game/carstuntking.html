Car Stunt King

How to Play Car Stunt King

Welcome to the high-octane world of Car Stunt King! Your main goal is to master thrilling obstacle courses by performing incredible stunts. The game offers several exciting modes to test your skills: engage in a Solo Race against the clock, outrun the law in a heart-pounding Police Chase, or compete head-to-head in Pair Racing mode. Explore five distinct maps, from the urban streets to the desolate Wasteland, each offering unique challenges and stunt opportunities.

Customization is key to becoming the king of stunts. Start in the garage where you can unlock a diverse fleet of eleven vehicles, including a powerful Dodge, a surprising school bus, and a massive monster truck. Don't just collect carsâ€”make them your own! You can purchase new parts and tune each vehicle's performance up to level 20, enhancing its speed, handling, and durability to conquer any course.

As you drive, race, and perform stunts, you'll collect gold coins scattered throughout the maps. The game also features a passive income system, where you earn both gold coins and CSK dollars over time. Use this currency to unlock new cars, purchase upgrades, and even exchange your dollars for more gold coins to accelerate your progress and build the ultimate stunt vehicle.
