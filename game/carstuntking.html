Car Stunt King

How to Play Car Stunt King

Welcome to Car Stunt King! Your main objective in this 3D driving game is to master exhilarating obstacle courses. As you drive, be sure to collect the coins scattered along the road. These coins, along with a passive income system that earns you both gold and dollars, are key to your progress. You can use this currency to dominate the game!

Head to the garage to explore a collection of eleven different vehicles, including a powerful Dodge, a classic school bus, and even a massive monster truck. The real fun lies in the deep customization. You can buy new car parts and tune them all the way up to level 20 performance, making your ride a true beast on the track.

Car Stunt King offers three exciting game modes to test your skills: challenge yourself in a Solo Race, outrun the law in a thrilling Police Chase, or compete against another driver in Pair Racing. Explore five beautifully designed maps, from the desert landscapes of Arizona to the iconic Golden Gate. Each map and mode provides a unique driving experience and a new set of challenges to conquer.
