Car Stunt King

How to Play Car Stunt King

Welcome to the driver's seat of Car Stunt King! Your main goal is to master incredible stunts, compete in high-speed races, and dominate various challenging obstacle courses. The game offers several exciting modes to test your skills.

Choose from game modes like Solo Race, where you race against the clock; Police Chase, where you must evade the law; and Pair Racing, where you go head-to-head with another driver. As you play, you'll explore five distinct maps, including Arizona, Port, and Golden Gate, each with its own unique layout and challenges.

Earning in-game currency (gold coins and dollars) is key to your success. Collect coins on the road and earn passive income to build your fortune. Use your earnings in the garage to unlock a fleet of eleven different vehicles, from sports cars to a school bus and even a monster truck! You can also purchase and tune car parts to upgrade your vehicle's performance up to the 20th level, giving you the edge you need to win.

The controls are intuitive for any racing game fan, typically using Arrow Keys or WASD for steering, acceleration, and braking. Keep an eye out for on-screen prompts for specific actions like using nitro or changing camera views. Now, start your engine and become the Car Stunt King!
