Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

How to Play Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

Your goal is to help Angela the cat win a fashion contest against a rival. Each round has a specific theme, like 'Back-to-School' or 'Evening Elegance,' so be sure to dress Angela appropriately to impress the judges.

Use your mouse or tap the screen to interact with the wardrobe. On the right side of the screen, you'll find a toolbar to select different categories like dresses, shoes, necklaces, and bags. Tap a category, then browse the available items. Simply click or tap an item to put it on Angela.

Feel free to mix and match until you've created the perfect outfit. Once you're satisfied with your design, hit the 'Preview' button to submit your look and head to the runway. Three judges will score your outfit against your opponent's. The contestant with the most stars wins the round and moves on to the next fashion challenge!
