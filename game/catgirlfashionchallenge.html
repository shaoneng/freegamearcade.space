Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

How to Play Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

Your mission is to become the ultimate stylist for Angela the Cat! Each level presents a fashion challenge with a specific theme, such as "Back-to-School" or "Evening Elegance." You'll face off against a rival, and your styling skills will be put to the test in a head-to-head runway battle.

Use your mouse or simply tap on the screen to play. On the right, you'll find a toolbar with different clothing and accessory categories. Tap an icon to open up options for full outfits, shoes, bags, necklaces, and earrings. Swipe through the carousel to see all the available items for each category.

Select pieces by tapping or dragging them onto Angela. They will automatically snap into place. Feel free to mix, match, and swap items as much as you like to create the perfect ensemble that fits the level's theme. When you are satisfied with your creation, hit the large "Preview" button to submit your look.

Once both stylists are ready, Angela and her opponent will walk the catwalk. A panel of three judges will rate each outfit, awarding stars for well-executed looks. To win the round and unlock the next challenge, you must earn more stars than your rival. Keep winning to progress through every theme and prove you're the top stylist!
