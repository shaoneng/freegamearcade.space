Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

How to Play Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

In Cat Girl Fashion Challenge, your goal is to style Angela the cat for a runway competition. Each round presents a unique fashion theme, like 'Back-to-School' or 'Evening Elegance'. You'll be competing against another stylist to see who can create the best outfit that fits the theme.

Use your mouse or touch screen to play. On the right side of the screen, you'll find a toolbar with icons for different clothing categories: outfits, shoes, earrings, necklaces, and bags. Tap an icon to open the wardrobe for that category. Swipe through the available items and simply tap or drag your chosen pieces onto Angela to dress her up.

Feel free to mix and match items until you're happy with your creation. Once you've finalized the look, hit the 'Preview' button to send Angela to the catwalk. Three judges will score your outfit against your opponent's. The stylist who earns the most stars from the judges wins the round and moves on to the next fashion challenge!
