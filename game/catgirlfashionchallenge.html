Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

How to Play Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

Welcome to the glamorous world of Cat Girl Fashion Challenge! Your goal is to become Angela the Cat's top stylist and help her win runway battles. Each round presents a specific fashion theme, like "Back-to-School" or "Spooky Halloween," where you'll compete against another stylish cat girl. Create the best outfit that fits the theme to win over the judges.

Playing is simple and intuitive. Use your mouse or touch to interact with the wardrobe. A toolbar on the right side of the screen lets you browse different categories: outfits, shoes, bags, necklaces, and earrings. Simply tap an icon and swipe through the options. Click or tap an item to instantly dress Angela. Feel free to mix and match until you've created the perfect look!

Once you're satisfied with your creation, hit the big "Preview" button to send Angela to the catwalk. Three judges will score your outfit and your opponent's. Earn more stars from the judges than your rival to win the round and unlock the next exciting fashion theme. Keep winning to conquer every challenge, all the way to the grand finale!
