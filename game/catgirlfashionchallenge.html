Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

How to Play Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

Your mission in Cat Girl Fashion Challenge is to become the ultimate stylist for Angela, a fabulous feline with a passion for fashion. Each round presents a unique fashion theme—like Evening Elegance or Back-to-School—and pits you against a rival stylist on the runway.

Use your mouse or simply tap on the screen to interact with the game. On the right side of the screen, you'll find a toolbar with icons for different clothing and accessory categories like outfits, shoes, bags, necklaces, and earrings. Tap an icon to open the category and swipe through the carousel to see all the available items.

To dress Angela, just click or drag an item onto her. Mix and match pieces freely until you've created the perfect ensemble for the given theme. Once your masterpiece is complete, hit the big "Preview" button to lock in your look and face the judges. The stylist with the most stars from the judges wins the round and moves on to the next exciting fashion challenge!
