Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

How to Play Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

Your goal is to become Angela the Cat's personal stylist and help her win fashion showdowns! The game is easy to control using your mouse or by tapping on the screen.

Each round begins with a specific fashion theme, such as "Back-to-School" or "Evening Elegance." Pay close attention to the theme to choose the right outfit. Use the vertical toolbar on the right to browse categories like full outfits, shoes, bags, necklaces, and earrings. Simply tap an icon and swipe through the options.

To dress Angela, just tap or drag the clothing and accessory items onto her. Feel free to mix and match until you create the perfect look. Once you're satisfied with your design, hit the big "Preview" button. Angela will then hit the runway alongside her opponent. Three judges will score both outfits. Collect more stars than your rival to win the round and advance to new, exciting fashion challenges!
