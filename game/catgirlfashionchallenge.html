Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

How to Play Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

Your mission is to help Angela the Cat win fashion battles on the runway! Each round presents a unique theme, like "Back-to-School" or "Christmas Sparkle," which you'll need to keep in mind as you style her.

Use your mouse or touch screen to interact with the wardrobe. A toolbar on the right lets you access different categories like outfits, shoes, necklaces, earrings, and bags. Simply tap or click on an item and drag it onto Angela to dress her up. Feel free to mix and match until you've created the perfect ensemble!

Once you're satisfied with your creation, hit the "Preview" button to send Angela to the catwalk. Three judges will score your look against your opponent's. Earn more stars than your rival to win the round and advance to the next exciting fashion challenge!
