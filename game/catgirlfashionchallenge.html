Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

How to Play Cat Girl Fashion Challenge

Your goal is to become Angela's personal stylist and help her win fashion battles. Each round has a specific theme, like "Back-to-School" or "Evening Elegance," so keep the theme in mind when choosing your outfit!

Use your mouse or touch screen to interact with the wardrobe. On the right side of the screen, a toolbar allows you to select different clothing categories: earrings, necklaces, bags, shoes, and full outfits. Tap a category to view the items.

Browse the available items by swiping through the carousel. Tap or drag a piece of clothing or an accessory onto Angela to dress her. You can mix, match, and swap items as many times as you like until you've created the perfect look.

Once you are satisfied with your creation, hit the large "Preview" button to submit your design and face off against your opponent on the runway. Three judges will score your outfit with stars. To win the round, you need to earn more stars than your rival. Keep winning to unlock new themes and advance to the final showdown!
