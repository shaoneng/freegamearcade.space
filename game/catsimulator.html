Cat Simulator

How to Play Cat Simulator

Welcome to the delightful world of Cat Simulator! Your goal is to experience life from the perspective of a house cat. Explore, play, and complete various tasks to become the ultimate feline.

When you start, you'll have the chance to select your cat's breed. Initially, some breeds are locked. To unlock new cats, you need to earn coins by completing missions and leveling up. The game features multiple modes, each offering unique challenges and objectives. Choose a mode that suits your mood and start your adventure!

As you gather coins, you can spend them in the shop. Unlock all 13 cat breeds, each with different stats for health, damage, and power. You can also personalize your cat with fun hats or customize your living space by purchasing furniture and decorations like TVs, paintings, and shelves to create the coziest cat paradise.
