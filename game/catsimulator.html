Cat Simulator

How to Play Cat Simulator

Welcome to the charming world of Cat Simulator! Your adventure begins by choosing your very own feline friend from a selection of breeds. While you start with one, you can unlock up to 13 different cat species by earning coins through gameplay. Each breed has its own unique stats for health, damage, and power, so choose wisely as you progress!

The game offers several exciting modes to explore, each with its own set of objectives and fun. As you complete tasks and level up, the challenges will increase, but so will your coin rewards. Spend your hard-earned coins in the shop to personalize your experience.

Customization is key! Give your cat a unique personality by purchasing stylish hats. You can also design your own cozy space by decorating your room with furniture and accessories like TVs, paintings, shelves, and more. Dive in, complete quests, and create the ultimate virtual cat life!
