Cat Simulator

How to Play Cat Simulator

Welcome to Cat Simulator! Your goal is to live the ultimate cat life. Begin by choosing your starting cat breed from a selection of 13 unique felines. Each breed has its own distinct health, damage, and power abilities, so pick one that suits your playstyle. Once you're in the game, you can explore various game modes, each offering different challenges and objectives.

The main objective is to earn coins by completing tasks and leveling up. As you progress, the difficulty will increase, but so will your coin rewards! Use your hard-earned coins in the shop to unlock the other 12 cat breeds, giving you new ways to play. You can also customize your cat's look with a variety of stylish hats.

Don't forget to make your home your own! Spend your coins to decorate your room with items like TVs, paintings, shelves, vases, and more. Create the purr-fect space to relax in after a long day of cat adventures. Dive in, explore, customize, and have a fantastic time being a cat!
