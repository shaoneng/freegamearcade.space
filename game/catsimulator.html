Cat Simulator

How to Play Cat Simulator

Welcome to the charming world of Cat Simulator! Your adventure begins by choosing your very first feline friend. You'll have the option to select from various cat breeds, with more becoming available as you progress. Each of the 13 breeds has its own unique health, damage, and power stats, so choose wisely!

Once you've picked your cat, it's time to explore! The game offers several different modes, each with unique objectives and challenges. Your main goal is to complete tasks and level up. As you do, you'll earn coins which are the key to unlocking the full potential of the game.

Use your hard-earned coins to unlock all the different cat breeds. You can also spend them on fun customizations, like buying adorable hats for your cat or designing the perfect living space. Purchase furniture like TVs, shelves, and paintings to create a cozy home for your virtual pet. Jump in and start your nine lives now!
