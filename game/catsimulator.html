Cat Simulator

How to Play Cat Simulator

Welcome to Cat Simulator, where you can live out your ultimate feline fantasies! Your main goal is to navigate the world as a cat, completing various tasks and challenges to earn coins and level up. At the start, you'll get to choose your first cat from a selection of breeds, each with its own unique stats for health, damage, and power.

Explore different game modes, each offering unique objectives and environments. As you complete tasks like catching mice, knocking things over, or simply exploring, you'll earn valuable coins. These coins are your key to unlocking more content and personalizing your experience.

Head to the in-game shop to spend your hard-earned coins. You can unlock 12 other cat breeds, purchase stylish hats to give your cat some flair, or even decorate your virtual home with items like TVs, paintings, and shelves. As you progress and level up, the challenges will become more difficult, but your potential earnings will also increase. Choose your mode, pick your purr-fect companion, and start your adventure!
