Cat Simulator

How to Play Cat Simulator

Welcome to the paws-itively charming world of Cat Simulator! Getting started is easy. Your main goal is to live the life of a house cat, exploring your surroundings, causing a little mischief, and completing various tasks.

When you begin, you can choose from several game modes, each offering unique challenges and objectives. You'll also select your first cat from a variety of breeds. Pay attention to their starting health, damage, and power, as each breed is a little different!

As you play, you'll complete missions and level up, which helps you earn coins. Use these coins to unlock all 13 cat breeds, purchase fun hats for your feline friend, and customize your living space with furniture and decorations like TVs, paintings, and shelves. The more you play, the more you can personalize your purr-fect experience!
