Cat Simulator

How to Play Cat Simulator

Welcome to Cat Simulator! Your goal is to live the life of a house cat, full of curiosity and mischief. Start by choosing your favorite cat from 13 different breeds, each with its own health, damage, and power stats.

Once in the game, you can explore the house and the yard. Use your keyboard and mouse to move around, jump, and interact with objects. You can complete various tasks and cause a bit of chaos to earn coins. These coins are essential for unlocking new cat breeds and for customizing your feline friend with cool hats and accessories.

The game features several modes, each offering a unique experience. As you play and level up, the challenges will increase, but so will your rewards! You can even use your earnings to decorate your room with items like TVs, paintings, and furniture. Dive in, explore your territory, and have a purr-fectly good time!
