Cat Simulator

How to Play Cat Simulator

Welcome to the purr-fectly delightful world of Cat Simulator! Your main goal is to embrace the life of a house cat. Begin by selecting your favorite feline from a variety of breeds, each with its own unique stats for health, damage, and power. Once you've chosen your companion, you're ready to explore.

The game offers several modes, each with its own objectives and challenges. As you navigate your environment, you'll earn coins for your activities. The more you play and level up, the more coins you can earn, but be prepared for the difficulty to increase as well. Use standard keyboard and mouse controls to move around, interact with objects, and cause a little mischief.

Your hard-earned coins are key to unlocking the full potential of the game. Head to the in-game shop to unlock all 13 cat breeds, purchase stylish hats to customize your cat's look, or even decorate your living space. Buy furniture like TVs, shelves, and paintings to create the ultimate cat paradise.
