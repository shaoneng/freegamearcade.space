Cat Simulator

How to Play Cat Simulator

Welcome to the paws-itively delightful world of Cat Simulator! Your main goal is to embrace the life of a house cat, exploring your surroundings, and completing various tasks. You begin by choosing your starting cat from a selection of breeds, each with its own health, damage, and power abilities.

Once in the game, you can explore different game modes, each offering unique objectives and challenges. As you play, you'll earn coins for your actions. These coins are essential for progression and customization. Use them to unlock all 13 available cat breeds, purchase stylish hats to give your feline friend a unique look, or buy furniture and decorations like TVs, paintings, and shelves to design your very own perfect cat room.

As you complete more tasks and cause a bit of mischief, you'll level up. Leveling up increases both the difficulty and the potential coin earnings, providing a continuously engaging experience. Choose your mode, customize your cat, and have a purr-fectly fun time!
