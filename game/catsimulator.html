Cat Simulator

How to Play Cat Simulator

Welcome to the charming world of Cat Simulator! Your main goal is to experience life through the eyes of a cat. Begin by choosing your favorite feline from a selection of 13 different breeds, each with its own health, damage, and power abilities. The game offers multiple modes, each with unique objectives and challenges, so you can pick the one that best suits your mood.

As you explore and play, you will earn coins. These coins are essential for progression. Use them to unlock the other cat breeds, giving you new ways to play. You can also spend your earnings to customize your character with fun accessories like hats or decorate your virtual home with items such as TVs, paintings, and furniture.

Leveling up is a key part of the experience. As your level increases, so do your coin earnings, allowing you to unlock content faster. However, the game's difficulty will also rise, keeping the gameplay engaging and challenging. Jump in, start purring, and have a fantastic time being a cat!
