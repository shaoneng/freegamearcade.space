Cat Simulator

How to Play Cat Simulator

Welcome to the charming and chaotic world of Cat Simulator! Your main goal is to embrace the life of a house cat. Begin by selecting your starting cat breed. As you play, you'll explore a detailed house and yard, interacting with various objects and completing missions to earn coins and experience points.

Use your coins wisely to unlock new things. You can choose from 13 different cat breeds, each with its own health, damage, and power statistics. You can also spend your earnings on fun cosmetic items like hats to give your cat a unique personality. Don't forget to customize your home base by purchasing furniture and decorations like TVs, paintings, and shelves.

The game features several different modes, each offering a unique set of challenges and objectives. As you level up, the game becomes more challenging, but your potential rewards also increase. Jump in, start purring, and see what kind of feline adventures you can get into!
