Cat Simulator

How to Play Cat Simulator

Welcome to the world of Cat Simulator! Your main goal is to experience life as a cat. Begin by selecting your favorite from 13 different cat breeds, each with its own unique health, damage, and power abilities. Explore your environment, interact with objects, and complete tasks to live out your ultimate feline fantasy.

The game offers several modes, each providing a unique challenge and set of objectives. As you complete tasks and level up, you'll earn coins. These coins are your key to unlocking more content. The difficulty will increase as you progress, keeping the gameplay exciting and rewarding.

Use your hard-earned coins to expand your experience. Unlock all the different cat breeds to find the one that best suits your playstyle. You can also personalize your cat by purchasing fun hats and accessories. Don't forget to customize your living space! Buy furniture like TVs, shelves, and paintings to create the coziest cat paradise imaginable.
