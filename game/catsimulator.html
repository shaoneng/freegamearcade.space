Cat Simulator

How to Play Cat Simulator

Welcome to the charming world of Cat Simulator! Your goal is to experience life as a curious and playful cat. When you begin, you'll be presented with several game modes, each offering unique challenges and environments. Choose the one that appeals to you most to get started.

As you explore and complete tasks, you'll earn coins. These coins are your key to unlocking a world of customization. You can use them to unlock 12 additional cat breeds, each with its own specific stats for health, damage, and power, allowing you to find the perfect feline for your playstyle.

But the fun doesn't stop there! Use your hard-earned coins to personalize your cat with adorable hats or to decorate your living space. Buy furniture like TVs, shelves, and paintings to create the coziest cat haven imaginable. Level up, earn more, and live your best nine lives!
