Cat Simulator

How to Play Cat Simulator

Welcome to Cat Simulator, where you step into the paws of a lovable feline! Your main objective is to explore and experience life as a cat, completing various activities and earning coins.

Upon starting, you'll be presented with an opening screen offering multiple game mode options. Each mode provides a unique way to play, so choose the one that suits your mood!

Select your main character from 13 different cat breeds. Each breed possesses unique health, damage, and power attributes. Earn coins by playing to unlock new cat species and expand your collection. You can also customize your cat's appearance by purchasing hats or design your playing environment with various items like TVs, paintings, and furniture.

As you progress and level up, the game's difficulty will increase, but so will your coin earnings, allowing for even more customization and unlocks. Dive in and enjoy the purr-fect simulation!
