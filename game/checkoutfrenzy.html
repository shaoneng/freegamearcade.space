Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Have you ever stood in line at a store and wondered, "What exactly are the cashiers doing on that screen?" Checkout Frenzy takes that cashier job and turns it into a super fun online game. Now, you get to be the one behind the register, running your own mini-mart from scratch. You're stocking chips, checking out customers, or hiring helpers. Everything's in your hands.

When you first enter the game, it’s just you, a register, a computer and an empty space. Walk up to the computer to order furniture like shelves, fridges, or freezers. Don’t forget to order the products too! Boxes will appear outside your store; go grab them and bring them in.

Now it's time to get your goods in place. Dry goods (chips, candy) go on regular shelves. Cold items (milk, eggs) go in the fridge. Frozen foods (ice cream, frozen pizza) belong in the freezer. You can rotate shelves to make your store layout smarter and create a more natural look!

Every product has a base price, but you get to decide what to charge. Hover on the price tag and click the "set price" button. Drag the slider to adjust your price. Raise the price to earn more—but don’t go too high over the market price, or customers will walk out. Find that sweet spot between profit and happy customers for store success.

When you're ready, head to the front door and flip the sign from “Closed” to “Open”. Watch as customers start rolling in! Customers come to your register. Click on each item to scan it and listen for that classic “beep”. Check how they want to pay and click on the card or cash they're holding. Be fast but careful, as giving the wrong change costs you money.

Once your store is booming, go to the computer and hire a cashier to handle checkouts or a cleaner to keep the place tidy. If you've got extra cash, buy more floor space to fit more shelves, attract more customers, and make more money. If you enjoy Checkout Frenzy, you might love other tycoon games like Supermarket Simulator.
