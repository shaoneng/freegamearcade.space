Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Begin your retail journey in an empty store. Your first step is to use the computer in the corner to order your initial stock and store furniture. You'll need shelves for dry goods, fridges for cold items, and freezers for frozen products. Don't forget to order the products themselves, like chips, drinks, and ice cream!

Once your delivery boxes arrive outside, bring them in and start stocking. Place items in their correct storage: chips on shelves, milk in fridges, and frozen pizzas in freezers. You can rotate furniture to optimize your store layout and create easy pathways for customers.

To set your prices, click on the price tag of a stocked item. A menu will show the market price and your profit. Adjust the slider to find the perfect balance—price too high, and customers will leave unhappy. Price it right to maximize your earnings!

When you're ready, walk to the front door and flip the sign to "Open". Customers will start to flood in. Head to the register to check them out. Click each item to scan it and then select their payment method (cash or card) to complete the sale. Be quick and accurate to keep the line moving!

As your store gets busier, you can hire help! Use the computer to hire cashiers or cleaners for the day. You can also invest your profits to expand your store's floor space, allowing you to add more shelves and serve even more customers.
