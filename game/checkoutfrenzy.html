Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Ever wondered what it's like to be a cashier? Checkout Frenzy turns that retail rush into a super fun online game. You'll start from scratch and build your own mini-mart, managing everything from stocking shelves to ringing up customers.

Getting Started: Your journey begins in an empty store with just a register and a computer. Head over to the computer to order your first set of furniture, like shelves and fridges. Don't forget to order products to sell, such as chips, soda, and milk. The delivery boxes will appear outside—grab them and bring them in!

Stocking and Organizing: Placement is key! Put dry goods like chips on regular shelves, cold items like milk in the fridge, and frozen foods in the freezer. Fresh produce goes on the grocery shelves. You can rotate furniture to optimize your store layout and make it easy for customers to navigate.

Pricing Your Products: You're the boss, so you set the prices. Click on a product's price tag to adjust it. A slider will show you the market price and your potential profit. Price your items competitively to attract customers, but don't go too high, or they'll walk away. Finding that sweet spot is crucial for success.

Open for Business: When your store is stocked and ready, go to the front door and flip the sign from "Closed" to "Open". Customers will start flooding in! Head to the register and get ready for the frenzy. Click on each item to scan it, listen for the satisfying "beep," and take payment by clicking on the cash or card the customer is holding. Be quick and accurate to keep everyone happy!

Expand Your Empire: As you earn more money, you can grow your business. Use the computer to hire a cashier to help with the checkout line or a cleaner to keep the store tidy. You can also purchase more floor space to add more shelves, serve more customers, and boost your profits. Ready to start your retail adventure? Jump in and build the best store on the block!
