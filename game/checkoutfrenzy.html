Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Welcome to your very own mini-mart! In Checkout Frenzy, you are in charge of everything from stocking shelves to ringing up customers. Your goal is to build a bustling, profitable store from the ground up.

Start by setting up your store. Walk to the computer in the corner to order furniture like shelves and refrigerators, as well as products to sell like chips, soda, and ice cream. Once you place an order, boxes will appear outside. Carry them into your store to begin stocking.

Organize your products logically: dry goods go on regular shelves, cold items in the fridge, and frozen foods in the freezer. You can rotate furniture to create an efficient and appealing layout for your customers. A well-organized store is key to success!

Next, set your prices. Click on a price tag to see the market price and your potential profit. Use the slider to adjust the selling price. Pricing items too high might scare customers away, while pricing them too low will hurt your profits. Find the perfect balance to maximize your earnings.

When you're ready, flip the sign on the front door to "Open" and watch the customers flood in! Stand at the register and click on each item to scan it. Pay attention to whether the customer is paying with cash or a card to complete the transaction. Be quick and accurate to keep the line moving and the customers happy.

As your store gets busier, consider hiring help. Use the computer to hire a cashier or a cleaner for the day. You can also purchase more floor space to expand your store, add more products, and serve even more customers. Good luck, manager!
