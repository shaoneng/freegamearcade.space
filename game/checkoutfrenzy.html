Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Welcome to your very own mini-mart! Your first task is to set up your store. Walk to the computer in the corner to order essential furniture like shelves, fridges, and freezers. Don't forget to order products to sell, such as chips, sodas, and ice cream. Once ordered, boxes will appear outsideâ€”bring them in to start stocking.

Organize your products logically. Dry goods like chips and candy go on standard shelves, cold items like milk go in the fridge, and frozen foods belong in the freezer. You can rotate furniture to optimize your store layout, ensuring customers have enough space to walk around. A well-organized store is key to success!

Next, it's time to set your prices. Click on an item's price tag and use the slider to adjust the cost. Keep an eye on the market price; setting your price too high might scare customers away, while setting it too low will hurt your profits. Find the sweet spot to keep customers happy and your cash register full.

When you're ready for business, head to the front door and flip the sign from "Closed" to "Open." Customers will start arriving with their shopping carts. Head to the register and get ready for the checkout frenzy! Click on each item to scan it and listen for the satisfying "beep." Pay attention to how customers want to pay (cash or card) and be precise, as giving incorrect change will cost you money.

As your store gets busier, you can hire help. Use the computer to hire cashiers to manage the checkout line or cleaners to keep the store tidy. If you have extra cash, you can also purchase more floor space to expand your store, add more shelves, and serve even more customers. Good luck building your retail empire!
