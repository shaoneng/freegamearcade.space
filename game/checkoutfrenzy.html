Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Welcome to Checkout Frenzy! Your goal is to build and manage your own mini-mart. You'll start with an empty space, but with smart decisions, you can turn it into a bustling retail hotspot.

Getting Started: Begin by using the computer in the corner of your store. This is your command center for ordering everything you need, from shelves and fridges to the products themselves like chips, soda, and ice cream. Once ordered, boxes will arrive outsideâ€”grab them and bring them inside to stock up.

Stocking and Organizing: Placement is key! Put dry goods on regular shelves, cold items in the fridges, and frozen foods in freezers. You can rotate furniture to optimize your store's layout, ensuring customers can easily navigate the aisles.

Setting Your Prices: To set a price, click on a product's price tag. You'll see the market price and your potential profit. Use the slider to adjust your selling price. A higher price means more profit, but if it's too far above the market rate, customers might leave empty-handed. Find the perfect balance to keep customers happy and your cash register full.

Open for Business: When you're ready, head to the front door and flip the sign to "Open." Customers will start to flood in! At the checkout, click each item to scan it. Pay attention to their payment method (cash or card) and be careful when giving change to avoid losing money.

Growing Your Empire: As business booms, you can hire a cashier or a cleaner for a day to help manage the workload. Use your profits to expand your floor space, allowing for more shelves, more products, and more customers!
