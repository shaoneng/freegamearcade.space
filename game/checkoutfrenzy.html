Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Welcome to your new mini-mart! Your goal is to build a bustling store from an empty room. Start by heading to the computer in the corner to order your first set of furniture and products. You'll need shelves for dry goods, fridges for cold items, and freezers for frozen foods.

Once your order arrives in boxes outside, bring them in and start stocking. Place items in their appropriate spots: chips on shelves, milk in the fridge, and ice cream in the freezer. You can rotate shelves to optimize your store layout and make it easy for customers to navigate.

Next, set your prices. Click on the price tag above a stocked shelf. You'll see the market price and your potential profit. Use the slider to adjust the price. Setting it too high might scare customers away, so find the sweet spot between profit and customer satisfaction.

When you're ready for business, go to the front door and flip the sign from "Closed" to "Open." Customers will start to come in. At the checkout, click on each item to scan it. Pay attention to their payment method (cash or card) and be careful to give the correct change to avoid losing money.

As your store gets busier, you can hire staff like cashiers and cleaners from the computer to help manage the workload. You can also purchase more floor space to expand your store, add more products, and earn more money!
