Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Have you ever stood in line at a store and wondered, “What exactly are the cashiers doing on that screen?” Checkout Frenzy takes that cashier job and turns it into a super fun online game. Now, you get to be the one behind the register, running your own mini-mart from scratch. From stocking chips to hiring helpers, everything's in your hands.

When you first start, it’s just you, a register, and an empty space. Use the computer in the corner to order furniture like shelves and fridges. Don’t forget to order products too! Boxes will appear outside; go grab them and bring them in.

It's time to get your goods in place. Dry goods (chips, candy) go on regular shelves. Cold items (milk, eggs) go in the fridge. Frozen foods (ice cream, pizza) belong in the freezer. You can rotate shelves to make your store layout smarter and create more space for customers.

Every product has a base price, but you decide what to charge. Hover on a price tag and click the "set price" button. Drag the slider to adjust your price. Raising the price earns more, but if you go too high over the market price, customers will walk out. Find that sweet spot between profit and happy customers for store success.

When you're ready, head to the front door and flip the sign from “Closed” to “Open.” Watch as customers start rolling in! Click on each item at the register to scan it. Check whether they're paying with cash or card, and be fast but careful—giving the wrong change costs you money.

Once your store is booming, you can hire help from the computer, like a cashier to handle checkouts or a cleaner to keep the place tidy. Got some extra cash? Buy more floor space to fit more shelves, attract more customers, and make more money.

Checkout Frenzy is a fast, fun simulation that gives you a real taste of what it's like to manage a retail store. Ready to hear that “beep” and make some cash? Jump in and start your retail adventure!
