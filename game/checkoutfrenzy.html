Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Welcome to your very own mini-mart! Your goal is to build a successful store from the ground up. Start by using the computer in the corner to order shelves, fridges, and products like chips, soda, and ice cream. Once the delivery boxes arrive outside, bring them in to stock your store.

Organize your products logically: dry goods on shelves, cold items in fridges, and frozen foods in freezers. You can rotate furniture to create an efficient layout for your customers. Click on a product's price tag to set your selling price. Be carefulâ€”pricing too high above the market average might make customers leave!

When you're ready for business, head to the front door and flip the sign to "Open". Customers will begin to shop. Man the register and scan each item they bring. Pay attention to their payment method (cash or card) and be quick and accurate to keep the line moving. Giving incorrect change will cost you money.

As your profits grow, you can reinvest in your store. Use the computer to hire cashiers or cleaners for a day to help with the workload. You can also purchase more floor space to expand your store, allowing for more products and more customers!
