Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Your journey begins in an empty store. Use the computer in the corner to order your first set of furniture, like shelves and refrigerators. Don't forget to order products to sell, such as chips, sodas, and ice cream. Once the delivery boxes appear outside, carry them into your store.

Time to organize! Place your items on the correct shelves: dry goods on standard shelves, cold items in the fridge, and frozen foods in the freezer. You can rotate furniture to create an efficient and welcoming layout for your customers.

You control the prices! Click on a product's price tag to adjust it. A slider will show you the market price and your potential profit. Set a competitive price to attract customers while still making money. If your prices are too high, shoppers might leave!

When you're ready for business, go to the front door and flip the sign to "Open." Customers will start arriving at your checkout counter. Click each item to scan it and listen for the satisfying 'beep.' Pay close attention to whether they are paying with cash or card to complete the transaction correctly. Be quick and accurate to keep your customers happy and your profits high!

As your store gets busier, you can hire help! Use the computer to hire cashiers or cleaners for the day. You can also invest your earnings to expand the floor space, allowing you to add more shelves, serve more customers, and grow your business.
