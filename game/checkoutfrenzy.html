Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Welcome to your very own mini-mart! Your goal in Checkout Frenzy is to build a successful store from the ground up. Start by using the computer in the corner to order your first set of shelves, fridges, and products.

Once your delivery boxes arrive outside, bring them in and start stocking. Place dry goods like chips on shelves, cold items like milk in the fridges, and frozen foods in the freezers. You can rotate furniture to create the perfect store layout. Don't forget to set your prices! Click on an item's price tag and use the slider to find the sweet spot between profit and fair market value. Price too high, and customers will leave!

When you're ready for business, head to the front door and flip the sign to "Open." Customers will start to come in. As they bring items to your register, click each product to scan it. Pay close attention to their payment method (cash or card) to complete the transaction correctly. Be quick and accurate to keep the line moving and your customers happy.

As you earn money, you can reinvest it into your store. Use the computer to hire cashiers or cleaners for the day to help manage the workload, or purchase more floor space to expand your mart, add more products, and attract even more shoppers!
