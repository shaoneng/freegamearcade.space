Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Welcome to your new mini-mart! Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to move around. Your first stop is the computer in the corner. Use it to order your initial stock, like chips and soda, and essential furniture like shelves and refrigerators.

Once you've placed your order, boxes will arrive outside. Carry them into your store and start stocking! Place dry goods on regular shelves, cold items in the fridge, and frozen products in the freezer. You can rotate furniture to create the perfect store layout for your customers.

To make a profit, you need to set your prices. Click on a product's price tag, then click the 'Set Price' button. You'll see the market price and your potential profit. Use the slider to adjust your priceâ€”aim for a sweet spot that keeps customers happy while earning you money!

When your store is stocked and ready, head to the front door and flip the sign to 'Open'. Customers will start arriving. Man the register, click each item to scan it, and handle cash or card payments as requested. Be quick and accurate to keep the line moving and avoid costly mistakes!

As your business grows, you'll need help. Use the computer to hire cashiers or cleaners for the day. You can also invest your earnings to expand your store's floor space, allowing you to add more products and serve more customers.
