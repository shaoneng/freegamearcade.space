Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Welcome to your very own mini-mart! Your goal in Checkout Frenzy is to build a bustling, profitable store from scratch. The game combines store management with fast-paced cashier action.

Begin by setting up your store. Walk to the computer in the corner to order your initial furniture, like shelves and refrigerators, and your first batch of products. Once the delivery boxes appear outside, carry them in and start stocking. Place items in their correct locations: chips on shelves, milk in the fridge, and ice cream in the freezer.

Pricing is key! Click on an item's price tag to adjust what you charge. You'll see the market price and your potential profit. Find the sweet spot to make money without scaring customers away with prices that are too high.

When you're ready for business, go to the front door and flip the sign to "Open". Customers will start arriving. Man the register, click each item to scan it, and correctly process their cash or card payments. Be quick and accurate to keep the line moving and avoid losing money on incorrect change.

As your store gets busier, you can hire staff like cashiers and cleaners via the computer to help out. You can also invest your earnings in expanding your store's floor space, allowing for more products and more customers!
