Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Have you ever stood in line at a store and wondered, "What exactly are the cashiers doing on that screen?" Checkout Frenzy takes that cashier job and turns it into a super fun online game. Now, you get to be the one behind the register, running your own mini-mart from scratch. You're stocking chips, checking out customers, or hiring helpers. Everything's in your hands.

This online game lets you experience the chaos and challenge of being a cashier and a store manager at the same time. From scanning groceries to calculating change, from setting prices to placing shelves. It's all about building the busiest store on the block. If you've ever imagined running your own shop, now's your chance to do it for real (without leaving your browser and spending a penny!)

When you first enter the game, it's just you, a register, a computer and an empty space. Walk up to the computer to order furniture like shelves, fridges, or freezers. Don't forget to order the products too, things like chips, soda, ice cream, and milk. Boxes will appear outside your store in a few seconds. Go grab them and bring them in.

Next, get your goods in place. Dry goods (chips, candy) go on regular shelves. Cold items (milk, eggs) go in the fridge. Frozen foods (ice cream, frozen pizza) belong in the freezer. Fruits and vegetables go in the grocery shelves. Tip: You can rotate shelves to make your store layout smarter and create more space for customers!

Every product has a base price, but you get to decide what to charge. Hover on a product's price tag and click the "set price" button. You'll see the current price, market price, and profit. Drag the slider to adjust your price. Raise the price to earn more—but don't go too high over the market price, or customers will walk out. Finding that sweet spot between profit and happy customers is the key to success.

When you're ready, head to the front door and flip the sign from "Closed" to "Open". Watch as customers start rolling in! This is where the real checkout frenzy begins. As customers come to your register, click on each item to scan it and listen for that classic "beep". Check if they're paying with cash or card and click the corresponding icon. Be fast but careful, as giving the wrong change costs you money.

Once your store is booming, go to the computer and hire a cashier to handle checkouts or a cleaner to keep the place tidy. Keep in mind: they're hired for one day only. Got some extra cash? Buy more floor space to fit more shelves, attract more customers, and make more money.

If you enjoy Checkout Frenzy, you might love other tycoon games like Supermarket Simulator. Ready to hear that "beep" and make some cash? Jump into Checkout Frenzy and start your retail adventure!
