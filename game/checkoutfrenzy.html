Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Welcome to your new mini-mart! Your goal is to build a bustling retail empire from the ground up. You'll start with an empty store, a computer, and a cash register. Use the WASD or arrow keys to move around your store.

First, walk up to the computer to order your initial inventory. You can buy furniture like shelves and fridges, as well as products to sell, like chips, soda, and ice cream. Once ordered, boxes will appear outside. Carry them inside and place the items on the correct shelves: dry goods on standard shelves, cold items in fridges, and frozen foods in freezers. You can rotate furniture to optimize your store layout.

Next, it's time to set your prices. Interact with a product's price tag to bring up the pricing menu. You'll see the market price and your potential profit. Drag the slider to adjust your selling price. Setting it higher earns more money, but if it's too far above the market price, customers will refuse to buy. Find the perfect balance for maximum profit and customer satisfaction.

When you're stocked and ready, head to the front door and flip the sign to "Open." Customers will start arriving. Stand behind the register to check them out. Click on each item to scan it, then look at the customer's payment method (cash or card) and click the corresponding button to complete the sale. Be quick and accurate to keep the line moving and the money flowing!

As you earn more cash, you can reinvest in your business. Use the computer to hire cashiers to help with the checkout rush or cleaners to keep the store tidy. You can also purchase more floor space to expand your store, add more shelves, and serve even more customers. Manage your mini-mart well and turn it into a retail giant!
