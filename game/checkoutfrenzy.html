Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Your journey begins in an empty store. Walk over to the computer in the corner to get started. Use it to order essential furniture like shelves, refrigerators, and freezers. Don't forget to order products to sell, such as chips, sodas, and ice cream. The delivery boxes will appear outside your store.

Once your orders arrive, carry the boxes inside and start stocking your shelves. Place dry goods like chips on regular shelves, cold items like milk in the fridges, and frozen foods in the freezers. You can rotate furniture to create an efficient and appealing store layout for your customers.

Pricing is key to your success! Click on a product's price tag to adjust it. A menu will show the current price, the market price, and your potential profit. Use the slider to find the sweet spotâ€”high enough for a good profit, but not so high that you scare away customers.

When you're ready, go to the front door and flip the sign to 'Open'. Customers will start arriving. At the register, click on each item to scan it. Pay attention to their payment method (cash or card) and complete the transaction. Be quick and accurate, as giving wrong change will cost you money!

As your store gets busier, you can use the computer to hire cashiers and cleaners to help you out for the day. You can also purchase more floor space to expand your store, add more products, and serve even more customers on your way to building a mini-mart empire.
