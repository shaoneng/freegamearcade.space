Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

In Checkout Frenzy, you start as the sole manager of your own mini-mart. Your first step is to use the in-game computer located in the corner of your store. From here, you can order essential furniture like shelves, fridges, and freezers. Don't forget to also order a variety of products, from snacks and drinks to dairy and frozen goods. Once ordered, boxes will appear outside your store â€“ grab them and bring them in!

Next, it's time to stock your shelves. Place dry goods on regular shelves, cold items in fridges, and frozen foods in freezers. Fruits and vegetables go on designated grocery shelves. You can rotate shelves to optimize your store layout and create a smooth flow for customers. Setting prices is crucial: hover over a product's price tag, click 'set price,' and adjust the slider. Aim for a balance between maximizing profit and keeping customers happy; set prices too high, and they might walk out!

When you're ready, head to the front door and flip the sign from "Closed" to "Open" to welcome your first customers. At the register, click each item to scan it, then select how the customer wishes to pay (cash or card). Be quick and accurate with change to avoid losing money! As your store grows busier, you can return to the computer to hire additional staff, such as cashiers to help with checkouts or cleaners to keep your store tidy. Remember, staff are hired for one day only. You can also expand your floor space to accommodate more shelves and customers, increasing your earning potential. Master the checkout line and build your retail empire!
