Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Ever wondered what it's like to be a cashier during a busy rush? Checkout Frenzy turns that daily grind into a super fun online game, putting you in charge of your very own mini-mart. From stocking shelves to ringing up customers, you'll build your store from the ground up.

Getting Started: Building Your Store

You begin with an empty space, a register, and a computer. Your first step is to use the computer in the corner to order furniture like shelves, fridges, and freezers. Don't forget to order products to sell, such as chips, soda, and ice cream. The delivery boxes will appear outside—grab them and bring them inside to get started.

Stocking and Organizing

Placement is key! Put dry goods like chips and candy on regular shelves, cold items like milk in the fridge, and frozen foods in the freezer. Fruits and veggies have their own special shelves. You can rotate shelves to create an efficient and customer-friendly layout.

Setting the Right Price

You control the prices. Click on a product's price tag to see its current price, the market price, and your potential profit. Use the slider to adjust it. A higher price means more profit, but if it's too far above the market price, customers will leave. Find that sweet spot to maximize your earnings while keeping shoppers happy.

Open for Business!

When you're ready, head to the front door and flip the sign to "Open." Customers will start coming in. At the register, click on each item to scan it and listen for the satisfying "beep." Pay attention to how they want to pay (cash or card) and be quick and accurate, as mistakes can cost you money.

Grow Your Empire

As your store gets busier, you can hire help. Use the computer to hire cashiers or cleaners for the day. If you've saved up enough cash, you can even buy more floor space to expand your store, add more shelves, and serve more customers. If you enjoy this type of management game, you might also like tycoon games like Supermarket Simulator.
