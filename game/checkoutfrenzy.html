Checkout Frenzy

How to Play Checkout Frenzy

Welcome to Checkout Frenzy, where you get to run your very own mini-mart! Experience the fun and challenge of being a cashier and a store manager. From stocking shelves to checking out customers, you're in charge of building the busiest shop on the block.

Getting Started: When you first enter the game, your store is empty. Walk up to the computer in the corner to order furniture like shelves and fridges. Don't forget to order products too! Boxes will appear outside your store; grab them and bring them inside.

Stocking and Pricing: Place your items on the correct shelves: dry goods on regular shelves, cold items in the fridge, and frozen foods in the freezer. You can rotate shelves to optimize your layout. To set a price, hover over the price tag, click the button, and use the slider. Find the right balance between profit and the market price to keep customers happy.

Open for Business: When you're ready, head to the front door and flip the sign from “Closed” to “Open.” Customers will start coming in. At the register, click on each item to scan it. Pay attention to their payment method (cash or card) and be careful when giving change!

Grow Your Empire: As your store gets busier, you can hire cashiers and cleaners from the computer to help out. Got extra cash? Expand your floor space to fit more shelves, attract more customers, and boost your earnings. Ready to hear that "beep" and make some cash? Jump into Checkout Frenzy and start your retail adventure!

If you enjoy tycoon games like Checkout Frenzy, you might also like Supermarket Simulator for a more complex management experience or other business-style games where you can run restaurants and cafes.
