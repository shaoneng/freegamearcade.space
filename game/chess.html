Chess

How to Play Chess

Welcome to the ultimate game of strategy, a timeless battle of wits on the 64-square battlefield. The objective in Chess is to checkmate your opponent's King, meaning the King is in a position to be captured ("in check") and cannot escape capture.

You command an army of 16 pieces: one King, one Queen, two Rooks, two Knights, two Bishops, and eight Pawns. Each piece has a unique way of moving across the board. Your challenge is to skillfully maneuver your pieces to attack your opponent while simultaneously defending your own King.

Plan your moves carefully, anticipate your opponent's next action, and strive to control the center of the board. To play, simply click on one of your pieces to see its available moves highlighted, then click on the desired square to complete your turn. Outthink your rival to claim victory!
