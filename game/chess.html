Chess

How to Play Chess

Welcome to the ultimate game of strategy! The objective in Chess is to checkmate your opponent's King. This occurs when the King is placed under an attack (in "check") from which it cannot escape. The game is a fantastic way to sharpen your mind, improve concentration, and develop strategic planning skills.

The game is played on a 64-square board with 32 pieces. Each player controls 16 pieces, each moving in a unique way. Use your mouse to click on a piece to see its possible moves highlighted on the board. Click on a highlighted square to move your piece. The key is to think ahead, anticipating your opponent's moves while setting up your own attacks.

Plan your strategy, control the center of the board, protect your valuable pieces, and always keep an eye on your King's safety. Outwit the AI, force a checkmate, and prove you are the master of the board!
