City Police Cars

How to Play City Police Cars

Take on the role of a police officer and bring justice to the city streets! Your objective in City Police Cars is to navigate through 30 action-packed levels, eliminating criminals and overcoming obstacles. Drive your police cruiser through the side-scrolling world, collect coins to upgrade your vehicle, and aim to earn a three-star rating on every mission.

Controls:

  • On PC: Use the W, A, S, D keys to accelerate, brake, and balance your car. Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and hazards.
  • On Mobile: Use the intuitive on-screen buttons to control your vehicle and jump.
