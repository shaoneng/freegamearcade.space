City Police Cars

How to Play City Police Cars

Take on the role of a dedicated police officer in City Police Cars, a fast-paced 2D action game. Your mission is to patrol the streets, eliminate dangerous criminals, and make it to the finish line across 30 challenging levels. Collect coins along your route to unlock new police vehicles and tougher challenges.

On PC: Use the WASD keys to move and balance your car. Press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles.

On Mobile: Simply tap the intuitive on-screen buttons to control your vehicle's movement and jumps.

Strive to earn a three-star rating on every level by completing your mission swiftly and skillfully. Can you clean up the streets and collect all the stars? Jump into your cruiser and find out!
