City Police Cars

How to Play City Police Cars

Your mission in City Police Cars is to patrol the streets, chase down criminals, and restore order. Navigate through 30 action-packed levels in this 2D side-scrolling adventure. Along your route, collect coins scattered on the road. These coins are crucial for unlocking new, more powerful police vehicles to aid you in your quest for justice.

The controls are straightforward, making it easy to jump right into the action. If you're playing on a PC, use the WASD keys to move your car and press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles. For mobile players, simply tap the on-screen buttons to control your vehicle and jump.

The ultimate goal is to reach the finish line in each level while completing your objectives. Challenge yourself to earn a 3-star rating on every mission by being fast and efficient. Can you clean up the streets and become the city's top cop?
