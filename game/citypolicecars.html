City Police Cars

How to Play City Police Cars

Your mission is to patrol the city streets, neutralize dangerous criminals, and reach the finish line of each level. Drive your police car through challenging terrains, overcoming obstacles and enemies. Collect coins scattered throughout the levels to save up and unlock new, improved police vehicles.

PC Controls:

Use the WASD keys to drive and balance your car. Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and evade obstacles.

Mobile Controls:

Tap the dedicated on-screen buttons to control your car's movement and jumping abilities.
