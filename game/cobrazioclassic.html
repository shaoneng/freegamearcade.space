CobraZ.io Classic

How to Play CobraZ.io Classic

Welcome to the intense world of CobraZ.io Classic! Before you jump into battle, head to the Equipment section to customize your soldier. You can change everything from your clothing and hairstyle to your loadout of weapons. Create a unique look and build a soldier that fits your playstyle.

CobraZ.io Classic features four exciting game modes: Team Deathmatch, Free For All, Capture The Flag, and Gun Game. You can select a specific mode by clicking the gear icon in the main menu, or choose 'All' to be placed in a random match. Each mode offers a unique challenge and objective.

Your arsenal is key to victory. In the Equipment section, you can view and equip your primary weapon, secondary gun, and melee weapon. Pay close attention to the stats of each weapon—Damage, Fire Rate, Accuracy, Range, and Mobility—to find the perfect balance for your combat strategy. Unlock new weapons as you progress!

At the start of each match, you'll be given a choice of four special skills. Choose one to take into the fight. These abilities provide a temporary advantage and can be activated by pressing the 'Q' key. Use them wisely to turn the tide of battle!
