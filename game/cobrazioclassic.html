CobraZ.io Classic

How to Play CobraZ.io Classic

Welcome to CobraZ.io Classic, a highly advanced First-Person Shooter (FPS) that puts you in control. Before you jump into battle, take time to customize your soldier and gear up for war with the best items available.

Use the main menu to navigate to the "Equipment" section. Here, you can change your soldier's appearance, from hats and hairstyles to face paint. You can also create custom weapon loadouts. Analyze the stats of each weapon—Damage, Fire Rate, Accuracy, Range, and Mobility—to find the perfect combination for your playstyle.

The game features four distinct modes: Team Deathmatch, Free For All, Capture The Flag, and Gun Game. In the main menu, click the gear icon to select your preferred mode, or choose "All" to be placed in a random match. When a match begins, you'll be prompted to choose one of four special skills. These powerful abilities are activated in-game by pressing the 'Q' key, giving you a tactical advantage when you need it most.
