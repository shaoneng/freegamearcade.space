CobraZ.io Classic

How to Play CobraZ.io Classic

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of CobraZ.io Classic! Your objective is to dominate the arena in this action-packed first-person shooter. Before you jump into the fight, you can fully customize your soldier's gear, appearance, and weapon loadout in the "Equipment" section to match your playstyle.

The game offers four exciting modes to choose from. You can select a specific mode like Team Deathmatch, Free For All, Capture The Flag, or Gun Game by clicking the gear icon in the main menu. If you're feeling spontaneous, let the game pick a random mode for you! Each mode presents a unique challenge and objective.

In the "Equipment" menu, you can analyze your weapons, checking stats like Damage, Fire Rate, and Accuracy to build the perfect loadout. You can also customize your soldier's look with different clothing, hairstyles, and even face paint.

Once you enter a match, the game will prompt you to select one of four special skills. These skills provide a tactical advantage and can be activated by pressing the 'Q' key during combat. Master your movement, aim true, and use your skills wisely to climb the leaderboard and achieve victory!
