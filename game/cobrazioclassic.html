CobraZ.io Classic

How to Play CobraZ.io Classic

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of CobraZ.io Classic! Your main objective is to outgun and outmaneuver players from around the world in fast-paced first-person shooter combat. Before you dive in, you can extensively customize your soldier to match your playstyle.

The game features four exciting modes to test your skills: Team Deathmatch, Free For All, Capture The Flag, and Gun Game. You can select a specific mode by clicking the gear icon in the main menu, or just hit the main play button to jump into a random match and get straight to the action.

Head to the 'Equipment' section to create your ultimate soldier. Here you can choose your primary weapon, sidearm, and melee weapon. Pay attention to weapon stats like Damage, Fire Rate, and Accuracy to build the perfect loadout. You can also customize your soldier's appearance with different clothing, hats, and even face paint to stand out on the battlefield.

At the beginning of each match, you'll be prompted to choose one of four special skills. These powerful abilities can turn the tide of a fight and are activated by pressing the 'Q' key. Master the standard FPS controls to dominate: use WASD to move, your mouse to aim and shoot, and R to reload your weapon. Good luck, soldier!
