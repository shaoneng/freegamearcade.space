CobraZ.io Classic

How to Play CobraZ.io Classic

Welcome to the battlefield of CobraZ.io Classic! Your goal is to dominate your opponents in fast-paced first-person shooter combat. Before you start, dive into the extensive customization options. In the 'Equipment' menu, you can personalize your soldier's appearance, from hats and hairstyles to weapons and gear. Create your perfect loadout by choosing primary weapons, sidearms, and melee items, paying attention to stats like Damage, Fire Rate, and Accuracy.

When you're ready to fight, choose from four exciting game modes: Team Deathmatch, Free For All, Capture The Flag, or Gun Game. You can select a specific mode using the gear icon in the main menu or let the game choose one randomly for you. Once in a match, use standard FPS controls (WASD to move, mouse to aim and shoot, R to reload) to navigate the map and take down enemies.

At the start of each round, you'll be prompted to select one of four special skills. Choose wisely, as this ability can turn the tide of a battle. Press the 'Q' key to activate your chosen skill when you need it most. Master your movement, aim, and strategic use of skills to climb the leaderboard and lead your team to victory!
