CobraZ.io Classic

How to Play CobraZ.io Classic

Welcome to the battlefield of CobraZ.io Classic, a highly advanced first-person shooter! Your objective is to dominate your opponents across various game modes by using superior tactics, customized gear, and sharp shooting skills.

Before you jump into the action, head to the main menu. Here, you can select your preferred game mode: Team Deathmatch, Free For All, Capture The Flag, or Gun Game. Click the gear icon to choose, or simply hit the play button to be placed in a random match.

Customize your soldier to fit your playstyle in the 'Equipment' section. Here, you can modify your loadout with different heavy weapons, guns, and melee options. You can also change your character's appearance, from hats and hairstyles to face paint. Create a unique soldier that stands out in the warzone!

At the beginning of each match, you'll be prompted to choose one of four special skills. These skills provide a temporary advantage in combat and can be activated by pressing the 'Q' key. Choose wisely to complement your strategy.

Use standard FPS controls to navigate and fight: WASD to move, Mouse to aim, Left-Click to shoot, Right-Click to aim down sights, R to reload, Space to jump, and Q to use your selected skill.
