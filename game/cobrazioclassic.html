CobraZ.io Classic

How to Play CobraZ.io Classic

Welcome to the battlefield of CobraZ.io Classic! This is an advanced first-person shooter where your primary goal is to outmaneuver and eliminate opponents in various competitive game modes. Before you jump into the action, you can fully customize your soldier to match your playstyle.

From the main menu, head to the 'Equipment' section to create your perfect loadout. Here, you can select your primary weapon, sidearm, and melee weapon. Pay attention to weapon stats like Damage, Fire Rate, and Accuracy to make informed choices. You can also customize your soldier's appearance, from their hat and hairstyle to face paint, creating a truly unique look for the warzone.

CobraZ.io Classic offers four distinct game modes for endless action. Choose from Team Deathmatch, Free For All, Capture The Flag, or the progressive Gun Game. You can select your preferred mode via the gear icon in the main menu or let the game choose a random mode for you by selecting 'All' and hitting play.

Once you enter a match, you'll be prompted to choose one of four special skills. These abilities provide a tactical advantage and can be activated in-game by pressing the 'Q' key. Choose wisely to complement your strategy and lead your team (or yourself) to victory!
