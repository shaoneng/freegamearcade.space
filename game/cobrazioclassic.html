CobraZ.io Classic

How to Play CobraZ.io Classic

Welcome to the battlefield of CobraZ.io Classic! This game is an advanced first-person shooter where your primary goal is to outgun your opponents in various competitive arenas. Before you jump into the action, you can deeply customize your soldier's appearance, gear, and abilities to match your playstyle.

The game features four distinct modes, each offering a unique challenge. You can choose to play Team Deathmatch, Free For All, Capture The Flag, or Gun Game. Select your preferred mode from the main menu, or let the game choose one randomly for you to test your skills across all formats.

Head to the Equipment section to manage your loadout. Here, you can view stats like Damage, Fire Rate, and Accuracy for your weapons, including heavy guns, pistols, and melee weapons. You can also customize your soldier's clothing, from hats and hairstyles to face paint, creating a unique look for the battlefield. As you play, you'll unlock new weapons and gear to enhance your soldier.

At the start of each match, you'll be prompted to choose one of four special skills. These skills provide a temporary advantage in combat and can be activated by pressing the 'Q' key. Choose wisely to complement your strategy and lead your team to victory!
