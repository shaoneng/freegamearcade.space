CobraZ.io Classic

How to Play CobraZ.io Classic

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of CobraZ.io Classic! Your main goal is to out-gun your opponents in fast-paced first-person shooter combat. Before you jump into a match, take time to customize your soldier and loadout to fit your playstyle.

The game features four distinct modes to master: Team Deathmatch, Free For All, Capture The Flag, and Gun Game. You can select your preferred mode from the main menu or choose 'All' to be placed in a random match. Each mode offers a unique challenge and objective.

Head to the Equipment section to personalize your soldier. You can change everything from weapons, armor, and gear to cosmetic items like hats and face paint. Analyze weapon stats like Damage, Fire Rate, and Accuracy to build the perfect loadout for the fight.

Once in a match, use your keyboard and mouse to move and aim. Use special skills by pressing the 'Q' key to gain a tactical advantage over your enemies. Work with your team (in team-based modes) to secure victory and show off your custom victory dance!
