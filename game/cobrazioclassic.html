CobraZ.io Classic

How to Play CobraZ.io Classic

CobraZ.io Classic is a feature-rich, first-person shooter that drops you into intense online battles. Before you start, dive into the extensive customization options. You can change your soldier's clothing, weapons, special abilities, and even their victory dance! Build your perfect soldier in the Equipment section and create custom loadouts to suit any combat situation.

The game features four exciting modes to match your playstyle. Engage in classic team-based combat in Team Deathmatch, fight for individual glory in Free For All, work with your squad to secure the objective in Capture The Flag, or cycle through a series of weapons in the challenging Gun Game mode. You can select a specific mode from the main menu or let the game choose a random one for you.

Once in the game, standard FPS controls apply (WASD to move, Mouse to aim and shoot, R to reload). Before each match, you can select one of four special skills. These skills provide a temporary advantage in battle and are activated by pressing the 'Q' key. Master your movement, aim, and the timely use of your skill to climb the leaderboard and lead your team to victory!
