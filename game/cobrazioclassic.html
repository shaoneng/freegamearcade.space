CobraZ.io Classic

How to Play CobraZ.io Classic

CobraZ.io Classic is an advanced first-person shooter (FPS) where your primary goal is to outgun and outmaneuver opponents across various battlefields. Before you jump into the action, you can extensively customize your soldier to match your playstyle.

Game Modes: This game features four exciting modes. You can select your preferred mode from the main menu or choose "All" for a random match.

  • Team Deathmatch: Work with your team to achieve a higher kill count than the enemy.
  • Free For All: It's every soldier for themselves. The player with the most kills wins.
  • Capture The Flag: Infiltrate the enemy base, steal their flag, and return it to your base while defending your own.
  • Gun Game: Progress through a series of weapons by getting kills. The first to get a kill with the final weapon wins.

Customization and Loadout: Head to the "Equipment" section to personalize your character. You can change everything from hats and hairstyles to weapons and gear. Examine weapon stats like Damage, Fire Rate, and Accuracy to build the perfect loadout for your strategy. You can also equip special abilities, which can be activated in-game by pressing the 'Q' key to gain a tactical advantage.

Controls:

  • WASD: Move your soldier
  • Mouse: Aim and look around
  • Left Mouse Button: Shoot
  • Right Mouse Button: Aim down sights
  • R: Reload your weapon
  • Spacebar: Jump
  • Shift: Sprint
  • Q: Activate your chosen special skill
