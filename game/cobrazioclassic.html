CobraZ.io Classic

How to Play CobraZ.io Classic

Welcome to the battlefield of CobraZ.io Classic! Your main goal is to out-gun and out-maneuver players from around the world in a fast-paced FPS environment. Before you jump in, you can choose from four exciting game modes: Team Deathmatch, Free For All, Capture The Flag, and Gun Game. Select your preferred mode from the main menu, or let the game choose a random one for you to keep things fresh!

A huge part of the fun is customizing your soldier. Head to the "Equipment" section to create your perfect loadout. Analyze weapon stats like Damage, Fire Rate, and Accuracy to choose the best primary, secondary, and melee weapons for your playstyle. You can also personalize your soldier's look with different clothing, hats, hairstyles, and even face paint to stand out in the arena.

When you start a match, you'll be prompted to choose one of four special skills. These skills provide a tactical advantage and can be activated in-game by pressing the 'Q' key. Choose wisely to complement your strategy! Basic controls are standard for FPS games: use the WASD keys to move, your mouse to aim and fire, the R key to reload your weapon, and the spacebar to jump. Good luck, soldier!
