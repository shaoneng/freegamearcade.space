Cookie Clicker Pro

How to Play Cookie Clicker Pro

Your goal is simple: bake as many cookies as possible! Start by using your mouse to click the giant cookie on the screen. Each click earns you cookies, which you can see tallied at the top.

Once you have enough cookies, it's time to automate. Head to the store on the right and purchase your first buildings. Start with Cursors that click for you, then hire Grandmas for a steady supply of baked goods. As you progress, you'll unlock farms, factories, and even mines to exponentially increase your cookies per second (CPS).

Keep an eye out for upgrades in the store; they'll supercharge your buildings and clicking power. Golden Cookies will randomly appear on the screenâ€”click them quickly for a massive, temporary boost to your cookie production!

After reaching certain milestones, you can 'ascend'. This resets your game but grants you powerful Prestige points and Sugar Lumps. Use these to unlock permanent upgrades that make every new playthrough faster and more productive, allowing you to reach even greater heights in your cookie empire.
