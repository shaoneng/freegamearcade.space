Cookie Clicker Pro

How to Play Cookie Clicker Pro

The goal in Cookie Clicker Pro is simple: make as many cookies as you possibly can! The game is controlled entirely with your mouse or by tapping on a mobile device.

Start Clicking: Begin your baking journey by clicking on the giant cookie in the center of the screen. Each click bakes one cookie, which is added to your total at the top.

Automate Your Bakery: Once you've baked enough cookies, spend them on buildings and upgrades from the shop on the right. Start with Cursors that automatically click for you, then hire Grandmas for a steady supply of fresh-baked goods. As you progress, you'll unlock farms, factories, and even cookie mines to skyrocket your production.

Catch Golden Cookies: Keep an eye out for Golden Cookies that randomly appear on the screen. Clicking them before they disappear grants you a significant, temporary boost to your cookie count or production rate!

Ascend for Prestige: After you've baked a massive number of cookies, you'll unlock the ability to "ascend." This resets your game but grants you Prestige points and permanent upgrades, making your next attempt to build a cookie empire even faster and more efficient. Strategic ascensions are key to reaching astronomical cookie numbers!
