Cookie Clicker Pro

How to Play Cookie Clicker Pro

Dive into the sweet world of Cookie Clicker Pro using just your mouse! The goal is simple: bake as many cookies as possible and build a sprawling cookie empire.

Start Clicking: Begin by clicking the giant cookie in the center of the screen. Each click bakes a fresh cookie, adding to your total at the top.

Automate Production: Once you have enough cookies, start buying buildings from the right-side menu. Purchase Cursors to click for you, Grandmas for steady baking, and eventually unlock Farms, Factories, and Mines to massively increase your cookies per second (CpS).

Buy Upgrades: Spend your cookies on upgrades to enhance the power of your clicks and buildings. These upgrades are crucial for boosting your efficiency and unlocking new possibilities.

Catch Golden Cookies: Keep an eye out for Golden Cookies that randomly appear on the screen. Clicking them provides a significant, temporary boost to your cookie production or gives you a large sum of cookies instantly!

Ascend for Prestige: After reaching a certain milestone, you can choose to "ascend." This resets your game but grants you Prestige levels and Heavenly Chips, which provide powerful, permanent upgrades for all future playthroughs. Strategic ascension is key to reaching astronomical cookie counts!
