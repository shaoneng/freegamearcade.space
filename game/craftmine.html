Craftmine

How to Play Craftmine

Welcome to the wild world of Craftmine! Your primary goal is to survive for as many days and nights as possible in this challenging 2D adventure. Start by gathering essential resources. Approach trees to collect wood, which you'll use to craft your very first tools: a Wood Sword and a Wood Pickaxe.

During the day, you'll encounter various animals like pigs, bears, and snakes. You must craft a sword to defend yourself and hunt them for valuable resources like skin and flesh. Use your pickaxe to mine the ground for stone, gold, and diamonds. These materials are crucial for upgrading your equipment and improving your chances of survival.

Be prepared, because when night falls, the environment becomes much more hostile. The temperature drops, and dangerous zombies emerge from the shadows. You'll need a weapon to fight them off, so make sure you're equipped before sunset. If you're not, your only option is to run and evade them until morning.

Continuously upgrade your gear from basic wooden tools to more durable stone, gold, and diamond equipment. The ultimate weapon, the Magma Sword, will grant you supreme power against the toughest foes. How long can you endure the wilderness?
