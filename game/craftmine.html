Craftmine

How to Play Craftmine

Welcome to the wilderness of Craftmine! Your goal is simple: survive for as many nights as possible. You'll need to explore, gather resources, and craft tools to protect yourself from the dangers that lurk in this 2D world.

Start by gathering wood from trees. With wood, you can craft your first essential items: a Wood Sword and a Wood Pickaxe. The sword is crucial for defending yourself against animals like pigs, bears, and snakes, while the pickaxe will let you mine valuable materials.

During the day, you can hunt animals for their skin and flesh. But be careful, as they will fight back! When night falls, the environment becomes much more dangerous. The temperature drops, and zombies will appear. You must have a weapon to fight them, or else your only option is to run and evade until sunrise.

Use your pickaxe to mine for stone, gold, and diamonds. These resources allow you to craft stronger swords and more durable pickaxes. Progress from basic wooden tools to powerful diamond gear to increase your chances of survival. For ultimate power, strive to craft the legendary Magma Sword!
