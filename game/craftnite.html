Craftnite

How to Play Craftnite

The objective in Craftnite is simple but challenging: be the last player standing. In this epic battle royale, you'll be dropped into a vast, blocky world with other players, and you must fight, build, and survive to claim victory.

Start by exploring the map to gather essential resources and scavenge for weapons. Just like in Minecraft, you can break down the environment to collect materials. Use these resources to craft structures for defense, like walls and forts, or to build ramps and stairs to gain a strategic advantage over your opponents.

Once you're armed, it's time to engage in combat. Keep an eye out for other players, use your structures for cover, and aim carefully to eliminate your foes. The safe zone will shrink over time, forcing players closer together for an intense final showdown. Keep track of your kills and the remaining players on the leaderboard in the top-right corner of the screen. Outlast, outbuild, and outfight everyone to win!
