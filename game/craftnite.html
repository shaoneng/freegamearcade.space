Craftnite

How to Play Craftnite

Welcome to the world of Craftnite, where building and battling collide! The objective is simple: be the last player standing to win the match. You'll be dropped into a large, blocky world with other players.

Your first priority is to gather resources and find weapons scattered across the map. Use your tools to break down objects and collect materials, which you can then use to build structures for defense or to reach strategic high ground.

Engage in fast-paced shootouts with other players. Keep an eye on the top-right corner of your screen to track your kill count and see the leaderboard. Outlast, out-build, and out-shoot everyone else to claim victory in this exciting battle royale!
