Craftnite

How to Play Craftnite

Your goal in Craftnite is simple but challenging: be the last player standing. This game combines building mechanics with fast-paced shooting in a massive multiplayer battle royale. You'll need to use both your strategic building skills and your combat prowess to survive.

Start by exploring the blocky world to gather resources and find powerful weapons. Use the materials you collect to build structures for defense, create vantage points, or trap your enemies. Always keep an eye on your surroundings, as other players are hunting for the same victory.

Engage in gunfights with opponents you encounter. You can keep track of your kills and the number of remaining players by checking the leaderboard in the top-right corner of the screen. Adapt your strategy, build smart, and shoot straight to outlast everyone and claim the top spot!
