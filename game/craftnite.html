Craftnite

How to Play Craftnite

Welcome to the blocky battleground of Craftnite! Your main goal is to be the last player standing in this intense battle royale. When you enter the game, you'll need to immediately start exploring the voxel-based world to gather resources and find weapons.

Just like in Minecraft, you can break down the environment to collect materials. Use these resources to craft and build structures like walls, ramps, and forts for cover or to gain a strategic high-ground advantage. This building mechanic is crucial for surviving firefights.

Once you're armed, it's time to hunt down other players. Engage in fast-paced multiplayer combat, using your building skills and shooting accuracy to outplay your opponents. Keep an eye on the top-right corner of your screen to track your kill count and see your rank on the leaderboard. Adapt, build, and fight to claim victory!
