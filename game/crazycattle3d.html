Crazy Cattle 3D

How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

Welcome to Crazy Cattle 3D, a hilariously chaotic battle royale where you control a bouncy sheep. Your goal is simple: be the last sheep standing by knocking everyone else out of the arena. Brace yourself for wild physics, as your sheep slides and bounces uncontrollably, leading to absolute comedy. If you get knocked out, watch your sheep explode in the funniest way possible!

You'll start your adventure on the green fields of Ireland, dodging wooden fences. Win a few rounds to unlock two new maps: the icy challenges of snowy Sweden and the sandy dunes of Egypt. Each victory adds a shiny trophy to your personal trophy room, so keep battling to collect them all!

Developed by Anna Mayberry, this indie gem went viral for its simple charm and nonstop fun. Crazy Cattle 3D is designed to run smoothly on any desktop or laptop browser, with no download required. Just open the game and start charging!

If you love the wild physics and quick, chaotic rounds of Crazy Cattle 3D, be sure to check out Duck Life Battle. It offers a similar brand of fun with trainable characters and hilarious battles.
