Crazy Cattle 3D

How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

Welcome to the wacky world of Crazy Cattle 3D! In this single-player battle royale, your objective is simple yet chaotic: be the last sheep standing. You control a bouncy sheep from a third-person perspective and must use its unpredictable momentum to ram into other sheep and knock them out of the arena.

The physics in this game are part of the fun! Your sheep will slide, bounce, and careen uncontrollably, making every match a hilarious challenge. If you get knocked out or fall off the edge, your sheep will explode in a comical fashion before you jump into the next round.

Win matches to earn trophies and unlock new, challenging maps. You'll start in the green pastures of Ireland, then progress to the slippery ice of Sweden, and finally to the sandy dunes of Egypt. Each map offers unique obstacles and requires different strategies to master. Jump in, start charging, and see if you can dominate the herd!
