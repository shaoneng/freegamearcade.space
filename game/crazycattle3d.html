Crazy Cattle 3D

How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

Welcome to the hilarious chaos of Crazy Cattle 3D! This is a one-player battle royale where your goal is to be the last sheep standing. You'll control a bouncy sheep with wonderfully strange physics, trying to knock every other sheep in the herd off the map. Be warned, the movement is hilariously uncontrollable, and if you get knocked off... your sheep explodes in the funniest way possible!

You'll start your adventure in Ireland, bouncing across green fields and dodging wooden fences. Win a few rounds to unlock new maps. First is snowy Sweden, where the icy ground makes every turn a comical challenge. Keep winning to unlock the sandy dunes of Egypt, filled with new obstacles. Every victory earns you a shiny trophy for your personal trophy room.

Developed by Anna Mayberry, Crazy Cattle 3D became a viral sensation shortly after its release. Its simple charm, crazy physics, and fast-paced rounds hooked players worldwide. You don't need to download anything to play—just open the game in your browser and start charging!
