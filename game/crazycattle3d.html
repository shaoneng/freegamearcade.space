Crazy Cattle 3D

About Crazy Cattle 3D

Crazy Cattle 3D is a one-player battle royale game where you control a bouncy sheep and try to knock other sheep in the herd off the map. The physics are strange, and the movement of other sheep is absolute comedy. With a third-person perspective, you'll try to be the last sheep standing, all while sliding and bouncing uncontrollably. And if you get knocked out... the sheep explodes in the funniest way.

There are three maps to unlock. You start in Ireland, racing over green fields and dodging wooden fences. Win a few rounds to unlock snowy Sweden, where ice makes every turn a challenge. Keep winning to open Egypt's sandy dunes, full of hidden fences and new hazards. Each victory drops a shiny trophy into your personal trophy room.

Released by indie developer Anna Mayberry on itch.io in April 2025, the game blew up almost immediately. Its simple charm, crazy physics, and fast rounds hooked players worldwide, with the hashtag #CrazyCattle3D racking up millions of views on TikTok and YouTube. Fans on Reddit share tips and tense last-sheep-standing moments, celebrating its nonstop fun.

Crazy Cattle 3D is developed by Anna Mayberry and is crafted to run smoothly on any desktop or laptop browser; no download is needed. Just open your browser and start charging! If you enjoy the wild physics and quick rounds, check out Duck Life Battle, a free online game where you train your duck for upcoming battles. It's easy to jump into, packed with chaos, and guaranteed to deliver just as many laugh-out-loud moments.
