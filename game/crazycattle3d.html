Crazy Cattle 3D

How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

Welcome to the wacky world of Crazy Cattle 3D! Your goal is simple: be the last sheep standing in this chaotic battle royale. You control a bouncy, uncontrollable sheep with hilariously strange physics. Charge at other sheep in the herd to knock them off the platform.

Navigate through different environments, each with its own challenges. Start in the green fields of Ireland, dodging wooden fences. As you win rounds and collect trophies, you'll unlock new maps like the icy plains of Sweden, where control is even trickier, and the sandy dunes of Egypt. Master the slidey movement, use the environment to your advantage, and send your opponents flying to claim victory!

Be careful! One wrong move and your sheep might explode in a comical puff. It's all about quick reflexes, a bit of luck, and enjoying the absolute comedy of watching sheep bounce and fly everywhere. Jump in and see if you have what it takes to rule the herd!
