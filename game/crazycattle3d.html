Crazy Cattle 3D

How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

Welcome to the wacky world of Crazy Cattle 3D! The rules are simple, but the physics are anything but. Your objective is to be the last sheep standing in this hilarious battle royale.

Use your controls to guide your bouncy sheep around the arena. Your main goal is to charge into other sheep and knock them off the edge of the map. Be careful, as they'll be trying to do the same to you!

The unpredictable, slidey physics make every movement a comical adventure. Master the bounce and use the environment to your advantage. Win rounds to unlock new maps like snowy Sweden and sandy Egypt, each with its own unique challenges. If you get knocked out, watch your sheep explode in the funniest way possible before jumping back into the next round!
