Crazy Cattle 3D

How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

Welcome to the zany arena of Crazy Cattle 3D! In this single-player battle royale, your objective is simple: be the last sheep standing. You control a bouncy sheep and must use its momentum to ram into other sheep, knocking them out of the play area.

Mastering the controls is part of the fun, as the game's physics are delightfully strange and unpredictable. Your sheep will slide, bounce, and careen across the map in the most comical ways. Use this chaotic movement to your advantage to outmaneuver your opponents.

You begin your journey in the lush green fields of Ireland. Win rounds to earn trophies and unlock new, more challenging maps. First, you'll unlock the icy terrain of Sweden, where slipping and sliding adds another layer of difficulty. Continue your winning streak to reach the sandy dunes of Egypt, filled with its own unique obstacles. Every victory adds a shiny trophy to your personal trophy room!
