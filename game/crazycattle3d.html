How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

Your goal in Crazy Cattle 3D is simple yet chaotic: be the last sheep standing! You control a surprisingly bouncy sheep from a third-person perspective. The main objective is to charge at other sheep in the herd and use the game's wild physics to knock them off the edge of the map.

Navigate the arena, but be warned â€“ the movement is deliberately slippery and unpredictable, which is half the fun! Use this to your advantage to build momentum and send your opponents flying. If you get knocked off, your sheep will explode in a hilarious fashion, and you'll have to start the round over.

As you win rounds, you'll earn trophies and unlock new maps. You begin in the grassy fields of Ireland, but with enough victories, you can take the battle to the icy challenges of Sweden and the sandy dunes of Egypt. Each map presents unique obstacles and a fresh look for your chaotic battles.
