Crazy Cattle 3D

How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

Crazy Cattle 3D is a hilarious one-player battle royale where you control a bouncy sheep with the goal of being the last one standing. Navigate the chaotic arena from a third-person perspective and use the strange, uncontrollable physics to your advantage to knock other sheep out of the herd. The movement of the other sheep is pure comedy, and if you get knocked out, your sheep explodes in the funniest way possible.

Progression in the game involves unlocking three different maps. You'll start in the green fields of Ireland, dodging wooden fences. After a few wins, you can unlock snowy Sweden, where the icy terrain makes every turn a slippery challenge. Continue your winning streak to access the sandy dunes of Egypt, filled with new obstacles. Every victory earns you a shiny trophy for your personal trophy room.

Developed by Anna Mayberry and released in April 2025, Crazy Cattle 3D quickly went viral for its simple charm and nonstop fun. The game runs smoothly in any desktop or laptop browser, with no download required. Just open the game and start charging!

If you enjoy the wild physics and quick rounds of Crazy Cattle 3D, be sure to check out Duck Life Battle. It's another free online game packed with chaotic action and guaranteed to deliver just as many laugh-out-loud moments.
