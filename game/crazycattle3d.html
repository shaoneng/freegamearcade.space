Crazy Cattle 3D

How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

The objective in Crazy Cattle 3D is simple: be the last sheep standing! You're in a hilarious battle royale against a herd of other bouncy sheep. Use your momentum and the wacky physics to knock your opponents off the map before they knock you off.

Control your sheep's movement to charge at others. The physics are intentionally chaotic, so expect to slide and bounce uncontrollably. Mastering this unpredictable movement is key to outmaneuvering the competition and surviving the frenzy. If you get knocked off, your sheep will explode in a comical puff!

As you win rounds, you'll earn trophies and unlock new arenas. Start in the green fields of Ireland, then conquer the icy terrain of Sweden, and finally navigate the sandy dunes of Egypt. Each map presents unique challenges and obstacles to use to your advantage.
