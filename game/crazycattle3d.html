Crazy Cattle 3D

How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

Welcome to the wild and wacky world of Crazy Cattle 3D! In this single-player battle royale, your mission is simple: be the last sheep standing. You'll control a hilariously bouncy sheep from a third-person perspective.

Charge into the herd and use your momentum to knock other sheep off the edge of the map. Be careful! The physics are unpredictable, leading to comical slides, bounces, and explosions if you get knocked out. A single wrong move could send you flying!

Win rounds to earn trophies and unlock new arenas. Start in the green fields of Ireland, then prove your skills on the icy terrain of Sweden, and finally conquer the sandy dunes of Egypt. Each map offers unique challenges and obstacles to master. Jump in, embrace the chaos, and see if you can reign supreme in the herd!
