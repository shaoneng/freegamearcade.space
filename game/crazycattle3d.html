Crazy Cattle 3D

How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

Welcome to Crazy Cattle 3D, a laugh-out-loud battle royale where you're a bouncy sheep! The main objective is simple: be the last sheep standing. You'll compete against a herd of other sheep in an enclosed arena. Use your momentum and the game's wild physics to knock your opponents off the map.

Control your sheep's movement and charge at others to send them flying. But be careful! The physics are unpredictable, and you can just as easily slide or bounce uncontrollably off the edge yourself. If you get knocked out, your sheep explodes in a hilarious fashion, and you'll have to start a new round.

As you win rounds, you'll earn trophies and unlock new, challenging maps. Start in the grassy fields of Ireland, master the slippery ice of Sweden, and conquer the sandy dunes of Egypt. Each map offers unique obstacles and a fresh layout to test your skills. Jump in and embrace the chaos!
