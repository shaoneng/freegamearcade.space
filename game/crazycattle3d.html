Crazy Cattle 3D

How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

Welcome to the wacky world of Crazy Cattle 3D! Your goal is simple but hilarious: be the last sheep standing. You'll compete in a battle royale against a herd of other sheep in various arenas. Use your momentum and the game's bouncy physics to your advantage to knock your opponents off the map.

Control your bouncy sheep to navigate the map. The physics are intentionally strange, making every movement a comical challenge. Charge at other sheep to send them flying, but be careful not to get knocked off yourself or explode by hitting a fence! Every round is fast, furious, and full of laughs.

As you win rounds, you'll earn trophies and unlock new maps. Start your journey in the green fields of Ireland, then prove your skills on the icy slopes of Sweden and the sandy dunes of Egypt. Each map presents unique challenges and obstacles to overcome. Master the chaos to become the ultimate crazy cattle champion!
