Crazy Cattle 3D

How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

Welcome to the chaotic pasture of Crazy Cattle 3D! The goal is simple: be the last sheep standing. You control a bouncy, slightly uncontrollable sheep in a battle royale against a whole herd.

Use your controls to charge and bump into other sheep, sending them flying. The wacky physics are part of the fun, so use the unpredictable bounces to your advantage to knock rivals off the map. Be careful not to fall off yourself, or you'll explode in a puff of wool!

Win rounds to earn trophies and unlock new maps. Start in Ireland's green fields, then challenge yourself on the icy terrain of Sweden and the sandy dunes of Egypt. Each map offers unique obstacles and challenges. Jump in, embrace the chaos, and aim for victory!
