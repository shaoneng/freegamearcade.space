How to Play Crazy Cattle 3D

Welcome to the wild and woolly world of Crazy Cattle 3D! Your goal is simple: be the last sheep standing. In this hilarious single-player battle royale, you'll control a super bouncy sheep and must knock all other sheep out of the arena to win the round.

Navigate the chaotic levels using your movement keys. The physics are intentionally wacky, so expect your sheep to slide, bounce, and careen uncontrollably. Use this momentum to your advantage to ram into other sheep and send them flying off the edge. But be carefulâ€”they'll be trying to do the same to you!

Start your journey in the green fields of Ireland. As you win rounds and collect trophies, you'll unlock two new maps: the slippery, icy terrain of Sweden and the sandy, obstacle-filled dunes of Egypt. Each map presents a new challenge and more opportunities for comedic chaos. Keep winning to fill your trophy room and prove you're the craziest cattle of all!
