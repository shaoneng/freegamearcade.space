About Crazy Cattle 3D

Crazy Cattle 3D is a unique battle royale where you control a bouncy, uncontrollable sheep. Your goal is to be the last one standing by knocking other sheep out of the arena. Brace yourself for some truly strange physics and hilarious movements that make every round unpredictable. And if you get knocked out? Your sheep explodes in the most comedic way possible!

Your chaotic journey begins in the green fields of Ireland. As you win rounds, you'll earn shiny trophies for your personal trophy room and unlock new maps. First up is snowy Sweden, where icy surfaces add a new layer of challenge. After that, you can take on the sandy dunes of Egypt, filled with its own unique obstacles. Can you conquer all three and become the ultimate sheep champion?

Developed by Anna Mayberry and released in April 2025, Crazy Cattle 3D quickly became a viral sensation. Its simple charm, wacky physics, and fast-paced rounds captured the internet's attention, with players sharing hilarious clips across TikTok and YouTube.
