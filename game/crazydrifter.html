Crazy Drifter

How to Play Crazy Drifter

Welcome to the high-octane world of Crazy Drifter! The core of the game is simple: race, drift, and win. Your main goal is to master the art of drifting around corners to maintain speed and outmaneuver your opponents.

Game Controls:

  • Arrow Keys / WASD: Steer, accelerate, and brake.
  • Spacebar: Engage the handbrake to initiate a drift.
  • C Key: Change the camera view.

Choose from four exciting game modes: Drift, where your score is based on your drifting skills; Time Attack, a race against the clock; Knockout, where the last-place driver is eliminated each lap; and Circuit, a classic race to the finish line.

As you win races and complete events, you'll earn money. Use your earnings in the garage to purchase one of five different vehicles or upgrade your current car with a wide range of modifications to improve its performance and appearance. Hit the streets, burn some rubber, and become the ultimate crazy drifter!
