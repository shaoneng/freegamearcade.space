Crazy Drifter

How to Play Crazy Drifter

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled streets of Crazy Drifter! Your goal is to dominate the asphalt by mastering the art of drifting and out-racing your opponents. Use your earnings from victories to upgrade your current vehicle or save up to purchase one of the five unique cars available in the garage.

Controls:

  • Accelerate: W or Up Arrow
  • Brake/Reverse: S or Down Arrow
  • Steer Left/Right: A/D or Left/Right Arrows
  • Handbrake: Spacebar (Use this to initiate drifts!)
  • Change Camera: C key

Game Modes:

  • Drift: Rack up points by performing long, stylish drifts.
  • Time Attack: Race against the clock to set the fastest lap time.
  • Knockout: The last racer in each lap is eliminated. Be the last one standing!
  • Circuit: Compete in a classic race against multiple opponents to finish first.

Choose the mode that best suits your style, hit the streets, and show everyone who the true Crazy Drifter is!
