Crazy Drifter

How to Play Crazy Drifter

Welcome to the high-octane world of Crazy Drifter! The goal is simple: dominate the streets with your superior driving and drifting skills. Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer, accelerate, and brake. Hit the spacebar to use your handbrake for sharp, stylish drifts around corners.

Crazy Drifter offers four exciting game modes to test your abilities. In Drift mode, your score is all about style. Time Attack challenges you to beat the clock. Knockout requires you to stay ahead of the pack, as the last-place driver is eliminated each lap. Finally, Circuit mode is a traditional race to the finish line.

As you compete and win, you'll earn cash. Use your earnings in the garage to either purchase one of the five available high-performance vehicles or to modify your current car. Upgrade your engine, tune your suspension, and change your car's appearance to create the ultimate drifting machine.
