Crazy Drifter

How to Play Crazy Drifter

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of Crazy Drifter! The objective is simple: become the ultimate street racing champion by mastering the art of the drift. Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer, accelerate, and brake. Press the Spacebar to engage the handbrake for tight, controlled drifts around corners.

The game offers four distinct and exciting modes to test your skills. In Drift mode, your goal is to score as many points as possible by sliding sideways. Time Attack challenges you to beat the clock on a solo run. In Knockout, you must avoid being in last place at the end of each lap. Finally, Circuit mode pits you against other racers in a classic, multi-lap race to the finish line.

As you compete and win races, you'll earn in-game cash. Your hard-earned money can be invested in the garage. You can either purchase one of the five different vehicles available or upgrade your current car with a wide range of modifications. Improve your engine, brakes, and handling, or customize the look with new paint jobs and rims to create a car that's uniquely yours. Choose your mode, hit the streets, and start drifting!
