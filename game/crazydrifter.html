Crazy Drifter

How to Play Crazy Drifter

Welcome to the high-octane world of Crazy Drifter! The goal is simple: dominate the streets and become the ultimate racing champion. Start by choosing your preferred game mode from the main menu. You have four exciting options to test your skills:

Drift: Focus on scoring points by executing long, stylish drifts around corners.

Time Attack: Race against the clock to set the fastest lap time possible.

Knockout: Compete in a multi-lap race where the last-place driver is eliminated after each lap.

Circuit: Engage in a classic race against other cars to finish in first place.

As you compete and win, you'll earn money. Use your earnings in the garage to unlock one of the five different high-performance vehicles or to modify your existing car. Upgrade your engine, tires, and more to gain a competitive edge. Now, get in the driver's seat and show them what you've got!
