Crazy Drifter

How to Play Crazy Drifter

Welcome to the high-octane world of Crazy Drifter! The goal is simple: dominate the streets by mastering the art of drifting. Use the arrow keys or WASD keys to steer, accelerate, and brake. Hit the spacebar for the handbrake to initiate sharp, stylish drifts around corners.

The game offers four distinct and challenging modes to test your driving prowess: Drift, Time Attack, Knockout, and Circuit. Each mode provides a unique experience, from scoring points for style to racing against the clock or other opponents. Choose the one that best suits your style and hit the asphalt!

As you compete and win races, you'll earn in-game currency. Use your earnings wisely to either purchase one of the five available high-performance cars or upgrade and modify your current ride. Customizing your vehicle is key to staying competitive and leaving your rivals in the dust.
