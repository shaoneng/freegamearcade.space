Crazy Drifter

How to Play Crazy Drifter

Getting started in Crazy Drifter is simple. Use your keyboard to control your car. Typically, the Arrow Keys or WASD keys are used for acceleration, braking, and steering. The main goal is to win races and earn money by outperforming your opponents.

Game Modes: The game offers four exciting modes to test your skills:

  • Drift: Score points by executing long and stylish drifts around the track.
  • Time Attack: Race against the clock to achieve the fastest possible lap time.
  • Knockout: In this elimination mode, the driver in last place is knocked out after each lap. Be the last one standing!
  • Circuit: A classic race against multiple opponents. The first to cross the finish line wins.

Upgrades and Customization: Winning races earns you cash. You can invest this money in the garage to either purchase one of the five available cars or upgrade your current one. Modify everything from performance to aesthetics to create the ultimate drifting machine and dominate the competition.
