Crazy Drifter

How to Play Crazy Drifter

Welcome to the high-stakes world of Crazy Drifter! The core of the game is simple: get behind the wheel and dominate the streets. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to control your vehicle's acceleration, braking, and steering. To execute those epic, point-scoring drifts, hit the Spacebar to use the handbrake as you turn into a corner.

Choose from four distinct game modes, each offering a unique challenge. In Drift mode, your goal is to rack up as many points as possible by sliding sideways. Time Attack pits you against the clock for the fastest lap. In Knockout, the last-place driver is eliminated each lap until only one remains. Finally, Circuit is a traditional race where you compete against others to finish first.

Winning races earns you cash, which is your key to progress. Head to the garage to spend your earnings on unlocking new, more powerful cars or use it to extensively modify your current ride. Upgrading your car's performance and aesthetics is crucial for staying competitive and winning tougher races.
