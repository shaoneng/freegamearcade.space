Crazy Drifter

How to Play Crazy Drifter

Welcome to the high-stakes world of Crazy Drifter! Your main goal is to conquer the streets by mastering the art of drifting. This racing simulator offers a thrilling experience that will get your adrenaline pumping. The controls are intuitive, making it easy to jump in and start burning rubber.

The game features four exciting modes to test your skills. In Drift mode, your score is based on your style and how long you can hold a drift. Time Attack challenges you to beat the clock on a solo run. In Knockout, you must avoid being in last place at the end of each lap. Finally, Circuit is a classic race against opponents to the finish line. Choose your favorite and hit the track!

Winning races earns you money. Use your earnings wisely in the garage to either buy one of five different cars or upgrade your current ride. You can modify everything from the engine to the paint job to gain an edge over the competition. Customize your car, dominate the races, and become the undisputed champion of the streets!
