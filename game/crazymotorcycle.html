Crazy Motorcycle

How to Play Crazy Motorcycle

Your mission in Crazy Motorcycle is to navigate a blocky, Minecraft-style hero named Nubik through a series of treacherous obstacle courses in the sky. There are no roads or safety rails—just you, your bike, and a floating path to the finish line.

Control your motorcycle using the arrow keys or WASD. Press the Up Arrow or 'W' to accelerate and the Down Arrow or 'S' to brake or reverse. Use the Left and Right arrow keys (or 'A' and 'D') to lean your rider forward and backward. Mastering your balance is crucial for climbing hills, landing jumps, and avoiding a dramatic fall into the void.

Dodge obstacles like chunky trees and stone walls, carefully navigate over icy patches, and maintain enough momentum to clear gaps between platforms. Your goal is to reach the red portal at the end of each short, challenging level. If you crash, don't worry—retries are instant, letting you get right back into the action!
