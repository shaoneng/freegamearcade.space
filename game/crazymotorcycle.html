Crazy Motorcycle

How to Play Crazy Motorcycle

Your mission in Crazy Motorcycle is to guide the blocky hero, Nubik, through a series of challenging obstacle courses floating in the sky. The controls are straightforward, designed for quick action and retries.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to control the motorcycle. Accelerate to gain speed for long jumps, brake to maintain control, and lean back or forward to keep your balance while airborne. The goal for each level is to safely navigate across the platforms, dodge all the obstacles like trees and stone pillars, and successfully reach the red portal at the end.

Be careful! One wrong move can send Nubik tumbling into the void below. But don't worry, the levels are short and you can restart instantly. Master the physics of your bike to conquer every crazy course!
