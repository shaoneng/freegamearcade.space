Crazy Motorcycle

How to Play Crazy Motorcycle

If Minecraft ever had a wild cousin who ditched mining for motorbikes, Crazy Motorcycle would be it. In this crazy bike game, you'll take a Minecraft-style noob (we call him Nubik), give him a shiny motorcycle, and drop into a floating obstacle course in the sky.

There are no roads, no rules, and definitely no safety rails. Your job is to navigate the blocky, platform-style levels, dodging obstacles like chunky trees and stone walls, and jumping across floating islands. The goal is to reach the red portal at the end of each level to advance.

The game features a playful voxel aesthetic that feels pulled straight from a Minecraft creative server. Built by AM-Games, it's designed to be a challenging but fun experience with short levels and instant retries, making it easy to jump right back in after a dramatic wipeout.

If you enjoy the sky-high stunts in Crazy Motorcycle, you might also like Obby But You're on a Bike. It offers a similar bike-platforming adventure but with a Roblox-inspired theme.
