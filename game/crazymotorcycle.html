Crazy Motorcycle

How to Play Crazy Motorcycle

Welcome to the chaotic, blocky world of Crazy Motorcycle! Your objective is simple but challenging: guide your character, Nubik, on his motorcycle through a series of floating obstacle courses to reach the red portal at the end of each level.

Use the arrow keys or WASD on your keyboard to control the motorcycle. The up arrow/W key accelerates, the down arrow/S key brakes or reverses, and the left/right arrow keys (or A/D) help you balance your bike in the air to land your jumps perfectly.

Navigate carefully through a landscape inspired by Minecraft, dodging chunky trees, stone pillars, and icy blocks.
