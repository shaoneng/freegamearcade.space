Crazy Motorcycle

How to Play Crazy Motorcycle

Your mission in Crazy Motorcycle is to guide your blocky character, Nubik, through a series of wild obstacle courses set high in the sky. The objective is simple: get to the red portal at the end of each level without falling off the platforms.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your motorcycle. Press the up arrow or 'W' to accelerate forward, and the down arrow or 'S' to brake or reverse. Use the left and right arrow keys (or 'A' and 'D') to balance your bike, especially when landing from jumps or navigating uneven terrain. A good balance is key to avoiding dramatic wipeouts!

Watch out for obstacles like trees, stone walls, and tricky gaps. Some levels feature slippery ice blocks and other challenges that require careful throttle control. Don't be afraid to fail—retries are instant, so you can jump right back into the action and master the course!
