Crazy Motorcycle

How to Play Crazy Motorcycle

Welcome to the wild world of Crazy Motorcycle! Imagine if Minecraft had a daredevil cousin who traded his pickaxe for a motorbike—that's the experience you're in for. You'll guide a blocky hero, Nubik, on his motorcycle across treacherous, floating obstacle courses high in the sky. There are no roads, no safety rails, just pure, unadulterated platforming action.

Your mission is to navigate each level's unique challenges, from pixelated grass blocks and mossy stone pillars to slippery ice patches and floating islands. Your goal is to dodge, jump, and skillfully maneuver your way to the red portal at the end of the course. Don't worry if you wipe out; instant retries let you jump right back into the action and try again!

The controls are simple and intuitive, designed to get you into the action quickly:

  • PC: Use the arrow keys or WASD to accelerate, brake, and balance your bike.
  • Mobile: Use the on-screen touch controls to steer and control your speed.

Crazy Motorcycle is a fun and challenging browser game created by AM-Games. If you enjoy this high-flying adventure, you might also like Obby But You're on a Bike, which offers a similar sky-high biking challenge but with a Roblox-inspired theme.
