Crazy Motorcycle

How to Play Crazy Motorcycle

Welcome to the wacky, blocky world of Crazy Motorcycle! Your goal is to guide the Minecraft-inspired character, Nubik, on his motorcycle through a series of floating obstacle courses in the sky. To complete a level, you must safely navigate the hazardous terrain and drive through the red portal at the end.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to control the motorcycle. The up arrow/W accelerates, the down arrow/S brakes or reverses, and the left/right arrows (or A/D) help you lean back and forth to maintain balance. Mastering your balance is crucial for landing jumps and climbing steep ramps without wiping out.

The challenge lies in the unforgiving, rule-free tracks. You'll need to dodge chunky trees, jump over stone walls, and carefully traverse slippery ice blocks.
