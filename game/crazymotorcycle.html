Crazy Motorcycle

How to Play Crazy Motorcycle

Welcome to the wacky, blocky world of Crazy Motorcycle! Your goal is simple but challenging: guide your motorcycle rider safely across a series of floating platforms and obstacles to reach the red portal at the end of each level.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or W/A/S/D to control the motorcycle. Press the up arrow or 'W' to accelerate forward and the down arrow or 'S' to brake or reverse. Use the left and right arrow keys (or 'A' and 'D') to lean your rider back and forth. Mastering this balance is key to landing jumps and climbing steep ramps without flipping over.

Navigate carefully through the Minecraft-inspired landscape. Dodge trees, jump over gaps, and be mindful of slippery ice blocks. One wrong move could send you tumbling into the void, but don't worry—retries are instant, so you can jump right back into the action!
