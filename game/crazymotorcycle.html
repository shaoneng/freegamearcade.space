Crazy Motorcycle

How to Play Crazy Motorcycle

If Minecraft ever had a wild cousin who ditched mining for motorbikes, Crazy Motorcycle would be it. In this crazy bike game, you'll take a Minecraft-style noob (we call him Nubik), give him a shiny motorcycle, and drop into a floating obstacle course in the sky. There are no roads, no rules, and definitely no safety rails. Just pure blocky platforming action.

Your job is to dodge, jump, and weave through a pixelated landscape of grass blocks, mossy stone, and ice. The goal is to reach the red portal at the end of each level without falling into the void. It’s a hilarious and challenging experience, especially when you see the square-headed daredevil wipe out in the most dramatic ways. With short levels and instant retries, you'll be hooked on reaching that next checkpoint.

Controls:

  • PC: Use the arrow keys or W/A/S/D to accelerate, brake, and balance your bike.
  • Mobile: Use the on-screen touch controls to ride and balance.

If Crazy Motorcycle grabbed your attention, try Obby But You're on a Bike. It's another sky adventure where you ride and jump, but with a Roblox-inspired theme instead!
