Crazy Motorcycle

How to Play Crazy Motorcycle

Welcome to the blocky, high-flying world of Crazy Motorcycle! Your mission is to guide the brave but clumsy Nubik on his motorcycle across a series of dangerous, floating obstacle courses. The controls are simple, but the challenge is anything but.

Use the arrow keys or WASD on your keyboard to control the motorcycle. Press the up arrow (or W) to accelerate forward and the down arrow (or S) to brake or reverse. Use the left and right arrow keys (or A and D) to lean back and forward, which is crucial for balancing your bike as you go over jumps and uneven terrain.

Your goal in each level is to navigate past all the obstacles—from stone walls and trees to slippery ice blocks—and safely reach the red portal at the end. Be careful not to wipe out or fall off the track, or you'll have to start over! With quick retries, you can jump right back into the action. Good luck, and enjoy the crazy ride!
