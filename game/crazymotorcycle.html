Crazy Motorcycle

How to Play Crazy Motorcycle

Welcome to a world where Minecraft meets motorbikes! In Crazy Motorcycle, you take control of Nubik, a blocky hero with a need for speed, and guide him through treacherous obstacle courses floating high in the sky. Forget mining and crafting; here, it's all about pure, unadulterated platforming on two wheels.

Your objective is to navigate a series of pixelated platforms, dodging chunky trees, mossy stone pillars, and slippery ice blocks to reach the red portal at the end of each level. There are no rules and no safety rails, so be prepared for some hilarious and dramatic wipeouts. The short levels and instant retries make it easy to jump right back into the action.

Controls:

  • PC: Use the W/A/S/D or Arrow Keys to accelerate, brake, and balance your motorcycle.
  • Mobile: Use the on-screen touch controls to steer and control your speed.

If you love the thrill of this stunt game, be sure to check out Obby But You're on a Bike for a similar sky-high adventure with a Roblox-inspired theme.
