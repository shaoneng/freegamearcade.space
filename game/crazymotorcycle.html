Crazy Motorcycle

How to Play Crazy Motorcycle

Welcome to the zany, block-filled world of Crazy Motorcycle! Your goal is simple: guide your fearless rider, Nubik, through a series of floating obstacle courses and reach the red portal at the end of each level.

Use your controls to accelerate, brake, and balance your motorcycle. The physics are playful but challenging, so you'll need to master your bike's momentum to make it over ramps, jump across gaps, and navigate around tricky obstacles like trees and stone walls.

There are no safety rails here! If you fall off the course, you'll restart the level instantly. Learn from each wipeout, time your jumps carefully, and prove you have the skills to conquer these crazy sky-high tracks. Good luck!
