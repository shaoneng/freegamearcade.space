Crazy Motorcycle

How to Play Crazy Motorcycle

In Crazy Motorcycle, your mission is to guide the blocky hero, Nubik, across a series of treacherous sky-high obstacle courses. The objective is simple but challenging: safely navigate your motorcycle from the starting point to the red portal at the end of each level without falling into the void.

Use your keyboard controls to accelerate, brake, and lean your bike forward and backward to maintain balance. Timing your jumps is crucial to clear gaps, while precise control is needed to weave through chunky trees and around stone walls. The physics are hilariously unpredictable, so expect some spectacular wipeouts!
