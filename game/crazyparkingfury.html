Crazy Parking Fury

How to Play Crazy Parking Fury

Welcome to the sun-soaked streets of Crazy Parking Fury! Your main goal is to navigate the city and park your car in the designated spots without getting busted by the police. Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your vehicle, accelerating, braking, and steering through the busy streets.

Keep an eye out for the police patrols! If they spot you, they'll give chase. You'll need to use your driving skills to evade them and reach your parking destination safely. As you progress through the game's ten levels, the parking challenges will become more difficult and the police presence more intense.

Need a new ride? You can steal any parked car you find. Simply drive or walk up to a vacant vehicle until you see the 'Enter Car' icon appear, then press the 'Enter' key to take control. Master the art of the getaway and perfect your parking to conquer every level!
