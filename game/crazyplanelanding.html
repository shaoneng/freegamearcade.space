Crazy Plane Landing

How to Play Crazy Plane Landing

Your main objective in Crazy Plane Landing is to launch your plane from a ramp, fly as far as you can, and try to land safely on the runway. The further you fly, the more you earn!

Control your plane's flight to cover more distance and perform awesome stunts like barrel rolls. Every meter traveled and every cool trick you perform adds to your cash earnings. But be careful—a crash landing can end your run prematurely!

Use the coins you collect to visit the hangar and upgrade your aircraft. You can improve your Engine for more power, enhance your Boost for a thrilling burst of speed, and increase your Bonus multiplier for even bigger payouts. Upgrading is the key to reaching the finish line on different terrains.

As you accumulate enough cash, you can unlock and buy new, more advanced planes. Each new jet starts with basic stats but has a higher potential for upgrades, allowing you to soar past your old records and conquer the skies.
