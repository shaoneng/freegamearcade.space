Crazy Plane Landing

How to Play Crazy Plane Landing

Welcome to the wild world of Crazy Plane Landing! Your main objective is to launch your plane from a ramp, fly as far as you can, and stick the landing on the runway at the end. The further you travel and the more stunts you pull off, the more coins you'll earn.

Use your mouse or finger to control the plane's flight path. Keep an eye on your altitude and speed. When you have it, activate your boost for a massive burst of speed to cover more distance. Performing stunts like barrel rolls will also add to your score and coin total.

After each flight, you'll be taken to the upgrade screen. Spend the coins you've earned to improve your plane's Engine for more power, enhance your Boost for longer bursts of speed, and increase your Bonus multiplier to earn even more money on future flights. Balancing these upgrades is key to success.

Keep pushing your limits and saving up your cash. Eventually, you'll be able to unlock new, sleeker planes that have higher potential for upgrades, allowing you to smash your old records and finally reach that distant finish line!
