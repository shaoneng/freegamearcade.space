Crazy Plane Landing

How to Play Crazy Plane Landing

Welcome to Crazy Plane Landing, the 3D stunt flight game that's all about pushing your luck and your aircraft to the limit. Your goal is simple: launch your plane from a ramp, fly as far as you can, and attempt a safe landing on a distant runway. It's a game of risk and reward where every stunt and every extra meter in the air adds to your earnings.

Use the coins you earn from each flight to upgrade your plane. Invest in a more powerful Engine for better launch speed, extend your Boost for longer airtime, or increase your Bonus multiplier to maximize your payout. Each upgrade gets you closer to conquering the course and setting new distance records.

As you progress, you'll unlock new, sleeker planes. While they start with basic stats, their upgrade potential is far greater, allowing you to smash your previous records. Master the art of boosting, barrel rolling, and landing to prove you're the ultimate pilot. Ready for takeoff? Click and fly, no installs required!
