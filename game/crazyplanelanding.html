Crazy Plane Landing

How to Play Crazy Plane Landing

Your main goal in Crazy Plane Landing is to launch your plane from a high ramp and fly it as far as possible, ultimately aiming for a safe landing on the designated runway. The further you travel and the more stunts you perform, like barrel rolls, the more coins you'll earn for your flight.

After each attempt, you can visit the upgrade shop. Spend your hard-earned coins to enhance your aircraft. Upgrade your Engine for more power, increase your Boost for a longer burst of speed, and improve your Bonus to multiply your earnings. Strategic upgrades are key to reaching new distances.

As you accumulate wealth, you'll be able to unlock entirely new planes. These advanced jets start with basic stats but have a much higher potential for upgrades, enabling you to soar past your old records and conquer every terrain. Keep flying, upgrading, and aim for that perfect, crazy landing!
