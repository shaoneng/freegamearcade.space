Crazy Plane Landing

How to Play Crazy Plane Landing

Your goal in Crazy Plane Landing is simple but challenging: launch your plane off a giant ramp, fly as far as you can, and attempt a safe landing. The further you fly and the more stunts you pull off, the more coins you'll earn.

Use your mouse to control your plane. After launching, steer your aircraft to maintain altitude and perform daring maneuvers like barrel rolls to maximize your score. Keep an eye on your fuel and use your boost strategically to cover more ground.

After each flight, head to the upgrade shop. Spend your hard-earned coins to improve your plane's Engine for more thrust, enhance your Boost for longer bursts of speed, and increase the Bonus multiplier for bigger payouts. As you save up, you can unlock entirely new planes with higher potential, allowing you to smash your previous distance records.
