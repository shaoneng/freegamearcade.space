Crazy Plane Landing

How to Play Crazy Plane Landing

The objective in Crazy Plane Landing is to launch your plane from a ramp and fly as far as you can before making a safe landing on the runway. The further you go and the better your landing, the more coins you'll earn!

Click or tap to launch your plane. Once you're in the air, use your boost strategically to gain extra speed and altitude. Every meter you travel and every stunt you perform, like a barrel roll, adds to your final payout. But be carefulâ€”you need to land in one piece to collect your reward.

Use the coins you earn from each flight to upgrade your aircraft. You can invest in a more powerful Engine for better acceleration, a longer-lasting Boost for extended flight time, or a bigger Bonus multiplier to increase your earnings. Each upgrade costs more than the last, so spend wisely!

When you've saved up enough, you can unlock entirely new planes. These advanced jets have higher potential for upgrades, enabling you to fly further and smash your previous records. Keep launching, upgrading, and chasing that distant finish line!
