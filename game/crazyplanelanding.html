Crazy Plane Landing

How to Play Crazy Plane Landing

Your mission in Crazy Plane Landing is simple but challenging: launch your plane and fly as far as you can! Start by launching your plane with maximum power from the ramp. Once airborne, your goal is to maintain flight for as long as possible. Use your boost strategically to gain extra speed and altitude. Steer your plane to perform stunts like barrel rolls, as every trick adds to your final score and coin payout.

The further you fly and the more stylishly you do it, the more coins you'll earn. Invest these coins in the hangar to upgrade your plane. You can improve your Engine for more power, enhance your Boost for longer bursts of speed, and increase your Bonus multiplier to earn even more money on each flight.

As you save up, you can unlock entirely new planes that have higher potential for speed and distance. Keep pushing your limits, beat your old records, and aim for that perfect landing on the distant runway. Good luck, pilot!
