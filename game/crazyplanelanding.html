Crazy Plane Landing

How to Play Crazy Plane Landing

Your goal in Crazy Plane Landing is to launch your aircraft from a ramp and fly it as far as possible, aiming for the runway at the end. At the start of each attempt, click and hold to rev up your engine and release at the perfect moment for maximum launch speed.

Once airborne, your main objective is to manage your fuel and boost strategically to cover the maximum distance. You'll earn coins for the distance you travel and for performing stunts like barrel rolls. Keep an eye on your altitude and try to maintain a steady flight path to avoid crashing prematurely.

Use the coins you earn from each flight to upgrade your plane. You can invest in a more powerful Engine for better speed, a longer-lasting Boost for extended flight time, and a bigger Bonus multiplier to increase your earnings. As you save up, you can even purchase entirely new, sleeker planes that have higher potential for breaking your old records. Plan your spending wisely and keep flying to conquer every terrain!
