Crazy Plane Landing

How to Play Crazy Plane Landing

Welcome to Crazy Plane Landing, a 3D stunt flying game where the sky's the limit! Your goal is to launch your plane from a ramp, fly as far as you can, and stick a perfect landing on the runway. The crazier your flight—think barrel rolls and near-misses—the more coins you'll earn.

Use your earnings to upgrade your aircraft. Invest in a more powerful Engine for better launch speed, a longer Boost for extra thrust in the air, or a bigger Bonus multiplier to maximize your score. Each upgrade costs more than the last, so spend wisely to push your limits.

Once you've saved up enough, you can unlock entirely new planes. These new models start with basic stats but have a much higher potential for upgrades, allowing you to smash your old distance records. Master the art of boosting, rolling, and landing to become a true pro pilot. Play free right in your browser—no downloads required!
