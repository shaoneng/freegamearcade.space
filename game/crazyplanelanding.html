How to Play Crazy Plane Landing

Your adventure begins on a massive ramp. The goal is to launch your plane and fly as far as possible across various terrains. Click and hold to rev your engine, and release at the perfect moment for maximum launch power!

Once airborne, your job is to keep the plane stable and flying. Use your controls to steer left and right, and hit the boost for a burst of speed.
