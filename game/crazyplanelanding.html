Crazy Plane Landing

How to Play Crazy Plane Landing

The goal in Crazy Plane Landing is to launch your plane from a ramp and fly it as far as possible to reach the finish line. Your journey is all about maximizing distance, performing stunts, and, ultimately, attempting a successful landing.

As you fly, you'll earn coins for the distance covered and for any stunts you pull off, like barrel rolls. The further you go and the more stylish your flight, the bigger your payout. Use these coins in the hangar between flights to upgrade your plane. You can improve your Engine for more power, enhance your Boost for extra speed, and increase the Bonus multiplier to earn even more cash on your next attempt.

Upgrades become more expensive as you level them up, so spend your earnings wisely. Once you've saved up enough, you can purchase entirely new planes. These advanced aircraft have a higher potential for upgrades, allowing you to fly farther and faster than ever before, breaking all your previous records.
