Crazy Plane Landing

How to Play Crazy Plane Landing

Your main objective in Crazy Plane Landing is to launch your aircraft off a gigantic ramp and fly as far as you can, ultimately aiming to reach the runway at the end of the course.

To begin, click and hold to launch your plane. Once airborne, use your mouse or finger to steer the plane. Keep an eye on your boost meter and activate it for a powerful burst of speed to cover more distance.

You earn coins for every meter you fly and for performing stunts like barrel rolls. A successful landing, especially on the designated runway, will grant you a significant coin bonus. Crashing will end your run, but you still keep the coins you earned during the flight.

Use your earned coins between flights to purchase crucial upgrades. You can boost your Engine for more thrust, enhance your Boost for longer-lasting speed, or increase the Bonus multiplier to earn coins faster on future runs.

As you accumulate wealth, you can unlock entirely new and better planes. These advanced aircraft have higher potential for speed and distance, enabling you to shatter your previous records and conquer new terrains.
