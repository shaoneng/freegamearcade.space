Crazy Plane Landing

How to Play Crazy Plane Landing

The objective in Crazy Plane Landing is to launch your plane from a ramp and fly it as far as possible, ultimately aiming to land safely on the runway at the end of each level.

Click and hold to launch your plane. Once airborne, your primary goal is to manage your fuel and use your boost strategically to cover more distance. Performing stunts like barrel rolls not only looks cool but also adds to your final score and earnings.

After each flight, you'll earn coins based on your distance and stunts. Use these coins in the hangar to upgrade your plane. You can improve your Engine for more power, your Boost for longer bursts of speed, and your Bonus multiplier to increase your earnings on future flights. Save up to unlock entirely new, more advanced aircraft.

Continuously upgrade your plane and refine your flying technique to reach the finish line on increasingly challenging terrains. The key is to balance distance, stunts, and fuel management to maximize your score and progress through the game.
