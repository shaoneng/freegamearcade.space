Crazy Plane Landing

How to Play Crazy Plane Landing

Crazy Plane Landing is a thrilling 3D stunt flight game where your goal is to launch a plane from a ramp, fly as far as you can, and attempt a safe landing. The further you fly and the more stunts you pull off, like barrel rolls, the more coins you'll earn.

Use the coins you collect to upgrade your aircraft. You can invest in a more powerful Engine for better speed, a longer Boost for extra propulsion, or a bigger Bonus multiplier to increase your earnings. Each upgrade level costs more, so spend your coins wisely to push your limits.

As you save up, you can unlock entirely new, sleeker jets. These new planes have higher potential for upgrades, allowing you to shatter your old distance records and conquer different terrains. Keep chasing that finish line!

Enjoy this game for free, right in your web browser with no installation needed. Just click, fly, and try to land in one piece!
