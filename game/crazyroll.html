Crazy Roll

How to Play Crazy Roll

Welcome to Crazy Roll, a thrilling 3D arcade game that will challenge your reflexes! The objective is to guide your ball along a fast-paced, endless platform suspended in the air. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or A/D keys to steer the ball left and right, dodging the numerous obstacles that appear in your path.

Your run continues for as long as you can stay on the platform. If you collide with an obstacle or fall off the edge, the game is over. While racing down the track, be sure to collect the shiny diamonds scattered along the way. These diamonds are your currency to unlock a variety of awesome new ball designs in the shop, including a football, a golf ball, and even the planet Earth!

This game is all about achieving the highest score possible. Challenge yourself to beat your personal best with every run. The gameplay is simple to learn but offers an endless challenge, much like the popular game Slope. Good luck, and see how far you can roll!
