Crazy Roll

How to Play Crazy Roll

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Crazy Roll! Your objective is simple: guide your ball as far as possible down an endless, twisting platform suspended in the sky.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys (or A/D keys) to steer the ball left and right. Your reflexes will be put to the test as you navigate sharp turns and dodge a variety of obstacles that appear in your path. Crashing into an obstacle or falling off the platform will end your run.

As you speed along the course, keep an eye out for shimmering diamonds. Collect as many as you can! These diamonds can be used in the game's shop to purchase new and exciting ball designs, adding a fresh look to your gameplay. Challenge yourself to beat your high score and unlock every ball!
