Crazy Roll

How to Play Crazy Roll

The objective in Crazy Roll is to guide your bouncing ball down an endless, high-speed platform for as long as possible without falling off or crashing into obstacles. Your survival depends on your quick reflexes to steer the ball left and right, avoiding the various barriers that appear in your path.

As you speed down the track, keep an eye out for shimmering diamonds. Collecting these diamonds allows you to visit the in-game shop and purchase new, unique ball skins. You can unlock a variety of styles, including a beach ball, a football, and even a model of the Earth!

Your score is determined by the distance you travel, so focus on survival and making split-second decisions. This game is a true test of endurance and reaction time. Good luck, and try to set a new high score!
