Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Welcome to the intense battlefield of Crazy Shooters! Your goal is to outsmart and outgun every opponent to achieve the highest number of kills and dominate the match.

Getting Started: To begin, you can either create your own game room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can give it a unique name, set the maximum number of players, and choose your game mode: FFA (Free-For-All) or Team Deathmatch. You'll also select from four distinct maps: Factory, Arena, Towers, or Gas Plant.

Gameplay & Controls:

  • Movement: Use WASD or the Arrow Keys to navigate the map.
  • Jump/Run/Crouch: Press Spacebar to jump, hold Shift to run, and press C to crouch for a lower profile.
  • Combat: Aim with your Right Mouse Button and fire with the Left Mouse Button. Press R to reload your weapon when you're out of ammo.
  • Weapons: Cycle through your arsenal using the number keys 1-9. Choose the right tool for the job, from a close-quarters knife to a long-range sniper rifle.
  • Communication: Press T to open the chat window and Enter to send messages to other players.

Keep an eye on your health and ammo, adapt to your surroundings, and trust your instincts to win!
