Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Crazy Shooters is a fast-paced, multiplayer first-person shooter where the goal is simple: eliminate your enemies and rack up the highest score. To succeed, you'll need to move quickly, aim accurately, and use your environment and vast arsenal to your advantage.

Before jumping into the action, you need to either create a game room or join one that's already active. When creating a room, you can give it a name, set the maximum number of players, and choose your game mode: Free-For-All (FFA), where it's every player for themselves, or Team Deathmatch.

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your character.
  • Left Mouse Button: Fire your weapon.
  • Right Mouse Button: Aim down sights.
  • R Key: Reload your weapon.
  • Space Bar: Jump.
  • Hold Shift: Sprint.
  • 1-9 Keys: Switch between your available weapons.
  • T Key: Open the in-game chat.
  • C Key: Crouch.

Master these controls as you battle across four distinct maps: Factory, Arena, Towers, and GasPlant. Keep an eye on your health and ammo, communicate with your team, and climb the leaderboard to prove you're the craziest shooter out there!
