Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Dive into the intense, fast-paced world of Crazy Shooters! The objective is simple: eliminate as many opponents as possible to achieve the highest score and dominate the leaderboard. You can either create your own game or join an existing one.

When creating a room, you can set the room name, the maximum number of players, and choose between two exciting game modes: Free-for-All (FFA) where it's every player for themselves, or Team Deathmatch where you cooperate with teammates to defeat the opposing squad. Compete across four distinct maps: Factory, Arena, Towers, and Gas Plant.

Use the following controls to navigate the battlefield and outgun your rivals:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Fire: Left Mouse Button
  • Aim: Right Mouse Button
  • Reload: R
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Run: Hold Shift
  • Change Weapon: 1-9 Keys
  • Crouch: C
  • Chat: T to open, Enter to send

Keep an eye on your health and ammo, adapt to your surroundings, and choose your weapons wisely to secure victory!
