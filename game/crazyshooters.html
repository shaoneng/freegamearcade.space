Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Welcome to the high-octane battlefield of Crazy Shooters! To get started, you'll need to either create your own game room or join one that's already active. When creating a room, you can set a name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: Free-For-All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch. Battle it out across four distinct maps: Factory, Arena, Towers, and Gas Plant.

The objective is simple: move fast, hunt down your enemies, and score the most kills to dominate the match and win. Arm yourself with a wide variety of weapons, including a Ballistic Knife, Deagle, MP5, RPG, and more, to gain an edge over your opponents. Keep an eye on the kill feed and your ammo count to stay on top of the action.

Master the controls to become a formidable force. Use WASD or the arrow keys to move around the map. Aim with your right mouse button and fire with the left mouse button. Press R to reload your weapon, Space to jump, and hold Shift to run. You can switch between your weapons using the 1-9 keys. To communicate, press T to open the chat, type your message, and hit Enter to send. Use C to crouch for a tactical advantage.
