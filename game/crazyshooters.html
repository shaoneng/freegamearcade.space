Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Welcome to the high-octane battlegrounds of Crazy Shooters! Your mission is to adapt, move fast, and eliminate every enemy in sight to achieve the highest score. Before you jump into the action, you'll need to either create your own game room—setting the name, max players, and game mode (Free-for-All or Team Deathmatch)—or join an existing room to battle against other players online.

Master the controls to navigate the battlefield and gain a competitive edge:

  • Movement: Use WASD or the Arrow Keys to move. Press Spacebar to jump, Hold Shift to run, and C to crouch.
  • Combat: Aim with your Right Mouse Button and fire with the Left Mouse Button. Press R to reload your weapon.
  • Weapons: Switch between your arsenal using the number keys 1-9.
  • Communication: Press T to open the chat window and Enter to send your message.

Choose your weapons wisely, from close-quarters knives to long-range sniper rifles and explosive RPGs. Keep an eye on your health and ammo, track the kill feed, and work with your team (or go solo) to dominate the match. Trust your instincts and prove you have what it takes to win!
