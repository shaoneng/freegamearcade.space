Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of Crazy Shooters! To begin, you'll need to either create a new game room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can set a name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: Free-For-All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch. Once you're in, it's time to gear up and get ready for a fast-paced firefight.

The objective is simple: outsmart and outgun your opponents to rack up the highest number of kills. Navigate through diverse maps, use cover to your advantage, and trust your instincts to survive. Keep an eye on your health and ammo, and don't hesitate to switch between your powerful weapons to adapt to any combat situation. Aim for precision, move swiftly, and dominate the arena!

Master the battlefield with these controls: Use WASD or the Arrow Keys to move. Aim with your mouse and use the Left Mouse Button to fire. Aim down sights with the Right Mouse Button. Press R to reload your weapon and Space to jump. To sprint, hold Shift. Cycle through your arsenal with the 1-9 keys. Use C to crouch for a lower profile. To communicate with your team and other players, press T to open the chat and Enter to send your message.
