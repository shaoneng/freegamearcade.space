Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of Crazy Shooters! Your goal is simple: adapt, move fast, and eliminate every enemy in sight to score the most kills and dominate the match. This is a fast-paced multiplayer FPS where your reflexes and strategy are put to the ultimate test.

To get started, you'll need to either create a game room or join one that's already active. When creating a room, you can set a custom name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: chaotic Free-For-All (FFA) or strategic Team Deathmatch. Battle it out across four distinct maps: Factory, Arena, Towers, and Gas Plant.

Controls

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Fire: Left Mouse Button
  • Aim: Right Mouse Button
  • Reload: R Key
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Run: Hold Shift
  • Change Weapon: 1-9 Keys
  • Crouch: C Key
  • Chat: T to open chat, Enter to send

Choose your weapons wisely, trust your instincts, and enjoy the intense action. Good luck, shooter!
