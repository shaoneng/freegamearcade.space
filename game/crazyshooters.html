Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

To begin your mission, you first need to join or create a game room. You can set the room name, the maximum number of players, and choose between game modes like Free-for-All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch. Once you're in, select one of the unique maps and prepare for battle!

Navigate the battlefield using the WASD or Arrow Keys. Use your mouse to look around and aim. Fire your weapon with the Left Mouse Button and aim down sights with the Right Mouse Button. When you run out of ammo, press the R key to reload.

Gain a tactical advantage by using advanced movements. Press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles and hold Shift to run. You can crouch for cover by pressing the C key. Switch between your weapons using the number keys 1-9. To communicate with your team or other players, press T to open the chat, type your message, and press Enter to send.
