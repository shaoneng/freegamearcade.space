Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Welcome to the high-octane world of Crazy Shooters! Your first step is to either create your own game room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can set the name, maximum number of players, and choose your game mode: Free-For-All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch. Select from four distinct maps—Factory, Arena, Towers, or Gas Plant—and jump into the fray. The objective is simple: move fast, hunt your opponents, and rack up the highest number of kills to dominate the match.

Master the controls to outmaneuver your enemies. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to move around the map. Aim with your mouse and fire with the Left Mouse Button. Use the Right Mouse Button to aim down sights for better accuracy. Press R to reload your weapon, Space to jump, and hold Shift to run. You can switch between your weapons using the number keys 1-9. Crouch with C, and communicate with your team by pressing T to open the chat and Enter to send your message.
