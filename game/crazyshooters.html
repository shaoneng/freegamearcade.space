Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Welcome to the battlefield! In Crazy Shooters, your goal is to dominate the arena by achieving the highest number of kills. To begin, you can either create your own private game room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can set a name, a maximum player count, and choose between Free-For-All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch modes. Select one of the four diverse maps—Factory, Arena, Towers, or Gas Plant—and get ready for intense combat.

Master the controls to outmaneuver your opponents. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to move around the map. Aim your weapon with the Right Mouse Button and fire with the Left Mouse Button. You can jump with the Spacebar, sprint by holding Shift, and crouch with the C key. Don't forget to reload with R and switch between your arsenal of weapons using the 1-9 keys. If you need to communicate with your team or other players, press T to open the chat and Enter to send your message.
