Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of Crazy Shooters! To start, you can either create your own game room or join one that's already in progress. When creating a room, you can set a custom name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: Free-For-All (FFA) or Team deathmatch. Battle it out across diverse maps like Factory, Arena, Towers, and GasPlant.

Master the controls to dominate the arena. Use WASD or the arrow keys to move your character. Aim with the right mouse button and fire your weapon with the left mouse button. Press R to reload your weapon, Space to jump, and hold Shift to run. You can crouch by pressing C and switch between your weapons using the number keys 1-9. Communicate with your team and other players by pressing T to open the chat.

The objective is simple: move fast, hunt down your opponents, and score the most kills. A wide variety of weapons are at your disposal to help you complete your mission. Keep an eye on your health and ammo, track the leading players, and trust your skills to climb the leaderboard and achieve victory.
