Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Welcome to the high-octane battlegrounds of Crazy Shooters! To start, you'll need to either create a new game room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can set a name, choose the maximum number of players, and select a game mode: Free-For-All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch. You'll battle across four distinct maps: Factory, Arena, Towers, and GasPlant, each demanding different strategies.

Mastering the controls is key to survival. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to move around the map. Aim with your Right Mouse Button and fire with the Left Mouse Button. Press R to reload your weapon, Spacebar to jump over obstacles, and hold Shift to sprint. You can crouch by pressing C and communicate with your team or other players using the chat (press T to open).

Your objective is simple: eliminate your opponents and rack up the highest score. Cycle through a massive arsenal of weapons using the number keys (1-9), from sidearms like the Deagle to powerful explosives like the RPG. Keep an eye on your health and ammo in the bottom-right corner, track the kill feed at the top-right, and stay aware of the match timer. Adapt, move fast, and shoot straight to claim victory!
