Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Dive into the thrilling world of Crazy Shooters! Your goal is to out-shoot your opponents and get the highest score. To start, you can either create your own game room or join one created by another player. When creating a room, you can set a name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your game mode: Free-For-All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch. Battle across four unique maps: Factory, Arena, Towers, and Gas Plant.

Master the controls to dominate the arena. Use WASD or arrow keys to move around the map. Aim with your mouse and fire with the Left mouse button. Use the Right mouse button to aim down sights for better accuracy. Reload your weapon with the R key and jump over obstacles with the Space bar. To move faster, hold Shift to run. Crouch for cover using the C key.

Your arsenal is key to victory. Switch between your weapons using the 1-9 number keys. The game features a variety of weapons, including pistols, SMGs, shotguns, sniper rifles, and even an RPG. Communicate with your team or taunt your enemies by pressing T to open the chat, typing your message, and hitting Enter to send.
