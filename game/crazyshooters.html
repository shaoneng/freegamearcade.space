Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Welcome to Crazy Shooters, a fast-paced multiplayer first-person shooter where your goal is to outgun the competition and rack up the highest number of kills. Adapt to your surroundings, move quickly, and hunt down every enemy in sight to prove you're the ultimate shooter.

To begin, you'll need to either create a game room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can give it a custom name, set the maximum number of players, and choose your game mode: Free-for-All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch. Battle it out across four distinct maps: Factory, Arena, Towers, and Gas Plant.

Master the controls to dominate the battlefield. Use WASD or the arrow keys to move. Aim with the right mouse button and fire with the left mouse button. Press R to reload your weapon and Space to jump. Hold Shift to run, C to crouch, and use the number keys 1-9 to switch between your weapons. You can communicate with other players by pressing T to open the chat.
