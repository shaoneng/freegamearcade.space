Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Welcome to Crazy Shooters, an intense and fast-paced first-person shooter where your reflexes and strategy are put to the test. The goal is simple: eliminate your enemies, rack up the highest score, and prove you're the best in a free-for-all frenzy or lead your team to victory.

To begin, you can either create your own game room or join one that's already active. When creating a room, you can set a name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: FFA (Free-For-All) or Team Deathmatch. Battle it out across four distinct maps: Factory, Arena, Towers, and Gas Plant.

Master the battlefield with these intuitive controls:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Fire Weapon: Left Mouse Button
  • Aim Down Sights: Right Mouse Button
  • Reload: R Key
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Run/Sprint: Hold Shift Key
  • Crouch: C Key
  • Change Weapon: 1-9 Keys
  • Chat: T to open, Enter to send

Success depends on quick movement and sharp shooting. Use the environment for cover, adapt to every situation, and choose your weapons wisely from a vast arsenal that includes knives, pistols, rifles, shotguns, and even RPGs. Keep an eye on your health and ammo, and trust your skills to win!
