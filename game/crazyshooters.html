Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Jump into the fast-paced arena of Crazy Shooters and prove your mettle against players from around the world. The objective is simple: get the most kills and lead your team to victory, or dominate in a free-for-all frenzy. Before you start, you can create your own private room or join an existing match to get right into the action.

Master the controls to outmaneuver your opponents. Use the WASD or arrow keys to move around the map. Left-click to fire your weapon and right-click to aim down sights for better accuracy. Reload your weapon with the R key, jump over obstacles with the Spacebar, and sprint by holding Shift. Crouch for a smaller target with the C key.

Switch between your arsenal of weapons using the number keys 1-9. A wide variety of guns, from pistols and SMGs to sniper rifles and RPGs, is at your disposal. Communicate with your team or taunt your enemies by pressing T to open the chat, typing your message, and hitting Enter to send. Keep an eye on the kill feed and your stats to track your performance!
