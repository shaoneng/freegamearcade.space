Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Dive into the intense, fast-paced world of Crazy Shooters, a multiplayer first-person shooter that will test your reflexes and tactical skills. The goal is simple: adapt to your surroundings, hunt down your enemies, and secure the most kills to dominate the match. Choose your weapons wisely to gain an edge and climb the leaderboard.

To start, you can either create your own game room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can set a custom name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: Free-For-All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch. Battle across four distinct maps: Factory, Arena, Towers, and Gas Plant, each offering unique tactical challenges.

Controls:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Fire: Left Mouse Button
  • Aim: Right Mouse Button
  • Reload: R Key
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Run: Hold Shift
  • Change Weapon: 1-9 Keys
  • Crouch: C Key
  • Chat: T to open, Enter to send
