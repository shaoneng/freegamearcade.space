Crazy Shooters

How to Play Crazy Shooters

Welcome to the chaotic battlefield of Crazy Shooters! Your mission is simple: adapt, move fast, and eliminate every opponent in sight. Before the action begins, you'll need to either create your own custom game room—setting the map, player limit, and game mode (FFA or Team)—or jump straight into a match by joining an existing room.

Master the controls to dominate the arena. Use the WASD or arrow keys to move around the map. Hold Shift to sprint and press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles. Use your mouse to aim and click the left mouse button to fire your weapon. For more precise shots, use the right mouse button to aim down sights. Press R to reload your weapon when you're running low on ammo.

Switch between your impressive arsenal of weapons using the number keys 1-9. To communicate with other players, press T to open the chat window, type your message, and hit Enter to send it. You can also press C to crouch, making you a smaller target for your enemies. Keep an eye on your health, ammo, and the kill feed to stay on top of the action!
