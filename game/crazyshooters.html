How to Play Crazy Shooters

Welcome to the intense arena of Crazy Shooters! Your mission is to out-shoot and out-maneuver your opponents to score the most kills. Before you start, you can create a private game roomâ€”naming it, setting the player limit, and choosing between Free-For-All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch modes. Alternatively, you can join a public room to jump straight into the action.

Master the controls to dominate the battlefield. Use WASD or the arrow keys to move around the map. Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to fire your weapon. Aim down sights for better accuracy with the right mouse button. Reload your weapon with R and jump over obstacles with the Spacebar. To move faster, hold Shift to run. You can crouch with C to make yourself a smaller target.

Cycle through your arsenal of weapons using the number keys 1-9. Keep an eye on the kill feed and game stats to track your progress. If you're playing in a team or just want to chat, press T to open the chat box and Enter to send your message.
