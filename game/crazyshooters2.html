How to Play Crazy Shooters 2

Welcome to the chaotic arena of Crazy Shooters 2! Your goal is to out-shoot and outmaneuver your opponents in a fast-paced, first-person shooter experience. Join a room or create your own and prepare for intense multiplayer combat across various maps.

Use the WASD keys to move around the environment and the mouse to aim your weapon. Click the left mouse button to fire and the right mouse button to aim down your sights for increased accuracy. Jump over obstacles with the Spacebar and sprint with the Shift key to cover ground quickly.

You have access to a wide variety of weapons. Press the number keys (1, 2, 3...) to switch between them. Choose the right tool for the job, whether it's a long-range rifle or a close-quarters shotgun.
