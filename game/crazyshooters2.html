How to Play Crazy Shooters 2

Welcome to the battlefield of Crazy Shooters 2! Your primary mission is to outgun and outmaneuver your opponents in fast-paced first-person combat. Join a room or create your own to jump straight into the action against other players online.

Master the classic FPS controls: use the WASD keys to move around the map, and use your mouse to aim and fire your weapon. The Spacebar allows you to jump over obstacles, while the Shift key lets you sprint for a burst of speed. Reload your weapon with the R key to ensure you're always ready for a firefight.

Before spawning, you can select from a variety of powerful weapons. Choose the right tool for the job, whether it's a sniper rifle for long-range engagements or a shotgun for close-quarters combat. Use the environment to your advantage, find cover, and work with your team (in team modes) to dominate the match and top the leaderboard!
