How to Play Crazy Shooters 2

Welcome to the fast-paced battlefield of Crazy Shooters 2! The objective is simple: join a room, choose your team, and eliminate as many enemy players as you can before the timer runs out. Work with your teammates to control the map and outgun the opposition.

Master the controls to navigate the battlefield and engage your foes:

  • WASD Keys: Move your character forward, backward, and side-to-side.
  • Mouse: Aim your weapon.
  • Left Mouse Button: Fire your weapon.
  • Right Mouse Button: Aim down sights for better accuracy.
  • Spacebar: Jump over obstacles.
  • R Key: Reload your weapon.
  • C Key: Crouch to take cover or steady your aim.
  • Number Keys (1-9): Switch between your available weapons.

Success in Crazy Shooters 2 requires more than just a quick trigger finger. Use the environment for cover, strategically choose your weapons for different combat scenarios, and always be aware of your surroundings to survive and climb the leaderboard.
