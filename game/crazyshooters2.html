How to Play Crazy Shooters 2

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of Crazy Shooters 2! Your primary objective is to out-shoot and out-survive your opponents in fast-paced, first-person combat. Join a room, choose your team, and get ready to fight across a variety of detailed environments.

The controls are intuitive for any FPS fan:

  • Move: Use the W, A, S, and D keys to navigate the map.
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar to leap over obstacles.
  • Aim and Shoot: Use your mouse to aim and click the left mouse button to fire your weapon.
  • Reload: Press the R key to reload your current weapon.
  • Switch Weapons: Use the number keys (1, 2, 3...) or the mouse wheel to cycle through your arsenal.
  • Crouch: Press the C key to take a lower stance.

Master your movement, learn the maps, and select the right weapon for the situation to dominate the leaderboard and lead your team to a decisive victory!
