How to Play Crazy Shooters 2

Welcome to the battlefield of Crazy Shooters 2! The objective is simple: eliminate your opponents and be the last one standing, or help your team achieve victory. Navigate the diverse maps using your strategic skills to find cover and ambush your enemies.

Use the standard FPS controls to move and fight. Use the WASD keys to move your character around the map. Aim your weapon with the Mouse and click the Left Mouse Button to shoot. Press the R key to reload your weapon, and jump over obstacles with the Spacebar. Switch between your weapons to adapt to different combat situations.

Join an existing room or create your own to start the chaos. Work with your teammates, communicate effectively, and use the environment to your advantage. The more enemies you defeat, the higher your score will be. Gear up, soldier, and show everyone your shooting prowess!
