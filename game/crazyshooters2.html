How to Play Crazy Shooters 2

Dive into the chaotic and thrilling world of Crazy Shooters 2! Your primary goal is to survive and eliminate your opponents in fast-paced, first-person combat. Join a room, choose your team, and get ready to fight across various detailed maps. Success requires not just quick reflexes, but also smart strategy and effective use of the environment.

Master the controls to dominate the battlefield: Use the W, A, S, D keys to move, and your mouse to aim and look around. Fire your weapon with the left mouse button and aim down sights with the right mouse button. Jump with the Spacebar, and reload your weapon by pressing R. You can switch between your weapons using the number keys or the mouse wheel. Work with your team, find the best weapons, and fight to be the top player!
