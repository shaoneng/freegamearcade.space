Crazy Strike Force

How to Play Crazy Strike Force

Get started by either joining an existing game room or creating your own. When you create a room, you can set the room name, maximum number of players, game mode, round time, number of bots, and choose from various maps to battle on.

Once in the game, your objective is to eliminate all opponents using a wide array of weapons. Arm yourself with everything from pistols like the Magnum to assault rifles like the M4A1 and sniper rifles like the AWP. Master your aim and movement to dominate the match.

Customize your soldier to fit your playstyle. Choose from over 20 different armors and enhance your abilities with special skills like Quickdraw ammo for faster reloads or Silence Walker for stealthy movement. Find the combination that gives you the edge in combat.

Track your progress in the profile section. Here you can monitor your K/D ratio, total wins, headshots, and more. You can also change your nickname and manage your load code to save and load your customized setups.
