Crazy Strike Force

How to Play Crazy Strike Force

To begin, you can either join a game room created by other players or create your own. When creating a room, you can set a name, the maximum number of players, the game mode, round time, and even add bots to the match to sharpen your skills.

Before heading into battle, visit your profile to customize your character. Choose from over 20 different armor sets and select your loadout from a vast arsenal of weapons, including pistols like the Magnum, assault rifles like the M4A1 and SC-AR, shotguns, sniper rifles, and even a crossbow. Each weapon offers a unique playstyle, so find what works best for you!

Unlock and equip additional skills like Quickdraw Ammo, Samurai, or UAV Ghost to gain a tactical advantage over your enemies. Your profile tracks all your important stats, including your K/D ratio, total wins, headshots, and more, so you can monitor your progress and become a legendary soldier. You can also earn gold and diamonds by watching rewarded ads to unlock more gear and customizations.
