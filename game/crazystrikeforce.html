Crazy Strike Force

How to Play Crazy Strike Force

To begin your battle, you can either join an existing game room created by other players or create your own. When creating a room, you can set a name, a maximum player count, the game mode, round time, and even add bots to the fight.

Once in the game, your objective is to eliminate opponents using a wide variety of weapons. Master your movement and aim to take down enemies. Develop your own strategy to outsmart the opposition and lead your team to victory.

Personalize your soldier with over 20 different armors. Choose your loadout from a vast selection of firearms, including pistols like the Magnum, SMGs like the MP5K, assault rifles like the M4A1, and powerful snipers like the AWP. You can also equip special skills such as UAV Ghost and Silence Walker to gain a tactical advantage.

Visit the profile section to change your nickname and track your combat statistics, including your K/D ratio, total wins, headshots, and more. You can earn gold and diamonds through gameplay and by watching rewarded ads to unlock new gear and loot boxes.
