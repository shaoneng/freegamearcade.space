Crazy Strike Force

How to Play Crazy Strike Force

Your goal in Crazy Strike Force is to dominate the battlefield by eliminating opponents in fast-paced first-person shooter combat. Before you jump into a match, customize your character's style and loadout. Choose from a massive arsenal of over 20 weapons, including fan-favorites like the MP5K, M4A1, AWP, and even a Crossbow. Equip powerful skills like Quickdraw for faster weapon switching or UAV Ghost to stay off enemy radar.

To start playing, you can either join an existing room created by another player or create your own custom game. When creating a room, you can set the room name, maximum number of players, game mode, round time, and even add bots to fill out the teams. Choose your favorite map and get ready for the action to begin!

Keep track of your combat performance in the Profile section. Here you can monitor your K/D ratio, total wins, headshots, and more. You can also change your nickname and manage your loadout codes. Earn in-game gold and diamonds by completing matches and watching rewarded ads to unlock new gear and loot boxes.
