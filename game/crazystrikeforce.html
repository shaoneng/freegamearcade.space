Crazy Strike Force

How to Play Crazy Strike Force

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of Crazy Strike Force! Your objective is to eliminate opponents and dominate the match. Begin by customizing your character. Head to the profile section to choose your nickname and explore a vast arsenal of over 20 armors and weapons, including classics like the MP5K, M870, and AWP.

Before each battle, you can equip special skills like Quickdraw Ammo for faster reloads or UAV Ghost to stay hidden from enemy radar. These skills can give you a significant advantage in combat. Once you're geared up, you can either join an existing game room or create your own. When creating a room, you can set the game mode, round time, map, and even add bots to practice against.

In-game, use your mouse to aim and your left-click to shoot. Use standard FPS keyboard controls (WASD) to move around the map. Work with your team, use cover effectively, and aim for headshots to take down enemies quickly. Track your performance, including your K/D ratio and total wins, in your profile to see how you stack up!
