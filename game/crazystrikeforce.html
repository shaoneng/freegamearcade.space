Crazy Strike Force

How to Play Crazy Strike Force

Get ready for intense combat! To begin, you can either join an existing game room created by other players or create your own. When creating a room, you can set the room name, max players, game mode, round time, number of bots, and choose your favorite map.

The objective is simple: take down all your opponents. Arm yourself with a wide selection of weapons, including pistols like the Magnum, SMGs like the MP5K, shotguns like the M870, and powerful rifles like the M4A1 and AWP. Don't hesitate to develop your own unique strategy to dominate the battlefield.

Customize your soldier with over 20 different armors and equip special skills to get an edge. Use abilities like Quickdraw Ammo, Silence Walker, Samurai, and UAV Ghost to increase damage, move stealthily, and protect yourself. You can even choose custom kill messages to display after defeating an enemy.

Head to the profile section to change your nickname and manage your progress using a load code. Here, you can also track your combat history, including your K/D rate, total games played, scores, wins, headshots, and more.

You can earn in-game currency by watching rewarded ads, which grant you gold and diamonds. Use these to open loot boxes for a chance to get even more diamonds and unlock powerful gear.
