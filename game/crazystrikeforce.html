Crazy Strike Force

About Crazy Strike Force

Join the ultimate battle competition in Crazy Strike Force! Customize your character with a unique style and gear up to resist waves of opponents. With a vast selection of weapons, you have the power to take down anyone who stands in your way. Develop your own strategy and dominate the arena.

Choose from over 20 different armor types and an impressive arsenal of weapons, including the Magnum, MP5K, M870, SC-AR, M4A1, AWP, and even a Crossbow. Boost your abilities with additional skills like Quickdraw ammo, Silence Walker, and UAV Ghost to gain an edge over your rivals. You can even select custom kill messages after you defeat an enemy.

Head to the profile section to track your gaming history, including your K/D rate, games played, scores, wins, headshots, and knife kills. Here, you can also change your nickname and manage your game progress by saving and loading unique codes.

You can jump into the action by joining a room created by another player or host your own. When creating a room, you can customize the name, maximum players, game mode, round time, bot count, and select your favorite map.

Please be aware: This game features content intended for a mature audience, including action-packed scenes, violence, or strong language. If you're sensitive to such material, we recommend checking out our other free online games.
