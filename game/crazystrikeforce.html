Crazy Strike Force

How to Play Crazy Strike Force

Welcome to the battlefield! To begin your fight in Crazy Strike Force, you can either jump into an existing game by joining a room or create your own custom match. When creating a room, you can set the name, maximum number of players, game mode, round duration, bot count, and choose from various maps to battle on.

Once in the game, your objective is simple: eliminate the opposition. Use your tactical skills and superior firepower to take down every enemy you see. Formulate your own strategy, whether it's an all-out assault or a more calculated, stealthy approach to dominate the match.

Before you deploy, head to your profile to customize your loadout. Choose from a massive arsenal of over 20 weapons, including classics like the MP5K, M4A1, AWP, and even a Crossbow. Equip special skills such as Quickdraw ammo for faster reloads, Silence Walker for stealth, or UAV Ghost to stay off enemy radar. You can even select unique kill messages to taunt your defeated foes.

Keep track of your progress in the profile section. Here you can monitor your K/D ratio, total games played, wins, headshots, and more. You can also save your progress with a load code and earn in-game currency like gold and diamonds to unlock more gear and loot boxes.
