Crazy Strike Force

How to Play Crazy Strike Force

Get ready for the ultimate first-person shooter competition! In Crazy Strike Force, your main goal is to out-gun and out-maneuver your opponents to dominate the leaderboard. You can jump into the action by joining an existing game room or by creating your own. When you create a room, you're in control: set the room name, maximum players, game mode, round time, number of bots, and choose from various maps.

Once in the game, it's all about combat. Arm yourself with a massive selection of over 20 weapons, from pistols like the Magnum to assault rifles like the M4A1 and sniper rifles like the AWP. Develop your own strategy, find the best vantage points, and take down every enemy in your sights. Don't hesitate to shoot, as every takedown gets you closer to victory.

Customize your soldier to match your playstyle. Equip different armors and unlock special skills like Quickdraw Ammo, Silence Walker, and UAV Ghost to gain an edge in battle. You can even choose custom kill messages to display after you've eliminated an opponent. Head to the profile section to change your nickname and track your combat statistics, including your K/D ratio, total wins, headshots, and more.
