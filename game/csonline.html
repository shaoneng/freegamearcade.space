CS Online

How to Play CS Online

Welcome to CS Online, the web-based tribute to the legendary tactical shooter, Counter-Strike. The objective is simple: join a team of either terrorists or counter-terrorists and work together to eliminate the opposing force or complete map-specific objectives.

To get started, simply create a profile and hit the "start" button. You'll see a list of available game rooms, showing the current map, game mode, and number of players. You can either jump into an existing match that looks interesting or create your very own room to play with friends. No downloads are requiredâ€”it all happens right in your browser!

Once in the game, use your keyboard and mouse to move, aim, and shoot. You can switch between your available weapons by pressing the number keys from 1 to 9. Teamwork and strategy are key to victory, so communicate with your teammates and choose your engagements wisely to lead your side to victory.
