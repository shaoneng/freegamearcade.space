CS Online

How to Play CS Online

Welcome to the intense world of CS Online! This game pits two teams against each other: Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists. Your objective is to work with your team to eliminate the opposing force and complete map-specific goals.

To get started, simply press the "Start" button from the main menu. You'll see a list of available game rooms. This list shows you the map, the game mode, and the number of players in each room. You can either join an existing room to jump right into the action or create your own room to play with friends or set your own rules.

Once in the game, use your standard FPS controls (WASD to move, mouse to aim and shoot). You can switch between your available weapons by pressing the number keys (1-9). Communicate with your team, watch your corners, and lead your side to victory!
