CS Online

How to Play CS Online

Getting into the action in CS Online is straightforward. To begin, you can create a unique player profile. Once you're ready, hit the "Start" button from the main menu. This will display a list of available game rooms. You can see the map, game mode, and number of players for each room. Simply choose a room that looks good and jump right in, or create your own private room to play with friends!

The core gameplay is a classic battle between two teams: Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists. Your objective is simple: work with your teammates to eliminate the opposing force. Strategy, quick reflexes, and teamwork are essential for victory. Communicate with your team and use the map to your advantage to outsmart your enemies.

Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot. Navigate the map with the standard WASD keys. You have a full arsenal at your disposal! Switch between your primary weapon, secondary pistol, knife, and grenades by pressing the number keys (1-9) on your keyboard. Choose the right tool for the right situation to dominate the battlefield.
