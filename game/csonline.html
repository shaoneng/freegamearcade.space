CS Online

How to Play CS Online

Getting into the action in CS Online is straightforward. Since this is a multiplayer game, you can start by creating a profile. Once that's set up, press the "start" button on the main menu. This will bring up a list of available game rooms. You can see details for each room, like the map being played and the current game mode. Simply choose a room that looks good to you and jump right in, or feel free to create your own custom room to play with friends!

Once you're in the game, the objective is classic tactical combat: work with your team to eliminate the opposing force. Navigate the map using your standard movement controls (WASD). To switch between your weapons, use the number keys from 1 to 9 on your keyboard. Each number corresponds to a different weapon in your arsenal, from pistols to rifles. Choose the right tool for the job, aim with your mouse, and lead your team to victory!
