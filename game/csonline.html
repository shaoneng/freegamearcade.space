CS Online

How to Play CS Online

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of CS Online! This game is a classic team-based first-person shooter where you will join either the terrorist or counter-terrorist team. Your objective is simple: work with your teammates to eliminate the opposing force.

To begin, simply press the "Start" button from the main menu. You'll see a list of available game rooms, showing the map and game mode currently being played. Choose a room that looks interesting and jump right into the action, or feel free to create your own custom room to play with friends.

Once in the game, use standard FPS controls: WASD keys to move, and your mouse to aim and shoot. You can switch between your available weapons by pressing the number keys from 1 to 9. Good luck, soldier!
