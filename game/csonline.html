CS Online

How to Play CS Online

Dive into the fast-paced world of CS Online, a tactical shooter where you can join the fight as either a terrorist or a counter-terrorist. The objective is simple: eliminate the opposing team using strategy, teamwork, and sharp shooting skills.

To get started, simply click the "Start" button on the main menu. You'll be presented with a list of available game rooms. You can see the map, game mode, and number of players in each room. Choose one that looks good and jump right into the action, or take control by creating your very own room with your preferred settings.

Once in the game, the controls are intuitive for any FPS fan:
- Move: Use the WASD keys.
- Aim: Move your mouse.
- Shoot: Click the left mouse button.
- Reload: Press the R key.
- Jump: Press the Spacebar.
- Switch Weapons: Use the number keys (1-9) to select from your arsenal.

Work with your teammates, learn the maps, and master your weapons to lead your side to victory!
