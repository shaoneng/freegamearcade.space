CS Online

How to Play CS Online

Getting into the action in CS Online is straightforward. First, you'll want to create a player profile to track your progress. Once you're set up, press the "Start" button from the main menu. This will display a list of available game rooms.

From the server list, you can see the map, current game mode, and number of players in each room. Simply click on a room that looks interesting to join the battle, or choose to create your own custom room to play with friends.

Once in the game, you'll join either the Terrorist or Counter-Terrorist team. Use your mouse to aim and the left-click button to shoot. Navigate the map with the WASD keys. You can switch between your available weapons by pressing the number keys from 1 to 9. Work with your team to eliminate all opponents and secure victory!
