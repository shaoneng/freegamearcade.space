CS Online

How to Play CS Online

CS Online brings the classic tactical shooter experience directly to your web browser. Getting started is simple and quick!

To begin, you can create a simple profile to track your progress. From the main menu, click 'Start' to see a list of available game rooms. This list will show you the map, current game mode, and the number of players in each room. Simply click on a room that looks interesting to join the action.

If you prefer, you can also create your own room. This allows you to choose the map and game mode, and invite your friends to play with you. Once in the game, the objective is to work with your team (either Terrorists or Counter-Terrorists) to eliminate the opposing force. Use your mouse to aim and shoot, and the keyboard to move. You can switch between your available weapons by pressing the number keys from 1 to 9. Good luck, soldier!
