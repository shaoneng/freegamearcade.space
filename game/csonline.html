CS Online

How to Play CS Online

CS Online brings the classic tactical shooter experience directly to your web browser. The objective is simple: join a team of either terrorists or counter-terrorists and work together to eliminate the opposing force or complete map-specific objectives.

To begin, you can create a profile and then find a game to join. From the main menu, press the "Start" button to see a list of available game rooms. This list will show you the map and game mode for each match. Simply choose a room that looks interesting and jump right into the action, or create your own room to play with friends.

Once in the game, you can switch between your weapons by pressing the number keys (1-9) on your keyboard. Standard FPS controls apply: use your mouse to aim and shoot, and the WASD keys to move around the map. Communication and teamwork are key to victory, so stay close to your squad and cover your angles!
