Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to Cube Worlds, your personal sandbox for unlimited creativity! If you love the idea of building magnificent structures without any pressure, this is the game for you. It's a chill, relaxing experience where your imagination is the only limit.

Getting started is easy. Use the classic WASD keys to walk around and the Spacebar to jump. Find the perfect spot in the vast, procedurally generated world to begin your masterpiece.

To access your building materials, press the B key to open your inventory. You'll find over 250 different blocks, from grass and stone to lava and glass. Select your desired blocks and assign them to your hotbar for quick access. Once you're ready, simply right-click to place a block and left-click to destroy one.

Take your building to new heights—literally! Press the Z key to activate flying mode, giving you a bird's-eye view of your creations. For even more freedom, press the X key for NoClip mode, allowing you to pass through your own structures to work on intricate interiors. Need to move faster? Hold Shift to sprint. For more precise placement while flying, hold Ctrl to slow down.

Don't lose your way! You can set a spawn point by pressing the Return key. If you ever wander too far, just press the R key to teleport back to your set location.

If you enjoy Cube Worlds, you might also like similar crafting games. Paper Minecraft offers a 2D take on the genre, while Minecraft Classic Online provides a dose of pure nostalgia. For a more social and action-oriented experience, check out MiniBlox with its multiplayer modes.
