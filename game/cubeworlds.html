Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to your own personal sandbox! Getting started in Cube Worlds is simple. Use the WASD keys to walk around and the Spacebar to jump. Explore the vast, open world and find the perfect spot for your first creation.

To access your building materials, press the B key to open your inventory. Here you'll find over 250 different blocks to choose from. Scroll through the list, select your favorites, and assign them to your hotbar at the bottom of the screen. Once you've selected a block, use your mouse to build: Right-click to place a block and Left-click to destroy one.

Ready to see your world from a new perspective? Press the Z key to activate flying mode, allowing you to soar through the air and get a bird's-eye view of your creations. For even more freedom, press X to enable NoClip mode, which lets you pass through solid objects. Need to move faster? Hold Shift to sprint or fly faster. For precision building, hold Ctrl to slow down your movement.

If you've found a place you want to return to, press Return to set a new spawn point. If you ever get lost, simply press the R key to instantly teleport back to your designated spawn location.
