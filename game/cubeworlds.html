Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Start your creative journey in a vast blocky world. To begin, press the B key to open your inventory. Here you can browse through over 250 different block types. Select your desired blocks and assign them to your hotbar for quick access.

Once you've chosen your materials, find the perfect spot to start building. To place a block, use the Right Mouse Button. To destroy a block, use the Left Mouse Button. It's that simple to start shaping the world around you!

Navigate the world using the standard WASD keys for movement and the Spacebar to jump. If you build something amazing and want to return to it easily, press Return to set a spawn point. You can teleport back to this point anytime by pressing R.

Take to the skies by pressing Z to toggle flying mode. This gives you a bird's-eye view of your creations and helps you build tall structures. For even more freedom, press X to activate NoClip mode, allowing you to pass through solid objects. To fine-tune your movement while flying, hold Shift to go faster or Ctrl to slow down for precise placements.
