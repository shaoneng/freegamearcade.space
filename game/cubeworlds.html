Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to your very own blocky sandbox! Get started by pressing the 'B' key to open your inventory. Here you'll find over 250 different types of blocks. Scroll through the list, pick the materials you want to use, and assign them to your hotbar for quick access during building.

Building is simple and intuitive. Use your mouse to look around and find the perfect spot for your creation. Right-click to place a block from your selected hotbar slot, and left-click to destroy a block. That's all you need to know to start shaping the world around you!

Move around the world using the standard WASD keys. Use the Spacebar to jump over obstacles. If you find a location you want to return to easily, press the 'Return' key to set a spawn point. You can teleport back to this point anytime by pressing the 'R' key.

For ultimate freedom, activate flying mode by pressing the 'Z' key. This allows you to soar through the air and get a bird's-eye view of your projects. For even more mobility, press 'X' to enable NoClip mode, which lets you pass through solid blocks. Hold 'Shift' to move faster or 'Ctrl' to move more slowly for precise placement.
