Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Get ready to build! In Cube Worlds, you start in an expansive blocky world with infinite possibilities. Your main goal is to unleash your creativity and construct anything you can imagine.

To begin, press B to open your inventory. Here, you'll find over 250 different types of blocks. Scroll through the options, select your favorites, and assign them to your hotbar for quick access. Once you have your desired blocks, find a suitable spot in the world.

Building is straightforward: use Right-click to place blocks and Left-click to destroy them. Experiment with different block types to create structures, landscapes, and more.

Movement is controlled with WASD. Press Spacebar to jump over obstacles. If you venture far from your base, press Return to set a spawn point, and then R to teleport back to it later.

For greater freedom, press Z to activate flying mode, allowing you to soar above your creations and explore. For ultimate flexibility, X toggles NoClip mode, letting you pass through blocks. Use Ctrl to slow down for precise movements while flying, and Shift to speed up.
