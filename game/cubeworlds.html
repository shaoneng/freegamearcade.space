Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to Cube Worlds, a vast sandbox universe where your creativity is the only limit! Forget about survival pressure and monsters; this game is all about the pure joy of building. It’s the perfect chill experience for anyone who loves to create.

Getting started is simple. Use the WASD keys to move around and the Spacebar to jump. To begin building, press the B key to open your inventory. You'll find over 250 different blocks to choose from! Assign your favorite blocks to your hotbar, then close the inventory.

Once you've found the perfect spot, it's time to build. Right-click to place a block and left-click to destroy one. That’s all there is to it! You can create anything from towering castles to secret underground bases. If you want to save your location, press Return to set a spawn point, and use the R key to teleport back to it anytime.

Ready to see your creation from a new perspective? Press the Z key to activate flying mode. Soar through the air and get a bird's-eye view of your world. While flying, hold Shift to get a speed boost or hold Ctrl to slow down for precise placements. For ultimate freedom, press the X key to enable NoClip mode, allowing you to pass through blocks and build inside mountains with ease.
