Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to Cube Worlds, a pure sandbox experience where your creativity is the only limit! If you love the freedom of Minecraft's creative mode, you'll feel right at home. There are no monsters or survival elements—just endless terrain and a massive library of blocks for you to build whatever you can imagine.

You start in a vast, blocky world. To begin your masterpiece, press B to open your inventory. You'll find over 250 different blocks, from grass and stone to lava, glass, and flowers. Select your desired blocks and assign them to your hotbar for quick access.

Once you have your materials, find the perfect spot to start building. The controls are simple: use your Right Mouse Button to place a block and the Left Mouse Button to destroy one. That's all you need to start shaping the world!

Navigating your world is intuitive. Use WASD to walk around and the Spacebar to jump. If you find a place you want to return to, press Return to set a spawn point, and you can teleport back anytime by pressing R.

For a better view of your creations, press Z to toggle flying mode. This allows you to soar through the air and get a bird's-eye view. For even more freedom, press X to enter NoClip mode, which lets you pass through solid blocks—perfect for exploring caves or building complex interiors. To get around faster, hold Shift to sprint. If you need to make precise adjustments, hold Ctrl to slow your movement.

If you enjoy the creative freedom of Cube Worlds, be sure to check out similar building games. Paper Minecraft offers a 2D take on the classic formula with fast loading and smooth gameplay. For a dose of nostalgia, Minecraft Classic Online lets you play the original version right in your browser. And if you're looking for a multiplayer experience with friends, MiniBlox offers various game modes like PvP, parkour, and collaborative building.
