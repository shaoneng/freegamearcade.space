Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to your own personal sandbox! Getting started is easy. Press the 'B' key to open your inventory, where you'll find over 250 different blocks. Scroll through the options and assign your favorites to the hotbar for quick access during building.

The core controls are simple and intuitive. To build, aim your cursor and right-click to place a block. To remove a block, just left-click it. Use the standard WASD keys to walk around and the Spacebar to jump. If you build something amazing, press 'Return' to set a new spawn point, and you can instantly get back to it by pressing 'R'.

Unleash your creativity with advanced movement. Press 'Z' to toggle flying mode, allowing you to soar above your world and build in hard-to-reach places. For even more freedom, press 'X' to activate NoClip mode, which lets you pass through blocks. Need to go fast? Hold 'Shift' to get a speed boost. For detailed work, hold 'Ctrl' to slow down your movement for precise block placement.
