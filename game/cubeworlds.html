Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to Cube Worlds, a wide-open sandbox where your creativity is the only limit! If you love the simple, pure building experience of classic Minecraft, you'll feel right at home. There are no enemies or survival elements—just you, a massive world, and over 250 different blocks to build whatever you can imagine.

Getting started is easy. Use WASD to walk around and the Spacebar to jump. To access your building materials, press B to open the inventory. Here you can browse through blocks like grass, stone, lava, and glass, and assign your favorites to your hotbar. Once you've picked your blocks, find a spot and get building! Use your mouse to look around, right-click to place a block, and left-click to destroy one.

Want a better view? Press Z to toggle flying mode, allowing you to soar over your creations and scout new building locations. For even more freedom, press X to enter NoClip mode, which lets you pass through solid objects. You can control your movement speed by holding Shift to go faster or Ctrl to slow down for precise placement. If you wander too far, press Return to set a spawn point, and hit R anytime to return to it.

If Cube Worlds ignites your passion for block-based building, you should also check out similar games. Paper Minecraft offers a 2D take on the genre with fast loading and smooth gameplay. For a dose of nostalgia, Minecraft Classic Online lets you play the original version right in your browser. And if you're looking for a more social experience, MiniBlox features multiplayer rooms with various game modes like PvP and parkour, where you can build and play with others.
