Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Getting started in Cube Worlds is all about your creativity! Press the 'B' key to open your block inventory, where you'll find over 250 different types of blocks. Scroll through the list, choose the materials for your project, and assign them to your hotbar for quick access.

The basic controls are simple and intuitive. Use the WASD keys to walk around the world and the Spacebar to jump. To build, aim your cursor and Right-click to place a block. To remove a block, simply Left-click on it. Find a nice spot and start shaping the world to your liking!

For a better view and more creative freedom, you can take to the skies! Press 'Z' to toggle flying mode, allowing you to soar over your creations and explore new areas. For even more freedom, press 'X' to enable NoClip mode, which lets you pass through solid blocks. Use Shift to move faster or hold Ctrl to slow down for precise block placement.

Never lose your way back to your favorite build site. Press the 'Return' key to set a spawn point at your current location. If you ever wander too far or want to get back quickly, just press the 'R' key to teleport back to your set spawn point.
