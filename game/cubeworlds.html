Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to your very own blocky sandbox! Getting started in Cube Worlds is simple. First, press the 'B' key to open your inventory. Here you'll find over 250 different blocks to choose from. Scroll through the list, pick your favorites, and assign them to your hotbar for quick access.

Once you've selected your materials, it's time to build! Use the standard WASD keys to walk around and the Spacebar to jump. Find the perfect spot and get creative. To place a block, simply aim and Right-Click. To remove a block, use Left-Click. That's all there is to it—you're now shaping the world!

For a better view and more building freedom, press 'Z' to toggle flying mode. While flying, you can hold 'Shift' to move faster or 'Ctrl' to slow down for precise placements. Want to build inside a mountain or check out underground caves? Press 'X' to activate NoClip mode, which allows you to pass through solid blocks.

If you've built something amazing and want to make sure you can always find your way back, press 'Return' to set a spawn point. If you wander off or get lost, just press the 'R' key to instantly teleport back to your set location.
