Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Remember the simple joy of building in Minecraft Classic? Cube Worlds recaptures that feeling in a casual, stress-free sandbox environment. There are no mobs, no survival meters—just an infinite world and your imagination. It's the perfect game to relax and build anything from a humble treehouse to a sprawling city.

Getting started is easy. Press 'B' to open your inventory and browse over 250 blocks. Assign your chosen materials to the hotbar for quick access. Find a spot you like and start building: right-click to place a block, and left-click to destroy it.

Use the standard WASD keys to move around and the spacebar to jump. If you build something special, press 'Return' to set a spawn point so you can get back to it instantly by pressing 'R'.

Take your creativity to new heights with flying mode by pressing 'Z'. For even more freedom, press 'X' to activate NoClip mode, allowing you to pass through your own structures to work on intricate interiors. Hold 'Shift' to speed up your movement or 'Ctrl' to slow down for precision building.

If Cube Worlds sparks your love for building, you should also check out similar games. Paper Minecraft offers a unique 2D perspective on crafting and exploring. Minecraft Classic Online delivers a pure, nostalgic trip back to the game's early days. And MiniBlox provides a more social, multiplayer-focused experience with various game modes.
