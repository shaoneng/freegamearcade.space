Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Getting started in Cube Worlds is all about imagination. Begin by pressing the 'B' key to open your inventory. Here, you'll find over 250 different block types to choose from. Scroll through the list, pick your favorites, and assign them to your hotbar for quick access.

Once you've selected your blocks, it's time to build! Find a spot you like and start creating. The controls are simple: use your Left Mouse Button to destroy blocks and your Right Mouse Button to place them. There are no limits, so you can build anything from towering castles to intricate underground bases.

Navigate your world with the standard WASD keys for movement and the Spacebar to jump. For a better view of your creations or to explore new areas, press the 'Z' key to toggle flying mode. If you need to move quickly, hold down the Shift key to sprint. For more precise placement while flying, hold the Ctrl key to slow down.

Set a custom respawn point by pressing the 'Return' key; you can instantly teleport back to this spot anytime by pressing 'R'. For ultimate freedom, press the 'X' key to activate NoClip mode, which allows you to pass through solid blocks, perfect for getting inside complex structures or caves.
