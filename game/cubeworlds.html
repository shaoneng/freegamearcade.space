Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to your very own blocky sandbox! Getting started in Cube Worlds is simple. Use the classic WASD keys to walk around the vast landscape and the Spacebar to jump over obstacles. Find the perfect spot to begin your creation.

To start building, press 'B' to open your block inventory. You'll find over 250 different blocks to choose from. Select your desired blocks and assign them to your hotbar for quick access. Once you're ready, simply aim with your mouse, right-click to place a block, and left-click to destroy one.

Unleash your creativity with special movement modes. Press 'Z' to activate flying mode, allowing you to soar through the air and get a bird's-eye view of your world. For even more freedom, press 'X' to enable NoClip mode, which lets you pass through solid blocks. To fine-tune your movement while flying or walking, hold Shift to move faster or Ctrl to slow down for precision placement.

Never lose your way home! Press the 'Return' key to set a spawn point at your current location. If you ever wander too far, just press 'R' to instantly teleport back to your set spawn point.
