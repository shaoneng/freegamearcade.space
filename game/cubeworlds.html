Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to your very own blocky sandbox! Getting started in Cube Worlds is simple. Use the WASD keys to move around and the Spacebar to jump. Your primary goal is to build anything you can imagine.

To access your creative tools, press the B key to open the block inventory. You'll find over 250 different blocks to choose from. Select your desired blocks and assign them to your hotbar for quick access. Once you're ready, find the perfect spot and start building. Use the Right Mouse Button to place a block and the Left Mouse Button to remove one.

For a better view and more building freedom, press the Z key to toggle flying mode. While flying, you can hold Shift to move faster or Ctrl to slow down for precise placement. If you want to explore inside your creations or through mountains, press the X key to enable NoClip mode, which lets you pass through solid blocks.

Don't forget to set a home base! Press the Return key to set your spawn point. If you ever get lost, simply press the R key to teleport back to your set location.
