Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to your own personal universe in Cube Worlds! Getting started is easy. Use the WASD keys to walk around and the Spacebar to jump.

To start building, press B to open your inventory. Here you'll find over 250 different blocks. Select your desired blocks and assign them to your hotbar for quick access. Once you've chosen a spot, use your mouse to build: Right-click to place a block and Left-click to destroy one.

Explore your world with total freedom. Press Z to toggle flying mode, giving you a bird's-eye view of your creations. For even quicker travel, hold Shift to sprint. If you need more precision while placing blocks, hold Ctrl to move slowly. You can even pass through solid objects by pressing X to activate NoClip mode.

Don't get lost! Press Return to set a spawn point at your current location. If you ever wander too far, just press R to instantly teleport back to your set spawn point.
