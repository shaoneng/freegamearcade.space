Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to your own personal sandbox! The goal in Cube Worlds is simple: build whatever you can imagine. There are no enemies or timers, just endless creative freedom.

Start by pressing the 'B' key to open your inventory. Here you'll find over 250 different blocks. Scroll through the options, select your favorites, and assign them to your hotbar at the bottom of the screen for quick access.

Once you've chosen your blocks, find a spot and start building! Use your mouse to look around. Right-click to place a block from your selected hotbar slot, and left-click to destroy a block.

Navigate the world using the standard WASD keys for movement and the Spacebar to jump. For a better view of your creations, press 'Z' to toggle flying mode. While flying, you can hold Shift to move faster or Ctrl to move slower for precise placements.

To explore inside your structures or through mountains, press 'X' to activate NoClip mode, which lets you pass through solid blocks. If you find a perfect spot you want to return to, press Return to set a spawn point, and then press 'R' anytime to teleport back to it.
