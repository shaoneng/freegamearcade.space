Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Getting started in Cube Worlds is simple! Use the standard WASD keys to walk around and the Spacebar to jump. To place a block, aim with your mouse and Right-click. To remove a block, simply Left-click it.

Press the B key to open your inventory, where you'll find over 250 different types of blocks. Scroll through the list, choose the blocks you want, and assign them to your hotbar at the bottom of the screen for quick access.

Want a better view of your creations? Press the Z key to toggle flying mode, allowing you to soar through the air. For even more freedom, press X to activate NoClip mode, which lets you pass through blocks. You can speed up your movement by holding Shift or slow down for precise placement by holding Ctrl.

If you find a perfect spot you want to return to, press Return to set your spawn point. Later, you can press the R key to teleport back to that saved location instantly.
