Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Get started by moving around the world using the WASD keys and jump with the Spacebar. To begin building, press 'B' to open your inventory and see all the available blocks. Select your desired blocks and assign them to your hotbar for quick access. Once you've found the perfect spot, simply right-click to place a block and left-click to destroy one.

Take your creativity to new heights with flying mode! Press the 'Z' key to toggle flight, allowing you to easily survey your world and build tall structures. For even more freedom, press 'X' to activate NoClip mode, which lets you pass through solid blocks. While flying, you can hold Shift to move faster or Ctrl to slow down for precise placements.

Found a spot you want to return to? Press the 'Return' key to set a spawn point. If you ever get lost or want to go back to your base, just press the 'R' key to instantly teleport to your saved spawn location. Now go on, let your imagination run wild and build your own cube world!
