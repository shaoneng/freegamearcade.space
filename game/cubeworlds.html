Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to your very own blocky paradise! To begin your creative journey, press the 'B' key to open your block inventory. Here you'll find over 250 different types of blocks. Scroll through the list, choose the ones you like, and assign them to your hotbar for quick access.

Once you've selected your materials, it's time to build! Find a spot you like in the vast, open world. Use your mouse to look around. To place a block, simply right-click. To destroy a block, left-click. It's that easy to start shaping the world around you.

Move around using the standard WASD keys. Use the Spacebar to jump over obstacles. If you build something amazing and want to make sure you can always find it again, press 'Return' to set a new spawn point. You can teleport back to this point anytime by pressing 'R'.

Take your creativity to new heights with flying mode! Press the 'Z' key to toggle flight, allowing you to soar through the air and get a bird's-eye view of your creations. For even more freedom, press 'X' to activate NoClip mode, which lets you pass through solid blocks—perfect for interior work or exploring caves. Need to move faster? Hold 'Shift' to sprint. For more precise placement while flying, hold 'Ctrl' to move more slowly.
