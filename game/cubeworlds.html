Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Getting started in Cube Worlds is simple! Use the WASD keys to walk around the landscape and press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles. To begin building, you first need to choose your materials.

Press the B key to open your block inventory. Here you'll find over 250 different blocks to choose from. Select your desired blocks and assign them to your hotbar for quick access. Once you've found the perfect spot, it's time to build! Use the Right-click to place a block and the Left-click to destroy one.

For a better view of your creations, press the Z key to toggle flying mode. While flying, hold Shift to move faster or Ctrl to slow down for precise placement. If you want to explore inside your structures or through solid ground, press the X key to activate NoClip mode.

Found a place you want to return to? Press the Return key to set a spawn point. If you ever get lost, just press the R key to teleport back to your designated spot.
