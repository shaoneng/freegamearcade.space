Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to your very own block-based sandbox! Getting started in Cube Worlds is simple. Your main goal is to build whatever you can imagine using the vast array of blocks available.

Movement & Building: Use the standard WASD keys to walk around and the Spacebar to jump. To place a block, aim your cursor and Right-Click. To remove a block, simply Left-Click it.

Inventory: Press the B key to open your inventory. Here you'll find over 250 different blocks. Scroll through the list, select your favorites, and assign them to your hotbar at the bottom of the screen for quick access.

Special Abilities: Unleash your full creative potential with special movement modes. Press Z to toggle flying mode, allowing you to soar through the air and get a bird's-eye view of your creations. Press X to activate NoClip, which lets you pass through solid blocks. While moving, hold Shift to go faster or Ctrl to slow down for precise placement.

Navigation: If you find a perfect spot for your base, press Return to set a spawn point. If you ever get lost, just press the R key to instantly teleport back to your set location.
