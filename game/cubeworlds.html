Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to your own personal sandbox! Getting started in Cube Worlds is simple. Use the WASD keys to walk around and the Spacebar to jump. The world is yours to shape: Left-click to destroy a block and Right-click to place a block from your hotbar.

To choose your building materials, press the B key to open your inventory. You'll find over 250 block types to scroll through. Select your favorites and assign them to your hotbar for quick access. If you find a perfect spot you want to return to, press Return to set your spawn point, and use the R key anytime to teleport back.

Unleash your full creative potential with special movement modes. Press the Z key to toggle flying mode, allowing you to soar above your creations and explore with ease. For even more freedom, press the X key to activate NoClip mode, which lets you pass through solid blocks. Need to go faster? Hold Shift to sprint or fly faster. For detailed work, hold Ctrl to slow your movement for precision placement.
