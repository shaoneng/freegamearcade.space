Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to your own personal blocky universe! Getting started in Cube Worlds is simple. Use the WASD keys to walk around and the Spacebar to jump over obstacles. To begin building, press the B key to open your inventory. Here you'll find over 250 different blocks to choose from. Select your desired blocks and assign them to your hotbar for quick access.

Once you've picked your materials, find the perfect spot and start creating! Use the Right Mouse Button to place blocks and the Left Mouse Button to destroy them. If you want to save your location, press Return to set a spawn point, and you can instantly teleport back to it later by pressing the R key.

Unleash your full creative potential with flying and noclip modes. Press the Z key to toggle flying, allowing you to soar above your creations and get a bird's-eye view. Press the X key to activate NoClip, which lets you pass through solid blocks, perfect for building intricate interiors or exploring underground. To fine-tune your movement, hold Shift to move faster and Ctrl to slow down for precise block placement.
