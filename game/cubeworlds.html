Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Getting started in Cube Worlds is simple! Use the classic WASD keys to walk around the vast, open world and press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles. Explore the endless terrain to find the perfect spot for your next creation.

To start building, press B to open your block inventory. You'll find over 250 different types of blocks to choose from. Select your desired blocks and assign them to your hotbar. Once you're ready, simply right-click to place a block and left-click to destroy one. It's that easy to bring your ideas to life!

Take your creativity to new heights with special movement modes. Press Z to toggle flying mode, allowing you to soar above your creations and get a bird's-eye view. For ultimate freedom, press X to activate NoClip mode, which lets you pass through solid blocks. Use Shift to move faster and cover ground quickly, or hold Ctrl to slow down for precise block placement.

Never lose your favorite building spot again. Press the Return (Enter) key to set a spawn point at your current location. If you ever wander too far or want to get back quickly, just press R to teleport back to your saved spot.
