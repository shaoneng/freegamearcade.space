Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to your very own blocky universe! Start by exploring the world using the WASD keys to move and the Spacebar to jump. When you're ready to build, press the 'B' key to open your inventory. Here you'll find over 250 different types of blocks. Scroll through, select your favorites, and assign them to your hotbar for quick access.

Building is simple and intuitive. Find a spot you like and use the Right Mouse Button to place a block. Made a mistake or want to dig into the earth? Just use the Left Mouse Button to destroy a block. There are no limits, so let your imagination run wild and create anything from a cozy cottage to a massive castle.

Want a better view of your masterpiece? Press 'Z' to activate flying mode and soar through the air. For even more freedom, press 'X' to toggle NoClip mode, allowing you to pass through blocks and explore inside mountains or underground. You can control your speed while flying: hold Shift to go faster and Ctrl to slow down for precise block placement.

Don't get lost in the vast world! Find a spot you want to return to and press the 'Return' key to set a spawn point. If you ever wander too far, simply press 'R' to instantly teleport back to your saved location.
