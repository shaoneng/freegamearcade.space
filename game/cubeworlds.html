Cube Worlds

How to Play Cube Worlds

Welcome to your own personal sandbox! Getting started is easy. Use the WASD keys to walk around the vast, open world and press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles. To get a better view of your creations or find the perfect building spot, press Z to toggle flying mode.

To start building, press B to open your inventory, which contains over 250 different types of blocks. Scroll through the list, select the blocks you want, and assign them to your hotbar for quick access. Once you've chosen your materials, simply right-click with your mouse to place a block and left-click to destroy one.

Cube Worlds offers a few extra controls for total creative freedom. While flying, hold Shift to move faster and cover more ground, or hold Ctrl to slow down for precise placement. You can also press X to toggle NoClip mode, allowing you to pass through solid objects. If you find a place you want to return to, press Return to set a spawn point, and press R anytime to instantly teleport back to it.
