Dino Evolution io

How to Play Dino Evolution io

Welcome to the wild world of Dino Evolution io! Your goal is simple: survive, evolve, and dominate the prehistoric food chain. You begin your journey as a small, humble dinosaur in a land filled with food and danger.

Use your mouse to navigate the world. Your primary objective is to eat to grow. Scour the map for fruits and other edible items to gain experience points (XP). As your XP bar fills, you will level up and eventually evolve into a new, more powerful species of dinosaur.

Be mindful of other players! The rule of the land is "eat or be eaten." You can hunt and consume dinosaurs that are smaller than you to gain a significant XP boost. However, larger dinosaurs will see you as a tasty snack. Always keep an eye out for bigger predators and use your speed to escape when necessary. The key is to adapt, grow, and strategically choose your battles until you are the one at the top.
