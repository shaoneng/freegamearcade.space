Dino Evolution io

How to Play Dino Evolution io

Welcome to the prehistoric arena of Dino Evolution io! Your primary goal is to survive and thrive. You begin as a small, humble dinosaur. Move around the map and eat the scattered fruits and food items to gain experience points (XP) and grow in size. Once you've collected enough XP, you will automatically evolve into a new, more powerful dinosaur, climbing higher up the food chain.

The world is full of other players, creating a classic "eat or be eaten" scenario. Dinosaurs smaller than you will have a green outline—these are your prey! Chase them down to score big points. Conversely, dinosaurs larger than you will have a red outline. Avoid them at all costs, as they see you as their next meal. Use the environment to your advantage to hide or trap smaller prey.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use your mouse to guide your dinosaur across the map; it will follow your cursor's direction. To get a quick burst of speed to either escape danger or catch your prey, left-click the mouse. Be strategic with your boosts, as they consume your energy!
