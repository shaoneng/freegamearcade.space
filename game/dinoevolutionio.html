Dino Evolution io

How to Play Dino Evolution io

Your journey in Dino Evolution io begins as a small, humble dinosaur in a vast prehistoric world. The primary goal is simple: survive, grow, and evolve to become the most powerful predator on the map.

Use your mouse to navigate your dinosaur around the environment. Your main task is to find and eat the scattered fruits to gain experience points and grow larger. As you grow, you will evolve into new, more formidable dinosaur species.

The world is filled with other players. A key rule to remember is: hunt players smaller than you and avoid players bigger than you. If a larger dinosaur catches you, it's game over. Conversely, chasing down smaller dinos will help you grow even faster. Stay vigilant, adapt your strategy, and climb the food chain to dominate the leaderboard!
