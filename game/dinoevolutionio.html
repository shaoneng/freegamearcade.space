Dino Evolution io

How to Play Dino Evolution io

Welcome to the prehistoric food chain! In Dino Evolution io, your primary goal is to survive, grow, and evolve into the most powerful dinosaur on the map. Use your mouse to control your dinosaur's movement across the lush, dangerous landscape.

Start your journey as a small, humble dino. To grow, you must eat! Look for fruits and other edibles scattered around the map. As you eat, your experience bar will fill up. Once it's full, you will automatically evolve into a new, stronger dinosaur, allowing you to take on bigger challenges.

Survival is key. While you're hunting for food, be aware of other players. Dinosaurs larger than you are predators and will try to eat you. Avoid them at all costs! Conversely, any dinosaur smaller than you is potential prey. Chase them down to gain a massive experience boost. The rule is simple: eat or be eaten as you climb the leaderboard!
