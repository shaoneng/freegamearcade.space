Dino Evolution io

How to Play Dino Evolution io

Your adventure in Dino Evolution io begins as a small, humble dinosaur in a vast prehistoric landscape filled with food and rivals. The primary objective is simple: eat, grow, and evolve to become the most dominant predator on the server.

Use your mouse to navigate the world; your dinosaur will automatically follow your cursor. Scour the map for fruits and other edibles scattered around. Each piece of food you consume fills your experience bar, helping you grow and level up, unlocking new and more powerful dinosaur forms.

As you explore, you'll encounter other players. A key rule of survival is to only attack dinosaurs smaller than you. Flee from any predator that is bigger, as they can easily make you their next meal. As you evolve, you'll be able to hunt a wider variety of prey and climb the food chain.

The key to success is a balance of careful foraging and opportunistic hunting. Keep an eye on the leaderboard, adapt your strategy, and survive long enough to become the king of the dinosaurs!
