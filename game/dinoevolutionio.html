How to Play Dino Evolution io

Welcome to the prehistoric arena of Dino Evolution io! You begin your journey as a small, humble dinosaur in a world teeming with life and danger. The primary objective is simple: survive, evolve, and become the most powerful creature on the map.

Use your mouse to guide your dinosaur around the lush landscape. Your first task is to eat the scattered fruits and other resources. Each item you consume fills up your experience bar. Once the bar is full, you will automatically evolve into a new, stronger, and bigger dinosaur.

As you explore, you'll encounter other players. Be strategic! Dinosaurs smaller than you will have a green outline and are safe to hunt for extra XP.
