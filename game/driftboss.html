Drift Boss

How to Play Drift Boss

Welcome to Drift Boss, an addictive and easy-to-learn 3D driving game. Your mission is simple: steer your car through a series of sharp bends and tricky corners. There are no brakes, so timing your drifts is key! The goal is to survive for as long as you can without falling off the platform into space.

The controls are straightforward, making it easy to pick up but challenging to master. Click or tap to turn right, and release to turn left. Anticipate the corners and tap just before you reach them to execute a perfect drift. The longer you hold, the sharper you'll turn.

To help you achieve a higher score, you can use one of three boosters before each run: Double Score, Car Insurance, or Coin Rush. Use them strategically to extend your drive and rack up points. Can you set a new high score?
