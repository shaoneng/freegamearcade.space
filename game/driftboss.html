Drift Boss

How to Play Drift Boss

Welcome to Drift Boss, a game where precision and timing are everything! The controls are deceptively simple, but mastering them is the key to a high score.

  • Steering: Click and hold your mouse button (or tap and hold on a mobile device) to turn your car to the right.
  • Changing Direction: Release the button to make your car turn to the left.
  • The Goal: Your objective is to navigate the treacherous, winding road for as long as you can without falling off the edge. Time your turns perfectly to drift around the sharp corners.

As you play, you'll collect coins which can be used to unlock new vehicles. You can also use special boosters like "Double Score," "Car Insurance," and "Coin Rush" before you start a run to gain an advantage. Pay close attention to the road ahead, anticipate the turns, and see how long you can survive!
