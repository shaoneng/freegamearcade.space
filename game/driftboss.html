Drift Boss

How to Play Drift Boss

The objective in Drift Boss is to drive as far as you can on a challenging, winding road without falling into the abyss. This is a game of pure timing, precision, and focus.

The controls are brilliantly simple yet hard to master. To steer your car, simply click (or tap on mobile) and hold. Your car will begin to turn right. Release the button to straighten out and continue along the path. You need to anticipate the sharp corners and time your drifts perfectly to stay on the road. There are no brakes, so your skill in turning is all that matters!

Along your drive, collect coins to spend on unlocking a variety of new and stylish vehicles. You can also activate powerful boosters before each run, such as 'Double Score' or 'Car Insurance,' to give you an edge and help you set a new high score.
