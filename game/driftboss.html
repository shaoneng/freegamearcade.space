Drift Boss

How to Play Drift Boss

Welcome to the ultimate test of timing and precision! In Drift Boss, your goal is simple: drive as far as you can on a challenging, winding road suspended in space without falling off. There are no brakes, only your skill at drifting!

The controls are incredibly easy to learn but difficult to master. Click and hold your mouse button (or tap and hold on a touch screen) to turn your car to the right. Release the button to straighten out and drift to the left. You'll need to time your turns perfectly to navigate the sharp corners and zig-zagging paths.

As you play, you'll collect coins scattered on the road. Use these coins in the main menu to unlock stylish new vehicles and purchase powerful one-time boosters like 'Double Score,' 'Car Insurance,' and 'Coin Rush' to help you achieve an even higher score on your next run!
