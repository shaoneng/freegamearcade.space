Drift Boss

How to Play Drift Boss

Welcome to the minimalist and addictive world of Drift Boss! The objective is simple: drive your car as far as possible on an endless, zig-zagging road without falling off the edge.

The controls are deceptively easy to learn but difficult to master. Click (or tap) and hold your mouse button to turn your car to the right. Release the button to make the car steer back to the left. You'll need to anticipate the sharp turns and time your drifts perfectly to stay on course.

As you navigate the track, be sure to collect coins. These can be used to unlock a variety of cool new cars and to purchase helpful boosters before a run, such as Car Insurance or a Coin Rush. Aim for the highest score and challenge your friends to see who is the true Drift Boss!
