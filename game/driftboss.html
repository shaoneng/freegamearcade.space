Drift Boss

How to Play Drift Boss

The goal in Drift Boss is simple: drive as far as possible without falling off the track. Your car moves forward automatically. To turn right, simply click and hold your mouse button (or tap and hold on a touch screen). Release the button to turn left and straighten out. The key is to time your turns perfectly to navigate the sharp, zig-zagging corners.

As you drift through the course, you'll see coins scattered on the road. Be sure to collect these! You can use the coins you earn to unlock a variety of cool new cars, from classic sedans to powerful trucks, each with its own handling characteristics.

For an extra edge, you can use boosters before starting a run. These power-ups, like "Double Score," "Car Insurance" (which gives you one free save if you fall), and "Coin Rush," can help you achieve a new high score. Use them strategically to conquer the road and become the ultimate Drift Boss!
