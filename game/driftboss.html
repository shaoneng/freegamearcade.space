Drift Boss

How to Play Drift Boss

The goal in Drift Boss is simple yet challenging: drive as far as you can on a winding road without falling into the abyss. Your car moves forward automatically, so your only focus is on steering through the sharp turns.

Controlling your car is incredibly easy. Click and hold your mouse button (or tap and hold on a touch screen) to turn right. Release the button to turn left. The key is to time your turns perfectly to stay on the track. A quick tap will make a slight adjustment, while a longer hold will result in a sharper drift.

As you play, you'll collect coins that can be used to unlock a variety of cool new cars. Before starting a run, you can also activate special boosters to help you out. These include 'Double Score' to multiply your points, 'Car Insurance' to save you from one fall, and 'Coin Rush' to increase the number of coins you find on the track. Use them strategically to set record-breaking high scores!
