Drift Boss

How to Play Drift Boss

The controls for Drift Boss are deceptively simple. To turn right, click and hold your mouse button or tap and hold on your screen. Release the button or your finger to straighten out and drift left. The key is to time your turns perfectly to stay on the twisting platform.

Your objective is to drive as far as you can without falling off the edges. The road is full of sharp, unpredictable corners, so you'll need sharp reflexes and precise timing. The longer you survive, the higher your score will be.

As you drift, be sure to collect coins scattered on the road. You can use these coins to unlock new and cooler cars. Before starting a run, you can also activate special boosters like 'Double Score' or 'Car Insurance' to give you a helpful advantage and achieve a new personal best!
