Drift Boss

How to Play Drift Boss

The goal in Drift Boss is simple: drive as far as possible without falling off the winding road. The challenge comes from the track's sharp, unpredictable turns. Your car moves forward automatically, so all you need to worry about is steering.

To make a right turn, simply click and hold your mouse button (or tap and hold on a touch screen). To go straight again, just release. The key to a high score is timing your turns perfectly to stay on the platform. A quick tap will make a slight adjustment, while holding it longer will result in a sharper, more prolonged drift.

As you play, you'll earn coins that can be used to unlock new cars and purchase helpful boosters before a run. Boosters like 'Car Insurance' and 'Double Score' can give you a significant advantage. Master the drift, collect coins, and see how long you can survive on the road!
