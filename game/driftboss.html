Drift Boss

How to Play Drift Boss

The goal in Drift Boss is simple: drive as far as you can without falling off the winding, floating road. Your car moves forward automatically, so you only need to focus on steering through the sharp turns.

To make a turn, simply tap and hold your mouse button or your screen. This will cause your car to start drifting to the right. To straighten out and continue driving forward, just release the button. The key to a high score is mastering the timing of your drifts to perfectly navigate the corners. The longer you hold, the sharper your car will turn.

As you race, collect coins scattered on the road. These can be used to unlock a variety of cool new vehicles and to purchase powerful one-time boosters before a run. Boosters like 'Car Insurance' can save you from a fall, while 'Coin Rush' increases the number of coins on the track, helping you unlock new content even faster.
