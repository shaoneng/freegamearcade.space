Drift Boss

How to Play Drift Boss

Welcome to the deceptively simple world of Drift Boss! Your objective is to drive as far as you can on a winding road full of sharp turns. The catch? Your car automatically drives straight, and there are no brakes!

To make a turn, simply click and hold your mouse button (or tap and hold on a touch screen) to start drifting right. Release the button to straighten out and drive left. The key is perfect timingâ€”start your drift just before the corner and release it as you exit to stay on the track.

As you play, you'll collect coins scattered on the road. Use these coins to unlock a variety of cool new cars. Before each run, you can also activate powerful boosters like 'Double Score,' 'Car Insurance' (which saves you from one fall), and 'Coin Rush' to help you achieve an even higher score.
