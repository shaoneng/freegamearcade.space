Drift Boss

How to Play Drift Boss

Welcome to the ultimate test of timing and precision! In Drift Boss, your goal is simple: drive as far as you can on an endless, winding road suspended in space. The catch? Your car only moves straight, and you have no brakes. You must master the art of drifting to navigate the sharp corners.

The controls are incredibly easy to learn but difficult to master. Simply click and hold your mouse button (or tap and hold on a touch screen) to turn your car to the right. Release the button to straighten out and continue driving forward. The key to a high score is anticipating the turns and timing your drifts perfectly to avoid falling off the edge.

As you play, you'll collect coins scattered on the road. Use these coins to unlock a variety of cool new cars. You can also pick up boosters before a run, such as "Double Score," "Car Insurance," and "Coin Rush," to give you a temporary advantage and help you set a new personal best!
