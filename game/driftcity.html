Drift City

How to Play Drift City

Welcome to the ultimate drifting challenge! Your goal in Drift City is to become a legend of the streets by mastering the art of the drift and completing a series of exciting missions.

Use your keyboard to control your car. Accelerate, brake, and steer into turns to initiate a drift. The key is finding the perfect balance of speed and angle to maintain a long, stylish slide without spinning out. Each mission will test your skills in a unique way, from precision drifting through tight corners to achieving high scores.

As you complete missions and showcase your skills, you'll earn coins and valuable customization cards. Take your earnings to the garage to unlock a fleet of new cars or to upgrade your current ride with performance boosts and fresh paint jobs. Keep practicing, complete all the missions, and build the ultimate drift car!
