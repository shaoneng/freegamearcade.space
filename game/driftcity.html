Drift City

How to Play Drift City

Welcome to the world of precision driving in Drift City! Your main objective is to become a master of drifting. Your journey begins by taking on various missions scattered throughout the city. Each mission will challenge your ability to control your car in a sustained drift, testing your timing, speed, and angle.

Successfully completing these missions is key to your progress. As you conquer challenges, you will be rewarded with coins. These coins can be used to unlock new vehicles and other in-game items. You'll also earn customization cards, which allow you to personalize your car's appearance and performance, making your ride truly unique.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your car's steering and acceleration. The spacebar typically engages the handbrake, which is essential for initiating and maintaining a long, stylish drift. Pay close attention to the mission objectives and try to beat your own high scores to earn the maximum rewards!
