Drift City

How to Play Drift City

Welcome to the streets of Drift City! Your primary goal is to master the art of drifting. Use your driving skills to slide your car around corners, complete challenging missions, and prove you're the best drifter around.

Control your vehicle using the standard keyboard controls. Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer, accelerate, and brake. To initiate a drift, build up some speed and then turn sharply while managing your throttle to maintain the slide.

As you successfully complete missions and perform epic drifts, you will be rewarded with coins. These coins can be used to purchase customization cards, allowing you to upgrade and personalize your vehicle. Keep playing to unlock new cars and visual upgrades to dominate the city in style!
