Drift City

How to Play Drift City

Welcome to the streets of Drift City! Your goal is to become the ultimate drift champion. In this browser-based driving game, you'll tackle a variety of exciting missions designed to test and improve your drifting skills. Use your keyboard to control your car: accelerate, brake, and steer into long, sweeping drifts around corners to meet the mission objectives.

Successfully completing missions will reward you with coins and special customization cards. Use these rewards to unlock new cars and upgrade their performance and appearance. Whether you're new to drifting or a seasoned expert, Drift City offers a thrilling challenge. It's time to hit the asphalt, burn some rubber, and show everyone who owns the city!
