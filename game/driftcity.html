Drift City

How to Play Drift City

Welcome to Drift City! Your main objective is to become a master of drifting. The game challenges you with a variety of exciting missions designed to test your control and precision behind the wheel. Whether you need to drift for a certain distance or follow a specific path, each mission offers a new challenge.

Use your keyboard controls to accelerate, brake, and steer your car. The key to success is to initiate and maintain long, controlled drifts around corners and through marked zones to meet your mission goals. Mastering the balance between speed and angle is crucial for high scores.

Successfully completing missions will earn you coins and valuable customization cards. Use these rewards in the garage to unlock new car parts, apply stylish paint jobs, and install performance upgrades. A better car will help you tackle tougher missions and dominate the leaderboards. Jump in, start your engine, and let the drift begin!
