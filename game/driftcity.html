Drift City

How to Play Drift City

Welcome to the smoke-filled streets of Drift City! Your primary objective is to become the ultimate drift master. The game will present you with a series of diverse and challenging missions, each designed to test a different aspect of your drifting technique. Successfully navigate these challenges by skidding around corners, maintaining control, and hitting mission targets.

As you complete missions, you'll be rewarded with valuable coins and special customization cards. Use these rewards in the garage to upgrade your car's performance or change its appearance. A better car will help you tackle tougher missions and achieve even higher scores.

The core of the gameplay is all about controlling your car through long, sweeping drifts. The longer and more controlled your drift, the more points you'll score. Keep an eye on your mission objectives, earn those rewards, and build the ultimate drift machine to conquer every challenge the city throws at you!
