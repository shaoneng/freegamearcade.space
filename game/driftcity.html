Drift City

How to Play Drift City

Welcome to the thrilling world of Drift City! Your primary objective is to become a master drifter by completing various missions. Each mission will challenge your driving and drifting abilities, pushing you to perfect your technique.

Use your keyboard controls to accelerate, brake, and steer your vehicle. The key to success is initiating and maintaining long, controlled drifts around corners and through specified zones. The better your drift, the higher your score and the more coins you'll earn.

As you complete missions and accumulate coins, you'll be rewarded with customization cards. Use these to upgrade and personalize your car, improving its performance and appearance. Get ready to burn some rubber and dominate the city streets!
