Drift City

How to Play Drift City

Welcome to Drift City, where the streets are your playground and style is everything! The goal is simple: become the ultimate drift master. Get behind the wheel and start tackling a series of diverse and challenging missions. Each mission will test your ability to control your car and execute perfect drifts.

Use your keyboard controls to accelerate, brake, and steer your car into a controlled slide around corners. The longer and more stylish your drift, the better your score. Completing these missions isn't just for bragging rights; you'll earn valuable coins and customization cards.

Head to the garage to spend your hard-earned rewards. Unlock new cars, upgrade performance parts, and apply visual customizations to create a vehicle that truly represents your drifting style. Get ready to burn some rubber and dominate the leaderboards!
