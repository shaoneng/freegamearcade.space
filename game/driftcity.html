Drift City

How to Play Drift City

Your goal in Drift City is to become a master drifter. The game will challenge you with a series of exciting missions designed to test and improve your driving and drifting abilities.

Navigate through the city, accept missions, and execute perfect drifts to complete your objectives. Successful missions will reward you with coins and special customization cards.

Use the coins and cards you earn to unlock new cars and customize their appearance. Show off your unique style as you conquer every corner of the city. Get ready to burn some rubber and slide your way to victory!
