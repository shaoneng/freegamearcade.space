Drift City

How to Play Drift City

Welcome to the thrilling world of Drift City! Your primary goal is to master the art of drifting and complete various missions to prove your skills. Get behind the wheel of your car and navigate through cityscapes, executing perfect drifts around corners and obstacles.

Each mission presents a unique challenge. Successfully completing them will reward you with coins. These coins are your key to progression, allowing you to unlock special customization cards. Use these cards to upgrade your vehicle's performance and change its appearance to match your style.

The controls are intuitive for driving games. Use your keyboard to accelerate, brake, and steer. The real skill comes in balancing your speed and turning angle to initiate and maintain a long, stylish drift. Practice makes perfect, so don't be afraid to push your car to its limits and find the sweet spot for maximum points. Get ready to burn some rubber!
