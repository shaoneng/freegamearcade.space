Drift City

How to Play Drift City

Welcome to the streets of Drift City! Your main goal is to become a drifting legend by taking on and completing various challenging missions. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or the WASD keys to control your car—accelerate, brake, and steer your way through the city. The key to success is initiating and maintaining long, controlled drifts to score points and complete objectives.

As you successfully finish missions, you'll be rewarded with coins and special customization cards. Use your earnings to unlock new, more powerful cars or to upgrade your current vehicle with better performance parts and stylish visual enhancements. Keep honing your skills, tackle tougher missions, and build the ultimate drifting machine to dominate the streets!
