Drift City

How to Play Drift City

Welcome to the ultimate urban playground in Drift City! Your main objective is to master the art of drifting by completing a variety of challenging missions scattered throughout the city. Each mission will test your precision, timing, and control behind the wheel.

Use your keyboard controls to accelerate, brake, and steer your vehicle. The key to a high score is initiating and sustaining long, smooth drifts around corners and through designated checkpoints. Successfully completing missions will reward you with coins and special customization cards.

Head to the garage to spend your earnings! Use the coins and cards to upgrade your car's performance and customize its appearance. A well-tuned car will help you tackle more difficult missions and solidify your reputation as the king of the drift. Get ready to burn rubber and own the streets!
