Drift Fury

How to Play Drift Fury

Welcome to the high-octane world of Drift Fury! Your goal is to become a master drifter by taking on thrilling challenges and races on winding mountain roads. Get in your sports car, fire up the engine, and prepare to leave your competitors in the dust.

To score points, you need to execute perfect drifts. Approach turns at high speed and steer skillfully to slide your car through the corner. The longer you can hold a drift without losing control, the higher your score will be. Keep an eye on the combo bar on your screenâ€”if you fill it up by drifting continuously, you'll triple your score!

As you play, you'll earn rewards that allow you to unlock new vehicles. There are 9 incredible cars to collect, each inspired by real-world supercars. To get your hands on the ultimate prize, a Lamborghini Huracan-inspired car, you'll need to complete 20 specific tasks found in the 'Achievements' section. Aim for three stars on every track by finishing as fast as possible!
