Drift Fury

How to Play Drift Fury

Get in the driver's seat and prepare to burn rubber on winding mountain roads! The objective in Drift Fury is to score points by executing long, stylish drifts through challenging turns. You'll need to master your vehicle's handling, entering corners at the perfect speed and angle to maintain control.

The key to a high score is chaining your drifts together. As you drift, a combo bar will appear on the screen. If you can fill this bar by drifting continuously without crashing or losing control, you will triple your score! This is how you set record-breaking point totals.

The game features a collection of 9 incredible sports cars, inspired by famous brands like BMW and McLaren. To get new rides, you must complete tasks in the "Achievements" section. Fulfill these challenges, complete tracks in the fastest time possible to earn three stars, and you'll eventually unlock the ultimate prize: a stunning Lamborghini Huracan.
