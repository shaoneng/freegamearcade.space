How to Play Drift Hunters

Welcome to the driver's seat of Drift Hunters! Your goal is to become the ultimate drift master. Start by selecting your first car and choosing a mapâ€”either a challenging track or a free-roaming playground to practice your skills.

Controls:

  • Arrow Keys or WASD: Steer, accelerate, and brake.
  • Spacebar: Pull the handbrake to initiate a drift.
  • C Key: Change the camera view.
  • Left Shift: Shift up (for manual gearbox).
  • Left Ctrl: Shift down (for manual gearbox).

As you drift, you'll earn cash. Use this cash to unlock over 25 awesome drift cars, each with unique handling characteristics. Head to the garage to upgrade your ride. In the "Parts" section, boost your car's performance by upgrading the brakes, weight, gearbox, turbo, and engine from stock to pro levels.

Don't forget to customize! In the "Tuning" section, you can adjust your car's camber, height, and wheel offset to fine-tune its handling for perfect drifts. You can also change the body color with glossy, metallic, or matte finishes and pick out a fresh set of rims to complete the look. Choose between automatic and manual transmission in the Options menu to suit your driving style. Now, hit the track and start burning rubber!
