Drift Hunters

How to Play Drift Hunters

Welcome to the high-octane world of Drift Hunters! Your goal is simple: become a master of the drift. Get behind the wheel of your car and hit the track. Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer, accelerate, and brake. Tap the spacebar to pull the handbrake for tight, controlled slides. The longer and more stylish your drift, the more points you'll earn. Use these points to build your ultimate drifting machine.

Drift Hunters is all about personalization. Head to the garage to spend your hard-earned cash. Unlock a wide range of iconic cars, each with unique handling characteristics. But don't stop there! Dive into the 'Parts' section to upgrade your vehicle's performance. Enhance the engine, turbo, brakes, and more, moving from stock parts to pro-level equipment. In the 'Tuning' section, you can fine-tune your car's setup, adjusting camber, height, and offset for the perfect stance and handling.

Make your car truly your own by customizing its appearance. Choose from a variety of paint finishes like matte, chrome, or glossy, and select the perfect set of rims to complete the look. Once your ride is ready, explore the various maps available, from challenging race tracks to open-world playgrounds perfect for practicing your skills. Choose your map, fire up the engine, and start burning rubber!
