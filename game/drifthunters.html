Drift Hunters

How to Play Drift Hunters

Welcome to the driver's seat of Drift Hunters! Your goal is to become a master of the drift. Start by selecting your first car and choosing a map to drive on. You have two types of environments: challenging 'Tracks' designed for precision drifting, and open 'Playgrounds' for freestyle fun.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your car. The spacebar is your handbrake, essential for initiating and controlling long, stylish drifts. As you drift, you'll earn cash. The longer and more skillful your drift, the more you earn! Use this cash to unlock a wide variety of new cars, each with its own unique handling characteristics.

Head to the 'Parts' section in the menu to upgrade your vehicle's performance. You can enhance the engine, turbo, gearbox, brakes, and reduce weight to improve handling. Dive into the 'Tuning' menu to fine-tune your car's setup, adjusting camber, ride height, and offsets for the perfect drift profile. Don't forget to customize your car's look with different paint finishes and rims to truly make it your own!
