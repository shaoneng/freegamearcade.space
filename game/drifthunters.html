Drift Hunters

How to Play Drift Hunters

The main objective in Drift Hunters is to score points by executing stylish and extended drifts. Get behind the wheel and use the W/A/S/D or Arrow Keys to steer and control your speed. The key to a great drift is the Spacebar, which acts as your handbrake to help initiate slides around corners. You can also change the camera view with the C key and shift gears manually with Left Shift and Left Ctrl if you prefer.

As you drift, you'll earn cash. This cash is your ticket to a bigger and better garage! Head to the main menu to buy new cars, with many models inspired by famous tuner vehicles. Each car handles differently, offering a unique driving experience. Once you have a car you love, you can take it to the next level.

Dive into the "Parts" section to upgrade your car's performance. You can boost the power of the engine, turbo, and gearbox, or improve handling with better brakes and weight reduction. For advanced control, the "Tuning" section lets you adjust your car's suspension, camber, and offsets. Finally, give your car a personal touch with custom paint jobs and a wide selection of rims to complete your perfect drift machine.
