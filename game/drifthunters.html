Drift Hunters

How to Play Drift Hunters

Welcome to the driver's seat of Drift Hunters! Your goal is to score points by executing perfect drifts. Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer, accelerate, and brake. The Space Bar is your handbrake for initiating sharp drifts, and C changes the camera view.

Start by choosing your car from a garage filled with legendary models. You begin with a classic, but as you earn cash from drifting, you can unlock more powerful vehicles. Each car has unique handling characteristics, so try them all to find your favorite!

Head to the "Parts" section to upgrade your car's performance. You can enhance the engine, brakes, turbo, gearbox, and reduce weight. In the "Tuning" menu, you can fine-tune every detail, from brake balance and front camber to ride height and offset. This allows you to create the ultimate drift machine tailored to your driving style.

Don't forget to customize your ride's appearance! Choose from a wide range of colors in matte, chrome, glossy, or metallic finishes, and select the perfect set of rims to complete the look. Once you're ready, hit the road on one of the many available tracks or playgrounds and start burning rubber!
