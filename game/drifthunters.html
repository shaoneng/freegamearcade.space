Drift Hunters

How to Play Drift Hunters

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of Drift Hunters! Your goal is to become a master drifter by earning points and cash on a variety of tracks. Use your earnings to unlock new cars and upgrade them to perfection.

Controls:

  • Drive: Use WASD or the Arrow Keys.
  • Handbrake: Press the Spacebar to initiate a drift.
  • Change Gears: Use Left Shift (upshift) and Left CTRL (downshift) for manual transmission.
  • Change Camera: Press the C key to cycle through different camera views.

Start by selecting your first car and choosing a track from the main menu. Once you're on the road, accelerate and use the handbrake while turning to start drifting. The longer and more stylish your drift, the more points you'll earn. These points convert to cash, which is your key to progress.

Head to the garage to spend your hard-earned cash. You can purchase over 25 iconic drift cars. In the "Parts" section, you can upgrade your car's performance, enhancing the brakes, weight, gearbox, turbo, and engine. For fine-tuning, visit the "Tuning" section to adjust camber, height, and offsets to create the perfect drift setup. Don't forget to customize your car's look with a wide range of body colors and rims to make your ride truly unique!
