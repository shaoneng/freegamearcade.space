Drift Hunters

About Drift Hunters

Get into the driver's seat and burn some rubber in Drift Hunters, a thrilling 3D car drifting game. Choose between challenging tracks and open-world playgrounds to test your skills. Select your ride from a garage of legendary cars inspired by famous brands and movies like The Fast and The Furious, and prepare for the ultimate driving experience.

Unlock and Customize Your Ride

In Drift Hunters, your garage is your sanctuary. Start with a stock vehicle and earn cash by executing perfect drifts to unlock a full roster of high-performance cars. Each car has unique handling characteristics, engine power, and braking capabilities, offering a distinct driving feel.

In-Depth Tuning System

Fine-tune your vehicle to match your drifting style. Head to the "Parts" section to upgrade your brakes, weight, gearbox, turbo, and engine from stock to pro level. In the "Tuning" menu, you can adjust your car's suspension, including camber, height, and wheel offset, for optimal performance on the asphalt.

Don't forget to give your car a personal touch. The visual customization options let you choose from a wide range of body colors—glossy, metallic, matte, or chrome—and select the perfect set of rims to complete the look.

Game Controls and Settings

Tailor your gameplay experience in the Options menu. Switch between automatic and manual gearboxes to suit your preference, and adjust the graphics quality for the smoothest performance on your device.

Drift Hunters was developed by the talented Ilya Kaminetsky. If you love the thrill of high-speed stunts, be sure to check out City Stunts for more airborne action. For even more drifting challenges, explore the drift games collection on our site. Happy drifting!
