Drift Hunters

How to Play Drift Hunters

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of Drift Hunters! The main goal is to score as many points as possible by performing long, stylish drifts. The longer you can hold a drift, the higher your score multiplier will climb, leading to massive points and cash rewards.

Use your earnings to build a garage of legendary drift cars, with models inspired by famous performance vehicles. Each car has a unique feel, so try them all to find your favorite. But don't stop there! Head to the garage to upgrade your vehicle's performance. You can enhance everything from the engine and turbo to the brakes and gearbox, taking your car from stock to a professional drifting beast. Fine-tune your setup in the Tuning menu by adjusting camber, offsets, and ride height for the perfect stance and handling.

Once your car performs like a dream, make it look the part. Customize your vehicle's appearance with different paint jobs—glossy, matte, metallic, or chrome—and a wide selection of rims. Choose from various tracks and open-world playgrounds to test your skills and show off your custom ride. Get ready to burn rubber and become the ultimate drift master!
