Drift Hunters

How to Play Drift Hunters

Welcome to the ultimate drifting challenge! In Drift Hunters, your goal is to score as many points as possible by executing long, stylish drifts. Start by choosing your car and one of the available maps, which include both dedicated race tracks and open-world playgrounds perfect for practicing your skills.

Get comfortable with the controls to master your vehicle: Use the W/Up Arrow to accelerate, S/Down Arrow to brake/reverse, and A/D or Left/Right Arrows to steer. The most crucial control for drifting is the Spacebar for the handbrake, which helps initiate your slides. You can also change gears manually with Left Shift (upshift) and Left CTRL (downshift) and switch your camera view with the C key.

As you drift, you'll earn cash. Use this cash in the garage to unlock over 25 legendary drift cars, each with its own unique feel. Don't stop there! Head to the "Parts" section to upgrade your car's performance. Enhance the engine, turbo, gearbox, and brakes from stock to pro levels. For the ultimate control, visit the "Tuning" menu to fine-tune your suspension, including camber, height, and offsets, to find the perfect setup for your driving style.

Finally, make your car your own by customizing its appearance. Choose from a wide range of paint colors in glossy, matte, metallic, or chrome finishes, and select the perfect set of rims to complete the look. Now get out there, burn some rubber, and become a drift hunter legend!
