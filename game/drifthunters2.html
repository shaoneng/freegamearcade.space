Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Welcome to the world of Drift Hunters 2! Your goal is to become the ultimate drift master. Start by selecting your first car from the garage. Take it out onto one of the many available tracks and start drifting to earn points. The longer and more stylish your drifts, the more cash you'll accumulate.

Master the controls to dominate the road: use the Arrow Keys or WASD to steer, accelerate, and brake. Pull the Spacebar for the handbrake to initiate sharp drifts, and press Left Shift to shift up and Left Ctrl to shift down if you're using a manual gearbox. You can change the camera view with the C key.

Use the cash you earn to purchase new and more powerful cars from a lineup of 26 vehicles. Don't stop there! Head into the detailed tuning section to upgrade your car's performance, adjust its stance for better drift angles, and customize its appearance with unique paint jobs and rims. Experiment with different settings to find the perfect setup for your driving style.

Explore 15 different tracks, from open playgrounds perfect for practice to challenging touge mountain passes. Each track offers a unique experience. Start drifting, earn money, and build your dream drift car!
