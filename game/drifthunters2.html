Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of Drift Hunters 2! Your journey begins in the garage, where you'll use your starting cash to buy your first drift car from a selection of 26 epic vehicles.

Get a feel for the road using your keyboard. Use the **Arrow Keys or WASD** to drive: **W/Up Arrow** to accelerate, **S/Down Arrow** to brake, and **A/Left Arrow** & **D/Right Arrow** to steer. To initiate a drift, hit the **Spacebar** for the handbrake. If you're using a manual gearbox, use **Left Shift** to shift up and **Left CTRL** to shift down.

The main objective is to rack up points by executing spectacular drifts. The longer and more angled your drift, the higher your score. Chain drifts together to build a combo multiplier for massive cash rewards. Spend your earnings on new, more powerful cars or upgrade the ones you already own.

Dive into the "Tuning" menu to customize your car to perfection. You can upgrade performance parts like the engine, turbo, and brakes. More importantly, you can fine-tune your car's stance by adjusting camber, height, and offset to create the ultimate drift machine. Finish it off with custom paint for the body and rims to truly make it your own!
