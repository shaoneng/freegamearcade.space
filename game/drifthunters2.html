Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Welcome to the driver's seat of Drift Hunters 2! Your main goal is to master the art of drifting to earn as many points as possible. The longer and more stylish your drift, the higher your score multiplier and cash reward.

Use the cash you earn from drifting to buy new cars from a selection of 26 stunning vehicles or upgrade your current ride. Head to the tuning shop to customize everything from engine performance and turbo pressure to wheel camber and offset for the perfect drift setup.

Controls:

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Steer your car
  • Spacebar: Use the handbrake to initiate drifts
  • Left Shift: Shift gear up (manual)
  • Left Ctrl: Shift gear down (manual)
  • C Key: Change camera view

Select one of the 15 unique tracks and start burning rubber. Experiment with different cars and tuning setups to find what works best for your style. The more you drift, the more you earn, and the closer you get to building the ultimate drift machine!
