Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Welcome to the high-octane world of Drift Hunters 2! Your goal is to score as many drift points as possible by sliding your car sideways through corners. Start in the garage where you can pick your first car. Use the initial cash to buy new vehicles or head straight to the tuning section to upgrade your ride's performance, stance, and appearance.

Once your car is ready, select a track from the main menu. There are playgrounds for practice, challenging racetracks, and intense touge mountain passes.

Controls:

  • Accelerate: W or Up Arrow
  • Brake/Reverse: S or Down Arrow
  • Steer Left/Right: A/D or Left/Right Arrows
  • Handbrake: Space Bar
  • Shift Up/Down (Manual): Left Shift / Left Ctrl
  • Change Camera: C

Earn cash by completing long, high-angle drifts. String together combos for even bigger scores! Use your earnings to unlock all 26 cars and find the perfect setup for your driving style.

Pro Tip (Money Hack):

Need a quick cash injection? Go to the settings menu and repeatedly switch the gearbox between 'Automatic' and 'Manual'. This simple trick will add a huge amount of cash to your account, letting you buy and customize any car you want!
