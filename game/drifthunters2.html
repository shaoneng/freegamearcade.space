Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Welcome to the driver's seat of Drift Hunters 2! Your main objective is to score as many points as possible by executing epic drifts. The longer you can maintain a drift, the higher your score multiplier will climb, earning you more cash to spend in the garage.

Use the cash you earn to purchase new vehicles from a lineup of 26 incredible cars, each with unique performance characteristics. You can also head to the "PARTS" section to upgrade your car's engine, turbo, brakes, and weight to improve its on-track performance.

The "TUNING" menu is where you can truly make a car your own. Adjust the brake balance, front and rear camber, ride height, and offset to create the perfect setup for your driving style. Don't forget to visit the "VISUALS" section to customize your car's body and rim paint with a variety of colors and finishes like glossy, matte, and chrome.

When you're ready to burn some rubber, select "MAPS" from the main menu. Choose from 15 diverse tracks, including open playgrounds for practice, professional racetracks for high-speed action, and challenging "touge" mountain passes for the ultimate test of skill.

Pro Tip: Need a quick cash injection? Go to "OPTIONS" in the main menu and repeatedly toggle the gearbox setting between "Automatic" and "Manual". This will give you a massive cash bonus, allowing you to unlock and customize your dream car right from the start!
