Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Welcome to the ultimate drifting experience! Your journey begins in the garage, where you'll start with an initial budget to purchase your first car. Browse a collection of 26 epic vehicles, each with unique handling characteristics and real-life counterparts.

Once you've selected your ride, head to the 'Tuning' section. Here, you can customize everything from engine performance and suspension stance to body paint and rims. Experiment with different settings to find the perfect setup for your driving style. Lowering offset, adding negative camber, and adjusting heights can significantly improve your drifting scores.

When you're ready, hit 'Race' to choose from 15 incredible tracks, ranging from open playgrounds for practice to challenging touge mountain passes for experts. The goal is to perform long, stylish drifts to rack up points. String drifts together for combos and a higher score multiplier. The more you drift, the more cash you'll earn to buy new cars and unlock more upgrades.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to control your car: Up/W for acceleration, Down/S for braking, and Left/Right or A/D for steering. The Space Bar is your handbrake, essential for initiating sharp drifts, and the C key changes your camera view.
