Drift Hunters 2

About Drift Hunters 2

Experience the ultimate thrill of drifting without the real-world risks in Drift Hunters 2. This sequel elevates the genre with premium 3D graphics, realistic physics, and a vast array of customization options. Get ready to spend hours perfecting your ride and chasing the highest drift scores across numerous challenging tracks.

Unleash Your Inner Mechanic: In-Depth Tuning

Drift Hunters 2 offers one of the most detailed tuning systems in any browser game. Fine-tune every aspect of your car's performance and appearance:

  • Performance: Upgrade your engine, turbo, gearbox, and more to push your car to its limits.
  • Stance: Adjust wheel offset, camber, and height. A lower offset, negative camber, and higher rear height are key for achieving monster drifts.
  • Paint & Rims: Choose from an endless spectrum of colors with glossy, metallic, matte, or chrome finishes for both your car's body and its 16 different rim styles.

A Garage Full of Legends and Tracks to Conquer

The game boasts 26 epic cars, each reminiscent of real-life legends. While the powerful Porsche 993 might be the king of pure performance, don't overlook cost-effective powerhouses like the Honda S2000, which can dominate the tarmac without breaking the bank.

Test your skills on 15 unique tracks, categorized by difficulty:

  • Playgrounds: Open, flat areas like the Countryside map, perfect for beginners to learn the physics.
  • Racetracks: Mid-level tracks with hills and sharp turns, like the slippery Icefield.
  • Touge: Narrow, winding mountain passes for pro drifters. The Forest track is a true test of skill.

Pro Tip: The Instant Money Cheat

Want to unlock all cars and parts right away? Drift Hunters 2 has a simple, built-in money hack. In the main menu, go to settings and repeatedly switch the gearbox option between "Automatic" and "Manual." Each switch adds a massive amount of cash to your account. Go build your dream car instantly!
