Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Your goal in Drift Hunters 2 is to score as many points as possible by executing epic drifts. The longer and more stylish your drift, the higher your score multiplier and the more cash you'll earn. Start by picking your first car from the garage and select one of the 15 unique tracks to begin your career.

Use the cash you earn to purchase new, more powerful cars from a selection of 26 vehicles. You can also visit the tuning shop to upgrade every aspect of your ride. Enhance performance parts like the engine and turbo, adjust the stance for better drift angles, and customize the look with unique paint jobs and rims.

Controls:
• Accelerate: W or Up Arrow
• Brake/Reverse: S or Down Arrow
• Steer: A/D or Left/Right Arrows
• Handbrake: Space Bar
• Change Camera: C
• Shift Up (Manual): Left Shift
• Shift Down (Manual): Left Ctrl

Pro Tip (Money Cheat): Need a quick cash injection? Go to the main menu, open the settings, and repeatedly switch the gearbox between "Automatic" and "Manual". Each time you switch, you'll gain more cash, allowing you to buy and customize any car you want!
