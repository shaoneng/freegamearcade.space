Drift Hunters 2

About Drift Hunters 2

Drift Hunters 2 is a premium 3D driving game that takes the thrill of drifting to the next level. Experience realistic physics and enhanced graphics as you get behind the wheel. The game offers an incredible amount of depth with 26 epic cars, 15 unique tracks, and a highly detailed tuning system that will keep you engaged for hours.

In-Depth Tuning

The heart of Drift Hunters 2 lies in its customization. Dive into the tuning section and modify every aspect of your vehicle across five departments:

  • Performance: Upgrade your engine, turbo, gearbox, and more to push your car to its limits.
  • Stance: Adjust camber, offset, and height to find the perfect setup for maximum drift scores. A lower offset and negative camber are often preferred.
  • Body & Rims Paint: Choose from a full color spectrum and apply glossy, metallic, matte, or chrome finishes to create a unique look.
  • Wheels: Select from 16 different metallic rims to match your car's style.

Cars, Tracks, and a Secret

Start with a modest 25K, but aim for the best rides in the garage. While the powerful Porsche 993 is a top performer, you can achieve incredible drifts with cost-effective options like the Honda S2000. Test your skills across diverse environments, from open playgrounds perfect for practice, to challenging racetracks and the narrow, winding touge mountain passes designed for pro drifters.

Money Hack: Want to unlock everything from the start? There's a simple trick to get 1,000,000 cash! Go to the main menu, open settings, and simply switch back and forth between the manual and automatic gearbox options a few times. Check your balance and enjoy your newfound wealth. You can repeat this trick anytime!
