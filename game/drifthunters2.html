Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Your goal in Drift Hunters 2 is to score as many points as possible by executing flawless drifts. The longer you can hold a drift and the higher your angle, the more points you'll earn. Chain drifts together to build up a score multiplier for massive rewards!

Controls: Use the Arrow Keys or WASD to control your car. The Spacebar is your handbrake, crucial for initiating drifts around tight corners. Press C to cycle through different camera angles to find your preferred view. If you are using a manual gearbox, use Left Shift to shift up and Left Ctrl to shift down.

Start in the garage, where you begin with enough cash to buy your first car. Earn more money by drifting on the game's 15 unique tracks. Use that cash to buy new cars from a roster of 26 vehicles or dive into the detailed tuning menu. Here, you can upgrade performance parts, adjust the car's stance (camber, height), and customize the paint on both the body and rims to create your ultimate drift machine.

Pro Tip: For a massive head start, go into the settings menu from the main screen and rapidly click back and forth between the manual and automatic gearbox options. This will activate a money cheat, giving you 1,000,000 in cash to buy and upgrade any car you want!
