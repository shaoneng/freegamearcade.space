Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Your goal in Drift Hunters 2 is to score as many points as possible by executing long, stylish drifts. The longer you can hold a drift, the higher your score multiplier will climb!

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD to steer, accelerate, and brake. Press the Space Bar to use the handbrake for initiating sharp drifts. You can change camera views with the C key and shift gears manually with the Left Shift and Left CTRL keys if you select a manual gearbox in the settings.

Start in the garage, where you can purchase from 26 epic cars and dive into the detailed tuning menu. Customize everything from engine performance and suspension stance to body and rim paint. Once your ride is ready, head to the 'RACE' menu to choose from 15 different tracks, ranging from wide-open playgrounds to challenging mountain roads. Hit the track, burn rubber, and earn cash to unlock and upgrade even more powerful cars!
