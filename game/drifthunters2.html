Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Welcome to the ultimate drifting experience! Your goal in Drift Hunters 2 is to score as many points as possible by executing long, stylish drifts. Start your career in the garage with an initial budget to purchase your first car. Once you're on the track, use your driving skills to slide around corners and rack up points.

Controls:

  • Arrow Keys or WASD: Steer, accelerate, and brake.
  • Spacebar: Engage the handbrake to initiate a drift.
  • C: Change the camera view.
  • Left Shift: Shift up (Manual Gearbox).
  • Left Ctrl: Shift down (Manual Gearbox).

The more points you earn, the more cash you'll accumulate. Use this cash to purchase new, more powerful cars from a selection of 26 vehicles, or head to the tuning shop to upgrade your current ride. Fine-tune your car's performance, adjust its stance, and customize its appearance to create the perfect drifting machine. Test your skills across 15 unique tracks and become a legend of the drift!
