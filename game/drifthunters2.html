Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of Drift Hunters 2! Your main goal is to master the art of drifting to rack up the highest possible score. The longer and more stylish your drift, the more cash you'll earn to upgrade your ride and unlock new cars.

Controls:

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Steer your car.
  • Spacebar: Engage the handbrake to initiate a drift.
  • C: Change camera view.
  • Left Shift: Shift up (manual gearbox).
  • Left Ctrl: Shift down (manual gearbox).

Start in the garage where you can purchase one of 26 unique cars. Head to the tuning section to modify everything from performance parts to your car's stance, paint, and rims. A well-tuned car is key to achieving epic drifts. Once you're ready, select one of the 15 tracks and hit the road. Experiment on open playgrounds, tackle professional racetracks, or challenge yourself on narrow mountain touge passes.

Pro Tip (Money Hack): Need a quick cash injection? Go to the main menu, open the settings, and repeatedly switch the gearbox setting between 'Automatic' and 'Manual'. Each cycle adds a massive amount of cash to your account, letting you buy and tune any car you want!
