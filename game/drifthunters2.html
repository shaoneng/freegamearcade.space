Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Get ready to burn rubber in Drift Hunters 2! The goal is simple: score as many points as possible by executing long, stylish drifts. The longer you can hold a drift, and the more angle you get, the higher your score multiplier will climb.

Controls:

  • Drive/Steer: Use the W, A, S, D or Arrow Keys.
  • Handbrake: Press the Spacebar to initiate a drift.
  • Change Camera View: Use the C key to cycle through different camera perspectives.
  • Gear Shift (Manual): Use Left Shift (upshift) and Left Ctrl (downshift).

Start by choosing a car from the garage. You'll begin with enough cash for an entry-level vehicle. Head to one of the 15 tracks and start practicing your drifts. The cash you earn can be used to purchase new cars from a selection of 26 vehicles, or to upgrade and tune your current ride. Dive deep into the tuning menu to adjust everything from performance parts to your car's stance, paint, and rims to get the perfect setup.

Pro Tip (Money Cheat):

Want to unlock everything right away? In the main menu, go to the settings and repeatedly switch the gearbox between 'Automatic' and 'Manual'. This will grant you a massive cash bonus, allowing you to buy and customize any car you want!
