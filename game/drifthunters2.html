Drift Hunters 2

About Drift Hunters 2

Experience the adrenaline rush of extreme drifting without the real-world risks! Drift Hunters 2 brings the thrill of the track to your browser with premium 3D graphics, realistic physics, and a massive amount of content to explore. With 15 unique tracks and 26 epic cars, you'll be hooked for hours.

In-Depth Car Tuning

The highly detailed tuning section allows you to customize every aspect of your car. You have full control over:

  • Performance: Upgrade your engine, turbo, gearbox, and more.
  • Stance: Adjust wheel offset, camber, and front/rear height. Pro-tip: lower offsets, negative camber, and higher rear height are great for drifting.
  • Paint & Rims: A full color picker with glossy, metallic, matte, or chrome finishes for both the car body and the rims.
  • Wheels: Choose from 16 distinct metallic rims to perfect your car's style.

Choosing Your Ride

With 26 cars, picking the right one is key. For raw performance, the Porsche 993 is a beast, thanks to its low weight and powerful engine. If you're on a budget, the Honda S2000 offers incredible cost-efficiency, providing excellent speed and handling for its price.

Master the Tracks

The game’s 15 tracks are divided into three types based on difficulty and style:

  • Playground: Open, flat areas ideal for beginners to practice and master the game's physics.
  • Racetrack: More challenging circuits with hills and sharp turns. The slippery Icefield track is a must-try!
  • Touge: Narrow, winding mountain passes designed for expert drifters seeking the ultimate challenge. Many consider Akagi the most difficult track in the game.

Unlimited Money Cheat

Want to unlock all the cars and upgrades immediately? There's a simple money hack! Go to the Settings in the main menu and repeatedly switch the gearbox option between Manual and Automatic. Each click adds cash to your account, allowing you to build a billion-dollar fortune in minutes!
