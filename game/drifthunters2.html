Drift Hunters 2

About Drift Hunters 2

Feel the adrenaline rush and the satisfaction of a perfect drift in Drift Hunters 2, the much-anticipated sequel to the popular drifting series. If you love the thrill of extreme sports without the real-world risks, this game is for you. With enhanced 3D graphics, realistic physics, and even more in-depth tuning, get ready to hunt for the highest drift scores.

Drift Hunters 2 is a premium web-based driving game that will captivate you for hours. Dive into a garage featuring 26 epic cars, test your skills on 15 unique tracks, and lose yourself in an incredibly detailed tuning system. From performance upgrades to visual customizations, you have complete control over your ride.

The tuning section is where the magic happens. It's divided into five departments: Performance, Stance, Body Paint, Rims Paint, and Wheels. Adjust your car's stance, like camber and offset, to perfect your drift angles. Experiment with different performance parts to boost your power and control.

Make your car truly yours with a vast array of customization options. Choose any color from a detailed picker, and apply a glossy, metallic, matte, or chrome finish. Don't forget to paint your rims to match, and select from sixteen different metallic rim styles to complete the look.

The game features a wide range of cars, from the cost-effective and nimble Honda S2000 to the high-performance Porsche 993. Test your chosen vehicle across diverse tracks, including open playgrounds for practice, challenging racetracks, and narrow, winding Japanese 'touge' mountain passes for the ultimate test of skill.

Pro-Tip: Want to unlock everything right away? There's a simple trick to get 1,000,000 cash! In the main menu, go to the settings and simply switch the gearbox option between 'Manual' and 'Automatic' repeatedly. Check your account balance and enjoy your newfound wealth. You can repeat this cheat as many times as you like!
