Drift Hunters 2

About Drift Hunters 2

Get ready for the ultimate adrenaline rush with Drift Hunters 2, the sequel that takes web-based driving games to a new level. With enhanced 3D graphics and realistic physics, you'll feel every turn and slide as you hunt for the highest drift scores. The game offers immense depth with 26 epic cars, 15 unique tracks, and a highly detailed tuning system to keep you engaged for hours.

The core of the game lies in its customization. In the tuning section, you can modify your car's performance, adjust its stance for better drift control, and completely change its appearance. Choose from a full color palette with glossy, metallic, matte, or chrome finishes for both the body and rims. With 16 different rim styles, you can create a car that truly reflects your style.

Among the impressive roster of 26 cars, the Porsche 993 stands out for its raw performance, while the Honda S2000 offers the best value for newcomers, proving you don't need to break the bank to achieve amazing drifts.

Explore a variety of tracks designed for every skill level. Start on open playgrounds like Countryside to practice, move on to challenging racetracks with hills and sharp turns, and finally test your skills on narrow, winding touge mountain passes like Akagi.

Money Cheat

Want to unlock everything from the start? There's an easy money cheat! Simply go to the settings menu and repeatedly switch the gearbox option between manual and automatic. Each time you do, you'll see your cash increase, allowing you to buy and customize any car you want. You can repeat this trick as many times as you like!
