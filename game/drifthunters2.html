Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Welcome to the driver's seat of Drift Hunters 2! Your main goal is to become the ultimate drift king by earning points and cash. Start by choosing your first car from the garage. As you play, you'll earn money that you can use to purchase new, more powerful vehicles from a selection of 26 epic cars.

Controls:

  • Arrow Keys / WASD: Steer, Accelerate, Brake
  • Spacebar: Handbrake
  • C Key: Change Camera View
  • Left Shift: Shift Up (Manual)
  • Left CTRL: Shift Down (Manual)

Take your car out onto one of the 15 tracks and start drifting! The longer and more skillful your drifts, the higher your score multiplier will climb. String together combos by transitioning smoothly from one drift to another to rack up massive points. Use the handbrake to initiate sharp drifts around tight corners.

Don't forget to visit the tuning shop! You can upgrade your car's performance parts, adjust its stance (camber, height, offset), and completely customize its look with different paints and rims. Experiment with different tuning setups to find the perfect balance for your driving style.

Pro Tip: Need some extra cash? Head to the settings menu in the main menu and quickly switch between the manual and automatic gearbox options multiple times. This will grant you a hefty sum of money to buy and upgrade any car you want!
