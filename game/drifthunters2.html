Drift Hunters 2

About Drift Hunters 2 & Gameplay Guide

Drift Hunters 2 is the thrilling sequel to the popular drifting series, now with enhanced 3D graphics, realistic physics, and even more in-depth customization. Dive into a world of high-octane drifting across 15 unique tracks with a garage of 26 epic cars. Whether you're a seasoned drifter or a newcomer, this web-based driving game offers hours of adrenaline-pumping fun.

In-Depth Tuning

The heart of Drift Hunters 2 lies in its detailed tuning section. You have full control over your vehicle's performance and appearance through five departments: Performance, Stance, Body Paint, Rims Paint, and Wheels.

Adjusting your car's stance is critical for mastering drifts. You can modify front and rear camber, offset, and height. For example, a lower offset, negative camber, and higher rear height are often preferred settings for achieving high drift scores. Unleash your creativity with extensive paint options. Choose any color and apply a glossy, metallic, matte, or chrome finish to both the car body and the rims. With sixteen different rim styles to choose from, you can create a truly unique look.

Epic Cars & Tracks

The game features 26 cars, many inspired by real-life legends. For pure performance, the Porsche 993 stands out with its low weight and powerful engine. If you're looking for cost-efficiency, the Honda S2000 offers incredible value, proving you don't need to break the bank to be competitive.

Explore 15 amazing tracks divided into three categories. Playgrounds like Countryside are perfect for beginners to practice freely. Racetracks like the slippery Icefield introduce hills and sharp turns for an intermediate challenge. For the ultimate test, take on the narrow and winding Touge mountain passes like Akagi.

Secret Money Cheat

Want to unlock all the cars and upgrades instantly? There's a simple money hack! In the main menu, go to Settings. Now, simply click to switch between the Manual and Automatic gearbox options repeatedly. Each switch adds cash to your account. Go check your balance and enjoy your newfound wealth!

With its incredible customization, diverse tracks, and realistic gameplay, Drift Hunters 2 is a top-tier online drifting game. Perfect your skills, build your dream car, and dominate the leaderboards. If you love this, be sure to check out the original Drift Hunters and other drift games available to play for free!
