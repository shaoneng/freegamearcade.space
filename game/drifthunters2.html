Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Welcome to the driver's seat of Drift Hunters 2! Your main goal is to score as many points as possible by performing long, stylish drifts. The longer you can hold a drift and the higher your angle, the more points and cash you'll earn. This money is your key to unlocking the game's full potential.

Controls:

  • Accelerate: W or Up Arrow
  • Brake/Reverse: S or Down Arrow
  • Steer Left/Right: A/D or Left/Right Arrows
  • Handbrake: Spacebar (essential for initiating drifts)
  • Change Camera View: C
  • Gear Up: Left Shift
  • Gear Down: Left Ctrl

Start by selecting a track and hitting the road. As you drift, a points multiplier will build up. Chain drifts together to achieve massive combos and maximize your earnings. Be careful not to crash, as this will reset your multiplier! Use your earnings in the garage to buy new cars or access the detailed tuning menu to upgrade your vehicle's performance, adjust its stance, and change its paint and rims.
