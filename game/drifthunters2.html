Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Welcome to the ultimate drifting experience! Your journey in Drift Hunters 2 starts in the garage with your first car and 25,000 in-game cash. The main objective is to score points by performing stylish and controlled drifts across various tracks. The longer you can hold a drift, the higher your score multiplier will climb.

Use the cash you earn from drifting to purchase new cars from a lineup of 26 epic vehicles or to upgrade your current one. Dive into the detailed 'Tuning' section to enhance your car's performance with engine, brake, and turbo upgrades. You can also fine-tune your car's handling by adjusting its stance, camber, and offset to create the perfect drift machine.

Once your car is ready, choose from 15 unique tracks, including open playgrounds for practice, challenging racetracks, and intense mountain 'touge' passes. Master each environment to become the top drifter.

Pro Tip: For a quick cash boost, head to the settings menu and rapidly switch between the manual and automatic gearbox options. This will instantly grant you 1,000,000 in cash, allowing you to unlock and customize your dream car right away!
