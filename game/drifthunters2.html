Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Welcome to Drift Hunters 2, the ultimate browser-based drifting experience! Get behind the wheel and feel the adrenaline as you master the art of the drift across various challenging tracks. The goal is simple: rack up the highest drift score possible by executing long, stylish slides. The longer you hold a drift and the higher your angle, the more points you'll earn. Link drifts together to create massive combos and multiply your score!

Take control of your vehicle with intuitive keyboard commands. Use the Arrow Keys or W, A, S, D keys to steer, accelerate, and brake. Pull the Spacebar to engage the handbrake for sharp turns and initiating drifts. For those who prefer manual transmission, use the Left Shift key to shift up and the Left Ctrl key to shift down. Press C to change your camera view for the perfect perspective.

Your journey begins in the garage, where you can choose from 26 incredible cars. Use the cash you earn from drifting to purchase new vehicles and unlock their full potential. Dive into the detailed tuning menu to customize everything from performance parts like the engine and turbo, to the stance of your car with camber and offset adjustments. You can also personalize your ride with custom body and rim paint jobs, choosing from matte, glossy, metallic, or chrome finishes.

Looking for a quick start? There's a well-known trick to boost your cash flow. In the main menu, go to the settings and repeatedly switch the gearbox between 'Automatic' and 'Manual'. This simple trick can give you a massive cash injection, letting you buy and tune your dream drift car right from the beginning. Now hit the track and start burning some rubber!
