Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Welcome to the high-stakes world of Drift Hunters 2! Your main goal is to score as many points as possible by executing long, stylish drifts. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer, accelerate, and brake. The Space Bar is your handbrake, essential for initiating sharp drifts around corners. Use the C key to change your camera view for the perfect perspective.

Start your career in the garage, where you'll receive your first car. Hit the tracks to earn cash by drifting. The more points you score, the more money you make! Use your earnings to purchase new and more powerful cars from a selection of 26 epic vehicles. Each car has unique characteristics that you can master.

The real depth of Drift Hunters 2 lies in its detailed tuning system. Before you race, dive into the tuning menu to modify your car's performance, parts, stance, and visuals. Adjust everything from brake balance and turbo pressure to camber and offset to create the ultimate drift machine. Don't forget to give your ride a fresh coat of paint to stand out on the track!

Once your car is tuned to perfection, choose from 15 unique tracks. Practice your skills on open playgrounds, test your limits on professional racetracks, or tackle the challenging mountain passes of the touge maps. Each track offers a different challenge to conquer.
