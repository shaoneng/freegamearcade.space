Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

The main objective in Drift Hunters 2 is to score as many points as possible by performing long, stylish drifts. The longer you can hold a drift and the higher your angle, the more points you'll rack up. Chain drifts together to build up a massive combo multiplier for the highest scores!

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD to steer, accelerate, and brake. The Space Bar is your handbrake, essential for initiating sharp drifts. Use Left Shift to shift up and Left Ctrl to shift down if you're using a manual gearbox. Press C to change the camera view.

You'll earn cash for every drift you perform. Use this cash in the garage to purchase any of the 26 available cars or to upgrade your current ride. Dive into the detailed tuning menu to adjust performance parts like the turbo and engine, or customize your car's look with new paint and rims to create the ultimate drifting machine.

For a quick start, there's a helpful trick. In the main menu, go to the settings and repeatedly click to switch between the 'Manual' and 'Automatic' gearbox options. This will grant you a huge cash bonus, allowing you to unlock and customize your favorite cars right away!
