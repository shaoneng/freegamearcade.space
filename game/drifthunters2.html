Drift Hunters 2

How to Play Drift Hunters 2

Welcome to the ultimate drifting challenge! Your goal in Drift Hunters 2 is to score as many points as possible by performing long, stylish drifts across a variety of tracks. The longer you can hold a drift and the higher your angle, the more points you'll earn.

Start in the garage, where you can purchase your first car. As you earn more money from drifting, you can buy new cars from a selection of 26 amazing vehicles and upgrade them. Dive into the detailed tuning section to customize everything from performance parts (brakes, turbo, engine) to the car's stance (camber, offset, height) and visual appearance (body and rim paint).

Once your car is ready, select one of the 15 unique tracks and hit the road. Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer, accelerate, and brake. The spacebar is your handbrake, which is essential for initiating sharp drifts. Chain drifts together to build up your combo multiplier for massive scores!

Pro Tip: Need some extra cash to build your dream car? Head to the settings menu from the main screen and rapidly switch between the "Manual" and "Automatic" gearbox options. This will activate a money cheat, giving you a huge cash injection to unlock and tune any car you want!
