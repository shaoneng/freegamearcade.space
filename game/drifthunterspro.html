How to Play Drift Hunters Pro

Welcome to the high-octane world of Drift Hunters Pro! Your main goal is to master the art of drifting to earn points and become the ultimate racing champion. Use your keyboard to control your car: accelerate, brake, and steer into long, sweeping drifts to maximize your score.

As you accumulate points from your impressive drifts, you'll earn cash. Take this cash to the garage where you can unlock and purchase a collection of 10 different high-performance vehicles. Each car has its unique handling characteristics, so find the one that best suits your drifting style.
