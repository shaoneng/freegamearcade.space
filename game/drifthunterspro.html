Drift Hunters Pro

How to Play Drift Hunters Pro

Your goal in Drift Hunters Pro is to become the ultimate drift champion. Start by selecting your first car and hitting one of the available tracks. Use your driving skills to perform long, stylish drifts around corners. The longer and more controlled your drift, the more points and cash you will earn.

Take your earnings to the garage, which is the heart of your drifting career. Here, you can spend your cash to purchase new, more powerful cars from a lineup of 10 unique vehicles. You can also heavily modify and upgrade your existing cars. Boost performance by upgrading the engine, brakes, turbo, and more. Fine-tune your car's handling by adjusting camber, ride height, and wheel offset to perfectly suit your drifting style.

Choose between playing solo to perfect your lines on 6 different tracks or jump into the multiplayer mode. In multiplayer, you can create your own room or join others to compete against friends and players from around the world to see who is the true drift king.
