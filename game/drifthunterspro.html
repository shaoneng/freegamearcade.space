Drift Hunters Pro

How to Play Drift Hunters Pro

Get behind the wheel and become a legend of the drift! In Drift Hunters Pro, your goal is to score points by executing long, stylish drifts across a variety of tracks. The more points you score, the more cash you earn to spend on new cars and upgrades.

Controls:

  • Arrow Keys or WASD: Steer your vehicle.
  • Spacebar: Engage the handbrake to initiate a drift.
  • C Key: Cycle through different camera angles.
  • Left Shift: Shift up (manual transmission).
  • Left Ctrl: Shift down (manual transmission).

Head to the garage to use your earnings. You can purchase from a selection of 10 unique cars, each with different handling characteristics. Once you own a car, you can fully customize it. Upgrade the engine, turbo, brakes, and gearbox, or tune the suspension and camber to find the perfect setup for your driving style. You can also change the paint and rims to make your car stand out.

You can practice your skills on 6 different tracks in single-player mode or take the competition online by selecting multiplayer to create a room and drift with friends or other players from around the world.
