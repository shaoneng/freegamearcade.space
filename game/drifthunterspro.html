Drift Hunters Pro

How to Play Drift Hunters Pro

Welcome to the driver's seat of Drift Hunters Pro! Your main goal is to execute flawless drifts to score points. The longer and more stylish your drift, the more points you'll earn. Use these points to unlock and upgrade a fleet of powerful cars.

Controls: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer, accelerate, and brake. Hit the Spacebar to use the handbrake for sharp turns and to initiate drifts. You can change your camera view with the C key for the perfect perspective.

Head to the Garage to customize your ride. With 10 different vehicles to unlock, the possibilities are endless. Spend your earnings on performance upgrades for the engine, brakes, turbo, and more. You can also fine-tune your car's suspension and change its paint color and wheels to create a unique drifting machine.

Choose your challenge! Practice your skills on 6 different tracks in single-player mode, or take the competition online by selecting Multiplayer from the main menu to race and drift against other players in real-time.
