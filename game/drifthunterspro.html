Drift Hunters Pro

How to Play Drift Hunters Pro

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of Drift Hunters Pro! Your primary goal is to become a legendary drifter. Take control of your car and hit one of the many tracks. The longer and more stylish your drifts, the more points and cash you'll rack up to spend on new cars and upgrades.

Use the cash you earn from drifting to build your ultimate drift machine. Head to the Garage to browse and purchase from a selection of 10 distinct vehicles, each with its own unique handling characteristics for you to master.

The Garage is also your workshop for tuning and upgrading. Boost your car's performance by enhancing the engine, brakes, turbo, and more. You can also personalize its appearance with custom paint jobs, new wheels, and even fine-tune the suspension to achieve the perfect setup for maximum drift points.

Feeling competitive? Take your customized car online in the multiplayer mode. Create a private room for your friends or join an existing one to drift against players from all over the world and prove you're the ultimate drift king.
