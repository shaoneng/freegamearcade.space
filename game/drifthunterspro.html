Drift Hunters Pro

How to Play Drift Hunters Pro

Welcome to the high-octane world of Drift Hunters Pro! The main goal is to become the ultimate drift king. Get behind the wheel and execute long, stylish drifts on various tracks to earn points. The more impressive your drift, the more cash you'll rack up. This money is your key to unlocking a garage full of powerful cars and performance upgrades.

Use your earnings in the Garage to build your dream drift machine. You can purchase from a selection of 10 different vehicles, each with unique handling characteristics. But buying the car is just the start! Dive into the tuning menu to upgrade your engine, turbo, gearbox, and brakes. You can also fine-tune your car's setup by adjusting camber, offset, and ride height to perfectly match your drifting style. Don't forget to give your car a fresh look with a custom paint job and new wheels.

Ready to race? You can practice your skills and set high scores in single-player mode across 6 challenging tracks. When you're confident, jump into the multiplayer mode to create a room and challenge your friends or other players from around the world to see who has the best drifting skills. Master the controls, tune your ride, and leave your rivals in a cloud of smoke!

Controls:

  • W / Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • S / Down Arrow: Brake
  • A / Left Arrow: Steer Left
  • D / Right Arrow: Steer Right
  • Spacebar: Handbrake
  • C: Change Camera View
  • Left Shift: Shift Up Gear
  • Left Ctrl: Shift Down Gear
