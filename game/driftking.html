Drift King

How to Play Drift King

Welcome to the world of Drift King, where your goal is to master the art of the drift. Get behind the wheel of powerful sports cars and slide sideways through challenging tracks to score points and prove your skill. The game's stunning 3D visuals and realistic smoke effects make for an immersive experience.

Start by heading to the garage, where you can choose from 10 different sports cars. Use the comprehensive tuning options to adjust your vehicle's performance to your liking, from suspension to engine power. With 6 unique maps to explore, you'll have plenty of space to burn rubber and leave your mark.

Ready for a challenge? Jump into the multiplayer mode! Simply enter a nickname, choose a map, and create a room to play with friends. You can race, hold drifting competitions, or just cause some chaos together. It's the perfect way to test your custom-tuned car against other players.

Controls:

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Steer, accelerate, and brake
  • Space Bar: Handbrake for sharp drifts
  • C Key: Change camera view
