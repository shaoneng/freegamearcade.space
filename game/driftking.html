Drift King

How to Play Drift King

Welcome to Drift King! Your goal is to master the art of drifting across various tracks. Unlike most racing games, you start with enough cash to begin customizing your vehicle right away.

Head to the garage to choose from 10 different sports cars. Here, you can access comprehensive tuning options to adjust your car's performance, handling, and appearance to perfectly match your drifting style. Experiment with different setups to find what works best for you.

Once your car is ready, you can hit one of the 6 available maps in single-player mode to practice your skills. For a more social experience, jump into the multiplayer mode. Simply enter a nickname, choose a map, and create or join a room to drift with friends, set up competitions, or just enjoy some chaotic racing fun!
