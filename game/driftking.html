Drift King

How to Play Drift King

Get ready to burn some rubber and master the art of the drift! In Drift King, your goal is to score as many points as possible by executing perfect drifts. The longer and more stylish your drift, the more points you'll rack up.

Controls:

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Steer, accelerate, and brake.
  • Spacebar: Engage the handbrake to initiate a drift.
  • C: Change the camera view to find your perfect angle.

Getting Started:

Begin in the garage where you can choose from 10 high-performance sports cars. A key feature of Drift King is that you start with plenty of cash, so you can immediately dive into the comprehensive tuning options. Upgrade your car to create the ultimate drifting machine, then select one of the 6 unique maps. You can practice your skills solo or jump into a multiplayer room to race, compete, and crash with friends online.
