Drift Rider

How to Play Drift Rider

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of Drift Rider! Your main objective is to execute the longest and most stylish drifts possible to earn coins. Master the core mechanic: accelerate to build up speed, then sharply hit the brakes to send your car into a controlled slide.

Navigate through five distinct racetracks, from wide-open highways to narrow city streets. The more you drift, the more you earn. Take your hard-earned coins to the garage to fully customize and upgrade your ride. In the 'Parts' section, you can boost your engine, turbo, and wheels. In 'Tuning', adjust your car's height and brake balance for optimal performance. Finally, visit 'Paints' to change the color and finish of your car and rims.

Save up to purchase new vehicles from a selection of 10 powerful cars. Keep drifting, keep earning, and build the ultimate drift machine!
