Drift Rider

How to Play Drift Rider

Welcome to the high-octane world of Drift Rider! Your main goal is to become a drift champion by executing the longest and most stylish drifts to earn coins. The longer you drift, the more you earn!

To initiate a drift, accelerate your car to a high speed and then sharply apply the brakes (or use the designated drift button) as you turn into a corner. Mastering this technique is key to racking up a high score and earning valuable coins.

Use the coins you collect to fully customize and upgrade your ride. Visit the Garage from the main menu where you'll find three sections: Parts, Tuning, and Paints. In the 'Parts' section, you can upgrade your engine, turbo, and wheels to boost performance. In 'Tuning', adjust your car's suspension and brake balance for optimal drift handling. Finally, in the 'Paints' section, give your car a unique look with a wide range of colors and finishes like matte, metallic, or chrome.

As you accumulate more wealth, you can expand your collection by purchasing new cars. There are 10 incredible vehicles waiting to be unlocked, from popular sports cars to luxury rides. Choose your favorite, tune it to perfection, and dominate the five distinct racetracks!
