Drift Rider

How to Play Drift Rider

The goal in Drift Rider is to become a master of the road. Your primary objective is to execute the longest and most stylish drifts possible across five distinct racetracks to earn coins. The more you drift, the more you earn!

To initiate a drift, accelerate your vehicle to a high speed and then sharply apply the brakes (or use the designated drift button). This will cause your car to lose traction and slide, allowing you to skillfully maneuver through corners. Mastering this technique is the key to achieving high scores and maximizing your coin rewards.

Use the coins you earn in the garage to fully customize your vehicle. You can upgrade your engine, turbo, and wheels in the "Parts" section, fine-tune your car's suspension and braking in the "Tuning" section, and give your car a unique look with countless colors and finishes in the "Paints" menu.

You can also save your earnings to purchase new and more powerful vehicles. There are 10 different cars to unlock, each with its own handling characteristics. Find the car that best suits your style and dominate the leaderboards!
