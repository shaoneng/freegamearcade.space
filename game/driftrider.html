Drift Rider

How to Play Drift Rider

Welcome to Drift Rider, a high-octane 3D drifting game! Your main goal is to execute the longest and most stylish drifts to earn coins. To start drifting, get your car up to speed and then hit the brakes to slide around corners on 5 distinct racetracks, from city streets to narrow highways.

Use the coins you earn to fully customize your ride. Head to the main menu to explore your options:

  • Parts: Upgrade your car's performance by improving the engine, turbocharger, and rear wheels. Each part can be upgraded through four levels: Stock, Street, Racing, and Pro.
  • Tuning: Fine-tune your vehicle's handling. Adjust brake balance and vehicle height to achieve the perfect setup for your driving style.
  • Paint: Give your car a unique look. Choose from a vast spectrum of colors for the body and wheels, and select a finish from glossy, matte, metallic, or chrome.

As you accumulate more coins, you can also expand your garage. There are 10 different vehicles to purchase, including popular sports cars. Challenge your driving skills, master the art of the drift, and build the ultimate custom car!
