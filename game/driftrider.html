Drift Rider

How to Play Drift Rider

The main goal in Drift Rider is to execute the longest and most impressive drifts to earn coins. Take control of your car and navigate through 5 distinct racetracks, including highways, city streets, and narrow, curving routes that will push your adrenaline to the limit.

To initiate a drift, get your car up to a high speed and then sharply apply the brakes while turning. Mastering this technique is the key to racking up high scores and earning more coins to spend on awesome upgrades.

Use the coins you've earned to customize and upgrade your vehicle. In the main menu, you'll find sections for "Parts", "Tuning", and "Paints". Upgrade your engine, turbo, and wheels for better performance. Adjust your car's suspension and braking for optimal drift control, and choose from a wide array of colors and finishes to make your ride stand out.

As you accumulate more coins, you can also purchase new cars. There are 10 different vehicles to unlock, ranging from popular sports cars to luxury rides, giving you plenty of options to find your perfect drift machine.
