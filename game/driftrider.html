Drift Rider

How to Play Drift Rider

Welcome to the driver's seat of Drift Rider! Your main objective is to perform the longest and most stylish drifts to accumulate coins. To initiate a drift, accelerate to a high speed and then sharply apply the brakes to send your car sliding sideways through the corners. Master this technique across five distinct racetracks, from city streets to winding mountain roads, and watch your score soar!

Your hard-earned coins are your ticket to building the ultimate drift machine. Head to the garage to explore a deep customization system. In the "Parts" section, you can upgrade your engine, turbo, and wheels through multiple levels. In "Tuning," you can adjust your car's suspension height and brake bias for perfect handling. Finally, visit the "Paints" section to choose from a huge spectrum of colors and finishes—including matte, metallic, and chrome—for both your car's body and wheels.

Ready for a new ride? Use your coins to purchase new vehicles from the "Cars" section in the main menu. There are 10 different high-performance cars to unlock, including popular sports models and even luxury vehicles. Choose your favorite, customize it to your liking, and hit the track to set new records!
