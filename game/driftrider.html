Drift Rider

How to Play Drift Rider

Welcome to the high-stakes world of Drift Rider! Your main objective is to become a drifting legend by executing the longest and most stylish drifts to earn coins. The longer your drift, the more coins you'll accumulate.

To start drifting, accelerate your vehicle to a high speed. Once you're moving fast, sharply apply the brakes to kick the car's rear out and initiate a drift. Master controlling the slide to link drifts together and maximize your score on 5 distinct racetracks, including highways and tight city streets.

Use the coins you earn in the garage to create your ultimate drift machine. You can visit three main sections for customization:

  • Parts: Upgrade your car's performance by improving the engine, turbocharger, and wheels. Progress through Stock, Street, Racing, and Pro level parts.
  • Tuning: Fine-tune your vehicle's handling. Adjust your car's height and brake balance to get the perfect setup for sliding sideways.
  • Paints: Customize the look of your car and wheels with a massive color palette and finishes like glossy, matte, metallic, or chrome.

As you save up more coins, you can also purchase entirely new vehicles from the 'Cars' section. With 10 different sports cars to unlock, the road to becoming the top Drift Rider is yours to conquer!
