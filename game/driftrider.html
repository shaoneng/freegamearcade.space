Drift Rider

How to Play Drift Rider

Welcome to the high-octane world of Drift Rider! The main objective is to execute the longest and most spectacular drifts to score points and earn coins. Choose from 5 distinct racetracks, including city streets and challenging intercity routes, and get ready to burn some rubber.

To initiate a drift, accelerate your vehicle to a high speed. Once you're moving fast, sharply apply the brakes to send your car into a controlled slide. The longer you can maintain the drift, the more coins you'll accumulate. Master this technique to dominate the tracks!

Use the coins you earn in the garage to fully customize and upgrade your ride. In the 'Parts' section, you can improve your engine, turbo, and wheels. The 'Tuning' section lets you adjust your car's suspension and brake balance for optimal drift performance. Finally, head to the 'Paint' shop to give your car a unique look with a wide range of colors and finishes. You can also use your coins to purchase 10 different high-performance vehicles!
