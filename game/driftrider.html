Drift Rider

How to Play Drift Rider

The main goal in Drift Rider is to become a drift king by performing the longest and most stylish drifts to earn coins. To start a drift, get your car up to a good speed and then hit the brakes to send the back end sliding. Control your slide through the corners of 5 different tracks, including highways and city streets, to maximize your score and your earnings.

Use the coins you earn to build your ultimate drift machine! Head to the garage from the main menu where you can access three key areas: Parts, Tuning, and Paints. In the 'Parts' section, you can upgrade your engine, turbo, and wheels through multiple levels (Stock, Street, Racing, and Pro) for a serious performance boost.

Fine-tune your vehicle's handling in the 'Tuning' section by adjusting brake balance and ride height. For the ultimate personalization, visit the 'Paints' section. Choose from a vast color spectrum and apply different finishes like glossy, matte, metallic, or chrome to both your car's body and wheels. You can also use your earnings to purchase and unlock 10 different high-performance vehicles.
