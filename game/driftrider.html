Drift Rider

How to Play Drift Rider

The main goal in Drift Rider is to execute the longest and most stylish drifts possible to earn coins. Get your car up to speed and then hit the brakes to initiate a drift. Control your slide through the corners on 5 distinct racetracks to maximize your score and earnings.

Use the coins you earn in the garage to create your ultimate drift machine. The garage is split into three main sections: Parts, Tuning, and Paints. You can also use your coins to purchase entirely new vehicles from a selection of 10 high-performance cars.

In the 'Parts' section, you can boost your car's performance. Upgrade key components like the engine, turbocharger, and rear wheels through multiple levels—from Stock to Street, Racing, and finally Pro—to gain a competitive edge.

Head to the 'Tuning' section to fine-tune your car's handling. Adjust settings like brake balance and suspension height to get the perfect setup for your driving style. In the 'Paint' section, unleash your creativity by choosing custom colors for your car's body and wheels, and select from finishes like matte, metallic, or chrome.
