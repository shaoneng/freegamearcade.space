Drift Rider

How to Play Drift Rider

The main objective in Drift Rider is to master the art of drifting to earn as many coins as possible. To initiate a drift, accelerate your vehicle to a high speed and then sharply apply the brakes. The longer and more controlled your drift, the more coins you will accumulate. Test your skills across 5 different tracks, including highways and winding city streets.

Use the coins you earn to fully customize and upgrade your ride. In the garage, you'll find three main sections. Visit 'Parts' to upgrade your engine, turbo, and wheels through multiple levels (Stock, Street, Racing, and Pro) for better performance. Head to the 'Tuning' section to adjust your car's suspension height and brake balance for the perfect drift setup.

Make your car truly unique in the 'Paints' section. Choose from a vast color spectrum and select different finishes like matte, metallic, or chrome for both the body and the wheels. As you build up your fortune, you can also purchase new vehicles from a selection of 10 incredible cars in the 'Cars' menu. Save up, upgrade, and hit the road to become the ultimate Drift Rider!
