Drift Rider

How to Play Drift Rider

Welcome to the high-octane world of Drift Rider! Your main goal is to become a master drifter by performing the longest drifts possible to earn coins. To start drifting, accelerate your vehicle to a high speed, then tap the brakes to kick the back end out and slide through the corners. Control your slide to rack up points and build your fortune.

The coins you earn are your ticket to a fully customized ride. Head to the garage to spend your earnings. In the 'Parts' section, you can upgrade your engine, turbo, and wheels through multiple levels (Stock, Street, Racing, and Pro) for better performance. In 'Tuning,' adjust your car's height and brake balance to perfect your drifting setup. Finally, visit the 'Paint' section to choose from a vast array of colors and finishes, including matte, metallic, and chrome, to make your car truly unique.

Put your skills and your customized car to the test on 5 distinct racetracks. Whether you're navigating a narrow, winding road or tearing up the streets of a city, each track offers a new challenge to conquer. As you earn more, you can also purchase new vehicles from a selection of 10 different cars to find your perfect drifting machine.
