Drunken Duel

How to Play Drunken Duel

Welcome to the wobbly world of Drunken Duel, a hilarious ragdoll shooting game where physics is not on your side! The objective is straightforward: be the first player to successfully shoot your opponent and score five points to claim victory in the duel.

You can enjoy this game in two different modes. Challenge the computer in single-player mode to hone your unsteady aim, or grab a friend and go head-to-head in the exciting 2-player mode, sharing the same device for some laugh-out-loud competition.

Controlling your character is part of the fun and the challenge. Both duelists are unsteady on their feet and their arms spin wildly, making aiming a true test of skill and timing. Press the designated key to fire your weapon and try to land a shot on the other player. Master the chaotic movement, out-shoot your rival, and become the Drunken Duel champion!
