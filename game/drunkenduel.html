Drunken Duel

How to Play Drunken Duel

The goal in Drunken Duel is to out-shoot your opponent in a wacky, physics-based showdown. To win the duel, you must be the first player to score five points by successfully shooting your rival.

You control a wobbly ragdoll character whose arms are constantly spinning. Your challenge is to time your shot perfectly to hit your opponent. Due to the hilarious dummy physics, aiming is part of the fun and chaos, so timing is everything!

Drunken Duel can be enjoyed in two ways: challenge the computer-controlled AI in single-player mode, or go head-to-head with a friend in the 2-player mode on the same device. Each victory brings you closer to ultimate, tipsy bragging rights.
