Drunken Duel

How to Play Drunken Duel

The goal in Drunken Duel is simple yet hilarious: be the first player to score five hits on your opponent to win the duel. Based on ragdoll physics, controlling your character is part of the fun and challenge!

You control a wobbly character who's had a bit too much to drink. Your arms will be spinning wildly, making it a challenge to aim your weapon. You must time your shots perfectly to hit your equally unsteady opponent. One wrong move and you could be the one on the ground!

You can take on the computer-controlled opponent in single-player mode, or you can challenge a friend in the chaotic 2-player mode on the same computer or device. Each successful hit gets you one point closer to victory and bragging rights!
