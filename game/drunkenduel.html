Drunken Duel

How to Play Drunken Duel

Welcome to the wacky world of Drunken Duel, where controlling your wobbly character is half the fun! The goal is simple: be the first player to successfully shoot your opponent five times to win the duel.

In this hilarious physics-based game, both characters are unsteady on their feet and their arms are constantly spinning. Your challenge is to time your shot perfectly to hit your target. You can choose to face off against an AI opponent in single-player mode or challenge a friend for a laugh-out-loud two-player battle on the same computer or device.

Each round presents a new, unpredictable challenge. Master the art of the wobbly aim, take down your rival, and claim victory in the most ridiculous duel you'll ever fight!
