Drunken Duel

How to Play Drunken Duel

Welcome to the wacky world of Drunken Duel! The goal is simple but hilarious: be the first player to shoot your opponent and score five points. Each hit earns you a point, and the first to five wins the duel and the bragging rights!

This game is all about physics-based chaos. Your character is a wobbly ragdoll, making aiming a real challenge. Your arms will spin wildly, and your legs will barely keep you upright. Time your shots carefully to hit your equally unstable opponent.

Drunken Duel can be enjoyed in two ways. You can challenge the computer in 1-Player mode for a solo test of your tipsy sharpshooting skills, or you can go head-to-head with a friend in 2-Player mode on the same device for some laugh-out-loud competitive fun.

Controls: Use the designated keys or on-screen buttons (one for each player) to fire your weapon. Master the timing of your spinning arm to land the perfect shot!
