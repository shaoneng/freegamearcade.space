Drunken Duel

How to Play Drunken Duel

Welcome to the hilarious world of Drunken Duel! In this physics-based shooting game, you control a wobbly ragdoll character who's had a bit too much to drink. The result is a chaotic and unpredictable duel where just staying upright is part of the challenge.

The objective is simple: be the first player to successfully shoot your opponent and score five points. Your character's arms will spin uncontrollably, so you'll need perfect timing and a bit of luck to land your shots. Each successful hit gets you one step closer to victory in this comical showdown.

Drunken Duel offers two exciting game modes. You can challenge the computer in a solo match or grab a friend and go head-to-head in the 2-player mode on the same device. Prepare for endless laughter as you both struggle to aim and shoot while wobbling all over the screen.
