Drunken Duel

How to Play Drunken Duel

Welcome to the hilarious and unsteady world of Drunken Duel! This is a one-on-one physics-based shooting game where controlling your wobbly character is half the fun. The objective is simple: be the first player to score five points by successfully shooting your opponent.

You can challenge the computer in 1-Player mode or grab a friend for a chaotic 2-Player duel on the same device. Each character's arms will spin uncontrollably, making aiming a true test of timing and luck. Wait for the perfect moment when your gun is pointing at your rival and fire! The first to reach a score of five wins the duel and the ultimate bragging rights.

Remember, these ragdolls are hard to control, so expect plenty of missed shots and laugh-out-loud moments. Embrace the chaos and see who can master the art of the drunken shootout!
