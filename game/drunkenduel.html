Drunken Duel

How to Play Drunken Duel

Welcome to the wobbly world of Drunken Duel! The goal is simple but hilarious: be the first player to score five points against your opponent. You and your rival are ragdoll characters who've had a bit too much to drink, making aiming a real challenge!

Your character's arms will spin continuously. Your job is to time your shot perfectly to hit the other player. One successful hit earns you a point. The first to reach a score of five wins the duel. The unpredictable physics is part of the fun, so expect some wild misses and surprising hits!

You can enjoy this game in two modes. Challenge the computer in 1-player mode to hone your skills, or grab a friend and battle it out in 2-player mode on the same device for some laugh-out-loud competition.
