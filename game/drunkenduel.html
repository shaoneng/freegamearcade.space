Drunken Duel

How to Play Drunken Duel

Welcome to Drunken Duel, a hilarious physics-based shooting game where the goal is simple: be the first to defeat your opponent!

Your characters are, well, a little tipsy, leading to incredibly comical and unpredictable movements. Use their wobbly ragdoll physics to your advantage (or disadvantage!) as you try to aim your shots.

The objective is to score five hits on your opponent to win the round. You can choose to challenge the computer AI for a solo experience or invite a friend to play head-to-head on the same device for maximum laughs. Master the controls of your spinning, weak-legged character and land those tricky shots to claim victory!
