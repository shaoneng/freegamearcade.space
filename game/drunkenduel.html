Drunken Duel

How to Play Drunken Duel

Welcome to the hilarious world of Drunken Duel! This game is all about chaotic physics and unpredictable shootouts. Your objective is simple: be the first player to score five points against your opponent to win the duel.

You control a ragdoll character who has had a bit too much to drink, making aiming a real challenge! Your character will sway and spin, and you must time your shot perfectly to hit the other player. You can choose to play against an AI opponent in 1-Player mode or challenge a friend right on the same device in 2-Player mode for even more laughs.

Master the wobbly controls, take aim, and fire away. Each hit brings you closer to victory in this silly and entertaining physics-based game. Good luck, and may the steadiest hand win!
