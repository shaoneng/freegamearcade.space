Drunken Duel

How to Play Drunken Duel

Welcome to the wobbly world of Drunken Duel! This game is all about hilarious, physics-based combat where two inebriated characters try to shoot each other. The objective is simple but challenging: be the first player to score five hits on your opponent to win the duel.

You can choose to face off against a computer-controlled opponent in single-player mode or challenge a friend for a laugh-out-loud 2-player battle on the same computer. Control your character's spinning arms and unsteady legs to aim your weapon. Due to the ragdoll physics, aiming is part of the fun and the challenge!

Master the chaotic movements, time your shots perfectly, and take down your rival to claim victory. The first to reach a score of five wins the match. Good luck, and try not to fall over!
