Drunken Duel

How to Play Drunken Duel

Welcome to the wacky world of Drunken Duel, a hilarious physics-based shooting game where control is just a suggestion! You take control of a wobbly ragdoll character whose only goal is to shoot their equally unstable opponent. The first player to successfully land five shots on their rival wins the duel and claims bragging rights.

The gameplay is simple but chaotic. Your character will sway and spin uncontrollably, making aiming a true challenge. Your task is to time your shot perfectly to hit the other player. You can choose to face off against an intelligent AI opponent in single-player mode or challenge a friend in the local 2-player mode on the same device. Embrace the floppy physics and prepare for some laugh-out-loud moments!
