Drunken Duel

How to Play Drunken Duel

Welcome to the hilarious and chaotic world of Drunken Duel! The goal is simple but challenging: be the first player to shoot your opponent and score five points to win the duel.

You can choose to play in two different modes:

  • 1 Player Mode: Face off against a computer-controlled opponent (AI) and test your unsteady aim.
  • 2 Player Mode: Challenge a friend on the same computer for a side-splitting local multiplayer battle.

Your character will sway and spin uncontrollably due to the game's wacky ragdoll physics. Your only action is to shoot. Time your shot perfectly to hit your opponent while they are also wobbling around. The first to reach a score of 5 wins the match and the bragging rights!
