Drunken Duel

How to Play Drunken Duel

Welcome to the chaotic world of Drunken Duel! The goal is simple but hilarious: be the first player to successfully shoot your opponent five times to win the match. The challenge? Both you and your opponent are hilariously wobbly, making aiming a true test of skill and luck.

You can face off against a computer-controlled opponent in single-player mode or challenge a friend in the 2-player mode on the same device. Use your designated key to fire your weapon. Time your shot perfectly as your arm spins uncontrollably. Master the ragdoll physics to out-shoot your tipsy rival and claim victory!
