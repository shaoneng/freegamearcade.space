How to Play DTA 6

Welcome to the frosty, crime-ridden streets of DTA 6! You take on the role of Jack, a fearless gang member trying to make a name for himself in a sprawling, snow-covered city. Your objective is to explore, survive, and cause as much mayhem as you wish in this open-world sandbox.

Use your controls to navigate Jack through the city on foot or behind the wheel of a stolen vehicle. Feel free to explore every corner, but be prepared for action. You can engage in intense shootouts with rival gangs or law enforcement. Evading the police is a key part of survival, so master your driving skills to outmaneuver them in high-speed chases.
