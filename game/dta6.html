How to Play DTA 6

Welcome to the lawless, snow-covered streets of DTA 6! You take on the role of Jack, a skilled gang member trying to make a name for himself. The objective is simple: survive and thrive in a city where chaos reigns supreme. Use your keyboard and mouse to control Jack's movements and actions.

Explore the vast open world on foot or by "borrowing" any vehicle you come across. From sports cars to trucks, each vehicle offers a different driving experience. Engage in thrilling police chases, participate in intense shootouts with rival factions, or simply explore the city to find hidden collectibles and secret areas.
