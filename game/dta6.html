How to Play DTA 6

Welcome to the action-packed streets of DTA 6! Your objective is to survive and thrive as the notorious gang member, Jack, in a vast, snow-covered city. The game is an open-world sandbox, meaning you are free to explore and create your own chaos.

Use your keyboard and mouse to control Jack. Navigate the city on foot or by "borrowing" any car you see. As you explore, you'll discover various opportunities for adventure and mischief. Keep an eye out for Jack's two special abilities, which can give you an edge in tight situations. There's no set path, so dive in, explore the city, and see how long you can last in this free-roaming world of crime!
