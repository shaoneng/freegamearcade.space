How to Play DTA 6

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled streets of DTA 6! Your objective is to thrive as the daring gang member, Jack, in a sprawling, snow-covered city. Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the open world. You can walk, run, and explore every corner of the map to discover new opportunities for chaos and adventure.

To get around faster, approach any vehicle and press the designated key to steal it. Once inside, you'll use standard driving controls to cruise through the city, evade police, or chase down rivals. Be prepared for action! When confrontations arise, use your mouse to aim and click to fire your weapon in intense shootouts with other gangs or law enforcement.

Keep an eye on your health and be strategic in your encounters. The city is your playground—explore freely, stir up trouble, and see how long you can dominate the streets. Note that DTA 6 contains mature themes and is intended for older players.
