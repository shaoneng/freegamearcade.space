How to Play DTA 6

Welcome to the intense, open world of DTA 6! You step into the shoes of Jack, a daring gang member trying to make a name for himself in a vast, snow-covered city. The core of the game is exploration and freedom. Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the city streets, either on foot or by "borrowing" any car you see.

Your goal is to survive and thrive through chaos. Engage in thrilling police chases by outmaneuvering law enforcement, or stand your ground in action-packed shootouts with rival gangs and police.
