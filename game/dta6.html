How to Play DTA 6

In DTA 6, you step into the shoes of Jack, a gang member in a sprawling, snowy city. Your goal is to navigate this open world, embracing the chaos and freedom it offers. You can explore the city, take on impromptu missions, or simply see how much mayhem you can create before the police catch up to you.

Use the standard WASD keys to move Jack on foot and the mouse to look around and aim. To get around faster, approach any vehicle and press the designated key (usually 'F' or 'E') to steal it. Once inside, use the same WASD or arrow keys to drive. Engage in combat by using your mouse to aim and the left-click to shoot.

Be mindful of the police! Causing too much trouble will increase your wanted level, bringing more aggressive law enforcement after you. Use your driving skills and Jack's special abilities to evade them and survive as long as possible.
