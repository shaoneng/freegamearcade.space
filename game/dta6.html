DTA 6

How to Play DTA 6

Welcome to the lawless, snow-covered streets of DTA 6! Your objective is to survive and thrive as the daring gang member, Jack. The city is your playground, offering endless opportunities for action and chaos. Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the open world.

Explore the city on foot or "borrow" any vehicle you find to get around faster. Be prepared for intense encounters with rival gangs and the police. Engage in shootouts, complete daring missions, and cause as much mayhem as you can. Keep an eye on your health and be ready to evade the police when your wanted level gets too high. The longer you survive, the more legendary you become. Good luck!

Note: This game contains mature themes, including violence and strong language, and is intended for mature audiences.
