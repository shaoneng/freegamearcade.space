How to Play DTA 6

Welcome to the lawless, snow-covered streets of DTA 6! Your goal is to thrive as the daring gang member, Jack, in a vast open world filled with action and chaos. Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the city, interact with the environment, and control your character.

Movement: Use the standard WASD keys to walk around the city.

Driving: Approach any vehicle and press the designated key (usually 'F' or 'Enter') to steal it. Once inside, use the same WASD keys to drive, steer, accelerate, and brake.

Combat: Engage in shootouts with rival gangs or evade the police. Use your mouse to aim and the left-click button to shoot. Be mindful of your health and ammo.
