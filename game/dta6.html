How to Play DTA 6

Welcome to the lawless, wintery metropolis of DTA 6! Your goal is to survive and thrive as the daring gang member, Jack. Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate this open world of crime and chaos.

Use the WASD keys to move Jack on foot. To steal a vehicle, walk up to it and press the 'F' key. Once you're in the driver's seat, the WASD keys will control the car's movement.

When things get heated, use your mouse to aim your weapon and the left mouse button to fire. Take on rival gangs, evade the police, or simply explore the vast, snow-covered city at your leisure. The more trouble you cause, the higher your wanted level will get, so be prepared for intense police chases!

Keep an eye out for opportunities and don't forget about Jack's special abilities to gain an advantage in dangerous situations. Let the chaos begin!
