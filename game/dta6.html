How to Play DTA 6

Welcome to the lawless, snow-covered streets of DTA 6! You step into the shoes of Jack, a daring gang member ready to make his mark on the city. Your primary goal is to survive and thrive in this open-world sandbox. Explore the city, find opportunities for mischief, and see how much chaos you can cause.

Navigate the world on foot using your keyboard's movement keys. When you find a car you like, approach it to steal it and get behind the wheel. The controls will then adapt for driving, allowing you to speed through the streets, drift around corners, and evade your pursuers. When conflict arises, use your mouse to aim and fire your weapons in thrilling shootouts.

As you play, you'll uncover Jack's two special abilities, giving you an edge in tight situations. The longer you survive, the more intense the action gets!
