How to Play DTA 6

Welcome to the sprawling, snow-covered world of DTA 6! Your goal is to thrive as the daring gang member, Jack. The city is your playground, filled with opportunities for chaos and adventure. Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the streets, either on foot or by "borrowing" any car you see.

Engage in high-octane action by starting missions, evading persistent police forces, or taking on rival gangs in intense shootouts. Keep an eye on your health and wanted level as you cause mayhem. Explore every corner of the city to discover hidden secrets and new ways to stir up trouble. Remember to utilize Jack's two special abilities to get an edge in tight situations. The choice is yours: will you become the most notorious figure in the city or just another casualty? Dive in and let the chaos begin!

⚠️ Mature Content Warning: This game contains intense action, violence, and adult themes suitable for mature audiences only. Player discretion is advised.
