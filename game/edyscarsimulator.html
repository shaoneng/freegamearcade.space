Edys Car Simulator

How to Play Edys Car Simulator

Welcome to Edys Car Simulator, where you have an entire city to explore at your leisure! The primary goal is to enjoy the freedom of the open road. Start by selecting your favorite vehicle and then hit the streets to drive, drift, and discover everything the city has to offer.

You have a diverse garage at your disposal with five different vehicles to choose from. Feel the speed in one of two high-performance sports cars, tackle any terrain with two powerful pick-up trucks, or take a more relaxed cruise in a large city bus. Each vehicle offers a unique driving experience.

For an extra thrill, locate and enter the "Test Track" area. This sandy, off-road playground is filled with ramps, jumps, and challenging terrain designed to push your driving skills to the limit and add a dose of adrenaline to your gameplay.

Looking for a specific task? Find the gray stone and press the 'E' key to interact with it. You can lift and load it onto the back of one of the pick-up trucks to test its hauling power.
