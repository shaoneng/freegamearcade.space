Edys Car Simulator

How to Play Edys Car Simulator

Welcome to the open world of Edys Car Simulator! The goal is simple: explore, experiment, and have fun. When you start, you can choose from 5 different vehicles to drive, including two sports cars, two pickup trucks, and a city bus. Drive freely around the sprawling city streets and discover everything it has to offer.

For a more adrenaline-fueled experience, find the "Enter Test Track" area. This sandy off-road course is filled with ramps, jumps, and challenging terrain perfect for testing your vehicle's limits. If you're driving one of the pickup trucks, you can also perform a load test. Find the gray stone block and press the 'E' key to pick it up and place it in the truck's bed. Enjoy the freedom of the road!
