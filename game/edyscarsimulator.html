Edys Car Simulator

How to Play Edys Car Simulator

Welcome to Edys Car Simulator, a free-roaming driving game where the city is your personal playground. Your main goal is to explore, drive, and have fun! Begin by choosing your favorite vehicle from the selection available. You have five options: two high-speed sports cars, two versatile pick-up trucks, and a city bus.

For a more adventurous experience, locate and drive to the "Enter Test Track" area. This sandy zone is filled with challenging ramps, jumps, and uneven terrain designed to test your driving abilities and provide an adrenaline rush. The unique physics of driving on sand adds an extra layer of difficulty and excitement.

While exploring, you can also interact with certain objects. Look for a large gray stone; by driving one of the pick-up trucks next to it and pressing the 'E' key, you can lift the stone and load it into the truck's bed for a fun physics-based challenge.

Use standard keyboard controls to navigate: WASD or Arrow Keys for steering, acceleration, and braking. Press 'E' for special interactions like loading the stone. Feel free to experiment with different vehicles to see how they handle the city streets and the rugged test track.
