Edys Car Simulator

How to Play Edys Car Simulator

Welcome to Edys Car Simulator, where the entire city is your personal playground! There are no missions or time limits, just pure driving freedom. Your main goal is to explore, test different vehicles, and have fun.

Begin by choosing your ride from a selection of 5 unique vehicles: two high-speed sports cars, two versatile pick-up trucks, and a large city bus. Each vehicle offers a different driving experience.

Use the standard driving controls (WASD or Arrow Keys) to accelerate, brake, and steer your vehicle. For an extra challenge, locate the gray stone in the city. Drive a pick-up truck next to it and press the 'E' key to load it onto the truck bed to test the vehicle's physics.

Looking for more adrenaline? Find the "Enter Test Track" area. This sandy zone is filled with ramps, jumps, and uneven terrain designed to push your car, and your driving skills, to the absolute limit. See what kind of crazy stunts you can pull off!
