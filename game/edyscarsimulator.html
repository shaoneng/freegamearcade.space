How to Play Edys Car Simulator

Welcome to Edys Car Simulator, where an entire city becomes your personal driving playground! The core of the game is freedomâ€”explore the streets, discover hidden corners, and drive however you want without rules or objectives.

To begin your adventure, choose from one of the five available vehicles. You have a choice between two sleek sports cars for high-speed thrills, two versatile pick-up trucks for off-road fun, and even a large city bus for a completely different driving experience.

For an extra dose of adrenaline, head over to the 'Enter Test Track' area. This sandy, off-road zone is filled with ramps, hills, and challenging terrain perfect for testing your vehicle's limits. For an interactive challenge, try using one of the pick-ups to move the large gray stone by pressing the 'E' key to lift and load it.

Ultimately, the goal is to have fun! Whether you're practicing your driving, performing wild stunts on the test track, or just enjoying a relaxing sightseeing tour, the city is yours to command. For the most immersive experience, we recommend playing with the sound on!
