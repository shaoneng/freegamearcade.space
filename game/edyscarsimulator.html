Edys Car Simulator

How to Play Edys Car Simulator

Welcome to Edys Car Simulator, where you have an entire city to explore at your leisure! The goal is simple: have fun driving. There are no missions or time limits, just pure freedom on the open road.

Begin by choosing your ride from a selection of five different vehicles. Pick between two sleek sports cars, two versatile pick-up trucks, or even a large city bus to cruise the streets in style.

For a more thrilling adventure, find the "Enter Test Track" area. This sandy zone is filled with ramps, jumps, and uneven terrain designed to test your driving skills and add a shot of adrenaline to your gameplay.

If you're driving a pick-up truck, you can try the loading test. Find the large gray stone and press the 'E' key to lift and move it, then try loading it into the back of your truck for an extra challenge!
