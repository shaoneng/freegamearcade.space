Edys Car Simulator

How to Play Edys Car Simulator

Welcome to Edys Car Simulator, where you have the ultimate freedom to drive! There are no races to win or missions to complete. Your only goal is to explore and have fun. Start by choosing your ride from a selection of 5 vehicles: two sports cars, two pickups, and a bus.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your chosen vehicle. Explore the open city streets or, for a more thrilling experience, find the "Enter Test Track" area. This sandy zone is filled with ramps, jumps, and challenging terrain that will test your driving prowess and add a shot of adrenaline to your gameplay.

If you choose one of the two pickup trucks, you can try out a special feature. Locate the gray stone block on the map. By driving near it and pressing the 'E' key, you can lift the stone and load it onto the back of your truck for a weight-loading challenge. Enjoy the freedom of the open road!
