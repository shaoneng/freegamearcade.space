Edys Car Simulator

How to Play Edys Car Simulator

Welcome to Edys Car Simulator! The city is your playground in this open-world driving experience. There are no strict goals or missions; you are free to explore the streets, discover hidden areas, and drive however you please. Start by choosing one of the five available vehicles to begin your adventure.

You have a diverse garage at your disposal, featuring two high-speed sports cars, two versatile pickup trucks, and a large city bus. For an extra dose of adrenaline, locate the 'Enter Test Track' area. This sandy, off-road zone is filled with ramps, jumps, and uneven terrain perfect for testing your vehicle's limits.

Want to try a unique challenge? Use one of the pickup trucks for a loading test. Find the large gray stone, approach it, and press the 'E' key to lift it and place it in the truck's bed. See how your truck's handling changes with the extra weight as you navigate the city or the test track!
