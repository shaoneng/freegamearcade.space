Edys Car Simulator

How to Play Edys Car Simulator

Welcome to the open-world driving freedom of Edys Car Simulator! The main goal is to explore and have fun in a vast city that's all yours. There are no missions or time limits, just pure driving enjoyment.

Start by choosing your ride from five distinct vehicle options: two high-speed sports cars, two versatile pickup trucks, and a large city bus. Each vehicle offers a unique driving experience.

Once you're on the road, you can drive anywhere you like. For a more thrilling challenge, locate the "Enter Test Track" area. This special zone is filled with sandy dunes, steep hills, and exciting ramps designed to test your driving skills and add a shot of adrenaline to your gameplay.

If you've chosen one of the pickup trucks, you can try out the cargo-loading feature. Find the large gray stone, drive up next to it, and press the 'E' key to lift and load it into the truck's bed. It's a great way to test the vehicle's handling and physics with extra weight.
