Edys Car Simulator

How to Play Edys Car Simulator

Welcome to Edys Car Simulator, where the entire city is your personal playground! The objective is simple: choose a vehicle and drive freely wherever you want. There are no rules or missions, just pure driving freedom.

Start by selecting one of the five available vehicles: two high-speed sports cars, two versatile pickup trucks, or a large city bus. Each vehicle offers a unique driving experience.

For a real challenge, head to the "Enter Test Track" area. This sandy zone is filled with ramps, jumps, and uneven terrain perfect for performing stunts and testing your car's limits. You can also interact with objects in the world—find the gray stone and press the 'E' key to pick it up and load it into the back of a pickup truck for a fun cargo challenge.
