How to Play Edys Car Simulator

Welcome to the open-world driving freedom of Edys Car Simulator! The main objective is to explore and enjoy the vast city at your own pace. Begin by choosing your preferred vehicle from a selection of five options, which includes two high-performance sports cars, two versatile pickup trucks, and a large city bus. Each vehicle offers a unique driving experience.

Once you've selected your ride, the entire city is yours to discover. Drive through the streets, take in the sights, or push your car to its limits. For a more thrilling challenge, locate the "Enter Test Track" area. This special zone is a sandy playground filled with ramps, jumps, and uneven terrain perfect for performing stunts and testing your vehicle's suspension.

You can also interact with objects in the world. Find the gray stone and press the 'E' key to lift and move it. Try loading it into the back of one of the pickup trucks for an extra physics-based challenge. There are no strict rules or missionsâ€”just pure driving enjoyment!
