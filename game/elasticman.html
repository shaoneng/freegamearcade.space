Elastic Man

How to Play Elastic Man

Playing Elastic Man is incredibly simple and intuitive, making it a perfect game for quick stress relief. There are no complex rules or objectives to follow. Your only goal is to interact with the elastic face on the screen and have fun!

Simply use your mouse cursor to engage with the character's face. Click and hold any part of the face—the eyes, nose, cheeks, or chin—and drag your mouse to stretch and pull it in any direction. You can poke it, squish it, and see how far you can warp its features.

No matter what you do, the face will always return to its original state thanks to the fun physics engine. For a more detailed visual experience, you can switch the game's quality to 'High' using the option in the top-right corner of the game screen.
