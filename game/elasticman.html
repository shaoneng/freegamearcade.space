Elastic Man

How to Play Elastic Man

Playing Elastic Man is all about fun and stress relief, with incredibly simple controls. There are no complex rules or objectives to worry about. Your goal is simply to interact with the elastic face on the screen.

Use your mouse cursor to grab any part of the face—the nose, eyes, ears, or cheeks. Click and hold the mouse button, then drag in any direction to stretch and distort the face in hilarious ways. Release the mouse button to watch it snap back into place thanks to the game's satisfying physics engine.

Feel free to pull, poke, and twist the face as much as you like. For a more detailed visual experience, you can toggle the graphics quality between medium and high using the button at the top right of the game screen.
