Elastic Man

How to Play Elastic Man

Playing Elastic Man is incredibly simple and intuitive, requiring no complex controls. The goal is simply to have fun and relieve stress by interacting with Morty's elastic face.

To play, you only need your mouse or cursor. Click and drag on any part of Morty's face to stretch, pull, poke, and distort it. Watch as the physics engine makes his features stretch and deform in humorous ways. No matter how much you pull, his face will always return to its original state, allowing for endless experimentation.

You can also opt to change the game quality from medium to high mode for a better visual experience, by tapping on the option located in the upper right side of the play screen. Enjoy the freedom to manipulate this elastic man's face and let off some steam!
