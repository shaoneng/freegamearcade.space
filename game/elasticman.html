Elastic Man

How to Play Elastic Man

Welcome to Elastic Man, the ultimate digital stress ball! There are no complex rules, levels, or objectives here. The goal is simply to have fun and relieve some stress by interacting with a hilariously stretchable face.

The controls are incredibly simple. Just use your mouse cursor to play:

  • Click and Hold: Grab any part of the face.
  • Drag your Mouse: Stretch, pull, and contort the face in any direction you desire.
  • Release the Mouse: Watch as the face jiggles and wobbles, eventually returning to its original shape thanks to the realistic physics engine.

For an enhanced visual experience, you can switch the game's graphics quality. Look for the quality setting, usually in the top-right corner of the game screen, to toggle between medium and high quality.
