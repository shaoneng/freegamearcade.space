Elastic Man

How to Play Elastic Man

Playing Elastic Man is incredibly simple and intuitive, making it the perfect game to relieve stress or just pass the time. There are no rules, levels, or objectives to worry about.

Simply use your mouse to interact with the elastic face. Click and hold the left mouse button on any part of the face—the eyes, cheeks, nose, or chin. Drag your mouse to stretch, pull, and distort the face in any way you can imagine. The realistic physics makes the skin jiggle, warp, and react hilariously to your every move.

Let go of the mouse button, and watch as the face snaps back to its original form, ready for more fun. For a better visual experience, you can switch the graphics quality to "high" using the button in the top-right corner of the game screen.
