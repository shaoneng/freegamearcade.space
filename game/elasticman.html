Elastic Man

How to Play Elastic Man

Playing Elastic Man is incredibly simple and all about fun! There are no complex rules or objectives to worry about. Your goal is simply to interact with the stretchy, physics-based face on the screen.

Use your mouse cursor to get started. Click and hold anywhere on the face to grab it. Once you have a grip, drag your mouse to pull, stretch, and distort the face in any way you can imagine. Poke the eyes or pull the nose to see the hilarious and realistic reactions.

No matter how much you stretch it, the face will always jiggle and wobble back to its original shape, making it a perfect digital stress ball. For an enhanced visual experience, you can switch the graphics quality to 'High' using the option in the top-right corner of the game screen. Enjoy the oddly satisfying fun!
