Elastic Man

How to Play Elastic Man

Elastic Man is an incredibly simple yet satisfying game designed purely for fun and stress relief. There are no complicated rules, objectives, or scores to worry about. Your only goal is to interact with the stretchy, rubbery face on the screen.

To play, simply use your mouse cursor. Click and hold anywhere on the face to grab it. Once you've got a grip, drag your mouse to pull and stretch the face in any direction you like. Poke it, pinch it, or try to stretch it to the corners of the screen! The realistic physics makes every interaction unique and amusing. When you let go, the face will jiggle and wobble back to its original shape, ready for more.

For an enhanced visual experience, you can switch the game's graphics from the default setting to high quality. Look for the option in the upper-right corner of the game screen to toggle between modes.
