How to Play Elastic Man

Welcome to Elastic Man, the ultimate digital stress ball! There are no complex rules or objectives hereâ€”just pure, unadulterated fun. The goal is simply to interact with the incredibly stretchy face on your screen.

Use your mouse cursor to click and hold anywhere on the face. Once you've got a grip, drag your mouse to pull, stretch, and distort the face in any way you can imagine. Poke the eyes, pull the nose, or stretch the cheeks to their limits! No matter what you do, the face will amusingly snap back to its original shape thanks to the game's realistic physics engine.

For an even more detailed visual experience, you can switch the graphics quality from medium to high using the option in the upper-right corner of the game screen. Just sit back, relax, and let off some steam!
