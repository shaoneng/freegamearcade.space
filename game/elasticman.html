Elastic Man

How to Play Elastic Man

Elastic Man is a simple yet captivating stress-relief game with no complex rules. The objective is simply to have fun by interacting with the character's incredibly stretchy face.

Use your mouse to get started. Click and hold anywhere on the face to grab it. Drag your cursor in any direction to stretch, pull, and distort the face. You can poke the eyes, pull the nose, or stretch the cheeks as far as they will go. No matter how much you manipulate it, the face will always spring back to its original form once you let go.

For an enhanced visual experience, you can adjust the game's quality. Look for the quality setting, usually in the top-right corner of the game screen, and switch it from medium to high for smoother, more detailed physics.
