Elastic Man

How to Play Elastic Man

Elastic Man is a unique and captivating physics-based game designed for pure stress relief and amusement. There are no levels to beat or points to score. Your only goal is to interact with the incredibly stretchable face on the screen and have fun with the hilarious results.

The controls are incredibly simple. Just use your mouse cursor to interact with the face. Click and hold on any part of the skin—the nose, eyes, cheeks, or ears—and drag your mouse to stretch and distort it. You can pull it in any direction, as far as you can, and watch it wobble and jiggle realistically.

No matter how much you pull or poke it, the face will always return to its original state thanks to the satisfying physics engine. For an enhanced visual experience, you can switch the graphics quality from medium to high using the button in the upper right corner of the game screen. Dive in, let off some steam, and enjoy the endless fun of Elastic Man!
