Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Epic Basketball! The objective is straightforward: score as many baskets as you can within the 60-second time limit. To shoot, simply click or tap the screen. Time your shot perfectly to sink the ball through the hoop.

Be warned, this isn't your average basketball game. The hoop moves around unpredictably, and various distractions will appear to throw off your aim. Stay focused, adjust your shots, and keep racking up points to climb the leaderboard.

As you play, you'll start with the character Javi. By achieving high scores, you can unlock a whole crew of colorful players. The ultimate goal for any pro is to score 300 points in a single game to unlock Nona, the legendary grandma baller!
