Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

The objective in Epic Basketball is to score as many baskets as possible in just 60 seconds. Use your mouse to aim the basketball—simply click and hold to set your trajectory, and then release to shoot. On a mobile device, you can tap and drag your finger to aim and release to take the shot.

Stay focused! The hoop doesn't stay still, and various on-screen distractions will try to break your concentration. Perfect your timing to overcome these challenges and rack up a high score.

Your performance unlocks a roster of colorful characters, each with a unique personality. The ultimate challenge is to score 300 points in a single game to unlock the legendary baller, Nona. Get practicing and show everyone who rules the court!
