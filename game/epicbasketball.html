Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Welcome to the wild courts of Epic Basketball! The goal is simple: score as many baskets as you possibly can within the 60-second time limit. Each successful shot adds to your score, bringing you closer to unlocking new characters and bragging rights.

To play, simply click (or tap) and hold to aim your shot. A guideline will appear to help you. Release the mouse button or your finger to shoot the basketball. Timing and accuracy are key, especially since the hoop won't stay still for long!

Be prepared for a challenge! The hoop will move around the screen, and various quirky distractions will pop up to break your concentration. Stay focused on your target and keep shooting to maximize your score before the clock runs out.

As you improve, you can unlock a whole crew of unique players. The ultimate prize is Nona, the grandma baller, who becomes available once you prove your skills by scoring a total of 300 points. Get practicing and show everyone you're an epic player!
