Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Get ready for a quirky and fast-paced streetball challenge! The controls are intuitive: use your mouse to aim the basketball. Click and hold the left mouse button, drag to set your power and trajectory, and then release to take your shot. On a mobile device, simply tap, drag, and release your finger.

Your mission is to score as many baskets as you can before the 60-second timer runs out. It's tougher than it sounds! The basketball hoop will move around the screen, and all sorts of funny distractions will pop up to block your view and test your focus. Adapt quickly and time your shots perfectly to maximize your score.

Start with Javi and earn points with every basket you sink. Use these points to unlock a whole crew of colorful characters, like Plin Plin, Bubu, and Alien. The ultimate prize is Nona, the grandma who's secretly a basketball legend. You'll need to score a whopping 300 points to add her to your roster. Start practicing and become an epic baller!
