Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Welcome to the vibrant world of Epic Basketball! Your objective is simple: score as many points as you can within the 60-second time limit. To shoot, simply click (or tap) and drag to aim your shot, then release to send the ball flying towards the hoop. Adjust your aim to account for distance and the hoop's movement.

This isn't your average basketball game. The hoop will move around the screen, and various distractions will appear to break your focus. Stay sharp and time your shots perfectly to maximize your score. Making consecutive shots can sometimes grant bonus points, so try to get into a rhythm!

As you play, you'll start with the character Javi. By achieving high scores, you can unlock a colorful cast of other players like Plin Plin, Bubu, and Bruce. The ultimate goal for any pro is to unlock Nona, the legendary grandma baller, which requires an impressive score of 300 points. Get practicing and show everyone your epic skills!
