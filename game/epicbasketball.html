Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Epic Basketball is a fun and quirky street-style sports game where the goal is simple: sink as many baskets as you can in just 60 seconds. But don't be fooled by the simple premise! The hoop is constantly on the move, and all sorts of distractions will try to throw you off your game. You'll need sharp focus and a true aim to rack up points.

You start your journey with Javi, but as you play, you can unlock a whole crew of colorful characters, including Plin Plin, Bubu, Patty Wally, Bruce, and even an Alien. Each player brings their own unique style to the court. The ultimate prize is Nona—she might look like a sweet grandma, but she's a basketball legend. To unlock her, you'll need to prove your skills by scoring a whopping 300 points!

If you love the high-energy action and fun visuals of Epic Basketball, be sure to check out Basket Random. It’s another hilarious basketball game with pixelated characters, wacky physics, and fast-paced 2-player matches.
