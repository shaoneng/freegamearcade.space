Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Epic Basketball! Your objective is to score as many points as possible by sinking baskets within the 60-second time limit.

Use your mouse to aim your shot. Click and hold the left mouse button to power up your throw, and release to shoot the ball. Timing and aim are crucial, as the hoop moves around the court, making each shot a new challenge.

As you play, you'll earn points that can be used to unlock a variety of quirky and fun characters. Keep an eye out for distractions that appear on the screen, designed to throw off your game. Stay focused, score high, and try to unlock the legendary baller, Nona, by reaching 300 points!
