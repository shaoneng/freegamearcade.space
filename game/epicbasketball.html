Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Epic Basketball is a fun and quirky street-style sports game you can play right in your browser. The goal is simple: sink as many baskets as you can in just 60 seconds. However, this isn't your average shootout—the hoop moves around, and various distractions will try to break your focus. You'll need to stay sharp, aim true, and shoot your shot to rack up a high score!

You'll start the game as Javi, but as you score more points, you can unlock a roster of colorful and unique players like Plin Plin, Bubu, Patty Wally, Bruce, Alien, Imhotep, and more. Each character brings their own personality to the court. The ultimate prize is Nona. She might look like a sweet grandma, but she's the best baller in the game. To unlock her, you'll need to prove your skills by scoring a total of 300 points. It's a challenge, so get practicing!

If you enjoy the high-energy gameplay and fun visuals of Epic Basketball, you should definitely check out Basket Random. It’s another hilarious 2-player basketball game with pixelated characters, wacky physics, and fast-paced matches.
