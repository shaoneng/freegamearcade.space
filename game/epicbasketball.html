Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Welcome to the court of Epic Basketball! This isn't your average hoops game. You have just 60 seconds to sink as many baskets as you can. The challenge? The hoop never stays still, and all sorts of crazy distractions will pop up to break your concentration.

Your main objective is to aim carefully and shoot accurately to maximize your score before the timer runs out. You'll start out playing as Javi, but as you score more points, you'll unlock a roster of unique characters, including Plin Plin, Bubu, Bruce, and even an Alien.

The ultimate baller is Nona, the grandma with surprising skills. To unlock her, you'll need to achieve a high score of 300 points. It's a tough challenge, so stay focused, keep shooting, and become the epic basketball champion!
