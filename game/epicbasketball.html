Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Step onto the court in Epic Basketball, a fast-paced streetball challenge where your goal is to score as many points as you can within a 60-second time limit. It's not as simple as it sounds! The hoop moves around unpredictably, and various distractions will try to throw off your aim. You'll need sharp focus and a quick release to rack up a high score.

You'll begin your basketball journey as Javi, but as you play, you can unlock a whole crew of colorful and quirky characters. Collect players like Plin Plin, Bubu, Patty Wally, Bruce, and even an Alien to bring their unique style to the court. Each character adds a new visual flair to the game.

The ultimate baller to unlock is Nona. Don't let her grandma appearance fool you—she's a legend on the court. To add her to your roster, you'll need to prove your skills by scoring a total of 300 points. It's a tough challenge, so keep practicing your shots!

If you enjoy the high-energy, quirky physics of Epic Basketball, be sure to check out Basket Random for another hilarious take on the sport, perfect for challenging a friend.
