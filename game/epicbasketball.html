Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Epic Basketball is a fast-paced, street-style basketball shootout game designed to test your aiming skills under pressure.

Your main goal is to sink as many baskets as possible within a 60-second time limit. Sounds simple? Not quite! The hoop moves around, and various distractions will try to throw off your aim. Stay sharp and focus on your shots to maximize your score.

You start with the character Javi, but you can unlock a colorful cast of new players as you progress, each with their unique style. The ultimate challenge is to unlock Nona, the legendary baller grandma, who requires you to score an impressive 300 points. Keep practicing your aim and timing to unlock all characters and become the ultimate basketball legend!
