Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Welcome to the fast-paced world of street hoops! In Epic Basketball, your goal is simple: score as many baskets as you can within the 60-second time limit. But don't get too comfortable—the hoop moves around, and various distractions will try to break your focus!

To play, simply use your mouse. Click and hold to aim your shot, then release to shoot the ball towards the hoop. Time your shots carefully to account for the moving backboard and the quirky physics of the court.

As you play, you'll start with the character Javi. Keep sinking baskets to earn points and unlock a whole crew of colorful characters like Plin Plin, Bubu, and even an Alien! The ultimate challenge is to score 300 points in a single game to unlock Nona, the grandma who's a secret basketball pro. Get practicing and show them what you've got!
