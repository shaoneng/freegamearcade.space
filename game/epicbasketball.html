Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Epic Basketball! Your objective is simple: score as many baskets as you can within the 60-second time limit. The more you sink, the higher your score!

To play, use your mouse to aim your shot. The ball will follow your cursor. When you have the perfect angle, click the left mouse button to shoot. Be warned, this isn't your average basketball game! The hoop will move around the screen, and crazy distractions will pop up to break your concentration. Stay focused, aim true, and shoot your shot!

As you play and improve your score, you'll unlock a roster of colorful and unique characters, from Javi to the alien Neil. The ultimate prize is Nona, the grandma who's a secret basketball pro. You'll need to achieve a score of 300 points to unlock her. Get practicing and become the epic baller you were meant to be!
