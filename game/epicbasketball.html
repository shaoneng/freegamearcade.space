Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Welcome to the fast-paced, quirky world of Epic Basketball! The objective is simple: score as many baskets as you can before the 60-second timer runs out. Use your mouse or finger to aim your shot, then click or release to shoot the ball towards the hoop.

Stay focused, because this isn't your average basketball game. The hoop moves around unpredictably, and various on-screen distractions will try to break your concentration. A clean shot (swish) will earn you bonus points, so aim for the perfect arc!

As you play, you'll start with the character Javi. Keep sinking baskets to unlock a colorful roster of unique players. The ultimate challenge? Score a whopping 300 points in a single game to unlock Nona, the grandma who's secretly the greatest baller of all time!
