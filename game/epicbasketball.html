Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Step onto the court and show off your skills in Epic Basketball! This fast-paced, addictive arcade game challenges you to sink as many baskets as you can. It's all about perfect timing and a steady hand.

Gameplay Controls:

  • Aiming: Use your mouse to aim the basketball. The trajectory line will show you the predicted path of your shot.
  • Shooting: Click and hold the left mouse button to start your shot. A power meter will appear.
  • Power: Release the mouse button at the right moment to shoot the ball with the desired power. Getting the power just right is key!

Scoring & Objectives:

Your goal is to score as many points as possible. Keep an eye out for scoring streaks and special bonuses. Making consecutive 'swishes' (shots that don't touch the rim) might earn you extra points or time. Challenge yourself to beat your own high score with every game.

Tips for Success:

  • Pay close attention to the moving hoop in later levels.
  • Mastering the power meter is more important than perfect aim.
  • Don't rush your shots. A moment of patience can lead to a perfect score.
