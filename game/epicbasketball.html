Epic Basketball

How to Play Epic Basketball

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Epic Basketball! The objective is simple: score as many baskets as you can within the 60-second time limit. To play, simply use your mouse to aim your shot. Click and hold to set your power and angle, then release to shoot the ball towards the hoop.

Be warned, this is no ordinary court! The hoop will move around, and various distractions will appear to throw off your aim. Stay focused and time your shots perfectly to rack up points. A clean shot (a "swish") might even grant you bonus points!

As you play, you'll start as the character Javi. By scoring points, you can unlock a whole crew of unique players. The ultimate challenge is to score 300 points in a single game to unlock the legendary baller, Nona. Get practicing and show everyone who's the king of the court!
