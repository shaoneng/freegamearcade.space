Epic Mine

How to Play Epic Mine

Use your mouse to control your miner. Your primary goal is to dig deeper into the earth to reach the core and find the Key of the Gods. Click on walls to smash them with your pickaxe, breaking them into pieces and revealing valuable resources like ore and gems.

Collect the resources you unearth and return to the village. Here, you can sell your findings for coins. Use these coins and materials to craft new, more powerful pickaxes or upgrade your existing ones. With over 50 unique pickaxes to discover, a stronger tool is always within reach, allowing you to break tougher walls and dig deeper.

For tougher obstacles, use dynamite for a quick blast. As you progress, you'll unlock special abilities like freezing walls to make them brittle or using fire to melt through them. Don't forget to upgrade your personal skills like strength, accuracy, and luck to improve your mining efficiency and combat prowess.

The mines are not empty! Be prepared to face dangers like spiders and other mysterious creatures. Deeper down, powerful bosses guard key areas. Use all your tools, abilities, and upgraded pickaxes to defeat them and claim their rare loot, which is essential for your quest to save the world.
