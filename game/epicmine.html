Epic Mine

How to Play Epic Mine

Your mission in Epic Mine is to dig deep into the Earth, reach its core, and find the legendary Key of the Gods to prevent a global disaster. Use your mouse to aim and click on walls to break them apart. Each click swings your pickaxe, chipping away at the earth before you.

As you smash through walls, they will crumble and reveal valuable resources like ore and gems. Collect everything you find! These materials are the foundation of your progress. Be on the lookout for hidden treasure chests, which contain rare items and special rewards.

Return to the village to sell your mined resources for coins. Use these coins and raw materials to upgrade your equipment. Visit the blacksmith to craft new, more powerful pickaxesâ€”there are over 50 to collect! Stronger tools will help you break tougher walls and dig deeper. For particularly stubborn obstacles, use dynamite for a quick and explosive solution.

Beyond just tools, you can also upgrade your personal abilities, such as strength, luck, and accuracy. Unlock special magical skills to aid your journey, like freezing walls to make them brittle or using fire to melt through obstacles. These abilities are crucial for navigating challenging areas and fighting powerful bosses.

Your adventure is filled with challenges. Take on daily missions for extra rewards and to level up your character. As you venture deeper, you'll encounter formidable bosses guarding key passages. Use your upgraded gear, dynamite, and magic to defeat them and continue your epic quest to save the world!
