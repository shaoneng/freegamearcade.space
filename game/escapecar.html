Escape Car

How to Play Escape Car

Welcome to the driver's seat of Escape Car! Your objective is simple: survive as long as you can while causing maximum chaos. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to steer your vehicle through the city streets.

As you drive, you'll be pursued by a relentless police force, including patrol cars, heavy-duty tanks, and even helicopters. Evade them with sharp turns, clever drifts, and high-speed maneuvers. The longer you last and the more police vehicles you outsmart, the higher your score will climb.

Keep an eye out for stacks of cash scattered throughout the city. Collect as much as you can! This money can be used in the Garage between runs to purchase a random new vehicle. Unlocking different cars, from sports cars to trucks, can change your strategy and help you achieve even higher scores. Good luck, and don't get caught!
