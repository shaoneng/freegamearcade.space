Escape Car

How to Play Escape Car

Get ready for a high-speed chase! In Escape Car, your main goal is to outrun the law for as long as possible. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to steer your vehicle through the bustling city streets.

As you drive, keep an eye out for police cars, helicopters, and even tanks trying to stop you. Evade them by making sharp turns and using the city layout to your advantage. Every police vehicle you destroy adds to your score!

Don't forget to collect the stacks of cash scattered on the roads. You can use this money in the Garage between runs to unlock new and better cars. Each new car offers a different driving experience, so try to collect them all and set a new high score!
