Escape Car

How to Play Escape Car

Welcome to Escape Car, an action-packed game where your primary goal is to outrun the law! Inspired by classic top-down driving games, you'll navigate a bustling mini-city from an overhead perspective.

Use your controls to drive, drift, and dodge an relentless pursuit from various police vehicles, including cars, tanks, and even helicopters. The longer you survive, the more cash you'll collect. Execute daring drifts and strategically total police cruisers to earn bonus points and maximize your score.

Collect enough money, and head to the Garage section. Here, you can spend your earnings on random mystery cars, each offering a unique driving experience. Experiment with different vehicles to find your favorite and build a powerful collection. Keep escaping, keep earning, and see how long you can survive the high-speed chase!
