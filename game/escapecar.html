Escape Car

How to Play Escape Car

Your goal in Escape Car is simple: survive as long as you can! You're dropped into a bustling city with the entire police force on your tail. Drive fast, dodge obstacles, and avoid getting busted by the relentless pursuit.

Use your keyboard (WASD or Arrow Keys) to steer your vehicle through the city streets. Master tight turns and sharp drifts to shake off pursuing police cars, evade mighty tanks, and escape the searching gaze of helicopters. The more police cruisers you manage to wreck, the higher your score will climb.

As you race through the city, keep an eye out for stacks of cash scattered on the roads. Collect as much as you can! This money is your ticket to better rides. Head to the Garage between runs to spend your earnings on new mystery cars, each offering a unique driving experience and a better chance at escape.
