Escape Car

How to Play Escape Car

Escape Car throws you into the driver's seat of a high-speed chase with a classic, top-down perspective inspired by the original Grand Theft Auto. Your goal is to survive for as long as possible, weaving through a bustling mini-city while evading a relentless police force that includes cruisers, tanks, and even helicopters. The longer you last, the higher your score.

Drift around corners, execute split-second dodges, and collect stacks of cash scattered across the map. Every successful evasive maneuver and every police car you outsmart adds to your score. The cash you collect isn't just for points—it's your ticket to a better ride.

Head to the Garage to spend your earnings on a random mystery car. Will you unlock a nimble sports car, a sturdy truck, or the ultimate supercar? Building your collection is part of the fun, as each vehicle offers a different driving experience. From the creators of Police Chase Drifter, Escape Car delivers a polished 3D experience with intense, action-packed pursuits.
