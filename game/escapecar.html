Escape Car

How to Play Escape Car

Your goal in Escape Car is simple: survive as long as you can while causing maximum chaos! Drive through the city, collecting cash scattered on the streets while being pursued by an ever-growing police force. The longer you last, the higher your score.

Use your keyboard to steer your vehicle. Master sharp turns and tight drifts to evade police cars, tanks, and even helicopters. Crashing into police cruisers will also boost your score, but be careful not to get cornered or take too much damage.

The cash you collect isn't just for points! After each run, head to the Garage to spend your earnings on a mystery car. Unlocking new vehicles, from sturdy trucks to speedy supercars, changes how you play and gives you a better shot at a new high score. Collect them all to complete your dream garage!
