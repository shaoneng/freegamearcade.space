Escape Car

How to Play Escape Car

The goal in Escape Car is to survive a chaotic police chase for as long as possible. You'll be driving through a vibrant 3D city from a top-down perspective, reminiscent of classic action games.

Use your keyboard (WASD or Arrow Keys) to steer your car, drifting around corners and weaving through traffic. Police cars, tanks, and even helicopters will be on your tail. Your objective is to evade them and collect the piles of cash scattered throughout the city to increase your score.

The cash you earn can be spent in the Garage between runs to unlock new mystery cars. Each new vehicle offers a different driving experience, so collect them all to find your perfect getaway ride and dominate the leaderboards!
