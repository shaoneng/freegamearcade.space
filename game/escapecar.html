Escape Car

How to Play Escape Car

Welcome to the high-speed chases of Escape Car! Your objective is to survive as long as possible while causing maximum chaos and collecting cash.

Controls: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer your vehicle through the bustling city streets. Master tight drifts and sharp turns to outmaneuver your pursuers.

Evade the Law: The police, tanks, and even helicopters are hot on your tail! Dodge their pursuit, and for extra points, try to make them crash. The longer you survive, the higher your score.

Collect Cash & Upgrade: As you drive, grab the stacks of cash scattered across the map. Use this money in the Garage to buy random mystery cars. Each new vehicle has different stats, so collect them all to find your favorite!
