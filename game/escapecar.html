Escape Car

How to Play Escape Car

Get ready for some old-school, top-down chaos reminiscent of the original Grand Theft Auto, but with a fresh, chunky 3D look. In Escape Car, you're thrown into a mini-city with one goal: survive and get rich. Floor it through the streets, dodging police cars, tanks, and even helicopters, all while scooping up stacks of cash.

Your score climbs with every daring maneuver. Execute tight drifts, perform split-second fake-outs, and watch police cruisers pile up behind you to maximize your earnings. The longer you last, the more money you'll bank for crucial upgrades.

Got cash to burn? Head over to the Garage and spend it on a random mystery car. From nimble sports cars to heavy-duty trucks, each new ride changes how you play. Can you collect them all and build the ultimate dream garage?

From the creators of the hit game Police Chase Drifter, Escape Car is its bigger, more polished 3D cousin. It delivers the same heart-pounding pursuits with enhanced graphics and smoother gameplay. Ready to prove you've got what it takes? Hit play, outrun the law, and start your car collection!
