Escape Car

How to Play Escape Car

Jump into the driver's seat and prepare for a chaotic chase! The goal in Escape Car is simple: survive as long as you can while outrunning an increasingly aggressive police force, including cars, tanks, and helicopters. Use your keyboard to steer your vehicle through the bustling city streets.

As you drive, keep an eye out for stacks of cash scattered around the map. Scoop them up to increase your earnings. Your score isn't just about survival; it's about style! Execute tight drifts, perform near-misses with traffic, and smash pursuing police cars to rack up bonus points and build your score multiplier.

The money you collect can be spent in the Garage between runs. Purchase mystery vehicles to expand your collection—from nimble sports cars to heavy-duty trucks. Each new car offers a different driving experience, giving you new ways to tackle the chase. Good luck, and don't get busted!
