How to Play Escape Car

Welcome to the driver's seat of Escape Car! Your objective is simple: survive as long as you can in a high-speed police chase. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer your vehicle through the bustling city streets.

The police are hot on your tail and will throw everything they have at you, from patrol cars to tanks and even helicopters. Your survival depends on your driving skills. Execute sharp turns, drift around corners, and use the city layout to your advantage to shake off your pursuers.

As you drive, be sure to scoop up the stacks of cash scattered across the map. The more cash you collect and the more police cars you evade or destroy, the higher your score will climb.
