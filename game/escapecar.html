Escape Car

How to Play Escape Car

Jump behind the wheel in Escape Car, a high-octane driving game that channels the classic top-down chaos of the original Grand Theft Auto. Your mission is simple: survive. Navigate a bustling 3D city, evade a relentless police force—including cruisers, tanks, and helicopters—and grab as much cash as you can along the way.

The longer you last and the more mayhem you cause, the higher your score climbs. Every daring drift, near-miss, and wrecked police car adds to your total. The cash you collect isn't just for bragging rights; it's your ticket to a better ride. Rack up enough money to spend in the Garage on a mystery car. Will you unlock a speedy sports car, a sturdy truck, or something even more exotic?

With a whole fleet of vehicles to collect, each run offers a new experience. Find your favorite car and master the art of the getaway. Developed by FreezeNova Games, Escape Car builds on the tense pursuit gameplay of their hit title, Police Chase Drifter, delivering a bigger, more polished 3D world to tear up. Ready to hit the gas?
