How to Play Escape Car

The goal in Escape Car is to survive as long as possible while causing maximum chaos! You're dropped into a bustling city with one mission: evade the police and grab all the cash you see. The longer you last and the more police cars you outsmart, the higher your score will climb.

Use your keyboard to steer your vehicle through the city streets. Execute sharp turns and daring drifts to shake off pursuing police cars, tanks, and even helicopters. Keep an eye out for stacks of money scattered around the mapâ€”driving over them adds to your wallet.

The cash you collect isn't just for a high score. Head to the Garage between runs to spend your earnings on new mystery cars. Each vehicle has its own unique handling and style, giving you a fresh experience every time you play. Try to collect them all and build the ultimate getaway fleet!
