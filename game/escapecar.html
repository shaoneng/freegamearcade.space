Escape Car

How to Play Escape Car

Get ready to hit the streets in Escape Car! Your main goal is to drive through the city for as long as possible, avoiding the relentless pursuit of police cars, tanks, and even helicopters. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to steer your vehicle.

As you navigate the urban chaos, keep an eye out for stacks of cash scattered on the roads. Scooping these up is crucial, as they add to your score and fill your wallet. The more daring your driving—think sharp drifts and close calls—the higher your score will climb.

The money you earn isn't just for show. Head over to the Garage between runs to spend your cash on mystery cars. You could unlock anything from a speedy supercar to a rugged monster truck, each with unique handling. Collect them all to master every escape!
