Escape Car

How to Play Escape Car

Get ready for a high-octane chase! In Escape Car, your primary goal is to drive through the city, evade the relentless police pursuit, and survive for as long as possible. The longer you last, the higher your score climbs.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys (or WASD) to steer your car. Master tight turns and sharp drifts to outmaneuver the police cars, tanks, and even helicopters that are hot on your tail.

Keep an eye out for stacks of cash scattered throughout the city. Scoop them up to build your fortune. You can use this money in the Garage between runs to purchase mystery cars, each with its own unique handling and style. Collect them all to build your ultimate escape fleet!
