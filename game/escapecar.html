Escape Car

How to Play Escape Car

Your mission in Escape Car is simple: survive for as long as possible while causing maximum chaos! You'll be driving through a dynamic city, but you're not on a leisurely tour. The police are hot on your tail, and they've brought everything from cruisers to tanks and helicopters to stop you.

Use your driving skills to weave through traffic, execute sharp drifts, and outsmart the pursuing law enforcement. Every police car you evade or take out adds to your score. Keep an eye out for stacks of cash scattered throughout the city. Grab as much as you can to fund your car collection.

The cash you collect isn't just for a high score. Head over to the Garage between runs to spend your earnings on mystery cars. Unlocking new vehicles isn't just for show; each car can change how you approach your next escape. See if you can collect them all and find your perfect ride!
