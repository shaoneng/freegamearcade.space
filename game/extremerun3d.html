Extreme Run 3D

How to Play Extreme Run 3D

Your objective in Extreme Run 3D is to guide a rolling ball to the end of each level while avoiding a series of treacherous obstacles. The controls are simple: use the arrow keys to move left and right to steer the ball. Press the spacebar to jump over gaps and hazards. For longer jumps, hold down the spacebar.

Each of the 11 levels introduces new challenges designed to test your reflexes and timing. You'll need to dodge sharp spikes, navigate around surprise invisible walls, time your movements to pass through rotating platforms, and duck under swinging hammers and deadly laser beams. Pay close attention to patterns and on-screen cues to survive. If you hit an obstacle, you'll have to restart the level, so stay focused and react quickly to conquer the course!
