Extreme Run 3D

How to Play Extreme Run 3D

Your goal in Extreme Run 3D is simple: guide your rolling ball to the finish line of each level. Use the arrow keys or WASD to move left and right, and press the Spacebar to jump. For longer gaps, hold down the Spacebar to extend your jump. Fast reflexes are key to survival!

Each of the 11 levels presents a unique and increasingly difficult set of obstacles. You'll need to dodge sharp spikes, memorize the location of invisible walls, and time your movements perfectly to pass through rotating platforms and deadly laser beams. Watch out for the unpredictable giant hammers that can crush you in an instant!

As you progress, the challenges will combine, testing all the skills you've learned. The final level pits you against a ghost boss who will actively try to stop you. Stay focused, learn the patterns, and prove you have what it takes to conquer this extreme course!
