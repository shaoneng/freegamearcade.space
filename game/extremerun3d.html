Extreme Run 3D

How to Play Extreme Run 3D

In Extreme Run 3D, you take control of a fast-moving rolling ball. Your primary goal is to navigate through 11 increasingly challenging 3D levels, each packed with unique and demanding obstacles designed to test your reflexes and timing.

To move your ball, use the **Arrow Keys** (Left/Right) or **A** and **D** keys. To jump, press the **Spacebar**. For longer jumps needed to clear wider gaps, hold down the Spacebar.

Each level introduces new mechanics and hazards. Be prepared to dodge sharp spikes, memorize paths through invisible walls, time your jumps precisely through rotating platforms, and avoid crushing giant hammers and laser beams. Later levels combine these challenges and introduce new ones, like unstable moving platforms and even a boss encounter where you must dodge attacks. Observe patterns, react quickly, and strategize your moves to reach the finish line in this high-octane running game!
