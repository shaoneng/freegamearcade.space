Extreme Run 3D

How to Play Extreme Run 3D

Welcome to the ultimate reflex challenge! In Extreme Run 3D, your goal is simple: guide a rolling ball to the finish line of each level. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to steer the ball left and right, dodging the myriad of obstacles in your path.

Timing your jumps is crucial for survival. Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and hazards. For longer distances, press and hold the Spacebar to execute a higher, longer jump. You'll need to master this technique to clear wide gaps and tricky platforming sections.

Each of the 11 levels introduces new and more difficult challenges. Prepare to face sharp spikes, sudden invisible walls, rotating platforms, unpredictable swinging hammers, and deadly laser beams. Stay alert, anticipate the patterns, and react quickly to survive. If you hit an obstacle, you'll have to restart the level, so precision is key. Good luck!
