Extreme Run 3D

How to Play Extreme Run 3D

Get ready for a heart-pounding challenge with Extreme Run 3D, one of the most intense and fast-paced running games available online. Your mission is to take control of a rapidly rolling ball and navigate through a series of increasingly difficult obstacles across 11 unique levels. The game's design immerses you in the action, giving you a VR-like experience without needing a headset. Can you conquer all the levels and prove your reflexes?

Controls & Basic Gameplay

The controls are simple but require precise timing. Use your keyboard to steer the ball and press the spacebar to jump. For wider gaps, hold the spacebar for a longer jump. Your goal is to reach the finish line of each level without falling off or hitting a fatal obstacle.

Level Obstacles Guide

Each level introduces a new, more difficult challenge to overcome:

  • Spikes: Watch out for sharp spikes emerging from the ground. A single touch means starting the level over.
  • Invisible Walls: These transparent barriers will test your memory and reaction time. Pay attention to patterns to find the safe path.
  • Rotating Platforms: Red circular platforms shift and rotate. Time your jumps to pass through the gaps, using the white guide circles on the screen to find your way.
  • Hammers: Massive hammers swing unpredictably, changing direction when you jump. This is a game-changer that requires careful planning.
  • Lasers: Deadly laser beams scan across the path. You'll need lightning-fast reflexes to dash and jump between them.
  • The Tunnel: A claustrophobic tunnel run combining many of the obstacles you've faced before.
  • Platform Gaps: A challenging stair-style level with massive gaps, demanding perfect jump distance calculation.
  • Moving Blocks: Platforms with blocks that can either support you or drop away. Think fast to choose the correct path.
  • The Gauntlet: A chaotic mix of everything—spikes, lasers, hammers, and more. A true test of all your acquired skills.
  • The Boss: In the final level, a ghost haunts the platform, throwing bombs and dropping blocks to stop you. Dodge the attacks and race to the finish to claim victory!

Extreme Run 3D was created by AlienWebGames. If you love this kind of intense obstacle course, be sure to check out other fast-paced running games!
