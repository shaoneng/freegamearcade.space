Extreme Run 3D

How to Play Extreme Run 3D

Your objective in Extreme Run 3D is to guide a fast-moving ball through a series of challenging levels, reaching the finish line without crashing. The game demands quick reflexes and precise control as you navigate an increasingly difficult course.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer the ball left and right, dodging obstacles in your path. Press the spacebar to jump over gaps and hazards. Holding the spacebar will allow for a longer jump, which is essential for clearing wider distances across the 11 unique levels.

Each level introduces new obstacles. You'll need to master your timing to avoid sharp spikes, invisible walls, rotating platforms, swinging hammers, and deadly laser beams. Stay alert, anticipate the patterns, and use your quick reflexes to survive each stage and ultimately face the final ghost boss!
