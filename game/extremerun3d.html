Extreme Run 3D

How to Play Extreme Run 3D

Your goal in Extreme Run 3D is to guide a rolling ball through a series of challenging levels, overcoming obstacles to reach the finish line.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your ball left and right. Press the spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles. For longer jumps, you can hold down the spacebar to extend your air time.

The game features 11 progressively difficult levels. You'll need to develop sharp reflexes to dodge a variety of hazards, including ground spikes, invisible walls, rotating platforms, swinging hammers, and laser beams. Pay close attention to patterns and time your movements carefully to survive. Each level introduces a new challenge, building up to a final boss fight that will test all the skills you've learned!
