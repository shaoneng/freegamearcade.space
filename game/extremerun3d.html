Extreme Run 3D

How to Play Extreme Run 3D

Your goal in Extreme Run 3D is to guide a rolling ball through a series of challenging levels. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to steer the ball left and right. Press the spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles. For longer distances, press and hold the spacebar to perform a higher, longer jump.

The game features 11 unique levels, each introducing new and more difficult challenges. You'll need lightning-fast reflexes to succeed. Be prepared to face a variety of obstacles designed to test your skills, including sharp spikes that appear from the ground, sudden invisible walls, rotating platforms, unpredictable swinging hammers, and deadly laser beams.

Pay close attention to patterns and time your movements carefully to survive. A single mistake can send you back to the beginning of the level. The ultimate challenge awaits in the final level, where you'll face a boss who throws bombs and drops blocks in your path. Stay focused, adapt to each new threat, and race to the finish line!
