Extreme Run 3D

How to Play Extreme Run 3D

Your objective in Extreme Run 3D is to guide a rolling ball to the finish line of each level by navigating a treacherous, obstacle-filled course. The game is designed to test your reflexes and timing in a fast-paced 3D environment.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your ball left and right to dodge obstacles. Press the spacebar to jump. For longer gaps, you'll need to hold down the spacebar to extend your jump. Precise control is key to survival!

Each of the 11 levels introduces new and more difficult challenges. You'll need to dodge sharp spikes, memorize paths through invisible walls, time your jumps through rotating platforms, avoid massive swinging hammers, and duck under laser beams. Stay alert and adapt quickly to conquer each stage and reach the final boss!
