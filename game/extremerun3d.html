Extreme Run 3D

How to Play Extreme Run 3D

Your goal in Extreme Run 3D is to guide a rolling ball through a series of challenging levels, reaching the finish line without being destroyed by obstacles. The game is designed to test your reflexes and timing in an immersive, fast-paced environment.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer the ball left and right to dodge hazards. Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles. For longer gaps, hold down the Spacebar to perform a longer jump. Mastering this is crucial for success in later levels.

Each of the 11 levels introduces new, more difficult challenges. You'll need to navigate past sharp spikes, avoid giant swinging hammers, slip through deadly laser grids, and even face a final boss. If you hit an obstacle, you'll have to restart the level, so stay focused, learn the patterns, and keep running!
