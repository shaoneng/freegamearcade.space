Extreme Run 3D

How to Play Extreme Run 3D

Get ready for a VR-like experience without the headset! In Extreme Run 3D, you take control of a fast-moving rolling ball and navigate through a series of challenging obstacle courses. Your goal is to reach the finish line in one piece by testing your reflexes and mastering the controls. Hold the spacebar for longer jumps and stay focused to survive.

The game features 11 unique levels, each introducing new and more difficult obstacles:

  • Spikes: Dodge and jump over sharp spikes that appear on the ground. A single touch sends you back to the start.
  • Invisible Walls: Test your memory and reflexes as walls appear out of nowhere. Look for patterns to find your way through.
  • Rotating Platforms: Time your jumps perfectly to pass through holes in rotating red platforms. Follow the white circles to find your safe path.
  • Hammers: Face massive hammers that change direction every time you jump. This unpredictable obstacle requires careful planning to avoid being crushed.
  • Laser Beams: Weave and jump through deadly laser barriers that pulse across your path. Quick timing is key to survival.
  • Tunnels & Stairs: Navigate confined tunnels and master long-distance jumps across wide gaps in stair-style levels.
  • Moving Platforms: Think fast to determine which platforms are safe and which will drop from under you.
  • The Gauntlet: A chaotic mix of all previous obstacles—spikes, lasers, hammers, and more—tests everything you've learned.
  • Boss Level: Face a ghost who throws bombs and drops blocks in your way. Dodge his attacks and make it to the finish to win!

Extreme Run 3D was created by AlienWebGames. If you enjoy this intense challenge, check out other running games. If it's too tough, try a game like Run 3 Space for a slightly easier experience!
