Extreme Run 3D

How to Play Extreme Run 3D

Ever find yourself so immersed in a game that you start moving along with your character? Extreme Run 3D is one of those games. It's a high-octane, fast-paced running game that will test your reflexes to the limit. Take control of a rolling ball and navigate through 11 intense levels packed with challenging obstacles and a VR-like environment, all without a headset!

Your goal is simple: reach the finish line. Get comfortable with the movement and jumping mechanics. Hold the spacebar for longer jumps to clear wider gaps. As you progress, each level introduces new and deadlier challenges to keep you on your toes.

Obstacles to Overcome:

  • Spikes: Sharp spikes appear on the ground, requiring precise timing to dodge and jump over. One wrong move and you start the level over.
  • Invisible Walls: Walls materialize out of nowhere, testing your memory and reaction time. Keep an eye on patterns to avoid getting trapped.
  • Rotating Platforms: Pass through holes in massive, shifting red platforms. Follow the white circles on the screen—they mark the safe path to jump through.
  • Swinging Hammers: These huge hammers change direction every time you jump! Plan your moves carefully to avoid being crushed by their unpredictable swings.
  • Laser Beams: Quick reflexes are essential to time your jumps and safely pass between laser barriers without getting zapped.
  • Complex Terrains: Run through tunnel-like platforms and navigate treacherous, stair-style levels with wide gaps that demand perfect distance judgment.
  • Unstable Platforms: Quickly figure out which moving platforms will hold your weight and which will disappear from under you.
  • The Boss Level: In the final challenge, a ghost haunts the platform, dropping blocks and throwing bombs. Dodge its attacks while navigating to the finish line in the ultimate test of endurance!

Extreme Run 3D was created by AlienWebGames. Love intense obstacle games like this? You'll also enjoy other fast-paced running games. If this one is too extreme, try Run 3 Space for a slightly easier, but still challenging, experience.
