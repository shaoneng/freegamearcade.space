Extreme Run 3D

How to Play Extreme Run 3D

Your goal in Extreme Run 3D is to guide a rolling ball through a series of challenging obstacle courses to reach the finish line of each level. The game is fast-paced and demands quick reflexes to succeed.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer the ball left and right along the path. To jump over gaps and avoid hazards, press the Spacebar. For longer jumps that require more distance, simply hold down the Spacebar to get more airtime.

The game features 11 progressively difficult levels, each introducing new dangers. You'll need to dodge sharp spikes, navigate through invisible walls, time your movements past rotating platforms, evade giant swinging hammers, and slip through laser beams. If you hit an obstacle, you'll have to restart the level, so precision and timing are crucial for victory!
