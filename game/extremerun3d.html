Extreme Run 3D

How to Play Extreme Run 3D

In Extreme Run 3D, your goal is to guide a rolling ball through a series of challenging obstacle courses to reach the finish line of each level. The game is designed to test your reflexes and precision in a fast-paced 3D environment.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your ball left and right to dodge obstacles. Press the spacebar to jump over gaps and hazards. For longer jumps that require more air time, simply press and hold the spacebar.

Each of the 11 levels introduces new and more difficult challenges. You'll need lightning-fast reflexes to avoid sharp spikes, memorize patterns to pass through invisible walls, time your jumps to navigate rotating platforms, and evade huge swinging hammers and deadly laser beams. Stay focused, anticipate the dangers ahead, and master the controls to conquer the extreme run!
