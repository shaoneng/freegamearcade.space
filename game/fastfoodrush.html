Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Your goal is to build a fast-food empire from the ground up. You start with 2,500 coins and a small, empty space. Use your character to walk over upgrade markers on the floor to purchase new equipment and unlock areas.

Begin by unlocking your first seating area, a counter table, and a grill. Once these are active, customers will start arriving. Grill burgers, serve them at the counter, and collect money to fuel your growth. Coins will generate automatically as long as you keep the operation running smoothly.

Invest your earnings wisely. Walk over to the HR manager's office to hire employees and upgrade their speed and carrying capacity. Visit the general manager to improve your own character's skills, like walking speed and profit margins. A well-trained team is key to earning money passively.

Don't forget to keep your restaurant clean! Walk around and clear dirty tables. If tables remain messy, new customers won't be able to sit, costing you potential income. Once your burger joint is booming, use the map to expand your empire by unlocking new coffee and donut shops.
