Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to your very own fast-food restaurant! Your goal is to build a burger empire from the ground up. You'll start with 2,500 coins to kick things off. Use your character to walk over the marked areas on the floor to purchase new equipment and unlock sections.

Begin by unlocking your first seating area, then a counter for taking orders, and a grill to start cooking. Once these are set up, customers will start arriving. Grill burgers, serve them at the counter, and collect cash to grow your business. You can perform all these tasks yourself, or hire staff to automate the process.

Invest your earnings wisely by purchasing upgrades. Unlock the HR Manager's office to hire employees and improve their speed and carrying capacity. Visit the General Manager to upgrade your own character's walking speed and profit margins. A well-trained and efficient team is the secret to maximizing your income!

Don't forget to keep your restaurant clean! Walk around and clear dirty tables left by customers. If tables remain messy, new customers won't sit down, which will cost you potential sales. Once your burger joint is a local success, use the map to expand your business and open new branches, including a coffee shop and a donut stand!
