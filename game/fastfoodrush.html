Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Step into the shoes of a restaurant manager in Fast Food Rush! Your goal is to transform a humble burger stand into a sprawling fast-food empire. You'll hire staff, manage resources, and serve a stream of hungry customers to build your fortune.

You begin your journey with 2,500 coins. Your first task is to get the restaurant operational. Walk over the glowing markers on the floor to spend your starting cash. Unlock your first seating area for $75, then add a counter for customers to order, and finally, open the grill for $85 to start cooking. Once the doors are open, customers will start arriving.

As you grill burgers and serve customers, you'll automatically earn coins. Keep an eye out for white rectangular markers scattered around the restaurant. Walking over these allows you to invest in crucial upgrades, expanding your menu and services to earn even more.

A successful business needs a great team. Unlock the HR manager's office to hire new workers and improve their skills. You can increase their speed, carrying capacity, and overall efficiency. A well-trained team is the key to automating your income and keeping customers happy.

Visit the general manager's office next door to upgrade your own character's walking speed, carrying capacity, and profit rates. Don't forget to keep the restaurant clean! Walk around the dining area to clear dirty tables. If they stay messy for too long, new customers won't sit down, which will hurt your sales.

Once your burger joint is a local success, it's time to expand! Click the map icon on your screen to unlock new branches, including a coffee shop and a donut stand, taking your American dream to the next level.
