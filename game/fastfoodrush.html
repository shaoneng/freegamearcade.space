Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to Fast Food Rush! Your goal is to build a successful restaurant empire from the ground up. Control your character by using your mouse to guide them or by dragging your finger on mobile devices to move around the restaurant.

You begin with 2,500 coins. Start by unlocking your first customer seating area and then the counter table for taking orders. Next, unlock the grill to start cooking. Once you're set up, customers will start arriving. Grill burgers, bring them to the counter, and collect payments to earn a steady stream of coins.

As your profits grow, invest in upgrades by walking over the white markers scattered around your restaurant. You can also visit the HR manager to hire more workers and boost their speed and carrying capacity. Don't forget to visit the general manager to upgrade your own character's abilities! A key to success is keeping your restaurant clean—make sure to clear dirty tables so new customers have a place to sit.

Once your burger joint is booming, expand your empire by opening new branches for coffee and donuts. Manage your resources wisely, keep your customers happy, and watch your fast-food dream come to life!
