Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to your very own fast-food business! You'll start with 2,500 coins to get your burger joint off the ground. To begin, walk your character over the marked areas on the floor to spend your coins. First, unlock a seating area, then a counter for taking orders, and finally, the grill to start cooking. Once these are set up, customers will start arriving and your business will come to life!

As you serve customers, you'll automatically earn coins. Reinvest this money wisely by walking over the white rectangular markers scattered around your restaurant. These allow you to purchase various upgrades. Don't forget to keep your restaurant clean! Walk over to dirty tables to clear them; if they stay messy for too long, you'll lose potential customers and income.

To run your restaurant more efficiently, unlock the HR Manager's office. Here, you can hire more workers and upgrade their speed and carrying capacity. Next, unlock the General Manager's office to improve your own character's speed, capacity, and overall profit margins. A well-managed team is the key to success!

Once your burger spot is a booming success, it's time to expand! Click the map icon on your screen to unlock new business ventures, such as a Coffee Shop and a Donut Shop, and turn your small stand into a massive fast-food empire.
