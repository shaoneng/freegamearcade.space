Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Ever dreamed of running your own fast-food joint? In Fast Food Rush, you get to experience firsthand what it's like to build a burger empire from the ground up. Manage your restaurant, serve hungry customers, and expand your business to become the ultimate food tycoon!

Getting Started

You begin your journey with 2,500 coins. To get your business rolling, you need to make some initial investments. Walk over the white markers on the floor to purchase your first assets:

  • Unlock the first seating area for $75.
  • Add a counter table for customers to place orders.
  • Unlock the grill for $85 to start cooking.

Once you're set up, customers will start flocking in. Grill burgers, bring them to the counter, and collect coins as they flow in automatically.

Managing Your Staff and Upgrades

A successful restaurant needs an efficient team and constant improvements. Unlock the corner rooms to hire specialized managers:

  • HR Manager: In her office, you can hire more workers and boost their skills. Increase their speed, carrying capacity, and overall efficiency to serve customers faster.
  • General Manager: In his office, you can improve your own abilities, including walking speed, carrying capacity, and profit rates, making your management more effective.

Keeping it Clean

A clean restaurant keeps customers happy and coming back for more. Be sure to walk around the dining area and clear dirty tables. If tables remain messy for too long, new customers won't sit down, which will cost you potential sales. A quick cleanup makes a big difference to your income.

Expanding Your Empire

After your burger spot becomes a local success, it’s time to expand! Click the map icon on the top left of your screen to grow your business. You can unlock new branches, like a Coffee Shop and a Donut Shop, turning your small burger stand into a true fast-food empire.
