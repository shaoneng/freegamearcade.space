Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Ever dreamed of running your own fast-food joint? In Fast Food Rush, you get to experience the hustle and bustle of building a burger empire from the ground up. You'll start with a small stand, Burger Blitz, and 2,500 coins to kickstart your journey. Your goal is to serve happy customers, earn money, and expand your business into a thriving franchise.

To begin, use your initial 2,500 coins wisely. Your first task is to unlock the initial seating area by walking over the marker that costs $75. Next, add a counter for customers to place orders, and then unlock the grill to start cooking. Once the grill is hot, the doors open, and your first customers will arrive!

As you serve burgers and check out customers, coins will automatically flow in. Keep an eye out for white rectangular markers scattered around the floor—these are your opportunities to invest in crucial upgrades. Unlock the HR manager's office to hire employees and boost their speed and carrying capacity. Visit the general manager next door to upgrade your own walking speed, capacity, and overall profit margins.

A clean restaurant is a profitable one. Make sure to walk around and clear dirty tables promptly, as messy tables will deter new customers. Once your burger spot is a success, it's time to expand! Click the map icon to unlock new ventures like a Coffee Shop and a Donut Shop, taking your American dream to the next level.

Use your mouse, WASD keys, or arrow keys to navigate your character through the restaurant. Gameplay is intuitive—simply walk over interactive markers to purchase upgrades, manage staff, and clean tables.
