Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to your very own fast-food restaurant! Your goal is to transform a small burger stand into a sprawling food empire. You'll start with 2,500 coins and a dream. Move your character around the restaurant to build, manage, and grow your business.

To begin, walk over the white markers on the floor to spend your starting coins. Unlock your first seating area, a counter for taking orders, and a grill to start cooking. Once the essentials are in place, customers will start arriving, and you'll begin earning money automatically as you serve them.

Keep an eye out for upgrade markers scattered around the floor. Investing in these will improve your restaurant. Unlock the HR manager's office to hire employees and upgrade their skills, increasing their speed and how much they can carry. A well-trained team is key to generating idle income!

Don't forget to manage your own abilities! Visit the general manager to improve your character's speed and profit rates. A clean restaurant is a profitable one, so make sure to walk around and clean up any dirty tables to make space for new, paying customers.

Once your burger joint is a success, use the map to expand your business into new ventures like a coffee shop and a donut stand. On PC, use your mouse to move. On mobile, tap and drag your finger on the screen.
