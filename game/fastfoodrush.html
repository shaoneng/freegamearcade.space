Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Step into the fast-paced world of restaurant management with Fast Food Rush! You'll start your journey with a humble burger joint, "Burger Blitz," and 2,500 coins in your pocket. Your goal? To transform this small stand into a thriving fast-food empire with multiple branches.

Getting Your Business Started

When you begin, you'll need to get your restaurant ready for customers. First, walk over to the white marker to unlock your first seating area for $75. Next, add a counter table for taking orders and payments. Finally, unlock the grill for $85 to start cooking. Once the grill is hot, you can start serving customers, and the coins will begin to flow automatically.

Upgrading Your Restaurant

As you earn more, reinvest your coins to grow your business. You'll find white rectangular markers scattered around the floor. Stepping on these allows you to purchase essential upgrades to improve service and efficiency. Keep an eye out for these opportunities to maximize your profits.

A clean restaurant is a profitable one! Make sure to walk around the dining area and clear dirty tables. If tables remain messy, new customers won't be able to sit down, costing you potential sales.

Managing Your Team and Yourself

To keep up with demand, you'll need help. Unlock the HR manager's office to hire workers and boost their skills. You can increase their speed, carrying capacity, and overall efficiency. A well-trained team is the secret to raking in coins without breaking a sweat.

Don't forget to upgrade yourself! Visit the general manager next door to improve your own walking speed, carrying capacity, and even increase your overall profit margins.

Expanding Your Empire

Once your burger spot is a local sensation, it's time to expand. Click the map icon on the top left of your screen to unlock new ventures, including a bustling Coffee Shop and a sweet Donut Place. Turn your single restaurant into a true fast-food dynasty!

Fast Food Rush, created by FreezeNova, perfectly blends idle income generation with active management strategy, making it a must-play for fans of business simulation games like Checkout Frenzy and Where's my Pizza?.
