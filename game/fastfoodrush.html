Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Ever dreamt of running your own fast-food empire? In Fast Food Rush, you get to do just that! Step into the shoes of a restaurant owner and experience the thrill of turning a small burger stand into a bustling franchise. It's time to flip patties, serve customers, and watch the coins roll in!

You begin your journey with 2,500 coins. Your first task is to get the restaurant operational. Walk over the marked areas on the floor to spend your starting cash: unlock your first seating area, add a counter for taking orders, and finally, open up the grill. Once the grill is hot, the doors open and your first customers will arrive!

The key to success is efficiency. Grill burgers, bring them to the counter, and check out customers to earn money automatically. As you serve more people, you'll see coins steadily flowing into your account. A clean restaurant is a busy restaurant—make sure to walk around and clear any dirty tables. If they stay messy, new customers won't sit down, and you'll lose out on potential income.

Reinvest your earnings to grow your business. Step on the white markers scattered around to purchase upgrades. To supercharge your operations, unlock the HR manager's office to hire more workers and improve their speed and carrying capacity. Next door, the general manager can help you upgrade your own walking speed, carrying capacity, and overall profit margins.

Once your burger joint is a local success, it's time to expand! Click the map icon on your screen to unlock new branches, including a Coffee Shop and a Donut Shop. Build your American dream one successful branch at a time!
