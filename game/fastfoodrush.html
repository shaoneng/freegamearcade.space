Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to your new restaurant! You'll begin your entrepreneurial journey with 2,500 coins. To get started, walk your character over the marked areas on the floor to unlock your first seating area, a counter for taking orders, and a grill to cook delicious burgers. Once these are set up, customers will start arriving, and the money will begin to roll in as you serve them.

Keep an eye out for white markers scattered around your restaurant. Stepping on these allows you to spend your hard-earned coins on essential upgrades. To build an efficient team, unlock the HR manager's office to hire more workers and improve their speed and carrying capacity. Next door, the general manager can help you upgrade your own walking speed and carrying capacity, making you a super-efficient boss!

A clean restaurant is a profitable one! Make sure to walk around and clear dirty tables promptly. If tables stay messy, new customers won't be able to sit, and you'll lose out on potential income. Once your burger joint is a roaring success, click the map icon to expand your empire by unlocking a coffee shop and a donut shop. Manage all three branches to become the ultimate fast food tycoon!
