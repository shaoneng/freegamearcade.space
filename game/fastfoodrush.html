Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Your goal in Fast Food Rush is to build a successful fast-food empire, starting from a single burger joint. You begin with a small amount of cash and must strategically unlock equipment and hire staff to grow your business.

Control your character using your mouse or finger and move around the restaurant. To buy upgrades or unlock new areas, simply walk over the white markers on the floor that display a cost. Start by unlocking your first seating area, then the order counter, and finally the grill to begin serving your first customers.

As you serve customers, you'll automatically earn coins. Reinvest this income by walking over other upgrade markers to purchase more tables, grills, and other essential equipment. A clean restaurant is crucial for business, so be sure to walk around and clear dirty tables to make space for new, paying customers.

To handle the rush, you'll need a team. Unlock the HR manager's office to hire employees and upgrade their speed and carrying capacity. You can also visit the general manager's office to improve your own character's stats and boost your overall profit margins.

Once your burger spot is a local hit, it's time to expand! Use the map icon to open new branches, including a coffee shop and a donut shop, and take your fast-food dream to the next level.
