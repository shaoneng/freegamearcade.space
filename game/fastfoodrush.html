Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to Fast Food Rush! Your goal is to transform a small burger stand into a thriving fast-food empire. You'll manage everything from grilling patties to hiring staff and expanding your business across three different branches.

Getting Started: You begin with 2,500 coins. To start, move your character over the marked areas on the floor to spend your initial cash. First, unlock a seating area, then a counter for taking orders, and finally, the grill to start cooking. Once these are active, customers will start arriving, and you'll begin earning money automatically.

Upgrades and Management: As coins flow in, reinvest them into your restaurant. Step on the white markers to unlock upgrades and new equipment. Keep an eye out for the HR Manager's office; here, you can hire more workers and improve their speed and carrying capacity. Visit the General Manager next door to upgrade your own character's speed and increase your overall profit margins.

Keeping Customers Happy: A clean restaurant is a profitable one! Make sure to walk around and clean any dirty tables left by customers. If tables stay messy, new patrons won't sit down, and you'll lose out on potential income.

Expanding Your Empire: Once your burger joint is a success, it's time to grow. Click the map icon on your screen to unlock new branches, including a Coffee Shop and a Donut Shop, taking your American dream to the next level!
