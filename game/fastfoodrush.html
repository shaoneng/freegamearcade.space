Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to your very own fast-food restaurant! You begin with 2,500 coins to kickstart your business. To get started, walk your character over the markers on the floor to unlock new areas. Your first priorities are unlocking a seating area, a counter table for orders, and the grill. Once these are set up, customers will start arriving!

The key to success is efficient service. Grill burgers, bring them to the counter, and check out customers to earn a steady stream of coins. As your money grows, look for more white markers scattered around your restaurant. Stepping on these allows you to invest in crucial upgrades, like more tables and cooking stations, which will boost your income.

You can't do it all alone! Unlock the HR Manager's office to hire workers and upgrade their skills. A faster, more efficient team can carry more items and serve customers quicker, significantly increasing your profits. Don't forget to visit the General Manager's office next door to upgrade your own walking speed and carrying capacity.

A clean restaurant is a profitable one. Keep an eye on dirty tables left by customers. You'll need to walk over and clean them yourself. If tables stay messy, new customers won't sit down, and you'll lose out on sales. Once your first burger joint is a success, you can expand your empire by opening new coffee and donut shops!
