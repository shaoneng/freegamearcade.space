Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Ever dreamed of running your own fast-food joint? Fast Food Rush gives you a taste of the action! Experience firsthand what it's like to build a bustling burger restaurant from a small stand into a multi-branch empire. Flip patties, serve customers, and watch the money roll in as you chase your American dream.

Getting Started

You begin your journey with 2,500 coins. Your first task is to get the restaurant operational. Walk over the white markers on the floor to spend your starting cash. First, unlock a seating area for $75, then a counter table for customers to order, and finally, the grill for $85. Once the grill is hot, the doors open and your first customers will arrive!

Upgrades and Management

As you serve burgers and check out customers, coins will start flowing in. Reinvest your profits by walking over the various white markers scattered around the restaurant to purchase upgrades. To supercharge your operations, unlock the HR manager's office. Here, you can hire more workers and upgrade their speed and carrying capacity. Next, unlock the general manager's office to improve your own walking speed, capacity, and overall profit margins. A well-trained, efficient team is key to success!

Keep It Clean and Grow!

A clean restaurant is a profitable one. Make sure to walk around and clear dirty tables, as new customers won't sit at messy spots. Once your burger joint is a hit, it's time to expand! Click the map icon on the top left of your screen to unlock new ventures, including a coffee shop and a donut stand, turning your small business into a true fast-food empire.
