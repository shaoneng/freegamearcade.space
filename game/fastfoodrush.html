Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to your very own fast-food restaurant! Your goal is to build a burger empire from the ground up. You'll start with 2,500 coins to get your business running.

Use your mouse to click and drag or your finger on mobile to move your character around the restaurant. To begin, walk over the glowing markers on the floor to spend your starting coins. Unlock your first seating area, a counter for orders, and a grill to start cooking. Once these are set up, customers will start arriving and the money will begin to flow!

As you earn more coins, reinvest them into your business. Unlock the HR Manager's office to hire more workers and upgrade their speed and carrying capacity. Visit the General Manager to improve your own character's abilities. Don't forget to keep the dining area clean by walking over dirty tablesâ€”a clean restaurant means happy, paying customers.

Once your burger joint is a success, click the map icon to expand your operations to a coffee shop and a donut shop. Manage your staff, upgrade your facilities, and watch your fast-food dream become a reality!
