Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to your very own fast-food restaurant! You start your journey with 2,500 coins and a dream to build a burger empire. Use your character to move around the restaurant. On a computer, use the WASD or Arrow keys. On mobile, use the on-screen joystick.

Your first task is to get the business running. Walk over the white markers on the floor to spend your starting coins. First, unlock the seating area, then purchase a counter for customers to order, and finally, unlock the grill to start cooking. Once these are set up, customers will start arriving automatically!

The core of the game is serving customers to earn coins. Grill burgers, bring them to the counter, and check out customers to keep the money flowing. As your restaurant gets busier, you'll need to invest in upgrades and hire help to keep up with demand.

To grow your business efficiently, unlock the HR Manager's office. Here, you can hire workers and upgrade their speed and carrying capacity. A well-trained team will handle tasks for you. Next, visit the General Manager to upgrade your own walking speed, carrying capacity, and overall profit margins.

A clean restaurant is a profitable one! Make sure to walk around and clean up any dirty tables. If tables remain messy, new customers won't be able to sit down, costing you potential income. Once your burger joint is a success, use the map to expand your empire by unlocking a Coffee Shop and a Donut Shop!
