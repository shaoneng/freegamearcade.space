Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to Fast Food Rush! Your objective is to build a thriving fast-food empire starting from a small burger stand. You'll begin with 2,500 coins to kickstart your business.

To start, move your character using the mouse or WASD keys. Walk over the marked white rectangles on the floor to make purchases. Your first goals are to unlock a seating area, a counter, and a grill. Once these are set up, customers will begin to arrive.

Cook patties on the grill, bring them to the counter, and serve your customers to start earning money automatically. As coins flow in, strategically reinvest them into more upgrades scattered around the restaurant.

Unlock the HR Manager's office to hire more employees and improve their speed and carrying capacity. Visit the General Manager to upgrade your own character's stats. Don't forget to keep the tables cleanâ€”dirty tables will deter new customers and cost you sales!

Once your burger joint is a success, use the map icon to expand your business by opening up coffee and donut shops. Manage your staff, upgrades, and expansions wisely to become the ultimate fast-food tycoon!
