Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to your very own fast-food restaurant! Your goal is to build a successful empire from the ground up. Use your mouse to click and drag or the WASD/arrow keys on your keyboard to move your character around the restaurant.

You start with 2,500 coins. Walk over the glowing white markers on the floor to spend your coins and unlock new equipment and areas. Begin by unlocking the first seating area, followed by a counter for orders, and then the grill. Once the grill is operational, customers will start arriving, and you can begin grilling burgers and serving them to earn coins automatically.

A happy customer is a paying customer! Reinvest your earnings into upgrades. Unlock the HR Manager's office to hire more staff and improve their speed and carrying capacity. Visit the General Manager's office to upgrade your own walking speed, carrying capacity, and overall profit margins. Don't forget to keep your restaurant clean by walking over dirty tables to clear them; a clean space attracts more customers.

Once your burger business is booming, expand your empire! Access the map to unlock new branches, including a coffee shop and a donut shop, to take your American dream to the next level.
