Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to your very own fast-food restaurant! You'll begin your journey with 2,500 coins. Your first objective is to get your burger stand operational. Walk over the glowing markers on the floor to spend your starting cash. First, unlock a seating area, then add a counter table for orders, and finally, unlock the grill to start cooking.

Once the grill is active, the doors will open and customers will start arriving. The core gameplay involves grilling burgers, placing them on the counter, and serving customers to earn coins automatically. Keep the flow steady to ensure a constant stream of income.

As you earn money, you'll see more white markers appear around the restaurant. Walking over these allows you to invest in crucial upgrades, such as expanding your seating or improving your equipment. Spend your coins wisely to grow your business efficiently.

To run a successful restaurant, you'll need help! Unlock the HR Manager's office to hire workers and boost their skills, increasing their speed and carrying capacity. Next door, the General Manager can help you upgrade your own walking speed, carrying capacity, and overall profit margins.

A clean restaurant is a profitable one! Make sure to walk around the dining area and clear any dirty tables. If tables remain messy, new customers won't be able to sit down, costing you potential sales.

Once your burger joint is a success, it's time to expand! Click the map icon in the top left of your screen to unlock new branches, including a bustling Coffee Shop and a sweet Donut Shop, turning your small stand into a true fast-food empire.
