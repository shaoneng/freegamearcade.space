Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Ever dreamed of running your own fast-food empire? In Fast Food Rush, you'll experience firsthand what it's like to build a business from the ground up. Start with a small burger stand and turn it into a sprawling, multi-branch franchise serving burgers, coffee, and donuts!

Getting Started

You begin your entrepreneurial journey with 2,500 coins. Your first goal is to get the restaurant operational. Walk over the white markers on the floor to spend your starting cash on essential unlocks: first, the seating area ($75), then a counter table for orders, and finally the grill ($85). Once the grill is fired up, you're officially in business!

Management and Upgrades

As customers come in, you'll start grilling burgers, serving them at the counter, and collecting payment. Coins will flow in automatically, which you can reinvest into your business. Step on the various white markers to purchase upgrades. To supercharge your growth, unlock the two management offices:

  • HR Manager: In her office, you can hire more employees and boost their skills, increasing their speed, carrying capacity, and overall efficiency. A well-trained team is the secret to raking in coins without breaking a sweat.
  • General Manager: Visit his office to improve your own character's walking speed, carrying capacity, and profit rates, making your direct efforts more impactful.

A clean restaurant keeps customers happy. Make sure to walk around the dining area and clear dirty tables. If tables stay messy, new customers won’t sit down, costing you potential sales.

Expansion is Key

After your burger spot becomes a local hit, it’s time to grow! Click the map icon on your screen to expand your empire. You can unlock a trendy Coffee Shop and a delightful Donut Shop, taking on new challenges and tapping into new revenue streams. Step into the world of Fast Food Rush and see how far your American dream can take you!
