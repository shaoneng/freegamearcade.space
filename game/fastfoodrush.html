Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

In Fast Food Rush, you'll embark on your journey to build a fast-food empire starting with 2,500 coins and a humble Burger Blitz. Your initial steps are crucial: walk over the white markers to unlock essential areas.

Begin by unlocking the first seating area for $75, followed by adding a counter table where customers can place their orders and check out. Next, activate the grill by stepping on the $85 sign. This will allow you to start grilling burgers and serving your first customers, automatically generating coins as they are served.

As you accumulate coins, invest them wisely in upgrades found by walking on various white rectangular markers around your restaurant. These include unlocking additional seating, adding more counter tables, and expanding your menu with a coffee machine or a donut machine to attract more customers and increase your revenue streams.

To boost efficiency, head to the corner room to unlock the HR Manager. She will help you hire new employees and upgrade their skills, improving their speed, carrying capacity, and overall productivity. Further enhance your operations by unlocking the General Manager in the next room, who will provide upgrades for your own walking speed, carrying capacity, and profit rates.

Remember to keep your restaurant pristine! Regularly walk around the dining area to clear dirty tables. If tables remain messy, new customers won't sit, leading to lost sales. A clean environment ensures a steady flow of happy customers and maximized daily income. Once your first branch is a success, use the map feature (top left of your screen) to unlock and expand to new branches, truly growing your fast-food empire!
