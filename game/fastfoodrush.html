Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Your journey begins with 2,500 coins and a dream. To get started, walk your character over the marked areas on the floor to spend your initial coins. First, unlock a seating area, then a counter for taking orders, and finally, the grill to start cooking. Once the grill is active, your restaurant will open its doors to the first hungry customers.

The main objective is to serve customers efficiently to earn money. Grill burgers, bring them to the counter, and collect payments. As you serve customers, coins will automatically flow in. Keep an eye on customer orders and make sure your kitchen is keeping up with demand. A smooth operation means a steady income stream for your business.

Reinvest your profits to grow your restaurant. You'll find upgrade markers scattered around the floor. Step on them to invest in new equipment and facilities. Unlock the HR Manager's office to hire more workers and upgrade their speed and carrying capacity. Visit the General Manager next door to boost your own character's speed and profitability. A well-trained team and an upgraded restaurant are key to success.

Don't forget to keep your dining area clean! Walk around and clear dirty tables, otherwise, new customers won't have a place to sit. Once your burger joint is a roaring success, it's time to expand. Click the map icon to unlock new branches, including a bustling coffee shop and a sweet donut stand, and build your very own fast-food empire!
