Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to Fast Food Rush! Your goal is to transform a small burger stand into a thriving fast-food empire. You'll begin your journey with 2,500 coins to get your business off the ground.

To start serving customers, walk your character over the marked areas on the floor. First, unlock the seating area, then the order counter, and finally the grill. Once these are active, customers will start arriving, and you'll automatically begin earning coins as you grill burgers and serve them.

As coins flow in, strategically reinvest them into your business. Step on the white rectangular markers scattered around the restaurant to purchase various upgrades that will improve your operations and increase your income.

A great team is key to success! Unlock the HR manager's office to hire new employees and upgrade their speed and carrying capacity. Next, visit the general manager to improve your own character's speed, capacity, and overall profit margins.

Don't forget to keep your restaurant clean! Walk around the dining area and clear away any dirty tables. If tables remain messy, new customers won't sit down, which will hurt your sales. A clean restaurant is a profitable one!

Once your burger joint is a hit, it's time to expand! Click the map icon on your screen to unlock new business ventures, including a bustling coffee shop and a delicious donut shop. Grow your empire and live out your fast-food dream!
