Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Your goal is to build a fast-food empire, starting with just 2,500 coins. Use your character to walk around the restaurant. Begin by walking over the white markers on the floor to unlock your first amenities. First, unlock a seating area (75 coins), then a counter for orders, and finally the grill (85 coins) to start cooking. Once the grill is active, your first customers will arrive!

As customers come in, your character will automatically start grilling burgers, serving them at the counter, and collecting money. Coins will flow in as long as you keep your customers happy. A key part of the game is keeping the restaurant clean. Walk over to dirty tables to clear them; if you don't, new customers won't be able to sit down, and you'll lose out on potential income.

Invest your earnings wisely by unlocking upgrades. Walk over the various markers scattered around the floor to purchase improvements. You can also unlock an HR Manager who allows you to hire more workers and upgrade their speed and carrying capacity. A well-trained team is essential for maximizing your profits. Next, unlock the General Manager to improve your own character's speed and capacity, making you more efficient.

Once your first burger joint is thriving, it's time to expand! Click the map icon on the top left of your screen to see new locations. You can unlock a Coffee Shop and a Donut Shop to grow your empire and take your American dream to the next level.
