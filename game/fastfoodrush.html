Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to your very own fast-food restaurant! Use your initial 2,500 coins to kickstart your burger empire. The controls are simple: use your mouse or finger to move your character around the restaurant.

Your first task is to unlock the essential areas. Walk over the markers on the floor to spend coins and open new stations. Start by unlocking a seating area, then a counter for taking orders, and finally the grill to start cooking. Once these are active, customers will start coming in, and you'll begin to earn money automatically.

To grow your business, you must invest wisely. Walk over the various upgrade markers scattered around to spend your earnings. You can unlock the HR Manager to hire more workers and improve their speed and carrying capacity. Visit the General Manager to upgrade your own character's speed and increase your overall profit margins.

A clean restaurant is a profitable one! Make sure to walk around the dining area and tap or click on dirty tables to clean them. If tables remain messy, new customers won't sit down, and you'll lose out on potential income. As you succeed, you can expand your empire by unlocking new branches like a coffee shop and a donut stand via the map!
