Fast Food Rush

How to Play Fast Food Rush

Welcome to your new restaurant! Your goal is to build a fast-food empire from the ground up. You start with 2,500 coins. Begin by walking your character over the white markers on the floor to unlock your first seating area and a counter for customers. Next, unlock the grill to start cooking and serving your first burgers. As you serve customers, you'll automatically start earning coins.

Invest your earnings wisely to grow your business. Look for other white markers scattered around the restaurant to purchase upgrades like new tables, better equipment, and expanded service areas. A well-equipped restaurant leads to happier customers and more income flowing in.

To speed things up, unlock the HR manager's office. Inside, you can hire more workers and upgrade their speed and carrying capacity. A skilled team is essential for efficiency. Don't forget to unlock the general manager to improve your own speed, capacity, and boost your overall profits.

A clean restaurant is crucial for success. Keep an eye on the tables and walk over to dirty ones to clean them up. If tables stay messy for too long, new customers won't sit down, and you'll lose out on potential sales. Once your first location is thriving, open the map to expand your empire by unlocking a coffee shop and a donut shop!
