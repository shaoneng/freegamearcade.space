Fireboy and Watergirl

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl

The objective in Fireboy and Watergirl is to guide both characters through a series of puzzles to reach their respective exits. Teamwork is essential, as these two elemental friends have unique abilities and weaknesses.

Control Fireboy using the Arrow Keys. He can safely walk through pools of fire but will be extinguished by water. Control Watergirl using the W, A, and D keys. She can move through water but will evaporate if she touches fire. Be careful! Both characters will perish if they touch the green goo, so avoid it at all costs.

Throughout each level, you'll need to collect gems. Fireboy can only pick up the red gems, and Watergirl can only grab the blue ones. Work together to activate levers, press buttons, and move platforms to clear a path for both characters. You can play solo by controlling both characters, or team up with a friend on the same keyboard for a truly cooperative experience!
