Fireboy and Watergirl

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl

Welcome to the Forest Temple! Fireboy and Watergirl is a cooperative puzzle-platformer where you control two characters with opposite elemental abilities. The goal is to guide them both safely to their respective exits in each level.

Controls: Use the Arrow Keys to move Fireboy and the WASD keys to move Watergirl. You can play with a friend, or challenge yourself by controlling both characters at once!

Elemental Rules: Remember, opposites attract, but here they repel! Fireboy can walk through pools of fire but will be extinguished by water. Watergirl can wade through water but will evaporate in fire. Be careful, as both characters will be defeated if they touch the green goo!

Teamwork is Key: Success depends on cooperation. You'll need to use both characters to push levers, press buttons, and move platforms to clear a path for each other. Collect all the gems on your way—red gems for Fireboy, blue gems for Watergirl—and guide them to their doors to complete the level.
