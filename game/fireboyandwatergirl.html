Fireboy and Watergirl

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl

Welcome to the Forest Temple, where teamwork is key! In Fireboy and Watergirl, you control two elemental characters who must work together to solve puzzles and navigate each level. The core rule is simple: opposites do not mix! Fireboy is harmed by water, and Watergirl is harmed by fire. Be careful, as both characters will be defeated if they touch the green slime.

The controls are designed for either a single player managing both characters or two players on the same keyboard. Use the Arrow Keys to move Fireboy and the A, W, D keys to move Watergirl. Your goal is to guide both heroes through the maze-like levels, collecting their respective gems along the wayâ€”red for Fireboy and blue for Watergirl.

To complete a level, you must successfully guide both Fireboy and Watergirl to their designated doors. This often involves pushing blocks, flipping levers, and timing your movements perfectly. Success depends on coordinating their actions to overcome obstacles that neither could pass alone. Good luck!
