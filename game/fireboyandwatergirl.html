Fireboy and Watergirl

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl

Welcome to the Forest Temple! In Fireboy and Watergirl, you control two elemental characters who must cooperate to solve puzzles. The key to success is teamwork and coordination. You can play solo by controlling both characters, or team up with a friend on the same keyboard for a true cooperative experience!

Controls: Use the Arrow Keys (←, ↑, →) to move Fireboy. Use the W, A, D keys to move Watergirl.

Rules of the Temple: Fireboy can walk through pools of lava and collect red gems, but will be extinguished by water. Watergirl can wade through water and collect blue gems, but will evaporate in lava. Both characters must avoid the green goo, as it is deadly to both!

The goal of each level is to collect all the gems and guide both Fireboy and Watergirl to their respective exit doors. You'll need to use levers, push blocks, and time your movements perfectly to solve the puzzles and advance to the next challenge.
