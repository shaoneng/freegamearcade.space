Fireboy and Watergirl

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl

Welcome to the Forest Temple! Fireboy and Watergirl is a cooperative puzzle-platformer where you control two characters with opposite abilities. Your goal is to guide them both safely to their respective exit doors in each level.

Controlling Fireboy: Use the Arrow Keys (←, ↑, →) to move and jump. Fireboy is immune to fire and can walk through lava pools, but he will be extinguished if he touches any water. He can only collect the red gems.

Controlling Watergirl: Use the A, W, and D keys to move and jump. Watergirl can safely walk through water pools but will evaporate if she touches the lava. She can only collect the blue gems.

Remember, teamwork is key! You'll need to use both characters to flip switches, push boxes, and solve puzzles together. Be careful of the green goo pools—they are deadly to both Fireboy and Watergirl! Work together to overcome the obstacles and complete all the levels.
