Fireboy and Watergirl

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl

Embark on a classic cooperative adventure with Fireboy and Watergirl! As the saying goes, "opposites attract," and in this game, you'll need to use their unique abilities to solve puzzles in the Forest Temple.

Fireboy can walk through pools of fire but will be extinguished by water. Watergirl can wade through water but will evaporate if she touches fire. Both characters must avoid the dangerous green ooze. Control Fireboy with the arrow keys and Watergirl with the A, W, and D keys.

Work together to flip switches, push boxes, and collect gems to clear a path to the exit. The goal is to get both characters to their respective doors at the end of each of the 30+ challenging levels. This popular arcade-style game, created by Oslo Albet, is a true test of teamwork and puzzle-solving skills.
