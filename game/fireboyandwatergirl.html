Fireboy and Watergirl

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl

The goal in Fireboy and Watergirl is to guide both characters safely to their respective exits in each level of the Forest Temple. Along the way, you must collect all the gemsâ€”red gems for Fireboy and blue gems for Watergirl. Teamwork is essential to overcome the obstacles and solve the puzzles presented in each stage.

Control Fireboy using the Arrow Keys (Up, Left, Right) and Watergirl using the W, A, and D keys. This setup makes it perfect for two players on the same keyboard, or for a single player looking for a coordination challenge.

Remember the core rule: opposites do not attract! Fireboy can walk through fire pools but will be extinguished by water. Watergirl can safely pass through water pools but will evaporate in fire. Both characters must avoid the green goo, as it is fatal to both. Use these properties to your advantage to navigate the temple's tricky layouts.

You will encounter various puzzle elements like levers, buttons, and pushable boxes. Often, one character will need to hold a switch or button to allow the other to pass an obstacle. Careful planning and communication (or quick finger-switching if playing solo) are the keys to success.
