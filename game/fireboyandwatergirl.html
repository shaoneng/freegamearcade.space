Fireboy and Watergirl

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl

Welcome to the Forest Temple, a classic cooperative puzzle-platformer! Your mission is to guide two unique characters, Fireboy and Watergirl, to their respective exits in each level. The catch? They have opposing elemental powers and must work together to overcome obstacles. You can control both characters yourself or team up with a friend on the same keyboard!

Control Fireboy using the Arrow Keys and Watergirl with the A, W, and D keys. Remember their fundamental rule: opposites attract, but they can't mix! Fireboy can walk through fiery lava but will be extinguished by water pools. Conversely, Watergirl can safely wade through water but will evaporate in lava. Both heroes must avoid the dangerous green goo, as it is fatal to both.
