Fireboy and Watergirl

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl

Welcome to the Forest Temple! In Fireboy and Watergirl, your goal is to guide two distinct characters through a series of challenging puzzles. This is a cooperative game, meaning you'll need both characters to work together to succeed, whether you're playing solo or with a friend!

The controls are simple: use the Arrow Keys to move Fireboy and the A, W, D keys to move Watergirl.

Remember the fundamental rule: opposites do not attract danger! Fireboy can safely walk through lava and collect red gems, but will be extinguished by water pools. Watergirl can glide through water and collect blue gems, but will evaporate in lava. Both characters must avoid the dangerous green ooze.

To complete a level, you must navigate both characters to their respective doors. Along the way, you'll need to push blocks, flip levers, and press buttons to clear a path. Teamwork is essential to solve each puzzle and escape the temple!
