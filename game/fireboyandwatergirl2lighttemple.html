Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

Welcome to the Light Temple! The goal in Fireboy and Watergirl 2 is to guide both characters through each level to their respective exit doors. Teamwork is essential, as many puzzles require both characters to act together. You can control both characters yourself or play with a friend on the same keyboard.

Control Fireboy with the Arrow Keys and Watergirl with the A, W, D keys. Remember their fundamental rule: Fireboy can safely walk through fire pits but will be extinguished by water pools, while Watergirl can navigate water pools but sizzles in fire. Both characters must avoid the dangerous green goo pools to survive.

The unique challenge in the Light Temple is manipulating light. You'll find mirrors and mirrored boxes throughout the levels. Push and position these objects to reflect beams of light onto sensors. Activating these sensors is the key to moving elevators, opening gates, and clearing your path. Think carefully about where you direct the light to solve the puzzles.

Beyond the light puzzles, you'll need to use levers, push buttons, and operate various mechanisms. Often, one character will need to hold a switch while the other proceeds. Collect all the diamonds in each level—red for Fireboy, blue for Watergirl—for a perfect score. Good luck navigating the ancient temple!
