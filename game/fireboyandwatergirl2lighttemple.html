Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

Embark on a new adventure in Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple, the second installment in the popular cooperative puzzle series. Your mission is to guide Fireboy and Watergirl through the intricate levels of the ancient Light Temple. Teamwork is crucial as you must navigate both characters to their respective exit doors to complete each stage.

The Light Temple introduces a unique challenge: manipulating beams of light. You'll need to use an array of mirrors to reflect and direct light beams onto detectors. Successfully activating these detectors will open doors, move elevators, and clear your path. Careful planning and logic are required before making any move, as you and your partner must act in concert to succeed.

Interact with the temple's mechanisms to solve its puzzles. You will need to pull levers, flip switches, and press buttons to activate platforms and other elements. A key feature is the ability to move special mirrored boxes to bend light around corners. Some levels also feature mirror sliders, which allow for more precise, free-angle rotation of mirrors, giving you greater control over the light beams.

Remember, Fireboy can traverse fire but is extinguished by water, while Watergirl can pass through water but is vaporized by fire. Both characters must avoid the green goo. Success lies in using each character's unique abilities and working together to conquer the temple's light-based puzzles.
