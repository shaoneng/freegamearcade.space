Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

Welcome to the Light Temple! Your mission is to guide the dynamic duo, Fireboy and Watergirl, through a series of challenging levels to reach their designated exit doors. Success depends on teamwork, so you'll need to control both characters to solve the puzzles, even when playing alone.

Control Fireboy using the Arrow Keys and Watergirl using the A, W, and D keys. Navigate them through the temple's intricate passages, pushing buttons, pulling levers, and collecting gems. Remember to gather the red gems for Fireboy and the blue gems for Watergirl.

The unique challenge in the Light Temple is manipulating beams of light. You will need to push and position mirrored boxes to reflect light onto sensors. Activating these sensors is crucial for operating elevators, opening gates, and clearing your path forward. Careful planning and precise positioning are key!

Be mindful of the elemental hazards! Fireboy is immune to fire but will be extinguished by water. Watergirl can safely pass through water but will evaporate in fire pits. Both characters must avoid the dangerous green goo at all costs. Work together, master the light, and conquer the temple!
