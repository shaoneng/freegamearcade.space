Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

Welcome to the Light Temple! The primary goal in this cooperative puzzle game is to guide both Fireboy and Watergirl to their respective exit doors in each level. Success hinges on teamwork, as many obstacles can only be overcome when the two characters work in perfect sync.

In this temple, light is your most important tool. You must manipulate beams of light using mirrors to activate sensors. These sensors control doors, elevators, and other mechanisms that block your path. You will need to push and pull levers, press buttons, and strategically position mirrored boxes to bend the light just right.

The temple features different types of mirrors. Standard mirrors can be moved at 90-degree angles, while special mirror sliders allow for more precise, rotational adjustments. Mastering how to use both types of mirrors is crucial for solving the more complex puzzles and ensuring the light beams hit their targets.

Remember, logic and planning are essential. Think through your moves before you act, and always consider how one character's actions will affect the other. Only by combining their elemental powers and your brainpower can you conquer the Light Temple!
