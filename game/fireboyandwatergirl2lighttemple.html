Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

Welcome to the Light Temple! Your primary objective in Fireboy and Watergirl 2 is to guide both characters through each puzzle-filled level to reach their designated exits, marked by their respective colors. This is a game of pure teamwork, requiring you to control both Fireboy and Watergirl to overcome obstacles together. Remember their elemental rules: Fireboy can walk through fire but is extinguished by water, while Watergirl can safely navigate water pools but is harmed by fire. Both characters must avoid the dangerous green goo!

The unique challenge in this temple is the manipulation of light. You'll encounter light beams that must be directed onto sensors to activate platforms, open doors, and clear your path. Use movable mirror boxes, levers, and buttons to reflect and bend the light correctly. Careful planning and coordination are essential, as many puzzles require both characters to perform actions in a specific sequence.

The controls are simple and designed for two players on one keyboard, or one player controlling both. Use the Arrow Keys to move Fireboy, and the W, A, S, D keys to move Watergirl. Good luck, and may the light guide your way!
