Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

Welcome to the Light Temple! Your mission is to guide Fireboy and Watergirl through each level and get them to their corresponding exit doors. Teamwork is crucial, as many puzzles require both characters to act together, whether playing solo or with a friend.

Control Fireboy using the Arrow Keys and Watergirl with the A, W, D keys. Remember their elemental rules: Fireboy can walk through pools of lava but is harmed by water, while Watergirl can safely pass through water but will be extinguished by lava. Both heroes must avoid the green goo!

The central puzzle element in this temple is light. You'll need to push and position mirrors to reflect beams of light onto sensors. Activating these sensors is key to opening doors, moving elevators, and clearing the way forward. You will also encounter levers, buttons, and sliding platforms that require coordinated action.

Plan your moves carefully, use logic, and coordinate between Fireboy and Watergirl to solve each intricate puzzle and successfully escape the Light Temple!
