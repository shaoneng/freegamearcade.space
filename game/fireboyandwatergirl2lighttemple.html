Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

Welcome back to the world of elemental cooperation! The goal in Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple is to guide both characters safely to their respective exits in each level. Use the arrow keys to control Fireboy and the W, A, S, D keys to control Watergirl. Remember, Fireboy cannot touch water, and Watergirl cannot touch fire lava. Both characters must avoid the green goo!

This temple introduces a new, brilliant challenge: light puzzles. You must manipulate light beams using movable mirrors and other contraptions to activate sensors, open doors, and power elevators. Push mirrored boxes and rotate special mirror sliders to direct the light exactly where it needs to go. Careful planning is key before you make a move.

Success depends entirely on teamwork. You'll need to coordinate the actions of both Fireboy and Watergirl to solve the puzzles. One character might need to hold a switch while the other passes through a gate, or both might need to work together to position a light beam. Cooperate to conquer the Light Temple's challenges and collect all the diamonds along the way!
