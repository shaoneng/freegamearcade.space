Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

Welcome to the dazzling challenges of the Light Temple! Your goal is to guide both Fireboy and Watergirl through a series of intricate levels to reach their respective exit doors. Teamwork is everything in this cooperative puzzle-platformer.

Controls:

  • Control Fireboy using the Arrow Keys.
  • Control Watergirl using the A, W, and D keys.

Remember their elemental strengths and weaknesses: Fireboy can safely walk through lava pools but will be extinguished by water. Watergirl can pass through water but will evaporate in lava. Both characters must avoid the dangerous green goo!

The unique challenge in the Light Temple is manipulating light. You'll need to push and position special mirrored boxes to reflect beams of light onto sensors. Activating these sensors is crucial for opening doors, moving elevators, and clearing your path. Think carefully before each move, as you'll often need both characters to work together to solve a single puzzle.
