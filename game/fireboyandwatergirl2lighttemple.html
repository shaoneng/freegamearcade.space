Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

Welcome to the Light Temple! In this exciting sequel, your goal is to guide Fireboy and Watergirl through a series of challenging puzzles. This game is designed for two players (or one player controlling both characters) and relies heavily on teamwork and logic.

The main objective is to collect all the gems and lead each character to their respective exit door. Remember the golden rule: Fireboy can walk through fire but is extinguished by water, while Watergirl can pass through water but is evaporated by fire. Both characters must avoid the hazardous green goo pools at all costs!

The unique challenge in the Light Temple is manipulating light. You'll need to push and position mirrors and mirrored boxes to reflect beams of light. Direct these beams into light detectors to activate doors, elevators, and other mechanisms. Plan your moves carefully, as cooperation is the only way to solve the intricate puzzles and escape the temple.

Controls:

  • Fireboy: Use the Arrow Keys to move and jump.
  • Watergirl: Use the W, A, S, D keys to move and jump.
