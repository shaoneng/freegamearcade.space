Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

Welcome back to the world of elemental puzzles! Your goal in Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple is to guide both characters to their designated doors in each level. Teamwork is essential for success. Use the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control Fireboy and the 'A', 'W', 'D' keys to control Watergirl. Remember, Fireboy can walk through fire but not water, and Watergirl can pass through water but not fire. Both must avoid the green goo!

This temple's main challenge revolves around manipulating light. You'll encounter beams of light that need to be directed onto detectors to activate doors, elevators, and other mechanisms. To do this, you'll need to push and position various types of mirrors. Some mirrors can be pushed around, while others are on sliders for more precise rotation and aiming.

Beyond mirrors, you'll need to use levers, buttons, and switches to clear your path. Often, one character will need to hold down a button or lever while the other proceeds through an opened passage. Careful coordination and planning are key to solving each level and collecting all the diamonds for a perfect score.
