Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

Your main objective is to guide both Fireboy and Watergirl to their respective exit doors in each level of the Light Temple. Cooperation is key, whether you're controlling both characters yourself or playing with a friend. Many puzzles are impossible to solve alone!

Control Fireboy using the Arrow Keys and Watergirl with the W, A, S, D keys. Remember their elemental rules: Fireboy can pass through pools of fire but will be extinguished by water. Watergirl can safely wade through water but cannot touch fire. Both characters must avoid the dangerous green goo.

The unique challenge in the Light Temple is manipulating beams of light. You'll need to push and position mirrored boxes to reflect light onto detectors. Activating these detectors will operate elevators, open doors, and clear your path forward. Use levers and buttons to adjust contraptions and solve the temple's intricate light-based puzzles.

Plan your moves carefully and think ahead. Success depends on using both characters' strengths and solving the light puzzles with logic and precision. Collect all the diamonds in each level to achieve a perfect score!
