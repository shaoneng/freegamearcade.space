Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 2: Light Temple

Welcome to the Light Temple, the exciting sequel in the Fireboy and Watergirl series! Your mission is to guide both characters through a series of challenging puzzles to reach their respective exit doors. This is a cooperative game, so you'll need to use both characters' abilities to succeed. You can play solo by controlling both characters, or team up with a friend on the same keyboard!

Controls:
- Use the A, W, D keys to move Watergirl.
- Use the Arrow Keys to move Fireboy.

The unique challenge in the Light Temple is manipulating light. You'll find mirrors and mirrored boxes that you can push and rotate. Your goal is to direct beams of light into detectors to activate elevators, open doors, and solve puzzles. Some levels feature special sliding mirrors that offer more precise control over the light's direction. Think carefully before each move to position the mirrors correctly.

Remember the classic rules: Fireboy is immune to fire pits but will be extinguished by water. Watergirl can walk through water but will evaporate in fire.
