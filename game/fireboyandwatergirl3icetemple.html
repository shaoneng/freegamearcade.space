Fireboy and Watergirl 3: Ice Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 3: Ice Temple

In Fireboy and Watergirl 3: Ice Temple, your objective is to guide both characters through each level to their respective exit doors. This cooperative puzzle game requires teamwork to succeed!

Controls: Control Watergirl with the W, A, S, D keys and Fireboy with the Arrow Keys. You can play solo by controlling both, or team up with a friend on the same keyboard!

Core Rules: Remember that Fireboy cannot touch water, and Watergirl cannot touch lava. Both characters must avoid the green goo. The main challenge in the Ice Temple is the slippery ice. Fireboy slides on ice, while Watergirl walks slowly on it. You'll also encounter puzzles that require you to melt ice with Fireboy or freeze water with Watergirl.

Work together to push buttons, pull levers, and collect all the diamonds (red for Fireboy, blue for Watergirl) to complete each level and escape the temple.
