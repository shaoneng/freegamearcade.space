Fireboy and Watergirl 3: Ice Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 3: Ice Temple

Welcome to the Ice Temple! The goal is to guide both Fireboy and Watergirl through each level to their respective exits. Teamwork is essential to overcome obstacles and solve puzzles. Fireboy collects red diamonds and is immune to fire pits, but will be extinguished by water. Watergirl collects blue diamonds and can safely pass through water, but will evaporate in fire pits. Both characters must avoid the green goo.

The controls are simple and designed for two players on one keyboard. Use the Arrow Keys to move Fireboy and the W, A, D keys to move Watergirl. You can also play solo by controlling both characters yourself!

The Ice Temple introduces a new challenge: ice and snow. Fireboy will slide on icy surfaces, while Watergirl will move slowly. Use Fireboy's heat to melt ice and Watergirl's coolness to freeze water, activating different mechanisms. Pay close attention to light beams and mirrors to direct light and solve puzzles. Cooperate to press buttons, pull levers, and navigate the frozen maze to complete all 36 levels!
