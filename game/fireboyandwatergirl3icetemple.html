Fireboy and Watergirl 3: Ice Temple

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 3: Ice Temple

Welcome to the Ice Temple! Your goal in Fireboy and Watergirl 3 is to guide both characters through each level to their respective exit doors. Along the way, you must collect the red diamonds for Fireboy and the blue diamonds for Watergirl. Teamwork is essential to solve the puzzles and overcome all the obstacles.

Control Fireboy using the Arrow Keys and Watergirl using the A, W, and D keys. This allows two players to play on the same keyboard, or one player to control both characters simultaneously.

Remember the basic rules: Fireboy cannot touch water pools, and Watergirl cannot touch fire pits. Both characters will be eliminated if they touch the green ooze. The new challenge in the Ice Temple is the icy surfaces. Fireboy will slide quickly across ice, making him hard to stop, while Watergirl will walk slowly on it.

Use levers, buttons, and moving platforms to clear a path for both characters. Coordinate their movements carefully to conquer all 36 levels of the Ice Temple and escape!
