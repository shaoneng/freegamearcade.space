Fireboy and Watergirl 5: Elements

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 5: Elements

The objective in Fireboy and Watergirl 5: Elements is to guide both characters through a series of intricate temples to their respective exit doors. Success hinges on cooperation and understanding each character's unique abilities.

Here’s how to navigate the elemental challenges:

  • Control Both Characters: You control both Fireboy and Watergirl simultaneously. Use the Arrow Keys to move Fireboy and the W, A, S, D keys to move Watergirl. You can play solo, managing both, or team up with a friend on the same keyboard!
  • Respect the Elements: Fireboy can walk through fire and lava but cannot touch water. Watergirl can pass through water but will be extinguished by lava. Be careful—the green goo is deadly for both!
  • Solve Puzzles Together: Work as a team to press buttons, pull levers, and move platforms. Many obstacles can only be overcome when both characters cooperate.
  • Collect the Gems: Along the way, have Fireboy collect the red gems and Watergirl collect the blue gems. Try to grab all the gems in each level to achieve a perfect score.
