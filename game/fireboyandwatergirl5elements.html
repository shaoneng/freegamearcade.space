Fireboy and Watergirl 5: Elements

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 5: Elements

Welcome to the elemental temples! In Fireboy and Watergirl 5, teamwork is the key to success. Your goal is to guide both characters to their respective doors at the end of each level while collecting as many diamonds as possible.

Control Fireboy using the Arrow Keys and Watergirl using the A, W, and D keys. This setup is perfect for one player managing both, or for two players sharing a keyboard for a true cooperative experience.

Remember the fundamental rule: Fireboy can walk through lava but will be extinguished by water, while Watergirl can safely pass through water but will evaporate in lava. Both characters must avoid the green goo! Work together to operate levers, push boxes, activate sensors, and use teleporters to clear a path for each other.

Each temple presents unique challenges and mechanics. Plan your moves carefully and communicate if you're playing with a friend to conquer all the puzzles and master the elements!
