Fireboy and Watergirl 5: Elements

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 5: Elements

Welcome to the elemental temples! The goal of Fireboy and Watergirl 5: Elements is to guide both characters through a series of puzzles to reach their respective exit doors. This requires teamwork, as many obstacles can only be overcome by using both characters' unique abilities in tandem.

You control both characters simultaneously. On a computer, use the Arrow Keys (←, ↑, →) to move Fireboy and the A, W, D keys to move Watergirl. On mobile devices, the controls will be displayed on-screen.

Remember the fundamental rules of the elements: Fireboy can walk through pools of fire and lava but will be extinguished by water. Watergirl can safely move through water but will evaporate in lava. Be careful, as both characters must avoid the green goo pits!

In each level, your objective is to collect all the gems (red for Fireboy, blue for Watergirl) and solve puzzles involving levers, buttons, boxes, and teleporters. Once you've collected all the gems, navigate both Fireboy and Watergirl to their corresponding doors to complete the level and advance to the next challenge.
