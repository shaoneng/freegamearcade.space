Fireboy and Watergirl 5: Elements

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 5: Elements

Welcome to the elemental temples! The goal in Fireboy and Watergirl 5 is to guide both characters through each level to their respective exit doors. Success requires teamwork, as many puzzles can only be solved by using both characters' unique abilities in tandem.

Controls: You can play solo by controlling both characters or team up with a friend on the same keyboard!
- Use the Arrow Keys to move Fireboy.
- Use the A, W, D keys to move Watergirl.

Elemental Rules: Remember the basic rules of their world. Fireboy can walk through fire and lava but will be extinguished by water. Watergirl can pass through water pools but will evaporate in lava. Both characters must avoid the green acid pools at all costs!

Collect all the diamonds scattered throughout the level—red for Fireboy, blue for Watergirl—and then reach your doors. Use levers, push boxes, and activate platforms to clear your path. The faster you complete the level, the higher your score will be!
