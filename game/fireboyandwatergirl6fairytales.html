Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales

Welcome to the enchanting world of Fireboy and Watergirl 6! The goal is to guide both characters through a series of puzzle-filled temples to reach their respective exit doors. Teamwork is crucial, whether you're playing solo or with a friend.

Control Watergirl using the A, W, and D keys, and guide Fireboy with the Arrow keys. Remember their elemental rules: Fireboy can safely navigate pools of fire but will be extinguished by water. Watergirl can wade through water but cannot touch fire. Both characters must avoid the green goo pits!

This installment introduces a magical new twist: fairies! These glowing companions can help you solve puzzles. Click and drag a fairy of a specific color to its corresponding activation point. They can push buttons, hold down levers, and give you the assistance needed to overcome otherwise impossible obstacles. Use these magical helpers wisely to clear a path for our heroes!
