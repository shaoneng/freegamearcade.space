Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales

Welcome to the enchanting world of Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales! Your mission is to guide both characters through a series of intricate temples to their respective exit doors. Teamwork is the key to solving the puzzles in each level. You can control both characters by yourself or team up with a friend on the same keyboard for a classic co-op experience.

Control Fireboy using the Arrow Keys and Watergirl with the W, A, S, D keys. Remember the basic rules: Fireboy is immune to fire but vulnerable to water, while Watergirl can pass through water but not fire. Both characters must avoid the hazardous green goo.

This sixth adventure introduces a magical new helper: fairies! These glowing companions can be used to solve complex challenges. Simply click and drag a fairy with your mouse to have it push buttons or pull levers, opening up new paths for our heroes. Master the use of these fairies to conquer every temple!
