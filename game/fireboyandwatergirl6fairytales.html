Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales

Welcome to Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales! The goal is to guide both Fireboy and Watergirl through a series of challenging temple puzzles to reach their respective exit doors. Teamwork is essential, whether you're playing alone or with a friend.

Control Fireboy using the Arrow Keys and Watergirl using the A, W, D keys. Remember their elemental rules: Fireboy can walk through lava but is harmed by water, while Watergirl can safely navigate water but is extinguished by lava. Both characters will be eliminated by green goo, so avoid it at all costs!

This adventure introduces a magical new feature: fairies! Click and drag these glowing companions to help you solve puzzles. They can be moved to press buttons, hold down levers, and give you the extra hand you need to clear a path. Coordinate their movements with Fireboy and Watergirl to overcome obstacles that would otherwise be impossible.

Work together to collect all the diamonds on each level—red diamonds for Fireboy, blue for Watergirl—and safely guide both characters to their doors to complete the stage and advance to the next fairytale challenge.
