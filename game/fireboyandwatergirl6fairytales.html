Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales

Your goal is to guide both Fireboy and Watergirl through a series of puzzling temples to their respective exit doors. Teamwork is crucial, as you'll need to coordinate their unique abilities to solve challenges. You can play solo by controlling both characters or team up with a friend for a classic co-op experience.

Control Fireboy using the Arrow Keys and Watergirl with the A, W, D keys. Remember their elemental rules: Fireboy can safely navigate through fire pits but will be extinguished by water, while Watergirl can pass through water pools but cannot touch fire. Both characters must avoid the dangerous green goo.

This installment introduces a magical new feature: fairies! These colorful, sparkling companions are key to solving many puzzles. Use your mouse to click and drag fairies of a specific color to activate corresponding buttons and levers. This allows the duo to overcome obstacles that would otherwise be impossible, adding a fresh layer of strategy to the adventure.
