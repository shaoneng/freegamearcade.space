Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales

How to Play Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales

Welcome to Fireboy and Watergirl 6: Fairy Tales! In this co-op puzzle adventure, you control two characters with unique abilities. Remember the basic rules: Fireboy can walk through lava but is extinguished by water, while Watergirl can pass through water but sizzles in lava. Both characters must avoid the green goo! Your goal is to work together to solve puzzles and reach the exit doors.

This installment introduces a magical new feature: fairies! These glowing companions are essential for solving the temples' trickiest puzzles. To use them, simply click and drag a fairy to a button or lever. The fairies can reach places Fireboy and Watergirl can't, helping you activate platforms, open doors, and clear paths. Match the fairy's color to the mechanism for it to work.

You can play solo by controlling both characters, or team up with a friend on the same keyboard for a true co-op experience. Use the Arrow Keys to move Fireboy and the A, W, D keys to move Watergirl. Teamwork is the key to success!
