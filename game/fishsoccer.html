Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the underwater arena of Fish Soccer! The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out. You can dive in solo against the computer or challenge a friend in the 2-player mode.

You control the large koi fish, while two smaller fish on your team move automatically to help defend and attack. Nudge the bubbly soccer ball with your fish, using clever angles and momentum to guide it toward the opposing net. Be mindful of the water physicsâ€”the ball won't travel in a straight line, adding a fun challenge to every shot!

Gain a tactical advantage by using the snack attack! Press the designated key (T for Player 1, P for Player 2) to drop a food pellet near your opponent's goal. Their small helper fish will get distracted and swarm the snack, leaving their goal wide open for you to score. The player with the highest score at the end of one minute wins the match!
