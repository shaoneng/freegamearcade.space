Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the wacky underwater arena of Fish Soccer! The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out. You can dive in solo against the computer or challenge a friend in the 2-player mode.

Control your main fish (Orange for Player 1, Blue for Player 2) to nudge the bubbly soccer ball towards the opponent's net. Your two smaller fish teammates move automatically. Since you're playing in water, the ball won't travel in a straight line, so you'll need to master the unique physics and use clever angles to score.

For a strategic advantage, use the snack attack! Press 'T' or 'P' to drop a food pellet. This will distract your opponent's smaller fish, luring them away from their goal and creating a perfect scoring opportunity. The player with the most goals at the end of the minute wins the match!
