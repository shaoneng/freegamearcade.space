Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the underwater arena of Fish Soccer! The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out. You can dive in solo against the AI or challenge a friend in the 2-player mode. Each player controls a large koi fish, supported by two smaller, automated teammates who help defend your goal.

Controlling your fish is all about momentum and angles. Guide your fish to nudge the wobbly, bubbly ball towards the opponent's net. Since you're underwater, the ball's movement is affected by drag, so you'll need to anticipate its path. Master sliding in from the side and flicking your tail to get the perfect shot.

For a clever tactical advantage, use the snack feature! Press the designated key (T or P) to drop a food pellet near your opponent's goal. Their small helper fish will get distracted and swarm the food, leaving their net wide open for you to score an easy goal. Use this trick wisely to turn the tide of the match!

If you just want to relax, select "Watch the Match" from the main menu. The game will play itself, allowing you to enjoy the chaotic, bubbly action as a spectator. Whether playing or watching, Fish Soccer offers a quick and delightful aquatic sports experience.
