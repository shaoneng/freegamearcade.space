Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the wacky underwater arena of Fish Soccer! The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out. You control a large koi fish, and your goal is to bump the bubbly soccer ball into the other team's net.

The game can be played in either 1-player or 2-player mode. Your fish follows your cursor (or finger on mobile). Nudge the ball with your fish to move it. Remember, you're underwater! The ball won't travel in a straight line due to the water drag, so you'll need to use clever angles and momentum to guide it toward the goal.

You are not alone in the water; two smaller fish will automatically help defend your goal. To gain a strategic advantage, you can distract your opponent's defenders. Press the 'T' or 'P' key to drop a food pellet near their goal. Their fish will swim over to eat it, leaving their net wide open for a perfect shot! The player with the highest score at the end of one minute wins the match.
