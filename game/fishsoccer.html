Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the underwater arena of Fish Soccer! The objective is simple: outscore your opponent within the 60-second time limit. Dive in solo against the computer or challenge a friend in the exciting 2-player mode.

You control the large fish on your team, while two smaller fish provide automated support. Due to the watery physics, you'll need to master angles and momentum to guide the bubbly ball into the net.

Player 1 (Orange Fish): Use the WASD keys to move. Press the T key to drop a food pellet and distract your opponent's helper fish.

Player 2 (Blue Fish): Use the Arrow Keys to move. Press the P key to drop a food pellet and distract your opponent's helper fish.

Use the food distraction wisely to create scoring opportunities. The player with the most goals when the clock hits zero is the champion of the fishy pitch!
