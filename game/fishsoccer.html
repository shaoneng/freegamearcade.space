Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the underwater arena of Fish Soccer! The goal is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out. You can dive in solo against the computer or challenge a friend in the 2-Player mode.

You control the main, larger fish on your team, while two smaller fish act as automated teammates. Nudge the wibbly, bubbly ball with your fish to guide it toward the opposing goal. Be mindful of the water's dragâ€”the ball will curve and float, so mastering angles is key to scoring!

For a tactical advantage, you can drop a food pellet to distract your opponent's smaller fish. This clever move will draw them away from their goal, creating a perfect scoring opportunity for you. The highest score at the end of the minute-long match wins the game!
