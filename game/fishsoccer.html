Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the underwater arena of Fish Soccer! The goal is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out. Choose to play against the computer in 1 Player mode or challenge a friend in 2 Player mode. Each player controls a large koi, assisted by two smaller, automated teammates.

Navigate your fish across the pitch to bump the bubbly ball towards the opposing net. The controls are intuitive, but you'll need to master the water physicsâ€”the ball won't travel in a straight line, so use clever angles and momentum to score.

For a strategic advantage, use the special food pellet ability! Player 1 can press 'T' and Player 2 can press 'P' to drop a tasty snack near the opponent's goal. This will distract their smaller helper fish, leaving their goal wide open for an easy shot.

If you just want to relax, select "Watch the Match" from the main menu. The game will play itself, allowing you to enjoy the chaotic, bubbly fun as a spectator. The player with the highest score at the end of the minute wins the match!
