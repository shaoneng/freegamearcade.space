Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the wacky world of underwater soccer! The objective in Fish Soccer is to score more goals than your opponent within the frantic 60-second time limit. You can challenge the computer in 1 Player mode or go head-to-head with a friend in 2 Player mode.

Take control of your designated fish (Player 1 is the Orange Fish, Player 2 is the Blue Fish). Your larger fish is your main player, while two smaller fish act as your automatic teammates. Nudge the ball with your body, using angles and the natural drag of the water to guide it toward the goal. The physics are slippery, just like real water polo!

To gain a strategic advantage, you can distract your opponent's helper fish. Press the 'T' key (for Player 1) or the 'P' key (for Player 2) to drop a food pellet on their side of the pitch. Their small fish will swarm the snack, leaving their goal wide open for a few precious seconds. The player with the highest score when the clock hits zero is the champion! If you'd rather just relax, select 'Watch the Match' from the menu to see the AI play.
