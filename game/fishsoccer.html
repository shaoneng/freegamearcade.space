Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the underwater arena! The goal in Fish Soccer is to score more goals than your opponent within the 60-second time limit. You control a large koi fish, flanked by two smaller, automated teammates.

You can play in 1-Player mode against the AI or challenge a friend in 2-Player mode. Use your controls to swim and bump the wobbly, bubbly ball towards the opponent's net. Because you're underwater, the ball's movement is affected by drag, so you'll need to use clever angles and momentum to score.

For a clever strategic move, press the T key (Player 1) or the P key (Player 2) to drop a food pellet. This will distract your rival's small helper fish, causing them to swarm the food and leave their goal wide open for a shot. The player with the highest score when the timer hits zero wins the match!

If you just want to watch the fun, select "Watch the Match" from the main menu and enjoy the show as the AI plays against itself.
