Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the underwater arena of Fish Soccer! This game is all about fast, frantic fun. You can play solo against the computer (1 Player) or grab a friend for a local showdown (2 Player). The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out!

Control your main fish (Orange for Player 1, Blue for Player 2) to nudge the bubbly ball towards the opposing net. The underwater physics means the ball won't travel in a straight line, so you'll need to master the art of angling your fish to guide your shots. Your two smaller teammates will move around automatically to help defend your goal.

To gain a tactical advantage, use the snack feature! Press 'T' (Player 1) or 'P' (Player 2) to drop a food pellet near your opponent's goal. Their small helper fish will get distracted and swim over to eat it, leaving their net wide open for an easy shot. Use this strategy wisely to outsmart your rival.

The player with the highest score when the clock hits zero is the winner. If you just want to relax, you can even choose "Watch the Match" from the main menu to see the AI play against itself.
