Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Dive into the wacky world of Fish Soccer! The goal is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out. You can challenge the computer in 1 Player mode or go head-to-head with a friend in 2 Player mode. Each player controls a large koi fish, supported by two smaller, automated teammates.

Player 1 (Orange Fish) uses the Arrow Keys to move, while Player 2 (Blue Fish) uses the WASD keys. Since you're playing underwater, the ball has a natural drag. You'll need to master angles and momentum to guide the bubbly ball into the net. A well-timed flick of your tail is key to scoring!

Gain a strategic advantage by distracting your opponent's goalie fish! Press the 'P' key (Player 1) or the 'T' key (Player 2) to drop a tasty food pellet near the rival's smaller fish. They'll abandon their post to go for the snack, leaving their goal wide open for an easy shot. If you'd rather just sit back and enjoy the show, select 'Watch the Match' from the main menu to see the AI play itself.
