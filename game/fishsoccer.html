Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the wacky world of Fish Soccer! The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out. You can dive in solo against the computer in 1-Player mode or challenge a friend on the same device in 2-Player mode.

You control the main large fish on your team (Orange for Player 1, Blue for Player 2), flanked by two smaller, automated teammates. Guide your fish to nudge the ball across the underwater pitch. Be mindful of the water's dragâ€”it affects the ball's trajectory, so you'll need to master your angles and momentum to get it into the net.

Gain a competitive edge with the snack attack! Press the 'T' key (Player 1) or 'P' key (Player 2) to drop a food pellet near your opponent's goal. Their smaller helper fish will get distracted and swim over to eat it, leaving their goal wide open for a shot!

Each match is a quick, one-minute showdown. The player with the highest score when the clock hits zero is declared the winner. If you'd rather sit back and relax, you can also select "Watch the Match" from the main menu to see the AI play against itself.
