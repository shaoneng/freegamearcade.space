Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Fish Soccer is a delightful and quirky free online game where football gets a fin-tastic twist! Take control of a chunky koi in a shallow, water-filled pitch. The goal is simple: bump the bubbly ball into your opponent's net before the 60-second timer runs out. Enjoy the charming plops and cheers in this light, quick, and fun aquatic party.

Choose between 1 Player (vs. AI) or 2 Player mode. Your large fish starts at midfield, supported by two smaller, automated teammates. Nudging the ball is all about physics and angles—slide in, flick your tail, and use the water's drag to your advantage. The ball won't travel in a straight line, adding to the challenge!

Distract your opponent's defenders by using snacks! Press 'T' (Player 1) or 'P' (Player 2) to drop a food pellet near the rival's smaller fish. They'll swarm the snack, leaving their goal wide open for a precious moment. The player with the highest score at the end of one minute wins the match.

Fish Soccer was created by Neplin Game Studio. If you enjoy the silly physics, try Soccer Random for more wacky kicks or Pill Soccer for larger arenas and power-ups.
