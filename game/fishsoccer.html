Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

The objective in Fish Soccer is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out. You'll control a large koi fish in an underwater arena, trying to nudge a giant, wobbly ball into the opposing net. Choose between 1 Player mode to challenge the AI or 2 Player mode to compete against a friend on the same device.

Maneuver your fish around the aquatic pitch to bump the ball. Because of the water's drag, the ball won't travel in a straight line, so you'll need to master angles and momentum to guide it accurately. Your two smaller fish teammates will swim around automatically, helping to defend your goal and push the ball forward.

Gain a strategic advantage by using snacks! Press 'T' (for Player 1) or 'P' (for Player 2) to drop a food pellet near your opponent's smaller helper fish. They will be momentarily distracted, swimming over to eat the snack and leaving their goal wide open for you to score. The player with the highest score when time expires wins the match!
