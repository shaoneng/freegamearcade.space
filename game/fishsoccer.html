Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the underwater arena of Fish Soccer! The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent within the 60-second time limit. You can choose to play against the computer in 1 Player mode or challenge a friend in 2 Player mode.

Controls:

  • Player 1 (Orange Fish): Use the WASD keys to swim and nudge the ball. Press T to drop a snack.
  • Player 2 (Blue Fish): Use the Arrow Keys to swim and nudge the ball. Press P to drop a snack.

You control the large fish, while two smaller fish act as your automated teammates. Nudge the ball with your body to guide it towards the opponent's net. Be mindful of the water's drag, which will affect the ball's trajectory and add a layer of challenge.

To gain a strategic advantage, you can distract the other team's helpers! Pressing the snack key drops a food pellet, luring the smaller fish away from their goal and leaving it wide open for a shot. The player with the most goals when the timer runs out wins!
