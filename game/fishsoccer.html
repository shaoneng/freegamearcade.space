Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the underwater arena of Fish Soccer! The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out. You can dive into the action solo against the computer or challenge a friend in the 2-player mode.

Control your main fish (Orange for Player 1, Blue for Player 2) to navigate the watery pitch. Your two smaller teammates move automatically, but your positioning is key. Nudge the ball with your body and use the unique water physics to your advantage. A well-timed flick of your tail can send the ball curving toward the goal. Remember, the ball won't travel in a straight line, just like in water polo!

Gain a tactical edge by using snacks! Press the 'T' key (Player 1) or 'P' key (Player 2) to drop a food pellet near your opponent's goal. Their smaller helper fish will be distracted, swimming over to eat the snack and leaving their goal wide open for an easy shot. Master this trick to outsmart your rival and claim victory!
