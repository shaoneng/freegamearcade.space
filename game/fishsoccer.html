Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the wacky, underwater world of Fish Soccer, where football gets a fin-tastic twist! The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent in a frantic 60-second match. Control your fish, bump the wobbly, bubbly ball into the net, and enjoy the delightful 'plop' sounds and cheerful celebrations with every play.

Choose between 1-Player or 2-Player mode. Player 1 controls the Orange Fish, and Player 2 controls the Blue Fish. Your main fish is supported by two smaller, automatic teammates. Gameplay is all about physics and angles. Nudge the ball from the side and use your momentum to guide it, but be mindful of the water drag—it adds a fun, unpredictable challenge to every shot.

To gain an edge, use the snack feature! Press 'T' or 'P' to drop a food pellet near your opponent's smaller fish. They'll get distracted by the tasty treat, leaving their goal wide open for a perfect scoring opportunity. Master the aquatic arena, outsmart your rival, and become the Fish Soccer champion!

Fish Soccer is created by Neplin Game Studio. For more silly sports fun with unique physics, try Soccer Random or Pill Soccer.
