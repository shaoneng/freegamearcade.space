Fish Soccer

How to Play Fish Soccer

Welcome to the underwater arena of Fish Soccer! The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out. You can choose to play solo against the computer (1 Player) or challenge a friend on the same device (2 Player).

Each player controls a large koi fish (Player 1 is orange, Player 2 is blue), supported by two smaller, automated teammates. Guide your main fish to nudge the ball towards the opponent's net. Be mindful of the water physics—the ball will drift and curve, requiring you to anticipate its movement and use strategic angles to score.

A unique feature is the snack attack! Press the 'T' key (for Player 1) or the 'P' key (for Player 2) to drop a food pellet near your rival's goal. Their small helper fish will get distracted and swarm the snack, leaving their goal wide open for a perfect shot. The player with the highest score at the end of one minute wins the match!
