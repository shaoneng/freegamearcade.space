Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive five consecutive nights, from 12 a.m. to 6 a.m., while keeping a close watch on the pizzeria's animatronic inhabitants.

Use your mouse to interact with the controls in your security office. Click on the arrows at the bottom of the screen to switch between different camera feeds, allowing you to monitor the movements of Freddy and his friends throughout the restaurant. Keep a close eye on them, as they tend to wander at night and will try to reach your office.

When an animatronic gets too close, you must act fast! Use the buttons next to your office doors to turn on the lights and check your blind spots, or to shut the heavy security doors to block them out. Be aware that using the cameras, lights, and doors consumes your limited electrical power.

The key to survival is power management. You can see your remaining power level in the lower-left corner of the screen. If your power drains to zero before 6 a.m., the lights will go out, the doors will open, and you'll be left completely vulnerable. Use your resources sparingly to make it through the night!

Warning: This game is a classic horror experience that includes flashing lights, sudden loud noises, and frequent jump scares. Play if you dare!
