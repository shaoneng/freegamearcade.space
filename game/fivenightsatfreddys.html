Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your primary objective is simple but terrifying: survive five consecutive nights, from 12 AM to 6 AM. Confined to your small security office, you are the last line of defense against the restaurant's animatronic characters who mysteriously wander the halls at night.

Your main tool is the security camera system. Click on the camera map at the bottom of your screen to switch between different locations in the restaurant. Keep a close eye on the animatronics, including Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy. They tend to move when you're not looking, so flipping through the cameras is crucial to tracking their progress towards your office.

To protect yourself, you have two security doors and two hallway lights, one for each side of your office. Use the light switches to check if an animatronic is lurking just outside your door. If you see one, immediately press the door button to seal them out. Be warned: they won't stay there forever, but they will drain your precious power while the door is closed.

The biggest challenge is managing your limited power supply. Every action—using cameras, turning on lights, and closing doors—consumes power. The power level is displayed in the lower-left corner of the screen. If you run out of power before 6 AM, the lights will go out, the doors will open, and you'll be completely vulnerable. Use your resources wisely and conserve energy whenever possible to survive the night.
