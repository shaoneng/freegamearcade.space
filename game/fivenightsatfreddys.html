Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive for five consecutive nights, from the stroke of midnight until 6 AM. Each night presents a new challenge as things get progressively more active.

You're not alone in the restaurant. The animatronic characters—Freddy Fazbear, Bonnie the Bunny, Chica the Chicken, and Foxy the Pirate Fox—wander the halls at night. Your main tool is the security camera system. Click the camera map at the bottom of your screen to cycle through different rooms and keep track of their positions.

When an animatronic gets too close to your office, you must act fast. Use the buttons on either side of you to operate the lights and check the blind spots just outside your doors. If you see one, immediately press the corresponding door button to seal the entrance and keep yourself safe.

Here's the catch: everything you do consumes power from a limited supply. Watching cameras, turning on lights, and keeping doors shut all drain your battery. Keep a close eye on your power level in the bottom-left corner. If you run out of power, the lights go out, the doors fly open, and you'll be completely defenseless. Use your resources wisely to survive until morning!

Warning: This game contains flashing lights, sudden loud noises, and numerous jumpscares. Play with caution!
