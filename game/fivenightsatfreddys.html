Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

As the new night watchman at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, your primary goal in Five Nights At Freddys is to survive five consecutive nights, from midnight to 6 AM. This seemingly simple job becomes a terrifying ordeal as the restaurant's animatronic characters begin to wander the halls.

Your office provides you with a monitor to access security cameras, allowing you to track the animatronics' movements throughout the building. Keep a close eye on their positions, especially those approaching your office.

To defend yourself, you have two crucial tools: automatic doors on either side of your office and lights to check the hallways. These tools consume power, which is a limited resource displayed in the lower-left corner of your screen. Use your power wisely; frequently closing doors or using lights will deplete it quickly, leaving you vulnerable if it reaches zero.

Listen carefully for sounds and react swiftly to any animatronic nearing your location. When an animatronic is at your door, quickly close it to prevent them from entering. Manage your power, monitor cameras, and react quickly to survive each harrowing night.
