Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night watchman at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive five consecutive nights, from midnight to 6 am. From your small office, you must keep an eye on the restaurant's animatronic characters, who have a tendency to wander at night.

Use your security monitor to switch between camera feeds and track the movements of Freddy and his friends throughout the pizzeria. Click on the camera buttons at the bottom of the screen to change your view. Knowing where they are is key to your survival.

When an animatronic gets too close to your office, you'll need to act fast. Use the buttons on either side of your view to control the lights and doors. The lights let you check the blind spots just outside your office, and the doors will seal you in for safety. But be warned, these actions consume power.

Your biggest challenge is managing the limited supply of electricity. Every action, from checking cameras to closing doors, drains your battery. Keep a close eye on your power level in the lower-left corner. If you run out of power, the lights go out, the doors open, and you'll be completely vulnerable. Use your resources wisely to make it through the night!

Warning: This horror game contains flashing lights, sudden loud noises, and numerous jump scares. Play at your own discretion!
