Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your goal is to survive for five consecutive nights, from midnight to 6 AM. From the safety of your office, you must monitor the security cameras to track the movements of the animatronic characters who wander the restaurant after hours.

Keep a close eye on your monitors. When an animatronic gets too close to your office, you must act fast! Use the buttons on either side of you to turn on the hallway lights or shut the security doors. These are your only lines of defense against the roaming characters.

Be strategic with your resources. Using the cameras, lights, and doors consumes a limited amount of electrical power. If you drain your power completely before your shift ends, you'll be left vulnerable in the dark. Conserve energy by only using defenses when absolutely necessary. Can you survive the night and make it to the morning?

Warning: This game contains flashing lights, sudden loud noises, and frequent jumpscares.
