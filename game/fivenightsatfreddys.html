Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your primary objective is to survive five consecutive nights, working a shift from 12 AM to 6 AM. It sounds simple, but you're not alone in the restaurant.

From your security office, you must use the security camera system to keep a close watch on the animatronic characters. Click through the different camera feeds to track their locations throughout the pizzeria. They tend to wander at night, and they're not friendly.

When an animatronic gets too close to your office, you must act quickly to protect yourself. Use the buttons to operate the lights in the hallways next to you to check your blind spots. If you see one, immediately press the corresponding door button to shut it and block their entry.

Your most crucial resource is power. Every action—using the cameras, turning on lights, and keeping the doors closed—drains your limited electricity supply. Keep an eye on your power level in the lower-left corner of the screen. If you run out of power before 6 AM, you'll be left completely vulnerable in the dark. Use your power wisely and only when absolutely necessary to survive the night!
