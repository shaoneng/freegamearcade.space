Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night watchman for Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! In Five Nights at Freddy's (FNAF), your goal is to survive five consecutive nights, from the stroke of midnight until 6 in the morning. It sounds simple, but you're not alone.

When darkness falls, the restaurant's animatronic characters come to life and roam the halls. Your office is your only safe haven. Use the security cameras to keep a close eye on their movements throughout the pizzeria. When they get too close to your office, you must quickly shut the security doors to block their entry.

Be warned: everything you use, from the cameras to the lights and security doors, drains your limited power supply. Keep an eye on the battery level in the lower-left corner of your screen. If you run out of power, you'll be left completely vulnerable. Use your resources wisely and conserve energy to make it through the night.

WARNING: This horror game contains flashing lights, loud noises, and numerous jumpscares. Player discretion is advised!
