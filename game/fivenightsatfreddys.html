Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night watchman at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! In this terrifying 2D horror game, your main objective is to survive five consecutive nights, working the shift from midnight to 6 a.m.

You are not alone in the restaurant. The infamous animatronic characters roam the halls freely at night. Your primary tool for survival is the security camera system. Monitor the animatronics' movements and anticipate their approach to your office.

To defend yourself, you can close the security doors and turn on lights to check the blind spots right outside your office. However, every action consumes power from a limited battery. If your power drains to zero before the night is over, the doors will open, the lights will go out, and you'll be left defenseless. Manage your power wisely by only using it when absolutely necessary.

WARNING! This game is a classic of the horror genre and contains flashing lights, loud noises, and many jumpscares. Player discretion is advised.
