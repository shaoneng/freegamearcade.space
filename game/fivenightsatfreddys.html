Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night watchman for Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! In this terrifying 2D survival game, your task is to stay on duty for five consecutive nights, from the stroke of midnight until 6 am. It sounds simple, but you're not alone.

The restaurant's animatronic characters have a tendency to wander the halls at night. Your only tools are the security cameras, door controls, and lights. Keep a close eye on the animatronics' movements through the camera feeds. If they get too close to your office, you must shut the doors immediately to protect yourself.

Be warned: using the doors, lights, and cameras all consumes a limited supply of electrical power. You must use your resources wisely to survive the entire night. Keep an eye on the battery level in the lower-left corner of the screen. If you run out of power, you'll be left in the dark and completely vulnerable.

This horror game is famous for its tense atmosphere, loud noises, and sudden jumpscares. Steel your nerves and see if you can survive all five nights at Freddy's!
