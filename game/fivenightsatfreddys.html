Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night watchman at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive for five consecutive nights, from the stroke of midnight until 6 AM. What seems like an easy gig quickly turns into a desperate struggle for survival.

You are not alone in the restaurant. The animatronic characters—Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy—come to life at night and wander the halls. Your job is to keep a close eye on them using the security camera system. Click through the different camera feeds to track their movements and anticipate their approach.

When an animatronic gets too close to your office, you must act fast! Use the controls to shut the security doors and turn on the hallway lights to check your blind spots. Be warned, they can appear at either door, so you'll need to be vigilant.

The biggest challenge is managing your limited electrical power. Every action, from checking cameras to closing doors and using lights, drains your battery. Keep a close eye on the power level in the lower-left corner. If you run out of power before 6 AM, the doors will open, the lights will go out, and you'll be completely vulnerable. Use your resources wisely to conserve energy and make it through the night.

Warning: This horror game contains flashing lights, loud noises, and frequent jump scares. Play with caution!
