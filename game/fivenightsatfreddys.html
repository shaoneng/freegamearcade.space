Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your goal is simple but terrifying: survive for five consecutive nights, from 12 AM to 6 AM. The challenge? The restaurant's seemingly friendly animatronic characters come to life after hours and wander the halls, and they don't take kindly to intruders.

From your small security office, you must keep a close watch on the animatronics using a network of security cameras. Click on the camera map at the bottom of your screen to switch between different locations and track their movements. Be aware, the animatronics can and will move when you're not looking.

If an animatronic gets too close to your office, you have two lines of defense: steel doors and hallway lights. Use the light switches to check your blind spots just outside your office doors. If you see one of them, quickly hit the door button to seal yourself in. But be carefulâ€”every action you take, from checking cameras to using lights and closing doors, consumes a limited amount of electrical power.

Keep a constant eye on your power level in the lower-left corner. If you run out of power before 6 AM, the lights go out, the doors open, and you'll be completely defenseless. Manage your resources wisely, conserve energy, and survive the night to see another day. Be prepared for flashing lights, loud noises, and plenty of jumpscares!
