Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive for five consecutive nights, from the stroke of midnight until 6 AM.

You are confined to your small security office. Use the monitor to switch between security cameras and keep a close eye on the animatronic characters—Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy. They have a tendency to wander the restaurant after hours and are not as friendly as they seem during the day.

When an animatronic approaches your office, you must act fast! Use your mouse to interact with the buttons on either side of your office. These control the lights to check the blind spots right outside your door, and the heavy security doors to block any unwanted visitors. This is your only defense against them.

Be warned: every action you take, from checking cameras to using lights and doors, consumes a limited amount of electrical power. Keep a close watch on your power level, displayed in the bottom left corner. If you run out of power before your shift ends, you'll be left in the dark and completely vulnerable. Manage your resources wisely to survive!

This is a horror game known for its tense atmosphere and sudden jump scares. Be prepared for loud noises and flashing lights. Good luck—you'll need it.
