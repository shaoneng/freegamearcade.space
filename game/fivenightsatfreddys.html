Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! In Five Nights At Freddy's, your objective is to survive five consecutive nights as the night security guard, from the chilling hours of midnight to 6 AM. This won't be a quiet shift, as the restaurant's animatronic characters have a tendency to wander the halls after dark.

Your primary tool is the security camera system. Click on the different camera feeds to monitor the locations of Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy. Keep a close eye on their movements, as they will try to make their way to your office. When an animatronic gets too close, you must act quickly!

To protect yourself, you can close the security doors to your left and right and turn on the hallway lights to check your blind spots. However, every action—using cameras, lights, and doors—consumes a limited supply of power. If you run out of power before 6 AM, you'll be left completely vulnerable in the dark. Manage your resources wisely, conserve energy, and stay vigilant to survive the night.

Warning: This horror game is famous for its loud noises and sudden jump scares. Play with caution!
