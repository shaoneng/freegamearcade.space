Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your one and only goal is to survive five consecutive nights, from the stroke of midnight until 6 AM. It sounds simple, but you're not alone in the dark.

The restaurant's animatronic characters—Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy—have a habit of wandering the halls at night. Use your security monitor to switch between cameras and keep a close eye on their movements. They don't like being watched for too long, and they love to move when you're not looking.

When an animatronic gets too close to your office, you must act fast! Use the buttons on either side of your view to turn on the door lights to check the blind spots, or shut the security doors to block them out. Be warned: every action you take, from checking cameras to closing doors, drains your limited power supply.

Keep a constant watch on your power level, displayed in the bottom-left corner of the screen. If you run out of power, the lights go out, the doors fly open, and you're left completely defenseless. Manage your resources wisely, conserve energy, and survive the night! Please be aware: this horror game contains flashing lights, loud noises, and numerous jumpscares.
