Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive for five consecutive nights, from midnight until 6 AM. The challenge? The restaurant's animatronic mascots have a tendency to wander the halls after hours, and they're not looking to make friends.

You are stationed in a security office with a few tools at your disposal. Use your monitor to switch between security cameras and keep an eye on Freddy and his friends. If an animatronic gets too close to your office, you can use the buttons on either side of you to turn on the hallway lights or shut the security doors to block their path.

Here's the critical part: everything uses power. The cameras, the lights, and the doors all drain your limited power supply, which is displayed in the lower-left corner of the screen. If you run out of power, the lights go out, the doors fly open, and you'll be completely vulnerable. Use your resources sparingly and only when absolutely necessary to conserve energy and make it through the night.

Be prepared! This horror game is known for its suspense, loud noises, and sudden jump scares. Keep your cool, watch your power, and good luck surviving the night.
