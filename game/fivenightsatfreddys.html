Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new night job at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! In this terrifying survival game, you take on the role of a security guard. Your objective is simple but challenging: survive five consecutive nights, from the stroke of midnight until 6 AM.

You're not alone in the restaurant. The friendly animatronic characters that entertain children by day become unsettlingly active at night. Your primary tool for survival is the security camera system. Keep a close watch on Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy as they wander through the pizzeria.

When an animatronic gets too close to your office, you must act fast! Use the controls to shut the security doors to block their path. You can also turn on the hallway lights to check your blind spots right outside your doors.

Here's the catch: everything you do drains your limited power supply. Using cameras, lights, and keeping the doors shut will consume precious energy. You can monitor your power level in the lower-left corner of the screen. Manage your power wisely, or you'll be left in the dark and vulnerable.

WARNING: This horror game contains flashing lights, loud noises, and frequent jump scares!
