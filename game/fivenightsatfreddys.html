Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Your objective is to survive five nights as the security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, from 12 AM to 6 AM. The animatronic characters wander the restaurant at night, and you must prevent them from reaching your office.

Use the security cameras to track the animatronics' movements throughout the building. Click on the different camera feeds at the bottom of your screen to switch views. Keep a close eye on their positions, as they move when you're not looking.

When an animatronic gets too close to your office, you must act quickly. Use the buttons on either side of your view to turn on the door lights to check for threats in the blind spots. If you see one, immediately press the corresponding door button to shut it. Be aware: this is a horror game with loud noises and jump scares!

Your primary challenge is managing a limited power supply. Using the cameras, lights, and doors all drains your battery, which is displayed in the lower-left corner. If your power reaches zero before 6 AM, the lights go out, the doors open, and you'll be vulnerable. Use your power wisely, only when absolutely necessary, to make it through the night.
