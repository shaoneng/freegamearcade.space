Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! In this terrifying survival game, your objective is to survive five consecutive nights as the night security guard. Your shift lasts from midnight to 6 a.m., but you're not alone. The restaurant's friendly animatronic characters have a tendency to wander at night.

From your security office, you can monitor the animatronics using a network of security cameras. Click through the different camera feeds to keep an eye on Freddy, Bonnie, Chica, and Foxy. When they get too close to your office, you must act quickly to stay safe.

Your main defenses are the two steel doors on either side of your office and the hallway lights. Use the lights to check if anyone is lurking just outside. If you see an animatronic, shut the door immediately! However, using the cameras, doors, and lights consumes a limited supply of electrical power. Keep an eye on your power level—if it runs out, you'll be left in the dark and completely vulnerable.

WARNING: This game contains flashing lights, loud noises, and frequent jump scares. Strategic power management is the key to your survival. Can you last all five nights?
