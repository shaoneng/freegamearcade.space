Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night watchman at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! In this thrilling 2D survival horror game, your mission is to survive five consecutive nights, from midnight to 6 am. But be warned, you're not alone. The restaurant's animatronic characters have a tendency to wander at night.

Your primary tool is the security camera system. Keep a close eye on the animatronics as they move through the restaurant. If one gets too close to your office, you must shut the security doors immediately to protect yourself. Listen carefully for sounds that might signal their approach.

Surviving isn't just about closing doors. Both the doors and the lights consume a limited supply of electrical power. You must use your resources wisely to make it through the night. Keep an eye on your battery level in the lower-left corner of the screen. If you run out of power, you'll be left vulnerable in the dark!

WARNING: This horror game contains flashing lights, loud noises, and lots of jumpscares!
