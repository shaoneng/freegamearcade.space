Five Nights At Freddys

How to Play Five Nights At Freddys

Welcome to your new job as the night watchman at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your objective in this survival horror game is simple but terrifying: survive for five consecutive nights. Each shift lasts from 12 AM to 6 AM, and you must protect yourself from the animatronic characters that come to life after dark.

You are confined to your security office. Your primary tool is the security camera system. Click on the map at the bottom of your screen to switch between different camera feeds and keep a close eye on the animatronics' movements. Knowing where they are is crucial to your survival.

When an animatronic gets too close to your office, you must act fast. Use the buttons on either side of you to control the lights in the hallways and shut the reinforced steel doors. Be warned: you can't see into the hallways without the lights on, and the doors are your only defense.

The biggest challenge is managing your limited power supply. Every action—using cameras, turning on lights, or closing doors—drains precious energy. Keep an eye on your power level in the bottom-left corner. If you run out of power before 6 AM, the lights go out, the doors fly open, and you'll be left completely vulnerable. Use your resources sparingly and only when absolutely necessary to make it through the night!

Warning: This horror game contains flashing lights, loud noises, and frequent jump scares.
