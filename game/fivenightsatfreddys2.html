Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night shift security guard at the revamped Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria! Your goal is simple but terrifying: survive from 12 AM to 6 AM for five nights straight. Unlike the previous location, there are no doors to protect you. Your survival depends on your wit and your limited resources.

Your main interface is the security camera system. Use your mouse to scan the different rooms and keep track of the animatronics. Be warned, they move when you're not looking. You'll also need to manage two other crucial mechanics: the music box and the flashlight. Keep the music box in the Prize Corner wound up to keep one particularly persistent puppet at bay. Fail to do so, and it's game over.

Your only real defense against most animatronics is the Freddy Fazbear mask. When an animatronic appears in your office or the vents, quickly put on the mask to trick them into thinking you're one of them. Your flashlight is essential for checking the main hall and the vents, but its battery is limited. Conserve power, stay vigilant, and prepare for heart-pounding jump scares as you try to survive the night.
