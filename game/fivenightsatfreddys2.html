Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new, slightly terrifying, job as the night security guard at the newly reopened Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria. Your objective is simple but challenging: survive your shift from 12 AM to 6 AM for five consecutive nights. The animatronics have a habit of wandering, and they don't take kindly to strangers.

In this sequel, you have no security doors to protect you. Your main tools are the security cameras, a flashlight, and an empty Freddy Fazbear head. Monitor the cameras to keep track of where the animatronics are. If an animatronic appears in your office, you must immediately put on the Freddy mask to trick them into leaving you alone. Be quick, or it's game over!

Your flashlight is essential for checking the main hall and the air vents, but be mindful of its limited battery power. Additionally, you must constantly keep the music box in the Prize Corner wound up by clicking on it through the camera feed. If the music stops, you'll have an unwelcome visitor that no mask can fool. Stay vigilant, manage your resources, and survive the night!
