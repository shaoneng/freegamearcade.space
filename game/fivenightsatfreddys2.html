Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at the reopened Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria. Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive five consecutive nights, from midnight to 6 AM, without getting caught by the roaming animatronics.

Unlike the previous location, you have no doors to protect you. Your office is wide open. Your primary defense is a limited-use flashlight to see down the main hall and a Freddy Fazbear mask. You must also monitor the security cameras to keep track of the animatronics' movements through the pizzeria. Pay special attention to the Prize Corner camera; you must keep the music box wound up to placate one particularly persistent character.

Use your mouse to look around the office, access the camera panel, and interact with controls. When an animatronic appears in your office or the vents, you must immediately put on the Freddy Fazbear mask to fool them into thinking you're one of them. Be quick! Hesitation will result in a jump scare and the end of your shift. Manage your flashlight battery and your nerves to survive the week.
