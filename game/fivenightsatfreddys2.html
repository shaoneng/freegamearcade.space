Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at the newly reopened Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive five nights, from 12 AM to 6 AM, without being caught by the animatronic characters that roam the restaurant after hours.

You are stationed in a security office with no doors to close. Your primary defense is a wearable Freddy Fazbear head. When an animatronic gets into your office, quickly put on the mask to fool them into thinking you're one of them. However, this won't work on every character, so stay vigilant!

Use your flashlight to check the main hall and the vents on your left and right. Some animatronics are repelled by the light. You must also regularly check Camera 11 and wind up the music box. If the music stops, a particularly relentless animatronic will come for you. Monitor your flashlight's battery power, as it is limited. Good luck, you'll need it.
