Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new, terrifying job as the night security guard at the reopened Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria. Your one and only goal is to survive five nights, from midnight to 6 AM, without being caught by the roaming animatronics. This time, there are no doors to protect you.

Use your mouse to look around your office and interact with the controls. Monitor the security cameras to keep track of the animatronics' movements throughout the pizzeria. Your most crucial defense is the empty Freddy Fazbear head. When an animatronic gets into your office, quickly put on the mask to fool them into thinking you're one of them.

You must also manage two other critical systems. Keep the music box in the Prize Corner wound up via the camera monitor; if it unwinds, you'll face a new, relentless enemy. Additionally, use your flashlight to check the dark main hallway and the two air vents on either side of your desk.
