About Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Five Nights at Freddy's 2 (FNaF 2) plunges you into a thrilling horror experience set in a spooky and narrative-rich environment. If you're brave enough, you can face the terror for free right here on Free Game Arcade.

The story begins at the newly reopened Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria, a place once known for fun and pizza, now infamous for a series of tragic events. Reports of missing children, rumors of murders, and horrifying tales of haunted, malfunctioning animatronics led to its initial closure. One gruesome incident involved an animatronic fatally biting a security guard.

Despite its dark past, the pizzeria is back in business and in desperate need of a night security guard. That's where you come in. Drawn by the high pay and perhaps a disbelief in ghost stories, you've taken the job. What could possibly go wrong?

Welcome to your new role. Your mission is to survive the night shift. Keep a close watch on the security cameras, but be warned: an animatronic can appear in your office at any moment. Prepare for heart-pounding jump scares and try to make it to morning in this intense horror game.
