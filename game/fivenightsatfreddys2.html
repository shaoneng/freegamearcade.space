Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night shift security guard at the reopened Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria. The place has a grim history—missing children, murder rumors, and stories of haunted animatronics. But the pay is good, and you need to pay the bills. What could possibly go wrong?

Your primary task is to survive the night until morning. You'll be stationed in a security office, monitoring a series of cameras placed throughout the pizzeria. Keep a close watch on the animatronics, as they have a tendency to wander at night. Conserve your limited power supply, which drains with every action.

Be prepared for intense jump scares and a constant feeling of dread. An animatronic might appear right in front of you when you least expect it. Stay vigilant, manage your resources wisely, and try to make it through all five nights. Good luck—you'll need it.
