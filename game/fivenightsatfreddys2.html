Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive five consecutive nights, from midnight to 6 AM, without getting caught by the haunted animatronics.

Unlike the previous location, you have no doors to protect you. Your office is wide open. Use your mouse to look around your office and to access the security camera monitor. Keep a close eye on the different rooms to track the movements of the animatronics. Don't forget to wind up the music box in the Prize Corner; if it unwinds completely, a particularly nasty surprise awaits.

Your primary defense tools are a flashlight and a Freddy Fazbear mask. Use the flashlight to check the main hallway and the air vents. Some animatronics are repelled by the light. When an animatronic gets into your office, you must quickly put on the Freddy mask to trick them into thinking you're one of them. Be fast, as your reaction time is all that stands between you and a game-ending jump scare. Manage your flashlight battery and your wits to survive the week!
