Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Your goal in Five Nights At Freddy's 2 is to survive five nights as the new security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Each shift lasts from midnight to 6 AM. Unlike the first game, your office has no doors to close for protection. Instead, you must use a variety of tools to keep the animatronics from reaching you.

Use your mouse to look around the office and interact with the controls. Click on the button at the bottom of your screen to pull up the security camera feed. Monitor the different rooms to track the movements of the animatronics. Be sure to frequently wind up the Music Box in CAM 11 to keep the Puppet contained. If you fail to do this, it's game over.

When an animatronic gets close, you have two primary defenses. For most animatronics that appear in your office, quickly put on the Freddy Fazbear mask by clicking the button in the bottom left corner. This will trick them into leaving. For others, like Foxy, you must flash your flashlight at them repeatedly by holding the 'Ctrl' key. Keep an eye on your flashlight's battery life, as it is limited! Survive the night to earn your paycheck and face the next challenge.
