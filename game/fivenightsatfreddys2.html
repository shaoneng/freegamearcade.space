Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night shift security guard at the newly reopened Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria. Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive five nights, from midnight to 6 AM, without getting caught by the animatronic characters.

Unlike the first game, there are no doors to close. Your office is wide open, with two side vents and a large hallway in front of you. Your primary defense is your Freddy Fazbear head mask. When an animatronic gets too close, you must quickly put on the mask to fool them into thinking you're one of them. Be fast, or it's game over!

You also have a flashlight to illuminate the dark hallway and check the vents. Be mindful of its battery power. Your other crucial tool is the security camera system. Use it to track the animatronics' movements throughout the pizzeria. Keep an eye on the Prize Corner, as you'll need to remotely wind up the Music Box. If the music stops, a new, unstoppable puppet will come for you.

Manage your time, your flashlight battery, and your nerves. Keep the Music Box wound, use your mask when necessary, and survive until morning. Good luck—you'll need it.
