Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at the newly reopened Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria. Your objective is to survive five nights, from midnight to 6 AM, while monitoring the restaurant's terrifying animatronics.

Unlike the first establishment, your office has no security doors. Your primary defense is an empty Freddy Fazbear mask. When you see an animatronic in your office or the vents, quickly put on the mask to fool them into thinking you're one of them. Be quick, as hesitation can be fatal!

Use your security cameras to track the animatronics' movements throughout the pizzeria. You must also keep the music box in the Prize Corner wound up remotely using the camera system. If the music stops, a particularly persistent enemy will come for you. Finally, use your flashlight to illuminate the main hallway and the air vents, but be mindful of its limited battery power. Balance all these tasks to survive the night!
