Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at the infamous Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria. Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive five consecutive nights, from midnight to 6 AM. You're stationed in a control room with no doors to protect you, only a desk, a fan, and your wits.

Your primary tool is the security camera system. Use your mouse to switch between different rooms and keep an eye on the animatronics. A crucial task is to keep the music box in the Prize Corner wound up. If it unwinds, a new, formidable foe will come for you. Don't let it run down!

When an animatronic gets too close and appears in your office, quickly put on your Freddy Fazbear mask to fool them into thinking you're one of them. Use your flashlight to check the main hall and the vents, as some animatronics are repelled by the light. Be mindful of your flashlight's battery life—it's not infinite!

Listen for audio cues, manage your resources wisely, and react quickly to survive each night. Each night gets progressively harder, with more aggressive animatronics. Good luck—you'll need it.
