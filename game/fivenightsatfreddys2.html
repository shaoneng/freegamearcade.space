Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new, terrifying job as the night security guard at the reopened Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria. Your objective is simple but challenging: survive five consecutive nights, from midnight to 6 AM, without being caught by the roaming animatronics.

You are stationed in a security office with no doors to close. Your primary defense is a set of security cameras. Use your mouse to scan the rooms and keep a close watch on the animatronics' movements. You also have a flashlight to check the dark hallways and the vents to your left and right. Be mindful of its limited battery life!

Your most crucial tool is a spare Freddy Fazbear head. When an animatronic gets into your office, quickly move your mouse to the red button at the bottom of the screen to put on the mask. This will fool most of them into thinking you're one of them. However, some animatronics require different tactics. Listen for audio cues, wind up the music box to keep The Puppet at bay, and react quickly to survive the jump scares and make it to the end of your shift.
