Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at the revamped Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria. Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive five nights, from midnight to 6 AM, while being hunted by a new cast of animatronic characters. These aren't the friendly entertainers they seem to be during the day.

Your primary tool is the security camera system. Click through the different camera feeds to keep track of the animatronics' movements. Be warned, they don't stay put for long. You also have a flashlight with limited battery power to illuminate dark areas like the main hallway and the air vents on your left and right.

When an animatronic gets too close and appears in your office, quickly put on your Freddy Fazbear mask to fool them into thinking you're one of them. This is your main defense, but it won't work on everyone. You must also keep the music box in the Prize Corner wound up by clicking on it via the camera feed. If it unwinds completely, a particularly nasty surprise awaits. Manage your time, your flashlight battery, and your nerves to survive the week!
