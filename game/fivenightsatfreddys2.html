Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at the newly reopened Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria! Your goal is simple but terrifying: survive your shift from 12 AM to 6 AM for five consecutive nights. You are not alone; the restaurant's animatronics roam the halls at night and are not friendly.

Your office has three entry points: a central hallway and two side air vents. Use your mouse to look around your office and interact with the controls. Move your cursor to the bottom of the screen to pull up your security camera monitor. Click through the different camera feeds to track the animatronics' movements.

Unlike the first game, there are no doors to close. Your primary defenses are your flashlight and a spare Freddy Fazbear head. Use the flashlight to check the dark hallway in front of you and to see what's lurking in the vents. If you see an animatronic in your office, quickly put on the Freddy head to trick it into leaving. Additionally, you must constantly wind up the music box via the camera monitor to keep a certain deadly puppet from coming after you. Manage your flashlight battery and your nerves to survive the night!
