Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive your shift from midnight to 6 AM for five consecutive nights. You are not alone; the restaurant's animatronic characters wander the halls at night, and they aren't friendly.

Unlike the previous location, you have no doors to protect you. Your primary defense is a Freddy Fazbear mask. When you see an animatronic in your office or vents, quickly put on the mask to trick them into thinking you're one of them. Timing is crucial!

Use your security camera system to keep an eye on the animatronics' movements throughout the pizzeria. Pay special attention to the Prize Corner camera and keep the music box wound up remotely. If it unwinds completely, a new threat will be unleashed. You also have a flashlight with limited battery power to check the main hallway and the two side air vents. Be mindful of your power usage!
