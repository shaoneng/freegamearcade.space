Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at the newly reopened Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive five consecutive nights, from midnight to 6 AM, without being caught by the roaming animatronics.

You'll be stationed in a security office with no doors to close. Your primary tools are the security cameras, which you must use to keep track of the animatronics' movements throughout the pizzeria. Use the lights to check the blind spots in the vents and the hallway in front of you. If an animatronic gets too close, quickly put on your Freddy Fazbear mask to fool them into thinking you're one of them. Be warned, the mask won't work on every character!

Managing your resources is key. The flashlight has limited battery power, so use it wisely. Keep an eye on the music box in the Prize Corner; if it winds down, a new threat will emerge. Stay alert, conserve your power, and prepare for heart-pounding jump scares as you try to make it to the morning.
