Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria! Your goal is to survive from midnight to 6 AM for five consecutive nights. You are stationed in a security office with no doors to protect you.

Your primary tool is the security camera system. Keep a close eye on the animatronics' movements throughout the pizzeria. Be mindful of your flashlight's battery power, which you'll need to check the vents and the main hallway in front of you.

The most crucial task is to keep the music box in the Prize Corner wound up. If it unwinds completely, a particularly dangerous animatronic will come for you. If an animatronic gets into your office, quickly put on your Freddy Fazbear mask to fool them into leaving. Be quick, as your survival depends on it! Prepare for intense jump scares and a test of your nerve.
