Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at the newly reopened Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria. Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive your shift from midnight to 6 a.m. for five consecutive nights. Unlike the previous location, this office has no security doors. Your survival depends on your wits and your limited tools.

Your primary defense is a wearable Freddy Fazbear head. When an animatronic makes its way into your office, you must immediately put on the mask to trick them into leaving. Timing is critical! You must also use your flashlight to check the main hallway and the two air vents on your left and right, as some animatronics are deterred by the light.

Continuously monitor the security cameras to track the movements of the animatronics. A crucial task is to keep the music box in the Prize Corner (Cam 11) wound up. If the music stops, another deadly animatronic will be unleashed, resulting in an almost certain game over. Stay vigilant, manage your resources, and prepare for heart-pounding jump scares as you try to make it to the morning.
