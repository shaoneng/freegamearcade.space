Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at the infamous Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria. Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive five nights, from 12 AM to 6 AM, without being caught by the roaming animatronics.

You are stationed in an office with no doors to protect you. Your main tools are the security cameras, which you can use to track the animatronics' movements throughout the pizzeria. Keep a close eye on all the rooms and vents.

To defend yourself, you have a limited-use flashlight to stun animatronics in the main hallway and check the vents. If an animatronic gets too close, quickly put on your Freddy Fazbear mask to fool them into thinking you're one of them. Be quick, as hesitation can be fatal! Don't forget to wind up the music box remotely to keep one particularly persistent puppet at bay.

Manage your resources carefully and stay vigilant. The animatronics are unpredictable, and the jump scares are intense. Can you make it to the end of the week?
