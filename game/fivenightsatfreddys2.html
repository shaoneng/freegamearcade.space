Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria! Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive five consecutive nights, from midnight to 6 AM. You're stationed in a control room with no doors to protect you, making this a true test of your nerves.

Your primary tool is the security camera system. Click on the camera map at the bottom of your screen to monitor the different rooms and track the movements of the animatronic characters. Be warned, they don't stay put for long and will actively try to reach your office.

Since you have no doors, your defense relies on three key actions. Use your flashlight by pressing the 'Ctrl' key to see down the main hallway and into the vents. If you see an animatronic, quickly put on your Freddy Fazbear mask by clicking the icon at the bottom left. This will fool most of them into thinking you're one of them. Lastly, keep the music box in the Prize Corner wound up by clicking on its camera feed. If it unwinds completely, a new, unstoppable threat will be unleashed. Manage your flashlight battery and your wits to make it to morning!
