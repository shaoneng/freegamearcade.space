Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Your goal is to survive five nights as the new security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria, from midnight to 6 AM. You're stationed in an office with no doors, but you have several tools to defend yourself against the haunted animatronics that roam the halls.

Use your mouse to look around the office and interact with your equipment. Your main defense is the security camera monitor. Click on the button at the bottom of your screen to pull it up. You can switch between cameras to track the animatronics' movements. You must also use the monitor to wind up a music box in the Prize Corner (CAM 11) to keep a specific animatronic, The Puppet, from attacking.

You have a flashlight with limited battery life, which you can use to check the main hallway and the two air vents on your left and right. Move your mouse to the corners of the screen to check the vents. If you see an animatronic in a vent or in your office, quickly put on the Freddy Fazbear mask by clicking the button at the bottom left. This will fool most animatronics into leaving. Be quick, as your survival depends on it!
