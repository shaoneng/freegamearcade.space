Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new, unsettling job at Freddy Fazbear's Pizzeria! As the night security guard, your primary goal is to survive five nights, from midnight to 6 AM. You are stationed in an office with no doors to protect you, making you vulnerable to the animatronics roaming the halls.

Your main tool is the security camera system. Use it to keep track of the animatronics' movements throughout the pizzeria. Be warned, some animatronics won't appear on the cameras and may sneak into your office through the large front opening or the side air vents.

To defend yourself, you have a flashlight to check the main hall and scare away certain characters. For animatronics that get too close, you must quickly put on your Freddy Fazbear mask to fool them into thinking you're one of them. Don't forget to wind up the music box remotely via the camera feed; if it unwinds completely, a particularly relentless animatronic will come for you. Manage your resources and your nerve to see the sunrise!
