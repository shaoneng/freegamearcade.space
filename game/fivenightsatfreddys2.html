Five Nights At Freddy's 2

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 2

Welcome to your new, slightly terrifying, summer job at the reopened Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! In Five Nights At Freddy's 2, you step into the shoes of the night security guard. Your goal is simple but incredibly challenging: survive five consecutive nights, from midnight to 6 AM, without getting caught by the roaming animatronic characters.

You are stationed in a security office with no doors to protect you. Your primary tool is the security camera monitor. Flip through the different camera feeds to keep track of the animatronics' movements throughout the pizzeria. Be aware that some animatronics won't appear on the cameras as they get closer, so listen carefully for audio cues.

Your survival depends on three key mechanics. First, use your flashlight to check the main hallway and the two air vents on your left and right. Some animatronics are repelled by the light. Second, one particular animatronic, The Puppet, is kept dormant by a music box in the Prize Corner (Cam 11). You must constantly wind it up remotely via your monitor. If the music stops, it's almost game over. Finally, for most animatronics that enter your office, you must immediately put on your Freddy Fazbear mask to trick them into leaving. Don't be too slow!
