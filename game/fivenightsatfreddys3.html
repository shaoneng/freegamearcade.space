Five Nights At Freddy's 3

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 3

Welcome to your new job as the night guard at Fazbear's Fright, a horror attraction built from the ruins of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Your goal is simple but terrifying: survive five nights from 12 AM to 6 AM.

Unlike previous installments, you only have one primary animatronic to worry about: the menacing Springtrap. Use your security camera system to track his movements through the attraction's hallways and vents. Keep a close eye on him, as he is relentless in his pursuit.

Your key defense is the audio system. You can play an audio lure in adjacent camera feeds to draw Springtrap away from your office. However, your systems—Audio, Cameras, and Ventilation—are prone to errors. When a system fails, you must open your maintenance panel to reboot it, leaving you vulnerable. Keep the ventilation running, or you'll start to experience hallucinations of old animatronics, which can distract you and cause system errors.

Master the art of distraction, manage your system reboots carefully, and keep Springtrap at bay to make it to the morning. Every second counts in this high-stakes game of survival.
