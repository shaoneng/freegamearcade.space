Five Nights At Freddy's 3

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 3

Welcome to your new job at Fazbear's Fright! Your primary goal in Five Nights At Freddy's 3 is to survive five consecutive nights, from 12 AM to 6 AM, as the night security guard. This time, you're not just watching for ghosts of the past; you're trying to avoid a very real and dangerous animatronic that roams the halls.

Use your mouse to interact with the security monitors. On the right side of your screen, you can toggle between the main security camera feed and the ventilation duct cameras. Keep a close eye on all areas to track the movement of the sole animatronic, Springtrap. Unlike previous games, the doors and lights are gone, so your main defense is strategy and misdirection.

To keep Springtrap away from your office, use the audio system. By clicking the "Play Audio" button on any camera feed, you can create a sound in that room to lure him towards it. Be strategic, as he is drawn to noise. If he gets too close, he will attack.

The attraction's old equipment is prone to failure. Your audio, video, and ventilation systems will frequently crash. When this happens, open your maintenance panel on the left to reboot the necessary system. Failing to maintain your ventilation will cause you to hallucinate, leading to more system errors and potential jump scares from phantom animatronics.
