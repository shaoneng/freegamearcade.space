Five Nights At Freddy's 3

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 3

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at 'Fazbear's Fright,' a horror attraction built from the eerie remnants of Freddy Fazbear's Pizza. Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive five consecutive nights, from midnight to 6 AM. You'll need to use your wits and manage a series of unreliable systems to keep a deadly animatronic at bay.

Your primary tool is your security monitor. Use your mouse to switch between different camera feeds throughout the attraction and the ventilation shafts. Keep a close watch on the animatronic, Springtrap, to track his location. You can also use an audio system to play sounds in adjacent rooms, luring Springtrap away from your office. This is your main defense, so use it strategically.

The old equipment is prone to failure. Your camera, audio, and ventilation systems will frequently crash, causing visual and audio disturbances and limiting your abilities. When a system fails, quickly open your maintenance panel (on the left) and reboot the corresponding system. Juggling these repairs while monitoring Springtrap's location is the key to survival. Stay vigilant, manage your systems, and don't let your guard down for a second!
