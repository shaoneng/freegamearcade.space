Five Nights At Freddy's 3

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 3

Your goal in Five Nights at Freddy's 3 is to survive five consecutive nights as the security guard at "Fazbear's Fright: The Horror Attraction." Each night shift lasts from 12 AM to 6 AM, during which you must monitor the building and defend yourself against a sinister animatronic named Springtrap.

Use your mouse to interact with three key systems. The main monitor allows you to view security cameras placed throughout the attraction. Keep a close eye on these feeds to track Springtrap's movements. You can toggle between camera views and a maintenance panel.

On the camera feed, you can play an audio clip in any room to lure Springtrap away from your location. Use this strategically to manipulate his path and keep him occupied. On the maintenance panel, you can reboot your Audio, Camera, and Ventilation systems, which will frequently go offline. Rebooting is critical, as a ventilation failure can cause hallucinations and leave you vulnerable.

Survive until 6 AM for five nights to win the game. If Springtrap makes it to your office and appears in the doorway, it's game over. Stay vigilant, manage your systems effectively, and don't let the fear take over!
