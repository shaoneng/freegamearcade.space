Five Nights At Freddy's 3

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 3

Welcome to your new job as the night security guard at Fazbear's Fright! In Five Nights At Freddy's 3, your primary goal is to survive five consecutive nights, from midnight to 6 AM. Unlike previous games, you'll face a single, deadly animatronic named Springtrap.

Your main tool is the security camera monitor. Click the button on your right to pull it up. Use it to track Springtrap's location throughout the attraction. To keep Springtrap away from your office, you can play an audio cue in an adjacent room by double-clicking on a camera feed. He is drawn to the sound, which will hopefully lure him away.

Throughout the night, your systems—Audio, Cameras, and Ventilation—will randomly fail. Click the button on your left to open the maintenance panel and reboot any system that has an error. Failing to reboot the ventilation will cause you to hallucinate and may lead to a jumpscare from a phantom animatronic. If your cameras fail, you won't be able to track Springtrap. If the audio fails, you can't use your lure. Manage your systems, watch the cameras, and keep Springtrap away to survive the night!
