Five Nights At Freddy's 4

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 4

Welcome to the most personal and terrifying chapter of the Five Nights At Freddy's series. In this installment, you are not a security guard; you are a child trying to survive the night in your own bedroom. The rules have changed, and your survival depends on your senses, not on security cameras.

Your primary goal is to survive from midnight to 6 AM. To do this, you must check two doors, a closet, and the bed behind you. Move to a door and listen carefully. If you hear breathing, immediately shut the door and hold it until the sound stops. If you don't hear anything, you can use your flashlight to check the hallway for any lurking animatronics.

Be vigilant and check behind you periodically, as small, nightmarish versions of Freddy Fazbear can accumulate on your bed. Use your flashlight to scare them away before they summon the real nightmare. The closet is another point of entry for monsters, so be sure to check and close it when necessary. Every sound matters, so turn up your volume and listen closely to survive the five nights.
