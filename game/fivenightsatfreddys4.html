Five Nights At Freddy's 4

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 4

Welcome to the most personal and terrifying chapter of Five Nights at Freddy's. Your goal is simple but daunting: survive from midnight until 6 AM for five consecutive nights. Trapped in a child's bedroom, you must defend yourself against monstrous animatronics that haunt the darkness.

Unlike previous games, you have no security cameras or power meter. Your ears are your most important tool. You must move between the two doors, the closet, and the bed behind you. At each door, listen carefully. If you hear breathing, hold the door shut until the sound stops. If you hear nothing, use your flashlight to check the hallway for any lurking dangers. Be quick, as hesitation can be fatal.

Don't forget to check the closet and the bed behind you periodically. Shine your light on the bed to scare away the small, creepy 'Freddles' before they can summon a bigger threat. Keep a close watch on the closet door, using your flashlight to keep whatever is inside from emerging. Every sound, every shadow, and every second counts. Stay alert, trust your instincts, and you might just see the morning light.
