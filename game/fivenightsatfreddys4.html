Five Nights At Freddy's 4

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 4

Welcome to your childhood bedroom, where the only thing separating you from nightmare animatronics is your own vigilance. Your goal is to survive from midnight until 6 AM for five consecutive nights. Unlike previous games, there are no security cameras or limited power. Your ears are your greatest tool.

Use your mouse to look around the room. You can interact with the two doors on your left and right, the closet in front of you, and the bed behind you. Move your cursor to the bottom of the screen to turn around and check the bed for "Freddles."

Approach a door and listen carefully. If you hear breathing, immediately press and hold the designated key (often SHIFT) or click to close the door until the sound stops. If you don't hear anything, use your flashlight (CTRL key or click) to scare away any animatronics lurking at the end of the hall. Be warned: shining your light when an animatronic is at the door will result in an instant game over. Regularly check the closet and scare away any monsters you find inside by closing the door on them.
