Five Nights At Freddy's 4

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 4

Your objective in Five Nights at Freddy's 4 is to survive from midnight to 6 AM for five consecutive nights. Unlike previous games, you are not in a security office but a child's bedroom, making the experience intensely personal and frightening.

Survival depends on your ability to listen carefully. Approach the doors to your left and right. Listen for breathing. If you hear breathing, hold the door shut until the sound stops. Be patient, as reacting too quickly or slowly can lead to a jumpscare.

If you don't hear any breathing at a door, use your flashlight to check the hallway. This will scare away any animatronics that might be lingering far down the hall. Also, be sure to periodically turn around to check the bed behind you and flash the light on the closet to keep other lurking nightmares at bay.

Stay vigilant, trust your ears, and use your flashlight wisely. Each sound, from a creak to a breath, is a vital clue to your survival. Can you endure the terror and make it through all five nights?
