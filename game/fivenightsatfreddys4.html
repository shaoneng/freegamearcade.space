Five Nights At Freddy's 4

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 4

In this terrifying chapter, you are not a security guard. You are a child, alone in your bedroom, and the nightmare animatronics are coming for you. Your goal is to survive from 12 AM to 6 AM by keeping them at bay. Forget security cameras and power levels; your ears are your greatest weapon.

Use your mouse to look around the room. You have four areas to watch: the left door, the right door, the closet in front of you, and the bed behind you. Move your mouse to the bottom of the screen to quickly turn around and check the bed.

Approach a door and listen carefully. If you hear breathing, immediately press and hold the 'Spacebar' or click to shut the door until the sound stops. If you hear nothing, it's safe to use your flashlight (hold 'Ctrl' or click the flashlight icon) to check the hallway. The same rules apply to the closet. Listen for sounds, and if you hear something, shut the door. From time to time, you must also check the bed behind you. Small, nightmarish versions of Freddy, called "Freddles," will appear. Use your flashlight to scare them away before three of them gather, as they will summon the much larger and deadlier Nightmare Freddy.
