Five Nights At Freddy's 4

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 4

Welcome to your childhood bedroom, now the stage for your worst nightmares. In Five Nights At Freddy's 4, your objective is to survive five nights until 6 AM. This time, there are no security cameras or power meters to help you. Your survival depends entirely on your senses, especially your hearing.

Your primary defenses are your doors, the closet, and a flashlight. Move to the left or right doors and listen carefully. If you hear breathing, hold the door shut immediately until the sound fades. If you hear nothing, use your flashlight to check the hall for any lurking animatronics. Be quick, as shining the light on a creature too close will result in a jumpscare.

Don't forget to check the closet in front of you and the bed behind you. Shine your light into the closet to keep Nightmare Foxy at bay, and scare off the small 'Freddles' from your bed before they summon Nightmare Freddy. Stay vigilant, listen closely, and manage your time to survive the night.
