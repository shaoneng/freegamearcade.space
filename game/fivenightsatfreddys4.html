Five Nights At Freddy's 4

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 4

Welcome to your childhood bedroom, now the stage for your worst nightmares. In Five Nights At Freddy's 4, your objective is simple yet terrifying: survive each night until 6 AM. Unlike previous games, there are no security cameras or power meters. Your survival depends entirely on your eyes and ears.

Use your mouse to look around the room. Move your cursor to the bottom of the screen to turn around and check the bed. To check the left and right doors or the closet, approach them and hold the SHIFT key to use your flashlight. Listen carefully at each door for breathing—if you hear it, close the door immediately by clicking. If you don't hear anything, use your flashlight to scare away any animatronics you see down the hall.

Be wary of Nightmare Freddy's minions ('Freddles') accumulating on your bed; use your flashlight to clear them before they form the full animatronic. Act quickly but cautiously—shining the light at the wrong moment or failing to close a door when you hear breathing will result in a terrifying jumpscare. Can you master your senses and endure the five nights of terror?
