Five Nights At Freddy's 4

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 4

In Five Nights at Freddy's 4, the familiar security office is gone. You are now a child trying to survive the night in your own bedroom, armed only with a flashlight. The rules have changed, and the horror is more personal than ever.

Your primary defense is your hearing. Move to the left and right doors and listen carefully for any sound. If you hear breathing, immediately shut the door and hold it until the sound fades. Do not use your flashlight if you hear breathing, or you will be met with a jumpscare.

If you don't hear anything at a door, it's safe to use your flashlight to quickly check down the hallway. This can scare away any animatronics that might be approaching from a distance. Be fast, as lingering too long can be dangerous.

Don't forget to check your other surroundings. Turn around to check the bed for small, creepy animatronics and use your flashlight to make them go away. You must also regularly check the closet. If you see an animatronic peeking out, close the closet door until it retreats back into the darkness.

Your goal is to survive each night from midnight to 6 AM. Stay alert, trust your ears, and react quickly to make it through all five nights of terror.
