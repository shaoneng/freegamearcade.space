Five Nights At Freddy's 4

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 4

Welcome to the final, terrifying chapter of the Five Nights at Freddy's story. In this installment, you are no longer a security guard. You are a child, alone in your bedroom, and the monsters are coming for you from every shadow. Your goal is to survive five nights until 6 AM.

Forget the cameras and power meters of previous games. Your survival now depends entirely on your senses, primarily your hearing. You must defend yourself from Nightmare Freddy, Nightmare Bonnie, Nightmare Chica, and Nightmare Foxy by monitoring the two doors to your room, the closet in front of you, and the bed behind you.

Move to a door and listen carefully. If you hear breathing, hold the door shut immediately until the sound fades. If you hear nothing, use your flashlight to scare away any animatronics you might see down the hall. Be quick, as hesitating can be fatal. Remember to also check the closet and the bed behind you regularly for lurking threats. Stay vigilant, listen closely, and you might just see the sunrise.
