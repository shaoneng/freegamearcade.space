Five Nights At Freddy's 4

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 4

Welcome to your worst nightmare. In Five Nights at Freddy's 4, you're not a security guard; you're a child trying to survive the night in your own bedroom. The objective is to make it from midnight to 6 AM for five consecutive nights, all while fending off horrifying animatronics that stalk you from the hallways, your closet, and even your bed.

Survival in this chapter is all about listening. Move to your left or right door and listen intently. If you hear breathing, immediately close the door and hold it shut until the sound fades. Acting too slowly or shining your light when they are close will result in a jumpscare. If you hear nothing, it's safe to use your flashlight to briefly check the hallway.

Don't forget to check behind you! Turn around to look at your bed and use the flashlight to scare away the small "Freddles" that accumulate over time. If you let too many gather, Nightmare Freddy will appear. You must also regularly check the closet in front of you. If you see Nightmare Foxy, close the closet doors on him until he reverts back to a plush toy.

Use your mouse to look around the room. Click on a door or the closet to run towards it. Press and hold the SHIFT key to close doors. Use the SPACEBAR to activate your flashlight. Your senses are your only tools—use them wisely to survive the nightmare.
