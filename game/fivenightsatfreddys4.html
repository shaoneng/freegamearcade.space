Five Nights At Freddy's 4

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 4

Welcome to the most personal nightmare yet. In Five Nights At Freddy's 4, your objective is to survive from midnight to 6 AM for five harrowing nights. You are a child in your bedroom, and the monsters are no longer confined to a pizzeriaâ€”they are right outside your door, in your closet, and on your bed.

This game changes the rules entirely. There are no security cameras or a power meter to manage. Your primary tool for survival is your hearing. You must move to the doors on your left and right and listen intently for breathing. If you hear breathing, hold the door shut immediately until the sound fades. If you hear nothing, it's safe to use your flashlight to check the hallway for any lurking animatronics. Be quick, as hesitating can be fatal.

Your vigilance must extend beyond the two doors. You must also regularly check the closet in front of you and the bed behind you. Shine your flashlight to ward off any creatures that might be gathering in these dark places. Quick reflexes and a keen ear are the only things standing between you and a terrifying jumpscare. Good luck.
