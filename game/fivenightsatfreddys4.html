Five Nights At Freddy's 4

How to Play Five Nights At Freddy's 4

Welcome to the final, terrifying chapter of the Five Nights at Freddy's saga. In this installment, the gameplay shifts dramatically. You are no longer in a security office; you are a child trying to survive the night in your own bedroom, stalked by nightmarish animatronics.

Your primary goal is to survive from midnight to 6 AM for five nights. Forget the cameras and power meters of previous games. Your survival now depends entirely on your senses, particularly your hearing. You must carefully listen for sounds at the two doors on either side of your room, in the closet in front of you, and on the bed behind you.

Move to a door and listen. If you hear breathing, immediately shut the door and wait for the sound to fade. If you hear nothing, use your flashlight to check the hallway for any lurking figures. Your flashlight is your only tool, but your ears are your best weapon. Stay alert, listen closely, and survive the nightmare.
