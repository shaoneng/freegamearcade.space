Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Your objective in Five Nights at Poppy is to survive five consecutive nights as a security guard in a haunted mental hospital. Each shift lasts from midnight (12:00 AM) to 6:00 AM, and your primary foe is the terrifying animatronic, Poppy.

From your small security office, you'll use a system of security cameras to track Poppy's movements throughout the facility. Click through the monitors to check different rooms, hallways, and even the morgue. Be vigilant, as Poppy will move when you're not looking and becomes more aggressive with each passing night.

Conserving power is crucial for your survival. Using cameras, lights, and closing doors will drain your limited power supply. If you run out of power before your shift ends, you'll be left defenseless. Manage your resources wisely, react quickly to Poppy's approach, and do whatever it takes to prevent her from reaching your office and delivering a bone-chilling jumpscare.

Disclaimer: This game contains intense horror themes, including sudden jump scares and unsettling imagery. It is intended for mature audiences.
