Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

In Five Nights at Poppy, you take on the role of a night security guard at a desolate mental hospital. Your primary objective is to survive five consecutive nights, from the haunting midnight hour until 6 AM.

From your small security office, you must use a system of security cameras to monitor the hospital's rooms and hallways. Keep a vigilant eye out for Poppy, the malevolent animatronic who roams the facility. It's your job to track her location at all times as she moves between the rooms.

Each night presents a greater challenge as Poppy becomes more aggressive, faster, and smarter in her attempts to reach you. You'll need to multitask effectively, manage limited resources, and react quickly to her movements. A single mistake could result in a terrifying jumpscare, ending your night prematurely. Stay alert, be strategic, and survive until morning!
