Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Your goal in Five Nights at Poppy is to survive five consecutive nights as a security guard in a haunted mental hospital. Each shift lasts from 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM. You must keep the menacing animatronic, Poppy, from reaching your office and delivering a game-ending jump scare.

From the safety of your small security office, you can monitor the hospital's various rooms and hallways using a system of security cameras. Click through the monitors to track Poppy's movements. She starts slow but becomes increasingly aggressive and unpredictable as the nights progress.

Managing your resources is crucial for survival. Using the cameras, lights, and door controls consumes a limited power supply. If you run out of power before 6:00 AM, you'll be left completely defenseless. React quickly, conserve energy, and do whatever it takes to make it through the night.

Warning: This game features intense horror elements, sudden jump scares, and unsettling audio. Player discretion is advised.
