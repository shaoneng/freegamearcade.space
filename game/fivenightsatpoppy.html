Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Your goal is to survive five consecutive nights as a security guard in an abandoned mental hospital. Each shift lasts from 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM. You must endure the night without getting caught by the menacing animatronic, Poppy.

From your security office, you have access to a network of cameras. Click on the different camera feeds to monitor various rooms and hallways. Keep a close watch on these monitors to track Poppy's movements as she makes her way toward you.

As the nights progress, Poppy becomes more aggressive, faster, and smarter in her attempts to reach you. You must react quickly to her changing patterns. Conserve your limited power supply, as using cameras, lights, and doors will drain it. If you run out of power before your shift ends, you'll be defenseless against Poppy's final, terrifying jump scare!
