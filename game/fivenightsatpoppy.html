Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Your goal is to survive five consecutive nights as a security guard in an abandoned mental hospital, working from midnight to 6 AM. From the safety of your small security office, you must keep a close watch on the hospital's sole, terrifying resident: the animatronic Poppy.

Use your mouse to interact with the security monitor system. Click through the various camera feeds to track Poppy's movements through the patient rooms, hallways, and morgue. It's crucial to know where she is at all times, as she becomes faster, smarter, and more aggressive with each passing night.

Be mindful of your resources. Wasting power or letting your camera systems fail will leave you vulnerable. Listen for audio cues, watch for static on the screens, and be ready to react quickly. If Poppy's shadow appears at your door, it might be too late. One mistake can lead to a terrifying jump scare, ending your night instantly.
