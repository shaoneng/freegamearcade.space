Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Welcome to your new night shift security job at an abandoned mental hospital. Your mission is simple, but far from easy: survive five nights, from 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM. The only problem? You're not alone. The hospital is home to Poppy, a terrifying animatronic with wide glowing eyes and a sinister smile, and she's not happy you're here.

From the safety of your small security office, you'll use a system of monitors to keep an eye on various rooms and hallways. Your primary task is to track Poppy's movements. She starts slow, but as the nights progress, she becomes faster, sneakier, and more aggressive. You must manage your resources carefully; if the cameras go out or you waste too much power, Poppy will find her way to you.

Each night presents a greater challenge, demanding quicker reflexes and sharp multitasking skills. One mistake could lead to a heart-pounding jump scare that ends your shift—and your game. Be prepared for a tense atmosphere, flickering lights, and unsettling sounds that will keep you on the edge of your seat.

Warning: This game features intense horror elements, including sudden jump scares, flashing lights, and disturbing audio. It is intended for mature players, and viewer discretion is advised.
