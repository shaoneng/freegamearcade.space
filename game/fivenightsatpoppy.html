Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Your objective in Five Nights at Poppy is simple yet terrifying: survive five consecutive nights as a security guard in an abandoned mental hospital. Each shift lasts from 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM, and you are not alone. The menacing animatronic, Poppy, is hunting you.

From your security office, you have access to a network of cameras. Use the monitors to switch between different rooms—patient areas, hallways, and the morgue—to keep a close eye on Poppy's location. She moves when you're not watching, so constant vigilance is key.

As the nights progress, Poppy becomes faster, smarter, and more aggressive. You must manage your limited resources, such as power for lights and cameras. One mistake, like leaving a camera broken for too long, can lead to a game-ending jumpscare. Multitask effectively and react quickly to her movements to make it through the week.

Warning: This game features intense horror elements, sudden jumpscares, and disturbing imagery. It is intended for mature audiences. Player discretion is advised.
