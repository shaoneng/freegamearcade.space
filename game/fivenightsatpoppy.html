Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Your objective in Five Nights at Poppy is to survive five consecutive nights as a security guard in a haunted mental hospital. Each shift lasts from midnight (12:00 AM) to 6:00 AM. You must prevent the animatronic, Poppy, from reaching your security office and delivering a game-ending jumpscare.

From the safety of your office, you have access to a network of security cameras. Click through the monitors to watch different rooms and hallways to keep track of Poppy's location. She moves when you're not looking, and her patterns become more unpredictable and aggressive as the nights progress.

Resource management is key to your survival. The cameras can go out, and you may need to manage power or reset systems. Pay close attention to audio cues like footsteps or strange noises, as they can signal Poppy's approach. If you see her shadow at your door or on a nearby camera, you must react quickly to secure your position. One mistake is all it takes for her to find you.

Disclaimer: This game features intense jump scares, flashing lights, and disturbing imagery. Player discretion is advised.
