Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Your objective in Five Nights at Poppy is to survive five consecutive nights as a security guard in a haunted mental hospital. Each shift lasts from 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM, and your only goal is to make it through without being caught by the menacing animatronic, Poppy.

You are stationed in a small security office equipped with a system of surveillance cameras. Click through the monitors to watch different areas of the hospital, including hallways, patient rooms, and the morgue. Your primary task is to keep track of Poppy's location at all times. She will move between rooms, and you must use the cameras to anticipate her path.

As the nights progress, Poppy becomes faster, more unpredictable, and increasingly aggressive. You must multitask effectively, managing your resources like power and reacting swiftly to her movements. If a camera goes out or you hear a noise nearby, be prepared to act fast. A single mistake could lead to a terrifying jumpscare, ending your night.

Disclaimer: This game features intense horror elements, including sudden jumpscares, flashing lights, and unsettling audio. Player discretion is advised, and it may not be suitable for all audiences.
