Five Nights at Poppy

About & How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Step into the eerie silence of an abandoned mental hospital in Five Nights at Poppy. You are the new night shift security guard, and your job is simple: survive five nights, from 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM. But you are not alone. The animatronic Poppy, with her wide glowing eyes and a haunting smile, is lurking in the shadows, and she's not here to play.

This creepy online horror game puts your nerves to the test. From your security office, you must use a limited power supply to watch security cameras, turn on lights, and secure the doors. Each action consumes precious energy. If you run out, the darkness—and Poppy—will find you.

Your Survival Guide:

  • Monitor Cameras: Keep a constant watch on the hospital's rooms and hallways to track Poppy's movements. She gets faster and smarter each night.
  • Manage Power: Conserve your electricity. Every light switch flipped and camera checked brings you closer to a blackout. Use your resources wisely.
  • Stay Alert: Listen for footsteps, static on the screens, and other audio cues. One mistake can lead to a terrifying jump scare. Your goal is to make it to 6 AM.

Warning: This game contains intense horror themes, including sudden jump scares, flashing lights, and unsettling imagery. It is intended for mature audiences. Parental guidance is recommended.
