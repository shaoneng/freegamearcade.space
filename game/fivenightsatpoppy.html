Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

In Five Nights at Poppy, your objective is to survive five consecutive nights as a security guard in a haunted mental hospital. Each shift lasts from 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM, and your only goal is to see the sunrise without getting caught by the malevolent animatronic, Poppy.

You are stationed in a small security office equipped with a system of surveillance cameras. Use your mouse to click through the monitors and watch different areas of the hospital. Your primary task is to keep a close eye on Poppy's location as she moves from room to room.

Poppy becomes more active, faster, and smarter as the nights progress. You must multitask effectively, managing your cameras and reacting to any disturbances. If a camera feed goes to static or you see a shadow at your door, you need to act quickly. Failure to do so will result in a terrifying jump scare, ending your night.

Warning: This game is designed for mature audiences and contains intense horror elements, including sudden jump scares, flashing lights, and disturbing sounds. Player discretion is advised.
