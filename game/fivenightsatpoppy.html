Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Welcome to your new job as a night security guard at an abandoned mental hospital, the new home of the animatronic Poppy. Your objective is simple but terrifying: survive five consecutive nights, from the stroke of midnight (12:00 AM) until the safety of dawn (6:00 AM).

From your small security office, you have access to a network of surveillance cameras. Use your mouse to switch between different camera feeds, keeping a close eye on all areas of the hospital, including patient rooms, hallways, and the morgue. Your primary task is to track Poppy's movements throughout the facility.

Poppy is not a stationary threat. She will move between rooms, and as the nights progress, she becomes faster, smarter, and more aggressive. Pay attention to camera static and audio cues, as they often signal her presence. If she appears at your door, you must react quickly to prevent a game-ending jumpscare. Multitasking and careful observation are your keys to survival.

Warning: This game features intense horror themes, including sudden jump scares, flashing lights, and disturbing sounds. It is intended for mature audiences. Player discretion is advised.
