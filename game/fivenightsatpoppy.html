Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Your goal in Five Nights at Poppy is to survive five consecutive nights as a security guard in a haunted mental hospital. Each night lasts from 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM. You are confined to your security office, and your primary threat is the animatronic, Poppy, who roams the halls.

Use your mouse to interact with the security system. Click on the monitors at the bottom of your screen to switch between different camera feeds and track Poppy's location. Be warned, using the cameras drains your limited power supply, which must last the entire night.

Poppy will try to reach your office. You have controls for the doors and lights on either side of your room. Use the lights to check if she is at your doorstep. If you see her, immediately close the door to block her path. Leaving the doors closed or the lights on also consumes power, so use them wisely. Manage your resources carefully to avoid a power outage, which leaves you completely vulnerable.

Each night gets progressively harder, with Poppy becoming more active and unpredictable. A single mistake can lead to a sudden and terrifying jumpscare. Pay close attention to audio and visual cues, react quickly, and you might just survive until morning.
