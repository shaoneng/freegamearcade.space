Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Your objective in Five Nights at Poppy is to survive five consecutive nights as a security guard in a haunted mental hospital. Each night lasts from 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM. You will spend your shift in a small security office, equipped with a system of surveillance cameras that you can use to monitor the various rooms and hallways.

Use your mouse to switch between different camera feeds on your monitors. Your primary task is to keep track of the animatronic, Poppy. She will move through the hospital, and it's crucial to know her location at all times. Pay close attention to visual and audio cues, such as static on the screen or strange noises, as they signal her activity.

As the nights progress, Poppy becomes faster, more unpredictable, and smarter. You must react quickly to her movements. If she gets too close to your office, you'll need to use your limited resources to protect yourself. One wrong move or a moment of inattention can lead to a sudden and startling jumpscare, ending your night. Conserve your power and stay vigilant to survive until morning!
