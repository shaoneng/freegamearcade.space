Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Welcome to your new job as a night security guard at an abandoned mental hospital. Your primary objective in Five Nights at Poppy is to survive five consecutive nights, from the chilling stroke of midnight (12:00 AM) until the safety of dawn (6:00 AM). The only thing standing in your way is Poppy, a relentless animatronic with a sinister smile.

From your security office, you have access to a network of cameras. Use your mouse to click through the different monitors and keep a close eye on Poppy's location. She moves when you're not looking, so constant vigilance is key. Tracking her progress through the hospital's dark hallways and creepy rooms is your first line of defense.

Be warned: every action consumes power. Using the cameras, lights, and security doors drains your limited supply. If you run out of power before the night is over, you'll be left vulnerable in the dark. As the nights progress, Poppy becomes faster, smarter, and more unpredictable. You must multitask effectively to manage your resources and react quickly to her movements before she appears at your door for a game-ending jumpscare.

Disclaimer: This game contains intense horror elements, including sudden jumpscares, flashing lights, and disturbing sounds. Player discretion is advised, and it may not be suitable for all audiences.
