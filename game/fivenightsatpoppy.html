Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Ever stayed late at school or walked through a dark hallway alone and felt like something was watching you? Now imagine that same creep, but at an abandoned mental hospital where animatronic Poppy stays. That's exactly where Five Nights at Poppy throws you.

This creepy online horror game puts you in the shoes of a night shift security guard. Your mission is to survive five long nights, from 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM, while monitoring the hospital where Poppy is kept. She's angrier than ever, with wide glowing eyes and an eerie smile, and she's definitely not here to play.

You begin each night in a small security office, surrounded by monitors showing different parts of the hospital. Your main job is to watch these cameras to track Poppy's movements. At first, she moves slowly, but as the nights go on, she becomes faster, sneakier, and more aggressive. You must manage your resources carefully; if your cameras stay broken too long or you waste power, she'll strike. One mistake, and you'll face one of the game's infamous jumpscares.

Warning: Five Nights at Poppy contains intense horror themes, including sudden jump scares, flashing lights, unsettling imagery, and disturbing audio effects. This game is intended for mature audiences. Viewer discretion is advised.
