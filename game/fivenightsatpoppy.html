Five Nights at Poppy

How to Play Five Nights at Poppy

Your mission in Five Nights at Poppy is to survive five consecutive nights as a security guard in a derelict mental hospital. Each shift lasts from the in-game time of 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM. You will operate from a small security office equipped with a system of surveillance cameras that are your only eyes on the dark, winding hallways.

Use your mouse to switch between the different camera feeds on your monitors. Your primary task is to track the location of the menacing animatronic, Poppy. She will move between rooms, and it's crucial to know where she is at all times. As the nights progress, she becomes faster, more aggressive, and her patterns grow more unpredictable.

Resource management is key to survival. Activities like using the cameras and door controls will consume your limited power supply. If Poppy disappears from the cameras, be prepared. You must use your wits and quick reflexes to prevent her from reaching your office. If she does, it’s game over with a terrifying jump scare. Stay alert, manage your power, and survive until morning!

Disclaimer: This game contains intense horror themes, including sudden jump scares, flashing lights, and unsettling audio. It is intended for mature audiences and may not be suitable for younger players or those sensitive to frightening content. Viewer discretion is advised.
