Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

Welcome to the deceptively simple yet notoriously difficult world of Flappy Bird! Your objective is to guide Faby, the bird, as far as you can through an endless series of green pipes.

The controls are straightforward:

  • On Mobile: Simply tap the screen to make the bird flap its wings and gain a little altitude.
  • On PC: You can either click the left mouse button or press the spacebar to achieve the same effect.

Timing is everything. Let the bird drop too low or fly too high, and your run will come to an abrupt end. For every set of pipes you successfully navigate, you'll earn one point.
