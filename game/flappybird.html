Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

Welcome to the addictively simple, yet incredibly challenging world of Flappy Bird! Your goal is to guide your bird, Faby, through a series of green pipes without crashing. The game is endless, and the only objective is to get the highest score you possibly can.

The controls are straightforward. If you're on a PC, simply click your left mouse button or press the spacebar to make the bird flap its wings and gain a little altitude. On a mobile device, just tap the screen. Each flap gives you a short burst upwards, but gravity is always pulling you down. You'll need to find the perfect rhythm of taps to navigate through the narrow gaps between the pipes.

For every set of pipes you successfully pass through, you earn one point. As your score increases, you can earn medals as a reward for your skill:

  • Bronze Medal: Awarded for scoring 10+ points.
  • Silver Medal: Awarded for scoring 20+ points.
  • Gold Medal: Awarded for scoring 30+ points.
  • Platinum Medal: The ultimate prize, awarded for scoring 40+ points.

Challenge yourself, beat your friends, and see if you have what it takes to master the flap!
