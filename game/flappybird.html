Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

Your mission in Flappy Bird is deceptively simple: guide your bird, Faby, through an endless series of green pipes without crashing. The goal is to fly as far as possible and rack up the highest score you can achieve.

The controls are straightforward but require precise timing. On a PC, you can either click the left mouse button or press the spacebar to make the bird flap its wings and gain a little altitude. On a mobile device, simply tap the screen to flap. Let go, and gravity will take over, so you'll need to find the right rhythm to navigate the gaps.

For every set of pipes you successfully pass through, you earn one point. As your score increases, you'll be rewarded with medals for your achievements. Earn 10 points for a Bronze medal, 20 for Silver, 30 for Gold, and if you're a true master, 40 points will secure you the coveted Platinum medal. Challenge yourself and see if you can collect them all!
