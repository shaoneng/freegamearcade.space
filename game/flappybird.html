Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

Welcome to the addictive, pixelated world of Flappy Bird! Your objective is straightforward: guide your bird through an endless series of pipes for as long as possible. The game is a true test of timing and reflexes.

The controls are incredibly simple. On a PC, you can either click your left mouse button or press the spacebar to make the bird flap its wings and fly upwards. On a mobile device, just tap the screen. Each flap gives you a small boost, but gravity is always pulling you down.

You must carefully time your taps to navigate the narrow gaps between the pipes. Every pipe you successfully pass earns you one point. Crash into a pipe or the ground, and it's game over! Challenge yourself to beat your high score and earn bronze, silver, gold, and even the elusive platinum medal.
