Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

The goal in Flappy Bird is simple: guide your bird as far as you can through an endless maze of green pipes. Your score is determined by the number of pipes you successfully pass through without crashing.

To keep the bird airborne, you need to make it flap. If you're on a desktop computer, click the left mouse button or press the spacebar to flap. On a mobile device, simply tap anywhere on the screen. Each action gives your bird a small upward boost, but gravity is constantly pulling it down.

Timing your flaps is crucial to navigate the gaps between the pipes. Hit a pipe or the ground, and it's game over! As you score more points, you'll earn medals: a Bronze for 10 points, Silver for 20, Gold for 30, and the elusive Platinum for reaching a score of 40 or more.
