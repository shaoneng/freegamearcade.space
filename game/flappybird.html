Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

Welcome to the pixelated world of Flappy Bird, the famously addictive tap-to-fly arcade game! The objective is simple but challenging: guide your bird, Faby, as it flies continuously to the right, navigating through a series of green pipes.

The controls are incredibly easy to learn. On a mobile device, simply tap the screen to make the bird flap its wings and gain a little altitude. On a PC, you can either click the left mouse button or press the spacebar to achieve the same effect. Timing your taps is crucial to avoid hitting the pipes or the ground.

For every set of pipes you successfully pass through, you'll earn one point. As your score increases, you'll be rewarded with medals for your achievement:

  • Bronze Medal: Awarded for scoring over 10 points.
  • Silver Medal: Awarded for scoring over 20 points.
  • Gold Medal: Awarded for scoring over 30 points.
  • Platinum Medal: The ultimate prize, awarded for scoring over 40 points!

You can also customize your experience by choosing your bird's color and toggling between day, night, or a dynamic sky that changes between both modes. Jump in and see how high you can score!
