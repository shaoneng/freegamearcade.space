Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

Dive into the pixelated chaos of Flappy Bird! The goal is simple: guide your bird, Faby, as it flies endlessly to the right. Your mission is to navigate through a series of green pipes without crashing.

The controls are incredibly straightforward. On a PC, you can either click the left mouse button or press the spacebar to make the bird flap its wings and gain a little altitude. On mobile devices, simply tap the screen. Timing your flaps is crucial to maintain the right height and pass through the gaps in the pipes.

You earn one point for every pipe you successfully pass. While easy to learn, the game is famously difficult to master. As you improve, you'll be rewarded with medals for reaching score milestones: earn a bronze medal for 10 points, silver for 20, gold for 30, and the prestigious platinum medal for achieving a score of 40 or more. Challenge yourself and see what you can achieve!
