Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

The goal of Flappy Bird is deceptively simple: guide your bird, Faby, through an endless series of pipes without crashing. The game is all about timing and precision. Your bird will fly continuously to the right, and it's your job to keep it airborne and safely navigate the gaps.

Controlling the bird is easy. If you're playing on a PC, simply press the spacebar or click the left mouse button to make the bird flap its wings and gain a bit of altitude. On a mobile device, just tap the screen. Each tap gives you a small upward boost. Be careful not to tap too much or too little, as you'll need to maintain the perfect height to pass through the pipes.

You earn one point for every pair of pipes you successfully fly through. As your score increases, you can earn medals for your achievements: achieve 10 points for a Bronze medal, 20 for Silver, 30 for Gold, and the prestigious Platinum medal for scoring 40 or more points. Challenge yourself to collect them all!
