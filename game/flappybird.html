Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

The goal in Flappy Bird is simple: guide your bird through an endless series of pipes without crashing. It's a game of pure timing and rhythm that's easy to learn but famously difficult to master.

Controls:

  • On PC: Click the left mouse button or press the Spacebar to make the bird flap and gain altitude.
  • On Mobile: Tap anywhere on the screen to flap.

Your bird flies forward automatically. Your only job is to control its vertical movement to navigate the gaps in the pipes. Each set of pipes you successfully pass through earns you one point. Aim for a high score and challenge yourself to unlock medals based on your performance:

  • Bronze Medal: Score 10+ points
  • Silver Medal: Score 20+ points
  • Gold Medal: Score 30+ points
  • Platinum Medal: Score 40+ points
