Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

The goal in Flappy Bird is to fly your bird for as long as possible without hitting the pipes or the ground. It's a game of endurance and precision.

Use a single action to make the bird flap its wings and fly upwards. On a PC, you can either click the left mouse button or press the spacebar. On a mobile device, simply tap the screen to flap.

Carefully time your flaps to navigate through the gaps in the green pipes. Each set of pipes you successfully pass earns you one point. The game is deceptively simple to learn, but extremely challenging to master. Keep practicing to beat your high score and earn prestigious medals!
