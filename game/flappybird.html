Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

Welcome to the infuriatingly addictive world of Flappy Bird! Your goal is simple: guide your bird, Faby, through a series of pipes without crashing. The game is designed to be easy to learn but incredibly difficult to master.

Controls:

  • On PC: Click the left mouse button or press the spacebar to make the bird flap its wings and gain altitude.
  • On Mobile: Simply tap the screen to flap.

Each time you successfully navigate through a pair of pipes, you'll score a point. Let gravity take its course between flaps, but be careful not to drop too low or fly too high. Timing is everything! As you accumulate points, you can unlock medals for your achievements, from Bronze for 10 points to the ultimate Platinum for scoring over 40.
