Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

The goal in Flappy Bird is simple: guide your bird through as many pipes as possible without crashing. The controls are straightforward. If you're playing on a PC, you can either click the left mouse button or press the spacebar to make your bird flap its wings and gain a little altitude. If you're on a mobile device, just tap the screen to achieve the same effect.

You earn one point for every set of pipes you successfully navigate through. The challenge lies in timing your taps perfectly to maintain the right height. As you accumulate points, you'll be rewarded with medals for your skill: a Bronze medal for 10 points, Silver for 20, Gold for 30, and the ultimate Platinum medal for reaching 40 points or more.

You can also customize your experience. Choose your bird's color to fly in style and toggle between day and night modes to change the scenery as you play.
