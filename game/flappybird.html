Flappy Bird

How to Play Flappy Bird

Welcome to the pixelated chaos of Flappy Bird! Your goal is simple but incredibly challenging: guide your bird, Faby, as it flies continuously to the right. You must navigate through a series of green pipes without touching them.

The controls are straightforward. On a PC, you can either click the left mouse button or press the spacebar to make the bird flap its wings and gain a little altitude. On mobile devices, simply tap the screen. Timing is everything, as you need to maintain the right height to pass through the gaps in the pipes.

For every set of pipes you successfully pass through, you earn one point. As you score higher, you'll be rewarded with medals: a Bronze medal for 10 points, Silver for 20, Gold for 30, and the prestigious Platinum medal for reaching an epic score of 40 points or more. Test your reflexes and see how many medals you can collect!
