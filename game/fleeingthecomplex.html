Fleeing the Complex

How to Play Fleeing the Complex

In Fleeing the Complex, you guide Henry Stickmin through a high-security prison escape. The game is driven by your decisions, presenting you with various choices at critical moments.

To play, simply use your **mouse to click** on the different action options that appear on screen. Each choice will lead to a unique outcome, sometimes successful, sometimes hilariously disastrous!

Your ultimate goal is to find a path that allows Henry to successfully escape "The Wall" and flee the complex. Experiment with different choices to discover all the possible endings and funny animations.
