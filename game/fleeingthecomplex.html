Fleeing the Complex

How to Play Fleeing the Complex

Welcome to The Wall, a high-security prison complex where the world's most dangerous criminals are held. Your favorite troublemaker, Henry Stickmin, has just been made its newest resident. But Henry isn't one to sit idly in a cell. Your mission is to guide him through a series of daring escape attempts!

Fleeing the Complex is an interactive, choice-based adventure game. At critical moments, time will freeze, and you'll be presented with a set of options. Your job is to choose the best (or funniest) course of action for Henry. Will you charge ahead, use a clever gadget, or call for an unexpected ally?

The controls are simple: just use your mouse to click on the action or item you want to use. Every decision matters and can lead to a successful escape, a different storyline path, or a hilarious fail. Part of the fun is exploring all the different outcomes and watching the comedic animations unfold. See if you can discover all the endings!
