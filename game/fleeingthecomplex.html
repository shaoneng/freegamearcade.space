Fleeing the Complex

How to Play Fleeing the Complex

Your mission in Fleeing the Complex is to help Henry Stickmin escape from The Wall, a remote and heavily guarded prison complex. The gameplay is simple, but the choices are tough!

At key moments in the story, you will be presented with several options for what to do next. To make a decision, simply use your mouse to click on the action or item you want Henry to use.

Each choice you make will trigger a unique animated sequence. Some choices will lead you closer to freedom, while others will result in a hilarious fail. Don't be afraid to experiment! Part of the fun is discovering all the different outcomes and references hidden in the game. Can you find all the possible endings and help Henry make his grand escape?
