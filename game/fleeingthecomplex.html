Fleeing the Complex

How to Play Fleeing the Complex

Your adventure begins with Henry Stickmin locked away in The Wall, a formidable high-security prison complex located in the snowy mountains of Canada. As the player, your objective is to guide Henry through a series of critical decisions to orchestrate a daring escape.

The gameplay is entirely interactive and choice-based. At key moments in the story, the game will pause and present you with several options for what to do next. Simply use your mouse to click on the action or item you want Henry to use. There's no single correct path; every choice has a consequence!

Each decision you make sends the story down a different branch, leading to one of five unique endings and over 60 hilarious fail screens. The fun lies in exploring every possibility, from brilliant escape plans to laugh-out-loud mishaps. Can you discover every ending and help Henry regain his freedom?
