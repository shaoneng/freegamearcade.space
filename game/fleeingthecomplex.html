How to Play Fleeing the Complex

Welcome to The Wall, the world's most secure prison complex. As Henry Stickmin, your objective is simple: escape! Fleeing the Complex is an interactive point-and-click adventure where your choices dictate the story.

At key moments in the story, you will be presented with several options or tools. To play, simply use your mouse to click on the action you want Henry to perform. There are no complex keyboard controls to learnâ€”just point, click, and watch the chaos unfold.

Be warned: not every choice leads to success. In fact, most will lead to hilarious and unexpected 'fail' screens, which are a core part of the fun! Experiment with different choices to discover all the possible outcomes and find the five unique successful endings. Can you guide Henry to freedom?
