Fleeing the Complex

How to Play Fleeing the Complex

Welcome to The Wall, the world's most secure and remote prison complex. As Henry Stickmin, your objective is simple: escape! Fleeing the Complex is an interactive, choice-driven adventure game where your decisions shape the story.

The gameplay is straightforward. At critical moments in the story, time will pause, and you'll be presented with several options. Using your mouse, simply click on the action you want Henry to take. Will you boost up a fellow inmate, call a lawyer, or just play dead? Each choice branches the story in a new direction.

Be prepared for a mix of clever solutions and hilarious failures. Part of the fun is discovering all the different outcomes. Some choices lead to epic escapes, while others result in comical "Fail" screens with witty descriptions. Don't be afraid to experiment and try every path to find all the endings and hidden secrets!
