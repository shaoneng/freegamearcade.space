Fleeing the Complex

How to Play Fleeing the Complex

Welcome to Fleeing the Complex, the fifth chapter in the iconic Henry Stickmin series! Your adventure begins with Henry locked up in The Wall, a maximum-security prison complex nestled in the snowy mountains. This isn't a place you check out of; you have to break out.

Your role is to guide Henry's escape by making crucial decisions. The game plays like an interactive cartoon. At various points in the story, time will freeze, and you'll be presented with a set of choicesâ€”different actions, gadgets, or dialogue options.

The controls are incredibly simple: just use your mouse to click on the option you think is best. There are no complicated key combinations to remember. Your intellect and sense of humor are your primary tools!

Be warned: not every choice leads to success. In fact, many lead to gut-bustingly funny "fail" animations. Part of the fun is discovering all the different outcomes! Will you go it alone, or will you team up with fellow inmate Ellie Rose? Try different paths to unlock all five unique endings and witness every possible hilarious mishap along the way.
