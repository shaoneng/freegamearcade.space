Fleeing the Complex

How to Play Fleeing the Complex

Your mission in Fleeing the Complex is to help the infamous Henry Stickmin escape from The Wall, a remote and heavily guarded prison complex. After being captured, Henry finds himself in a transfer cell, and it's here that his next great escape begins. The objective is simple: make choices that lead him to freedom.

The gameplay is entirely based on your decisions. At various points in the story, you will be presented with several options for Henry to take. To play, simply use your mouse to click on the action or item you want Henry to use. There are no complex keyboard controlsâ€”just point and click to determine his fate.

Every choice matters! Your decisions can lead to a successful escape, but more often than not, they result in a hilarious 'FAIL' screen with a funny description. Don't be discouraged by failure; it's part of the fun! Fleeing the Complex features multiple endings and dozens of unique fails to discover. Try different paths and choices to see all the chaotic possibilities and find all five successful escape routes.
