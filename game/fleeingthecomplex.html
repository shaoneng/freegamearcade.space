Fleeing the Complex

How to Play Fleeing the Complex

Welcome to Fleeing the Complex, the fifth chapter in the Henry Stickmin saga! Our hero, Henry, has been captured and thrown into The Wall, a formidable prison complex. Your mission is to help him escape by making critical decisions at key moments.

The gameplay is simple and interactive. At various points in the story, you'll be presented with a set of choices. To make a decision, simply use your mouse to click on the action or item you want Henry to use.

Each choice you make will drastically alter the outcome. Some paths lead to a successful escape, while others result in hilarious and unexpected fail screens. There are multiple endings to discover, so don't be afraid to experiment with different options and see all the comical ways Henry's plans can unfold!
