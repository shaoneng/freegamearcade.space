Fleeing the Complex

How to Play Fleeing the Complex

Your mission in Fleeing the Complex is to guide Henry Stickmin out of The Wall, a formidable high-security prison. After being captured, Henry finds himself in a transfer cell, and it's here that his escape attempt begins. Your role is to make critical decisions that will determine his fate.

The gameplay is simple and intuitive. At various points in the story, you'll be presented with several choices on the screen, each represented by an icon or text. To make a decision, simply use your mouse to click on the action you want Henry to take.

Every choice you make branches the story in a new direction. Some paths will lead you closer to freedom, while many others will result in one of the game's famous and funny "Fail" sequences. Don't be afraid to experiment! Part of the fun is discovering all the unique endings and hilarious outcomes. Can you find all five successful escapes and collect every medal?
