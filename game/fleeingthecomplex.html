Fleeing the Complex

How to Play Fleeing the Complex

Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to help the legendary stickman, Henry, escape from The Wall, a heavily-guarded prison complex. The gameplay is simple, but the choices are anything but!

Use your mouse to make all decisions. At critical points in the story, time will slow down and you will be presented with a set of options. Click on the action, item, or dialogue choice you think will best help Henry proceed.

Choose wisely! One path might lead to a brilliant escape, while another could result in a hilarious fail. Part of the fun is discovering all the different outcomes. There are five major endings and dozens of unique fails to find, so don't be afraid to experiment with different choices on subsequent playthroughs!
