Fleeing the Complex

How to Play Fleeing the Complex

In Fleeing the Complex, you are in control of Henry Stickmin's destiny. The game begins with Henry locked inside The Wall, a heavily guarded prison complex. Your objective is simple: help him escape!

The gameplay is entirely based on your choices. At key moments, you'll be presented with several options or items. Use your mouse to click on the action you want Henry to take. Each choice will trigger a unique, fully animated cutscene.

Be warned: not all choices lead to freedom! Many options result in hilarious fails, which are a signature part of the fun. Experiment with different paths to discover all five unique endings and collect every medal. Sometimes, the most absurd choice might just be the right one. Will you charge ahead, call for help, or team up with your cellmate, Ellie?
