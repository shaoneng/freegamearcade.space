Fleeing the Complex

How to Play Fleeing the Complex

Welcome to Fleeing the Complex, the fifth installment in the action-packed Henry Stickmin saga! Your mission is to help Henry escape from The Wall, a formidable prison complex reserved for the world's most dangerous criminals.

The gameplay is simple and driven by your choices. At key moments, the game will pause and present you with several options for how to proceed. These can range from logical to completely absurd!

Use your mouse to click on the action or item you think will best help Henry advance. Will you charge head-on, use a clever disguise, team up with a fellow inmate, or call for an unexpected assist? The choice is yours.

Each decision has consequences. A correct choice will move the story forward, while an incorrect one often leads to a humorous and unique fail animation. Don't be afraid to experiment! Discovering all the different fail screens is part of the fun. Your choices will determine which of the multiple endings you achieve, so play again to see every possible outcome!
