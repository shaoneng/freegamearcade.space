Fleeing the Complex

How to Play Fleeing the Complex

Your mission in Fleeing the Complex is to help Henry Stickmin escape from a formidable high-security prison known as The Wall. The gameplay is straightforward and entirely choice-driven.

At various points in the story, time will freeze, and you'll be presented with several options for Henry to take. These can range from using an item, performing an action, or choosing a dialogue response. Simply use your mouse to click on the choice you think is best.

Be warned: not all choices lead to success! Many options result in hilarious and unexpected "FAIL" scenarios. Part of the fun is discovering all the different outcomes. Your decisions will shape the narrative, introducing you to new allies like Ellie Rose and leading you down one of several unique escape paths. To see everything the game has to offer, try replaying and making different choices!
