Flip Bottle

How to Play Flip Bottle

The goal in Flip Bottle is to test your precision and timing. Your objective is to flip a water bottle from one platform to another without letting it fall to the floor. You'll be jumping across a variety of everyday household items, from shelves and toasters to TVs and microwaves.

The controls are simple: just use your mouse's left-click to make the bottle jump. The key is to time your click perfectly to control the height and distance of your flip. Be aware that some platforms, like shelves, might move, adding an extra layer of challenge to your jumps!

As you successfully complete levels, the obstacle courses become more difficult. For every level you conquer, you'll earn 100 coins. Save up your coins to unlock a variety of fun new bottle styles, like soda or champagne, to customize your flipping experience.
