Flip Bottle

How to Play Flip Bottle

Your goal in Flip Bottle is to skillfully flip a bottle from one platform to the next without it falling to the floor. The controls are incredibly simple: just use your mouse's left-click to make the bottle jump. The key is to time your clicks perfectly to control the height and distance of each flip.

Navigate through various household environments, landing on everyday objects like microwaves, speakers, and shelves. Be aware that some platforms may move, adding an extra layer of difficulty to your challenge. Each successfully completed level rewards you with 100 coins.

Use the coins you earn to unlock a variety of new and stylish bottles, from soda to champagne, to customize your flipping experience. The levels get progressively harder, so focus and precision are essential to becoming a Flip Bottle master!
