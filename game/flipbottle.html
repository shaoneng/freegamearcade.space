Flip Bottle

How to Play Flip Bottle

The goal in Flip Bottle is to launch a plastic bottle from one platform to another, landing it perfectly upright. A simple left-click on your mouse will make the bottle jump. For a higher, more powerful flip, click and hold the mouse button.

You'll navigate through various household scenes, using everyday objects like toasters, microwaves, and shelves as your landing spots. Pay close attention, as some platforms might move, requiring you to time your flips perfectly to avoid falling.

Each successful level completion earns you 100 coins. You can use these coins to unlock cool new bottle designs, like soda or champagne, to add some extra flair to your flips. Test your focus and precision, and see if you have what it takes to master the flip!
