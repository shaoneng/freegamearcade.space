How to Play FNF Garcello

Welcome to FNF Garcello, a unique and emotional Friday Night Funkin' mod. The goal is to outperform Garcello in a series of rhythm battles. As the song plays, colored arrows will scroll up the screen. Your task is to press the corresponding key on your keyboard when the moving arrow aligns perfectly with the static arrow at the top of your play area.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys to match the notes. Hitting notes accurately will increase your score and keep your health bar in the green. Missing too many notes will drain your health, and if it empties completely, you'll have to start the song over. Stay focused, feel the beat, and guide Boyfriend through this touching musical story!
