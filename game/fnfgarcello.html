FNF Garcello

How to Play FNF Garcello

In FNF Garcello, you enter a rhythm battle against the uniquely cool and surprisingly emotional character, Garcello. The main objective is to outperform him by accurately hitting all the musical notes in his custom songs.

As the music plays, arrows will scroll up from the bottom of the screen. Your task is to press the corresponding key on your keyboard when the moving arrows perfectly align with the stationary arrow outlines at the top. You can use either the Arrow Keys or the WASD keys to match the notes.

Pay attention to the progress bar at the bottom. Hitting notes successfully will push the bar towards your side (green), while misses will let Garcello take the lead (red). To win the musical battle, you need to have more progress than Garcello by the end of the song. Stay focused, feel the beat, and experience the moving story of this fan-favorite mod!
