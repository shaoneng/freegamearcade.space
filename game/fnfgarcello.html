FNF Garcello

How to Play FNF Garcello

FNF Garcello is a unique and emotionally charged mod for the popular rhythm game, Friday Night Funkin'. The game introduces a new opponent, Garcello, who you'll challenge in a series of musical battles. His story, which touches on themes of addiction and health, unfolds through the music and visual changes in his character.

Your goal is to outperform Garcello by hitting the correct musical notes at the right time. Follow the on-screen prompts and press the corresponding keys to match the rhythm of the songs.

  • Controls: Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard.
  • Gameplay: As the colored arrows scroll up the screen, press the matching key when they align with the transparent arrows at the top.
  • Objective: Successfully hitting notes will move the progress bar at the bottom in your favor. Miss too many notes, and the bar will shift towards Garcello. Win the round by keeping the progress bar mostly green by the end of the song!
