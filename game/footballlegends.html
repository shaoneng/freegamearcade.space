Football Legends

How to Play Football Legends

Welcome to the pitch! The goal in Football Legends is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the timer runs out. You can play a quick match on your own against the AI, or grab a friend for an exciting 2-player showdown. For the ultimate challenge, enter the tournament and battle your way to the championship trophy!

Before each match, you'll choose your team and your legendary player. Each player has a unique special ability that can turn the tide of the game. Watch for the special power bar to fill up, then unleash a devastating move to outplay your rivals!

Controls

Master these controls to become a true legend:

Player 1

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Shoot / Slide Tackle: X Key
  • Supershot: Z Key

Player 2

  • Move: W, A, S, D Keys
  • Shoot / Slide Tackle: K Key
  • Supershot: L Key
