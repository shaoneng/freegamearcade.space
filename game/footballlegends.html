Football Legends

How to Play Football Legends

Welcome to the pitch! In Football Legends, your goal is to score more goals than your opponent before time runs out. Begin by choosing your preferred game mode: a Quick Match for instant action, or a Tournament to prove you're the best. You can play solo against the computer or challenge a friend in the 2-player mode.

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Shoot / Slide Tackle: X Key
  • Supershot: Z Key

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: W, A, S, D Keys
  • Shoot / Slide Tackle: L Key
  • Supershot: K Key

Keep an eye on the energy bar at the bottom of the screen. When it's fully charged, you can unleash a devastating Supershot that is nearly impossible for the goalie to block. Use your slide tackle to dispossess opponents and create scoring opportunities. Master these controls to dominate the field and lead your team to victory!
