Football Legends

How to Play Football Legends

Welcome to the pitch! Football Legends puts you in control of iconic football stars for fast-paced, arcade-style matches. You can play solo against the AI or challenge a friend in the 2-player mode.

Before the kickoff, select your game mode. Choose a 'Quick Match' for a casual game, or enter a 'Tournament' to prove you're the champion. You can also customize your match with 1v1, 2v2, or even a challenging 1v2 setup.

The controls are simple to learn but hard to master:

  • Player 1: Move with the Arrow Keys, shoot/tackle with the X key, and unleash your supershot with the Z key.
  • Player 2: Move with the W, A, S, D keys, shoot/tackle with the L key, and use your supershot with the K key.

Each legend has a unique special ability, like a powerful fireball shot or a quick teleport. Keep an eye on the power bar at the bottom of the screen. Once it's full, press your supershot key to perform a game-changing move and score an incredible goal!
