Football Legends

How to Play Football Legends

Welcome to the pitch! The goal in Football Legends is simple: score more goals than your opponent before time runs out. Begin by choosing your preferred game mode. You can fly solo in a 1 Player match against the computer, team up with or against a friend in 2 Player mode, or enter the competitive Tournament mode to prove you're the best.

After selecting your mode, pick your favorite legendary team and player. Each player has unique strengths and a powerful special ability. During the match, use your movement keys to run and jump, and use your action keys to kick the ball. Keep an eye on the special ability icon at the bottom of the screen. Once it's fully charged, press the 'Z' key to unleash a game-changing move like a super-shot or a teleport to outplay your rivals and secure victory!
