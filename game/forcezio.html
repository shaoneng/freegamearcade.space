ForceZ.io

How to Play ForceZ.io

ForceZ.io is a high-octane 3D multiplayer shooter where strategy and quick reflexes are essential. Your objective is to outgun opponents in various game modes, complete challenging missions, and become the top player on the server.

Player Controls:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: Left Mouse Click
  • Aim: Right Mouse Click
  • Reload: R key
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Switch Weapons: 1-9 keys or Mouse Wheel
  • Open Chat: T key
  • Pause Menu: Tab key

Gameplay & Modes:

Jump into online PvP action by creating your own room or joining an existing one. You can customize the match with different maps (like Isaneya and Sanctum) and modes (Free For All, Teams, Doomsday). You can also apply modifiers like double speed or armor for extra chaos!

Tackle single-player missions to sharpen your skills and earn cash. These missions have diverse objectives, such as achieving a kill streak or surviving for a specific duration. Use your earnings to buy superior armor and intimidating masks from the armory to gain an edge in combat.
