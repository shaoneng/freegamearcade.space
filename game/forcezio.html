How to Play ForceZ.io

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of ForceZ.io, where strategy and quick reflexes are your keys to victory. In this fast-paced online shooter, you'll jump, hide, hunt, and shoot your way through intense battles. Whether you're a lone wolf or a team player, your combat skills will be put to the ultimate test.

Before deploying, choose your battle. Jump into the Online PvP mode to create your own custom room or join an existing fight. You can tweak the rules with modifiers like double speed, jump force, or armor. Compete across various maps in classic modes such as Free For All (FFA), Teams, and the chaotic Doomsday mode.

Looking for a different challenge? Take on a variety of Missions with specific objectives. You might need to be the first to 15 kills in a deathmatch, secure a 3-kill streak without dying, or simply survive for 50 seconds against all odds. Completing missions is a great way to hone your skills and earn rewards.

Mastering the controls is essential for survival. Use WASD or the Arrow Keys to move, the Left Mouse Button to shoot, and the Right Mouse Button to aim down sights. Press R to reload your weapon and the Spacebar to jump. You can cycle through your arsenal using the 1-9 keys or the mouse wheel. To communicate with other players, press T to open the chat, and hit Tab to access the pause menu.
