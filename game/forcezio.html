How to Play ForceZ.io

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled arenas of ForceZ.io! This is a fast-paced 3D shooter where strategy and quick reflexes are your greatest assets. Whether you're battling players from around the world in intense PvP matches or tackling challenging solo missions, your goal is to outsmart, outmaneuver, and outgun your opponents. Jump, take cover, and pick the perfect moment to strike to climb the leaderboards and become a legend.

ForceZ.io offers several ways to play. Jump into Online PvP to create or join a room, choosing from maps like Isaneya and Sanctum and modes such as Free For All (FFA), Teams, or the chaotic Doomsday. You can even customize match rules like speed and armor multipliers. Alternatively, test your skills in Missions, where you'll face objectives like winning a deathmatch, achieving an elimination streak, or simply surviving against the odds for a set time. As you play, you'll earn money to upgrade your Armor and masks, giving you better protection in future fights.

Mastering the controls is essential for survival. Use WASD or the Arrow Keys to move around the map. Aim your weapon with the Right Mouse Button and fire with the Left Mouse Button. When you're out of ammo, press R to reload. You can switch between your weapons using the number keys (1-9) or the Mouse Wheel. To get a better vantage point or dodge enemy fire, press the Spacebar to jump. If you need to communicate with your team, press T to open the chat window. Finally, you can press Tab to access the pause menu.
