ForceZ.io

How to Play ForceZ.io

ForceZ.io is an adrenaline-fueled 3D multiplayer shooter where strategy and quick reflexes are key to survival. The objective is to outmaneuver and eliminate your opponents in fast-paced combat across various maps and game modes.

Controls:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: Left Mouse Click
  • Aim: Right Mouse Click
  • Reload: R Key
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Switch Weapons: 1-9 Keys or Mouse Wheel
  • Chat: T Key
  • Pause Menu: Tab Key

You can jump into the action through two main paths: Online PvP or Missions. In PvP, create or join a room to battle other players in modes like Free For All (FFA), Teams, or Doomsday. Missions offer specific objectives, such as achieving a certain number of kills or surviving for a set amount of time. As you play, you'll earn money to purchase powerful armor and masks, enhancing your defensive capabilities. Keep an eye on your health, ammo, and the radar to stay one step ahead of the enemy.
