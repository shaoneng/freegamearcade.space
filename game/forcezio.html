ForceZ.io

How to Play ForceZ.io

ForceZ.io is an intense, strategic first-person shooter where your tactical skills are put to the ultimate test. The core of the game is fast-paced action, requiring you to jump, hide, and hunt down your enemies with precision. Master the controls to dominate the battlefield.

  • Movement: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: Left Mouse Click
  • Aim: Right Mouse Click
  • Reload: R Key
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Change Weapon: 1-9 Keys or Mouse Wheel
  • Chat: T Key
  • Pause Menu: Tab Key

Dive into the action with several game modes. In Online PvP, you can create your own custom room or join an existing one. Choose from maps like Isaneya, Sanctum, and Sumerra, and set the rules with modifiers like double speed or jump force. Compete in classic modes such as Free For All (FFA), Teams, and the challenging Doomsday mode. You can also take on a variety of single-player missions, from winning a deathmatch to surviving against waves of enemies.

As you play, you'll earn XP and money. Use your earnings in the Armor section to purchase protective gear like the Hellbound Armor or Death Mask to improve your survivability. Check your profile regularly to track your kills, level, and progress!
