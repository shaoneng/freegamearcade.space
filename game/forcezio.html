ForceZ.io

How to Play ForceZ.io

ForceZ.io is a fast-paced, strategy-based first-person shooter. Your primary goal is to outmaneuver and eliminate your opponents across various maps and game modes. Whether you're fighting for yourself in Free-For-All or coordinating with teammates, quick reflexes and smart positioning are essential for victory.

Master the controls to dominate the battlefield. Use WASD or the Arrow Keys to move your character. Aim with your mouse and use the Left Mouse Button to shoot. Aim down sights for better accuracy with the Right Mouse Button. Press R to reload your weapon, and jump over obstacles with the Spacebar. You can cycle through your available weapons using the number keys (1-9) or the mouse wheel. If you need to communicate with your team, press T to open the chat window. The Tab key will open the pause menu.

Before you jump in, you can choose from several game modes like Free For All (FFA), Teams, and Doomsday. You can also create your own custom room and add modifiers like double speed, double jump force, or double armor to spice up the match. The game also features a mission system, offering specific objectives like winning a deathmatch or getting a kill streak, which help you level up and earn rewards.
