ForceZ.io

How to Play ForceZ.io

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of ForceZ.io, a fast-paced multiplayer shooter where your tactical skills and quick reflexes are put to the ultimate test. The objective is to outmaneuver and eliminate your opponents across various dynamic maps and game modes.

Controls:

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your character.
  • Left Mouse Button: Shoot your weapon.
  • Right Mouse Button: Aim down sights.
  • R Key: Reload your weapon.
  • Spacebar: Jump.
  • 1-9 Keys or Mouse Wheel: Cycle through weapons.
  • T Key: Open chat.
  • Tab Key: Toggle pause menu.

Gameplay Modes:

Jump into online PvP matches by creating your own room or joining an existing one. Choose from game modes like Free For All (FFA), Teams, or the challenging Doomsday mode. You can also take on solo missions with specific objectives, such as winning a deathmatch or surviving for a set amount of time. As you play, you'll earn money to purchase powerful armor, giving you better protection in future battles. Keep an eye on your health, ammo, and the radar to stay ahead of the competition!
