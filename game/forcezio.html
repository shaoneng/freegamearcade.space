ForceZ.io

How to Play ForceZ.io

ForceZ.io is an intense 3D multiplayer shooter where strategy and quick reflexes are your greatest assets. Jump into a futuristic battlefield, outmaneuver your opponents, and prove your combat prowess. The core of the game is to run, jump, hide, and find the perfect moment to strike down your enemies with a diverse arsenal of weapons.

Master the Controls:

  • Movement: Use WASD or the Arrow Keys to move your soldier.
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar to leap over obstacles or dodge fire.
  • Shoot: Use the Left Mouse Button to fire your weapon.
  • Aim: Hold the Right Mouse Button to aim down sights for better accuracy.
  • Reload: Press the R key to reload your weapon.
  • Switch Weapons: Use the number keys 1-9 or the Mouse Wheel to cycle through your arsenal.
  • Chat: Press T to open the chat window and communicate with other players.
  • Pause Menu: Press Tab to open the menu.

Game Modes & Features:

Dive into the action by choosing your preferred game mode. In Online PvP, you can create your own custom room or join an existing one. Battle across unique maps like Isaneya, Sanctum, and Sumerra in modes such as Free For All (FFA), Teams, or the chaotic Doomsday. You can also tackle a variety of single-player missions to sharpen your skills, from winning deathmatches to achieving kill streaks. Don't forget to visit the Armor section to customize your soldier with protective gear and powerful masks!
