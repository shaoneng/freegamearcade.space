ForceZ.io

How to Play ForceZ.io

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of ForceZ.io! This is a fast-paced first-person shooter where strategy is just as important as a quick trigger finger. Your goal is to outmaneuver and eliminate your opponents across various challenging maps. Jump, take cover, and find the perfect moment to strike. Whether you're playing solo missions or engaging in chaotic online PvP, every match is an adrenaline rush.

The core of the game lies in its diverse modes. In online PvP, you can create a custom room—choosing the map, mode (Free For All, Teams, Doomsday), and even gameplay modifiers like double speed or jump force—or join an existing one. Alternatively, test your skills in the mission mode, where you'll face objectives like achieving a certain number of kills or surviving for a set amount of time. As you play, you'll earn XP and money to upgrade your profile and unlock powerful armor and cosmetic masks to customize your look.

Mastering the controls is key to survival and victory. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to move around the map and the Spacebar to jump. Aim with the Right Mouse Button and fire your weapon with the Left Mouse Button. Press the R key to reload your weapon. You can cycle through your arsenal using the 1-9 number keys or the mouse wheel. If you need to communicate with your team, press the T key to open the chat. To access the menu, press the Tab key.
