ForceZ.io

How to Play ForceZ.io

ForceZ.io is an intense 3D multiplayer shooter where strategy and quick reflexes are key to victory. You can dive into online PvP matches by creating your own custom room or joining an existing one. Battle it out in various game modes like Free For All (FFA), Teams, and Doomsday across several unique maps. If you prefer to play solo, take on challenging missions with specific objectives, such as achieving a kill streak or surviving for a set amount of time.

Mastering the controls is essential for dominating the arena. Use **WASD** or the **Arrow Keys** to move your character. Aim your weapon with the **Right Mouse Button** and fire with the **Left Mouse Button**. Press **R** to reload your weapon. You can cycle through your available weapons using the number keys **1-9** or the **Mouse Wheel**. Jump over obstacles with the **Spacebar**. Press **Tab** to open the pause menu and **T** to chat with other players in the match.
