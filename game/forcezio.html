How to Play ForceZ.io

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of ForceZ.io, where strategy and quick reflexes are key to survival. Your main goal is to outsmart and outgun your opponents in various competitive modes. Whether you're fighting for yourself in Free For All, collaborating in Teams, or taking on special missions, the objective is to be the last one standing or to achieve the highest score.

Master the controls to dominate the battlefield:

  • Move: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to run and navigate the maps.
  • Shoot: Aim with your mouse and press the Left Mouse Button to fire your weapon.
  • Aim Down Sights: Hold the Right Mouse Button for a more precise shot.
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles or evade enemy fire.
  • Reload: Hit the R key to reload your weapon and stay ready for action.
  • Switch Weapons: Cycle through your arsenal using the 1-9 keys or the Mouse Wheel.
  • Chat: Press T to open the chat and communicate with other players.
  • Pause Menu: Press Tab to access the menu, view scores, or change settings.

Jump into online PvP by creating or joining a room. You can customize match settings like player count, speed, and armor multipliers. Or, tackle challenging single-player missions to hone your skills, from achieving kill streaks to surviving against the clock. Good luck, soldier!
