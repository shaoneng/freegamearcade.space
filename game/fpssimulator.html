FPS Simulator

How to Play FPS Simulator

Get ready for intense combat in FPS Simulator! Before you enter the battle, customize your game experience. Give your soldier a unique name, then choose your preferred game mode: fight alongside allies in Team mode or go it alone in a free-for-all Deathmatch. Select your arena from two large military maps, each filled with buildings, water tanks, and strategic vantage points.

Master the battlefield using standard first-person shooter controls: use your mouse to aim and fire, and the WASD keys to move around the map. An extensive arsenal is at your command. Easily switch between weapons like a deagle, shotgun, sniper rifle, or even a bazooka by using the 1-9 number keys or your mouse scroll wheel. Choose the right tool for the job to gain an edge over your opponents.

A key strategy for success is to use the environment to your advantage. Look for ladders to climb onto rooftops, providing you with an excellent sniper's nest. From a high vantage point, you can spot and eliminate enemies from a distance. Whether you're playing solo or with a team, smart positioning and skillful gunplay will lead you to the top of the leaderboard!
