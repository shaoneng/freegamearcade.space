FPS Simulator

How to Play FPS Simulator

Before you jump into the action, set up your game. Give your soldier a unique name, then choose your preferred game mode: fight alongside others in Team mode or go it alone in a classic Deathmatch. Next, select your battleground—Military 1 or Military 2. Both maps are vast and filled with buildings, cover, and strategic high ground.

Navigate the battlefield using the standard WASD keys for movement. Aim with your mouse, and click the left mouse button to fire your weapon. Use the right mouse button to aim down sights for better accuracy. Jump with the Spacebar and crouch with the C key.

You have a full arsenal at your disposal. Switch between weapons like a pistol, shotgun, assault rifle, or a powerful sniper rifle using the number keys (1-9) or your mouse scroll wheel. For tight situations, don't forget you have grenades and even a bazooka to clear out groups of enemies.

To gain an advantage, use the environment. Climb ladders to get onto rooftops, which are perfect sniper nests. Crouching will make you a smaller target and can improve your aim. In Deathmatch, a good sniper position can quickly rocket you to the top of the leaderboard. Work with your team or be a lone wolf—the choice is yours. Good luck, soldier!
