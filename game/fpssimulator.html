FPS Simulator

How to Play FPS Simulator

Welcome to the battlefield, soldier! In FPS Simulator, your goal is to eliminate opponents and climb the leaderboard in intense first-person combat. Before you deploy, you can customize your experience. Give your soldier a unique name, and then choose your game mode: fight as part of a squad or go solo in a chaotic Deathmatch.

Select your battleground from two large military arenas, each offering unique tactical advantages, from open spaces to tight corridors and elevated rooftops perfect for sniping. You can also set the maximum number of players for the match to control the intensity of the battle.

Once in the game, use the standard WASD keys to move and your mouse to aim and shoot. Press the Spacebar to jump and C to crouch. Switch between your diverse arsenal of weapons—including pistols, shotguns, rifles, and even a bazooka—using the 1-9 number keys or your mouse scroll wheel. Use grenades to clear out groups of enemies and find the perfect weapon for every situation to lead your team to victory!
