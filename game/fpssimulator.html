FPS Simulator

How to Play FPS Simulator

Welcome to the battlefield! Before you jump into the action, you can customize your experience. Give your soldier a unique name, then choose your game mode: join forces with others in a team battle or prove your solo skills in the intense Deathmatch mode. You can also select your arena—Military 1 or Military 2—and set the maximum number of players for the match.

Use the standard FPS controls to navigate the arena: W, A, S, D keys for movement, and your mouse to aim. Use the Space Bar to jump and C to crouch. These maps are large, so explore to find the best strategic positions.

Your arsenal is key to victory. Switch between weapons like a deagle, shotgun, and sniper rifle using the number keys (1-9) or your mouse scroll wheel. For a tactical advantage, climb ladders to reach rooftops, grab your sniper, and pick off enemies from a distance. If you're outnumbered, don't forget to use your grenades or bazooka to clear the area and dominate the leaderboard!
