How to Play FPS Simulator

Begin your tour of duty by customizing your game. First, give your soldier a unique name. Then, choose your battle style: team up with other players in a squad-based match or fight for personal glory in the free-for-all Deathmatch mode. Select your arena from two large maps, Military 1 or Military 2, and set the maximum number of players before you deploy.

Navigate the battlefield using standard FPS controls (WASD for movement, mouse to aim and shoot). Your arsenal is diverse; switch between weapons like the deagle, shotgun, sniper rifle, and even a bazooka using the number keys (1-9) or your mouse scroll wheel. Choose the right tool for the job â€“ a sniper for long-range takedowns from a rooftop, or a shotgun for intense close-quarters combat.

Use the environment to your advantage. Both maps feature buildings, water tanks, and other structures. Climb ladders to reach rooftops, providing excellent vantage points for sniping. Crouching will improve your aim and make you a smaller target. Whether you're playing solo or with a team, smart positioning and weapon selection are key to dominating the leaderboard.
