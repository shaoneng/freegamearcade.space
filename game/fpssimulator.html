How to Play FPS Simulator

Begin by giving your soldier a unique name. Before deploying, you'll need to make some crucial decisions. Choose your preferred game mode: team up with allies for tactical superiority or go it alone in the thrilling Deathmatch mode. Next, select your battlefield from two expansive maps, Military 1 or Military 2, and set the maximum number of players for the match.

Once in the game, use the WASD keys to move and your mouse to aim and shoot. Explore the vast arenas to find strategic advantages. Look for ladders to climb onto rooftops, which provide excellent vantage points for sniping unsuspecting enemies from a distance. Use the crouch key to steady your aim and become a smaller target.

You have a full arsenal at your disposal. Use the number keys (1-9) or your mouse wheel to switch between a variety of weapons, including a powerful deagle, a close-quarters shotgun, a long-range sniper rifle, and even explosive grenades and a bazooka.
