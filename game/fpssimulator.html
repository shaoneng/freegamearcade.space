FPS Simulator

How to Play FPS Simulator

Begin by setting up your game. Give your soldier a unique name, then choose your combat style. Select Team Deathmatch to cooperate with teammates or choose Deathmatch to fight for individual glory against all other players. Next, pick your battlefield from two large arenas, Military 1 or Military 2, and set the player limit before deploying.

Use the standard FPS controls: WASD keys to move, Spacebar to jump, and your mouse to aim and shoot. The vast maps offer many strategic points. A smart tactic is to find ladders and climb to the rooftops of buildings or water tanks. This high ground provides an excellent vantage point for sniping enemies from afar. Crouching will steady your aim and make you a smaller target for opponents.

You have access to a wide variety of weapons. Use the number keys (1-9) or the mouse scroll wheel to cycle through your arsenal, which includes a deagle, shotgun, sniper rifle, grenades, and even a bazooka. Choose your weapon based on the tactical situation. If you're outnumbered in a confined area, switching to a grenade or bazooka can effectively clear out multiple foes at once.
