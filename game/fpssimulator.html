FPS Simulator

How to Play FPS Simulator

Welcome to the battlefield! Your first step in FPS Simulator is to customize your game. Give your soldier a unique name, then decide your combat style: team up with other players or fight for yourself in the intense Deathmatch mode. Next, choose your arena—Military 1 or Military 2—both offering large, complex environments with plenty of places to take cover.

Use standard FPS controls to navigate the map: WASD keys to move, your mouse to aim and shoot, and the Spacebar to jump. Keep an eye out for ladders to climb onto rooftops, which provide excellent vantage points for sniping unsuspecting enemies.

Your arsenal is key to victory. Switch between a wide variety of weapons using the 1-9 number keys or your mouse scroll wheel. Equip a sniper rifle for long-range precision, a shotgun for close encounters, or even a bazooka to clear out groups of opponents. Adapt your weapon choice to the situation to climb the leaderboard and prove you're the ultimate soldier!
