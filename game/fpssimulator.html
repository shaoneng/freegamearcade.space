FPS Simulator

How to Play FPS Simulator

Welcome to the battlefield! Before you jump into the action, you'll need to set up your game. Start by giving your soldier a name. Then, choose your game mode: will you cooperate with a squad in team mode, or fight for individual glory in Deathmatch?

Select your arena from two vast military maps, each filled with buildings, water tanks, and tactical hiding spots. Use the WASD keys to move, the mouse to aim and shoot, and the number keys (1-9) or your mouse scroll wheel to switch between a wide array of weapons.

Your arsenal includes everything from a deagle and shotgun to a powerful sniper rifle and even a bazooka. For a strategic advantage, find ladders to climb onto rooftops. From there, you can crouch down, equip your sniper rifle, and eliminate enemies from a distance. The goal is simple: get the most kills and dominate the leaderboard!
