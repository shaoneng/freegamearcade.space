FPS Simulator

How to Play FPS Simulator

Get ready for intense combat in FPS Simulator! Before you jump into the action, you'll need to set up your game. Start by choosing your game mode: fight alongside allies in Team Deathmatch or prove your skills solo in the classic Deathmatch mode. Then, select your battlefield from two large arenas, Military 1 or Military 2, and give your soldier a unique name.

Use the standard FPS controls to navigate the arena: WASD keys for movement and the mouse to aim and shoot. Your arsenal is vast and versatile. You can switch between weapons like a deagle, shotgun, sniper rifle, and even heavy-hitters like grenades and bazookas by using the 1-9 number keys or your mouse scroll wheel.

To gain a tactical advantage, explore the map and find strategic positions. Both arenas feature buildings and large water tanks with ladders. Climb to the rooftops, find a good hiding spot, crouch down, and use your sniper rifle to pick off enemies from a distance. Whether playing in a team or on your own, smart positioning and skillful shooting will lead you to the top of the leaderboard!
