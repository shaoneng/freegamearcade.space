FPS Simulator

How to Play FPS Simulator

To start your mission in FPS Simulator, first, give your soldier a unique name from the main menu. Next, decide whether you want to join a team for cooperative play or engage in a solo Deathmatch against all opponents.

Choose your battlefield from two large and detailed arenas: Military 1 or Military 2. Both maps offer strategic hide-and-seek spots and multiple rooftops perfect for long-range engagements. You can also set the maximum number of players for your game.

Once in the game, use your keyboard for movement and the mouse for aiming and shooting. Switch between a wide variety of weapons like the Deagle, shotgun, sniper rifle, grenades, and bazooka using the 1-9 number keys or your mouse scroll wheel. For an advantage, climb ladders to rooftops, grab your sniper, crouch, and take down enemies from above. This tactical approach is especially effective in Deathmatch mode!
