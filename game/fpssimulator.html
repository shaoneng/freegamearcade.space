How to Play FPS Simulator

Welcome to FPS Simulator! Your mission is to dominate the battlefield. Before you start, customize your game settings. First, decide if you want to fight as part of a team or go solo in the thrilling Deathmatch mode.

Next, choose your arena: Military 1 or Military 2. Both maps are large and filled with buildings, water tanks, and plenty of places to take cover or set up an ambush. You can also set the maximum number of players for the match.

Once in the game, use your keyboard and mouse to move and aim. Use the number keys (1-9) or your mouse scroll wheel to switch between a wide variety of weapons, including a pistol, shotgun, sniper rifle, and even a bazooka for maximum impact.

A great strategy, especially in Deathmatch, is to find a ladder and climb to a rooftop. From this vantage point, you can use your sniper rifle to pick off enemies from a distance and control the map. Aim carefully and lead your team to victory!
