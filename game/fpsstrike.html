FPS Strike

How to Play FPS Strike

Dive into FPS Strike, a fast-paced shooter designed for instant action. The game is simple: load it, press start, and you're immediately dropped into a live battlefield. There's no need for lengthy installations or complex loadout customizations before you can play.

Choose between two classic game modes. In Free-for-All (FFA), it's every soldier for themselves in a chaotic battle for supremacy. In Team Deathmatch (TD), you'll join a squad and work together to eliminate the opposing team and achieve the highest score. Victory depends on your skill, reflexes, and teamwork.

Master the controls to navigate the battlefield and outmaneuver your enemies. Use your mouse to aim and shoot, and the keyboard to move, jump, and crouch. Explore five unique maps and experiment with over 18 different weapons to find the combination that best suits your playstyle.
