How to Play FPS Strike

Getting into the action in FPS Strike is fast and easy. The goal is simple: eliminate your opponents to win the match. The game offers two exciting modes: Free-for-All (FFA), where it's every soldier for themselves, and Team Deathmatch (TD), where you'll cooperate with your team to achieve victory.

Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot. Navigate the battlefield using the standard WASD keys for movement. Reload your weapon with the 'R' key and jump with the Spacebar. You can switch between your primary and secondary weapons to adapt to different combat situations.
