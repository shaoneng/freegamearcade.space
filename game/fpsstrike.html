FPS Strike

How to Play FPS Strike

FPS Strike throws you directly into fast-paced first-person shooter combat. The objective is simple: eliminate your opponents to score points. In Free-for-All (FFA) mode, it's every soldier for themselvesâ€”the player with the most kills wins. In Team Deathmatch (TD), you'll join a team and work together to outscore the enemy team before the timer runs out.

Mastering the controls is key to victory. Use the WASD keys to move around the map. Aim your weapon with the mouse, and click the left mouse button to fire. Use the right mouse button to aim down sights for better accuracy. Reload your weapon with the R key and jump over obstacles with the Spacebar. You can switch between your primary and secondary weapons using the number keys or mouse wheel.

Explore the five distinct maps to learn their layouts, find strategic vantage points, and discover the best routes. Experiment with the 18+ available weapons to find a loadout that suits your playstyle. Keep moving, use cover, and work with your teammates in TD mode to dominate the battlefield. The action is immediate, so be ready to fight as soon as you spawn!
