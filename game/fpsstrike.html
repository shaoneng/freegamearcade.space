FPS Strike

How to Play FPS Strike

FPS Strike is designed for instant action. To begin, simply let the game load and press the 'Start' button. You'll be immediately placed into a live multiplayer match, choosing between Free-for-All (FFA) and Team Deathmatch (TDM) modes.

The objective is straightforward: eliminate your opponents. In Free-for-All, it's every soldier for themselves. In Team Deathmatch, coordinate with your allies to outscore the enemy team. Use the standard FPS controls to navigate and fight:

  • Movement: Use the W, A, S, D keys to move.
  • Aim & Shoot: Use your mouse to aim and the Left Mouse Button to fire.
  • Reload: Press the R key to reload your weapon.
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles.
  • Switch Weapons: Use the number keys or mouse wheel to cycle through your arsenal.

Explore the five unique maps, master over 18 different weapons, and climb the leaderboards to prove you're the ultimate soldier!
