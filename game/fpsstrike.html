FPS Strike

How to Play FPS Strike

Getting into a match is fast and easy. Simply load the game and press the start button to be thrown directly into the action. There's no need for lengthy installations or complex setups.

Engage in thrilling combat across two classic game modes. Test your individual skill in a chaotic Free-for-All (FFA) match where it's every soldier for themselves, or coordinate with your teammates to achieve victory in a tactical Team Deathmatch (TD) battle.

Use standard FPS controls (WASD to move, Mouse to aim and shoot, R to reload) to navigate the battlefield. The objective is simple: eliminate your opponents to score points for yourself or your team. Explore the five unique maps and master over 18 different weapons to become a true FPS Strike champion.
