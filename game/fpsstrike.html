FPS Strike

How to Play FPS Strike

FPS Strike is designed for instant action. To get started, simply load the game and hit the start button. You'll be thrown directly into a live multiplayer match without any complex setup or installation, perfect for when you crave a quick shooter experience.

The controls are standard for any first-person shooter. Use the WASD keys to move your character, and use your mouse to aim and shoot. Press the R key to reload your weapon and the Spacebar to jump. Your objective depends on the game mode: in Free-for-All (FFA), it's every soldier for themselves. In Team Deathmatch (TDM), cooperate with your teammates to outscore the opposing team.

Explore five distinct maps and experiment with over 18 different weapons to find your perfect loadout. Adapt your strategy to the environment, outmaneuver your opponents, and climb the leaderboard to prove you're the ultimate soldier!
