FPS Strike

How to Play FPS Strike

FPS Strike throws you directly into the action for a fast-paced shooting experience. To begin, simply load the game and press the start button. You'll be placed into a multiplayer match instantly, with no complicated setup required.

The game offers two exciting modes: Free-for-All (FFA), where every player is your enemy, and Team Deathmatch (TD), where you'll cooperate with teammates to defeat the opposing side. Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the maps, aim, and shoot. Experiment with over 18 different weapons to find your perfect loadout and dominate the battlefield.
