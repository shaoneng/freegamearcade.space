FPS Strike

How to Play FPS Strike

Getting into the action in FPS Strike is quick and easy. Simply load the game and press the 'Start' button to be instantly dropped into a match. There's no need for lengthy installations or complicated loadout customizations before you can play.

Once in the game, you can choose between two exciting modes: Free-for-All (FFA), where it's every soldier for themselves, or Team Deathmatch (TD), where you'll work with your squad to outscore the enemy team. Navigate through 5 unique maps and use the environment to your advantage.

Master a wide variety of over 18 weapons to find the perfect fit for your playstyle. The controls are standard for FPS games: use your mouse to aim and shoot, and keyboard keys (typically WASD) to move around the battlefield. Hone your skills, rack up kills, and climb the leaderboard!
