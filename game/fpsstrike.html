FPS Strike

How to Play FPS Strike

FPS Strike is designed for instant action. To begin, simply load the game and press the start button. You'll be immediately placed into a live multiplayer match, no lengthy installs or sign-ups required.

The objective is straightforward: eliminate your opponents to score points. You can choose between two exciting game modes: Free-for-All (FFA), where it's every soldier for themselves, or Team Deathmatch (TD), where you'll collaborate with your team to defeat the enemy.

Master the controls to dominate the battlefield: Use the WASD keys to move, your mouse to aim and shoot, the R key to reload your weapon, and the Spacebar to jump. Cycle through your arsenal of over 18 weapons to find the perfect tool for any situation across five distinct maps.
