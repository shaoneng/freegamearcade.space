How to Play FPS Strike

Getting into the action in FPS Strike is designed to be quick and easy. Simply load the game, and with a press of the start button, you'll be dropped directly into the heart of the battle. There's no complex setup or customization required to begin.

Use standard first-person shooter controls to navigate the battlefield. Move your character with the WASD keys and use your mouse to aim. Fire your weapon with the Left Mouse Button, and aim down your sights for better accuracy with the Right Mouse Button. Press the R key to reload your weapon, the Space Bar to jump, and the Shift key to sprint.
