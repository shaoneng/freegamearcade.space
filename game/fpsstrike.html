FPS Strike

How to Play FPS Strike

Getting into the action with FPS Strike is incredibly simple. There's no need for lengthy installations or complicated setups. Just load the game, press the start button, and you'll be instantly dropped into a live battlefield, ready to play.

Choose your preferred game mode: jump into a chaotic Free-for-All (FFA) where every other player is an enemy, or team up with others in a strategic Team Deathmatch (TD) to outscore the opposing team. The goal is to get the most eliminations before the timer runs out.

Explore five distinct maps and experiment with an arsenal of over 18 different weapons to find your perfect loadout. Master the realistic game mechanics to outmaneuver and outgun your opponents in this fast-paced online shooter.
