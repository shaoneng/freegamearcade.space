FPS Strike

How to Play FPS Strike

FPS Strike throws you directly into the action without the long wait times of traditional shooters. To begin, simply load the game, press the start button, and you'll be dropped into a live match almost instantly.

You can test your skills in two classic game modes. Choose 'Free-for-All' (FFA) to fight against every other player in a chaotic battle for supremacy, or join a team in 'Team Deathmatch' (TD) to work together and outscore your opponents.

Use standard first-person shooter controls: your mouse to aim and your keyboard (WASD keys) to move around the map. Fire your weapon to take down enemies and explore the environment to find strategic advantages. With over 18 different weapons and 5 distinct maps, every match offers a new challenge. The goal is simple: get the highest score before the time runs out!
