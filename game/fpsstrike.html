FPS Strike

How to Play FPS Strike

FPS Strike drops you directly into fast-paced first-person shooter combat with minimal fuss. The goal is simple: eliminate your opponents and climb the leaderboard. When you start, you can choose between two exciting game modes to test your skills.

In Free-for-All (FFA), it's every soldier for themselves. Your objective is to get more kills than anyone else on one of the five diverse maps. In Team Deathmatch (TD), you'll join a team and work together to defeat the opposing squad. Communication and strategy are key to securing victory for your side.

Explore the arsenal of over 18 different weapons to find your perfect loadout. Whether you prefer sniping from a distance or rushing in with a shotgun, there's a weapon for every playstyle. Jump into a match now and show everyone your skills!
