Fragen

How to Play Fragen

Step into the intense urban warzone of Fragen, a modern first-person shooter that tests your strategy and reflexes. Your goal is to outsmart and outgun your opponents in fast-paced combat.

You can choose between two thrilling game modes. In Team Deathmatch, you'll join a squad and cooperate with your allies to dominate the enemy team. In Free For All, it's every soldier for themselves in a chaotic battle for supremacy where you can trust no one.

As you fight in gritty, war-torn city maps, every kill and assist will earn you experience and currency. Use these rewards to unlock new weapons, upgrade your armor, and customize your loadout to match your preferred combat style. The more you play, the more powerful you become.

Don't forget to log in daily to claim free cash, gear, and other valuable rewards to give you an edge in your next match. Choose your gear, sharpen your skills, and jump into the fight!
