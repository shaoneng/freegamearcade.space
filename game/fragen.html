Fragen

How to Play Fragen

Welcome to the battlefield of Fragen, a modern first-person shooter that tests your reflexes, strategy, and combat skills. Your mission is to dominate in fast-paced urban warfare against players from around the world.

Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to fire your weapon. Navigate the maps using the WASD keys. Choose your loadout before each match, selecting from a variety of weapons and gear to suit your playstyle.

You can compete in two exciting game modes. In Team Deathmatch, you'll join a squad and work together to achieve a higher kill count than the opposing team. In Free For All, it's every soldier for themselves—trust no one and fight to be the last one standing.

With every match, you'll earn experience and in-game currency. Use these rewards to unlock new weapons, upgrade your armor, and customize your soldier. Log in daily for extra bonuses and climb the ranks to become a legendary fighter!
