Fragen

How to Play Fragen

Welcome to the urban warzone of Fragen! Your mission is to dominate the battlefield in this fast-paced, modern first-person shooter. Use your keyboard and mouse to move, aim, and shoot like a pro.

You can choose from two exciting game modes:

  • Team Deathmatch: Join a squad and work together with your allies to out-gun and out-maneuver the enemy team. Communication and teamwork are key to victory.
  • Free For All: It's every soldier for themselves in this chaotic mode. Trust no one, stay sharp, and fight to be the last one standing on the leaderboard.

As you play, you'll earn experience and in-game currency for every kill and assist. Use your earnings to unlock powerful new weapons, upgrade your armor, and customize your loadout to match your preferred combat style. Remember to log in daily to claim free rewards and get an edge on the competition!
