Fragen

How to Play Fragen

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of Fragen, a modern first-person shooter that tests your reflexes and strategy. The goal is to outgun your opponents in fast-paced urban combat. You'll use standard FPS controls to navigate the maps, aim, and engage enemies.

Choose from two exciting game modes:

  • Team Deathmatch: Join forces with a squad and work together to achieve a higher kill count than the opposing team.
  • Free For All: It's every soldier for themselves in this chaotic mode. Trust no one and fight to be the top player on the leaderboard.

As you play, you'll earn experience and currency for every kill and assist. Use these rewards to unlock a wide arsenal of new weapons, upgrade your gear, and customize your soldier's loadout to match your preferred combat style. Log in daily for extra bonuses and prove you're the best soldier on the field!
