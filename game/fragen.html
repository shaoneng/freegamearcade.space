Fragen

How to Play Fragen

Welcome to the intense urban battlefields of Fragen! Your mission is to prove your skills as an elite soldier in fast-paced first-person shooter combat. Before each match, you can select your weapons and gear to best fit your strategy.

The controls are standard for FPS games: use the W, A, S, D keys to move, your mouse to aim and shoot, the R key to reload, and the Spacebar to jump. Sharp reflexes and precise aim are your greatest assets on the battlefield.

You can choose between two distinct game modes. In Team Deathmatch, you'll coordinate with your allies to eliminate the enemy team and secure victory. In Free For All, it's a true test of individual skill where you must fight against everyone to climb to the top of the ranks.

With every match you play, you'll earn experience and currency. Use these to unlock new weapons, upgrade your armor, and customize your soldier. Log in daily to claim free rewards and gear up for the fight ahead!
