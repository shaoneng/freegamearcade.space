Fragen

How to Play Fragen

Step into the warzone with Fragen, a modern first-person shooter that blends action, strategy, and fast-paced gameplay. Prepare for explosive urban combat by choosing your gear, sharpening your reflexes, and jumping right into the fight.

Engage in competitive matches across gritty, war-torn city maps. Every kill and assist helps your squad and boosts your rank in seasonal events. Each match rewards you with experience and currency, which you can use to unlock a vast arsenal of new weapons, gear, and cosmetic items.

Choose your preferred combat style with two distinct game modes:

  • Team Deathmatch: Team up with allies and work together to dominate the enemy.
  • Free For All: It's every soldier for themselves in this chaotic mode. Trust no one and fight to be the last one standing.

Log in daily to claim free cash, gear, and new weapons. Customize your loadout by choosing your favorite weapons, upgrading your armor, and fine-tuning your combat style to match your play.

Fragen is a must-play for fans of tactical shooters and competitive FPS combat. With responsive controls, deep customization, and regular rewards, it’s time to gear up and join the fight!
