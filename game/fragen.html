Fragen

How to Play Fragen

Fragen is a modern first-person shooter that throws you into intense, fast-paced urban warfare. The goal is to out-gun your opponents by demonstrating superior reflexes, strategy, and teamwork.

You can choose between two distinct game modes. In Team Deathmatch, you'll join a squad and work together to eliminate the opposing team and achieve the highest score. For a true test of individual skill, select Free For All, where it's every soldier for themselves and only the strongest will survive.

As you compete in matches, you'll earn experience and currency. Use these rewards to unlock a wide arsenal of new weapons, upgrade your armor, and customize your soldier's loadout to match your preferred combat style. Log in daily for extra bonuses and gear to get an edge on the battlefield!
