Fragen

How to Play Fragen

Step into the intense world of Fragen, a modern first-person shooter where your tactical skills and quick reflexes are key to survival. The objective is to outgun your opponents in fast-paced urban combat. You'll earn experience and in-game currency with every match, allowing you to unlock new weapons, customize your gear, and enhance your soldier's capabilities.

Choose your preferred combat style from two exciting game modes. In Team Deathmatch, you'll join a squad and work together to achieve victory over the opposing team. In Free For All, it's every soldier for themselves—trust no one and fight to be the last one standing.

Master the controls to dominate the battlefield. Use the WASD keys to move your soldier, your mouse to aim and look around, and the left mouse button to fire your weapon. Reload with the R key and switch weapons as needed. Jump into the action, climb the ranks, and prove you have what it takes to be a top-tier operator.
