Fragen

How to Play Fragen

Welcome to the intense battlefield of Fragen, a modern first-person shooter that demands strategy, skill, and quick reflexes. Your objective is to outgun your opponents in fast-paced urban combat.

Choose your game mode to get started:

  • Team Deathmatch: Join a squad and work together to eliminate the opposing team. Communication and teamwork are key to victory.
  • Free For All: It's every soldier for themselves in this chaotic mode. Trust no one and fight to be the last one standing at the top of the leaderboard.

As you compete in matches, you'll earn experience (XP) and in-game currency. Use these rewards to unlock a wide arsenal of new weapons, upgrade your armor, and customize your soldier's gear to fit your unique combat style. Log in daily to claim free bonuses and stay ahead of the competition. Grab your gear, choose your map, and join the fight!
