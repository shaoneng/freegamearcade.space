Fragen

How to Play Fragen

Fragen is a fast-paced, modern first-person shooter set in gritty urban warzones. Your goal is to outsmart and outgun your opponents through strategy and sharp reflexes.

You can compete in two primary game modes. In Team Deathmatch, you'll join a squad and work together to eliminate the opposing team. In Free For All, it's every soldier for themselves in a true test of individual skill.

As you play, you'll earn experience and in-game currency. Use these rewards to unlock a wide range of new weapons, upgrade your armor, and customize your soldier's gear. This allows you to tailor your loadout to your preferred combat style. Don't forget to log in daily to claim free cash, gear, and even new weapons to get an edge on the competition.
