Fragen

How to Play Fragen

Welcome to the warzone of Fragen, a modern first-person shooter that tests your reflexes and tactical skills. Your objective is simple: eliminate the opposition and dominate the battlefield. Before each match, you can choose your loadout, selecting weapons and gear that fit your combat style.

Jump into one of two intense game modes. In Team Deathmatch, you'll join a squad and work together to achieve a higher kill count than the enemy team. Communication and teamwork are key. In Free For All, it's every soldier for themselves—trust no one and fight to be the last one standing at the top of the leaderboard.

As you play, you'll earn experience and in-game currency. Use these rewards to unlock a wider arsenal of weapons, upgrade your armor, and customize your soldier's appearance. Log in daily for extra bonuses to accelerate your progress. Master the maps, outsmart your enemies, and climb the ranks to prove you're the best.
