Fragen

How to Play Fragen

Welcome to the battlefield of Fragen! This modern first-person shooter tests your reflexes and strategic thinking in fast-paced urban combat. Your main objective is to outgun your opponents and lead your side to victory.

Control your soldier using standard FPS controls: use the WASD keys to move around the map, your mouse to aim, and the left mouse button to fire your weapon. Use the right mouse button to aim down sights for improved accuracy.

You can choose from two distinct and competitive game modes:

  • Team Deathmatch: Join a squad and work together to eliminate the opposing team. Teamwork and coordinated attacks are crucial to winning the match.
  • Free For All: In this chaotic mode, it's every soldier for themselves. Trust no one, stay alert, and fight to reach the top of the leaderboard.

As you compete in matches, you'll earn experience and currency for every kill and assist. Use these rewards to unlock a powerful arsenal of new weapons, upgrade your armor, and customize your loadout to fit your unique combat style. Don't forget to log in daily to claim free cash and gear to get an edge in your next fight!
