Fragen

How to Play Fragen

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of Fragen! Your objective is to outgun and outmaneuver your opponents in fast-paced first-person shooter combat. Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the urban environments, aim your weapon, and fire at enemy combatants.

You can choose between two primary game modes:

  • Team Deathmatch: Join forces with a squad of teammates. Work together to achieve a higher kill count than the opposing team to secure victory.
  • Free For All: It's every soldier for themselves in this chaotic mode. Trust no one and fight to be the player with the most kills at the end of the match.

As you compete in matches, you will earn experience points and in-game currency. Use these rewards to unlock a wide arsenal of new weapons, upgrade your armor, and customize your soldier's gear to fit your personal combat style. Log in daily for extra bonuses and keep fighting to climb the ranks!
