Fragen

How to Play Fragen

Step into the warzone with Fragen, a modern first-person shooter that blends action, strategy, and fast-paced gameplay. Use your standard FPS controls (keyboard and mouse) to navigate the battlefield, aim with precision, and outmaneuver your opponents in explosive urban combat.

You can choose between two distinct game modes. In Team Deathmatch, you'll team up with allies to dominate the enemy squad and achieve victory together. For a true solo challenge, jump into Free For All, where it's every soldier for themselves and you can trust no one.

Every match you play earns you experience and currency. Use these rewards to unlock a vast arsenal of new weapons, upgrade your gear, and customize your soldier's combat style to fit your strategy. Log in daily to claim free cash, gear, and new weapons to stay ahead of the competition.
