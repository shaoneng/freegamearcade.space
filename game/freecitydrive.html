Free City Drive

How to Play Free City Drive

Free City Drive offers an immersive car simulation experience with complete freedom to explore a vast virtual city. Get ready to drive, customize, and complete thrilling missions!

You can modify your car's appearance by changing its color, license plate, and adding a spoiler to make it uniquely yours.

The game features several modes:

  • Free Drive: Explore the open world at your leisure, driving wherever you want with maximum speed.
  • Missions and Events: Take on 30 distinct challenges to test your driving skills and earn valuable prizes upon completion.
  • Test Area and Entertainment: Practice your moves and enjoy various interactive elements in a dedicated space.

Controls:

  • Use WASD keys to control your car (W: Accelerate, S: Brake/Reverse, A/D: Steer Left/Right).
  • Press Left Shift for a burst of nitro speed.
  • Use the Spacebar to engage the handbrake for sharp turns or quick stops.
  • Press Escape or Tab to pause the game and access menus.
  • Press C to cycle through different camera views.
