Free City Drive

How to Play Free City Drive

Welcome to Free City Drive! Get behind the wheel and start exploring a vibrant city at your own pace. Your primary goal is to drive, complete missions, and customize your vehicle for the ultimate driving experience. Before you start, choose your favorite car and personalize it by changing its color, license plate, and even adding a spoiler.

The game offers several exciting modes. In 'Free Drive,' you have the entire city as your playground. In 'Missions and Events,' you can take on 30 different challenges to earn rewards. There's also a 'Test Area' for you to practice your skills and have some fun.

Use the WASD keys to steer your car. To get a burst of speed, press Left Shift to activate the nitro. For sharp turns and drifts, use the Spacebar for the handbrake. You can switch your camera view by pressing C, and if you need to pause the game, simply press Escape or Tab.
