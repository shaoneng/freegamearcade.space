Free City Drive

How to Play Free City Drive

Welcome to Free City Drive, the ultimate car simulator where the open road is yours to command. Your goal is to explore the vibrant city, complete exciting missions, or just enjoy the thrill of high-speed driving. Select your favorite car and customize its color, plate, and spoiler before you hit the asphalt.

Getting started is easy with these simple controls:

  • Control Car: Use the WASD keys to steer, accelerate, and brake.
  • Nitro Boost: Press the Left Shift key for a burst of speed.
  • Handbrake: Use the Spacebar to perform sharp turns and drifts.
  • Change Camera: Press the C key to switch between different camera views.
  • Pause Menu: Hit Escape or Tab to pause the game and access the menu.

The game features multiple modes to keep you entertained. In Free Drive, you have complete freedom to explore the city at your own pace. Switch to Missions and Events to take on 30 unique challenges and earn cool prizes. You can also head to the Test Area to practice your stunts and driving skills in a safe environment.
