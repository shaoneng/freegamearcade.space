Free City Drive

How to Play Free City Drive

Welcome to Free City Drive, the ultimate car simulator that puts you in the driver's seat! Your main goal is to explore a vast virtual city with complete freedom. Before you hit the streets, you can select your car and customize it to your liking by changing its color, spoiler, and even the license plate to make it truly yours.

The game offers several exciting modes to keep you entertained. In Free Drive, you can roam the city without any objectives, perfecting your driving and discovering new areas. If you're looking for a challenge, jump into the Missions and Events mode to tackle 30 unique missions and earn prizes. There's also a dedicated Test Area for you to experiment with your car's capabilities and have some fun.

Mastering the controls is simple: Use WASD to control your car, press Left Shift to activate nitro for a burst of speed, and use the Spacebar for the handbrake to perform sharp turns and drifts. You can change your view with the C key and pause the game by pressing Escape or Tab.
