Free City Drive

How to Play Free City Drive

Welcome to Free City Drive, the ultimate car simulator where the open road is your playground! Start by selecting your favorite car and then head to the garage to customize it. You can change the paint color, update the license plate, and even add a custom spoiler to make it uniquely yours. Once your ride is ready, it's time to hit the streets.

The game offers several modes to keep you entertained. In Free Drive, you have complete freedom to explore the city, test your car's top speed, and perform stunts. For a more structured challenge, dive into the Missions and Events mode, where you can complete 30 different tasks to earn cool prizes. There's also a dedicated Test Area for pure entertainment and experimentation.

Mastering the controls is simple: use WASD to steer your car, press Left Shift for a nitro boost, and use the Spacebar for the handbrake. If you need to pause the game, just press Escape or Tab. To get the perfect view of your car and the city, use the C key to change camera angles.
