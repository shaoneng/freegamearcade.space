Free City Drive

How to Play Free City Drive

Welcome to Free City Drive! Your goal is to explore the city, complete missions, and customize your dream car. Begin by selecting a vehicle and choosing one of the game modes: Free Drive for casual exploration, Missions and Events for a challenge, or the Test Area for fun.

Master the controls to dominate the streets. Use the WASD keys to steer, accelerate, and brake your car. When you need a burst of speed, press the Left Shift key to activate your nitro boost. For sharp turns or to perform drifts, use the Spacebar for the handbrake. If you need to pause the game or access the menu, press the Escape or Tab key. To get the best view of the action, you can cycle through different camera angles by pressing the C key. Now, hit the road and enjoy the drive!
