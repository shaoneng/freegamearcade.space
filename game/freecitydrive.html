How to Play Free City Drive

Get behind the wheel in Free City Drive, a car simulator offering complete freedom. Begin by choosing your vehicle and personalizing it to your likingâ€”change the paint, customize the license plate, and even add a spoiler. Once you're ready, hit the streets of the open-world city and explore at your own pace.

The game features several modes to keep you entertained. In Free Drive, the city is your playground. For a more structured experience, take on 30 different challenges in the Missions and Events mode to earn rewards. You can also visit the Test Area for pure driving entertainment and to push your car's limits.

Mastering the controls is key: use WASD to drive, hit Left Shift for a nitro boost, and pull the Spacebar for the handbrake. You can change your camera view with the C key and pause the game by pressing Escape or Tab.
