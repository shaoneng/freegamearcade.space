Free City Drive

How to Play Free City Drive

Free City Drive is a dynamic car simulator that gives you the freedom to explore a sprawling open world. Take the wheel of your favorite car and cruise through the city streets, testing your vehicle's top speed and handling. Customize your ride by changing its color, license plate, and spoiler to make it uniquely yours.

The game offers several exciting modes to keep you entertained:

  • Free Drive: Roam the city without any restrictions or objectives.
  • Missions and Events: Take on 30 different missions to earn rewards and prove your driving prowess.
  • Test Area: A special zone designed for stunts and entertainment.

Master the controls to dominate the road:

  • WASD: Steer your car.
  • Left Shift: Activate the nitro boost.
  • Spacebar: Engage the handbrake for sharp drifts.
  • C: Cycle through different camera angles.
  • Escape / Tab: Pause the game.
