Free City Drive

How to Play Free City Drive

Welcome to Free City Drive, the ultimate car simulator where the city is your playground. Select your vehicle and dive into a world of open roads and endless possibilities. Before you start, personalize your ride by changing its color, spoiler, and even the license plate.

Master the controls to dominate the streets. Use the WASD keys to drive your car. When you need an extra burst of speed, press the Left Shift key to activate your nitro boost. For tight corners and stylish drifts, use the Spacebar to engage the handbrake. You can switch your viewpoint by pressing the C key to cycle through different camera angles. If you need to pause the game, simply press Escape or Tab.

Explore various game modes to test your skills. In 'Free Drive' mode, you have the complete freedom to explore the city at your own pace. For a challenge, take on the 'Missions and Events' mode, where you can complete 30 unique missions to earn rewards. Or, head over to the 'Test Area and Entertainment' zone to practice stunts and have fun.
