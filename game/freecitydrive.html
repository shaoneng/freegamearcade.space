Free City Drive

How to Play Free City Drive

Welcome to the driver's seat of Free City Drive! Your adventure begins by choosing a car and giving it a personal touch. You can modify its color, change the license plate, and even add a custom spoiler. Once your ride is ready, it's time to hit the streets.

Navigating the city is simple. Use the WASD keys to control your car's movement. When you need a burst of adrenaline, press the Left Shift key to activate the nitro. For tight corners and stylish drifts, use the Spacebar for the handbrake. You can also change your perspective by pressing the C key to cycle through different camera views. If you need to take a break, just press Escape or Tab to pause the game.

Explore the vast open world in Free Drive mode, challenge yourself with over 30 unique missions and events, or have some fun in the dedicated Test Area. The city is your playground!
