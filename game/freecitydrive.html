Free City Drive

How to Play Free City Drive

Welcome to the open roads of Free City Drive! Your goal is to explore the city, complete missions, and customize your dream car. Master the controls to navigate the streets like a pro. Use the WASD keys to steer, accelerate, and brake. When you need a burst of speed, press Left Shift to activate your nitro. For sharp turns and stylish drifts, use the Spacebar for the handbrake. You can also press C to cycle through different camera views to find your perfect driving perspective. If you need to pause the game, simply press Escape or Tab.

Free City Drive offers several ways to play. In Free Drive mode, the city is your playgroundâ€”explore every corner without any objectives. For a challenge, switch to Missions and Events mode, where you can tackle 30 unique missions to earn special prizes. If you're feeling adventurous, head to the Test Area and Entertainment zone for ramps, stunts, and fun. Before you hit the road, don't forget to customize your vehicle! You can change the color, update the license plate, and add a custom spoiler to make your car stand out.
