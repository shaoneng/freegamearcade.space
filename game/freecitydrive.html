Free City Drive

How to Play Free City Drive

Welcome to Free City Drive, the ultimate car simulator where the city is your playground. The goal is to explore, complete missions, and enjoy the freedom of the open road. Start by selecting your car and personalizing it to your style by changing the color, license plate, and even adding a spoiler.

The game offers several modes to keep you entertained. In 'Free Drive,' you can explore the virtual world without any restrictions, pushing your car to its limits. For a more structured experience, jump into 'Missions and Events' to complete 30 unique challenges and earn rewards. You can also visit the 'Test Area' for some extra fun and entertainment.

Mastering the controls is key to dominating the streets:

  • Movement: Use WASD keys to drive your car.
  • Nitro Boost: Press the Left Shift key for a burst of speed.
  • Handbrake: Use the Spacebar for sharp turns and drifts.
  • Change Camera: Press the C key to switch between different camera views.
  • Pause Menu: Press Escape or Tab to pause the game and access the menu.
