Free City Drive

How to Play Free City Drive

Welcome to Free City Drive, the ultimate car simulator where the open road is your playground! Your main goal is to drive freely, explore the city, and complete various challenges. Before you start, you can select a car and customize its color, spoiler, and even the license plate to make it your own. Dive into different game modes, from a relaxing Free Drive to completing all 30 missions for prizes.

Master the controls to rule the streets. Use the WASD keys to steer, accelerate, and brake. When you need an extra burst of speed, press the Left Shift key to activate your nitro boost. For sharp turns or to perform drifts, use the Spacebar for the handbrake. You can also change your perspective by pressing the C key to switch camera views. If you need to pause the game, simply press Escape or Tab.
