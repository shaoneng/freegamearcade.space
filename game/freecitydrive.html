Free City Drive

How to Play Free City Drive

Welcome to Free City Drive! Your goal is to explore a vast virtual city, complete exciting missions, and customize your dream car. Whether you prefer a relaxing drive or high-stakes challenges, this game offers the ultimate driving experience. Choose your car, modify it to your liking, and hit the streets to begin your adventure.

Before you start, personalize your vehicle by changing its color, license plate, and even adding a spoiler. Once you're ready, you can choose from several game modes. Jump into 'Free Drive' to explore the city without any objectives, or head to the 'Test Area' for some entertainment. For a real challenge, take on the 'Missions and Events' mode, where you'll need to complete all 30 unique missions to earn special prizes.

Mastering the controls is key to dominating the road. Use the WASD keys to steer and accelerate your car. When you need a burst of speed, press Left Shift to activate the nitro. Use the Spacebar for a sharp handbrake turn. To switch your perspective, press the C key to change the camera view. If you need to take a break, simply press Escape or Tab to pause the game.
