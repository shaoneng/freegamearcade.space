Free City Drive

How to Play Free City Drive

Welcome to Free City Drive, the ultimate car simulator where you rule the open roads! Begin by selecting your favorite car. Before you hit the streets, head to the garage to customize your ride. You can change the paint color, update the license plate, and even add a cool spoiler to make it your own.

The game offers several exciting modes to explore. In "Free Drive," you have the entire city as your playground with no rules or objectivesâ€”just pure driving freedom. If you're looking for a challenge, jump into "Missions and Events" mode, where you can complete 30 unique missions to earn prizes. Finally, the "Test Area and Entertainment" mode provides a dedicated space to practice stunts and push your car's limits.

Mastering the controls is key to dominating the city streets. Use the WASD keys to steer, accelerate, and brake. When you need an extra burst of speed, press Left Shift to activate the nitro. For sharp turns and drifts, use the Spacebar for the handbrake. You can switch camera views by pressing C, and if you need to pause the game, simply press Escape or Tab.
