Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the chaotic world of Gangsters! The goal is to eliminate the rival gang in a series of fast-paced rounds. On the title screen, you can choose to practice solo in 'P1 vs CPU' mode or battle a friend on the same keyboard in 'P1 vs P2' mode. The first team to score five points wins the match.

The controls are deceptively simple. Each player uses just two buttons. Press your 'jump' key to make your entire gang of three fighters hop together. They will slide left or right as they land, so timing and gentle taps are key. To attack, hold the 'shoot' key to raise your weapons and release it to fire. Your shots will travel in the direction your arms are pointing when you let go.

Keep an eye on the sky! Wooden crates will parachute down during each round. Touch a crate to break it open and automatically equip a surprise weapon, which could be anything from pistols and machine guns to chainsaws and the mighty bazooka. Be mindful of your surroundings, as later maps introduce environmental hazards like lava pits and water walls that can instantly knock out your fighters.
