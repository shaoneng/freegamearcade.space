Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the wacky world of Gangsters! The goal is simple: eliminate all three members of the opposing gang to score a point. The first player to reach five points wins the match. You can play solo against the computer (P1 vs CPU) or challenge a friend on the same keyboard (P1 vs P2).

The controls are deceptively simple, with just two buttons per player. Press the Jump key to make your entire gang hop and slide. Be careful, as their wobbly balance means they'll tilt left or right with each jump. Gentle taps are more effective than holding the key down for precise movement.

To attack, hold the Shoot key to raise your gang's arms. Your aim will sway as your characters wobble. Release the key to fire in the direction the arms are pointing. Time your release carefully to land a straight shot!

Keep an eye out for wooden crates parachuting down. Touch a crate to break it open and automatically equip a new weapon, which could be anything from a pistol or machine gun to a powerful bazooka or a deadly chainsaw.
