Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the chaotic streets of Gangsters! Your objective is to eliminate the rival gang before they eliminate you. You can play solo against the computer (P1 vs CPU) or battle a friend on the same keyboard (P1 vs P2). The first player to score five points wins the match.

The controls are deceptively simple. Each player controls their entire three-person gang with just two buttons. Press the JUMP key to make your whole squad hop. They will also slide slightly left or right, so tap gently for precise movements. Hold the SHOOT key to raise your gang's arms, and release it to fire. Your shots will travel in the direction the arms are pointing when you let go, so timing is key to landing a hit!

Keep an eye out for wooden crates that parachute from the sky. Touch a crate to break it open and automatically equip a surprise weapon, such as pistols, machine guns, chainsaws, swords, or the powerful bazooka. Be mindful of your surroundings; later levels feature environmental hazards like lava pits and water walls that can instantly knock out your fighters. Master the jump, grab weapons, and out-shoot your rival to claim victory!
