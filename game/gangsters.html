Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

The goal in Gangsters is to eliminate the opposing team of three fighters to score a point. The first player to reach five points wins the match! You can choose to play solo against the computer (P1 vs CPU) or challenge a friend on the same keyboard in the two-player mode (P1 vs P2).

The controls are deceptively simple. Press your 'jump' key to make your entire gang hop and slide. Tapping it gently is key to precise movement. To attack, hold the 'shoot' key to raise your gang's arms, and release it to fire. Your shots will travel in the direction your wobbly arms are pointing when you let go, so timing is crucial!

Throughout each round, wooden crates will parachute down from the sky. Run into a crate to break it open and automatically equip a new weapon. You might find anything from pistols and machine guns to chainsaws and the powerful bazooka!

Be aware of your surroundings! Later levels feature environmental dangers like pools of lava or deep water. Falling into these hazards will instantly knock out a character, so jump carefully. Use the wobbly physics to your advantage and master tricky shots to outsmart your opponent.
