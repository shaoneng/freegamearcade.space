Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to Gangsters, a hilarious physics-based brawler! The goal is to eliminate the opposing team of three fighters to score a point. The first player to reach the target score wins. You can choose to play against the computer (P1 vs CPU) or battle a friend on the same keyboard (P1 vs P2).

The controls are deceptively simple, with just two buttons per player. Press the 'Jump' key to make your entire crew hop and slide. Gentle taps are key for precise movement. Hold the 'Shoot' key to raise your weapons and release it to fire. Your aim depends on the wobbly sway of your characters, so timing is everything!

Keep an eye out for wooden crates parachuting from the sky. Touch a crate to break it open and automatically equip a new weapon, ranging from pistols and chainsaws to the powerful bazooka. Be mindful of your surroundings, as later levels introduce hazards like lava pits and water traps that can instantly knock out your fighters.
