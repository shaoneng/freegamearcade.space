Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the chaotic streets of Gangsters! The goal is simple: eliminate all three members of the rival gang to score a point. The first player to reach five points wins the match. You can challenge the computer in `P1 vs CPU` mode or battle a friend on the same keyboard in `P1 vs P2` mode.

The controls are deceptively simple, with just two buttons per player. Press your `Jump` key to make your entire gang hop into the air. Your gang will sway and slide with each jump, so use short taps for precise positioning. Be careful, as a clumsy jump can send your whole crew into a stage hazard!

To attack, press and hold the `Shoot` key to raise your gang's arms. The longer you hold, the more your aim might change due to the wobbly physics. Release the key to fire in the direction your arms are pointing. Keep an eye out for crates falling from the sky! Touch a crate to break it open and equip powerful weapons like pistols, machine guns, or even a mighty bazooka.

Pay attention to your environment. Later stages introduce dangerous obstacles like lava pits and deep water that will instantly knock out any gangster who falls in. Use the wobbly movement to your advantage, time your shots carefully, and grab the best weapons to dominate the streets and claim victory.
