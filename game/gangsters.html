Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the chaotic streets of Gangsters! The goal is to eliminate the opposing team of three fighters to score a point. The first player to reach five points wins the match. You can battle against the computer in `P1 vs CPU` mode or challenge a friend on the same keyboard in `P1 vs P2` mode.

The controls are deceptively simple, with just two buttons per player. Press the Jump key to make your entire crew hop and slide. Tapping it gently is key to precise movement. To attack, hold the Shoot key to raise your weapons. Release the key to fire! Since your gang sways back and forth, timing your release is crucial for aiming accurately.

Keep an eye out for wooden crates that fall from the sky. Run into a crate to break it open and automatically equip a powerful new weapon, such as pistols, machine guns, chainsaws, or even a mighty bazooka. Be mindful of your surroundings, as later maps introduce hazards like lava pits and water walls that can instantly knock out a character.
