Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the wacky world of Gangsters, a two-player street fight where chaos reigns supreme! Your goal is to eliminate the rival gang using an arsenal of weapons that drop from the sky. The game is controlled with just two buttons per player, making it incredibly easy to pick up but surprisingly deep to master.

Choose your game mode from the title screen: P1 vs CPU to practice your skills alone, or P1 vs P2 to challenge a friend on the same keyboard. Each player controls a trio of fighters who move, jump, and shoot together as one wobbly unit.

Controls: Use your designated Jump key to make your entire gang hop and slide. Tapping it gently is key to precise movement. Press and hold the Shoot key to raise your weapons, and release it to fire. Your aim depends on the gang's sway, so timing your shots is crucial for accuracy!

Keep an eye out for wooden crates parachuting onto the battlefield. Touch a crate to break it open and automatically equip its contents, which could be anything from pistols and machine guns to chainsaws and the powerful bazooka!

You score a point by eliminating all three members of the opposing team. Be careful of environmental hazards like lava pits, which can knock out your characters instantly. The first player to reach the target score (usually five points) wins the match!
