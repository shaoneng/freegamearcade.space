Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the chaotic, pixelated world of Gangsters! The goal is simple: eliminate all three members of the rival gang to score a point. The first player or team to reach five points wins the match. You can choose to challenge the computer in P1 vs CPU mode or battle a friend on the same keyboard in P1 vs P2 mode.

The controls are deceptively simple, with just two buttons per player. Press the Jump key to make your entire trio hop into the air. Your gang will sway and slide, so timing your jumps is crucial for positioning. Hold the Shoot key to raise your weapons and release it to fire. Since your characters are always wobbling, you'll need to time your release carefully to aim your shots.

Keep an eye out for wooden crates that parachute down from the sky. Touch a crate to break it open and automatically equip a surprise weapon, which could be anything from a pistol or machine gun to a chainsaw or the mighty bazooka! Be mindful of your surroundings; later levels introduce hazards like lava pits and water traps that can instantly knock out a character. Master the jump, grab a weapon, and blast your way to victory!
