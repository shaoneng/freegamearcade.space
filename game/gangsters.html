Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the chaotic world of Gangsters! The goal is simple: be the last gang standing. Whether you're playing solo against the CPU or battling a friend on the same keyboard, the controls are deceptively simple.

Controls: Each player uses just two buttons. One button makes your entire three-person crew jump, causing them to tilt and slide. The other button makes your crew raise their arms to aim. Release the button to fire! Since your gang is always wobbling, timing your shots is key to hitting your target.

Weapons and Power-ups: Keep an eye out for wooden crates parachuting from the sky. Touch a crate to break it open and automatically equip a new weapon. You might find anything from pistols and machine guns to swords, chainsaws, or the game-changing bazooka!

Winning the Game: Eliminate all three members of the opposing gang to score one point. The first player to reach the target score (usually five points) wins the match. Watch out for environmental hazards on different maps, like lava pits or water, which can instantly knock out your characters!
