Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the chaotic streets of Gangsters! This game is all about hilarious, physics-based combat. You can play solo against the computer (P1 vs CPU) or challenge a friend on the same keyboard (P1 vs P2). The controls are deceptively simple, with just two buttons for each player.

Use the Jump key to make your entire trio of gangsters hop into the air. They will also slide slightly left or right, so tap gently for precise movements. Use the Shoot key by holding it down to raise your characters' arms and aim. Release the key to fire! Because your gang constantly sways, timing your release is crucial for accurate shots.

Keep an eye on the sky! Wooden crates on parachutes will drift down during the match. Touch a crate to break it open and automatically equip whatever is inside. You can find everything from pistols and machine guns to chainsaws, swords, and the fan-favorite bazooka.

The goal is to eliminate all three members of the opposing gang. When you wipe out the other team, you score one point. The first player to reach the target score (usually five points) wins the match. Watch out for environmental hazards on different maps, like lava floors or deep water, which will instantly knock out any character that falls in!
