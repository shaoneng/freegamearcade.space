Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the chaotic streets of Gangsters! This hilarious two-player game is all about physics-based firefights. You can play solo against the computer (P1 vs CPU) or grab a friend for a local battle on the same keyboard (P1 vs P2).

The controls are deceptively simple. Each player uses only two buttons to control their entire gang of three fighters. One button makes your whole squad jump, causing them to tilt and slide. The other button makes everyone raise their arms; release it to fire. Because your gang is constantly swaying, you'll need to time your shots perfectly to hit your target.

Keep an eye out for wooden crates parachuting from the sky. Break them open to get a random weaponâ€”pistols, machine guns, swords, chainsaws, or the powerful bazooka. Use these weapons to eliminate all three members of the opposing gang to score a point. The first player to reach five points wins the match.

Be careful of the environment! Later levels introduce hazards like pools of lava and deep water. A clumsy jump can knock out one of your characters instantly, so watch your step. Master the wobbly movement and grab the best weapons to become the top gangster!
