Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the chaotic streets of Gangsters! This game is all about hilarious, physics-based battles using a simple two-button control scheme. Your goal is to eliminate the rival gang to score points. The first player to reach five points wins the match!

On the title screen, choose your battle. Select P1 vs CPU to practice your skills solo, or grab a friend for P1 vs P2 and share the keyboard for an intense local duel.

The controls are simple: use one key to Jump and another to Shoot. Pressing Jump makes your entire gang of three hop into the air. They will tilt and slide based on their balance, so tap gently for precise movements. Hold the Shoot key to raise your gang's arms, and release to fire. Your aim depends on the direction your arms are pointing when you let go, so timing is everything!

Keep an eye out for wooden crates parachuting from the sky. Touch a crate to break it open and automatically equip a surprise weapon—it could be anything from pistols and chainsaws to the mighty bazooka. Be mindful of your surroundings, as later maps feature deadly lava pits and water traps that will instantly knock out any character who falls in.
