Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Get ready for a pixelated street fight! Gangsters is a hilarious two-player online game where tiny gangs wobble, leap, and shoot each other with whatever weapons fall from the sky. With just two buttons per player, it’s easy to learn but hard to master, leading to endless rematches.

Choose your mode: P1 vs CPU for solo practice or P1 vs P2 to battle a friend on the same keyboard. Each team starts with three fighters. Press the jump key once to make your entire crew hop and slide together. Gentle taps are more effective than holding the key down.

Hold the shoot key to raise your gang's arms, and release it to fire. Bullets travel in the direction the arms are pointing when you let go. Since the gang sways constantly, timing your release is crucial for accurate shots.

Throughout each round, wooden crates parachute down. Touch a crate to break it and automatically equip its contents: pistols, machine guns, chainsaws, swords, or the powerful bazooka.

Later maps introduce hazards like lava floors and tall water walls that block bullets. Falling into these traps will knock a character out instantly, so jump carefully! When all three of your opponent's fighters are eliminated, you score a point. The first player to fill their scoreboard (usually five points) wins the match.
