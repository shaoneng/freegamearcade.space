Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to Gangsters, the ultimate pixelated street fight! This game is designed for two players (or one player against the computer) using a simple two-button control scheme. Each player controls a trio of gangsters who move, jump, and shoot together as a single, wobbly unit.

The controls are straightforward but tricky to master. Press your designated Jump key to make your entire crew hop. They will tilt and slide with each jump, so use short taps for precise movement. To attack, hold the Shoot key to raise your gang's arms, and release it to fire. Since your crew is always swaying, timing your release is crucial to aim your shots accurately.

Keep an eye on the sky! Wooden crates will parachute down during each round. Run into a crate to break it open and automatically equip a surprise weapon. You might find anything from pistols and machine guns to swords, chainsaws, or the powerful bazooka.

The objective is to eliminate all three members of the opposing gang to score a point. The first player to reach the target score (usually five points) wins the match. Be mindful of the environment—later stages feature hazards like lava pits and water walls that can instantly knock out your characters!

Choose your mode on the title screen: P1 vs CPU for solo practice or P1 vs P2 to battle a friend on the same keyboard. The simple controls and chaotic physics ensure that every match is filled with laughter and intense moments.
