Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the chaotic streets of Gangsters! This game is a physics-based shooter that is easy to learn but difficult to master. You can choose to play solo against the computer (P1 vs CPU) or challenge a friend on the same keyboard (P1 vs P2).

The controls are deceptively simple, with only two buttons per player. Press the jump key to make your entire gang of three characters hop into the air. They will tilt and slide based on their balance, so use gentle taps for precise movement. To attack, hold the shoot key to raise your gang's arms. Release the key to fire in the direction they are pointing. Since your gang is constantly swaying, timing your shots is crucial for accuracy.

Throughout each round, wooden crates will parachute from the sky. Touch a crate to break it open and automatically equip its contents, which can range from pistols and machine guns to chainsaws and powerful bazookas. Be mindful of the environment, as later maps introduce hazards like lava pits and water traps that can instantly eliminate your characters.

The goal is to eliminate all three members of the rival gang to score a point. The first player to fill their scoreboard (typically five points) wins the match. Good luck, and may the best gangster win!
