Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the wacky world of Gangsters, a two-player street fight where chaos reigns supreme! The game features incredibly simple controls but offers surprisingly deep and hilarious gameplay. You can face off against the computer (P1 vs CPU) or battle a friend on the same keyboard (P1 vs P2).

Each player controls their trio of gangsters with just two keys. Use your designated Jump key to make your entire gang hop. Each jump also causes them to slide, so tap carefully to position your crew. Use the Shoot key to fire your weapon. Hold the key to raise your gang's arms and release it to shoot. Since your gang is constantly swaying, timing your release is crucial for accurate shots!

Keep an eye on the sky! Wooden crates will parachute down during each round. Simply touch a crate to break it open and automatically equip its contents. You can find everything from pistols and machine guns to swords, chainsaws, and the fan-favorite bazooka. Grabbing the right weapon at the right time can instantly turn the tide of battle.

The objective is simple: eliminate all three members of the opposing gang to score a point. The first player to reach five points wins the match. Be mindful of the environment! Later maps introduce hazards like lava pits and deep water, which will instantly knock out any character that falls in. A poorly timed jump can be just as deadly as a rocket blast!
