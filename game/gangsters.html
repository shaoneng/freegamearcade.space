Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the chaotic streets of Gangsters! The objective is simple: eliminate the opposing gang to score a point. The first player or team to fill their scoreboard (typically five points) wins the match. You can choose to play against the computer (P1 vs CPU) for practice or battle a friend on the same keyboard in P1 vs P2 mode.

The controls are deceptively simple, with only two buttons per player. Press the 'jump' key to make your entire trio of gangsters hop into the air. They will wobble and slide, so mastering short, gentle taps is key to precise movement. To attack, hold the 'shoot' key to raise your gang's arms. Release the key to fire! The bullets travel in the direction the arms are pointing, so timing is crucial to land your shots.

Keep an eye out for wooden crates that parachute down from the sky. Touch a crate to break it open and automatically equip a powerful new weapon for your crew. You might find anything from pistols and machine guns to swords, chainsaws, or the fan-favorite bazooka. Grabbing these power-ups can quickly turn the tide of a battle.

Be mindful of your surroundings! As you progress, maps will introduce dangerous hazards like pools of lava or deep water walls that block shots. Falling into a hazard will instantly knock a character out of the round, so a misplaced jump can be just as deadly as enemy fire. Jump, shoot, and survive to become the top gangster!
