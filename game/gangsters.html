Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the chaotic streets of Gangsters! The goal is to eliminate the opposing gang to score points. The first player to fill their scoreboard (usually five points) wins the match. You can play solo against the computer (P1 vs CPU) or challenge a friend on the same keyboard (P1 vs P2).

The controls are deceptively simple, with only two buttons per player. Press the Jump key to make your entire trio of fighters hop simultaneously. Your gang will tilt and slide with each jump, so use short, gentle taps for precise movement. Be careful, as a clumsy jump can send your crew into deadly environmental hazards like lava or water for an instant knockout.

To attack, press and hold the Shoot key to raise your gang's arms. Your aim is affected by the constant swaying of your crew. Release the key to fire your weapon. Time your release carefully to land straight shots. Throughout the round, wooden crates will parachute down. Touch a crate to break it open and automatically equip its contents, which could be anything from pistols and machine guns to chainsaws and the powerful bazooka!
