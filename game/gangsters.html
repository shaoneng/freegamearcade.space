Gangsters

How to Play Gangsters

Welcome to the chaotic streets of Gangsters! The goal is simple: eliminate all three members of the rival gang to score a point. The first player to reach five points wins the match. You can practice your skills solo against the computer (P1 vs CPU) or grab a friend for a hilarious battle on the same keyboard (P1 vs P2).

The controls are deceptively simple. Each player uses just two buttons. Press the JUMP key to make your entire trio of gangsters hop into the air. They will wobble and slide based on their momentum, so timing your jumps is crucial. To attack, press and hold the SHOOT key to raise your weapons. Release the key to fire! Your shots will travel in the direction your gangsters are aiming, which changes as they sway back and forth.

Keep an eye on the sky! Wooden crates will parachute down periodically. Touch a crate to break it open and automatically equip a powerful new weapon for your entire squad. You might find anything from pistols and machine guns to swords, chainsaws, and the fan-favorite bazooka.

Be mindful of your surroundings. Later maps introduce environmental hazards like pools of lava or deep water. Falling into these traps will instantly eliminate a character, so a poorly timed jump can lose you the round without a single shot being fired. Adapt your strategy to each new map to stay on top!
