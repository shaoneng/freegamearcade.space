Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Geometry Arrow! The controls are deceptively simple, but the challenge is immense. Your goal is to guide the white arrow through a series of treacherous geometric tunnels without crashing into any spikes or obstacles.

Control the Arrow: Use a single tap or a click of your mouse to change the arrow's direction. When you tap, the arrow will instantly switch from moving upwards to downwards, or vice versa. This is your only move, so timing is everything!

Survive the Levels: Each of the 12 levels (plus a hidden bonus stage!) is synchronized to its own unique, upbeat soundtrack. Use the rhythm of the music to anticipate obstacles and time your taps perfectly. One wrong move, and you'll have to restart the level. Stay focused, stay calm, and guide your arrow to the finish line!
