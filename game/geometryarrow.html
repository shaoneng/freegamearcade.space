Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Dive into the sleek, rhythm-driven world of Geometry Arrow. Your mission is to guide a simple white arrow through a dark, minimalist tunnel filled with deadly spikes and sharp angles.

The controls are deceptively simple: tap or click to make the arrow rise, and release to let it fall. Your timing is everything. You must navigate the course, synchronizing your movements with the upbeat music to survive.

With 12 difficult levels and a hidden bonus stage, your reflexes will be pushed to the limit. If you enjoy the pulse-pounding challenge, try similar games like Space Waves for more one-tap, rhythm-based action. Just remember to take a breather when things get intense!
