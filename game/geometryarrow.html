Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Geometry Arrow is a game of pure reflex and rhythm. Your objective is to guide a sleek white arrow through a series of hazardous geometric tunnels without crashing into any spikes or walls. Survive the course to complete the level.

The controls are deceptively simple: just tap your screen or click your mouse to make the arrow rise. Releasing the tap or click will cause it to fall with gravity. You'll need to time these movements perfectly to navigate the narrow passages and avoid the deadly obstacles in your path.

Each of the 12 levels features a unique layout and a pulse-pounding soundtrack that often syncs with the gameplay. Try to feel the rhythm of the music to anticipate challenges and master the course. Be prepared for a significant challenge – this game will test your patience, precision, and endurance!
