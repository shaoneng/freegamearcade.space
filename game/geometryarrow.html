Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Welcome to the intense, rhythmic world of Geometry Arrow! Your mission is to guide a sleek white arrow through a dangerous, obstacle-filled tunnel. The game is all about precision, timing, and reflexes.

The controls couldn't be simpler: just tap or click anywhere on the screen to make your arrow rise. Release the tap or click to let it fall. Your goal is to use this simple mechanic to navigate through the level, avoiding the sharp geometric spikes that line the top and bottom of the tunnel.

Each of the 12 levels is set to its own unique, upbeat soundtrack. Use the music to help you find the rhythm and anticipate the obstacles. Survive until the end of the level to advance. It gets tricky, so don't be afraid to take a break if you get stuck!
