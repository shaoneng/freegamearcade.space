Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

The controls in Geometry Arrow are brilliantly simple: all you need is a single tap or click. When you press the screen or click your mouse, your arrow will ascend. Release, and it will descend. This intuitive mechanic is the key to mastering the game.

Your objective is to guide the white arrow through a series of dark, geometric mazes. You must skillfully time your taps to avoid crashing into the spikes and sharp corners that line the top and bottom of the tunnel. Stay focused and anticipate the path ahead to survive.

The game features 12 distinct levels, each with its own unique layout and upbeat musical track. The rhythm of the music often syncs with the obstacles, so listening to the beat can help you time your moves perfectly. Successfully complete all levels to unlock a hidden bonus stage for an even greater challenge!

This game is designed to test your patience and reflexes. If you find a level particularly difficult, don't be afraid to take a short break. A clear mind is your best tool for navigating these geometric gauntlets.
