Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Get ready for a test of pure reflex in Geometry Arrow! In this sleek, rhythm-based platformer, you control a swift white arrow navigating a dangerous, minimalist world. The goal is simple: survive.

The controls are incredibly easy to learn but difficult to master. A single tap or click causes your arrow to rise, and releasing it will make it fall. Use this simple mechanic to weave through tight corridors and dodge deadly spikes and obstacles that are perfectly synced to the upbeat soundtrack.

Challenge yourself across 12 increasingly difficult levels, plus a hidden bonus stage for those who truly master the rhythm. Remember, this game can be intense, so don't be afraid to take a quick break if you feel the rage building. Can you guide the arrow to the end?
