Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Get ready for a test of pure reflex and rhythm in Geometry Arrow! When you begin, you become the arrow, soaring through a minimalist, high-contrast world. This game is all about precision and timing, challenging you to navigate treacherous geometric landscapes.

The controls are deceptively simple: a single tap or click is all you have. Tap to make your arrow rise, and release to let it fall. Your goal is to skillfully guide it through a series of sharp angles, spikes, and tight passages without crashing. Each level is synchronized with its own unique, upbeat soundtrack, turning every run into a pulse-pounding dance with danger.

With 12 challenging levels and a secret bonus stage to unlock, Geometry Arrow offers a satisfyingly difficult experience.
