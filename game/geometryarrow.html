Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Geometry Arrow! In this game, you don't just control the arrow â€“ you become it. Your objective is to navigate a sleek, dark tunnel, avoiding a maze of geometric obstacles and deadly spikes that appear in sync with the rhythm of the music.

The controls are deceptively simple. Use a single tap or a click of your mouse to change the arrow's vertical direction. When the arrow is on the floor, a tap will send it soaring towards the ceiling. When it's on the ceiling, another tap will send it diving back down. Timing is everything; you must react instantly to avoid crashing.

Survive each of the 12 challenging levels to progress. Each stage features a unique layout and its own upbeat soundtrack. Stay focused, learn the patterns, and keep your reflexes sharp to conquer the geometry and master the game. Don't forget to take a break if it gets too intense!
