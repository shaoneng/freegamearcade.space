Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Geometry Arrow! The controls are deceptively simple, but mastering the game requires sharp reflexes and perfect timing.

Your Goal: Guide the white arrow through a series of challenging geometric tunnels. Avoid crashing into the spikes and other obstacles that block your path.

Controls: Use a single tap or click of your mouse to make the arrow rise. Release to let it fall. You'll need to time your taps precisely to navigate the narrow passages and react to the rhythm of the music.

Each of the 12 levels presents a unique challenge and a new beat to follow. Stay focused, learn the patterns, and try to conquer all the stages, including the hidden bonus level!
