Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Welcome to the fast-paced, rhythm-driven world of Geometry Arrow! Your objective is to guide a sleek white arrow through a series of challenging, obstacle-filled tunnels. The controls are deceptively simple, but mastering them is the key to victory.

Controls: Use a single tap or click to make your arrow ascend. Release the tap or click to let it descend due to gravity. This is your only move, so timing is everything!

Navigate through the maze, carefully avoiding the sharp spikes and geometric barriers that block your path. Each level is synced to its own unique, upbeat soundtrack. Try to feel the rhythm of the music to anticipate jumps and drops. Stay focused, keep your reflexes sharp, and guide your arrow to the end of each stage!
