Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Your goal in Geometry Arrow is to guide a white arrow through a hazardous geometric tunnel without crashing. The game is a true test of reflexes and rhythm, similar to Geometry Dash.

The controls are incredibly simple. Use a single tap or click to make the arrow rise. Releasing the tap or click will cause the arrow to fall due to gravity.

You must time your movements perfectly to navigate through narrow passages and avoid hitting the sharp spikes that line the top and bottom of the tunnel. Each of the 12 levels features a unique layout and is synced to an upbeat soundtrack, making rhythm a key part of your success.

Stay focused, anticipate the path ahead, and try to find the beat of the level to master the course. Be warned, it's a challenging game that will test your patience!
