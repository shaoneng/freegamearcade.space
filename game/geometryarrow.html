Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

The goal in Geometry Arrow is to guide your arrow safely through a series of challenging, rhythm-based levels without crashing. The controls are deceptively simple, making it easy to learn but difficult to master.

Controls: Use a single tap or click your mouse button to make the arrow change its vertical direction. When you tap, the arrow will begin to rise; release to let it fall. Your timing is crucial to navigate the narrow passages and avoid the deadly spikes that line the top and bottom of the tunnel.

Objective: Each level presents a unique track set to its own upbeat music. You must react quickly to the geometric obstacles in your path. Survive from the start to the finish line to complete the level and unlock the next. With 12 main levels and a hidden bonus stage, your precision and reflexes will be put to the ultimate test!
