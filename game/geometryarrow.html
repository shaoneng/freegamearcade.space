Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Your goal in Geometry Arrow is to guide a swift white arrow through a series of challenging, obstacle-filled tunnels. The game automatically moves forward, so your only job is to control its vertical movement.

The controls are deceptively simple: just tap your screen or click your mouse to make the arrow rise. When you release the tap or click, the arrow will begin to fall due to gravity.

You must time your taps perfectly to navigate through narrow gaps and avoid hitting the spikes and geometric shapes that line the top and bottom of the tunnel. One collision and it's game over, forcing you to restart the level.

Stay focused, react quickly to the rhythm of the music, and conquer the geometry to complete all 12 levels and discover the hidden bonus stage!
