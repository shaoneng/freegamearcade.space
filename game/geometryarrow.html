Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Geometry Arrow! The controls are deceptively simple, but mastering them is the key to victory.

Your goal is to guide the white arrow through a series of challenging geometric tunnels. The arrow moves forward automatically. To control its vertical movement, simply click or tap anywhere on the screen. Each tap will cause the arrow to ascend. Releasing will make it descend.

Time your taps carefully to navigate through narrow gaps and avoid colliding with the deadly spikes and obstacles that line the path. Your reflexes and rhythm will be put to the ultimate test as you progress through each unique, music-driven level.
