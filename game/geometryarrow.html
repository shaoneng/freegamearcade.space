Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

In Geometry Arrow, you become the arrow. This minimalist, rhythm-based platformer will challenge your focus and reflexes.

Controls: The game uses a simple one-tap control system. Tap or click anywhere on the screen to make your arrow ascend. Release the tap or click to let it descend. Master this simple mechanic to navigate the environment.

Objective: Your goal is to guide the white arrow safely through a dark, geometric tunnel. You must skillfully avoid hitting any sharp angles, spikes, or other obstacles that line the path. Timing your rises and falls to the beat of the music is key to survival.

Levels: Race through 12 increasingly difficult levels, each with its own unique layout and upbeat soundtrack. For those who conquer the main stages, a hidden bonus level awaits. Remember to take a break if you feel the rage building!
