Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Welcome to the minimalist and challenging world of Geometry Arrow! The goal is simple: guide your arrow through a dangerous geometric tunnel for as long as possible without crashing into any obstacles.

The controls are incredibly straightforward:

  • Tap or click anywhere on the screen to make your arrow ascend.
  • Release the tap or click to make your arrow descend.

Your objective is to skillfully navigate the narrow passages, dodging spikes and sharp angles. Each of the 12 levels presents a new layout and is synchronized to an upbeat soundtrack. Time your taps perfectly with the rhythm to survive the course and reach the end. Be warned, it's a test of reflexes and patience!
