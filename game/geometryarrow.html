Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Geometry Arrow is a rhythm-driven platformer that challenges your reflexes with simple, one-tap controls.

Your goal is to guide a simple white arrow through a dark, challenging tunnel filled with sharp angles and spikes. The arrow moves automatically forward, and your only action is to tap to make it jump or rise. Release to fall.

Timing your taps precisely is crucial to navigate the geometric obstacles and avoid crashing. There are 12 difficult levels to master, each accompanied by its own upbeat music, plus a hidden bonus stage for those who conquer the main game. Focus, rhythm, and quick reactions are key to success in this minimalist, high-speed adventure!
