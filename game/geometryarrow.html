Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Geometry Arrow! Your objective is to guide the white arrow through a dangerous geometric tunnel, avoiding spikes and walls at all costs. The game is all about timing, rhythm, and reflexes.

The controls are incredibly simple: a single tap or click is all you need. When you tap the screen or click your mouse, the arrow will ascend. When you release, the arrow will descend. You must skillfully balance these movements to navigate the narrow passages.

Each of the 12 levels is set to its own unique, upbeat music. Try to feel the rhythm of the music, as it often syncs up with the obstacles, helping you time your moves perfectly. One wrong move and you'll have to start the level over, so stay focused and guide your arrow to the finish line!
