Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Getting started with Geometry Arrow is simple, but mastering it is a true test of skill. Your objective is to guide the white arrow through a series of challenging geometric mazes without crashing into any spikes or walls.

The controls are incredibly intuitive. Use a single tap on your screen or a left-click with your mouse to make the arrow rise. Releasing the tap or click will cause the arrow to fall. You must time your movements perfectly with the rhythm of the music to navigate the narrow passages and avoid obstacles.

Progress through all 12 unique levels, each with its own track and increasing difficulty. Stay focused, keep your cool, and let the beat guide you to victory!
