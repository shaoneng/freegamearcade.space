Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Welcome to the intense, rhythm-based world of Geometry Arrow! Your objective is to guide a fast-moving white arrow through a dark, geometric tunnel. The path is littered with sharp spikes and dangerous obstacles that will shatter your arrow on contact, forcing you to restart.

The controls are elegantly simple. **Click your mouse or tap the screen** to make the arrow fly upwards. When you release, gravity will pull it back down. Your success depends entirely on timing. You must master the rhythm of tapping and releasing to navigate through narrow gaps and avoid crashing into the walls and spikes.

Challenge yourself across 12 difficult levels, each with a unique layout and an upbeat, electronic soundtrack. Stay focused, anticipate the twists and turns, and let the music guide your movements. Can you conquer every level and unlock the hidden bonus stage?
