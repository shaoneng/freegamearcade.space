How to Play Geometry Arrow

In Geometry Arrow, you take control of a fast-moving arrow navigating a dangerous geometric tunnel. Your goal is to survive as long as possible by avoiding the sharp spikes and obstacles that block your path.

The controls are deceptively simple. Use a single tap or a click of your mouse to make the arrow rise. Release the tap or click to let it fall. Your timing is crucial to successfully maneuver through the narrow gaps and past the deadly spikes.

Progress through 12 distinct and increasingly difficult levels, each with its own rhythm and challenges.
