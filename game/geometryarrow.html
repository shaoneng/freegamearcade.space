Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

Welcome to the challenging world of Geometry Arrow! The controls are simple, but the game is anything but easy.

Your goal is to guide the white arrow through a dark tunnel, avoiding all the spikes and geometric obstacles in your path.

Use a single tap or click to make the arrow rise. Releasing the tap or click will make it fall. You must perfectly time your movements to the rhythm of the music to navigate the maze-like levels.

One wrong move and you'll have to start the level over. Progress through all 12 levels and see if you have what it takes to conquer geometry itself!
