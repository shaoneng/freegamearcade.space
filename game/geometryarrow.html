Geometry Arrow

How to Play Geometry Arrow

In Geometry Arrow, you don't just shoot the arrow—you become it! Your mission is to guide a sleek white arrow through a treacherous tunnel filled with sharp geometric obstacles and spikes. The controls are deceptively simple: a single tap or click makes your arrow rise, and releasing it makes it fall. Master this rhythm to navigate the maze-like levels.

The game challenges you across 12 difficult levels, plus a hidden bonus stage, each with its own unique and upbeat soundtrack. The goal is to survive and reach the end of each stage. With its clean, minimalist graphics and instant-play accessibility, it’s the perfect game for a quick, intense challenge. Just remember to take a break if you feel the rage building!

If you enjoy the fast-paced, rhythm-based gameplay of Geometry Arrow, you'll love Space Waves. It offers a similar one-tap challenge but with vibrant, neon visuals and an electronic score.
