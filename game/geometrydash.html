Geometry Dash

How to Play Geometry Dash

Welcome to the intense, rhythm-based world of Geometry Dash! Your objective is simple but incredibly challenging: guide your geometric cube through a gauntlet of spikes, pits, and other deadly obstacles to reach the end of each level.

The controls are straightforward. Use a mouse click or press the spacebar to make your cube jump. In some sections of the game, holding down the click or spacebar will allow you to fly or jump continuously. Timing is everything, so you'll need to develop a feel for quick taps versus longer holds.

Pay close attention to the music! Each level's obstacles are perfectly synchronized to the beat of the soundtrack. Listening to the rhythm can help you anticipate upcoming jumps and hazards. We recommend turning up your volume for the best experience. Prepare for a tough challenge, practice your timing, and see if you can conquer the beat!
