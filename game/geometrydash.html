How to Play Geometry Dash

Welcome to the ultimate test of reflexes and rhythm! In Geometry Dash, your goal is simple: guide your cube through a series of hazardous levels without crashing. The controls are straightforwardâ€”click your mouse, press the spacebar, or tap the screen to make your character jump.
