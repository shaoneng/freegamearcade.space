Geometry Vibes X-Ball

How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

The controls for Geometry Vibes X-Ball are deceptively simple, but mastering them is the key to survival. Your glowing ball moves forward automatically. Your only action is to **tap or click the screen**. Tapping makes the ball move upwards. When you release, the ball's gravity flips, and it will fall towards the opposite surface. Use this single control to navigate through tight corridors, avoid deadly spikes, and climb slopes to reach the end of each level.

The main objective is to guide the X-Ball through various obstacle courses without crashing into any traps. The game features several modes to test your skills: conquer 10 unique levels in Classic mode, see how long you can survive in Endless mode, or take on even tougher levels in Challenge mode.

In Race Mode, you can challenge up to 3 friends on the same device! The controls are split across the keyboard, allowing each player to control their own ball in a frantic race to the finish line. There are no respawns, so the last one standing wins!
