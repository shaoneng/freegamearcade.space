Geometry Vibes X-Ball

How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

The goal in Geometry Vibes X-Ball is to guide your glowing ball through a hazardous, neon-lit obstacle course. Your survival depends on quick reflexes and perfect timing.

The controls are deceptively simple: tap your screen or click your mouse to make the ball move upwards. When you release the tap or click, the ball will flip its direction and begin moving downwards. Use this one-button mechanic to navigate through narrow passages and avoid deadly traps.

Your primary objective is to survive. You must dodge deadly spikes, sharp corners, and giant slopes to reach the end of each level. A single mistake will send you back to the start. The game features several modes to challenge you: Classic mode with 10 levels, a local multiplayer Race mode for up to 4 players, an Endless mode to test your endurance, and an intense Challenge mode for expert players.
