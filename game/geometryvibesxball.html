How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

The controls for Geometry Vibes X-Ball are deceptively simple: itâ€™s all about a single tap or click. Tap your screen or click the mouse to make the ball go upwards. Your goal is to navigate a glowing X-Ball through a series of neon-lit levels filled with deadly spikes and sharp corners.

The game offers several modes to test your skills:

  • Endless Mode: See how far you can travel in an infinitely generating level where the speed constantly increases.
  • Challenge Mode: Take on 5 unique, high-speed levels designed to push your reflexes to the absolute limit.

Master the timing of your taps and releases to avoid obstacles and reach the end. It's a true test of focus, reaction time, and rhythm!
