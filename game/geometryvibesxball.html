How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

Welcome to the slick, neon-drenched world of Geometry Vibes X-Ball! Your mission is to guide a glowing ball through a series of challenging obstacle courses. The controls are deceptively simple: tap and hold your screen or mouse button to make the ball ascend. When you release, gravity flips, and the ball will move in the opposite direction. Master this one-button mechanic to navigate narrow paths, avoid deadly spikes, and conquer giant slopes to reach the end of each level.

Geometry Vibes X-Ball offers four distinct game modes to keep the action fresh. In Classic Mode, you'll tackle 10 handcrafted levels in a single-player challenge. Test your endurance in Endless Mode and see how far you can travel for a high score. For the ultimate test of skill, Extreme Mode offers 5 intensely difficult levels that will push your reflexes to the absolute limit.
