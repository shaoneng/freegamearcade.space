Geometry Vibes X-Ball

How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

The controls for Geometry Vibes X-Ball are deceptively simple: it's all about one button. Your glowing ball moves automatically. Tap or click your mouse to make the ball go upwards. When you release the button, the ball's gravity will flip, causing it to fall in the opposite direction. Master this tap-and-release rhythm to navigate the treacherous, spike-filled courses.

The game features four exciting modes to test your skills:

  • Classic Mode: The main single-player experience. Your goal is to navigate through 10 progressively difficult levels and reach the finish line.
  • Race Mode: Challenge up to 4 friends on the same device! Each player gets a designated control key. The first to the finish line wins, but be carefulâ€”one hit and you're out for the round.
  • Endless Mode: See how far you can travel in an infinitely generating level. The speed increases over time, and your score is measured in meters.
  • Challenge Mode: For expert players, this mode offers 5 unique, high-speed levels with extremely tight paths and dense obstacles to push your reflexes to the limit.
