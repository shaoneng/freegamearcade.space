Geometry Vibes X-Ball

How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

Welcome to the electrifying challenge of Geometry Vibes X-Ball! The controls are deceptively simple: you only need one button. Tap (or click) to make your glowing ball move upwards. When you release the button, the ball's direction will flip. Master this simple mechanic to navigate through treacherous neon levels filled with deadly spikes, sharp corners, and massive slopes. Your timing and rhythm are key to survival.

The game offers four distinct modes for every type of player:

  • Classic Mode: The core single-player experience. Your goal is to survive 10 meticulously designed levels and reach the finish line.
  • Race Mode: Challenge up to 3 friends in a chaotic local multiplayer race on the same device. Avoid the spikes and be the first to finish to claim victoryâ€”there are no respawns!
  • Endless Mode: Test your endurance and see how far you can go. The level is infinite, and the speed gradually increases, pushing your skills to the limit. Your high score is measured in meters.
  • Challenge Mode: For expert players, this mode features 5 unique levels with faster movement, tighter spaces, and an insane level of difficulty. Beat one to unlock the next!
