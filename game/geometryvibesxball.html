Geometry Vibes X-Ball

How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

The controls for Geometry Vibes X-Ball are deceptively simple: it's all about one button. Your glowing X-Ball moves automatically through each level, and your job is to keep it safe.

Tap or click your mouse button to make the ball move upwards or change its direction along a surface. Releasing the button will cause it to flip its direction or fall with gravity. Mastering the timing of your taps is key to navigating the tight corners and deadly spike traps that fill every level.

Your objective is to guide the ball safely from the start to the finish line in one piece. A single mistake means starting over! The game features several modes to test your skills. Classic Mode offers 10 progressively difficult levels. Race Mode lets you compete against up to 3 friends locally on the same device. Endless Mode challenges you to survive for as long as possible, while Challenge Mode presents five ultra-difficult levels for true experts.
