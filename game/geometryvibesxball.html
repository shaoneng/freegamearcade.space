Geometry Vibes X-Ball

How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

Your goal in Geometry Vibes X-Ball is to guide your glowing ball through a series of obstacle-filled levels. The controls are deceptively simple: just tap or click to make the ball move upwards. When you release the tap or click, the ball will flip direction and move downwards. You must master this one-button mechanic to navigate sharp corners, avoid deadly spikes, and survive the course.

The game offers several modes to test your skills. In Classic Mode, you'll tackle 10 challenging levels to reach the finish line. For a test of endurance, try Endless Mode, where you survive as long as possible to set a high score. For the ultimate trial, Challenge Mode features 5 intensely difficult levels for expert players.

You can also bring friends into the action with Race Mode, where up to 4 players can compete locally on the same device. The controls are split for multiplayer: Player 1 uses 'W', Player 2 uses 'Up Arrow', Player 3 uses 'I', and Player 4 uses 'Numpad 8'.
