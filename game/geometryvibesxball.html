Geometry Vibes X-Ball

How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

Welcome to Geometry Vibes X-Ball! The controls are deceptively simple but hard to master. Your goal is to guide the glowing X-Ball through a neon course filled with deadly spikes and tricky slopes. All you need is one button or a single tap. Tap and hold to make the ball move upwards. Release the tap to make the ball flip direction and move downwards. Master this simple mechanic to navigate the complex levels.

The game offers four distinct modes to test your skills. In Classic Mode, you'll tackle 10 single-player levels, trying to reach the finish line. For competitive fun, grab your friends for Race Mode, where up to 4 players can compete on the same device. The last one standing wins! Test your endurance in Endless Mode and see how far you can travel before crashing, with your score measured in meters. Finally, for the ultimate test, Challenge Mode offers five brutally difficult levels with faster speeds and tighter spaces.

No matter which mode you choose, focus, quick reactions, and a good sense of rhythm are key to survival. Good luck!
