Geometry Vibes X-Ball

How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

Geometry Vibes X-Ball is a game of precision and timing with a deceptively simple control scheme. Your goal is to guide the glowing X-Ball through a hazardous neon course. The controls are entirely based on a single action: tap or click. When you press and hold, your ball moves upwards. When you release, the ball's direction flips, and it begins moving downwards. Master this simple mechanic to navigate tight corners, dodge deadly spikes, and climb giant slopes.

The game features four distinct modes, each offering a unique challenge:

Classic Mode: This is the standard single-player experience. Progress through 10 carefully designed levels, each more challenging than the last. Your objective is simply to reach the finish line without crashing into any obstacles.

Race Mode: Compete against up to 4 friends locally on the same device! Each player gets their own control key. It's a last-man-standing race to the finish line—if you hit a spike, you're out. The first to the end wins, making it a perfect chaotic party game.

Endless Mode: Test your endurance and see how far you can go. In this mode, the level is procedurally generated and never ends. Your score is measured in meters, and the speed gradually increases the longer you survive. Aim for a new high score and practice your muscle memory.

Challenge Mode: For expert players, this mode offers 5 unique, high-intensity levels with faster movement and even more obstacles. These levels are designed to push your skills to the absolute limit. You must beat each level to unlock the next.
