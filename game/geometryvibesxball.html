Geometry Vibes X-Ball

How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

Welcome to the slick, futuristic world of Geometry Vibes: X-Ball! This platformer challenges your focus and reaction time with simple one-button controls. Guide your glowing X-Ball through treacherous neon levels filled with spikes and sharp turns. The controls are easy to learn but hard to master: tap to move upwards, and release to flip direction. That's all you need to survive!

The game features four exciting modes to test your skills:

Classic Mode

The core single-player experience. Navigate through 10 distinct levels, each with its own deadly layout. Your goal is simple: reach the finish line without crashing into the spikes.

Race Mode (Up to 4 Players)

Challenge your friends in local multiplayer! Up to 4 players can compete on the same device. The last one surviving wins the race. There are no respawns, so every move counts. Customize your spinner and trail to stand out in the chaos!

Endless Mode

Test your endurance in this survival mode. The level goes on forever, and the speed gradually increases. Your score is measured in meters. See how far you can go and set a new high score!

Challenge Mode

For the true masters. This mode features 5 unique, high-intensity levels with faster movement and tighter spaces. You must beat each level to unlock the next, facing extreme and even "insane" difficulties.

If you enjoy the fast-paced, rhythm-based action of Geometry Vibes: X-Ball, you should also check out Geometry Dash or Extreme Run 3D for more high-energy, reflex-testing fun!
