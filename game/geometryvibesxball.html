Geometry Vibes X-Ball

How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

Welcome to the slick, neon-drenched world of Geometry Vibes X-Ball! The controls are deceptively simple: you only need one button. Tap or click to make your glowing X-Ball jump upwards. When you release the button, the ball will flip its direction and move downwards. Mastering this tap-and-release rhythm is the key to navigating the deadly levels.

The game features several exciting modes to test your skills:

Classic Mode: This is the core single-player experience. Your goal is to guide the ball through 10 challenging levels filled with sharp spikes, tight corners, and tricky slopes. Reach the finish line to advance.

Race Mode: Challenge up to 3 friends in a chaotic local multiplayer race! Compete on the same keyboard to see who can survive the longest and reach the end first. There are no respawns, so one mistake means you're out!

Endless Mode: Test your endurance and see how far you can get. In this mode, there is no finish line. The level goes on forever, and the speed gradually increases, pushing your reaction time to the limit. Your score is measured in meters traveled.

Challenge Mode: For expert players, this mode offers 5 unique, high-intensity levels with faster movement and even less room for error. You must beat each level to unlock the next, facing some of the toughest obstacles the game has to offer.
