Geometry Vibes X-Ball

How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

Welcome to the fast-paced, rhythmic world of Geometry Vibes X-Ball! The controls are deceptively simple, but mastering them is the key to survival. Your goal is to guide your glowing X-Ball through neon levels packed with spikes and other deadly obstacles.

Main Control: The entire game is controlled with a single action. Tap (or click and hold) your screen or button to make the ball move upwards. When you release, the ball's gravity will flip, causing it to fall in the opposite direction. Use this mechanic to navigate tight corners, dodge spikes, and soar over gaps.

Game Modes & Controls:

  • Classic: Progress through 10 challenging levels, aiming to reach the finish line.
  • Race Mode (Local Multiplayer): Compete with up to 4 friends on the same device! The last one standing wins. The controls are: Player 1 (W key), Player 2 (Up Arrow), Player 3 (I key), and Player 4 (Mouse Click).
  • Endless: See how far you can travel in an infinitely generating level. Your score is measured in meters.
  • Chaos: Take on 5 extremely difficult levels designed to push your skills to the absolute limit.

Focus on your timing, anticipate the path ahead, and tap to the rhythm to conquer the vibrant, challenging courses of Geometry Vibes X-Ball!
