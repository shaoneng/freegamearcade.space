Geometry Vibes X-Ball

How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

The controls in Geometry Vibes X-Ball are deceptively simple: it's all about one button. Your glowing X-Ball moves forward automatically through a series of neon-lit obstacle courses.

Your only action is to **tap or click** the screen (or use the designated key). Tapping makes the ball move upwards. When you **release the button**, gravity takes over, and the ball will flip its direction to move along the opposite surface (ceiling or floor). Mastering the timing of your taps and releases is crucial to navigate through narrow passages, avoid deadly spikes, and climb giant slopes.

The main goal is to guide the ball from the start to the finish line in one piece. A single mistake will send you back to the beginning of the level, so precision and focus are key. In Race Mode, up to 4 players can compete on the same device using different keys to control their own ball. The last one standing wins!
