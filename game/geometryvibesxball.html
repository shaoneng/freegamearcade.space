Geometry Vibes X-Ball

How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

The core mechanic of Geometry Vibes X-Ball is incredibly simple yet challenging to master. You control a glowing ball that moves forward automatically. Your only action is to tap or click.

Tapping the screen or clicking your mouse causes the ball to ascend. Releasing the tap allows the ball to descend due to gravity. Your goal is to navigate the ball through intricate, neon-lit obstacle courses. You must avoid hitting any of the deadly spikes, walls, or other hazards. A single touch to a trap means starting over!

The game offers several modes to test your skills. In Classic Mode, progress through 10 handcrafted levels. In Race Mode, compete against up to three friends on the same device to see who can survive the longest. Test your endurance in Endless Mode for a high score, or push your limits with the ultra-difficult levels in Challenge Mode.
