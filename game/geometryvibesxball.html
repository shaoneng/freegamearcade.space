Geometry Vibes X-Ball

How to Play Geometry Vibes X-Ball

Geometry Vibes: X-Ball is a thrilling platformer where you control a glowing ball through an obstacle course filled with spiky traps using just one button. The goal is to keep the X-Ball alive as it navigates neon levels full of sharp corners, deadly spikes, and steep slopes. All you do is tap to go upwards and release to flip direction, but this simple mechanic is enough to get your heart racing.

The game offers four distinct modes, each providing a unique challenge:

Classic Mode

This is the core single-player experience. Navigate through 10 precisely designed levels, each packed with deadly spikes and narrow paths, to reach the finish line in one piece.

Race Mode

Challenge up to 4 friends in a local multiplayer race on the same device. Each player chooses a custom spinner and races across the same spike-filled levels. There are no respawns—if you hit a spike, you're out. The first to the finish line wins!

Endless Mode

This mode is all about endurance. There's no finish line; your goal is to survive for as long as possible. Your score is measured in meters, and the speed gradually increases, making it a great way to practice your muscle memory and reaction time.

Challenge Mode

For the ultimate test, this mode features 5 unique levels with faster movement, tighter spaces, and denser obstacles. Levels are locked, so you must beat the current one to unlock the next. From "extreme" to "insane" difficulty, this mode will push even the most skilled gamers to their limits.

If you enjoy the fast-paced, reflex-based gameplay of Geometry Vibes: X-Ball, you might also like Geometry Dash or Extreme Run 3D for more high-energy, neon-drenched action.
