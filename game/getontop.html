Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Get On Top is a hilarious and competitive 2-player physics game where the objective is simple: make your opponent's head touch the ground. The first player to score the set number of points wins the match!

This isn't your typical fighting game. The unique physics engine means your stickman characters are constantly flailing and tumbling. Controlling your character is a challenge in itself, leading to unpredictable and funny moments.

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use the W, A, D keys to move your character.
  • Player 2: Use the Up, Left, and Right Arrow Keys to move your character.

Strategy:

While attacking is important, defense is crucial. Always try to protect your head and keep it away from the floor. Use your character's weight and momentum to your advantage. A well-timed push or a sudden shift in balance can send your opponent toppling over. Embrace the chaos, master the movement, and get on top!
