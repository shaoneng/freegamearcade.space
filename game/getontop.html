Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Welcome to the virtual wrestling ring! Get On Top is a fun and chaotic 2-player physics game where your main objective is to dominate your opponent. The rules are simple: to win a round, you must maneuver your character to get on top of the other player and make their head touch the ground.

This is a two-player game, designed for you and a friend to play on the same device. Each player controls their stickman using simple directional keys to move, jump, and push. The physics are intentionally wacky, so you'll be flipping and tumbling together in a human ball of limbs!

While aggression is key, defense is just as important. Be careful not to flip over and hit your own head on the ground while attacking. You need to skillfully shift your body's mass to maintain balance and use your opponent's momentum against them. Keep your head safe and slam theirs to the floor to claim victory!
