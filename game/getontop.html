Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Welcome to Get On Top, the ultimate 2-player wrestling challenge where physics is your best friend and worst enemy! The objective is simple: push, pull, and flip your opponent to make their head touch the ground. The first player to score wins the round.

Each player controls a stickman character. Use your movement keys to shift your body weight and maneuver around your opponent. Be careful! The game's unpredictable physics means an aggressive attack can easily send you tumbling and cause you to hit your own head on the ground.

Success in Get On Top is all about balance and strategy. Focus on defending your own head while looking for the perfect opportunity to topple your rival. Use your body as a lever, shift your mass strategically, and prove you have what it takes to stay on top!
