Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Get On Top is a deceptively simple yet wildly entertaining 2-player fighting game. The objective is straightforward: get on top of your opponent and make their head touch the ground to score a point. Each player controls their stickman, who are locked together in a wobbly embrace.

Use your movement keys to shift your character's weight left and right. This is your primary way to unbalance your opponent and push them over. The game features wacky physics, so every movement can have unpredictable and hilarious consequences. A poorly timed push might cause you to flip over and lose the round yourself!

Because of the unpredictable physics, defense is just as important as offense. Focus on keeping your own head away from the floor while looking for an opportunity to topple your rival. Use your body mass strategically to gain leverage and dominate the duel. The first player to reach the score limit wins the match!
