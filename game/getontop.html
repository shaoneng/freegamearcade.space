Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Get On Top is a chaotic and fun 2-player wrestling game where the objective is simple: force your opponent's head to touch the ground. Settle your sibling rivalries or challenge a friend in this hilarious physics-based duel!

The game features two stickman characters locked in a perpetual wrestle. Your goal is to maneuver your character to get on top of the other player, causing them to lose their balance and hit their head on the floor. Each time you succeed, you score a point.

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use the Arrow Keys to move your character.
  • Player 2: Use the W, A, and D keys to move your character.

Due to the game's quirky physics, aggressive attacks can often backfire and cause you to flip over and hit your own head. Therefore, a good defense is just as important as a strong offense. Focus on keeping your balance, protecting your head, and using your body's momentum to your advantage to take your opponent down!
