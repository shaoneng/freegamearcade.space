Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Welcome to the hilarious wrestling ring of Get On Top! This is a chaotic 2-player game where the objective is simple but challenging: get on top of your opponent and make their head touch the ground to score a point. The first player to reach the winning score is crowned the champion.

The controls are straightforward. One player uses the W, A, and D keys to control their stickman, while the second player uses the Up, Left, and Right Arrow keys. Use these keys to move, jump, and shift your weight to gain an advantage.

Be warned: the game's physics are wild and unpredictable! An aggressive attack might cause you to flip over and knock your own head on the ground. Success requires a mix of offense and defense. Master your movement, keep your balance, and strategically use your body to pin your opponent and claim victory!
