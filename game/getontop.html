Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Get On Top is an engaging 2-player stickman wrestling game that pits you against a friend in a hilarious physics-based duel. The primary objective is to make your opponent's head touch the ground.

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use the W, A, S, D keys to move your stickman.
  • Player 2: Use the Arrow keys (Up, Left, Down, Right) to control your stickman.

Gameplay Tips:

  1. Get On Top: Your main goal is to position your character on top of your opponent, pressing them down.
  2. Mind Your Head: Be careful! If your own head touches the ground, you lose the round. Defense is just as crucial as offense.
  3. Strategic Movement: The game features interesting physics. Small, precise movements can be more effective than wild flailing. Use your body weight and momentum to your advantage.
  4. Balance: Maintain your balance while trying to unbalance your opponent. A well-timed flip or push can lead to victory!
