Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Welcome to Get On Top, a hilarious and chaotic 2-player stickman wrestling game! The goal is simple but challenging: force your opponent's head to touch the ground to score a point. You and your friend control two stickmen who are locked together, creating a whirlwind of flailing limbs and unpredictable movements.

This is a duel of physics and timing. Use your movement keys to shift your body weight and leverage your position. An aggressive attack can easily backfire, causing you to flip and hit your own head on the ground. Strategy is key, so think before you flail!

Defense is just as important as offense. Focus on keeping your head away from the floor at all times. Use your body mass as an advantage to push, pull, and ultimately topple your opponent. Settle old scores and prove who is the true champion in this virtual wrestling match!
