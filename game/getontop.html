Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Get On Top is a hilarious and competitive 2-player physics game. The goal is simple: wrestle your opponent and make their head touch the ground to score a point. You and your friend control two stickmen who are locked together, creating chaotic and unpredictable movements.

Use your movement keys to pull, push, and jump. The key to victory lies in mastering the game's unique physics. Use your character's body weight as leverage to topple your rival. Be careful, though! An aggressive attack can easily backfire, causing you to flip over and lose the round.

Defense is just as important as offense, so always focus on keeping your own head off the ground. Challenge a friend on the same computer and see who can master the art of getting on top!
