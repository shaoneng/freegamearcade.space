Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Get On Top is a hilarious and chaotic two-player physics game. The objective is simple: be the one to make your opponent's head touch the ground to score a point. You and your friend control two stickmen who are locked together in a wrestling match.

Use your movement keys to shift your body weight and maneuver your character. The controls are straightforward, but the game's wacky physics engine means every match is unpredictable. You might flip over and hit your own head while trying to attack!

A good defense is key. Focus on keeping your own head away from the floor while looking for an opportunity to push, pull, or flip your opponent into a losing position. The first player to reach the score limit wins the duel!
