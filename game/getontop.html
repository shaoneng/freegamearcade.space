Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Get ready for a hilarious and chaotic battle in Get On Top! This is a two-player stickman wrestling game where you and a friend go head-to-head in a test of physics and balance. The objective is straightforward but challenging: you must maneuver your character to get on top of your opponent and force their head to touch the ground to score a point.

The game features unique physics that make every match unpredictable. As you and your opponent are linked together, your movements affect both of you. You'll flip, tumble, and grapple in a human ball of flailing limbs. Simple controls allow you to move your character left and right, but mastering the momentum is key. Be careful—an aggressive attack can easily backfire, causing you to flip over and hit your own head on the ground!

Success in Get On Top requires a mix of offense and defense. Strategically shift your body mass to unbalance your rival while always protecting your own head. Keep your neck and head away from the floor at all costs. Use the crazy physics to your advantage, outsmart your opponent, and take them down to claim victory!
