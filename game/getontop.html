Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Welcome to the virtual wrestling ring of Get On Top! This is a chaotic and fun 2-player stickman game where you'll go head-to-head with a friend in a duel of physics and balance.

The goal is straightforward: maneuver your character to get on top of your opponent and force their head to touch the ground. The first player to make their rival's head hit the floor scores a point. It's a hilarious battle of leverage and timing!

Be warned, the game's physics are tricky! An aggressive attack can easily backfire, causing you to flip and hit your own head. Defense is just as important as offense. Use the movement keys to shift your body weight, maintain your balance, and protect your head from hitting the ground. Master the wobble and take down your opponent!
