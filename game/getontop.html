Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Welcome to the virtual wrestling ring! Get On Top is a hilarious and challenging 2-player physics game where you battle a friend for dominance. The objective is simple: maneuver your stickman to get on top of your opponent and make their head hit the ground to score a point.

You and your opponent control two stickmen who are locked together. Use your character's controls to jump, push, and pull, trying to unbalance your rival. The game's unique physics engine means every action has a reaction, often leading to unpredictable and funny flips and tumbles across the screen.

Strategy is key. Rushing in blindly can easily lead to your own defeat. You must balance offense with defense, carefully positioning your character to protect your head while looking for an opening to take down your opponent. Use your body mass as an advantage, shifting your weight to gain leverage and push your rival to the ground. The first to reach the score limit wins the ultimate bragging rights!
