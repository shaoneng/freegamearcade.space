Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Get On Top is a hilarious and competitive 2-player stickman wrestling game with a simple objective: make your opponent's head touch the ground to score a point.

The game features quirky physics, meaning your characters will flip, flop, and tumble in unpredictable ways. You and your opponent are linked together, so every action you take will affect both of you. You must use this to your advantage!

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use the Arrow Keys to move, jump, and push.
  • Player 2: Use the WASD Keys to move, jump, and push.

Strategy is key. It's not just about attacking; you must also defend by keeping your own head away from the floor. Master shifting your body weight to overpower your rival and claim victory!
