Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Relive those classic sibling wrestling matches, but this time in the digital world! Get On Top is a hilarious and chaotic 2-player stickman fighting game where the objective is simple: get on top of your opponent and make their head touch the ground to score a point.

This game is all about mastering the unpredictable physics. As you and your friend flip and flail around like a human ball, you'll need to be strategic. While attacking is fun, one wrong move could cause you to flip over and knock your own head on the floor. Defense is just as crucial as offense!

Use the controls to move left and right, skillfully adjusting your position to protect your head and neck. Use your body's momentum as an advantage to shift your weight and pin your opponent down. Whether you're playing on a computer or a mobile device, the challenge remains the same: take down your rival and prove who's the true king of the wrestle!
