How to Play Get On Top

Welcome to Get On Top, the ultimate 2-player physics-based wrestling challenge! The objective is simple but chaotic: your goal is to make your opponent's head touch the ground. The first player to score the required number of points wins the match. It's a hilarious test of balance, momentum, and timing.

Each player controls their character with a simple set of keys. Use your assigned movement keys (typically arrow keys for one player and WASD for the other) to move your stickman left and right, and to jump. There's no dedicated attack buttonâ€”all your movement comes from shifting your character's weight and using the jump action to gain leverage.

Mastering Get On Top is all about using the quirky physics to your advantage. Don't just rush in! Use your body to push, pull, and unbalance your opponent. A well-timed shift can send your rival tumbling. Remember, defense is just as important as offense. Keep your own head away from the floor at all costs. Be strategic, be patient, and wait for the perfect moment to strike and get on top!
