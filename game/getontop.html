Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Welcome to the ultimate virtual wrestling match! Get On Top is a hilarious and competitive 2-player game where your only goal is to dominate your opponent. The objective is simple: maneuver your character to get on top of the other player and make their head touch the ground to score a point.

This game is all about physics and momentum. You and your friend control your characters' movements, but the floppy, unpredictable nature of your bodies makes every move a gamble. Push, pull, and flip to gain the upper hand, but be careful! An aggressive attack can easily backfire, causing you to flip over and knock yourself out.

The key to victory is a mix of offense and defense. Use your body weight strategically to pin your opponent down while always protecting your own head. The first player to reach the score limit wins the match and earns ultimate bragging rights!
