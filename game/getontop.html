Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Welcome to Get On Top, the ultimate 2-player physics-based wrestling game! The goal is simple but challenging: maneuver your character to get on top of your opponent and force their head to touch the ground to score a point. It's a hilarious duel of balance, strategy, and chaotic physics.

This game is designed for two players on the same device. Each player controls their stickman, using simple directional keys to move left and right. These movements will cause your characters to push, pull, and tumble in unpredictable ways. Use your body's momentum to gain an advantage and pin your rival down.

Be careful! While being aggressive is key, defense is just as important. The game's wacky physics mean that a poorly timed attack could cause you to flip over and hit your own head on the ground. Keep an eye on your character's balance and try to always keep your head safe. The first player to reach the score limit wins the match!
