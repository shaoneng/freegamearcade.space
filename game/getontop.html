Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Welcome to the wacky wrestling arena of Get On Top! This is a hilarious physics-based game perfect for challenging a friend on the same computer.

The objective is simple: you must force your opponent's head to touch the ground to score a point. The first player to reach the score limit wins the match! You control your stickman using a few simple keys to move, jump, and push.

The trick is in the game's unique physics. Your characters are linked and will flip and tumble in unpredictable ways. A strong offense is great, but a smart defense is crucial. Watch your own character's position carefully, as it's very easy to flip over and make your own head hit the floor. Use your body weight as leverage, time your moves perfectly, and push your rival down to get on top and claim victory!
