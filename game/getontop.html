Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Get On Top is a hilarious and competitive 2-player stickman game where the goal is straightforward: get on top of your opponent and force their head to touch the ground. Each time you succeed, you score a point. It's a virtual wrestling match where strategy and chaotic physics collide!

The controls are simple, but mastering them is the key. Use the arrow keys (for Player 1) and WASD keys (for Player 2) to move your character left, right, and jump. However, this isn't just about attacking. The game's unique physics mean you can easily flip over and knock yourself out. A strong defense is crucial! Focus on keeping your own head away from the floor while using your body's momentum to pin your rival.

The first player to reach the score limit wins the match. Challenge a friend on the same computer and see who has the better balance and wrestling tactics. Use your body as a weapon and a shield to dominate the duel and claim victory!
