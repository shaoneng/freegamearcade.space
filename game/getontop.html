Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Welcome to Get On Top, a hilarious and chaotic 2-player physics-based wrestling game! The goal is simple: maneuver your stickman to get on top of your opponent and make their head touch the ground to score a point. The first player to reach the score limit wins the match.

This game is all about controlling your momentum. The two stickmen are linked together, so every move you make affects your opponent and vice-versa. Use your movement keys to push, pull, and flip your character. Be careful! The floppy physics mean that an aggressive attack can easily backfire, causing you to flip over and knock yourself out. Defense is just as important as offense.

To succeed, you must master balance and timing. Learn to use your body's weight to your advantage, shifting it strategically to pin your rival down. Grab a friend, share a keyboard, and find out who can truly 'get on top' in this wacky wrestling duel!
