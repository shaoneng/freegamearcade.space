Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Welcome to Get On Top, a hilarious and chaotic 2-player physics-based wrestling game! The objective is simple: get on top of your opponent and force their head to touch the ground to score a point.

This game is designed for two players on the same device. Each player controls their stickman, trying to outmaneuver the other. The controls are straightforward, involving moving your character to shift their weight and gain leverage over your rival.

Due to the wacky physics, offense can be risky. An aggressive push might cause you to flip over and hit your own head on the ground, scoring a point for your opponent! Therefore, defense is just as important. Focus on keeping your balance, protecting your head, and using your body's momentum strategically to pin your opponent. The first player to reach the score limit wins the duel!
