Get On Top

How to Play Get On Top

Welcome to Get On Top, the ultimate 2-player stickman wrestling challenge where physics is your greatest ally and your funniest enemy! This game brings the classic sibling rivalry to your screen in a less painful, but equally competitive way. Grab a friend and prepare for a duel of balance and momentum.

Your objective is simple: get on top of your opponent and force their head to touch the ground. The first player to score the required number of points wins the match. Each round is a frantic, hilarious scramble as you and your friend become a tangled, flipping ball of stickman limbs.

Control your character's movement to shift your weight, push your opponent, and protect your own head. Use your body mass strategically to gain an advantage, but be careful! The game's quirky physics means an aggressive attack can easily backfire, causing you to flip and knock yourself out. Defense is just as crucial as offense. Keep your head off the floor, outmaneuver your rival, and claim victory!
