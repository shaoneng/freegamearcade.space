Gold Miner

How to Play Gold Miner

Welcome to the timeless classic, Gold Miner! Your goal in each level is simple: collect enough gold and treasures to meet the target amount of money before the timer runs out. Use your trusty claw to grab valuable items buried underground.

The claw swings back and forth on its own. Your job is to time your drop perfectly. Press the down arrow key to release the claw and grab whatever is in its path. Larger items like massive gold nuggets and diamonds are worth more but are heavier and take longer to reel up. Be strategic and grab smaller, quicker items if you're short on time!

Be careful to avoid grabbing worthless rocks, as they give you very little money and waste a lot of time. If you accidentally hook a rock, you can press the up arrow key to use a stick of dynamite to destroy it and release the claw. Between levels, you can visit the shop to spend your earnings on helpful items like more dynamite, strength potions, or lucky clovers to improve your chances in the next round.
