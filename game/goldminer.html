Gold Miner

How to Play Gold Miner

Welcome to Gold Miner! Your goal in this classic arcade game is to collect enough gold and treasures to meet the level's money target before the timer runs out. Success will take you to the next, more challenging level, while failure ends your run.

The claw at the top of the screen swings back and forth automatically. Press the down arrow key or click the screen to drop the claw and grab whatever is in its path. Your timing is crucial! Aim for valuable items like large gold nuggets and diamonds. Be aware that heavier items, like massive gold chunks and worthless rocks, take much longer to reel in, consuming valuable time.

If you accidentally grab a worthless rock, you can press the up arrow key to use a stick of dynamite to destroy it and free up your claw. Use your dynamite wisely, as you have a limited supply.

Between levels, you'll visit a shop where you can spend your earnings on useful items. Purchase upgrades like stronger cables to pull items faster, dynamite for clearing obstacles, or good luck charms to increase your chances of finding valuable items in the mystery bags. Spend smart to make your future mining endeavors more profitable!
