Gold Miner

How to Play Gold Miner

Welcome to the classic quest for riches! In Gold Miner, your goal is to collect a target amount of money in each level before the timer runs out. Your primary tool is a swinging claw that you control. Press the down arrow key or click the screen to drop the claw and grab whatever is in its path.

Timing is everything! Aim carefully to snatch valuable items like large gold nuggets and sparkling diamonds. Smaller gold pieces are quicker to reel in but less valuable. Be wary of grabbing heavy, worthless rocks, as they take a long time to pull up and give you very little money, wasting precious seconds on the clock.

If you accidentally grab a rock, you can use dynamite to blow it up by pressing the up arrow key. Between levels, you'll have the chance to visit a shop where you can spend your hard-earned cash on useful power-ups like dynamite, strength potions, or lucky clovers to help you in the next stage. Plan your purchases wisely to maximize your earnings!
