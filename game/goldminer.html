Gold Miner

How to Play Gold Miner

Your goal is to become the richest miner around! In each level, your claw swings back and forth. Click the screen or press the down arrow key to drop it and grab valuable items from the ground.

Your primary targets are gold nuggets and shiny diamonds. Larger pieces of gold are worth more but take more time to reel in. Be strategic to meet the level's cash goal before the timer runs out. Watch out for worthless rocks that waste precious time!

If you accidentally grab a heavy rock, you can use dynamite by pressing the up arrow key to destroy it and free up your claw. Between levels, visit the shop to spend your hard-earned cash on helpful power-ups like dynamite, strength potions, and good luck charms to make the next stage easier.

Gold Miner is a classic game of timing and strategy. Plan your grabs carefully, manage your resources, and aim for the biggest treasures to beat each level and rack up a high score. Enjoy this free browser game and see how much gold you can collect!
