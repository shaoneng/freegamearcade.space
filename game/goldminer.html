Gold Miner

How to Play Gold Miner

Welcome to the classic treasure-hunting adventure of Gold Miner! Your goal is to collect enough gold and other valuable items to meet the level's monetary target before the timer runs out. Your trusty claw swings back and forth automatically at the top of the screen.

Press the down arrow key or click the screen to drop the claw and grab whatever is in its path. Timing is everything! Aim for valuable gold nuggets and sparkling diamonds. Be aware that larger items, like massive gold chunks and worthless rocks, are heavier and will take much longer to reel in, wasting precious time.

If you accidentally snag a useless rock, you can press the up arrow key to use a stick of dynamite and destroy it, freeing up your claw. Remember that dynamite is limited! Between levels, you can visit the shop to spend your hard-earned cash on useful items like more dynamite, strength potions, or lucky clovers to help you in the next stage.
