Gold Miner

How to Play Gold Miner

Welcome to the gold rush! In Gold Miner, your goal is simple: collect enough gold and treasures to meet the money goal for each level before the timer runs out. It's a classic test of timing, precision, and strategy that's easy to learn but hard to master.

Your claw swings back and forth automatically at the top of the screen. Press the down arrow key or click your mouse to drop the claw and grab whatever is in its path. Be mindful that heavier items, like large gold nuggets and pesky rocks, will take much longer to reel in, using up valuable time.

Focus on grabbing the most valuable items first. Gold nuggets and diamonds are your best friends, while mystery bags can contain pleasant surprises. Avoid grabbing worthless rocks, as they give you very little money for the time they take to pull up. If you accidentally hook a rock, you can use dynamite by pressing the up arrow key to destroy it and free your claw.

Between levels, you'll get a chance to visit the shop. Here, you can spend the money you've earned on useful power-ups like dynamite, strength potions to reel in items faster, or lucky clovers to get better items from mystery bags. Spend wisely to prepare for the challenges of the next level!
