Gold Miner

How to Play Gold Miner

Your goal in each level is to collect enough gold and valuables to meet the target amount of money before the timer runs out. Your claw swings back and forth automatically at the top of the screen.

Press the down arrow key to drop your claw and grab an item. Timing is everything! Aim for valuable items like large gold nuggets and shiny diamonds. Be aware that heavier items, like big gold chunks or boulders, will take longer to reel in.

Avoid grabbing worthless rocks as they provide very little money and waste a lot of time. If you accidentally hook a rock, you can press the up arrow key to use dynamite and destroy it, allowing you to quickly move on. Remember that dynamite is limited.

After successfully completing a level, you can visit the in-game shop. Here, you can spend the money you've earned on useful power-ups and items like more dynamite, strength potions, or lucky clovers to help you in the next stages.
