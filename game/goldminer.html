Gold Miner

How to Play Gold Miner

Your objective in Gold Miner is simple yet addictive: collect enough gold and treasures to meet the level's money goal before the timer runs out. Your claw swings back and forth automatically at the top of the screen. Press the down arrow key or click the screen to drop it and grab whatever is in its path.

Timing is everything! Aim for valuable items like large gold nuggets and sparkling diamonds. Be wary of grabbing heavy, low-value rocks, as they take a long time to reel in and waste precious seconds. Smaller gold nuggets are quicker to pull up but less valuable, so you'll need to grab many of them.

If you accidentally hook a worthless rock, you can press the up arrow key to use a stick of dynamite to destroy it and release your claw. Use your dynamite wisely, as you have a limited supply. Between levels, you can visit the in-game shop to spend your hard-earned cash on helpful items like more dynamite, strength potions to reel items up faster, and good luck charms.
