Gold Miner

How to Play Gold Miner

Your objective in Gold Miner is simple: collect enough gold and treasures to meet the level's money goal before the clock runs out. The claw at the top of the screen swings back and forth automatically. Press the down arrow key or click the screen to drop the claw and grab whatever is in its path.

Timing is everything! Aim for valuable gold nuggets and sparkling diamonds. Be aware that larger items, while often more valuable, take significantly more time and effort to reel in. Avoid grabbing heavy, low-value rocks, as they will waste your time and prevent you from meeting your goal.

If you accidentally hook a worthless rock, you can press the up arrow key to use a stick of dynamite to destroy it and release your claw. Use your dynamite wisely, as it is a limited resource. Between levels, you can visit the shop to spend your earnings on helpful items like more dynamite, strength potions to reel items in faster, or lucky clovers for better treasures.
