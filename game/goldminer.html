Gold Miner

How to Play Gold Miner

Welcome to the gold rush! In Gold Miner, your goal is to collect a specific amount of money within the time limit for each level. Your trusty claw swings back and forth automatically at the top of the screen. Simply click your mouse or press the down arrow key to drop the claw and grab whatever is in its path.

Timing is everything! Aim carefully for gold nuggets, valuable diamonds, and mysterious money bags. Be cautious, as larger items like massive gold chunks are worth more but take significantly longer to reel in. Avoid grabbing worthless rocks, as they give you very little money and waste precious time.

If you accidentally snag a rock, you can use dynamite to blow it up and free your claw. Press the up arrow key to use dynamite, but be mindful that you have a limited supply. Between levels, you'll get a chance to visit the shop where you can spend your hard-earned cash on useful items like more dynamite, strength potions to pull items faster, or lucky clovers to increase your chances of finding valuable items in the money bags.
