Gold Miner

How to Play Gold Miner

Welcome to Gold Miner, a timeless classic where your goal is to get rich by mining for gold! In each level, you'll be presented with a money target that you must reach before the timer runs out to advance.

Your claw will swing back and forth automatically. Press the down arrow key or click the screen to drop it. Time your drop carefully to grab gold nuggets, valuable diamonds, and mystery bags. Be strategicâ€”larger items like massive gold chunks are worth more but take much longer to reel in.

Avoid grabbing worthless rocks, as they provide very little money and waste a lot of your precious time. If you accidentally snag a rock, you can use dynamite by pressing the up arrow key to destroy it and free up your claw. Remember that dynamite is a limited resource!

Between levels, you can visit the shop to spend your earnings on useful power-ups. Purchase items like dynamite, strength potions to reel in objects faster, or lucky clovers to increase your chances of finding valuable items in the mystery bags.
