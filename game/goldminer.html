Gold Miner

How to Play Gold Miner

Your objective in Gold Miner is to collect as much treasure as possible to meet the level's money goal before the timer runs out. Your claw swings back and forth automatically at the top of the screen. Press the down arrow key or click the screen to drop the claw and grab whatever is in its path.

Each item you collect has a different value. Large gold nuggets are worth more but are heavier and take longer to reel in. Diamonds and mystery bags are also highly valuable. Be careful to avoid grabbing worthless rocks, which take a long time to pull up and give you very little money, wasting precious time.

If you accidentally grab a worthless rock, you can use dynamite to destroy it and release your claw. Press the up arrow key to use dynamite, but be aware that you have a limited supply.

Between levels, you can visit the shop to spend your hard-earned cash on useful items. Purchase dynamite, strength potions to reel in items faster, or lucky clovers to increase your chances of getting valuable items from mystery bags. Strategic shopping can be the key to passing more difficult levels!
