Gold Miner

How to Play Gold Miner

In Gold Miner, your goal is to collect enough gold and treasures to meet the money target for each level. A mechanical claw swings back and forth at the top of the screen. Simply click or press the down arrow key to drop the claw when it's aimed at a valuable item like a gold nugget, a mystery bag, or a sparkling diamond.

Timing is everything! Once the claw grabs an object, it will automatically start reeling it in. Be aware that larger items, like massive gold chunks, are worth more but are much heavier and take longer to pull up. Small rocks are worthless and will waste valuable time, so try to avoid them. Each level has a strict time limit, so make every grab count.

If you accidentally snag a worthless rock, you can press the up arrow key to use a stick of dynamite to destroy it and free up your claw. You start with a limited supply of dynamite. Between levels, you can visit the shop to spend your hard-earned cash on more dynamite, strength potions to reel items up faster, and other useful power-ups to help you in the next stage.
