Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your primary objective in Golf Orbit is to launch the golf ball as far as you can with a single powerful shot. When you start a round, a swing meter will appear. Simply tap or click when the indicator is in the green zone for a perfect, powerful stroke.

As your ball flies through the air, it will collect coins. The farther it travels, the more coins you'll earn. Use these coins between rounds to upgrade your golfer's abilities, including Power (for stronger initial hits), Speed (to travel faster and farther), and Bounce (to get extra distance from the ground).

Aim for the hole to earn big rewards! A hole-in-one scores an eagle, multiplying your earnings by 10x. If you land near the hole, you'll get a second chance to putt for a birdie, which triples your earnings. Be mindful of obstacles like trees, sand traps, and water hazards that can slow you down. Master your timing to set new distance records and become the ultimate golf champion!
