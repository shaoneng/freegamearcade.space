Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Welcome to Golf Orbit, where a single swing can send your ball to the stars! The goal is simple: launch your golf ball as far as you can and try to land it in the hole for big rewards.

To start, a swing meter will appear. Tap your screen or click your mouse when the indicator is in the green zone to execute a perfect, powerful stroke. Watch as your ball soars, but be mindful of obstacles like trees, bunkers, and water hazards that can slow you down.

If your ball lands near the hole, you'll get a second shot. The mechanic changes to a precision-based aiming arrow. Tap and release at the right moment to putt the ball in. Landing a hole-in-one on your first shot scores an "Eagle," multiplying your earnings by 10x! Making it in two shots earns a "Birdie," tripling your coins.

Use the coins you collect from each round to upgrade your golfer's abilities. Improve your power to hit farther, increase speed to cover more ground, and enhance your bounce for extra distance. Keep upgrading to set new records and conquer increasingly challenging courses. Can you hit the perfect shot?
