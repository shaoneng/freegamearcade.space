Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your primary goal in Golf Orbit is to launch your golf ball as far as you can with a single, powerful stroke. The further your ball travels, the more coins you'll earn to improve your golfer!

To start, a swing meter will appear. Simply tap or click when the moving bar is inside the green zone to execute a perfect shot. Timing is everything! A well-timed swing will send your ball soaring across the course and potentially even into space.

As your ball flies, it will collect coins. Use these coins in the shop to upgrade your golfer's core abilities: Strength (for a more powerful initial hit), Speed (to maintain momentum), and Bounce (to get extra distance from surfaces). Continuously upgrading is the key to setting new distance records.

Aim for the hole! If you manage a hole-in-one (an "eagle"), your earnings for that round are multiplied by 10x! If you land near the hole, you'll get a second chance to putt. Sink it for a "birdie" and triple your coins. Watch out for obstacles like trees, sand traps, and water hazards that can slow you down.
