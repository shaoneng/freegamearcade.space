Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your goal in Golf Orbit is simple yet challenging: launch your golf ball as far as possible! Each round begins with a single, powerful shot that could send your ball across the course and even into space.

To start, a swing meter will appear. Your job is to tap or click at the perfect moment to stop the indicator in the green zone. A perfect hit will send your ball soaring. As it flies, it will collect coins, but watch out for obstacles like trees, bunkers, and water hazards that can slow you down.

If your ball lands near the hole, you get a second shot to putt. The mechanic changes to a precision-based arrow. Tap and release at the right time to sink the ball. A hole-in-one earns you an "Eagle" and a massive 10x coin bonus! Sinking it on the second shot gets you a "Birdie" and triples your earnings.

Use the coins you collect to upgrade three crucial stats: Power (for a stronger initial hit), Speed (to make the ball travel faster), and Bounce (to get extra distance from hitting the ground). Consistently upgrading these abilities is the key to breaking your records and reaching new orbital heights!
