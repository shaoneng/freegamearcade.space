Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your mission in Golf Orbit is simple: launch your golf ball as far as you can! To start, watch the swing meter move back and forth. Tap or click when the indicator is in the green zone to execute a perfect, powerful stroke that sends your ball flying.

As your ball travels, it will collect coins. If it lands near the hole, you get a second chance to putt. An aiming arrow will appear; tap and release at the right moment to sink the ball. Scoring on the second shot earns you a "birdie" and triples your coin earnings. The ultimate prize is a "hole-in-one" (an eagle), which multiplies your coins by 10x!

Use the coins you earn to upgrade three essential stats: Power (for a stronger initial hit), Speed (to maintain momentum), and Bounce (to get extra distance from surfaces). Continuously upgrading is key to reaching new distances and setting high scores.

Be warned, the course is full of surprises! You'll need to avoid traditional hazards like sand bunkers and water, as well as unconventional obstacles like trees, eagles, and even UFOs. Master your timing and aim to conquer the course and launch your ball into orbit!
