Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

In Golf Orbit, your primary goal is to launch the golf ball as far as possible with a single powerful shot, aiming to send it across the course and even into outer space!

When starting a round, a swing meter will appear. Your objective is to tap the screen or click the mouse when the meter is within the green zone for a perfect stroke. This will send your ball soaring.

As the ball flies, it might encounter various obstacles like trees, sand traps, or water hazards, which can slow its progress. Be mindful of these, and also watch out for unusual events like eagles snatching your ball or UFO abductions!

If your ball lands near the hole but doesn't go in, you'll get a second chance. For this putt, an aiming arrow will guide you. Tap and release at the right moment to sink the ball.

Scoring is key to success: land a hole-in-one to achieve an "Eagle," multiplying your earnings by 10x! If you sink it on the second attempt, you'll score a "Birdie," tripling your coins. Use the coins you earn to upgrade your golfer's abilities: Power (how far you hit), Accuracy (how well you control the shot), and Bounces (how many times your ball can bounce before stopping). Upgrading these stats is crucial for hitting new distance records and mastering the game.
