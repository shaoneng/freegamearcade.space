Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your mission in Golf Orbit is simple yet addicting: launch your golf ball as far as you possibly can. With each powerful shot, you'll travel across the course, collect coins, and even soar into outer space. The further you hit the ball, the more coins you'll earn to upgrade your skills.

To start your swing, watch the power meter that moves back and forth. For the most powerful shot, tap or click when the indicator is inside the green zone. Perfect timing is crucial for sending your ball to incredible distances and setting new records.

Use the coins you collect during your flight to enhance your golfer's abilities. You can upgrade three key stats: Power (for a stronger initial hit), Speed (to make the ball travel faster through the air), and Bounce (to get extra distance after landing). Consistent upgrading is the key to progress.

Aim for the hole for bonus rewards! If you land your ball near the hole, you'll get a second, more precise shot to score a birdie for 3x the coins. If you manage an incredible hole-in-one on your first shot, you'll score an eagle and multiply your earnings by 10x! Along the way, you'll need to navigate obstacles like trees, sand traps, and even UFOs.
