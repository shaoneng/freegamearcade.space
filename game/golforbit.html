Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Welcome to Golf Orbit, where your goal is to launch your golf ball as far as humanly (and inhumanly) possible! A single powerful shot could send your ball soaring through the clouds and even into outer space. Timing is everything: when you start, a swing meter will appear. Tap your screen or click your mouse when the indicator is in the green zone for a perfect, powerful stroke.

As your ball travels, it will interact with the environment. It might bounce off trees, get stuck in a sand bunker, or take a swim in a water hazard. Watch out for stranger things, too, like UFO abductions! If your ball lands close to the hole, you'll get a second chance to putt. For this shot, an aiming arrow will appear—tap and release at the right moment to sink the ball.

Scoring big is key to upgrading your golfer. If you manage an incredible hole-in-one on your first shot, you'll score an "eagle" and multiply your coin earnings by 10x! If you sink the putt on your second attempt, you'll score a "birdie," which still triples your earnings for the round.

Use the coins you earn to upgrade three critical stats: Power (for a stronger initial hit), Speed (to make the ball travel faster and farther), and Bounce (to get extra distance from hitting the ground). Continuously upgrading these abilities is essential for setting new distance records and reaching new courses.

Master your timing, upgrade your stats, and see how far you can launch your ball in Golf Orbit. Can you reach the next galaxy?
