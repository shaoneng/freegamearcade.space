Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your primary goal in Golf Orbit is to launch your golf ball as far as you can with a single, powerful shot. The game is controlled with a simple tap or click, making it easy to pick up and play.

To start, a swing meter will appear, moving back and forth. Your mission is to stop the meter inside the green zone for a perfect, powerful stroke. Tap at just the right moment to send your ball flying across the course and potentially into orbit!

As your ball travels, it will collect coins. If you land a hole-in-one (an Eagle), your earnings for that round are multiplied by 10! If you land near the hole, you'll get a second chance to putt. An aiming arrow will appear—tap and release to sink the shot for a Birdie, which triples your earnings.

Use the coins you earn to upgrade three key stats: Strength (for more powerful hits), Speed (to make the ball fly faster), and Bounce (to get extra distance from ground hits). Upgrading is crucial for setting new distance records.

Watch out for obstacles! Sand bunkers, trees, and water hazards can stop your ball in its tracks. You might even encounter more unusual challenges, like being snatched by an eagle or abducted by a UFO. Master your timing to avoid these hazards and achieve the perfect shot!
