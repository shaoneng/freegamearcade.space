Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your main goal in Golf Orbit is to launch your golf ball as far as you can, maybe even into space! The further you hit it, the more coins you'll earn to upgrade your skills and set new distance records.

To start, a swing meter will appear. Tap or click at the perfect moment to stop the indicator in the green zone for the most powerful shot. As your ball flies, it will bounce off various objects and terrain. Try to avoid obstacles like trees, sand bunkers, and water hazards, as they can slow you down or stop your shot completely.

Use the coins you collect to upgrade three crucial stats: Power (for a stronger initial hit), Speed (to make the ball travel faster), and Bounce (to get extra distance from surfaces). Continuously upgrading these abilities is the key to reaching new distances and unlocking new environments.

If you're skilled (or lucky) enough to land near the hole, you'll get a second chance to putt. An aiming arrow will appear; tap and release at the right time to sink the ball. A hole-in-one on your first shot is an "Eagle" and multiplies your earnings by 10x! Sinking it on the second shot is a "Birdie" and triples your earnings. Aim for these special shots to maximize your coin collection and upgrade faster.
