Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your primary objective in Golf Orbit is to launch your golf ball as far as you can. To start, a swing meter will appear, moving back and forth. Click or tap when the indicator is inside the green zone to execute a perfect, powerful shot. The closer to the center of the green zone you stop the meter, the better your hit will be!

As your ball soars through the air and bounces along the course, it will collect coins. Use these coins between rounds to upgrade your golfer's strength, speed, and bounce. These upgrades are crucial for achieving new distance records and sending your ball even further, potentially into outer space!

If your ball lands near the hole, you'll get a second chance to putt. This uses a different aiming mechanic with a guiding arrow to help you sink the ball. Scoring a hole-in-one (an eagle) or getting it in on the second shot (a birdie) will grant you massive coin bonuses. Be careful, though! The course is filled with wacky obstacles like sand traps, trees, water hazards, and even the occasional UFO or eagle that can snatch your ball.
