Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Welcome to Golf Orbit, where your goal is to launch the golf ball as far as possible, maybe even into space! The further your ball travels, the more coins you earn for powerful upgrades.

How to Swing: When you start a round, a swing meter will appear. Your goal is to tap or click when the moving bar is inside the green zone for a perfect, powerful stroke. Time it just right to send your ball soaring across the landscape.

Putting: If your ball lands near the hole, you'll get a second shot to putt. An arrow will show the ball's path. Tap and release at the right moment to sink the putt. Sinking the ball on your first shot is a hole-in-one (Eagle), which multiplies your earnings by 10x! Making it on the second shot is a Birdie, which triples your earnings.

Upgrades: Use the coins you collect to upgrade three key stats: Strength (for more powerful hits), Speed (for faster ball travel), and Bounce (for extra distance on impact). Upgrading is essential for reaching new distances and setting high scores.

Watch out for obstacles like trees, sand traps, and water hazards. You might even encounter strange events like UFO abductions! Master your timing, upgrade your stats, and see how far you can launch your golf ball in Golf Orbit.
