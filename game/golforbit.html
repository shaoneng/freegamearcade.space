Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your main goal in Golf Orbit is to launch your golf ball as far as possible, collecting coins and setting new distance records. When you begin, a swing meter will appear. Tap your screen or click your mouse when the indicator is in the green zone for a perfect, powerful stroke.

As your ball soars through the air—and sometimes space—it will collect coins. Beware of obstacles like trees, sand traps, and water hazards that can slow you down. The game is full of surprises, so don't be shocked if your ball gets snatched by an eagle or abducted by a UFO!

If your shot lands near the hole, you'll get a second chance to putt. An arrow will show the ball's trajectory. Tap and release at the right moment to sink the putt. Landing a hole-in-one (an "eagle") multiplies your earnings by 10x, while sinking it on the second shot (a "birdie") triples them.

Use the coins you earn to upgrade your golfer's stats: Power for longer initial hits, Speed for more momentum, and Bounce for extra distance after landing. Continuously upgrading is the key to reaching new orbits and topping your high score!
