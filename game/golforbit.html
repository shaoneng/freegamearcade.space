Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your main goal in Golf Orbit is to launch your golf ball as far as possible, collecting coins and setting new distance records. You might even launch it into space!

To start, a swing meter will appear. Tap or click at the perfect moment when the indicator is inside the green zone to execute a powerful and accurate shot. The farther your ball travels, the more coins you'll earn along the way.

Use your collected coins to upgrade three essential stats: Power (for a stronger initial hit), Speed (to make the ball travel faster and farther), and Bounce (to get extra distance from hitting the ground). Strategically upgrading these abilities is key to smashing your old records.

If your shot lands near the hole, you'll get a second chance to putt. A guiding arrow will appear; tap and release to sink the ball. A hole-in-one scores an "eagle" for 10x earnings, while a two-shot success is a "birdie" for 3x earnings. Watch out for obstacles like trees, bunkers, and even UFOs that can end your run!
