Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your objective in Golf Orbit is to launch your golf ball as far as you can, navigating quirky courses and aiming for the ultimate hole-in-one. With a bit of skill and some wild luck, you might even send your ball into outer space!

The Swing: When you start a round, a swing meter will appear. To get the most powerful and accurate shot, tap your screen or click your mouse when the moving indicator is inside the green "perfect" zone. Perfect timing is crucial for maximizing your distance.

Putting: If your ball lands close to the hole, you'll get a second shot. The gameplay switches to a precision putting mechanic. An arrow will show the ball's intended path. Tap and release at the right moment to sink the putt and finish the hole.

Scoring and Upgrades: Landing a hole-in-one (an Eagle) will multiply your coin earnings by 10x! Making the shot on your second try (a Birdie) will triple your earnings. Use the coins you collect to upgrade three key stats:

  • Power: Increases the initial force of your swing.
  • Speed: Helps the ball travel faster and farther through the air.
  • Bounce: Makes the ball bounce more upon landing, gaining extra distance.

Watch out for various obstacles like trees, sand traps, and water hazards that can stop your ball in its tracks. Master your timing, upgrade your golfer, and see how far you can launch your ball in Golf Orbit!
