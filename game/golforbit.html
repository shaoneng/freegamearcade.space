Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

The objective in Golf Orbit is simple: launch your golf ball as far as you can with a single, powerful swing. To start, you'll see a swing meter moving back and forth. Click or tap at the perfect moment when the indicator is inside the green zone to execute a perfect stroke and send your ball flying!

As your ball travels through the air and across the course, it will collect coins. Be mindful of obstacles! Landing in a sand bunker, hitting a tree, or splashing into water will slow your ball down. The course also has some strange hazards, so watch out for UFO abductions or eagles snatching your ball mid-flight.

If your ball lands close enough to the hole, you'll get a second shot to putt. The mechanic changes to a precision-based aim. An arrow will show the ball's intended path; tap and release at the right time to sink the putt and finish the hole.

Scoring big is key to progressing. Landing a hole-in-one on your first shot is called an "Eagle" and multiplies your coin earnings by 10! If you sink it on your second shot, you'll score a "Birdie," which triples your earnings. The more coins you collect, the faster you can upgrade.

Spend your hard-earned coins in the shop to upgrade three crucial stats: Power (for a stronger initial hit), Speed (to maintain momentum), and Bounce (to get extra distance from ricochets). Continuously improving your golfer is the only way to reach new distances and top the leaderboards. Good luck!
