Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your objective in Golf Orbit is simple: launch your golf ball as far as you can! When you begin, a swing meter will appear. Tap or click at the perfect moment when the indicator is in the green zone to unleash a powerful shot.

As your ball soars through the air—and sometimes space—it will collect coins. Be careful of obstacles like trees, sand traps, and water hazards that can slow you down. The further your ball travels, the more coins you earn.

If your shot lands near the hole, you'll get a chance to putt. For this shot, an arrow will show the ball's path. Tap and release to sink it. Landing a hole-in-one (an Eagle) multiplies your earnings by 10x, while making it in two shots (a Birdie) triples your earnings!

Use the coins you collect to upgrade your golfer's stats: Power for stronger initial hits, Speed to make the ball travel faster, and Bounce to get extra distance from the ground. Keep upgrading to set new distance records and reach new orbits!
