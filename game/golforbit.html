Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Welcome to Golf Orbit, where a single powerful swing can send your ball to the moon! Your primary goal is to launch your golf ball as far as you can across the course and into outer space. With each shot, you'll earn coins that help you become an even better golfer.

To start, a swing meter will appear. Simply tap or click when the moving bar is inside the green zone to execute a perfect, powerful stroke. The closer you are to the center, the better your shot will be!

As your ball soars through the air, it will collect coins. Watch out for obstacles like trees, sand bunkers, and water hazards that can slow you down. The game adds a fun twist with unexpected events like UFO abductions or eagles snatching your ball! If you land near the hole, you'll get a second chance to putt.

Use the coins you collect to upgrade your golfer's abilities. You can improve your Power for a stronger initial hit, Speed to make the ball travel faster, and Bounce to get extra distance from surfaces. Upgrading is the key to breaking your distance records.

Aim for the ultimate prize: a hole-in-one! Sinking the ball in a single shot will grant you a massive coin bonus. Master your timing, upgrade your stats, and see how far you can launch your golf ball.
