Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Welcome to Golf Orbit, the online golf game where a single powerful shot can send your ball flying across the course and even into space! Your mission is to launch the ball as far as you can with a single tap, collecting coins to upgrade your golfer's abilities. With each shot, aim to beat your previous distance, but watch out for strange obstacles like bunkers, trees, water hazards, and even UFOs!

To play, focus on the swing meter that appears at the start of each round. Tap when the indicator is in the green zone for a perfect, powerful stroke. As your ball soars, it will interact with the environment. If it lands near the hole, you'll get a second shot. This time, an aiming arrow appears; tap and release at the right moment to sink the putt.

Aim for the ultimate prize: a hole-in-one! Sinking the ball in a single shot earns you an 'eagle' and multiplies your coin earnings by 10x. If you make it on the second try, you'll score a 'birdie,' which triples your earnings. Use these coins to boost your golfer's power, speed, and bounce.

The coins you collect are crucial for improving your performance. You can spend them on three key upgrades to hit the ball farther, increase its speed, and enhance its bounce. Upgrading your skills is the key to setting new distance records and conquering increasingly challenging courses.

As you progress, the courses become more difficult, with more obstacles designed to stop you. Mastering the timing of your swing and understanding the best angles are essential for success. Challenge yourself to hit new records and become a master of Golf Orbit!
