Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your main objective in Golf Orbit is to launch your golf ball as far as possible with a single, powerful stroke. Each successful shot earns you coins, which you can use to upgrade your golfer's abilities and hit even farther on your next attempt.

To start, a swing meter will appear, moving back and forth. Your goal is to tap or click when the indicator is inside the green zone for a perfect shot. The closer to the center, the more powerful your launch will be!

As your ball flies, it will interact with the environment. It might bounce off trees, get stuck in sand traps, or splash into water hazards. Keep an eye out for strange occurrences tooâ€”your ball might get abducted by a UFO!

If your shot lands near the hole, you'll get a second chance to putt. The game switches to a precision-aiming mechanic, where an arrow shows the ball's path. Tap and release at the right moment to sink the ball. Scoring a hole-in-one (an Eagle) multiplies your earnings by 10x, while making it in two shots (a Birdie) triples your earnings.

Use the coins you collect to purchase three key upgrades: Power (for a stronger initial hit), Speed (to maintain momentum), and Bounce (for extra distance from surfaces). Continuously upgrading these stats is the key to reaching new distances and setting high scores.
