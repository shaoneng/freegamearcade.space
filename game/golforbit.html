Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your primary goal in Golf Orbit is to hit the ball as far as you can with a single, powerful shot. When you start, a swing meter will appear. Click or tap when the moving bar is inside the green zone to execute a perfect swing. The closer to the center, the better your shot!

As your ball flies through the air, it will collect coins. Be prepared for a wild ride! Your ball might bounce off trees, get stuck in sand traps, or even get abducted by a UFO. Each round presents a new, unpredictable challenge.

The coins you collect are crucial for improving your game. You can spend them in the shop to upgrade three key stats: Power (for stronger initial hits), Speed (to maintain momentum), and Bounce (for extra distance after landing). Upgrading is essential to break your own distance records.

If you land near the hole, you'll get a second chance to putt. Sink it for a 'Birdie' and triple your coin earnings. The ultimate achievement is a hole-in-one, known as an 'Eagle,' which multiplies your earnings by a massive 10x! Master your timing and aim to rack up huge scores.
