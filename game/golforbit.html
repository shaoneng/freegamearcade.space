Golf Orbit

How to Play Golf Orbit

Your main objective in Golf Orbit is to launch your golf ball as far as you can, even reaching outer space! When you begin a round, a swing meter will appear. To get the most powerful shot, tap or click when the indicator is inside the green zone for a perfect stroke.

As your ball travels through the air and across the course, it will collect coins. You can use these coins between rounds to upgrade three key stats: Power (for a stronger initial hit), Speed (to make the ball travel faster), and Bounce (to get extra distance from ground hits). Upgrading is crucial for setting new distance records.

The ultimate achievement is a hole-in-one! If you sink the ball in a single shot (an Eagle), your earnings for that round are multiplied by 10x. If your ball lands near the hole, you get a second chance to putt using a precision arrow. Successfully making this shot scores you a Birdie, tripling your earnings.

The course isn't always a straight shot. Be prepared to navigate around obstacles like trees, sand bunkers, and water hazards. Sometimes, you might even encounter something out of this world, like a UFO! Mastering your timing and aim is the key to conquering every challenge.
