Granny

How to Play Granny

Welcome to Granny's house, a place you'll desperately want to leave. Your primary objective is to escape the house. You have five days to find all the necessary items, solve puzzles, and unlock the main door before your time is up.

The biggest obstacle is Granny herself. She has exceptionally sharp hearing and will investigate any sound you make. Dropping an item, creaking a floorboard, or making any noise will attract her attention. You must move with extreme caution and use stealth to your advantage to avoid being caught.

Explore the house to find keys, tools like hammers and wire cutters, and other crucial objects hidden in drawers, cupboards, and secret compartments. Use these items to open locks and access new areas. If you hear Granny approaching, find a hiding spot quickly! You can hide under beds, inside wardrobes, or in other dark corners to evade her.

Use your wits, stay quiet, and manage your five days wisely to orchestrate your escape. Good luck—you'll need it!
