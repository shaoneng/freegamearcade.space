Granny

How to Play Granny

Granny is a heart-pounding horror-puzzle game where your only goal is to survive. You wake up trapped in Granny's eerie house, and your mission is to escape. The catch? The door is locked, and the relentless Granny is always listening, ready to hunt you down at the slightest sound.

Navigate the creepy house, solve puzzles to find keys and tools, and be as stealthy as possible. Your survival depends on your ability to outsmart Granny and remain hidden. This game is a terrifying mix of fear, strategy, and problem-solving that will keep your heart racing from start to finish. Ready for some jump scares? Put on your headphones and see if you have what it takes to escape.
