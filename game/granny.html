Granny

How to Play Granny

Your objective in Granny is simple but terrifying: escape the house. You have a total of five days to find all the necessary items, unlock the main door, and get out. If Granny catches you, you lose a day and wake up back in your room. If you're caught five times, it's game over.

Move quietly through the house using your controls to explore different rooms. Interact with objects to find keys, tools like hammers or wire cutters, and other essential items needed for your escape. Be extremely careful—dropping an item will make a noise, and the ever-vigilant Granny will immediately come to investigate your location.

Stealth is your greatest weapon. Use hiding spots like under beds and inside closets to avoid Granny when she is near. Success depends on your ability to solve puzzles under pressure while remaining completely silent. Stay quiet, stay hidden, and plan your every move to outsmart Granny and win your freedom.

⚠️ Mature Content Warning: This horror game features intense fear-based elements and jump scares. It is intended for mature audiences. Player discretion is advised.
