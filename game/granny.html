Granny

How to Play Granny

Granny is a heart-pounding horror-puzzle game where your survival skills, quick thinking, and stealth are pushed to their limits. You awaken in a bloody bed inside Granny's eerie house, trapped. Your ultimate goal is to find a way to escape this nightmare, but it won't be easy.

The main obstacle is Granny herself. She has surprisingly sharp hearing for her age and is constantly on the hunt. Make a sound, and she'll be on your trail. This game is a terrifying mix of fear, strategy, and problem-solving that will keep your heart racing. For the full experience with all the jumpscares, put on your headphones and immerse yourself in the horror.
