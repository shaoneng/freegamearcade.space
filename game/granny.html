Granny

How to Play Granny

Welcome to Granny's house, a place you'll desperately want to leave. In this terrifying horror-puzzle game, your primary objective is to escape the house within five days. You start locked in a room, and your first task is to find a way out without making a sound.

Stealth is your greatest asset. The antagonist, Granny, has incredibly sharp hearing and will investigate any noise you make, from a creaking floorboard to a dropped item. Use your mouse and keyboard (or touch controls on mobile) to navigate the eerie house, interact with objects, and solve puzzles to unlock doors and find the tools necessary for your escape.

Explore every dark corner to find keys, hammers, cutters, and other items that will help you disable traps and open new areas. But be warned: Granny is always lurking. If she catches you, you'll wake up back in the starting room, losing a day. Can you keep your cool, solve the mysteries, and escape before your time runs out?
