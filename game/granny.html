Granny

How to Play Granny

Your objective is simple, yet terrifyingly difficult: escape Granny's house. You wake up trapped and have a total of five days to find the necessary items and unlock the main exit to freedom.

The house is not empty. Granny is constantly patrolling, and her hearing is incredibly sharp. Any noise you make, from a creaking floorboard to a dropped item, will draw her to your location immediately. Stealth is your greatest ally. Move silently and use your surroundings to your advantage.

Explore the eerie rooms to find keys, tools like hammers or wire cutters, and other objects that will help you bypass locks and obstacles. Solve puzzles to progress and uncover the secrets of the house. If you hear Granny approaching, hide! You can take cover under beds, inside wardrobes, or in any dark corner. If she catches you, she'll knock you out, and you'll wake up back in the starting room, having lost a precious day. After five days, your time is up.

⚠️ Mature Content Warning: This game is designed for a mature audience. It contains intense jumpscares, unsettling imagery, and a pervasive atmosphere of fear. Player discretion is strongly advised.
