Granny

How to Play Granny

Welcome to Granny's house, where your nightmare begins. You awaken in a locked room with a singular, terrifying goal: escape within five days. Each day is a new chance, but time is not on your side. Your survival depends on your stealth, wits, and ability to stay calm under pressure.

To escape, you must explore the creepy, multi-level house and find the necessary items to unlock the main door. This involves searching for keys, tools like hammers or wire cutters, and parts of a weapon to defend yourself temporarily. Every drawer you open and every step you take could lead you closer to freedom or straight into a trap.

The biggest obstacle is Granny herself. She roams the house, and her hearing is incredibly sharp. If you drop an item, step on a creaky floorboard, or make any noise, she will come to investigate. You'll need to hide under beds or inside closets to avoid being caught. If she finds you, you'll be knocked out, waking up on the next day. After five failed attempts, the game is over.
