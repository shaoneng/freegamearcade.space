Granny

How to Play Granny

In Granny, you awaken in a dark room with one simple, terrifying goal: escape the house. You have a total of five days to find your way out. Each day is a new chance, but if Granny catches you, you'll wake up in the bed again, having lost a day.

Your survival depends on being silent and stealthy. Granny has exceptional hearing and will investigate any sound you make, whether it's a dropped item or a creaky floorboard. Move quietly, hide under beds or in wardrobes, and use distractions to your advantage to avoid her grasp.

Explore the eerie house to find keys, tools like hammers and wire cutters, and other crucial items. These objects are essential for unlocking doors, disabling traps, and solving puzzles that block your path to freedom. Your wits and nerves will be tested as you piece together the escape plan before your time runs out.
