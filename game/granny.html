Granny

How to Play Granny

In Granny, your primary goal is to escape the house you're trapped in. You have five days to find all the necessary items and solve the puzzles to unlock the main door. If you're caught by Granny, you lose a day and wake up back in the starting room.

Stealth is your greatest weapon. Granny has incredibly sharp hearing and will investigate any sound you make, from a creaking floorboard to a dropped item. Move slowly and crouch to minimize noise. If she's near, find a hiding spot quickly—under a bed, inside a wardrobe, or anywhere out of sight—and wait for her to pass.

Explore the house to find keys, tools like hammers and wire cutters, and other essential objects. Each item serves a specific purpose, allowing you to access new areas, disable traps, or solve puzzles that bring you one step closer to freedom. Be strategic about what you carry and where you use your items.

Warning: This is an intense horror game with frequent jump scares and a suspenseful atmosphere. It is intended for mature audiences. For the most immersive and terrifying experience, we recommend playing with headphones. Good luck—you'll need it!
