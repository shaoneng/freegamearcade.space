Granny

How to Play Granny

In Granny, your primary goal is to escape the house within five days. You wake up trapped and must find various items to unlock doors, disarm traps, and ultimately find your way out. Each day represents a new opportunity to make progress, but also brings you closer to permanent capture.

Granny has extremely sensitive hearing, so every creak, dropped item, or missed step can alert her to your presence. Move slowly and silently, use hiding spots like beds or wardrobes, and distract Granny with noise if necessary. Explore every room to find clues and tools, but be warned: one wrong move can lead to a terrifying encounter. Prepare for jumpscares and intense suspense as you try to outwit Granny and break free before time runs out!
