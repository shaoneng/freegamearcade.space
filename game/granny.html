Granny

How to Play Granny

Your mission in Granny is one of pure survival: escape the terrifying house before it's too late. You have a total of five days to find a way out. The game begins with you trapped in a room, and your first task is to find a way to unlock the door and begin your search for freedom.

Stealth is your greatest ally. Granny has incredibly sharp hearing and will rush to investigate any sound you make. Dropping an item, a creaky floorboard, or a hurried footstep will alert her to your position. Move slowly and crouch to minimize noise. Use hiding spots like closets or under beds to evade her when she gets too close.

The house is a complex puzzle. You must explore every dark corner to find keys, tools (like hammers or wire cutters), and other objects needed to unlock new areas and disable traps. Solving these environmental puzzles is crucial to piecing together your escape route.

Warning: This game is designed to be frightening and features intense jump scares, unsettling sounds, and a constant sense of dread. It is recommended for mature players. If you can handle the fear, use your wits and courage to outsmart Granny and escape her grasp!
