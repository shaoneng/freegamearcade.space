Granny

How to Play Granny

Your objective is to escape Granny's house within five days. You wake up trapped in a room, and your first task is to find a way out of it. The main door is locked with multiple locks, and you must find all the necessary items like keys, cutting pliers, and a hammer to undo them.

The biggest obstacle is Granny herself. She roams the house and has incredibly sensitive hearing. Making any noise, like dropping an item or stepping on a creaky floorboard, will alert her to your location. She will come to investigate, and if she catches you, you'll lose a day. Use stealth to your advantage by hiding under beds or in closets to avoid her.

Explore the house carefully, opening drawers and cupboards to find items. Each object has a specific purpose for solving puzzles or disabling traps. You might find a weapon to temporarily knock Granny out, giving you a short window to explore more freely. Plan your moves, manage your inventory, and think quickly to outsmart Granny and make your escape before the five days are up.

⚠️ Mature Content Warning: This horror game features intense fear-based elements and jump scares. Player discretion is advised.
