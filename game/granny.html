Granny

How to Play Granny

Your primary objective in Granny is to escape the house. You have five days (or five attempts) to find all the necessary items, unlock the main door, and get out before Granny catches you for good.

Stealth is your greatest weapon. Granny has incredibly sensitive hearing and will investigate any sound you make. Walk quietly, crouch to move silently, and be careful not to drop items. If you make a noise, hide quickly in a wardrobe, under a bed, or anywhere you can find to evade her.

Explore the house to find keys, tools like hammers and wire cutters, and other objects. Each item has a specific purpose for solving puzzles and opening new areas. Your success depends on your ability to piece together clues and figure out how to use the items you discover.

Mature Content Warning: Be prepared for intense moments and jump scares. This horror game is designed to be frightening and is intended for a mature audience. Player discretion is advised.
