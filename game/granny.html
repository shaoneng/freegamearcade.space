Granny

How to Play Granny

Your primary objective in Granny is to escape the house within five days. You wake up trapped, and you must navigate the creepy corridors and rooms to find the keys and tools necessary to unlock the main exit.

Stealth is your greatest weapon. Granny has exceptionally sharp hearing and will investigate any sound you make. Move cautiously, avoid knocking things over, and be mindful of creaky floorboards. If you make a noise, she will be on your trail in an instant.

Explore the house to find items like hammers, wire cutters, and various keys. These are essential for bypassing obstacles and unlocking new areas. If you hear Granny approaching, find a hiding spot quickly—under a bed or inside a wardrobe can save your life. Use your wits to solve puzzles and piece together your escape plan before time runs out!

⚠️ Mature Content Warning

This horror game features intense fear-based elements, graphic imagery, and disturbing themes. It is intended for mature audiences. Player discretion is advised.
