Granny

How to Play Granny

Welcome to Granny's house—a place you'll be desperate to leave. In this terrifying first-person horror game, your main objective is to escape the house. You wake up in a locked room, and your first task is to find a way out of it.

Your greatest threat is Granny. She roams the house and has incredibly sensitive hearing. Making any noise, like dropping an object or creaking a floorboard, will alert her to your location, and she will come hunting for you. Use stealth to your advantage: hide under beds, in closets, or anywhere you can to avoid being caught.

To escape, you must solve a series of puzzles by finding keys, tools like hammers and wire cutters, and other items scattered throughout the house. Each item has a specific purpose that will help you unlock doors, disable traps, and clear your path to freedom. Explore carefully, think quickly, and above all, stay quiet if you want to survive.

⚠️ Mature Content Warning: This horror game features intense fear-based elements, graphic imagery, and disturbing themes. It is intended for mature audiences and may not be suitable for all players. Player discretion is advised.
