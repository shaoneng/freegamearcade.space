Granny 2

How to Play Granny 2

Your main goal in Granny 2 is to escape the house within five days. You wake up trapped and must explore the creepy residence to find a way out, all while being hunted by the menacing Granny and Grandpa.

Be extremely quiet! Granny has excellent hearing and will come running if you drop an item or step on a creaky floorboard. Grandpa is hard of hearing but has sharp eyesight, so stay out of his line of sight. If you're spotted, you need to run and hide. You can conceal yourself under beds or inside wardrobes to evade them.

There are two primary ways to escape: through the main door or via a boat in the basement. The main door is secured with multiple locks, requiring you to find various keys and tools scattered throughout the house. To escape by boat, you'll need to locate parts to repair it and unlock the sewer gate. You can also find weapons to temporarily incapacitate Granny or Grandpa, buying you a few precious seconds to continue your search.
