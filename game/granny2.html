Granny 2

How to Play Granny 2

Your objective in Granny 2 is to escape from a creepy house within five days. You wake up trapped and disoriented, and the clock is ticking. You must explore your surroundings to find a way out before time is up.

The house is patrolled by two formidable foes: Granny and Grandpa. Granny has incredibly sharp hearing and will come running to investigate any noise, such as a dropped item or a creaky floorboard. Grandpa is slower, but his eyesight is keen, and he will chase you if he spots you. To survive, you must use stealth to hide under beds or inside wardrobes.

There are two primary escape routes. You can search the house for keys and tools to unlock the heavily secured main door. Alternatively, you can find your way to the basement sewer, where a rusty boat awaits. Find the missing parts to repair the boat, start its engine, and unlock the gate to make your getaway by water.

While exploring, you can find weapons that can temporarily stun Granny and Grandpa, buying you a few precious moments to advance. Choose your difficulty level wisely, ranging from "Practice" mode to learn the layout without danger, to the heart-pounding "Extreme" mode for the ultimate survival horror challenge.
