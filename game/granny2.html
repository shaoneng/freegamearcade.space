How to Play Granny 2

Your goal in Granny 2 is to escape a terrifying house within five days. You are not alone; the relentless Granny and Grandpa are hunting you. You must outsmart them and find a way out before your time is up.

Explore the eerie rooms to find keys, tools, and other crucial items. Be incredibly quietâ€”Granny has exceptional hearing and will rush to any sound you make, like dropping an object or stepping on a creaky floorboard. Grandpa may be slower, but his eyesight is sharp, so stay out of his line of sight.

There are two ways to escape: unlock the heavily secured main door or repair the rusty boat in the basement sewer. You can find weapons to temporarily incapacitate Granny and Grandpa, giving you a precious window of opportunity to search for items. Use these moments wisely, as they won't be down for long.

If you're spotted or make a noise, quickly hide in wardrobes or under beds to avoid getting caught. The game offers multiple difficulty levels, from Practice mode where you can explore safely to Extreme mode for the ultimate survival challenge. Choose your difficulty and see if you have what it takes to survive!
