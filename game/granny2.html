Granny 2

How to Play Granny 2

Your terrifying adventure begins as you wake up trapped in a dark room. The objective in Granny 2 is simple: escape the house within five days. However, you're not alone. The house is patrolled by both Granny and Grandpa, and you must avoid them at all costs.

Use your mouse and keyboard (WASD for movement, mouse to look around, and interaction keys as prompted) to explore the eerie house. Search for keys, tools, and other crucial items needed to unlock the main door's multiple locks. Be incredibly quiet—Granny has exceptional hearing and will investigate any sound you make, like dropping an item or stepping on a creaky floorboard.

Grandpa, while a bit slower, has sharp eyesight. If he spots you, you'll need to run and hide quickly. Use wardrobes and beds as hiding spots to break their line of sight. You can also find weapons to temporarily incapacitate them, giving you a precious few moments to progress. In addition to the main door, there's an alternative escape route via a boat in the basement. Find the necessary parts to repair it and make your getaway. Good luck—you'll need it!
