Granny 2

How to Play Granny 2

Your objective in Granny 2 is to escape from a creepy house within five days. You wake up trapped and must find all the necessary items to unlock the main door. Be warned, you are not aloneâ€”Granny and Grandpa are constantly patrolling the house, and they are out to get you.

Stealth is your best friend. Granny has exceptional hearing and will investigate any sound you make, like dropping an item or stepping on a creaky floorboard. Grandpa is hard of hearing but has keen eyesight. If he spots you, you'll need to run and hide quickly. Use wardrobes and beds to stay out of sight, but be careful, as they can still find you if they see you hide.

Explore the house thoroughly to find keys, tools, and other objects to solve puzzles and unlock the main door. There is an alternative escape route through the basement sewer via a boat, which requires its own set of items to operate. You can also find weapons to temporarily incapacitate Granny or Grandpa, buying you a few precious moments to advance your escape plan.

The game offers multiple difficulty levels to match your skill. 'Practice' mode lets you explore the house without any threats, 'Easy' slows your pursuers down, 'Normal' provides a balanced challenge, while 'Hard' and 'Extreme' increase their speed and add more locks, making survival a true test of your nerves.
