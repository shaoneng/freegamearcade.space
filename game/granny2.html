Granny 2

How to Play Granny 2

Your mission in Granny 2 is simple but terrifying: escape the house in five days. You wake up trapped with two relentless captors. Granny has exceptional hearing and will investigate any sound you make, from a dropped item to a creaky floorboard. Grandpa, while a bit slower, has sharp eyesight and will chase you on sight. You must use stealth to survive, hiding under beds or inside wardrobes to avoid being caught.

To escape, you have two options. The first is the main door, which is secured by multiple locks. You'll need to explore the house thoroughly to find keys, tools like a cutting plier, and other items to disable the security systems. Your second option is a boat in the basement sewer, but it requires a different set of items to repair and unlock the gate.

Throughout the house, you can find weapons that will temporarily stun Granny or Grandpa, giving you a precious few seconds to continue your search. Be strategic about when you use them, as they only buy you a small window of time. With multiple difficulty levels, from Practice to Extreme, you can tailor the challenge to your skill. Can you piece together the clues and escape before your five days are up?
