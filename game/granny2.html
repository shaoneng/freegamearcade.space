Granny 2

How to Play Granny 2

Welcome to the terrifying sequel, Granny 2! Your objective is simple but challenging: escape the house within five days. You wake up trapped and disoriented, with the relentless Granny and Grandpa hunting you down. Every second counts.

Stealth is your best friend. Granny possesses incredibly sharp hearing and will rush to investigate any noise, from a dropped item to a creaky floorboard. Grandpa, on the other hand, may not hear well, but his eyesight is deadly. If he spots you, you'll need to run and hide quickly. Use hiding spots like wardrobes and under beds to evade them, but be careful they don't see you entering your hiding place.

There are two paths to freedom. The primary exit is the main door, secured by multiple locks. You must carefully search the house for keys and tools like the hammer or bolt cutters to overcome these obstacles. Alternatively, you can escape through the basement, where a rusty boat awaits in the sewer. Find the necessary parts to repair the boat, start its engine, and unlock the water gate to make your getaway.

You are not entirely defenseless. Find weapons scattered around the house to temporarily stun Granny and Grandpa. Plan your moves, manage your five-day time limit, and solve the puzzles to survive.
