Granny 2

How to Play Granny 2

You find yourself trapped in a creepy house with Granny and Grandpa on the prowl. Your objective is to escape within five days. The main exit is heavily locked, and you must explore the house to find keys, tools, and other items needed to unlock it.

You are not alone. Granny has exceptional hearing and will investigate any sound you make, from a dropped item to a creaky floorboard. Grandpa is hard of hearing but has keen eyesight; if he spots you, you'll need to run and hide. Use wardrobes and beds as hiding spots, but be careful, as they can still find you if you're not quick enough.

Besides the main door, a secondary escape route exists through the basement sewer via a boat, which also requires specific items to become operational. You can find weapons scattered around the house to temporarily knock out Granny or Grandpa, buying you precious time to search for clues and solve puzzles.

The game features multiple difficulty levels, from a "Practice" mode where you can explore safely to an "Extreme" mode where your pursuers are faster and more relentless. Choose the difficulty that best suits your courage and skill level to begin your terrifying escape attempt.
