Granny 2

How to Play Granny 2

Your main objective in Granny 2 is to escape the house within five days. You start in a dark room with the main door heavily locked. To escape, you must explore the eerie house to find keys, tools, and other crucial items that will help you unlock the door and flee to safety.

You are not alone. The house is patrolled by both Granny and Grandpa. Granny has exceptionally sharp hearing and will investigate any sound, like a dropped item or a creaky floorboard. Grandpa is a bit slower, but his eyesight is keen, and he will chase you if he spots you. Stealth and silence are your best allies.

To avoid being caught, you must move quietly and use hiding spots like wardrobes and under beds. If you are cornered, you can find weapons scattered around the house to temporarily knock out Granny or Grandpa, giving you a precious few moments to continue your search or make a run for it.

Besides the main door, there is an alternative escape route through the basement. A rusty boat is waiting in the sewer, but you'll need to find the necessary parts to start it and unlock the gate. Choose your escape plan wisely and try to survive before your five days are up!
