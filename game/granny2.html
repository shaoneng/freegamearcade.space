Granny 2

How to Play Granny 2

Your main goal in Granny 2 is to escape the house within a five-day limit. To do this, you must navigate the creepy rooms and solve puzzles to unlock the main door or repair a boat in the basement for an alternate escape route. Be warned, you are not alone. Granny has exceptional hearing and will investigate any sound you make, while Grandpa has sharp eyesight and will pursue you if he spots you.

To survive, you must be stealthy. Move quietly to avoid making noise from creaky floorboards. If you drop an item, Granny will be alerted to your location instantly. You can hide from them by crouching under beds or concealing yourself inside wardrobes. Use these hiding spots strategically to evade capture when they are near.

Explore the house thoroughly to find essential items like keys, tools, and weapon parts. These items are crucial for unlocking doors, disabling traps, and defending yourself. While weapons can temporarily stun Granny and Grandpa, they only provide a brief window of opportunity, so use them wisely to advance your escape plan. Choose your difficulty and see if you have what it takes to survive!
