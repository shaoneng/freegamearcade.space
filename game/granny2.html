Granny 2

How to Play Granny 2

Welcome to the terrifying world of Granny 2! Your goal is simple but difficult: escape the house within five days. You awaken in a dark room, trapped with the menacing Granny and Grandpa. To win, you must explore the house, find keys and tools, and unlock the heavily secured main door before your time runs out.

Stealth is your greatest weapon. Granny has incredibly sharp hearing and will rush to investigate any noise you make, like dropping an item or stepping on a creaky floorboard. Grandpa, on the other hand, is hard of hearing but has a deadly gaze. If he spots you, you'll need to run and hide. Use wardrobes and crawl under beds to evade them, but be carefulâ€”they can still find you if you're not fully hidden.

There are two ways to escape. The primary method is unlocking the main door, which requires finding a variety of items scattered throughout the house. A secondary escape route lies in the basement: a rusty boat. To use it, you must locate all the missing parts, repair it, and unlock the sewer gate. Choose your path wisely, as both present unique challenges.

You're not completely defenseless. Weapons can be found around the house, allowing you to temporarily stun Granny or Grandpa, giving you a brief window to progress. The game offers five difficulty levels, from "Practice" mode where you can explore without danger, to "Extreme" mode, where your pursuers are faster and more relentless, and additional locks bar your escape. Select your difficulty and see if you have what it takes to survive.
