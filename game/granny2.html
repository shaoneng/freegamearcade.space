Granny 2

How to Play Granny 2

Your primary objective in Granny 2 is to escape the house within five days. You wake up trapped and must navigate the eerie rooms to find items that will help you unlock the main door or escape via a boat in the basement.

You are not alone. The house is patrolled by both Granny and Grandpa. Granny has incredibly sharp hearing and will investigate any sound you make, like dropping an item or stepping on a creaky floorboard. Grandpa is slower, but his eyesight is keen; if he spots you, you'll need to run and hide quickly. Use wardrobes and beds as hiding spots to evade them.

As you explore, you'll find various tools and keys necessary for your escape. You can also find weapons like a shotgun or a stun gun. These can temporarily incapacitate Granny or Grandpa, giving you a precious window of time to explore more freely, but be warned—they will get back up.

The game offers multiple difficulty levels, from a "Practice" mode where you can explore without danger, to an "Extreme" mode where Granny and Grandpa are faster and more aggressive. Choose the level that best suits your skill and nerve!
