Granny 2

How to Play Granny 2

Your main goal in Granny 2 is to escape the house within five days. You wake up trapped, and your survival depends on your stealth, wits, and ability to solve puzzles under pressure.

You are not alone. The house is patrolled by two menacing figures: Granny, who has incredibly sharp hearing and will investigate any sound you make, and Grandpa, who has excellent eyesight. If either of them catches you, your day ends, and you lose one of your five chances.

To escape, you must explore the house to find keys, tools, and other essential items. These items are used to unlock the multiple locks on the main door or to repair and launch a boat in the basement for an alternate escape route. Be careful when moving around, as dropping an item or stepping on a creaky floorboard will alert Granny to your location.

You can defend yourself by finding weapons scattered throughout the house. These can temporarily incapacitate Granny or Grandpa, giving you a precious window of time to explore more freely. However, they will recover, so use this advantage wisely.

When you hear them approaching, quickly hide under beds or inside wardrobes to avoid being seen. The game features multiple difficulty levels, from a "Practice" mode where you can learn the layout without danger, to an "Extreme" mode for the ultimate nerve-wracking challenge.
