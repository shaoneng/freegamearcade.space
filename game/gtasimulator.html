How to Play GTA Simulator

Welcome to the gritty world of GTA Simulator! Your adventure begins when you receive a message from an ally named Jackson. He needs your special skills as a shooter for a critical cause: to stop the corrupt mayor's plan to sell off the island.

To start your missions, keep a close eye on the mini-map located on your screen. It will guide you to key characters like Jackson who will assign you various tasks. These missions will test your combat and driving skills as you take on the island's criminal element.

Successfully completing missions will earn you valuable rewards and diamonds. Use these to progress further in the game. Navigate the city, engage in intense firefights, and follow the story to defeat the gangsters and ultimately save the island. The city is yours to explore, but stay focused on your objectives to succeed!
