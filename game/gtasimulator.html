GTA Simulator

How to Play GTA Simulator

The game begins with Jackson texting you, calling you to his side. Locate him on the small in-game map and head to his position. Once you meet Jackson, he will recognize your skills as a shooter and assign you various missions.

Your primary objective is to prevent the mayor from selling the island. You'll need to defeat gangsters and other opposing parties who are disrupting the island's peace. As you successfully complete these tasks, you will earn valuable rewards and diamonds. Utilize the map to navigate and track your objectives, engage in combat, and make strategic decisions to save the island from its impending doom.
