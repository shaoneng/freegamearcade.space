How to Play GTA Simulator

Dive into the action-packed world of GTA Simulator, a free online game that brings the thrilling experience of the famous GTA series directly to your web browser. No downloads are needed—just jump in and start your adventure!

Your story begins when you receive a text from a man named Jackson. He needs a skilled shooter like you for a critical mission. The mayor is planning to sell the island for next to nothing, and ruthless gangsters are causing chaos. It's up to you to step in and restore order.

Follow the mini-map to find Jackson and receive your tasks. As you complete missions, you'll fight rival gangs, disrupt corrupt deals, and become the hero the island needs. Successfully fulfilling your objectives will earn you valuable rewards and diamonds to help you on your journey.

Please Note: This game includes mature themes, such as violence and strong language, that may not be suitable for all players.
