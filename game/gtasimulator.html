GTA Simulator

How to Play GTA Simulator

Welcome to the gritty world of GTA Simulator! Your adventure begins when you receive a message from Jackson. He needs your skills as a shooter for a critical mission. Your first task is to locate him using the mini-map and make your way to his position to receive your briefing.

The island is in peril! A corrupt mayor is planning to sell it for his own gain, and ruthless gangsters are eliminating anyone who stands in the way. It's up to you to intervene. Follow Jackson's instructions, complete challenging missions, and take down the criminal elements threatening the city. As you successfully complete tasks, you'll earn valuable rewards and diamonds to help you on your quest.

Please note: This game features mature themes, including violence and strong language, that may not be suitable for all players.
