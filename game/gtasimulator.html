GTA Simulator

How to Play GTA Simulator

Dive into the gritty world of GTA Simulator and become the island's last hope. Your journey begins when you receive a text from Jackson. He needs your skills as a shooter for a critical mission. Your first task is to locate him by following the marker on your mini-map.

Once you meet Jackson, you'll learn about the corrupt mayor's plan to sell off the island. You're not the only one against this deal, but other factions are causing chaos and violence. It's up to you to handle the situation with precision and skill.

Follow the objectives given to you to progress through the story. Complete missions to earn valuable rewards and diamonds, which will help you in your fight. Navigate the city, engage in intense shootouts, and thwart the plans of rival gangsters to ultimately save the island from being lost forever.

Note: This game features mature themes, including violence and strong language, and is intended for a mature audience.
