How to Play GTA Simulator

Welcome to the high-stakes world of GTA Simulator! Your adventure starts when you receive a text from a character named Jackson. He needs your help as a skilled shooter to tackle a major crisis on the island.

Your primary objective is to follow the missions he and other characters give you. Use the mini-map on your screen to find mission locations, key characters, and objectives. The central plot revolves around stopping the corrupt mayor from selling the island, a deal that threatens everyone's way of life.

As you navigate the city, you'll encounter rival gangs who are also against the mayor's plan, but their methods are violent and chaotic. It's up to you to complete tasks, take down enemies, and bring order to the streets. Successfully completing missions will earn you valuable rewards and diamonds, allowing you to progress and ultimately save the island from collapse.
