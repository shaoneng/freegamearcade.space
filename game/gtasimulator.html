GTA Simulator

How to Play GTA Simulator

Welcome to the high-stakes world of GTA Simulator! Your adventure begins when you receive a text message from a man named Jackson. He needs a skilled shooter like you to help save the island from a corrupt mayor who's trying to sell it for a pittance.

Your primary objective is to follow the missions Jackson gives you. Use the mini-map on your screen to navigate the city and find your mission locations. As you complete these tasks, you'll uncover the conspiracy and work towards thwarting the mayor's plans.

The city isn't friendly. You'll encounter rival gangsters who want to stop you. Be prepared to fight your way through them to complete your objectives. For every successful mission, you'll earn valuable rewards and diamonds, helping you progress in the game. Can you defeat the gangsters and save the island?

Please Note: This game includes mature themes that may not be suitable for all players.
