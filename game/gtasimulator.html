How to Play GTA Simulator

Welcome to the high-stakes world of GTA Simulator! Your adventure begins when you get a text from an associate named Jackson, who needs your unique set of skills. Your first objective is to locate him by following the marker on your mini-map.

Once you meet Jackson, you'll learn about the city's corrupt mayor who is trying to sell off the island. It's up to you to stop him. Jackson will assign you a series of missions where you'll confront rival gangsters and disrupt the mayor's corrupt dealings. Pay close attention to your mission objectives and use the city map to navigate to key locations.

As you successfully complete tasks, you will earn rewards and diamonds, allowing you to progress further. The game is played using standard controls for driving and shooting, which will feel familiar to fans of the genre. Immerse yourself in the story, take down the bad guys, and become the hero the island needs! Please note, this game features mature themes and is intended for an older audience.
