How to Play GTA Simulator

Welcome to GTA Simulator, the free-to-play browser version of the legendary open-world action series! In this game, your main objective is to complete a series of missions to earn rewards and progress through the story. You don't need to download anythingâ€”just jump right into the action.

Your adventure begins when you receive a text from a character named Jackson. He needs your help. Use the mini-map on your screen to find his location and head over to him to receive your first task. As a renowned shooter, your skills are in high demand.

The central conflict revolves around a corrupt mayor who plans to sell the island for a pittance. You are hired to put a stop to this deal. However, you're not the only one with an interest in the island's fate.
