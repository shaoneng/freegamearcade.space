How to Play GTA Simulator

Welcome to the island! In GTA Simulator, you step into the shoes of a skilled shooter tasked with a critical mission: saving the island from a corrupt mayor and ruthless gangsters. This game brings the classic open-world action experience to your browser, no download required.

Your adventure begins when you receive a text from a contact named Jackson. Use the mini-map at the bottom of your screen to navigate the city and find his location. He will be your guide, providing you with a series of missions to undermine the mayor's plan to sell the island for a pittance.

As you progress, you'll engage in intense firefights, complete challenging tasks, and confront hostile gangs who want a piece of the action. Successfully completing these missions will earn you rewards and diamonds, allowing you to become a more formidable force. Keep an eye on your objectives, use the map to your advantage, and show everyone who really runs this city!
