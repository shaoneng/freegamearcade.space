GTA Simulator

How to Play GTA Simulator

Dive into GTA Simulator, the free-to-play, browser-based version of the iconic open-world action series. There's no need to download anything; you can jump straight into the action and start your adventure on a crime-ridden island.

The story kicks off when you get a text from a man named Jackson. He recognizes your skills as a shooter and has a critical mission for you. The mayor is planning to sell the island for a pittance, and it's up to you to intervene. Follow the mini-map to find Jackson and begin receiving tasks. As you complete these missions, you'll earn valuable rewards and diamonds.

Your ultimate objective is to thwart the mayor's plan and defeat the ruthless gangsters who are trying to seize control. Put your combat skills to the test, save the island, and become a local hero!

Disclaimer: This game includes mature themes such as violence and strong language that may not be suitable for all players. Player discretion is advised.
