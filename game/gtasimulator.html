How to Play GTA Simulator

Welcome to the gritty world of GTA Simulator! You are a talented shooter who has just arrived on an island with a big problem. The mayor is trying to sell it, and ruthless gangsters are causing chaos. It's up to you to step in and save the day.

Your journey begins when you receive a text from a man named Jackson. He needs your help and has missions for you. Check the mini-map on your screen to find his location and drive over to meet him. This will kickstart your first set of tasks.

As you complete missions, you will engage in combat, navigate the city, and follow the unfolding story. Successfully finishing tasks will earn you valuable rewards and diamonds. Use your skills to defeat the gangsters and put a stop to the corrupt deal to sell the island.
