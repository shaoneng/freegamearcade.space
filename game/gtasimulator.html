GTA Simulator

How to Play GTA Simulator

Welcome to the island, where your skills as a shooter are desperately needed! In GTA Simulator, your adventure begins when you receive a text from a man named Jackson. He needs your help to stop a corrupt mayor from selling the island.

Your main objective is to complete missions to thwart this plan and take down rival gangs. Keep an eye on the mini-map on your screen to find the location of mission givers like Jackson. Drive or run to their location to receive your tasks.

As you successfully complete these missions, you'll earn valuable rewards and diamonds. Use your earnings to become the ultimate force against the island's criminal element. The fate of the city is in your hands—get ready to fight for its future!
