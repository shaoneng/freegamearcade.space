GTA Simulator

How to Play GTA Simulator

Welcome to the world of GTA Simulator! Your journey begins when you receive a text from a character named Jackson. He needs your help and has some high-stakes missions for you. Your first task is to find his location on the mini-map and meet him to get started.

The island is under threat! The local mayor has a corrupt plan to sell off the land, and violent gangsters are standing in the way of anyone who opposes them. As a skilled shooter, you are the only one who can step in to restore order and save the island from being sold out.

Navigate the city using the on-screen controls and follow the map to your mission objectives. As you complete tasks given to you by Jackson, you'll fight rival gangs, disrupt corrupt deals, and earn valuable rewards and diamonds. Can you complete all the missions and become the hero the city needs?
