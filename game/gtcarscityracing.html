GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

GT Cars City Racing is a thrilling 3D driving game that offers a variety of modes for every type of racer. Whether you prefer exploring a sandbox city, competing in intense races, or customizing your dream car, this game has it all. You can even challenge a friend in the two-player split-screen mode!

Game Modes

Toy City (Sandbox Mode): This is where the real fun begins. Toy City is a sandbox-style mode full of short, physics-based missions and mini-games. Drive through a giant toy playset, knock over bowling pins, smash robots, or jump into pools. It's the perfect mode to goof around, practice stunts, and earn in-game money at your own pace.

Face to Face (Race Mode): Ready for a proper race? In this competitive mode, you'll speed toward the finish line, but there's a twist! After each lap, your car automatically switches to a new vehicle, from a truck to a sports car. You'll need to adapt quickly to complete all 5 laps and beat your rivals to the finish line.

Mega Ramps (Time Trial): Prepare for a high-stakes time trial. In Mega Ramps, you'll race down massive ramps while a giant robot tries to destroy your car. Dodge obstacles and attacks to survive and cross the finish line before your time runs out or your car's health hits zero.

Customization

Head to the "Customize" tab from the main menu to make your GT car uniquely yours. You can change its body color, add cool effects, and even paint the rims. Show off your style on the streets of Toy City!
