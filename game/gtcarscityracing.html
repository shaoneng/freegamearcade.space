GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

Welcome to the driver's seat of GT Cars City Racing! Your goal is to master three unique game modes, customize your dream car, and have a blast, either solo or with a friend in split-screen mode.

Player 1 Controls: Use the WASD keys to drive your car.
Player 2 Controls: Use the Arrow keys to drive.

From the main menu, you can select your game mode or visit the Customize tab to give your car a personal touch with different colors and rim effects.

Game Modes:

Toy City: This is a sandbox-style mode where you can drive freely through a giant toy playset. Complete fun, physics-based mini-missions like smashing robots or knocking over bowling pins to earn money and have fun.

Face to Face: A competitive race mode with a twist! After each lap, your car instantly switches to a new one, like a truck or a mini SUV. Be the first to adapt and complete all five laps to claim victory.

Mega Ramps: Race against the clock in this intense time trial. Speed down massive ramps while a giant robot tries to destroy you. Dodge its attacks and reach the finish line before your health bar depletes or time runs out!
