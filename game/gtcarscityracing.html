GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

GT Cars City Racing is a wild 3D driving game where you can race solo or challenge a friend in split-screen mode. Get behind the wheel of super sport GT cars and dive into a world of sandbox exploration, intense races, and even robot battles!

Game Modes

Toy City (Sandbox Mode): This is where the real fun begins. Toy City is a sandbox-style mode where the entire map is a giant toy playset. Drive around a colorful world filled with quick, physics-based mini-games and missions. Smash robots, knock over bowling pins, jump into pools, and complete fun stunts to earn money at your own pace.

Face to Face (Race Mode): Ready for a proper race? In this competitive mode, you'll speed through 5 laps. The catch? Your car changes every time you cross the finish lineâ€”switching between trucks, mini SUVs, and sports cars. You'll need to adapt quickly to be the first to complete all 5 laps and win!

Mega Ramps (Time Trial): Test your survival skills in this time trial mode. Speed down massive ramps while a giant robot tries to destroy you by throwing objects or attacking your car directly. Keep an eye on your health bar and the clock to cross the finish line before you're taken out!

Customization

Head to the 'Customize' tab from the main menu to personalize your ride. You can change your car's color, add effects, and even paint the rims to make your GT car uniquely yours.

Controls

Player 1: Use WASD keys to drive.

Player 2: Use Arrow keys to drive.

With its vibrant 3D graphics, insane stunts, and multiple game modes, GT Cars City Racing offers endless fun. Buckle up and start your engine!
