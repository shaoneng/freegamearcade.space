GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

Welcome to the high-speed world of GT Cars City Racing! This vibrant 3D driving game offers action-packed fun for solo players and competitive split-screen action for two. Master your driving skills across three exciting game modes.

Toy City (Sandbox): Jump into a massive sandbox playground filled with mini-games and challenges. Drive around freely, smash giant bowling pins, battle robots, or launch your car into pools. It's the perfect mode to practice stunts and earn cash at your own pace.

Face to Face (Race): Challenge a friend or the AI in this unique race. The twist? Your car changes every single lap! Adapt quickly to different vehicles, from sports cars to trucks, and be the first to complete five laps to claim victory.

Mega Ramps (Time Trial): It's you against the clock and a giant, car-destroying robot! Speed down enormous ramps while dodging attacks and obstacles. Reach the finish line before your time runs out or your car is wrecked to win.

Controls:

Player 1: Use W, A, S, D to drive.

Player 2: Use the Arrow Keys to drive.

Don't forget to visit the garage to customize your GT car with fresh paint and cool rims!
