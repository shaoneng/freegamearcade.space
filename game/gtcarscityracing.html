GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

Get ready to burn rubber in GT Cars City Racing! This game offers multiple ways to play, whether you're flying solo or racing against a friend in split-screen mode. The controls are simple and intuitive:

For Player 1, use the W, A, S, D keys to drive your car. To get a burst of speed, press the Left Shift key for nitro.

For Player 2, use the Arrow Keys for navigation and the N key to activate your nitro boost.

Dive into three exciting game modes. In Toy City, you enter a sandbox world where you can freely explore and complete fun, physics-based mini-missions like knocking over bowling pins or smashing robots to earn cash. For a competitive rush, try Face to Face, a unique five-lap race where your car automatically switches to a new vehicle each lap. Adapt quickly to win! Finally, test your survival skills in Mega Ramps, a time trial where you must speed down enormous ramps while a giant robot tries to destroy you. Use your earnings to customize your GT car in the garage, changing colors and even painting the rims!
