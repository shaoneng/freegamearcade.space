GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

GT Cars City Racing is a high-octane 3D driving game that offers something for every racing fan. You can enjoy the game solo or challenge a friend in the exciting split-screen mode. Get ready to drive incredible super sport GT cars, explore a vast sandbox playground, engage in head-to-head races, and even battle robots!

The game features three distinct modes and a robust customization garage to make your ride truly unique.

Toy City (Sandbox Mode): This is where the real fun begins! Toy City is a sandbox-style mode set in a giant, colorful toy playset. It's filled with short, physics-based missions and mini-games. Knock over bowling pins, smash robots, or launch your car into pools. These missions are all about experimenting, performing fun stunts, and earning money at your own pace. It's the perfect mode to just goof around and have a blast.

Face to Face (Race Mode): Ready for a proper race? In this mode, you'll speed toward the finish line, but there's a twist! Every time you complete a lap, your car instantly changes to a new vehicle, like a truck, mini SUV, or another sports car. You'll go through 5 vehicle swaps, and the first racer to complete all 5 laps wins!

Mega Ramps (Time Trial): This mode is pure adrenaline. You'll drive down massive ramps in a time-trial race while a giant robot tries to destroy you. It will throw objects and directly attack your car. If your health bar depletes, you fall off the edge, or time runs out, it's game over. Can you survive the chaos and reach the finish line?

Customization: Head to the Customize tab from the main menu to personalize your GT car. Whether you want a fiery red paint job, glowing blue accents, or neon rims, you can make your car look exactly how you want it. Change colors, apply effects, and even paint the rims to stand out on the track.
