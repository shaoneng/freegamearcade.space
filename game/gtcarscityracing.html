GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

GT Cars City Racing is a fun 3D driving game where you can race solo or compete with a friend in split-screen mode. The game packs up everything you love in car games: super sport GT cars, a sandbox mode with a great playground, head-to-head races and robot wars!

Game Modes

There are three different game modes plus a customization garage in GT Cars City Racing. Each offers a unique way to play.

Toy City (Sandbox Mode): This is where the real fun begins. Toy City is a sandbox-style mode full of short missions and mini-games. The entire map feels like a giant toy playset! Each mission is designed to be quick, fun, and physics-based, allowing you to experiment, perform stunts, and earn money. Play at your own pace and replay your favorites—it's the perfect mode to just goof around in a fun, colorful world.

Face to Face (Race Mode): Ready for a proper race? In this mode, you speed toward the finish line, but with a twist: every time you complete a lap, you instantly switch to a different vehicle (like a truck, mini SUV, or sports car). You'll go through 5 vehicle swaps. The first to complete all 5 laps wins!

Mega Ramps (Time Trial): In this intense time trial, you drive down massive ramps while a giant robot tries to stop you by throwing objects or damaging your car directly. If your health bar hits zero, you fall off the edge, or time runs out, the race is over. Can you survive the madness and cross the finish line?

Customization

Want a fiery red GT over a glowing blue sports car with neon rims? It's all here. Head to the 'Customize' tab on the main menu and make your car look exactly how you want. You can change its color, paint the rims, and add special effects.

With its fun toylike maps, multiple game modes, and two-player split-screen feature, GT Cars City Racing is your new go-to browser game for laughs, races, and stunt madness. So buckle up, customize your ride, and play it free now!
