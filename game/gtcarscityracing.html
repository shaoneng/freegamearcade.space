GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

Welcome to the high-octane world of GT Cars City Racing! This game offers a thrilling driving experience with multiple game modes you can enjoy solo or with a friend in split-screen.

The game features three main modes:

  • Toy City (Sandbox): Jump into this free-roam paradise! Toy City is a giant playground filled with fun mini-games and challenges. Drive around, knock over bowling pins, smash robots, and complete quick, physics-based missions to earn cash for customizing your ride. It's the perfect mode to practice your stunts and just have fun.
  • Face to Face (Race): Ready for a competitive challenge? In this mode, you'll race against rivals for 5 laps. The twist? Your car automatically changes after each lap! You'll need to adapt quickly to different vehicles, from sports cars to trucks, to stay ahead. The first racer to complete all five laps wins.
  • Mega Ramps (Time Trial): Test your survival skills in this intense mode. You'll race down massive ramps against the clock while a giant robot tries to destroy your car. Dodge attacks and stay on the track to reach the finish line before your health bar depletes or time runs out.

Before you start, don't forget to visit the Customize garage to personalize your GT car. Change its color, add cool effects, and even paint the rims to create a ride that's uniquely yours. Controls are displayed in-game, typically using WASD or Arrow Keys for Player 1 and a separate set for Player 2.
