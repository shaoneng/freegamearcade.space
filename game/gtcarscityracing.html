GT Cars City Racing

About GT Cars City Racing

GT Cars City Racing is a thrilling 3D driving game that offers a variety of experiences, whether you want to race solo or challenge a friend in split-screen mode. Dive into a world filled with super sport GT cars, a creative sandbox playground, intense head-to-head races, and even robot battles!

Game Modes

Toy City (Sandbox Mode): This is where the real fun begins. Toy City is a sandbox-style mode set in a giant toy playset, filled with short, physics-based missions and mini-games. Knock over bowling pins, smash robots, jump into pools, and complete fun stunts to earn money. It's the perfect mode to goof around and explore at your own pace.

Face to Face (Race Mode): Ready for a proper race? In this competitive mode, you'll speed towards the finish line, but there's a twist! Your car changes every lap—switching between trucks, mini SUVs, and sports cars. You must adapt quickly to complete all five laps and be the first to win.

Mega Ramps (Time Trial): In this intense time trial, you'll drive down massive ramps while a giant robot tries to stop you by throwing objects and damaging your car. If your health bar hits zero, you fall off the edge, or time runs out, the race is over. Can you survive the madness and cross the finish line?

Customize Your Ride

Head to the Customize tab on the main menu to make your car truly your own. Whether you want a fiery red GT or a glowing blue sports car with neon rims, the options are there. You can change car colors, add effects, and even paint the rims to create your perfect look.

With its bright 3D visuals, wild stunts, quick load times, and fun challenges, GT Cars City Racing is your new go-to browser game for laughs, races, and stunt madness. Buckle up and play for free now!
