GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

Welcome to GT Cars City Racing! This game offers a variety of exciting modes for both solo and two-player fun. Get behind the wheel, master the controls, and explore everything the city has to offer.

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: W, A, S, D keys
  • Nitro: Left SHIFT

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Nitro: M key

Game Modes:

  • Toy City (Sandbox): Jump into a massive playground full of fun mini-games. Drive around, complete physics-based missions like knocking over bowling pins or smashing robots, and earn in-game currency to upgrade your ride.
  • Face to Face (Race): Compete in a head-to-head race with a twist! After each of the five laps, your car automatically changes, forcing you to adapt your driving style. The first racer to complete all five laps wins.
  • Mega Ramps (Time Trial): Race against the clock down enormous ramps while a giant robot attempts to destroy you. Dodge obstacles and attacks to survive and reach the finish line before time runs out or your car is wrecked.

Don't forget to visit the Customize garage to spend your earnings! You can change your car's color, apply cool effects, and even paint the rims to create a unique look.
