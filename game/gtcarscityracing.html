GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

Welcome to the high-octane world of GT Cars City Racing! Your goal is to master three unique game modes, earn money for stunts and victories, and customize your ultimate GT car. You can play solo or bring a friend for split-screen competition.

Controls:

Player 1:
- Move: W, A, S, D
- NOS (Boost): Shift
- Restart: R

Player 2:
- Move: Arrow Keys
- NOS (Boost): N
- Restart: P

Game Modes:

Toy City (Sandbox Mode): This is your personal playground! Drive freely through a giant toy-themed city. Complete fun, physics-based mini-missions like knocking over bowling pins or smashing robots to earn cash and experiment with wild stunts at your own pace.

Face to Face (Race Mode): A competitive race with a twist! In this 5-lap race, your vehicle changes every time you cross the finish line. You might start in a sports car and find yourself in a truck on the next lap. Adapt quickly to different vehicles to be the first to complete all five laps.

Mega Ramps (Time Trial): It's you against the clock and a giant robot! Speed down massive ramps while a huge robot tries to destroy your car. Dodge obstacles and attacks to reach the finish line before your health bar or the timer runs out.

Customize: Head to the garage from the main menu to spend your earnings. Change your car's body paint, add effects, and even paint the rims to make your ride stand out.
