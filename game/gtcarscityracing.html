GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

GT Cars City Racing is a high-octane 3D driving game that you can enjoy solo or with a friend using the split-screen feature. It combines everything you love about car games: sleek GT sports cars, a creative sandbox mode, intense races, and even robot battles!

Explore three unique game modes and a detailed customization garage:

Toy City (Sandbox Mode): Dive into a giant toy playset! This sandbox mode is filled with short, physics-based missions and mini-games. Smash through bowling pins, crash into robots, or launch your car into pools. It's the perfect place to practice stunts, earn money, and have fun without the pressure of a race.

Face to Face (Race Mode): Get ready for a competitive twist! In this mode, you race towards the finish line, but there's a catch—your car changes every lap. You might be in a sports car one moment and a truck the next. The first player to complete all five laps with five different vehicles wins!

Mega Ramps (Time Trial): Race against the clock on massive ramps while a giant robot does everything it can to stop you. Dodge obstacles and direct attacks to keep your car's health from hitting zero. If you fall off, run out of time, or your car is destroyed, it's game over. Can you survive the chaos and reach the finish line?

Customization: Visit the Customize tab to personalize your ride. Choose from a variety of colors for the body and rims, and add special effects to make your GT car truly stand out from the crowd.
