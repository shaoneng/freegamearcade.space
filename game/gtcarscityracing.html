How to Play GT Cars City Racing

Welcome to the driver's seat of GT Cars City Racing! This game is packed with high-speed action and can be played solo or with a friend in split-screen mode. Hereâ€™s how to get started:

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: W, A, S, D or Arrow Keys
  • NOS / Nitro Boost: Left SHIFT
  • Change Camera View: C
  • Restart Position: R

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • NOS / Nitro Boost: M
  • Change Camera View: K
  • Restart Position: L

The game features three unique modes:

  • Toy City: Jump into a sandbox playground filled with mini-games. Knock over bowling pins, smash robots, and perform awesome stunts to earn cash at your own pace.
  • Face to Face: A competitive race where your car changes every lap! Adapt quickly to trucks, sports cars, and SUVs to finish all five laps before your opponent.
  • Mega Ramps: A thrilling time trial where you race down enormous ramps while a giant robot tries to obliterate you. Dodge attacks and reach the finish line before it's too late!

Head to the Customize tab from the main menu to personalize your ride with cool paints, effects, and custom rims to race in style!
