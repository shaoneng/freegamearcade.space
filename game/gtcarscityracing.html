GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

Welcome to the high-octane world of GT Cars City Racing! This game offers multiple modes for single-player and two-player split-screen action. Master the controls to dominate the city streets and mega ramps.

Player 1 Controls: Use WASD keys to drive. Player 2 Controls: Use Arrow keys to drive. Navigate the menus to select your desired game mode: Toy City, Face to Face, or Mega Ramps.

In Toy City, explore a vibrant sandbox environment. This mode is a free-roam playground filled with mini-games and challenges. Complete tasks like knocking down bowling pins or smashing robots to earn cash for customizing your car.

In Face to Face mode, compete in a 5-lap race against a friend or AI. The twist? Your car changes with every lap! You'll need to adapt quickly to different vehicles, from sports cars to trucks, to be the first to cross the finish line.

The Mega Ramps mode is a thrilling time-trial challenge. Speed down massive ramps while a giant robot attempts to destroy your car. Dodge attacks and obstacles to survive and reach the end before time runs out or your car is destroyed.

Don't forget to visit the Customize garage to spend your earnings on new paint jobs, effects, and even colored rims to make your GT car truly your own!
