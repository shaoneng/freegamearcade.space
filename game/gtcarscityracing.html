GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

GT Cars City Racing is a fun 3D driving game where you can race solo or compete with a friend in split-screen mode. The game packs up everything you love in car games: super sport GT cars, a sandbox mode with a great playground, head-to-head races, and robot wars!

Game Modes

There are three different game modes plus a customization garage in GT Cars City Racing.

  • Toy City (Sandbox Mode): This is where the real fun begins. Toy City is a sandbox-style mode full of short, physics-based missions and mini-games. The entire map feels like a giant toy playset! Drive around smashing robots, knocking over bowling pins, jumping into pools, and earning money while performing fun stunts. It's the perfect mode to just goof around.
  • Face to Face (Race Mode): Ready for a proper race? In this mode, you'll speed toward the finish line, but there's a twist! Once you complete a lap, you'll instantly switch to a different car (like a truck, mini SUV, or sports car). The first to complete all 5 laps with 5 different vehicles wins!
  • Mega Ramps (Time Trial): In this time trial race mode, you drive down massive ramps while a giant robot tries to stop you by throwing objects or damaging your car directly. If your health bar hits zero, you fall off the edge, or time runs out, you're out! Can you survive the madness and cross the finish line?

Customization

If you want a fiery red GT car or a glowing blue sports car with neon rims, it's all here. Head to the "Customize" tab on the main menu and make your car look exactly how you want. You can change the paint, rims, and add cool effects.
