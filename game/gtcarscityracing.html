GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

Welcome to GT Cars City Racing! This game offers three exciting modes, a customization garage, and the option to play solo or with a friend in split-screen mode. Your goal is to race, perform stunts, and have fun across the various challenges.

Toy City (Sandbox Mode): This is a free-roam sandbox world that feels like a giant toy playset. Drive around and complete fun, physics-based mini-games like smashing robots or knocking down bowling pins. It’s a great way to practice your driving and earn money for customizations without the pressure of a race.

Face to Face (Race Mode): In this competitive mode, you'll race against rivals for 5 laps. The catch? Your car changes with every lap! You must adapt quickly to different vehicles—from sports cars to trucks—to stay ahead and claim victory.

Mega Ramps (Time Trial): Test your survival skills in this time-based race. You'll speed down massive ramps while a giant robot tries to destroy your car. Dodge attacks and avoid falling off the edge to reach the finish line before your health or time runs out.

Controls:
Player 1: Use the WASD keys to drive.
Player 2: Use the Arrow Keys to drive.
