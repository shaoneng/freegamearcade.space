GT Cars City Racing

How to Play GT Cars City Racing

Get behind the wheel in GT Cars City Racing, an exciting 3D driving adventure with multiple ways to play. You can race by yourself or challenge a friend in a thrilling two-player split-screen mode. The goal is to master each game mode and become the ultimate city racer!

Game Modes:

- Toy City (Sandbox): In this free-roam mode, you can explore a massive toy-themed city. Discover fun mini-games like smashing robots or knocking down giant bowling pins. It's a great way to practice your skills, perform stunts, and earn in-game cash.

- Face to Face (Race): A unique competitive race where your car switches every lap! You'll need to adapt to new vehicles on the fly to complete all five laps before your opponent.

- Mega Ramps (Time Trial): A high-stakes race against time. Speed down enormous ramps while a giant robot attempts to wreck your car. Survive the chaos and cross the finish line to win!

Customization: Don't forget to visit the garage! Use the money you've earned to change your car's paint, add special effects, and even color the rims to create a ride that's uniquely yours.
