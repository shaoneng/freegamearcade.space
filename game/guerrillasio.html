Guerrillas io

How to Play Guerrillas io

Welcome to the high-stakes world of Guerrillas io! You are a member of an elite tactical unit, and your mission is to dominate the battlefield. Before the action begins, you'll need to select a game mode from the main menu. Each mode offers a unique objective, so choose wisely and prepare for intense combat on maps that might feel familiar to fans of classic first-person shooters.

Mastering the controls is key to survival. Use the WASD keys to move your character around the map. Aim your weapon using the mouse, and click the left mouse button to fire. Press the R key to reload your weapon when you're out of ammo, and use the Spacebar to jump over obstacles or get to a better vantage point.

Gain a tactical advantage by customizing your arsenal. Navigate to the "Armoury" section from the game's menu. Here, you can spend your earnings to buy new, more powerful weapons. You can also upgrade your existing firearms by adding attachments like scopes for better accuracy, silencers for stealthy takedowns, and new skins to personalize your look. A well-equipped soldier is a dangerous one, so make sure your gear is always ready for the fight!
