Guerrillas io

How to Play Guerrillas io

Welcome to Guerrillas io, a fast-paced multiplayer shooter where you join an elite team in heated online battles. The game maps are inspired by the classic tactical shooter, Counter-Strike, so veteran players might find the layouts familiar.

Before deploying, you must select a game mode. Once you're in the game, your objective is to work with your teammates to eliminate the opposing force and dominate the match. Use standard FPS controls to move, aim, and shoot your way through the enemy lines.

Gain an edge by visiting the "Armoury" from the main menu. Here, you can customize your loadout by purchasing new weapons or upgrading your existing ones. Enhance your guns with attachments like scopes and silencers, and change their appearance to match your style. Prepare your equipment, join your squad, and show everyone your combat skills!
