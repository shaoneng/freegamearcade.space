Guerrillas io

How to Play Guerrillas io

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of Guerrillas io! As a member of an elite team, your mission is to outgun and outmaneuver your opponents in fast-paced multiplayer combat. The game draws inspiration from classic shooters like Counter-Strike, so veteran players will feel right at home on the familiar maps.

Use the standard WASD keys to move your soldier around the map. Aim with your mouse and click the left mouse button to fire your weapon. Use the right mouse button to aim down sights for better accuracy. Spacebar allows you to jump, and the Shift key lets you sprint. Reload your weapon with the R key.

Before deploying, you can select from various game modes. Once in the main menu, don't forget to check out the 'Armoury'. Here, you can customize your weapons with new scopes, silencers, and skins, or even purchase entirely new guns to perfect your loadout for the battles ahead. Now, gear up and lead your team to victory!
