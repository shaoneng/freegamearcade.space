Guerrillas io

How to Play Guerrillas io

Step into the boots of an elite soldier in Guerrillas.io, a fast-paced multiplayer shooter that pits you against players from around the world. Your mission is simple: eliminate the opposing team and lead your squad to victory on battlegrounds inspired by the iconic maps of Counter-Strike.

Before deploying, you'll need to select a game mode. Whether you prefer the classic chaos of Team Deathmatch or other strategic objectives, there's a mode that fits your playstyle. Once you've chosen your battle, it's time to gear up and join the fray.

Gain an edge over your opponents by customizing your arsenal. Visit the "Armoury" from the main menu to unlock, purchase, and modify your weapons. Equip your guns with powerful attachments like scopes for long-range accuracy, silencers for stealthy takedowns, or simply change the appearance with unique skins. A well-prepared soldier is a deadly one!
