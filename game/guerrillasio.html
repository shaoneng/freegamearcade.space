Guerrillas io

How to Play Guerrillas io

Step into the boots of an elite soldier in Guerrillas.io, a fast-paced multiplayer combat game. Your mission is to join a team and dominate the battlefield against other players online.

Before the battle begins, you must select a game mode. Each mode offers a unique objective and challenge, so choose wisely based on your preferred playstyle. Teamwork is crucial for victory!

Gain a tactical advantage by customizing your loadout. Visit the 'Armoury' from the main menu to view and upgrade your weapons. You can purchase new guns, modify existing ones with better scopes and silencers, and change their appearance to match your style.

The controls are intuitive for any FPS fan: Use the WASD keys to move, aim with your mouse, and click the left mouse button to fire. Aim down sights with the right mouse button, reload with the R key, and jump using the Spacebar.
