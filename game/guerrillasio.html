How to Play Guerrillas io

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of Guerrillas io! You are a member of an elite special forces team, and your mission is to dominate the opposition in fast-paced multiplayer combat. Your weapons are ready, your gear is preppedâ€”all that's missing is you.

Before deploying, you must select a game mode. Engage in classic team-based battles on maps that may feel familiar, as they are inspired by the iconic locations from Counter-Strike. Work with your teammates to outsmart and outgun the enemy team to secure victory.

Gain a tactical advantage by customizing your arsenal. Navigate to the "Armoury" from the main menu to view, modify, and purchase weapons. You can upgrade your firearms, change their appearance with new skins, and attach accessories like scopes and silencers to suit your playstyle.

The basic controls are intuitive for any FPS player. Use the WASD keys to move around the map, your Mouse to aim and shoot, the R key to reload your weapon, and the Spacebar to jump.
