Guerrillas io

How to Play Guerrillas io

Step into the boots of an elite soldier in Guerrillas.io, a fast-paced multiplayer FPS where your tactical skill is everything. You've been called into battle, and your team is counting on you.

Before diving into the action, select your preferred game mode. The maps, inspired by legendary titles like Counter-Strike, will test your strategic thinking and reflexes against players from around the world.

A key part of the battle is preparation. Head to the 'Armoury' from the main menu to fully customize your weapon loadout. You can purchase powerful new guns, modify existing ones with different scopes and appearances, or even equip a silencer for a quieter, more tactical approach.

Once your gear is set and your strategy is in mind, join a match and lead your team to victory. The battle awaits!
