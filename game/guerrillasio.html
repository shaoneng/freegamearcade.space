Guerrillas io

How to Play Guerrillas io

Welcome to Guerrillas io, a fast-paced multiplayer shooter where you join an elite combat team. Your mission is to dominate the battlefield in intense online matches. The game maps are inspired by classic tactical shooters like Counter-Strike, so veteran players will feel right at home!

To begin, you must choose a game mode from the main menu. Select from options like Team Deathmatch or Free-for-All to suit your playstyle. Once you've selected a mode and joined a match, your objective is to eliminate enemy players and lead your team to victory before time runs out.

Gain an advantage by customizing your loadout. Head to the "Armoury" section from the main menu to view, modify, and purchase new weapons. You can upgrade your guns with different scopes, add silencers for stealth, and change their appearance to create the ultimate weapon.

Controls are standard for PC shooters: use the WASD keys to move, your Mouse to aim and shoot, R to reload your weapon, and the Spacebar to jump over obstacles. Prepare your weapons, choose your strategy, and jump into the action!
