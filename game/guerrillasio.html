Guerrillas io

How to Play Guerrillas io

Welcome to the elite squad, soldier! In Guerrillas.io, your objective is to outgun the opposing team in a fast-paced multiplayer shootout. Before you deploy, you'll need to choose a game mode that suits your style. Once in the game, use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the map, aim, and fire at enemies. The controls are standard for FPS games: WASD keys for movement, mouse to aim, and left-click to shoot.

Don't forget to visit the "Armoury" from the main menu. Here, you can customize your loadout by purchasing new weapons or upgrading your existing ones. Enhance your guns with different scopes, change their appearance, or add a silencer for a stealthy approach.

Prepare your gear, join a match, and lead your team to victory on the battlefield!
