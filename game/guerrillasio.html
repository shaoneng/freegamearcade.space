Guerrillas io

How to Play Guerrillas io

Your mission in Guerrillas io is to join an elite team and dominate the battlefield in intense multiplayer combat. The game draws inspiration from classic shooters like Counter-Strike, so the maps and fast-paced action might feel familiar.

Use the W, A, S, D keys to move your character around the map. Aim your weapon using the mouse, and click the left mouse button to shoot. Use the right mouse button to aim down sights for better accuracy. Reload your weapon with the R key and jump with the Spacebar.

Before you jump into a match, you can choose from different game modes. To gain an advantage, head to the 'Armoury' from the main menu. Here, you can customize your loadout, buy new weapons, and upgrade your existing firearms with attachments like improved scopes, silencers, and new skins. Choose your gear wisely and lead your team to victory!
