Guerrillas io

How to Play Guerrillas io

Welcome to the high-stakes battlefield of Guerrillas.io! You are a member of an elite special forces team called into action. Your mission is to outgun and outmaneuver the opposing team in fast-paced combat arenas reminiscent of classic shooters like Counter-Strike.

To begin, simply choose a game mode that suits your style. Once in the game, use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the map and take down enemies. The controls are standard for FPS games:

  • WASD Keys: Move your character forward, backward, and side-to-side.
  • Mouse: Aim your weapon and look around the environment.
  • Left Mouse Button: Fire your weapon.
  • Right Mouse Button: Aim down sights (ADS).
  • R Key: Reload your weapon.
  • Spacebar: Jump.

Don't forget to visit the "Armoury" from the main menu! Here, you can customize your loadout, purchase new weapons, and upgrade existing ones with attachments like scopes, silencers, and new skins to enhance your combat effectiveness and personalize your look.
