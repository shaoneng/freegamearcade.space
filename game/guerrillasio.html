Guerrillas io

How to Play Guerrillas io

Welcome to the battlefield of Guerrillas.io! Your mission is to join an elite team and dominate your opponents in intense multiplayer combat. The game draws inspiration from classic shooters like Counter-Strike, so veteran players will feel right at home on the familiar maps.

Before deploying, you must first select a game mode. Choose the style of combat that suits you best and prepare to enter the fray. Once in-game, use your tactical awareness and sharp shooting to outplay the enemy team and lead your side to victory.

Gain an edge by customizing your loadout. Visit the "Armoury" from the main menu to unlock, purchase, and modify your weapons. You can equip different scopes for better accuracy, add silencers for stealthy takedowns, and upgrade your gear to match your playstyle.

Controls:
- WASD: Move
- Mouse: Aim and look
- Left Mouse Button: Shoot
- Right Mouse Button: Aim down sights
- R: Reload
- Spacebar: Jump
- Shift: Sprint
- 1, 2, 3: Switch weapons
