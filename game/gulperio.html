Gulper io

How to Play Gulper io

Welcome to the glowing depths of Gulper io! The goal is simple: become the biggest snake in the arena. Guide your gulper with your mouse, consuming the luminous orbs scattered across the map to grow longer. Be careful, thoughâ€”if your head collides with another player's body or the boundary walls, your game is over and you'll have to start again.

Mastering the controls is key to survival. Your gulper will automatically follow your mouse cursor. For a burst of speed, press and hold the Left Mouse Button or the Spacebar. This speed boost is perfect for escaping danger or racing to collect the valuable remains of a defeated opponent. However, use it wisely, as accelerating will gradually decrease your size.

Strategy is everything. When another player is eliminated, they drop all the orbs they've collected, creating a high-value target. Use your speed boost to get there first! As you grow larger, you can use your own body as a weapon. Encircle smaller players to trap them, forcing them to crash into you and leaving their precious orbs for you to collect. Dominate the leaderboard by growing massive and outsmarting the competition!
