Gulper io

How to Play Gulper io

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Gulper io! Your main objective is to become the biggest and longest snake in the arena. Control your gulper by moving your mouse; it will follow your cursor's direction. To grow, you must consume the small, glowing orbs scattered across the map.

Survival is paramount. You must avoid colliding with the arena walls or any other player's snake. A single touch will cause you to be eliminated, and you'll have to start over from the beginning. Stay alert and keep your distance, especially from larger, more dangerous opponents.

Gain a tactical advantage by using your speed boost. Press the spacebar or use the left mouse click to accelerate. This is incredibly useful for escaping tight spots or quickly swooping in to collect the valuable pellets left behind when another snake is defeated. As you grow longer, you can use your body to trap smaller snakes, forcing them to crash into you and allowing you to claim their mass.

Before you begin, don't forget to customize your gulper! Give yourself a cool nickname and choose a unique color to stand out in the vibrant, competitive battlefield. Good luck, and may the biggest gulper win!
