Gulper io

How to Play Gulper io

In Gulper io, your main objective is to grow your snake (gulper) into the largest creature on the map. You achieve this by consuming glowing light cycles scattered across the playing field. Navigate your gulper using your mouse to guide its movement.

Be cautious! The game features a multiplayer arena filled with other gulpers, and touching any of them or the map boundaries will lead to your instant elimination, forcing you to restart as a small snake. Always be aware of your surroundings, especially larger opponents.

To gain an advantage, you can temporarily boost your gulper's speed by clicking the left mouse button or pressing the spacebar. This acceleration is vital for quick escapes, swiftly collecting pellets dropped by eliminated opponents, or trapping smaller players within your growing body. When a snake is defeated, be quick to snatch their fallen pellets!

As your gulper expands, leverage its length strategically. You can encircle smaller opponents, trapping them with your body and leaving them with no escape route. Before you begin, personalize your gulper by choosing a nickname and selecting your favorite color from the slider. You can also join public game rooms or create/access private ones by adding "@roomname" to your nickname.
