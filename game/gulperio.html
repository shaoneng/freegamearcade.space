Gulper io

How to Play Gulper io

Welcome to the glowing abyss of Gulper io! The objective is simple: grow your snake-like gulper into the largest one in the arena. Guide your gulper using your mouse, collecting the bright, scattered pellets to increase your size and score.

Survival is key. You must skillfully avoid colliding with any other players and the outer walls of the playground. Any contact will cause your gulper to disintegrate, forcing you to start over from the very beginning. When another player is eliminated, they drop all the pellets they've collected, creating a feeding frenzy for those quick enough to grab them.

Gain a competitive edge by using your speed boost. Press and hold the left mouse button or the spacebar to accelerate. This is perfect for escaping dangerous situations, chasing down smaller foes, or quickly scooping up the valuable remains of a fallen opponent. As you grow longer, use your body to trap smaller gulpers, cutting off their escape routes and forcing them into a collision.

Before you begin, don't forget to customize your experience! Give your gulper a unique nickname and choose your favorite color using the slider on the main screen to stand out in the colorful battlefield.
