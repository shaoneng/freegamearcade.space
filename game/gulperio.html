Gulper io

How to Play Gulper io

Welcome to the vibrant, competitive world of Gulper.io! Your main objective is to become the largest gulper in the arena. Guide your glowing snake with your mouse to collect the luminous pellets scattered across the map. Each pellet you consume will make your gulper grow longer.

Be careful! The arena is filled with other players who are also trying to dominate. If your gulper's head touches another player's body or the walls of the arena, you will be eliminated and have to start over from the beginning. When a player is eliminated, they drop all the pellets they've collected, creating a feast for those quick enough to grab them.

You can gain a tactical advantage by using a speed boost. Press and hold the left mouse button or the spacebar to accelerate. This is perfect for escaping dangerous situations, chasing down smaller opponents, or quickly grabbing the pellets from a defeated foe. As you grow larger, you can use your body to trap other players, forcing them to crash into you. Customize your gulper with a unique nickname and color, and slither your way to the top of the leaderboard!
