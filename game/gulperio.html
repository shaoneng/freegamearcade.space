Gulper io

How to Play Gulper io

Welcome to Gulper io, an intense multiplayer arcade game where you control a glowing, snake-like creature. Your goal is simple: navigate the arena and consume as many glowing pellets as you can to grow longer and climb the leaderboard. The bigger you get, the more dominant you become!

Use your mouse to guide your gulper around the map. Be careful! A single collision with another player's body or the arena walls will cause your gulper to disintegrate, forcing you to start over. Stay vigilant and use your growing length as a strategic advantage.

Gain a tactical edge by using a speed boost. Press the Left Mouse Button or the Spacebar to accelerate. This is perfect for escaping dangerous situations or quickly snatching up the valuable pellets left behind by a defeated opponent. However, be aware that boosting will slowly decrease your size.

As you grow, you can use your body to trap smaller opponents. Encircle them carefully, leaving them no room to escape. By forcing them to crash into you, you can eliminate them from the game and feast on their remains to grow even faster!
