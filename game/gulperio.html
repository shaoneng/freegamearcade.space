Gulper io

How to Play Gulper io

Welcome to the electrifying depths of Gulper.io! In this multiplayer snake-style game, you control a luminous gulper with a simple goal: become the biggest and baddest creature in the arena. Navigate the dark playground and consume the glowing pellets scattered around to increase your length and score.

Controlling your gulper is intuitive. Simply move your mouse to direct its path. For a burst of speed, press and hold the left mouse button or the spacebar. This boost is crucial for escaping larger rivals, chasing down smaller prey, or quickly gobbling up the remains of a fallen opponent.

The ultimate rule is survival: do not collide with any other player's body or the glowing walls of the arena. A single touch means instant elimination, turning your gulper back into all the pellets you've collected. Use your growing size to your advantage by encircling and trapping smaller players, forcing them to crash into you. Stay vigilant, climb the leaderboard, and dominate the colorful chaos!
