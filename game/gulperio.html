Gulper io

How to Play Gulper io

Gulper.io is a thrilling multiplayer snake game where you control a deep-sea gulper. Your primary objective is to grow as large as possible by consuming glowing light cycles scattered across the arena. Be careful, though—colliding with another snake or the arena walls will instantly eliminate you, forcing you to start over.

Before diving in, you can customize your gulper. Choose a nickname and select your favorite color using the slider. The controls are simple: your gulper follows your mouse cursor. To get a quick burst of speed, press and hold the left mouse button or the spacebar. This is crucial for escaping danger or chasing down pellets from a defeated foe.

As you grow larger, you become a bigger target but also a greater threat. Use your size to your advantage by encircling smaller players, trapping them with your body. When another snake is eliminated, they drop all their collected pellets—a massive opportunity for growth. Use your speed boost strategically to collect these spoils before others can.

You can play in public rooms with everyone or create a private room to play with friends. To create or join a private room, simply add an '@' symbol followed by the room name to your nickname (e.g., 'MyNick@MyRoom'). If enough players use the same room name, a private game will be created.
