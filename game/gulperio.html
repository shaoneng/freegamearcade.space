Gulper io

How to Play Gulper io

Welcome to Gulper io, a thrilling multiplayer snake game where you compete to become the biggest gulper in the arena. Your main objective is to navigate the deep-sea playground, collecting glowing Tron-like light cycles to increase your size and length. Be warned: the arena is filled with other players and unforgiving walls. A single collision with either will instantly eliminate you, forcing you to start over as a tiny gulper.

Before you dive into the action, you can customize your gulper. Choose a unique nickname and select your favorite color using the slider on the main screen. Once you're ready, enter the colorful warzone. Use your mouse to steer your gulper. To gain a competitive edge, you can activate a speed boost by holding down the left mouse button or pressing the spacebar. This acceleration is crucial for dodging larger opponents and for quickly snatching up the valuable pellets left behind when another snake is eliminated.

As you grow, strategy becomes key. Use your increased length to your advantage by trapping smaller players. By strategically moving and creating circles with your body, you can corner opponents, leaving them with no escape route and forcing them to crash into you. This defensive and offensive maneuver is essential for climbing the leaderboard and dominating the game.

Gulper io also features a room system. You can join public rooms filled with players from around the world or create a private room to play with friends. To create or join a private room, simply add an '@' sign followed by the room name to your nickname (e.g., "MyName@MyRoom"). If enough players use the same room name, a private game will be created.
