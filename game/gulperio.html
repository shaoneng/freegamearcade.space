Gulper io

How to Play Gulper io

Welcome to the competitive world of Gulper.io! In this multiplayer snake game, you control a gulper, a deep-sea eel with an insatiable appetite. Your primary goal is to grow as large as possible by collecting the glowing pellets, or Tron light cycles, scattered across the arena. But be warned: the sea is full of other gulpers, and any contact with them or the walls will eliminate you, forcing you to start over as a tiny creature.

Before you dive in, you can customize your gulper. Give it a unique nickname and choose your favorite color using the slider at the bottom right of the screen. You can play in public rooms or create a private room by adding an '@' symbol followed by a room name to your nickname (e.g., "MyNick@MyRoom").

Controlling your gulper is simple: it follows your mouse cursor. To gain a competitive edge, you can activate a speed boost by holding down the left mouse button or the spacebar. This acceleration is perfect for escaping dangerous situations or for quickly snatching up the valuable pellets left behind when another snake is eliminated.

As you grow larger, strategy becomes key. Use your length to your advantage by trapping smaller opponents. By drawing circles around them, you can cut off their escape routes and force them into a collision. Dominate the playground, become the biggest gulper in the sea, and write your name on the leaderboard!
