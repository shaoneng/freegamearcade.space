Gulper io

How to Play Gulper io

Welcome to the deep-sea arena of Gulper io! Your goal is simple: become the largest gulper eel in the game. Control your snake using your mouse cursor, guiding it around the map to consume the glowing pellets scattered everywhere. Each pellet you eat makes you grow a little bit longer.

Master the controls to dominate the competition. Your gulper will automatically follow your mouse pointer. For a burst of speed, press and hold the left mouse button or the spacebar. This boost is perfect for escaping a dangerous situation or quickly gobbling up the remains of a defeated opponent. Be warned, using the boost will cause your gulper to shrink, so use it wisely!

The most important rule in Gulper io is survival. Avoid colliding with any other player's snake or the boundaries of the arena at all costs. Any contact will eliminate you, turning your hard-earned mass into pellets for others to feast on. As you grow larger, you can use your length as a weapon. Try to encircle smaller players, trapping them and forcing them to crash into your body to score an easy kill.

Before you dive in, don't forget to customize your gulper! Pick a cool nickname and use the color slider to choose your favorite look. Now you're ready to slither your way to the top of the leaderboard!
