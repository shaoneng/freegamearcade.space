How to Play Head Soccer 2022

Welcome to the wacky world of Head Soccer 2022! The goal is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the time runs out. Jump, kick, and use your character's giant head to outmaneuver the competition in fast-paced, one-on-one matches.

Begin by choosing your game mode. Select "1 Player" to take on the computer in a challenging arcade tournament, or grab a friend and battle it out in "2 Player" mode for local bragging rights.

Next, pick your favorite international soccer star. In 1 Player mode, use your mouse to select your player. In 2 Player mode, Player 1 uses the WASD keys and Player 2 uses the Arrow keys to choose their characters and play the match.
