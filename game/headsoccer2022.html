Head Soccer 2022

How to Play Head Soccer 2022

Welcome to the pitch of Head Soccer 2022! In this action-packed 2D sports game, your goal is to score more goals than your opponent in fast-paced, timed matches. Choose your favorite international football superstar and get ready to jump, kick, and head your way to victory!

Select your preferred game mode from the main menu. In 1 Player mode, you'll face off against the CPU in a series of challenging matches, each lasting 45 seconds. If you want to challenge a friend, select 2 Player mode for an intense 60-second duel on the same screen.

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use WASD keys to move and the N and M keys to kick.
  • Player 2: Use the Arrow Keys to move and the 1 and 2 keys to kick.

Master your player's movements and special kicks to outsmart your rival and claim the World Cup trophy!
