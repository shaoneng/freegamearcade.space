Head Soccer 2022

How to Play Head Soccer 2022

Welcome to the pitch! The main goal in Head Soccer 2022 is to score more goals than your opponent within the time limit. Use your player's oversized head and powerful kick to dominate the match.

First, pick your game mode from the main menu. You can play solo against the computer in 1 Player mode or challenge a friend in the exciting 2 Player mode. Next, select your favorite international football star to represent on the field.

The controls are simple and intuitive. For Player 1, use the Arrow Keys to move your character and jump. For Player 2, use the W, A, D keys. Use the designated kick buttons to perform shots and headers. Time your jumps and kicks perfectly to defend your goal and score spectacular points!
