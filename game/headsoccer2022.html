Head Soccer 2022

How to Play Head Soccer 2022

Welcome to the pitch! First, choose your game mode. Select '1 Player' to challenge the computer in fast-paced arcade matches, or choose '2 Player' to go head-to-head against a friend on the same device.

Next, pick your champion! Browse a roster of renowned international soccer stars, each with their own giant head. In single-player mode, simply click on your desired player. For two-player mode, each player will use their keys to select their character.

The objective is to score more goals than your opponent within the time limit. Use your character's head and feet to knock the ball into the opposing goal. In 1 Player mode, you have 45 seconds to prove your skill against an increasingly difficult AI. In 2 Player mode, you have a full 60 seconds to settle the score with your friend. The player with the most goals at the end wins!
