Head Soccer 2022

How to Play Head Soccer 2022

Welcome to the pitch! The main objective in Head Soccer 2022 is to outscore your opponent within the time limit. First, choose your game mode: select "1 Player" to challenge the computer or "2 Player" to go head-to-head with a friend on the same keyboard.

After picking a mode, select your favorite international football star to control. In the single-player arcade, you'll face increasingly difficult opponents in quick 45-second matches. In two-player versus mode, you'll have 60 seconds to prove who's the better player.

Player 1 Controls: Use the WASD keys to move and the N or M keys to kick.

Player 2 Controls: Use the Arrow Keys to move and the 1 or 2 keys to kick.

For mobile devices, simply use the on-screen touch controls to dominate the field. Jump, kick, and use your head to score goals and claim victory!
