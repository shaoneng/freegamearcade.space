Head Soccer 2022

How to Play Head Soccer 2022

Welcome to the wacky world of Head Soccer 2022! Your first decision is to choose your game mode. Challenge the computer in 1 Player mode for a solo test of skill, or grab a friend and go head-to-head in the exciting 2 Player mode on the same computer.

Next, pick your champion! Choose from a roster of big-headed football superstars representing countries from around the globe. Once you've selected your mode and player, you're ready to hit the pitch.

The controls are simple and designed for frantic fun. Master movement and kicking to outplay your opponent.

Player 1 Controls: Use the WASD keys to move and the N or M key to kick.

Player 2 Controls: Use the Arrow Keys to move and the 1 or 2 key (on the number pad) to kick.

The objective is straightforward: score more goals than your opponent before the timer runs out! Matches are fast-paced and full of action, so stay on your toes, defend your goal, and unleash powerful kicks to secure victory and become the Head Soccer champion!
