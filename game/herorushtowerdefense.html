Hero Rush Tower Defense

How to Play Hero Rush Tower Defense

Your primary goal in Hero Rush Tower Defense is to strategically defend your land from waves of attacking enemies. You'll achieve this by building and upgrading a variety of powerful towers and deploying unique heroes to aid in the fight.

To begin, select a level and choose your desired difficulty. Before the battle starts, you can assemble your team by picking up to three heroes from a roster of 12, each possessing distinct skills. A balanced team is key to overcoming diverse threats!

Once the game begins, you will see a map with designated circular areas. Click on these spots to build your towers. Consider the enemy path carefullyâ€”placing your towers at critical chokepoints is essential for a successful defense. You'll face different types of enemies, including ground units like pirates and barbarians, as well as flying threats, so plan your tower types accordingly.

As you progress, you can upgrade your towers to increase their power and effectiveness. Don't forget to utilize your heroes' special abilities to turn the tide of battle. You can also visit the in-game store to acquire powerful booster items like the Frost Gem or Meteor Call for an extra defensive edge when things get tough.
