Hero Rush Tower Defense

How to Play Hero Rush Tower Defense

Welcome to Hero Rush Tower Defense! Your mission is to defend your lands from waves of attacking enemies. Before each level, you'll choose your map, select up to three powerful heroes to fight alongside you, and set the game's difficulty level.

Once the game begins, you must strategically build defensive towers. Click on the designated circular areas on the map to place your towers. Your placement is key, as you'll face different types of enemies, including ground units like pirates and barbosas, and flying foes. Upgrade your towers as you progress to increase their power and effectiveness.

You're not alone in this fight! Choose from a roster of 12 unique heroes, each with special abilities to turn the tide of battle. You can unlock new heroes by opening chests or purchasing them from the in-game hero catalog. Strengthening your heroes is essential for tackling tougher challenges.

For extra firepower, visit the in-game Store to acquire powerful booster items like the Frost Gem or the devastating Meteor Call. Use these items during a battle to gain a temporary advantage and wipe out difficult waves of enemies. Good luck, commander!
