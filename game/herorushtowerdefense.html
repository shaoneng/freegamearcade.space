Hero Rush Tower Defense

How to Play Hero Rush Tower Defense

The goal of Hero Rush Tower Defense is to protect your land from waves of attacking enemies. Use your strategic mind to place and upgrade towers effectively along the enemy's path.

Before each level begins, you get to prepare for battle. First, choose the map you wish to defend. Next, assemble your team by selecting up to three heroes from a roster of 12, each possessing unique skills. Finally, set the game's difficulty to match your desired challenge.

During the game, click on the designated circular spots on the map to build your towers. You'll face a variety of enemies, including ground units like pirates and barbarians, as well as flying foes. You must build a diverse set of towers to counter all threats. Placing your towers in the most strategic locations is key to victory.

As you defeat enemies, you'll earn resources. Use these resources to upgrade your existing towers, making them stronger and more effective. You can also unlock new heroes and purchase powerful one-time-use booster items from the in-game store to help you in tough situations.
