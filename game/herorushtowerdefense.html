Hero Rush Tower Defense

How to Play Hero Rush Tower Defense

Your primary goal in Hero Rush Tower Defense is to strategically defend your territory from incoming waves of enemies. To do this, you'll need to build and upgrade a variety of defensive towers along the enemy's path.

To begin, select a level and prepare your strategy. You can choose up to three heroes from a roster of 12, each with unique skills to aid your defense. After assembling your team, set the game's difficulty level to match your desired challenge and start the battle!

Once the game starts, you'll see a map with designated building spots marked by circular areas. Click on these spots to construct your towers. Think carefully about your placement! Placing the right towers in critical locations is key to stopping both ground and flying enemies.

You'll face a diverse range of foes, from pirates to barbosas and nimble lupins. Each enemy has unique characteristics, so you'll need to adapt your tower strategy accordingly. Your chosen heroes will automatically fight alongside your towers, providing crucial support.

As you progress, you can unlock new heroes and upgrade existing ones to make them more powerful. Don't forget to visit the in-game store to purchase helpful booster items like the Frost Gem or Meteor Call, which can turn the tide of a difficult battle.
