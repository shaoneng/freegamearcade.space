Hero Rush Tower Defense

How to Play Hero Rush Tower Defense

Your main mission is to defend your land from waves of attacking enemies. To do this, you'll need to strategically build and upgrade defensive towers on designated spots on the map. Use your mouse to interact with the game interface.

Begin by selecting a level and a difficulty setting. You can then choose up to three heroes from your roster to aid you in battle. Each of the 12 heroes has unique abilities, so choose wisely to complement your strategy for the upcoming map.

During the game, click on the circular areas on the map to build your towers. You'll earn resources by defeating enemies, which you can use to build more towers or upgrade existing ones.
