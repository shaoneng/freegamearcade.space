Hexanaut io

How to Play Hexanaut io

Welcome to the competitive world of Hexanaut.io! Your primary goal is to conquer as much territory as possible. You control a character that leaves a trail when moving outside its home base. To capture new land, simply draw a line out from your territory and loop back to it. The enclosed hexagonal area will then become yours.

The biggest challenge is other players. While you are outside your colored zone, your trail is vulnerable. If an opponent crosses your trail, you are eliminated and lose all your progress. Be careful, as you can also be eliminated by crossing your own trail! The key to survival is to expand cautiously and always be aware of your surroundings.

To gain an advantage, keep an eye out for the five different types of totems that appear on the map. Capturing these totems provides powerful, temporary boosts that can help you expand faster, move quicker, or gain other strategic benefits. Use these power-ups wisely to outmaneuver your opponents and claim the crown. As you play, you can also unlock various skins and badges to customize your look on the battlefield!
