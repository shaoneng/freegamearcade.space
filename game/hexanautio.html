Hexanaut io

How to Play Hexanaut io

The objective in Hexanaut io is to conquer as much territory as possible. Use your mouse to guide your character around the map. To capture land, you must venture out from your existing hexagonal base, create a loop with your trail, and return to your territory to claim the enclosed area.

Be strategic and cautious! While inside your colored territory, you are completely safe. However, when you are outside your base creating a trail, you are vulnerable. If another player crosses your trail, you will be eliminated. Likewise, you can eliminate opponents by cutting across their trails while they are expanding.

Keep an eye out for special Totems scattered across the map. Capturing these provides powerful boosts and advantages that can help you dominate the arena. Grow your land, defeat your enemies, and climb the leaderboard to become the Hexanaut king!
