Hexanaut io

How to Play Hexanaut io

The goal in Hexanaut.io is to conquer as much of the hexagonal map as possible. Use your mouse to guide your character. To capture new territory, move out from your colored base, create a loop with your trail, and connect back to your land. The enclosed area will then become part of your empire.

Be extremely careful when you are outside your base! Your trail is vulnerable, and if another player runs into it, you will be eliminated. Similarly, you can eliminate other players by crossing their trails. It's always safe inside your own territory, so plan your expansions carefully and try to keep your trail short when enemies are nearby.

Keep an eye out for special totems scattered across the map. Capturing these totems will grant you powerful boosts and advantages that can help you expand faster and defend your territory more effectively. There are several types of totems, each with a unique effect.

Expand your land, eliminate your opponents, and climb the leaderboard. The player with the largest percentage of the map claimed is crowned the Hexanaut King! You can also unlock various skins and badges to customize your look and show off your achievements.
