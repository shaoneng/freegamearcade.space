Hexanaut io

How to Play Hexanaut io

Welcome to the competitive world of Hexanaut.io! Your goal is to conquer as much hexagonal territory as possible and become the ruler of the map. Use your mouse to guide your character across the arena.

To expand your empire, move your character out from your colored base to create a trail. Connect this trail back to your territory to capture all the hexagonal tiles within the loop. The new area will then convert to your color.

Be extremely careful when you are outside your base. Your trail is your weak point! If another player collides with your trail, you will be eliminated. Likewise, you can eliminate other players by cutting their trails. You are only safe when you are inside your own established territory.

Keep an eye out for special totems scattered across the map. Capturing these can grant you powerful bonuses, such as increased speed or a wider capture radius, giving you a crucial edge over your rivals. Strategize your expansion, eliminate your foes, and claim the crown!
