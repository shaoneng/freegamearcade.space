Hexanaut io

How to Play Hexanaut io

The objective in Hexanaut.io is to expand your hexagonal territory and conquer the largest area on the map. You control a character that leaves a trail; use your mouse to guide them. Returning to your own territory will capture all the land you've enclosed with your trail.

Be strategic and cautious! While you are outside your territory, your trail is vulnerable. If another player crosses your trail, you will be eliminated. Likewise, you can eliminate other players by crossing their trails. It's always safe inside your own colored land, so plan your expansions carefully.

Keep an eye out for special Totems on the map. Capturing these provides powerful but temporary boosts that can give you a significant advantage. Use them to speed up, create gates, or gain other abilities to outsmart your opponents and become the Hexanaut King!
