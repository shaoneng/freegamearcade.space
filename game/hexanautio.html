How to Play Hexanaut io

Your main goal in Hexanaut io is to expand your territory by capturing as much of the hexagonal map as possible. Use your mouse to guide your character. As you move outside your colored area, you'll leave a trail. To capture new land, simply return to your existing territory to close the loop. Everything inside the loop will become yours!

Be cautious when you're outside your safe zone! If another player crosses your trail, you will be eliminated, and they can claim your land. As you play, you can also unlock a variety of skins and badges to customize your character and show off your style on the battlefield. Good luck, and may the biggest hexagon empire win!
