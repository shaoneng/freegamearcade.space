Hexanaut io

How to Play Hexanaut io

The main goal in Hexanaut io is to expand your hexagonal territory and become the largest player on the map. You control a character that leaves a trail when you move outside your colored base.

To capture new land, simply draw a line with your character and loop back to your existing territory. The enclosed area will then become yours. You are always safe while inside your own base.

Be extremely cautious when you leave your base, as your trail is vulnerable. If another player crosses your trail, you will be eliminated from the game. Similarly, running into your own trail is also fatal. A good strategy is to make smaller, quicker captures to minimize risk.

Keep an eye out for special totems scattered across the map. Capturing these can grant you powerful advantages to help you dominate the arena. Customize your character with various skins and badges to stand out as you climb the leaderboard!
