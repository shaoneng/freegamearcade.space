Hexanaut io

How to Play Hexanaut io

Your main goal in Hexanaut.io is to expand your hexagonal territory. Move your character using your mouse to leave your base and draw a line. Return safely to your colored area to capture all the tiles enclosed by your trail. The more territory you control, the higher your score.

Be extremely cautious when you are outside your base. If another player crosses your trail, you will be eliminated. Likewise, don't cross your own trail! Your territory is your only safe zone. The longer your trail is, the more vulnerable you are, so plan your expansion carefully.

Keep an eye out for special totems scattered across the map. Capturing these totems grants you powerful, temporary abilities that can give you a significant advantage. There are different types, so learn what each one does to use them strategically against your opponents.

The ultimate objective is to become the Hexanaut King by conquering a large percentage of the map. Eliminate other players, capture their land, and climb the leaderboard to prove your dominance in this intense .io arena.
