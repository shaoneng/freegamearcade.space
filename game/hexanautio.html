Hexanaut io

How to Play Hexanaut io

Welcome to the competitive world of Hexanaut.io! Your main objective is to expand your hexagonal territory. Use your mouse to guide your character across the map. When you leave your colored area, you will draw a line. Return to your own territory to connect this line and capture all the hexagons within the loop.

Be extremely careful when you are outside your base. Your line is your weak point. If another player crosses your line before you can connect it, you will be eliminated. Likewise, you can eliminate other players by cutting their lines. It's a high-risk, high-reward battle for dominance!

As you explore the map, you'll find various totems. Capturing these totems grants you special abilities and advantages. These can be crucial for gaining an edge over your opponents, so try to secure them whenever it's safe. The player with the largest territory at the end of the round, or the one who conquers 20% of the map, is crowned the Hexanaut King.

Show off your style by unlocking and equipping various skins and badges from the main menu. Hexanaut.io also supports a colorblind mode, which can be enabled in the settings for better accessibility.
