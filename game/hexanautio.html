Hexanaut io

How to Play Hexanaut io

The goal in Hexanaut.io is to expand your hexagonal territory and become the biggest player on the map. Move your character using your mouse, which creates a trail behind you. To capture new land, you must venture out from your base and draw a complete loop back to your own territory. All the hexagons enclosed within your loop will be added to your empire.

Be extremely cautious when you are outside your safe zone. If an opponent crosses your trail while you are expanding, you will be eliminated from the game. Likewise, you can take out other players by strategically cutting their trails. Always be aware of your surroundings and try to protect your own tail, especially when enemies are nearby.

As you explore the map, keep an eye out for special totems. Capturing these totems grants you powerful but temporary bonuses, such as increased speed or other advantages. A solid strategy is to expand gradually, keeping your trail short to minimize risk. Dominate the map, capture the most land, and become the king of Hexanaut.io!
