Hexanaut io

How to Play Hexanaut io

Your primary objective in Hexanaut io is to conquer the map by expanding your hexagonal territory. You start with a small base and must venture out to claim more land, growing your empire one section at a time.

To capture new territory, move your character out of your base using your mouse. This will create a trail behind you. To claim the area you've outlined, you must safely return to your existing territory. The area you've enclosed with your trail will then turn into your color.

Be extremely cautious when outside your base! Your trail is your weak point. If another player runs into your trail, you will be eliminated from the game. Likewise, you can eliminate opponents by cutting off their trails. You are only safe when you are inside your own colored hexagons.

As you explore the map, look for special totems. Capturing these provides powerful temporary boosts and advantages, such as increased speed or a wider view, which can help you dominate the competition.

The goal is to become the Hexanaut King by controlling the largest percentage of the map. Strategize your expansion, eliminate your rivals, and use totems wisely to reign supreme!
