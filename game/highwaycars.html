Highway Cars

How to Play Highway Cars

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Highway Cars! The objective is simple but challenging: drive as far as you can on a busy highway without crashing into other vehicles. Use your quick reflexes to steer left and right, weaving through traffic.

You'll earn points for every car you successfully dodge. For a massive score boost and an adrenaline rush, try to pass cars as closely as possible without making contact. These "near misses" are the key to racking up a high score and earning in-game currency.

The game features multiple modes to test your skills. In one-way mode, all traffic flows in the same direction. In two-way mode, you'll face the added challenge of oncoming traffic. There is also a level mode with 50 distinct stages set in various environments. Use the currency you earn to unlock new maps and continue your highway adventure!
