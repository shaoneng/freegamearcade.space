Highway Cars

How to Play Highway Cars

Welcome to Highway Cars! The goal is to drive as far as possible on a busy highway, avoiding collisions with other vehicles. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to steer your car left and right, skillfully weaving through the flow of traffic.

You earn points and in-game cash for every car you successfully dodge. For a bigger bonus and a true adrenaline rush, try to pass other cars as closely as you can without touching them. The more risks you take, the greater your reward!

Explore different game modes for varied challenges. In One-Way mode, all traffic moves in the same direction. In Two-Way mode, you'll face oncoming cars, requiring extra caution. There is also a Levels mode with 50 stages across different environments, from deserts to snowy roads. Use the cash you earn to unlock new maps and continue your highway adventure!
