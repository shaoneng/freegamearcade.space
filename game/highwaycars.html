Highway Cars

How to Play Highway Cars

Welcome to Highway Cars! The objective is simple but challenging: drive as far as you can on a busy highway without crashing. The key to a high score is not just avoiding other vehicles, but weaving through them. You'll earn bonus points for "near misses," so the closer you get to other cars without hitting them, the more you'll score!

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys to control your car. Accelerate into the flow of traffic, brake when needed, and steer left and right to dodge other drivers.

The game features several exciting modes. In One-Way mode, all traffic moves in the same direction. In the more challenging Two-Way mode, you'll have to contend with oncoming traffic. There is also a Level mode with 50 stages set across different environments, from deserts to snowy landscapes.

As you play and rack up points, you'll earn in-game currency. Use this cash to unlock new cars and maps, adding even more variety to your highway driving experience. Show off your skills and become the king of the road!
