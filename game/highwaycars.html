Highway Cars

How to Play Highway Cars

Welcome to the fast lane! In Highway Cars, your objective is to navigate through heavy traffic for as long as possible without crashing. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to steer your vehicle left and right, skillfully weaving between other cars.

The key to a high score is risky driving! You'll earn points for every car you successfully dodge. For a massive score boost and an adrenaline rush, try to pass other vehicles as closely as you can. These "near-misses" are your ticket to earning in-game currency.

Choose your challenge from several game modes. Start with the One-Way mode to get a feel for the controls, or jump into the Two-Way mode for a true test of your reflexes against oncoming traffic. You can also progress through 50 distinct levels, driving through different environments like deserts and snowy landscapes.

Use the currency you earn from your daring overtakes to unlock new and exciting maps. The longer you drive and the more risks you take, the more you can unlock. Keep your eyes on the road, anticipate traffic patterns, and show everyone you're the king of the highway!
