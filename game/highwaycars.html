Highway Cars

How to Play Highway Cars

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Highway Cars! Your objective is simple but challenging: drive as far as you can on a busy highway without crashing. Use your keyboard's arrow keys (or A/D keys) to steer your vehicle left and right, weaving through the flow of traffic.

The key to a high score is skillful dodging. You earn points for every car you successfully pass. For a massive score boost and a real adrenaline rush, try to pass cars as closely as possible without making contact. These "near misses" will reward you with bonus points and help you earn in-game currency faster.

Highway Cars offers several exciting game modes. In "One-Way," all traffic moves in the same direction, providing a great starting point. For a greater challenge, try "Two-Way" mode, where you'll face oncoming traffic. The game also features a comprehensive "Level" mode with 50 stages set across different environments, from deserts to snowy landscapes. Use the currency you earn from your driving skills to unlock new maps and continue your high-speed adventure!
