Highway Racer Pro

How to Play Highway Racer Pro

Get ready to burn rubber in Highway Racer Pro! Your main goal is to drive as far as you can through dense highway traffic, earning points by weaving dangerously close to other cars without crashing. The closer the call, the more points you get!

Controls:

  • W / Up Arrow Key: Accelerate
  • S / Down Arrow Key: Brake
  • A / Left Arrow Key: Steer Left
  • D / Right Arrow Key: Steer Right

Challenge yourself in single-player mode to set new high scores, or take the competition online! In the multiplayer mode, you can create your own room, invite friends, and race against them in real-time to see who is the true pro of the highway.
