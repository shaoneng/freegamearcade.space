Highway Racer Pro

How to Play Highway Racer Pro

Highway Racer Pro puts you in the driver's seat for an intense, high-speed journey through dense traffic. The primary goal is to drive as far as possible without crashing. Earn bonus points by performing daring overtakes and driving close to other vehicles. Use your keyboard controls to steer, accelerate, and brake your way through the chaos.

Take the competition to the next level with the exciting Multiplayer mode. You can create your own private room and invite friends to join you. Once in the room, select your car and race against your friends in a shared traffic environment. Compete to see who is the ultimate pro, mastering the art of weaving through traffic and coming out on top.
