How to Play Highway Racer Pro

Get ready to put the pedal to the metal in Highway Racer Pro! The main goal is to drive as far as possible through heavy traffic without crashing. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or W, A, S, D to accelerate, brake, and steer your car through the tight gaps between other vehicles.
