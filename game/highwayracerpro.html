Highway Racer Pro

How to Play Highway Racer Pro

The objective in Highway Racer Pro is to navigate through heavy traffic at high speeds, testing your reflexes and driving prowess. Your goal is to drive as far as possible without crashing, skillfully weaving between cars to earn points. The closer you get to other vehicles without hitting them, the higher your score. Master the art of the near-miss to become a true pro.

Highway Racer Pro offers various game modes, including an exciting multiplayer option. In multiplayer, you can create your own private room and invite friends to join the action. Compete head-to-head in a shared traffic environment to see who is the ultimate highway racer. Pick your favorite car, hit the gas, and challenge your rivals for bragging rights on the leaderboard.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your vehicle, accelerate, and brake. Look for specific in-game instructions for special abilities or nitro boosts if available. The key to success is smooth control and anticipating the movement of other cars.
