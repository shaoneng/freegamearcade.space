Highway Racer Pro

How to Play Highway Racer Pro

The goal in Highway Racer Pro is to become the ultimate traffic-weaving champion. Your objective is to drive as far as possible through dense, high-speed highway traffic without crashing. Use your sharp reflexes to dodge other vehicles, find gaps, and maintain your speed to rack up a high score.

Control your car using your keyboard. Use the arrow keys or WASD to navigate: accelerate with the Up Arrow/W, brake with the Down Arrow/S, and steer with the Left/Right Arrows or A/D keys. The faster and closer you pass other cars, the more points you'll earn, rewarding risky and skillful driving.

Highway Racer Pro also features an exciting Multiplayer mode. You can create a private room, invite your friends, and compete head-to-head in the same chaotic traffic environment. Pick your favorite car, hit the highway, and prove who has the superior driving skills to conquer the road.
