Highway Road Racing

How to Play Highway Road Racing

Get behind the wheel and prepare for a high-octane driving experience in Highway Road Racing! The primary goal is to speed down busy highways, dodge traffic, and complete missions to achieve the highest score possible. Your driving prowess is rewarded with points for total distance traveled, daring near misses with other vehicles, maintaining a speed over 100km/h, and driving in the opposite direction for extra risk and reward.

Before you start, you can choose your ride from a selection of 12 different cars, each with its own unique ratings for speed, handling, and braking power. You can also select from three challenging game modes: navigate traffic all going the same direction in One Way, face oncoming cars in the intense Two Ways mode, or race against the clock in Time Attack. Finally, pick your scenic backdrop, from the Grand Canyon to Santa Monica.

Controlling your vehicle is simple. Use the Arrow keys or the WASD keys on your keyboard to steer your car through the traffic. Be careful not to crash, as that will end your run and show your final score breakdown. Master the controls, choose the right car for your style, and dominate the highway!
