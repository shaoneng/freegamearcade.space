Highway Road Racing

How to Play Highway Road Racing

Your objective in Highway Road Racing is to drive as far as you can, at the highest speed possible, all while dodging traffic and avoiding a crash. It's a true test of your reflexes and driving prowess on a bustling highway.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD on your keyboard to control your vehicle. Press the Up Arrow or W to accelerate, Down Arrow or S to brake, and the Left/Right Arrows or A/D to steer your car through traffic.

Before hitting the road, customize your experience. Choose from 12 distinct cars, each with unique ratings for speed, handling, and braking. Select your preferred game mode: 'One Way' for a standard challenge, 'Two Ways' for the thrill of oncoming traffic, or 'Time Attack' to race against the clock. You can also pick your environment, from the Grand Canyon to Santa Monica.

Rack up your score by covering long distances, executing 'Near Miss' maneuvers, keeping your speed above 100km/h, and driving in the opposite direction in the Two Ways mode. Keep an eye on your score at the top left and aim to set a new high score with every run!
