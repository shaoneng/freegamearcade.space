Highway Road Racing

How to Play Highway Road Racing

Welcome to the fast lane! Your goal in Highway Road Racing is to drive as far as you can, navigating busy traffic to achieve the highest possible score. Use the Arrow Keys or WASD to steer, accelerate, and brake your vehicle.

Before starting, choose your challenge. Pick from 12 distinct cars, each with its own ratings for speed, handling, and braking power. Then, select your game mode: One Way for a standard highway drive, Two Ways for the extra risk of oncoming traffic, or Time Attack to race against the clock. You can also pick your scenic route, from the Grand Canyon to Santa Monica.
