Highway Road Racing

How to Play Highway Road Racing

The goal in Highway Road Racing is to drive as far as you can on a busy highway, dodging traffic and completing missions to achieve the highest possible score. Your driving skills will be put to the ultimate test!

Controls: Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to steer your car left and right, accelerate, and brake.

Game Setup: Before you start, you can customize your experience:

  • Choose Your Car: Select from a garage of 12 different cars, each with its own ratings for speed, handling, and braking power.
  • Select a Mode: Pick from three distinct game modes: One Way (traffic flows in your direction), Two Ways (face oncoming traffic for a bigger challenge), or Time Attack (race against the clock).
  • Pick a Theme: Drive through beautiful environments like the Grand Canyon, Santa Monica, or Bryce Canyon.

Scoring Points: To maximize your score, focus on performing risky maneuvers. You'll earn points for the total distance traveled, pulling off "Near Misses" by closely passing other vehicles, driving in the opposite direction lane in Two Way mode, and maintaining a speed above 100km/h. Avoid crashing, as that will end your run!
