Highway Road Racing

How to Play Highway Road Racing

Welcome to the fast lane! Your goal in Highway Road Racing is to drive as far as possible, weaving through traffic to achieve the highest score. Before hitting the road, you'll need to make a few choices.

First, select your ride from a garage of 12 distinct cars, each with its own ratings for speed, handling, and braking. Next, pick your challenge: "One Way" for a standard drive, "Two Ways" for the added danger of oncoming traffic, or "Time Attack" to race against the clock. You can also choose your scenery, from the Grand Canyon to Santa Monica.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys to control your car. Steer to avoid other vehicles and collect points for total distance, risky "Near Miss" maneuvers, driving in the opposite direction, and maintaining a speed over 100km/h. A single crash ends your run, so stay sharp and keep your eyes on the road!
