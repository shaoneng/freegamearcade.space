Highway Road Racing

How to Play Highway Road Racing

Get ready to burn rubber on the open road! In Highway Road Racing, your goal is to push your car to its limits, dodge traffic, and set the highest score possible. Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to steer your vehicle through the bustling highway lanes.

Before you start, you'll have a chance to customize your experience. Choose from a garage of 12 distinct cars, each with its own ratings for speed, handling, and braking. Then, select your challenge from three exciting game modes: One Way for a classic race, Two Ways for the added danger of oncoming traffic, or Time Attack to race against the clock. Finally, pick your scenic backdrop, from the Grand Canyon to Santa Monica.

On the road, earn bonus points for daring maneuvers like Near Misses with other vehicles, driving in the Opposite Direction (in Two Way mode), and maintaining a speed above 100km/h. Keep an eye on your distance traveled, as this is your main score. Avoid crashing at all costs, as one bad collision will end your run!
