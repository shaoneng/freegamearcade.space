Highway Road Racing

How to Play Highway Road Racing

Get behind the wheel in Highway Road Racing and put your driving skills to the ultimate test! The main objective is to drive as far as possible on a busy highway, weaving through traffic and avoiding catastrophic crashes to achieve the highest score.

Controls:

  • Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys to steer your car, accelerate, and brake.

Getting Started:

  1. Select Your Car: Choose from a garage of 12 unique vehicles. Each car has different ratings for speed, handling, and braking, so pick one that suits your driving style.
  2. Choose Your Mode: The game offers three exciting modes:
    • One Way: All traffic moves in the same direction. A great mode to get started.
    • Two Ways: Face oncoming traffic for a more challenging and high-risk experience.
    • Time Attack: Race against the clock to see how far you can get before time runs out.
  3. Pick a Location: Race through three beautifully rendered environments: Grand Canyon, Santa Monica, or Bryce Canyon.

Scoring Points:

Your final score is based on several factors, including the total distance traveled, performing "near misses" by closely passing other vehicles, maintaining a speed above 100km/h, and driving in the opposite direction lane in Two Ways mode. Be carefulâ€”one crash and your run is over!
