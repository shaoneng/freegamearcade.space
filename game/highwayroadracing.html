Highway Road Racing

How to Play Highway Road Racing

Your primary objective in Highway Road Racing is to drive as far as possible on a busy highway, racking up a high score by performing risky maneuvers. Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to control your car's movementâ€”accelerate, brake, and steer left or right to navigate through traffic.

Before you hit the road, you can choose from 12 different cars, each with unique stats for speed, handling, and braking. You can also select your preferred game mode: 'One Way' for a simpler challenge, 'Two Ways' for intense head-on traffic, or 'Time Attack' to race against the clock. Finally, pick your scenic route, from the Grand Canyon to Santa Monica.

Score points by driving long distances, having 'near misses' with other vehicles, staying above 100kmph, and daringly driving in the opposite direction in Two Way mode. Be careful not to crash, as that will end your run! Good luck, and aim for the top score!
