Highway Road Racing

How to Play Highway Road Racing

Your goal in Highway Road Racing is to drive as far as you can on a busy highway, racking up the highest score possible by performing daring maneuvers. Before you start, you can choose your ride from a selection of 12 different cars, each with unique stats for speed, handling, and braking power.

The game offers several modes to test your skills:

  • One Way: All traffic flows in the same direction, a perfect mode to get started.
  • Two Ways: Face the ultimate challenge with oncoming traffic for bigger risks and rewards.
  • Time Attack: Race against the clock to see how far you can get before time runs out.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to steer your car, accelerate, and brake. Earn bonus points for near misses with other vehicles, driving over 100 km/h, and traveling in the opposite lane. Be careful not to crash, as that will end your run and show your final score. Race through beautiful locations like the Grand Canyon and Santa Monica and aim for the top of the leaderboard!
