Highway Road Racing

How to Play Highway Road Racing

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Highway Road Racing! Your goal is to navigate busy highways, complete missions, and achieve the highest score possible without crashing. Master the art of high-speed driving and become a legend of the road.

Controls: Use the Arrow Keys or WASD Keys to steer your vehicle left and right. Simple controls make it easy to jump right into the action!

Getting Started:

  • Choose Your Ride: Select from a garage of 12 different cars, each with unique stats for Speed, Handling, and Brakes. Pick the one that best suits your driving style.
  • Select a Game Mode: Test your skills in three distinct modes: One Way (all traffic flows in the same direction), Two Ways (oncoming traffic adds to the challenge), or Time Attack (race against the clock).
  • Pick Your Scenery: Enjoy the drive through beautiful environments, including the Grand Canyon, Santa Monica, and Bryce Canyon.

Scoring Points:

Your final score is calculated based on several factors. To maximize your points, focus on: Total Distance traveled, executing daring Near Misses with other vehicles, Keeping your speed Above 100km/h, and driving in the Opposite Direction lane (in Two Ways mode) for extra bonuses.
