How to Play Highway Traffic

Get ready to test your reflexes in Highway Traffic! The core objective is simple: drive as far as you can down a busy highway without crashing. Use your keyboard controls to steer your vehicle left and right, weaving through traffic and dodging other cars.

Be prepared for a challenge! Other drivers will slow down, speed up, or even stop suddenly, requiring you to stay alert and react quickly. The longer you drive without an accident, the more points you'll earn. These points can be used to unlock new car models and other exciting upgrades in the garage.
