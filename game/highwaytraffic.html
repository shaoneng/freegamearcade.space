Highway Traffic

How to Play Highway Traffic

The objective in Highway Traffic is simple yet challenging: drive as far as you can on a busy highway without crashing. You'll need sharp reflexes and quick thinking to succeed!

Use your keyboard controls (WASD or Arrow Keys) to steer your vehicle left and right. The other cars on the road won't make it easy; they might slow down, speed up, or stop suddenly, so you must always be alert. Weave through the traffic to avoid collisions.

The longer you survive and the more near-misses you have, the more points you'll earn. These points can be used to unlock new cars and other upgrades, helping you tackle even greater challenges.

To keep the experience fresh, Highway Traffic offers four different game modes and various weather conditions. Test your skills on a sunny day, at night with your headlights blazing, or on a slippery, rain-soaked road. Choose your favorite car and hit the highway!
