Highway Traffic

How to Play Highway Traffic

Your goal in Highway Traffic is to survive the chaotic freeway for as long as possible. Drive your car down the highway, expertly dodging other vehicles that can slow down, speed up, or stop without warning. The longer you drive without crashing, the higher your score.

Earn bonus points for daring 'near miss' maneuvers and maintaining high speeds. These points are your ticket to unlocking a variety of new car models and styles in the garage. Build your skills and your car collection to conquer the asphalt!

Highway Traffic offers four distinct game modes to keep the action fresh and challenging.
