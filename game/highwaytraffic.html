Highway Traffic

How to Play Highway Traffic

The main goal in Highway Traffic is to drive as far as possible down a busy highway without crashing. You'll need to stay alert, as other cars will swerve, slow down, speed up, or even stop suddenly in your path. Use your quick reflexes to maneuver around them and avoid accidents.

The longer you survive and the more near-misses you have, the more points you'll accumulate. These points can be used to unlock new cars and other upgrades. The game features four different modes, each with its own set of challenges to keep the gameplay exciting.

You can also customize your experience by choosing different weather conditions, such as a clear sunny day, a dark night drive, or a slippery rainy road, adding an extra layer of difficulty. Keep an eye on your dashboard display to track your score, speed, and distance traveled. Master the art of high-speed dodging to become the king of the highway!
