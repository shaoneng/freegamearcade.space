Highway Traffic

How to Play Highway Traffic

Your goal in Highway Traffic is to drive as far as possible on a busy highway without crashing. You'll need to be alert and react quickly, as other cars will brake, accelerate, and change lanes without warning.

The longer you survive and the more daring your maneuvers (like close overtakes), the more points you'll earn. These points can be used to unlock a variety of new cars in the garage, each with its own style. Keep an eye on your score, distance, and high-speed combos on the screen to track your progress.

Highway Traffic offers multiple ways to play. Choose from four distinct game modes for different challenges. You can also customize the weather, driving in sunny, rainy, or nighttime conditions to really test your skills. Select your favorite car, pick your mode, and hit the road!
