Highway Traffic

How to Play Highway Traffic

The main objective in Highway Traffic is to drive as far as possible on a busy highway without crashing. You'll need to stay alert and react quickly, as other vehicles will change speed, switch lanes, and stop without warning. Your goal is to skillfully dodge these cars to survive and set a high score.

As you drive, you'll earn points for distance traveled and for close calls with other cars (combos). The more points you accumulate, the more you can spend on unlocking new, faster cars in the garage. Surviving longer and performing risky maneuvers will help you get upgrades faster.

Highway Traffic offers a variety of ways to play. You can choose from four different game modes, each with its own set of rules and challenges. To further test your skills, you can also change the weather conditions. Try driving on a clear sunny day, navigating through the dark at night, or handling your car on a slippery, rain-soaked road.
