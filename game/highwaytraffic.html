Highway Traffic

How to Play Highway Traffic

Your primary goal in Highway Traffic is to drive as far as possible on a busy highway without crashing. You'll need to skillfully weave through traffic, avoiding other cars that can change speed or stop without warning.

Use your keyboard controls to steer your vehicle left and right. Keep a close eye on the road ahead to anticipate the movements of other drivers and react quickly to avoid collisions.

The longer you drive and the more near-misses you achieve, the more points you'll earn. These points can be used to unlock new cars and other upgrades in the garage. Strive to beat your high score with every run!

To keep things exciting, you can choose from four different game modes and even change the weather conditions. Test your skills on a sunny day, at night, or on a slippery rainy road for an extra challenge.
