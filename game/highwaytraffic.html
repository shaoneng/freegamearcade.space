Highway Traffic

How to Play Highway Traffic

Your main goal in Highway Traffic is to survive for as long as possible on a dangerously busy highway. You'll need sharp reflexes to dodge other vehicles that can slow down, speed up, or stop abruptly without any warning. Keep your eyes on the road and be prepared to make split-second maneuvers to avoid a crash.

The longer you drive without an accident, the more points you will accumulate. These points are crucial for unlocking new cars and getting valuable upgrades to improve your performance. Aim for high-speed overtakes and combos to maximize your score and climb the leaderboards.

To keep the challenge fresh, Highway Traffic offers four distinct game modes. You can also customize your experience by choosing different car models and adjusting the weather. Test your skills on a bright sunny day, navigate through the dark with your headlights, or handle the slippery conditions of a rainy road. Good luck, driver!
