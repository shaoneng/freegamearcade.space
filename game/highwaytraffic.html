Highway Traffic

How to Play Highway Traffic

The goal in Highway Traffic is simple but challenging: drive as far as you can on a busy highway without crashing. You'll need sharp reflexes to weave through traffic, as other vehicles will change speed and lanes unexpectedly.

Use your arrow keys or WASD to steer your vehicle left and right. Pay close attention to the road ahead to anticipate the movements of other cars and avoid collisions. The longer you drive without an accident, the more points you'll earn for upgrades.

Highway Traffic features four distinct game modes to keep the fun going, each with its own set of challenges. You can also customize your experience by choosing different weather options, such as driving on a sunny day or navigating a slick, rainy road at night. Keep an eye on your score, speed, and distance to track your progress and set new high scores!
