Highway Traffic

How to Play Highway Traffic

The core objective in Highway Traffic is to survive for as long as possible by driving down a busy highway. You must skillfully dodge other cars and avoid accidents at all costs. Be warned: other vehicles behave unpredictably, slowing down, speeding up, or stopping abruptly. Quick reflexes and sharp attention are your keys to success!

The longer you drive without crashing, the more points you'll accumulate. These points are crucial for unlocking upgrades and a variety of new car models to customize your ride. As you progress, the game becomes increasingly challenging, pushing your driving skills to the limit.

To keep the experience fresh and exciting, Highway Traffic features four different game modes, each with its own unique challenges.
