Hole Digger

How to Play Hole Digger

Welcome to Hole Digger! Your mission is to dig deep into the earth to find valuable resources, from common stone and coal to rare materials and even hidden chests of money. The deeper you go, the more valuable the treasures you'll uncover.

Use the WASD keys to move your character around. Press the Spacebar to jump. To dig and gather resources, simply use the Left Mouse Button. When you find interactive items, press the E key to engage with them. Need to pause? Press Q to return to the main menu.

As you dig, your backpack will fill up. Once it's full, return to the surface to sell your haul in the Shop. Use the cash you earn to visit the Upgrades area. Here, you can improve your shovel for faster digging, expand your backpack capacity, boost your energy, or even purchase a powerful jetpack to explore new depths safely.

The jetpack is a game-changer! Unlock it and then Double Press and Hold the Spacebar to fly. Be careful: falling too deep without a jetpack can get you stuck. Also, always watch your energy bar. If it depletes before you get home, you'll lose all the resources you've collected on that run! Don't forget to save your progress from the home area to protect your hard-earned upgrades.
