Hole Digger

How to Play Hole Digger

Embark on an underground adventure in Hole Digger! Your goal is to dig as deep as possible, collecting valuable resources and hidden treasures along the way. Explore the earth to find everything from simple stone and coal to rare materials and secret money cases.

Master the controls to navigate the depths. Use WASD to move around, the Spacebar to jump, and the Left Mouse Button to dig and collect resources. When you find an item you can interact with, press the E key. If you need to return to the main menu, press Q. Once you unlock the jetpack, use Double Space and Hold to fly.

As you dig, your backpack will fill up. Return to the surface to sell your loot in the Shop and earn cash. Use this money in the Upgrades area to improve your shovel for faster digging, expand your backpack's capacity, boost your energy, or even purchase a jetpack for easier travel. Don't forget to save your progress from the Home area!
