Hole Digger

How to Play Hole Digger

In Hole Digger, your mission is to dig into the deepest parts of the earth to find valuable resources and hidden treasures. Use your earnings to become the ultimate digging tycoon!

Master the controls to navigate the underground world. Use the WASD keys for movement, the Spacebar to jump, and the Left Mouse Button to dig and gather materials. To interact with specific items, press the E key. If you want to exit to the main menu, press the Q key.

The core gameplay involves a cycle of digging, selling, and upgrading. Venture underground and fill your backpack with stone, coal, iron, and even rare treasures. When your backpack is full, return to the surface and sell your haul in the Shop area. Then, visit the Upgrades area to improve your shovel, increase your backpack's capacity, boost your energy, or buy a high-tech jetpack.

The jetpack is a game-changer for deep exploration. Once acquired, you can fly by pressing the Spacebar twice and holding it down. This helps you escape deep pits and explore more efficiently. Remember to save your game periodically from the Home area. Most importantly, watch your energy bar! If it runs out while you're underground, you'll lose everything in your backpack.
