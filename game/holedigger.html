Hole Digger

How to Play Hole Digger

Welcome to Hole Digger, a captivating online simulation where you'll delve deep into the earth to find priceless treasures and valuable resources. Your mission is to dig, collect, sell, and upgrade your way to becoming the ultimate mining tycoon.

Controls:

  • Move: WASD keys
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Dig / Collect: Left Mouse Button
  • Interact with Items: E key
  • Fly (with Jetpack): Double-press and hold Spacebar
  • Exit to Menu: Q key

Begin your adventure by digging into the ground below. As you collect resources like stone, coal, iron, and even secret money cases, your backpack will fill up. Before it gets too heavy, you must return to the surface. Sell your collected loot in the Shop to earn cash. Use this money in the Upgrades area to improve your shovel, expand your backpack capacity, boost your energy, or purchase a powerful jetpack.

Pro Tips:

  • Always keep an eye on your energy bar. If it depletes before you get back home, you'll lose all the resources in your backpack!
  • Remember to save your progress from the Home area to ensure your hard-earned upgrades are safe.
  • Be careful when digging deep. Without a jetpack, falling into a chasm can leave you trapped, forcing you to start over from the surface.
