Hole Digger

How to Play Hole Digger

Welcome to Hole Digger, the ultimate underground adventure! Your goal is to dig as deep as you can, unearthing valuable resources and hidden treasures buried beneath the surface. Sell your findings to earn cash and upgrade your equipment to become a master excavator.

Use your mouse and keyboard to navigate the world. As you dig, you'll collect everything from stone and coal to rare materials and secret money cases. Keep an eye on your backpack's capacity; once it's full, return to the surface to sell your loot in the Shop area. Use your earnings to improve your shovel, expand your backpack, or even buy a jetpack for easier travel!

Controls:

  • Move: WASD keys
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Dig / Collect: Left Mouse Button
  • Interact with Items: E key
  • Fly (with Jetpack): Double tap and hold Spacebar
  • Exit to Home: Q key

Pro Tip: Always monitor your energy bar. If it depletes before you get back to the surface, you'll lose all the resources in your backpack for that trip. Dig smart, upgrade often, and see what mysteries you can uncover in the deep!
