Hole Digger

How to Play Hole Digger

Welcome to Hole Digger! Your mission is to dig as deep as you can to find valuable resources, hidden treasures, and even secret cash. The deeper you go, the more valuable the materials you'll find!

Controls:

  • Move: Use WASD keys.
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar.
  • Dig/Collect: Use the Left Mouse Button.
  • Interact: Press the E key to engage with items.
  • Jetpack: After unlocking, double-tap and hold the Spacebar to fly.
  • Exit to Menu: Press the Q key.

As you dig, your backpack will fill up. Return to the surface to sell your loot in the Shop. Use the cash you earn in the Upgrades area to improve your shovel, increase your backpack capacity, boost your energy, or purchase a powerful jetpack. Don't forget to save your progress at the Home area!

Pro-Tip: Always keep an eye on your energy bar. If it depletes before you get back home, you'll lose all the precious resources you've gathered in that run. Dig smart, upgrade often, and become the ultimate treasure hunter!
