Hole Digger

How to Play Hole Digger

Embark on an exciting underground adventure in Hole Digger! Your mission is to dig as deep as you can, exploring the earth to find and collect valuable resources. Gather everything from simple stone and coal to precious iron, rare materials, and even secret chests filled with cash.

Master the controls to become an efficient digger:

  • Move: Use the WASD keys.
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar.
  • Dig & Collect: Use the Left Mouse Button.
  • Interact with Items: Press the E key.
  • Return Home: Press the Q key.
  • Fly (with Jetpack): Double-tap and hold the Spacebar.

As you dig, your backpack will fill up. Once it's full, return to the surface to sell your findings in the Shop area for cash. Use your earnings in the Upgrades area to improve your shovel, expand your backpack's capacity, boost your energy, or purchase a powerful jetpack to navigate deep caverns.

Important Tips: Always remember to save your game from the Home area to protect your progress. Keep a close eye on your energy bar; if it runs out while you're underground, you'll lose all the resources in your backpack! Be careful when digging deep, as you can get trapped without a jetpack to fly back out.
