Hole Digger

How to Play Hole Digger

In Hole Digger, your mission is to dig into the earth to find valuable resources and hidden treasures. Use the WASD keys to move and the Spacebar to jump. To dig and collect materials, simply use the Left Mouse Button. You can interact with specific items using the E key. If you need to return to the main menu, press the Q key.

As you collect resources, your backpack will fill up. Return to the surface to sell your loot in the Shop and earn cash. Use this money in the Upgrades area to improve your shovel, increase your backpack space, boost your energy, or buy a jetpack. The jetpack is a game-changer, allowing you to fly by pressing the Spacebar twice and holding it, which helps you escape from deep pits.

Keep a close watch on your energy bar! If it runs out while you're underground, you'll lose all the resources in your backpack. Remember to return home periodically to save your game progress in the Home area, ensuring your hard-earned treasures and upgrades are safe.
