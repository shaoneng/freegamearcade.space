Hole Digger

How to Play Hole Digger

In Hole Digger, your goal is to dig as deep as you can to find valuable resources and hidden treasures. As you explore the earth, you'll collect everything from stone and coal to iron and rare materials. Once your backpack is full, return to the surface to sell your loot for cash.

Use the WASD keys to move around and the Spacebar to jump. To dig and collect resources, use the Left Mouse Button. You can interact with specific items using the E key. If you need to return to the home screen, press Q.

Use the cash you earn at the Shop to buy powerful upgrades. You can improve your shovel, increase your backpack's capacity, boost your energy, or even buy a jetpack! Once unlocked, you can fly by pressing the Spacebar twice and holding it. This is crucial for escaping deep pits!

Always keep an eye on your energy bar. If it runs out while you're underground, you'll lose all the resources in your backpack! Don't forget to visit the Home area periodically to save your game and secure your progress.
