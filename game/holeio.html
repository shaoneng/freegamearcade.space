Hole io

How to Play Hole io

Welcome to the chaotic cityscape of Hole io! Your objective is simple yet addictive: become the biggest black hole on the map. You start as a small, humble hole, capable of swallowing only small objects like traffic cones, trash cans, and unsuspecting pedestrians.

To grow, you must continuously consume. Guide your hole through the streets, devouring everything in your path. As you get larger, you'll be able to absorb bigger items, from cars and trucks to entire parks and massive skyscrapers. The more you eat, the faster your score and size increase.

Be mindful of other players! In this competitive arena, larger holes can consume smaller ones. Hunt down players smaller than you to absorb their mass and get a significant boost, but be sure to flee from any hole bigger than you. The last hole standing, or the biggest at the end of the timer, wins!

Controls: Simply move your mouse to guide the hole around the city. Alternatively, you can use the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to navigate.
