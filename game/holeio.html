Hole io

How to Play Hole io

Your objective in Hole.io is to become the largest black hole in the city within a two-minute time limit. You'll compete against other players to see who can cause the most destruction and rack up the highest score.

Control your hole by moving your mouse or using the arrow keys on your keyboard. The hole will follow your cursor's movement or the direction you press. Guide it over objects to consume them.

Start small! Begin by swallowing pedestrians, traffic cones, and benches. As you consume more objects, your hole will grow in size, allowing you to devour larger items like cars, trucks, and eventually, entire buildings.
