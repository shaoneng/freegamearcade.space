Hole io

How to Play Hole io

The goal in Hole io is simple: become the biggest black hole on the map before the time runs out! You control your hole's movement around the city using your mouse or the arrow keys on your keyboard.

At the beginning of each round, your hole is small. Start by consuming small objects like street signs, pedestrians, and benches to increase your size. As you grow, you'll be able to swallow larger items such as cars, trucks, and eventually, entire buildings. The more you consume, the faster you'll grow.

You are not alone in the city! Compete against other players who are also trying to grow their holes. You can consume any player's hole that is smaller than yours for a massive size boost. However, be cautious of larger holes, as they can swallow you and end your rampage! A good strategy is to find areas with a high density of objects to maximize your growth quickly.
