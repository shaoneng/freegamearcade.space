Hole io

How to Play Hole io

Welcome to the chaotic world of Hole.io! Your objective is simple but addictive: become the biggest black hole in the city before the timer runs out.

Use your mouse or the arrow keys on your keyboard to navigate your hole around the map. At the start of the game, your hole is small, so you'll need to focus on consuming smaller objects like pedestrians, street cones, and mailboxes to increase your size.

As you grow, you'll gain the ability to swallow larger items. Move on to cars, then trucks, and eventually entire buildings! The more you consume, the faster you'll grow and the higher your score will climb. Keep an eye out for other playersâ€”if their hole is smaller than yours, you can consume them for a massive growth spurt. But be careful, as bigger holes can do the same to you!

The key to winning is constant movement and strategic consumption. Find areas with a high density of objects and players to maximize your growth and dominate the leaderboard. Good luck, and happy consuming!
