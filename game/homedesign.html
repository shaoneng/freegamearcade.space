Home Design

How to Play Home Design

Welcome to Home Design, where your creativity and organizational skills are put to the test! The goal of the game is simple: to furnish and decorate various rooms by placing items in their correct spots.

To play, use your mouse to click and drag items from the boxes. Move them around the room and find their designated place. The item will snap into position when you've found the right location. You'll know you've placed an item correctly when it fits perfectly and contributes to the room's overall design.

Progress through 20 different levels, each presenting a new space to design, from bedrooms and kitchens to unique zen gardens. Complete each room to unlock the next and see the house transform through your efforts!
