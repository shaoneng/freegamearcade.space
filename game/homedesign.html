Home Design

How to Play Home Design

Welcome to Home Design, where your creativity and organizational skills take center stage! The gameplay is simple, intuitive, and incredibly satisfying.

Your main objective is to furnish and decorate various rooms by placing items in their correct spots. To do this, simply use your mouse to click and hold on an item from the unpacked boxes. Drag the item to where you think it belongs in the room, and then release the mouse button to place it.

Each level presents a new space, from bedrooms to kitchens and even zen gardens. Pay attention to the outlines or hints in the room to figure out the right placement for each piece of furniture and decor. Successfully place all items to complete the level and move on to your next design project!
