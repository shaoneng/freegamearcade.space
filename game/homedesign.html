Home Design

How to Play Home Design

Welcome to Home Design, where your creativity and organizational skills are put to the test! The goal is to furnish and decorate a variety of rooms by placing items in their correct locations. Each level presents you with a new space and a collection of furniture, decor, and other household items.

The controls are simple and intuitive. To place an item, just click and hold your mouse button on it, drag it to where you think it belongs in the room, and release the mouse button. The game will indicate when an item is in its correct spot, helping you progress through the level.

Work your way through 20 different spaces, from cozy bedrooms to peaceful gardens, and enjoy the satisfying process of transforming each one. There's no time limit, so you can relax, take your time, and find the perfect spot for everything.
