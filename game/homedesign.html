Home Design

How to Play Home Design

Welcome to Home Design! Your goal is to become a master interior designer by organizing and furnishing a variety of rooms. Each level presents you with a new, empty space and a collection of furniture, appliances, and decorative items.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use your mouse to click and hold on any item from the inventory. Drag it into the room and release the mouse button to place it. Look for the highlighted outlines on the floor and walls—these are the correct spots for each object.

Your task is to place every item in its designated location to complete the room's design. Once all items are correctly positioned, the level is complete, and you can move on to the next exciting design challenge. With 20 different spaces to style, from bedrooms to kitchens and even zen gardens, your creative journey is just beginning!
