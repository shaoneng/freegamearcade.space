Home Design

How to Play Home Design

Welcome to Home Design, where your creativity and organizational skills are put to the test! The goal is to furnish and arrange various rooms in a house, transforming empty spaces into beautifully designed living areas.

The controls are incredibly simple. Just use your mouse to click and hold on any item from the inventory. Drag it into the room and release the mouse button to place it. Your task is to find the correct location for each object, whether it's a sofa, a plant, or a tiny decorative piece.

Progress through 20 unique levels, each presenting a new room or space to design, from bedrooms and kitchens to even a serene zen garden. There's no time limit, so you can relax, experiment, and enjoy the calming process of creating a perfect home!
