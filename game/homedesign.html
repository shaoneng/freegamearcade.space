Home Design

How to Play Home Design

Welcome to Home Design! Your objective is to furnish and organize a variety of empty rooms and spaces. Each level presents a new challenge with a set of furniture and decorative items waiting to be placed.

The controls are incredibly simple. Use your mouse to click, hold, and drag an item from the unpacked boxes. Move it to the highlighted silhouette within the room where you believe it belongs. Release the mouse button to place the item. If you're correct, the item will lock into place, and you can move on to the next one.

Successfully place all the items to complete the level and reveal a beautifully designed room. Progress through 20 unique levels, from bedrooms and kitchens to peaceful outdoor gardens, and enjoy the satisfying process of bringing each space to life!
