Home Design

How to Play Home Design

Welcome to Home Design, where your creativity and organizational skills are put to the test! Your main objective is to design and furnish 20 different rooms and spaces, ranging from living rooms and bedrooms to beautiful outdoor zen gardens.

The gameplay is simple and intuitive. Each level presents you with a room and a set of unpacked items. To play, use your mouse to click, drag, and drop each piece of furniture or decor into the room.

The challenge lies in finding the correct location for every item. Look for silhouettes or outlines on the floor and walls to guide you. Once all items are placed correctly, the level is complete, and you can move on to the next exciting design project!
