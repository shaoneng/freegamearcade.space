Home Design

How to Play Home Design

Welcome to Home Design! Your mission is to channel your inner decorator and bring order and style to a variety of empty rooms. The game presents you with a series of spaces, each filled with unopened boxes containing furniture and decorations.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use your mouse to click on the boxes to unpack the items inside. Once an item is revealed, click and hold to drag it around the room. Your goal is to place each item in its correct, logical position. A faint outline will often show you where larger pieces of furniture belong.

Successfully place all the items to complete the room and move on to the next challenge. With 20 unique spaces to design, from bedrooms and kitchens to peaceful zen gardens, you'll have plenty of opportunities to test your organizational skills. Good luck, and have fun creating beautiful homes!
