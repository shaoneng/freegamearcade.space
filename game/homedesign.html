Home Design

How to Play Home Design

Welcome to Home Design! Your goal is to furnish and organize a series of empty rooms and spaces, transforming them into beautifully designed areas.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Just use your mouse to click and drag items from the inventory at the bottom of the screen.

Place each item in its logical and correct location within the room. The silhouette outlines will guide you. Once all items are placed correctly, the level is complete, and you can move on to the next design challenge!
