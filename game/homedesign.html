Home Design

How to Play Home Design

Welcome to Home Design! Your goal is to bring order and beauty to 20 different living spaces, from bedrooms to kitchens and even zen gardens.

The controls are simple and intuitive. To play, use your mouse to click, hold, and drag items from the unpacked boxes. Move the items around the room and release the mouse button to place them in what you believe is the correct location.

Each item has a designated spot. You'll know you've placed an item correctly when it settles into place. Continue organizing until every item is perfectly positioned to complete the level and move on to the next design challenge!
