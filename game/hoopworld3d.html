Hoop World 3D

How to Play Hoop World 3D

Get ready for a high-flying basketball adventure in Hoop World 3D! The goal is simple: jump, flip through the air, and land the perfect slam dunk. But this is no ordinary court. You'll be launching yourself from trampolines, navigating floating platforms, and even flying through wormholes.

Timing is everything. Use your mouse to control the jump and the flips. The more flips you successfully complete before you dunk, the more coins you'll earn. These coins can be used to unlock cool new characters. Be careful, though! A mistimed jump will send your player crashing down with hilarious ragdoll physics.

Each level presents a new, wild challenge, testing your timing and precision. Discover secret routes, master the wacky physics, and enjoy the over-the-top graphics as you become a dunking legend. The game saves your progress in your browser's cache, so you can always come back to beat your high score.
