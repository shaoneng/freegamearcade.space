Hoop World 3D

How to Play Hoop World 3D

Get ready for a high-flying basketball experience unlike any other in Hoop World 3D! Your goal is simple: get the ball in the hoop. But how you get there is a wild ride filled with trampolines, wormholes, and hilarious physics.

The key to success is timing. Use your mouse to control your player's jumps and flips. The more flips you successfully perform before dunking, the more coins you'll earn. These coins can be used to unlock awesome new characters, like Hulk or Spiderman, to make your dunks even more epic.

Each level presents a new, over-the-top challenge. You'll bounce across floating platforms, launch through space portals, and try to stick the perfect landing. Be careful, though! If you mess up your timing, you'll faceplant in spectacular, ragdoll fashion. It's all part of the fun. Master the rhythm of flipping and flying to become a dunking legend!

Controls: Use your mouse to jump and flip.
