Hoop World 3D

How to Play Hoop World 3D

Your main objective in Hoop World 3D is simple: get the ball in the hoop. But the journey there is anything but ordinary! You'll be launching your character through a series of wild, physics-based obstacle courses.

The controls are straightforward. Use your mouse to aim your jump. Click and hold the left mouse button to make your character start flipping in the air. The longer you hold, the more flips you'll perform.

Timing is everything. Release the mouse button at the right moment to stop flipping and let gravity guide you towards the hoop for a spectacular dunk. You'll need to navigate trampolines, bounce off platforms, and even fly through wormholes to reach your target.

Try to perform as many flips as you can before dunking. More flips mean more coins, which you can use to unlock cool new characters. Don't worry if you mess up; the ragdoll falls are part of the fun! Now go on, start flipping and dunking!
