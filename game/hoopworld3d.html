How to Play Hoop World 3D

Welcome to the high-flying action of Hoop World 3D! The objective is simple: get your character from the starting point to the basketball hoop at the end of each level and land the perfect dunk.

Use your mouse to control the action. Click and hold to make your character start flipping. Release the mouse button at the right moment to launch them into the air. The timing of your release is crucial for controlling the jump's trajectory and landing.

As you fly through the air, try to complete as many flips as possible before dunking. The more flips you successfully perform, the more coins you'll earn. These coins can be used to unlock new and fun characters to play with. Navigate past obstacles like trampolines and fly through wormholes to reach the hoop. Be careful – a poorly timed jump will result in a hilarious crash!
