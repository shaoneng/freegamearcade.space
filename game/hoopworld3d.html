Hoop World 3D

How to Play Hoop World 3D

Your goal in Hoop World 3D is to navigate your character through a series of wild, physics-based levels and land the perfect slam dunk. This isn't just about shooting hoops; it's about high-flying acrobatics and precise timing!

Use your mouse or tap the screen to control your character. Click or tap to jump from platform to platform. While in the air, click and hold (or tap and hold) to perform flips. The more flips you successfully complete before dunking, the more coins you'll earn.

Each level introduces new challenges, including trampolines that launch you higher and wormholes that transport you across the stage. Time your jumps carefully to avoid crashing. Use the coins you collect to unlock awesome new characters and skins to show off your dunking style.
