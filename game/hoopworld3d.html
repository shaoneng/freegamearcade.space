Hoop World 3D

How to Play Hoop World 3D

Your objective in Hoop World 3D is to successfully guide your character from the starting point to the basketball hoop at the end of each physics-defying level.

The gameplay is all about timing. On a computer, use your mouse; on a mobile device, use your finger. Click and hold (or tap and hold) to make your character start flipping in the air. The key is to release at the perfect moment to launch them forward.

Aim for the trampolines, platforms, and even wormholes scattered throughout the level. A well-timed release will propel you to the next checkpoint, while a mistake might send you comically crashing down. Don't worry, the ragdoll falls are part of the fun!

The more flips you successfully perform before landing a jump or a dunk, the more coins you will earn. Use these coins in the game's shop to unlock awesome new characters and add a personal flair to your high-flying dunks.
