Hoop World 3D

How to Play Hoop World 3D

The goal in Hoop World 3D is to successfully navigate each level by jumping and flipping your way to the basketball hoop for an epic slam dunk.

Your character will automatically jump. To control the flips, simply click and hold your mouse button. The longer you hold, the more your character will flip in the air. The key to success is timing your release. Let go of the mouse button at the perfect moment to straighten out your character and stick the landing right in the hoop.

Each level presents a new challenge, featuring trampolines that launch you higher and wormholes that transport you across the map. The more flips you complete before dunking, the more coins you'll earn. Use these coins to unlock fun new characters and show off your high-flying style!

Be prepared for some wacky physics! A mistimed jump can lead to hilarious fails, but a perfect dunk is incredibly satisfying.
