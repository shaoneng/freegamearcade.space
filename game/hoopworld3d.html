Hoop World 3D

How to Play Hoop World 3D

The goal in Hoop World 3D is simple yet challenging: guide your character through a series of dynamic platforms to land the perfect slam dunk at the end of each level. This isn't your standard basketball game; it's a high-flying adventure powered by physics!

Using your mouse or finger, click and hold (or tap and hold) to make your character jump and start flipping. The key is timing. Release the mouse button at the right moment to stop flipping and prepare for landing. The longer you hold, the more flips you'll perform.

Each successful flip you complete before dunking earns you coins. These coins can be used to unlock cool new characters. Levels get progressively trickier, introducing trampolines, long-distance jumps, and even portals that teleport you across the map. A mistimed jump can lead to a hilarious crash, so master your timing to become a dunking pro!
