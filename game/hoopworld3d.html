Hoop World 3D

How to Play Hoop World 3D

Welcome to Hoop World 3D, a high-flying basketball adventure where physics gets a little wild! Your goal is to navigate through crazy levels, using trampolines and portals to reach the hoop and land the perfect slam dunk.

Timing is everything. As you soar through the air, perform as many flips as you can before you dunk. Successful flips earn you coins, which you can use to unlock cool new characters. But be careful – a mistimed jump will send your player crashing down in a hilarious ragdoll fall!

Controls:

  • Computers: Use your mouse to control the timing of your jumps and flips.
  • Mobile Devices: Tap the screen to jump and flip.

Master the rhythm, stick the landings, and become a dunking hero in this addictive and satisfying sports game!
