Hoop World 3D

How to Play Hoop World 3D

Welcome to Hoop World 3D, where the goal is to perform spectacular dunks across a series of wild, physics-based levels. Your mission is simple: get your character from the starting platform to the basketball hoop, but with as much style as possible!

The controls are intuitive but require perfect timing. Use your mouse to control your character. Click and hold the left mouse button to start flipping in the air. The longer you hold, the more flips you'll perform. To make your shot, release the mouse button at the right moment to launch your character towards the hoop. Time your release carefully to stick the landing and score the perfect dunk!

Each level introduces new challenges, including trampolines that launch you higher and portals that teleport you across the map. Successfully landing flips earns you coins, which you can use to unlock awesome new characters. Don't worry if you mess up; the ragdoll physics make every fall a funny experience. Now, get ready to flip, jump, and become a dunking legend!
