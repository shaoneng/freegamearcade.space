How to Play Hurakan City Driver HD

Welcome to the driver's seat of Hurakan City Driver HD! Your goal is to become the ultimate city driver by mastering speed, stunts, and style. Navigate the sprawling urban landscape, take on exciting missions, and push your car to its limits.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys to drive and steer your vehicle. When you need an extra burst of speed, hit the F key to activate your nitro boost. For those cinematic, high-precision moments, press the G key to enter slow-motion. To get the best view of the action, use the C key to cycle through different camera angles.

As you race and complete challenges, you'll earn coins. Save up your earnings to purchase new, faster cars or upgrade the performance of your current ride. Choose between different game modes like Racing and Drift to test your skills in various disciplines. Get ready to burn rubber and dominate the city streets!
