Hurakan City Driver HD

How to Play Hurakan City Driver HD

Your goal in Hurakan City Driver HD is to become the ultimate urban driver by mastering speed, drifting, and stunts. Navigate a detailed city, take on exciting missions, and pass challenges to earn coins. Use these coins to purchase new supercars and upgrade their performance, including top speed, acceleration, handling, and braking.

The game features different modes like Racing and Drift, giving you various ways to test your driving skills. Start with the powerful Spyder and work your way up to unlock even faster vehicles like the Vyper, GT Wavy, and the top-tier Defani by collecting credits.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use the Arrow Keys or W, A, S, D keys to drive and steer your vehicle. When you need a burst of speed, press the F key to activate your boost. For cinematic, slow-motion moments during jumps or drifts, press the G key. To get the perfect view of the action, press the C key to cycle through different camera angles.
