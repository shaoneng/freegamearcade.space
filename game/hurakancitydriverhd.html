Hurakan City Driver HD

How to Play Hurakan City Driver HD

Welcome to the high-octane world of Hurakan City! Your goal is to become the ultimate driver by exploring the city, completing missions, and pulling off incredible stunts. Earn coins by showcasing your skills in either Racing or Drift mode. Use these coins to purchase new cars like the Vyper, GT Wavy, and Defani, or upgrade your current ride to improve its top speed, acceleration, handling, and braking.

Master the controls to dominate the streets:

  • Arrow Keys / WASD: Drive and steer your vehicle.
  • F Key: Activate your nitro boost for a burst of speed.
  • G Key: Engage slow-motion to handle tricky maneuvers or savor a perfect drift.
  • C Key: Change your camera perspective to find the perfect view.

Hit the road, complete challenges, and build your ultimate car collection!
