Idle Breakout

How to Play Idle Breakout

Idle Breakout combines the satisfaction of classic brick-breaker games with the addictive progression of an idle clicker. Your main goal is simple: destroy all the bricks on the screen to advance to the next level.

Start by clicking on the bricks to deal damage and earn money. As you accumulate cash, you can purchase various types of balls that will automatically bounce around and break bricks for you. Each new type of ball you buy unlocks even more powerful options.

Head to the Upgrades menu to supercharge your gameplay. You can increase the power and speed of your balls, making them more effective at clearing the screen. You can also unlock powerful abilities like lasers and other temporary power-ups to gain a massive advantage for 30 seconds.

The game features hundreds of levels. Once you've conquered them all, you can use the prestige system to reset your progress in exchange for powerful, permanent bonuses, allowing you to become the ultimate brick-breaker.
