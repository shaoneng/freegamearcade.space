Idle Breakout

How to Play Idle Breakout

Getting started in Idle Breakout is simple! Your main objective is to destroy all the numbered bricks on the screen. Initially, you'll need to click on the bricks yourself to earn your first coins.

Once you have enough money, head to the shop to buy your first Basic Ball. This ball will automatically bounce around the screen, breaking bricks for you and earning passive income. As you earn more, you can buy more balls and unlock new, more powerful types of ballsâ€”from Plasma to Cannonballs.

Don't forget to visit the "Upgrades" menu! Here, you can spend your coins to increase the power and speed of your balls, making them more effective. You can also purchase powerful, temporary boosts like lasers and gold multipliers to supercharge your brick-breaking for 30 seconds.

Clear levels, unlock achievements, and when you've conquered everything, use the Prestige option to reset with permanent bonuses, allowing you to progress even faster on your next run. Your game is automatically saved in your browser, so you can close the tab and return later to see the wealth your balls have earned!
