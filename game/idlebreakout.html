Idle Breakout

How to Play Idle Breakout

Welcome to Idle Breakout, where classic arcade action meets addictive idle gameplay! The main objective is to clear the screen of all the bricks, level by level. At the start, you'll need to manually click on the bricks to break them and earn gold.

Use your earned gold to purchase balls that will automatically bounce around and break bricks for you. As you acquire your first ball of each type, new, more powerful ball types will unlock. Head to the "Upgrades" section to spend more gold on increasing your balls' speed and power, making them more effective. You can also unlock powerful abilities like lasers to clear bricks even faster!

The game features hundreds of levels to conquer. Once you've beaten them all, you can choose to "Prestige," resetting your progress but granting powerful permanent bonuses for your next playthrough. Don't worry about losing your progress if you close the tab; the game automatically saves in your browser. For advanced players, you can even export a save code from the settings menu to back up your game locally.
