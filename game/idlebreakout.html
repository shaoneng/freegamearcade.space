Idle Breakout

How to Play Idle Breakout

Idle Breakout is an addictive clicker game where your main goal is to destroy all the bricks in each room. You can break bricks by clicking them directly or, more efficiently, by deploying various types of balls. As you progress, earn coins to acquire new, more powerful balls and unlock different types.

Head to the 'Upgrades' section to enhance your balls' speed and power, making them even more destructive. Utilize five unique power-ups, including boosts for brick-breaking performance and a x2 Gold bonus, accessible from the upgrades menu. Don't forget to check the 'Achievements' section for challenging goals.

Your game progress is automatically saved in your browser, so you can pick up where you left off. For local backups, use the 'Export' feature in the settings to copy your progress code, and 'Import' it back whenever you wish to resume your ultimate brick-breaking journey!
