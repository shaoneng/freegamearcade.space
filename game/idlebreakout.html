Idle Breakout

How to Play Idle Breakout

Idle Breakout combines the classic fun of brick-breaker games with the addictive progression of an idle clicker. Your main goal is to clear all the bricks on the screen to advance through hundreds of levels. You start by simply clicking on the bricks to break them and earn gold.

Use the gold you earn to purchase various types of balls. Each ball you buy will automatically bounce around the screen, breaking bricks for you. Purchasing your first ball of a specific type unlocks the next, more powerful type. Head to the Upgrades section to spend your gold on increasing your balls' power and speed, making them more effective at clearing levels.

To give yourself an edge, you can use powerful boosts like lasers and temporary multipliers. These power-ups, accessible in the upgrades menu, provide a significant but short-term advantage to help you clear tough stages. When you've conquered all the levels, you can choose to "prestige," resetting your progress but gaining permanent bonuses for your next playthrough.

Don't worry about losing your hard work! The game automatically saves your progress in your browser's cache, allowing you to close the tab and return later to find your balls still working and earning passive income. For extra security, you can use the export/import feature in the settings to save your game data locally.
