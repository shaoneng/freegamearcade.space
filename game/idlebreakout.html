Idle Breakout

How to Play Idle Breakout

Welcome to Idle Breakout, the game where destruction is progress! The main goal is to clear the screen of all bricks. At the very beginning, you'll need to manually click on the bricks to break them and earn your first coins.

Once you've collected enough coins, head over to the shop to purchase balls. These balls will automatically bounce around, breaking bricks for you and generating passive income. There are several types of balls to unlock, each more powerful than the last. Don't forget to visit the "Upgrades" menu to increase the speed and power of your balls, making your brick-breaking army even more efficient.

To speed things up, you have access to five powerful abilities, like lasers, that give you a temporary but massive boost in performance. Keep an eye on the achievements section to guide your progress and give you goals to aim for. Once you've conquered all the levels, you can prestige to start over with significant permanent bonuses, allowing you to progress even faster!

Your progress is automatically saved in your browser, so you can close the game and come back later to find your balls have been hard at work earning you cash. If you need to clear your browser data or switch devices, use the export/import save feature in the settings to create a backup of your game.
