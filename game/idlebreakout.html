Idle Breakout

How to Play Idle Breakout

Welcome to Idle Breakout, the game where you smash bricks without breaking a sweat! The objective is simple: clear every brick on the screen to advance to the next level. You start by manually clicking on the bricks to destroy them and earn your first coins.

Use your earned coins to purchase balls. These balls will automatically bounce around the screen, breaking bricks for you. As you buy more, you'll unlock new and more powerful types of balls. This is where the "idle" part comes in – let the balls do the work!

To crush bricks faster, head over to the Upgrades section. Here, you can spend your coins to increase the power, speed, and quantity of your balls. You can also purchase powerful temporary boosts, like lasers, to supercharge your progress for 30 seconds.

Keep an eye on the achievements section for goals to work towards. Once you've conquered all the levels, you can use the Prestige feature. This resets your progress but grants you permanent bonuses, making you an even more formidable brick-breaker on your next run!

Don't worry about losing your hard work. The game automatically saves your progress in your browser. You can close the tab and come back later to find your balls have earned you some passive income. For extra security, you can export your save file from the settings menu.
