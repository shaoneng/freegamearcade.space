Idle Breakout

How to Play Idle Breakout

Welcome to Idle Breakout, the addictive game where your goal is to demolish every brick on the screen! Start your journey by simply clicking on the bricks to break them and earn gold. The more you click, the more you earn.

Use your gold to purchase balls that will automatically bounce around the screen, breaking bricks for you. This is where the "idle" part comes in! Unlock a variety of ball types, each with unique properties, as you progress.

Head to the Upgrades menu to supercharge your gameplay. You can increase the power and speed of your balls, making them more effective. You can also purchase powerful abilities like lasers for a temporary but massive boost in breaking power.

Clear all the bricks in a level to advance to the next. With hundreds of levels to conquer, the fun never stops. Once you master the game, you can prestige to reset your progress but gain permanent bonuses, allowing you to break bricks even faster on your next run!
