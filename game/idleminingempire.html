Idle Mining Empire

How to Play Idle Mining Empire

Your goal in Idle Mining Empire is to build a sprawling mining business from the ground up and become a wealthy tycoon. You'll start with a single mine shaft and modest earnings, but with smart investments, you can grow your operation into a powerful empire.

Begin by clicking on your miners to send them down the elevator to dig for coal. The coal is then transported to the surface and sold for cash. Use this initial income to upgrade your mine shaft, elevator, and surface transport to increase their capacity and speed, leading to higher profits.

To maximize your earnings and automate the process, hire managers for each department. A manager keeps your workers going even when you're not clicking, and more importantly, allows you to earn passive income while you're away from the game. This is the key to becoming a true idle tycoon!

The most significant way to boost your profits is by unlocking new, deeper mine shafts. Each new level you unlock brings more valuable resources and increases your overall business capacity. Keep reinvesting your earnings to expand deeper and build the ultimate mining empire!
