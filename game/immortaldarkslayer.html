Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

Welcome to the dark and challenging world of Immortal: Dark Slayer. Your goal is to guide your reborn knight through cursed lands, defeat powerful enemies, and grow stronger with every attempt.

Controls & Combat: Movement is key to survival. Use your mouse on PC or your finger on mobile to guide your character around the map. Combat is automaticâ€”your knight will attack any enemy that enters their range. Your main role is to strategically position your hero, dodge incoming attacks, and choose your battles wisely.

The Cycle of Death & Rebirth: When your health reaches zero, you are not defeated permanently. You will respawn at your home base. This is where your true progress begins. Use the gold and diamonds collected during your runs to purchase permanent upgrades for your health, attack power, and other stats.

Gear and Upgrades: As you progress, you'll find a variety of weapons like axes, hammers, and twin blades. Each weapon changes your combat style. You can manage your equipment by clicking the armor icon on the right side of the screen. Here, you can switch and upgrade your weapons and armor to prepare for tougher challenges.

Character Progression: Leveling up unlocks new perks and even new areas to explore. At level 3, you'll make a key decision by choosing a religion, each offering a unique and powerful passive skill that will define your journey.
