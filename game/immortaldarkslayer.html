How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

Welcome to the grim world of Immortal: Dark Slayer! Your main goal is to guide your resurrected knight through treacherous lands. Combat is handled automaticallyâ€”your knight will attack any enemies within range. Your crucial role is strategic movement. Use your mouse to navigate on PC or your finger on mobile devices. Dodge enemy attacks and position yourself to take on foes one by one.

As you vanquish skeletons, beasts, and demons, you'll collect valuable gold and diamonds. These are the keys to your growth. Don't fear death; it's part of the cycle. When your health is depleted, you will be reborn at your home base.

Back at the base, seek out the mysterious old woman. Interact with her to spend your hard-earned currency on permanent upgrades to your health, attack power, defense, and more. Each upgrade makes you stronger for your next run, allowing you to venture further and face tougher challenges.

As you level up, you'll unlock new areas, powerful weapons, and pivotal choices, like choosing a religion from a priest to gain a powerful passive skill. Keep an eye on your inventory to equip and upgrade new axes, hammers, and armor to turn the tide of battle in your favor.
