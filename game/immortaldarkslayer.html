Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

Immortal: Dark Slayer is an action RPG where survival and strategic progression are key. Your knight automatically engages in combat, so your primary role is to master movement, dodging enemy attacks, and choosing when to engage. As you journey through cursed lands, defeat enemies to collect valuable coins and diamonds.

When your health reaches zero, you'll respawn at your home base. Here, you can spend your collected gold and diamonds with the mysterious old woman to significantly upgrade your character's Health, Damage, Defense, Critical Chance, Critical Damage, Movement Speed, and Runes. These upgrades are crucial for overcoming increasingly difficult enemies and bosses.

Progressing through levels unlocks new areas and perks. At Level 3, you'll encounter a priest who allows you to choose a religion, granting a powerful passive skill. Keep an eye out for various weapons like axes, hammers, and twin blades, each offering a unique combat style. Manage your inventory by accessing the armor icon to switch or upgrade your gear, ensuring you're always prepared for the challenges ahead.
