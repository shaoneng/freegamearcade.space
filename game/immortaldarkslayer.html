Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

Welcome to the treacherous world of Immortal: Dark Slayer! Your goal is to battle through cursed lands, grow stronger with each attempt, and ultimately conquer the darkness.

Controls:

  • PC: Use your mouse to click and drag your character to move around the map.
  • Mobile: Tap and drag on the screen to control your knight's movement.

Gameplay Loop:

  • Combat is Automatic: Your knight will automatically attack enemies that are within range. Your main task is to strategically position your character, dodge enemy attacks, and choose your battles wisely.
  • Collect Resources: Defeat enemies to gather gold and diamonds. These are essential for upgrading your character.
  • Death and Rebirth: When your health reaches zero, you will be reborn at your home base. Don't worry, this is a core part of the game! All the gold and diamonds you collected are kept.
  • Upgrading: At the home base, find the old woman to spend your gold and diamonds on permanent upgrades to your health, attack power, defense, and more. You can also equip and upgrade new weapons and armor from your inventory (armor icon).

With each run, you'll venture further, face tougher enemies, and unlock new areas. Keep upgrading, choose your path wisely, and prove that you are the ultimate Dark Slayer!
