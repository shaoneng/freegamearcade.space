Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

In Immortal: Dark Slayer, you take on the role of a reborn knight on a perilous journey. Your primary goal is to navigate through dark, monster-infested lands, survive as long as you can, and grow stronger with each attempt. The controls are simple, focusing on movement and positioning.

Combat is handled automaticallyâ€”your knight will attack any nearby enemies. Your job is to strategically move your character to dodge incoming attacks and choose your battles wisely. Defeat enemies to collect gold and diamonds, which are crucial for your progress. Don't fear death; it's a key part of the game! When you fall, you'll respawn at your home base.

Back at your base, you can spend your collected gold and diamonds to permanently upgrade your stats, making you tougher for the next run. As you progress, you'll unlock new weapons like axes and twin blades, each offering a unique combat style. You can manage and upgrade your equipment through the armor icon on your screen. Keep fighting, upgrading, and conquering the cursed lands!
