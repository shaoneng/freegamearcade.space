Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

Welcome to Immortal: Dark Slayer, a free-to-play action RPG where you embody a once-mighty knight, now reborn and on a quest through cursed lands. Inspired by classics like Dark Souls but presented in an accessible top-down style, this browser game offers deep fantasy adventure with simple controls.

In this dark world, combat is automatic—your knight will attack nearby foes on their own. Your role is strategic: move wisely to dodge enemy attacks, choose your battles carefully, and know when to retreat. Each run through the demon-infested lands will earn you coins and diamonds, even if you fall in battle.

Death is not the end, but a new beginning. When your health is depleted, you will respawn at your home base. Here, a mysterious old woman will help you spend your collected treasures to permanently upgrade your abilities, strengthening your knight for the next fight.

As you level up, you'll unlock new areas and powerful perks. A crucial turning point arrives at Level 3, where you must choose a religion. Each deity grants a unique and powerful passive skill that will significantly shape your journey and combat style.

Discover a variety of weapons like axes, hammers, and twin blades, each offering a different combat feel. Manage your equipment by clicking the armor icon on the screen, where you can switch or upgrade your weapons and armor to face increasingly difficult enemies and powerful bosses.

Your primary control is movement. Use your mouse, keyboard (WASD/Arrow keys), or on-screen controls to navigate the world, evade attacks, and position yourself for victory. Master movement to turn deadly encounters into rewarding loot runs.
