Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

Embark on an epic journey in Immortal: Dark Slayer, a free-to-play action RPG that brings the intense atmosphere of games like Dark Souls and Elden Ring to your browser. Reimagined in a top-down style, you play as a mighty knight, now reborn and on a quest to conquer cursed lands teeming with monsters and demons. The core loop is simple yet addictive: you fight, you die, and you rise again, stronger than before.

Combat in Immortal: Dark Slayer is automatic, freeing you to focus on strategy. Your key role is to master movement, deftly dodge enemy attacks, and choose your battles wisely. As you traverse ruined villages and dark, foreboding zones, you'll defeat foes to collect essential coins and diamonds. Every run is a chance to gather more resources for your inevitable return to base.

Death is not the end, but a new beginning. When your health is depleted, you respawn at your home base, where a mysterious old woman awaits. Here, you can spend your hard-earned gold and diamonds to purchase crucial upgrades, permanently boosting your stats and abilities for your next attempt.

As you level up, you'll unlock new areas and powerful perks. A significant milestone is meeting the robed priest who introduces the religion system. Choosing a god grants you a unique and powerful passive skill, defining your character's path. Prepare for increasingly difficult challenges, including formidable boss fights that will put your upgraded gear and skills to the ultimate test.

Discover a variety of weapons like axes, hammers, and twin blades, each offering a distinct combat style. Some weapons favor quick dodges, while others deliver heavy, staggering blows. Manage your equipment by clicking the armor icon, where you can switch or upgrade your weapons and armor to counter tougher enemy waves.
