Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

In Immortal: Dark Slayer, you step into the boots of a reborn knight. The goal is simple: survive as long as you can, defeat enemies, and grow stronger with each attempt. The gameplay is intuitive, focusing on strategy over complex controls.

Controls & Combat: Your character attacks enemies automatically when they are in range. Your primary role is to control your movement. Use your mouse to click where you want to go or hold to move continuously (on PC), or simply tap and drag on the screen (on mobile). The key is to dodge enemy projectiles and position yourself to take on foes one by one or in manageable groups.

The Cycle of Death and Rebirth: Don't fear defeat! When your health drops to zero, you are transported back to your home base. This is where you make permanent progress. Seek out the mysterious old woman to spend the gold and diamonds you've collected on powerful upgrades for your health, attack, and defense.

Gear and Skills: As you level up and explore, you will find new weapons and armor. Access your inventory via the armor icon to equip or upgrade your gear. At certain levels, you'll also unlock new abilities and even choose a religion, granting you powerful passive skills that will define your journey.
