Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

Step into the dark fantasy world of Immortal: Dark Slayer, a free-to-play action RPG where death is just the beginning. You are a once-mighty knight, now reborn, on a quest to conquer cursed lands filled with skeletons, demons, and monstrous beasts. Inspired by soulslike classics, this game offers deep RPG mechanics with simple, accessible controls right in your browser.

Combat in Immortal: Dark Slayer is automatic, so you don't need to memorize complex combos. Your skill lies in strategic movement. Dodge enemy attacks, choose your battles wisely, and position yourself for victory. As you defeat foes, you'll collect valuable coins and diamonds.

Each run takes you through dark, atmospheric zones like ruined villages and treacherous bridges. But when your health runs out, your journey isn't over. You will respawn at your home base, where the real progress happens. A mysterious old woman awaits to help you spend your collected gold and diamonds on crucial upgrades like Health, Damage, and Attack Speed, making you stronger for your next attempt.

As you level up, you'll unlock new areas and powerful perks. At Level 3, you'll meet a priest who offers a choice of religion. Each god grants a unique passive skill, marking a significant turning point for your character's build and strategy.

Discover and equip various weapons, including axes, hammers, and twin blades. Each weapon type changes your combat style, from swift and agile to slow and powerful. Manage your equipment through the inventory screen (the armor icon) to switch and upgrade your weapons and armor, preparing you for increasingly difficult enemy waves and formidable boss fights.
