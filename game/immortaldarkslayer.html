Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

Welcome to the dark and unforgiving world of Immortal: Dark Slayer! Your goal is to guide a reborn knight through cursed lands, growing stronger with every attempt. The controls are simple, making it easy to jump right into the action.

Movement is Key: Combat in this game is automatic! Your character will attack any nearby enemies on their own. Your main task is to strategically move your knight around the battlefield. Use your mouse, keyboard (WASD or arrow keys), or finger (on mobile) to dodge enemy attacks, position yourself advantageously, and decide which foes to engage.

Collect and Upgrade: As you defeat skeletons, demons, and other foul creatures, they will drop gold and diamonds. Collect as much as you can during your run. While death is inevitable, it's not the end. When your health reaches zero, you'll be reborn at your home base.

The Path to Power: At your home base, find the mysterious old woman. Here, you can spend your collected gold and diamonds to permanently upgrade your stats, such as health, attack power, and defense. Each upgrade makes your next run easier, allowing you to venture further and face tougher enemies.

Discover New Gear and Abilities: As you level up, you'll unlock new areas, powerful weapons like axes and twin blades, and unique perks. Keep an eye out for priests who offer religious alignments, granting powerful passive skills that can dramatically change your playstyle. Manage your inventory to equip the best gear for the challenges ahead!
