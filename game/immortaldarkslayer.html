Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

In Immortal: Dark Slayer, your goal is to grow stronger with every life. The controls are intuitive: use your mouse on PC or your finger on mobile to move your knight across the map. Your character attacks enemies automatically when they are in range, so your focus is on smart positioning and dodging enemy strikes.

Venture through cursed lands, defeating skeletons, beasts, and demons to collect coins and diamonds. When your health reaches zero, you aren't defeated for good. Instead, you respawn at your home base, where your real progress begins. Use the loot from your previous run to get stronger for the next one.

Back at your base, find the mysterious old woman to spend your gold and diamonds on permanent upgrades for your attack, health, and other vital stats. As you gain levels, you'll unlock new areas, powerful perks, and even different religions that grant unique passive skills. You can also find and equip new weapons like axes and hammers that change your combat style. Manage your inventory by clicking the armor icon on your screen.
