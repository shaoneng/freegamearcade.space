Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

Welcome to the cursed lands of Immortal: Dark Slayer! Your main goal is to guide your reborn knight through hordes of demonic creatures. The combat is automaticâ€”your hero will attack any enemy that comes within range. Your skill lies in strategic movement and dodging.

PC Controls: Use your mouse to click where you want to go. Hold the mouse button to move continuously.

Mobile Controls: Tap and drag on the screen to direct your character's path.

As you slay enemies, you'll collect gold and diamonds. Death is not the end in this game; it's a strategic retreat! When your health runs out, you will be transported back to your home base. There, you can spend your collected treasure to purchase permanent upgrades for your health, attack, and defense from a mysterious old woman. Each upgrade makes your next run easier.

Leveling up unlocks new territories, stronger bosses, and powerful perks. You'll also discover new weapons like axes and twin blades, each offering a unique combat style. Manage your gear through the inventory screen to ensure you're always ready for the next challenge.
