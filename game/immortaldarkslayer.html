Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

Welcome to the dark and challenging world of Immortal: Dark Slayer! Your goal is to guide a resurrected knight through cursed lands, growing stronger with every battle.

Movement and Combat: Use your mouse (on PC) or finger (on mobile) to move your character around the map. Your knight will automatically attack any enemies that come within range. Your skill lies in strategic positioningâ€”maneuver to avoid enemy attacks while letting your auto-attacks do the work.

The Cycle of Life and Death: Defeat enemies to collect gold and diamonds. Don't be afraid to fall in battle! When your health drops to zero, you are reborn at the home base. This is where your true progress begins.

Upgrading Your Hero: In the home base, seek out the old woman to spend your collected currency on permanent upgrades. You can improve your Attack, Defense, Health Points (HP), and Speed, making you more formidable for your next journey.

Gear and Inventory: As you explore, you'll find powerful new weapons like axes, hammers, and twin blades. To manage your equipment, click or tap the armor icon on the right side of the screen. Here, you can switch or upgrade your weapons and armor.

Leveling Up and Choosing Your Path: As you level up, you will unlock new areas and powerful perks. A key moment arrives at Level 3, where you can choose a religion, granting you a unique and powerful passive skill that will shape your playstyle.
