Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

Welcome to the cursed lands of Immortal: Dark Slayer. Your goal is to guide a reborn knight through hordes of demonic foes. The core combat is automaticâ€”your knight will attack nearby enemies on his own. Your main task is strategic movement and survival.

Controls: Use your mouse to click and hold, or WASD/Arrow keys on your keyboard, to guide your character around the map. On mobile devices, use the on-screen joystick. Focus on dodging enemy attacks and positioning yourself to take on enemies one by one.

The Cycle of Death and Rebirth: When your health reaches zero, you will respawn at your home base. Don't worry, this is part of the game! Death allows you to spend the gold and diamonds you've collected. Approach the mysterious old woman at your base to upgrade your health, attack power, defense, and other vital stats.

Progression and Gear: Each run makes you stronger. As you level up, you'll unlock new zones with more powerful enemies and challenging boss fights. You can also find new weapons like axes and hammers, each offering a different combat style. To manage your equipment, click the armor icon on the right side of the screen to switch or upgrade your gear.

At level 3, you'll unlock the ability to choose a religion, which grants a powerful passive skill. Choose wisely, as this decision will significantly impact your journey.
