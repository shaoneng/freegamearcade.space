Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

Welcome to the dark and challenging world of Immortal: Dark Slayer. The controls are simple, but survival is anything but. Your knight attacks enemies automatically when they are in range. Your primary job is to guide your character through the treacherous landscapes, skillfully dodging enemy attacks and projectiles.

Embark on runs through cursed villages and ruined bridges, defeating hordes of skeletons, beasts, and demons. Slaying enemies rewards you with gold and diamonds. Your goal is to survive as long as possible, but don't fear death! When your health reaches zero, you are reborn at your home base.

Back at your base, a mysterious old woman awaits. Use the gold and diamonds you've collected to purchase permanent upgrades for your character. You can enhance your attack power, increase your maximum health, boost your movement speed, and improve your chances of landing critical hits. Each upgrade makes your next run easier.

As you level up, you'll unlock new areas, face tougher bosses, and discover new weapons like axes and twin blades. You can manage your equipment by clicking the armor icon on the screen. Choose your gear wisely to match your playstyle and conquer the darkness!
