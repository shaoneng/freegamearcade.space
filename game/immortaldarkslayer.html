Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

Welcome to the dark and unforgiving world of Immortal: Dark Slayer! Your quest is to guide a reborn knight through cursed lands, growing stronger with every attempt. The controls are simple: use your mouse on PC or your finger on mobile to move your character around the screen.

Combat is automatic! Your knight will attack any enemies that come within range. Your main task is to strategically position your hero, dodge incoming attacks, and choose your battles wisely. Surviving enemy waves is key to your success.

As you defeat foes, you'll collect valuable gold and diamonds. When your health runs out, you'll be sent back to your home base. Don't worry, this is part of the game! At the base, find the mysterious old woman to spend your collected currency on permanent upgrades to your health, damage, and other stats.

Keep an eye on the armor icon to manage your inventory. Here you can equip and upgrade different weapons and armor you find, each changing your combat style. As you level up, you'll unlock new areas and even powerful passive skills by choosing a religion. Die, upgrade, and rise again to become the ultimate Dark Slayer!
