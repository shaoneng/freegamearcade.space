Immortal: Dark Slayer

How to Play Immortal: Dark Slayer

Your journey as a reborn knight is one of strategy, not complex button-mashing. Combat in Immortal: Dark Slayer is automatic! Your primary role is to guide your character through the treacherous landscapes. Use your mouse or finger to move, skillfully dodging enemy attacks and positioning yourself to strike. Your survival depends on wise movement and picking the right battles.

Death is not the end, but a new beginning. When you fall in battle, you'll respawn at your home base. Here, you can spend the gold and diamonds collected on your runs to permanently upgrade your stats like health, attack power, and speed. Talk to the mysterious old woman to access the upgrade menu. Getting stronger is key to conquering tougher areas.

As you level up, you'll discover new weapons like axes, hammers, and twin blades, each offering a unique combat style. You can manage and upgrade your equipment by clicking the armor icon on the screen. At level 3, you'll also make a crucial choice: select a religion, each granting a powerful passive skill that will define your playstyle. Venture forth, slay your foes, and become the ultimate Dark Slayer!
