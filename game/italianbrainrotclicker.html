How to Play Italian Brainrot Clicker

Getting started with Italian Brainrot Clicker is incredibly simple! The main objective is to click or tap as much as you can to rack up points and unlock hilarious, over-the-top upgrades and surprises. This game is designed for pure, mindless fun and stress relief.

Use your mouse to click on the central character on the screen. If you are on a phone or tablet, simply tap the screen to play. Each click adds to your score, which you can use to purchase upgrades that increase your clicking power or generate points automatically. As you accumulate clicks, you'll discover all the madness the game has to offer and watch it transform into a fast-paced, addictive experience!
