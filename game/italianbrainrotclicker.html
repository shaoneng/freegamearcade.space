How to Play Italian Brainrot Clicker

Getting started with Italian Brainrot Clicker is as easy as it gets! The goal is pure, unadulterated fun through simple actions. There are no complicated controls to learn, making it the perfect game to jump right into for a quick break or some stress relief.

Controls: Use your mouse to click on the main character on the screen. If you're playing on a mobile device or tablet, simply tap the screen. Each click or tap contributes to your progress, unlocking new, crazier elements and surprises as you go.

The more you click, the more chaotic and hilarious the game becomes. Keep tapping to discover all the madness this game has to offer and see how high you can push the brainrot meter!
