Jacksmith

How to Play Jacksmith

In Jacksmith, you're not the hero on the battlefield, but the genius behind the weapons! You play as Jacksmith, a talented donkey blacksmith, tasked with crafting a wide variety of weapons for your clan of warriors. Your goal is to help them defeat monsters, progress across the land, and ultimately rescue Princess Lilliana from the clutches of the evil wizard Dudley.

The gameplay is controlled entirely with your mouse and is divided into two main phases: crafting and battling. Each day, warriors will approach your forge with orders for specific weapons. You'll then head to the crafting stations to bring their orders to life.

The crafting process involves a series of skill-based mini-games:

  • Smelting & Pouring: Choose your ore, melt it down, and carefully pour the molten metal into the mold without spilling or overheating.
  • Hammering: Once the weapon is cast, you'll hammer it into shape. Follow the on-screen guides and strike with precision to maximize the weapon's power.
  • Assembly: Select the correct handle, hilt, and other parts, and assemble the weapon according to the blueprint. A well-aligned construction increases durability.

After you've crafted all the weapons for the day, you'll watch your warriors use them in battle. The quality of your work directly impacts their success! Better weapons mean they'll defeat enemies faster and collect more loot and weapon blueprints. Pay attention to enemy weaknesses and use special gems to imbue your weapons with elemental powers for a strategic advantage.
