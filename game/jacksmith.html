Jacksmith

How to Play Jacksmith

In Jacksmith, you take on the role of a skilled donkey blacksmith on a crucial mission. With your apprentice Scout, you're tasked with forging weapons for warriors who are fighting to rescue Princess Lilliana from the clutches of the evil wizard Dudley. Instead of fighting, your battle is at the forge!

Each day, warriors will bring you orders for specific weapons. The crafting process is hands-on and requires your full attention. You'll start by selecting the right ore, melting it down, and carefully pouring it into a weapon mold. Then, you'll hammer the weapon to strengthen it and finish by assembling the hilt, handle, and any other required parts.

The entire game is controlled with your mouse. Use it to pour the molten metal, swing the hammer, and place the weapon parts. Precision is the key to success. A perfectly poured and hammered weapon will have higher power and durability, making it much more effective in combat.

Once you've crafted the weapons for the day, you'll watch your warriors go into battle. The quality of your craftsmanship directly influences their performance. Victorious battles earn you loot and gems, which you can use to buy new weapon blueprints and better materials for the tougher fights ahead.

As you progress, you'll unlock a wide variety of weapon types like bows, axes, and pikes, along with stronger metals such as bronze, iron, and steel. The enemies also get stronger, some with elemental weaknesses. Crafting weapons with the right elemental parts will be crucial to helping your warriors succeed!
