Jeep Driver

How to Play Jeep Driver

Welcome to the driver's seat of Jeep Driver! Your objective is to successfully navigate your rugged jeep through 30 levels of challenging off-road terrain. Each level is filled with hills, gaps, and tricky platforms that will test your balance and control.

Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to drive. Press Up/W to accelerate, Down/S to brake or reverse, and use Left/A and Right/D to balance your vehicle, especially when airborne, to prevent it from flipping over.

While driving, make sure to collect as many coins as you can. These coins can be used in the garage to unlock a variety of new and better jeeps, or to upgrade the performance and change the color of your current vehicle. Master the controls, collect coins, and conquer all the tracks!
