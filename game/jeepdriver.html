Jeep Driver

How to Play Jeep Driver

Welcome to the wild world of Jeep Driver! Your mission is to navigate your rugged jeep through challenging off-road courses without crashing or flipping over.

Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your vehicle. Press the up arrow or 'W' to accelerate, and the down arrow or 'S' to brake or reverse. Use the left and right arrow keys (or 'A' and 'D') to balance your jeep while in the air or on uneven ground. This is crucial for landing safely after jumps and climbing steep hills.

As you drive through each of the 30 levels, make sure to collect as many coins as you can. These coins can be used in the garage to unlock a variety of new and powerful jeeps, or to upgrade the performance and change the color of your current vehicle. The goal is to reach the finish line in one piece. Good luck, driver!
