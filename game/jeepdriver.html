Jeep Driver

How to Play Jeep Driver

The objective in Jeep Driver is to successfully navigate your vehicle through challenging off-road tracks to reach the finish line. The key is to maintain balance and control, preventing your jeep from flipping over as you tackle hills, gaps, and wobbly platforms.

Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your jeep. Press the Up/W key to accelerate, the Down/S key to brake or reverse, and the Left/A and Right/D keys to balance your vehicle in mid-air or on uneven ground.

Along the way, collect coins scattered throughout the levels. You can use these coins in the garage to unlock a variety of awesome new jeeps and to upgrade the performance and appearance of your current ride. Good luck, and have a fun drive!
