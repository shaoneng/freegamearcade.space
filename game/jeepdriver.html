Jeep Driver

How to Play Jeep Driver

Your goal in Jeep Driver is to navigate your vehicle through a series of challenging off-road courses. The key is to maintain your jeep's balance as you drive over bumpy terrain, cross unstable bridges, and tackle steep hills. Keep moving forward and try to reach the finish line without flipping over!

The controls are straightforward and easy to master:

  • Use the W key or the Up Arrow key to accelerate forward.
  • Use the S key or the Down Arrow key to brake and reverse.
  • Use the A and D keys or the Left and Right Arrow keys to balance your jeep while in the air.

As you progress through the 30 exciting levels, be sure to collect as many coins as you can. These coins can be used in the garage to unlock a variety of new, more powerful jeeps and to upgrade your vehicle's performance and appearance. Good luck, driver!
