Jeep Driver

How to Play Jeep Driver

Welcome to the wild world of Jeep Driver! Your mission is to navigate your tough little jeep through a series of challenging off-road courses. The key to success is maintaining balance and control as you drive over bumpy hills, cross precarious bridges, and fly over gaps. Your main goal is to reach the end of each level without crashing or flipping your vehicle.

The controls are straightforward and easy to master. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your jeep. Press the up arrow or 'W' to accelerate, and the down arrow or 'S' to brake or reverse. Use the left and right arrows (or 'A' and 'D') to balance your jeep in the air, preventing it from flipping over on rough terrain.

As you drive, be sure to collect all the coins scattered throughout the 30 exciting levels. These coins are your ticket to unlocking a garage full of awesome new jeeps, from sleek desert racers to rugged off-road monsters. You can also use your earnings to upgrade your vehicle's performance and customize its color to suit your style. Master each level, collect those coins, and build the ultimate jeep collection!
