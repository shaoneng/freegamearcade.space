Jeep Driver

How to Play Jeep Driver

Your mission in Jeep Driver is to navigate your vehicle through a series of challenging off-road courses. The main objective is to maintain balance, avoid flipping over, and reach the end of each level safely. Along the way, collect as many coins as you can to spend on cool upgrades and new vehicles!

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your jeep. Press W or the Up Arrow to accelerate forward, and S or the Down Arrow to brake or reverse. Use A/D or the Left/Right Arrows to tilt your jeep and maintain balance while in the air or on uneven terrain.
