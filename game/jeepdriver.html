Jeep Driver

How to Play Jeep Driver

Your mission in Jeep Driver is to navigate a series of challenging off-road courses without crashing. You'll be behind the wheel of a rugged jeep, facing bumpy terrain, steep hills, and precarious platforms across 30 exciting levels. The key to success is maintaining your balance, controlling your speed, and keeping your jeep upright to reach the finish line.

The controls are straightforward and easy to master. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your jeep. Press the up arrow or 'W' to accelerate forward, and the down arrow or 'S' to brake or reverse. Use the left and right arrow keys (or 'A' and 'D') to balance your vehicle in the air and prevent it from flipping over on rough ground.
