Jeep Driver

How to Play Jeep Driver

Jeep Driver is a fun and fast-paced driving game where your goal is to navigate challenging off-road courses without flipping your jeep.

Use the WASD keys or Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your jeep. The 'W' or 'Up Arrow' accelerates, and 'S' or 'Down Arrow' brakes/reverses. 'A' or 'Left Arrow' and 'D' or 'Right Arrow' are used for balancing your jeep as you drive over hills and obstacles, which is crucial for not crashing.

Your main objective in each of the 30 levels is to reach the finish line. Along the way, collect as many coins as possible. These coins can be used to unlock new and more powerful jeeps, or to upgrade your current vehicle's performance. You can also change your jeep's color to personalize your ride. Keep your balance, hit the gas, and see if you can master every tricky terrain!
