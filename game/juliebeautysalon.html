Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to Julie Beauty Salon, where you're the lead stylist! Your mission is to give your client a fabulous head-to-toe makeover. Use your creative flair to transform her look and make her shine.

The game is easy and fun to play using just your mouse. Simply click or tap on the different beauty categories on the screen, such as makeup, hairstyles, and nail polish. Within each category, you'll find a wide variety of options. Click on the eyeshadows, lipsticks, hair colors, and nail designs to apply them to your client.

Don't be afraid to experiment! Mix and match different colors and styles to create a truly unique masterpiece. From bold and dramatic to soft and natural, the choice is yours. Once you're happy with every detail of the makeover, you can admire your finished work. Enjoy the process and let your creativity run wild!
