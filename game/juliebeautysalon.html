Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to your very own beauty salon! In Julie Beauty Salon, your goal is to give your client a stunning makeover from head to toe. The game is intuitive and perfect for anyone who loves fashion and creativity.

Simply use your mouse to interact with the game. Follow the on-screen prompts and arrows to move through the different stages of the makeover process. You'll be guided from one beauty station to the next, starting with hair, then makeup, and finally nails.

At each station, click on the available options to apply them to your client. Experiment with different hairstyles, vibrant makeup palettes, and dazzling nail polish colors. There are no wrong choices, so let your imagination run wild and create a unique and glamorous look for Julie!
