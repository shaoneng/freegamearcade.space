Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to Julie Beauty Salon, where you are the master stylist! Your goal is to give your client a stunning makeover from head to toe. This game is all about creativity and having fun with fashion, so let your imagination run wild.

Playing is simple and intuitive. Use your mouse to click on the different cosmetic items, hairstyles, and nail polish colors. Follow the on-screen prompts to move through the different stages of the makeover.

Start with the makeup, choosing from a variety of eyeshadows, mascaras, and lipsticks to create a bold look. Next, move on to the hair salon to select a trendy cut and color. Finally, finish the transformation with a colorful manicure, picking the perfect polish and patterns for your client's nails. There's no time limit, so take your time to create the perfect masterpiece!
