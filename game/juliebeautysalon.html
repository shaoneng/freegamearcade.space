Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to Julie Beauty Salon! Your goal is to give your client a complete and fabulous makeover. The game is played entirely with your mouse or by tapping on the screen.

Start by following the on-screen prompts. You will be guided through different stages of the makeover, including applying makeup like eyeshadow and lipstick, choosing a trendy hairstyle, and painting beautiful nail art for a perfect manicure.

Simply click or tap on the various tools and color palettes to apply them to your client. Mix and match different styles to create a unique and stunning look. There are no time limits, so take your time and let your creativity run wild to design the ultimate glam-up!
