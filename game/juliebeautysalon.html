Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to Julie's Beauty Salon! Your mission is to give your client a fabulous head-to-toe makeover. The game is simple and intuitive, allowing your creativity to shine.

Start by choosing a category: makeup, hairstyling, or manicure. Within each section, you'll find a wide array of options to choose from. Use your mouse to click (or tap on a touchscreen) on the different cosmetics, hair colors, styles, and nail polishes.

Feel free to experiment! Mix and match vibrant eyeshadows with bold lipsticks, try on different trendy hairstyles, and design a stunning manicure with colorful polishes and patterns. There are no wrong choices, so let your inner stylist run wild and create a look that's uniquely yours. When you're happy with your masterpiece, you can admire the final transformation!
