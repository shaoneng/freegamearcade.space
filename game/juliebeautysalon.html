Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to your very own beauty salon! Playing Julie Beauty Salon is simple and intuitive, making it easy for anyone to become a master stylist. The game is controlled entirely with your mouse or by tapping on the screen.

Follow the on-screen prompts to move through the different makeover stages. Start by selecting from a wide range of makeup options, including eyeshadow, lipstick, and blush. Click on the colors and styles you like best to apply them to Julie.

Next, move on to hairstyling. Choose from different cuts and colors to find the perfect look. Finally, complete the transformation with a colorful manicure. Experiment with different combinations to create your unique masterpiece!
