Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to your very own beauty salon! The goal in Julie Beauty Salon is to unleash your creativity and give your client a stunning makeover. All actions are performed using your mouse to click or your finger to tap on the screen.

Begin by choosing a makeover station: makeup, hairstyle, or manicure. Each area offers a wide range of tools and options to explore.

In the makeup section, click on cosmetics like lipstick, eyeshadow, and mascara. Then, apply them directly to your client's face. Feel free to mix and match different shades and styles to craft the perfect look.

When you're ready to style hair, select from various cuts, colors, and accessories. Click on the tools to apply your chosen style. For the final touch, head to the manicure station to design a beautiful set of nails with vibrant colors and patterns.
