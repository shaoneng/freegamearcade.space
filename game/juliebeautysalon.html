Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to your very own beauty salon! In this game, your mission is to give your client a stunning head-to-toe makeover. All interactions are done simply by using your mouse to click or your finger to tap on the screen.

Follow the on-screen arrows and guides to progress through each beauty station. You'll start with a refreshing facial, move on to creative makeup application, style a trendy haircut, and finish with a colorful manicure.

For each step, select from a wide array of tools, colors, and accessories. Experiment with different combinations to create a unique and fabulous look. Let your creativity shine and transform your client into a beauty icon!
