Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to your very own beauty parlor! The goal in Julie Beauty Salon is to give your client a complete and fabulous makeover. The game is controlled entirely with your mouse or by tapping on the screen, making it simple and intuitive to play.

Follow the on-screen prompts to move through the different stages of the makeover. You'll start with makeup, selecting from various options like eyeshadow, lipstick, and blush to create the perfect look. Simply click on the cosmetic you want to use and apply it to the model.

Next, move on to the hairstyle. Browse through different cuts and colors to find the one that best suits your client's new look. Finally, complete the transformation with a colorful manicure. Choose your favorite nail polish and patterns to make her nails pop. There's no time limit, so take your time and let your creativity shine!
