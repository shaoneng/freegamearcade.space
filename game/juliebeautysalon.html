Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to Julie Beauty Salon! Your mission is to give your client a complete and fabulous makeover. The game is played entirely with your mouse (on a computer) or by tapping the screen (on mobile devices).

Follow the on-screen instructions to progress through each stage of the beauty treatment. You will start with makeup, where you can apply foundation, eyeshadow, blush, lipstick, and more. Simply click or tap on the cosmetic tools and then on the client's face to apply them.

After the makeup is perfect, you'll move on to hairstyling to pick a trendy cut and color. Finally, complete the look with a colorful and stylish manicure. Click through the options and let your creativity guide you to create the ultimate glam look!
