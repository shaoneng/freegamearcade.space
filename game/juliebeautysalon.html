Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to your very own beauty salon! In Julie Beauty Salon, your goal is to give your client a complete and fabulous makeover. The game is simple and intuitive, designed for you to express your creativity.

Start by choosing a category, whether it's makeup, hair, or nails. Use your mouse to click on the different options available. For makeup, you can select from various eyeshadows, lipsticks, and blushes. Click on the colors and styles you like to apply them to your client.

Next, move on to the hairstyle. Browse through a selection of trendy cuts and colors and pick the one that best suits the new look. Finally, complete the transformation with a stunning manicure. Select nail polish colors and patterns to create the perfect set of nails. There are no timers or scores, just pure creative fun!
