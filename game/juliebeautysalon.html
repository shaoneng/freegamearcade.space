Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to Julie Beauty Salon! Your goal is to give your client a fabulous makeover from head to toe. This game is played entirely with your mouse or by tapping on the screen.

Follow the on-screen prompts to move through the different stages of the makeover. Start by selecting from a wide range of makeup options, including eyeshadow, lipstick, and blush. Click or tap on the colors and styles you like best to apply them.

Next, move on to the hair salon to choose a trendy new hairstyle. Finally, finish the look with a perfect manicure, selecting from a variety of vibrant nail polish colors and patterns. Let your creativity shine and turn every client into a masterpiece!
