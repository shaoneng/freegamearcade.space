Julie Beauty Salon

About Julie Beauty Salon

Julie Beauty Salon is a glam-up makeover game where you run a beauty salon. Every makeover is a mini masterpiece in this fun, stylish browser game. You’re a beauty expert in charge of transforming a fabulous girl with bold makeup, trendy hairstyles, and colorful manicures.

If you've ever dreamed of being a stylist or just love mixing and matching beauty looks, this free game is perfect for you. Simply use your mouse or trackpad to select tools and apply different styles to your client.

More Information

Creator: Julie Beauty Salon is created by FreezeNova Games.

Is it Kid-Friendly? Yes! Julie Beauty Salon is a kid-friendly makeover game with no violence or inappropriate content. It's a perfect game for kids who enjoy fashion and creativity.
