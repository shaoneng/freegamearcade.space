Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to Julie Beauty Salon, where you are the head stylist! Your goal is to give your client a stunning makeover from head to toe. This game is all about creativity and style, so let your imagination run wild and design the perfect look.

All actions in the game are performed using your mouse. Simply click on the different cosmetic and styling categories, such as makeup, hair, and nails. Within each category, you'll find a wide variety of options to choose from. There are no wrong answers, just endless possibilities!

Start by selecting eyeshadows, lipsticks, and blushes to create the perfect makeup look. Then, move on to the hairstyle, picking from various cuts and vibrant colors. Finally, complete the transformation with a fabulous manicure by choosing nail polish colors and patterns. Mix and match to create your ultimate masterpiece!
