Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to your very own beauty salon! In Julie Beauty Salon, your goal is to give your client a complete and fabulous makeover. The game is controlled entirely with your mouse or by tapping on the screen. Follow the on-screen prompts to move through the different stages of the makeover.

Start with makeup application, choosing from a variety of eyeshadows, lipsticks, and blushes. Then, move on to hairstyling, where you can select trendy cuts and colors. Finally, complete the look with a perfect manicure, picking from a wide range of nail polish colors and designs. Simply click or tap on the options you like best to apply them and create your masterpiece!
