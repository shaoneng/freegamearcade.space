Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Welcome to your very own beauty salon! The goal in Julie Beauty Salon is to give your client a stunning makeover from head to toe. The game is controlled entirely with your mouse or by tapping on the screen.

Follow the on-screen prompts to move through the different stages of the makeover. You'll start with makeup, selecting from a variety of options like lipstick, eyeshadow, and blush. Simply click on your desired colors and styles to apply them.

Next, move on to hairstyling, where you can choose a trendy new cut and color. Finally, complete the look with a colorful manicure, picking out the perfect nail polish. Mix and match to your heart's content and create the ultimate glam look for Julie!
