How to Play Jul Moto RacingÂ

Master the controls to dominate the track. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys to move your motorcycle forward, backward, and maintain your balance while in the air. Need to clear a big gap or get over an obstacle? Press the Spacebar to jump.

Your main goal is to race to the finish line as quickly as possible. Along the way, collect all the coins you see scattered throughout the levels. These coins are crucial for increasing your score and can be used in the garage to unlock a collection of new and better bikes.

The game may seem easy at first, but don't be fooled! As you advance, the levels become more complex and challenging. Maintaining your balance is key to success. If you lose your pace or tip over, you'll have to restart the level. Compete against other racers online and show them who has the best skills on two wheels!
