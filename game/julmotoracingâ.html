How to Play Jul Moto RacingÂ

Master the controls to dominate the track! Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys to control your motorcycle's movement and balance. When you approach a ramp or need to clear an obstacle, press the Spacebar to perform a jump.

The main objective in each level is to race to the finish line while collecting as many coins as possible. Your reflexes and timing are key to navigating the challenging city streets and high ramps. Be careful with your balance—if you lose your pace and tip over, you'll have to restart the level from the beginning.

Use the coins you collect to visit the garage and unlock a collection of new and improved bikes. For an extra challenge, you can compete against other players in real-time online races to prove your skills and become the top rider!
