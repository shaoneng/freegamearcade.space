How to Play Jul Moto RacingÂ

Get ready for a high-speed challenge! In Jul Moto Racing, your goal is to race your motorcycle through city streets filled with ramps and obstacles. The controls are straightforward, but mastering the game takes skill.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to control your bike's movement and balance. Lean forward and backward to navigate the tricky terrain and maintain your speed. Press the Spacebar to jump, launching your bike over gaps and onto high ramps.

As you race through each level, be sure to collect all the coins you see. These coins are crucial for upgrading your ride! Head to the garage to spend your earnings on new, more powerful bikes. Maintaining your balance is key; if you lose your pace and tip over, you'll have to restart the level. You can also challenge other players in real-time online races to prove you're the ultimate rider!
