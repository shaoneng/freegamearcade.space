How to Play Jul Moto RacingÂ

Get behind the handlebars in Jul Moto Racing! Your main objective is to navigate your motorcycle through dynamic city levels, collecting coins and crossing the finish line before your opponents. While the controls are straightforward, mastering the physics and maintaining your balance on high ramps is key to victory. A single crash could mean restarting the level, so keep your focus sharp!

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to accelerate, brake, and balance your bike in the air.

Press the Spacebar to perform jumps, which is essential for clearing obstacles and soaring off ramps for extra airtime.

As you race, be sure to collect all the coins scattered along the track. You can then visit the garage to spend your earnings on unlocking a variety of new and more powerful bikes. Good luck, and enjoy the ride!
