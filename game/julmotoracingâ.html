How to Play Jul Moto RacingÂ

Get behind the handlebars and master the streets! The controls are simple to learn: use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys to accelerate, brake, and maintain your balance. When you need to clear an obstacle or launch off a huge ramp, press the Spacebar to jump.

Your primary goal is to race through dozens of challenging levels, reaching the finish line as fast as you can. While you race, keep an eye out for gold coins scattered along the road. Collecting these coins is essential, as you can use them in the garage to unlock a collection of new and improved motorcycles.

Don't be fooled by the simple start; the difficulty ramps up as you progress. Maintaining your balance is critical, as losing your pace can cause you to tip over and force a restart. For the ultimate test of skill, you can even challenge other players in real-time online races and prove you're the best rider on the block!
