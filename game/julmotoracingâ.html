How to Play Jul Moto RacingÂ

Your main goal in Jul Moto Racing is to navigate your motorcycle through challenging city tracks, collect all the coins you can, and reach the finish line without crashing. While it starts simple, the levels become progressively more difficult, demanding precise control and perfect timing to avoid tipping over.

The controls are straightforward. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow keys on your keyboard to move your motorcycle forward, backward, and maintain balance in the air. To perform jumps off ramps, press the Spacebar.

As you race, gather coins scattered throughout the levels. These coins are your currency to unlock a variety of new and improved bikes from the garage. The game also features real-time online races where you can challenge other players to prove you're the fastest rider on the track!
