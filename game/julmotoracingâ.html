How to Play Jul Moto RacingÂ

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Jul Moto Racing! The objective is to guide your motorcycle through a series of challenging levels set in a vibrant city. Accelerate down streets, launch off massive ramps, and try to collect every coin in your path while maintaining perfect balance. If you tip over, you'll have to start the level again!

Mastering the controls is key to victory. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your bike's movement and balance. To clear large gaps or hop over obstacles, press the Spacebar to jump.

Each level increases in difficulty, pushing your skills to the limit. Spend the coins you earn in the garage to unlock new and better bikes. You can also take on other players in real-time online races to see who is the ultimate street racing champion!
