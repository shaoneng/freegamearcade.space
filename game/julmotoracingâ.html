How to Play Jul Moto RacingÂ

Get ready to hit the streets! The controls are simple to learn but challenging to master. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys to control your motorcycle's movement and balance. When you see a ramp or need to clear an obstacle, press the Spacebar to jump.

Your main objective is to race through each level, reaching the finish line as quickly as you can. Along the way, make sure to collect all the coins in sight. These coins are crucial for upgrading your ride and unlocking new, faster bikes from the garage. Be careful, as the levels get progressively harder. Maintaining your balance is key—if you tip over, you'll have to restart!

Think you're the fastest? You can also challenge other players in exciting real-time online races. Be the first to cross the finish line to prove your skills and become the ultimate champion.
