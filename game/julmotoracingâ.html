How to Play Jul Moto RacingÂ

Take control of your motorcycle and navigate through challenging urban tracks. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow keys to move your bike forward, backward, and maintain balance while airborne. To perform jumps off ramps and clear obstacles, press the Spacebar.

The main goal is to reach the finish line as quickly as possible. Along the way, make sure to collect all the coins scattered throughout each level. These coins are crucial for increasing your score and can be used in the garage to unlock a collection of new and better bikes.

What starts simple quickly becomes a true test of skill. As you progress, the levels get harder, and maintaining your balance is key to success. Lose your momentum, and you might tip over, forcing a restart. You can also test your abilities against others in exciting online races!
