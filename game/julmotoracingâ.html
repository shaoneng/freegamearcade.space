How to Play Jul Moto RacingÂ

Welcome to the high-speed world of Jul Moto Racing! Your goal is to navigate your motorcycle through challenging urban tracks, collect coins, and reach the finish line as fast as possible. Be mindful of your balance, as one wrong move can cause you to tip over and force you to restart the level.

The controls are simple and intuitive:

  • Move & Balance: Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys to accelerate, brake, and balance your bike.
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar to leap over obstacles and soar off ramps for big air.

As you race, make sure to grab all the coins scattered throughout the levels. You can use these earnings in the garage to unlock a variety of new and improved bikes. Compete against others in real-time races and prove you have the best timing and skill on two wheels!
