How to Play Jul Moto RacingÂ

Your goal in Jul Moto Racing is to navigate your motorcycle through dynamic cityscapes, conquer high ramps, and race to the finish line. Collect all the coins you can to boost your score and save up for awesome new bikes in the garage.

Master the simple controls to rule the road. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys to accelerate, brake, and maintain your balance while in the air. When you need to get some airtime to clear an obstacle or a gap, press the Spacebar to jump.

While the game starts easy, the levels become increasingly challenging. Maintaining your balance is key to success, as one wrong move can cause you to tip over and force a restart. Put your skills to the ultimate test by challenging other players in real-time online races!
