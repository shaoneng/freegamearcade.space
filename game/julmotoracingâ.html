How to Play Jul Moto RacingÂ

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Jul Moto Racing! Your main goal is to race your motorcycle through vibrant city levels, reach the finish line, and collect as many coins as possible along the way.

The controls are straightforward. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys to move your motorcycle forward, backward, and maintain balance in the air. When you approach a ramp or need to clear an obstacle, press the Spacebar to jump.

As you progress through the game, the levels become more challenging. You'll need sharp reflexes and precise timing to land your jumps and keep your speed. Use the coins you've collected to visit the garage and unlock new, better bikes to improve your performance!
