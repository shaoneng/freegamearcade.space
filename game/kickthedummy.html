Kick The Dummy

How to Play Kick The Dummy

Feeling stressed? Kick The Dummy is the perfect game to vent your frustrations! Your goal is simple: unleash a barrage of attacks on a robotic dummy to relieve your stress. There are no complicated rules, just pure, unadulterated chaotic fun.

The controls are incredibly straightforward. Simply use your mouse and click the left mouse button to hit the dummy. You can click as fast as you can to land a flurry of blows.

To spice things up, explore the vast arsenal of weapons available at the top of the screen. You'll find icons for different categories, from firearms and explosives to melee weapons and even everyday objects like a park bench! Click an icon to select your weapon of choice and click on the dummy to use it. When you're done, simply use the 'drop weapon' button to go back to your fists.
