Kick The Dummy

How to Play Kick The Dummy

Feeling stressed? Kick The Dummy is the perfect outlet! The goal is simple: unleash your frustration on a durable robotic dummy. Use your mouse to interact with the dummy. Simply click and drag to throw it around the room, or click repeatedly to punch and slap it. Watch the dummy's health bar at the bottom of the screen to see how much damage you're dealing.

To really ramp up the fun, explore the wide variety of weapons available. At the top of the screen, you'll find a row of icons. Click on these to equip different tools of destruction, including firearms, melee weapons, explosives, and much more. Select a weapon and click on the dummy to use it. When you're ready to switch, simply click the "drop" icon to go back to your bare hands or choose another weapon. It's all about creative destruction and stress relief!
