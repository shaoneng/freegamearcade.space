Kick The Dummy

How to Play Kick The Dummy

Feeling the weight of a stressful day? Kick The Dummy is the perfect outlet! The goal is simple: unleash your frustration on a resilient robot dummy and watch your stress disappear. There are no complicated rules, just pure, satisfying action.

Getting started is easy. Simply use your left mouse button to start hitting, slapping, and punching the dummy. Each hit contributes to wearing down its durability, indicated by the health bar at the bottom of the screen.

To really ramp up the fun, explore the wide array of weapons available. Look at the icons at the top of the screen to browse through different categories. You can equip everything from firearms and explosives to everyday objects. Select your weapon of choice and let the mayhem begin!
