Kick The Dummy

How to Play Kick The Dummy

Welcome to Kick The Dummy, the ultimate stress-relief game! Your objective is straightforward: interact with the dummy on screen to let off some steam. Use your mouse to punch, toss, and drag the dummy around the room.

To escalate the fun, look to the top of the game screen. You'll find a selection of icons representing different weapon categories. Click on an icon to open a menu of items, including melee weapons, firearms, explosives, and more. Simply click on a weapon to equip it and then use your mouse to unleash it on the dummy.

There are no complicated rules or objectives. The entire game is controlled with your mouse. Experiment with all the different tools at your disposal and enjoy the satisfying physics as you find creative ways to demolish the dummy. When you want to switch back to your bare hands, simply click the drop-weapon icon.
