Kick The Dummy

How to Play Kick The Dummy

Kick The Dummy is your personal digital punching bag, designed to help you blow off steam in a fun and harmless way. The objective is simple: inflict as much damage as possible on the interactive dummy using a wide array of tools and weapons.

Getting started is easy. Simply use your left mouse button to click or tap on the dummy to start punching it. Watch its durability bar at the bottom of the screen decrease with every hit.

To really ramp up the action, explore the weapon icons at the top of the screen. You'll find categories for firearms, melee weapons, explosives, and even miscellaneous objects. Click an icon to select your weapon of choice, then click on the dummy to use it. When you're done with a weapon, click the drop button (the last icon) to go back to your fists.
