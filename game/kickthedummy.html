Kick The Dummy

How to Play Kick The Dummy

Kick The Dummy is a straightforward and satisfying stress-relief game. The goal is simple: unleash your frustration on a durable robotic dummy using a variety of weapons and tools.

Use your mouse to interact with the game. Click the left mouse button repeatedly on the dummy to punch and kick it. Watch its durability bar at the bottom of the screen decrease as you inflict damage.

To switch things up, explore the weapon menu located at the top of the screen. You'll find icons representing different categories of weapons, including firearms, explosives, melee weapons, and even miscellaneous objects. Click an icon to select your weapon of choice and use it on the dummy for maximum impact. When you're done with a weapon, you can drop it using the designated button in the menu.

There's no complex objective or time limit. Just enjoy the cathartic experience of causing cartoonish mayhem and relieving your stress!
