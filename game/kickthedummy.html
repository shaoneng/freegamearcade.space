Kick The Dummy

How to Play Kick The Dummy

Kick The Dummy is your personal digital playground for stress relief! The goal is simple: unleash your frustrations on the resilient dummy using a variety of fun and explosive tools. There are no levels to beat or scores to achieve, just pure, satisfying gameplay.

Controlling the chaos is straightforward. Use your left mouse button to interact with the dummy. A simple click will deliver a punch or slap. When you select a weapon, the left mouse button will be used to aim and fire it.

To switch up your method of destruction, look at the icons at the top of the game screen. These represent different categories of weapons, from melee items to powerful firearms and explosives. Click an icon to browse the available tools, select one, and let the fun begin! You can drop your current weapon at any time by clicking the last icon in the row.
