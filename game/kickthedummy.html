Kick The Dummy

How to Play Kick The Dummy

Kick The Dummy is a simple yet satisfying stress-relief game. The objective is to unleash your frustration on a test dummy using a wide variety of tools and weapons. There are no complex rules or timers, just pure destructive fun!

Controls: The game is played entirely with your mouse. Use the left mouse button to interact with the game and attack the dummy.

Weapons: To start, you can simply click on the dummy to punch it. For more firepower, look at the icons at the top of the screen. Click on an icon to select a weapon, from firearms and explosives to everyday objects. Once a weapon is equipped, use your mouse to aim and click to attack. To drop your current weapon, click the final icon in the row.

Your goal is to deplete the dummy's health bar, located at the bottom of the screen. Experiment with different weapons to find the most satisfying way to relieve your stress!
