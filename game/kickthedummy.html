Kick The Dummy

How to Play Kick The Dummy

Welcome to Kick The Dummy, the perfect action game for blowing off some steam! If you've had a long day, this game provides a safe and fun outlet for your stress. Your goal is simple: unleash a barrage of attacks on the resilient dummy and watch its durability bar decrease with every hit.

Getting started is incredibly easy. Simply use your left mouse button to click on the dummy to punch and slap it. Each click lands a blow, providing immediate satisfying feedback. This is the most basic way to interact and begin your stress-relieving session.

To take the action to the next level, explore the arsenal of weapons available at the top of the screen. You'll find icons for various categories, from firearms and melee weapons to explosives and even everyday objects. Click an icon to equip a weapon, then click on the dummy to use it. When you're done with a weapon, simply click the drop button to go back to your bare hands.
