Kick The Dummy

How to Play Kick The Dummy

Kick The Dummy is your personal digital stress ball! The objective is simple: unleash your frustration on a hapless test dummy using a wide array of fun and destructive tools.

Controls: All you need is your mouse! Simply click the left mouse button on the dummy to hit it with your currently selected weapon or your bare hands.

Weapons: Look at the top of the screen to find your arsenal. You can select from various categories, including melee weapons, firearms, explosives, and even everyday objects. Click on an icon to equip a weapon and start the mayhem. Click the final icon to drop your current weapon.

The more damage you inflict, the more in-game money you'll earn. Use this money to unlock even more powerful and entertaining weapons to continue your stress-relieving session!
