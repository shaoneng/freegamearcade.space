Kick The Dummy

How to Play Kick The Dummy

Kick The Dummy is the ultimate stress-relief game with straightforward controls. The objective is simple: inflict as much damage as possible on the robotic dummy to let off some steam!

Use your mouse to interact with the game. Click the left mouse button repeatedly on the dummy to punch and slap it. This is your basic attack and a great way to start!

To ramp up the fun, explore the wide array of weapons available. Look at the icons at the top of the game screen. Each icon represents a different category of weapon. You can choose from firearms, explosives, sharp objects, and even blunt items like a bench. Click an icon to select your weapon, then use it on the dummy.

There are no complicated rules or objectives. Simply choose your favorite way to cause mayhem and enjoy the satisfying visual and sound effects. When you're done with a weapon, you can drop it using the last button in the weapon selection row. Play, experiment, and find your favorite way to kick the dummy!
