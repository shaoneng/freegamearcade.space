Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

Welcome to Kick the Pirate, the ultimate stress-relief game! The objective is simple: use your arsenal of weapons to hit the pirate puppet. Just click or tap on the screen to use your currently equipped weapon and send him flying.

Each hit you land earns you coins. The more damage you deal, the more coins you collect. Your goal is to rack up as much gold as possible to upgrade your gear.

Use the buttons at the top of the screen to switch between the weapons you've unlocked. Save up your coins to purchase new and more powerful items from the shop. The better your weapons, the faster you'll earn coins and become the ultimate pirate-kicking champion!
