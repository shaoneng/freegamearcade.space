Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

The goal in Kick the Pirate is delightfully simple: inflict as much damage as possible on the pirate character. Use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with the game and your starting weapon, a cannon. Aim and fire to send the pirate flying and start earning coins for every hit you land.

As you accumulate coins, you can unlock a variety of new and powerful weapons. Look for the weapon icons at the top of the screen to browse and purchase upgrades. The more advanced your weapons, the more damage you'll deal and the faster you'll earn coins. Experiment with different weapons to maximize your pirate-punishing efficiency!
