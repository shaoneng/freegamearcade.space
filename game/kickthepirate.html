Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

Welcome aboard, captain! Your mission in Kick the Pirate is simple and satisfying: give the grumpy pirate a good kicking! The game is all about stress relief and chaotic fun.

To play, simply click or tap on the pirate to hit him with your currently selected weapon. Each hit will earn you valuable gold coins.

Use the coins you collect to unlock a huge arsenal of wacky and powerful weapons. From classic cannonballs to more... unconventional tools, each new weapon makes the pirate-punishing even more fun.

You can easily switch between your unlocked weapons using the icons at the top of the screen. The better your weapons, the more coins you'll earn, allowing you to unlock everything faster. Aim, click, and enjoy the show!
