Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

The goal in Kick the Pirate is simple and satisfying: use a variety of weapons to hit the pirate and earn coins. The more you hit him, the more coins you'll collect!

Controls: Simply click or tap on the pirate to use your currently equipped weapon. You'll see him react to every hit.

Weapons: At the top of the screen, you'll find your arsenal of weapons. Click the buttons to switch between them. As you earn more coins, you can unlock new and more powerful weapons to deal even more damage.

Objective: Keep hitting the pirate to rack up coins, complete levels, and unlock the entire collection of wacky weapons. It's the perfect game to let off some steam!
