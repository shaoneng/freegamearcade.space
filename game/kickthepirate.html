Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

Welcome to Kick the Pirate, the ultimate stress-relief game! The objective is straightforward: cause as much mayhem as possible by using the pirate as your personal target.

Simply click or tap on the pirate to hit him with your currently equipped weapon. Each successful hit will earn you coins. The more damage you do, the more coins you'll collect.

Use the coins you earn to unlock a variety of wild and powerful weapons. You can switch between your unlocked weapons using the icons at the top of the screen. Experiment with different tools to maximize your coin earnings and find the most satisfying ways to send that pirate flying!
