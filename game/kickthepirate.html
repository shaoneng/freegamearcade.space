Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

Your goal in Kick the Pirate is all in the name: use your arsenal to beat up the pirate! Use your mouse to click (or your finger to tap) on the pirate to hit him. With every hit, you'll earn gold coins that fall to the bottom of the screen.

Use the coins you earn to buy new and more powerful weapons. You can switch between your unlocked weapons using the icons at the top of the screen. The stronger your weapons, the more damage you'll do and the faster you'll rack up coins.

This game is the perfect way to blow off some steam. There are no complex levels or objectives, just the pure satisfaction of upgrading your arsenal and sending that smug pirate flying. Keep kicking to unlock everything and achieve maximum pirate-punishing power!
