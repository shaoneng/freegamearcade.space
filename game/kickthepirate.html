Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

The goal in Kick the Pirate is simple and satisfying: use an arsenal of weapons to hit the pirate and earn as many coins as you can. It's the perfect game for relieving stress!

To begin, simply click or tap on the pirate to strike him with your current weapon. Each successful hit will add coins to your total, which is displayed on the screen.

Use the coins you accumulate to purchase new and more powerful weapons from the shop. The selection of weapons is available at the top of the game screen. The stronger your weapon, the more coins you'll earn per hit.

You can switch between any of your unlocked weapons at any time. Experiment with different tools of destruction to find your favorites and maximize your earnings. Keep playing to unlock the entire wacky arsenal and show that pirate who's boss!
