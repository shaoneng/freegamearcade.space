Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

The goal in Kick the Pirate is straightforward and satisfying: inflict as much damage as possible on the pirate character to earn coins. He's just standing there, waiting for you to unleash your arsenal!

Simply click or tap on the pirate to attack him with your currently equipped weapon. Each hit will send him flying around the screen and earn you valuable coins based on the damage you deal.

Use the coins you earn to purchase new and more powerful weapons. You can switch between your unlocked weapons using the icons at the top of the screen. The better your weapons, the more damage you'll do and the faster you'll accumulate coins to unlock everything!
