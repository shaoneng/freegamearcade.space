Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

The goal in Kick the Pirate is simple: cause as much mayhem as possible! Your primary objective is to hit the pirate with various weapons to earn coins. Simply click or tap on the pirate to use your currently equipped weapon. The more you hit him, the more coins you'll accumulate.

Use the coins you earn to unlock a wide arsenal of fun and powerful weapons. You can switch between your unlocked weapons using the buttons located at the top of the game screen. Experiment with different tools of destruction to maximize your coin earnings and enjoy the satisfaction of sending the pirate flying!
