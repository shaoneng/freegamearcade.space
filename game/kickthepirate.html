Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

Welcome to Kick the Pirate, the ultimate stress-relief game! Your mission is simple: use the cannon and a variety of other weapons to kick the grumpy pirate as much as you can. He's just standing there, waiting for you to unleash your arsenal.

The controls are incredibly easy. Just click or tap on the pirate to fire your currently equipped weapon. Each successful hit will earn you gold coins. Watch the coins rack up as you send the pirate flying across the screen!

Use the coins you earn to unlock a huge collection of wacky and powerful new weapons. You can switch between your unlocked weapons using the icons at the top of the game screen. The better the weapon, the more coins you'll earn, allowing you to complete levels and unlock even more satisfying tools of destruction. Have fun!
