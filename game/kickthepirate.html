Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

Welcome to Kick the Pirate, your go-to game for some harmless, stress-relieving fun! The goal is straightforward: give the grumpy pirate on screen a good walloping to earn coins and blow off some steam.

Simply click or tap on the pirate to attack him with your currently equipped weapon. Each hit earns you coins, which you can use to upgrade your arsenal.

At the top of the screen, you'll find your collection of unlocked weapons. Click on the icons to switch between them. Experiment with different tools of destruction—from cannons and guns to more surprising items—to maximize your coin earnings and send that pirate flying!

Keep an eye out for opportunities to unlock new and more powerful weapons. The better your gear, the faster you'll rack up points and complete levels. Now, get to kicking!
