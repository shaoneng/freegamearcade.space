Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

Welcome to Kick the Pirate, the ultimate stress-relief game! Your objective is straightforward: inflict as much damage as possible on the pirate character to earn coins.

Use your mouse or finger to click and interact with the weapons. At the start, you'll have basic options like a cannon. Aim and fire to send the pirate flying!

As you deal damage, you'll earn gold coins. Use these coins to unlock a wide variety of new and powerful weapons. You can switch between your unlocked weapons using the icons at the top of the game screen. The better your weapons, the more coins you'll earn, allowing you to unlock everything and cause maximum mayhem!
