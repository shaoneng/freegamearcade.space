How to Play Kick the Pirate

Welcome to Kick the Pirate, the ultimate stress-relief game! Your objective is simple: use your arsenal of weapons to hit the grumpy pirate as much as you can. Every successful hit will earn you coins, which are key to your progress.

To attack, simply click or tap on the pirate with your equipped weapon. The more damage you deal, the more coins you'll accumulate. Keep an eye out for the weapon selection buttons at the top of the screenâ€”this is where you can browse and choose different weapons you've unlocked.

Spend your hard-earned coins in the shop to unlock new and more powerful weapons. Stronger weapons help you earn coins faster, allowing you to complete levels and get even crazier tools of destruction. The goal is to maximize your pirate-punishing efficiency and have fun!
