Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

Ahoy matey! Pirates may be brave on the high seas, but they’ve never met you. In Kick the Pirate, your job is simple: turn this grumpy pirate into your personal punching bag. It’s oddly satisfying to send him flying with a cannonball. This destroy game is the perfect way to blow off some steam.

There are tons of wacky weapons to unlock, each more satisfying than the last. You can switch between weapons using the buttons at the top of the screen. The stronger your weapons, the faster you can rack up coins and complete levels.

Kick the Pirate was developed by FreezeNova with casual fun in mind. While it doesn’t take itself seriously, it delivers a surprisingly satisfying gameplay loop perfect for quick sessions or all-out pirate punishment marathons.

While Kick the Pirate uses cartoon-style visuals and lets you toggle off blood effects, parents should know the core gameplay revolves around violence toward a pirate character. It’s meant to be humorous and exaggerated, not realistic. Recommended for players 10 and up with parental guidance if needed.
