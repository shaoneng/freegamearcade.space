Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

The goal in Kick the Pirate is simple and satisfying: make the grumpy pirate your personal punching bag! Click or tap on the pirate to hit him and earn coins for every successful blow.

Use the coins you earn to buy a wild arsenal of weapons. Select your weapons from the icons at the top of the screen. From basic cannonballs to more elaborate and wacky tools, each weapon helps you deal more damage and earn coins faster.

The more you play, the more you earn, and the better your weapons become. Keep upgrading your gear to complete levels and unleash maximum punishment on the pirate. It's the perfect way to have some fun and blow off steam!
