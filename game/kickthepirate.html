Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

Ahoy matey! Pirates may be brave on the high seas, but they've never met you. In Kick the Pirate, your job is simple: turn this grumpy pirate into your personal punching bag. He's muttering curses or just standing there looking smug. But it's oddly satisfying to send him flying with a cannonball. Seriously. Bad day at school? Boss annoyed you? This destroy game is the perfect way to blow off some steam without flipping a table.

Some games make you think. Others make you feel. And this one? It just lets you kick a pirate with a cannon. And honestly, that's all we needed today. In case you still have some steam left to blow off, we have a full collection of dummy games. You can kick the alien who tries to colonize your planet with some laser guns and sci-fi weapons. Or perhaps you may want to beat an Evil Santa who keeps the gifts to himself instead of giving them away. Oh, you still need more? Write "Kick the" in the search bar or go and explore all destroying games we have!

There are tons of wacky weapons to unlock, each more satisfying than the last. You can switch between weapons using the buttons at the top of the screen. The stronger your weapons, the faster you can rack up coins and complete levels.

Kick the Pirate was developed by FreezeNova with casual fun in mind. While it doesn't take itself seriously, it delivers a surprisingly satisfying gameplay loop perfect for quick sessions or all-out pirate punishment marathons.

While Kick the Pirate uses cartoon-style visuals and lets you toggle off blood effects, parents should know the core gameplay revolves around violence toward a pirate character. It's meant to be humorous and exaggerated, not realistic. Recommended for players 10 and up with parental guidance if needed.
