Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

Had a bad day? In Kick the Pirate, you get to take out your frustrations on a grumpy pirate who's just asking for it. Your one and only job is to turn him into your personal punching bag, and it's incredibly satisfying to send him flying with a well-aimed cannonball.

The game is all about causing mayhem with an ever-growing arsenal. Use the buttons at the top of the screen to switch between tons of wacky weapons, each more destructive than the last. From cannons to more exotic tools, find your favorite way to punish the pirate.

The more damage you inflict, the more coins you'll earn. Use these coins to unlock and upgrade your weapons, which will help you rack up points even faster and complete levels. It's a simple, addictive loop perfect for blowing off some steam!
