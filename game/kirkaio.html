Kirka io

How to Play Kirka io

Welcome to Kirka.io, a fast-paced 3D first-person shooter set in a vibrant, blocky world reminiscent of Minecraft. Your mission is to compete against other players online across a variety of exciting battlefields and game modes.

Before you jump into the action, you can choose your weapon loadout. Select from five different weapon options to find the one that best suits your combat style. The primary goal in most modes is to achieve the highest kill score and dominate the leaderboard. Don't worry about running out of ammo; you have an infinite supply, allowing you to focus purely on strategy and aim!

Controls are simple and intuitive for desktop players: use the WASD keys to move your character, the Space Bar to jump, and your mouse to aim and shoot. You can switch your weapon at any time during the game by opening the change weapon menu. Choose a map, pick a mode, and prove you're the top player in the Kirka.io arena!
