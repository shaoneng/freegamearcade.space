Kirka io

How to Play Kirka io

Kirka io is an exciting 3D first-person shooter set in a blocky, Minecraft-inspired universe. Your objective is to combat other online players and achieve the highest kill score on the server.

Before jumping into a match, you can choose from seven unique game modes, each with its own objectives and rules. You'll also select from various maps, providing diverse battlefields for every game. Once you're ready, pick your weapon loadout from five different options to best suit your combat style.

During the game, you can switch your weapon at any time by opening the change weapon menu. Don't worry about conserving ammo; in Kirka io, you have an infinite supply of bullets, so you can focus entirely on your aim and strategy. Jump, shoot, and outmaneuver your opponents to dominate the leaderboard!
