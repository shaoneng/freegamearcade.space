Kirka io

How to Play Kirka io

Kirka.io is a thrilling 3D first-person shooter set in a blocky, voxel-based world. Your mission is to compete against players from around the globe in fast-paced combat. The primary goal across most game modes is to achieve the highest kill score and lead your team to victory.

The controls are intuitive for any FPS fan. Use the WASD keys to move your character around the map. Aim your weapon with the mouse, and left-click to fire. Jump over obstacles with the Spacebar and use Shift to dash for a quick burst of speed. You can crouch using the C key.

Before you jump into a match, you can choose from various game modes, maps, and weapon loadouts. Select a weapon that fits your playstyle, whether it's an assault rifle, a sniper, or a shotgun. Don't worry about running out of ammunition; you have an infinite supply! You can also change your weapon at any time during the game by accessing the menu.
