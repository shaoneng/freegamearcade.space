Legendary Sniper

How to Play Legendary Sniper

Step into the boots of an elite soldier in Legendary Sniper. As the most skilled marksman in your unit, the outcome of the war rests squarely on your shoulders. Your commander will provide you with a series of missions, each with specific objectives.

Your primary goal is to neutralize all enemy targets to complete each mission. Use your mouse to aim your high-powered sniper rifle. Right-click to look through your scope for a more precise view of the battlefield.

When you have an enemy in your crosshairs, left-click to fire. For the best results and to truly earn your title, aim for headshots to eliminate your opponents with a single, decisive shot. Complete all missions to prove you are the ultimate Legendary Sniper!
