Legendary Sniper

How to Play Legendary Sniper

Your objective in Legendary Sniper is to prove your elite marksmanship by following direct orders from your commander. The war has begun, and as the most talented sniper in the squad, you must complete each mission to turn the tide of battle.

Use your mouse to aim your sniper rifle at enemy soldiers. To get a closer, more accurate view of your target, use the right mouse button to look through your scope. This is crucial for lining up the perfect shot from a distance.

When your target is in your sights, press the left mouse button to fire. For maximum efficiency and to truly prove your skill, aim for headshots. Complete all mission objectives to secure victory and solidify your status as a legendary sniper.
