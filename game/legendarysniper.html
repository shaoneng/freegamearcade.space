Legendary Sniper

How to Play Legendary Sniper

Welcome to the front lines, soldier! In Legendary Sniper, your objective is to follow orders from your commander and eliminate enemy forces using your high-powered sniper rifle. The fate of the battle is in your hands, so precision is key.

Each mission will present you with a new set of targets. Use your mouse to aim your rifle and scan the environment. When you've acquired a target, use the Right Mouse Button to look through your scope for a magnified view. This will help you line up the perfect shot.

To fire your weapon, simply click the Left Mouse Button. For maximum effectiveness and to complete your missions successfully, aim for headshots. Prove your legendary skills by completing every objective and leading your side to victory!
