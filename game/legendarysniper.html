Legendary Sniper

How to Play Legendary Sniper

In Legendary Sniper, you are the squad's elite sniper, tasked with turning the tide of battle. Your mission is to follow the commands of your general and eliminate enemy threats from a distance. The success of each operation depends entirely on your steady hand and keen eye.

The controls are intuitive and designed for precision. Use your mouse to look around and aim your sniper rifle. To get a closer look at your target, use the right mouse button to zoom in with your scope. Once your target is in your sights, press the left mouse button to fire.

Each level presents a new mission. Listen carefully to your commander's briefing to identify the correct targets. Your goal is to complete the missions by neutralizing the specified enemies. For the best results and to prove your legendary status, aim for headshots to eliminate your foes with a single, clean shot.
