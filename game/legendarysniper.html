Legendary Sniper

How to Play Legendary Sniper

The war has begun, and you are the elite marksman destined to turn the tide. In Legendary Sniper, your objective is to follow the general's commands and complete a series of high-stakes missions. The fate of the battle rests on your steady hand and keen eye.

Use your mouse to aim your sniper rifle. Look through the scope to find your targets as described in your mission briefing. Your primary goal is to eliminate enemies efficiently and cleanly, with headshots being the most effective way to prove your skill.

Listen carefully to your commander's instructions for each level. Complete each mission to progress and prove you are the most talented sniper in the squad. Take a deep breath, hold steady, and make every shot count!
