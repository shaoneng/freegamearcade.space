Legendary Sniper

How to Play Legendary Sniper

Your objective in Legendary Sniper is to follow your commander's orders and eliminate all designated enemy targets. The battlefield is live, and your precision is paramount to the success of each mission.

Controls:

  • Aim: Use your mouse to move the crosshairs and aim your sniper rifle.
  • Zoom: Right-click to look through your scope for a more precise view of your target.
  • Shoot: Left-click to fire your weapon.

Listen carefully to your mission briefings. Each level will present a new challenge and a new set of targets. For maximum points and to prove your elite status, aim for headshots! A steady hand and a sharp eye are your greatest assets. Good luck, soldier.
