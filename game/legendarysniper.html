Legendary Sniper

How to Play Legendary Sniper

Welcome to the front lines, soldier! In Legendary Sniper, you are the squad's most elite marksman, and the fate of the battle rests squarely in your hands. Your general will issue commands and missions that you must complete with precision and speed. The war has begun, and your exceptional aiming skills are the key to victory.

Your primary objective is to eliminate all designated enemy targets in each mission. Use your mouse to aim your high-powered sniper rifle. To get a closer look at your target, right-click to use your scope. When you have the enemy in your sights, left-click to fire. Aim for headshots to neutralize enemies instantly and prove your legendary status to your commander.

Listen carefully to your mission briefings, remain patient, and make every shot count. The enemy won't wait forever, so steady your hand, control your breathing, and become the hero your squad needs.
