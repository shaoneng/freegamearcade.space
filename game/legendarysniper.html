Legendary Sniper

How to Play Legendary Sniper

In Legendary Sniper, you are an elite sniper tasked with crucial missions during a war. Your objective is to follow the orders of your commander and eliminate designated enemy targets to help your side win the battle.

Use your mouse to aim your sniper rifle. Right-click to look through your scope for a magnified view, allowing for more precise shots. When you have a target in your sights, left-click to fire. The key to success is patience and a steady hand.

Each mission will present a new challenge. Pay close attention to your briefing to identify the correct targets. Aiming for headshots is the most effective way to neutralize enemies and will prove your skill. Complete all objectives to advance and solidify your status as a legendary sniper.
