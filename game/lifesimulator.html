Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

In Life Simulator, you get to live a whole new life from birth to death. Your goal is to guide your character by making important decisions about their family, friends, career, and finances. The controls are simple: just use your mouse to navigate and the left-click to interact with menus and make choices.

To succeed and live a full life, you'll need to keep an eye on these key stats:

Info & Money: The top-left corner shows your character's basic info (name, job, etc.), while the top-right shows your money. Manage your finances carefully, especially before you land a job!

Happiness: This meter changes based on life events like your love life, career success, and family relationships. Keeping it high is key to a fulfilling life.

Health: Don't let your health bar get too low! Visit a doctor when you're sick. If you can't afford one, you might need to try alternative options. Remember that low happiness can also negatively impact your health.

Traits: These personal characteristics change as you age and experience life. They'll affect your career opportunities and relationships.

You have the freedom to set your own goals. Become a famous actor, marry into royalty, or just explore the endless possibilities this simulation has to offer. The choice is yours!
