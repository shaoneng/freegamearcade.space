How to Play Life Simulator

Welcome to Life Simulator, where you have the power to live a whole new life from start to finish! The goal is to navigate your character from birth to old age by making all their important life choices.

The controls are simple: use your mouse to move the cursor and left-click to select options and interact with the game's menu. There are no complicated key combinations to remember, allowing you to focus on your decisions.

Pay close attention to your character's core stats. Your happiness, health, smarts, and looks will all play a crucial role in the opportunities you receive in life. Keep your health bar high by visiting a doctor when needed, and remember that low happiness can lead to depression, which negatively impacts your health.
