Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Welcome to your new life! In Life Simulator, your goal is to navigate a virtual life from birth to death by making key decisions. The entire game is controlled with your mouse; simply point and left-click to interact with the different options and choices presented to you as you age.

Keep a close watch on your vital statistics displayed on the screen. Your primary stats are Happiness, Health, Smarts, and Looks. These will influence the opportunities available to you in your career, relationships, and daily life. Your personal information, like your name and job, is on the top left, while your financial status is on the top right.

As you go through life, you'll be faced with choices about your education, career path, relationships, and activities. Each decision can impact your stats. For example, neglecting your health will cause the health bar to drop, requiring a visit to a doctor. If you're feeling down, your happiness might decline. Make choices that align with the life you want to live, whether it's becoming a millionaire, a criminal mastermind, or a beloved family person. The possibilities are endless!
