Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Welcome to Life Simulator, an immersive online game where you get to guide a character through their entire life, from birth to death. Your choices matter, shaping their career, relationships, finances, and overall happiness. Will you become a famous actor, a world traveler, or even royalty? The path is yours to create!

The controls are simple and intuitive. Just use your mouse to navigate the menus and left-click to make decisions and interact with the game world. No complex key combinations to remember!

To succeed in your virtual life, you'll need to keep an eye on several key stats:

  • Info: Check the top-left corner for basic details about your character, like their name, job, and location.
  • Money: Your financial status is displayed in the top-right. Manage your money wisely, especially when making big life decisions.
  • Happiness: This bar reflects your character's emotional state, influenced by everything from love life and family relations to career success.
  • Health: Keep this bar high. If it gets too low, you'll need to see a doctor. If you can't afford one, you might have to explore alternative options. Remember that low happiness can also negatively impact your health.
  • Traits: These personal characteristics evolve with age and experience, affecting your relationships and career opportunities.

That's everything you need to know to get started. Dive in, explore the endless possibilities, and live the life you've always imagined in Life Simulator!
