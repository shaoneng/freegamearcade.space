Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Getting started in Life Simulator is as simple as life itself! The game is entirely controlled by your mouse. Use your cursor to navigate the on-screen options and left-click to make your decisions and interact with the world.

Your primary goal is to guide your character through their entire life, from birth to old age. You will start as a baby and progress through the years by clicking the "Age" button. At each stage, you will face choices that impact your core attributes: Happiness, Health, Smarts, and Looks. These stats will influence the opportunities available to you in your career, relationships, and personal life.

Keep a close watch on your stats, visible on screen. Manage your finances, which are displayed at the top right. A good education can lead to a better job, but might put you in debt.
