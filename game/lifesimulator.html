Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Welcome to Life Simulator, a game where you control a person's entire life, from birth until their final days. Your goal is to navigate the ups and downs of life by making key decisions that shape their future.

The controls are simple: use your mouse to navigate the menus and left-click to make choices and interact with the game world. No complex keyboard commands are needed!

Health & Happiness: These bars at the top of the screen are crucial. Keep your character happy through positive life events and relationships. If their health gets low, seek medical attention. Remember that low happiness can also negatively impact health!

Finances: Keep track of your money in the top right. You'll need to get a job to earn an income, which will allow you to afford housing, healthcare, and other activities that can boost happiness.

Life Choices: As you age, you'll be presented with choices about your education, career, relationships, and major life events. Your choices directly impact your stats and the path your life takes. Will you strive for fame and fortune, or live a simple, happy life? The story is yours to write!
