Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Welcome to your new life! In Life Simulator, your goal is to guide a character from birth all the way to old age, making crucial decisions that will shape their destiny. Every choice matters, from their career path and relationships to their financial and personal well-being. The entire game is controlled using your mouse; simply point and left-click to interact with the various options and events that appear.

Keep a close eye on your character's vital stats to ensure they live a full and successful life. Your Happiness, Health, and Money are displayed at the top of the screen. Making good decisions will keep these stats high, while poor choices can lead to trouble. Your personal traits, like looks and intelligence, will also affect your opportunities in life.

From choosing a career to finding a partner, every decision is yours to make. Will you become a rockstar, a doctor, or even a criminal? The possibilities are endless. Click the "Age" button to advance time and see the consequences of your choices unfold. Good luck!
