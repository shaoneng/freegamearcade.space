Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Your journey in Life Simulator begins at birth! The goal is to navigate your character through their entire life, making choices that shape their destiny. From childhood to old age, every decision matters.

The game is controlled entirely with your mouse. Simply use your mouse to navigate the menus and left-click to make selections and interact with the different options presented to you throughout your life.

Pay close attention to your core stats, which are crucial for a successful life:

  • Happiness: This is influenced by your relationships, career, and life events. Low happiness can lead to depression and affect your health.
  • Health: Keep an eye on your health bar. If it gets too low, you'll need to seek medical attention—whether from a doctor, a chiropractor, or even a witch doctor if funds are tight!
  • Smarts & Looks: These traits can influence your career path and romantic opportunities.
  • Money: Manage your finances carefully. You'll need money for housing, education, healthcare, and leisure. Get a job to start earning!

As you age, new opportunities and challenges will arise. Choose your career, find love, start a family, or travel the world—the choice is yours. There are countless paths to take, so experiment and see where life leads you!
