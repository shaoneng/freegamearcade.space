Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

In Life Simulator, you guide a character from birth to death, making all the pivotal decisions that shape their journey. Will they become a famous actor, a world traveler, or even royalty? The choice is entirely yours. This engaging simulation game lets you explore endless possibilities and live a life you've only dreamed of.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use your mouse to navigate menus and left-click to make choices and interact with the game world. There are no complicated key combinations to remember.

To succeed, you'll need to keep a close eye on several key aspects of your character's life:

  • Info: Check the top-left section for essential details like your character's name, job, gender, and location.
  • Money: Your financial status is displayed on the top right. Manage your money wisely, especially before you secure a job.
  • Happiness: This crucial metric reflects your character's emotional state. It's influenced by everything from relationships and career success to tragic events.
  • Health: Monitor your health bar. If it gets too low, seek medical help. If you can't afford a doctor, alternatives like a chiropractor or even a witch doctor might be necessary. Remember that low happiness can also negatively impact your health.
  • Traits: Looks, Smarts, and other skills will change as you age and make life choices. These traits significantly affect your career prospects and love life.

With these basics in mind, you're ready to dive in and explore the rich, detailed world of Life Simulator. Start your new life today!
