Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Getting started in Life Simulator is incredibly simple. All interactions are handled with your mouse. Use your mouse pointer to navigate through the various menus and left-click to make choices and decisions for your character. There are no complicated keyboard controls to remember, allowing you to focus completely on living your new virtual life.

Pay close attention to your character's vital statistics, which are displayed on the screen. On the top left, you'll find key information like their name, job, and location. Your money is shown on the top right, a crucial factor when making financial decisions. Most importantly, keep an eye on your happiness and health bars. These stats will fluctuate based on your life choices, relationships, career success, and random events.

The core of the game is making decisions as your character ages from birth to old age. You'll choose whether to go to school, what career to pursue, who to date, and how to spend your time.
