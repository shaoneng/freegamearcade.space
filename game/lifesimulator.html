Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Ever wanted a fresh start? In Life Simulator, you guide a character through their entire existence, from birth to the very end. Your choices shape their destiny, creating a unique story every time you play. Decide on their career, manage their money, choose who they love, and navigate all the ups and downs life throws their way.

The controls are simple: use your mouse to move and left-click to interact with the game and make decisions. No complex keyboard commands to remember!

As you play, keep an eye on these key stats to manage your character's life effectively:

  • Info Section (Top Left): Get a quick overview of your character's basic details like name, job, and location.
  • Money (Top Right): Watch your bank balance closely, especially when making big financial decisions like buying a house or choosing a career path.
  • Happiness: This crucial stat is influenced by everything from your love life and family relationships to your job satisfaction. Low happiness can lead to problems!
  • Health: If your health bar drops, it's time to see a doctor. Be aware that prolonged unhappiness can also negatively impact your physical health.
  • Traits: These personal attributes develop over time based on your age and experiences, affecting your relationships and opportunities.

That's all you need to know. You're ready to jump in and explore the endless possibilities of a new life. What story will you create?
