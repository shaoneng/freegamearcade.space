Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Your journey in Life Simulator begins at birth. From that moment on, you will guide your character through every stage of life, making pivotal decisions that shape their destiny. Every choice, from what to study to who to date, has consequences that will influence your character's future.

The game is controlled entirely with your mouse. All actions, decisions, and interactions are performed with a simple left-click. There are no complicated keyboard controls to remember, allowing you to focus on the narrative and choices at hand.

Keep a close eye on your character's core stats. You'll need to manage your Happiness, Health, and Money to live a successful life. These stats are displayed on the screen and will fluctuate based on your decisions. For instance, a stressful job might increase your money but decrease your happiness and health. If your health gets too low, be sure to see a doctor!

There is no single way to 'win' Life Simulator. Your goal is to live the life you want. Will you chase fame and fortune, or prioritize family and happiness? Explore different paths, react to random life events, and see what kind of story you can create from start to finish.
