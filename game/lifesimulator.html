Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Your goal in Life Simulator is to guide a character through their entire life, from birth to death. You will make all the important decisions that shape their destiny, career, relationships, and well-being.

The game is controlled entirely with your mouse. Simply use your cursor to navigate menus and left-click to make selections and interact with the various life choices presented to you. There are no complicated keyboard controls to remember.

Pay close attention to your character's stats. Monitor their Happiness, which is influenced by relationships, career, and events. Keep an eye on their Health – visit a doctor if it gets too low. Manage your Money wisely, especially before you land a job. All your character's core information is available at a glance, allowing you to make informed decisions to live the best life possible.
