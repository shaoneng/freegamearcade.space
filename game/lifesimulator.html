Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Ever wanted a do-over? In Life Simulator, you can live a completely new life from birth to death, making all the crucial decisions along the way. Manage a new identity with a different family, job, and set of friends. The goal is to live the life you've always dreamed of, or perhaps one you never imagined.

The game is controlled entirely with your mouse; simply use your cursor to navigate and the left-click to interact and make choices. To succeed, you must pay attention to several key stats that define your character's life:

  • Info: Check the top left for your character's basic details like name, gender, job, and location.
  • Money: Your financial status is displayed in the top right. Watch your funds carefully, especially before you land a job.
  • Happiness: This vital stat fluctuates based on life events like career success, family relationships, love life, and loss. Low happiness can lead to depression.
  • Health: Keep your health bar up by visiting a doctor when needed. If you're short on cash, you might need to consider alternative healers. Remember, your happiness level can directly affect your physical health.
  • Traits: Your character's looks, intelligence, and other attributes will evolve over time and impact everything from relationships to career opportunities.

With endless possibilities at your fingertips, you can strive to become a famous actor, marry royalty, or simply see where life takes you. Dive in and start your new journey in Life Simulator!
