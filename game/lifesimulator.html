Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Welcome to your new life! The goal of Life Simulator is to guide a character from birth to death, making all the important decisions that shape their destiny. All interactions in the game are handled with your mouse. Simply point and left-click to choose actions, make decisions, and advance through your character's life.

To live a successful life, you'll need to keep a close eye on your character's core stats. Your Happiness, Health, and Money are displayed at the top of the screen. Happiness is affected by your relationships, career, and life events. Health is crucial; if it gets too low, you'll need to seek medical attention. Your money, displayed on the top right, dictates the choices you can afford to make.

A summary of your character's current status, including their name, job, and location, can always be found in the info section on the top left. As you age, new options and activities will become available. Will you focus on education, pursue a high-paying career, chase fame, or start a family? The choice is entirely yours. Live out your dream life, or a completely different one, and see what possibilities await!
