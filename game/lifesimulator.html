Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Start a new life from birth to death in Life Simulator! This engaging online game lets you guide a character through all of life's major milestones. Your choices will shape their destiny, from their career and finances to their family and love life. Be who you want to be: a successful professional, a world traveler, or even a petty criminal.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use your mouse to navigate the menus and left-click to make decisions and interact with the world. No complex key combinations to remember!

To live a full and successful life, you'll need to keep an eye on several key indicators. At the top of the screen, you'll find your character's basic info, money, happiness level, and health. Making smart financial choices is crucial, and you must monitor your health and happiness bars. If your health gets too low, seek medical help. Remember that happiness can also affect physical health if your character gets depressed.

The goal is entirely up to you. You have the freedom to set your own objectives, whether that's becoming famous, wealthy, or just finding happiness. Dive in and explore the endless possibilities that await in Life Simulator!
