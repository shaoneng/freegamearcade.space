Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Welcome to Life Simulator, where you get to guide a person's life from their very first breath to their last. The goal is to make choices that lead to a fulfilling life, whatever that means to you! You can become anything from a criminal to a celebrity—the path is yours to create.

The game is entirely controlled with your mouse. Simply click on the choices and options presented on the screen to advance your character's life. There are no complicated keyboard controls to remember, making it easy for anyone to jump in and start a new life.

To succeed, you must keep a close watch on your character's core stats. Your Happiness, Health, and financial status are crucial. Happiness can fluctuate based on relationships, career events, and major life decisions. Your Health bar indicates physical well-being; if it drops too low, you'll need to seek medical attention. Your money, displayed at the top right, will determine the opportunities available to you.

Every decision matters and contributes to your character's traits, relationships, and ultimate destiny. Explore the endless possibilities, set your own goals, and see what kind of story you can tell in Life Simulator.
