Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Embark on an entirely new life in Life Simulator! Your goal is to guide a character from birth to old age, making all the important decisions along the way. Every choice you make will shape their future, so think carefully!

The game is controlled entirely with your mouse. Simply use your left-click to select the options and activities you want your character to pursue. There are no complicated keys to remember, making it easy to jump right in and start living your new life.

Keep a close watch on your character's core stats. Happiness is influenced by relationships, career success, and life events. Health is crucial; make sure to visit a doctor if it gets low. Your Money determines what you can afford, from education to housing. Finally, your Looks and Smarts can open up new opportunities in your career and love life.

As you age, new options will become available. Will you focus on education, find a job, start a family, or even try to win the lottery? The path is yours to create. See if you can live a happy, healthy, and successful life!
