Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

The controls for Life Simulator are simple and intuitive. All you need is your mouse to navigate menus and your left mouse button to make choices. There are no complicated keyboard commands to remember, allowing you to focus on the life-changing decisions ahead.

Your goal is to guide your character from birth to death, making choices that shape their destiny. You'll decide on their education, career path, relationships, and how they spend their money. Each choice has consequences, influencing your character's stats and life story.

Keep a close eye on your character's core stats. Happiness is affected by relationships, career, and life events. Health is crucial; visit a doctor if it gets too low. Money dictates your options, from where you live to the medical care you can afford. Your character's basic information is always visible in the top left, so you can track your progress as you age.
