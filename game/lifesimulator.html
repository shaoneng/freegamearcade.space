Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Your goal in Life Simulator is to guide a character through their entire life, from birth to death, by making decisions for them. The game is entirely controlled with your mouse. Simply use your left-click to select the options and choices presented to you as your character ages each year.

Pay close attention to the key stats displayed on the screen. The info section at the top left shows your character's basic details like name, job, and location. Your money is on the top right, which is crucial for making financial decisions. Keep an eye on your Happiness and Health bars; low levels can have negative consequences. If your health drops, consider visiting a doctor.

As you age, you'll also develop traits like Looks, Smarts, and other skills. These attributes will influence your career opportunities, relationships, and overall life path. There's no single way to win; you can set your own goals, whether it's to become rich, famous, or simply live a happy life. Explore the endless possibilities and see what kind of life you can create!
