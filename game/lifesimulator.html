Life Simulator

How to Play Life Simulator

Welcome to a new virtual life! The game is controlled entirely with your mouse. Simply use your left-click to select options, make decisions, and advance your character's life story from birth to death.

Your primary goal is to navigate life's challenges and opportunities. Every choice matters, from what you study in school to the career you pursue and the relationships you build. There is no single way to win; you can set your own goals, whether it's to become a millionaire, find true love, or simply live a quiet, happy life.

Pay close attention to your main stats. Your Happiness, Health, and Money are critical. Keep your happiness high through positive life choices, see a doctor when your health is low, and manage your finances wisely by getting a job. Your decisions will directly impact these bars, shaping the outcome of your unique life story.
