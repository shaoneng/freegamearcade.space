Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Ever dreamed of turning a tiny delivery startup into a globe-spanning shipping empire? Logistics Inc lets you do just that. You'll start with just five employees, ten dollars, and a single bicycle. From there, every hire, upgrade, and marketing push is up to you in this rich, in-depth management simulation.

To begin, your screen will show a single bicycle line. Click the orange "BUY 1" button to purchase your first ride. Orders will immediately start appearing on the left. Next, hit the plus sign in the Staff column to assign a worker to the bike. A staffed vehicle moves and completes deliveries; an empty one just sits there.

A single bike won't build an empire. Reinvest your earnings into new vehicle tiers. After buying five of one vehicle type, the next tier—vans, trucks, planes, and eventually ships—will unlock. Purchase at least one of each new vehicle, and remember that advanced vehicles like planes and ships require multiple workers to operate.

Staff are your most valuable asset. Assign them to vehicles to speed up deliveries or to departments to unlock crucial support systems. Early on, place a worker in the HR panel to start recruiting new staff automatically. If growth is too slow, research the "Contractors" upgrade in the R&D tree, which allows you to hire new staff instantly for cash.

The Research & Development (R&D) department is where your growth truly accelerates. Staff assigned here unlock upgrades that slash costs, speed up HR, and boost marketing. While they don't generate income directly, their work multiplies all future profits. Prioritize R&D early for faster overall progress.

Idle vehicles mean lost profits. Open the Marketing department and assign staff to different vehicle types to increase the number of incoming orders. A few marketers spread across your fleet will keep everything balanced and busy.

Vehicles degrade over time. Once unlocked through R&D, the Vehicle Maintenance department lets you hire mechanics to keep your fleet at 100% efficiency. You'll also need to "Upskill" your mechanics to train them to work on more advanced vehicles, ensuring your entire operation runs smoothly.

As your revenue skyrockets, use the x10 or MAX multipliers to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is a perfectly balanced operation with zero idle staff, full order queues, and a money counter that never stops spinning. Logistics Inc. is crafted by Kodiqi.
