Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Ever dreamed of turning a tiny delivery startup into a globe-spanning shipping empire? Logistics Inc lets you do just that. In this online idle game, you begin with a handful of employees, a few dollars, and a single bicycle courier. From there, every strategic decision is yours, from hiring and R&D to marketing and vehicle maintenance.

Your journey begins with a single bicycle line. Click the orange "BUY 1" button to purchase your first ride. As soon as you do, orders will start appearing. To get things moving, hit the plus sign in the Staff column to assign a worker to the bike. A staffed vehicle completes deliveries and earns money; an empty one just sits idle.

Bikes won't build an empire. Reinvest your earnings to expand your fleet. After buying 5 of one vehicle type, the next tier unlocks, introducing vans, trucks, planes, and ships. Purchase at least one of each new vehicle and assign the required number of staff—some larger vehicles need more than one operator. Balance your growth carefully, as higher-tier vehicles are more expensive.

Your staff are your most valuable asset. Assigning them to vehicles speeds up deliveries, while placing them in departments unlocks crucial support systems. Put a worker in HR early on to start recruiting new staff automatically and for free. For faster growth, unlock the R&D center and research the Contractors upgrade, which allows you to hire staff instantly for cash.

The Research & Development (R&D) department is where your growth truly accelerates. Staff assigned here unlock efficiencies that cut costs, speed up HR, and boost marketing. While they don't generate direct income, the upgrades they provide multiply your future earnings significantly.

To keep the orders flowing, manage your Marketing department. Drag staff into the marketing bars for different vehicle types to increase their order volume. A well-balanced marketing strategy ensures all your vehicles stay busy. You'll also need to unlock Vehicle Maintenance to keep your fleet in top condition. Hire mechanics and upskill them to service everything from bikes to planes, ensuring your operations run at 100% speed.

As your revenue grows, use the purchase multipliers (x10, MAX) to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is a perfectly optimized dashboard: no idle staff, a constant flow of orders, and a money counter that never stops. Logistics Inc. is crafted by Kodiqi.
