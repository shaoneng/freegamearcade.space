Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Ever dreamed of turning a tiny delivery startup into a globe-spanning shipping empire? Logistics Inc lets you do just that, right in your browser. This online idle game challenges you to start with just five employees, ten bucks, and a single bicycle courier. From there, you'll manage everything from hiring and R&D to marketing and vehicle maintenance in a simulation that feels surprisingly like running a real business.

You start with a single bicycle line, five available employees, and ten dollars. Your first move is to click the orange "BUY 1" button for the bike. As soon as you do, orders will begin to appear. Next, assign a worker to the bike by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column. A staffed vehicle moves and completes orders; an empty one just sits there.

Bikes won't cut it for long. Reinvest your earnings to expand your fleet. After buying 5 vehicles of one type, the next tier unlocks, introducing vans, trucks, planes, and ships. Remember to staff these new vehicles—some, like planes, require multiple workers. Balance your vehicle purchases with your hiring rate to manage cash flow effectively.

Your staff are the engine of your company. Assign them to vehicles to speed up deliveries or to departments to unlock crucial support functions. Early on, place a worker in HR to start automatic, free staff recruitment. For faster growth, unlock the Contractors upgrade in R&D to hire staff instantly for cash.

Research & Development is where your business truly scales. Assign staff here to unlock upgrades that boost efficiency, speed up hiring, and enhance marketing. While R&D staff don't generate direct income, their work multiplies your future profits, making it a critical long-term investment.

An idle fleet is a profit loss. Use the Marketing department to ensure a steady stream of orders. Assign staff to different vehicle marketing lines to boost incoming orders for those specific vehicle types. This helps keep every part of your operation busy and profitable.

Vehicles degrade over time, slowing down your entire operation. Once unlocked via R&D, the Vehicle Maintenance department allows you to hire mechanics. Assign them to different vehicle types to keep your fleet running at 100% speed. You'll need to "Upskill" your mechanics to enable them to work on more advanced vehicles like planes and ships.

As your revenue grows, use the x10 or MAX multipliers to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly balanced operation: zero idle staff, full order queues, all vehicles fully maintained, and a constantly spinning money counter. See how large you can grow your logistics empire!

Logistics Inc. is crafted by Kodiqi. If you enjoy this style of management game, check out Idle Startup Tycoon for another deep dive into production lines and strategic growth.
