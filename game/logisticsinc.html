Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Your journey begins with a small team and a bit of cash. Your first goal is to get your initial vehicle, a bicycle, operational. Click the "BUY 1" button for the bike row. Once purchased, you must assign an employee to it by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column. A staffed vehicle will start completing orders and earning you money, which you can see on the left.

Don't stick with just bikes! Reinvest your profits to expand your fleet. After you purchase 5 of one vehicle type (e.g., 5 bikes), the next tier (vans) will unlock. Continue this process to unlock trucks, planes, and ships. Remember that larger vehicles can handle bigger orders but may require more than one staff member to operate, so balance your vehicle purchases with your hiring.

Your employees are the engine of your company. You can assign them to vehicles or to different departments. Early on, it's crucial to assign a worker to HR to start recruiting new staff automatically and for free. Unassigned workers don't generate any value, so make sure everyone has a job! For a quick boost in personnel, unlock the 'Contractors' upgrade in the R&D department to hire staff instantly for cash.

To truly scale your business, you'll need to focus on support departments. Assign staff to Research & Development (R&D) to unlock powerful upgrades that improve efficiency and speed up your growth. Use the Marketing department to ensure a steady stream of orders for all your vehicle types. Finally, don't forget Vehicle Maintenance! Assign mechanics to keep your fleet running at 100% speed, and remember to 'Upskill' them so they can work on more advanced vehicles.
