Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Logistics Inc is an online idle game where you build a shipping empire from the ground up. You start with just five employees, ten dollars, and a single bicycle. Your goal is to manage hiring, research, marketing, and vehicle maintenance to grow your tiny startup into a global powerhouse.

Your journey begins on a screen showing a bicycle line. Click the orange "BUY 1" button to purchase your first bike. Orders will immediately start appearing. To get moving, you must assign an employee by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column for the bike row. A staffed vehicle completes deliveries; an empty one does not.

Reinvest your earnings to expand your fleet. After buying 5 of any vehicle type, the next tier (vans, trucks, planes, ships) unlocks. Purchase at least one of each new type as you can afford it. Be aware that larger vehicles like planes and ships require multiple workers to operate. Balance your vehicle purchases with your hiring rate to ensure you have enough staff.

Employees are your most valuable asset. Assign them to vehicles to speed up deliveries or to support departments to unlock new abilities. A key early move is to assign a worker to HR, which will begin recruiting new staff automatically for free. For faster growth, unlock the Contractors upgrade in the R&D tree, which lets you hire staff instantly for cash.

Research & Development (R&D) is crucial for long-term success. Staff assigned here unlock upgrades that increase efficiency, speed up hiring, and boost marketing effectiveness. While they don't generate direct income, the upgrades they unlock will multiply your future profits significantly.

To keep the orders flowing, manage your Marketing department. Assign staff to different vehicle categories (bikes, vans, etc.) to increase the number of incoming orders for that line. This ensures your vehicles are always busy and generating revenue.

Vehicles require maintenance over time. Once unlocked via R&D, the Vehicle Maintenance department allows you to hire mechanics. Assign them to vehicles to keep them running at 100% speed. You'll also need to upskill your mechanics to work on more advanced vehicles like planes and ships.

As your company grows, use the x10 and MAX multipliers at the top of the board to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly balanced operation with no idle staff, a constant stream of orders, and a rapidly growing bank account. Logistics Inc. is crafted by Kodiqi.
