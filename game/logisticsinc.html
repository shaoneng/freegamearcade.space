Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Your journey begins with a humble setup: five employees, ten dollars, and a single bicycle line. Your first move is to click the orange "BUY 1" button next to the bike row to purchase your first vehicle. Once bought, orders will start appearing. To fulfill them, you must assign an employee by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column. A staffed vehicle moves and earns money; an empty one does not.

A single bike won't make you a tycoon. Reinvest your profits into expanding your fleet. After purchasing 5 of a certain vehicle type, the next tier unlocks. Progress from bikes to vans, trucks, planes, and eventually ships. Remember to assign staff to each new vehicle. Note that more advanced vehicles like planes and ships require multiple workers to operate, so balance your vehicle purchases with your hiring rate to maintain efficiency.

Your employees are your most valuable asset. The HR department is key to growth. Assign a worker to the HR panel, and they will start recruiting new staff automatically and for free. If you need a faster influx of personnel, invest in the Research & Development tree to unlock the "Contractors" upgrade, which allows you to hire staff instantly for cash.

Research & Development (R&D) is where you supercharge your growth. Assigning staff to the R&D center doesn't generate direct income but unlocks powerful upgrades that slash costs, speed up HR, and boost marketing. Prioritizing R&D early on will significantly accelerate your progress across all other aspects of the game.

To keep the orders flowing, you'll need the Marketing department. Assign staff to different marketing channels (bikes, vans, etc.) to increase the number of incoming orders for each vehicle type. Keep an eye on your vehicles' maintenance levels. Once unlocked via R&D, hire mechanics in the Vehicle Maintenance department to keep your fleet running at 100% speed. You'll also need to upskill your mechanics so they can work on more advanced vehicles.

As your revenue skyrockets, use the x10 and MAX purchase multipliers at the top of the screen to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly balanced operation with no idle staff, a constant stream of orders, a fully maintained fleet, and a rapidly growing bank account. Master the logistics and build your empire!
