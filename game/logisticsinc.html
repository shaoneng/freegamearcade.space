Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Your journey to becoming a shipping magnate starts small: with just a few employees, ten dollars, and a dream. Your first step is to purchase a bicycle by clicking the "BUY 1" button. Once you own a vehicle, orders will begin to appear. To start making money, you must assign an employee to the bike by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column. An unstaffed vehicle won't complete any deliveries!

One bike won't build an empire. Reinvest your profits into more vehicles. After you buy five of a single vehicle type, the next tier unlocks, allowing you to progress from bikes to vans, trucks, planes, and eventually massive ships. Be strategicâ€”larger vehicles can handle bigger, more profitable orders, but they have higher upfront costs and may require multiple employees to operate. Balance your purchasing with your hiring to keep your business growing smoothly.

Staff are the lifeblood of your company. You can assign them to vehicles to speed up deliveries or to special departments to unlock crucial upgrades. A great early move is to assign a worker to the HR department, which will automatically recruit new staff for free over time. If you need a faster influx of workers, invest in the R&D department to unlock the "Contractors" upgrade, allowing you to hire staff instantly for cash.

The key to exponential growth lies in three support departments. The Research & Development (R&D) center unlocks permanent upgrades that increase efficiency and slash costs. The Marketing department ensures your vehicles always have orders to fill. Finally, the Vehicle Maintenance department keeps your fleet in top shape; hire and upskill mechanics to ensure your vehicles operate at 100% speed.

As your revenue skyrockets, use the x10 or MAX purchase multipliers to expand your fleet rapidly. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly optimized logistics machine with zero idle staff, full order queues, and a bank account that never stops growing. Good luck, CEO!
