Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Your journey begins with a small team and a ten-dollar bill. Your first move is to purchase a bicycle by clicking the "BUY 1" button. Once you own a vehicle, assign an employee to it by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column. With a worker assigned, your bike will start completing deliveries, earning you cash.

To expand, reinvest your earnings into more vehicles. After buying five of one type (e.g., bikes), you'll unlock the next tier, like vans, trucks, and eventually planes and ships. Remember to staff these new vehicles; some larger ones require multiple employees to operate.

Your staff are your most valuable asset. Assign them to vehicles to generate income, or put them in support departments. Place a worker in HR to start recruiting new employees automatically for free. For faster growth, unlock the Contractors upgrade in R&D to hire staff instantly with cash.

Don't neglect Research & Development (R&D). Assigning staff here unlocks crucial upgrades that improve efficiency, speed up hiring, and boost your marketing power. While R&D doesn't generate direct income, its long-term benefits are essential for explosive growth.

Keep your business booming with the Marketing and Maintenance departments. In Marketing, assign staff to specific vehicle types to increase the number of incoming orders. In Maintenance, hire and train mechanics to keep your fleet running at 100% speed, preventing profit loss from breakdowns.

As your company grows, use the x10 or MAX multipliers to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly optimized logistics machine where every employee is busy, every vehicle is running, and your bank account is constantly growing.
