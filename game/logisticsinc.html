Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Your journey to becoming a logistics tycoon starts small. In Logistics Inc, you begin with a handful of employees, a small amount of cash, and a single bicycle. Your first move is to buy the bike by clicking the "BUY 1" button. Once purchased, assign a staff member to it. Now, you're officially in business! Orders will start coming in, and your bank balance will begin to grow with each successful delivery.

A single bicycle won't make you a millionaire. Reinvest your profits to expand your fleet. After purchasing five vehicles of one type, the next tier will unlockâ€”from vans and trucks to massive planes and ships. Each new vehicle type can handle larger, more profitable orders. Remember to assign the required number of staff to operate them; some advanced vehicles need more than one person.

Your employees are the engine of your company. You can assign them to vehicles to speed up deliveries or to specialized departments to unlock crucial upgrades. Head to the HR panel and assign a worker to start recruiting new staff automatically and for free. If you need a faster influx of talent, invest in the R&D department to unlock the "Contractors" upgrade, allowing you to hire staff instantly for cash.

To truly scale your operations, you must manage your support departments. The Research & Development (R&D) center is where you unlock powerful upgrades that boost efficiency and slash costs. The Marketing department ensures your vehicles always have orders to fulfill. Finally, the Vehicle Maintenance department keeps your fleet running at peak performance. Assign mechanics to repair vehicles and upskill them to handle more advanced machinery.

As your revenue skyrockets, use the multipliers at the top of the screen (x10, MAX) to buy vehicles in bulk. The goal is to create a perfectly balanced system where no staff are idle, order queues are full, and your money counter never stops spinning. Master the art of logistics and build an empire that spans the globe!
