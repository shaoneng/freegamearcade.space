Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

In Logistics Inc, your goal is to transform a small delivery service into a massive global logistics empire. You'll manage every aspect of the business, from hiring couriers and buying vehicles to researching new technologies and launching marketing campaigns. Success is all about smart investment and efficient management.

You begin with a small budget and a few employees. Your first step is to buy a bicycle from the main dashboard. Once purchased, orders will start coming in. Don't forget to assign an employee to the bike by clicking the plus sign in the 'Staff' column; a staffed vehicle is a working vehicle!

A single bike won't make you rich. Reinvest your profits into more vehicles. After buying 5 of one vehicle type, the next tier (like vans, trucks, and eventually planes) will unlock. Higher-tier vehicles handle bigger, more profitable orders, but require more staff and a larger upfront investment.

Your employees are your most valuable asset. Assign them to vehicles to speed up deliveries, or place them in specialized departments. Put a worker in HR to automatically recruit new staff over time. Send employees to the Research & Development (R&D) department to unlock powerful upgrades that improve efficiency across your entire operation.

To keep your vehicles busy, assign staff to the Marketing department. This ensures a steady stream of orders for your fleet. As you expand, your vehicles will require maintenance. Unlock the Vehicle Maintenance department through R&D and hire mechanics to keep your trucks, planes, and ships running at 100% speed and profitability.

As your revenue skyrockets, use the x10 or MAX multipliers to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly balanced system where no staff are idle, orders are always flowing, and your company's value climbs into the billions. Good luck, CEO!
