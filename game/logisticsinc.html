Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Welcome to Logistics Inc, where you'll transform a humble delivery startup into a global shipping powerhouse. You begin with just a few employees, a bit of cash, and a single bicycle line. Your goal is to manage every aspect of the business—from hiring and R&D to marketing and maintenance—to build a sprawling empire.

Your journey starts small. On the main screen, you'll see a bicycle line with zero staff assigned. Click the orange "BUY 1" button to purchase your first bike. Orders will immediately start appearing. To get things moving, click the plus sign in the Staff column to assign a worker to the bike. A staffed vehicle completes deliveries; an unstaffed one is just for show.

To expand, you'll need more than bikes. Reinvest your profits into new vehicle tiers. Once you buy five of a particular vehicle type, the next level unlocks, introducing vans, trucks, planes, and ships. Remember that larger vehicles like planes and ships require multiple workers to operate. Balance your vehicle purchases with your hiring to keep the orders flowing.

Your employees are your most valuable asset. Assign them to vehicles to speed up deliveries, or place them in departments to unlock crucial upgrades. A top priority early on is to staff the HR panel. HR will automatically recruit new employees for free, albeit slowly. For faster growth, invest in the R&D tree to unlock the Contractors upgrade, allowing you to hire staff instantly for cash.

Research & Development is the key to exponential growth. Assigning staff to the R&D center unlocks efficiencies that cut costs, accelerate HR, and amplify your marketing efforts. While these employees don't generate direct income, their work multiplies your future profits, making early investment in R&D a very smart move.

An idle fleet is a profit loss. Head to the Marketing department to ensure you always have a backlog of orders. Drag staff members onto the marketing bars for different vehicle types to boost incoming orders. Spreading your marketers across various lines helps maintain a balanced and efficient operation.

Vehicles degrade with use, slowing down your profits. Once you unlock Vehicle Maintenance through R&D, you can hire mechanics. Assign them to different vehicle types to keep your fleet running at 100% speed. You'll need to use the "Upskill" button to train your mechanics to handle more advanced vehicles like planes and ships.

As your company's value soars, use the purchase multipliers (x10, MAX) to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is a perfectly optimized dashboard: every employee assigned, orders flooding in, all vehicles perfectly maintained, and a revenue counter that never stops climbing.
