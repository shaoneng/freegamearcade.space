Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Your journey begins with just a few employees and a small amount of cash. Your first task is to buy your initial vehicle, a bicycle. Click the "BUY 1" button next to the bike row. Once purchased, orders will start appearing. To fulfill them, you must assign an employee to the bike by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column. A staffed vehicle will start making deliveries and earning you money.

A single bike won't make you rich. Reinvest your earnings into more vehicles. After buying five of one type, the next tier (like vans, then trucks) will unlock. Remember to staff these new vehicles; some, like planes and ships, require multiple workers to operate. Balance your vehicle purchases with your hiring rate to ensure you have enough staff to run your growing fleet.

Your employees are your most valuable asset. Assign them to vehicles to generate income, or to specialized departments to unlock crucial upgrades. Early on, place a worker in the HR department to start recruiting new staff automatically for free. For faster growth, unlock the 'Contractors' upgrade in the R&D tree to hire staff instantly for cash.

The Research & Development (R&D) department is key to scaling your business. Staff assigned here will unlock powerful upgrades that improve efficiency, speed up hiring, and boost marketing. To keep your vehicles busy, assign staff to the Marketing department. This ensures a steady stream of orders, preventing your fleet from sitting idle and losing potential profits.

As your fleet grows, vehicles will require maintenance to run at 100% speed. Unlock the Vehicle Maintenance department through R&D and hire mechanics to keep your trucks, planes, and ships in top condition. Your ultimate goal is to create a perfectly balanced operation with no idle staff, a constant flow of orders, and a rapidly increasing company valuation. Use the purchase multipliers (x10, MAX) to expand your empire even faster!
