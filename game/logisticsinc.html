Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Welcome to Logistics Inc, where you'll build a delivery empire from scratch! You start with just $10, five employees, and a dream. Your first move is to buy a bicycle and assign a worker to it. Click the "BUY 1" button for the bike, then the plus sign in the Staff column to get your first courier on the road. Orders will start appearing and your income will begin to trickle in.

To expand, you'll need a bigger fleet. After purchasing 5 of one vehicle type, the next tier unlocks—from bikes to vans, trucks, planes, and ships. Reinvest your profits to grow your fleet, but remember that advanced vehicles cost more and may require multiple staff members to operate. Balance your vehicle purchases with your hiring rate to ensure steady growth.

Your employees are your most valuable asset. Assign them to vehicles to complete orders or to specialized departments to unlock crucial upgrades. Placing a worker in Human Resources (HR) will start automatic, free staff recruitment. For faster growth, unlock the Contractors upgrade in Research & Development (R&D) to hire staff instantly for cash.

Success lies in managing your departments. R&D is where you unlock permanent efficiencies, cost reductions, and even new game features like Vehicle Maintenance. Marketing ensures a steady stream of orders for your fleet; assign staff here to boost demand. Once unlocked, the Maintenance department is vital for keeping your vehicles running at 100% speed—hire and upskill mechanics to service everything from bikes to jumbo jets.

As your company grows, use the x10 and MAX multipliers to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly balanced operation with no idle staff, a full order queue, and a constantly spinning revenue counter. Good luck, CEO!
