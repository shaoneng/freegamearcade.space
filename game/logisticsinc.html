Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Your journey in Logistics Inc begins with just a handful of employees, ten dollars, and a single bicycle courier line. To get started, click the 'BUY 1' button to purchase your first bike. Orders will immediately start appearing. Assign an employee to the bike by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column, and watch them start making deliveries and earning you cash.

A single bike won't make you a tycoon. Reinvest your profits into more vehicles. Once you purchase five of a specific vehicle type, the next tier (vans, trucks, planes, and ships) unlocks. Higher-tier vehicles handle larger, more profitable orders but require more staff to operate. Balance your vehicle purchases with your hiring to maintain steady growth.

Your staff are the engine of your company. You must assign them strategically across different departments. Send employees to the HR department to begin automatically recruiting new staff for free. This is crucial for early-game expansion. Don't let any employee sit idle; an unassigned worker is a missed opportunity.

Unlock powerful upgrades through the Research & Development (R&D) department. Staff assigned here will work to unlock efficiencies that cut costs, speed up hiring, and improve marketing. For a quick staffing boost, unlock Contractors in the R&D tree to hire new personnel instantly for cash. Meanwhile, the Marketing department ensures your vehicles always have orders. Assign staff to different marketing channels to increase the number of orders for each vehicle type.

Vehicles degrade over time, which slows them down and cuts into your profits. Once you unlock Vehicle Maintenance through R&D, you can hire mechanics to keep your fleet in top condition. Assign mechanics to specific vehicle types and upskill them as neededâ€”a bike mechanic can't fix a plane without proper training! Keep your maintenance icons green to ensure your fleet runs at 100% speed.

As your revenue skyrockets, use the x10 and MAX multipliers at the top of the screen to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly optimized logistics machine: no idle staff, a constant stream of orders, a fully maintained fleet, and a bank account that grows endlessly. How large can you build your empire?
