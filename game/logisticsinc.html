Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Dive into the world of logistics and build your shipping empire from the ground up! In Logistics Inc., you start with a handful of employees and a single bicycle. Your goal is to manage resources, expand your fleet, and optimize operations to become a global tycoon.

Your journey begins with ten dollars and five employees. On the main screen, find the bicycle row and click the orange 'BUY 1' button. Once you own a vehicle, orders will appear. To fulfill them, you must assign an employee to the bike by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column. Now you're officially in business, and cash will start rolling in with each completed delivery!

A single bike won't make you rich. Reinvest your profits into more vehicles. After purchasing five of one type, the next tier (like vans, trucks, and even planes) will unlock. Remember that larger vehicles handle bigger orders but cost more and may require multiple staff members to operate. Balance your vehicle purchases with your hiring rate to ensure steady growth.

Your staff are your most valuable asset. Assign them strategically to different departments to boost your company's efficiency. Place staff in HR to automatically recruit new employees, in R&D to unlock crucial upgrades, in Marketing to generate more orders, and in Maintenance to keep your fleet running at 100% speed.

As your revenue skyrockets, use the x10 and MAX purchase multipliers to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly balanced system where no staff are idle, order queues are full, and your profits continuously climb. Once you've mastered the logistics loop, see how big you can grow your empire!
