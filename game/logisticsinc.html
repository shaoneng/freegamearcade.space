Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Begin your logistics empire by purchasing your first vehicle. Click the 'BUY 1' button next to the bicycle row. Once you own a vehicle, you must assign staff to operate it. Click the plus sign in the Staff column to put an employee to work. A staffed vehicle will start completing orders, which appear on the left, earning you your first profits.

Use your earnings to expand your fleet. After buying 5 of one vehicle type, the next tier (like vans, trucks, and planes) will unlock. Purchase new vehicles and remember to assign staff to them. More advanced vehicles handle larger orders but may require more than one employee to run, so keep an eye on your available workforce.

Your staff is your most valuable asset. Assign employees to the HR department to automatically recruit new workers for free over time. To accelerate your growth, place staff in the Research & Development (R&D) center. R&D unlocks powerful upgrades that improve efficiency, speed up hiring, and boost your overall income. Investing here early pays off significantly in the long run.

To ensure a steady stream of profitable orders, assign staff to the Marketing department. This will keep your vehicles busy and your revenue flowing. As your fleet grows, vehicles will require maintenance. Unlock the Vehicle Maintenance department through R&D and hire mechanics to keep your trucks, planes, and ships running at 100% speed. Don't forget to upskill your mechanics so they can work on more advanced vehicle types!

As your company grows, use the x10 and MAX buttons to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly balanced operation with no idle staff, a constant flow of orders, and a money counter that never stops spinning. Strategize your investments and grow your small startup into a global logistics giant!
