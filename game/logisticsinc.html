Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Your goal in Logistics Inc is to grow a small delivery startup into a massive global empire. You start with just $10 and five employees. Your first move is to buy a bicycle by clicking the orange "BUY 1" button. Once purchased, assign at least one employee to it by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column. With a worker assigned, the bike will start completing orders, earning you money.

Use your earnings to expand your fleet. After buying 5 of one vehicle type (e.g., bikes), the next tier (vans) will unlock. Continue this progression to unlock trucks, planes, and ships. Each new vehicle type can handle larger, more profitable orders. Remember that more advanced vehicles like planes and ships require more than one worker to operate, so balance your vehicle purchases with your hiring rate.

Staff are the engine of your company. You can assign them to vehicles to speed up deliveries or to specialized departments. Place an employee in the HR panel to start recruiting new staff automatically and for free. If you need a faster influx of workers, invest in the R&D center to unlock the "Contractors" upgrade, which lets you buy staff for cash.

The Research & Development (R&D) department is key to long-term success. Assigning staff here unlocks powerful upgrades that improve efficiency, speed up HR, and boost marketing. While R&D staff don't generate direct income, their work multiplies your future profits significantly. To keep your vehicles busy, use the Marketing department. Assign employees to different vehicle marketing campaigns to increase the number of incoming orders for each type.

Vehicles require maintenance to run at peak performance. After unlocking it via R&D, you can hire mechanics in the Vehicle Maintenance panel. Assign them to different vehicle types to keep their speed at 100%. Mechanics must be upskilled to work on more advanced vehicles, a quick process that ensures your entire fleet stays profitable. As your revenue grows, use the x10 and MAX multipliers to buy vehicles in bulk and accelerate your growth towards building a logistics dynasty!
