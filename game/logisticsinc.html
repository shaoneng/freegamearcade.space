Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Welcome to Logistics Inc, where your mission is to build a massive shipping empire from the ground up! You'll start with just a handful of employees, a small amount of cash, and a single bicycle. Your goal is to manage resources, expand your fleet, and optimize every part of your business to become a logistics tycoon.

Your journey begins on the main dashboard. Purchase your first bicycle by clicking the "BUY 1" button. This will start generating orders. To fulfill them, you must assign an employee to the vehicle by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column. Once staffed, your bike will start making deliveries and earning you cash.

Reinvest your profits to expand your fleet. After buying five of one vehicle type, the next tier unlocks, progressing from bikes to vans, trucks, planes, and even massive cargo ships. Higher-tier vehicles handle larger, more profitable orders, but remember to hire enough staff to operate them effectively.

Your employees are your most valuable asset. Assign them strategically to different departments to unlock crucial benefits. Place staff in HR to automatically recruit new workers for free. Send employees to the Research & Development (R&D) center to unlock powerful upgrades that improve efficiency, speed up hiring, and more.

To keep the orders flowing, you need a solid marketing strategy. Assign staff to the Marketing department to generate more orders for specific vehicle types. As your vehicles get used, they'll require maintenance. Unlock the Vehicle Maintenance department through R&D and hire mechanics to keep your fleet running at 100% speed and efficiency.

As your company grows, use the x10 and MAX purchase multipliers to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly balanced operation with no idle staff, a steady stream of orders, and a constantly spinning money counter. Can you build a billion-dollar logistics empire?
