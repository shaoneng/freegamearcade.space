Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Ever dreamed of turning a tiny delivery startup into a globe-spanning shipping empire? Logistics Inc lets you do just that, right in your browser. This online idle game challenges you to manage every aspect of a growing logistics company, from hiring your first courier to optimizing a global fleet. It’s a deep management simulation that’s perfect for both a quick break and a long strategy session.

You begin with a single bicycle line, five available employees, and ten dollars. Your first move is to click the orange 'BUY 1' button to purchase a bike. Orders will immediately start appearing. To get things moving, click the plus sign in the Staff column to assign a worker to the bike. Remember, a staffed vehicle completes deliveries; an empty one doesn't.

Bikes won't be enough for long. Reinvest your profits into new vehicle tiers—vans, trucks, planes, and ships. A new vehicle type unlocks after you purchase five of the previous one. Higher-tier vehicles handle larger orders but cost more, so balance your expansion with your budget and workforce. Note that some advanced vehicles, like planes and ships, require multiple workers to operate.

Your employees are your most valuable asset. Assign them to vehicles to speed up deliveries or to departments to unlock crucial upgrades. A key early move is to staff the HR panel, which will start recruiting new employees automatically and for free. If you need a faster influx of staff, unlock the Contractors upgrade in the R&D tree to hire workers instantly for cash.

R&D is your key to exponential growth. While staff in this department don't generate direct income, they unlock powerful upgrades that increase efficiency, boost HR speed, and enhance marketing. Investing in R&D early on, especially in upgrades like Efficiency and Improved HR, will significantly accelerate your progress.

Keep your vehicles busy by assigning staff to the Marketing department. This ensures a steady stream of orders for all your vehicle types. Don't forget about Vehicle Maintenance, which unlocks through R&D. Hire mechanics to keep your fleet running at 100% speed. You'll also need to upskill your mechanics to allow them to work on more advanced vehicles.

As your revenue grows, use the x10 or MAX multipliers to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly balanced operation with no idle staff, a constant flow of orders, and a rapidly increasing company valuation. Logistics Inc. is crafted by Kodiqi. If you enjoy this type of strategic management, check out Idle Startup Tycoon for another compelling business simulation!
