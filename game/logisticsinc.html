Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Your journey as a logistics mogul begins humbly with a single bicycle, ten dollars, and five eager employees. Your first move is to buy the bicycle by clicking the "BUY 1" button. Once purchased, orders will begin to appear. To fulfill them, you must assign an employee to the bike by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column. A staffed vehicle gets to work immediately, earning you your first profits.

Bicycles are just the start. Reinvest your earnings to expand your fleet. After purchasing five of one vehicle type, the next tier unlocks—from vans and trucks to massive planes and ships. Remember that larger vehicles can handle bigger, more profitable orders but also require more staff to operate. Keep your hiring in pace with your vehicle purchases to ensure your fleet is always active.

Your employees are the lifeblood of your company. Assign them strategically to different departments to unlock crucial abilities. Put a worker in the HR panel to start recruiting new staff automatically and for free. If you need a faster influx of personnel, unlock the "Contractors" upgrade in the R&D tree, which allows you to hire staff instantly for cash.

To truly scale your operations, you'll need to invest in Research & Development (R&D) and Marketing. Assigning staff to the R&D center unlocks powerful upgrades that increase efficiency, speed up hiring, and more. Meanwhile, the Marketing department ensures you have a constant flow of orders. Drag staff into different marketing channels to boost demand for specific vehicle types.

Vehicles wear down with use, slowing your entire operation. Unlock the Vehicle Maintenance department through R&D and hire mechanics to keep your fleet running at 100% efficiency. You'll need to "Upskill" your mechanics to allow them to work on more advanced vehicles like planes and ships. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly balanced system where staff are always busy, orders are always flowing, and your company's value skyrockets.
