Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Your journey begins with a small startup: just ten dollars, five employees, and a dream. The goal is to grow this tiny operation into a global shipping empire. Your first task is to get your delivery service off the ground by purchasing your first vehicle.

On the main screen, find the bicycle row and click the orange "BUY 1" button. Once purchased, orders will begin to appear on the left. To start fulfilling them, assign a worker by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column for the bicycle. A staffed vehicle will start making deliveries, earning you cash.

Reinvest your earnings to expand your fleet. After buying five of one vehicle type, the next tier (vans, trucks, planes, ships) will unlock. Higher-tier vehicles handle bigger, more profitable orders but cost more and may require multiple staff members to operate. Balance your purchases with your hiring rate to ensure you always have enough workers.

Staff are your most valuable asset. Assign employees to different departments to unlock crucial abilities. Place a worker in HR to start recruiting new staff automatically for free. Put staff in the Research & Development (R&D) center to unlock powerful upgrades, such as Efficiency boosts or the ability to hire "Contractors" for instant cash-for-staff trades.

To keep the orders flowing, assign staff to the Marketing department. This ensures your vehicles are never idle. As your vehicles are used, they will require maintenance. Unlock the Vehicle Maintenance department through R&D and hire mechanics to keep your fleet running at 100% speed. Don't forget to upskill your mechanics so they can work on more advanced vehicles!

As your revenue grows, use the multipliers at the top (x10, MAX) to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly balanced system where staff are always busy, orders are plentiful, and your company's value skyrockets.
