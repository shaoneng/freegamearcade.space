Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Your journey to becoming a tycoon begins small. You start with just five employees, ten dollars, and a single bicycle courier line. To get things moving, click the orange 'BUY 1' button to purchase your first bike. Once you own a vehicle, orders will appear on the left. But a bike can't deliver on its own! Click the plus sign in the 'Staff' column to assign a worker to the bike, and watch them start completing deliveries and earning you cash.

One bike won't make an empire. Reinvest your earnings into new vehicle types to handle bigger orders. After you purchase five vehicles of one type (e.g., 5 bikes), the next tier (vans) will unlock. Continue this pattern to unlock trucks, planes, and massive ships. Remember to assign staff to your new vehicles; some larger ones like planes and ships require multiple workers to operate.

Your employees are the lifeblood of your company. Assign them to vehicles to generate income, or put them to work in specialized departments. Early on, place a worker in the HR panel. This department will automatically and freely recruit new staff over time. If growth feels slow, unlock the 'Contractors' upgrade in the R&D tree, which allows you to hire new staff instantly for cash.

The R&D department is your key to exponential growth. Staff assigned here don't earn money directly, but they unlock powerful upgrades that improve efficiency, speed up hiring, and boost your marketing efforts. Investing in R&D early pays off significantly in the long run, making every other part of your business more profitable.

Keep your fleet busy with a steady stream of orders by using the Marketing department. Assign staff to different vehicle marketing campaigns to increase the number of available jobs for them. Over time, your vehicles will suffer from wear and tear. Unlock Vehicle Maintenance through R&D, hire mechanics, and assign them to repair your fleet. You can also upskill mechanics to work on more advanced vehicles, ensuring your entire operation runs at 100% speed.

As your revenue skyrockets, use the purchase multipliers (x10, MAX) at the top of the screen to buy vehicles in bulk and accelerate your expansion. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly balanced system where you have no idle staff, a constant flow of orders, and a money counter that never stops spinning. Master the logistics, and build your fortune!

Logistics Inc. is created by Kodiqi. If you enjoy building empires from the ground up, check out Idle Startup Tycoon for another fantastic management challenge. Now go on and build your billionaire shipping operation!
