Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Your journey begins with a tiny startup: just five employees, ten dollars, and a single bicycle courier option. To start, click the orange 'BUY 1' button next to the bike to purchase your first vehicle. Once you do, orders will begin to appear. Assign a worker to the bike by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column to get it moving and start earning cash.

Bikes are just the beginning. Reinvest your profits to purchase new and better vehicles. After buying five of one vehicle type, the next tier unlocks, revealing vans, trucks, planes, and eventually massive ships. Remember that larger vehicles handle bigger orders but require more staff to operate, so balance your expansion with your hiring.

Your employees are your most valuable asset. Assign them to vehicles to speed up deliveries or to specialized departments to unlock crucial upgrades. A key early move is to assign a worker to HR, which will automatically (and freely) recruit new staff over time. For faster growth, you can later unlock the ability to hire expensive contractors for an instant workforce boost.

Unlock game-changing efficiencies in the Research & Development (R&D) department. Staff assigned here don't generate direct income but unlock upgrades that slash costs and speed up everything else. To keep your vehicles busy, use the Marketing department. Assign staff to different vehicle types to increase the number of incoming orders, ensuring a steady flow of profits.

Vehicles wear down over time, slowing your operation. Unlock the Vehicle Maintenance department through R&D and hire mechanics to keep your fleet at 100% efficiency. You'll need to 'upskill' your mechanics to handle more advanced vehicles like planes and ships. The ultimate goal is a perfectly optimized business with all staff assigned, a constant stream of orders, and a rapidly growing bank account. Good luck, CEO!
