Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Welcome to your new shipping startup! Your goal is to grow it into a massive empire. You start with a little cash and five employees. Your first move is to buy a bicycle by clicking the "BUY 1" button. Once you own a vehicle, assign an employee to it by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column. A staffed vehicle will start completing orders and earning you money.

As you earn money, reinvest it into your fleet. After purchasing five of one type of vehicle (like bikes), you'll unlock the next tier, such as vans, trucks, and eventually planes and ships. Remember to assign staff to each new vehicle. Larger vehicles can handle bigger, more profitable orders but may require more than one employee to operate.

Your employees are your most valuable asset. Assign them to vehicles to generate income, or place them in specialized departments to unlock powerful bonuses. Put a worker in the HR department early on to start recruiting new staff automatically for free. If you need a faster workforce boost, unlock the Contractors upgrade in the R&D tree to hire staff instantly for cash.

Unlock and staff key departments to maximize your efficiency. The Research & Development (R&D) center lets you unlock crucial upgrades that speed up everything from hiring to delivery. The Marketing department ensures your vehicles always have orders to fulfill. As your company grows, use the x10 and MAX buttons to purchase vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly balanced operation with no idle staff, a constant flow of orders, and a profit counter that never stops climbing. Master the logistics, and build your fortune!
