Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

In Logistics Inc, you start with a modest operation: five employees, ten dollars, and a single bicycle courier. Your goal is to build a global shipping empire!

To begin, click the orange "BUY 1" button next to the bicycle row to acquire your first ride. Once purchased, orders will start appearing. Next, assign at least one employee to the bicycle by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column – a staffed vehicle is a working vehicle!

Reinvest your earnings to expand your fleet. After buying 5 units of a vehicle type, the next tier (Vans, Trucks, Planes, Ships) unlocks. Remember that higher-tier vehicles can handle bigger orders but require more upfront cash and often more than one worker. Pace your purchases to match your hiring rate.

Staff are crucial. Assign them to vehicles to speed up deliveries or place them in departments. Early on, put a worker in HR to enable automatic staff recruitment. For faster growth, invest in R&D to unlock upgrades like "Contractors," allowing you to buy staff instantly.

The R&D department is vital for long-term growth, unlocking efficiencies, speeding up HR, and boosting marketing power. The Marketing department ensures a steady flow of orders; assign staff here to increase order bonuses for specific vehicle types. Lastly, don't forget Vehicle Maintenance, unlocked via R&D, where you hire and upskill mechanics to keep your fleet running at peak performance.

As your company grows, use the purchase multipliers (x1, x10, MAX) to buy vehicles in bulk. Your ultimate objective is a perfectly balanced operation: no idle staff, a constant stream of orders, well-maintained vehicles, and an ever-increasing company valuation. How many zeros can you add to your company's worth?
