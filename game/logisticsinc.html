Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Dream of building a global shipping empire from the ground up? Logistics Inc challenges you to do just that. In this idle management game, you start with a tiny operation—a single bicycle, five employees, and ten dollars. Your goal is to manage every aspect of your growing business, from hiring staff and researching new technologies to running marketing campaigns and keeping your fleet in top condition.

Your journey begins on a simple screen with a single bicycle line. Use your starting cash to purchase your first bike by clicking the "BUY 1" button. Once you do, orders will start flowing in. Assign an employee to the bike by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column—a staffed vehicle moves and completes deliveries, while an empty one sits idle.

A lone bicycle won't make you a tycoon. Reinvest your profits to expand your fleet. After purchasing five vehicles of one type, you'll unlock the next tier: vans, trucks, planes, and eventually, ships. Remember that more advanced vehicles, like planes and ships, require multiple workers to operate. Balance your vehicle purchases with your hiring rate to maintain a steady cash flow.

Your employees are your most valuable asset. Assign them to vehicles to speed up deliveries or to specialized departments to unlock powerful bonuses. Your first move should be to staff the HR panel, which will begin recruiting new employees automatically and for free. For faster growth, unlock the Contractors upgrade in the Research & Development tree to hire staff instantly for cash.

The R&D department is your key to exponential growth. Staff assigned here unlock upgrades that increase efficiency, boost HR speed, and enhance marketing power. In the Marketing department, you can assign staff to specific vehicle types to increase their order flow, ensuring your fleet is never idle. Finally, don't neglect Vehicle Maintenance; hire and upskill mechanics to keep your vehicles running at 100% speed, preventing profit loss from breakdowns.

As your revenue skyrockets, use the x10 and MAX multipliers to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is to create a perfectly optimized logistics machine with no idle staff, a constant stream of orders, and a money counter that never stops spinning. How big can you grow your empire?

Logistics Inc. is developed by Kodiqi. If you enjoy this style of management simulation, check out Idle Startup Tycoon for another deep dive into building a business from scratch!
