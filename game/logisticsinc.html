Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Begin your logistics empire with just a ten-dollar bill and five employees. Your first step is to purchase a bicycle by clicking the 'BUY 1' button. Once you have a vehicle, assign an employee to it by clicking the plus sign in the Staff column. A staffed vehicle will start completing orders, earning you money. Keep an eye on the orange bar in the Status cell to track your delivery progress.

Bicycles are just the beginning. Reinvest your profits to expand your fleet. After buying 5 of one vehicle type, the next tier (like vans, trucks, and planes) will unlock. Remember that larger vehicles can handle bigger orders but also require more staff to operate. Balance your vehicle purchases with your hiring rate to ensure you always have enough workers.

Your employees are your most valuable asset. Assign them to vehicles to speed up deliveries or to specialized departments. Early on, it's crucial to assign a worker to the HR department. This will start automatic, free staff recruitment, providing a steady stream of new hires to fuel your growth. For faster growth, unlock the 'Contractors' upgrade in R&D to hire staff instantly for cash.

To truly scale your business, you need to invest in support systems. Assign staff to the Research & Development (R&D) department to unlock powerful upgrades that increase efficiency and speed. Use the Marketing department to ensure a steady flow of orders for all your vehicle types. Finally, don't forget Vehicle Maintenance. Hire and upskill mechanics to keep your fleet running at 100% speed and maximize your profits.
