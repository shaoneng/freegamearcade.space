Logistics Inc

How to Play Logistics Inc

Ever dreamed of turning a tiny delivery startup into a globe-spanning shipping empire? Logistics Inc lets you do just that. You start with a single bicycle line, five employees, and ten dollars. Your first move is to click the 'BUY 1' button to purchase your first bike. Once you do, assign a worker to it by hitting the plus sign in the Staff column. A staffed vehicle gets to work immediately, completing orders and earning you cash.

Bikes alone won't build an empire. Reinvest your earnings to unlock new vehicle tiers. After buying 5 of one vehicle type, the next one becomes available—vans, trucks, planes, and eventually, massive ships. Remember that larger vehicles might require more than one staff member to operate. Balance your fleet expansion with your hiring rate to keep everything moving.

Your employees are your most valuable asset. Assign them to vehicles to speed up deliveries or to support departments for crucial upgrades. Drop a worker into the HR panel early on to start recruiting new staff automatically, for free. If you need a faster boost, unlock the 'Contractors' upgrade in R&D to hire staff instantly for cash, though they come at a premium.

Research & Development is your key to exponential growth. Staff assigned here unlock upgrades that slash costs, improve HR, and boost marketing. It's a smart move to keep a dedicated team in R&D. To keep your vehicles busy, head to the Marketing department. Assign staff to different vehicle lines to increase the number of incoming orders, ensuring your fleet is always generating profit.

Vehicles wear down over time. Unlock Vehicle Maintenance via R&D and hire mechanics to keep your fleet running at 100% efficiency. You'll need to 'Upskill' your mechanics to work on more advanced vehicles like planes and ships. As your revenue soars, use the x10 or MAX multipliers to buy vehicles in bulk. The ultimate goal is a perfectly balanced operation with zero idle staff, full order queues, and a constantly spinning money counter.

Logistics Inc. is crafted by Kodiqi. If you enjoy this deep management simulation, try your hand at Idle Startup Tycoon, another free browser game with similar satisfying loops of upgrades and growth. Good luck building your empire!
