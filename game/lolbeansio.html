LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

LOLbeans io is a hilarious multiplayer racing game where you compete against others in a series of chaotic obstacle courses. The goal is simple: reach the finish line and qualify for the next round before the elimination quota is met!

Controls:

  • Move: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to control your jellybean character.
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and dodge low-level obstacles.
  • Look: Move your mouse to look around and plan your route.

Each round throws you into a new, randomly selected course filled with wacky obstacles like swinging hammers, rolling balls, and slippery floors. Stay on the platforms, time your jumps carefully, and use the environment to your advantage. The last player standing at the end of all rounds is crowned the winner!

You can also create or join a private party to play exclusively with your friends. Just click the "Party" button in the main menu, share the room code, and get ready for some friendly, chaotic competition!
