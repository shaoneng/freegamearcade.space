LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

In LOLbeans io, your goal is to guide your jellybean character through a series of wild obstacle courses and be among the first to cross the finish line. Each round is a chaotic race against other players, and only a certain number of beans will qualify for the next level!

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD keys to run and steer your bean. Press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles, leap across gaps, and bounce off platforms. Timing your jumps is crucial to avoid falling off the course or getting hit by moving hazards like swinging hammers and wrecking balls.

As you progress, the courses become more challenging. Stay focused, adapt to each new obstacle, and outmaneuver your rivals to reach the final round and compete for the crown. You can also play with friends by creating a party room and sharing the room code!
