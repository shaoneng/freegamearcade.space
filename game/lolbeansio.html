LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

LOLbeans io is a fun and frantic battle-royale-style platformer. Your goal is simple: guide your jellybean character through a series of wild obstacle courses and be one of the first to cross the finish line to qualify for the next round. Keep racing until only one winner remains!

The controls are straightforward. Use your mouse to look around and your keyboard (W, A, S, D keys) to move your bean. Use the Spacebar to jump over gaps and dodge obstacles. Timing your jumps and movements is key to navigating the chaotic courses filled with swinging hammers, slippery floors, and bouncing platforms.

Want to play with friends? Click the 'Party' button in the main menu to create a private room. Share the room code with up to 30 friends to compete in your own private championship. It's a great way to experience the hilarious falls and epic victories together!

As you play and qualify for more rounds, you'll earn coins. Head to the in-game shop to spend your coins on cool new skins, hats, and other cosmetics to make your bean stand out from the crowd.
