LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

LOLbeans io is a competitive battle royale platformer where the goal is simple: be the first one to cross the finish line! You'll compete against numerous other players in a series of elimination-style obstacle courses. Only the best (or luckiest!) beans will make it to the final round for a chance at victory.

The controls are easy to learn. Use your WASD or Arrow Keys to move your bean around the course. Press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles or dive ahead of your competitors.

Each round begins with a mad dash. Pay attention to the obstacles aheadâ€”you'll need to navigate past swinging hammers, bouncy pads, slippery slopes, and tricky moving platforms. If you fall off the course, you'll respawn at the last checkpoint, but you'll lose precious time. The key is to run, jump, and dodge your way through the chaos to qualify for the next level before the player limit is reached.

As you play and succeed, you'll earn coins. Head over to the shop to spend your earnings on a wide variety of cool skins and hats to customize your bean. Want to play with friends? Use the 'Party' feature to create a private room and share the room code for a private, laughter-filled competition!
