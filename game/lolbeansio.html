How to Play LOLbeans io

Welcome to the wacky world of LOLbeans io! The goal is simple: guide your jellybean character through a series of chaotic obstacle courses and be one of the first to cross the finish line to qualify for the next round. Each race is a knockout-style competition, so you need to be quick and agile to survive!

Control your bean using your mouse to look around and keyboard (WASD/Arrow Keys) to run. Use the space bar to jump over gaps, bounce on pads, and dodge the crazy obstacles that stand in your way. Timing is everything, so watch the patterns of moving objects like swinging hammers and spinning platforms to find the perfect moment to proceed.

As you successfully complete rounds and improve your rank, you'll earn coins. These coins can be spent in the in-game shop to customize your bean with cool new skins, hats, and other cosmetics to stand out from the crowd.
