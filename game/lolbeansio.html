LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

Welcome to the wacky world of LOLbeans io! In this vibrant platformer, you take control of a tiny, clumsy jellybean and compete against other players in a series of chaotic obstacle courses. The objective is to run, jump, and dodge your way through the mayhem to qualify for the next round. Only the best (or luckiest!) beans will make it to the final course to compete for the crown!

The controls are simple: use your mouse to look around, and use the WASD or Arrow keys to move your bean. Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps, bounce on pads, and avoid swinging obstacles. Timing is everything, so watch your step and try to anticipate the movement of traps to stay ahead of the competition.

Want to play with friends? Click the "Party" button in the main menu to create a private room. You can then share the unique room code with your friends, allowing up to 30 players to join a single game. It's the perfect way to see who among you is the ultimate LOLbean champion!

As you play and complete rounds, you'll earn coins. These can be spent in the in-game shop to unlock a variety of fun skins, hats, and other cosmetics to customize your bean and show off your style on the course.
