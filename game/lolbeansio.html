LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

Welcome to the wacky world of LOLbeans io! The goal is simple: guide your jellybean character through a series of chaotic obstacle courses and be the first to cross the finish line. Each round, a certain number of players will qualify for the next, more challenging course, until only one champion remains.

Navigate the treacherous arenas filled with swinging hammers, slippery slopes, and bouncing platforms. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to run, and press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles. Timing is everything, so watch your step and plan your moves carefully to avoid being knocked out of the race!

As you play and qualify for more rounds, you'll earn coins. Head over to the in-game shop to spend your earnings on a wide variety of fun hats and skins to customize your bean and stand out from the crowd.

Want to challenge your friends? Use the "Party" feature to create a private room and share the room code. You can compete with up to 30 friends in your own private championship for ultimate bragging rights!
