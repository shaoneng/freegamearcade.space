LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

Welcome to the wacky and chaotic races of LOLbeans io! The goal is simple: guide your jellybean character through a series of wild obstacle courses and be the first to cross the finish line, or at least qualify for the next round before time runs out. Each race is an elimination round, so you need to be quick and agile to avoid being left behind.

The controls are straightforward:

  • Move: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to run around the course.
