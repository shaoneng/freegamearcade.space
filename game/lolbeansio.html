LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

Dive into the chaotic and hilarious world of LOLbeans io! In this vibrant multiplayer game, you control a clumsy yet determined jellybean character. Your mission? To race against dozens of other players across a series of wild and wacky obstacle courses.

Each round presents a new challenge, from swinging hammers and slippery slopes to giant bouncing balls. The goal is simple: navigate the obstacles, avoid falling off the edge, and be one of the first to cross the finish line to qualify for the next round. Only a certain number of players advance, so speed and agility are key!

Keep qualifying through multiple rounds until you reach the final showdown. The last bean standing is crowned the champion! Use your earnings to customize your bean with funny hats and skins to stand out in the crowd. Want to challenge your friends? Create a private party room and share the code to see who among you is the ultimate LOLbean.
