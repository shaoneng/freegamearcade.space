LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

Welcome to the wacky world of LOLbeans io! The goal is to guide your jellybean character through a series of chaotic obstacle courses and be one of the first to reach the finish line. Each round eliminates players, so you need to be quick and agile to advance to the final championship race.

The controls are incredibly simple. Use your mouse to look around and the WASD or Arrow Keys to run. Press the Space Bar to jump over gaps, bounce on pads, and dodge swinging obstacles. Timing your jumps is crucial to navigating the course successfully and not getting knocked out!

As you play, you'll earn coins that you can spend in the shop to customize your bean with cool skins and hats. You can also create a party to play directly with your friends! Just create a room, share the code, and get ready for some hilarious competitive fun. Remember, it's not just about speed, but also about surviving the mayhem!
