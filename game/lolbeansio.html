LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

Welcome to the wacky world of LOLbeans io! The goal is simple: guide your jellybean character through a series of chaotic obstacle courses. In each round, you'll race against dozens of other players. To advance, you must finish before the qualification spots run out. The ultimate objective is to be the last bean standing in the final round to claim the crown!

Controlling your bean is straightforward. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to run around the course. Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and dodge low-hanging obstacles. Use your mouse to look around and plan your path through the mayhem.

Each course is filled with unique challenges, from swinging hammers and slippery slopes to bouncing pads and collapsing platforms. Pay attention to the patterns and time your movements carefully to avoid getting knocked off. Don't give up if you fall; get back up quickly and keep running towards that finish line!

Want to play with friends? Click the "Party" button in the main menu to create a private room. Share the room code with up to 30 friends to compete in your own private championship. It's a fantastic way to see who among you is the ultimate LOLbean champion!
