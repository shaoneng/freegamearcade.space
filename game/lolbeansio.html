LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

Welcome to the wacky and wonderful arena of LOLbeans io! Your objective is simple: guide your jellybean character through a series of chaotic obstacle courses and race to the finish line. You'll be competing against dozens of other players online, so speed and agility are key.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to run and steer your bean. To leap over obstacles or across gaps, press the Space Bar. The controls are easy to learn, but mastering the physics and timing your jumps perfectly to avoid swinging hammers, rolling balls, and slippery slides is the real challenge.

Each game is a knockout-style championship. Only a certain number of players will qualify from each round, so you must place high enough to advance. The rounds get progressively harder, culminating in a final showdown where only one bean can be crowned the winner!

You can also create a party and play with your friends! Simply click the "Party" button, create a room, and share the code. It's a fantastic way to enjoy the hilarious mayhem with people you know.
