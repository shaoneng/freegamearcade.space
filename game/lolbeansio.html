LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

LOLbeans io is an exciting online platformer where you control a wobbly jellybean character through a series of challenging obstacle courses. The goal is to qualify for the next round by reaching the finish line before a set number of players are eliminated.

Use your keyboard to move and jump your bean. As soon as the race starts, run, jump, and navigate through a variety of hilarious obstacles like swinging pendulums, rotating platforms, and moving walls. Stay agile and don't fall off the course!

Earn coins by successfully completing rounds. Visit the in-game shop to spend your coins on fun new hats and skins for your jellybean, adding a personal touch to your character.

For an even more fun experience, play with friends in 'Party Mode'. Click the "Party" button in the main menu to create a private room and invite up to 30 friends using a unique invite code, or join a friend's existing room. Race against each other in real-time and chat while you wait for everyone to join!
