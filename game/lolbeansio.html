LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

In LOLbeans io, you control a small jellybean character competing in chaotic obstacle course races against dozens of other players online. The goal is simple: run, jump, and dodge your way through a series of wild challenges to reach the finish line before others.

Each race is a knockout round. You must qualify by finishing in the top group to advance to the next, more challenging course. The last bean standing in the final round is crowned the champion! Use your mouse to look around and your keyboard (WASD or Arrow Keys) to move, and press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles.

Want to play with friends? Click the "Party" button in the main menu to create a private room. Share the room code with your friends so they can join, and you can all compete in the same races together for ultimate bragging rights!
