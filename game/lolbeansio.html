LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

In LOLbeans io, you take control of a colorful jellybean character and compete in a series of frantic obstacle course races against other players online. The goal is simple: reach the finish line before time runs out or before a certain number of players have already qualified. Only those who make the cut will advance to the next, more challenging round.

Use your mouse to look around and your keyboard (WASD or arrow keys) to run. Press the spacebar to jump over gaps, bounce on platforms, and dodge the wacky obstacles in your path. Timing is crucial, as one wrong move can send you tumbling off the course and cost you precious seconds.

Want to play with friends? Use the "Party" feature in the main menu to create a private room. Just share the unique room code with your friends, and you can all compete in the same races together. As you play, you'll earn coins that can be spent in the shop to unlock fun new skins and hats to customize your bean!
