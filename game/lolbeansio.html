LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

Welcome to the wacky world of LOLbeans io! The goal is simple: be one of the first beans to cross the finish line in a series of chaotic obstacle courses. Navigate through a gauntlet of swinging hammers, slippery slopes, and bouncy platforms. Only a certain number of players will qualify for the next round, so you'll need speed, agility, and a little bit of luck to survive!

The controls are straightforward. Use your mouse to look around and the WASD or Arrow Keys to move your bean. Press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles and gain an edge on your opponents. Timing your jumps is crucial to avoid falling off the course!

Want to race against your friends? Click the 'Party' button in the main menu to create a private room. Share the room code with up to 30 friends and see who is the ultimate LOLbean champion. It's the perfect way to add a personal competitive twist to the fun!
