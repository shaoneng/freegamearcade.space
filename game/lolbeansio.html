LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

Welcome to the wacky world of LOLbeans io! In this game, you take control of a colorful jellybean character competing in a series of chaotic obstacle course races, similar to the TV show *Total Wipeout* or the popular game *Fall Guys*. The goal is simple: survive each round by reaching the finish line before a certain number of other players.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD keys to run and steer your bean. Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps, bounce on platforms, and dodge oncoming obstacles. The key is to maintain momentum while navigating the treacherous course filled with swinging hammers, rolling balls, and slippery surfaces.

Each race is a battle royale where only a fraction of the players qualify for the next, more challenging round. Keep playing to earn coins, which you can spend in the shop to unlock cool new skins and hats to customize your bean.

Want to play with friends? Click the "Party" button in the main menu to create a private room. Share the room code with up to 30 friends to compete in your own private championship. It's the ultimate test of friendship and falling with style!
