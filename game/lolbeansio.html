LOLbeans io

How to Play LOLbeans io

In LOLbeans io, you take control of a colorful jellybean character and compete against numerous other players in a series of chaotic obstacle courses. Your primary goal is to survive each round by reaching the finish line before a certain number of spots are filled. The last bean standing in the final round wins the crown!

The controls are straightforward. Use your mouse to look around and the WASD or Arrow Keys to run. Press the Spacebar to jump. Timing your jumps is crucial to clear gaps, bounce on pads, and dodge the wacky obstacles like swinging hammers and moving platforms that stand in your way.

Each match consists of multiple knockout rounds. After each race, a number of players are eliminated. Pay attention to the most efficient paths and watch how other players navigate tricky sections. Don't give up if you fall; get back up and keep running! You can also play with friends by creating a private party and sharing the room code.
