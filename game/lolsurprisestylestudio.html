LOL Surprise Style Studio

How to Play LOL Surprise Style Studio

Step into the glamorous world of LOL Surprise Style Studio and become the ultimate fashion stylist! Your mission is to help the fabulous dolls—Royal Bee, Neon Licious, Lady Diva, and Swag—get ready for their big photoshoots. With your keen eye for style, you'll complete fashion missions, earn rewards, and build your reputation as a top social media influencer.

Each level presents a specific fashion task. Your first step is to head to the store and use your in-game cash to purchase the perfect outfit that matches the mission's requirements. Browse through a huge collection of tops, skirts, trousers, and stunning dresses to find the right pieces.

Once your doll is perfectly styled, it's time to hit the photo studio! Choose a dazzling background that complements the outfit and then select the perfect pose to capture the doll's fierce attitude. Press the shutter button to take the picture and create a masterpiece.

Share your amazing photos on the in-game social media channels to get likes and attract new followers. The more likes you get, the more money you'll earn, allowing you to unlock even more stylish clothes and accessories. Grow your influence and expand your wardrobe to take on bigger and better fashion challenges!

This game is played using your mouse or by tapping on the screen, making it easy to select and try on different items. Besides the main missions, you can also enjoy a free-dress mode to experiment with your own unique styles and create endless fabulous looks.
