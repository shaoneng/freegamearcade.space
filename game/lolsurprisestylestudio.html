LOL Surprise Style Studio

How to Play LOL Surprise Style Studio

Welcome to the dazzling world of LOL Surprise Style Studio! Your main goal is to help the famous O.M.G. dolls—Royal Bee, Neon Licious, Lady Diva, and Swag—get ready for glamorous photo shoots. Each level presents a unique fashion mission you must complete to succeed.

Begin by checking your fashion task. You'll need to create a specific look. Head to the in-game store to browse and purchase the perfect tops, skirts, dresses, and accessories using the money you earn. Make sure your choices match the mission's requirements!

Once your doll is dressed to impress, it's time for the photo shoot! Choose a stunning background that complements the outfit, then select the perfect pose for the camera. When you're ready, snap the picture to capture your masterpiece.

After the shoot, share your photos on the game's social media channels. The more likes you get, the more followers you'll attract, and the more in-game money you'll earn. Use your earnings to expand your wardrobe and take on even more challenging style missions. Become a top fashion influencer!
