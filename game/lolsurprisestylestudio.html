LOL Surprise Style Studio

How to Play LOL Surprise Style Studio

Your main objective in LOL Surprise Style Studio is to become a top fashion influencer. You'll receive fashion missions that require you to style the L.O.L. Surprise dolls in specific outfits for photoshoots.

Start by checking the style task. Then, head to the in-game store to purchase the required clothing and accessories using the money you've earned. Mix and match tops, skirts, dresses, and a wide range of accessories to create the perfect look.

Once your doll is styled, it's time for the photoshoot! Select a stunning background, choose a dynamic pose, and snap the picture. Share your creations on the in-game social media to earn likes and followers, which will help you earn more money for even more fabulous outfits.

The game also features a free-play mode where you can dress up the dolls in any style you can imagine, letting your creativity run wild. Complete missions to expand your wardrobe and become the ultimate style icon!
