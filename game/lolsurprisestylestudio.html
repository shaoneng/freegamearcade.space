LOL Surprise Style Studio

How to Play LOL Surprise Style Studio

Welcome to the LOL Surprise Style Studio, where your fashion skills are put to the test! Your main goal is to help the fabulous LOL Surprise dolls—like Royal Bee, Neon Licious, and Lady Diva—get ready for glamorous photo shoots by completing specific fashion missions. Each mission will give you a style theme you need to match.

Start by accepting a fashion task. You'll need to purchase the right outfits from the in-game store using your starting money. Browse through a wide selection of dresses, tops, skirts, trousers, and amazing accessories. Once you've bought the perfect items, head to the dressing room and style your doll to meet the mission's requirements.

After your doll is dressed to impress, it's time for the photo shoot! Choose a stunning background, have your doll strike a cool pose, and snap the perfect picture. Share your creation on the in-game social media to get likes and attract new followers. The more popular your photos are, the more money you'll earn to expand your wardrobe and take on even bigger style challenges!

The game is easy to control on any device. Simply use your mouse to click or your finger to tap on the items and options to style your doll and navigate the menus.
