LOL Surprise Style Studio

How to Play LOL Surprise Style Studio

Your main objective in LOL Surprise Style Studio is to become a top fashion influencer. You'll complete style missions by dressing up the iconic LOL Surprise dolls—like Royal Bee and Neon Licious—for special photoshoots.

Each level begins with a specific fashion task. Pay close attention to the required theme. Use your in-game money to visit the store and purchase the perfect tops, skirts, trousers, and accessories. Ensuring your outfit matches the mission's brief is the key to success!

Once your doll is perfectly styled, it's time for the photo shoot! Choose a stunning background, select a dynamic pose, and capture the moment. Your photos will be shared on social media, earning you likes and followers, which gives you more in-game money to expand your wardrobe.

The game is played using your mouse or by tapping on the screen. Simply click or tap on the clothing items and accessories you want to select and purchase. It's easy and intuitive for players on both desktop and mobile devices.
