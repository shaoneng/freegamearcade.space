LOL Surprise Style Studio

How to Play LOL Surprise Style Studio

Welcome to the ultimate fashion challenge! In LOL Surprise Style Studio, your goal is to become a top stylist by dressing up the famous OMG dolls for glamorous photoshoots. Your journey begins with a fashion task that specifies the style you need to create.

Use your in-game money to visit the store and purchase the perfect outfits, from dresses and tops to skirts and trousers. Don't forget to grab matching accessories like sunglasses to complete the look! Once you've bought the required items, it's time to dress your doll.

After styling, you'll head to the photo studio. Select a stunning background that complements your outfit, then help your doll strike the perfect pose. Snap the picture and share it on the game's social media channels to earn likes and followers. The more likes you get, the more money you'll earn to buy even more fabulous clothes and expand your virtual wardrobe!
