LOL Surprise Style Studio

How to Play LOL Surprise Style Studio

Welcome to the ultimate fashion challenge! In LOL Surprise Style Studio, your goal is to become a top stylist and social media influencer. You'll team up with iconic dolls like Royal Bee, Neon Licious, Lady Diva, and Swag on their glamorous fashion missions.

Each mission presents a unique style task for a photo shoot. Your first step is to carefully read the requirements. Then, head to the in-game store to purchase the perfect tops, skirts, trousers, and accessories using your starting cash. Once you've bought the necessary items, it's time to dress up your doll to match the theme perfectly.

After styling, you'll enter the photo studio. Choose a stunning background that complements the outfit and select a dynamic pose for your doll. When everything looks perfect, snap the picture! Your photos will be shared on social media, earning you likes and followers, which translate into more in-game money. Use your earnings to expand your wardrobe and take on even more fabulous styling challenges!
