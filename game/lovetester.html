Love Tester

How to Play Love Tester

Wondering about the strength of your connection with someone special? Love Tester offers a fun and easy way to find out! The gameplay is incredibly simple.

Start by typing your name into the first text box. Then, in the second box, enter the name of your crush, friend, or partner. Once both names are entered, hit the button to let the love meter work its magic.

In just a moment, the Love Tester will reveal your compatibility score as a percentage, along with a brief comment on your relationship potential. Will it be a match made in heaven? There's only one way to find out! Remember, it's all in good fun, so test as many name combinations as you like!
