Love Tester

How to Play Love Tester

Ever wondered how much your crush likes you back? Love Tester is a fun and simple online game that lets you explore the potential of your romance. Just enter your name and your crush's name into the designated fields to get started.

Once you've entered the names, the Love Tester will perform its digital calculations and reveal your compatibility score as a percentage. You'll also get a short evaluation of your potential as a couple. Be warned, the results might not always be what you hope for, but don't be discouraged! You can always test other crushes and see what the future holds.

Think of Love Tester as a modern-day fortune teller, a fun digital twist on old tarot or oracle games. While it's a great way to have a laugh, remember that true love can't be measured by an algorithm. The best way to know if someone has feelings for you is to trust your instincts and observe how they act around you. So, enjoy the game, have fun, and don't take the results too seriously!

If you enjoy this, you might also like to try other Love Games for more fun, like the popular Romantic Secret Kiss.
