Love Tester

How to Play Love Tester

Ever wondered about the sparks between you and your crush? Love Tester offers a fun and playful way to find out! Simply enter your name and the name of the person you're curious about into the designated fields.

Once you've entered the names, hit the test button and let the magic happen! The Love Tester will perform its "calculations" and reveal your potential love compatibility as a percentage. You'll also get a short, fun evaluation of your connection.

Remember, this is all in good fun! True love can't be measured by a computer, so don't take the results too seriously. Enjoy the game, have a laugh, and maybe it will give you the little nudge of courage you need. Have fun and see what the Love Tester has in store for you!
