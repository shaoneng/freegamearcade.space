Love Tester

How to Play Love Tester

Want to know if you and your crush are meant to be? Love Tester is here to help you find out! The gameplay is simple and fun.

First, enter your name in the first box. Then, type your crush's name into the second box. Once both names are in, click the button and let the magic happen! The Love Tester will perform its calculations to determine your compatibility.

You will receive a result in the form of a percentage, along with a brief evaluation of your potential as a couple. Don't worry if the results aren't what you hoped for; you can always try again with another crush! Remember, it's all just for fun—the only person who truly knows is you. Enjoy the game and have a great time!
