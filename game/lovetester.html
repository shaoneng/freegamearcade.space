Love Tester

How to Play Love Tester

Ever wondered if you and your crush are a perfect match? Love Tester is here to help you find out in a fun and simple way! Playing the game is as easy as it gets.

First, find the two empty boxes on the screen. Type your name into the first box and the name of your crush, friend, or partner into the second box. Once both names are entered, click the button to start the love calculation.

The Love Tester will then analyze the names and reveal your compatibility score as a percentage, along with a short comment about your relationship potential. Don't take the results too seriously—it's all in good fun! Test different names and see what scores you get!
