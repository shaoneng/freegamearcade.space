Love Tester

How to Play Love Tester

Want to know if you and your crush are a perfect match? Love Tester is a fun online game where you can find out! The gameplay is simple: just type your name and your crush's name into the boxes provided.

Once the names are entered, the love calculator will work its magic and show you a compatibility percentage. You'll also get a short, fun evaluation of your potential relationship. Think of it as a modern, digital version of a fortune teller, just for matters of the heart.

While the results might be encouraging, remember it's all in good fun! True love can only be felt, not measured by an algorithm. So enjoy the game, have a laugh, and maybe test a few different crushes to see what happens!
