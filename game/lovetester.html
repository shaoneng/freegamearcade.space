Love Tester

How to Play Love Tester

Curious about the connection between you and someone special? Love Tester is a simple and fun game designed to measure the potential of your romance. Getting your love score is easy and only takes a few seconds!

To start, you'll see two input fields on the screen. Type your full name into the first box. Then, enter the full name of your crush, friend, or partner into the second box.

Once both names are entered, click the heart-shaped "Calculate" button. The love meter will spring into action, analyzing the names to determine your compatibility. Your result will be displayed as a percentage, along with a short, fun message about your relationship's potential.
