Love Tester

How to Play Love Tester

Wondering if you and your crush are meant to be? Playing Love Tester is incredibly simple and fun! The goal is to get a "love score" based on two names.

To start, you will see two text boxes on the screen. Type your name into the first box and the name of your crush, friend, or partner into the second box.

Once both names are entered, click the "Test It" button. The love meter will then perform its calculations and reveal your compatibility score as a percentage.
