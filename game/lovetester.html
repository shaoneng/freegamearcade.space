Love Tester

How to Play Love Tester

Wondering if you and your crush are meant to be? Love Tester is here to help you find out in a fun, simple way! Playing is as easy as can be.

Start by typing your name into the first text box. Then, enter the name of your crush, friend, or someone special in the second box. Once both names are entered, just click the "Test" button and let the magic happen!

The Love Tester will then calculate your compatibility and display the result as a percentage, along with a short, fun comment about your potential relationship. It's all for fun, so don't take the results too seriously! Feel free to test as many different name combinations as you like.
