Love Tester

How to Play Love Tester

Wondering if sparks could fly between you and your crush? Love Tester is a simple and fun game to playfully gauge your compatibility. Getting started is incredibly easy:

First, type your name into the designated field. Next, enter the name of your crush, friend, or that special someone you've been thinking about. Once both names are entered, simply click the button to let the Love Tester work its magic.

In just a moment, the game will reveal your love compatibility as a percentage and provide a short, fun evaluation of your potential as a couple. Remember, it's all in good fun! Use it to have a laugh, but always trust your own feelings when it comes to matters of the heart.
