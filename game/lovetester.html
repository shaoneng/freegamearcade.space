Love Tester

How to Play Love Tester

Ever wondered about the potential romance between you and your crush? Playing Love Tester is incredibly simple and fun! The game presents you with two text boxes. In the first box, type your name. In the second box, type the name of the person you're curious about.

Once both names are entered, just hit the 'Test' button. The love meter will start its calculations, and in a few moments, it will reveal your compatibility score as a percentage. You'll also get a short, fun evaluation of your potential as a couple.

Don't take the results too seriously! This is a game for fun and entertainment. If you don't get the score you hoped for, don't worry! You can always try again with another crush's name or simply have a laugh with your friends. Remember, true love can't be measured by a game, but it's a fun way to dream a little!
