Love Tester

How to Play Love Tester

Wondering if you and your crush are a perfect match? Love Tester makes it easy and fun to find out! The gameplay is incredibly simple.

First, locate the two text boxes on the screen. In the first box, type your name. In the second box, type the name of your crush, friend, or anyone you're curious about. Once both names are entered, click the button to start the love calculation.

The Love Tester will work its magic and reveal your compatibility score as a percentage. You'll also get a short, fun message about your relationship potential. Don't like the result? No worries! You can try again with different names as many times as you like. Remember, it's all just for fun, so enjoy the game and share your results!
