Love Tester Story

How to Play Love Tester Story

Love Tester Story is an interactive game with multiple modes to explore your romantic curiosities. The controls are simple, primarily using your mouse or touch screen to enter names and make selections.

Classic Love Tester Mode: Simply type your name and the name of your crush into the designated fields. Press the large heart button to calculate your love compatibility and receive your result as a percentage in just a few seconds!

Story Mode: For a narrative experience, choose the Story Mode. Here, you'll step into the shoes of a high school student named Grace. You will be presented with different scenarios in her love life. Click on the choices you want to make on her behalf. Your decisions matter and will lead to one of several different endings, so play again to explore them all!

Matchmaking Mode: Ready to play cupid? In the Matchmaking Mode, the game will give you a random name. It's your job to find the perfect match for that person based on your gut feeling. Test your matchmaking instincts and see if you can create the perfect couple!
