Love Tester Story

How to Play Love Tester Story

Welcome to Love Tester Story! This game is simple and fun, controlled entirely with your mouse or by tapping on the screen. The main goal is to explore the different game modes and see what the future of love holds.

In the classic Love Tester mode, simply type your name and your crush's name into the designated fields. Click the large heart button to calculate your love compatibility and receive a percentage score in seconds!

For a more narrative experience, choose the Story Mode. Here, you'll guide a high school girl named Grace through her love life. Make important decisions for her that will affect her relationships and lead to one of several different story endings. Your choices truly matter!

Feeling like a matchmaker? Try the Matchmaking Mode! You'll be given a random name and your task is to choose the most suitable partner for them based on your intuition. Test your skills and see if you can create the perfect couple.
