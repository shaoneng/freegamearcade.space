Love Tester Story

How to Play Love Tester Story

Love Tester Story is an interactive game with three fun modes, all controlled simply with your mouse or by tapping on the screen. Choose the mode you want to play from the main menu.

Classic Love Tester: This is the original mode. Simply type your name and your crush's name into the designated boxes. Click the large heart button to calculate your love compatibility and see your result as a percentage!

Matchmaker Mode: Put your matchmaking instincts to the test! In this mode, you'll be given a random name. Your goal is to browse through the other names and choose the one you think is the best match. See how well your predictions line up!

Story Mode: Step into the shoes of high schooler Grace and guide her through her love life. You will be presented with different scenarios and choices. Click on the decision you want to make on her behalf to advance the story. Your choices matter and will lead to one of several different endings, so play again to explore them all!
