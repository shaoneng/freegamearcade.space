Love Tester Story

How to Play Love Tester Story

Welcome to Love Tester Story, a game with three fun ways to explore the world of romance! Getting started is easy, just choose your preferred game mode from the main menu.

Classic Love Tester: This is the traditional mode. Simply type your name into the first box and your crush's name into the second. Click the large heart button and wait for the meter to reveal your love compatibility score as a percentage!

Story Mode: Step into the shoes of a high school student named Grace. In this mode, you'll read through her story and make important decisions about her love life and friendships. Your choices will shape the narrative and lead to one of several different endings. Replay to explore all the possibilities!

Matchmaker Mode: Put your matchmaking instincts to the test! The game will give you a random name, and your goal is to find the most suitable partner for them from a list of options. Trust your gut and see if you can create the perfect couple.
