Love Tester Story

How to Play Love Tester Story

Love Tester Story offers three exciting ways to play, all controlled with simple clicks or taps on your screen. Choose your preferred mode from the main menu to begin.

In the Classic Love Tester mode, your goal is to check love compatibility. Simply enter your name in the first field and your crush's name in the second. Once the names are in, press the large heart icon to get your compatibility score, shown as a percentage. Have fun testing different name combinations!

The Story Mode immerses you in the life of a high school student named Grace. You will guide her through her romantic journey by making important decisions. Each choice you make influences the story, leading to multiple different endings. Play again and choose a new path to see them all!

If you trust your instincts, try the Matchmaking Mode. In this mode, the game gives you a random name, and you must choose the most suitable partner for them from a list of options. Test your matchmaking skills and see if you can create the perfect pair!
