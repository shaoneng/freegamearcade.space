Love Tester Story

How to Play Love Tester Story

Love Tester Story offers three exciting game modes to explore! To get started, simply choose the mode you want to play from the main menu.

Classic Love Tester: In this mode, type your name into the first box and your crush's name into the second. Click the large heart button to calculate your love compatibility and see the results in a percentage!

Story Mode: Follow the romantic story of a high school girl named Grace. You will be presented with different scenarios and choices. Click on the decision you think is best to guide her journey. Your choices will determine which of the multiple endings you unlock.

Photo Match Mode: Test your matchmaking skills! You'll be given a name and must choose the best partner for them from a selection of photos. Trust your gut and see if you can make the perfect couple.
