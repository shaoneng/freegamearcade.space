Love Tester Story

How to Play Love Tester Story

Welcome to Love Tester Story, where you can explore love and compatibility in three fun modes! The controls are simple and intuitive, mainly using your mouse or touch screen to click and type.

Classic Love Tester: This is the original mode. Simply type your name and the name of your crush into the designated fields. Click the big heart button to calculate your love compatibility and see the results in a percentage!

Story Mode: Step into the shoes of a high school student named Grace. In this interactive story, you'll be presented with choices about her love life. Click on the options you think are best to guide her story and discover one of the multiple endings. Your decisions matter!

Matchmaker Mode: Put your matchmaking instincts to the test! In this mode, you'll be given a random name. Your goal is to type in a name that you think would be a perfect match. See how well your intuition works!
