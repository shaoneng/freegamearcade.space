Love Tester Story

How to Play Love Tester Story

Love Tester Story offers a fun and simple way to explore the mysteries of love through three exciting game modes. The controls are intuitive, primarily using your mouse or touch screen to navigate menus and make selections.

Classic Love Tester: This is the original mode. Simply type your name and the name of your crush into the designated fields. Press the large heart button to calculate your love compatibility and see the results in a percentage!

Story Mode: Step into the shoes of Grace, a high school student navigating her love life. In this mode, you will be presented with different scenarios and choices. Your decisions will directly impact the story, leading to one of several possible endings. Explore different paths to unlock the full story!

Matchmaker Mode: Put your matchmaking skills to the test! You'll be given a random name and your goal is to find their perfect match based on your intuition. See how well you can pair up different characters!
