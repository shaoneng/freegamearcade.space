Love Tester Story

How to Play Love Tester Story

Welcome to Love Tester Story! This game offers three fun ways to explore love and relationships. Your primary interaction will be using your mouse or trackpad to click on options and enter names into the text boxes.

Classic Love Tester: In this mode, simply type your name and your crush's name into the designated fields. Click the large heart button to see your love compatibility score as a percentage. It's a quick and fun way to see the potential sparks!

Story Mode: Step into the shoes of a high school student named Grace. You'll be presented with various scenarios in her love life. Read the story and click on the choices you want to make for her. Your decisions will lead to one of several different endings, so choose wisely!

Matchmaking Mode: Put your intuition to the test! The game will give you a random name, and your goal is to find the best possible match for them from the options provided. See how well you can play cupid and create happy couples.
