Love Tester Story

How to Play Love Tester Story

Love Tester Story is a delightful game with multiple modes, all controlled simply with your mouse. Here's how to dive into the fun:

Classic Love Tester: This is the heart of the game! Enter your name in the first field and the name of your crush, friend, or anyone else in the second field. Click the large heart button to calculate your love compatibility and see the percentage result in seconds.

Story Mode: Step into the shoes of high schooler Grace. In this mode, you will be presented with different scenarios in her love life. Read the story and click on the choices you want to make for her. Your decisions will influence the story and lead to one of several unique endings!

Matchmaker Mode: Put your matchmaking skills to the test! The game will give you a random name, and your goal is to choose the best possible match for that person based on your instincts. See how well you can play cupid!
