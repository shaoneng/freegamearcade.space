Madalin Stunt Cars Pro

How to Play Madalin Stunt Cars Pro

Getting started in Madalin Stunt Cars Pro is easy! First, dive into the garage and pick your dream ride from an incredible selection of supercars, including a Bugatti Veyron, Lamborghini Veneno, and even a police cruiser. Once you've chosen your vehicle, customize its color to match your style. Next, select one of the three unique maps from the top of the screen. Each map offers a different playground full of possibilities for you to explore.

Once you're on the track, the world is your oyster! There's no timer and no rulesâ€”just pure driving freedom. Accelerate, drift, and launch your car off massive ramps, through thrilling loops, and over various obstacles. The goal is to perform the most daring stunts you can imagine. Explore every corner of the map, from vast highways to intricate cityscapes, and see what kind of chaos you can create.

Want to play with friends? Switch to 'Multiplayer' mode from the main menu. Simply choose a username, create a room with a unique name, and share it with your friends to join your private stunt session. Compete to see who can pull off the most insane tricks or just cruise around the map together.

Controls:

  • Drive: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Handbrake: Spacebar
  • Nitro: Left Shift
  • Change Camera: C key
  • Police Lights (on police car): H key
  • Reset Car: R key
