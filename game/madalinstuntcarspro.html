How to Play Madalin Stunt Cars Pro

Welcome to the ultimate driving playground! Getting started in Madalin Stunt Cars Pro is simple. First, browse the incredible selection of high-performance vehicles, from a Lamborghini Veneno to a Bugatti Veyron, and even a police cruiser. Pick your favorite and customize its color to match your style.

Next, choose your arena. There are three distinct maps available, each offering a unique driving experience packed with ramps, loops, and wide-open spaces for you to explore. Simply select one from the top of the screen to begin your adventure.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your car, the Spacebar for the handbrake to perform sharp drifts, and Left Shift for a nitro boost. The goal is to drive freely, master incredible stunts, and explore every corner of the map. There are no rules or time limitsâ€”just pure driving freedom.

Want to play with friends? Select 'Multiplayer' from the main menu. You can create your own room, give it a name, and invite others to join for some epic online stunting action.
