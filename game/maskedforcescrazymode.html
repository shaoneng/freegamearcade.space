Masked Forces Crazy Mode

How to Play Masked Forces Crazy Mode

Welcome to the high-octane world of Masked Forces Crazy Mode! Your objective is simple: eliminate all enemies and dominate the battlefield. Whether you're tackling single-player missions or diving into online PvP, strategy and quick reflexes are key to victory.

Controls

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Fire: Left Mouse Click
  • Aim: Right Mouse Click
  • Reload: R Key
  • Change Weapon: Mouse Scroll
  • Pick Up Weapon: F Key
  • Menu: Tab Key

Gameplay & Progression

Start by customizing your soldier. Visit the armory to purchase and upgrade a wide range of weapons, from pistols and SMGs to miniguns and sniper rifles. Equip protective armor and intimidating masks to enhance your survivability. As you complete missions and defeat opponents, you'll earn cash and XP, allowing you to unlock even better gear. Don't forget to claim your daily rewards!

Game Modes

Single-Player Missions: Engage in a variety of challenges, such as winning deathmatches, eliminating a specific number of enemies, or surviving against the odds for a set amount of time. Each completed mission rewards you with valuable resources.

Online PvP: Ready for a real challenge? Jump into the online mode to create your own game room or join an existing one. Battle against up to 16 players across diverse maps in modes like Free For All (FFA), Teams, and Doomsday.
