Masked Forces Crazy Mode

How to Play Masked Forces Crazy Mode

Welcome to the high-octane world of Masked Forces Crazy Mode! Your primary goal is to eliminate all opponents and establish yourself as the ultimate warrior on the battlefield. Whether you're tackling solo missions or diving into online multiplayer, strategy and quick reflexes are key.

Controls:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Fire: Left Mouse Click
  • Aim: Right Mouse Click
  • Reload: R key
  • Change Weapon: Mouse Scroll
  • Pick Up Weapon: F key
  • Menu: Tab key

Before you jump into a match, visit the armory to equip your soldier. You can purchase and upgrade a wide variety of weapons, from pistols and SMGs to miniguns and sniper rifles. Don't forget to equip powerful armor and masks for added protection. Earn cash and XP by completing missions and logging in for daily rewards to unlock even better gear.

The game offers two main ways to play: a single-player mission mode with various objectives (like deathmatches or survival challenges) and an online PvP mode where you can create or join rooms to battle against up to 16 other players in modes like Free For All (FFA), Teams, and Doomsday.
