Masked Forces Crazy Mode

How to Play Masked Forces Crazy Mode

The goal in Masked Forces Crazy Mode is to dominate the battlefield by eliminating your enemies. You can engage in a variety of single-player missions or jump into online PvP matches. Before each battle, visit the armory to equip your soldier with powerful weapons and protective gear to gain an edge.

Master the battlefield with these simple controls. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character. Aim your weapon with the mouse, fire with the Left Mouse Button, and use the Right Mouse Button to aim down sights. Press R to reload your weapon, scroll the Mouse Wheel to switch between your equipped firearms, and press F to pick up weapons from fallen enemies. You can open the game menu by pressing the Tab key.

The game features two main modes. In the single-player campaign, you'll tackle missions with specific objectives like winning a deathmatch, eliminating a set number of foes, or surviving for a certain time. Completing these missions earns you cash and XP. In the online PvP mode, you can create your own room with custom rules or join an existing one to fight against players from around the world in modes like Free For All (FFA), Teams, and Doomsday.
