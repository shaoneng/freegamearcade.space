Masked Special Forces

How to Play Masked Special Forces

Welcome to the battlefield of Masked Special Forces! This game is an action-packed multiplayer first-person shooter. Your primary objective is to team up with other players from around the world, strategize, and eliminate the opposing team to claim victory. Jump straight into the action by joining an existing room, or take control by creating your own custom match with your preferred map and game mode.

Before you dive in, explore the extensive customization options on the main menu. You can equip yourself with a powerful arsenal of upgradable weapons, including rifles, shotguns, and melee weapons. Fine-tune your loadout, select unique perks, and even customize your victory dances and kill messages to stand out in the arena. You can also track your stats like K/D ratio, total kills, and wins in the profile section.

The controls are standard for FPS games, ensuring you can jump right into the fight:

  • WASD to move your character.
  • Mouse to aim and look around.
  • Left Mouse Button to shoot.
  • Right Mouse Button to aim down sights.
  • R to reload your weapon.
  • Spacebar to jump.
  • Shift to sprint.
