Masked Special Forces

How to Play & Game Features

Masked Special Forces is a high-octane multiplayer first-person shooter that drops you into the heart of the battle arena. As a skilled commander, you'll need to strategize with your team to take down opponents in a graphically rich environment that rivals console games.

Before diving into the fast-paced action, you can thoroughly customize your soldier. From upgradable weapons and armor to unique kill messages and victory dances, you have full control over your in-game persona. You can join an existing room or create your own on one of four detailed maps.

Player discretion is advised: This game includes mature elements such as intense action and violent sequences.

Game Modes & Maps

Choose from 6 insane game modes and battle across four hyper-realistic maps, each with its own tactical advantages and hidden gems.

Armory & Perks

Equip yourself for combat with two customizable load-outs. Choose your primary and secondary weapons from a vast selection of shotguns, rifles, and melee options to create the perfect combination for your playstyle. Unlock powerful Perks to gain a tactical edge on the battlefield.

Track Your Progress

The profile section allows you to monitor your performance with detailed statistics, including K/D Ratio, Games Played, Total Kills, Headshots, and more. You can also save and load your experience progress using a unique code, ensuring you never lose your hard-earned stats.

Developed by FreezeNova, Masked Special Forces has become a beloved classic in the online shooter community, paving the way for a successful sequel of games. Jump in and test your skills against players from around the world!
