Masked Special Forces

How to Play Masked Special Forces

Masked Special Forces is an action-packed multiplayer first-person shooter that drops you into intense battle arenas. As a commander, your mission is to team up with other players, devise winning strategies, and eliminate the opposing force. The game features ultra-realistic visuals and deep customization options for a console-like experience right in your browser.

Before diving into a match, you can customize your character with an array of upgradable weapons, armor, and even cosmetic items like kill messages and victory dances. You can either jump straight into an available room or create your own, selecting from a variety of detailed maps.

Note: This game includes mature elements such as intense action and violence. Player discretion is advised.

Game Modes & Maps

Choose from six insane game modes and four distinct maps, each with realistic visuals and hidden tactical spots. Create a room on your favorite map and select a mode that suits your playstyle.

Weapons and Loadouts

You have two loadouts to fill with a powerful combination of weapons. Equip yourself with a mix of shotguns, rifles, and melee weapons to prepare for any combat scenario. Available weapon categories include:

  • Shotguns
  • Rifles
  • Melee Weapons

Perks and Profile

Unlock potent Perks to gain a tactical advantage in the arena. In the profile section, you can track your stats, including K/D Rate, total playtime, kills, scores, and more. You can also use a load/save code to transfer your experience progress.

Masked Special Forces was developed by FreezeNova.
