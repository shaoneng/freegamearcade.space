Masked Special Forces

How to Play Masked Special Forces

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of Masked Special Forces! Your primary goal is to team up with other players online and defeat the opposing force. Utilize strategy, teamwork, and sharp shooting to dominate the arena and lead your team to victory.

Before you jump into the action, you can customize your experience. Choose from a list of available game rooms or create your own, selecting from various detailed maps and game modes. Head to the armory to equip your soldier with two custom load-outs, choosing from an array of powerful rifles, shotguns, and melee weapons. You can also unlock potent perks to give you a strategic edge in combat.

The controls are intuitive for any FPS fan. Use the WASD keys to move, the Mouse to aim and look, and the Left Mouse Button to shoot. Press R to reload your weapon, Spacebar to jump, and the Right Mouse Button to aim down your sights. Master these controls to outmaneuver and outgun your opponents.
