Masked Special Forces

About Masked Special Forces

Masked Special Forces is a multiplayer first-person shooter game with a myriad of customization options. The game puts you in the shoes of a commander in the battle arena. As a talented warrior, team up, strategize, and take down the opponents one by one.

Access an array of upgradable weapons, armory, kill messages, and victory dances on the main menu. Ultra-realistic visuals and smooth gameplay will give you a feel at least as captivating as an iconic console game.

In this fast-paced action game, you can dive into an intense battle immediately or customize your gaming experience thoroughly before the gunfight. Select from available rooms, or create your own by choosing between detailed maps.

This game includes mature elements such as intense action, strong language, or violent sequences. Player discretion is advised.

Game Features

  • Extensive Customization: Tailor your character with unique weapons, armor, and perks to dominate the battlefield.
  • Multiple Game Modes: Choose from 6 insane game modes to keep the action fresh and exciting.
  • Detailed Maps: Battle across four different maps with realistic visuals and strategic hiding spots. Create your own room on your favorite map.
  • Epic Arsenal: Fill two load-outs with a variety of shotguns, rifles, and melee weapons to create the perfect combat combination.
  • Unlockable Perks: Gain a tactical edge in the arena by unlocking potent perks as you progress.
  • Detailed Profile Stats: Track your K/D Rate, Games Played, Total Play Time, Kills, Scores, Wins, Headshots, and more in the profile section.

Masked Special Forces was developed by FreezeNova and has become a beloved title in the online shooter community, even spawning a sequel of games. Ready for a virtual shooting experience? Join players from around the world and prove your skills!
