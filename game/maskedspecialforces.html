Masked Special Forces

About Masked Special Forces

Masked Special Forces is a multiplayer first-person shooter game with a myriad of customization options. The game puts you in the shoes of a commander in the battle arena. As a talented warrior, team up, strategize, and take down the opponents one by one.

With ultra-realistic visuals and smooth gameplay, this game delivers an experience as captivating as an iconic console title. Access an array of upgradable weapons, armor, kill messages, and even victory dances right from the main menu.

Gameplay and Customization

In this fast-paced action game, you can dive into an intense battle immediately or thoroughly customize your gaming experience before the gunfight. Select from available rooms, or create your own by choosing one of the detailed maps. The game stands out among online shooters thanks to its lifelike details and has been a pioneer for a whole series of Masked Forces games.

Game Modes and Maps

Choose your battleground from four different maps, each with realistic visuals and hidden areas to discover. The game features six insane modes to compete in, offering a wide variety of combat scenarios to test your skills.

Weapons, Perks, and Profile

You have two load-outs to fill with epic weapons. Equip yourself with a powerful combination of shotguns, rifles, and melee weapons. Unlock potent Perks to gain a strategic edge in the arena. Your profile tracks key statistics like K/D Rate, Total Play Time, Kills, Wins, and Headshot Counts, allowing you to monitor your progress and save your experience with a load code.

Player Discretion Advised: This game includes mature elements such as intense action, strong language, and violent sequences.

Developed by FreezeNova, Masked Special Forces invites you to a thrilling virtual shooting experience. Start playing now against others from around the world!
