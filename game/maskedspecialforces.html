Masked Special Forces

About Masked Special Forces

Step into the boots of a commander in Masked Special Forces, an intense multiplayer first-person shooter with a huge array of customization options. Team up with players from around the world, devise winning strategies, and eliminate your opponents in fast-paced, action-packed battles.

Before you even enter the fight, you can meticulously customize your gaming experience. The main menu gives you access to a massive armory of upgradable weapons, protective gear, custom kill messages, and even unique victory dances to celebrate your wins.

Game Modes & Maps

Create your own room or join an existing one on one of four highly detailed and realistic maps. Choose from six insane game modes to keep the action fresh and exciting, including Free for All, Team Deathmatch, Bomb Defuse, and Gun Game.

Armory and Perks

Prepare for battle by setting up two distinct load-outs with an epic combination of weapons. Choose from a variety of shotguns, powerful rifles, and deadly melee weapons. To gain a tactical advantage in the arena, you can also unlock potent Perks that enhance your abilities.

Track Your Progress

In the profile section, you can monitor your performance with detailed statistics, including your K/D Rate, total games played, kills, scores, wins, headshots, and more. You can also save your experience progress with a load code.

Masked Special Forces was developed by FreezeNova and stands out as a premier online shooter thanks to its lifelike details and smooth gameplay.
