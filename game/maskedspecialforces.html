Masked Special Forces

About Masked Special Forces

Masked Special Forces is a multiplayer first-person shooter game with a myriad of customization options. The game puts you in the shoes of a commander in the battle arena. As a talented warrior, team up, strategize, and take down the opponents one by one.

Access an array of upgradable weapons, armor, kill messages, and victory dances on the main menu. Ultra-realistic visuals and smooth gameplay will give you a feel at least as captivating as an iconic console game. In this fast-paced action game, you get to dive into an intense battle immediately or customize your gaming experience thoroughly before the gunfight. Select between available rooms, or create your own by selecting between detailed maps.

This game includes mature elements such as intense action, strong language, or violent sequences. Player discretion is advised.

Maps and Modes

Four different maps with realistic visuals and hidden gems are available. Choose your favorite and create your room on the map. The game has 6 insane modes you can choose from:

  • Free For All
  • Team Deathmatch
  • Capture the Flag
  • Bomb Defuse
  • Gun Game
  • Arcade Mode

Armory and Load-outs

You have 2 load-outs to fill with epic weapons. Pick between various shotguns, rifles, and melees to equip yourself with a great combination. Available weapons include:

  • Rifles (AK-47, M4A1, etc.)
  • Shotguns
  • Snipers
  • Pistols
  • Melee Weapons

Perks

Unlock potent Perks to gain an edge in the arena:

  • Increased Health
  • Faster Reload
  • Extra Ammo
  • And more to discover!

Player Profile & Development

In the profile section, you can change your nickname and manage your load/save code to keep your experience points. Track your stats including K/D Rate, Games Played, Total Play Time, Kills, Scores, Wins, Headshot Counts, Knife Kills, and Assist Counts.

Masked Special Forces was developed by FreezeNova.
