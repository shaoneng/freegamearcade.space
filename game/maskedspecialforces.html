Masked Special Forces

About Masked Special Forces

Masked Special Forces is a multiplayer first-person shooter game with a myriad of customization options. The game puts you in the shoes of a commander in the battle arena. As a talented warrior, team up, strategize, and take down the opponents one by one.

Access an array of upgradable weapons, armory, kill messages, and victory dances from the main menu. Ultra-realistic visuals and smooth gameplay provide a captivating experience, rivaling iconic console games. In this fast-paced action game, you can dive into an intense battle immediately or thoroughly customize your gaming experience before the gunfight. Select from available rooms, or create your own by selecting between detailed maps.

This game includes mature elements such as intense action, strong language, or violent sequences. Player discretion is advised. If you’re looking for a different type of gameplay, explore our family-friendly game selection.

Game Modes & Maps

Choose your favorite map and create a room. The game has 6 insane modes you can choose from: Free for All, Team Deathmatch, Capture the Flag, Gun Game, Arcade, and Doomsday.

Armory and Load-outs

You have 2 load-outs to fill with epic weapons. Pick between various shotguns, rifles, and melees to equip yourself with a great combination. Available weapons include:

  • FAMAS
  • AK-47
  • M4A1
  • SCAR-H
  • And many more powerful options!

Perks and Profile

Unlock potent Perks to gain an edge in the arena. In the profile section, you can change your nickname and track your stats, including K/D Rate, Games Played, Kills, Wins, and Headshot Counts. You can also save your experience via a load code.

Masked Special Forces was developed by FreezeNova. If you enjoy this game, check out the other titles in the Masked Forces series!
