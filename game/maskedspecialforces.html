Masked Special Forces

How to Play Masked Special Forces

Masked Special Forces is an immersive multiplayer first-person shooter. To begin, navigate to the main menu where you can customize your experience. Select from an array of upgradable weapons and armory to create your perfect loadout.

You can choose to join an existing game room or create your own, selecting from various detailed maps. The game offers six intense modes, including Team Deathmatch, Deathmatch, Gun Game, Bomb Defuse, Survival, and Training. Once in a match, team up with allies, strategize, and use your combat skills to eliminate opponents.

Utilize your chosen weapons effectively and unlock powerful perks as you progress to gain an advantage. Monitor your performance through your profile, tracking stats like kills, wins, and K/D rate. Aim to dominate the battlefield and lead your team to victory!
