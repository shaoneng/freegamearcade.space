Mathematical Crossword

How to Play Mathematical Crossword

Welcome to Mathematical Crossword, a brain-teasing puzzle that gives a numerical twist to the classic word game! Your objective is to fill the crossword grid not with letters, but with numbers and mathematical symbols (+, -, ×, ÷) to create valid equations both horizontally and vertically.

To start, select your desired difficulty level. This allows you to tailor the challenge to your comfort, from simple puzzles suitable for younger players to more complex grids for seasoned math enthusiasts. You can also choose whether to include arithmetic symbols in the puzzle, adding another layer of complexity.

Simply click or tap on an empty square in the grid and then select the correct number or symbol from the options provided to place it. Continue filling the grid until all equations are solved correctly. With an infinite number of randomly generated puzzles, the fun and the challenge never end!
