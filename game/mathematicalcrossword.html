Mathematical Crossword

How to Play Mathematical Crossword

Welcome to Mathematical Crossword, where numbers replace letters in a fun and challenging puzzle! The goal is to fill the entire grid with numbers and arithmetic symbols (like +, -, ×, ÷) to form correct mathematical equations both horizontally and vertically.

To begin, select a puzzle and your desired difficulty. Click or tap on an empty square in the grid and then choose the correct number or symbol from the provided options to place it. Look at the intersecting rows and columns to figure out which numbers and operations will satisfy all equations simultaneously.

The game offers infinite puzzles thanks to its random generation, ensuring a new challenge every time you play. You can customize your experience by setting the difficulty and deciding whether to include all arithmetic symbols, making it perfect for all skill levels. Good luck!
