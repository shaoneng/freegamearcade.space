Mega Lamba Ramp

How to Play Mega Lamba Ramp

Get behind the wheel and prepare to defy gravity in Mega Lamba Ramp! The objective is simple: drive your car up massive, sky-high ramps and perform the most incredible stunts imaginable. Use your skills to drift around corners, execute perfect flips, and fly through the air.

For PC players: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your vehicle. Press the F key to enter a car, and if you flip over, hit the R key to reset. Need a speed boost? Hold down the Shift key. To get a better look at your surroundings, simply click and drag your mouse to control the camera.

For mobile players: The controls are right on your screen. Use the virtual steering wheel to steer and tap the forward and backward buttons to accelerate and brake. Explore the city, unlock new cars, and show off your craziest tricks!
