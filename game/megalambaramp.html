Mega Lamba Ramp

How to Play Mega Lamba Ramp

Welcome to the high-flying world of Mega Lamba Ramp! Your goal is to drive your car off enormous ramps, perform incredible stunts, and explore the vast city from above. Earn points for your tricks to unlock new cars and characters in the garage.

PC Controls:
Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to accelerate, brake, and steer your vehicle.
Press the F key to enter or exit your car.
If you get stuck upside down, press the R key to flip your vehicle upright.
Hold down the Shift key to activate a powerful speed boost.
Control your camera perspective by clicking and dragging the mouse.

Mobile Controls:
For touch devices, use the on-screen steering wheel to guide your car. Accelerate and brake by tapping the dedicated forward and backward buttons on the screen.
