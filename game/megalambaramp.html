Mega Lamba Ramp

How to Play Mega Lamba Ramp

Your goal in Mega Lamba Ramp is to become the ultimate stunt driver. Take control of your favorite car, hit the massive ramps, and perform death-defying tricks in the sky. Earn points for your stunts to unlock new vehicles and characters. The city is your playground, so get ready to drift, flip, and fly!

If you're playing on a PC, use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer, accelerate, and brake. Press the F key to get in or out of your vehicle. If you crash and get stuck upside down, press the R key to flip your car back over. For an extra burst of speed, hold down the Shift key to use your boost. You can also control the camera view by clicking and dragging your mouse.

On a mobile device, the controls are touch-based and intuitive. Use the on-screen steering wheel to steer your car left and right. To control your speed, simply tap the forward (accelerate) or backward (brake/reverse) buttons on your screen.
