Mega Lamba Ramp

How to Play Mega Lamba Ramp

Take control of your high-performance vehicle and prepare for sky-high stunts! The controls are designed for precision driving on both PC and mobile devices.

On PC: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer, accelerate, and brake. Press the F key to enter your vehicle. If you find yourself upside down, press the R key to flip your car back over. For an extra burst of speed, hit the Shift key to use your boost. You can also control the camera view by clicking and dragging your mouse.

On Mobile: The controls are streamlined for touchscreens. Use the on-screen steering wheel to guide your car. To accelerate or brake, simply tap the forward and backward pedal buttons on your screen.
