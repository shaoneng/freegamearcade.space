Mega Lamba Ramp

How to Play Mega Lamba Ramp

Your goal in Mega Lamba Ramp is to perform breathtaking stunts, drift like a pro, and launch your car into the sky from enormous ramps. Master the controls to unlock new cars and achieve the highest scores on epic tracks.

On a PC: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to move your vehicle. Press F to enter or exit your car, and R to flip it back over if you crash. To get an extra burst of speed, hold down the Shift key for a boost. You can control the camera view by clicking and dragging your mouse.

On a Phone/Tablet: Controls are touch-based. Use the on-screen steering wheel to steer your car. Tap the forward and backward buttons on the screen to accelerate and brake.
