Mega Lamba Ramp

How to Play Mega Lamba Ramp

Get behind the wheel and prepare for sky-high action! The main objective in Mega Lamba Ramp is to drive your car at top speed, hit massive ramps, and perform breathtaking stunts in the air. The crazier your tricks and drifts, the higher your score. Use your earnings to visit the garage and unlock new, more powerful vehicles to take on even more challenging tracks.

PC Controls: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive. Press F to enter or exit a vehicle, and R to flip your car back over if you crash. To get a speed boost, hold down the Shift key. You can also control your camera view by clicking and dragging with the mouse.

Mobile Controls: If you're playing on a phone or tablet, use the on-screen steering wheel to steer. Tap the dedicated forward and backward buttons on your screen to accelerate and brake.
