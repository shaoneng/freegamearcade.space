Mega Lamba Ramp

How to Play Mega Lamba Ramp

Welcome to the high-flying world of Mega Lamba Ramp! Your goal is to perform breathtaking stunts, explore epic sky tracks, and unlock new cars and characters. Use your precision driving and courage to conquer the ramps and rule the city skyline.

PC Controls:
Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer, accelerate, and brake your vehicle.
Press the F key to enter or exit a vehicle.
If you get stuck upside down, press the R key to flip your car back over.
Need a speed boost? Hit the Shift key.
Change your perspective by clicking and dragging your mouse to control the camera view.

Mobile Controls:
Use the on-screen steering wheel to guide your car.
Tap the on-screen forward and backward buttons to accelerate and brake.

Explore the city, drift like a pro to earn points, and visit the Garage to spend your earnings on awesome new vehicles. The crazier the stunt, the better the reward!
