Mega Lamba Ramp

How to Play Mega Lamba Ramp

Get ready to take to the skies! The objective in Mega Lamba Ramp is to perform incredible stunts, drift around the city, and launch your car off massive ramps. Precision, courage, and speed are key to mastering the game, earning high scores, and unlocking awesome new vehicles from the garage.

On PC: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer your vehicle. Press F to enter or exit your car, and press R if you need to flip your vehicle back upright. For an extra burst of speed, hold the Shift key to use your boost. To get the perfect camera angle for your stunts, simply click and drag your mouse.

On Mobile: If you're playing on a phone or tablet, the controls are all on-screen. Use the responsive on-screen steering wheel to steer. Tap the dedicated forward and backward buttons on your screen to accelerate and brake.
