Mega Lamba Ramp

How to Play Mega Lamba Ramp

Your goal in Mega Lamba Ramp is to become the ultimate stunt driver. Launch your car off colossal ramps, perform insane tricks, and explore a city in the sky. Earn points for your craziest stunts and use them to unlock a fantastic collection of new cars.

PC Controls: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer your vehicle. Press F to enter or exit a car. If you flip over, press R to reset your car's position. For a speed boost, hold down the Shift key. To change your camera angle, simply click and drag your mouse.

Mobile Controls: On your phone or tablet, use the on-screen steering wheel to navigate. Tap the dedicated forward and backward buttons on the screen to accelerate and brake. The controls are designed to be simple and responsive, so you can focus on the high-flying action!
