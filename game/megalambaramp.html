Mega Lamba Ramp

How to Play Mega Lamba Ramp

Welcome to the world of extreme stunts in Mega Lamba Ramp! The objective is simple: drive your high-performance car, launch off massive ramps, and perform incredible tricks to score points. Use your earnings to visit the garage and unlock an even wider selection of awesome vehicles.

PC Controls:

  • Move: Use the `WASD` or `Arrow Keys` to steer, accelerate, and brake your vehicle.
  • Boost: Press the `Shift` key for a powerful nitro boost to gain incredible speed.
  • Flip Vehicle: If you find yourself upside down, press the `R` key to reset your car's position.
  • Enter/Exit Vehicle: Use the `F` key to get in or out of your car.
  • Camera Control: Click and drag your mouse to change your camera view and get the perfect angle for your stunts.

Mobile Controls:

  • Steer: Use the on-screen steering wheel to control your car's direction.
  • Accelerate/Brake: Tap the forward and backward pedal icons on the screen to manage your speed.
