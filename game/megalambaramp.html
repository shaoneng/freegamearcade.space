Mega Lamba Ramp

How to Play Mega Lamba Ramp

Your goal in Mega Lamba Ramp is to become the ultimate stunt driver. Launch your vehicle off massive ramps suspended in the sky, perform breathtaking tricks, and drift like a pro to earn points. Use these points to visit the garage and unlock an awesome collection of new cars!

PC Controls:

  • Move: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to accelerate, brake, and steer your car.
  • Enter Vehicle: Press the F key.
  • Flip Vehicle: If you crash, press the R key to reset your car.
  • Boost: Hold down the Shift key for a powerful speed boost.
  • Control View: Click and drag your mouse to change your camera angle for the perfect view.

Mobile Controls:

  • Steer: Use the on-screen steering wheel to guide your vehicle.
  • Accelerate/Brake: Tap the forward (gas pedal) or backward (brake pedal) buttons on the screen to control your speed.
