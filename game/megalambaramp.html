Mega Lamba Ramp

How to Play Mega Lamba Ramp

Get ready to take to the skies! In Mega Lamba Ramp, your goal is to drive your car on massive ramps, perform incredible stunts, and explore the vast cityscape. Earn points for your crazy tricks to unlock new and better cars from the garage. Precision driving and courage are key to mastering the tracks and becoming the ultimate stunt driver.

On PC:
- Move: Use `WASD` or the `Arrow Keys` to steer, accelerate, and brake.
- Enter Vehicle: Press `F` to get into your car.
- Flip Vehicle: If you get stuck, press `R` to reset your car.
- Boost: Hold `Shift` for a burst of speed.
- Control View: `Click and drag` your mouse to change the camera angle.

On Phone:
- Steer: Use the on-screen steering wheel.
- Accelerate/Brake: Tap the on-screen forward and backward buttons to control your speed.
