Mega Lamba Ramp

How to Play Mega Lamba Ramp

Mastering the controls is your first step to becoming a stunt-driving legend. Whether you're on a PC or a mobile device, the controls are simple and intuitive, letting you focus on the high-flying action.

On PC: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer, accelerate, and brake. Press the F key to enter or exit your vehicle. If you get stuck upside down after a wild flip, press R to reset your car's position. Need a burst of speed for a massive jump? Hold down the Shift key to activate your boost. You can also get the perfect camera angle by clicking and dragging with your mouse.

On Mobile: Gameplay is just as easy on the go. Use the on-screen virtual steering wheel to guide your car. Tap the dedicated forward and backward arrow buttons to control your acceleration and braking. Precision and courage are your best tools, so buckle up, hit the gas, and see what incredible stunts you can pull off!
