Mega Soccer

How to Play Mega Soccer

Welcome to the pitch! In Mega Soccer, you control a dynamic three-player team: a goalkeeper, a defender, and a striker. Your objective is to score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second match timer runs out.

The controls are intuitive. Move your selected player to win possession of the ball, dribble around the opposing team's players, and aim your shot to get it past the keeper. Each goal you score earns you valuable coins.

Between matches, you can build the ultimate squad. Head to the "Players / Kits" menu to spend gems on upgrading your athletes' Speed, Power, and Technic stats. Unlocking rarer kits can even grant your players special abilities like rainbow flicks or devastating Power Shots!

You can also fully customize your game's atmosphere. Before you kick off, visit the main menu to change the stadium, select a different match ball, and even set the weather conditions to create your perfect soccer experience.
