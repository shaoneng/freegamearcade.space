Mega Soccer

How to Play Mega Soccer

Welcome to the pitch! In Mega Soccer, you manage a compact team of three players: a goalkeeper, a defender, and a striker. The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out. Use your mouse or touch controls to move your selected player, dribble the ball, and shoot for the goal.

Before you play, head to the 'Players / Kits' menu. Here, you can view each player's stats for Speed, Power, and Technic. Spend the pink gems you earn to upgrade these stats or unlock players with better skills, including special moves like rainbow flicks and powerful bicycle kicks. You can also customize the stadium, weather, and even the ball to create your perfect match environment.

You earn rewards for playing well! Completing achievements (like "50 Dribbles") and winning matches will grant you gems and coins. Winning consecutive matches fills up an XP bar, unlocking even more goodies to enhance your team. Keep playing to build the ultimate squad and dominate the league!
