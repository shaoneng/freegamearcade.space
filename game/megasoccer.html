Mega Soccer

How to Play Mega Soccer

Welcome to the pitch! In Mega Soccer, you control a compact squad of three players: a goalkeeper, a defender, and a striker. The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out. Navigate the field, tackle to win possession, dribble around the other team, and take your shot on goal!

Your team's success depends on your players' abilities. Each athlete has unique ratings for Speed, Power, and Technic. You can improve these stats by spending pink gems, which are earned by completing achievements (like making 50 dribbles) and winning matches. Upgrading your players or unlocking rarer player cards can also grant special moves like rainbow flicks or powerful bicycle kicks.

Before you kick off, you can fully customize your match experience. Head to the main menu to change the stadium, select the weather conditions, pick your team's kit colors, and even choose the design of the match ball. Make the game your own and lead your custom squad to victory!
