Mega Soccer

How to Play Mega Soccer

Mega Soccer is a fast-paced 3v3 online soccer game where your objective is to outscore your opponent on compact pitches before the 60-second timer runs out. Engage in lively matches, winning possession, weaving around markers, and expertly steering your shots past diving keepers.

You'll control a squad of three players: a keeper, a utility midfielder/defender, and a striker. Each player has unique Speed, Power, and Technique ratings, which you can enhance. Visit the Players/Kits menu to spend pink gems, earned from completing achievements and end-match rewards, to bump up stats or unlock rarer card tiers. Discover special moves like flashy rainbow flicks or blistering power shots with outstanding and legendary kits.

Before kick-off, customize your game experience! Dive into the Stadiums, Balls, and Weather menus to change the environment, match ball, and even your team's kit colors. Every goal nets you coins, and consecutive wins contribute to your XP bar, leading to even more goodies as you progress.
