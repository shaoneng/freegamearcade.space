Mega Soccer

How to Play Mega Soccer

Welcome to the pitch in Mega Soccer! The goal is simple: outscore your opponent in fast-paced, 60-second matches. You command a squad of three specialized players: a reliable keeper, a versatile defender, and a sharp-shooting striker.

Use your controls to dribble the ball, weave past the other team's players, and take your shot on goal. Each player on your team has unique stats for Speed, Power, and Technic. Winning matches and completing achievements, like landing a bicycle kick, will earn you valuable gems.

Head to the 'Players / Kits' menu to spend your gems. You can upgrade your players' stats or unlock rarer player cards that come with special moves like rainbow flicks and devastating Power Shots. Don't forget to customize your game experience in the main menu, where you can change the stadium, weather, kit colors, and even the match ball!
