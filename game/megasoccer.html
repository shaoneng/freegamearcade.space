Mega Soccer

How to Play Mega Soccer

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Mega Soccer! Your objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the 60-second timer runs out. You control a squad of three specialized players: a goalkeeper, a defender, and a striker.

Use your controls to move your active player around the pitch. Dribble the ball, weave past the other team, and time your shots perfectly to get past the keeper. Each player has unique stats for Speed, Power, and Technic, so choose your actions wisely.

After each match, you'll earn rewards like coins and gems. Head to the 'Players / Kits' menu to spend your gems and upgrade your athletes' abilities or unlock rarer player cards. These advanced kits can even grant you special skills like rainbow flicks or devastating Power Shots to give you an edge on the field.

Before you kick off, you can fully customize your game experience in the 'Stadiums / Balls / Weather' menu. Change the look and feel of every match to make it your own. Complete in-game achievements to earn even more gems and build the ultimate soccer squad!
