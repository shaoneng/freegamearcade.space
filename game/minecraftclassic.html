Minecraft Classic

How to Play Minecraft Classic

Welcome to the limitless world of Minecraft Classic! The goal is simple: build whatever your imagination desires in this open-world sandbox. There are no enemies, monsters, or objectives, just pure creative freedom.

Use the WASD keys to walk around the world. To start building, press the B key to open your inventory and browse the different types of blocks available.

Once you've chosen a block, find the perfect spot and use the Right Mouse Button to place it. If you make a mistake or want to remove a block, simply use the Left Mouse Button to destroy it.

For a better view or to build tall structures, you can fly! Press the Z key to toggle flight mode on and off. While flying, use the Q key to ascend and the E key to descend. Enjoy the freedom and build something amazing!
