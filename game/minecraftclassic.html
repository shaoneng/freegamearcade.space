Minecraft Classic

How to Play Minecraft Classic

Welcome to the world of Minecraft Classic, where your imagination is the only limit! The goal is simple: create. You have an unlimited supply of blocks to build anything you desire in this vast, open-world sandbox, free from monsters and other dangers.

Use the W, A, S, and D keys to move around the landscape and the Spacebar to jump. Your mouse controls your point of view, allowing you to look in any direction.

To start building, press the B key to open your block inventory. Click on any block type to select it. Then, aim your cursor where you want to build and Right-Click to place the block. If you make a mistake or want to clear some space, simply Left-Click on a block to destroy it.

For a bird's-eye view of your creation, you can take to the skies! Press the Z key to toggle flight mode. While flying, use the Q key to go up and the E key to go down. This freedom of movement is perfect for constructing large and intricate structures.
