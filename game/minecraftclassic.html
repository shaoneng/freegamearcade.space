Minecraft Classic

How to Play Minecraft Classic

Welcome to the wonderfully creative world of Minecraft Classic! The controls are simple and intuitive, allowing you to start building right away. Use the W, A, S, and D keys to walk around the map and the mouse to look in different directions.

Building is the heart of the game. To destroy an existing block, aim at it and Left-Click. To place a new block, aim where you want it and Right-Click. Need a different material? Press the B key to open your inventory, where you can select from 32 different types of blocks.

Want to get a bird's-eye view of your masterpiece? You can fly! Press the Z key to toggle flight mode on and off. While airborne, use the Q key to fly down and the E key to fly up. Enjoy the freedom to build anything you can imagine!
