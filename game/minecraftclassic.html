Minecraft Classic

How to Play Minecraft Classic

Minecraft Classic is an intuitive building experience focused on creativity.

To select blocks, press the 'B' key to open your inventory. Choose the type of block you wish to use.

Once a block is selected, move your cursor to the desired location and right-click to place it.

For quick traversal, you can activate flight mode by pressing the 'Z' key. While flying, use 'Q' to move up and 'E' to move down.

Explore the hacks menu for additional options, including the ability to maximize your jump height for an even faster way to navigate the world.
