Minecraft Classic

How to Play Minecraft Classic

Welcome to the boundless world of Minecraft Classic! This is a stress-free, creative sandbox where your imagination is the only limit. There are no monsters or survival elements, just pure building freedom.

Use the WASD keys to move around the world. To place a block, press the B key to open your inventory and select a block type. Then, aim with your mouse and Right-Click to place it. To remove a block, simply Left-Click on it.

Want to get a bird's-eye view? Press the Z key to toggle flying mode. While flying, use the Q and E keys to move up and down. Explore, build, and create anything from a simple home to a sprawling metropolis!
