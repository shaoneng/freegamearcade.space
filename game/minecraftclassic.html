Minecraft Classic

How to Play Minecraft Classic

Welcome to the ultimate sandbox experience! In Minecraft Classic, your creativity is the only limit. This version is all about building in a stress-free environment, with no monsters or survival elements to worry about.

Core Controls: Use the WASD keys to move around the world and the Spacebar to jump. Use your mouse to look around.

Building and Mining: Press the B key to open your inventory of blocks. Select the block you want to use. Use the Right Mouse Button to place a block in the world, and the Left Mouse Button to destroy a block.

Flying: To get a bird's-eye view of your creations, press the Z key to toggle flight mode. When flying, you can use the Q key to fly up and the E key to fly down. This allows for precise placement of blocks in hard-to-reach places.

Let your imagination run wild and construct anything from a humble home to a magnificent castle. The world is yours to shape!
