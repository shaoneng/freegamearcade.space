Minecraft Classic

How to Play Minecraft Classic

Welcome to the world of Minecraft Classic, a pure sandbox experience where your creativity is the only limit! The goal is simple: build whatever you can imagine. There are no enemies, no survival pressure, and no need to gather resources. Just relax and let your architectural dreams come to life.

Use the W, A, S, and D keys to move around the world and the Spacebar to jump. You can destroy existing blocks with a Left-Click and place new ones with a Right-Click.

To choose what to build with, press the B key to open your inventory of blocks. Select any block type you like, and then use the right mouse button to place it in the world.

Want a better view? You can fly! Press the Z key to toggle flight mode. While flying, use the Q key to ascend and the E key to descend, giving you the perfect vantage point for your grand designs.
