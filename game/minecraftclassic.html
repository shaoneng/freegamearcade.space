Minecraft Classic

How to Play Minecraft Classic

Welcome to the limitless world of Minecraft Classic! Use the standard WASD keys to move around the landscape and the mouse to control your view. To remove a block, simply point at it and left-click. To place a block, point to where you want it and right-click.

This is a creative sandbox, so all blocks are available from the start. Press the 'B' key to open your inventory. Here you can browse and select from a wide variety of blocks. Once you've chosen your desired block, close the inventory and use the right mouse button to build your creations.

Want a better view or need to reach a high place? You can fly! Press the 'Z' key to toggle flight mode. While flying, use the 'Q' key to ascend and the 'E' key to descend. This makes building large-scale projects much easier. Enjoy the freedom to explore and build anything you can imagine!
