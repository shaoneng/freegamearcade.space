Minecraft Classic

How to Play Minecraft Classic

Welcome to the creative world of Minecraft Classic! This is a pure sandbox experience with no enemies or resource gathering. Your only goal is to build and create whatever you can imagine.

To start building, press the B key to open your inventory. Here you can see all the available blocks. Select the one you want to use.

Move to the location where you want to place your block. Use the Right Mouse Button to place the block. To remove a block, use the Left Mouse Button.

Explore your world with ease! Press the Z key to toggle fly mode. While flying, you can use Q to go up and E to go down, giving you the perfect vantage point for your grand designs.
