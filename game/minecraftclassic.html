Minecraft Classic

How to Play Minecraft Classic

Welcome to the creative world of Minecraft Classic! Use the WASD keys to walk around the world and the mouse to change your view. To destroy a block, simply point your cursor at it and left-click. To place a block, use the right-click button.

To select which type of block you want to build with, press the 'B' key to open your inventory. Here you will find a wide variety of materials to choose from. Click on a block to select it, then press 'B' again or 'Esc' to close the menu and start building.

This version of Minecraft lets you soar through the sky! Press the 'Z' key to toggle fly mode on and off. While flying, you can use the 'Q' and 'E' keys to move up and down, respectively, giving you the perfect vantage point for your grand architectural creations.
