Miniblox io

About Miniblox io

Miniblox io is a vast multiplayer sandbox game where your creativity is the only limit! Dive into a world built from mini blocks and discover an array of user-created maps, including challenging parkour courses, intense survival scenarios, and a variety of other fun mini-games. Each map offers unique features, so be sure to explore them all to find your favorite.

Ready for a challenge? Engage in thrilling duels with other players online. Test your skills in "The Bridge Duels," where the goal is to knock your opponents off the bridge into the void below. Arm yourself with a knife for close-quarters combat or a bow for ranged attacks, and fight to be the last one standing in furious multiplayer battles.

If you're a fan of creative survival games like Minecraft, you'll feel right at home. The survival mode lets you dig, carve, and build whatever you can imagine. Unleash your creativity and construct magnificent structures in this dynamic sandbox environment.
