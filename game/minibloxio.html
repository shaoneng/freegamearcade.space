Miniblox io

How to Play Miniblox io

Welcome to the expansive world of Miniblox io! Your main goal is to explore, create, and compete in a variety of game modes. Use your creativity to build amazing structures in survival mode, much like in Minecraft, or test your agility on challenging parkour maps.

Engage in exciting player-versus-player combat across different maps. In modes like Bridge Duels, your objective is to knock your opponents off the platform to win. Equip yourself with a knife for swift close-range attacks and a bow and arrow to pick off enemies from a distance. Each map offers unique features and challenges, so be sure to try them all to find your favorite way to play!

The controls are intuitive for a sandbox game. Typically, you will use the WASD keys to move, the Spacebar to jump, and the Mouse to look around, attack, and place blocks. Dive in and let your creativity run wild!
