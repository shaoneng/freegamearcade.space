Miniblox io

About Miniblox io

Welcome to Miniblox.io, a vast multiplayer sandbox game where your creativity is the only limit! Jump into a world built from mini blocks and explore a wide variety of player-created maps, including challenging parkour courses, intense survival scenarios, and other unique mini-games. Each map offers a different adventure, so try them all to find your favorite!

Ready for a fight? Miniblox.io features thrilling combat on various maps. Arm yourself with a knife for up-close encounters or a bow for long-range attacks. Test your skills in the "Bridge Duels" mode, where the goal is to knock your opponents off the precarious red and blue bridges. Stay sharp and be the last one standing in a field of fierce competitors!

If building and crafting is more your style, you'll feel right at home. Much like classics such as Minecraft, you can dig, carve, and construct anything you can imagine in the survival modes. Let your creativity run wild and build magnificent structures, deep mines, or intricate contraptions. The world is your canvas!
