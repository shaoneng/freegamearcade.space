Mini Cars Racing

How to Play Mini Cars Racing

Mini Cars Racing offers thrilling car racing action for one or two players. Upon loading the game, you'll find two main modes to choose from:

Solo Mode: Race against CPU-controlled cars in a single-player experience. Test your skills and try to beat the computer opponents across various tracks.

2 Player (Versus) Mode: Engage in exciting split-screen local multiplayer with a friend on the same keyboard. This mode is perfect for competitive head-to-head racing.

You can also select from three difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, and Expert. Be warned, Expert mode makes the steering more challenging, demanding precise control of your mini car!

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use the Arrow Keys (Up, Down, Left, Right) to steer and accelerate/brake.
  • Player 2: Use the WASD keys (W, A, S, D) to steer and accelerate/brake.

Master the controls, choose your preferred mode and difficulty, and hit the tracks for some mini car racing fun!
