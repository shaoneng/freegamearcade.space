Mini Cars Racing

How to Play Mini Cars Racing

Welcome to the miniature racetrack! Mini Cars Racing is a vibrant racing game that you can enjoy solo or with a friend, perfectly capturing the nostalgia of playing with toy cars.

Upon starting the game, you'll find two exciting game modes to choose from:

  • Solo Mode: Race against computer-controlled opponents and test your skills against the clock on a variety of twisty tracks.
  • 2 Player Mode: Challenge a friend in a local, split-screen battle on the same device. It's the perfect way to settle a friendly rivalry!

You can also adjust the challenge by selecting one of three difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, or Expert. Be warned, Expert mode will make steering your tiny vehicle a real test of skill!

Controls

  • Player 1: Use the WASD keys to steer your car.
  • Player 2: Use the Arrow keys to drive your car.
