Mini Cars Racing

How to Play Mini Cars Racing

Get ready for some high-speed, tiny-sized fun with Mini Cars Racing! This delightful racing game captures the nostalgic feeling of playing with toy cars on a road traffic rug, but with the added thrill of competitive racing. You can challenge the computer in solo mode or go head-to-head with a friend in the exciting 2-player split-screen mode, all on the same device.

When you start, you can choose between two main modes:

  • Solo: Race against crafty CPU opponents and fight for the top spot on the podium.
  • 2 Player: Grab a friend and battle it out in a split-screen showdown to see who's the ultimate mini-car champion.
