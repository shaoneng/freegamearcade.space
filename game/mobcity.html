Mob City

How to Play Mob City

Welcome to the dangerous streets of Mob City! Your objective is to take down the entire mafia organization, one mission at a time. This 3D shooting game challenges you with 30 progressively difficult tasks. You'll start by needing to eliminate a specific number of mobsters, but each new mission brings a unique objective.

Use your mouse to aim and your left-click to shoot. Navigate the urban environment using the standard WASD keys. As you defeat enemies, they will often drop cash. Be sure to collect this money, as you can use it in the "Shop" menu to purchase more powerful weapons and essential tools. Upgrading your gear is crucial for surviving later levels and taking on tougher foes.

Stay strategic, use cover, and manage your resources wisely. Your ultimate goal is to complete all 30 missions and face off against the formidable big boss in the final showdown. Good luck, and clean up this city!
