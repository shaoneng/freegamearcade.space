Mob City

How to Play Mob City

Welcome to the dangerous streets of Mob City! In this action-packed 3D shooter, your goal is to single-handedly take down a powerful mafia organization. You'll need sharp shooting skills and clever strategy to survive against overwhelming odds.

The game unfolds across 30 challenging missions. Each mission has a specific objective, such as eliminating a certain number of enemies. As you successfully complete one mission, the next, more difficult one will unlock, leading you closer to the final confrontation with the big boss.

As you defeat mafia members, they will often drop cash. Be sure to collect this money, as it's crucial for your success. You can use the earned cash in the "Shop" menu to purchase more powerful weapons, tools, and essential power-ups that will give you an edge in combat.

Master your movement, aim carefully, and strategically upgrade your gear to dismantle the mob's operations. Good luck, agent!
