Mob City

How to Play Mob City

Welcome to Mob City, where your mission is to single-handedly take down a powerful mafia organization. This 3D shooting game will test your combat and strategy skills as you progress through a series of challenging missions to reclaim the city from the bad guys.

The game features 30 distinct missions, each with a unique objective. Your journey begins with a task to defeat 100 mafia members. Completing a mission unlocks the next one, leading you through an engaging storyline that culminates in a final showdown with the big boss. You'll need more than just firepower; use your environment and tactical thinking to outsmart a numerically superior enemy.

As you defeat enemies, they will drop money. Be sure to collect it! You can use this cash in the in-game 'Shop' to purchase a wide array of powerful guns and essential tools. Upgrading your arsenal is key to surviving the tougher missions and eventually facing the final boss. Spend your earnings wisely to give yourself the edge you need to win.
