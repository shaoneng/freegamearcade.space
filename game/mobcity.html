Mob City

How to Play Mob City

Welcome to Mob City, a thrilling 3D shooting game where your strategic skills are put to the test. Your primary objective is to take on and dismantle a powerful mafia organization all on your own. You'll navigate through various missions, using your wits and firepower to outsmart and outgun your numerous enemies.

The game is structured around a series of 30 missions. You begin with the task of eliminating 100 mafia members. As you successfully complete each mission, you'll unlock the next one, facing new and more difficult challenges that build up to a final confrontation with the big boss.

As you defeat enemies, they will often drop money. Be sure to collect this cash! You can use it in the in-game 'Shop' to purchase a wide variety of upgraded guns, tools, and other power-ups. Managing your resources and choosing the right equipment will be key to your success in overcoming the increasingly tough opposition.
