Mob City

How to Play Mob City

Welcome to the gritty streets of Mob City! Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to single-handedly dismantle a powerful mafia organization. This isn't just mindless shooting; it's a test of strategy and skill across 30 action-packed levels.

Each level presents a unique mission. You might be tasked with eliminating a specific number of henchmen (starting with 100 in the first mission!), surviving an ambush, or completing other critical objectives. Success unlocks the next, more challenging stage in your quest to clean up the city.

As you defeat enemies, keep an eye out for cash they drop. This is your key to survival! Collect the money and visit the in-game "Shop" between missions. Here, you can purchase a wide array of powerful new guns and essential tools that will give you the edge you need against an ever-growing threat.

Strategize your approach, manage your resources, and fight your way to the top. The entire city is counting on you to take down the big boss in the final, epic confrontation. Good luck, agent!
