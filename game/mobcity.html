Mob City

How to Play Mob City

Welcome to the dangerous streets of Mob City! Your primary goal is to single-handedly dismantle a powerful mafia syndicate. You'll progress through a series of 30 action-packed missions, each with its own unique objective.

Use your keyboard and mouse to move your character and aim your weapon. Left-click to shoot down mobsters. Be strategic and use your environment for cover, as you'll be heavily outnumbered. When you defeat an enemy, they will often drop cash â€“ make sure to run over it to collect your earnings.

The money you collect is crucial for your success. Visit the "Shop" from the main menu to purchase a wide array of more powerful guns and essential tools. Upgrading your arsenal will be key to surviving the increasingly difficult missions that lie ahead.

Complete all 30 missions to work your way up the criminal ladder. Your ultimate challenge is a final, epic showdown with the big boss. Can you clean up the city and put an end to the mob's reign? Good luck!
