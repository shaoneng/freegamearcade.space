Mob City

How to Play Mob City

Welcome to the high-stakes world of Mob City! Your objective is to single-handedly dismantle a powerful mafia syndicate through a series of intense missions. The game unfolds over 30 levels, each with a unique objective that you must complete to advance.

Your journey begins with a task like defeating 100 mobsters. As you take down enemies, be sure to collect the money they drop. This cash is your key to survival and success.

You can use the money you've collected in the in-game "Shop" to purchase a wide array of powerful weapons and essential tools. Upgrading your gear is crucial for tackling tougher missions and more numerous enemies. Plan your strategy, manage your resources, and fight your way to the very end, where a final confrontation with the big boss awaits you!
