Mob City

How to Play Mob City

Mob City is an engaging 3D shooting game that challenges you to eradicate a dangerous mafia gang across 30 thrilling missions.

Your primary objective in each mission is to defeat a set number of mafia members or complete specific tasks. For instance, your first mission requires you to eliminate 100 bad guys. Successfully completing a mission unlocks the next, presenting new responsibilities and challenges. The game culminates in a final showdown with the notorious big boss.

To aid your progress, you can visit the 'Shop' section in the game menu. Here, you'll find a variety of weapons and tools available for purchase. You earn money by defeating enemies, who often drop cash upon being eliminated. Collect these funds to acquire better guns and essential power-ups, enhancing your ability to take down the increasingly challenging foes. Strategize your purchases and utilize your tools effectively to conquer Mob City.
