Mob City

How to Play Mob City

Welcome to the dangerous streets of Mob City! Your objective is to take down a powerful mafia gang all by yourself. You'll navigate through various urban environments, using your wits and firepower to complete a series of high-stakes missions.

The game is structured across 30 distinct missions. Completing one mission unlocks the next, leading you through a compelling story that ends with a face-off against the formidable mafia boss. Your first task, for example, is to eliminate 100 mobsters to prove your mettle. Each subsequent mission will present new challenges and objectives.

Use your mouse to aim and click the left mouse button to fire your weapon. Use the standard WASD keys on your keyboard to move around the map. Be tactical, use cover, and stay on the move to avoid getting overwhelmed by the enemy's superior numbers.

As you defeat enemies, they will drop cash. Make sure to collect this money, as it's your ticket to better gear. Visit the in-game "Shop" between missions to purchase powerful new guns and other useful tools that will give you an edge in your fight against the mob.
