Mob City

How to Play Mob City

Welcome to Mob City! You are a lone hero tasked with taking down a powerful mafia gang. This 3D shooting game will test your aim and strategic thinking as you face overwhelming odds. Your goal is to complete a series of missions to dismantle the criminal organization from the inside out.

The game features 30 distinct missions, each with a unique objective. Your journey begins with a task to eliminate 100 mafia members. As you successfully complete each assignment, a new, more challenging mission will unlock, leading you through an engaging story that culminates in a final, epic battle against the big boss.

Use your mouse to aim and your keyboard to move through the city. Defeated enemies will often drop cash. Be sure to collect this money, as it's crucial for your success. You can visit the in-game 'Shop' between missions to spend your earnings on a wide variety of powerful new guns and essential equipment, giving you the edge you need to win.
