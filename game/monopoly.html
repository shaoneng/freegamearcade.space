Monopoly

How to Play Monopoly

Welcome to the digital version of the classic real estate trading game! The main objective in Monopoly is to become the wealthiest player by buying, selling, and trading properties. Your ultimate goal is to drive your opponents into bankruptcy, leaving you as the sole property tycoon on the board.

Getting started is simple. To begin a game, just enter a nickname and click the 'Play' button to be matched with other players online. The game automates all the tricky parts like managing money and shuffling cards, letting you focus purely on your winning strategy.

Each turn, you'll roll the dice and move your token around the board. If you land on an unowned property, you have the option to purchase it. If you land on a property someone else owns, you must pay them rent. The key to winning is to acquire sets of properties (monopolies) of the same color, which allows you to build houses and hotels, dramatically increasing the rent your opponents have to pay.

Keep an eye on your cash and make strategic deals with other players to complete your property sets. The game ends when all other players have gone bankrupt. The last player standing with money and assets is declared the winner!
