Monopoly

How to Play Monopoly

Welcome to the digital world of high-stakes real estate! The goal in this online version of Monopoly is the same as the classic board game: become the wealthiest player by buying, renting, and selling properties. Your ultimate objective is to drive your opponents into bankruptcy. This free browser game automates all the tedious parts—like handling cash and moving pieces—so you can focus on your winning strategy.

To begin, simply enter a nickname and join a game. You'll start with $1500. On your turn, roll the two dice and move your token that many spaces around the board. If you land on an unowned property, you can buy it. If you land on a property owned by another player, you must pay them rent. Passing the 'GO' space earns you a $200 salary. The game supports 2 to 6 players, making it perfect for playing with friends or new opponents online.

The key to winning is creating monopolies—owning all properties of the same color group. This allows you to build houses and hotels, which dramatically increases the rent other players have to pay you. Watch out for special spaces like 'Go to Jail,' where you'll need to pay a fine or roll a double to get out. Use your cash wisely, make smart trades, and bankrupt the competition to be crowned the ultimate tycoon!
