Monopoly

How to Play Monopoly

Welcome to the digital world of Monopoly, the classic game of real estate and high finance! The objective is straightforward: travel around the game board, buy up properties, and bankrupt your opponents by charging them rent when they land on your squares. This online version automates all the tedious parts, like managing money and shuffling cards, so you can focus purely on strategy.

To begin, simply enter a nickname and join a game. You'll start with a set amount of cash ($1500 by default). On your turn, roll the two dice and move your token that many spaces around the board. If you land on an unowned property, you have the option to purchase it. If you land on a property owned by another player, you must pay them the required rent.

The key to winning is to acquire full color sets of properties, known as a monopoly. Once you own a complete set, you can build houses and hotels on those properties, dramatically increasing the rent you can charge. Watch out for special spaces like Chance and Community Chest, which can bring you fortune or trouble. Landing in Jail can be a setback, but you can pay a fine or roll a double to get out.

Strategically trade properties with other players to complete your sets and block your opponents from completing theirs. Manage your cash flow wisely, deciding when to buy, when to build, and when to save your money. The game ends when only one player remains who has avoided bankruptcy. That player is the ultimate Monopoly tycoon!
