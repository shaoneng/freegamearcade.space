Monopoly

How to Play Monopoly

Welcome to the digital world of Monopoly, the timeless game of property trading and financial strategy! The objective is to become the wealthiest player by buying, renting, and selling properties, ultimately driving your opponents into bankruptcy.

To start, simply enter a nickname and join a game. You'll begin with a set amount of cash. On your turn, roll the two dice and move your token clockwise around the board that many spaces. Where you land determines your action: you might buy an unowned property, pay rent to another player, draw a Chance or Community Chest card, or even go to Jail!

The key to winning is to acquire full color sets of properties, which allows you to build houses and hotels. These upgrades dramatically increase the rent you can charge other players who land on them. Make smart trades, manage your money wisely, and aim for a monopoly to secure your victory in this classic game of financial domination.
