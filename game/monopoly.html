Monopoly

How to Play Monopoly

The goal in Monopoly is to become the wealthiest player by buying, renting, and selling property. Your ultimate aim is to drive every other player into bankruptcy, leaving you as the sole tycoon on the board.

Each turn, you'll roll the dice and move your token around the board. When you land on an unowned property, you have the option to buy it. If you land on a property someone else owns, you must pay them rent. Landing on spaces like Community Chest or Chance will require you to draw a card and follow its instructions.

To increase your income, aim to collect a full set of properties of the same color. Once you own a color set (a monopoly), you can build houses and hotels on those properties, dramatically increasing the rent other players have to pay when they land there.

This online version handles all the money and card shuffling for you, so you can focus on making strategic deals and bankrupting your opponents. The game ends when only one player remains who has not gone bankrupt. That player is the winner!
