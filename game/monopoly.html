Monopoly

How to Play Monopoly

Welcome to the digital version of the classic real estate trading game! The goal in Monopoly is to become the wealthiest player by buying, renting, and selling property. Drive your opponents into bankruptcy to win the game.

To start, simply enter a nickname and join a game. You can play with friends in a private room or match up with players from around the globe. Each player starts with $1500. On your turn, roll the dice and move your token around the board.

When you land on an unowned property, you can buy it. If you land on a property owned by another player, you must pay them rent. Collect complete color sets to build houses and hotels, which will dramatically increase the rent you can charge. This online version automates all transactions, so you can focus purely on your winning strategy!

Watch out for the Jail, Chance, and Community Chest spaces, which can help or hinder your progress. The first person to bankrupt all other players is declared the winner. Good luck, tycoon!
