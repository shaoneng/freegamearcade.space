Monopoly

How to Play Monopoly

Welcome to the digital world of Monopoly! The goal remains the same as the classic board game: become the wealthiest player by buying, selling, and trading properties. Your ultimate aim is to drive all your opponents into bankruptcy, leaving you as the sole tycoon on the board.

To start, choose a nickname and join a game. Each player begins with a set amount of cash. On your turn, roll the dice and move your token that many spaces around the board. If you land on an unowned property, you have the option to buy it. If it's already owned by another player, you must pay them rent. Landing on 'Go' earns you extra cash, while landing in 'Jail' will cost you a turn or a fee to get out.

The key to winning is collecting complete color sets of properties. Once you own all properties in a color group, you can start building houses and hotels on them, which dramatically increases the rent other players have to pay. Make smart trades, manage your money wisely, and strategize to monopolize the board and bankrupt your competition. The game is automated, so you can focus on your tactics without worrying about managing paper money or cards.
