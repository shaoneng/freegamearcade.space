Monopoly

How to Play Monopoly

Welcome to the digital world of Monopoly! The goal is to become the wealthiest player and drive your opponents into bankruptcy. To start, each player receives a set amount of money (typically $1500). On your turn, roll the two dice and move your token clockwise around the board by the number of spaces shown. Your actions depend entirely on the space you land on.

If you land on an unowned property, you can buy it from the bank. If you choose not to, it goes up for auction. If you land on a property someone else owns, you must pay them rent. The amount increases if they own a full set of colors or have built houses and hotels. Landing on 'Go' earns you $200, while landing in 'Jail' restricts your movement. 'Chance' and 'Community Chest' cards can bring you fortunes or unexpected bills.

The key to winning is collecting complete color sets of properties. Once you own a full set, you can build houses and then hotels on them, dramatically increasing the rent you can charge. Make strategic trades with other players to complete your sets. The game ends when only one player remains, having successfully bankrupted everyone else. Manage your cash wisely, make smart investments, and may the best tycoon win!
