Monopoly

How to Play Monopoly

Welcome to the digital world of Monopoly! The goal remains the same as the classic board game: become the wealthiest player by buying, renting, and selling property, ultimately driving your opponents into bankruptcy.

To start, each player receives a set amount of starting cash. On your turn, you'll roll the dice and move your token around the board. If you land on an unowned property, you have the option to buy it. If you land on a property owned by another player, you must pay them rent.

The key to winning is to collect color-sets of properties, which allows you to build houses and hotels. These structures dramatically increase the rent you can charge, making them powerful assets. A full set of properties is called a monopoly and is your primary path to victory.

The board also features special spaces like Chance and Community Chest, which can bring you fortune or trouble, and the dreaded Jail. This online version handles all the calculations, money, and property deeds automatically, so you can focus on your strategy. Simply enter a nickname and click play to join a game with others from around the world!
