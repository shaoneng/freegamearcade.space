Monopoly

How to Play Monopoly

Welcome to the digital world of Monopoly! Getting started is simple: just enter a nickname and hit the play button to join a game. The goal remains the same as the classic board game: travel around the board, buy up properties, and bankrupt your opponents by charging them rent.

Each player starts with $1500. On your turn, roll the dice and move your token that many spaces. If you land on an unowned property, you have the option to buy it. If you land on a property owned by another player, you must pay them rent. Collect a full set of colored properties to build houses and hotels, which will dramatically increase the rent you can charge!

Be mindful of special spaces like "Go," "Jail," "Free Parking," and the "Chance" and "Community Chest" cards, which can help or hinder your progress. If you end up in jail, you'll need to pay a $50 fine or roll a double on your turn to get out. Manage your money wisely, make smart trades, and strategize your way to becoming the ultimate real estate tycoon!
