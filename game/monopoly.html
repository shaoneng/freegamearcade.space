Monopoly

How to Play Monopoly

Welcome to the digital version of the classic real estate trading game! The objective in Monopoly is to become the wealthiest player by buying, renting, and selling properties. The last player remaining after everyone else has gone bankrupt is the winner.

Each turn, you'll roll the dice and move your token around the board. When you land on an unowned property, you can choose to buy it from the bank. If you land on a property someone else owns, you must pay them rent. Be mindful of special spaces like "Go," "Jail," "Free Parking," and the card-drawing spaces, which can either help or hinder your progress.

The key to winning is collecting complete color sets of properties. Once you own all properties in a color group, you can start building houses and eventually hotels on them. This significantly increases the rent other players have to pay you when they land there, bringing you closer to bankrupting them and securing your victory.

This online version simplifies the experience by automating the banking and property management, so you can focus entirely on your strategy. Simply enter a nickname, join a game, and start rolling the dice to build your empire!
