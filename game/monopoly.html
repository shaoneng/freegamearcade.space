Monopoly

How to Play Monopoly

Welcome to the digital version of the classic real estate trading game! The objective is simple: move around the game board, buy up properties, and try to bankrupt all of your opponents. The last player with money left is the winner.

Each player starts with a set amount of cash. On your turn, roll the two dice and move your token that many spaces clockwise around the board. Depending on where you land, you'll have different actions to take, which the game will guide you through.

If you land on an unowned property, you can buy it from the bank. If you land on a property owned by another player, you must pay them rent. The key to winning is to collect complete color sets of properties, which allows you to build houses and hotels. This dramatically increases the rent you can charge your opponents, pushing them closer to bankruptcy.

This online version handles all the rules and calculations for you, from managing money to paying rent. This lets you focus purely on your strategy. Whether you're playing with friends or challenging opponents from around the globe, the goal remains the same: build your empire and dominate the board!
