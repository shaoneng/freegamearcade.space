Monopoly

How to Play Monopoly

The objective in Monopoly is to become the wealthiest player by buying, renting, and trading properties, with the ultimate goal of driving all your opponents into bankruptcy. The last player standing with any money wins the game!

To start, each player chooses a token and receives a starting sum of money. On your turn, you roll two dice and move your token clockwise around the board. If you land on an unowned property, you have the option to buy it from the bank. If you decline, the property is auctioned off to the highest bidder.

When an opponent lands on a property you own, they must pay you rent. The rent amount increases if you own all properties of the same color group (a "monopoly"). You can then build houses and hotels on these properties to charge even higher rents. Be strategic about which properties you acquire and develop!

The game board also features special spaces like "Chance" and "Community Chest," which require you to draw a card and follow its instructions. You might receive a cash bonus or have to pay a fee. Landing on "Go to Jail" sends your token directly to the Jail space, and you'll need to pay a fine or roll a double to get out on a future turn. This online version automates all the rules, so you can focus on your strategy!
