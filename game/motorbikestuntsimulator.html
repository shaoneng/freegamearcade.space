How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

Welcome to the ultimate stunt arena! Your goal in Motorbike Stunt Simulator is to explore vast, open-world maps and perform jaw-dropping tricks. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your motorcycle. When you need a quick stop or want to pull off a drift, press the Spacebar to engage the handbrake. For a burst of speed to hit those huge ramps, hold down the Shift key to activate your boost.

To get the perfect view of your stunts, use the C key to switch between different camera angles. If a trick goes wrong or you get stuck, simply press R to reset the scene or G to reset just your bike. Did you pick the police bike? Press E to turn on the police lights for extra style!
