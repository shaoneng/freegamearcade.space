Motorbike Stunt Simulator

How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

In Motorbike Stunt Simulator, your goal is to explore dynamic 3D stunt maps and become a master of two-wheeled tricks. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your motorcycle. For sharp turns or to initiate a drift, hit the Spacebar to use the handbrake.

Need a burst of speed to clear a huge jump? Hold down the Shift key to activate your powerful nitro boost. You can switch between 8 different bikes at any time by pressing the Number Keys (1-8) on your keyboard, each with a unique feel. Find the one that's perfect for your style!

To get the best view of the action, press the C key to change camera angles. If you crash or get stuck, press G to reset your bike, or R to reset the entire level. Now, get out there and start racking up your stunt score!
