How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

Welcome to the world of high-flying stunts! In Motorbike Stunt Simulator, your goal is to explore vast 3D maps and perform the most incredible tricks imaginable. Choose from four dynamic stunt arenas, each offering unique challenges with ramps, loops, and obstacle courses designed to test your skills.

Get comfortable with the controls to dominate the arena:

  • Drive/Steer: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys.
  • Handbrake: Press the Spacebar for sharp turns and drifts.
  • Boost: Hit the Shift key to activate your nitro for extra speed.
  • Change Camera: Press C to switch between different camera views.
  • Police Lights: Use key E to turn on the police lights on the special bike.
  • Reset: If you get stuck, press R to reset the scene or G to reset just your bike.
