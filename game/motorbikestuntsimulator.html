Motorbike Stunt Simulator

How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

Welcome to the world of extreme stunts! The goal in Motorbike Stunt Simulator is to explore the vast stunt arenas and perform the most incredible tricks imaginable. Use the ramps, loops, and obstacles to your advantage to chain together combos and earn a massive score.

Basic Controls: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your motorbike. Steer left and right, accelerate, and brake to navigate the courses.

Stunt Actions: For tight turns or drifts, press the Spacebar to use the handbrake. Need a burst of speed for a huge jump? Hold down the Shift key to activate your boost!

Other Controls: Change your perspective by pressing the C key to switch camera views. If you get stuck or crash, press R to reset the scene or G to reset just your bike. Press Tab to go back to the main menu.

Pro Tip: Don't like your current ride? You can switch between 8 different motorbikes on the fly! Simply press the number keys (1-8) to instantly change your vehicle, including a fun police bike with working lights (press E to activate them).
