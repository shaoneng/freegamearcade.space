How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

Get ready to ride! Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer your motorbike through the sprawling stunt arenas. When you need a quick burst of speed for a massive jump, hold down the Shift key to activate your boost. To bring your bike to a sudden stop or initiate a drift, press the Spacebar for the handbrake.

Explore every angle of your amazing stunts by pressing C to switch camera views. Found the perfect bike? If not, you can switch between 8 different models on the fly by pressing the number keys (1â€“8). You can even turn on police lights with the E key on the police bike! If you ever get stuck or want a fresh start, press R to reset the scene or G to reset just your bike.

To hide the on-screen instructions, press X. If you need to return to the main menu to select a different arena, simply press the Tab key.
