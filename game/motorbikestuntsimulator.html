Motorbike Stunt Simulator

How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

Your objective is to explore four dynamic stunt maps, performing the craziest tricks possible to rack up your score. Master the controls to become the king of the stunt world!

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your motorbike. Press the Spacebar to use the handbrake for sharp stops and drifts. For an extra burst of speed to tackle huge ramps, hit the Shift key to activate your boost.

You can change your point of view by pressing the C key to switch between camera angles. If you get into a tricky spot, press R to reset the scene or G to reset just your bike. Press Tab to return to the main menu at any time.
