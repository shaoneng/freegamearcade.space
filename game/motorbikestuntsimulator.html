How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

Welcome to the world of extreme stunts! In Motorbike Stunt Simulator, your goal is to explore vast 3D stunt arenas and perform the most incredible tricks imaginable. Navigate challenging courses filled with ramps, loops, and obstacles to rack up your score and become a stunt legend.

Get a handle on your bike with these simple controls. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive and steer your motorcycle. When you need to stop quickly or drift, press the Spacebar for the handbrake. To get that extra burst of speed for a huge jump, hold down the Shift key to activate your boost.

Switch up your view by pressing the C key to find the perfect camera angle for your stunts. If you crash, don't worry! Press R to reset the scene or G to reset just your bike. If you've chosen the police bike, press E to turn on the flashing police lights!
