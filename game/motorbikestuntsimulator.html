How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

Get ready to tear up the asphalt and fly through the air! The objective in Motorbike Stunt Simulator is to master your bike's controls to perform incredible stunts across various open-world arenas. Use your skills to rack up points and become the undisputed stunt king.

To control your bike, use the WASD or Arrow Keys for driving and steering. For sharp turns or to initiate a drift, hit the Spacebar to use the handbrake. When you need an extra burst of speed to clear a massive jump, press and hold the Shift key to activate your nitro boost.

You're not limited to just one bike! You can switch between 8 different models on the fly by pressing the number keys (1â€“8). Find the one that best suits your stunting style. You can also change your perspective by pressing 'C' to switch camera views. If you crash or get stuck, press 'G' to reset your bike or 'R' to reset the entire scene.
