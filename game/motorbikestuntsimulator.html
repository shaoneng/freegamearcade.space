How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

Get ready to tear up the asphalt and fly through the air in Motorbike Stunt Simulator! Your main goal is to navigate the expansive stunt arenas and perform the wildest tricks imaginable. Mastering the controls is your key to becoming a stunt legend.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer your bike. For sharp drifts and quick stops, hit the Spacebar to engage the handbrake. When you need a burst of speed to tackle a huge ramp, hold down the Shift key to activate your nitro boost.

Want to change your perspective? Press the C key to cycle through different camera views. If you get into a tricky spot, you can press R to reset the scene or G to reset just your bike. A fun tip: you can switch bikes on the fly! Press the number keys (1 through 8) to instantly hop onto a new ride, including a cool police motorcycle (press E to toggle its lights!).
