Motorbike Stunt Simulator

How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

The goal in Motorbike Stunt Simulator is to become the ultimate stunt rider. Explore vast, open-world arenas filled with ramps, loops, and obstacles designed to test your skills. Perform breathtaking tricks to earn points and establish your dominance.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your motorbike. Press the Spacebar to use the handbrake for sharp turns and stops. When you need an extra burst of speed for a big jump, hold down the Shift key to activate your boost.

Switch between different camera angles by pressing the C key to find the perfect view for your stunts. If you crash or get stuck, press R to reset the scene or G to reset just your bike. You can also press the Number Keys (1-8) at any time to instantly switch between eight different motorbikes, including a cool police bike with working lights (activate with the E key)!
