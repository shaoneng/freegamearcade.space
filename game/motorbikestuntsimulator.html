How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

Welcome to the ultimate stunt arena! In Motorbike Stunt Simulator, your goal is to explore vast 3D environments and perform the most jaw-dropping tricks imaginable. Choose from four unique stunt maps, each loaded with ramps, loops, and challenging courses designed to test your skills.

Get a handle on the controls to dominate the course. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your bike. For sharp stops and drifts, press the Spacebar to engage the handbrake. Need a burst of speed to clear a massive jump? Hold down the Shift key to activate your boost. You can also switch camera views with the C key, reset the scene with R, or reset just your bike with G.
