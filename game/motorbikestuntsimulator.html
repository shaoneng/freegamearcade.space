How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

Your goal in Motorbike Stunt Simulator is to explore vast 3D stunt arenas and perform the most incredible tricks imaginable. Choose from four dynamic maps, each loaded with ramps, loops, and obstacles designed to test your riding skills.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer and accelerate your motorbike. Press the Spacebar to use the handbrake for sharp stops and drifts. Need an extra burst of speed for a huge jump? Hit the Shift key to activate your boost.

Switch your camera perspective with the C key to find the best angle for your stunts. If you crash, press G to reset your bike or R to reset the entire scene.
