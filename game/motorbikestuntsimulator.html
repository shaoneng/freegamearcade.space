Motorbike Stunt Simulator

How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

Welcome to the ultimate playground for motorcycle enthusiasts! In Motorbike Stunt Simulator, your goal is to explore vast 3D stunt arenas and perform the most incredible tricks imaginable. Choose from four unique maps, each loaded with ramps, loops, and challenging courses designed to test your skills.

Master the controls to become a stunt legend. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your bike. Hit the Spacebar for the handbrake to execute sharp turns or stops. When you need a burst of speed, press Shift to activate your boost. You can also switch camera views with the C key, reset the scene with R, and reset your bike's position with G.

Want to try a different ride? You're not limited to just one bike! Press the number keys (1-8) on your keyboard to instantly switch between 8 different models, including a powerful police motorcycle. Find the bike that best fits your stunting style and start racking up those high scores!
