Motorbike Stunt Simulator

How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

Welcome to the ultimate stunt arena! Your objective in Motorbike Stunt Simulator is to navigate vast, dynamic stunt maps and perform the most incredible tricks imaginable. Choose from four exciting arenas, each filled with ramps, loops, and challenging courses designed to test your skills.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your motorcycle. To hit the brakes or perform a drift, press the Spacebar. For an extra burst of speed to tackle a huge ramp, hold down the Shift key to activate your boost.

You can switch your camera view at any time by pressing the C key. If you get into a tricky spot or crash, press R to reset the scene or G to just reset your bike. To try a different vehicle, simply press the number keys (1-8) to switch between 8 unique motorbike models, including a police bike with working lights (activated with the E key)!
