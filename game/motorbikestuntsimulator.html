Motorbike Stunt Simulator

How to Play Motorbike Stunt Simulator

Welcome to the ultimate playground for motorcycle enthusiasts! In Motorbike Stunt Simulator, your mission is to explore incredible 3D stunt arenas and pull off jaw-dropping tricks. Navigate through a world filled with ramps, loops, and challenging courses to become a true stunt legend.

Master the controls to dominate the arena. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your bike. Hit the Spacebar for the handbrake, and press Shift to activate your speed boost. You can change your perspective by pressing the C key to switch camera views.

If you get into a tricky spot, press R to reset the entire scene or G to reset just your bike. Want to try a new ride? Simply press the number keys (1-8) to instantly switch between 8 different motorcycle models, including a police bike. Find your favorite and start shredding!
