Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Your goal in Motor Tour is to become the ultimate motorcycle racer. Weave through dense traffic on busy highways, collect cash and nitrous boosts, and complete various challenges. The key to success is sharp reflexes and strategic use of your speed.

Begin by selecting your preferred game mode. Endless Mode is ideal for practice, allowing you to ride until you crash and get a feel for the controls. In Career Mode, you'll face a series of timed missions that test your skills under pressure. For competitive action, choose Race Mode to challenge an AI opponent in a thrilling one-on-one showdown. Don't forget to try Special Event Mode for unique challenges and big rewards.

As you play, you'll earn cash. Use this cash in the garage to unlock new, more powerful motorbikes or upgrade your current ride. Better bikes offer improved speed, handling, and braking, giving you the edge you need to conquer the toughest races.
