Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

In Motor Tour, your goal is to become the ultimate motorbike racer. Hop on your powerful bike and hit the endless highways, skillfully weaving through traffic to avoid crashing. Along the way, collect valuable items like cash, nitrous boosts for extra speed, and XP to level up.

Motor Tour offers several exciting game modes. For beginners, Endless mode is the perfect place to practice your skills and get a feel for the controls. In Career mode, you'll tackle a series of missions with increasing difficulty and time pressure, proving your ability to handle tight turns and reckless drivers.

If you're looking for a direct challenge, jump into Race mode to go head-to-head against a tough AI opponent. For something different, Special Event mode offers unique tasks and big rewards. Use the cash you earn across all modes to unlock new bikes and purchase upgrades to dominate the competition.
