Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Get ready to feel the rush in Motor Tour! In this premium motorbike racing game, you'll hop on powerful bikes and navigate through busy traffic, endless highways, and intense one-on-one races. Your goal is to weave through traffic, execute thrilling near misses, and collect cash, nitrous boosts, or XP points on the road to upgrade your ride and dominate the competition.

You can choose from several exciting game modes. If you're new, start with Endless Mode to get a feel for the bike's handling as you cruise down a busy highway until you crash. Ready for a challenge? Try Career Mode, where you'll take on missions with increasing difficulty and time pressure. In Race Mode, you'll face tough AI opponents in head-to-head showdowns, using nitrous to gain an edge. For something different, check out Special Event Mode for unique tasks and big rewards.

Master the realistic bike physics and stunning 3D visuals to become a true speed demon. It’s time to rev your engine, show off your skills, and get ready for the ride of your life!
