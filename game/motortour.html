Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Welcome to the driver's seat of Motor Tour! Your main goal is to master the art of high-speed motorcycle racing on busy highways. Use your keyboard or on-screen controls to steer your bike, weaving through traffic to avoid crashing. The closer you get to other vehicles without hitting them (a "near miss"), the more points and rewards you'll earn.

Motor Tour offers several exciting game modes to choose from:

  • Endless Mode: Perfect for beginners! Cruise down the highway for as long as you can without crashing. It's a great way to practice your handling and get a feel for the game's physics.
  • Career Mode: Take on a series of challenging, timed missions. The difficulty increases as you progress, testing your ability to handle tight turns and avoid reckless drivers while collecting cash for upgrades.
  • Race Mode: Challenge a tough AI opponent in a head-to-head race. Collect nitrous boosts on the road to gain a speed advantage and leave your rival in the dust.
  • Special Event Mode: Participate in unique tasks and challenges to earn big loot rewards and unlock exclusive content.

As you play, collect cash, nitrous, and XP points scattered on the road. Use your earnings to unlock new, more powerful motorbikes and upgrade them to improve your performance. Good luck, and enjoy the ride!
