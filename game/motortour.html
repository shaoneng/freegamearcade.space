Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Get started in Motor Tour by choosing your preferred game mode. For new players, the Endless Mode is the perfect place to start. Here, your goal is to ride for as long as possible on a busy highway, weaving through traffic to get a feel for the bike's controls and physics. The run ends when you crash, making it an excellent way to practice your skills.

Ready for a challenge? Jump into Career Mode, where you'll face a series of missions with specific objectives and time limits. The difficulty increases as you progress, testing your ability to handle tight turns and avoid other drivers. In Race Mode, you'll go head-to-head against a tough AI opponent. Collect nitrous boosts on the road to gain a speed advantage and be the first to cross the finish line.

For something different, try the Special Event Mode, which offers unique tasks and the chance to earn significant rewards. Across all modes, keep an eye out for cash, nitrous, and XP points scattered on the road. Use these to unlock new bikes and upgrade your current one to dominate the competition.
