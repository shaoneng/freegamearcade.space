Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Motor Tour! Your main goal is to hop on your motorbike and dominate the highways. You'll need sharp reflexes to weave through traffic, overtake other vehicles, and avoid crashing.

The game offers several exciting modes to choose from:

  • Career Mode: Take on a series of missions with increasing difficulty. Complete objectives under time pressure to earn cash and unlock powerful upgrades for your bike.
  • Race Mode: Challenge skilled AI opponents in a thrilling one-on-one showdown. Collect nitrous boosts on the road to gain a speed advantage and leave your rival in the dust.
  • Endless Mode: Perfect for beginners or those looking to hone their skills. Cruise down a busy highway for as long as you can without crashing. This is a great way to get a feel for the bike's handling and practice near-misses.
  • Special Event Mode: Looking for something different? This mode offers unique and fun tasks with big loot rewards for completing them.

As you race, be sure to collect cash, nitrous, and XP points scattered on the road. Use the cash you earn to upgrade your bike's performance or purchase new, more powerful models. Master the controls, choose your mode, and get ready for the ride of your life!
