Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Motor Tour! Your main goal is to become the best motorcycle racer by navigating through heavy traffic, completing challenges, and upgrading your ride. Use your keyboard to control your motorbike, swerving to avoid cars and trucks while collecting cash and power-ups scattered on the road.

Motor Tour offers several exciting game modes to test your skills. If you're new, start with Endless Mode to practice handling your bike on a busy highway until you crash. In Career Mode, you'll face a series of missions with increasing difficulty. Prove you can handle tight corners and reckless drivers to earn cash for upgrades. For a direct challenge, jump into Race Mode to compete against tough AI opponents in a head-to-head showdown. Finally, Special Event Mode offers unique tasks and generous rewards for skilled riders.

Don't forget to collect nitrous boosts on the road! Activating nitrous will give you a burst of speed, which is crucial for overtaking rivals in Race Mode or achieving high scores. Use the cash you earn to unlock new bikes and upgrade their performance to dominate the highways.
