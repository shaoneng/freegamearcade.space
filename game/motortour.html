Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Get ready to hit the open road in Motor Tour! The main objective is to navigate your high-performance motorcycle through busy highway traffic, getting as close as you can to other vehicles for "near miss" bonuses. Along the way, collect cash, nitrous boosts, and XP to improve your score and unlock new features. Your goal changes depending on the game mode, but the core skills of sharp reflexes and expert weaving are always key to success.

Motor Tour offers several exciting game modes to choose from. For a relaxed practice session, try Endless Mode, where you ride for as long as you can without crashing. Career Mode presents a series of challenging missions with specific objectives and time limits. If you're feeling competitive, jump into Race Mode to go head-to-head against a tough AI opponent. Finally, keep an eye out for Special Events, which offer unique tasks and valuable rewards.

As you complete races and missions, you'll earn cash. Use this cash in the garage to upgrade your current bike or save up to purchase new, more powerful motorcycles. Upgrading your bike's speed, handling, and braking will give you the edge you need to conquer tougher challenges and dominate the leaderboards.
