Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Your goal in Motor Tour is to become the ultimate motorcycle racer. Navigate through busy highways, outpace your rivals, and upgrade your ride to dominate the streets.

The game offers several exciting modes to test your skills. For practice, jump into Endless mode and see how long you can survive the chaotic traffic. Career mode presents a series of missions with increasing difficulty, pushing your handling abilities to the limit. In Race mode, you'll go head-to-head against tough AI opponents. Don't forget to check out the Special Event mode for unique challenges and big rewards.

As you race, focus on weaving through traffic to avoid crashing. Collect cash, nitrous boosts, and XP points scattered on the road. Use the nitrous for a burst of speed to overtake rivals or escape tight situations. Use the cash you earn to unlock new bikes and purchase upgrades, enhancing your performance for the next race.
