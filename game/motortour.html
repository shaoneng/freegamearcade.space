Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Welcome to the high-speed world of Motor Tour! Your primary objective is to race your motorcycle through traffic, complete missions, and defeat opponents. Use your controls to accelerate, brake, and steer your bike, carefully weaving between cars and trucks to avoid crashing.

The game offers several modes to test your skills. For new players, Endless mode is the perfect place to start. Here, you can practice handling your bike on a busy highway for as long as possible. In Career mode, you'll face a series of missions with increasing difficulty and time pressure. For a direct challenge, try Race mode, where you'll compete against an AI opponent and use nitrous boosts to gain an edge. Finally, Special Event mode provides unique tasks with big rewards.

While riding, keep an eye out for collectibles on the road like cash, XP, and nitrous. Use the cash you earn to unlock faster bikes and purchase upgrades, giving you a better chance to dominate the highway. Master the art of the near miss to earn bonus points and show everyone you're the best rider on the road!
