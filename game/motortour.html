Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Rev your engines and get ready for the ultimate motorbike racing experience in Motor Tour! This free-to-play browser game puts you in the driver's seat of powerful motorbikes to race through traffic, tackle endless highways, and engage in thrilling one-on-one showdowns.

Feel the adrenaline rush as you skillfully weave through busy roads, collecting cash, nitrous boosts, or XP points along the way. Your goal is to leave your rivals in the dust while pulling off electrifying near misses with other vehicles to score big. It’s all about speed, skill, and strategy.

Motor Tour offers multiple game modes to keep you entertained. For newcomers, Endless mode is the perfect place to practice handling your bike on a busy highway until you crash. Career mode presents a series of missions with increasing difficulty, challenging you to master tight turns and avoid reckless drivers under time pressure. In Race mode, you can challenge tough AI opponents in an intense head-to-head duel. For something different, Special Event mode offers fun tasks with big loot rewards.

Use the cash you earn to unlock and upgrade a wide variety of motorbikes. Enhance your bike's performance to conquer the toughest challenges and show everyone who owns the road. Developed by Wolves Interactive, Motor Tour blends realistic bike physics with gorgeous 3D visuals, offering endless replay value for all racing fans.
