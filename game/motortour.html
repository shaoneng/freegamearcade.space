Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Rev up your engine and dive into the high-speed world of Motor Tour! This premium browser game puts you in control of powerful motorbikes to race through dense traffic on endless highways. Experience the thrill of near misses, collect valuable upgrades, and leave your competition in the dust. Weave through traffic, grab cash, nitrous boosts, and XP to enhance your ride and prove your skills.

Motor Tour offers several exciting game modes to keep you engaged. For new riders, Endless Mode is the perfect place to start. Cruise down a busy highway and practice your handling skills until you crash. Ready for a challenge? Jump into Career Mode, where you'll tackle a series of missions with increasing difficulty and time pressure. In Race Mode, you can challenge tough AI opponents in intense head-to-head showdowns. Don't forget to check out Special Event Mode for unique tasks and generous rewards.

Developed by Wolves Interactive, Motor Tour features realistic bike physics and stunning 3D visuals for an immersive experience with endless replayability. If you enjoy the thrill of highway racing, you might also like similar titles such as Highway Moto, which offers its own unique set of endless modes and cool bikes. Get ready for the ride of your life!
