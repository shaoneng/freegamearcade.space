Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Rev up your engines and dive into the high-octane world of Motor Tour! This premium browser game puts you on powerful motorbikes to race through endless highways and dense traffic. Feel the adrenaline rush as you weave past cars, collect cash and power-ups, and leave your rivals in the dust. If you love electrifying near-misses and customizing your ride with tons of upgrades, Motor Tour is the ultimate racing experience for you.

Motor Tour offers several exciting game modes to test your skills. For newcomers, the Endless mode is the perfect place to practice handling your bike on a busy highway until you crash. Ready for a challenge? Jump into Career mode to tackle missions with increasing difficulty and earn cash for upgrades. In Race mode, you'll go head-to-head against tough AI opponents, using nitrous boosts to gain an edge. Don't forget to check out Special Event mode for unique tasks and big rewards.

Your goal is to become the ultimate road warrior by mastering tight turns, avoiding reckless drivers, and collecting enough cash to unlock better bikes and upgrades. Developed by Wolves Interactive, Motor Tour combines realistic bike physics and gorgeous 3D visuals for endless replay value. Hit the road now and get ready for the ride of your life!
