Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Get ready for the ultimate motorbike racing experience with Motor Tour! This game puts you in the fast lane, challenging you to weave through busy traffic, take on rivals, and become the king of the highway. With powerful motorbikes, multiple game modes, and tons of upgrades, the thrill never stops.

Motor Tour offers several exciting game modes to test your skills. For newcomers, Endless Mode is the perfect place to practice, letting you ride as far as you can on a busy highway to master your bike's handling. Career Mode presents a series of challenging missions with increasing difficulty, where you'll need to prove your skills under pressure to earn cash for upgrades. In Race Mode, you'll go head-to-head against tough AI opponents in intense showdowns. Finally, Special Event Mode offers unique tasks with big rewards.

Your main goal is to navigate the roads at high speed, executing thrilling near misses to earn extra points and cash. Collect nitrous boosts on the road to gain a speed advantage over your rivals. Use the cash you earn from races and missions to unlock new, more powerful motorbikes and upgrade them to dominate the competition. Rev your engine, hit the road, and show everyone your mad racing skills!
