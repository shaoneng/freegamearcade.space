Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Welcome to the high-speed world of Motor Tour! Your main goal is to navigate your powerful motorbike through dense traffic, complete missions, and outrace your opponents. The key to success is sharp reflexes and strategic use of boosts as you weave through cars and trucks.

The game offers several exciting modes. For new players, Endless Mode is the perfect place to start. You can cruise down the highway and get a feel for the bike's controls without any pressure—your ride only ends when you crash. Ready for a challenge? Jump into Career Mode to tackle a series of missions with increasing difficulty. Complete objectives under time pressure and earn cash to unlock powerful upgrades for your bike.

If you're feeling competitive, test your skills in Race Mode against tough AI opponents in thrilling one-on-one showdowns. Don't forget to grab nitrous boosts on the road to gain a crucial speed advantage. For something a little different, check out the Special Event Mode, which features unique tasks and offers big rewards for your efforts. Weave through traffic, collect cash and XP, and become the king of the road!
