Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Welcome to Motor Tour, the ultimate motorcycle racing challenge! The main goal is to navigate through dense highway traffic, complete various missions, and become the top racer on the road. Weave through cars, collect valuable items like cash and nitrous, and avoid crashing to keep your speed and score high.

Mastering the controls is key to success. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to steer your bike. W/Up Arrow accelerates, and S/Down Arrow is for braking. Keep an eye out for nitrous pickups on the road and use them to get a powerful speed boost and overtake your opponents!

Motor Tour features several exciting game modes to test your skills. For beginners, Endless Mode is a great place to start, allowing you to drive until you crash and get a feel for the controls. Career Mode presents you with a series of missions with increasing difficulty. In Race Mode, you'll go head-to-head against tough AI opponents. Finally, Special Event Mode offers unique challenges with big rewards.

As you play, you'll earn cash that you can use in the garage. Spend your earnings to unlock new, more powerful motorcycles or upgrade your current bike's engine, handling, and braking. A better bike will give you the edge you need to conquer the toughest challenges and races.
