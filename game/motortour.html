Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Get ready for a high-octane racing experience in Motor Tour! The main goal is to ride your motorcycle through busy highways, weaving through traffic to cover as much distance as possible without crashing. The closer you get to other vehicles without hitting them (a near miss), the more points and cash you earn. Collect items like cash, nitrous boosts, and XP directly on the road to improve your performance and unlock new content.

Motor Tour offers several exciting game modes to test your skills. For beginners, Endless Mode is the perfect place to practice handling your bike and learning the traffic patterns. In Career Mode, you'll take on a series of challenging missions with specific objectives and time limits. For a competitive thrill, jump into Race Mode to go head-to-head against skilled AI opponents. Don't forget to check out Special Event Mode for unique tasks and big rewards.

As you earn cash from your races, be sure to visit the garage. Here, you can spend your earnings to purchase new, more powerful motorcycles or upgrade your current ride's engine, handling, and braking. A well-upgraded bike is key to conquering the toughest challenges and leaving your rivals in the dust!
