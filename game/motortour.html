Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Get ready to hit the asphalt in Motor Tour! The main goal is to navigate your high-speed motorcycle through busy highway traffic, completing various objectives across different game modes. Weave between cars, collect cash and power-ups, and avoid crashing to prove your skills.

Motor Tour offers several exciting game modes to choose from. If you're new, start with Endless Mode to get a feel for the controls and practice dodging traffic until you crash. In Career Mode, you'll take on a series of challenging missions with increasing difficulty. For competitive action, jump into Race Mode to go head-to-head against a tough AI opponent. Finally, check out Special Event Mode for unique tasks and big rewards!

While racing, keep an eye out for collectibles on the road. Grab cash to save up for powerful upgrades and new bikes. Snag nitrous boosts to get a burst of speed and leave your rivals behind. Use the controls to steer your bike, accelerate, and brake as you master the art of the near miss for extra points.
