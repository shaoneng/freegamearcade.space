Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Motor Tour! Your main goal is to ride your motorbike through heavy traffic, completing missions and out-racing opponents without crashing. Use your skills to weave through cars, get dangerously close for extra points, and push your bike to its limits.

Motor Tour offers several exciting game modes to choose from:

  • Career: Take on a series of missions with increasing difficulty. Complete objectives under time pressure to earn cash and unlock upgrades.
  • Endless: Perfect for practice! See how long you can survive on a busy highway. The goal is to get the highest score before you eventually crash.
  • Race: Go head-to-head against a tough AI opponent. Collect nitrous boosts on the road to gain a speed advantage and claim victory.
  • Special Event: Participate in unique challenges to earn fun rewards and big loot.

As you race, collect cash, nitrous packs, and XP points scattered on the road. Use the cash you earn to buy new, more powerful motorbikes or upgrade your current one. Master the controls, avoid reckless drivers, and show everyone who rules the highway!
