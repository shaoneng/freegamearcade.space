Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Welcome to the high-speed world of Motor Tour! The core of the game is to ride your motorcycle as fast as possible, skillfully weaving through traffic and completing objectives without crashing. Collect items on the road like cash, nitrous for a speed boost, and XP points to level up.

Motor Tour offers several exciting game modes to choose from. For newcomers, Endless Mode is the perfect place to start. Here, you can cruise down a busy highway to get a feel for the bike's handling and practice your traffic-dodging skills. Your ride only ends when you crash.

Ready for a challenge? Jump into Career Mode, where you'll face a series of missions with increasing difficulty and time pressure. In Race Mode, you'll go head-to-head against a tough AI opponent. Be sure to grab nitrous boosts on the road to gain a crucial advantage. For unique challenges and big rewards, don't miss the Special Event Mode.

Use the cash you earn from races to unlock and upgrade new motorbikes, improving their performance and giving you an edge on the road. Master the controls, choose your mode, and hit the highway to become the ultimate motorcycle champion!
