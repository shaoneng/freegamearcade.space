Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Get ready for a high-octane racing adventure in Motor Tour! The main objective is to ride your powerful motorcycle through busy highways, avoiding crashes while completing various challenges. You'll need to master weaving through traffic at high speeds, collecting valuable items like cash, nitrous boosts, and XP points along the way to upgrade your ride and outperform your competition.

Motor Tour offers several exciting game modes to test your skills. For new players, Endless Mode is the perfect place to start, allowing you to practice handling your bike on an infinite highway. In Career Mode, you'll tackle a series of missions with increasing difficulty. Race Mode pits you against tough AI opponents in intense one-on-one showdowns. For unique challenges and big rewards, jump into the Special Event Mode.

Use your keyboard controls (W/A/S/D or Arrow Keys) to accelerate, brake, and steer your bike. Keep an eye out for nitrous pickups on the road; use them to get a speed burst and leave your rivals in the dust. The closer you get to other vehicles without crashing (a "near miss"), the more points you'll earn. Invest the cash you collect in the garage to unlock new bikes and upgrade their performance.
