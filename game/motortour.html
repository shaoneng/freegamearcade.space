Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Embark on thrilling motorbike races in Motor Tour! The game offers several exciting modes to choose from: Career, Special Event, Endless, or Race.

If you're new to the game, start with Endless mode. This allows you to cruise down a busy highway, weaving through traffic and collecting cash, nitrous, and XP points without pressure, helping you get a feel for your bike's handling before you crash.

In Career mode, you'll take on various missions under time pressure, starting easy and gradually increasing in difficulty. Hone your skills by navigating tight turns and avoiding reckless drivers while collecting cash to unlock powerful upgrades for your motorbike.

Challenge formidable AI opponents in intense head-to-head battles within Race mode. Collect nitrous on the road to gain a speed advantage and leave your rival in the dust. For unique challenges and big rewards, explore the Special Event mode.

Control your motorbike to steer through traffic, collect valuable items on the road, and perform electrifying near misses. Master the art of high-speed racing and become the ultimate Motor Tour champion!
