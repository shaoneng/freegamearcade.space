Motor Tour

How to Play Motor Tour

Welcome to the high-speed world of Motor Tour! Your main objective is to navigate your motorcycle through busy highways, completing objectives and out-racing opponents. Start by choosing a game mode that suits your style.

Endless Mode: Perfect for beginners. In this mode, you'll drive down an endless highway, learning the bike's controls and how to dodge traffic until you crash. It's a great way to practice and get a feel for the game.

Career Mode: Take on a series of challenging missions with time limits. The difficulty increases as you progress, testing your ability to handle tight turns and avoid reckless drivers while collecting cash for essential upgrades.

Race Mode: Go head-to-head against tough AI rivals. Collect nitrous on the road to get a speed boost and gain the upper hand to cross the finish line first.

Special Event Mode: Looking for unique challenges? This mode offers fun tasks and the chance to earn big rewards to customize and upgrade your ride.
