How to Play Moto X3M

Your goal in Moto X3M is to race your motorcycle through a series of obstacle-filled levels and reach the finish line in the fastest time possible. The quicker you are, the more stars you'll earnâ€”up to three per level! These stars are key to unlocking cool new bikes and riders.

The controls are simple but require skill to master. Use the Up Arrow key to accelerate and the Down Arrow key to brake. While in the air, use the Left and Right Arrow keys to lean your rider forward or backward. This is crucial for performing front flips and backflips, which shave precious seconds off your total time.

Be prepared for a wild ride! You'll face everything from giant loops and crushing walls to explosive TNT. React quickly to avoid crashing. If you do crash, you'll restart from the last checkpoint you passed. While speed is important for getting three stars, some obstacles require a more careful and steady approach. Balance is everything!
