Moto X3M

How to Play Moto X3M

The goal in Moto X3M is to race your motorbike to the finish line of each level as fast as you can. You'll face a variety of treacherous obstacles, from explosive TNT to giant spinning saws, that you must navigate to survive.

Use the arrow keys to control your bike. The Up Arrow key accelerates, the Down Arrow key brakes, and the Left and Right Arrow keys are used to lean your rider back and forward. Leaning is crucial for maintaining balance and performing stunts.

To get the best time and earn three stars on each level, you need to perform flips. Use the Left and Right Arrow keys while airborne to do front flips and back flips. Not only do these look cool, but they also shave precious seconds off your final time. Be careful, thoughâ€”landing on your head will force you to restart from the last checkpoint!

The stars you earn can be used to unlock new bikes and riders, each with a unique style. Keep playing to collect them all!
