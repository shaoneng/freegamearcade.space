Moto X3M

How to Play Moto X3M

Your goal in Moto X3M is to race your motorcycle through a series of challenging levels, overcoming wild obstacles to reach the finish line as quickly as possible. The faster you are, the more stars you'll earn!

Use the arrow keys to control your bike:

  • Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • Down Arrow: Brake
  • Left Arrow: Lean back / Rotate backward in the air
  • Right Arrow: Lean forward / Rotate forward in the air

Performing stunts is key to getting the best time. Use the Left and Right arrow keys while airborne to do frontflips and backflips. Each successful flip shaves time off your final score, helping you earn those coveted 3 stars. Use the stars you collect to unlock cool new bikes and riders.

Be careful! Crashing will send you back to the last checkpoint. While speed is often your friend, some obstacles require a more careful and steady approach. Master the balance between speed and control to conquer all 22 insane tracks!
