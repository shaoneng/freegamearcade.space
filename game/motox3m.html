Moto X3M

How to Play Moto X3M

Get ready for a wild ride! The goal in Moto X3M is to race your motorbike to the finish line of each level as quickly as you can. The tracks are filled with dangerous obstacles like giant saws, spikes, and explosives, so you'll need quick reflexes to survive!

The controls are simple to learn but hard to master:

  • Up Arrow / W Key: Accelerate
  • Down Arrow / S Key: Brake
  • Left Arrow / A Key: Lean Back / Rotate Backwards
  • Right Arrow / D Key: Lean Forward / Rotate Forwards

Leaning back and forward while in the air allows you to perform awesome back flips and front flips. Not only do these stunts look cool, but each successful flip shaves time off your final score, helping you earn more stars. The faster you finish, the more stars you get!

Earning stars is key to progressing. Use them to unlock awesome new bikes and quirky riders, like a ghost on a spectral bike or Santa on his sleigh! Some levels require pure speed, while others need careful balance. Don't be afraid to crash a few times—checkpoints will save your progress, so you can get right back into the action.
