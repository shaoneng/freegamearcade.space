Moto X3M

How to Play Moto X3M

Your goal in Moto X3M is to race your motorcycle through a series of hazardous levels, reaching the finish line in the fastest time possible. Each track is packed with unique obstacles, from giant saws and spikes to incredible loops and ramps that will test your reflexes.

Control your bike using your keyboard. Press the Up Arrow key to accelerate and the Down Arrow key to brake. Use the Left and Right Arrow keys to lean your rider back and forward. These leaning controls are crucial for maintaining balance and performing stunts.

To get the best time and earn three stars, you need to perform flips in the air. Use the Left and Right Arrow keys while airborne to execute backflips and frontflips. Each successful flip shaves time off your final score, but be careful! Landing on your head will force you to restart from the last checkpoint.

The stars you earn can be used in the shop to purchase new, faster bikes and unlock cool new characters. The more you play and the better you perform, the more awesome cosmetic upgrades you can acquire to customize your ride.
