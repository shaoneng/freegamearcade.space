Moto X3M

How to Play Moto X3M

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of Moto X3M! Your goal is to race your motorcycle through a series of challenging levels filled with obstacles, loops, and massive jumps. The faster you complete a level, the more stars you'll earn—up to three for a perfect run!

Use the following controls to master your bike:

  • Up Arrow / W: Accelerate
  • Down Arrow / S: Brake
  • Left Arrow / A: Lean back. Use this in the air to perform a backflip.
  • Right Arrow / D: Lean forward. Use this in the air to perform a frontflip.

Performing flips and stunts will shave seconds off your final time, but be careful! If you land on your head, you'll crash and have to restart from the last checkpoint. Use your stars to unlock cool new bikes and riders. React quickly, time your stunts, and aim for that 3-star finish!
