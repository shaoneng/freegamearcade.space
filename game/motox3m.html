Moto X3M

How to Play Moto X3M

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of Moto X3M! Your goal is to race your motorcycle through a series of challenging levels packed with obstacles, ramps, and death-defying jumps. The main objective is to reach the finish line in the shortest time possible to earn up to three stars.

Use the Up Arrow Key to accelerate and the Down Arrow Key to brake. Control your bike's rotation in mid-air with the Left and Right Arrow Keys. Mastering these aerial controls is crucial for landing safely and performing stunts.

Performing frontflips and backflips not only looks cool but also reduces your final time, helping you secure those coveted three-star ratings. However, be careful! Crashing will send you back to the last checkpoint. Sometimes, a slower, more controlled approach is better than reckless speed.

As you complete levels and earn stars, you'll unlock awesome new bikes and quirky riders. Each of the 22 tracks presents a unique and increasingly difficult challenge. React quickly, time your jumps perfectly, and master your flips to conquer every course!
