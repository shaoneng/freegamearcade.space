Moto X3M

How to Play Moto X3M

Your goal in Moto X3M is simple: race your motorcycle to the finish line in the shortest time possible. Each of the 22 levels is packed with dangerous obstacles, from giant circular saws to explosive TNT. The faster you complete a level, the more stars you earn.

The controls are intuitive. Use the Up Arrow key to accelerate and the Down Arrow key to brake. Use the Left and Right Arrow keys to lean your rider forward or backward. Mastering your balance is key to landing jumps and navigating tricky terrain.

To get the best time and earn three stars, you need to be bold! Perform front flips and back flips by using the lean keys while airborne. Successful flips will shave seconds off your final time. However, be careful! Crashing will send you back to the last checkpoint, costing you precious time. Some levels require speed and stunts, while others demand a more careful, steady approach.

The stars you earn aren't just for bragging rights. Use them in the garage to unlock cool new bikes and even crazier riders, like a skeleton on a bone bike or a pumpkin-headed ghost.
