Moto X3M

How to Play Moto X3M

Get ready for a high-octane thrill ride! In Moto X3M, your goal is to race your motorcycle through a series of challenging levels packed with dangerous obstacles, loops, and ramps. The main objective is to reach the finish line in the shortest time possible.

The controls are simple to learn but hard to master:

  • Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • Down Arrow: Brake
  • Left Arrow: Lean back (used for backflips)
  • Right Arrow: Lean forward (used for frontflips)

To get the best time and earn all three stars on each level, you'll need to perform stunts. Executing flips in the air shaves precious seconds off your final time. But be careful! If you land incorrectly and crash your rider, you'll have to restart from the last checkpoint. Use your stars to unlock cool new bikes and riders to show off your style on the track!
