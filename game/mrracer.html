Mr Racer

How to Play Mr Racer

Welcome to the high-octane world of Mr Racer! Your goal is to dominate the streets and become the ultimate racing champion. The core of the game is simple: steer your car skillfully through dense traffic, avoiding collisions with other vehicles at all costs. The better you drive, the more you earn!

Mr Racer offers six exciting game modes to test your driving skills, including Endless Ride, a challenging Chase mode, and a race against the clock in Time Trial. Note that some modes need to be unlocked by playing.

Use the money you win from races to build your dream garage. You can purchase new, faster cars or spend your cash on upgrading the performance of the vehicles you already own. You'll start in the scenic Farm Land, but four more distinct locations are waiting to be unlocked as you progress. Jump in, hit the gas, and show everyone who the real Mr Racer is!
