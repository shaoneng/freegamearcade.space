Mr Racer

How to Play Mr Racer

Welcome to the high-speed world of Mr Racer! Your main objective is to become the ultimate racer by competing in various challenges and mastering the art of driving in heavy traffic. Listen to Maria's announcements as she guides you through your racing career. Choose your favorite game mode and hit the road, but be careful—one wrong move can lead to a spectacular crash!

Use your keyboard or on-screen controls to steer your car through the bustling streets. Your primary skill is to navigate through flowing traffic without colliding with other vehicles. Success in races earns you money, which is crucial for advancing in the game. Spend your hard-earned cash in the garage to unlock a variety of new, faster cars or to upgrade the performance of the vehicles you already own, improving their speed, handling, and braking.

Mr Racer offers six unique game modes, including thrilling Challenges, an Endless mode, a relaxing Free Ride, and intense Chase and Time Trial modes that you can unlock as you progress. You'll start your journey in Farm Land, but there are four other exciting locations to unlock. In Endless and Free Ride modes, you have the freedom to choose your favorite track and just enjoy the drive.
