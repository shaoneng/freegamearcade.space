Mr Racer

How to Play Mr Racer

Get ready to burn rubber in Mr Racer! The primary goal is to become the ultimate racing champion by competing in various high-speed events. Your main challenge is to navigate through dense, flowing traffic, skillfully avoiding collisions with other vehicles.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to steer, accelerate, and brake. The game offers six distinct modes, each with its own objective. Start with modes like Endless or Free Ride to get a feel for the controls. As you win races, you'll earn cash that can be used to purchase new, faster cars or to upgrade the performance of the vehicles you already own. You can also unlock new and exciting locations to race in. Master the road, complete the challenges, and build your dream garage!
