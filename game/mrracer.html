Mr Racer

How to Play Mr Racer

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Mr Racer! Your primary objective is to become the best racer on the road by navigating through dense, flowing traffic. The game is compatible with both desktop and mobile devices, offering a seamless racing experience wherever you are.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys (or on-screen controls for mobile) to steer your car left and right. Your focus is on expert steering and avoiding collisions with other vehicles. Weave through traffic with precision to earn points and cash. Crashing will end your run, so stay sharp!

Mr Racer features six exciting game modes to keep you engaged, including Challenge, Endless, Free Ride, and the unlockable Chase and Time Trial modes. Each offers a different objective, from outrunning the police to racing against the clock. Completing races successfully earns you money which you can use to unlock faster, more stylish cars or upgrade the performance of your existing vehicles. You can also unlock new beautiful locations to race in!
