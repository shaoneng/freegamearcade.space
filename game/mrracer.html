Mr Racer

How to Play Mr Racer

Welcome to Mr Racer! Your goal is to become the best driver on the road. The main objective is to skillfully navigate your car through flowing traffic, complete race objectives, and avoid crashing into other vehicles. Use your keyboard controls or on-screen controls on mobile to master steering, accelerating, and braking to weave through cars and prove your skills.

The game offers six unique modes to keep the action fresh. Start with Challenge mode or enjoy a relaxing drive in Free Ride. As you progress and earn experience, you can unlock more intense modes like Chase and Time Trial. Each mode presents different challenges and goals, from outrunning pursuers to racing against the clock for the best lap time.

Success on the track earns you money. Use your winnings to purchase new, faster cars from the garage or to upgrade your existing vehicles. Improving your car's performance is key to tackling tougher challenges. You can also unlock new racing locations, starting from the Farm Land and expanding to four other exciting environments to conquer.
