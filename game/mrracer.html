Mr Racer

How to Play Mr Racer

Welcome to the high-stakes world of Mr Racer! Your primary goal is to master the art of high-speed driving through dense traffic. Use your keyboard controls or tap on your mobile screen to steer your car, accelerate, and brake.

Navigate the busy roads carefully to avoid crashing into other vehicles. Each successful race earns you money, which is crucial for advancing in the game. Use your earnings to unlock a garage full of new, faster cars or to upgrade the performance of the vehicles you already own.

The game features six distinct modes, including Endless and Free Ride, with others like Chase and Time Trial waiting to be unlocked. You can also unlock new environments to test your skills. Choose your mode, pick your car, and hit the road to become the ultimate Mr Racer!
