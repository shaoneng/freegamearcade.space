Mr Racer

How to Play Mr Racer

Your goal in Mr Racer is to become the ultimate driver by competing in various high-speed challenges and navigating through busy traffic without crashing. Use your keyboard or on-screen controls to steer your car and expertly weave between other vehicles on the road.

The game features six exciting modes to test your skills. You can start with Challenge, Endless, or Free Ride modes immediately. As you prove your skill, you can unlock the thrilling Chase and Time Trial modes for even greater challenges.

Success in races earns you cash. Use this money to upgrade the performance of your current vehicle or save up to unlock new, more powerful cars from the garage. Personalize your ride and dominate the asphalt!

Your racing journey starts in the scenic Farm Land, but you can unlock four additional locations as you progress. In Endless and Free Ride modes, you have the freedom to choose your favorite track to race on.
