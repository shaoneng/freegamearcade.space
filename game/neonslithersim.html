Neon Slither Sim

How to Play Neon Slither Sim

Your objective in Neon Slither Sim is to grow your neon trail by collecting glowing lights scattered across the arena. Skillfully maneuver your bike to trap opponents, forcing them to crash into your tail. When they are eliminated, you can collect their lights to grow even bigger and stronger.

PC Controls: Drag your mouse across the screen to direct your neon bike. Your bike will follow the cursor's movement in real-time. To get a burst of speed, left-click your mouse. Use your speed boost wisely to outmaneuver rivals or escape danger.

Mobile Controls: On a mobile device, tap and hold the screen to move. To activate your speed boost, simply tap the designated "fire" button.
