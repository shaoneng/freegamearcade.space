Neon Slither Sim

How to Play Neon Slither Sim

The objective in Neon Slither Sim is to become the largest neon snake in the arena. Pilot your bike through a glowing digital space, collecting colorful lights to extend your trail. As you grow, you can trap opponents by making them crash into your tail. Once an opponent is eliminated, quickly collect their dropped lights to grow even bigger.

PC Controls: Use your mouse to control the direction of your neon bike; it will follow your cursor in real-time. To activate a speed boost, simply left-click your mouse. Use this boost strategically to cut off rivals or escape from tight spots.

Mobile Controls: On a mobile device, tap and hold the screen to move your bike. A dedicated fire button is available to activate your speed boost.

Stay vigilant and avoid crashing into the outer edges of the arena or into other players' trails, as this will end your run. The game features 10 levels of increasing difficulty. If you conquer all 10, you'll unlock Infinity Mode for an endless challenge. You can also test your skills against real people in the exciting Multiplayer Mode.
