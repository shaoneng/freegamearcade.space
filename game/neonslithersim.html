Neon Slither Sim

How to Play Neon Slither Sim

Mastering Neon Slither Sim is intuitive and fun! Your objective is to grow your neon trail by collecting glowing lights while outsmarting other players. Encircle your opponents to make them crash into your trail, then collect the lights they drop to grow even bigger.

PC Controls:

  • Move: Drag your mouse across the screen to steer your neon bike.
  • Speed Boost: Left-click your mouse to activate a temporary speed boost. Use it strategically to trap enemies or escape danger, but watch your fuel!

Mobile Controls:

  • Move: Tap and hold on the screen to guide your bike.
  • Speed Boost: Tap the dedicated fire button on the screen to go faster.

Be careful not to crash into other players' trails or the edges of the arena. Complete all 10 levels to unlock the endless Infinity Mode and aim for the highest score!
