Neon Slither Sim

How to Play Neon Slither Sim

The goal in Neon Slither Sim is to grow your glowing trail as long as possible by consuming luminous pellets scattered across the arena. Maneuver your neon bike to trap other players, forcing them to crash into your trail. Once they're eliminated, you can collect their remains to grow even bigger and score more points. But be carefulâ€”don't crash into the walls or other players' trails, or your run will end!

On PC: Use your mouse to guide your bike. It will follow your cursor's movement in real-time. Left-click to activate a speed boost, but use your fuel wisely to outmaneuver opponents.

On Mobile: Tap and hold the screen to direct your bike. To get a burst of speed, tap the dedicated fire button.

Work your way through 10 increasingly difficult levels. If you conquer them all, you'll unlock the ultimate challenge: Infinity Mode, where you can play endlessly to set an unbeatable high score.
