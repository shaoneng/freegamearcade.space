Nova Clicker

How to Play Nova Clicker

Welcome to Nova Clicker! Your goal is to become the ultimate star tycoon. Begin by clicking or tapping on FreezeNova, the smiley character at the center of your screen. Every click earns you stars, which are the main currency in the game. Consistent clicking, especially at the start, is key to building your cosmic empire quickly.

As you collect stars, look to the Nova Shop on the right side of the screen. Here, you can invest your stars into powerful tools and upgrades. Start with essentials like the nova atom booster, bombs, and asteroids. As you reach new star milestones, even more powerful items will unlock, so check the shop frequently to boost your star production.

Your star empire will grow by unlocking new Novas, each with a unique design and greater star-generating power. Discover characters like Police Nova, King Nova, and the funky Immortal Nova to significantly increase your clicking power. If you have a favorite character, you can set it as your default by clicking the t-shirt icon at the top of the screen.

Keep an eye out for Golden Novas that drift across your screen. Clicking these rare Novas will grant you a massive star bonus, often enough to afford a major upgrade instantly. For another boost, you can activate the x2 Speed Boost by watching a short ad. This doubles all your star generation for 60 seconds, perfect for when you're close to a big purchase.

As you progress, you'll unlock new cosmic backgrounds like the moon, mars, and the galaxy by clicking the cloud icon. When your progress starts to slow, it's time to 'ascend'. This prestige option resets your progress but grants you permanent bonuses and multipliers for your next playthrough, allowing you to reach even greater heights much faster.
