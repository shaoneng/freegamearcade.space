Nova Clicker

How to Play Nova Clicker

Your cosmic journey in Nova Clicker begins with a single click! Tap on your first character, the smiley FreezeNova, to generate your first stars. Stars are the main currency you'll use to expand your universal empire. At the start, consistent clicking is key to building up your initial star supply quickly.

On the right side of the screen, you'll find the Nova Shop. This is where you'll spend your stars to get more powerful. Invest in early upgrades like the nova atom booster, bombs, and asteroids to automate and increase your star production. As you hit new star milestones, more potent upgrades will unlock, so check the shop regularly!

As you progress, you'll unlock a variety of new Novas, each with a unique look and star-generating ability. Discover characters like Police Nova, King Nova, and Baby Nova to boost your clicking power. If you have a favorite, you can easily switch back to it by clicking the t-shirt icon at the top of the screen.

Keep an eye out for Golden Novas that drift quickly across the screen! Clicking these provides a huge burst of stars, often enough for a major upgrade. For an extra edge, activate the x2 Speed Boost by watching a short ad. This doubles your star generation for 60 seconds, perfect for pushing towards your next big unlock.

Your adventure will take you to new locations, from the Moon and Mars to deep space. You can change your background by clicking the cloud icon. When your progress starts to slow down, it might be time to Ascend! This prestige feature resets your progress but grants you permanent multipliers, making each new playthrough faster and more powerful.
