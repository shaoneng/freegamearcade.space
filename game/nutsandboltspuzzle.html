Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

How to Play Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

The objective in Nuts and Bolts Puzzle is simple yet challenging: remove all the wooden planks from the board. To do this, you must strategically unscrew the bolts holding them in place and move them to empty holes.

Use your mouse to point and click (or tap on mobile devices) on a bolt to select it. Then, click on an empty, available hole to screw it in there. A bolt can only be moved to an unoccupied hole.

Think a few moves ahead! Before you unscrew a bolt, look at the board and plan your sequence. Removing a bolt might cause a plank to fall and block an essential hole you needed later. The key is to find the right order of removal to clear the path for all planks to drop.

Each level is a race against the clock, adding a layer of excitement. If you get stuck or are running low on time, look for special power-ups at the bottom of the screen. These can provide helpful boosts, like an extra hole or a time freeze, to help you conquer the trickiest puzzles.
