Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

How to Play Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

Welcome to Nuts and Bolts Puzzle! Your goal is simple but challenging: clear all the wooden planks from the board. To do this, you must strategically unscrew the bolts holding them in place and move them to empty holes.

The controls are intuitive. Simply click or tap on a bolt to select it, and then click or tap on an empty hole to screw it in there. Once a plank is no longer supported by any bolts, it will fall away from the board.

Think before you move! Before unscrewing, take a look at the layout. Removing the wrong bolt might cause a plank to fall in a way that blocks other important bolts or holes. Planning a few steps ahead is the key to success and will help you avoid getting stuck.

Feel the pressure with a ticking clock that adds a layer of excitement to each level. If you find yourself in a tight spot, keep an eye out for special power-ups like extra holes or a timer freeze to help you out. Master the art of chain reactions by removing a single bolt that holds multiple planks to clear the board faster!
