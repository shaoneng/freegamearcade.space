Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

How to Play Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

Welcome to the workshop! Your goal in Nuts and Bolts Puzzle is simple yet tricky: remove all the wooden planks from the board. To do this, you must strategically unscrew the bolts holding them in place.

The controls are intuitive. Simply point-and-click (or tap) on a bolt to select it. Once selected, click on an empty, available hole to move the bolt there. When a plank is no longer held by any bolts, it will fall away from the board. Clear all the planks to win the level!

Strategy is key. Before making a move, scan the board to understand how the planks overlap and which bolts are critical. Removing a bolt might cause a plank to fall and block an essential empty hole, so try to plan a few moves ahead. Creating a chain reaction by removing a bolt that supports multiple planks can be a very efficient way to clear the board.

Keep an eye on the clock! A timer adds an exciting layer of pressure to the puzzle. If you get stuck or are running out of time, look for special tools at the bottom of the screen. These power-ups, like an extra hole generator, can be lifesavers in a tight spot.
