Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

How to Play Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

Welcome to Nuts and Bolts Puzzle, where your goal is to clear the board by strategically removing all the wooden planks. The gameplay is simple to learn but challenging to master. Use your mouse to click (or tap on mobile) to select a bolt you wish to unscrew.

Once a bolt is selected, you must move it to an empty screw hole on the board. The key is to plan your moves carefully. Removing bolts in the wrong order can cause a plank to fall and block a critical hole you need later, trapping you. Think a few steps ahead to create a clear path for victory!

Each level is a race against the clock, adding an exciting layer of urgency. Don't spend too much time pondering your next move. If you get stuck, look for special power-ups at the bottom of the screen, like an extra hole generator or a timer freeze, to help you out of a tight spot.

A clever strategy is to identify and remove bolts that support multiple planks at once. This can trigger a satisfying chain reaction, clearing a large portion of the board in a single move. Just make sure the falling planks don't obstruct other important bolts! Jump in and start solving!
