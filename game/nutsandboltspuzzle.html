Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

How to Play Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

The goal in Nuts and Bolts Puzzle is simple yet challenging: remove all the wooden planks from the board. You'll do this by strategically unscrewing bolts and moving them to empty holes. It's a game of logic, planning, and sometimes, a race against the clock!

Use your mouse to point and click (or tap on your screen) to interact with the bolts. Click a bolt once to select it, then click an empty hole to move it there. Once a plank is no longer supported by any bolts, it will fall away.

Think before you move! Look at the puzzle and identify which bolts are holding which planks. Removing a key bolt can cause a chain reaction, but be careful not to block access to other important bolts or holes. Planning a few steps ahead is the key to success in more complex levels.

As the levels get trickier, you'll have access to helpful tools. Keep an eye out for special power-ups like extra hole generators or timer freezes. Use them wisely when you're in a tight spot to clear the board and claim victory!
