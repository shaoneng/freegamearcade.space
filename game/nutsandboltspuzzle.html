Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

How to Play Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

Welcome to the workshop! In Nuts and Bolts Puzzle, your objective is simple yet challenging: clear all the wooden planks from the board. To do this, you'll need to strategically unscrew bolts and move them to empty holes. Use your mouse to point and click (or tap on your screen) to select a bolt you want to unscrew, and then click on an empty hole to move it there. When a plank is no longer supported by any bolts, it will satisfyingly fall away.

This isn't just a random clicking game; it requires strategy! Before making a move, take a moment to analyze the board. Removing the wrong bolt too early might cause a plank to fall in a way that blocks access to other crucial bolts or empty holes. Try to plan your moves a few steps ahead to create a clear path and avoid trapping yourself.

A ticking clock adds a layer of excitement and urgency to each level. You'll need to think fast and act decisively to clear the board before time expires. If you find yourself running low on seconds, don't panic! Sometimes a quick, bold move is better than no move at all. Keep an eye on the timer and manage your time wisely.

When you're in a tight spot, look for special power-ups at the bottom of the screen. These tools can be game-changers, offering lifelines like an extra hole generator or a timer freeze. Use them strategically to overcome difficult puzzles, especially when a stubborn plank is in your way or you're about to run out of time.
