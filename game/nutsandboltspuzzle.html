Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

How to Play Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

The objective in Nuts and Bolts Puzzle is to clear all the wooden planks from the board. To do this, you must strategically unscrew the bolts holding them in place and move them to empty screw holes. A level is won only when every plank has successfully fallen off the board.

Use your mouse to point-and-click or your finger to tap. First, select a bolt you want to unscrew. Then, select an empty hole to screw it into. It's crucial to plan your moves carefully. Before starting, scan the board to see which bolts support key planks. Removing the wrong bolt could cause a plank to drop in a way that blocks other essential holes, trapping you.

A timer adds a sense of urgency to each level, so you'll need to be both strategic and quick. If you get stuck, look for special tools at the bottom of the screen. These lifelines, such as an extra hole generator or a timer freeze, can help you escape a tricky situation and conquer the puzzle.
