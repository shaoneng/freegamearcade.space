Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

How to Play Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

The goal in Nuts and Bolts Puzzle is simple: remove every wooden plank from the board. To do this, you must strategically unscrew the bolts holding them in place and move them to empty slots.

Use your mouse to point-and-click (or tap on a touchscreen). First, select a bolt you wish to unscrew. Next, click on an empty hole to move the selected bolt there. This frees up the plank it was holding, and if no other bolts are securing it, the plank will fall away.

Think before you act! Each level is a new puzzle. Look at the arrangement of planks and bolts. Removing a bolt in the wrong order can cause a plank to drop in a way that blocks a crucial empty hole, trapping you. Try to plan your moves a few steps ahead to clear the board efficiently.

A ticking clock adds to the excitement and pressure. You can't take forever, so be decisive. If you get stuck, look for special power-ups at the bottom of the screen, such as an extra hole generator or a timer freeze. Use these lifelines wisely to solve the toughest puzzles!
