Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

How to Play Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

Your goal in Nuts and Bolts Puzzle is simple yet challenging: remove all the wooden planks from the board. To do this, you'll need to unscrew the bolts holding them in place. Use your mouse to point and click (or tap on your screen) to select a bolt you want to unscrew.

Once you select a bolt, you must move it to an empty, available hole on the board. This is where the puzzle begins! You need to carefully plan the order in which you unscrew and relocate bolts. Removing a bolt in the wrong order might cause a plank to fall and block a crucial empty hole you needed for a later move.

Think a few steps ahead to avoid getting stuck. The game gets more intense with a timer counting down, so you'll need to balance careful planning with quick decisions. If you're in a tough spot, look for special power-ups at the bottom of the screen, which can give you an extra hole or freeze the timer to help you out.

A great strategy is to look for key bolts that are holding multiple planks at once. Removing one of these can create a satisfying chain reaction, clearing a large portion of the board in a single move. Now, get ready to test your puzzle-solving skills and have fun taking it all apart!
