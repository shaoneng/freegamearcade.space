Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

How to Play Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

Welcome to Nuts and Bolts Puzzle! The objective is simple yet challenging: remove all the wooden planks from the board. To do this, you must strategically unscrew bolts and move them to empty holes. With each successful move, you'll clear the way for more planks to fall, bringing you closer to solving the puzzle.

Use your mouse to point and click (or tap on mobile) to select a bolt you wish to unscrew. Then, click on an empty hole to move it. Before you make a move, take a moment to look at the layout. Removing a key bolt too early might cause a plank to block other crucial spots. Planning a few steps ahead is the key to success in this tricky puzzle.

Many levels are timed, adding an exciting layer of urgency to your puzzle-solving. Keep an eye on the clock and make decisive moves. If you get stuck, look for special power-ups like extra holes or a timer freeze to help you out. Sometimes, targeting a single bolt that holds multiple planks can create a satisfying chain reaction and clear the board faster!
