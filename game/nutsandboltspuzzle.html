Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

How to Play Nuts and Bolts Puzzle

Your goal in Nuts and Bolts Puzzle is simple yet challenging: remove all the wooden planks from the board. To do this, you must strategically unscrew and relocate the bolts holding them in place. Each level presents a new arrangement, requiring careful thought and planning.

The controls are intuitive. Simply point and click (or tap on mobile devices) to select a bolt you wish to unscrew. Once selected, click on an empty screw hole to move the bolt there. Remember, every bolt you remove must be placed in a vacant spot, so managing your available space is key to success.

Before making a move, take a moment to analyze the board. Identify which bolts are holding multiple planks and plan a few steps ahead. Removing the wrong bolt can cause a plank to fall and block access to other important holes. Watch the timer, as it adds a layer of urgency to your puzzle-solving. If you get stuck, look for special power-ups at the bottom of the screen, which can provide extra holes or freeze the clock to give you a much-needed advantage.
