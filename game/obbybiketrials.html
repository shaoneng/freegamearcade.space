Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

Obby Bike Trials is an exhilarating parkour bike game where you navigate challenging courses filled with stunts and obstacles. Your goal is to reach the finish line without falling off the floating platforms.

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: W, A, S, D (or Arrow Keys in 1 Player game mode)
  • Jump: SPACE
  • Run: Left Shift
  • Camera: Move mouse + Right-click
  • Zoom: Mouse scroll

Player 2 Controls (in 2 Player mode):

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Jump: L
  • Run: K

Game Modes:

  • Normal Mode: Ride at your own pace with no timer, focusing on pure enjoyment and exploration.
  • Challenge Mode: Race against the clock to beat your best time and conquer each level.
  • 2 Player Mode: Compete head-to-head with a friend on the same screen to prove who has the superior bike parkour skills.

Customization:

Personalize your character to stand out! Choose from various facial expressions, mix and match clothing colors, select fun hats, add stylish glasses, and even equip unique wings for a magical flair. As you progress, you can unlock and purchase new bikes with fresh designs and awesome wheels to ride in style!
