Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

Welcome to Obby Bike Trials! Your mission is to master your bike and navigate through treacherous obstacle courses suspended high in the sky. Jump between floating platforms, speed up giant ramps, and skillfully avoid an array of traps to reach the end of each level.

Player 1 Controls: Use W, A, S, D to move (or the Arrow Keys if playing alone), press the Spacebar to jump, and hold Left Shift to run. You can control the camera by moving your mouse while holding the right-click button, and zoom in or out with the mouse scroll wheel.

Player 2 Controls: In 2 Player mode, the second player uses the Arrow Keys to move, the L key to jump, and the K key to run.

The game features three exciting modes! In Normal Mode, you can ride at your own pace. In Challenge Mode, you'll race against the clock. And in 2 Player Mode, you can compete against a friend on the same screen to see who has the best bike skills!
