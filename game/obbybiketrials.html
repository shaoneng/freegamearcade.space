Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

Welcome to Obby Bike Trials, the ultimate test of your bike parkour skills! The goal is to ride your bike across treacherous floating platforms, overcome obstacles, and reach the finish line without falling. Master the controls to conquer every level.

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: W, A, S, D (or Arrow Keys in 1 Player mode)
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Run/Boost: Left Shift
  • Camera Control: Move the mouse while holding the right-click button
  • Zoom: Use the mouse scroll wheel

Player 2 Controls (in 2 Player Mode):

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Jump: L key
  • Run/Boost: K key

Choose from three exciting game modes: Normal Mode lets you ride at your own pace, Challenge Mode pits you against the clock, and 2 Player Mode lets you race a friend on a split screen. Don't forget to customize your character's look and unlock new bikes to ride in style!
