Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

Your goal in Obby Bike Trials is to navigate treacherous sky-high obstacle courses on your bike. Test your balance and precision as you ride across floating platforms, jump over wide gaps, climb giant ramps, and dodge moving traps to reach the finish line safely.

Player 1 / Single Player Controls:

  • Move: W, A, S, D or Arrow Keys
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Run/Sprint: Left Shift
  • Camera Control: Move Mouse + Right-Click
  • Zoom: Mouse Scroll Wheel

Player 2 Controls (in 2 Player Mode):

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Jump: L key
  • Run/Sprint: K key

The game features multiple modes for endless fun. In Normal Mode, you can take your time and master the courses at your own pace. If you're looking for a thrill, switch to Challenge Mode to race against the clock. For competitive action, grab a friend for 2 Player Mode and race head-to-head on the same screen to see who has the best bike parkour skills!
