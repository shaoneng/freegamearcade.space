Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

Ready to jump, race, and roll through crazy floating platforms? Obby Bike Trials is the ultimate parkour bike game where every level is packed with wild stunts, tricky traps, and exciting surprises. Ride your bike across floating platforms, climb giant ramps, jump gaps, and dodge moving obstacles, all while trying not to fall off!

Controls

Player 1:

  • Move: W, A, S, D (or Arrow Keys in 1P mode)
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Run: Left Shift

Player 2:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Jump: L Key
  • Run: K Key

Camera: Move the mouse while holding Right-click to change your view. Use the mouse scroll to zoom in and out.

Customization

Personalize your character to look awesome while riding! You can customize facial expressions, clothing colors, hats, glasses, and even add magical wings. You can also unlock and buy brand-new bikes with fresh designs to show off your style!
