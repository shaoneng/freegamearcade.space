Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

Welcome to Obby Bike Trials, the ultimate test of your bike parkour skills! Your goal is to navigate your bike through a series of challenging obstacle courses built on floating platforms in the sky. Ride carefully, maintain your balance, and use your momentum to jump across gaps, climb ramps, and avoid moving traps. Reach the finish line of each level to prove your mastery!

The game offers several ways to play:

  • Normal Mode: Explore the levels at your own pace without any time pressure. It's the perfect way to practice and enjoy the ride.
  • Challenge Mode: Race against the clock! Push your skills to the limit and try to reach the finish line before time runs out.
  • 2 Player Mode: Grab a friend and compete head-to-head on a split screen. See who can conquer the course first!

Controls:

Player 1

  • Move: W, A, S, D Keys
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Run/Boost: Left Shift
  • Camera: Move Mouse + Right-click
  • Zoom: Mouse Scroll

Player 2 (in 2 Player Mode)

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Jump: L Key
  • Run/Boost: K Key

Don't forget to customize your character and unlock new bikes to show off your unique style as you soar through the skies!
