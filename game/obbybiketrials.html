Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

Your goal in Obby Bike Trials is to ride your bike through challenging obstacle courses set in the sky without falling off. Master the controls to jump gaps, climb giant ramps, and dodge tricky traps to reach the finish line of each level.

Player 1 Controls:
- Move: W, A, S, D (or Arrow Keys in 1 Player mode)
- Jump: SPACE
- Run: Left Shift
- Camera: Move mouse + Right-click

Player 2 Controls (in 2 Player Mode):
- Move: Arrow Keys
- Jump: L
- Run: K

Choose your preferred way to play! In Normal Mode, explore levels at your own pace with no time limit. For a more intense experience, select Challenge Mode and race against the clock. Or, grab a friend for 2 Player Mode and compete on a split-screen to see who is the ultimate bike parkour champion!
