Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

Welcome to Obby Bike Trials! Your mission is to navigate your bike through a series of wild, floating obstacle courses. Ride across platforms, jump over gaps, and dodge tricky traps to reach the finish line without falling off. The levels start easy but will quickly test your balance and reflexes.

Controls (1 Player Mode):
- Move: W, A, S, D or Arrow Keys
- Jump: Spacebar
- Run/Sprint: Left Shift
- Camera Control: Move the mouse while holding the right-click button
- Zoom: Use the mouse scroll wheel

Controls (2 Player Mode):
Player 1:
- Move: W, A, S, D
- Jump: Spacebar
- Run/Sprint: Left Shift
Player 2:
- Move: Arrow Keys
- Jump: L Key
- Run/Sprint: K Key

Choose your preferred game mode! In Normal Mode, you can explore the levels at your own pace. For a real test, try Challenge Mode and race against the clock. Or, grab a friend and go head-to-head in 2 Player Mode to see who is the ultimate bike parkour champion!

Don't forget to personalize your experience! You can customize your character's facial expression, clothing, and accessories like hats, glasses, and even wings. You can also unlock and purchase new bikes to show off your unique style.
