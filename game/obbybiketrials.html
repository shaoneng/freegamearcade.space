Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

Welcome to the ultimate parkour bike challenge! In Obby Bike Trials, your goal is to ride your bike through incredible obstacle courses set in the sky. Navigate across floating platforms, climb huge ramps, and dodge tricky traps without falling off. Each level increases in difficulty, testing your balance, timing, and skill.

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: W, A, S, D (or Arrow Keys in 1 Player mode)
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Run/Sprint: Left Shift
  • Camera Control: Move Mouse + Right-Click
  • Zoom: Mouse Scroll

Player 2 Controls (in 2-Player Mode):

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Jump: L Key
  • Run/Sprint: K Key

Choose your preferred game mode to start the fun. Normal Mode lets you explore the levels at your own pace without a timer. Challenge Mode pits you against the clock for an extra thrill. And in 2-Player Mode, you can race a friend on the same screen to see who is the true bike trials champion!

Don't forget to customize your character and unlock new bikes to ride in style!
