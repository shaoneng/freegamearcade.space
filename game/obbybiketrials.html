Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

Welcome to Obby Bike Trials! Your objective is to guide your rider through challenging courses built from floating platforms in the sky. You'll need to master your balance, jump over wide gaps, navigate around moving traps, and climb steep ramps to reach the finish line of each level.

Controls

Player 1:

  • Move: W, A, S, D (or Arrow Keys in 1 Player mode)
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Run/Sprint: Left Shift
  • Camera: Move mouse + Right-click
  • Zoom: Mouse scroll

Player 2:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Jump: L Key
  • Run/Sprint: K Key

Game Modes & Customization

Choose from three exciting game modes: Normal Mode for a relaxed, untimed experience; Challenge Mode to race against the clock; or 2-Player Mode to compete with a friend on the same screen! You can also personalize your character with different faces, clothes, and accessories, and unlock new bikes to ride in style.
