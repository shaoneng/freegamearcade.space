Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

Your goal in Obby Bike Trials is to ride your bike through challenging obstacle courses set on floating platforms. You'll need to jump over gaps, dodge moving traps, and climb steep ramps, all while maintaining your balance to avoid falling off.

For single-player mode, use the W, A, S, D or Arrow Keys to move your bike. Press the SPACE bar to jump and hold Left Shift to run. You can control the camera by moving the mouse while holding the right-click button, and zoom in or out using the mouse scroll wheel.

When playing with a friend in 2-Player mode, the controls are split. Player 1 uses W, A, S, D for movement, SPACE to jump, and Left Shift to run. Player 2 uses the Arrow Keys to move, the L key to jump, and the K key to run.

Choose from three exciting game modes! Normal Mode lets you explore levels at your own pace without a timer. Challenge Mode adds a clock, pushing you to race to the finish line as fast as possible. 2-Player Mode allows you to compete against a friend on a split-screen to see who has the best bike parkour skills.
