Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

Welcome to the ultimate bike parkour challenge! In Obby Bike Trials, your goal is to ride your bike through increasingly difficult obstacle courses made of floating platforms. You'll need balance, precision, and quick reflexes to jump over gaps, climb ramps, and dodge moving traps without falling.

Player 1 Controls:
Use W, A, S, D keys to move your character. In single-player mode, you can also use the Arrow Keys.
Press the SPACE bar to jump.
Hold Left Shift to run.
Control the camera by moving your mouse while holding the Right-click button. Use the mouse scroll to zoom in and out.

Player 2 Controls (in 2 Player Mode):
Use the Arrow Keys to move.
Press the L key to jump.
Hold the K key to run.

Choose from three exciting game modes! Normal Mode lets you explore the levels at your own pace. Challenge Mode adds a timer for an extra thrill. And in 2 Player Mode, you can race against a friend on the same screen to see who is the true bike trials champion!

Don't forget to personalize your experience! You can customize your character's facial expression, clothing, and accessories. You can also unlock and purchase new bikes with the points you earn.
