Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

The goal in Obby Bike Trials is to ride your bike through challenging obstacle courses set high in the sky. You'll need to maintain your balance, time your jumps perfectly, and navigate tricky moving platforms to reach the finish line without falling off!

Master the controls to become a parkour pro. The controls vary depending on the number of players:

Player 1: Use W, A, S, D (or Arrow Keys in 1-player mode) to move your bike. Press the SPACE bar to jump and hold Left Shift to get a speed boost. You can control the camera by moving your mouse while holding the right-click button.

Player 2: Use the Arrow Keys to move. Press L to jump and K to run for a speed boost.

Choose from different game modes to suit your style. In Normal Mode, explore the levels at your own pace. In Challenge Mode, race against the clock to prove your speed. For a competitive experience, grab a friend and battle it out in 2 Player Mode on a split screen! Don't forget to customize your character and unlock new bikes to ride in style.
