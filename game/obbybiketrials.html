Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

Welcome to Obby Bike Trials, the ultimate parkour bike challenge in the sky! Your goal is to ride your bike across floating platforms, navigate giant ramps, jump over gaps, and dodge tricky obstacles to reach the finish line without falling.

The game offers several exciting modes to test your skills. In Normal Mode, you can explore the levels at your own pace without any time pressure. For a thrilling race against time, switch to Challenge Mode and push for the fastest completion. And for some friendly competition, grab a friend and battle it out in the split-screen 2 Player Mode!

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: W, A, S, D (or Arrow Keys in 1 Player mode)
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Run/Sprint: Left Shift
  • Camera Control: Move mouse + Right-click
  • Zoom: Mouse scroll

Player 2 Controls (in 2 Player Mode):

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Jump: L key
  • Run/Sprint: K key

Don't forget to customize your rider! You can change facial expressions, clothing, and add cool accessories like hats and wings. As you progress, you can also unlock and buy new bikes with unique designs to show off your style.
