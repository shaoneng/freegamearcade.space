Obby Bike Trials

How to Play Obby Bike Trials

Welcome to Obby Bike Trials, where your goal is to master extreme parkour on a bike! Ride your way through challenging levels filled with floating platforms, massive ramps, tricky gaps, and moving obstacles. Your objective is to reach the end of each course without falling off.

The controls are simple to learn but hard to master. For Player 1, use W, A, S, D to move, SPACE to jump, and Left Shift to run. To control the camera, move your mouse while holding the right-click button, and use the mouse scroll to zoom in and out. In single-player mode, you can also use the Arrow Keys to move.

If you're playing with a friend in 2 Player Mode, Player 2 uses the Arrow Keys to move, the L key to jump, and the K key to run.

Choose your preferred way to play! Normal Mode lets you explore and practice at your own pace without any time pressure. For a real test of skill, jump into Challenge Mode and race against the clock to reach the finish line. Or, go head-to-head with a friend in 2 Player Mode on a split screen to see who is the ultimate bike parkour champion!

Don't forget to personalize your experience! You can customize your character's look with different facial expressions, clothing, hats, and even wings. As you progress, you'll also be able to unlock and buy new bikes to show off your style.
