Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Your goal in Obby But You're on a Bike is to navigate your character through challenging 3D obstacle courses, all while riding a bike. Master the controls to conquer ramps, loops, and tricky parkour sections.

Each of the 8 worlds is filled with hundreds of flags. Try to collect as many as you can to prove your skill. As you progress, you can upgrade your bike and unlock powerful boosters to help you on your journey.

Be strategic and ride carefully! You are given only 8 lives for each level. If you fall off the platform, you lose a life. If you lose all 8 lives, you'll have to restart the level from the very beginning. Good luck!
