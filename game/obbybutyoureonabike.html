Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Welcome to the thrilling world of Obby But You're on a Bike! The goal is simple but challenging: guide your character on a bike through a series of increasingly difficult obstacle courses.

Use your controls to steer, accelerate, and brake as you navigate a variety of 3D platforms. You'll encounter everything from steep ramps and dizzying loops to narrow pathways that demand precise bike handling. Your main objective is to reach the end of each level without falling off the course.

Along the way, keep an eye out for flags to collect. There are hundreds scattered across 8 different worlds, adding an extra layer of challenge and replayability. Be warned: you only have 8 lives for each level. If you fall, you lose a life, and if you run out of all 8, you'll have to start the level over from the beginning. Spend your collected rewards to upgrade your bike and unlock special boosters to help you conquer the toughest stages!
