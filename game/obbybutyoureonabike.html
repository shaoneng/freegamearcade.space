Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

In Obby But You're on a Bike, your goal is to navigate a series of challenging 3D obstacle courses while riding your bike. Control your Roblox character to ride through ramps, loops, and intricate parkour sections.

Explore 8 distinct worlds, each filled with hundreds of flags to collect. Your progress relies on skillful maneuvering, as falling off the platform will cost you a life. You are granted 8 lives per level; if you lose them all, you will restart the current level from the beginning.

Collect flags to earn rewards, and use them to upgrade your bike or unlock powerful boosters to help you overcome the most difficult obstacles. Master the controls, manage your lives, and prove you're the ultimate Obby bike master!
