Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Your goal in Obby But You're on a Bike is to ride your bike through challenging 3D obstacle courses. Navigate tricky ramps, daring loops, and tight parkour sections to reach the end of each level.

Use your controls to steer your bike, accelerate, and maintain balance. Your primary objective is to collect flags scattered throughout the 8 different worlds while staying on the platform.

You are given 8 lives for each level. Be extremely careful, as falling off the course will cost you a life. If you lose all 8 lives, you will have to restart the level from the very beginning. Look for opportunities to upgrade your bike and unlock special boosters to help you conquer the toughest challenges!
