Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Get ready for a wild ride in Obby But You're on a Bike! In this unique 3D platformer, you'll guide your character on a bicycle through challenging obstacle courses filled with ramps, loops, and tricky parkour sections.

The main goal is to navigate through 8 different worlds, collecting hundreds of flags along the way to mark your progress. Be careful, though! You only have 8 lives for each level. If you fall off a platform, you lose a life, and if you run out of lives, you'll have to restart the level from the very beginning. Use your skills to maintain balance and speed.

As you progress, you can upgrade your bike and unlock special boosters to help you conquer the toughest courses. Can you prove you're the ultimate Obby master on two wheels? Start your cycling adventure now!

Obby But You're on a Bike is created by GingerPlay. If you love this kind of challenge, be sure to check out Only Up Obby! for another thrilling parkour experience.
