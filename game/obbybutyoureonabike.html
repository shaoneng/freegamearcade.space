Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Your goal is to guide your character on a bike through a series of challenging obstacle courses, known as 'obbies'. Navigate through ramps, loops, and narrow platforms to reach the end of each level.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to control your bike's movement—accelerate, brake, and steer left or right. Balance is key, so make careful adjustments to avoid falling off the platforms.

Each of the 8 worlds is filled with flags to collect. The main challenge is that you only have 8 lives for each level. If you fall, you lose a life. Lose all 8, and you'll have to restart the level from the very beginning. Look for opportunities to upgrade your bike and unlock boosters to help you overcome the toughest sections.
