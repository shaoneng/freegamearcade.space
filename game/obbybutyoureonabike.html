Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Get ready for a wild ride in Obby But You're on a Bike! In this unique 3D platformer, you'll guide your character through insane obstacle courses, all while balancing on a bike. Navigate treacherous ramps, daring loops, and tricky parkour sections across 8 distinct worlds.

Your main goal is to collect hundreds of flags scattered throughout each level. But be careful—you only have 8 lives per level! Every time you fall off the course, you lose a life. If you run out of lives, you'll have to start the level all over again. Master the controls to keep your balance and push forward.

As you progress, you can upgrade your bike and unlock powerful boosters to help you conquer the toughest challenges. Prove your skills and become the ultimate obby master on two wheels!

Obby But You're on a Bike is created by GingerPlay. If you enjoy this type of challenge, be sure to check out Only Up Obby! for another thrilling parkour adventure.
