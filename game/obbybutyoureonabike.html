Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Welcome to the thrilling world of Obby But You're on a Bike! Your goal is to guide your character on a bicycle through a series of challenging 3D obstacle courses. Navigate treacherous ramps, dizzying loops, and narrow parkour paths to reach the end of each level.

As you ride through 8 distinct worlds, keep an eye out for flags to collect. The main challenge is to stay on the platform. You start each level with only 8 lives. If you fall off the course, you lose a life. Use up all your lives, and you'll have to restart the level from the very beginning, so ride carefully!

To help you on your journey, you can upgrade your bike and unlock special boosters. These will improve your performance and help you conquer even the toughest stages. Master the controls, collect all the flags, and prove you have what it takes to be an Obby bike champion!
