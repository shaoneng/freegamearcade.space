Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Your objective in Obby But You're on a Bike is to guide your Roblox-style character through a series of wild 3D obstacle courses. Instead of running, you'll be riding a bike, adding a fun and challenging twist to the classic obby formula.

Navigate your bike through ramps, loops, and narrow platforms across 8 different worlds. Your main goal is to reach the end of each level while collecting as many flags as you can along the way. Be mindful of your balance and speed to avoid tumbling into the void!

You start each level with a limited set of 8 lives. Every time you fall off the course, you lose one life. If you run out of all your lives, you'll have to restart the level from the very beginning. As you progress, you can earn upgrades for your bike and unlock special boosters to help you overcome the most difficult parkour challenges. Good luck, and happy riding!
