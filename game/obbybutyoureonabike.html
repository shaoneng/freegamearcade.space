Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Welcome to the high-flying, wheel-spinning challenge of Obby But You're on a Bike! Your goal is to navigate your character on a bicycle through a series of increasingly difficult 3D obstacle courses. Master your balance and control as you ride over narrow platforms, zoom through loops, and launch off massive ramps.

Use your keyboard controls to steer your bike and maintain momentum. The objective is to reach the end of each level while collecting as many flags as you can. These courses are spread across 8 unique worlds, each offering a new set of challenges to conquer.

Be warned: this isn't a leisurely ride in the park! You are given only 8 lives for each level. If you fall off the course, you'll lose a life and respawn at the last checkpoint. If you lose all 8 lives, you must restart the entire level from the beginning. Spend your collected flags to upgrade your bike and unlock boosters to help you on your journey to becoming the ultimate bike obby champion!
