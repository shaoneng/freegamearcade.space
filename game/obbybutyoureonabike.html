Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

The goal in Obby But You're on a Bike is to guide your character on a bicycle through a series of challenging 3D obstacle courses. Your mission is to reach the end of each level without falling off the platforms.

Use your controls to steer your bike, accelerate, and brake. Navigate through tricky ramps, tight corners, and wild loops. As you progress, collect the flags scattered throughout the 8 different worlds to mark your progress and score points.

Be strategic with your movements, as you only have 8 lives for each level. If you fall off the course, you lose a life. Lose all your lives, and you'll have to restart the level from the very beginning. Look for opportunities to upgrade your bike and unlock special boosters to help you overcome the toughest obstacles and become the ultimate bike obby champion!
