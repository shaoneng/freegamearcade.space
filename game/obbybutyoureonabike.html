Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Welcome to the thrilling world of Obby But You're on a Bike! Your main goal is to navigate your character on a bicycle through a series of challenging 3D obstacle courses. Ride across ramps, speed through loops, and carefully balance on narrow platforms to reach the end of each level.

As you ride, keep an eye out for flags to collect. The real challenge is to stay on the course! You are given only eight lives for each level. If you fall off a platform, you lose one life. If you lose all eight lives, you'll have to restart the level from the very beginning, so ride carefully!

To help you on your journey, you can earn upgrades for your bike and unlock special boosters. These will help you tackle the tougher courses and improve your chances of success. Master the controls, collect all the flags, and prove you're the ultimate bike Obby champion!
