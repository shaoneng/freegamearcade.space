Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Get ready for a wild ride! In this 3D platformer, you control your character on a bike, navigating through insane obstacle courses. The goal is to traverse ramps, loops, and challenging parkour sections to collect hundreds of flags spread across 8 different worlds.

You have to be careful, as you only have 8 lives per level. If you fall off the platform, you lose a life. Use up all your lives, and you'll have to restart the level from the very beginning. As you progress, you can upgrade your bike and unlock special boosters to help you conquer the toughest courses and prove you are the ultimate Obby master!
