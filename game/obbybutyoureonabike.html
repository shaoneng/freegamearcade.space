Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Your goal is to ride your bike through challenging 3D obstacle courses, known as 'obbies', without falling off. Use your keyboard controls to steer your bike, accelerate, and brake. Navigate carefully through narrow paths, up steep ramps, and around crazy loops to reach the finish line.

Each of the 8 unique worlds is filled with flags to collect. Try to grab as many as you can! You start each level with 8 lives. If you fall off the course, you lose a life. If you run out of all 8 lives, you'll have to restart the level from the beginning.

As you progress, you can earn upgrades for your bike and unlock boosters to help you conquer even the toughest sections. Master the controls, collect the flags, and prove you're the ultimate Obby rider!
