Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Get ready for a unique twist on the classic obby genre! In Obby But You're on a Bike, your goal is to navigate your character through elaborate 3D obstacle courses, all while riding a bike. Master your control over the bike to conquer ramps, loops, and narrow parkour paths across 8 different worlds.

As you ride, keep an eye out for hundreds of flags scattered throughout each level. Your primary mission is to collect these flags to progress. However, the challenge is steep! You are given only 8 lives for each level. If you fall off a platform, you lose one life. If you lose all 8 lives, you'll have to restart the current level from the very beginning.

To gain an edge, you can upgrade your bike and unlock special boosters that will help you tackle the tougher sections. Use your skills, manage your lives carefully, and prove you have what it takes to complete every world in this exciting bike adventure!
