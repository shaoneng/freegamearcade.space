Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Welcome to the wild world of Obby But You're on a Bike! Your mission is to navigate your character on a bicycle through a series of challenging 3D obstacle courses. Use your keyboard or on-screen controls to steer, accelerate, and maintain balance as you tackle steep ramps, dizzying loops, and narrow platforms.

As you race through each of the 8 unique worlds, keep an eye out for flags to collect. The main challenge? You only have 8 lives to complete each level. If you fall off the course, you lose a life. Lose all 8, and you'll have to start that level over from the beginning. Spend your earnings to upgrade your bike and unlock boosters to help you conquer the toughest parkour sections and become the ultimate bike obby champion!
