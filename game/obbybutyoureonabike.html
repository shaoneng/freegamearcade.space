Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Jump on your bike and get ready for a wild ride in Obby But You're on a Bike! In this exciting 3D platformer, you'll guide your Roblox character through challenging obstacle courses filled with ramps, loops, and tricky parkour sections. Your goal is to navigate these crazy tracks and collect hundreds of flags scattered across 8 unique worlds.

Be careful on your journey! You only have 8 lives for each level. If you fall off a platform, you'll lose a life. Use up all your lives, and you'll have to start that level all over again. Precision and control are key to conquering each course.

As you progress, you can upgrade your bike to improve its performance and unlock powerful boosters to help you overcome the toughest obstacles. Prove your skills and become the ultimate Obby master on two wheels! This game is created by GingerPlay.

If you enjoyed this epic bike adventure, try Only Up Obby! where climbing to the top is the only way to go!
