Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Obby But You're on a Bike! The goal is simple but challenging: hop on your bike and navigate through intricate 3D obstacle courses. Your objective is to reach the end of each level without falling off the platforms.

Use your keyboard to control the bike's movement. Steer left and right to guide your character through narrow paths, up ramps, and around loops. On mobile devices, use the on-screen controls to navigate. Keep your balance and anticipate the twists and turns ahead to stay on course.

You have a limited number of lives—only 8 per level! Each time you fall off the track, you'll lose one life and respawn at the last checkpoint. If you use all 8 lives, you'll have to restart the level from the very beginning. Be strategic and ride carefully!

As you ride, keep an eye out for flags to collect. You can also upgrade your bike and unlock powerful boosters to help you conquer the toughest challenges. Prove you have what it takes to master every obby and become the ultimate bike rider!
