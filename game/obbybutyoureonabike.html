Obby But You're on a Bike

How to Play Obby But You're on a Bike

Get ready for a unique challenge in Obby But You're on a Bike! In this exciting 3D platformer, you'll guide your Roblox character through wild obstacle courses, not on foot, but on two wheels. Navigate treacherous ramps, daring loops, and tricky parkour sections to reach the finish line.

Your main goal is to collect hundreds of flags scattered across 8 distinct and adventurous worlds. But be careful—you only have 8 lives for each level. If you fall off the course, you lose a life, and losing all of them means you'll have to start the level over. Use your collected rewards to upgrade your bike and unlock powerful boosters to help you conquer the toughest stages.

Obby But You're on a Bike is created by GingerPlay. If you love the thrill of this game, be sure to check out Only Up Obby! for another challenging parkour adventure!
