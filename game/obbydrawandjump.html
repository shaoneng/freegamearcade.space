Obby: Draw and Jump

How to Play Obby: Draw and Jump

Your mission is to guide your character through a series of challenging obstacle courses. Use your mouse or finger to draw paths, bridges, and platforms to help your character navigate the level and reach the finish line.

Your drawing power comes from a magic pen with a limited ink supply. Keep an eye on your ink meter! As you draw, the ink depletes. To get more ink, you must erase some of your previous drawings. This strategic element requires you to think ahead and manage your resources wisely.

While you focus on drawing, don't forget to move your character using the standard controls (WASD or on-screen joystick). You'll need to skillfully navigate your drawn paths while dodging deadly obstacles like spikes, swinging wrecking balls, and even enemies chasing you.

You're not alone on the course! Compete against other online players in real-time. Use your drawing skills not only to survive but to outpace your rivals and be the first one to cross the finish line.
