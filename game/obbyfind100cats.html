Obby: Find 100 Cats

How to Play Obby: Find 100 Cats

In Obby: Find 100 Cats, your primary mission is to locate and rescue 100 cats that have been scattered and hidden across ten unique and colorful 3D maps. A glitch in the cosmic Cat Distribution System has left these felines stranded, and it's up to you to find them all.

You'll need to explore each map thoroughly. The cats can be anywhere—stuck in trees, perched on rooftops, or tucked away in cleverly concealed spots. Use your observation skills and platforming abilities to scour the environment, solve simple puzzles, and reach every last one.

Navigate your character using standard keyboard controls. Use the W, A, S, and D keys to move around, and press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles. To look around and change your view, simply move your mouse. Collect all 100 cats to complete the game and restore order to the universe!
