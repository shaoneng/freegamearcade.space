How to Play Obby: Find 100 Cats

Welcome to the delightful world of Obby: Find 100 Cats! Your mission is a noble one: the universe's Cat Distribution System has malfunctioned, leaving 100 adorable cats scattered and lost across the world. It's up to you to find every single one of them and help them on their way to finding their forever homes.

The game is a 3D scavenger hunt spread across ten uniquely themed maps. You will need to explore every nook and cranny of these environments to locate the hidden felines. Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate your character through the world. Simply walk up to a cat once you've spotted it to collect it. Keep track of your progress as you try to find all 100. Complete the challenge and become the hero these kitties need!
