How to Play Obby: Find 100 Cats

Welcome to Obby: Find 100 Cats! The cosmic Cat Distribution System has malfunctioned, and it's up to you to set things right. Your main goal is to explore ten unique, vibrant 3D maps and locate all 100 lost cats.

Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the environments. Standard controls like W, A, S, D keys are used for movement, while your mouse controls the camera to look around. Explore every nook and cranny, as the cats are cleverly hidden in all sorts of placesâ€”stuck in trees, perched on rooftops, and concealed within buildings.

Each map presents a new puzzle and a new set of adorable felines to rescue. Keep an eye out for any meows or unusual shapes. Once you find a cat, simply walk up to it to collect it. Track your progress and work your way through all ten levels to complete your mission and restore order to the universe!
