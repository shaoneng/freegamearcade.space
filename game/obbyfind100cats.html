Obby: Find 100 Cats

How to Play Obby: Find 100 Cats

Your mission in Obby: Find 100 Cats is to locate and rescue all 100 lost felines that are spread across ten distinct and colorful maps. The magical Cat Distribution System has failed, and it's up to you to set things right!

Navigate the 3D world using your standard controls. Use the WASD keys to move your character forward, backward, and side-to-side. Use the Spacebar to jump, which is essential for overcoming obstacles and reaching high-up places where cats might be hiding.

Each of the ten maps is a unique puzzle. Search thoroughly in every area, from the tops of buildings to hidden rooms and tricky corners. Some cats are easy to spot, but others are cleverly concealed and will require some platforming skill to get to. Simply walk up to a cat to collect it.

Your ultimate goal is to find every single one of the 100 cats. Keep track of your progress as you play and ensure no kitty is left behind. Once you've rescued them all, you will have restored balance to the universe's feline distribution system!
