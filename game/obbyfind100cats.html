Obby: Find 100 Cats

How to Play Obby: Find 100 Cats

Your main objective in "Obby: Find 100 Cats" is to locate and rescue 100 felines that have been misplaced due to a glitch in the cosmic Cat Distribution System. These adorable cats are not just hiding; they are stranded in tricky spots across ten unique and vibrant maps.

Explore each 3D map thoroughly. The cats could be anywhere—stuck in trees, trapped on rooftops, or hidden inside rooms. You'll need to jump, climb, and navigate through the "obby" (obstacle course) style levels to reach every nook and cranny.

Keep your eyes peeled and your wits sharp! Each map presents its own set of challenges and clever concealments. Finding all 100 cats is the only way to restore order and help them find their true homes. The great cat hunt is on—good luck!
