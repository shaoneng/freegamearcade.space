Obby: Find 100 Cats

How to Play Obby: Find 100 Cats

Your mission in Obby: Find 100 Cats is simple yet challenging: locate all 100 lost cats hidden across ten distinct and colorful maps. The universe's Cat Distribution System has glitched, and these poor kitties are stuck in all sorts of strange places. It's your job to explore thoroughly and rescue every single one.

Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the 3D environments. Use the W, A, S, D keys to move your character forward, backward, and side-to-side. Use the mouse to look around and change your camera angle. When you need to reach higher places, press the Spacebar to jump.

Keep your eyes peeled! The cats are cleverly concealed. Look behind objects, on top of buildings, inside rooms, and even up in trees. Each map is a puzzle, so explore every inch to find all the hidden felines. Once you've found all 100, you'll have successfully fixed the cosmic glitch!
