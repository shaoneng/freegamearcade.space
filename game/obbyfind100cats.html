How to Play Obby: Find 100 Cats

Your mission in Obby: Find 100 Cats is to locate and rescue 100 lost felines! Due to a glitch in the "Cat Distribution System," these cats are stranded in the most peculiar places across ten vibrant and unique maps.

Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the 3D world:

  • WASD Keys: Move your character forward, backward, left, and right.
  • Mouse: Look around the environment to spot hidden cats.
  • Space Bar: Jump to reach higher platforms and tricky spots.
