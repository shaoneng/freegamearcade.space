Obby: Find 100 Cats

How to Play Obby: Find 100 Cats

Your mission in Obby: Find 100 Cats is to locate and rescue all 100 felines scattered across ten unique and colorful maps. The cosmic Cat Distribution System has malfunctioned, and it's up to you to set things right!

Navigate through each 3D world using your keyboard and mouse:

  • WASD Keys: Move your character forward, backward, left, and right.
  • Spacebar: Jump to reach higher platforms and overcome obstacles.
  • Mouse: Move your mouse to look around and change your camera angle.

To rescue a cat, simply walk up to it. The game will automatically register that you've found it. Keep track of your progress as you hunt for every hidden kitty. Remember to explore thoroughly—cats can be hiding anywhere, from high on rooftops to tucked away in hidden corners. Good luck on your feline-finding adventure!
