Obby: Find 100 Cats

How to Play Obby: Find 100 Cats

Welcome to a delightful 3D adventure where the famous "Cat Distribution System" has gone haywire! Instead of finding loving homes, 100 adorable cats are lost and hidden across ten unique and colorful maps. Your mission is to locate and rescue every single one of them to restore cosmic order.

Use your mouse to look around and the WASD or arrow keys on your keyboard to move your character. The spacebar allows you to jump, which is essential for navigating the 'obby' (obstacle course) style levels and reaching high-up or tricky hiding spots.
