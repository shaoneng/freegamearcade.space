How to Play Obby: Find 100 Cats

Welcome to Obby: Find 100 Cats! The universe's magical Cat Distribution System has malfunctioned, leaving 100 adorable felines stranded and lost. Your mission is to travel across ten unique and colorful maps to find and rescue every single one of them.
