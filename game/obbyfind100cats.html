Obby: Find 100 Cats

How to Play Obby: Find 100 Cats

In "Obby: Find 100 Cats," your primary mission is to explore ten unique, themed worlds to locate and rescue 100 hidden cats. The universe's "Cat Distribution System" has failed, leaving these poor kitties stranded in strange and hard-to-reach places.

Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate your character through each 3D map. This is a game of observation and exploration, so be sure to check everywhere! Look up on rooftops, behind buildings, inside rooms, and in trees. The cats can be cleverly concealed, blending into their environment.

Each level is a vibrant puzzle waiting to be solved. As you find each cat, you get one step closer to restoring order. You'll need sharp eyes and a persistent spirit to complete this grand scavenger hunt and become the ultimate cat rescuer!
