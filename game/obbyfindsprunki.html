Obby: Find Sprunki

How to Play Obby: Find Sprunki

Your main objective in Obby: Find Sprunki is to explore four unique maps and track down all 40 hidden Sprunks. Each map contains 10 of these quirky, and sometimes spooky, characters for you to discover.

Use your keyboard to run, jump, and climb through various obstacle courses. Navigate the terrain carefully to reach hidden areas. To collect a Sprunk, simply walk over it, and it will be added to your collection.

If you find yourself stuck on a particularly tricky part of the course, look to the left side of your screen. There, you'll find power-up icons that you can click to help you overcome the obstacle.

The ultimate goal is to find every single Sprunk and complete your collection. Explore every nook and cranny, from the cheerful zones to the eerie ones, and see if you can catch 'em all!
