Obby: Find Sprunki

How to Play Obby: Find Sprunki

Your primary goal in Obby: Find Sprunki is to embark on a grand scavenger hunt across a world that shifts from cheerful to eerie. The game features four distinct maps, and your mission is to explore each one thoroughly to locate and collect 10 hidden Sprunki characters per map, for a total of 40 unique finds.

Navigate the vibrant and sometimes spooky environments using standard computer controls. Use the WASD keys to move your character forward, backward, left, and right. Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and climb obstacles. Use your mouse to look around and guide your direction as you traverse the challenging parkour courses.

To collect a Sprunki, simply spot it and walk over it. It will automatically be added to your collection. The maps are filled with obstacles, so your running and jumping skills will be put to the test. If you find yourself stuck on a particularly tricky section, don't forget to use the power-up icons on the left side of the screen for a helpful boost!
