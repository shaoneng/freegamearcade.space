Obby: Find Sprunki

How to Play Obby: Find Sprunki

Your mission in Obby: Find Sprunki is to explore a vast world and locate all 40 of the hidden Sprunki characters. The game is a classic "obby" (obstacle course) that will test your platforming skills.

Use your keyboard controls to run, jump, and climb through four distinct maps. Each map contains 10 unique Sprunki. To collect a Sprunki, simply spot it and walk over it. It will then be added to your collection.

Explore every corner of the maps, from the bright and cheerful areas to the dark and mysterious zones. If you find yourself stuck on a particularly tricky part of the course, look for the power-up icons on the left side of your screen for a helpful boost. Your goal is to find them all and complete your collection!
