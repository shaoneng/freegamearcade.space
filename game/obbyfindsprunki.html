Obby: Find Sprunki

How to Play Obby: Find Sprunki

Welcome to the world of Obby: Find Sprunki! Your main objective is to explore diverse maps and find all 40 of the hidden Sprunki characters. Each Sprunki has a unique look and personality, adding to the fun of discovery.

To find the Sprunkis, you'll need to master the obstacle course (or "obby"). Use your computer controls to run, jump, and climb your way through each level. Explore every corner, from the bright and cheerful areas to the darker, more mysterious zones.

The game features four different maps, and each one contains 10 hidden Sprunkis. To collect a Sprunki, simply spot it and walk your character over it. It will then be added to your collection. If you find yourself stuck on a tricky part of the course, look for the power-up icons on the left side of your screen for a helpful boost.
