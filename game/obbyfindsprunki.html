Obby: Find Sprunki

How to Play Obby: Find Sprunki

Your primary mission in Obby: Find Sprunki is to explore four unique maps and locate all 40 hidden Sprunki characters, with 10 cleverly placed on each map. These quirky beings range from cheerful to mysterious and are waiting to be discovered.

Use your keyboard to navigate the world. Master running, jumping, and climbing to overcome challenging platforming sections and obstacle courses. To collect a Sprunki, simply walk over it, and it will be added to your collection.

Each map has its own distinct theme and difficulty. If you get stuck on a tricky jump or obstacle, keep an eye out for power-up icons on the left side of your screen. Activating these can give you the extra help you need to progress. Explore every corner and complete your Sprunki collection!
