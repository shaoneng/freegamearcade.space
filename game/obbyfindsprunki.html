Obby: Find Sprunki

How to Play Obby: Find Sprunki

Your main objective in Obby: Find Sprunki is to explore four unique maps and find all 40 hidden Sprunks. Each map contains 10 of these quirky, and sometimes spooky, characters. To collect a Sprunk, simply spot it and walk over it to add it to your collection.

Navigate through each level by mastering the obstacle course, or "obby". Use your keyboard to control your character. Press the WASD keys to move forward, backward, left, and right. Use the Spacebar to jump over gaps and climb obstacles. The mouse controls your camera view, allowing you to look around and spot hidden Sprunks.

If you find yourself stuck on a particularly tricky part of the course, don't worry! Look for the power-up icons on the left side of your screen. Clicking these can give you the boost you need to overcome the challenge. Explore every corner, from the bright and cheerful zones to the darker, more mysterious areas, and complete your Sprunki collection!
