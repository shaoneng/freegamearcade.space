Obby: Find Sprunki

How to Play Obby: Find Sprunki

Your adventure in Obby: Find Sprunki is a grand scavenger hunt! The main goal is to explore four distinct maps and find all 40 hidden Sprunki characters, with 10 cleverly placed in each one. These quirky creatures range from cheerful to mysteriously eerie, making each discovery a unique experience.

Navigate the world using your keyboard and mouse. Use the WASD keys to move your character forward, backward, and side-to-side. Press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles and reach new platforms. Use your mouse to look around and spot the hidden Sprunks. To collect a Sprunki, simply walk over it, and it will be added to your collection.

Each map is designed as a challenging obstacle course, or 'obby'. You'll need to run, jump, and climb your way through. If you find yourself stuck, don't forget to check the power-up icons on the left side of your screen for a helpful boost. Explore every corner, from the bright, happy zones to the darker, more mysterious areas, to complete your collection!
