Obby: Parkour Lava

How to Play Obby: Parkour Lava

The goal in Obby: Parkour Lava is to guide your avatar through a challenging obstacle course, or "obby," before you're consumed by the rising lava below. The entire platform is slowly sinking, so speed and precision are key. You must jump and climb your way to the higher parts of the parkour to survive!

Mastering the controls is essential for your escape:

  • On Desktop: Use the WASD keys or Arrow keys to move, and press the Spacebar to jump. Use your mouse to control the camera and look around.
  • On Mobile Devices: Use the on-screen joystick to control your movement and the dedicated button to jump. Swipe the screen to adjust your view.

Think fast, move faster, and beat the heat to conquer the course!
