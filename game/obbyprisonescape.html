Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

Your mission in Obby Prison Escape is to guide the prisoner Noob on a daring breakout! The prison is full of intricate parkour courses (obbies) that you must conquer. Luckily, the guard on duty, Barry, is notoriously clumsy and easily distracted, giving you the perfect opportunity to make your move.

Navigate through each level by running, jumping, and sliding past a variety of obstacles. Be careful not to fall, or you'll be sent back to the last checkpoint. The controls are simple and intuitive:

For PC: Use the WASD keys to move and the Spacebar to jump.

For Mobile: Use the on-screen joystick to move and the jump button to leap over gaps.

As you progress, be sure to collect coins scattered throughout the levels. You can use these coins to customize Noob's appearance, transforming him from a plain prisoner into a stylish escape artist. Master the obby, avoid Barry's gaze, and secure your freedom!
