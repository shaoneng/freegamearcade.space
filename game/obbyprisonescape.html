Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

Your mission in Obby Prison Escape is to help the prisoner, Noob, break out of a supposedly high-security jail. The good news? The guard on duty, Barry, is hilariously inept, giving you the perfect opportunity to make a run for it! Navigate through a series of challenging and funny parkour levels, also known as 'obbies', to secure your freedom.

For PC Players: Use the WASD keys to move and the Spacebar to jump. Use your mouse to look around and guide your direction. For Mobile Players: Use the on-screen joystick to move and the dedicated jump button to clear obstacles.

As you successfully traverse the prison's obstacles, be sure to collect coins scattered throughout the levels. You can use these coins to customize Noob's appearance, upgrading him from a basic 'noob' to a stylish escape artist. Jump, slide, and dodge your way past every trap and outsmart Barry to win!
