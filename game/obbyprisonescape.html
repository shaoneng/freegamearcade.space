Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

Your mission in Obby Prison Escape is to guide the character, Noob, through a series of challenging parkour obstacle courses to break out of jail. The prison is under the not-so-watchful eye of Barry, a clumsy guard who makes your escape a bit easier—and a lot more fun!

Navigate through each level by running, jumping, and sliding past various traps and obstacles. The goal is to reach the end of each stage without falling or getting caught. Use your agility and timing to overcome every challenge the prison throws at you.

Controls:

  • PC: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to move, and the Spacebar to jump.
  • Mobile: Use the on-screen joystick to move and the dedicated button to jump.

As you progress, be sure to collect coins scattered throughout the levels. You can use these coins to unlock cool customizations for Noob, transforming his look from a plain prisoner to a stylish escape artist!
