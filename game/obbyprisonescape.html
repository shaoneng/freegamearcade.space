Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

Your mission is to guide the prisoner, Noob, through a series of challenging obstacle courses (obbies) to escape the prison. The bumbling guard, Barry, is on watch, but he's not very observant, giving you the perfect opportunity to make a break for it!

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to run and navigate the levels. Press the spacebar to jump over gaps, climb ledges, and avoid traps. On mobile devices, simply use the on-screen joystick to move and the dedicated jump button to leap over obstacles.

As you make your way through the prison, be sure to collect coins scattered throughout the levels. You can use these coins to unlock cool new looks and customize your character, transforming Noob from a generic prisoner into a stylish escape artist. Master the parkour, outsmart Barry, and earn your freedom!
