Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

Your mission in Obby Prison Escape is to guide Noob through a series of challenging and funny parkour obstacle courses to break out of jail. The guard, Barry, isn't the brightest, giving you the perfect chance to make a run for it! Navigate tricky jumps, slides, and other obstacles to reach the end of each level and get one step closer to freedom.

The controls are straightforward:
On PC: Use the WASD keys to move and the Spacebar to jump. Use your mouse to look around and guide your direction.
On Mobile: Use the on-screen joystick to move and the dedicated jump button to leap over obstacles.

As you master the courses, be sure to collect all the coins you can find. You can use these coins to customize Noob's appearance and transform him from a basic prisoner into a stylish escape artist. Good luck!
