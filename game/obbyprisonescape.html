Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

Your mission is simple: guide the prisoner, Noob, to freedom! Take advantage of the bumbling guard, Barry, being on duty to make your escape. Navigate through a series of challenging and hilarious parkour obstacle courses. Jump, slide, and dodge your way through each level to progress.

Use your keyboard and mouse on PC, or the on-screen controls on mobile, to control Noob. The goal is to reach the end of each obby without falling. Along the way, be sure to collect coins. These coins can be used to customize Noob's avatar, so you can escape in style! Pay attention to the platforms, time your jumps perfectly, and outsmart the prison's layout to win your freedom.
