Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

Your mission in Obby Prison Escape is to guide your character, Noob, through a series of challenging obstacle courses to break out of prison. You'll need sharp reflexes and precise timing to overcome every jump, slide, and trap in your path. The clumsy guard, Barry, is on duty, giving you the perfect opportunity to make a run for it!

For PC players: Use the WASD keys to move your character and press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles and across gaps.

For mobile players: Use the on-screen joystick to control your movement and tap the jump button to leap.

As you navigate the prison's obby, be sure to collect coins scattered throughout the levels. You can use these coins to customize Noob's appearance and make your escape in style. Watch out for tricky platforms, lasers, and other hazards designed to stop you. Good luck, and enjoy the escape!
