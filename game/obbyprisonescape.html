Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

Your mission is to help the prisoner Noob break out of a high-security jail guarded by the world's most careless guard, Barry! This is your chance to make a run for it through a series of exciting and challenging parkour levels, also known as an "obby."

Use your keyboard controls (WASD or Arrow Keys) to move Noob around the environment. Press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles, across platforms, and away from danger. On mobile devices, simply use the on-screen joystick for movement and the dedicated button to jump.

Navigate the intricate prison layout by jumping, sliding, and carefully maneuvering through each stage. As you progress, be sure to collect coins scattered throughout the levels. You can use these coins to customize Noob's appearance and make your escape artist truly unique!
