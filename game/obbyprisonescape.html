Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

Your mission in Obby Prison Escape is to guide the prisoner, Noob, to freedom! The prison is full of intricate parkour courses, and you'll need sharp reflexes to overcome them. The good news is that the guard on duty, Barry, is notoriously clumsy and easily distracted.

Use your controls to run, jump, and slide through each level. Carefully time your movements to avoid falling and navigate the tricky obstacles. The game is an 'obby' (obstacle course), so precision is key to progressing through the prison's layers of security.

As you explore, be sure to collect all the coins you can find. These coins can be used to unlock awesome customization options for Noob, transforming his look from a standard prisoner to a stylish escape artist. Can you master the parkour and lead Noob out of Barry's Prison?
