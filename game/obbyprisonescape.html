Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

Your mission in Obby Prison Escape is to guide the prisoner, Noob, to freedom! The prison's security is unexpectedly light today, as the bumbling guard Barry is on duty. This is your chance to make a run for it.

Navigate through a series of challenging and fun parkour levels, or 'obbies'. You'll need to run, jump, and slide your way through the prison's intricate layout, avoiding traps and overcoming obstacles to reach the exit of each stage.

As you dash through the levels, keep an eye out for coins. You can use these collected coins to unlock cool customizations for your character, transforming Noob's look. Master the parkour, outwit Barry, and execute the perfect prison escape!
