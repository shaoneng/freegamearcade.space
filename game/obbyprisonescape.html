Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

Welcome to the ultimate prison break! In Obby Prison Escape, your goal is to help your character, Noob, navigate a series of challenging parkour obstacle courses to escape the clutches of the hilariously clumsy guard, Barry. You'll need to run, jump, and slide your way through a gauntlet of traps and platforms to achieve freedom.

PC Controls:

  • Move: W, A, S, D keys
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Look/Aim: Mouse

Mobile Controls:

  • Move: Use the on-screen joystick.
  • Jump: Tap the on-screen jump button.

Don't forget to collect coins scattered throughout the levels! You can use these coins to customize your character's appearance and make your escape in style.
