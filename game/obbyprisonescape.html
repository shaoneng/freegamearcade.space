Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

Your mission in Obby Prison Escape is to guide the plucky prisoner, Noob, on a daring breakout! The prison is a maze of challenging parkour obstacle courses, but you have a secret weapon: the guard on duty, Barry, is hilariously inept. Seize this opportunity to navigate through a series of levels filled with jumps, slides, and tricky platforms to secure your freedom.

Use your keyboard to control Noob. The WASD keys or the Arrow keys will move you forward, backward, and side-to-side. Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles. On mobile devices, use the on-screen joystick to move and the jump button to leap.

As you make your way through the prison, be sure to collect all the coins you can find. These coins can be used to customize Noob's appearance, so you can escape in style. Good luck, and don't get caught by Barry!
