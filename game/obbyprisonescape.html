Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

Your mission in Obby Prison Escape is to guide the prisoner, Noob, through a series of challenging parkour levels and break out of jail! The clumsy guard, Barry, is on watch, giving you the perfect opportunity to make a run for it.

Controls for PC: Use the WASD keys for movement and the Spacebar to jump over obstacles. Use your mouse to control the camera and look around.

Controls for Mobile: Use the on-screen joystick to move your character and tap the jump button to leap over gaps and hazards.

Navigate the intricate obstacle courses (obbies) by carefully timing your jumps, slides, and sprints. As you explore, collect coins scattered throughout the prison to unlock cool new customizations for your character. Help Noob escape and avoid getting caught by Barry!
