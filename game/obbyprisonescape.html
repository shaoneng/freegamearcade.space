Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

In Obby Prison Escape, your mission is to guide Noob through a series of challenging parkour obstacles to escape Barry's prison. The game is designed for both PC and mobile devices.

For PC players: Use the W, A, S, D keys to move Noob. Press the spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles. You'll need precise movements to navigate the tricky prison layout.

For Mobile players: Use the on-screen joystick or touch controls to move and jump. The controls are intuitive and designed for a seamless mobile experience.

As you progress, collect coins scattered throughout the levels. These coins can be used to customize Noob's avatar, giving him a unique look and helping him shed his "noob" image!

Master your jumps, slides, and dodges to outsmart Barry and make your grand escape!
