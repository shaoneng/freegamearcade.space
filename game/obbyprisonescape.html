Obby Prison Escape

How to Play Obby Prison Escape

Your mission in Obby Prison Escape is to guide the prisoner, Noob, to freedom by navigating a series of challenging parkour obstacle courses (obbies). The guard on duty, Barry, isn't the sharpest, giving you the perfect chance to escape!

Use your keyboard or on-screen controls to run, jump, and slide through each level. Be careful to avoid traps and tricky jumps that could send you back to the start. Along your escape route, collect coins scattered throughout the prison. You can use these coins to customize your character's appearance and make Noob look like a true escape artist. Master the controls and outsmart Barry to complete your daring prison break!
