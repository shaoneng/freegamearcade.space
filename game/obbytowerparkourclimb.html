Obby Tower Parkour Climb

How to Play Obby Tower Parkour Climb

To begin your parkour adventure, start the game and choose between playing in single-player mode or challenge a friend in the exciting two-player split-screen mode.

Next, select one of the three unique maps available: Tunnel, Tower, or Stairs. Each map offers a distinct set of obstacles and climbing challenges.

For an added twist, on each map, you can choose between 'Normal' mode for a standard climbing experience or 'Challenge' mode for a more demanding and intense parkour run. Your primary goal is to navigate through various colorful platforms and intricate obstacle courses, utilizing your jumping and climbing skills to reach the very top of the tower or the end of the course.

Master your timing and precision to avoid falling and successfully conquer every platform on your way to victory!
