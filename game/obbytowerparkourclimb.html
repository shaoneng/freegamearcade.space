Obby Tower Parkour Climb

How to Play Obby Tower Parkour Climb

Your goal in Obby Tower Parkour Climb is simple: reach the very top! Navigate through vibrant, blocky worlds by jumping from one platform to the next, avoiding obstacles along the way. Master your timing and precision to conquer the climb.

The game offers multiple ways to play. You can tackle the challenge solo or team up with a friend in the exciting two-player split-screen mode. Race against each other or help one another to the finish line. Choose from three distinct maps—Tunnel, Tower, and Stairs—each with its own unique layout and challenges.

For an extra test of skill, each map features two modes: Normal and Challenge. Normal mode provides a standard parkour experience, while Challenge mode ramps up the difficulty for seasoned players. Use the WASD or Arrow keys to move and press the Spacebar to jump.
