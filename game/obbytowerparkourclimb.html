Obby Tower Parkour Climb

How to Play Obby Tower Parkour Climb

Welcome to Obby Tower Parkour Climb! Your goal is simple: reach the top of the tower by navigating a series of colorful platforms and challenging obstacle courses. Use your parkour skills to jump, climb, and race your way to victory.

Controls for Player 1: Use the WASD keys to move your character and press the Spacebar to jump. Look around using your mouse to plan your next move.

The game features three distinct maps to conquer: Tunnel, Tower, and Stairs. Each map can be played in two modes: Normal Mode for a standard climb, and Challenge Mode for an extra layer of difficulty. Test your limits and try to beat your own best time!

Want to challenge a friend? Select the two-player mode for an exciting split-screen race to the top! Player 2 uses the Arrow keys to move and the right Shift key to jump. See who can conquer the obby tower first!
