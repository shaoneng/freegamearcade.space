Obby Tower Parkour Climb

How to Play Obby Tower Parkour Climb

Welcome to Obby Tower Parkour Climb, a dynamic parkour game with a fun, blocky aesthetic! Your objective is to climb to the very top of massive structures by jumping across colorful platforms and navigating challenging obstacle courses. Precision and timing are key to successfully ascending without falling.

The game features three distinct maps to conquer: Tunnel, Tower, and Stairs. You can tackle these challenges in two ways. Play in Single Player mode to master the courses at your own pace, or invite a friend and go head-to-head in the exciting two-player split-screen mode to see who can reach the summit first!

For an added layer of challenge, each map offers a Normal mode and a Challenge mode. Normal mode provides a standard parkour experience, perfect for learning the layouts. When you're ready to test your limits, switch to Challenge mode for a tougher, more intense climb. Good luck, and don't look down!
