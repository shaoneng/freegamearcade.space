Obby Tower Parkour Climb

How to Play Obby Tower Parkour Climb

Welcome to Obby Tower Parkour Climb! Your main objective is to ascend to the very top of massive structures by jumping across colorful platforms and navigating tricky obstacle courses. Use your parkour skills to time your jumps perfectly and avoid falling.

The game features three unique maps to conquer: the winding Tunnel, the classic vertical Tower, and the challenging Stairs. You can tackle these climbs on your own in Single Player Mode to set personal records, or you can bring a friend along for the fun in the split-screen Two Player Mode for an epic race to the finish line!

To add another layer of excitement, each map offers two different difficulties. Normal Mode provides a standard, fun challenge, while Challenge Mode ramps up the difficulty for those seeking a true test of their parkour prowess. Choose your map, pick your mode, and start your climb!
