How to Play Obby Tower Parkour Climb

Welcome to Obby Tower Parkour Climb! Your goal is simple but challenging: climb to the very top of massive obstacle courses. Master your jumps and timing to navigate through three distinct and colorful mapsâ€”Tunnel, Tower, and Stairs. Each level presents a unique set of platforms and hurdles designed to test your parkour skills.

One of the best features of this game is the ability to play how you want. You can take on the challenge alone in Single Player mode, perfecting your runs and setting personal bests. Or, grab a friend and compete head-to-head in the exciting two-player split-screen mode to see who can reach the summit first!

Each map also offers two distinct difficulty levels. Start with 'Normal' mode to get a feel for the course, or if you're feeling confident, jump straight into 'Challenge' mode for a much tougher and more intense climbing experience. Use your keyboard to control your character: WASD keys for movement and the Spacebar to jump.
