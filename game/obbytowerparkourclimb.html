Obby Tower Parkour Climb

How to Play Obby Tower Parkour Climb

Your goal in Obby Tower Parkour Climb is to ascend to the very top of massive, colorful structures. Navigate through a series of platforms, jumps, and tricky obstacle courses to reach the finish line. The game offers three distinct maps to conquer: the winding Tunnel, the towering Tower, and the spiraling Stairs.

You can enjoy the game in two ways. In Single Player mode, you can take your time to master each jump and learn the layout of the maps. For a more competitive experience, grab a friend and dive into the Two Player split-screen mode to race each other to the top!

Each map also features two difficulty settings. Normal mode offers a standard parkour experience, perfect for learning the ropes. When you're ready for a true test of skill, switch to Challenge mode for a more intense and demanding climb. Master the controls, time your jumps perfectly, and don't look down!

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use WASD to move and press the Spacebar to jump.
  • Player 2: Use the Arrow Keys to move and press M to jump.
