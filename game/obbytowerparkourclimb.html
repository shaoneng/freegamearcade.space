Obby Tower Parkour Climb

How to Play Obby Tower Parkour Climb

Welcome to Obby Tower Parkour Climb! Your goal is simple: climb to the top of massive, colorful structures by navigating a series of challenging obstacle courses. Use your parkour skills to jump between platforms, avoid hazards, and race against the clock.

Controls:
- Use WASD or the Arrow Keys to move your character.
- Press the Spacebar to jump.

The game features three distinct maps to conquer: Tunnel, Tower, and Stairs. Each map offers unique layouts and challenges. You can tackle these maps in two different modes:

  • Single Player Mode: Play on your own to master the courses and set new personal best times.
  • Two Player Mode: Challenge a friend in a thrilling split-screen race to see who can reach the top first!

Furthermore, each map has a Normal Mode for a standard parkour experience and a more difficult Challenge Mode for seasoned players seeking a true test of their skills. Choose your mode, pick your map, and start climbing!
