Obby Tower Parkour Climb

How to Play Obby Tower Parkour Climb

Welcome to Obby Tower Parkour Climb! Your goal is simple: reach the top of the tower by jumping and navigating through a series of colorful platforms and tricky obstacles. Master your timing and precision to avoid falling and set a new record.

This game offers multiple ways to play:

  • Game Modes: Choose between Normal mode for a standard climbing experience or Challenge mode for a tougher, more intense ascent.
  • Maps: Test your skills across three distinct maps: Tunnel, Tower, and the dizzying Stairs.
  • Single Player: Play alone to practice your parkour skills and conquer the tower at your own pace.
  • Two Player: Challenge a friend in an exciting split-screen race to the top! See who can conquer the course first.

Use your keyboard's movement keys (typically WASD or Arrow Keys) to run and the spacebar to jump. In two-player mode, the controls will be split between players. Good luck, and don't look down!
