Obby Tower Parkour Climb

How to Play Obby Tower Parkour Climb

Welcome to Obby Tower Parkour Climb! Your goal is simple: ascend to the very top of massive, colorful structures. Inspired by popular Roblox parkour games (known as "obbies"), this game tests your timing, precision, and jumping skills across various obstacle courses.

Game Modes:

  • Single Player: Take on the challenge alone and perfect your run. Learn the layouts of the three unique maps—Tunnel, Tower, and Stairs—at your own pace.
  • Two Player: Challenge a friend in an exciting split-screen race to the top! See who has the better parkour skills.

Difficulty Levels:

  • Normal Mode: A standard experience perfect for learning the maps and enjoying the climb.
  • Challenge Mode: For experienced players, this mode adds extra difficulty and trickier jumps to test your limits.

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use WASD keys to move and the Spacebar to jump.
  • Player 2: Use the Arrow keys to move and the Right Shift key to jump.
