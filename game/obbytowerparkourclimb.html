Obby Tower Parkour Climb

How to Play Obby Tower Parkour Climb

Your goal in Obby Tower Parkour Climb is to ascend to the very top of massive structures by jumping across a series of colorful platforms and navigating tricky obstacle courses. Precision and timing are key to avoiding falls and reaching the summit.

The game offers fantastic flexibility with its modes. You can tackle the climb solo in Single Player mode, or race against a friend in the competitive two-player split-screen mode. Each of the three maps—Tunnel, Tower, and Stairs—also features a 'Normal' mode for a standard climb and a 'Challenge' mode for an extra layer of difficulty.

Use the WASD keys to move your character and the Spacebar to jump. In two-player mode, one player will use WASD + Space, while the other uses the Arrow keys and another key for jumping. Look carefully before you leap and master your jumps to conquer every level!
