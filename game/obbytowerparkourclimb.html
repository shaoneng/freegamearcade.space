Obby Tower Parkour Climb

How to Play Obby Tower Parkour Climb

In Obby Tower Parkour Climb, your goal is to ascend to the very top of towering structures. You'll need to run, jump, and navigate through a series of colorful platforms and tricky obstacles. Each level presents a new parkour challenge, testing your timing and precision.

The game offers fantastic flexibility with its game modes. You can embark on a solo adventure or challenge a friend in the two-player split-screen mode for a head-to-head race to the summit. Additionally, each of the three maps (Tunnel, Tower, and Stairs) comes with two difficulty settings: a 'Normal' mode to learn the ropes and a 'Challenge' mode to push your parkour skills to the limit.

The controls are intuitive and easy to master. Use the WASD keys to move your character and the Spacebar to jump. For two-player mode, one player will use WASD and Space, while the second player uses the Arrow Keys and the Right Shift key to jump. Navigate carefully, as one wrong step could send you tumbling down!
