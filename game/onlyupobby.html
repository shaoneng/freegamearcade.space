Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Welcome to the sky-high challenge of Only Up Obby! Your goal is to guide your character through a series of intense obstacle courses, known as "obbies," competing against other players to be the best. The fundamental rule? Don't look down!

Controls: Use the WASD keys on your keyboard to move your character forward, backward, left, and right. Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles.

The game features several thrilling mini-game modes, each with its own unique twist:

  • Colorful Floor: Race across a grid of tiles. A color is displayed at the top of the screen—you must quickly jump onto a tile of that matching color before the others disappear.
  • Glass Bridge: A true test of nerve and luck. Choose between two glass tiles to jump on. One will hold your weight, while the other will shatter, sending you back to the start. Choose wisely to cross the bridge.
  • Falling Plates: Run across a platform where the plates you step on fall away after a moment. You can't hesitate! Keep moving forward to reach the finish line.
  • Raining Blocks: It's pure chaos! Dodge a constant barrage of falling blocks as you try to navigate the course and reach the end without getting crushed.
