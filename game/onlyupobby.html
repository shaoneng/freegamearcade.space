Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Welcome to the sky-high challenge of Only Up Obby! Your goal is to guide your character through a series of intense obstacle courses (obbies), competing against other players to be the first to reach the finish line. Use the WASD keys to move your character around and press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles.

The game features several exciting mini-games, each with its own unique twist. In Colorful Floor, you must quickly move to tiles matching the color displayed on screen to avoid falling. Test your nerve in Glass Bridge, where you must choose the correct glass panel to step on, as the wrong one will shatter beneath you. Or, race against time in Falling Plates, a mode where the platforms disappear right after you step off them. Don't look down, and keep climbing!
