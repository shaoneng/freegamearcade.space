Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Your goal in Only Up Obby is to climb as high as possible by overcoming various parkour challenges. Compete against other players to see who can reach the finish line first!

Use the WASD keys to move your character around the map. Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and onto platforms. The camera will follow your mouse movements, allowing you to look around and plan your next move.

The game features several exciting mini-games, each with a unique rule set:

  • Colorful Floor: Quickly step onto tiles that match the color shown on the screen. If you're on the wrong color when it changes, you fall!
  • Glass Bridge: Test your luck and memory. Choose the correct glass panel to jump on; the wrong one will shatter beneath your feet.
  • Falling Plates: The platforms you run on will disappear moments after you step off them. You must keep moving forward to avoid falling into the void.
  • Raining Blocks: Dodge and weave through a chaotic storm of falling blocks as you race to the end of the course.

Enter the different doors in the town square to start a mini-game. Be fast, be precise, and don't look down!
