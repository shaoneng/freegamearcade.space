Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Welcome to the sky-high challenge of Only Up Obby! Your goal is to navigate a series of treacherous obstacle courses, competing against other players to be the first to the top. Use the WASD keys to move your character and the Spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles. From the starting town square, you can enter different buildings to access various mini-games.

Colorful Floor: Pay close attention to the color displayed at the top of your screen. You must quickly move to a tile of the matching color before it changes. Step on the wrong color, and you're out!

Glass Bridge: A test of nerve and luck. You'll face two paths of glass panels. One will hold your weight, but the other will shatter on impact. Choose your path wisely to cross successfully without falling into the void.

Falling Plates: In this fast-paced challenge, the platforms you run across will fall away just a second after you step off them. You can't hesitate! Keep moving forward to reach the finish line.

Raining Blocks: It's pure chaos! Dodge a relentless downpour of massive blocks while trying to complete the course. Your agility and timing are crucial to avoid getting crushed and reach the end.
