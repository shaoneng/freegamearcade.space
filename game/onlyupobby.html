Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Use the WASD keys to move your character and press the Spacebar to jump. Your goal is to navigate a series of challenging obstacle courses, or 'obbies', climbing ever higher to outmaneuver other players and finish first. Start in the town square and enter different buildings to access various mini-games.

Each mini-game offers a unique challenge. In Colorful Floor, you must quickly step on tiles that match the color displayed on screen. In Glass Bridge, carefully choose your path as some tiles will break beneath you. Falling Plates requires you to keep moving forward, as the platforms disappear behind you. Finally, test your reflexes in a mode where you must dodge falling blocks to reach the finish line. Good luck, and don't look down!
