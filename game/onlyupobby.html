Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Welcome to Only Up Obby, a thrilling multiplayer parkour game where the only direction is up! Your goal is to outmaneuver 29 other players across a variety of challenging obstacle courses, or 'obbies', set high in the sky. Use your keyboard to guide your character: the WASD keys to move and the Spacebar to jump. Start in the town square, choose a mini-game by entering a door, and begin your ascent!

Each mini-game offers a unique challenge. In Colorful Floor, you must quickly step on tiles that match the color displayed on screen before time runs out. In Glass Bridge, you'll face a test of luck and memory, choosing between two glass panes—one holds, and the other shatters. Other modes include Falling Plates, where you must race across a path that crumbles behind you, and It's Raining Blocks, where you must dodge falling obstacles to reach the finish line.

Your objective across all modes is to finish the race first, collect coins, and prove you're the ultimate parkour master. Be quick, be smart, and whatever you do, don't look down! Good luck!
