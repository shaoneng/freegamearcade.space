Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Welcome to the sky-high world of Only Up Obby! Your adventure begins in a town square where each building leads to a different mini-game. Choose a door and prepare to guide your blocky character through intense obstacle courses against 29 other players.

Controls:

  • Move: Use the WASD keys to run around the platforms.
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar to leap over gaps and obstacles.

The main goal is to navigate a variety of challenging "obby" paths, finish the race first, and collect coins. Be careful, because one wrong step can send you tumbling down! From colorful tiles to treacherous glass bridges, each mode offers a unique and thrilling challenge. Don't look down!
