Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Your objective is to guide your character through a series of challenging obstacle courses, or 'obbies', with the ultimate goal of climbing ever higher. Compete against 29 other players to see who can reach the finish line first or be the last one standing. Use the WASD keys to move your character around and press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles.

The game features several exciting mini-game modes, each offering a unique challenge. In Colorful Floor, you must quickly step on tiles that match the color displayed on screen. The Glass Bridge tests your luck and memory as you choose between solid and breakable panels. Falling Plates requires you to run across a path where platforms disappear behind you, while Raining Blocks has you dodging a constant barrage of obstacles from above. Each mode demands a different strategy to survive and win!

To begin, you'll find yourself in the town square. Each building represents a different mini-game. Simply walk through the door of the game you want to play and begin your sky-high adventure!
