Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Use the WASD keys to move your character around the vibrant world. When you need to clear a gap or reach a higher platform, press the Spacebar to jump. Your adventure begins in a central town square, where different buildings lead to unique mini-game challenges. Simply walk into a door to start a game!

Only Up Obby features several exciting mini-games. In 'Colorful Floor,' you must quickly step on tiles that match the color shown on the screen to avoid falling. 'Glass Bridge' tests your nerve and luck as you choose between two paths, where one tile is safe and the other will break. 'Falling Plates' is a race against time where platforms disappear behind you, and in 'Raining Blocks,' you must dodge a chaotic shower of blocks to reach the finish line. The goal in each mode is to outlast and outrun your opponents to claim victory!
