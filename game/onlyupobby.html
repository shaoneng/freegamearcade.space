Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Welcome to the vertical challenge of Only Up Obby! Your main goal is to climb as high as you can by navigating a series of challenging obstacle courses, or "obbies". You'll be competing against 29 other players to see who can reach the top first.

Use the WASD keys on your keyboard to move your character and press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles. Your journey begins in a central town square, where different buildings lead to unique mini-game challenges.

Choose a door to enter a mini-game. You might find yourself on a Glass Bridge, where you must guess the safe path, or on a Colorful Floor, where you must quickly step on the correct color tile before it changes. Other challenges include Falling Plates that disappear after you step on them and avoiding a chaotic rain of blocks. Be quick, be precise, and whatever you do, don't look down!
