Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Your adventure in Only Up Obby begins in a central town square. The goal is simple: go up! Control your blocky character using the WASD keys for movement and the Spacebar to jump. Navigate your character through a series of challenging obstacle courses set high in the sky, competing against other players to reach the finish line first and collect coins.

Each building in the town square leads to a different mini-game with unique rules. Test your skills across various modes like Colorful Floor, where you must quickly step on tiles of the correct color, or the nerve-wracking Glass Bridge, where you must guess the safe path. You can also face challenges like Falling Plates and a chaotic mode where it's Raining Blocks. Choose a door, enter a challenge, and start your climb!
