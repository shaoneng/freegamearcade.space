Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Your adventure in Only Up Obby begins in a central town square. From here, you can enter various doors, each leading to a unique and challenging mini-game high in the sky. The main goal is to outlast and outmaneuver up to 29 other players in these thrilling obstacle courses.

Use the WASD keys on your keyboard to move your character and press the Spacebar to jump. Precise timing and quick reflexes are essential to navigate the treacherous paths and avoid falling.

Each mini-game offers a different test of skill. In Colorful Floor, you must rush to stand on tiles that match the color displayed on screen. In the nerve-wracking Glass Bridge, you must guess which panel will hold your weight. Other modes include Falling Plates, where the ground disappears behind you, and Raining Blocks, where you must dodge a constant barrage of obstacles. Finish first, collect coins, and prove you're the ultimate parkour master!
