Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

In Only Up Obby, your adventure begins in the town square. Each building represents a unique mini-game, inviting you to guide your blocky avatar on daring adventures high in the sky. Use the WASD keys to move your character and the Spacebar to jump. Your primary goal is to navigate various obstacle courses, finish first, and collect coins.

The game offers several exciting mini-game options with distinct mechanics:

  • Colorful Tiles: Race against 29 other players. You must step only on tiles matching the color displayed at the top of the screen. As soon as the color changes, quickly find another matching tile or risk being eliminated!
  • Glass Bridge: A test of nerve and intuition. Choose wisely which glass tile to jump on; one will hold, while the other will shatter, sending you into the void.
  • Falling Plates: This unlockable mini-game challenges you to run to the finish line on a platform up in the sky. The twist? Plates you've already run on will fall away after a second, demanding precise timing and quick thinking.
  • Raining Blocks: Become a block-dodging ninja! Complete the course while avoiding dynamic chaos as blocks rain down from above, threatening to crush you.

Each mode brings a mix of fun and daring challenges. Can you master them all and prove your parkour prowess?
