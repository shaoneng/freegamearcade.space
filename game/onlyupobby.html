Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Your adventure in Only Up Obby begins in a central town square. The goal is to climb as high as you can by conquering various obstacle courses, or "obbies." Use your keyboard to guide your character through these thrilling challenges against other players.

Controls:

  • Move: Use the WASD keys to run around.
  • Jump: Press the Spacebar to leap over gaps and obstacles.

Enter the different buildings in the square to access unique mini-games. Compete in modes like the Colorful Floor, where you must step on the correct color before it's too late. Test your nerve on the Glass Bridge by choosing the right path, or race against time on the Falling Plates. The ultimate objective is to outmaneuver your opponents, reach the finish line first, and prove your parkour skills. Don't look down, and keep climbing!
