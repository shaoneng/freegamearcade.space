Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Welcome to the exhilarating world of Only Up Obby! Your goal is to navigate a series of challenging obstacle courses, racing against other players to reach the top. Use the WASD keys on your keyboard to move your character and press the Spacebar to jump. The key is to keep climbing and avoid falling at all costs!

The game features several exciting mini-game modes, each with its own unique twist:

Colorful Floor: Pay close attention to the color displayed at the top of the screen. You must quickly jump onto a tile of the matching color. If you're on the wrong color when the timer runs out, you'll fall!

Glass Bridge: Test your luck and nerve! Choose between two glass panels to jump on. One will hold your weight, but the other will shatter, sending you into the void. Choose wisely to make it across.

Falling Plates: In this mode, the platforms you step on will disappear shortly after you leave them. You must keep moving forward with precise timing to avoid a long drop.

Raining Blocks: It's chaos from above! Dodge a constant barrage of falling blocks as you race through the course. Quick reflexes are your best friend here.
