Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Welcome to the dizzying heights of Only Up Obby! Your mission is simple: climb, jump, and survive. Navigate your character through various obstacle courses (obbies) against 29 other players. Use the WASD keys to move around and press the Spacebar to jump.

You'll begin in a central town square where each building's door leads to a different mini-game challenge. Enter a door to start your ascent and race to the finish line to earn coins and claim victory.

Master several unique game modes:

  • Colorful Floor: Race across a floor of tiles, but only step on the color displayed at the top of the screen. Move quickly as the color changes, or you'll be eliminated!
  • Glass Bridge: A test of nerve and luck. Choose between two glass panels to jump onâ€”one holds, and the other shatters. Choose wisely to cross the gap.
  • Falling Plates: The platforms you run on will crumble and fall just a second after you step off them. It's a race against your own footsteps!
  • Raining Blocks: It's chaos from above! Dodge a constant shower of falling blocks as you try to complete the course without getting crushed.
