Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

In Only Up Obby, your primary goal is to ascend through a variety of challenging obstacle courses, or "obbies," while competing against 29 other players. Use your parkour skills to be the first one to the finish line!

Desktop Controls:

- Use the WASD keys to move your character forward, backward, left, and right.

- Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles.

The game features several unique mini-games to test your agility and nerve. In Colorful Floor, you must quickly step on tiles matching the color shown on screen. In Glass Bridge, you must guess the correct path, as one of two glass panels will break. Other modes include classic parkour, dodging raining blocks, and running across falling plates. Your objective is always the same: keep moving up and don't fall!
