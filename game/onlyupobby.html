Only Up Obby

How to Play Only Up Obby

Welcome to the ultimate sky-high parkour challenge! In Only Up Obby, your goal is simple but demanding: climb ever upward through a series of challenging obstacle courses. Use the WASD keys on your keyboard to move your character and press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles. Don't look down!

You'll start in a central hub where each door leads to a different mini-game. Compete with 29 other players to see who can conquer the course first and collect the most coins. Each mode offers a unique twist on the parkour formula.

Test your reflexes in Colorful Floor, where you must quickly step on tiles that match the color displayed on screen. In the nerve-wracking Glass Bridge, you must guess the correct path, as one tile holds firm while the other shatters beneath your feet. Other modes include Falling Plates, where the path disappears behind you, and Raining Blocks, where you must dodge deadly projectiles from above.

Master your timing, choose your path wisely, and race to the finish line to prove you are the ultimate obby champion. Good luck, and keep climbing!
