Organize It

How to Play Organize It

Welcome to Organize It, where your tidying skills are put to the test! Your mission is to bring order to various cluttered rooms, from closets and kitchens to playrooms and bathrooms. Each level presents a new, messy challenge for you to solve.

The controls are incredibly simple. Just use your mouse (or your finger on a touchscreen device) to click and drag items. Look for the designated spots for each object—you'll often see faint outlines or dedicated spaces like shelves, drawers, and hangers.

Your task is to place every item in its correct location. Hang the clothes, shelve the shoes, arrange the toiletries, and sort the toys. Once everything is perfectly placed, the level is complete, and you can move on to the next room for another satisfying session of cleaning and organizing!
