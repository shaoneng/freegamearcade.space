Organize It

How to Play Organize It

Your mission in Organize It is to bring order to chaos! In each level, you're presented with a messy room. Your job is to find the correct spot for every item, from clothes and toys to kitchen utensils and toiletries, creating a perfectly tidy space.

The controls are incredibly intuitive. Simply use your mouse or touchscreen to click and drag items from their jumbled positions. Move them over their designated outlines or storage spaces (like drawers, shelves, and closets) and release to place them correctly.

Work your way through five distinct levels, each set in a different room of the house: a cluttered wardrobe, a messy pet room, a disorganized bathroom, a chaotic kitchen, and a toy-strewn kids' room. Each room offers a unique puzzle and a satisfying sense of accomplishment once you've put everything back in its place. Clear one room to unlock the next and prove you're the ultimate organizer!
