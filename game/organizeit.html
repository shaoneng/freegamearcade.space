Organize It

How to Play Organize It

Welcome to Organize It, a relaxing puzzle game where your goal is to bring order to messy rooms. The gameplay is simple and intuitive, making it easy for anyone to pick up and play.

Your main task is to drag and drop each item to its correct location. Use your mouse to click and hold an item, then drag it to the spot where you think it belongs. If you're on a mobile device, simply use your finger to tap, hold, and drag.

Each level presents a different room with unique challenges. You'll find yourself organizing a cluttered wardrobe by hanging clothes and arranging shoes, tidying up a kitchen by putting away dishes and tossing out trash, and sorting through a mountain of toys in a child's room. Find the perfect place for everything to complete the level and move on to the next organizational challenge!
