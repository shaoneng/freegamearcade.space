Organize It

How to Play Organize It

Your goal in 'Organize It' is to bring order to messy rooms. Each level presents a cluttered space, and your job is to put every item in its correct place. Using your mouse or touchscreen, simply click and drag items to their designated spots.

You'll tackle five different rooms, each with its own unique challenge. Start by organizing a messy wardrobe, hanging clothes and arranging shoes. Then, move on to a room full of pets, a cluttered bathroom, a chaotic kitchen, and a toy-strewn kids' room.

The level is complete when every item, from clothes and toys to kitchen utensils, has been neatly put away. Get rid of the trash, arrange the drawers, and enjoy the satisfaction of a perfectly tidy home!
