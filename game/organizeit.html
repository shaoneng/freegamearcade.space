Organize It

How to Play Organize It

Welcome to Organize It, the ultimate game for those who love a tidy space! Your mission is to step into the shoes of a professional organizer and tackle the clutter in various rooms of a house. Each level presents a new, messy challenge for you to solve.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Just use your mouse to click and drag items, or use your finger on a touchscreen device. Your goal is to move every object to its correct location. Look for visual clues and logical placements to figure out where everything belongs.

You'll start by organizing a chaotic wardrobe, hanging clothes and arranging shoes. From there, you'll move on to a pet-filled room, a messy bathroom counter, a disastrous kitchen, and a kids' room overflowing with toys. Tidy up each space to reveal a clean and satisfying result. Put everything in its place to complete the level and move on to the next organizing challenge!
