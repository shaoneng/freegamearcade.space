Organize It

How to Play Organize It

The goal in Organize It is simple: bring order to chaos! In each level, you'll be presented with a messy room full of items that are out of place.

Using your mouse or touchscreen, click and drag each item to its correct spot. Look for visual clues and logical placements. For example, clothes go on hangers, books on shelves, and trash in the bin.

You'll work your way through various rooms in the house, including a cluttered wardrobe, a chaotic kitchen, a bathroom, a pet area, and a toy-filled kids' room. Each room presents a unique and satisfying puzzle to solve.

There are no timers or penalties, so you can relax and enjoy the calming process of tidying up. Complete all the levels to become the ultimate virtual organizer!
