Organize It

How to Play Organize It

Welcome to Organize It, where your goal is to transform messy rooms into perfectly tidy spaces. In this relaxing puzzle game, you'll take on the role of an expert organizer hired to clear the clutter.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Just use your mouse to click and drag items, or use your finger on a touchscreen device. Move each object to its correct place, whether it's hanging clothes in a wardrobe, placing toys in a chest, or arranging utensils in a kitchen drawer.

Each level presents a new room with a unique challenge. You'll need to figure out where everything belongs. Organize clothes and shoes in the closet, sort skincare products in the bathroom, and even tidy up after some messy pets! Take your time, find a spot for every item, and enjoy the satisfaction of a job well done.
