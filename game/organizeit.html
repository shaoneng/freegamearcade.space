Organize It

How to Play Organize It

Welcome to Organize It, the ultimate tidying up challenge! Your goal is to bring order to chaos by arranging items in various messy rooms. Each level presents a new space for you to clean and organize.

The controls are simple: just use your mouse or touchscreen to drag and drop items. Pick up an object and move it to its correct spot. For example, hang clothes in the wardrobe, place shoes on shelves, and put toys in the toy box.

You'll tackle everything from a cluttered closet and a messy kitchen to a room full of pets and a playroom scattered with toys. Pay attention to the outlines and shapes to figure out where each item belongs. Clean up, get organized, and enjoy the satisfaction of a perfectly tidy room!
