Organize It

How to Play Organize It

Welcome to Organize It, where your tidying skills are put to the test! Your mission is to bring order to a variety of messy rooms, transforming chaos into a clean and satisfying space.

The controls are simple: just use your mouse or touchscreen to click and drag items to their designated spots. Whether it's hanging clothes in a wardrobe, placing toys in their boxes, or sorting utensils in the kitchen, everything has its proper place.

Each level presents a new room with unique challenges. You'll tackle cluttered closets, messy kitchens, and even rooms filled with playful pets and scattered toys. Look for visual cues to figure out where each item belongs. Successfully organize every item in the room to complete the level and move on to the next tidying challenge!
