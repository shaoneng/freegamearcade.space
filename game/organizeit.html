Organize It

How to Play Organize It

Your goal in Organize It is to tidy up a series of messy rooms. Each level presents you with a cluttered space, and your job is to put every item back in its proper place, from wardrobes and kitchens to kids' rooms.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Just use your mouse (on a computer) or your finger (on a touchscreen device) to click and drag items. Move clothes to the wardrobe, toys to the toybox, dishes to the dishwasher, and trash into the bin.

Work your way through different rooms of the house. Each level has its own unique set of items and challenges. Pay attention to the outlines and shapes to figure out where each object belongs. Complete a room to prove you're the ultimate professional organizer!
