Organize It

How to Play Organize It

Welcome to Organize It, the ultimate tidying-up puzzle game! Your mission is simple: transform chaos into order. In each level, you're presented with a messy room, and it's your job to put every item back in its correct spot.

The controls are intuitive and easy to master. Simply use your mouse to click and hold an item, then drag it to where you think it belongs. If you're playing on a mobile device, just use your finger to tap, hold, and drag. Release the item to place it.

You'll tackle various challenges across different rooms of a house. Start with the wardrobe by hanging clothes and arranging shoes. Move on to a room full of pets, placing them with their favorite toys. Tidy up a cluttered vanity, organize a messy kitchen by loading the dishwasher and sorting utensils, and finally, bring order to a kids' room scattered with toys. Each level offers a unique puzzle to solve.

The goal is to clear the clutter completely. A level is finished once you've successfully found the right home for every single object. Enjoy the satisfying feeling of creating a perfectly organized space!
