Organize It

How to Play Organize It

Welcome to Organize It, a relaxing puzzle game where your job is to bring order to chaos! In each level, you'll be presented with a messy room filled with scattered items. Your goal is to clean up by placing every object in its correct spot.

The controls are intuitive and simple. Use your mouse to click, hold, and drag an item from its current position. On a touchscreen device, simply tap and drag with your finger. Move the item to where you think it belongs and release it to place it down.

You'll tackle various challenges, from hanging clothes in a wardrobe and organizing makeup on a vanity to sorting toys in a playroom and putting away groceries in the kitchen. Look for visual clues like shapes, sizes, and outlines to figure out where everything goes. Complete the level by successfully organizing every single item to create a perfectly tidy space!
