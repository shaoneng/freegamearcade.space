Organize It

How to Play Organize It

Welcome to Organize It, where your tidying skills are put to the test! The goal is simple: bring order to chaos by putting everything in its proper place. Each level presents you with a different messy room, from a cluttered closet to a toy-strewn playroom.

Using your mouse on a computer or your finger on a touchscreen device, simply click (or tap) and drag items to their correct locations. Look for visual clues and logical placements. Clothes go on hangers, shoes on shelves, toys in their bins, and trash in the garbage can.

As you progress, you'll tackle a variety of challenges, including organizing a kitchen full of utensils, a bathroom scattered with toiletries, and even a room full of adorable pets and their belongings. Successfully clear a room to move on to the next organizing challenge!
