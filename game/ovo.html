OvO

How to Play OvO

Welcome to the challenging world of OvO! Your goal is to navigate a stick-figure character through a series of complex levels as quickly as possible. Use your Arrow Keys for basic movement: Left and Right to run, and Up to jump.

Mastering advanced moves is key to success. To slide under low obstacles, press the Down Arrow key while running. To break through transparent platforms from above, perform a ground smash by jumping with the Up Arrow and then pressing the Down Arrow in mid-air.

Throughout the 52 levels, you'll encounter various power-ups that can grant you temporary advantages like increased speed or invincibility. Use them strategically to overcome difficult sections and improve your time. The game requires precision, speed, and quick adaptation, so get ready to test your platforming skills!
