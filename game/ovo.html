OvO

How to Play OvO

Welcome to OvO, a high-speed platformer where your goal is to guide a stickman through challenging levels as quickly as possible. The game demands quick reflexes and precise control to overcome the many obstacles in your path.

The controls are simple to learn but difficult to master. Use your keyboard to guide your character:

  • Arrow Keys: Use the Left and Right arrow keys to run, and the Up arrow key to jump.
  • Dive: Press the Down arrow key while running to perform a fast slide or dive, perfect for getting under low obstacles.
  • Ground Smash: Jump by pressing the Up arrow key, then quickly press the Down arrow key in mid-air to smash the ground. This move is essential for breaking through transparent platforms.

Your mission is to reach the flag at the end of each of the 52 levels. Along the way, you'll encounter a variety of hazards, from deadly spikes to tricky slides. Strategically use your moves and keep an eye out for power-ups that can grant you extra speed or even invincibility to conquer all the challenges!
