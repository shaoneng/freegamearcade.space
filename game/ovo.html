OvO

How to Play OvO

Get ready for a high-speed platforming challenge! The goal in OvO is to navigate your stickman character through each level to reach the flag at the end as quickly as possible. The controls are simple to learn but difficult to master.

Use the Arrow Keys to run left, right, and jump. To perform advanced moves, combine these basic controls. For instance, press the Down Arrow while running to slide under low obstacles. To perform a powerful ground smash, which can break through certain platforms, jump with the Up Arrow and then immediately press the Down Arrow in mid-air.

As you progress through the 52 unique levels, you'll encounter new mechanics and more complex obstacles. Watch out for spikes, moving platforms, and tricky jumps. Master your timing and combine your moves strategically to conquer every challenge OvO throws at you!
