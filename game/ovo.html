How to Play OvO

Welcome to OvO, a high-speed platformer that demands precision and quick reflexes. The objective is simple: guide your stickman character to the flag at the end of each level. While the controls are straightforward, mastering them is the key to conquering the game's 52 challenging stages.

Use the Arrow Keys to control your character. The Left and Right keys move you, and the Up key makes you jump. To perform advanced moves, you need to combine these basics. While running, press the Down arrow key to execute a slide, perfect for ducking under low obstacles.

One of the most crucial techniques is the ground smash. Jump into the air with the Up key and then immediately press the Down key to slam into the ground. This move is essential for breaking through transparent platforms and can also help you control your descent. Combine these skills to navigate spikes, walls, and other hazards to prove your platforming prowess!
