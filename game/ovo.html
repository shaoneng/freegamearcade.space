OvO

How to Play OvO

OvO is a thrilling and challenging platformer where speed and precision are key. Your goal is to guide your stickman character to the flag at the end of each level as quickly as possible, navigating a gauntlet of tricky obstacles.

Use the Arrow Keys to control your character. The Up Arrow makes you jump, while the Left and Right Arrows control your movement. Mastering the controls is the first step to conquering the game's 52 challenging levels.

To master the game, you'll need to use advanced techniques. While running, press the Down Arrow to perform a slide, which helps you get under low barriers. To break through thin platforms from above, jump with the Up Arrow and then press the Down Arrow mid-air to perform a powerful ground smash.

Keep an eye out for power-ups scattered throughout the levels. They can grant you a speed boost or even make you invincible for a short time. Each level gets progressively harder, so be prepared to adapt your strategy and time your moves perfectly!
