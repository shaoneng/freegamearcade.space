OvO

How to Play OvO

OvO is a high-speed, strategic arcade game that will test your stamina and adaptability. Your goal is to navigate a stickman character through 52 challenging levels filled with spikes, slides, and other deadly obstacles.

Use the Arrow Keys for basic movement. The Left and Right arrows make you run, and the Up arrow makes you jump. The real skill in OvO comes from mastering advanced maneuvers.

To perform a slide and slip under tight spaces, press the Down Arrow while running. To break through transparent floors or gain extra momentum, jump with the Up Arrow and then press the Down Arrow in mid-air to execute a powerful ground smash. Combine these moves to master each level and beat the clock!
