OvO

How to Play OvO

OvO is a fast-paced and challenging platformer where your goal is to navigate a stickman character through a series of complex levels to reach the flag at the end. The game requires quick reflexes, precision, and strategic use of your character's abilities.

The controls are simple but mastering them is key to success:

  • Arrow Keys: Use the Left and Right arrow keys to move your character.
  • Up Arrow: Press the Up arrow to jump over obstacles and gaps.
  • Down Arrow (Slide): While running, press the Down arrow to slide under low obstacles.
  • Down Arrow (Smash): While in the air, press the Down arrow to perform a ground smash. This powerful move allows you to break through transparent platforms and can give you a burst of speed.

As you progress through the 52 levels, you'll encounter various power-ups that can grant you speed boosts, invincibility, or other special abilities. Use them wisely to overcome the toughest sections and achieve a faster time!
