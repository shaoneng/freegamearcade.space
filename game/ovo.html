OvO

How to Play OvO

Welcome to OvO, a challenging platformer where speed and precision are key! The goal is to navigate your stickman character through a series of increasingly difficult levels to reach the flag at the end.

Use your Arrow Keys to move left, right, and to jump. Mastering the basic movements is just the beginning. The real challenge lies in combining them to perform advanced maneuvers.

To perform a dive, press the Down Arrow key while you are running. This move is essential for getting through tight spaces quickly. To perform a ground smash, jump and then press the Down Arrow key in mid-air. This powerful move allows you to break through certain transparent floors and can also give you a speed boost upon landing.

As you progress, you'll encounter various obstacles like spikes, slides, and portals. You'll need to use all your skills—running, jumping, diving, and smashing—strategically to conquer all 52 levels. Keep an eye out for power-ups that can grant you temporary advantages like increased speed or invincibility!
