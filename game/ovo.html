OvO

How to Play OvO

Welcome to OvO, a minimalist yet incredibly challenging platformer where speed and precision are key. Your goal is to guide your stick-figure character to the flag at the end of each level as quickly as possible, navigating a series of complex obstacles.

The controls are simple to learn but difficult to master:

  • Arrow Keys: Use the left and right arrow keys to run, and the up arrow key to jump.
  • Dive/Slide: While running, press the down arrow key to perform a quick dive or slide, perfect for getting under low obstacles.
  • Ground Smash: Jump and then press the down arrow key to smash into the ground. This powerful move is essential for breaking through specific transparent platforms.

Combine these moves strategically to wall-jump, slide, and bounce your way through 52 increasingly difficult levels. Every second counts, so practice your timing to master the course and achieve the best possible time!
