OvO

How to Play OvO

The goal in OvO is to navigate your character to the flag at the end of each level as quickly as possible. This is a game of speed, precision, and quick thinking. Use your keyboard's Arrow Keys to move left and right, and the Up Arrow to jump.

OvO features special moves that are essential for overcoming obstacles. While running, press the Down Arrow to perform a slide, which helps you get through low passages. To break through transparent floors or gain extra downward momentum, jump into the air and then press the Down Arrow to execute a powerful ground smash.

The game is divided into 52 challenging levels across different stages. As you progress, you'll encounter a variety of obstacles that require you to master all your moves. Keep an eye out for power-ups that can grant you temporary advantages like increased speed or invincibility. Use them wisely to conquer the toughest sections!
