OvO

How to Play OvO

OvO is a fast-paced platformer where the goal is to guide your stick-figure character to the flag at the end of each level. Master the controls to navigate a world filled with spikes, moving platforms, and other challenging obstacles.

The controls are simple to learn but difficult to master. Use the Arrow Keys to run left, right, and to jump. As you progress, you'll need to learn more advanced techniques to overcome the increasingly complex levels.

To perform a slide or a dive, press the Down Arrow key while you are running. To smash through thin platforms from above, jump using the Up Arrow and then quickly press the Down Arrow while in mid-air. Combining these moves is essential for completing all 52 unique levels and proving your platforming prowess!
