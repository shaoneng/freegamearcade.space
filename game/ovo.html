OvO

How to Play OvO

OvO is a challenging and fast-paced platformer where your goal is to guide your character to the flag at the end of each level. Master the simple yet deep controls to overcome a gauntlet of spikes, slides, and other deadly obstacles.

Use the Arrow Keys to run left and right, and to jump. Timing your jumps is crucial to clear gaps and avoid hazards. To perform a slide and get through tight spaces, press the Down Arrow key while running. For a powerful ground smash, which can break through certain platforms, jump first and then press the Down Arrow key while in mid-air.

With 52 levels of increasing difficulty, you'll need to combine all these moves strategically. Practice your timing, adapt quickly, and see if you have the reflexes to conquer the world of OvO!
