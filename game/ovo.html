OvO

How to Play OvO

OvO is a fast-paced and challenging platformer where your goal is to guide a stick figure to the flag at the end of each level. Precision and speed are key to mastering the game's 52 unique levels.

The controls are simple to learn but difficult to master. Use the Arrow Keys for movement. The Up Arrow makes you jump, while the Left and Right Arrows control your running direction.

To overcome various obstacles, you'll need to use special moves. Press the Down Arrow while running to perform a slide, which is perfect for getting under low barriers. To break through transparent floors, jump with the Up Arrow and then quickly press the Down Arrow in mid-air to execute a powerful ground smash.

Combine these moves to navigate the tricky level designs, avoid spikes, and master the complex mechanics to become an OvO champion.
