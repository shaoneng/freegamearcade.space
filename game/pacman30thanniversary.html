Pacman 30th Anniversary

How to Play Pacman 30th Anniversary

The goal is simple but addictive: guide Pac-Man through the maze and eat all the small dots (Pac-dots) to advance to the next level. Use your arrow keys to control Pac-Man's movement.

While you munch your way through the maze, you must avoid the four colorful ghosts. If a ghost touches you, you lose a life! However, you can turn the tables by eating one of the larger, flashing dots called "energizers" or "power pellets". This will temporarily turn the ghosts blue, allowing you to eat them for extra points.

Want to share the fun? This special version includes a two-player co-op mode! Simply click the "Insert Coin" button twice. This will add Ms. Pac-Man to the game. One player controls Pac-Man with the arrow keys, and the second player controls Ms. Pac-Man with the W, A, S, and D keys. Work together to clear the maze!

Keep an eye out for bonus fruits that appear periodically for extra points. This version was originally created as a Google Doodle, and you can even see the Google logo cleverly integrated into the first level's maze design. Enjoy this faithful recreation of an arcade legend!
