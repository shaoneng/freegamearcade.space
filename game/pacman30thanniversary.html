Pacman 30th Anniversary

How to Play Pacman 30th Anniversary

Welcome to the special 30th Anniversary edition of Pac-Man! The goal is simple but addictive: guide Pac-Man through the maze and eat all the small dots (Pac-Dots) to clear the level. Use your keyboard's Arrow Keys to navigate up, down, left, and right.

While clearing the maze, you must avoid the four colorful ghosts: Blinky (red), Pinky (pink), Inky (cyan), and Clyde (orange). If a ghost catches you, you will lose a life. Watch their patterns to outsmart them!

Turn the tables on your ghostly pursuers by eating one of the large, flashing Power Pellets located in the corners of the maze. This will temporarily turn the ghosts blue, allowing you to eat them for extra points. Once eaten, their eyes will scurry back to the center box to regenerate.

Two-Player Mode: You can even play with a friend! To activate the co-op mode, click the "Insert Coin" button twice. This will add Ms. Pac-Man to the game. Player 1 controls Pac-Man with the Arrow Keys, and Player 2 controls Ms. Pac-Man with the WASD keys. Work together to clear the board!
