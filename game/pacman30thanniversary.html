Pacman 30th Anniversary

How to Play Pacman 30th Anniversary

The goal in Pacman 30th Anniversary is simple but challenging: guide Pac-Man through the maze and eat all the pac-dots to advance to the next level. Use your Arrow Keys to navigate up, down, left, and right. Be careful to avoid the four colorful ghosts—Blinky, Pinky, Inky, and Clyde—as touching them will cost you a life!

Turn the tables on your ghostly pursuers by eating one of the larger, flashing Power Pellets located in the corners of the maze. This will temporarily make the ghosts vulnerable, turning them dark blue. Now's your chance to chase them down and eat them for bonus points! But be quick, the effect wears off, and they'll be back to hunting you.

Want to share the fun? This special anniversary edition includes a two-player cooperative mode! Simply click the "Insert Coin" button twice to bring Ms. Pac-Man into the game. Player 1 controls Pac-Man with the Arrow Keys, while Player 2 controls Ms. Pac-Man with the W, A, S, D keys. Work together to clear the maze and double your chances of success!
