Pacman 30th Anniversary

How to Play Pacman 30th Anniversary

Welcome to the special 30th Anniversary edition of Pac-Man! The goal is simple yet captivating: guide Pac-Man through the maze and eat all the small pac-dots to advance to the next level. Use your keyboard's Arrow Keys to navigate up, down, left, and right.

Be careful to avoid the four colorful ghosts—Blinky, Pinky, Inky, and Clyde—as they patrol the maze. If a ghost catches you, you'll lose a life! To turn the tables, eat one of the large, flashing Power Pellets. This will make the ghosts turn blue and vulnerable, allowing you to chase them down for extra points.

Want to play with a friend? This version includes a special two-player mode! Simply click the "Insert Coin" button twice. The second player can then join the fun as Ms. Pac-Man, controlled with the WASD keys. Work together to clear the maze and get the highest score!
