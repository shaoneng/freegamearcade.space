Pacman 30th Anniversary

How to Play Pacman 30th Anniversary

Relive the classic arcade action with this special 30th Anniversary edition of Pac-Man! The objective is simple: guide Pac-Man through the maze and eat all the Pac-dots to advance to the next level. Use your arrow keys to navigate up, down, left, and right, but be careful to avoid the colorful ghosts—Blinky, Pinky, Inky, and Clyde—who are constantly hunting you down!

Turn the tables on your ghostly pursuers by eating one of the large, flashing Power Pellets located in the corners of the maze. This will temporarily make the ghosts vulnerable, allowing you to chase them down for extra points. For a fun twist, you can even play with a friend!

To start a two-player game, simply click the "Insert Coin" button twice. This will introduce Ms. Pac-Man into the game. Player one controls Pac-Man with the arrow keys, and player two controls Ms. Pac-Man with the WASD keys. Work together to clear the board and achieve the highest score!
