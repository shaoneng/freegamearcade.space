Pacman 30th Anniversary

How to Play Pacman 30th Anniversary

Welcome to the timeless challenge of Pac-Man! The objective is simple: guide Pac-Man through the maze using your arrow keys. Your goal is to eat all the small dots, known as Pac-Dots, scattered throughout the level to advance to the next stage. While you're munching away, be sure to avoid the four colorful ghosts—Blinky, Pinky, Inky, and Clyde—as touching one will cost you a life.

Turn the tables on your ghostly pursuers by eating one of the larger, flashing dots called Power Pellets. Once you eat one, the ghosts will turn dark blue and become vulnerable for a short period. This is your chance to chase them down and eat them for extra points! After a while, they will return to their original dangerous state, so be quick.

Want to share the fun? This version includes a two-player co-op mode! To activate it, simply click the "Insert Coin" button twice. This will bring Ms. Pac-Man into the game. Player one controls Pac-Man with the Arrow Keys, and player two controls Ms. Pac-Man with the W, A, S, D keys. Work together to clear the maze!
