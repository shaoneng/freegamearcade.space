Pacman 30th Anniversary

How to Play Pacman 30th Anniversary

Your goal in Pacman 30th Anniversary is simple but challenging: guide Pac-Man through the maze and eat all the pac-dots to advance to the next level. Use your arrow keys to navigate the twists and turns of the corridors, but be careful! Four colorful ghosts are always on your tail, and if they catch you, you'll lose a life.

Look for the large, flashing dots known as Power Pellets or energizers. Eating one of these will temporarily turn the ghosts blue, allowing you to chase them down and eat them for extra points! Don't forget to gobble up the bonus fruits that appear for an even higher score.

Want to share the fun? This version includes a special two-player co-op mode. Simply press the "Insert Coin" button a second time, and Ms. Pac-Man will join the game! Player one controls Pac-Man with the Arrow Keys, and player two controls Ms. Pac-Man with the WASD keys. Work together to clear the maze and double your ghost-chomping power!
