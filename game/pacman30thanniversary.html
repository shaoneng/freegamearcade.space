Pacman 30th Anniversary

How to Play Pacman 30th Anniversary

Welcome to the Pacman 30th Anniversary edition, a special tribute created by Google to celebrate the iconic arcade game. The goal is simple but endlessly challenging: guide Pac-Man through the maze and eat all the pac-dots to complete the level.

Controls: Use the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to navigate Pac-Man up, down, left, and right through the corridors of the maze.

Gameplay: Your primary objective is to clear each level of all the small dots while avoiding the four colorful ghosts. If a ghost touches you, you'll lose a life. Look for the larger, flashing dots called "Power Pellets" or "Energizers." Eating one of these will temporarily turn the ghosts blue, allowing you to eat them for bonus points!

Two-Player Mode: Want to play with a friend? Simply click the "Insert Coin" button twice to start a co-op game. The first player controls Pac-Man with the Arrow Keys, and the second player controls Ms. Pac-Man with the W, A, S, D keys. Work together to clear the maze!
