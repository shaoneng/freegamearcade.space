Pacman 30th Anniversary

How to Play Pacman 30th Anniversary

Welcome to the classic maze craze! The goal in Pacman 30th Anniversary is simple but challenging: guide Pac-Man through the maze to eat all the Pac-dots while avoiding the four colorful ghosts: Blinky, Pinky, Inky, and Clyde. Clearing the maze of all dots will advance you to the next level.

Use your Arrow Keys to navigate Pac-Man up, down, left, and right. Be strategic in your movements to outsmart the ghosts. In each corner of the maze, you'll find a larger, flashing dot called an Energizer (or Power Pellet). Eating one of these will temporarily turn the ghosts blue, allowing you to chase them down and eat them for bonus points!

Want to share the fun? This version includes a two-player cooperative mode! To activate it, simply click the "Insert Coin" button twice. This will bring Ms. Pac-Man into the game. Player 1 controls Pac-Man with the Arrow Keys, and Player 2 controls Ms. Pac-Man with the W, A, S, and D keys. Work together to clear the board and achieve a new high score!
