Pacman 30th Anniversary

How to Play Pacman 30th Anniversary

Welcome to the special 30th Anniversary edition of Pac-Man, famously released as a Google Doodle! The objective is a timeless classic: guide Pac-Man through the maze to eat all the Pac-Dots while avoiding the ghosts.

Use your arrow keys to control Pac-Man's movement. Navigate carefully to avoid the four ghosts: Blinky (red), Pinky (pink), Inky (cyan), and Clyde (orange). If a ghost catches you, you lose a life.

Turn the tables on your pursuers by eating one of the large, flashing Power Pellets in the corners of the maze. This will temporarily make the ghosts turn blue and vulnerable, allowing you to chase and eat them for bonus points.

Two-Player Mode: To play with a friend, click the "Insert Coin" button twice. This will bring Ms. Pac-Man into the game! Player 1 controls Pac-Man with the arrow keys, and Player 2 controls Ms. Pac-Man with the W, A, S, and D keys. Work together to clear the maze!
