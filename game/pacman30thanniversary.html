How to Play Pacman 30th Anniversary

The goal of Pacman 30th Anniversary is simple: navigate your character through a maze and eat all the small Pac-dots spread across the level. Use the **arrow keys** to move Pac-Man (Up, Down, Left, Right) through the labyrinthine passages.

As you move, you'll encounter four colorful ghosts â€“ Blinky, Pinky, Inky, and Clyde. These ghosts will try to catch Pac-Man, which results in losing a life. To protect yourself, strategically eat the larger, flashing **Power Pellets (Energizers)** located at the corners of the maze. Eating a Power Pellet temporarily turns the ghosts blue and vulnerable, allowing Pac-Man to eat them for bonus points. Once eaten, ghosts return to their central "home" base before reappearing to chase you again.

Periodically, bonus fruits or items will appear in the maze, offering extra points when collected. Clear all the Pac-dots in a level to advance to the next, with each level increasing in difficulty. For a unique experience, some versions, including the original Google Doodle, allow for a 2-player co-op mode. To activate, typically you would "insert a second coin" (often by pressing '5' twice or a dedicated in-game button) and Mrs. Pac-Man appears, controlled by the **WASD keys**.
