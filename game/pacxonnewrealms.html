PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

The objective in PacXon New Realms is to capture at least 80% of the playing field. Control your character using the arrow keys to move into the open space. As you move, you'll leave a wall trail behind you. To claim a section of the field, create a complete loop by returning to your captured territory (the solid orange area). The enclosed space will then be filled, adding to your progress bar at the top of the screen.

Be strategic and cautious! The main challenge comes from the various ghosts roaming the field. If a ghost touches your character or the wall trail you are currently drawing, you will lose a life. Carefully observe their unique movement patterns to find the perfect opportunity to expand your territory without getting caught.

As you progress, look for special power-ups that appear on the map. These sweet treats provide powerful, temporary boosts that can help you clear levels faster, freeze enemies, or increase your speed. Use them wisely to conquer the new realms and achieve a high score!
