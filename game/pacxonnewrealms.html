PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

Welcome to PacXon: New Realms, a modern twist on the classic arcade game that combines elements of Pac-Man and Xonix. This sequel is loaded with new realms, enemies, and power-ups for endless fun.

Your primary objective is to capture territory by building walls. Guide your character out from the safety of the sides into the open field to leave a trail. Connect your trail back to your captured territory to claim the enclosed area. The goal is to fill at least 80% of the screen to complete each level. Keep an eye on the progress bar at the top to see how close you are.

The field isn't empty, however. It's filled with ghostly enemies that move in different patterns. If an enemy touches you or your unfinished wall trail, you lose a life. Track your remaining lives on the bottom left. You must study their patterns and strike at the right moment to grab territory without getting caught.

To help you on your quest, collect the sweet treats that appear on the map. These items grant you powerful boosts, giving you an edge over the ghosts and helping you clear the stage. This game is a satisfying blend of strategy and action—see if you can master every level!
