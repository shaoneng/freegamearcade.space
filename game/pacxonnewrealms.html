PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

Welcome to PacXon New Realms! Your primary goal in each level is to claim at least 80% of the play area. Use your arrow keys to control your character, moving from your secured territory (orange dots) into the open space (yellow dots).

As you move through the open space, you leave a trail behind you. To capture an area, you must create a complete loop by returning to your territory. Once you do, the enclosed section will be filled in and added to your score, which is tracked by the progress bar at the top of the screen.

Be careful! The screen is patrolled by ghostly enemies. If a ghost touches you or the trail you are currently drawing, you will lose a life. Your remaining lives are shown in the bottom-left corner. Study their movement patterns to find the perfect moment to build your walls.

Keep an eye out for special power-ups that appear on the map. Collecting these sweet treats will grant you powerful boosts, helping you to clear levels faster and avoid the ghosts more effectively. Good luck!
