PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

Your main objective in PacXon New Realms is to capture at least 80% of the game field to complete each level. You control a Pac-Man-like character and build walls to claim territory. The progress bar at the top of the screen shows you how much of the field you have successfully captured.

Use your arrow keys or swipe on your screen to move your character. Move out from the safety of your existing territory (the orange-dotted area) into the open space (yellow dots). As you move, you'll leave a trail. To capture a section, you must create a complete loop by returning to your territory. The area you've enclosed will then turn orange.

Be careful! The field is patrolled by ghostly enemies. If a ghost touches you or your wall trail before you've completed a loop, you will lose one of your lives, which are displayed in the bottom-left corner. Study their movement patterns to find the perfect moment to expand your territory.

Keep an eye out for special power-ups that appear on the map. Collecting these sweet treats can give you powerful boosts, such as freezing enemies or increasing your speed, making it easier to claim the board.
