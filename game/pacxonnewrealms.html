PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

Welcome to the addictive world of PacXon! Your objective is to capture at least 80% of the playfield to advance to the next level. Use your arrow keys to guide your character.

Move out from the safe, captured edges of the screen into the open space. As you move, you'll leave a temporary trail. To capture territory, create a closed loop by returning to any part of your already-captured area. The section you've enclosed will fill in, adding to your total progress.

Be careful! Ghosts roam the open field. If an enemy touches your character or your temporary wall trail before you've completed a loop, you will lose a life. Study their movement patterns to find the perfect moment to expand your territory.

Keep an eye on the progress bar at the top of the screen to track how close you are to victory. Throughout the levels, you can also collect various power-ups, like sweet treats, that provide powerful boosts and temporary advantages against the ghosts.
