PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

Welcome to PacXon New Realms, a modern twist on the classic arcade-style puzzle game that combines mechanics from Pac-Man and Xonix. The objective is to claim territory by building walls and filling up the screen.

Your primary goal is to capture at least 80% of the field to complete each level. You control a Pac-Man-like character, moving into the open space to create a wall trail. To capture an area, you must draw a complete loop that connects back to your existing territory (the orange-dotted area). Once enclosed, the new section becomes yours.

However, the field is patrolled by ghostly enemies with unique movement patterns. If a ghost touches you or your active trail before you've closed the loop, you lose a life. Carefully observe their patterns to find safe opportunities to expand your territory.

As you play, look for sweet treats on the map. Collecting these items will grant you powerful boosts to help you clear the level more easily. Keep an eye on the progress bar at the top to see how close you are to the 80% goal, and watch your remaining lives in the bottom-left corner.
