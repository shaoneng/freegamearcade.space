PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

Welcome to the updated classic, PacXon: New Realms! Your main objective is to fill at least 80% of the game screen by building walls. You control a Pac-Man-like character, and as you move through the open space (yellow dots), you leave a trail. Successfully connect this trail back to your own territory (orange dots) to capture that section.

Be careful! Ghosts roam the open area. If an enemy touches you or the wall trail you're building before you complete the enclosure, you'll lose one of your lives. Keep an eye on your life count at the bottom left and track your progress with the bar at the top of the screen.

Observe the movement patterns of the different ghosts to find the perfect moment to expand your territory. Along the way, you can collect sweet treats that appear on the map. These items grant you special power-ups, giving you a temporary advantage to help clear the level.
