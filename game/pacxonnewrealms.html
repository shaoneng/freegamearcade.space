PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

Welcome to PacXon New Realms, a modern take on the classic arcade game that blends the strategy of Xonix with the iconic style of Pac-Man. Your objective is to claim territory and conquer the level by filling at least 80% of the screen.

You control PacXon, a Pac-Man-like character. Move into the open space to leave a trail behind you. To capture a section, you must create a complete loop by returning to your existing territory (the orange-dotted area). The enclosed space will then be filled in, adding to your progress.

Be warned! The screen is patrolled by ghostly enemies. If a ghost touches you or the trail you are currently drawing, you will lose a life. Study their movement patterns and time your moves carefully to avoid them. Keep an eye on your remaining lives on the bottom left and your progress towards the 80% goal on the bar at the top.

Along the way, look for sweet treats scattered across the map. Collecting these will grant you powerful boosts to help you clear the level. It's a game of strategy, quick reflexes, and satisfying territory-carving action!
