PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

Your primary goal in PacXon New Realms is to conquer the board by capturing at least 80% of the field. The area is divided into open space (yellow dots) and your captured territory (orange dots). Use your arrow keys to guide your PacXon character.

To capture new territory, move from your safe zone into the open space. Your character will leave a trail behind. Create a complete loop by returning to your territory, and the enclosed area will become yours! Keep an eye on the progress bar at the top to track your progress towards the 80% goal.

Be careful! The board is patrolled by ghostly enemies. If a ghost touches your character or the trail you are currently drawing, you will lose a life. Study their movement patterns to find the perfect moment to expand your territory safely. Your remaining lives are shown in the bottom-left corner.

As you play, look out for special items and sweet treats that appear on the map. Collecting these will grant you powerful boosts and advantages to help you clear the level more easily.
