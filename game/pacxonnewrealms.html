PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

Welcome to the fast-paced, territory-capturing world of PacXon New Realms! Your objective is to fill at least 80% of the screen to conquer the level and advance.

Use the arrow keys on your keyboard or swipe on your screen to move your character. You start on the edges of the board, which is your safe, captured territory.

Venture out into the open space to draw a trail. To capture a new section, you must create a complete loop by returning to an area you already own. Once enclosed, this new section becomes part of your territory.

The main challenge comes from the ghostly enemies that patrol the field. If a ghost touches you or the trail you are currently drawing, you will lose a life. Study their movement patterns to time your expansion safely.

Keep an eye on the power-ups! Collect the various treats that appear on the map to gain special abilities, like freezing enemies or moving faster, giving you a crucial advantage.
