PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

Your main goal in PacXon New Realms is to capture at least 80% of the game field to complete each level. Use the arrow keys (or swipe on mobile) to move your character, PacXon, across the open space.

As you move, you'll leave a trail. To capture a section of the field, draw a complete loop by returning to your existing territory (the orange-dotted area). The enclosed space will then be filled in and added to your territory. Keep an eye on the progress bar at the top to see how much you've captured.

Be careful! The field is patrolled by ghostly enemies. If a ghost touches you or your uncompleted trail, you will lose a life. Your remaining lives are displayed in the bottom-left corner of the screen. Study the enemies' movement patterns to find the safest time to expand your territory.

Throughout the levels, you can collect special power-ups that appear as sweet treats. These can grant you temporary advantages like increased speed, freezing enemies, or other helpful boosts. Grab them whenever you can to make your mission easier!
