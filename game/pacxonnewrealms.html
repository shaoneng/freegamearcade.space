PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

Welcome to PacXon New Realms! The main objective is to capture at least 80% of the game area to advance to the next level. You control the PacXon character and build walls to section off parts of the screen.

Use your arrow keys or WASD to move. Start from your secured territory (orange dots) and venture into the open space (yellow dots). As you move, you'll leave a trail. To capture an area, create a complete loop by returning to your territory. The enclosed section will then be filled in and added to your total.

Be careful! The screen is patrolled by ghostly enemies. If a ghost touches you or the trail you are currently building, you will lose a life. Study their movement patterns to find the safest time to build your walls.

Keep an eye out for sweet treats and other items that appear on the map. Collecting these power-ups can give you powerful boosts, such as freezing enemies or increasing your speed, making it easier to claim territory.
