PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

In PacXon New Realms, your primary goal is to capture at least 80% of the game screen to complete each level. You control Pacxon, a character that leaves a trail behind him as he moves across the open yellow spaces.

To claim territory, you must draw a wall by moving from your existing orange territory, creating a trail, and then successfully looping back to any part of your already captured orange area. Once the loop is closed, the section you enclosed will turn orange, marking it as your territory.

Be careful! Ghostly enemies roam the map. If an enemy touches Pacxon or your unfinished wall trail before you close the loop, you will lose a life. Keep an eye on your remaining lives displayed in the bottom left corner.

The progress bar at the top of the screen shows you how close you are to reaching the 80% capture target. Additionally, look out for sweet treats on the map, as collecting them will grant you powerful boosts to help you on your quest!
