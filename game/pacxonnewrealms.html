PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

Welcome to the updated world of PacXon! The objective in PacXon New Realms is to capture at least 80% of the game area. You control a Pac-Man-like character, and instead of eating dots, you build walls to claim territory.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to navigate. Start from your secured territory (the orange-dotted area) and venture into the open space. As you move, you'll leave a trail behind you. To capture a section, you must draw a complete loop that connects back to your existing territory.

Be careful! The open area is patrolled by ghostly enemies. If a ghost touches your character or the wall you are currently drawing, you will lose a life. Study their movement patterns to find the safest time to build. Your remaining lives are shown in the bottom-left corner.

Keep an eye out for special power-ups that appear on the map! These items can give you temporary advantages like freezing enemies or increasing your speed, making it easier to capture the board and reach the 80% goal to advance to the next level.
