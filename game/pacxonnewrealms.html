PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

Welcome to the retro-futuristic world of PacXon New Realms! Your objective is to claim at least 80% of the game board to advance to the next level. You control the Pac-like character and must strategically build walls to capture territory.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys to navigate. Start from the safety of your existing territory (the orange-dotted area) and venture into the open space (the yellow-dotted area). As you move, you'll leave a trail. To capture the area you've outlined, you must return to your territory without being intercepted.

Be warned! The board is patrolled by various ghosts. If a ghost touches you or your active trail before you complete a wall, you will lose a life. Watch their patterns carefully and time your moves to avoid them. Keep an eye on your remaining lives at the bottom of the screen and track your progress with the bar at the top.

To help you on your quest, power-ups will occasionally appear on the map. Grab these sweet treats to gain temporary advantages, like freezing enemies or moving faster. Good luck, and have fun reclaiming the realms!
