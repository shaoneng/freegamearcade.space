How to Play PacXon New Realms

Welcome to the challenging and addictive world of PacXon New Realms! Your objective is to conquer at least 80% of the game board to advance to the next level. Hereâ€™s how you can become a master wall-builder:

  • Control Your Character: Use the arrow keys or swipe on your screen to move your PacXon character. You start from the safety of the orange-dotted territory on the edges.
  • Build Walls: Move into the open field (the yellow dot area) to start building a wall. Your character will leave a trail behind.
  • Capture Territory: To capture an area, safely guide your character back to your existing orange territory. The area you've enclosed will fill in and become yours.
  • Avoid the Ghosts: The level is patrolled by ghosts with unique movement patterns. If a ghost touches your character or your active building trail, you will lose a life. Study their movements to find the perfect time to strike.
  • Track Your Progress: Keep an eye on the progress bar at the top of the screen to see how close you are to reaching the 80% goal. Your remaining lives are displayed in the bottom-left corner.
  • Grab Power-ups: Look out for special items and treats that appear on the map. Collecting them will grant you powerful temporary boosts to help you clear the level!
