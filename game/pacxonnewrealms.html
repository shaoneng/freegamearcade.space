PacXon New Realms

How to Play PacXon New Realms

Welcome to PacXon New Realms, a thrilling mix of arcade action and puzzle strategy! The primary goal is to capture at least 80% of the game field to complete each level. You control the PacXon character, moving it to build walls and claim territory.

Use your arrow keys to navigate. Start from your secured territory (the orange-dotted area) and venture into the open space. As you move, you'll leave a trail. To capture a new section, guide your character back to your existing territory, closing the loop. The enclosed area will then become yours.

Be careful! Each level is patrolled by ghostly enemies. If a ghost touches you or the trail you are currently building, you will lose a life. Keep an eye on your remaining lives at the bottom of the screen. Study the enemies' movement patterns to find the perfect moment to expand your territory safely.

Track your progress with the bar at the top of the screen. Along the way, be sure to grab any sweet treats that appear on the map. These are power-ups that will give you special boosts and help you clear the board more easily.
