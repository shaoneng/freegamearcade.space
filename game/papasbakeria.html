Papa's Bakeria

How to Play Papa's Bakeria

Welcome to Papa's Bakeria! Your main goal is to manage the entire pie-making process from start to finish. You'll juggle four different stations: the Order Station, the Build Station, the Bake Station, and the Top Station. Everything is controlled using your left mouse button.

Start by taking a customer's order at the Order Station. Then, move to the Build Station to choose the correct crust and pour in the fillings. Be precise! Next, head to the Bake Station to cook the pie until it's perfectly done—don't let it burn! Finally, finish it off at the Top Station with whipped cream, drizzles, and other toppings according to the customer's ticket.

As you successfully serve customers, you'll earn tips and points. Use your earnings to purchase upgrades for the bakery, which can help you bake faster and keep customers happier. You can also buy new furniture and decorations for the lobby to improve your waiting score. After each day, you can play fun mini-games to win new clothing and items for your character.
