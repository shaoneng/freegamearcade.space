Papa's Bakeria

How to Play Papa's Bakeria

Welcome to your new job at Papa's Bakeria! Your goal is to keep your customers happy by creating the most delicious pies in Whiskfield Mall. The entire game is controlled using your left mouse button, making it easy to jump right in and start baking.

The process begins at the Order Station. A customer will arrive with a specific pie order. Pay close attention to their requests for the crust, fillings, and toppings to ensure you get it right.

Next, move to the Build Station to assemble the pie. You'll select the correct bottom crust, pour in the requested fillings, and carefully add any special ingredients before placing the top crust on.

After building, it's time to head to the Bake Station. Place the pie in the oven and watch the timer closely. A perfectly baked pie is key to a happy customer and a big tip, so don't let it overcook!

Finally, finish your creation at the Top Station. Here, you'll add the final touches like whipped cream, drizzles, and other decorative toppings. Precision matters! Once done, serve the pie to your customer and collect your earnings.

Use your hard-earned cash to buy upgrades for the lobby and kitchen. These improvements will make customers more patient and help you bake pies faster, leading to bigger profits and a thriving bakery kingdom!
