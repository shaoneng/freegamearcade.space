Papa's Bakeria

How to Play Papa's Bakeria

Welcome to Papa's Bakeria, where your new career as a pie-making prodigy begins! Your goal is to keep your customers happy by baking the perfect pies to their exact specifications. All actions in the game are performed using your left mouse button.

The gameplay is divided into four main stations. At the Order Station, you'll greet customers and take their detailed pie orders. Next, move to the Build Station to choose the right crust and pour in the delicious fillings. At the Bake Station, you'll need to cook each pie for the right amount of time to achieve a perfect bake. Finally, at the Top Station, add the final touches like whipped cream, drizzles, and shakers.

As you successfully serve customers, you'll earn tips and points. Use your earnings to purchase upgrades for the bakery, which can help you bake faster and make customers happier while they wait. You can also buy new furniture and decorations. After each workday, you can play fun mini-games to win new clothing items and accessories to customize your character's look!
