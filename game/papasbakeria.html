Papa's Bakeria

How to Play Papa's Bakeria

Welcome to Papa's Bakeria! Your main goal is to successfully manage the bakery by crafting delicious pies for your customers. All actions in the game are performed using your left mouse button to click, drag, and drop ingredients and items.

The process is divided into four main stations. At the Order Station, you'll greet customers and take their detailed pie orders. Next, move to the Build Station to choose the correct crust and pour in the specified fillings. After that, it's off to the Bake Station where you'll need to cook the pie for the right amount of time. Finally, at the Topping Station, you'll add the finishing touches like drizzles, shakers, and whipped cream exactly as the customer requested.

Your performance at each station is graded, which determines your final score and the size of your tip. Happy customers mean more money! You can use your weekly paycheck and tips to purchase upgrades for the bakery. Buying new equipment can speed up your workflow, while lobby decorations will keep customers happier and more patient while they wait.

After each workday, you can play fun mini-games for a chance to win special prizes, including new clothing for your character and unique furniture for your restaurant. Master the art of pie-making and turn Papa's Bakeria into the most popular spot in Whiskview Mall!
