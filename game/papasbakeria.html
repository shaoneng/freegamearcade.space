Papa's Bakeria

How to Play Papa's Bakeria

Welcome to Papa's Bakeria! Your main objective is to manage a bustling pie shop and keep all your customers happy. You'll take orders, prepare the pie crust, add the correct fillings, bake it to perfection, and finish it with the specified toppings. The entire game is controlled using just your left mouse button.

The gameplay is divided into four key areas. At the Order Station, you'll greet customers and take down their detailed pie requests. Next, you'll head to the Build Station to choose the right crust and add the delicious fillings. After that, it's off to the Bake Station, where you must keep a close eye on the timer to ensure the pies are perfectly baked and not burnt. Finally, at the Topping Station, you'll add the finishing touches like whipped cream and drizzles.

As you successfully serve customers, you'll earn tips and a weekly paycheck. Use your earnings in the shop to buy valuable upgrades that can make your job easier, as well as decorations to improve customer patience. After each workday, you can also play fun mini-games to win prizes like new clothing for your character and furniture for your bakery lobby.
