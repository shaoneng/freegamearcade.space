Papa's Bakeria

How to Play Papa's Bakeria

Welcome to your new job at Papa's Bakeria! Your main tool for this delicious job is your mouse. Use it to click, drag, and drop ingredients and interact with all the stations in the bakery.

The gameplay is divided into four main areas:

Order Station: Greet your customers and carefully take down their custom pie orders. Pay close attention to every detail to get it right!

Build Station: Head here to prepare the pie. You'll choose the correct crust, pour in the requested fillings, and artfully arrange the top crust, like a lattice or crumble.

Bake Station: Slide your pies into the oven and keep a watchful eye on the timer. Perfect baking is keyâ€”don't let them overcook or undercook!

Top Station: Once baked, it's time for the finishing touches. Add whipped cream, drizzles, and shakers precisely as the customer ordered to complete your masterpiece.

Your goal is to satisfy every customer by fulfilling their order accurately and efficiently. Happy customers mean higher scores and bigger tips, which you can use to upgrade the bakery. Purchase new equipment to bake faster or decorate the lobby to keep waiting customers happy. After a long day's work, enjoy daily mini-games to win new outfits and furniture!
