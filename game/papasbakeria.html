Papa's Bakeria

How to Play Papa's Bakeria

Welcome to your new job at Papa's Bakeria! Your goal is to bake delicious pies for the hungry customers of Whiskfield Mall. The entire game is controlled using your left mouse button, making it easy to jump right in.

Your workday is split into four key areas: the Order Station, the Build Station, the Bake Station, and the Top Station. Start by taking a customer's order at the Order Station. Then, head to the Build Station to choose the right crust and pour in the perfect filling. Next, carefully monitor your pies at the Bake Station to ensure they're cooked to perfection.

Finally, finish your creation at the Top Station with whipped cream, drizzles, and various toppings exactly as the customer requested. The better your pie matches the order, the happier your customer will be, leading to bigger tips and higher scores!

Use the money you earn to purchase upgrades for your restaurant. New equipment can speed up your baking process, while decorations for the lobby will keep waiting customers patient and happy. After each workday, you can play fun mini-games to win new clothing and items to customize your character.
