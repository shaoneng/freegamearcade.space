Papa's Bakeria

How to Play Papa's Bakeria

Welcome to Papa's Bakeria! Your goal is to run a successful pie shop by keeping your customers happy and serving up delicious baked goods. You'll use your left mouse button to perform all actions, from taking orders to decorating the final product.

The gameplay is divided into four main stations. First, at the Order Station, you'll greet your customers and take down their specific pie requests. Pay close attention to the details! Next, head to the Build Station to assemble the pie, choosing the correct crust and adding the requested fillings. After that, it's off to the Bake Station, where you'll need to cook the pie for the right amount of time. Finally, at the Top Station, you'll add the finishing touches like drizzles and toppings before serving it to the customer for their review.

Doing a great job earns you bigger tips and a higher score. Use your weekly paychecks and tips to buy upgrades for the shop. New furniture and posters will make waiting customers more patient, while better equipment will make your baking process faster and easier. You can also buy new outfits to customize your character. The better you do, the more you can grow your bakery kingdom!
