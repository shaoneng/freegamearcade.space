Papa's Bakeria

How to Play Papa's Bakeria

Welcome to Papa's Bakeria, where your goal is to become the ultimate pie-making master! The gameplay is broken down into simple, fun steps, all controlled with your left mouse button.

First, head to the Order Station to take a customer's pie order. Pay close attention to their specific requests for crust, fillings, and toppings. Next, move to the Build Station to assemble the pie. Choose the correct crust and meticulously add the layers of filling as requested.

Once built, it's time for the Bake Station. Place the pie in the oven and watch the timer carefully to bake it to golden-brown perfection. Don't let it burn! Finally, at the Top Station, add the finishing touches like whipped cream, drizzles, and other toppings according to the order ticket.

Serve the perfect pie to your customer to get a great score and a big tip! Use your earnings to purchase upgrades for the bakery, which will help you work faster and make customers happier. After each workday, you can play fun mini-games to win prizes like new clothing for your character and decorations for your shop.
