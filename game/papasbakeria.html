Papa's Bakeria

How to Play Papa's Bakeria

Welcome to Papa's Bakeria! Your mission is to manage a bustling pie shop at the Whiskfield Mall. You'll be responsible for taking customer orders and crafting the perfect pies to their exact specifications.

The game is controlled entirely with your left mouse button. Gameplay is split across four stations. At the Order Station, you'll greet customers and take down their unique pie orders. Pay close attention to their chosen crust, fillings, and toppings.

Next, head to the Build Station. Here, you'll select the correct pie crust, pour in the requested fillings, and add any special top crusts or lattice designs. Precision is crucial for a high score!

Once built, the pie moves to the Bake Station. Keep a close eye on the timer to bake each pie to golden-brown perfection without burning it. Finally, at the Top Station, add the finishing touches like whipped cream, drizzles, and shakers as per the order ticket.

The better your pie, the higher your score and the bigger your tips! Use your earnings to purchase upgrades for the bakery to cook faster and decorations to keep customers happy while they wait. After each shift, you can play fun mini-games to win new outfits and items to customize your character.
