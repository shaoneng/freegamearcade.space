Papa's Freezeria

How to Play Papa's Freezeria

Welcome to your new summer job! Your goal is to manage Papa's Freezeria and serve delicious sundaes to a flood of customers from a newly arrived cruise ship. Use your mouse to control everything and keep the customers happy to earn bigger tips!

The game is divided into four stations. First, at the Order Station, a customer will arrive and place their order. Drag the ticket to the holder to begin.

Next, move to the Build Station. Here, you'll pour the ice cream and add the correct mixable syrups and solid ingredients into the cup. Timing and accuracy are key to a good score.

At the Mix Station, blend the sundae to the perfect consistency. A meter will show you when to stop mixing. Don't over or under-blend!

Finally, finish the masterpiece at the Top Station. Add whipped cream, sprinkles, sauces, and cherries exactly as the customer ordered. Once done, serve the sundae to the customer and collect your hard-earned tips!
