Papa's Freezeria

How to Play Papa's Freezeria

Welcome to your relaxing new summer job at Papa's Freezeria on the beautiful Calypso Island! Things take a wild turn when a cruise ship docks, unleashing a wave of dessert-craving tourists. Your simple job just got a lot busier, and it's up to you to keep everyone happy!

The entire game is controlled with your mouse. Your goal is to create the perfect frozen sundaes by moving through four distinct stations. First, take the customer's order at the Order Station. Pay close attention to every detail on the order ticket.

Next, head to the Build Station to pour the ice cream base and add the correct mixable syrups and ingredients. Then, slide over to the Mix Station to blend the sundae to the perfect, smooth consistency. Timing is crucial for a good score!

Finally, finish your creation at the Top Station. Carefully add whipped cream, drizzles, and all the toppings the customer asked for. Once it's perfect, serve it to the customer and wait for their verdict. The better you do, the bigger your tips, which you can use to buy cool upgrades for the shop and new clothes for your character!
