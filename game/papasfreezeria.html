Papa's Freezeria

How to Play Papa's Freezeria

Welcome to your new summer job at Papa's Freezeria! Your goal is to create delicious freezer sundaes for a line of eager customers. The gameplay is divided into four main stations, and you'll use your mouse for everything.

First, head to the Order Station to take a customer's specific sundae request. Pay close attention to their desired size, mix-ins, and toppings.

Next, move to the Build Station. Here, you'll pour the correct ice cream base and add the requested syrups and solid ingredients into the cup.

At the Mix Station, blend the sundae to the perfect consistency. Watch the meter and stop it at the right moment for a flawless blend.

Finally, at the Top Station, you'll finish the masterpiece. Carefully add whipped cream, drizzles, and other toppings in the correct order and placement. Serve the finished sundae to the customer and collect your tips! The happier the customer, the better your score and the more money you'll earn.
