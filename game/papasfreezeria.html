Papa's Freezeria

How to Play Papa's Freezeria

Your relaxing summer job at Papa's Freezeria on Calypso Island takes an unexpected turn when a cruise ship full of tourists arrives! It's time to get to work and serve up some delicious frozen treats.

The game is divided into four key stations: The Order Station, the Build Station, the Mix Station, and the Top Station. You'll need to manage your time effectively across all four to succeed.

First, take a customer's order at the Order Station. Then, head to the Build Station to pour the ice cream and add the right mix-ins. At the Mix Station, blend the sundae to the perfect consistency. Finally, finish it off at the Top Station with whipped cream, syrups, and other toppings as requested by the customer.

Use your mouse to drag, drop, pour, and blend everything. The closer your creation is to the customer's order, the higher your score and the bigger your tip! Use your hard-earned tips to buy upgrades for the shop and new clothes for your character.
