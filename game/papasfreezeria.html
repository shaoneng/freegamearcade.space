Papa's Freezeria

How to Play Papa's Freezeria

Welcome to your new summer job at Papa's Freezeria! Your goal is to keep the customers happy by creating their perfect frozen sundaes. The entire game is played using your mouse, making it easy to jump right in.

The process is broken down into four key stations:

Order Station: Greet your customers and take their detailed order. Pay close attention to every ingredient and topping they ask for!

Build Station: Head here to pour the right amount of ice cream and add the correct mixable syrups and ingredients into the cup.

Mix Station: Place the cup on the blender and mix it to the perfect consistency. Don't over or under-mix!

Top Station: Finish the sundae with a variety of whipped creams, sprinkles, sauces, and cherries. Arrange the toppings neatly for the best score.

The more accurately you build the sundae, the higher your score and the bigger your tips! Use your earnings to buy new clothing and upgrade the shop to make your job easier.
