Papa's Freezeria

How to Play Papa's Freezeria

Welcome to your new summer job at Papa's Freezeria! Your mission is to create delicious frozen sundaes for a rush of customers from a newly arrived cruise ship. The entire game is controlled using your mouse, so get ready to click, drag, and drop your way to sundae success.

The gameplay is broken down into several key steps. First, you'll head to the Order Station to take a customer's specific order. Pay close attention to every detail! Next, move to the Build Station to pour the ice cream base and add the right mixable ingredients. After that, it's time for the Mix Station, where you'll blend the sundae to the perfect texture.

Finally, finish your creation at the Top Station by adding whipped cream, syrups, sprinkles, and a cherry on top, just like the customer asked. The more accurately and quickly you fulfill the order, the higher your score will be and the more tips you'll earn. Use your tips to purchase upgrades for the shop and new outfits. Can you handle the summer rush?
