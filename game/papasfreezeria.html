Papa's Freezeria

How to Play Papa's Freezeria

Welcome to your new summer job at Papa's Freezeria! What starts as a relaxing island gig quickly heats up when a cruise ship docks, unleashing a horde of sundae-loving customers. Your goal is to keep them happy and earn big tips by crafting the perfect frozen treats.

The gameplay is divided into four key stations, all controlled with your mouse. First, take the customer's order at the Order Station. Then, head to the Build Station to pour the ice cream and add the correct mix-ins. Next, blend it all to perfection at the Mix Station, stopping the blender at just the right time.

Finally, finish your creation at the Top Station by adding whipped cream, syrups, and toppings exactly as the customer requested. Precision and speed are crucial! The closer your sundae is to the order, the higher your score and the better your tips will be. Use your earnings to upgrade the shop and buy new outfits!
