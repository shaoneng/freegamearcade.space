Papa's Scooperia

How to Play Papa's Scooperia

Welcome to Papa's Scooperia! Your journey begins after an unfortunate turn of events leaves you stranded in Oniontown. To earn your way home, you've taken a job at Papa Louie's newest dessert shop, specializing in incredible cookie sundaes. Your mission is to satisfy a diverse cast of customers by crafting their perfect treats.

The game is divided into four key stations you'll need to master. First, at the Order Station, you'll greet customers and take their specific orders. Pay close attention to their desired cookie, ice cream flavors, and toppings.

Next, head to the Dough Station. Here, you'll scoop the correct cookie dough and prepare it for baking. Then, move to the Bake Station to bake the cookies until they're perfectly golden brown. Timing is crucial to avoid burnt or undercooked cookies!

Finally, at the Build Station, the real artistry begins. You'll add scoops of ice cream, drizzle on delicious syrups, and sprinkle a variety of toppings, all according to the customer's ticket. A well-made sundae earns you bigger tips and higher scores. Use your earnings to purchase upgrades for your shop, new clothing for your character, and furniture to improve customer wait times. Keep your patrons happy to grow your business and become the sundae-making champion of Oniontown!
