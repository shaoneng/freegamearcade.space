Papa's Scooperia

How to Play Papa's Scooperia

Welcome to Oniontown, the new home of Papa Louie's latest culinary venture: Papa's Scooperia! Your goal is to manage the shop, creating mouth-watering cookie sundaes for a line of hungry customers. It's a sweet challenge that will test your time management and multitasking skills.

The gameplay is divided into four key stations, and you'll use your mouse to navigate through them:

  • Order Station: Begin by taking your customer's unique sundae order. Pay close attention to their cookie, ice cream, and topping preferences.
  • Dough Station: Head here to scoop the right amount of cookie dough and form it into a perfect circle for baking.
  • Bake Station: Place the cookie dough in the oven and bake it until it's just right. Be careful not to overcook or undercook it!
  • Build Station: This is where the magic happens! Scoop the correct flavor of ice cream onto the warm cookie, then add the requested syrups and toppings with precision to complete the perfect dessert.

As you successfully serve customers, you'll earn tips that can be used to purchase upgrades for your shop. These upgrades can help you cook faster, improve customer waiting times, or simply decorate your lobby to your liking. New customers and ingredients will be unlocked as you progress through the days. Serve with a smile and turn your little shop into the most popular dessert destination in town!
