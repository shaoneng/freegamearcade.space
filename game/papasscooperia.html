Papa's Scooperia

How to Play Papa's Scooperia

Welcome to Oniontown! After a sightseeing trip goes wrong, you find yourself working at Papa's Scooperia to earn your way back home. Your goal is to serve up delicious cookie sundaes to a lineup of hungry customers. The gameplay is all about multitasking and attention to detail as you move between different stations.

First, head to the Order Station to take a customer's specific order. Pay close attention to their requested cookie, ice cream flavor, and toppings.

Next, move to the Dough Station. Here, you'll scoop the correct cookie dough and place it in the oven to bake. Time the baking perfectly to get a warm, gooey cookie.

Once the cookie is ready, it's off to the Build Station. This is where you'll drag and scoop the ice cream onto the cookie, trying to create a perfect, centered scoop just like the customer ordered.

Finally, at the Topping Station, you'll add the finishing touches. Drizzle syrups and add sprinkles, nuts, or other toppings as requested on the ticket. Once the sundae is complete, serve it to the customer and hope for a great score and a big tip! Use your earnings to upgrade the shop and buy new outfits.
