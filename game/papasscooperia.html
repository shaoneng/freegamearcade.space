Papa's Scooperia

How to Play Papa's Scooperia

Your journey in Papa's Scooperia begins after an unfortunate incident leaves you penniless in Oniontown. Luckily, Papa Louie offers you a job at his brand-new ice cream shop! Your goal is to serve delicious cookie sundaes to a wide array of customers and become the town's top dessert destination.

The gameplay is centered around four distinct stations. First, at the Order Station, you'll greet customers and take their detailed sundae orders. Pay close attention to their requests for cookie dough, ice cream flavors, syrups, and toppings.

Next, move to the Dough Station to scoop the perfect ball of cookie dough. Then, head to the Bake Station to bake it into a warm, gooey cookie. Timing is key here – don't overbake or underbake it!

Finally, at the Build Station, you'll bring the creation to life. Add scoops of the requested ice cream flavors, drizzle on the correct syrups, and finish it with a sprinkle of toppings. The more accurately you match the customer's order, the better your score and the bigger your tip! Use your earnings to upgrade your shop and unlock new items.
