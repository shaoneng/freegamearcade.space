How to Play Papa's Scooperia

Welcome to Oniontown! In Papa's Scooperia, your adventure begins after losing all your belongings on a sightseeing trip. To earn money for your trip home, you've taken a job at Papa Louie's newest shop, where you'll serve delicious cookie sundaes to a diverse cast of customers.

The gameplay is divided into four key stations, and you'll need to master each one to succeed. First, at the Order Station, you'll greet customers and take their specific orders. Pay close attention to their requests for cookie type, ice cream flavors, and all the toppings they want.

Next, head to the Dough Station to scoop the correct cookie dough. Then, it's off to the Bake Station where you'll bake the cookie to golden-brown perfection. Upgrades can help improve your scores and make your job easier. Good luck becoming the sundae-serving master of Oniontown!
