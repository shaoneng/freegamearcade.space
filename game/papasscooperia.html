How to Play Papa's Scooperia

Welcome to Papa's Scooperia, where your goal is to manage a bustling ice cream and cookie sundae shop in Oniontown. Your mission is to keep a diverse cast of customers happy by crafting their perfect desserts quickly and accurately. The better you serve, the bigger your tips and the more your shop will grow!

The gameplay is divided into four key stations, and you'll use your mouse to navigate between them. It all starts at the Order Station, where you'll greet customers and take their detailed sundae orders.

Next, you'll head to the Dough Station to scoop and place the perfect cookie dough onto the baking sheet. Then, move to the Bake Station to bake the cookies to golden-brown perfection. Timing is crucial here â€“ don't let them burn!

Finally, at the Build Station, you'll bring the dessert to life. Add scoops of delicious ice cream, drizzle on syrups, and finish with a variety of tasty toppings, all according to the customer's order ticket. Deliver the finished sundae to earn your score and tips. Use your earnings to upgrade your shop, buy new outfits, and unlock new ingredients to attract more customers!
