How to Play Papa's Scooperia

Welcome to Papa's Scooperia! Your mission is to serve delicious cookie sundaes to the residents of Oniontown. To become the top dessert shop, you'll need to master the four stations of the ice cream-making process.

1. Order Station: Begin by greeting your customer and taking their detailed order. Pay close attention to the type of cookie, ice cream flavors, syrups, and toppings they want. An accurate order is the first step to a big tip!

2. Dough Station: Move to the Dough Station to prepare the cookie base. Select the correct dough, scoop it, and roll it into a perfect ball. Precision here will affect the final bake.

3. Bake Station: Next, place your cookie dough in the oven. Keep an eye on the bake timer to ensure the cookie is cooked to golden-brown perfection. Nobody likes a burnt or undercooked cookie!

4. Build Station: At the final station, bring your creation to life. Add scoops of the requested ice cream flavors onto the warm cookie. Carefully drizzle the correct syrups and add the finishing touches with various toppings. Serve the finished sundae to your eager customer and enjoy your earnings!
