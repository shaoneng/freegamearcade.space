Papa's Scooperia

How to Play Papa's Scooperia

Welcome to your new job at Papa's Scooperia! Your adventure begins after a trip to Oniontown goes awry, leaving you to work off your debt by serving the best cookie sundaes in town. The goal is to keep your customers happy by crafting their orders perfectly and efficiently.

Your workday is split between four stations. First, at the Order Station, you'll greet your customers and take their detailed cookie sundae orders. Pay close attention to their preferences for cookies, ice cream flavors, and toppings!

Next, head to the Dough Station. Here, you'll scoop the correct cookie dough and roll it into a perfect ball. Accuracy is key to a happy customer and a great score.

Once the dough is ready, move to the Bake Station. Place the cookie in the oven and bake it until it's just right. Don't let it burn, or you'll have to start over!

Finally, it's time to assemble the masterpiece at the Build Station. Add a perfect scoop of the requested ice cream flavor, then finish it off with the correct syrups and toppings, placing them exactly as shown on the order ticket. Serve the sundae to your customer and hope for a big tip!

Use the tips you earn to purchase upgrades for your shop, which can help you cook faster and score better. You can also buy new clothing to customize your character. Master each station to become the best scooper in Oniontown!
