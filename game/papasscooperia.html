Papa's Scooperia

How to Play Papa's Scooperia

Welcome to your new job at Papa's Scooperia! Your main goal is to make your customers happy by creating the perfect cookie sundaes. Customers will arrive with specific orders, and it's up to you to get every detail right to earn the best tips and grow the shop's reputation.

The process starts at the Order Station, where you'll greet customers and take their requests. Next, move to the Dough Station to scoop the right cookie dough and place it on the baking sheet. At the Bake Station, you'll need to watch the oven carefully to bake the cookies to perfection.

Once the cookie is ready, it's time for the fun part at the Build Station! Add scoops of delicious ice cream, drizzle on various syrups, and finish with a sprinkle of toppings like chocolate chips or nuts. The more accurate your creation is to the customer's order, the higher your score will be. Use your earnings to upgrade the shop and unlock new ingredients!
