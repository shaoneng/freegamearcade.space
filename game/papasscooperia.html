Papa's Scooperia

How to Play Papa's Scooperia

Welcome to Papa's Scooperia! Your goal is to manage a bustling ice cream and cookie shop, keeping your customers happy with delicious, custom-made sundaes. The gameplay is divided into four key stations, and you'll need to master each one to succeed.

The Order Station: Your journey begins here. A customer will arrive and place their order. Pay close attention to their requested cookie, ice cream flavors, syrups, and toppings. A happy customer starts with an accurate order!

The Dough Station: Head to this station to prepare the cookie base. Select the correct cookie dough and roll it into a perfect ball. Timing and precision are key to getting a good score.

The Bake Station: Place the cookie dough into the oven. Watch the timer carefully! Overbaking or underbaking the cookie will affect your customer's satisfaction. Aim for a perfectly baked, golden-brown cookie every time.

The Build Station: This is where the magic happens. Drag the scoop across the ice cream tub to create a perfect scoop, then place it on the cookie. Finally, add the correct syrups and toppings as requested in the order. Once the sundae is complete, serve it to the customer and collect your tips!

Use the money you earn to purchase upgrades for your shop, new clothing for your character, and furniture for the lobby. These upgrades can help you earn better scores and attract more customers. Good luck!
