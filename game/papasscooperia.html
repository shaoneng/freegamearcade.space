Papa's Scooperia

How to Play Papa's Scooperia

Welcome to Oniontown and Papa's newest venture! Your main goal in Papa's Scooperia is to manage an ice cream sundae shop. You'll take orders from a variety of quirky customers and craft delicious desserts to perfection to earn the best tips and scores.

The gameplay is divided into four key stations. At the Order Station, you'll greet customers and take their detailed sundae orders. Pay close attention to their preferences for cookies, ice cream, syrups, and toppings.

Next, head to the Dough Station. Here, you'll scoop the correct cookie dough and place it on the baking sheet. Then, move to the Bake Station to bake the cookies until they are perfectly golden brown. Timing is crucial for a great score!

Finally, at the Build Station, the real artistry begins. You'll add scoops of the requested ice cream flavors, drizzle on the right syrups, and sprinkle the correct toppings to complete the sundae exactly as the customer ordered. Serve the finished treat and collect your tips!

Use your hard-earned tips to purchase upgrades for your shop's lobby and equipment. Upgrades can help you score better, make customers happier, and make your job easier. Master each station, keep your customers happy, and grow your business into the most popular spot in town!
