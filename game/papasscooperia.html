Papa's Scooperia

How to Play Papa's Scooperia

Welcome to Oniontown, where you've just landed a new job at Papa's Scooperia! Your goal is to manage the shop and create delicious ice cream sundaes for your customers. Start by taking a customer's order at the Order Station. Pay close attention to their specific requests for cookies, ice cream flavors, and toppings.

Once you have the order, it's time to get to work. Head to the Dough Station to scoop and prepare the cookie dough. Next, move to the Bake Station to bake the cookies until they are perfectly golden brown. Finally, at the Build Station, you'll add scoops of their chosen ice cream, drizzle on syrups, and sprinkle on the final toppings to complete the sundae.

The key to success is speed and accuracy. The better you match the customer's order, the higher your score and the bigger your tip! Use your hard-earned money to buy upgrades for the shop, new outfits for your character, and furniture for the lobby. Happy customers will help you grow your business and become the best scooper in town!
