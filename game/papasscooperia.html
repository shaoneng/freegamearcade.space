Papa's Scooperia

How to Play Papa's Scooperia

Welcome to Papa's Scooperia! Your goal is to manage a bustling ice cream shop, serving delicious cookie sundaes to the residents of Oniontown. You'll need to multitask and manage your time effectively to keep your customers happy and earn big tips.

The game is divided into four main stations. At the Dough Station, you'll take a customer's order and then scoop the perfect amount of cookie dough for their treat.

Next, you'll move to the Bake Station to place the dough in the oven and bake the cookies to golden-brown perfection. Don't let them burn!

Finally, head to the Build Station where you'll add scoops of ice cream, drizzle on syrups, and sprinkle a variety of tasty toppings according to each customer's specific order. Precision is key to a high score!

A happy customer means a good score and more money for shop upgrades and new clothing. Master each station, serve your customers quickly and accurately, and grow your business into the most popular spot in town! Use your mouse to click, drag, and drop items throughout the game.
