Papa's Scooperia

How to Play Papa's Scooperia

Welcome to Papa's Scooperia, where you'll create delicious cookie sundaes for your customers!

First, you'll start at the Order Station to take customer requests. Pay close attention to their specific desires for cookies, ice cream, syrups, and toppings.

Next, move to the Bake Station to prepare the perfect cookies. You'll need to select the right dough, bake it to perfection, and ensure it's ready for the next step.

After baking, head to the Scoop Station. Here, you'll add scoops of various ice cream flavors onto your freshly baked cookies. Make sure to get the serving size just right!

Finally, at the Build Station, you'll complete the masterpiece. Drizzle the requested syrups, sprinkle the correct toppings, and add any other garnishes. Present the finished dessert to your customer, aiming for high scores based on accuracy and speed. Master all stations to keep your customers happy and grow your scooperia!
