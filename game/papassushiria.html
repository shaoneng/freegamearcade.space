Papa's Sushiria

How to Play Papa's Sushiria

Your journey as a sushi chef begins after an unfortunate accident lands you in charge of Papa's newest restaurant. The goal is to successfully manage the sushiria by taking customer orders and preparing them accurately and quickly to earn points and tips.

Each day, customers will arrive with specific sushi and bubble tea orders. Pay close attention to their ticket at the Order Station, as it contains all the details you needâ€”from the type of rice and fillings to the toppings and their drink choice.

Next, head to the Cook Station. Here, you'll cook the rice. Timing is crucial; scoop the right amount, start the cooker, and remove it when it's perfectly done to avoid burning. This sets the foundation for a great sushi roll.

The Build Station is where the magic happens. Lay down the nori, spread the perfectly cooked rice, add the correct fillings in the right order, and roll it all up. After rolling, you'll slice the sushi into even pieces and add any final toppings or sauces requested by the customer.

Finally, at the Tea Station, you'll prepare the customer's bubble tea. Choose the right tea, flavor, ice, and boba pearls. Once the sushi and tea are ready, drag the ticket to the customer to serve them. They will rate your performance on speed, accuracy, and presentation, which determines your score and tips!
