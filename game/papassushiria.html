Papa's Sushiria

How to Play Papa's Sushiria

Your journey in Papa's Sushiria begins after an unfortunate accident leaves you in charge of Papa Louie's newest restaurant. Your goal is to become a master sushi chef by crafting delicious sushi rolls and bubble teas for a host of quirky customers.

The game is divided into four main stations. First, at the Order Station, you'll greet customers and carefully note down their specific sushi and drink requests. Attention to detail is key!

Next, head to the Cook Station. Here, you'll scoop the perfect amount of rice and cook it to perfection. Timing is crucialâ€”don't let it overcook! While the rice cooks, you can prepare for the next steps.

The Build Station is where your creativity shines. Lay down the nori (seaweed), spread the perfectly cooked rice, add the correct fillings in the right order, and roll it all up. Once rolled, you'll slice the sushi into even pieces and add any final toppings or sauces.

Finally, at the Tea Station, you'll mix up the customer's bubble tea. Choose the right tea, flavor, ice, and boba pearls to complete the order. Once everything is ready, serve it to the customer and get rated on your performance. High scores earn you bigger tips, which can be used to upgrade your shop and unlock new ingredients!
