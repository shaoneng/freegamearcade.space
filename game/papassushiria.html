Papa's Sushiria

How to Play Papa's Sushiria

Welcome to Papa's Sushiria! Your goal is to become the ultimate sushi chef by taking orders, preparing sushi rolls, and making bubble tea for a lineup of quirky customers. The game is all about precision, timing, and keeping your customers happy to earn bigger tips and unlock new items.

The process starts when a customer walks in. Pay close attention to their order ticket. It will detail the type of rice, the fillings for the sushi, any toppings or sauces, and their preferred bubble tea flavor. Accuracy is key to a high score!

Next, head to the Cook Station to prepare the rice. Time it perfectly to avoid burning it. Then, move to the Build Station. You'll lay out the nori (seaweed), spread the cooked rice, add the fillings in the correct order, and roll it all up. After rolling, you'll slice the sushi and arrange it neatly on the plate, adding any final toppings.

Don't forget the drink! At the Drink Station, you'll mix the right tea, flavor, and boba pearls to match the customer's order. Once both the sushi and the bubble tea are ready, serve them to the customer. They will rate your performance based on speed, accuracy, and presentation. Good scores lead to more tips, which you can use to upgrade your shop and customize your character.
