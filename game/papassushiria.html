Papa's Sushiria

How to Play Papa's Sushiria

Welcome to Papa's Sushiria! Your journey begins after an unfortunate accident with the restaurant's lucky cat statue. To pay for the damages, you're now in charge of running the sushi bar. Your goal is to keep customers happy by creating delicious sushi and bubble tea exactly as they order it.

Each day starts with customers arriving at the Order Station. Pay close attention to their order ticket, which details everything they want, from the type of rice and fillings for their sushi to the flavor and toppings for their bubble tea.

Next, head to the Cook Station. Here, you'll need to cook the rice to perfection. Scoop the right amount and monitor the cooker's timer. Well-cooked rice is the foundation of great sushi and will earn you higher points!

The Build Station is where the magic happens. Lay down the nori (seaweed), spread the rice, add the correct fillings in the right order, and roll it all up. Once rolled, you'll slice the sushi and add any final toppings or sauces as requested by the customer.

Don't forget the drink! At the Tea Station, you'll mix the perfect bubble tea. Choose the correct tea and flavor, add the right amount of ice, and finish it off with the requested boba pearls. Shake it up and it's ready to go!

Finally, present the complete order to the customer. They will rate you on your performance in each station. Higher scores mean bigger tips, which you can use to buy upgrades for the restaurant or new outfits for your character. As you progress, you'll unlock more ingredients and face more challenging orders!
