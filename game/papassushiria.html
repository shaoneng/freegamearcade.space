How to Play Papa's Sushiria

Welcome to Papa's Sushiria! Your goal is to run a successful sushi restaurant by keeping your customers happy. The gameplay is divided into several key stations where you'll prepare each order with care and precision.

First, a customer will arrive at the Order Station. Pay close attention to their ticket, which details their specific sushi roll and bubble tea preferences. Accuracy is crucial for a good score!

Next, head to the Cook Station. Here, you'll scoop rice into the high-tech rice cooker. Timing is everything â€“ make sure to cook it perfectly without letting it burn. While the rice is cooking, you can prepare for the next steps.

Once the rice is ready, it's time for the Build Station. This is where you bring the sushi to life. Lay down the nori seaweed, evenly spread the cooked rice, add the correct fillings in the right order, and roll it all up neatly. Afterwards, you'll slice the roll and arrange it on a plate with any requested toppings and sauces.

Finally, move to the Drink Station to create the customer's bubble tea. Choose the right tea, add the specified flavor, and top it off with the correct amount of ice and boba pearls. Once the entire order is complete, serve it to the customer. They will rate your performance on speed, accuracy, and presentation. High scores earn you bigger tips, which you can use to upgrade your restaurant and unlock new ingredients!
