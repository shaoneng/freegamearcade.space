Papa's Sushiria

How to Play Papa's Sushiria

Welcome to Papa's Sushiria! Your goal is to keep customers happy by crafting the perfect sushi and bubble tea orders. It all begins when a customer walks in and gives you their order ticket. Pay close attention to every detail, from the rice and fillings to the sauces and drink preferences.

First, head to the Cook Station. Here, you'll use the all-in-one "Sushi Square" cooker. Scoop the right amount of rice, add the correct vinegar, and spread it on the nori seaweed. Timing is crucial for perfectly cooked rice!

Next is the Build Station, where the real artistry happens. Add the fillings and ingredients the customer ordered in the correct sequence. Roll the sushi tightly, then move it to the cutting board to slice it into even pieces. Finally, arrange the sushi on a plate and add any required toppings or sauces for a beautiful presentation.

Don't forget the drink! At the Drink Station, you'll prepare bubble tea. Select the right tea and flavor, then add the correct amount of ice and boba pearls. Mix it up and serve it alongside the sushi.

Once the entire order is ready, serve it to the customer. They will rate you on your timing, accuracy, and presentation. High scores earn you bigger tips, which you can use to buy new clothing for your character and upgrade your restaurant's lobby, making your waiting customers even happier!
