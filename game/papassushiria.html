Papa's Sushiria

How to Play Papa's Sushiria

Your journey as a sushi chef begins when a customer enters the restaurant. Click on them to take their order ticket. Pay close attention to every detail they request, from the type of rice and fillings for their sushi to their specific bubble tea flavor.

Head to the Cook Station to prepare the rice. Scoop the correct amount into the cooker and start the timer. Perfect timing is key here—don't let the rice overcook! Once it's done, spread it over the nori (seaweed) sheet.

Next is the Build Station, where the real artistry happens. Add the fillings and toppings exactly as the customer ordered. Roll the sushi up neatly and then move to the cutting board to slice it into perfect pieces. Arrange it on the plate and add any final sauces.

Don't forget the drink! At the Tea Station, you'll mix the correct tea, flavor, and boba pearls to create the perfect bubble tea accompaniment. Once the complete order is ready, drag the ticket to the customer to serve them.

Your performance is rated on order accuracy, cooking time, and presentation. Higher scores earn you bigger tips, which you can use to buy upgrades for the shop, new decorations, and stylish outfits for your character. As you level up, you'll unlock new ingredients and face more challenging orders!
