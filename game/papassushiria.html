How to Play Papa's Sushiria

Welcome to Papa's Sushiria! Your journey begins after an unfortunate accident leaves you in charge of Papa's newest restaurant. Your goal is to keep the customers happy by crafting delicious sushi and bubble tea. Pay close attention to each order to earn the best tips!

First, a customer will arrive and place their order. Note down all the details carefully, from the type of rice and fillings for their sushi to the specific flavor and ingredients for their bubble tea. Accuracy is key to a high score.

Next, head to the Cook Station to prepare the rice. Scoop the right amount and cook it perfectly. Timing is crucial hereâ€”don't let it overcook! While the rice is cooking, you can prepare for the next steps.

At the Build Station, you'll assemble the sushi. Lay down the nori (seaweed), spread the rice evenly, add the fillings in the correct order, and roll it all up. After rolling, slice the sushi into neat pieces and add any requested toppings or sauces.

Finally, move to the Drink Station to make the customer's bubble tea. Choose the right tea, add the correct flavors, and top it off with ice and boba pearls. Once the entire order is complete, serve it to the customer and await your rating. Better scores earn bigger tips, which you can use to buy upgrades, new outfits, and unlock more ingredients!
