How to Play Papa's Sushiria

Welcome to Papa's Sushiria, where a simple mistake lands you the job of a top sushi chef! Your main goal is to keep your customers happy by preparing their sushi and bubble tea orders perfectly. Success is measured by your speed, accuracy, and presentation.

The process starts when a customer enters. Head to the Order Station to take their order, paying close attention to every detail, from the type of rice and fillings to the sauces and bubble tea preferences. Accuracy here is key to a good score.

Next, it's time to cook. At the Cook Station, you'll prepare the rice. Timing is crucialâ€”don't let it overcook! Once the rice is ready, you'll move to the Build Station. Here, you'll assemble the sushi by laying down the nori, spreading the rice, adding the correct fillings in the right sequence, and rolling it all up neatly.

Many customers also want a beverage. At the Tea Station, you'll mix their custom bubble tea, choosing the right tea, flavor, ice, and boba. After preparing the drink, slice the sushi, arrange it on the plate, add the final toppings, and serve the complete order to your waiting customer.

Customers will rate your service, and high scores will earn you bigger tips. Use your tips to purchase upgrades for the restaurant, new uniforms for your character, and unlock a wider variety of ingredients to attract more customers and tackle new challenges.
