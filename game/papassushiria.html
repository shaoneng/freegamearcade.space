Papa's Sushiria

How to Play Papa's Sushiria

Your journey in Papa's Sushiria begins with an accident: you break the restaurant's lucky cat statue! To pay off your debt, Papa Louie leaves you in charge of his newest venture. Now, it's up to you to become a master sushi chef and keep the customers happy.

The gameplay is divided into several stations. First, at the Order Station, you'll greet your customers and take their detailed orders. Pay close attention to every ingredient they want in their sushi roll and the specific flavors for their bubble tea.

Next, head to the Cook Station. Here, you'll scoop the correct amount of rice and cook it to perfection. Timing is everythingâ€”perfectly cooked rice is the foundation of great sushi. While the rice cooks, you can prepare for the next steps.

At the Build Station, you'll assemble the sushi. Lay down the nori seaweed, spread the freshly cooked rice, add the fillings in the right sequence, and roll it up tightly. Once rolled, you'll slice the sushi into even pieces and add any requested toppings or sauces.

Finally, move to the Drink Station to create the perfect bubble tea. Choose the correct tea and flavor, then add the right amount of ice and boba pearls to complete the order. Once everything is ready, serve it to the customer and get rated on your performance. High scores lead to bigger tips, which you can use to upgrade your restaurant and customize your character!
