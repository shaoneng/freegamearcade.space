How to Play Papa's Sushiria

Welcome to Papa's Sushiria, where your journey from customer to sushi chef begins with a bang! Here's how to master the art of sushi and bubble tea:

Take Orders: When a customer arrives, pay close attention to their order. Every detail matters, from the type of rice and fillings to their preferred sauces and bubble tea specifics.

Cook the Rice: Head to the rice station. Scoop the correct amount of rice and start the cooker. Timing is crucial â€“ ensure it's cooked perfectly and doesn't burn!

Build Sushi Rolls: Lay out the seaweed, spread the rice evenly, and carefully add the customer's chosen fillings. Roll the sushi tightly, then slice it precisely. Don't forget to add any requested toppings or sauces for presentation.

Prepare Bubble Teas: Most customers will want a bubble tea. At the drink station, select the right tea base, flavor, and add the correct amount of ice and boba.

Serve and Earn: Once the order is complete, hand it over to the customer. They'll rate you based on accuracy, timing, and neatness. High scores lead to bigger tips, which you can use to purchase upgrades, furniture, and snazzy uniforms to improve your restaurant. As you level up, more exciting ingredients and demanding customers will be unlocked!
