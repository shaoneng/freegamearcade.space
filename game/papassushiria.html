Papa's Sushiria

How to Play Papa's Sushiria

Welcome to Papa's Sushiria, where a simple visit to a new sushi spot turns into an unexpected job! After accidentally breaking the restaurant's lucky cat statue, you're tasked with running the place to pay off your debt. It's time to roll up your sleeves and master the art of sushi making.

Your journey begins when a customer walks in. Pay close attention to their order slip, as every detail matters—from the type of rice and fillings to the sauces and toppings. Once you have the order, head to the cook station to prepare the rice. Perfect timing is crucial; you need to cook it just right without letting it burn.

Next is the build station, where the real fun happens. Lay down the nori (seaweed), spread the rice, add the correct fillings in the right order, and roll it all up neatly. After rolling, you'll slice the sushi into perfect pieces and add any final sauces or toppings. Don't forget the drink station! Many customers will also order a custom bubble tea, so you'll need to mix the right flavors, tea, and boba to complete their meal.

Once the order is complete, serve it to the customer and await their verdict. You'll be graded on your timing, accuracy, and presentation. High scores earn you bigger tips, which can be spent on new outfits, furniture for the lobby, and handy upgrades for your stations. As you level up, you'll unlock new ingredients and face more challenging customers, keeping every day exciting and wonderfully chaotic!
