Papa's Sushiria

How to Play Papa's Sushiria

In Papa's Sushiria, your goal is to become a master sushi chef by running a busy restaurant. The game is controlled entirely with your mouse. It all begins when a customer enters and gives you their order. Pay close attention to every detail on the order ticket, from the type of rice and fillings to the sauces and bubble tea flavor.

First, head to the Cook Station. Here, you'll scoop the correct amount of rice into the cooker. Timing is crucial â€“ let it cook until it's perfect, but don't let it sit too long or it will burn! Once the rice is done, it's time to move to the Build Station.

At the Build Station, you'll assemble the sushi. Lay down the nori (seaweed), spread the perfectly cooked rice, and add all the fillings the customer requested in the correct order. Roll it up neatly, then slice it into even pieces. Finally, add any requested toppings or sauces to complete the dish. Precision and presentation are key to a good score!

Many customers will also order a refreshing bubble tea. At the Tea Station, you'll select the right tea, add the correct flavor, and pour in the right amount of ice and boba pearls. This mini-game is a fun and fast-paced part of the order.

Once the sushi and tea are ready, serve the order to your customer. They will rate you based on your performance at each station. Higher scores earn you bigger tips, which you can use to buy new clothing, furniture for the lobby, and helpful upgrades for your restaurant. As you progress, you'll unlock more ingredients and face more challenging orders!
