Papa's Sushiria

How to Play Papa's Sushiria

Your adventure in Papa's Sushiria begins with a bang—literally! After accidentally breaking the restaurant's lucky cat statue, you're put in charge to pay off the debt. It's time to roll up your sleeves and learn the art of sushi making.

Your first task is to take customer orders at the Order Station. Pay close attention to every detail, from the type of rice and fillings to the sauces and toppings. Each customer will also request a unique bubble tea, so make sure you note down their preferences for tea, flavor, and boba.

Next, head to the Cook Station to prepare the rice. Timing is crucial here—cook it just right, then spread it evenly on the nori. At the Build Station, you'll layer the fillings and toppings precisely as ordered, roll the sushi tightly, and slice it into perfect pieces.

Before serving, visit the Tea Station to create the custom bubble tea. Mix the right flavors, add the correct amount of ice and bubbles, and get it ready. Finally, present the complete order to your customer. They will rate you on accuracy and presentation. High scores lead to bigger tips, which you can use to buy upgrades for the shop, new outfits, and unlock new ingredients to keep things exciting!
