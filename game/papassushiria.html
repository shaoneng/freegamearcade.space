Papa's Sushiria

How to Play Papa's Sushiria

Welcome to Papa's Sushiria, where a simple mistake lands you the job of a lifetime! Your goal is to manage the new sushi restaurant, keeping customers happy by preparing their orders perfectly.

The process starts when a customer walks in. Carefully take their order at the Order Station, paying close attention to every detail—the type of rice, the fillings for the sushi, the toppings, and their bubble tea choice. Accuracy is key!

Next, head to the Cook Station. Here, you'll cook the rice. Scoop the right amount and start the cooker. Timing is crucial; don't let it overcook! Once done, spread it on the nori (seaweed) and get ready for the fun part.

At the Build Station, you'll assemble the sushi roll. Add the fillings in the correct order, roll it up tightly, and then slice it into perfect pieces. Finish it off with any requested sauces or toppings to make it look delicious.

Finally, move to the Drink Station to prepare the customer's bubble tea. Choose the right tea flavor, add ice, and pour in the boba pearls. It's a quick mini-game that completes the order.

Serve the complete order to your customer and get rated on your performance. High scores earn you bigger tips, which you can use to buy upgrades for the restaurant, new decorations, or cool outfits for your character. As you level up, you'll unlock more ingredients and face more challenging orders!
