Papa's Wingeria

How to Play Papa's Wingeria

Welcome to Papa's Wingeria! Your goal is to run a bustling wing restaurant and keep your customers happy. The game is easy to control: use your mouse on a computer to click, drag, and drop ingredients and tools. On a mobile device or tablet, simply tap the screen to interact with the game.

Your workday is divided across four key areas. Start at the Order Station to take down each customer's specific wing order. Next, head to the Fry Station to cook the chicken wings to crispy perfection. Once fried, move to the Sauce Station to toss the wings in a variety of delicious sauces. Finally, assemble the complete meal at the Build Station, carefully arranging the sauced wings with sides and dips exactly as requested.

As you successfully serve customers, you'll earn tips that you can use to improve your restaurant. Purchase upgrades for your kitchen equipment to cook faster and more efficiently. You can also buy new furniture and decorations for the lobby, which will make your customers happier and more patient. The better you do, the more new ingredients and customers you'll unlock!

Pro-tip: Don't forget to use your three tickets at the end of each day to play fun mini-games! You can win cool prizes like new clothing for your character and furniture for your restaurant.
