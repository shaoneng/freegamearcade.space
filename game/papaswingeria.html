Papa's Wingeria

How to Play Papa's Wingeria

Welcome to Starlight City! Your goal in Papa's Wingeria is to run a successful wing restaurant by keeping your customers happy. The game is controlled entirely with your mouse on a computer or by tapping the screen on a mobile device. Simply click, drag, and drop to manage every aspect of your new job.

Your workday is split across four distinct areas: the Order Station, Fry Station, Sauce Station, and Build Station. First, take a customer's order at the Order Station. Then, head to the Fry Station to drop the correct number of wings into the fryer and cook them until they're perfectly crispy. Next, move to the Sauce Station to toss the cooked wings in the customer's chosen sauce. Finally, at the Build Station, carefully arrange the sauced wings, sides, and dips on the plate exactly as requested on the order ticket.

As you successfully serve customers, you'll earn tips and points. Use your hard-earned cash to buy upgrades for the restaurant, like better fryers, new sauces, or decorations for the lobby. Upgrades can help you cook faster and make your customers happier, leading to even better scores and bigger tips. Don't forget to play the daily mini-games after your shift for a chance to win cool prizes for your character!
