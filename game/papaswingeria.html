How to Play Papa's Wingeria

Welcome to Starlight City! In Papa's Wingeria, you're in charge of a bustling chicken wing restaurant. Your mission is to fry, sauce, and plate delicious wings to keep your hungry customers happy and coming back for more. You can play as Chuck, Mandi, or create your very own custom character.

The controls are simple and intuitive. On a computer, use your mouse to select, drag, and drop ingredients and tools. If you're on a mobile device or tablet, just tap the screen to interact with the game.
