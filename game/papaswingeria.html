How to Play Papa's Wingeria

Welcome to Papa's Wingeria! Your goal is to run a successful wing restaurant by satisfying the unique cravings of your customers. Whether you play as Chuck, Mandi, or a custom worker, your journey to becoming a wing master starts here.

The game is controlled easily with your mouse on a computer or by tapping on a touch screen. You'll manage your tasks across four distinct stations to create the perfect plate of wings.

First, head to the Order Station to take each customer's detailed order. Pay close attention to the number of wings, the type of sauces, and the arrangement on the plate. Next, move to the Fry Station to cook the wings to perfectionâ€”don't let them overcook! After frying, take them to the Sauce Station to toss them in the exact sauces the customer requested. Finally, at the Build Station, carefully arrange the wings, sides, and dips on the plate exactly as shown on the order ticket.

As you successfully serve customers, you'll earn tips and points. Use your money to purchase upgrades for your restaurant to improve your speed and efficiency, and decorate the lobby to keep customers happier. At the end of each day, you can play fun mini-games to win prizes for your character and restaurant!
