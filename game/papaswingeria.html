Papa's Wingeria

How to Play Papa's Wingeria

Welcome to Starlight City! Your goal in Papa's Wingeria is to run a successful chicken wing restaurant by keeping your customers happy and serving up perfect orders. Whether you choose to play as Chuck, Mandi, or your own custom character, your multitasking skills will be put to the test!

The controls are simple and intuitive. On a desktop or laptop, use your mouse to click, drag, and drop ingredients and tickets. If you're playing on a mobile device or tablet, just tap the screen to interact with the game elements.

Your workday is split across four distinct areas:

  • Order Station: Greet your customers and carefully write down their specific wing orders.
  • Fry Station: Drag the correct number of wings into the fryer and cook them until they are perfectly golden. Don't overcook them!
  • Sauce Station: Once fried, toss the wings in the requested sauces, making sure they are evenly coated.
  • Build Station: Carefully arrange the sauced wings, sides, and dips on the plate exactly as the customer ordered for the best score.

As you successfully complete orders, you'll earn tips and points. Use your earnings to buy upgrades for your restaurant, which can help you cook faster and make customers happier. You can also purchase new clothing and accessories for your character. At the end of each day, you get to play fun mini-games for a chance to win cool prizes!
