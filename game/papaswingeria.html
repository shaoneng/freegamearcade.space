Papa's Wingeria

How to Play Papa's Wingeria

Your goal is to successfully run Papa's wing restaurant by satisfying every customer. On a computer, use your mouse to click, drag, and drop ingredients and interact with the game. If you're on a mobile device or tablet, simply tap the screen to play.

The gameplay is divided into four main areas. Start at the Order Station to take each customer's specific request. Next, head to the Fry Station to cook the chicken wings to the correct doneness. After frying, move to the Sauce Station to toss the wings in the requested sauces. Finally, go to the Build Station to carefully arrange the wings, sides, and dips on the plate exactly as ordered.

As you successfully serve customers, you'll earn tips that you can use to upgrade your restaurant's equipment and decorations. Better equipment helps you work faster, and a nicely decorated lobby makes customers happier and more patient. Don't forget to play the daily mini-games to win cool prizes for your character!
