Papa's Wingeria

How to Play Papa's Wingeria

Your goal in Papa's Wingeria is to run a bustling chicken wing restaurant in Starlight City. You'll take on the role of Chuck, Mandi, or a custom-designed character. Your mission is to keep your customers happy by preparing their wing orders perfectly and efficiently.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use your mouse to click, drag, and drop all items in the game. From taking order tickets to tossing wings in sauce, every action is performed with a simple mouse movement.

You'll manage your tasks across four distinct areas. Start at the Order Station to take each customer's specific order. Next, head to the Fry Station to cook the wings to perfection. Then, move to the Sauce Station to toss them in the correct sauces. Finally, at the Build Station, you'll carefully arrange the wings, sides, and dips on the plate exactly as requested.

As you successfully serve customers, you'll earn tips that you can use to upgrade your restaurant. Buy new fryers, sauces, decorations, and posters to improve your efficiency and make the Wingeria a more pleasant place for customers. A happy customer leaves a bigger tip!

Pro Tip: At the end of each workday, you get three tickets to play one of the fun mini-games. You can win cool prizes for your character and decorations for your restaurant lobby!
