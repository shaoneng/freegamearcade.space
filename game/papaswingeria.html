Papa's Wingeria

How to Play Papa's Wingeria

Welcome to Starlight City! In Papa's Wingeria, your goal is to manage a bustling wing restaurant all by yourself. The controls are simple: use your mouse on a computer to click, drag, and interact with all the game elements. On a phone or tablet, just tap the screen.

Your work is split across four distinct stations. First, take a customer's order at the Order Station. Pay close attention to the number of wings, the type of sauce, and the arrangement of sides they want.

Next, head to the Fry Station. Drop the correct number of wings into the fryer and cook them until the timer hits the green line for a perfect cook. Don't overcook them!

Once fried, take the wings to the Sauce Station. Pour the correct sauce over the wings and tumble them in the bowl until they're evenly coated. Finally, at the Build Station, carefully arrange the sauced wings, sides, and dips on the plate exactly as the customer ordered. The closer you get to their order, the better your score and the bigger your tip!

Use your hard-earned tips to buy upgrades for your restaurant. Better equipment will help you cook faster, while decorations will keep your customers happy while they wait. Good luck becoming the top wing chef in town!
