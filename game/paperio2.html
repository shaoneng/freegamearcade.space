Paper io 2

How to Play Paper io 2

Welcome to the fast-paced and colorful arena of Paper io 2! The main goal is to claim as much territory as possible by enclosing areas with your colored trail. You start as a small square on a large shared map. Move your square around to create a line behind you.

To capture territory, you must draw a complete loop that starts from and returns to your existing colored area. The space inside your loop will then be filled with your color, expanding your domain. The larger the loop, the more points you score, but be careful—this also increases your risk.

The key to survival is protecting your tail. While you are outside your territory creating a line, your tail is vulnerable. If another player crosses your line, you are eliminated from the game. You can use this to your advantage! Cross the tails of other players to eliminate them and clear the way for your expansion. Play safe by staying close to your base, or risk it all for huge territorial gains!

Controls are simple: use your mouse or finger (on touch devices) to guide your character's direction. The movement is smooth and intuitive, allowing you to focus on your strategy.
