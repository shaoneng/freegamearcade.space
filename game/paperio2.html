Paper io 2

How to Play Paper io 2

The goal in Paper io 2 is to expand your territory and conquer the entire map. You start with a small colored area. To expand, move your character out of your base to draw a line using your mouse or by swiping on your screen.

Once you return to your own territory, the area you've enclosed with your line will be captured and filled with your color. The bigger the loop, the more points you get and the higher you climb on the leaderboard!

Be careful! While you are outside your base, your line is vulnerable. If another player runs into your line, you will be eliminated. You can use this to your advantage by cutting off other players' lines to defeat them. You can also conquer their territory by drawing over it. Stay sharp, expand your domain, and dominate the arena!
