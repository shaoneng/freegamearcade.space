Paper io 2

How to Play Paper io 2

Welcome to the colorful and competitive world of Paper io 2! The goal is simple: capture as much territory as you can and become the biggest player on the map. You start with a small colored area. To expand, you need to venture out into the white, unclaimed space.

Use your mouse or finger to guide your character. As you move, you will leave a trail behind you. To claim a new piece of land, create a complete loop by returning to your existing territory. The area you've enclosed will then be filled with your color.

Be careful! While you are outside your territory, your trail is vulnerable. If another player runs into your trail, you will be eliminated from the game. The key is to make quick, strategic captures and return to the safety of your base as soon as possible.

You can also play offensively. To eliminate an opponent, simply cross their trail while they are expanding. This strategic element adds a thrilling layer of risk and reward. Conquer the map, defend your space, and climb to the top of the leaderboard!
