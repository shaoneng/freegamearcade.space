Paper io 2

How to Play Paper io 2

Welcome to the colorful and competitive world of Paper io 2! The objective is simple: conquer as much territory as you can and become the biggest player on the map.

To play, use your mouse or finger to control your character. Start by moving out from your colored territory. As you move, you'll leave a trail behind you. To capture a new area, simply draw a line and loop back to your own territory. The area you've enclosed will then become yours!

Be careful! While you are outside your base creating a trail, you are vulnerable. If another player runs into your line before you've connected it back, you will be eliminated. The same rule applies to them—you can take out opponents by strategically crossing their trails. Stay alert, defend your land, and conquer the arena!
