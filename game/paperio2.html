Paper io 2

How to Play Paper io 2

Welcome to the colorful and competitive world of Paper io 2! The objective of this exciting multiplayer game is simple yet challenging: expand your territory and become the largest player on the map.

To claim your territory, start by drawing lines from your existing colored area into the white, unclaimed space. The key is to successfully return to your own colored territory, completing a loop. The enclosed space will then turn your color, adding to your growing empire.

Be careful while drawing! If an opponent crosses your line before you complete your loop, you will be eliminated from the game. You can also eliminate other players by strategically cutting their lines while they are expanding their territory. This is a risky but rewarding move.

Continue to expand your area, strategically cutting off opponents and defending your own land. The more territory you claim, the higher you climb on the leaderboard. Play smart, be quick, and outmaneuver your rivals to conquer the Paper io 2 arena!
