Paper io 2

How to Play Paper io 2

Welcome to the fast-paced, colorful world of Paper.io 2! The objective is simple but challenging: conquer as much territory as you can and become the biggest on the map.

Control your colored block and draw lines to create a closed loop back to your existing territory. The area you enclose will turn into your color. Be strategic and expand your empire, but watch out for other players!

While you are outside your territory drawing a line, your trail is vulnerable. If an opponent crosses your trail, you will be eliminated. You can use this same tactic to eliminate others. The key is to claim areas quickly while protecting your own trail from being captured. Good luck!

You can play Paper.io 2 for free without any downloads on your desktop, mobile, tablet, or Chromebook.
