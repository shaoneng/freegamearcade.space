Paper io 2

How to Play Paper io 2

The goal in Paper io 2 is to conquer as much territory as possible. You start with a small colored square on a large, shared map.

Use your mouse (on desktop) or finger (on mobile/tablet) to guide your block. As you move out from your colored base, you will draw a line. To capture new territory, you must create a closed loop by returning to your own color. The area you've enclosed will then become part of your domain.

Be strategic and cautious! While you are outside your territory drawing a line, your tail is vulnerable. If another player crosses your tail, you will be eliminated from the game. Similarly, you can eliminate other players by intercepting their tails while they are expanding.

You are safe only when you are within your own colored territory. Keep expanding, outsmart your opponents, and try to cover 100% of the map!
