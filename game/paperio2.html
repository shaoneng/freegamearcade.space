How to Play Paper io 2

The goal in Paper io 2 is simple yet challenging: conquer the largest territory on the map! You start with a small colored area that is your base.

Use your mouse on a desktop or your finger on a mobile device to control your character. Move out of your base to start drawing a line. To capture new territory, you must create a closed loop by returning to any part of your colored area. The space you've enclosed will then become yours.

Stay alert, expand your domain wisely, and aim for 100% domination of the map to become the champion!
