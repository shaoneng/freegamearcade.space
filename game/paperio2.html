Paper io 2

How to Play Paper io 2

Welcome to the fast-paced and colorful arena of Paper io 2! The goal is simple yet challenging: conquer as much territory as possible. You control a small square that leaves a colored trail behind it.

Use your mouse or finger to guide your square. Move out from your existing territory to start drawing a line. To capture new land, you must create a complete loop that connects back to your own colored area. The enclosed space will then become part of your domain.

Be careful! While you are outside your territory, your trail is vulnerable. If another player runs into your trail, you will be eliminated. Likewise, you can eliminate opponents by crossing their trails. The key to victory is a balance of aggressive expansion and careful defense. Claim your space, eliminate rivals, and aim for the top of the leaderboard!
