Paper io 2

How to Play Paper io 2

Welcome to the competitive and colorful arena of Paper io 2! Your goal is simple: capture as much territory as you can and become the leader of the game.

Control your character using your mouse or the arrow keys on your keyboard. Start from your colored base and move into the white, neutral space to draw a line. To capture an area, you must create a closed loop that connects back to your existing territory. Everything inside that loop will be filled with your color!

Be strategic and careful! While you are outside your safe zone drawing a line, your tail is exposed and vulnerable. If another player crosses your tail, you will be eliminated from the game. Similarly, you can eliminate opponents by cutting across their exposed tails. Watch out, as running into your own tail will also result in elimination. Balance bold expansion with cautious defense to climb the leaderboard and dominate the map!
