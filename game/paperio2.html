Paper io 2

How to Play Paper io 2

Welcome to the colorful and competitive world of Paper.io 2! Your mission is to claim as much territory on the map as possible. Control your block to draw lines and create closed loops, converting the enclosed space into your color.

The key strategy is to expand your territory while defending it from other players. Be careful when you are outside your colored zone—if an opponent crosses your trail, you are eliminated! You can use this same tactic to eliminate your rivals and take over their space.

Paper.io 2, created by Voodoo, is a safe and fun game for all ages that you can play for free on your desktop, mobile, or tablet without any downloads. Balance aggressive expansion with careful defense to dominate the leaderboard!
