Paper io 2

How to Play Paper io 2

The goal in Paper io 2 is simple yet challenging: conquer as much territory as possible. You control a colorful square that leaves a trail behind it. To capture an area, move out from your existing territory, draw a shape on the blank white grid, and return to your colored zone. The area you've enclosed will then become part of your domain.

Strategy is key to survival and dominance. While expanding your territory is the main objective, you must also be mindful of your opponents. When you are outside your colored zone, your trail is vulnerable. If another player runs into your trail, you are eliminated from the game. Use this to your advantage by cutting off other players' trails to defeat them.

You can also capture territory from other players by drawing over their colored areas. Be bold but cautious. A bigger territory means more points and a higher rank on the leaderboard, but it can also be harder to defend. Balance aggressive expansion with defensive maneuvering to become the ultimate ruler of the paper world!
