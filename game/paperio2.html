Paper io 2

How to Play Paper io 2

Welcome to the fast-paced, territorial battle of Paper io 2! The main objective is to conquer as much of the map as possible by claiming it with your color. Control your character using your mouse or finger to move around the arena.

To claim new territory, move out from your colored zone to draw a line. Once you return to your own territory, the area you enclosed will be captured and filled with your color. The key is to make big loops to claim large areas, but be careful!

While you are drawing a line, your tail is vulnerable. If another player runs into your line, you will be eliminated from the game. Likewise, you can eliminate other players by cutting their lines while they are exposed. Always be aware of your surroundings and protect your tail to survive and dominate the leaderboard!
