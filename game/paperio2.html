Paper io 2

How to Play Paper io 2

Welcome to the fast-paced and colorful arena of Paper io 2! The objective is simple: conquer as much territory as you can by claiming it with your unique color. Use your mouse, keyboard arrows, or finger to control your character and move around the map.

To capture an area, you must move out from your existing territory, draw a line, and return to your colored zone. The area you've enclosed will then become yours! The bigger the loop, the more points you score.

Be strategic and cautious! While you are outside your territory drawing a line, you are vulnerable. If another player runs into your line, you will be eliminated from the game. Similarly, you can eliminate other players by crashing into their unfinished lines. Stay safe, capture territory, and aim to cover the entire board!
