Paper io 2

How to Play Paper io 2

Welcome to the colorful and competitive arena of Paper io 2! The goal is simple: claim as much territory as you can. Control your colored block using your mouse or finger, moving around the map to draw a line.

To capture new territory, you must create a closed loop. Start from your existing colored area, venture out into the white space, and return to your territory. The area you've enclosed will then turn into your color, expanding your domain.

Be strategic and cautious! While you are drawing a line outside your territory, you are vulnerable. If another player runs into your line before you've closed the loop, you will be eliminated from the game. Stay vigilant and protect your tail!

You can also play offensively by cutting the lines of other players. If you see an opponent drawing a line, intercept it to eliminate them and claim a tactical advantage. The last player standing, or the one with the most territory, wins!
