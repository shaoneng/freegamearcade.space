Paper Minecraft

How to Play Paper Minecraft

Welcome to Paper Minecraft, the 2D version of the legendary sandbox game! Your main objective is to survive and thrive in a blocky, procedurally generated world. Use your creativity to craft tools, build structures, and explore the vast landscape.

To begin your adventure, click the "New Game" button from the main menu. If you're returning, you can use "Load Game" to pick up right where you left off. Once in the game, you'll need to gather resources like wood and stone to craft your first tools. Keep an eye on your hunger bar; you'll need to hunt animals for meat to avoid starving.

The controls are simple and intuitive: use the WASD keys to move and jump, and use your mouse to break blocks and place items. The E key opens your inventory and crafting menu, where you can combine resources to create everything from pickaxes and swords to crafting tables and furnaces. Explore, build, and survive!
