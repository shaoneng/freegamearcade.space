Paper Minecraft

How to Play Paper Minecraft

Welcome to the 2D world of Paper Minecraft! To start your adventure, click 'New Game' from the main menu. You can customize your world type and character skin before you begin. If you're a returning player, simply use the 'Load Game' option to pick up right where you left off.

Paper Minecraft offers two distinct game modes. In Survival Mode, your objective is to stay alive. You must gather resources like wood and stone by mining blocks, craft tools and weapons, hunt animals for food to manage your hunger, and build a shelter to protect yourself from hostile mobs that appear at night.

In Creative Mode, you are free from all constraints. You have unlimited resources, you can't take damage, and you can fly. This mode is perfect for letting your imagination run wild and building magnificent structures without the pressure of survival. The controls are simple: use the WASD keys to move and jump, and use your mouse to mine blocks and place them. Press the E key to open your inventory and access the crafting grid, and use the F key to eat food.
