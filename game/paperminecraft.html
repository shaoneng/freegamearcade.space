Paper Minecraft

How to Play Paper Minecraft

Welcome to the 2D world of Paper Minecraft! Your main goal is to survive and thrive by crafting, building, and exploring. Begin your adventure by starting a 'New Game' from the main menu. You can choose between different game modes, like Survival for a challenge or Creative for unlimited building possibilities.

In Survival mode, you'll need to manage your hunger by hunting animals for food. Gather resources like wood from trees and stone from the ground by breaking blocks. Use these materials to craft essential tools, weapons, and a shelter to protect yourself from creatures that come out at night.

The controls are straightforward: use the WASD keys to move and jump, and use your mouse to mine blocks and place items. The E key opens your inventory and crafting menu, where you can combine resources to create new items. Let your imagination run wild and see what you can build!
