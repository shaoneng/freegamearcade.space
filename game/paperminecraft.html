How to Play Paper Minecraft

Welcome to Paper Minecraft, the beloved 2D fan-made version of the iconic sandbox game! Getting started is simple. From the main menu, click "New Game" to generate a brand new world and begin your adventure. If you're returning, just select "Load Game" to pick up right where you left off.

The game offers different modes to suit your playstyle. In Survival mode, your primary goal is to stay alive. You'll need to explore the world, gather resources like wood and stone, and craft tools. Keep an eye on your hunger barâ€”hunt animals for meat to prevent starvation and weakness. As night falls, be prepared to defend yourself against hostile creatures!

If you prefer unlimited freedom, switch to Creative mode. Here, you have access to all the blocks and items in the game, allowing you to build magnificent structures, vast cities, or anything else you can imagine without the pressure of survival. The choice is yours: survive against the odds or build your dream world, one block at a time.
