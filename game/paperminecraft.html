Paper Minecraft

How to Play Paper Minecraft

Welcome to Paper Minecraft, the beloved 2D version of the legendary sandbox game! Your adventure begins in a randomly generated world full of possibilities. Use your creativity to survive, build, and thrive.

When you start a new game, you can choose between different modes, including the classic Survival and the boundless Creative mode. In Survival, you'll need to gather resources by mining blocks and chopping trees. Use these materials to craft tools, weapons, and a shelter to protect yourself from hostile creatures that come out at night. Don't forget to hunt for food to keep your hunger bar full!

If you prefer pure building, Creative mode is for you. You'll have access to unlimited resources and the ability to fly, allowing you to construct magnificent structures without any limitations. The controls are simple: use the WASD keys to move your character and the mouse to break and place blocks. Press 'E' to open your inventory and crafting menu. If you need to take a break, your game can be saved and loaded later, so you never lose your progress.
