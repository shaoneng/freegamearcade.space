Paper Minecraft

How to Play Paper Minecraft

Welcome to the 2D block-building universe of Paper Minecraft! Your adventure begins right from the main menu. If you're new, click "New Game" to generate a unique world and choose your preferred game mode and character skin. If you're a returning player, simply hit "Load Game" to pick up where you left off.

The controls are intuitive and familiar to fans of the genre:

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your character left, right, jump, and crouch.
  • Mouse: Move your cursor to aim. Click and hold to dig or mine blocks. Click to place blocks or use items.
  • E Key: Open your inventory and crafting menu.
  • Number Keys (1-9): Select items from your hotbar.
  • F Key: Eat food to restore hunger.

In Survival mode, your primary goal is to stay alive. You'll need to gather resources like wood and stone to craft tools, weapons, and a shelter. Keep an eye on your health and hunger bars—hunt animals for food and avoid hostile mobs that come out at night. In Creative mode, you have unlimited resources and the freedom to build anything you can imagine without the threat of danger. Let your creativity run wild!
