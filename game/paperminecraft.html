Paper Minecraft

How to Play Paper Minecraft

Welcome to the 2D world of Paper Minecraft, a brilliant fan-made game inspired by the legendary original! Your adventure begins by choosing your path: will you brave the challenges of Survival mode or unleash your imagination in Creative mode?

In Survival mode, your primary goal is to stay alive. You'll need to punch trees to gather wood, mine for stone and other precious resources, and craft tools to make your work easier. Build a shelter to protect yourself from monsters that come out at night, and don't forget to hunt for food to keep your hunger bar full. Every block you place and every tool you craft is a step towards mastering your environment.

If building is your passion, Creative mode is your paradise. Here, you have access to unlimited resources, the ability to fly, and immunity from all dangers. Let your creativity run wild and construct anything you can dream of, from a cozy cottage to a sprawling castle, without any limitations.

Controls:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Mine / Place Block: Left Mouse Click
  • Open Inventory / Craft: E
  • Select Item/Block: 1-9 Keys or Mouse Wheel
  • Eat / Hold Item: Hold Left Mouse Button
