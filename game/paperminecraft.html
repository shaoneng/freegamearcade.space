Paper Minecraft

How to Play Paper Minecraft

Welcome to Paper Minecraft, the beloved 2D fan-made version of the iconic sandbox game! In this blocky world, your imagination is the only limit. You can choose to survive against the elements and creatures of the night, or unleash your creativity in a peaceful building mode. Gather resources, craft powerful tools, and build anything from a simple shelter to a sprawling castle.

To begin your adventure, simply click "New Game" from the main menu. You can customize your world and character to your liking. If you're returning to a previous world, select "Load Game" to pick up right where you left off.

The controls are intuitive. Use the WASD or Arrow keys to move your character. The Mouse is your primary tool for mining blocks and placing new ones. Press E to open your inventory and access the crafting menu, where you can combine materials to create new items. Use the Spacebar to jump over obstacles.

In Survival mode, keeping your hunger bar full is crucial. Hunt animals for food and press F to eat. As night falls, hostile mobs will appear, so be sure to craft weapons and build a shelter to stay safe. In Creative mode, you have unlimited resources and can fly, allowing you to focus purely on building your dream creations without any threats.
