How to Play Paper Minecraft

Welcome to Paper Minecraft, the 2D version of the legendary sandbox game! To begin your adventure, select 'New Game' from the main menu. You can choose between Survival mode, where you must gather resources and fend off dangers, or Creative mode, where you have unlimited resources to build anything you can imagine. If you're returning, simply click 'Load Game' to pick up where you left off.

Use the WASD keys or Arrow Keys to move your character around the world. Use your mouse to interact with the environment: click and hold on blocks like trees or dirt to mine them for resources. Once you've gathered materials, press the E key to open your inventory and crafting menu. Here, you can combine resources to create tools, weapons, and building blocks.

In Survival mode, keep an eye on your health and hunger bars. Hunt animals for food to replenish your hunger, and build a shelter to protect yourself from monsters that appear at night.
