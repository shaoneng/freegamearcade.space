Paper Minecraft

How to Play Paper Minecraft

Welcome to Paper Minecraft, the beloved 2D fan-made version of the iconic Minecraft game. In this blocky world, your creativity and survival skills are put to the test. You can choose between two main modes: Survival, where you must gather resources, craft tools, and fend off dangers to stay alive, or Creative, where you have unlimited resources to build magnificent structures without any threats.

Getting started is simple. From the main menu, select 'New Game' to generate a brand new world. You can customize your character's skin and choose your preferred game mode. If you've played before and saved your progress, simply click 'Load Game' to pick up right where you left off.

The controls are intuitive. Use the WASD keys to move your character and the Mouse to look around, break blocks, and place items. Your inventory is key; open it with the E key to manage your resources and access the crafting grid. Combine materials to craft everything from basic tools like pickaxes and swords to complex structures and mechanisms. Remember to eat to keep your hunger bar full in Survival mode!

Paper Minecraft was created by the talented Griffpatch. If you enjoy the freedom of building and exploring, you might also like to try Minecraft Classic for a pure, creative experience. Explore our other crafting games for even more adventures. What will you build first?
