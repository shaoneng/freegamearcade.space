How to Play Paper Minecraft

Welcome to Paper Minecraft, the beloved 2D fan-made version of the iconic sandbox game! Whether you're a veteran miner or a first-time builder, getting started is easy. Use the WASD or Arrow keys to move your character around the world. The mouse is your primary tool for interacting with the environmentâ€”click and hold to break blocks and gather resources like wood, stone, and dirt.

To open your inventory and access the crafting menu, press the E key. Here, you can combine resources to create tools, weapons, and building materials. Start by crafting a workbench for more advanced recipes. In Survival mode, your goal is to stay alive! Keep an eye on your health and hunger bars. Hunt animals for food and build a shelter to protect yourself from hostile mobs that come out at night.

If you prefer unlimited freedom, choose Creative mode when starting a new game. In this mode, you have access to every block and item, allowing you to build magnificent structures without the need to gather resources. To start your adventure, simply click "New Game" from the main menu and select your preferred mode and world type. If you're returning, use "Load Game" to pick up right where you left off.
