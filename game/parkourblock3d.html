Parkour Block 3D

How to Play Parkour Block 3D

Welcome to the ultimate test of agility and precision in Parkour Block 3D! Your objective is straightforward but challenging: guide your character across a series of blocky platforms to reach the purple portal at the end of each stage. Be warned, the floor is literally lava! One wrong step or a slip from a platform will send you back to the beginning of the level.

Before you make your first jump, take a moment to survey the path ahead. Each of the 35 levels presents a unique layout of obstacles. Use your movement controls to run and jump from one block to another. The key to success is managing your speed—moving too fast can cause you to slide right off the slippery platforms. Aim your jumps carefully to land securely in the center of the blocks.

To complete a level, you must successfully leap into the large purple portal. For an extra challenge, try to finish each level as quickly as possible and with the fewest falls to earn a three-star rating. Good luck, and don't look down!
