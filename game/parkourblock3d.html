Parkour Block 3D

How to Play Parkour Block 3D

The main objective in Parkour Block 3D is simple yet challenging: guide your character across a series of block platforms to reach the purple portal at the end of each level. Be warned, the floor is lava! One wrong step or slip will send you back to the start.

Use your movement controls to run and jump. Before you start, it's a good idea to look at the path ahead and plan your route. The platforms can be slippery, so controlling your speed is crucial. Moving too fast might cause you to slide off the edge. Aim for precise landings in the middle of the blocks.

To complete a level, you must successfully jump into the large purple portal. The game features 35 challenging levels to conquer. For a perfect score, try to finish each level as quickly as possible and with the fewest attempts to earn three stars. Good luck, and don't fall!
