How to Play Parkour Block 3D

Your goal in Parkour Block 3D is to guide your character across a series of floating blocks to reach the safety of the purple portal at the end of each level. But be warnedâ€”the floor is literally lava! If you misjudge a jump or slip off a platform, you'll fall into the molten sea below and have to restart the level.

Before you start running, take a moment to look at the path ahead. The platforms can be slippery, so controlling your speed is crucial. Run too fast, and you might slide right off the edge. You must find the perfect balance of speed and precision for each jump.

Use your movement controls to run, jump, and carefully navigate the course. To make precise jumps, you can use your character's hand to help guide your direction. The game features 35 challenging levels to conquer. The faster you complete a level with fewer mistakes, the more stars you'll earn!
