How to Play Parkour Block 3D

Your objective in Parkour Block 3D is simple yet challenging: navigate your character across a series of floating block platforms to reach the purple portal at the end of each level. But be warnedâ€”the floor is a sea of molten lava! One wrong step or a slip from a block will send you back to the start.

Use your keyboard's WASD or arrow keys to move and the spacebar to jump. On mobile devices, use the on-screen joystick and jump button. Control your camera with the mouse (or by swiping on mobile) to aim your leaps precisely before you commit.

The platforms can be slippery, so controlling your speed is crucial. A reckless dash might send you sliding off the edge. Find the perfect balance between a running start and careful steps to conquer all 35 levels. Aim to complete each stage quickly and with the fewest falls to earn a perfect three-star rating!
