Penalty Challenge

How to Play Penalty Challenge

Penalty Challenge puts you in the heart of a high-stakes soccer shootout, where you'll alternate between being the striker and the goalkeeper. Your goal is to outscore your opponent and advance through the tournament to claim the World Cup trophy!

Taking a Penalty: When it's your turn to shoot, use your mouse to aim. The angle and intensity of your kick are determined by how you move the mouse. A quick, strong forward movement will result in a powerful shot, but be careful not to overshoot! Aim for the corners to beat the keeper.

Saving a Penalty: When you are the goalkeeper, your job is to block the opponent's shot. Watch the kicker's movements carefully to predict where the ball will go. Once they kick, quickly move your mouse to guide the goalie's gloves and make the save. A quick reaction is key to victory!
