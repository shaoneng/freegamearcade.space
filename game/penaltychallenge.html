Penalty Challenge

How to Play Penalty Challenge

Penalty Challenge puts you right in the middle of a high-stakes soccer shootout, where you play as both the kicker and the goalkeeper.

Taking a Penalty: When it's your turn to shoot, use your mouse to aim. The angle and the speed of your mouse movement will determine the direction and power of your kick. A faster, stronger swipe will result in a more powerful shot. Be careful, as the opposing goalkeeper is skilled and ready to block your attempt!

Saving a Penalty: When you are the goalkeeper, it's your time to defend the net. Watch the opponent's movements closely to predict where they will shoot. As they kick, quickly move your mouse to guide the goalie's gloves and make the save. Your goal is to block as many shots as possible.

Win each shootout to progress through the tournament and compete for the ultimate prize!
