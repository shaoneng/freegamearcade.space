Penalty Challenge

How to Play Penalty Challenge

When it's your turn to take a penalty, your goal is to score past the opposing goalkeeper. Use your mouse to aim your shot. The angle and the speed of your mouse movement will determine the direction and power of your kick. A faster, more decisive movement will result in a more powerful shot. Be precise to find the back of the net!

When you are the goalkeeper, your objective is to save your opponent's penalty. Watch the striker's movements carefully to anticipate the direction of the ball. As they shoot, quickly move your mouse to guide the goalie's gloves and make the save. Quick reflexes are key to keeping a clean sheet!

Win the shootout by scoring more goals than your opponent. Each victory will help you progress further in the tournament, with the ultimate goal of reaching the World Cup final. Good luck!
