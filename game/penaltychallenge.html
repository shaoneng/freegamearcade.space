Penalty Challenge

How to Play Penalty Challenge

Penalty Challenge places you right in the heart of a tense soccer shootout. You'll alternate between two crucial roles: the penalty taker and the goalkeeper. Your goal is to score more goals than your opponent to win the match and advance through the tournament.

When it's your turn to shoot, use your mouse to aim. Click and drag to set the angle and power of your kick. The faster you move the mouse, the more powerful your shot will be. Aim carefully to beat the keeper and find the back of the net!

When you are the goalkeeper, your job is to defend the goal. Watch the opponent's movements closely to predict where they will shoot. Click and drag your goalie's gloves into the path of the ball to make a heroic save. Quick reflexes are key to keeping a clean sheet!
