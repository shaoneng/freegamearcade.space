Penalty Challenge

How to Play Penalty Challenge

Penalty Challenge puts you in the boots of both the penalty taker and the goalkeeper in a high-stakes soccer shootout. Your goal is to score more goals than your opponent to win the match and advance through the tournament.

Taking the Penalty: When it's your turn to shoot, use your mouse to aim. The angle and power of your kick are determined by how you move the mouse. A quick, strong forward motion will result in a powerful shot. Aim carefully to get past the opponent's skilled goalkeeper!

Playing as Goalkeeper: When you are defending, watch the opponent's movements closely to predict where they will shoot. Use your mouse to move the goalie's gloves and dive in the direction of the ball to make a save. Quick reflexes are key to keeping a clean sheet!
