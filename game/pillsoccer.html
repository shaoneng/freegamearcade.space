Pill Soccer

How to Play Pill Soccer

Welcome to the wacky world of Pill Soccer! This isn't your average soccer match. In this hilarious 1v1 or 2-player game, you take control of a cute, pill-shaped character. The objective is simple: outscore your opponent by getting the ball into their net before the time runs out. Jump, kick, and headbutt your way to victory in this fast-paced sports showdown.

The controls are easy to learn but challenging to master. For Player 1: Use the 'A' and 'D' keys to move left and right, the 'W' key to jump, and the 'S' key to kick. For Player 2: Use the left and right arrow keys to move, the up arrow key to jump, and the down arrow key to kick. Coordinate your moves to defend your goal and launch powerful attacks.

Winning is rewarding! You earn diamonds by winning matches in either Quick Match or Tournament mode. You can also complete special achievements to boost your diamond collection. The more you play and win, the more you can customize your experience.

Head over to the Shop to spend your hard-earned diamonds! You can unlock a wide variety of awesome new character skins, from superheroes to pirates. You can also purchase different ball skins to change up the look of your game. Compete in tournaments to prove your skills and earn even bigger rewards!
