Pill Soccer

How to Play Pill Soccer

Welcome to the quirky world of Pill Soccer, a unique 1-on-1 soccer game where the players are... pills! The objective is straightforward: maneuver your pill-shaped character across the field and score more goals than your opponent within the time limit. Jump, kick, and use your character's head to send the ball flying into the net.

You can play against the computer or challenge a friend in the exciting 2-player mode. The controls are simple and intuitive, allowing you to move your player and perform kicks. Master your timing to block your opponent's shots and execute powerful kicks to score.

Compete in quick matches for casual fun or enter tournaments to test your skills and climb the ranks. Winning matches and completing achievements will reward you with diamonds, the in-game currency. Save up your diamonds to spend in the shop!

Visit the shop to customize your experience. Use your hard-earned diamonds to unlock a variety of new character skins (from pirates to superheroes!), different ball designs, and unique outfits to make your pill stand out on the field.
