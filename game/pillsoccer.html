Pill Soccer

How to Play Pill Soccer

Welcome to the wacky world of Pill Soccer! The goal is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the time runs out. Control your pill-shaped player on a 2D field and use a combination of movement, jumping, and kicking to send the ball into the enemy's net. You can play solo against the computer or challenge a friend in the exciting 2-player mode.

Use the on-screen controls or your keyboard to maneuver your character. Master the timing of your jumps and kicks to perform powerful shots or make crucial defensive blocks. Each match is a fast-paced battle of physics and skill!

Winning matches earns you diamonds, the in-game currency. Compete in tournaments for bigger rewards and complete achievements to boost your collection. Spend your hard-earned diamonds in the shop to unlock a variety of new player skins, unique outfits, and different ball designs to customize your game experience. Choose your favorite pill, enter the arena, and become a soccer legend!
