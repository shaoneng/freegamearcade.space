Pill Soccer

How to Play Pill Soccer

Welcome to the fun-filled pitch of Pill Soccer! The objective is straightforward: outscore your opponent before the timer hits zero. Control your adorable pill-shaped athlete to jump, dash, and kick the ball into the goal.

You can play in a quick match against an AI opponent, challenge a friend in the 2-player mode, or compete in exciting tournaments to earn valuable diamonds. Winning is your ticket to unlocking cool new characters, unique ball skins, and stylish outfits from the in-game shop!

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use the Arrow Keys to move and jump. Press 'P' or Down Arrow to kick.
  • Player 2: Use the 'A', 'W', 'D' keys to move and jump. Press 'S' to kick.

Master the controls, time your kicks perfectly, and become the Pill Soccer champion!
