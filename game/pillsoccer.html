Pill Soccer

How to Play Pill Soccer

Welcome to the hilarious world of Pill Soccer! In this wacky one-on-one sports game, your objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent before time runs out. Control your pill-shaped character, jump, and kick your way to victory in fast-paced matches that are full of fun surprises.

The game is perfect for playing solo against the AI or challenging a friend in the 2-player mode. The controls are easy to master:

Player 1: Use A/D to move left and right, W to jump, and S to kick.
Player 2: Use the Left/Right Arrow keys to move, the Up Arrow key to jump, and the Down Arrow key to kick.

As you compete in quick matches and tournaments, you'll earn diamonds for your victories and for completing special achievements. Head over to the shop to spend your hard-earned diamonds on a variety of cool unlocks, including new character skins, different ball designs, and unique outfits to make your pill stand out on the field!
