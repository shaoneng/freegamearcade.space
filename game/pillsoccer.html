Pill Soccer

How to Play Pill Soccer

Welcome to the quirky world of Pill Soccer! The goal is simple: score more goals than your opponent to win the match. You'll control a charming, pill-shaped character in fast-paced, physics-based soccer action. Jump, kick, and headbutt the ball into the net to secure your victory.

You can jump into a quick match for instant fun or test your skills in exciting tournaments. Winning matches and completing achievements will reward you with diamonds. These diamonds are your key to unlocking more content and proving your prowess on the field!

Head to the in-game shop to spend your hard-earned diamonds. You can unlock a wide variety of new pill characters, cool outfits, and different ball skins to customize your gameplay experience and show off your style.

The game can be played solo against an AI opponent or with a friend in 2-player mode on the same computer. The controls are straightforward and displayed in the game, typically using arrow keys and/or WASD to move and dedicated keys to jump and kick. Get ready to score some goals!
