Pill Soccer

How to Play Pill Soccer

Welcome to the wacky world of Pill Soccer! This isn't your average soccer game. Here, you'll control a cute, 3D pill-shaped character in a fast-paced 1v1 showdown. The goal is simple: outscore your opponent before time runs out. You can play against the computer or challenge a friend in the 2-player mode for even more fun.

Mastering the controls is key to victory. For Player 1, use the Arrow Keys to move and the Up Arrow to jump, with 'P' or Down Arrow to kick. Player 2 uses W, A, D to move, W to jump, and 'S' or Spacebar to kick. Time your movements perfectly to head, block, and send the ball flying into the opponent's net. Keep an eye out for special power-ups that appear during the match to gain a temporary advantage!

Winning matches and tournaments is how you progress and earn rewards. Every victory adds diamonds to your collection. Head over to the shop to spend your hard-earned diamonds on a variety of unlocks, including new character skins (like Batman or a Pirate!), different ball designs, and unique outfits to customize your pill and play in style.
