Pill Soccer

How to Play Pill Soccer

Welcome to the quirky world of Pill Soccer! In this fun and fast-paced 1v1 sports game, your objective is simple: control your pill-shaped character and score more goals than your opponent before time runs out. Outmaneuver your rival, perform powerful kicks, and defend your goal to win the match.

The controls are intuitive, whether you're playing against the AI or challenging a friend in 2-player mode. For Player 1, use the A and D keys to move left and right, the W key to jump, and the S key to kick. Player 2 uses the Left and Right Arrow keys to move, the Up Arrow to jump, and the Down Arrow to kick.

Winning matches and competing in tournaments will earn you diamonds. You can use these diamonds in the in-game shop to unlock a variety of awesome customizations, including new player skins, stylish outfits, and different ball designs to personalize your game!
