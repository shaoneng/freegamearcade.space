Pill Soccer

How to Play Pill Soccer

Welcome to the quirky world of Pill Soccer! The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent before the time runs out. Control your pill-shaped player to jump, kick, and headbutt the ball into the opposing net. You can face off against the computer or challenge a friend in an exciting two-player match.

The controls are easy to master. For a solo match or as Player 1 in a two-player game, use the A and D keys to move left and right, and the W key to jump. If you're Player 2, use the Left and Right Arrow Keys to move and the Up Arrow Key to jump. Use these movements strategically to outmaneuver your opponent and strike the ball.

Pill Soccer offers different ways to play. Jump into a Quick Match for instant fun, or enter a Tournament to prove your skills and earn bigger rewards. Winning matches and completing achievements will earn you diamonds, which you can spend in the shop to unlock cool new characters (like a Batman or Pirate pill!), different ball skins, and fun outfits to customize your game.
