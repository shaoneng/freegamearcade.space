Pill Soccer

How to Play Pill Soccer

Welcome to the fun-filled pitch of Pill Soccer! The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent before time runs out. You'll control a cute, pill-shaped character in a fast-paced 1v1 or 2-player soccer match.

Master the controls to dominate the field. You can play against the computer or challenge a friend right on the same keyboard!

  • Player 1 Controls: Use the Arrow Keys to move your pill character. Press the Up Arrow to jump and the P key or Down Arrow to kick the ball.
  • Player 2 Controls: Use the W, A, D keys to move. Press the W key to jump and the S key to perform a kick.

Compete in quick matches or enter exciting tournaments to prove your skills and earn rewards. Winning matches and completing special achievements will earn you diamonds. You can then visit the in-game shop to spend your diamonds on new player skins, cool new soccer balls, and other fun cosmetic items to personalize your experience!
