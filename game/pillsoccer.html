Pill Soccer

How to Play Pill Soccer

Ole, ole, ole! Jump into a wacky sports showdown with Pill Soccer. This unique 2-player game features cute 3D characters shaped like pills, adding a fun twist to traditional 1v1 soccer. The main objective is to outscore your opponent, win matches, and conquer tournaments to earn valuable diamonds!

Earning & Spending Diamonds

Diamonds are the key to success and style in Pill Soccer. You earn them primarily by competing in tournaments and completing special achievements. Winning quick matches also adds to your diamond collection. Spend your diamonds in the in-game shop to unlock new players (like Batman, a Pirate, or even a Minion!), unique ball skins, and cool outfits. Diamonds are also used to pay for tournament entry fees.

Tournaments & Platforms

The game features several tournaments you can unlock, each with its own entrance fee and rewards. Please note that Pill Soccer is a desktop-only game, playable on desktops, laptops, and Chromebooks via a web browser; it is not available on mobile devices.
