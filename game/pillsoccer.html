Pill Soccer

How to Play Pill Soccer

Welcome to the quirky pitch of Pill Soccer! The goal is just like regular soccer, but with a fun twist: score more goals than your opponent before the time runs out. You'll control a cute, pill-shaped character in a fast-paced 1v1 match.

The controls are simple and easy to master, allowing you to jump, move, and kick the ball into your opponent's net. You can play against the computer or challenge a friend in the exciting 2-player mode.

Controls:
Player 1: Use the Arrow Keys to move and jump. Press the P key or Down Arrow to kick.
Player 2: Use the W, A, D keys to move and jump. Press the S key or Spacebar to kick.

Winning matches in both quick play and tournaments will earn you valuable diamonds. You can also complete special achievements for extra rewards. Head over to the shop to spend your diamonds on unlocking cool new characters—from a Pirate to Batman—as well as different ball skins and fun outfits to customize your game. Feeling competitive? Enter tournaments to test your skills against tougher opponents for bigger diamond prizes!
