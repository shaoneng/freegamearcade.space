Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Your goal in Pixel Battle Royale is to be the last player standing in a thrilling 3D shootout. The match begins with you in an airplane; press the 'F' key to parachute down onto the vast island below. Once you land, your immediate priority is to find gear.

Explore the buildings scattered across the map to scavenge for weapons, ammo, and other supplies. Use the 'F' key again to interact with doors and pick up items. Once armed, it's time to hunt! Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot. Be strategic, as your opponents are quick and will be hunting you too!

Master the controls to dominate the battlefield: Use WASD or arrow keys to move, Left Shift to run, and Space Bar to jump. Aim with the Right Mouse Button and shoot with the Left. Reload with R, crouch with C, and open your inventory with Tab. You can switch weapons with keys 1-4 and change your camera view with V.
