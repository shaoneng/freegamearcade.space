Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Welcome to the chaotic, blocky battlefield of Pixel Battle Royale! Your main goal is to be the last player alive. The match begins with you in an airplane high above the map. When you're ready to make your move, press the F key to parachute down to the ground below.

Once you've landed, your immediate priority is to find gear. Scour the various buildings scattered across the map to find weapons, ammunition, and other helpful items. Be quick and efficient, as other players are doing the same! Keep an eye on the map to stay within the safe zone and track the number of players still in the fight.

Before jumping into the action, you can select your game mode â€” the classic Battle Royale or a more freeform Sandbox mode. You can also pick from a fun cast of characters like the Soldier, Farmer, Medic, and more!

Controls:

  • Movement: Use WASD or the Arrow Keys to move.
  • Jump: Press the Space Bar.
  • Run: Hold Left Shift.
  • Crouch: Press C.
  • Shoot: Use the Left Mouse Button.
  • Aim: Use the Right Mouse Button.
  • Reload: Press R.
  • Interact / Pick Up / Parachute: Press F.
  • Inventory: Press Tab.
  • Change Weapon: Use keys 1, 2, 3, 4.
  • Change Camera: Press V.
