Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Pixel Battle Royale is an action-packed 3D shooting game. Your main objective is to be the last player standing in intense battles against other opponents. To begin, press the 'F' key to parachute from the plane and land safely on the map.

Once on the ground, your immediate task is to search for essential weapons and ammunition within the various buildings. Be quick, as your opponents are agile and dangerous. With your gear ready, start hunting and eliminating other players. Employ good strategy and quick reflexes to outmaneuver them and secure your victory.

You can choose between different game modes, including 'Battle Royale' for classic last-man-standing action, and 'Sandbox' for a more open experience. Select your preferred character from options like Soldier, Farmer, Medic, Salaryman, or Police to personalize your gameplay.

Keep track of your vital stats displayed on the screen: your Health is at the bottom center, the number of Pixels (players) still alive is at the top right, and a helpful Map is located at the bottom right corner.

Controls:
WASD or Arrow Keys: Move
Left Mouse Button: Shoot
Right Mouse Button: Aim
F: Interact, Pick up weapon, or Parachute
Space Bar: Jump
Left Shift: Run
R: Reload
C: Crouch
Tab: Open Inventory
1, 2, 3, 4: Change Weapon
V: Change Camera
