Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Welcome to the high-stakes world of Pixel Battle Royale! Your adventure begins high above the battlefield in a plane. Press the F key to parachute down into the action. Once you land, your immediate goal is to find weapons and ammunition. Scour the buildings and structures around you to gear up. With weapons in hand, it's time to hunt down your opponents and fight to be the last player standing in this intense 3D shooter.

Before you jump in, you can customize your experience. Choose from two exciting game modes: the classic, high-pressure Battle Royale or the free-form Sandbox mode. You can also select your character from a diverse roster, including the Soldier, Farmer, Medic, Policeman, and more, each with a unique pixelated look.

Controls:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Jump: Space Bar
  • Run: Left Shift
  • Crouch: C
  • Aim: Right Mouse Button
  • Shoot: Left Mouse Button
  • Interact / Pick Up / Parachute: F key
  • Reload: R key
  • Change Weapon: 1, 2, 3, 4 keys
  • Open Inventory: Tab key
  • Change Camera View: V key
