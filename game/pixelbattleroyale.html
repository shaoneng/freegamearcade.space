Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Welcome to the frantic, blocky world of Pixel Battle Royale! Your primary goal is to be the last player standing. The game starts with you on a plane high above the battlefield. When you're ready, press the F key to parachute down to the sprawling map below. Pick your landing spot strategically, as your survival depends on finding good loot quickly!

Once you've landed, your first mission is to gear up. Search buildings and other structures for weapons, ammo, and vital supplies. After you're armed, it's time to hunt. Keep an eye on your map, listen for enemy movement, and use the environment for cover. Take down every opponent you see to secure your victory.

The game offers two exciting modes: the classic, high-stakes Battle Royale and a more creative Sandbox mode. You can also choose from a variety of characters like the Soldier, Farmer, or Medic. Master the controls to outplay your rivals:

Movement: WASD or Arrow Keys
Shoot: Left Mouse Button
Aim: Right Mouse Button
Jump: Space Bar
Interact/Pick Up/Parachute: F Key
Run: Left Shift
Reload: R Key
Crouch: C Key
Change Camera: V Key
Inventory: Tab Key
Change Weapon: 1, 2, 3, 4 Keys
