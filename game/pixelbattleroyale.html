Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Welcome to the blocky battlefield! Your mission in Pixel Battle Royale is to be the last player standing. Each match starts with you parachuting from a plane—press the F key to jump and guide your descent to a strategic location on the map.

Once you land, your top priority is to scavenge for weapons, ammo, and gear. Explore buildings and be quick, as other players are hunting for the same loot. Use the map to navigate and keep an eye on the player count to know how many opponents remain. With a blend of fast-paced action and survival strategy, every decision matters.

Get familiar with the controls to outmaneuver your foes:

  • Movement: Use WASD or the arrow keys to move around.
  • Aim & Shoot: Hold the right mouse button to aim and click the left mouse button to fire.
  • Action: Press the F key to interact with doors, pick up items, or deploy your parachute.
  • Mobility: Use the Space Bar to jump, Left Shift to sprint, and C to crouch.
  • Weapon Management: Press R to reload, Tab to open your inventory, and the 1-4 number keys to switch weapons.
  • Camera: Press V to change your camera perspective.
