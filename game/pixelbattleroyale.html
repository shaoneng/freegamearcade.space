Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Welcome to the blocky battleground of Pixel Battle Royale! Your mission is to be the last player standing. The game begins with you in a plane; press the F key to parachute down to the island below. Once you land, your top priority is to search buildings for weapons, ammo, and gear. Keep an eye on the map to navigate the terrain and the player count to see how many rivals are left. You can choose between the classic, high-stakes Battle Royale mode or a free-for-all Sandbox mode to practice your skills.

Mastering the controls is key to survival. Use WASD or the arrow keys to move your character. Aim with the right mouse button and fire your weapon with the left mouse button. Press F to interact with objects, pick up items, or initiate your parachute jump. You can jump with the Space bar, run with Left Shift, crouch with C, and reload your weapon with R. To manage your gear, open your inventory with Tab, and quickly switch between weapons using the 1, 2, 3, and 4 keys.

Choose a character that fits your style, whether it's the Soldier, Farmer, Medic, or another unique persona. Keep an eye on your health bar at the bottom of the screen. Use the environment to your advantage, taking cover behind buildings and using strategic positioning to get the drop on your opponents. Press V to switch your camera perspective for a better view of the action. Now get out there and claim your victory!
