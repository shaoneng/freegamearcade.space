Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Welcome to the blocky battleground of Pixel Battle Royale! Your mission is to outlast and out-gun all other players to be the final survivor. The game starts with you in an airplane; press the 'F' key to jump and parachute down to the island below.

Once you land, your first priority is to find equipment. Scour buildings for weapons, ammunition, and other supplies. Keep an eye on the map to stay within the shrinking safe zone and watch the 'Pixels alive' counter at the top of the screen to see how many rivals are left.

Master the controls to dominate the battlefield:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: Left Mouse Button
  • Aim: Right Mouse Button
  • Interact / Pick Up / Parachute: F Key
  • Jump: Space Bar
  • Run: Left Shift
  • Reload: R Key
  • Crouch: C Key
  • Inventory: Tab Key
  • Change Weapon: 1, 2, 3, 4 Keys
  • Change Camera: V Key
