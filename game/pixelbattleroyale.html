Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Welcome to the pixelated battlefield! The goal in Pixel Battle Royale is simple: be the last player standing. You'll drop into a massive map with other armed opponents, and you must scavenge for weapons, ammo, and supplies to eliminate your rivals and survive.

Each match begins aboard a plane flying over the island. When you're ready to make your move, press the **F key** to parachute down. Strategically choose your landing spot, aiming for buildings where you're likely to find valuable loot. Once you land, start searching for gear immediately!

Use **WASD or the arrow keys** to move. Aim your weapon with the mouse, use the **left mouse button to shoot**, and the **right mouse button to aim down sights** for better precision. Press **F** to interact with objects or pick up weapons. You can jump with the **Space bar**, sprint with **Left Shift**, reload with **R**, and crouch with **C**. To manage your gear, press **Tab** to open your inventory. Use the number keys **1, 2, 3, and 4** to quickly switch between your equipped weapons.

Pay attention to your health bar and the number of players left alive. Use the mini-map to navigate the terrain and stay ahead of the shrinking safe zone. Good luck, soldier!
