Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Welcome to the intense, blocky battlefield of Pixel Battle Royale! Your main goal is simple but challenging: be the last player standing. You'll be dropped into a large map with other players, and you must find weapons and gear to eliminate everyone else.

The game begins with you in a plane. Press the F key to parachute down to the island. Use WASD or the arrow keys to move your character. Once you land, your priority is to search buildings for weapons, ammo, and other useful items. To pick up an item or interact with objects, press the F key.

Combat is key to victory. Use the left mouse button to shoot and the right mouse button to aim down your sights for better accuracy. Press R to reload your weapon. You can jump with the Space bar, run with Left Shift, and crouch with C to be a smaller target. Manage your equipment by opening the inventory with the Tab key and switch between your weapons using the 1, 2, 3, and 4 keys. You can also change your camera view with the V key.

Keep an eye on your health at the bottom of the screen and the number of players still alive in the top right. Use the map in the bottom right to navigate the island and stay within the shrinking safe zone. Good luck, and may the best pixel win!
