Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Welcome to the blocky battlefield of Pixel Battle Royale! Your mission is simple: be the last player standing. The game begins with you in an airplane. Press the F key to parachute down onto the sprawling map below. Try to aim for areas with buildings, as this is where you'll find the gear you need to survive.

Once on the ground, your top priority is to find weapons and ammunition. Explore buildings and scavenge for loot to arm yourself. Keep an eye on the top right of your screen to see how many players are still alive. Use the map in the bottom right to navigate the area and anticipate enemy locations. Aim carefully and take down every opponent you see to secure your victory!

Controls

  • Movement: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Shoot: Left Mouse Button
  • Aim: Right Mouse Button
  • Jump: Space Bar
  • Run: Left Shift
  • Crouch: C
  • Interact / Pick Up / Parachute: F
  • Reload: R
  • Open Inventory: Tab
  • Change Weapon: 1, 2, 3, 4
  • Change Camera View: V

Before you jump into the action, you can choose your game mode (Battle Royale or Sandbox) and select a character skin that suits your style, from a Soldier to a Farmer.
