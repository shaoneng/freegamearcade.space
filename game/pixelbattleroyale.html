Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Welcome to the high-stakes world of Pixel Battle Royale! Your mission is simple: be the last player standing. The game begins as you parachute from a plane; press the 'F' key to start your descent onto the battleground. Once you land, your top priority is to find weapons and ammunition. Scour the buildings and structures scattered across the map to gear up for the fight.

Use the WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Aim with the right mouse button and fire with the left mouse button. To interact with objects, pick up weapons, or use your parachute, press the F key. You can also jump with the space bar, run by holding the left shift key, crouch with C, and reload your weapon with R. Manage your equipment by opening your inventory with the Tab key and switch between your collected weapons using the 1, 2, 3, and 4 keys. Keep an eye on your health at the bottom of the screen and the number of players left alive at the top right.

Before you start, you can choose your game mode (Battle Royale or Sandbox) and select a character that suits your style, including a Soldier, Farmer, Medic, and more. Use smart tactics, stay alert, and eliminate all opponents to claim victory!
