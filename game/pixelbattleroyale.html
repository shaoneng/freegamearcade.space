Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Welcome to the blocky battleground of Pixel Battle Royale! Your main objective is simple: be the last player standing. The match begins with you high in the sky on a plane. Press the 'F' key to parachute down onto the sprawling map below.

Once you've landed, your first priority is to gear up. Scour the various buildings to find weapons, ammunition, and other essential supplies. A good loadout is crucial for survival, so be quick and thorough in your search.

With weapons in hand, it's time to hunt. Keep an eye on the top right of your screen to see how many players are still alive. Use the map in the bottom right to navigate the area and track the shrinking safe zone. Be strategic, use cover, and take down every opponent you see to claim victory!

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move
  • Left Mouse Button: Shoot
  • Right Mouse Button: Aim
  • F Key: Interact / Pick up weapon / Parachute
  • Space Bar: Jump
  • Left Shift: Run
  • R Key: Reload
  • C Key: Crouch
  • Tab Key: Open Inventory
  • 1, 2, 3, 4 Keys: Change Weapon
  • V Key: Change Camera View
