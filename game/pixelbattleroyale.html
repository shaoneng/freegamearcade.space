Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Your adventure in Pixel Battle Royale begins high in the sky. Press the F key to parachute from the plane and land on the sprawling map. Once you've landed, your top priority is to find weapons and ammunition. Search inside buildings and around the environment to gear up. The goal is simple but challenging: hunt down all other players and be the last one standing to claim victory!

Master the controls to dominate the battlefield. Use WASD or the arrow keys to move your character. Aim with the right mouse button and fire with the left mouse button. Press F to interact with objects, pick up weapons, or parachute. You can jump with the Space bar, run with Left Shift, reload with R, and crouch with C. Manage your gear by opening the inventory with Tab, and switch between your weapons using the 1, 2, 3, and 4 keys. Change your camera view with the V key.

Before you jump in, you can choose your game mode: the classic, high-stakes Battle Royale or the creative freedom of Sandbox mode. You can also select a character to play as, including a Soldier, Farmer, Medic, and more, each with a unique look to bring to the fight.
