Pixel Battle Royale

How to Play Pixel Battle Royale

Welcome to the blocky, high-stakes world of Pixel Battle Royale! Your mission is simple: be the last player standing. The game begins in a plane flying high above the map. When you're ready to make your move, press the F key to parachute down to the battlefield below. Try to aim for areas with buildings, as that's where you'll find the gear you need to survive.

Once you've landed, your top priority is to find weapons and ammunition. Scour the buildings and explore the map to loot essential gear. Keep an eye on the top right of your screen to see how many players are still alive. Use the map in the bottom right to navigate and stay within the safe zone. Hunt down your opponents and eliminate them before they get you!

Master the controls to dominate the arena:

  • Movement: Use WASD or the Arrow Keys to move around. Press the Space Bar to jump and Left Shift to run. Use C to crouch.
  • Combat: Aim with the Right Mouse Button and fire with the Left Mouse Button. Press R to reload your weapon.
  • Interaction: Press F to interact with objects, pick up weapons, or initiate parachuting.
  • Inventory & Weapons: Use the number keys (1, 2, 3, 4) to switch between your weapons. Press Tab to open your inventory.
  • Camera: Press V to change your camera view.

Before you jump in, you can choose your game mode (Battle Royale or Sandbox) and select a character, including a Soldier, Farmer, Medic, and more. Good luck, and may the best pixel win!
