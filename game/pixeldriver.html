Pixel Driver

How to Play Pixel Driver

Welcome to the charming blocky world of Pixel Driver! Getting started is as simple as the game itself. Your main goal is to drive around the vibrant pixel city and enjoy the freedom of the open road. Use the arrow keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to control your car. Press the up arrow or 'W' to accelerate, the down arrow or 'S' to brake and reverse, and the left/right arrow keys or 'A'/'D' to steer.

The city is yours to explore. Weave through traffic, which includes other cars, police cruisers, and ambulances, to make the world feel alive. You can play in two ways: either race against the clock to set a new personal best time, or simply cruise around in a more relaxed mode, soaking in the retro 3D atmosphere. There are no complicated missions, just pure driving fun. So, hop in, press the gas, and see where the pixelated streets take you!
