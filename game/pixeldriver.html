Pixel Driver

How to Play Pixel Driver

Pixel Driver is all about the simple joy of driving. There are no complex missions or objectives. Your main goal is to explore the vibrant, blocky city at your own pace.

Use the standard keyboard controls to navigate your vehicle through the bustling city streets:

  • W / Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • S / Down Arrow: Brake / Reverse
  • A / Left Arrow: Steer Left
  • D / Right Arrow: Steer Right

You can choose between two modes: race against the clock to set a new personal best time, or simply enter a free-roam mode to cruise around the city, observing the pixelated traffic and enjoying the retro-inspired scenery. Just get in, hit the gas, and enjoy the drive!
