Pixel Driver

How to Play Pixel Driver

Welcome to the charming, blocky world of Pixel Driver! The goal is simple: drive and enjoy the city. There are no complex missions or stressful objectives, just pure driving freedom.

Use the arrow keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to control your car. Steer left and right, accelerate, and brake as you navigate the bustling city streets. Watch out for other vehicles like police cars and fire trucks that make the world feel alive!

You can choose how you want to play. For a challenge, try to race against the clock and beat your best time. Or, if you just want to unwind, select the free roam mode and cruise through the vibrant, pixelated landscape at your own pace. Jump in, hit the gas, and enjoy the ride!
