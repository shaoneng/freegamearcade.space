Pixel Driver

How to Play Pixel Driver

Welcome to the driver's seat of Pixel Driver! The goal is simple: enjoy the drive. There are no complex missions or objectives to worry about. Just immerse yourself in a colorful, blocky city and explore at your own pace. The streets are alive with other vehicles, making your drive feel like a real urban cruise.

Controlling your car is intuitive and follows standard driving game controls:

  • Drive/Steer: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys on your keyboard.
  • Handbrake: Press the Spacebar for sharp turns or to stop quickly.
  • Change View: Press the C key to switch between different camera angles for the best view.

Pixel Driver offers two distinct ways to play. You can enter a time-attack mode and race through the city, trying to beat your best time. Alternatively, you can select the free-roam mode for a more casual and relaxing experience, perfect for just cruising around and enjoying the nostalgic, pixelated scenery.
