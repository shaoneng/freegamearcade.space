Pixel Driver

How to Play Pixel Driver

Welcome to the delightful world of Pixel Driver! The goal is simple: enjoy the drive. There are no complex missions or rules, just you, your car, and an open city to explore.

Use the arrow keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to control your vehicle. Press the Up Arrow or 'W' to accelerate, the Down Arrow or 'S' to brake and reverse, and the Left/Right Arrows or 'A'/'D' to steer.

You have the freedom to play however you like. If you're feeling competitive, put the pedal to the metal and race through the city streets to set a new best time. If you'd rather unwind, simply cruise at your own pace, taking in the sights of the bustling, blocky metropolis. Enjoy the freedom of the open road in this charming, retro-style driving game!
