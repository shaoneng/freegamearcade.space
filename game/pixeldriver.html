Pixel Driver

How to Play Pixel Driver

Getting started in Pixel Driver is incredibly easy! The goal is to simply enjoy the drive. Hop into your pixel-style car and navigate the bustling, blocky city streets. The city is alive with other vehicles like police cars and ambulances, adding to the immersive experience.

You can choose your own objective. If you're feeling competitive, try to race through the city and beat your best time. For a more relaxed session, just cruise around, explore the colorful world, and enjoy the nostalgic blend of pixel art and 3D graphics. There are no complex missions—just you, your car, and the open road.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to steer, accelerate, and brake. The controls are intuitive, making it easy for anyone to jump in and start driving immediately.
