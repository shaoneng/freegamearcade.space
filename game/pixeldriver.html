Pixel Driver

How to Play Pixel Driver

Welcome to the charming, blocky world of Pixel Driver! The goal is simple: enjoy the drive. There are no complicated missions or stressful objectives, just you, your car, and an open city to explore.

You have two main ways to enjoy the game. You can enter a race mode where you try to beat your best time, pushing your driving skills to the limit. Alternatively, you can choose to simply cruise around the city at your own pace, taking in the sights and sounds of the bustling pixelated metropolis. This mode is perfect for relaxation and enjoying the game's nostalgic atmosphere.

The controls are straightforward and intuitive. Use the Arrow Keys or WASD Keys on your keyboard to steer your vehicle, accelerate, and brake. Simply hit the road and start exploring the colorful city streets!
