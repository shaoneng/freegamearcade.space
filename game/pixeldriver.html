Pixel Driver

How to Play Pixel Driver

Welcome to the blocky, vibrant world of Pixel Driver! The game is all about freedom and simplicity. Your main goal is to simply drive and explore the colorful city at your own leisure. There are no complex missions or stressful objectives, just the open road waiting for you.

Use the standard keyboard controls to navigate your vehicle. Press the Up Arrow Key or W to accelerate, and the Down Arrow Key or S to brake and reverse. Steer your car using the Left/Right Arrow Keys or A and D.

You can race against the clock to set a new personal best time or switch to a more relaxed mode where you can just cruise through the lively city streets. Watch out for other pixelated vehicles like police cars and fire trucks that make the world feel alive. Jump in, hit the gas, and enjoy the simple, nostalgic fun!
