Pixel Driver

How to Play Pixel Driver

Get ready for a straightforward and enjoyable driving experience in Pixel Driver! The goal is simple: hop into your pixel car and explore the colorful, blocky city at your own pace. The city is alive with other vehicles, making your drive feel like a real urban adventure.

You have two main ways to enjoy the game: - **Race Mode:** Put your skills to the test and try to beat your best time as you navigate the city streets. - **Free Roam Mode:** If you're looking for a more relaxed session, simply cruise around the city with no pressure. Enjoy the sights, the simple graphics, and the freedom of the open road.

Use your keyboard to control the car's acceleration, braking, and steering. The controls are intuitive, designed to get you driving and having fun in no time. Just hit the gas and start your pixelated journey!
