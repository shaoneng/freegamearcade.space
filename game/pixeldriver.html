Pixel Driver

How to Play Pixel Driver

Welcome to the charming blocky world of Pixel Driver! The goal is simple: just drive and have fun. When you start, you can choose between two exciting game modes to suit your mood.

In Race Mode, your objective is to speed through the city and beat your best time. Test your driving skills and see how fast you can navigate the busy streets filled with other pixelated vehicles.

If you prefer a more relaxed experience, select Free Roam Mode. Here, there are no timers or objectives. Simply cruise around the vibrant pixel city, explore the sights, and enjoy the casual drive. The city is full of other vehicles like police cars and fire trucks, making it feel alive and dynamic.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to steer, accelerate, and brake. It's an easy-to-learn, relaxing driving game perfect for all ages. So, pick your mode, start your engine, and hit the pixelated pavement!
