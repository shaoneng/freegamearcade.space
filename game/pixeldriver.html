Pixel Driver

How to Play Pixel Driver

Pixel Driver is all about the simple joy of driving. Your goal is to navigate your blocky car through a colorful, bustling city. There are no complicated missionsâ€”just you, your car, and the open road. You can choose to play in one of two modes: race to achieve the best time or simply cruise around and explore the vibrant pixel world at your own pace.

The controls are straightforward, making it easy for anyone to jump in and start driving:

  • Accelerate: Press the W or Up Arrow key.
  • Brake/Reverse: Press the S or Down Arrow key.
  • Steer Left/Right: Press the A/D or Left/Right Arrow keys.
  • Change Camera: Press the C key to switch your view.

Get behind the wheel, hit the gas, and enjoy the relaxing drive through this charming pixelated city!
