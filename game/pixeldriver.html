Pixel Driver

How to Play Pixel Driver

Welcome to the charming, blocky world of Pixel Driver! The goal is simple: get behind the wheel of your pixel car and explore a vibrant city. There are two modes to choose from: race against time to beat your best score or select Free Roam mode for a more casual, relaxing drive.

The controls are straightforward and intuitive. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to navigate the city streets:

  • W / Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • S / Down Arrow: Brake or Reverse
  • A / Left Arrow: Steer Left
  • D / Right Arrow: Steer Right

Drive freely, avoid crashing into other vehicles like police cars and ambulances, and immerse yourself in the nostalgic, 3D pixelated environment. Jump in, hit the gas, and enjoy the pure simplicity of driving!
