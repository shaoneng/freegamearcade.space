Pixel Driver

How to Play Pixel Driver

Welcome to the charming, blocky world of Pixel Driver! The goal is simple: get in your car and enjoy the drive. Navigate through a bustling city filled with pixelated police cars, fire trucks, and ambulances, making the streets feel vibrant and alive.

Use the Arrow Keys or W/A/S/D keys on your keyboard to control your vehicle. Up/W accelerates, Down/S brakes and reverses, and Left/Right or A/D steers your car.

Pixel Driver offers two distinct ways to enjoy the experience. You can choose to race against the clock in a time trial mode, pushing your driving skills to the limit to achieve the best possible time. Alternatively, if you're looking for a more relaxing session, you can select the free roam mode. Just cruise through the city at your own pace, soaking in the retro atmosphere and enjoying the freedom of the open road.
