Pixel Driver

How to Play Pixel Driver

Welcome to the blocky world of Pixel Driver! The goal is simple: enjoy the freedom of the open road. When you start, you can choose between two different ways to play. One mode challenges you to race against the clock, pushing your driving skills to the limit to set a new best time. The other mode is all about relaxation—just cruise through the vibrant city, explore the streets, and enjoy the scenery with no pressure or objectives.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or the WASD keys to control your vehicle. Press the up arrow or 'W' to accelerate, the down arrow or 'S' to brake and reverse, and the left/right arrows or 'A'/'D' to steer. The city is alive with traffic like police cars and ambulances, so keep your eyes on the road to avoid collisions. Hop in, choose your driving style, and hit the gas!
