Pixel Path

How to Play Pixel Path

Your objective in Pixel Path is to guide your character safely to the portal at the end of each of the 50 challenging levels. Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to move your character. Press the Esc key to access the in-game menu.

Be prepared for an unforgiving journey! The terrain is often unstable and can crumble away beneath your feet. You'll need to avoid fast-moving spikes and other deadly traps. In some levels, your character might even grow to a massive size, completely changing how you navigate the world.

Remember, there are no second chances—a single mistake will send you right back to the beginning of the level. Patience and precision are key to your success!
