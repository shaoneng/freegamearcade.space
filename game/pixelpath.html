Pixel Path

How to Play Pixel Path

Your goal in Pixel Path is to guide your pixelated character to the portal at the end of each of the 50 challenging levels.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to move your character left, right, and to jump. Use the Esc key to access the menu at any time.

Be prepared for a tough journey! The path is filled with unstable ground that can crumble beneath your feet, unforgiving traps, and moving spikes. The game introduces unique physics and mechanics as you progress, sometimes causing your character to grow uncontrollably, which changes how you must navigate the level.
