Pixel Path

How to Play Pixel Path

Your mission in Pixel Path is to guide your character through 50 increasingly difficult levels to reach the exit portal. The path is perilous, with crumbling floors, fast-moving spikes, and other unexpected traps designed to test your limits.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to move your character. Be precise and quick with your movements, as the game requires sharp reflexes.

Be warned: the game is unforgiving. A single mistake will send you right back to the start of the level. Stay focused, learn from your errors, and patiently master each stage. Some levels introduce unique mechanics, like your character growing to a massive size, which completely changes how you navigate the environment. Press the Esc key to access the menu at any time.
