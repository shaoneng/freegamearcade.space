How to Play Pixel Path

The objective in Pixel Path is to guide your pixelated hero to the portal at the end of each of the 50 challenging levels. The path is treacherous, filled with unstable ground that might crumble under you and unforgiving traps that require precise timing to avoid.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys to move your character. Be prepared for the unexpected, as some levels introduce unique physicsâ€”your hero might even grow uncontrollably, forcing you to adapt your strategy on the fly.

This game is a true test of patience and reflexes. There are no checkpoints; one mistake sends you back to the start of the level. Stay focused, learn the patterns, and see if you have what it takes to conquer every path. Press the Esc key to access the in-game menu.
