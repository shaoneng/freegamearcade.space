Pixel Path

How to Play Pixel Path

Your goal in Pixel Path is to guide your character through 50 challenging levels and reach the portal at the end of each one. The path is filled with obstacles, so you'll need quick reflexes and precise control to succeed.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to move your character. Press the Esc key to access the game menu at any time.

Be warned: the terrain is unstable and can crumble beneath your feet. You'll encounter unforgiving traps like moving spikes, and in some levels, your character might grow uncontrollably, changing the physics and how you play. There are no second chances; a single mistake will send you back to the start of the level. Stay focused, learn the patterns, and patiently navigate your way to victory!
