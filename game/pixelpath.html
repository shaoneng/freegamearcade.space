Pixel Path

How to Play Pixel Path

Welcome to Pixel Path, a demanding retro platformer where precision and patience are key. Your goal in each of the 50 levels is simple: guide your pixelated hero to the exit portal. However, the journey is anything but easy.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to move your character. The controls are straightforward, but the environment is not. Be prepared for ground that crumbles unexpectedly, deadly spikes that move with surprising speed, and other unforgiving traps designed to stop you.

The game loves to change the rules. You might find your character suddenly growing to an enormous size, forcing you to navigate the level in a completely new way. There are no checkpoints or second chances; one mistake will send you right back to the beginning of the level. Stay focused, learn from your errors, and conquer the path!
