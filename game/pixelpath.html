Pixel Path

How to Play Pixel Path

Your goal in Pixel Path is to guide your character through 50 challenging levels and reach the exit portal. The controls are simple, but the journey is not!

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to move your pixel hero left, right, and to jump. Navigate carefully, as the terrain can be unstable and crumble beneath your feet. Watch out for fast-moving spikes and other unforgiving traps.

Each level introduces new mechanics and challenges, sometimes even causing your character to grow uncontrollably, changing how you interact with the environment. If you make a mistake, you'll be sent back to the start of the level. Press the Esc key to access the menu at any time.
