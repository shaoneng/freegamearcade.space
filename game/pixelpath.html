Pixel Path

How to Play Pixel Path

Your objective in Pixel Path is to guide your character through 50 challenging levels and safely reach the portal at the end of each one. The journey is anything but simple, requiring quick reflexes and careful planning.

Use the Arrow Keys or the WASD keys on your keyboard to move your pixel hero. The controls are simple, but the environment is not. Navigate carefully, as what appears to be solid ground might crumble away beneath your feet.

Be on high alert for various traps and hazards, such as fast-moving spikes. The game introduces unique physics and mechanics as you progress, and sometimes your character may even grow uncontrollably, forcing you to adapt your strategy on the fly. One wrong move will send you back to the beginning of the level, so patience is key! Press the Esc key to access the menu at any time.
