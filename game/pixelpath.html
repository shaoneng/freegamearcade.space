Pixel Path

How to Play Pixel Path

Your goal in Pixel Path is to guide your character through 50 challenging levels and reach the portal at the end of each one. The journey is anything but simple, as the world is designed to test your limits.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys to move your character. Be prepared for quick reactions, as the terrain can crumble beneath you without warning. You'll need to navigate around fast-moving spikes and other unforgiving traps.

Each level introduces new mechanics and twists, sometimes causing your character to grow to enormous sizes, changing how you interact with the environment. There are no checkpoints or second chances; a single mistake will send you right back to the beginning of the level. Press the Esc key to access the menu at any time.

Patience, precision, and quick thinking are your keys to success. Can you master the path and conquer all 50 stages?
