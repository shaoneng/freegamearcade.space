Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Welcome to the driver's seat of Police Chase Drifter! Your main goal is to outrun the relentless police cars while collecting stacks of cash scattered throughout the city. Follow the on-screen arrows to locate the money, but stay alert—the cops are right behind you!

Use your superior driving skills to master the art of the drift. The key to escaping is to lure the police cars into tight corners and obstacles, causing them to crash. A well-timed drift can be the difference between freedom and getting busted. Be strategic and use the city layout to your advantage.

The cash you collect isn't just for show. Head to the garage to spend your earnings on new, faster cars or to upgrade your current ride. Better vehicles will give you the edge you need to stay ahead of the increasingly determined police force.

Controls: Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to steer your car.
