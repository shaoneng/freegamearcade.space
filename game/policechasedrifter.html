How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Welcome to the high-stakes world of Police Chase Drifter! Your main goal is to navigate the bustling city streets, collect scattered piles of cash, and escape the relentless pursuit of the police. Follow the on-screen arrows to locate the money, but always stay one step ahead of the law.

Master the art of the drift to take sharp corners and outmaneuver the police. The key to shaking them off is to strategically use the city's obstacles. Lure the police cars into tight spots and make them crash into buildings or other barriers to destroy them. The more you drift and the longer you survive, the higher your score!

Controls: Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your car's movement.
