Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Welcome to the high-stakes world of Police Chase Drifter! Your main objective is to navigate the bustling city streets, collect as much money as possible, and most importantly, escape the relentless police cars hot on your tail.

Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your car. Master acceleration, braking, and steering to weave through traffic and obstacles.

The key to survival is your drifting skill. Execute sharp turns and long drifts to outmaneuver the police. A clever strategy is to lure the pursuing cops into tight corners or obstacles, causing them to crash and eliminating them from the chase.

Follow the on-screen arrows to locate stacks of cash. The money you collect can be used in the garage to purchase new, faster cars or to upgrade your current vehicle, improving its speed and handling. Keep driving, keep drifting, and become the most wanted driver in the city!
