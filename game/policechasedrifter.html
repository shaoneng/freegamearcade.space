Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Your goal in Police Chase Drifter is to become the ultimate getaway driver by outsmarting the relentless police force. Use your expert driving and drifting skills to navigate the bustling city streets while avoiding capture.

The key to survival is making the police cars crash. Lure them into tight corners and weave through obstacles to make them destroy themselves. Keep an eye out for arrows on the roadâ€”they will guide you to piles of cash scattered throughout the city.

Collecting money is crucial. Use your earnings in the garage to purchase new, more powerful cars or to upgrade the performance of your current vehicle. Better speed and handling will give you the edge you need to stay ahead of the law.

  • Control Your Car: Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys to steer, accelerate, and brake.
  • Objective: Evade the police, collect money, and avoid crashing your own car.
  • Strategy: Use sharp turns and drifts to trick the police into crashing into buildings and other obstacles.
