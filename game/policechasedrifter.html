Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Your goal in Police Chase Drifter is to become the ultimate getaway driver. The city streets are your arena, and you must outmaneuver the relentless police cars hot on your pursuit. The key to survival is mastering the art of the drift. Use sharp turns and the city's obstacles to trick the police into crashing, all while collecting cash scattered throughout the map.

Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to steer your vehicle. Follow the on-screen arrows to locate bundles of money. This cash is crucial, as you can use it in the garage to purchase new, faster cars or upgrade your current vehicle. A better car means better speed and handling, giving you the edge you need to escape. Drive fast, drift hard, and shake off the law to claim victory!
