Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Your goal in Police Chase Drifter is to become the most elusive driver in the city. You're being pursued by relentless police cars, and your only way to escape is to use your expert driving and drifting skills to make them crash into obstacles or each other.

Keep an eye out for arrows on the road; they will guide you to piles of cash. Collecting this money is crucial as it allows you to purchase new, faster cars or upgrade your current vehicle's performance. The better your car, the better your chances of a successful getaway.

Controlling your vehicle is straightforward. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow keys on your keyboard to steer, accelerate, and brake. Master the controls to execute perfect drifts around corners and leave the police in the dust!
