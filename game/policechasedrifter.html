Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Welcome to the high-stakes world of Police Chase Drifter! Your goal is to become the ultimate escape artist by outsmarting a relentless police force. Use your expert driving and drifting skills to navigate the city streets while collecting cash.

Controls:

  • Steer your car: Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys.

Gameplay:

  • Evade the Police: The police will stop at nothing to catch you. Use sharp turns and clever drifts to make them crash into each other or into city obstacles.
  • Collect Cash: Follow the on-screen arrows to locate and collect piles of money scattered throughout the city.
  • Upgrade Your Ride: Use the cash you've collected to buy new, faster cars or to upgrade your current vehicle. A better car means a better chance of escape!

Survive as long as you can, master the art of the drift, and show the cops who truly owns these streets. Good luck, drifter!
