Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Your mission in Police Chase Drifter is to become the ultimate getaway driver. The city streets are your playground, but the police are relentlessly pursuing you. Your goal is to not only escape but to do it with style, collecting cash scattered throughout the city to fund your career.

Use your expert driving and drifting skills to navigate tight corners and weave through obstacles. The key to shaking the cops is to trick them into crashing into each other or into parts of the environment. Follow the on-screen arrows to locate bundles of cash, which you'll need to upgrade your vehicle.

Control your vehicle using the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard. Use the money you collect to purchase new, faster cars and upgrade them to improve your chances of a successful escape and become a true drifter champion.
