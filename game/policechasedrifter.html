Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Your goal in Police Chase Drifter is to become the ultimate getaway driver. Navigate the city streets, collect scattered cash, and evade the persistent police cars trying to take you down. The longer you survive and the more cash you collect, the better!

Use the WASD keys or the Arrow keys on your keyboard to control your car. Master steering, accelerating, and braking to perform sharp turns and epic drifts.

The key to escaping is not just speed, but strategy. Lure the police cars into tight corners and obstacles to make them crash. Use the money you collect to buy new cars and upgrade them in the garage for better speed and handling. Follow the arrows to find the next cash pile and keep the chase going!
