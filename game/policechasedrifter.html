Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Your mission in Police Chase Drifter is to outrun a relentless police pursuit while collecting cash scattered across the city. The longer you survive and the more money you collect, the closer you get to becoming a drifting legend. The key to survival isn't just speed, but strategy and skill.

Use your expert drifting abilities to navigate the urban landscape. Lure the police cars into tight corners and around obstacles, causing them to crash and eliminate themselves from the chase. Follow the on-screen arrows to locate stacks of money, which are essential for your progress. Use the cash you earn to unlock new, faster cars and upgrade your current vehicle's performance in the garage.

Controlling your car is simple. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to accelerate, brake, and steer. Master these controls to execute perfect drifts and escape the law!
