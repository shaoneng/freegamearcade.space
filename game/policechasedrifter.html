How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Your mission in Police Chase Drifter is to become the ultimate getaway driver. The city streets are your playground, but the police are relentlessly pursuing you. Your goal is to survive as long as possible, collect cash scattered throughout the city, and use your expert drifting skills to make the police cars crash into obstacles or each other.

Take control of your vehicle using either the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard. Accelerate, brake, and steer your way through tight corners and crowded streets.

The key to shaking the cops is sharp, well-timed drifts. Lure them into tight spaces and watch them crash and burn. Evade, drift, and collect to become the champion of the chase!
