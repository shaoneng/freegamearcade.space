Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

The objective in Police Chase Drifter is to become the ultimate getaway driver. You must evade the relentless police cars while collecting money to upgrade your ride and become a drifting champion.

Use your expert drifting skills to navigate the city's tight corners and various obstacles. The key to escape is to cleverly maneuver so that the pursuing police cars crash into each other or into the city's environment. A well-timed drift around a corner is your most effective strategy for shaking the cops.

Control your vehicle using the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard. Follow the on-screen arrows that point you toward piles of cash scattered throughout the city. Collecting this money is crucial, as it allows you to purchase new, faster cars and upgrade your current vehicle in the garage.
