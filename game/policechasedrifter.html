Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Your mission in Police Chase Drifter is simple: outrun the relentless police force hot on your tail! Navigate the bustling city streets, collect scattered piles of money, and avoid getting busted. The longer you survive and the more cash you collect, the closer you'll get to becoming the ultimate drifter.

The key to survival is your drifting skill. Use tight corners and city obstacles to your advantage. Lure the police cars into a chase and then skillfully drift around a corner, causing them to crash into buildings and each other. A well-executed drift is your best weapon against the law!

Follow the on-screen arrows to locate cash. This money is crucial for your success. Head to the garage to spend your earnings on powerful new vehicles or to upgrade the performance of your current car, boosting its speed and handling.

Controls: Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to steer your car.
