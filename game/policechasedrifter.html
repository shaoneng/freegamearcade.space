Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Your goal in Police Chase Drifter is to become the ultimate getaway driver. Navigate the city streets, collect scattered cash, and evade the relentless police cars hot on your tail. The longer you survive and the more money you collect, the better!

Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your car. Steer your vehicle through tight corners and around obstacles to keep the chase going and outmaneuver the authorities.

The key to success is masterful drifting. Lure the police into crashing into buildings and other obstacles to take them out of the pursuit. Follow the arrows on the screen to locate bundles of cash. Use the money you collect to buy new, faster cars or upgrade your current ride to improve your performance and escape chances.
