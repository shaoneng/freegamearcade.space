Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Your mission in Police Chase Drifter is to outrun the police and collect as much money as you can. Use your driving skills to navigate the city streets, following the arrows to locate stashes of cash.

The key to survival is mastering the drift. When the police are hot on your tail, take sharp turns to make them crash into buildings or each other. The only way to get rid of the police cars is to trick them into destroying themselves. Be careful not to crash your own car!

The money you collect is crucial. Use it in the garage to buy new, faster cars or to upgrade your current ride. Better cars will give you the edge you need to escape more challenging pursuits and become the ultimate drifter champion.

Controls: Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys to steer your car.
