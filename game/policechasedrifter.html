Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Welcome to the high-stakes world of Police Chase Drifter! Your main objective is to become the ultimate getaway driver by escaping the police and collecting as much money as you can.

Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your car. Master the art of drifting around tight corners to evade the pursuing police cars. The key to survival is to use the city's environment to your advantage. Lure the cops into crashing into obstacles or into each other to take them out of the chase.

As you drive, follow the on-screen arrows to locate stacks of cash. Collect this money to build your fortune. You can use your earnings in the garage to purchase new, more powerful vehicles or upgrade your current ride for better speed and handling. Can you outdrive the law and become a drifting legend?
