How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Your goal in Police Chase Drifter is to become the ultimate urban driver by outmaneuvering a relentless police force. Speed through the city streets, collect scattered cash, and do whatever it takes to avoid getting busted. Your escape relies on more than just speedâ€”it requires clever driving and strategic drifting.

Use your expert drifting skills to whip around tight corners. The main strategy is to bait the police cars into crashing into buildings and other city obstacles. A well-timed drift is your most effective weapon! Keep an eye out for on-screen arrows that guide you toward piles of money.

The cash you collect is vital for success. Spend it in the garage to purchase new, faster cars or to upgrade the performance of your current vehicle. With better speed and handling, you'll find it much easier to shake the chase and secure your victory.

Control your vehicle using either the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard. Press 'W' or the Up Arrow to accelerate, and use the left/right keys to steer your car and initiate drifts around corners.
