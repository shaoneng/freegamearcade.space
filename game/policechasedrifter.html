Police Chase Drifter

How to Play Police Chase Drifter

Your objective in Police Chase Drifter is to become the ultimate getaway driver. Navigate a bustling city, evade relentless police cars, and collect as much money as you can. The police are hot on your tail, and your only way to escape is by using your superior drifting skills to outmaneuver them.

Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your car. Press the up arrow or 'W' to accelerate, and use the left/right keys to steer. Mastering the controls is key to executing sharp turns and spectacular drifts.

The key to survival is not just speed, but strategy. Lure the police cars into tight corners and obstacles, causing them to crash and destroy themselves. Follow the arrows on the screen to locate bundles of cash. Use this money in the garage to buy new, more powerful vehicles or to upgrade your current car's performance. Keep drifting, keep earning, and stay one step ahead of the law to claim victory!
