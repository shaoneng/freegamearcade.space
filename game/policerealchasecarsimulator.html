How to Play Police Real Chase Car Simulator

Get behind the wheel and become the city's finest in Police Real Chase Car Simulator! Your primary objective is to take on challenging missions, from high-speed chases to navigating through dense city traffic. Prove your exceptional driving skills as you hunt down criminals and maintain order. As you progress, you'll establish yourself not just as an expert driver, but as a top-tier police officer.

To begin your duty, explore the city and look for yellow flagsâ€”these will guide you to blue portals where missions await. Once you enter a portal, you'll receive your instructions and a time limit to complete the objective. Before you head out, don't forget to visit the garage, where you can choose from a selection of 7 powerful police cars to suit your mission's needs.

Controls:

  • W: Accelerate
  • S: Backward
  • A: Steer Left
  • D: Steer Right
  • Space: Jump
  • L-Shift: Sprint / Nitro
  • C: Change Camera
  • R: Reset Car
  • H: Police Siren
  • F: Enter/Exit Car
  • M: Map
  • I: Instructions
  • P/Esc: Pause
