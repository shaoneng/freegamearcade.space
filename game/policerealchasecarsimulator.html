Police Real Chase Car Simulator

How to Play Police Real Chase Car Simulator

Welcome to the force, officer! In Police Real Chase Car Simulator, your mission is to patrol a dynamic city, hunt down criminals, and complete challenging missions. Put your driving skills to the ultimate test as you navigate through realistic traffic in high-speed pursuits to bring the bad guys to justice.

To begin a mission, explore the city and look for the yellow flags. Drive through them to be guided towards a blue mission portal. Entering the portal will give you your objectives and start the timer. You can also visit the garage to choose from 7 different high-performance police cars.

Master your vehicle with the following controls:

W, A, S, D: Accelerate, Steer Left, Backward, Steer Right
L-Shift: Sprint / Nitro
Spacebar: Jump
C: Change Camera View
R: Reset Car
F: Enter / Exit Car
H: Toggle Police Siren
M: Open Map
I: View Instructions
P / Esc: Pause Game
