Police Real Chase Car Simulator

How to Play Police Real Chase Car Simulator

Your mission, officer, is to become the ultimate police driver in a bustling city. In Police Real Chase Car Simulator, you'll take on a variety of challenging missions, from high-speed chases to navigating dense traffic, all to bring criminals to justice. Prove your driving prowess and show everyone you're the best cop on the force.

To begin your duty, explore the city and look for the yellow flags. These will guide you to blue mission portals. Drive into a portal to receive your assignment and start the clock! As you progress, you can choose from a selection of 7 powerful police cars in the garage, each with its own unique handling and speed.

Master your vehicle with these essential controls:
Movement: Use W to accelerate, S to drive backward, and A/D to steer left and right.
Actions: Press Spacebar to jump, L-Shift for a nitro boost, and F to enter or exit your vehicle.
Utilities: Toggle the police siren with H, change your camera view with C, reset your car with R, and open the map with M.
Game Menu: Press P or Esc to pause the game and review instructions.
