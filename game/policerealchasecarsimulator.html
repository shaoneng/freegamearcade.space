Police Real Chase Car Simulator

How to Play Police Real Chase Car Simulator

In Police Real Chase Car Simulator, you step into the shoes of a police officer tasked with keeping the city safe. Your main objective is to patrol the bustling city streets, chase down bad guys, and successfully complete a variety of challenging missions. Show off your elite driving skills as you navigate through realistic traffic to hunt down your targets.

To start a mission, get in your car and explore the city to find the yellow flags. These flags will guide you to blue mission portals. Drive into a portal to receive your mission briefing and start the timer. Before you head out, don't forget to visit the garage where you can choose from 7 different high-performance police cars to take on your assignments.

Master the controls to become the city's top officer:
• W, A, S, D: Drive/Steer
• Space: Jump
• L-Shift: Use Nitro/Sprint
• H: Toggle Police Siren
• C: Change Camera Angle
• F: Enter or Exit Vehicle
• M: View Map
• R: Reset Car
• P/Esc: Pause Game
