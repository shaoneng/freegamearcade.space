How to Play Police Real Chase Car Simulator

Welcome to the force, officer! In Police Real Chase Car Simulator, your mission is to patrol a sprawling city, hunt down criminals, and complete a series of challenging assignments. To start a mission, hop in your police vehicle and follow the yellow flags on your map. These will guide you to blue portals where you'll receive your objectives and a time limit. Use your expert driving skills to navigate busy streets, catch the bad guys, and prove you're the best on the beat.

Your garage holds up to 7 unique police cars, so be sure to find the perfect ride for the job. Master the controls to maintain law and order:

Movement: Use W to accelerate, S to go backward, and A and D to steer left and right. Use the Spacebar to jump over obstacles and L-Shift for a nitro boost.

Vehicle & UI: Press F to enter or exit your car. Change your view with C and reset your car's position with R. Toggle the police siren with H. You can open the map with M, view instructions with I, and pause the game with P or Esc.
