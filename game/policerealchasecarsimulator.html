How to Play Police Real Chase Car Simulator

Welcome to the force, officer! In Police Real Chase Car Simulator, your mission is to clean up the city streets. Get behind the wheel of your police cruiser and prove your exceptional driving skills by navigating realistic city traffic and chasing down criminals. You'll face a variety of challenging missions that will test your speed, precision, and strategy.

To begin a mission, drive around the city and look for the yellow flags. These will guide you to blue portals where you'll receive your objectives and a time limit. Controls:
Movement: W (Accelerate), S (Backward), A (Steer Left), D (Steer Right)
Actions: Space (Jump), F (Enter/Exit Car), Q (Roll), E (Inventory), Z (Missions)
Car Functions: L-Shift (Nitro), H (Siren), C (Change Camera), R (Reset Car)
Game: M (Map), I (Instructions), P/Esc (Pause)
