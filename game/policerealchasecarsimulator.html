How to Play Police Real Chase Car Simulator

Welcome to the force, officer! In Police Real Chase Car Simulator, your duty is to keep the city streets safe. Begin by visiting the garage to select one of seven high-performance police cars. Once you're on the streets, your main objective is to complete a variety of challenging missions. To find a mission, look for the yellow flags scattered throughout the cityâ€”they will guide you to blue portals where your new assignment awaits.

Upon entering a mission portal, you'll receive specific instructions and a time limit to complete your task. These missions will test your driving prowess as you navigate realistic city traffic, chase down criminals, and restore order. Show off your expert driving skills and prove you have what it takes to be a top police officer.

Master the controls to effectively patrol the city and hunt down the bad guys. Use **W, A, S, D** to drive your vehicle. Activate your **Nitro** with **Left Shift** for a burst of speed during intense chases, and hit the **Space** bar to jump over obstacles. Use **H** to turn on your police siren and let everyone know you're on a mission. The city is counting on you!
