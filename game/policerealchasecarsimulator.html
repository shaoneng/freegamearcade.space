How to Play Police Real Chase Car Simulator

Welcome to the force, officer! Your mission in Police Real Chase Car Simulator is to clean up the city streets. You'll take the wheel of a high-performance police car and tackle a variety of challenging missions, from high-speed chases to navigating through realistic city traffic. Before you start, head to the garage to choose from one of the 7 available police cars, each with its own unique feel.

To find a mission, keep your eyes peeled for the yellow flags scattered throughout the city. Follow these markers to a blue portal, which will give you your mission briefing and start the clock. Use the W, A, S, D keys to accelerate, steer, and brake. Hit the Nitro with Left Shift for a burst of speed during intense pursuits. Use the Spacebar to jump over obstacles and C to change your camera view for the best perspective.

Mastering your vehicle and its features is key to success. You can enter or exit your vehicle with the F key. Turn on your police siren with H to clear traffic. If you get stuck, simply press R to reset your car's position. You can check the map with M, view instructions with I, and pause the game with P or Esc. Use all the tools at your disposal to become the ultimate police chase expert!
