Police Real Chase Car Simulator

How to Play Police Real Chase Car Simulator

Welcome to the force! In Police Real Chase Car Simulator, your objective is to clean up the city by completing a series of high-stakes missions. Get behind the wheel of your patrol car and navigate the bustling city streets. To start a mission, look for the yellow flags that will guide you to a blue portal. Drive into the portal to receive your instructions and begin your pursuit of justice. You can visit the garage to choose from 7 unique police cars to suit your driving style.

Master your vehicle with these simple controls: Use W, A, S, D to drive and steer your car. Hit the L-Shift key for a burst of speed with Nitro. You can change your camera view with C, reset your car with R, and even perform a jump with the Spacebar. To interact with your vehicle, press F to enter or exit. Toggle the police siren with H and view the city map with M. If you need a reminder, press I for instructions or P/Esc to pause the game.
