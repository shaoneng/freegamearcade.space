Police Real Chase Car Simulator

How to Play Police Real Chase Car Simulator

Get behind the wheel and enforce the law! In Police Real Chase Car Simulator, you become an elite police officer navigating a realistic city. Your goal is to complete challenging missions, from high-speed chases to navigating tricky city traffic. Start by visiting the garage to choose from 7 different high-performance police cars.

To begin a mission, drive around the city and look for the yellow flags. These flags will guide you to a blue portal, which is the starting point for your next assignment. Drive into the portal to receive your mission instructions and a time limit. Put your driving skills to the test and hunt down the bad guys to become the city's top cop!

Master the controls to dominate the streets:
• W/S/A/D: Accelerate, Brake/Reverse, Steer Left, Steer Right
• L-Shift: Use Nitro for a speed boost
• Spacebar: Jump
• H: Toggle Police Siren
• C: Change Camera View
• R: Reset Car
• F: Enter or Exit Vehicle
• M: View the Map
• P/Esc: Pause Game
