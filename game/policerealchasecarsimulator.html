Police Real Chase Car Simulator

How to Play Police Real Chase Car Simulator

Welcome to the force! In Police Real Chase Car Simulator, you'll immerse yourself in the life of a police officer, taking on difficult missions and high-speed chases through a realistic city. Your objective is to showcase your expert driving skills by navigating traffic, hunting down criminals, and successfully completing a variety of challenges.

To start a mission, drive around the city and follow the yellow flags. These markers will lead you to blue mission portals. Drive into a portal to receive your assignment details and a timer to begin the challenge. You can also visit the garage to select from 7 different police cars to take on your patrol.

Controls:

  • W: Accelerate
  • S: Backward
  • A: Steer Left
  • D: Steer Right
  • L-Shift: Sprint / Nitro
  • Space: Jump
  • C: Change Camera
  • R: Reset Car
  • H: Police Siren
  • M: Map
  • F: Enter/Exit Car
