Police Real Chase Car Simulator

How to Play Police Real Chase Car Simulator

Your mission is to step into the role of a police officer, taking on challenging missions and high-speed chases through a bustling city. Your main goal is to navigate the streets, hunt down criminals, and complete your assigned objectives to prove your skill as an expert driver and a dedicated officer of the law.

To begin a mission, drive around the city and look for the yellow flags. These flags will guide you to blue portals. Drive into a portal to receive your mission instructions and the time limit to complete your task. You can also press 'Z' to view available missions at any time.

Control your police vehicle using the keyboard. Use W, A, S, D to accelerate, steer left, brake/reverse, and steer right. Activate your nitro boost with L-Shift for extra speed in a pursuit. You can switch camera views with C, reset your car with R, and sound the police siren with H. Choose from 7 different police cars in the garage to find the perfect ride for the job!
