Police Real Chase Car Simulator

How to Play Police Real Chase Car Simulator

Your goal is to patrol a realistic city, take on difficult missions, and hunt down criminals. To begin a mission, explore the city and follow the yellow flags on your map. These flags will lead you to blue portals where you'll receive your assignment and a time limit to complete it. Head to the garage to choose from 7 different police cars to start your shift.

Mastering control of your vehicle is crucial for success. Use the standard keyboard controls to navigate the city streets: W to accelerate, S to go backward, A to steer left, and D to steer right. When you need a burst of speed to catch a suspect, press Left Shift to activate your nitro boost. If you get stuck or flip over, simply press R to reset your car's position.

Utilize your police equipment and interface to your advantage. Press H to activate your police siren, F to get in or out of your car, and C to cycle through different camera angles for a better view of the action. You can pull up the city map with M, review instructions with I, and pause the game at any time with P or Esc.
