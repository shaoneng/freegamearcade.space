Police Stunt Cars

How to Play Police Stunt Cars

Welcome to the force! In Police Stunt Cars, the city is your personal stunt arena. Forget chasing criminals; your mission is to explore vast maps, hit insane jumps, and rack up the highest stunt score possible. You have total freedom to drive wherever you want!

Basic Controls:

  • Drive your vehicle using the WASD or Arrow Keys.
  • Pull off sharp turns or stop quickly by pressing the Spacebar for the handbrake.
  • For a massive speed increase to clear big gaps, hold down Left Shift to use your nitro boost.

Car and Game Functions:

  • Switch between 10 different police cars (including a limo!) on the fly with the number keys 1 through 0.
  • Turn on your police lights with the E key for an authentic feel.
  • Change your camera view by pressing the C key.
  • If you get stuck, press the G key to reset your car's position.
  • To completely reset the scene, press the R key.
  • Pause the game at any time by pressing Tab or Esc.
