Police Stunt Cars

How to Play Police Stunt Cars

Welcome to the ultimate police car driving sandbox! In Police Stunt Cars, you have the freedom to explore massive, open-world maps and perform incredible stunts. The goal is to push your driving skills to the limit, discover all the hidden ramps and loops, and rack up the highest stunt score you can.

Mastering the controls is key to becoming a stunt legend. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer your vehicle. When you need a quick burst of speed for a huge jump, press Left Shift to activate the nitro boost. For tight turns and stylish drifts, use the Spacebar to engage the handbrake.

The game also offers several handy features. Press C to cycle through different camera views. You can turn on your police lights with the E key for an authentic feel. If you get stuck, press G to get your car moving again, or R to reset the entire scene. To instantly switch between the 10 available vehicles (including a limousine!), use the number keys from 1 to 0.
