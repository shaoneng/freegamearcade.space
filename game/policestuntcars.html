Police Stunt Cars

How to Play Police Stunt Cars

Get ready to burn rubber and patrol the streets in Police Stunt Cars! This game gives you total freedom to explore vast, open-world maps designed for one thing: epic stunts. Choose from a fleet of 10 different police cars, from standard cruisers to a high-speed limousine, and find all the ramps, loops, and hidden paths. There are no criminals to chase, just your own high score to beat. Push your driving skills to the limit and become a legend of the force!

Controls

Master these controls to dominate the streets:

  • Drive: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Handbrake: Spacebar
  • Nitro Boost: Left Shift
  • Unstuck Car: G Key
  • Reset Scene: R Key
  • Toggle Police Lights: E Key
  • Change Camera: C Key
  • Change Car: Number Keys (1-0)
  • Pause Menu: Tab or Esc
