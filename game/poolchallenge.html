Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Your objective in Pool Challenge is to sink all of your designated balls into the pockets. It takes skill and precision to clear the table without missing a shot!

Aiming: Move your mouse around the cue ball to line up your shot. A helpful guideline will appear, showing the predicted path of the cue ball and the object ball, helping you plan your next move.

Power Control: Once you've aimed, click and hold the left mouse button. Drag the cue stick backward to set the power of your shot. The further back you pull the cue, the harder you will hit the ball.

Shooting: Release the mouse button to strike the cue ball. Plan your shots carefully, use the angles, and clear the table to prove you're a true pool champion!
