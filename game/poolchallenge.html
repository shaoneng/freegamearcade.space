Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Welcome to Pool Challenge! Your goal is to sink all the colored balls into the pockets on the billiard table. Master the controls to become a true pool shark.

Aiming: Use your mouse to move the cue stick and aim your shot. Pay attention to the guide lines that appear at the tip of the cue; they will show you the predicted path of the cue ball and the target ball.

Shooting: Once you've lined up your shot, press and hold the left mouse button. Drag the cue backward to set the power of your shot. The further back you pull the cue, the more powerful your strike will be.

Release: Let go of the mouse button to release the cue and make the shot. Plan your moves carefully, use precise angles, and clear the table to win!
