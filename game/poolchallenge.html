Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Welcome to the ultimate test of your billiards skills! In Pool Challenge, your objective is to clear the table by sinking all the colored balls into the pockets. It requires precision, strategy, and a steady hand.

Aiming: Use your mouse to move the cue stick around the cue ball. A guideline will appear, showing the projected path of both the cue ball and the target ball, helping you line up the perfect shot.

Shooting: Once you've lined up your shot, click and hold the left mouse button. Drag your mouse backward to pull the cue away from the ball. The further back you pull, the more power you'll apply to the shot. A power meter will indicate the strength of your strike.

Making the Shot: When you're happy with your power level, simply release the mouse button to shoot. Watch as the cue ball strikes and hopefully sends your target ball right into the pocket!
