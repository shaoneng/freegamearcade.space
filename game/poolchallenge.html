Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Welcome to Pool Challenge! Your goal is to use the cue stick to pocket all the balls on the table. Mastering your aim and power is the key to victory in this classic game of skill.

Aiming: Move your mouse around the cue ball to line up your shot. A helpful guideline will appear from the tip of the cue, showing the potential path of the cue ball and the target ball. Use this guide to plan your angles carefully.

Shooting: Once you've aimed, click and hold the left mouse button. Drag the cue stick backward to set the power of your shot. The further you pull it back, the more powerful your strike will be. Release the mouse button to shoot.

Clear the table by sinking each ball into the pockets one by one. Take on the challenge and see if you can make every shot count!
