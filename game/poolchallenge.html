Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Welcome to Pool Challenge! Your objective is to sink all the colored balls into the pockets on the billiard table. Precision and strategy are your best friends in this classic game.

Aiming: Move your mouse around the table to aim the cue stick. A helpful guideline will show you the predicted path of the cue ball and the object ball, helping you line up the perfect shot.

Shooting: Once you've lined up your shot, press and hold the left mouse button. Drag the mouse backward to pull the cue stick back. The further you pull it, the more power you'll put into your shot. Release the mouse button to strike the cue ball.

Master your aim and control your power to clear the table and prove you're a true pool shark!
