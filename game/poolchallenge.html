Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Welcome to Pool Challenge! Your goal is simple: use the cue stick to sink all the colored balls into the pockets on the billiard table. It's a game of skill, precision, and a little bit of physics.

To play, use your mouse to aim the cue stick. A helpful guideline will appear, showing the projected path of the cue ball. Once you've lined up your shot, press and hold the left mouse button.

While holding the mouse button, drag the cue stick backward. The further back you pull it, the more power you will apply to your shot. Find the right balance between power and precision for the perfect break or a delicate tap.

Release the mouse button to strike the cue ball. Watch as the balls scatter, and plan your next move. Clear the table to prove you're the ultimate pool champion!
