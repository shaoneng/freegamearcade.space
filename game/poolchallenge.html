Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Welcome to Pool Challenge! Your goal is to use the cue stick to pocket all the balls on the table. Precision and a good eye are your best tools in this classic game of skill.

To play, use your mouse to aim the cue stick. A guide line will appear, showing you the projected path of the cue ball and the target ball, helping you line up the perfect shot.

Once you've aimed, click and hold the mouse button. Drag the cue backward to set the power of your shot. The further back you pull the cue, the harder you'll hit the ball. Release the mouse button to take the shot. Clear the table to win!
