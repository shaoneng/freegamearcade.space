Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Welcome to the ultimate test of your billiards skills! The goal in Pool Challenge is simple: sink all your designated balls into the pockets before your opponent does.

Aiming: Move your mouse around the cue ball to line up your shot. Pay close attention to the guide lines that appearâ€”they will show you the predicted path of both the cue ball and the target ball.

Power Control: Once you have your shot lined up, click and hold the left mouse button. Drag the cue stick backward to set the power of your shot. The further you pull the stick back, the harder you will hit the ball.

Shooting: When you're happy with your aim and power, simply release the mouse button to take the shot. Plan your moves carefully, use the rails, and clear the table to become a pool champion!
