Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Welcome to Pool Challenge! Your objective is to sink all of your designated balls (either solids or stripes) into the pockets on the billiard table. It's a test of skill, precision, and strategy that requires you to clear the table one shot at a time.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use your mouse to aim the cue stick. To prepare your shot, click and hold the left mouse button, then drag the cue backward. The further you pull the cue back, the more powerful your shot will be. Release the mouse button to strike the cue ball.

To help with your aim, a guideline will appear from the tip of the cue, showing the potential path of the cue ball and the object ball. Use this guide to line up your shots perfectly. Master the angles and the power of your shots to clear the table and claim victory!
