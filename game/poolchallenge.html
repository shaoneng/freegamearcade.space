Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Your goal in Pool Challenge is to sink all the colored balls into the pockets on the billiard table. The controls are simple and intuitive, designed to give you precise control over every shot.

To aim, move your mouse around the table. An aiming guide will appear from the tip of your cue stick, showing the projected path of the cue ball and the target ball. Use this guide to line up the perfect shot.

Once you have your shot lined up, press and hold the left mouse button. Drag your mouse backward to pull the cue stick away from the ball. The further back you pull the cue, the more power you'll apply to your shot.

Release the mouse button to strike the cue ball. Watch as your carefully planned shot unfolds! Plan your moves, use the right amount of power, and clear the table to win.
