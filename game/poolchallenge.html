Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Welcome to Pool Challenge! Your objective is to pocket all the colored balls on the billiard table using the cue ball.

Aiming: Move your mouse cursor around the cue ball to line up your shot. A helpful guideline will appear from the tip of the cue, showing the potential path of the ball you're targeting. Precision is key to success!

Shooting: Once you have aimed, click and hold the left mouse button. Drag the cue stick backward to adjust the power of your shot. A power meter on the side will indicate the intensity. The further you pull back, the more powerful your shot will be.

Release: Let go of the mouse button to strike the cue ball. Plan your shots carefully, use the angles, and try to clear the table to win the challenge!
