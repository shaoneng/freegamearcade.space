Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Welcome to the ultimate Pool Challenge! The goal is simple: sink all your designated balls into the pockets before your opponent does, and then finish by pocketing the 8-ball.

Aiming: Move your mouse around the table to aim the cue stick. Use the projected lines that appear from the cue tip to predict the path of the cue ball and the target ball.

Shooting: Once you've lined up your shot, press and hold the left mouse button. Drag the cue stick backward to set the power of your shot. The further you pull back, the harder you'll hit the ball.

Making the Shot: Release the mouse button to strike the cue ball. A combination of precise aiming and careful power control is the key to mastering the game and clearing the table!
