Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Welcome to Pool Challenge, where your billiards skills are put to the test! The objective is simple: sink all the balls into the pockets on the table. Mastering the controls is key to victory.

To play, use your mouse to aim the cue stick. The dotted line will show you the projected path of the cue ball and the target ball, helping you line up the perfect shot. Once you have your aim, click and hold the left mouse button on the cue.

To set the power of your shot, drag the cue stick backward. The further you pull it back, the more powerful your shot will be. When you're ready, simply release the mouse button to strike the cue ball. Think strategically and clear the table to win!
