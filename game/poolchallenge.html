Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Welcome to the ultimate Pool Challenge! The objective is simple: sink all of your designated balls into the pockets on the billiard table before pocketing the 8-ball last.

Use your mouse to aim the cue stick. A handy guideline will appear, showing you the projected path of the cue ball and the first ball it will strike. This is crucial for planning your shots and setting up future plays.

Once you're happy with your aim, click and hold the left mouse button on the cue. Drag the cue backward to set the power of your shot. The further you pull it back, the harder you'll hit the ball. Release the mouse button to take the shot. Master the art of aiming and power control to clear the table and become a pool champion!
