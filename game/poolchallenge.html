Pool Challenge

How to Play Pool Challenge

Welcome to the Pool Challenge! Your goal is to sink all the colored balls into the pockets on the billiard table. Precision and strategy are key to becoming a master.

Aiming: Use your mouse to move the cue stick and aim your shot. Pay close attention to the guide lines that appear; they will show you the predicted path of the cue ball and the target ball, helping you line up the perfect angle.

Shooting: Once you've lined up your shot, press and hold the left mouse button. Drag the mouse backward, away from the cue ball, to set the power of your shot. The further you pull the cue stick back, the harder you will hit the ball. Release the mouse button to take the shot.

Master the combination of precise aiming and perfect power to clear the table and conquer the Pool Challenge!
