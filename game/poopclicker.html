Poop Clicker

How to Play Poop Clicker

Dive into the wonderfully weird world of Poop Clicker! This is an idle clicker game with a humorous twist. You don't need complex strategies or quick reflexes, just a dedication to clicking and a good sense of humor.

Your main objective is to click on the large poop displayed in the center of the screen. Each click or tap will reward you with coins. The more you click, the more you earn!

Use the coins you collect to purchase upgrades from the shop. These upgrades are crucial for your progress. You can buy items that increase the number of coins you get per click or purchase assets that automatically generate coins for you over time, even when you're not clicking. The key is to reinvest your earnings to grow your plumbing empire faster!
