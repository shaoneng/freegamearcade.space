Poop Clicker

How to Play Poop Clicker

Welcome to the strangely satisfying world of Poop Clicker! Your goal is to become a master plumber by... well, clicking on poop. It's a messy job, but someone's got to do it, and the rewards are fantastic!

The gameplay is simple: use your mouse or tap the screen to click on the poop. Each click earns you valuable coins. The more you click, the more coins you'll collect.

As you accumulate coins, you can spend them on various upgrades. These upgrades will help you earn coins faster, even when you're not actively clicking. Look for improvements like better tools, automated helpers, and other efficiency boosts to maximize your income.

Continue clicking and upgrading to watch your earnings soar. The objective is to become the ultimate poop-clicking tycoon in this quirky and surprisingly addictive idle game!
