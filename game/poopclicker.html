Poop Clicker

How to Play Poop Clicker

Welcome to the wonderfully weird world of Poop Clicker! Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to become the ultimate plumber by clicking your way to riches.

The core gameplay is incredibly simple: use your mouse or touch screen to click on the large poop emoji in the center of the game. Each click earns you valuable coins. The more you click, the more you earn!

Use your hard-earned coins to purchase upgrades from the shop. These upgrades, like toilets, plungers, and other plumbing tools, will automatically generate coins for you over time, even when you're not clicking. You can also buy upgrades that increase the number of coins you receive per click. Keep clicking and upgrading to build your empire and see how high your score can go!
