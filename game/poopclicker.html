Poop Clicker

How to Play Poop Clicker

Welcome to the wonderfully weird world of Poop Clicker! Your main goal is to click on the large poop icon at the center of the screen. Each click earns you coins, which are the currency of this quirky plumbing empire.

Use the coins you earn to purchase upgrades and automated helpers. These upgrades will increase the number of coins you get per click or will automatically generate coins for you over time, even when you're not clicking. This is the key to becoming a true poop-clicking tycoon!

The objective is to keep clicking, keep upgrading, and watch your coin count soar to astronomical heights. See how quickly you can unlock all the upgrades and become the most successful plumber in the game!
