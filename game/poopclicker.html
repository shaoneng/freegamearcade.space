Poop Clicker

How to Play Poop Clicker

Welcome to the wonderfully weird world of Poop Clicker! Your job is simple but essential: you're a plumber, and it's time to clear the pipes. This game is all about clicking!

The Main Goal: The objective is to click on the big poop icon in the center of the screen. Each click earns you valuable coins.

Upgrades and Rewards: Use the coins you collect to purchase upgrades. These upgrades will help you earn coins faster, even when you're not actively clicking. Invest wisely to maximize your plumbing empire and become the ultimate poop-clicking tycoon!

Just keep clicking, keep upgrading, and watch your rewards pile up. It's a messy job, but someone's got to do it!
