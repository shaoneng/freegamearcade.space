Poop Clicker

How to Play Poop Clicker

Your main objective in Poop Clicker is straightforward and... unique! Simply click or tap on the large poop icon in the center of the screen. Each click will generate coins, which are your primary currency in the game.

As you accumulate coins, you can spend them on various upgrades. These upgrades will help you earn more coins per click or even start generating coins automatically, even when you're not clicking. This idle game mechanic is key to building your plumbing empire.

Keep clicking, keep upgrading, and watch your earnings skyrocket. The goal is to maximize your coin production and unlock all the available upgrades. Get ready to embrace this quirky and surprisingly addictive challenge!
