Poop Clicker

How to Play Poop Clicker

Welcome to the wonderfully weird world of Poop Clicker! In this unique idle game, you embrace the unsung hero role of a plumber. Your objective is straightforward but strangely addictive.

To play, simply use your mouse to click (or tap on mobile) the large poop icon in the center of the screen. Each click earns you valuable coins. The more you click, the more you earn!

Keep an eye out for upgrades and rewards that you can purchase with your coins. These will help you automate your earnings and increase your clicking power, turning your humble plumbing operation into a coin-collecting empire. Get ready to click your way to riches in this messy, but hilarious adventure!
