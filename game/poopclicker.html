Poop Clicker

How to Play Poop Clicker

Welcome to the wonderfully weird world of Poop Clicker! This isn't your average idle game. You're a dedicated plumber, and your mission is to tackle a... messy situation. Your goal is to click your way to riches by, you guessed it, clicking on poop!

The controls are incredibly simple. Just use your mouse to click (or your finger to tap on mobile devices) on the main poop icon in the center of the screen. Each click earns you valuable coins.

As you accumulate coins, you can spend them on powerful upgrades. These upgrades will help you earn coins faster, even when you're not clicking. Invest wisely to build your plumbing empire and become the ultimate Poop Clicker champion!
