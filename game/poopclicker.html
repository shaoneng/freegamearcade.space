Poop Clicker

How to Play Poop Clicker

Welcome to the wonderfully weird world of Poop Clicker! Your goal in this idle game is to embrace the life of a dedicated plumber and turn a messy situation into a profitable enterprise. The gameplay is simple but incredibly addictive.

Controls: Use your mouse to click (or tap on your screen) on the poop in the center. Every click earns you coins.

As you collect more coins, you can spend them on various upgrades. These upgrades will help you earn coins faster, either by increasing the amount you get per click or by automatically generating coins for you over time. Keep an eye on the upgrade menu and invest wisely to maximize your earnings. The more you click and upgrade, the richer you'll become in this surprisingly satisfying game!
