Princess Wedding

How to Play Princess Wedding

Welcome to Princess Wedding! Your mission is to be the ultimate royal wedding stylist and prepare the princess for her big day. Every detail, from her hair to her shoes, is in your hands.

Use your mouse or touch screen to navigate through the various customization categories on the side of the screen. You'll find options for hairstyles, wedding gowns, tops, skirts, veils, shoes, jewelry, and even the flower bouquet. Simply click on a category to see the available options.

Click on any item to see how it looks on the princess. Mix and match different pieces to create the perfect ensemble. Some exclusive items can be unlocked by watching a short ad. Once you've created the perfect look, the princess will be ready for her royal ceremony!
