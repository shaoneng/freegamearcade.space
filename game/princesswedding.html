Princess Wedding

How to Play Princess Wedding

Welcome to the royal wedding! In Princess Wedding, you are the chief stylist responsible for creating the bride's dream look for her magical day. Your goal is to select the perfect combination of clothing and accessories to make her shine.

The controls are simple and intuitive. All the dress-up categories, such as hairstyle, gowns, veils, and jewelry, are listed on the screen. Use your mouse to click on a category to view the available options. Then, click on the item you wish for the princess to wear.

Explore a vast collection of items, including over 25 unique wedding gowns! You can also mix and match by choosing a separate top and skirt. Don't forget to complete the look with essential royal accessories like elegant furs and gloves, a beautiful veil, and a stunning flower bouquet.

Experiment with different combinations until you've created the most breathtaking bridal outfit. Once you're happy with your design, the princess will be ready to walk down the aisle in style!
