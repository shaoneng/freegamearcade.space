Princess Wedding

How to Play Princess Wedding

Welcome to the royal dressing room! In Princess Wedding, your goal is to style the princess for her magical wedding ceremony. It's your moment to shine as the ultimate royal stylist!

Playing is simple and intuitive. All you need is your mouse. On the screen, you'll find various categories for the bride's outfit, such as hairstyles, wedding dresses, veils, jewelry, shoes, and even the flower bouquet.

Click on each category to browse the available options. Experiment with different combinations to find the perfect look. You can choose from over 25 unique gowns or get creative by mixing and matching separate tops and skirts. Don't forget the essential royal accessories like furs and gloves to complete the ensemble. Some exclusive items can be unlocked by watching a short video, giving you even more choices to create a stunning bridal masterpiece.
