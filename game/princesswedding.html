Princess Wedding

How to Play Princess Wedding

Your royal duty awaits in Princess Wedding! Your goal is to become the ultimate bridal stylist and prepare the princess for her unforgettable wedding day. The controls are simple and intuitive, making it easy for anyone to play.

Using your mouse or touch screen, navigate through the various customization categories listed on the screen. These categories include everything you need for a stunning bridal look: hairstyles, veils, exquisite jewelry, shoes, and a beautiful flower bouquet.

Click on a category to view the options. Select your favorite items to see them on the princess. You can choose from over 25 unique wedding gowns or get creative by mixing and matching different tops and skirts. Don't forget to complete the look with elegant furs and gloves, essential for a royal ceremony. Keep an eye out for special items that can be unlocked to expand your wardrobe even further!
