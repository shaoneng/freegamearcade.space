Princess Wedding

How to Play Princess Wedding

Get ready to design the wedding of a lifetime in Princess Wedding! Your role is to be the ultimate royal stylist, preparing the beautiful princess for her magical ceremony. The controls are simple and intuitive, making it easy for anyone to play.

Use your mouse or tap the on-screen icons to navigate through the extensive wardrobe. You'll find categories listed for every part of the outfit, including hairstyles, gowns, veils, jewelry, shoes, and even the flower bouquet. Simply click on a category to view all the available options.

Select items to instantly see them on the princess. You can choose from over 25 unique gowns or mix and match tops and skirts for a custom look. Don't forget essential royal accessories like furs and gloves to complete the ensemble. Some exclusive items can be unlocked by watching a short video. Mix, match, and let your creativity shine to create the most stunning bride ever!
