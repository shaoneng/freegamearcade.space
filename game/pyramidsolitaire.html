Pyramid Solitaire

How to Play Pyramid Solitaire

Pyramid Solitaire is a classic card game where your objective is to clear a pyramid of cards by matching pairs that sum up to 13. Begin by looking for available pairs among the cards that are not covered by others.

Understanding card values is key: Kings are worth 13 and can be removed by themselves. Queens count as 12, Jacks as 11, Aces as 1, and all other number cards are taken at their face value. You can match cards of any suit or color.

Only cards that are fully uncovered are available for matching. If you can't find any matches in the pyramid, click on the deck to draw new cards. You can cycle through the deck multiple times. A good strategy is to prioritize matches that will expose more hidden cards in the layers above, giving you more options for future moves. There's no timer, so take your time and plan your moves strategically to clear the pyramid!
