Pyramid Solitaire

How to Play Pyramid Solitaire

Pyramid Solitaire is a relaxing free online card game where the goal is to clear a pyramid of cards. You achieve this by selecting pairs of uncovered cards that add up to a total of 13.

Understanding card values is key. Kings are worth 13 and can be removed by themselves. Queens are 12, Jacks are 11, and all number cards hold their face value (Aces are 1). You can match any two cards, regardless of suit or color, as long as their values sum to 13. For example, a 6 and a 7, or a Queen and an Ace.

Only cards that are fully uncovered can be played. Start by looking for pairs in the bottom layers of the pyramid. If you can't find any matches, click the stock pile at the bottom to draw a new card. You can cycle through the deck multiple times. For a better score, think strategically: if you have a choice between two pairs, choose the one that will uncover more new cards.

Beyond the fun, playing Pyramid Solitaire offers a gentle mental workout. It helps improve concentration, memory, and basic math skills. It's a perfect way to de-stress, take a mindful break, and sharpen your focus in a calm, no-pressure environment. Enjoy the game!
