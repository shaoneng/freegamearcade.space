Pyramid Solitaire

How to Play Pyramid Solitaire

The objective in Pyramid Solitaire is to clear a pyramid of cards by selecting pairs that add up to 13. Only cards that are fully uncovered are available to be played.

Card values are as follows: Kings are worth 13 and can be removed on their own. Queens are 12, Jacks are 11, and all number cards have their face value (Aces are 1). You can match cards of any suit or color to reach the total of 13.

Begin by selecting pairs from the uncovered cards at the bottom of the pyramid. If you run out of available matches on the board, click the deck to draw a new card from the stock pile. You can cycle through the deck as many times as you need to find new pairs.

For a better score, play strategically. When you have a choice between two matches, try to pick the one that will expose more new cards, opening up more possibilities for future moves. Planning ahead is the key to clearing the entire pyramid!
