Pyramid Solitaire

How to Play Pyramid Solitaire

The goal of Pyramid Solitaire is to clear a pyramid of cards from the game board. You achieve this by selecting pairs of uncovered cards that have a total value of 13.

Card values are straightforward: Number cards are worth their face value (e.g., a 7 is worth 7 points), an Ace is 1, a Jack is 11, and a Queen is 12. Kings are unique; they are valued at 13 and can be removed by themselves without needing a pair.

Only cards that are not covered by any other card are available to be played. If you cannot find any matching pairs in the pyramid, you can click on the stock pile to draw a new card. This card can be used to form a pair with any uncovered card in the pyramid. Think strategically to uncover as many cards as possible with each move.
