Pyramid Solitaire

How to Play Pyramid Solitaire

The main goal in Pyramid Solitaire is to clear the pyramid of cards by selecting pairs that add up to 13. Only cards that are fully uncovered in the pyramid can be selected for a match.

Card values are as follows: Aces are 1, number cards are their face value, Jacks are 11, and Queens are 12. Kings are worth 13 by themselves and can be removed individually by simply clicking on them.

If you cannot find any matching pairs in the pyramid, you can click the deck at the bottom to draw a new card. This card can be paired with any available card in the pyramid or from the waste pile. You can go through the deck multiple times.

To win, you must strategically remove all cards from the pyramid. A good tip is to choose matches that will expose the most new cards, giving you more options for your next move.
