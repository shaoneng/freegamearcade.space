Racing Horizon

How to Play Racing Horizon

Get ready to burn rubber in Racing Horizon! The goal is simple: take the wheel of a high-performance sports car and weave through dense highway traffic to cross the finish line. Use your driving skills to avoid collisions and complete various challenges.

Controls:

  • Steer & Accelerate: Use the W, A, S, D keys or the Arrow Keys to control your car.
  • Change View: Press the N or C key to switch between different camera angles for the best view of the action.

Game Modes & Upgrades:

Racing Horizon offers several exciting game modes to test your abilities, including 15 challenging missions, an endless driving mode, a race against the clock in "Time Up," and a thrilling police chase in "Escape" mode. As you race, you'll earn money that you can use in "My Garage" to buy faster cars and apply crucial upgrades to your vehicle's engine (Chip Tuning), brakes, and handling. Save up, upgrade your ride, and dominate the highway!
