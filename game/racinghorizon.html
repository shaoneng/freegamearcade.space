Racing Horizon

How to Play Racing Horizon

Your goal in Racing Horizon is to master the highway, weaving through traffic to reach your objectives. Use the W, A, S, D keys or the ARROW KEYS to steer, accelerate, and brake your car. For a different perspective on the action, press the N key to change the camera view.

The game offers several exciting modes to test your skills. In Missions mode, you'll tackle 15 distinct challenges. Endless mode lets you drive freely for as long as you can survive the traffic. Race against the clock in Time Up mode, or feel the adrenaline in Escape mode as you try to outrun a relentless police chase.

Head to My Garage to spend your earnings. You can purchase a fleet of powerful cars, from nimble coupes to robust SUVs. Each vehicle can be improved by upgrading its Chip Tuning (power), Brake Level, and Handling Level. A well-tuned car is your key to conquering the toughest missions and setting new high scores.
