Racing Horizon

How to Play Racing Horizon

Your goal in Racing Horizon is to become the ultimate highway driver. Navigate through dense traffic, complete thrilling missions, and earn cash to upgrade your ride. The game features several exciting modes: tackle 15 unique stages in Mission mode, drive as far as you can in Endless mode, beat the clock in Time Up mode, or outrun the cops in the intense Escape mode.

Mastering the controls is key to victory. Use the W, A, S, D keys or the Arrow Keys to steer, accelerate, and brake your car. To get a better view of the action, press the N or C key to cycle through different camera angles. Focus on weaving through traffic skillfully to avoid crashes and reach your objective.

Visit "My Garage" to spend your hard-earned cash. You can purchase a fleet of high-performance vehicles, each with unique stats for power, handling, and braking. You can also upgrade your existing cars by improving their Chip Tuning for more speed, enhancing the Brake Level for better stopping power, and increasing the Handling Level for superior control in tight situations. Choose your car, upgrade it wisely, and hit the road!
