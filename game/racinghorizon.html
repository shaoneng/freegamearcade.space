Racing Horizon

How to Play Racing Horizon

Your goal in Racing Horizon is to master the art of high-speed highway driving. Jump into your sports car and navigate through dense traffic to reach your objectives without crashing. Use the W, A, S, D keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to steer, accelerate, and brake.

To get a better view of the action, press the N key to cycle through different camera angles. The game offers several exciting modes to test your abilities: complete 15 unique Missions, drive as far as you can in Endless mode, beat the clock in Time Up mode, or outrun the law in the thrilling Escape police chase.

Visit 'My Garage' to spend the cash you earn. You can purchase a fleet of new vehicles, from nimble coupes to powerful SUVs. Each car can be upgraded to improve its performance. Invest in Chip Tuning for more power, enhance your Brake Level for better stopping power, and improve the Handling Level to take corners like a pro.
