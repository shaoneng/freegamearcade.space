Racing Horizon

How to Play Racing Horizon

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Racing Horizon! Your main goal is to navigate through busy highways, complete missions, and reach the finish line without crashing. Use your earnings to purchase and upgrade a variety of powerful sports cars and SUVs in your garage.

Controls:

  • Drive: Use the W, A, S, D keys or the Arrow Keys to steer, accelerate, and brake your vehicle.
  • Change Camera: Press the N key to cycle through different camera perspectives for the best view of the action.

Game Modes:

  • Missions: Tackle 15 unique stages with specific objectives, testing your driving precision.
  • Endless: Drive for as long as you can, weaving through traffic and aiming for a new high score.
  • Time Up: Race against the clock to reach checkpoints before your time runs out.
  • Escape: A thrilling police chase where you must use your skill to evade capture and escape.

Master the controls, choose your favorite mode, and upgrade your ride to become the king of the road!
