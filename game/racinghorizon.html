Racing Horizon

How to Play Racing Horizon

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Racing Horizon! Your main objective is to navigate your high-performance car through bustling highway traffic, completing objectives and reaching the finish line without crashing. Earn money from your races to spend in the garage on new cars and powerful upgrades.

Mastering the controls is key to dominating the road:

  • Move/Steer: Use the W, A, S, D keys or the ARROW KEYS to control your vehicle.
  • Change Camera: Press the N key to cycle through different camera perspectives for the best view of the action.
  • Special Action: Use the C key to help you secure your path to the finish line.

The game features multiple exciting modes to test your driving prowess. Take on 15 challenging stages in Missions mode, see how long you can last in Endless mode, beat the clock in Time Up, or feel the adrenaline of a high-speed pursuit in Escape mode where you must outrun the police.

Visit "My Garage" to customize your experience. Purchase a variety of cars, from nimble coupes to powerful SUVs, and invest in upgrades for Chip Tuning, Brake Level, and Handling to gain a competitive edge on the highway.
