Racing Horizon

How to Play Racing Horizon

Your goal in Racing Horizon is to master the art of high-speed highway driving. Navigate through dense traffic, complete challenging objectives, and prove you're the fastest on the road. Start with a basic car and earn money by completing races to upgrade your vehicle or buy new, more powerful ones from the garage.

Controls:

  • Drive: Use the W, A, S, D keys or the ARROW KEYS to steer, accelerate, and brake.
  • Change Camera: Press the N key to cycle through different camera perspectives for the best view.
  • Reset Car: If you get stuck or crash, press the C key to reset your vehicle's position.

Game Modes & Features:

  • Missions: Take on 15 unique stages with specific objectives.
  • Endless: Enjoy a relaxing drive with no time limits or goals, just you and the open road.
  • Time Up: Race against the clock to reach checkpoints before time runs out.
  • Escape: A thrilling police chase where you must evade the cops to win.
  • My Garage: Visit the garage to spend your earnings on new cars or upgrade your current ride's engine (Chip Tuning), brakes, and handling.
