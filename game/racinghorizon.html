Racing Horizon

How to Play Racing Horizon

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Racing Horizon! Your goal is simple: get to the finish line by skillfully navigating your high-performance car through busy highway traffic. Whether you're on a mission, racing against time, or just enjoying the open road, masterful driving is key to success.

Use the W, A, S, D keys or the ARROW KEYS to steer, accelerate, and brake your vehicle. For a different perspective on the action, press the N key to change the camera view.

The game features several exciting modes: Missions, where you complete 15 unique challenges; Endless, for a free-driving experience; Time Up, a race against the clock; and Escape, a thrilling police chase. Earn money from your races to visit "My Garage", where you can purchase a fleet of impressive cars and apply crucial upgrades to your Chip Tuning, Brake Level, and Handling to stay ahead of the competition.
