Racing Horizon

How to Play Racing Horizon

In Racing Horizon, your primary goal is to become a master of the highway. You'll need to drive your car through dense traffic to reach the finish line, all while avoiding collisions. Complete missions, earn cash, and prove you have the skills to handle the most powerful cars.

Use the W, A, S, D keys or the ARROW KEYS to accelerate, brake, and steer your vehicle. To get a different perspective on the action, press the N key to change your camera view.

The game features several exciting modes to test your driving prowess. Take on 15 challenging levels in Missions mode, drive for as long as you can in Endless mode, race against the timer in Time Up mode, or evade the authorities in the thrilling Escape mode. Use the money you earn to buy new cars or upgrade your current vehicle's engine, brakes, and handling in the "My Garage" section.
