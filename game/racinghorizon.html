Racing Horizon

How to Play Racing Horizon

Take control of your high-speed vehicle using your keyboard. Use the W, A, S, D keys or the Arrow Keys to steer, accelerate, and brake through the busy highway traffic. To get a better view of the action, press the N key to cycle through different camera angles. The main objective is to navigate the road and complete your mission without crashing.

Racing Horizon offers several exciting game modes. Take on 15 challenging levels in Missions mode, enjoy a limitless drive in Endless mode, race against the clock in Time Up, or feel the adrenaline of a police chase in Escape mode. As you race, you'll earn money that you can use in "My Garage" to purchase faster cars or upgrade your current vehicle's engine performance, brakes, and handling.
