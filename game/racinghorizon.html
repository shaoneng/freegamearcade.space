Racing Horizon

How to Play Racing Horizon

Master the road using your keyboard. Use the W, A, S, D keys or the ARROW KEYS to steer, accelerate, and brake your vehicle. Weave through traffic with precision to avoid collisions and reach the finish line in one piece.

Your main goal is to conquer the highway across various exciting game modes. Test your skills in Missions mode with 15 unique stages, enjoy a relaxing drive in Endless mode, race against the clock in Time Up, or evade law enforcement in the thrilling Escape mode. You can also press N to cycle through different camera views for the perfect driving perspective.

Don't forget to visit My Garage! Use the cash you earn from racing to purchase new, faster cars and apply crucial upgrades. Improve your vehicle's Chip Tuning for more power, enhance its Brake Level for better stopping power, and fine-tune its Handling Level to take corners like a pro.
