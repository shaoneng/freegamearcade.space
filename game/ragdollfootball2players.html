How to Play Ragdoll Football 2 Players

Welcome to the silliest football pitch you've ever seen! In Ragdoll Football 2 Players, the goal is simple: score more goals than your opponent. However, controlling your floppy stickman player is where the real fun and challenge begins. Use the physics-based controls to jump, flop, and kick the ball toward the opponent's net.

You can choose to play solo against a challenging AI opponent or grab a friend for a hilarious head-to-head match. Before the game starts, you can customize the controls to your liking. The standard keyboard controls are:

  • Player 1: Use the Arrow Keys to move and jump.
  • Player 2: Use the W, A, S, D keys to move and jump.
