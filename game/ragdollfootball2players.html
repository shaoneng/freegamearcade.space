Ragdoll Football 2 Players

How to Play Ragdoll Football 2 Players

Welcome to the silliest football match you'll ever play! In Ragdoll Football 2 Players, the goal is simple: get the ball into your opponent's net. The catch? Your players are floppy ragdolls controlled by physics, leading to hilarious and unpredictable gameplay. Flop, jump, and kick your way across the field to score goals.

You can customize your controls before each match. For keyboard players, one player typically uses the WASD keys, while the other uses the Arrow Keys to control their ragdoll. The game also supports mouse, gamepad, and mobile touch controls for a flexible playing experience. Master the art of the awkward jump and the perfectly timed floppy kick to outmaneuver your opponent.

Challenge a friend in the exciting 2-player mode, or test your skills against the computer AI. Each match is a new adventure filled with random bounces and surprising plays. Keep an eye out for special boosters that appear on the field—they can give you a crucial advantage, like making your player bigger or freezing your opponent!
