Ragdoll Football 2 Players

How to Play Ragdoll Football 2 Players

Welcome to the wacky world of Ragdoll Football 2 Players! The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent. However, the gameplay is anything but ordinary. You'll control floppy, physics-based stickman players who tumble, jump, and kick their way around the field. Embrace the chaos as you try to guide the ball into the net. You can challenge the computer in a solo match or grab a friend for some hilarious head-to-head action in 2-player mode.

The controls are straightforward, but mastering the ragdoll movement is the real challenge! Before each match, you have the option to customize the controls to your liking. The game also supports mouse, gamepad, and mobile controls for flexible play.

Keep an eye out for special boosters that appear on the field. These can give you a crucial advantage, from freezing your opponent to giving your player a speed boost.
