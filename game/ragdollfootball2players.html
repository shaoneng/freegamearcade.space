How to Play Ragdoll Football 2 Players

Welcome to the wacky world of Ragdoll Football 2 Players! The objective is simple: score more goals than your opponent. The challenge? Controlling your floppy, unpredictable stickman player!

Before the match starts, you can customize the controls to your liking. The game supports keyboard, mouse, gamepad, and mobile touch controls. Here are the default keyboard controls:

  • Player 1: Use the arrow keys to move your player.
  • Player 2: Use the W, A, S, D keys to move your player.
