Ragdoll Football 2 Players

How to Play Ragdoll Football 2 Players

Get ready for a football game unlike any other! In Ragdoll Football 2 Players, the goal is simple: get the ball into your opponent's net. The twist? Your players are floppy, physics-based ragdolls, leading to hilarious and unpredictable gameplay.

Before the match begins, you can customize the controls to your liking. The game supports keyboard controls for two players, as well as mouse and gamepad options. Jump, flop, and kick your way across the field. Use the chaotic physics to your advantage to block shots, tackle opponents, and score unbelievable goals. You can challenge the computer in a solo match or grab a friend for an exciting local 2-player showdown.

Keep an eye out for special boosters that appear on the field. These can give you a temporary advantage, like increased speed or a bigger player, to help you secure the win. The first player to reach the score limit wins the match!
