Ragdoll Football 2 Players

How to Play Ragdoll Football 2 Players

Welcome to the most chaotic and amusing football match you'll ever play! In Ragdoll Football 2 Players, the goal is simple: score on your opponent. However, the gameplay is anything but ordinary. You control floppy, physics-driven stickman players who will stumble, jump, and kick with unpredictable results. Master the art of controlled chaos to outmaneuver your opponent and land hilarious goals.

You can challenge a friend in local 2-player mode or test your skills against the computer. The controls are straightforward, but timing your jumps and kicks perfectly is the key to victory. Keep an eye out for special boosters that appear during the match to gain a surprising advantage.

The game supports keyboard, mouse, and gamepad controls, which can be configured before you start a match. The standard keyboard layout for two players is:

  • Player 1: Use the Arrow Keys to move and kick.
  • Player 2: Use the W, A, S, D keys to move and kick.
