Ragdoll Football 2 Players

How to Play Ragdoll Football 2 Players

Ragdoll Football 2 Players offers a hilariously simple gameplay experience with flexible controls. Your goal is to guide your floppy stickman player to kick the ball into the opponent's net, just like traditional football, but with a comedic ragdoll twist!

Before each match, you have the option to customize your controls. The game supports various input methods, including mouse, gamepad, and mobile touch controls, making it accessible on different devices. You can play against the computer or challenge a friend in the exciting 2-player mode for even more chaotic fun!

Mastering your ragdoll player's floppy movements is key to scoring surprising goals and outmaneuvering your opponent. Experiment with different kicks, jumps, and flops to find your unique style and enjoy the unpredictable nature of every match.
