Rainbow Obby Parkour

How to Play Rainbow Obby Parkour

Welcome to Rainbow Obby Parkour! Your goal is to navigate through a series of vibrant and challenging obstacle courses to reach the finish line. This game tests your precision, timing, and agility across several unique game modes.

Controls on PC:

  • Use the WASD keys or Arrow Keys to move your character forward, backward, left, and right.
  • Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and avoid obstacles.
  • Use your mouse to look around and aim your jumps.

Controls on Mobile:

  • Use the on-screen joystick to control your movement.
  • Tap the on-screen jump button to leap over obstacles.

Each level presents a new set of challenges, from simple jumps to complex moving platforms. Watch your step, time your moves carefully, and have fun conquering every course the game throws at you!
