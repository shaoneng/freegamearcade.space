Rainbow Obby Parkour

How to Play Rainbow Obby Parkour

Your goal in Rainbow Obby Parkour is to navigate through a series of increasingly difficult and vibrant obstacle courses. Each level presents unique challenges that will test your timing, precision, and agility. The game is simple to pick up but offers a satisfying challenge for players of all skill levels.

On PC: Use the W, A, S, and D keys to move your character. Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps, dodge obstacles, and climb platforms. Control your camera view by moving the mouse to look around.

On Mobile: Use the on-screen joystick to control your character's movement. Tap the dedicated jump button on the screen to leap over obstacles. Swipe anywhere on the screen to adjust your camera and get the perfect angle on the action.

The game features several different modules, each with its own unique twist. Some levels might have you running on narrow beams, while others could have you rolling as a ball or even driving a vehicle. Pay close attention to the patterns of moving platforms and be patient. The key to victory is persistence, so keep trying until you conquer every colorful challenge!
