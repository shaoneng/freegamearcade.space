Rainbow Obby Parkour

How to Play Rainbow Obby Parkour

Welcome to the dazzling and challenging world of Rainbow Obby Parkour! Your goal is to navigate through a series of vibrant and tricky obstacle courses by running, jumping, and carefully timing your moves to reach the finish line. This game tests your platforming skills across various unique levels.

The controls are intuitive and easy to learn. On PC, use the WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character, and press the Spacebar to jump. On Mobile devices, simply use the on-screen joystick for movement and tap the dedicated jump button to leap over obstacles.

The game features four distinct and hilarious modules, each presenting a different kind of challenge. You might find yourself on your feet in one, rolling as a ball in another, or even driving a vehicle! Adapt to each new gameplay style to conquer every course the game throws at you. Pay attention to moving platforms, disappearing blocks, and other tricky traps along the way. Good luck, and have fun!
