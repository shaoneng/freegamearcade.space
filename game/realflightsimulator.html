Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Get ready for a realistic flight experience! In Real Flight Simulator, you take command of powerful aircraft. Choose your plane and prepare for takeoff by following the correct procedures.

Takeoff Sequence: First, start the engines with the 'C' key. Lower the flaps and slats using 'X' to increase lift. Throttle up the engines by pressing and holding 'R' or 'V'. Once ready, release the parking brakes with 'B'. When your speed reaches over 150 knots, press the Down Arrow to rotate and lift off. Safely retract the landing gear with 'M' once you're above 100ft.

Flight Controls: Use 'W' and 'S' to control the pitch (nose up/down), 'A' and 'D' to roll, and 'Q' and 'E' to yaw (turn the nose left/right). The 'R' and 'F' keys control your engine throttle.

Combat: If your aircraft is armed, press '1' to change weapons, '2' to lock a target, and 'Space' to fire. You can press 'H' to hide the control information display for a more immersive view.
