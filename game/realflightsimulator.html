How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome, pilot! Getting started in Real Flight Simulator involves a realistic start-up and takeoff procedure. Choose your aircraft, such as the A-10, F-35, or KC-10, and follow these steps to get airborne safely.

Takeoff Procedure:

  1. Start Engines: Press the 'C' key and wait for the startup sequence to complete.
  2. Lower Flaps: Press the 'X' key to lower flaps for better lift.
  3. Increase Throttle: Press and hold 'R' or 'V' to power up your engines.
  4. Release Brakes: Press the 'B' key to release the parking brakes.
  5. Rotate: Once your speed reaches approximately 150 knots, press the 'Down Arrow' key to lift the nose.
  6. Raise Landing Gear: After you are airborne (above 100ft), press 'M' to retract the landing gear.

Basic Flight & Combat Controls:

  • Pitch (Nose Up/Down): W / S keys
  • Roll (Bank Left/Right): A / D keys
  • Yaw (Rudder Left/Right): Q / E keys
  • Engine Throttle Control: R / F keys
  • Change Weapon: 1 key
  • Lock Target: 2 key
  • Fire Weapon: Spacebar
  • Return to Menu: Escape or F2 key
