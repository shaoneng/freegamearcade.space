Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome, pilot! Real Flight Simulator puts you in command of powerful aircraft with realistic controls. Your journey begins on the runway, where you must follow a proper takeoff sequence before you can rule the skies.

To take off: First, lower your flaps with the X key and start the engines with the C key. Wait for the engine startup to complete. Then, press and hold R or V to throttle up, and release the parking brakes with the B key. When your speed exceeds 150 knots, press the Down Arrow to rotate and lift off the ground. Once you're above 100ft, raise the landing gear by pressing the M key.

In-Flight Controls: Use W and S to control the pitch (nose up/down), A and D to roll the aircraft, and Q and E for yaw (turning the nose left/right on a flat plane). Adjust your speed with the R and F keys for throttle control.

Combat and Other Functions: Press 1 to cycle through your weapons and 2 to lock onto a target. Fire your weapons with the Spacebar. You can press H to hide the controls overlay, and press Escape or F2 to access the main menu.
