Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome to the cockpit! In Real Flight Simulator, you can pilot a selection of advanced aircraft, including the A-10, A-29, F-35, and KC-10. Your mission is to master the flight controls, from engine startup to soaring through the skies.

Take-Off Procedure

To get your aircraft airborne, follow this sequence carefully:

  1. Start Engines: Press C to begin the engine startup sequence.
  2. Lower Flaps: Press X to lower the flaps and slats for better lift.
  3. Increase Throttle: Press and hold R or V to power up the engines.
  4. Release Brakes: Once at full power, press B to release the parking brakes.
  5. Rotate: When your speed is above 150 knots, press the Down Arrow to lift the nose off the runway.
  6. Retract Gear: Above 100ft, press M to raise the landing gear.

In-Flight Controls

Once you are flying, use these keys to navigate:

  • Roll (Bank Left/Right): A / D
  • Pitch (Nose Up/Down): W / S
  • Yaw (Turn Left/Right): Q / E
  • Engine Throttle: R (Increase) / F (Decrease)

Combat & System Controls

  • Change Weapon: 1
  • Lock Target: 2
  • Fire Weapon: Spacebar
  • Hide Controls Info: H
  • Return to Menu: Escape or F2
