Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Get ready to become a pilot! To start your flight, you need to follow a proper takeoff sequence. First, lower the flaps and slats by pressing 'X'. Then, start the engines with 'C' and wait for them to fully power up. Increase the engine throttle by holding 'R' or 'V' to build speed. Release the parking brakes with 'B'. Once your speed is above 150 knots, press the Down Arrow to rotate the aircraft and lift off the runway. When you are safely above 100ft, raise the landing gear by pressing 'M'.

Once you are airborne, control your aircraft with the following keys. Use 'W' and 'S' to control the pitch (nose up/down) and 'A' and 'D' to roll the plane left or right. For fine-tuning your direction, use 'Q' and 'E' to yaw. Control your engine power with 'R' and 'F'. For combat-ready aircraft, you can press '1' to change weapons, '2' to lock a target, and 'Space' to fire. Press 'H' to hide the control info display, and use 'Escape' or 'F2' to return to the main menu.
