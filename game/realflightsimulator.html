Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome, pilot! Your mission is to master the skies. Begin by selecting your aircraft from a fleet that includes the A-10, A-29, F-35, KC-10, and M-262. Follow the authentic pre-flight and takeoff procedures to get airborne.

Takeoff Sequence: Start your engines by pressing 'C' and wait for them to spool up. Lower your flaps with 'X', then increase the throttle by holding 'R' or 'V'. Once you're ready, release the parking brakes with 'B'. As you accelerate down the runway, wait until you reach at least 150 knots, then use the Down Arrow to rotate and lift off. Once you're airborne, raise your landing gear with 'M'.

Flight Controls: Use 'W' and 'S' to control your pitch (up/down) and 'A' and 'D' to roll (left/right). 'Q' and 'E' control your yaw. Manage your speed with 'R' and 'F' for throttle control. If you're in a combat plane, use '1' to change weapons, '2' to lock a target, and 'Space' to fire. Press 'H' to toggle the on-screen control information.
