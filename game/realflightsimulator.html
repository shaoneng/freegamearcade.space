Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome to the cockpit! Real Flight Simulator offers a detailed and immersive flying experience where you can pilot various aircraft, including the A-10, F-35, and KC-10. To get airborne, you'll need to follow a realistic startup and takeoff procedure.

Takeoff Procedure:

First, get your aircraft ready on the runway. Lower your flaps and slats with the X key. Then, start the engines by pressing C and wait for the startup sequence to complete. Once the engines are running, press and hold R or V to increase the throttle. When you're ready to move, release the parking brakes with the B key. As you accelerate, keep an eye on your airspeed. Once you reach 150 knots, gently pull back by pressing the Down Arrow to lift off. After you're safely in the air (above 100ft), retract the landing gear by pressing M.

Basic Flight Controls:

Once you are flying, use W/S to control the pitch (nose up/down), A/D to roll (bank left/right), and Q/E to yaw (turn the nose horizontally). You can adjust your engine power at any time using the R/F keys.

Combat & Menu:

For combat-equipped aircraft, press 1 to cycle through weapons and 2 to lock onto a target. Press the Spacebar to fire. If you need to access the main menu, press Escape or F2. You can also hide the on-screen controls overlay by pressing H for a more immersive view.
