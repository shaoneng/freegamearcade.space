Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Real Flight Simulator offers a realistic and immersive flight experience, putting you in the cockpit of various advanced aircraft. Your mission is to master the controls, from startup to soaring through the skies. Choose your plane and prepare for takeoff!

You can pilot a selection of powerful aircraft, including:

  • A-10
  • A-29
  • F-35
  • KC-10
  • M-262

Takeoff Procedure:

Follow these steps carefully to get your aircraft airborne:

  1. Turn on aircraft lights (Press Z): This is optional and may cause lag on some systems.
  2. Lower flaps/slats (Press X): If available on your aircraft.
  3. Start engines (Press C): Wait for the startup sequence to complete.
  4. Throttle up (Press and hold R or V): Increase engine power to build speed.
  5. Release parking brakes (Press B).
  6. Rotate (Press Down Arrow): Pull up once your speed is above 150 knots.
  7. Raise landing gear (Press M): Do this once you are above 100ft.

In-Flight Controls:

  • W/S: Pitch Up / Down
  • A/D: Roll Left / Right
  • Q/E: Yaw Left / Right
  • R/F: Increase / Decrease Engine Throttle
  • 1: Change Weapon
  • 2: Lock Target
  • Space: Fire Weapon
  • H: Hide Controls Info
  • Escape or F2: Main Menu
