Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome, pilot! In Real Flight Simulator, your mission is to master the art of flying sophisticated aircraft. Choose from planes like the A-10, F-35, and more. To get started, follow the realistic take-off procedure.

Take-off Sequence:
First, start the engines by pressing C. Lower your flaps with X for better lift. Next, increase the throttle by holding R or V, then release the parking brakes with B. Once you reach a speed above 150 knots, press the Down Arrow to rotate and lift off. Finally, once you're airborne (above 100ft), raise the landing gear by pressing M.

Flight Controls:
Use W/S to control the pitch (nose up/down) and A/D to roll the aircraft. Use Q/E to yaw (turn left/right). Manage your engine power with R/F. For combat, use 1 to switch weapons, 2 to lock a target, and Space to fire. Press Escape or F2 to return to the main menu.
