Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome to Real Flight Simulator, an incredibly realistic game where you get to experience the thrill of piloting various aircraft! Your goal is to become an expert pilot, navigating through the skies with precision and skill. You can choose from a range of aircraft including the A-10, A-29, F-35, KC-10, and M-262.

To begin your flight, follow these essential steps: First, press C to start up the engines and wait for the sequence to complete. Next, increase engine power by pressing and holding R or V. Don't forget to release the parking brakes by pressing B. Once your aircraft reaches a takeoff speed of over 150 knots, press the Down arrow to rotate and lift off. Finally, raise your landing gear by pressing M once you are above 100 feet. (Optionally, press Z for lights or X for flaps/slats).

While in the air, here are the main controls for maneuvering your plane: Use A and D to roll, W and S to pitch, and Q and E to yaw. Adjust your engine throttle (power) with R and F. For additional actions, you can press 1 to change weapons, 2 to lock a target, and Space to fire. If you need to access the main menu, press Escape or F2, and press H to hide the controls information. Prepare for an immersive flight experience!
