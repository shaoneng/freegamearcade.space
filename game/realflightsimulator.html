Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Real Flight Simulator offers a realistic and immersive flight experience, putting you in the cockpit of advanced military aircraft. Learn the ropes, master the controls, and explore the vast skies. You can test your piloting skills with the following planes:

  • A-10
  • A-29
  • F-35
  • KC-10
  • M-262

Takeoff Procedure:

  1. Turn on aircraft lights (Z key): Optional, but adds to the realism.
  2. Lower flaps/slats (X key): Prepare for takeoff.
  3. Start engines (C key): Wait for the startup sequence to complete.
  4. Throttle up (Hold R or V): Increase engine power.
  5. Release parking brakes (B key).
  6. Rotate (Down Arrow): Pull up once you reach a takeoff speed of over 150 knots.
  7. Raise landing gear (M key): Retract the gear once you are above 100ft.

General Controls:

  • A / D: Roll left/right
  • W / S: Pitch up/down
  • Q / E: Yaw left/right
  • R / F: Engine Throttle (Increase/Decrease Power)
  • 1: Change weapon
  • 2: Lock target
  • Spacebar: Fire weapon
  • H: Hide/Show controls info panel
  • Escape / F2: Return to the main menu

Follow the procedures carefully to become an expert pilot and dominate the skies!
