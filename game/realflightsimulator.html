Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Get ready for an authentic flight experience! To get your plane off the ground, follow the detailed takeoff sequence. Begin by starting the engines with the C key and wait for the startup sequence to complete. Next, lower your flaps by pressing X. Increase your engine power by pressing and holding R or V. When you're ready, release the parking brakes with the B key.

As you accelerate down the runway, keep an eye on your speed. Once you reach 150 knots, press the Down Arrow key to rotate and lift off the ground. After you're airborne (above 100ft), raise the landing gear by pressing M. Now you are free to explore the skies!

In-Flight Controls:

  • W/S Keys: Pitch (Nose Up/Down)
  • A/D Keys: Roll (Bank Left/Right)
  • Q/E Keys: Yaw (Turn Left/Right)
  • R/F Keys: Increase/Decrease Engine Throttle
  • Z Key: Toggle Lights (optional, may cause lag)
  • 1 Key: Change Weapon
  • 2 Key: Lock Target
  • Spacebar: Fire Weapon
  • H Key: Hide/Show Controls Info
  • Escape or F2: Return to Main Menu
