How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome to the cockpit! In Real Flight Simulator, your goal is to master the skies. Choose from advanced aircraft like the A-10, F-35, and KC-10 and follow the realistic procedures to get airborne.

Takeoff Procedure:

  • Lower Flaps: Press the X key to lower your flaps and slats.
  • Start Engines: Press C to begin the engine startup sequence.
  • Throttle Up: Press and hold R or V to increase engine power.
  • Release Brakes: Press B to release the parking brakes.
  • Rotate: Once you reach a speed above 150 knots, press the Down Arrow key to lift off.
  • Raise Landing Gear: Press M to retract the landing gear once you are safely in the air (above 100ft).

Flight Controls:

  • Pitch (Up/Down): Use the W/S keys.
  • Roll (Left/Right): Use the A/D keys.
  • Yaw (Rudder): Use the Q/E keys.
  • Engine Throttle: Adjust with R/F keys.
  • Weapons: Press 1 to change weapons, 2 to lock a target, and Space to fire.
  • Menu/UI: Press H to hide the controls info, and Escape or F2 to return to the main menu.
