Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Real Flight Simulator offers the ultimate flight experience, allowing you to become an expert pilot exploring the skies. Choose your favorite aircraft and prepare for takeoff!

Available aircraft to test:

  • A-10
  • A-29
  • F-35
  • KC-10
  • M-262

Takeoff Instructions:

  1. Turn on the aircraft lights (Press Z) - this is optional and may cause lag.
  2. Lower flaps and slats if available (Press X).
  3. Start the engines and wait for the startup sequence to complete (Press C).
  4. Throttle up the engines (Press and hold R or V to increase power).
  5. Release parking brakes (Press B).
  6. Once takeoff speed is reached (above 150 knots), rotate the aircraft (Press Down Arrow).
  7. Raise the landing gear once you are above 100ft (Press M).
  8. Continue with normal flight!

General Controls:

A / D - Roll W / S - Pitch Q / E - Yaw R / F - Engine Throttle 1 - Change Weapon 2 - Lock Target Space - Fire Escape / F2 - Main Menu H - Hide Controls Info
