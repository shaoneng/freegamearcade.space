Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome to the cockpit! Real Flight Simulator offers a realistic and engaging flight experience. Your mission is to master the controls of various aircraft and successfully take off, fly, and explore the open skies. Choose from a selection of powerful planes, including the A-10, A-29, F-35, and KC-10, and get ready for your first flight.

Takeoff Procedure: To get airborne, follow the pre-flight and takeoff sequence carefully. First, start the engines by pressing C and wait for the startup sequence to complete. Lower the flaps with X for better lift. When you're ready, increase engine power by pressing and holding R or V. Release the parking brakes with B. Once your speed exceeds 150 knots, gently pull back on the stick by pressing the Down Arrow to rotate and lift off the runway. Once you are safely in the air (above 100ft), retract the landing gear by pressing M.

Flight Controls: Once airborne, use the following controls for normal flight. Use W/S to control the plane's pitch (up/down) and A/D to control the roll (bank left/right). Use Q/E to control the yaw (turn left/right). The R/F keys adjust your engine throttle. If your aircraft is armed, press 1 to change weapons, 2 to lock a target, and Spacebar to fire. Press H to hide the controls info for a clearer view.
