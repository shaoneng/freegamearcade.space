Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Real Flight Simulator is a realistic game that gives you the ultimate flight experience. Choose your aircraft, master the controls, and become an expert in controlling planes, reaching new heights and exploring the skies.

Available Aircraft:
  • A-10
  • A-29
  • F-35
  • KC-10
  • M-262
Takeoff Procedure:
  1. Turn on aircraft lights (Press Z). This is optional and may cause lag.
  2. Lower flaps and slats if available (Press X).
  3. Start the engines and wait for the startup sequence to complete (Press C).
  4. Throttle up the engines (Press and hold R or V).
  5. Release parking brakes (Press B).
  6. Rotate once takeoff speed is reached (above 150 knots) (Press Down Arrow).
  7. Raise landing gear above 100ft (Press M).
Flight Controls:

A / D: Roll
W / S: Pitch
Q / E: Yaw
R / F: Engine Throttle (Power)
1: Change Weapon
2: Lock Target
Space: Fire
Escape / F2: Main Menu
H: Hide Controls Info
