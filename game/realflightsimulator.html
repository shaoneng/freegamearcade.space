How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome to the cockpit! In Real Flight Simulator, your mission is to master the art of flying sophisticated aircraft. Choose your preferred plane, such as the A-10, A-29, F-35, or KC-10, and prepare for a realistic flight experience.

Takeoff Procedure: To get airborne, follow this pre-flight and takeoff sequence carefully. First, lower your flaps with the X key. Next, start the engines by pressing C and wait for them to spool up. Increase engine power by pressing and holding R or V. When you're ready, release the parking brakes with B. Once your speed exceeds 150 knots, gently pull back on the controls (Down Arrow) to rotate and lift off. Finally, raise your landing gear by pressing M once you're above 100ft.

Flight Controls: Once you're in the air, use the following keys for standard flight maneuvers: W/S to control pitch (nose up/down), A/D to roll (bank left/right), and Q/E to control yaw (turn left/right on the spot). Adjust your engine throttle with the R/F keys.

Combat & Menu: This simulator also includes combat features. Press 1 to switch between weapons, 2 to lock onto a target, and Spacebar to fire. If you need a break or want to change settings, press Escape or F2 to access the main menu. Press H to toggle the controls display on and off.
