Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome to the cockpit! In Real Flight Simulator, your goal is to become an expert pilot. Begin by choosing your aircraft from a selection including the A-10, A-29, F-35, and KC-10. Each plane offers a unique flying experience as you explore the open skies.

Takeoff Procedure: To get airborne, follow this realistic start-up sequence. First, lower your flaps by pressing the 'X' key. Next, start the engines with the 'C' key and wait for them to fully engage. Increase engine power by pressing and holding 'R' or 'V'. When you're ready, release the parking brakes with 'B'. Once your speed exceeds 150 knots, press the 'Down Arrow' to rotate and lift off the runway. Finally, raise your landing gear by pressing 'M' once you are above 100ft.

Flight Controls: Use 'W' and 'S' to control the pitch (up/down) and 'A' and 'D' to roll your aircraft. For yaw (turning the nose left/right), use 'Q' and 'E'. Manage your engine throttle with 'R' (increase power) and 'F' (decrease power). If your aircraft is armed, press '1' to switch weapons, '2' to lock onto a target, and 'Spacebar' to fire. You can press 'H' to hide the on-screen controls info.
