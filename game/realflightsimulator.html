Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome, pilot! Real Flight Simulator offers a detailed and immersive flight experience where you can fly various aircraft like the A-10, F-35, and KC-10. To get airborne, you'll need to follow a realistic take-off procedure. Once you're in the sky, you'll have full control over your aircraft's movement and systems.

Take-off Procedure:
To begin your flight, first lower the flaps with the 'X' key. Start your engines by pressing 'C' and wait for them to spool up. Increase engine power by holding 'R' or 'V'. When ready, release the parking brakes with 'B'. As you accelerate down the runway, watch your speed. Once you reach 150 knots, press the Down Arrow key to rotate and lift off. Finally, once you are safely above 100 feet, raise your landing gear by pressing 'M'.

Flight Controls:
In the air, use 'W' and 'S' to control your pitch (nose up/down) and 'A' and 'D' to roll your aircraft left or right. Use 'Q' and 'E' for yaw, which turns the nose of your plane left or right without rolling. The 'R' and 'F' keys control your engine throttle.

Combat & Other Controls:
Some aircraft are equipped for combat. Press '1' to cycle through your available weapons and '2' to lock onto a target. Press 'Space' to fire. If you need a break or want to change settings, press 'Escape' or 'F2' to access the main menu. You can also press 'H' to hide the on-screen controls info for a more cinematic view.
