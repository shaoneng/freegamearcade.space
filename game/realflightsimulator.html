Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome, Captain! Your journey begins on the tarmac. To prepare for takeoff, follow the authentic startup sequence. First, engage the aircraft's lights by pressing 'Z' (this is optional). Next, lower the flaps with 'X' to increase lift. Start the engines by pressing 'C' and wait for the sequence to complete. Once the engines are running, release the parking brakes with 'B'.

With your plane ready, it's time to fly! Press and hold 'R' or 'V' to increase the engine throttle and build speed. Once you reach a takeoff speed of around 150 knots, gently pull back (Down Arrow) to rotate and lift off. When you're safely airborne (above 100ft), retract the landing gear by pressing 'M'. You are now free to explore the skies!

Mastering your aircraft is key. Use 'W' and 'S' to control the pitch (up/down) and 'A' and 'D' to roll (left/right). For finer rudder control, use 'Q' and 'E' to yaw. If the situation calls for it, switch weapons with '1', lock a target with '2', and fire with the 'Space' bar. Press 'H' to hide the on-screen controls info, and 'Escape' or 'F2' to access the main menu.
