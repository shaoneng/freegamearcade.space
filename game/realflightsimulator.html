How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome to the cockpit! In Real Flight Simulator, your goal is to master the controls of various aircraft and experience the thrill of flight. Choose your plane, such as the A-10, F-35, or KC-10, and prepare for takeoff by following the pre-flight checklist.

To get airborne, follow these steps in order: First, lower your flaps with the 'X' key. Start the engines by pressing 'C' and wait for them to spool up. Increase engine power by holding 'R' or 'V', then release the parking brakes with 'B'. Once your speed exceeds 150 knots, press the Down Arrow to rotate and lift off the runway. Finally, raise your landing gear with 'M' once you're above 100 feet.

During flight, use 'W' and 'S' to control the pitch (up/down), 'A' and 'D' to roll (bank left/right), and 'Q' and 'E' for yaw (turn left/right on the spot). 'R' and 'F' control your engine throttle. For combat aircraft, use '1' to change weapons, '2' to lock a target, and 'Space' to fire. Press 'H' to hide the controls overlay for a more immersive view.
