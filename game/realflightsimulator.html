Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Welcome to the cockpit! Real Flight Simulator provides an immersive and detailed flight experience. Before you can rule the skies, you need to master the takeoff procedure. Follow these steps carefully to get your plane airborne.

Takeoff Checklist:

  • Engine Start: Press 'C' to begin the engine startup sequence. Wait for it to complete.
  • Flaps: Press 'X' to lower the flaps and slats for takeoff.
  • Throttle Up: Press and hold 'R' or 'V' to increase engine power.
  • Brakes: Press 'B' to release the parking brakes.
  • Rotate: When your speed exceeds 150 knots, press the 'Down Arrow' to lift the nose off the runway.
  • Gear Up: Once you are safely in the air (above 100ft), press 'M' to raise the landing gear.

In-Flight Controls:

  • Roll (Bank Left/Right): A / D Keys
  • Pitch (Nose Up/Down): W / S Keys
  • Yaw (Turn Left/Right): Q / E Keys
  • Engine Power: R / F Keys
  • Combat: '1' to change weapon, '2' to lock a target, 'Space' to fire.
  • Toggle Lights: Z Key (optional, may impact performance)
  • Menu: Press 'Escape' or 'F2' to access the main menu.
