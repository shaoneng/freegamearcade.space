Real Flight Simulator

How to Play Real Flight Simulator

Real Flight Simulator is a realistic game that offers the ultimate flight experience. Take control of the plane, reach new heights, and become an expert at exploring the skies. You can test your skills with a variety of aircraft, including the A-10, A-29, F-35, KC-10, and M-262.

Take-Off Procedure:

Follow these steps to get airborne:
1. Turn on aircraft lights (Z) - this is optional and may cause lag.
2. Lower flaps and slats if available (X).
3. Start up the engines and wait for the sequence to complete (C).
4. Throttle up the engines by pressing and holding R or V.
5. Release parking brakes (B).
6. Rotate once takeoff speed is above 150 knots (Down Arrow).
7. Raise the landing gear when you are above 100ft (M).

General Flight Controls:

A / D: Roll
W / S: Pitch
Q / E: Yaw
R / F: Engine Throttle (Power)
1: Change Weapon
2: Lock Target
Space: Fire
H: Hide Controls Info
Escape / F2: Main Menu
