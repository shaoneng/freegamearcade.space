Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

Inspired by the famous scene from the TV series Squid Game, your objective is to reach the finish line at the other end of the field without being eliminated.

Listen for the giant doll's commands. When she says "Green Light," you are safe to move towards the finish line. Use your mouse or finger to drag your character forward.

When the doll shouts "Red Light" and turns to face you, you must stop moving immediately. If you are caught moving, even slightly, you will be eliminated from the game.

Time your movements carefully and freeze in place to survive each round. Reach the finish line before the timer runs out to win!
