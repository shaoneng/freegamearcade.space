Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

Welcome to the deadly playground! In Red Light Green Light, you take on the role of a contestant trying to survive the iconic children's game with a terrifying twist. The objective is simple: cross the finish line at the other end of the field before the timer runs out. But be warned, one wrong move is your last.

The rules are based on timing and precision. When the giant doll has her back to you and calls out "Green Light," you are free to run towards the finish line. To move your character, simply click and hold your mouse button. The longer you hold, the farther you'll run.

However, the moment the doll starts to turn and calls "Red Light," you must immediately stop. Release the mouse button to freeze in place. If the doll catches you moving, even slightly, you will be eliminated from the game. Stay sharp, manage your time, and reach the goal to survive and win!
