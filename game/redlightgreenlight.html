Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

Step into the tense world of the globally famous TV series, *Squid Game*, with this iconic challenge. In Red Light Green Light, you'll don the tracksuit of a contestant and face the ultimate test of timing and nerve.

The rules are simple but deadly. Your goal is to run from the starting line to the finish line across the field. You can only move when the giant, creepy doll at the far end has its back turned and signals "Green light!"

The moment the doll turns to face you and signals "Red light!", you must freeze instantly. Any detected movement during the "Red light" phase results in your immediate elimination. Only the players who successfully reach the finish line will survive.

This fan-made online version captures the thrill of the show with a clean design and responsive controls. Test your reflexes, manage your risk, and see if you have what it takes to win. Hit play and survive the deadly playground!
