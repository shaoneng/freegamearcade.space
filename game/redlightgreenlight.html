Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

Step into the high-stakes world inspired by the famous TV series, Squid Game. In this nerve-wracking game of timing and precision, you'll find yourself on a deadly playground where the only goal is to survive.

The rules are simple but unforgiving. You must run from the starting line to the finish line. A giant, ominous doll dictates your every move. When the doll has its back turned and the signal is "Green light," you are free to run towards the finish line. However, the moment the doll turns to face you and the signal changes to "Red light," you must freeze instantly.

Any movement, no matter how small, detected during the "Red light" phase will result in your elimination. Your survival depends entirely on your ability to react quickly and control your movement. Do you have the nerve to cross the line and win?
