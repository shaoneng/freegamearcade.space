How to Play Red Light Green Light

Welcome to the deadly playground of Red Light Green Light, a game of survival where every step counts. Your goal is simple: run from the starting line to the finish line without being eliminated.

The rules are based on the classic children's game with a terrifying twist. When the giant doll has its back turned and says "Green Light," you must run towards the finish line. Use your mouse or keyboard to control your character's movement.

However, when the doll shouts "Red Light" and quickly turns around, you must stop moving immediately. Freeze in place! If the doll catches you moving even a tiny bit during the "Red Light" phase, you will be eliminated from the game.

Listen carefully to the audio cues and watch the doll's movements. You have a limited amount of time to reach the finish line, so balance your speed with caution. Good luck, contestant!
