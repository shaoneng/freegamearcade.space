Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

Inspired by the iconic challenge from the hit TV series, your objective in Red Light Green Light is simple yet terrifying: cross the field and reach the finish line before the timer runs out.

Listen for the giant doll's chant. When it turns its back and sings "Green Light," it's your chance to run. Click and hold your mouse button to move your character forward as quickly as possible.

Pay close attention! When the doll suddenly stops singing and shouts "Red Light," it will turn to face the players. You must immediately release the mouse button and stop moving. If the doll catches you in motion during the "Red Light" phase, you will be eliminated from the game. Timing is everything!

Survive each round of Red Light, Green Light, manage your time, and make it across the line to win. Be patient, be swift, and most importantly, don't get caught!
