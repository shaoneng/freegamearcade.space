Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

The goal is simple but deadly: run from the starting line to the finish line without being caught. The game is a test of your nerve, timing, and reflexes, inspired by the thrilling scene from the famous TV series.

When the giant doll has its back to the players and the light is green, it's your chance to move. Click and hold your mouse button (or tap and hold on a touch screen) to run towards the finish line. The faster you are, the better your chance of survival.

Pay close attention! When the doll suddenly shouts "Red light!" and begins to turn around, you must stop immediately. Release the mouse button or lift your finger from the screen to freeze in place. Any movement, no matter how small, will be detected.

If the doll catches you moving during a "Red light" phase, you are eliminated from the game. Time your runs perfectly, freeze instantly, and make it across the line to survive!
