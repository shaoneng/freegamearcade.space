How to Play Red Light Green Light

Inspired by the iconic challenge from the hit series Squid Game, your objective in Red Light Green Light is to cross the field and reach the finish line before time runs out. But it's not a simple raceâ€”you must follow the commands of the giant doll.

When the doll has its back turned and shouts "Green Light," you are safe to run towards the finish line. Use your mouse to move your character forward. Click and hold to run.

Listen carefully! When the doll suddenly turns around and shouts "Red Light," you must stop all movement immediately by releasing the mouse button. Freeze in place and don't even flinch. If the doll detects any motion while it's watching, you will be eliminated from the game.

The key to survival is timing and patience. Only move when it's safe, and be prepared to stop in an instant. Can you make it to the other side and win?
