Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

Step into the thrilling and intense world of the famous TV series Squid Game. In this fan-made version, you are a contestant in the deadly children's game, Red Light Green Light. The objective is to reach the finish line at the far end of the field.

Pay close attention to the giant, creepy doll. When she has her back turned and says "Green Light," it's your signal to run. Move your character forward as quickly as possible. However, the moment she starts to say "Red Light," she will begin to turn around. You must stop all movement immediately and freeze in place.

If the doll spots any movement during the "Red Light" phase, your character will be eliminated. Only those who can successfully blend speed with perfect timing will survive and cross the finish line. Test your reflexes and nerve in this high-stakes game!
