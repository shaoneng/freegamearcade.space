Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

The objective of Red Light Green Light is to run from the starting line to the finish line at the other end of the field without being caught.

When the giant doll has its back turned and the light is green, you can run towards the finish line. Use your mouse to click and hold (or tap and hold on mobile) to move your character forward.

Listen and watch carefully! When the light turns red and the doll starts to turn around, you must immediately stop moving. Release the mouse button to freeze in place.

If the doll catches you moving during a 'Red Light' phase, you will be eliminated from the game. Time your movements perfectly to cross the finish line before the timer runs out to win!
