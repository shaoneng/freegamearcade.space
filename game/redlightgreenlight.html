Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

Welcome to the nerve-wracking world of Red Light Green Light, inspired by the unforgettable challenge from the hit series, Squid Game. Your objective is simple but deadly: run from the starting line to the finish line at the other end of the field.

Pay close attention to the giant doll. When its back is turned and it calls out "Green light!", you are safe to run towards the finish line. Click and hold (or tap and hold on mobile) to move your character forward.

The moment the doll starts to turn and calls "Red light!", you must immediately stop moving. Release your mouse button or finger to freeze in place. If the doll catches you moving even slightly during a "Red light" phase, you will be eliminated from the game. It's a test of timing, reflexes, and your ability to handle pressure. Good luck!
