Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

Red Light Green Light is a game of precision and timing. Your goal is to cross the finish line without being caught moving.

When the giant doll turns its back and says "Green Light!", you are free to move forward. This is your chance to sprint towards the finish line.

However, as soon as the doll shouts "Red Light!" and turns around, you must freeze instantly. Any movement detected during "Red Light" will result in elimination from the game.

Pay close attention to the doll's commands and movement. Your survival depends on your ability to react swiftly and accurately. Good luck!
