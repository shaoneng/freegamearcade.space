Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

Step into the high-stakes playground inspired by the hit series Squid Game. The objective is to run from the starting line to the finish line at the far end of the field before the timer runs out.

When the giant doll has its back turned and says "Green Light," you are free to run. Use your mouse or finger to guide your character towards the finish line as quickly as possible.

Pay close attention! When the doll suddenly turns to face you and says "Red Light," you must stop all movement immediately. Freeze in place and don't move a muscle. If the doll detects any movement while it's watching, you will be eliminated from the game.

You must reach the finish line within the time limit to survive and win. Balance speed and caution to conquer this deadly challenge!
