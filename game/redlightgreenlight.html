Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

Step into the shoes of a contestant in the high-stakes game of Red Light Green Light. The objective is to cross the field and reach the finish line before the timer runs out.

Listen carefully for the doll's commands. When the doll has its back turned and says "Green light!", you are safe to move. Click and hold (or press and hold the designated key) to run towards the finish line.

The moment the doll starts to turn and says "Red light!", you must stop moving immediately. Release your mouse button or key to freeze in place. If the doll catches you moving, even slightly, you will be eliminated from the game.

You must reach the finish line within the given time limit to win. Balance speed with caution, time your movements perfectly, and survive the deadly game!
