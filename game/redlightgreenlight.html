Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

Welcome to the high-stakes playground of Red Light Green Light! Your objective is simple but challenging: make it from the starting line to the finish line without being eliminated. You are competing against the clock and other players, but your biggest threat is the giant doll watching over the field.

To move your character, simply click and hold your mouse button, or press and hold the W key or the Up Arrow key. You must only move when the doll's back is turned and the signal is "Green Light."

When the doll shouts "Red Light!" and swivels its head to face the players, you must freeze instantly. Release the mouse button or key to stop all movement. Any motion, however small, detected during the "Red Light" phase will result in your immediate elimination from the game.

Success requires perfect timing, sharp reflexes, and nerves of steel. Watch the doll, listen for the cues, and run for your life to survive and claim victory!
