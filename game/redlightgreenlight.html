Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

Step into the high-stakes world of the famous TV series with Red Light Green Light. The rules are deceptively simple: make a run for the finish line while the giant doll has its back turned and says "Green light!". But be warned, the moment it says "Red light!" and turns to face you, you must freeze in place instantly.

In this thrilling online game, you control a contestant trying to survive this deadly childhood game. Your goal is to cross the field and reach the finish line before the timer runs out. Any movement detected during the "Red light" phase will result in immediate elimination.

The controls are intuitive. Click and hold your mouse button to run forward during the "Green light" phase. Release the button to stop a-b-s-o-l-u-t-e-l-y still when the doll turns around for the "Red light" phase. Timing and reflexes are everything. One false move could be your last.

This fan-made experience captures the tension of the original series with clean graphics and responsive controls. Do you have the nerve to survive and win? Hit play and find out!
