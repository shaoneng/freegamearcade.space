Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

Welcome to the intense world of Red Light Green Light, inspired by the iconic scene from the hit series Squid Game. The objective is simple but challenging: you must run from the starting line to the finish line at the other end of the field before the timer runs out.

Pay close attention to the giant doll at the finish line. When she turns her back and shouts "Green Light," it's your signal to move. Click and hold your mouse button (or press and hold the screen on mobile) to make your character run towards the finish line.

The real test of your nerve comes when the doll suddenly shouts "Red Light" and turns to face the players. You must immediately release the mouse button to freeze in place. Any movement, no matter how small, will be detected by the doll.

If you are caught moving during a "Red Light" phase, you will be instantly eliminated from the game. Stay sharp, time your movements perfectly, and make a mad dash for survival to win!
