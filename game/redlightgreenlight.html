Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

The goal in Red Light Green Light is simple yet challenging: cross the finish line at the far end of the field before the timer runs out. You are one of many contestants trying to survive this deadly game.

When the giant doll at the finish line has its back turned and the signal is "Green Light," it's your chance to run. Move your character forward by clicking and holding the mouse button or by holding your finger down on your mobile screen.

Pay close attention! When the doll suddenly turns to face you and the signal becomes "Red Light," you must stop moving immediately. Release the mouse button or lift your finger to freeze in place. If the doll detects any movement during a "Red Light" phase, you will be eliminated from the game.

Success is all about timing and reflexes. Be bold during the green light, but be ready to stop in an instant. Good luck, contestant!
