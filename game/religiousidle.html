Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Your journey to spiritual leadership begins with a single click! Start by hitting the "Recruit Followers" button to gather your first believers. The core of the game is about growing your fledgling faith into a worldwide movement.

Once you reach your first follower milestone, you'll get to customize your religion. Give it a memorable name, design a sacred symbol, and write the holy words that will guide your followers. These personal touches don't just add flavor—they also boost morale and increase donations.

Your followers will automatically contribute funds (tithe), so keep an eye on your growing income. Spend this money wisely on building new structures like shrines, temples, and eventually, a grand Divine Monument. Each new building expands your reach and attracts more followers, creating a self-sustaining cycle of growth.

As your movement expands, you'll have unclaimed zealots ready for duty. Assign them to specialized roles to further enhance your progress. Don't forget to check the news feed—a small envelope icon—to see quirky headlines, reports of miracles, and letters from both fans and critics. This feature adds a fun narrative layer, showing you the real-world impact of your growing religion.
