Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Your divine journey begins with a single click! Start by hitting the "Recruit Followers" button to attract your first believers. The more you click, the faster your congregation will grow, laying the foundation for your new faith.

Once you reach your first milestone, it's time to define your faith. Give your religion a catchy name, design a unique sacred symbol, and write the holy words that will guide your followers. These personal touches will boost morale and encourage more donations from your growing flock.

Keep an eye on the green money counter. Your followers will automatically tithe, providing the funds needed for expansion. Spend this cash wisely on new buildings like temples, shrines, and mega-temples. Each new structure increases your influence and attracts more followers automatically, turning your clicker into a self-sustaining empire.

As your religion spreads, check the news feed (the envelope icon) for updates. You'll see quirky headlines, reports of miracles, and even letters from fans and critics. This feed shows the cultural impact of your growing faith, from your symbol appearing on toast to followers reporting personal blessings. Manage your reputation and keep the faithful happy to ensure your movement's continued success!
