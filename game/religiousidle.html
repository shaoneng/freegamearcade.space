Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Forge a fresh faith and watch it bloom! In Religious Idle, you start a brand-new religion from a single believer and grow it into a globe-spanning movement. It's a satisfying idle loop with a playful twist on spirituality.

To begin, hit Recruit Followers to convince your first believers. Once you cross the first milestone, you can give your movement a catchy name, choose a sacred symbol, and pen some holy words. These personal touches boost morale and, more importantly, donations!

Followers tithe automatically, so keep an eye on the green money counter. Use this cash to expand your influence by constructing buildings like temples, shrines, mega-temples, and even a towering Divine Monument. Every upgrade attracts more curious souls, turning your clicker into a self-sustaining empire of belief.

As your following grows, you'll need to assign roles. Put your unclaimed zealots to work to further scale your reach. Open the little envelope icon in the corner to see a stream of quirky headlines, miracle reports, and letters from fans and foes. These short blurbs, from "The Sign of Maneizm Appears on a Piece of Toast" to gushing follower mail, hint at your creed’s growing cultural footprint and keep things entertaining.

Religious Idle is crafted by Kodiqi, an indie studio known for its clean UI, steady progression, and clever humor. If you love watching numbers rise while tweaking your strategy, this game is for you.
