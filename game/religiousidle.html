Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Begin your spiritual journey by clicking the 'Recruit Followers' button to attract your first believers. Once you reach your initial follower milestone, you can customize your new faith: give it a memorable name, choose a sacred symbol, and write your own holy words to inspire the masses.

Your followers will automatically contribute donations, which you can see in the money counter. Use these funds wisely to expand your influence by building structures like temples, shrines, and eventually, a towering Divine Monument. Each new building increases your reach and attracts more followers, turning your manual clicking into a self-sustaining empire.

As your religion grows, you can assign your dedicated followers to specific roles, further enhancing your faith's power and income. Keep an eye on the mail icon in the corner for world news, miracle reports, and letters from both devoted fans and skeptical critics. These messages add flavor to the game and can even impact your recruitment, so manage your public image to keep the faith strong!
