Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Your journey begins with a single believer. Click the 'Recruit Followers' button to gather your first followers and kickstart your new faith.

Once you reach your first milestone, it's time to define your creed. Give your religion a unique name, select a sacred symbol, and write the holy words that will guide your followers. These personal touches will boost morale and increase donations.

Your followers will automatically contribute funds. Use this money to expand your influence by constructing buildings. Start with simple temples and shrines, then progress to mega-temples and even a Divine Monument. Each new structure will attract more followers and increase your income.

As your following grows, you can assign believers to special roles. Keep an eye on the news feed (the envelope icon) to see how your religion is impacting the world, from miracle reports to quirky headlines. Manage both positive and negative press to keep your movement growing strong!
