Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Step 1: Found Your Faith. Begin with a single believer and click the "Recruit Followers" button to start growing your congregation. This initial step is crucial for establishing your spiritual movement.

Step 2: Define Your Creed. Once you reach your first milestone, it's time to personalize your faith! Name your religion, select a unique sacred symbol, and compose your holy words. These foundational actions will significantly boost follower morale and increase incoming donations, setting the stage for expansion.

Step 3: Build Your Empire. Watch as donations (visible in the green money counter) automatically roll in from your tithes. Spend your accumulated funds strategically on various buildings like Temples, Shrines, Mega-Temples, and eventually a magnificent Divine Monument. Each building upgrade expands your reach and attracts more curious souls, turning your clicker into a self-sustaining empire of belief.

Step 4: Appoint Roles. As your religious buildings grow in size and prestige, new roles become available. Assign your loyal zealots as Missionaries, Bishops, or Prophets to further accelerate your religion's expansion and influence across the globe. Each role offers unique benefits to your growth.

Step 5: Monitor the World. Keep an eye on the in-game mail envelope for quirky headlines, miracle reports, and letters from both fans and foes. These blurbs reflect your faith's growing cultural footprint and can impact recruitment, so manage your public perception wisely to keep the faithful happy and the donations flowing. Enjoy watching your spiritual empire bloom!
