Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Your journey begins with a single believer. Your primary goal is to grow your following. Click the "Recruit Followers" button to convince new people to join your cause. The more you click, the faster your initial group will expand.

Once you reach your first milestone, you can customize your new faith. Give your religion a catchy name, design a sacred symbol, and write the holy words that will guide your followers. These personal touches not only define your movement but also boost morale and increase donations.

Your followers will automatically contribute money (tithes) to the cause. Keep an eye on your funds and spend them wisely on new buildings. Start with small shrines and work your way up to massive temples and even a Divine Monument. Each new structure increases your income and attracts more people to your religion.

Stay engaged with the world's reaction to your faith by checking the mail icon. Here you'll find a stream of news headlines, reports of miracles, and letters from both devoted fans and skeptical critics. These events add a fun narrative layer, showing the cultural impact of your growing religion.
