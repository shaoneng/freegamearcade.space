Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Welcome to Religious Idle, where you get to create a brand-new religion and guide it from a humble beginning to a global movement. Your goal is to attract followers, manage resources, and expand your influence, all with the satisfying loop of an idle clicker game.

Getting Started: The First Believers

Begin your journey by clicking the "Recruit Followers" button. Once you've gathered your first few disciples, you'll need to establish the core tenets of your faith:

  • Name Your Faith: Give your new religion a catchy name.
  • Choose a Symbol: Select a sacred symbol that represents your beliefs.
  • Write Holy Words: Pen some sacred text to inspire your followers.

These initial steps are crucial, as they boost morale and increase the donations that fund your expansion.

Growing Your Empire of Belief

Your followers will automatically contribute tithes, which you can see in the green money counter. Use this income strategically to build structures that attract more people:

  • Temples & Shrines: Start with smaller buildings to grow your local presence.
  • Mega-Temples: Invest in larger structures to scale your reach significantly.
  • Divine Monument: The ultimate project to cement your religion's legacy.

As your following grows, you can assign specialized roles to your most dedicated zealots, further optimizing your growth.

Stay Updated with the Holy Newsfeed

Keep an eye on the little envelope icon! This is your newsfeed, filled with miracle reports, quirky headlines, and letters from both fans and critics. These blurbs provide a fun narrative, hinting at your religion's cultural impact and sometimes presenting challenges you'll need to manage to keep recruitment steady.
