Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Forge a fresh faith and watch it bloom! Religious Idle is a free online idle game that lets you start a brand-new religion from a single believer and grow it into a globe-spanning movement. Click to recruit followers, build holy sites, and manage your growing influence as donations roll in.

Your journey begins with a single click. Hit "Recruit Followers" to convince your first believers. Once you reach an early milestone, you can customize your movement by giving it a catchy name, choosing a sacred symbol, and penning the holy words. These personal touches will boost morale and increase donations.

Followers will automatically tithe, so keep an eye on your funds. Use this income to expand your reach by constructing buildings like temples, shrines, mega-temples, and even a towering Divine Monument. Each upgrade attracts more curious souls, turning your clicker into a self-sustaining empire of belief.

As your following grows, you'll need to delegate. Assign unclaimed zealots to specialized roles to boost your progress. With bigger buildings come dedicated roles, so you can assign followers as preachers, missionaries, or guardians to accelerate your growth.

Stay connected with your flock by checking the little envelope in the corner. Here you'll find a stream of quirky headlines, miracle reports, and letters from both fans and foes. Read about signs appearing on toast, children looking forward to your holy day, or even a balding believer whose hair miraculously grew back! These stories add flavor and hint at your creed's growing cultural footprint. Be prepared for a few grumpy letters too—managing your public image is part of the challenge.

Religious Idle is crafted by Kodiqi, an indie studio known for its clean UI and clever humor in clicker games. If you enjoy watching numbers snowball, you might also like Logistics Inc, another title that lets you scale an empire from the ground up.
