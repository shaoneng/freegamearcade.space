Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Embark on a divine quest in Religious Idle, a captivating clicker game where you cultivate a new faith from the ground up. Your mission is to grow a single believer into a global spiritual movement. No divine intervention required—just strategic clicking and savvy management!

Begin by clicking the "Recruit Followers" button to attract your first disciples. Once you reach your initial milestone, you'll get to define your new faith: give it a memorable name, design a sacred symbol, and write your holy scriptures. These personal touches will boost morale and encourage more donations from your growing flock.

Followers will automatically contribute tithes, so keep an eye on your funds. Use this wealth to construct a variety of buildings that expand your influence. Start with humble temples and shrines, then progress to impressive mega-temples, and ultimately, the awe-inspiring Divine Monument. Each new structure attracts more followers, accelerating your growth.

As your religion expands, you can assign dedicated followers to specialized roles. Don't forget to check the news feed (the envelope icon) for quirky headlines, miracle reports, and letters from both fans and foes. These events shape your public image and can impact recruitment, adding a fun layer of strategy to the idle gameplay.
