Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Your journey to global influence begins with a single click! Start by hitting the "Recruit Followers" button to gather your first believers. As your initial group grows, you'll unlock the ability to customize your new faith.

Give your religion a unique name, choose a sacred symbol that represents your beliefs, and write down the holy words that will guide your followers. These personal touches aren't just for show—they boost morale and increase the donations that fund your expansion.

Your followers will automatically tithe, so watch your money grow and invest it in new buildings. Construct temples, shrines, and eventually towering monuments to attract more souls to your cause. Each upgrade expands your reach and automates your growth. Check the in-game news and mail to see the world's reaction to your burgeoning faith!
