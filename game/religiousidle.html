Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Begin your spiritual journey by clicking 'Recruit Followers' to gather your first believers. Once you reach your initial milestone, you'll get to customize your new faith. Give it a catchy name, design a sacred symbol, and write your holy words. These personal touches are key to boosting morale and increasing donations from your followers.

Your followers will automatically contribute funds, so keep an eye on your growing treasury. Spend this money wisely on constructing buildings like temples, shrines, and eventually, a towering Divine Monument. Each new structure expands your influence and attracts more curious souls to your cause, turning your simple clicker game into a self-sustaining empire of belief.

As your following grows, you'll need to assign roles to your most zealous members. Keep an eye on the news feed, which shows quirky headlines, reports of miracles, and letters from both fans and critics. This feedback gives you a sense of your religion's cultural impact and helps you keep your followers happy and engaged.
