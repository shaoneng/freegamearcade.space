Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Your journey to spiritual leadership begins with a simple click. Start by hitting the 'Recruit Followers' button to gather your first believers. As your initial group grows, you'll unlock the ability to establish and customize your new faith.

Give your religion a memorable name, choose a sacred symbol to represent its core beliefs, and pen the holy words that will guide your followers. These personal touches are crucial, as they boost morale and significantly increase the donations you receive from the faithful.

Your followers will automatically contribute funds over time. Use this income to build an infrastructure of belief, starting with humble temples and shrines and eventually constructing a magnificent Divine Monument. Each new building expands your influence and attracts more curious souls, creating a self-sustaining cycle of growth.

Stay engaged by checking the news feed in the corner. It provides a running commentary on your religion's impact, featuring miracle reports, quirky headlines, and letters from both devoted fans and concerned critics. This adds a fun, narrative layer to your quest for global spiritual dominance.
