Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Your journey begins with a single click! Start by hitting the "Recruit Followers" button to gather your first believers. As your small group grows, you'll reach milestones that allow you to customize your new faith. Give your religion a catchy name, design a sacred symbol, and write your own holy words to inspire your followers and increase donations.

Your devoted followers will automatically contribute funds, shown by the green money counter. Strategically spend this income on new buildings like temples, shrines, and eventually, a grand Divine Monument. Each new structure expands your influence and attracts even more followers, turning your manual clicks into an automated empire of belief.

As your religion expands, you can assign followers to specialized roles to further enhance your growth. Don't forget to check the news feed, represented by a small envelope icon. Here you'll find everything from reports of miracles and gushing fan mail to grumpy complaints about your enthusiastic flock. These events provide a humorous look at your growing cultural impact and can affect your recruitment efforts, so keep your followers happy!
