Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Your divine journey begins with a single action. Start by clicking the 'Recruit Followers' button to gather your first believers. The core of the game is to grow your following and manage the resources they provide to expand your influence.

Once you reach your first major milestone, you can truly define your new faith. Give your religion a unique name, select a sacred symbol to represent your beliefs, and even write your own holy words. These personal touches help boost morale and encourage more donations from your followers.

Your devoted followers will automatically contribute funds through tithes, which you can track with the money counter. Spend this wealth wisely to construct buildings that increase your reach and attract more people. Start with humble temples and shrines, and work your way up to building mega-temples and a towering Divine Monument. Each upgrade accelerates your growth and income.

Stay informed by checking the small envelope icon in the corner. This news feed provides updates on your religion's growing influence through quirky headlines, reports of miracles, and letters from both devoted fans and concerned critics. This feature adds a fun, narrative layer to your idle progression, showing the real-world impact of your faith.
