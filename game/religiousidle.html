Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Your journey begins with a single believer. Click the 'Recruit Followers' button to expand your initial group. Once you reach your first milestone, you can customize your new faith by giving it a name, choosing a sacred symbol, and even writing its holy words. These personal touches will boost morale and increase donations.

Your followers will automatically contribute funds, which you can see in the green money counter. Use this wealth to construct buildings like temples, shrines, and eventually, a massive Divine Monument. Each new building increases your influence and attracts more followers, accelerating your growth.

As your religion grows, you'll need to assign roles to your most dedicated followers. You can make them Priests to manage temples, Inquisitors to maintain order, or Missionaries to spread the word. Keep an eye on the news feed (the envelope icon) to see how the world is reacting to your movement, from miracle reports to grumpy letters about loud chanting!
