Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Start your spiritual empire from scratch in Religious Idle! Your mission is to grow a tiny group of believers into a worldwide faith. It all begins with a simple click.

Click the "Recruit Followers" button to gather your first disciples. Once you reach your initial milestone, you'll get to customize your new faith: give it a memorable name, select a sacred symbol, and even write your own holy text. These personal touches will inspire your followers and boost donations.

Your followers will automatically contribute funds. Keep an eye on your money counter and spend it wisely on buildings to expand your influence. Start with humble shrines and temples, and eventually work your way up to magnificent mega-temples and a grand Divine Monument. Each new structure attracts more followers, accelerating your growth.

As your religion grows, you can assign specialized roles to your followers, turning them into dedicated zealots who further your cause. Don't forget to check your messages! You'll receive a stream of news headlines, reports of miracles, and letters from both devoted fans and skeptical critics. These events add flavor and can impact your recruitment, so keep your believers happy and watch your influence spread across the world!
