Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Your journey begins with a single click. Start by pressing the "Recruit Followers" button to gather your first believers. Once you reach a milestone, you'll unlock the ability to customize your new faith. Give it a unique name, design a sacred symbol, and write your own holy words to inspire your flock and boost their morale and donations.

Your followers will automatically contribute funds to your cause. Keep an eye on the money counter and invest your earnings wisely. Construct buildings like temples, shrines, and eventually, a towering Divine Monument to expand your reach. Each new structure attracts more followers, turning your small cult into a self-sustaining empire of belief.

As your religion grows, you'll need to manage your followers by assigning them dedicated roles. Stay informed by checking the mail icon in the corner. This news feed provides quirky headlines, miracle reports, and letters from both fans and foes. Positive press can boost recruitment, while negative feedback might slow it down, so manage your public image to achieve global influence!
