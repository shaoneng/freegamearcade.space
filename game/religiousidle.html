Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Welcome to Religious Idle, where you are the founder of a new faith! Your goal is to grow your religion from a single believer into a worldwide phenomenon through strategic clicking and upgrading.

Start Your Movement: Begin by clicking the "Recruit Followers" button to attract your first believers. Once you reach an early milestone, you'll be prompted to customize your faith. Choose a unique name, a sacred symbol, and write your own holy words to inspire your followers and boost donations.

Grow and Expand: Your followers will automatically generate income through tithes and donations. Keep an eye on your money counter and use your funds to build structures that increase your influence. Start with humble shrines and temples, then expand to mega-temples and even a colossal Divine Monument to attract more souls to your cause.

Assign Roles & Stay Informed: As your following grows, you can assign dedicated zealots to specific roles, helping to automate your progress. Don't forget to check the news feed (the envelope icon) for quirky headlines, miracle reports, and letters from both fans and critics. This feature shows the growing cultural footprint of your religion and adds a fun narrative twist to your divine journey!
