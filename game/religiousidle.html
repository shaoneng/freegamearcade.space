Religious Idle

How to Play Religious Idle

Your journey begins with a single click! Start by hitting the "Recruit Followers" button to gather your first believers. As your small group grows, you'll reach milestones that allow you to customize your new faith. Give your religion a unique name, design a sacred symbol, and write the holy words that will guide your followers. These personal touches are key to boosting morale and increasing donations.

Keep an eye on the green money counter, as your devoted followers will automatically contribute funds. Use this income to expand your influence by constructing various buildings. Start with humble Temples and Shrines, and as your wealth grows, invest in impressive Mega-Temples and eventually a towering Divine Monument. Each new structure attracts more curious souls, accelerating your growth and turning your clicker game into a self-sustaining empire of belief.

As your movement expands, you can assign specific roles to your most dedicated followers to optimize progress. Don't forget to check the small envelope icon in the corner! This is your news feed, filled with quirky headlines, reports of miracles, and letters from both devoted fans and skeptical critics. Reading about your faith appearing on a piece of toast or hearing complaints about loud chanting adds a humorous and engaging layer to your quest for global spiritual dominance.
