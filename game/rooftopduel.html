Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

The objective in Rooftop Duel is simple but chaotic: knock your opponent off the rooftop to win the round! The challenge comes from the hilarious ragdoll physics and the powerful recoil of your weapons.

Once a round begins, your character will be perched precariously on a rooftop. To play, tap your action button to jump. To fire your weapon, press and hold the button to aim, and then release it to shoot. Be warned: every shot has a strong recoil that will blast you in the opposite direction. You can use this to your advantage to dodge incoming fire or save yourself from falling, but it can also send you flying into the abyss!

You can play solo against an AI opponent or challenge a friend in the local 2-player mode. If you stay on the roof while your opponent falls, you win the round and collect coins. Use these coins in the shop to unlock even sillier and more powerful weapons for your next showdown.
