Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

Welcome to the chaotic heights of Rooftop Duel! The objective is simple: be the last ragdoll standing. You must use your weapon to knock your opponent off the rooftop before they do the same to you.

The controls are designed for hilarious, physics-based action. Tap your jump button to leap and dodge. To fire, press and hold your shoot button to aim your weapon, and then release it to shoot. Be careful! Every shot has a powerful recoil that sends you flying backwards. You can use this recoil to make daring saves from the edge of the roof or to quickly reposition, but an ill-timed shot can send you plummeting into the abyss.

Rooftop Duel features modes for both solo and multiplayer fun. Challenge an AI opponent to hone your skills, or grab a friend and battle it out in the local 2-player mode. Win rounds to earn coins, which you can use to unlock bigger and more ridiculous weapons for even more mayhem in the next showdown!
