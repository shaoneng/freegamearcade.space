Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

Welcome to the chaotic heights of Rooftop Duel! This is a hilarious physics-based shooter where the goal is simple: be the last one standing. You must knock your opponent off the rooftop to score a point. The first player to win five rounds is crowned the champion.

The controls are straightforward, but mastering the wild recoil is the key to victory. Each shot you fire sends your character flying in the opposite direction! Use this recoil to your advantage to perform evasive maneuvers or even save yourself from falling off the edge.

  • Player 1: Use 'W' to jump and 'E' to shoot.
  • Player 2: Use 'I' to jump and 'O' to shoot.

To play, simply tap your jump key to get airborne, then press and hold your shoot key to aim your weapon. Release the key to fire. Time your jumps and shots carefully to outwit your opponent in this goofy, ragdoll-filled showdown!
