Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

Welcome to the chaotic world of Rooftop Duel! The objective is simple: be the last one standing on the roof. You'll need to use your weapon to knock your opponent off the edge before they do the same to you.

The controls are straightforward but mastering them is key. Press your jump key to leap into the air. While airborne, press and hold the shoot button to aim your weapon. Release the button to fire! Be warned: every shot has a powerful recoil that will push you in the opposite direction, so use it wisely to dodge or reposition.

Mastering the game's goofy ragdoll physics is essential for victory. Use the floaty jumps to evade enemy fire and the recoil from your own shots to save yourself from falling. Each round you win earns you points. You can play solo against an AI opponent or challenge a friend in the 2-player mode for some hilarious local multiplayer action.
