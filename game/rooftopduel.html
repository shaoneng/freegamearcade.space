Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

Welcome to the wacky world of Rooftop Duel! The goal is simple: be the last one standing on the roof. Each round, you and your opponent will face off in a high-stakes shootout where physics and recoil are your biggest friends and foes.

The controls are straightforward but take skill to master. Use your jump key to get airborne, which also allows you to aim your weapon. Hold the jump/aim key to aim, and release it to fire. Be warned: every shot has a powerful recoil that can send you flying across the screen! You can use this to your advantage to dodge incoming fire or make a last-ditch save from falling off the edge.

Winning a round means knocking your opponent off the rooftop. Each victory earns you coins that you can spend in the shop to unlock even more powerful and sillier weapons for your next showdown. The game features modes for both solo play against an AI and local 2-player battles with a friend. Jump in, embrace the chaos, and see how long you can survive the duel!
