Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

Welcome to the hilarious, physics-based chaos of Rooftop Duel! The objective is simple: be the last one standing by knocking your opponent off the rooftop. Master the wacky controls to outplay your rival in this goofy shooter.

Once the round begins, your ragdoll character will be on a narrow rooftop. To win, you must successfully hit your opponent to send them tumbling into the abyss below. But be careful! Every shot you take has a powerful recoil that will push you in the opposite direction. You can use this to your advantage to dodge attacks or save yourself from a fall, but an ill-timed shot can also send you flying off the edge!

The controls are straightforward:

  • Jump: Tap your jump key to leap into the air. The jumps are floaty, giving you time to aim or dodge.
  • Aim and Shoot: Press and hold your shoot key to aim your weapon. Release the key to fire!

Winning a round earns you coins, which you can use in the shop to unlock even sillier and more powerful weapons for the next showdown. You can challenge the computer in solo mode or grab a friend for an epic two-player duel on the same screen. Good luck, and try not to laugh too hard to aim!
