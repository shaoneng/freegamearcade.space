Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

Welcome to the chaotic rooftops where physics is your greatest ally and enemy! In Rooftop Duel, your objective is to knock your opponent off the building to win the round. The controls are simple but mastering them is key to victory.

Controls: Tap your jump key to leap into the air. While airborne, press and hold the shoot key to aim your weapon. Release the key to fire! Be warned: every shot has a powerful recoil that can send you flying, so use it strategically to either save yourself from a fall or gain a better position.

Gameplay: Use the game's floaty physics and unpredictable recoil to outmaneuver your opponent. A well-timed shot can send them tumbling into the abyss, securing you a round win. Winning rounds earns you coins, which you can use in the shop to unlock new, crazier weapons for the next showdown. You can challenge the AI in single-player mode or grab a friend for hilarious 2-player duels on the same keyboard.
