Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

The goal in Rooftop Duel is simple but chaotic: be the last one standing! You and your opponent are placed on a narrow rooftop, and the first to knock the other off five times wins the match. Embrace the wild ragdoll physics and use your overpowered weapons to gain the upper hand.

The controls are easy to learn but hard to master. Tap your action key to jump, which is essential for dodging and repositioning. To fire your weapon, press and hold the action key to aim, then release it to shoot. Timing is everything, as a well-aimed shot can send your rival flying.

Be warned: every shot comes with a powerful recoil! This isn't just a side effect; it's a core mechanic. A powerful blast can push you away from the edge, saving you from a fall. However, a mistimed shot can just as easily send you hurtling into the void. Use the recoil strategically to outmaneuver your opponent.

You can challenge the computer in single-player mode or grab a friend and battle it out in the hilarious two-player mode on the same screen. Let the rooftop madness begin!
