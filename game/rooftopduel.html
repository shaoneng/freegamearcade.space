Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

Welcome to the wacky physics of Rooftop Duel! The objective is straightforward: be the last one standing by knocking your opponent off the city rooftop. Each round is a fast-paced, hilarious battle against both your foe and gravity itself.

The controls are simple to learn but tricky to master. Tap your jump key to get airborne. Once in the air, press and hold the shoot button to aim your weapon. Release the button to fire! Remember, you can only shoot while jumping.

Every shot you take comes with powerful recoil, sending your character flying in the opposite direction. Use this to your advantage! A well-timed shot can save you from falling off the edge or help you dodge an incoming bullet. But be careful, a mistimed shot can just as easily send you sailing into the abyss!

Win a round by successfully knocking your opponent off the roof. Winning earns you coins that you can use in the shop to unlock even more powerful and sillier weapons for the next showdown. You can challenge the computer in solo mode or grab a friend for an epic 2-player duel.
