Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

The goal in Rooftop Duel is simple but chaotic: be the last one standing! You and your opponent are placed on a narrow rooftop, and your mission is to knock the other player off into the abyss below. Each round is a fast-paced test of balance, aim, and timing. The first player to win five rounds is crowned the rooftop champion.

The controls are easy to learn but hard to master. Tap the jump button to leap into the air. While airborne, hold the fire button to aim your weapon. When you're ready, release the button to shoot. Be warned: every shot has a powerful recoil that will push you in the opposite direction! You can use this recoil to your advantage to dodge attacks or make a last-second save from falling off the edge.

Rooftop Duel offers both a 1-player mode against the computer and a 2-player mode to challenge a friend on the same device. As you win rounds, you'll earn coins that can be spent in the shop between matches. Use these coins to unlock new, more powerful, and often sillier weapons to gain an edge in your next showdown.
