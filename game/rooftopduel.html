Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

The goal in Rooftop Duel is simple but chaotic: be the last one standing on the rooftop! You win a round by knocking your opponent off the edge before they do the same to you.

Use your weapon to shoot at your opponent, but be warned—every shot has a powerful recoil that can send you flying backward. You can use this recoil to your advantage to dodge attacks or save yourself from falling, but it can also be your downfall!

The controls are straightforward. To play, simply tap to jump and hold to aim your weapon. Release the button to fire. The floaty, ragdoll physics make every jump and shot an unpredictable and hilarious adventure.

Challenge a friend in 2-player mode or take on the computer in a solo duel. Winning rounds earns you coins, which you can use in the shop to unlock even more powerful and silly weapons for your next showdown.
