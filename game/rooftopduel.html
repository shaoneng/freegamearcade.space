Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

The objective in Rooftop Duel is simple but chaotic: be the last one standing! Your goal is to knock your opponent off the narrow city rooftop before they do the same to you. Use your weapons, your wits, and the wacky ragdoll physics to your advantage to win each round.

The controls are straightforward. Tap the jump button to leap into the air. To fire your weapon, press and hold the shoot button to aim your trajectory, then release it to shoot. Be warned: every shot has a powerful recoil that will send you flying in the opposite direction! You can use this to your advantage to save yourself from a fall or to launch a surprise attack.

Rooftop Duel can be enjoyed solo against an AI opponent or with a friend in a local two-player showdown. As you win rounds, you'll earn coins that can be spent in the shop to unlock new, more powerful, and sillier weapons for future duels. Master the floaty jumps and unpredictable recoil to become the king of the roof!
