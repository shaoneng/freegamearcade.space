Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

In Rooftop Duel, your goal is simple: knock your opponent off the narrow city rooftop. Both ragdoll fighters start balanced at the beginning of each round.

Controls: Tap to jump, hold to aim, and then release to shoot. Be mindful of the powerful recoil â€“ it can either save you from the edge or send you flying into the abyss! Floaty jumps can help you dodge incoming shots, but mistiming them will result in a fall.

Winning a Round: If you remain on the roof while your opponent falls, you win the round. Collect coins from your victories to unlock even sillier and more powerful weapons from the in-game shop before the next showdown.

Rooftop Duel features three main game modes, catering to both solo players and those looking to challenge a friend.
