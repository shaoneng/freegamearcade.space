Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

Welcome to the wacky, high-altitude chaos of Rooftop Duel! The objective is simple: be the last ragdoll standing. You must use your wits, your aim, and the game's zany physics to knock your opponent off the rooftop before they send you plummeting to the street below.

The controls are easy to learn but difficult to master. Tap the jump button to send your character flying. While you're in the air, hold the button to aim your weapon, and then release it to fire a shot. But be careful—every shot packs a powerful recoil that will send you careening in the opposite direction. You can use this recoil strategically to dodge attacks or save yourself from a fall, but an ill-timed shot can also be your downfall!

Win rounds to collect coins, which you can spend in the shop to unlock even more powerful and sillier weapons. You can challenge the computer in solo mode or grab a friend for hilarious local two-player battles. Jump in and see how long you can survive the duel!
