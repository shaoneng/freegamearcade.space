Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

Welcome to the zany world of Rooftop Duel! The objective is straightforward: be the last player standing by knocking your opponent off the precarious rooftop. This game is all about mastering the wacky ragdoll physics and the powerful recoil from your weapons.

The controls are simple to learn but tricky to master. Once a round begins, both players will try to maintain their balance. To attack, you need to jump, aim, and shoot:

  • Jump: Tap your jump button to get airborne. The floaty jumps give you time to aim or dodge.
  • Aim: While in the air, press and hold the fire button to aim your weapon.
  • Shoot: Release the fire button to unleash your shot. Watch out for the recoil, as it will push you in the opposite direction! You can use this to your advantage for a last-second save or an aggressive push.

Each time your opponent falls off the rooftop, you win the round. Winning rounds earns you coins, which you can use in the shop to unlock new, even more powerful and silly weapons for the next showdown. You can play solo against an AI opponent or challenge a friend in the local 2-player mode. Good luck, and try not to laugh so hard you can't aim!
