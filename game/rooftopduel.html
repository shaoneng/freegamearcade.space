Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

Welcome to the chaotic world of Rooftop Duel! This game combines the goofy physics of classic ragdoll games with intense rooftop shoot-outs. The objective is simple: knock your opponent off the roof before they knock you off. There are no downloads or sign-ups; just pure, instant action.

The controls are straightforward but challenging to master due to the wild physics. Tap to jump, hold the button to aim your weapon, and release to shoot. Be warned: every shot has a powerful recoil that can send you flying across the screen. You can use this to your advantage to dodge bullets or make a desperate leap back to safety, but a mistimed shot will send you sailing into the abyss.

Rooftop Duel features three main game modes, perfect for playing solo or challenging a friend. If you manage to stay on the roof while your opponent falls, you win the round and collect coins. Use these coins in the shop to unlock even sillier and more powerful weapons for your next showdown. Grab a friend, hit play, and see who can master the recoil and reign supreme on the rooftop!
