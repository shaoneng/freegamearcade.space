Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

Welcome to the chaotic rooftops where balance and firepower collide! The goal in Rooftop Duel is simple: be the last one standing by knocking your opponent off the building. The catch? Every shot you take has a powerful recoil that can send you flying, and the game's goofy ragdoll physics makes every movement unpredictable.

The controls are straightforward. Tap your jump button to get airborne. To shoot, press and hold the button to aim your weapon, and then release it to fire. Be mindful of the recoil! A powerful blast can save you from falling off the edge or, if you're not careful, send you spiraling into the abyss yourself.

You can challenge the computer in solo mode or grab a friend for hilarious 2-player duels. Win rounds to earn coins, which you can use in the shop to unlock even more powerful and sillier weapons for your next showdown. Master the floaty jumps, unpredictable recoil, and have a blast!
