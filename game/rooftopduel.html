Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

Welcome to Rooftop Duel, a chaotic physics-based shooter where the goal is simple: knock your opponent off the roof! Get ready for hilarious ragdoll limbs, overpowered weapons, and some seriously wild recoil.

The controls are easy to learn but challenging to master. Tap the jump button to leap into the air. To fire, hold the button to aim your weapon, and then release it to shoot. Be careful! Every shot has a powerful recoil that will send you flying in the opposite direction. You can use this to your advantage to dodge bullets or save yourself from falling, but a mistimed shot can send you sailing into the abyss.

You can play solo against the computer or grab a friend for a two-player duel on the same device. If you manage to stay on the rooftop while your opponent falls, you win the round. Winning earns you coins, which you can use in the shop to unlock even crazier weapons for your next showdown. The game features three main modes, offering different ways to enjoy the floppy firefights.

Rooftop Duel is created by FreezeNova, a studio known for popular unblocked ragdoll games. If you're a fan of this style, try other chaotic shooters available on our site. All games are free and play directly in your browser with no downloads required—perfect for quick, action-packed fun!
