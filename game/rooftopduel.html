Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

The goal in Rooftop Duel is simple but chaotic: knock your opponent off the rooftop to win the round! You'll need to master the game's wacky physics to stay on top.

The controls are easy to learn but hard to master. Tap the jump key to leap into the air. While airborne or on the ground, press and hold the shoot button to aim your weapon. Release the button to fire. Be careful! Every shot has powerful recoil that can send you flying across the roof, which can be a strategic move or a disastrous mistake.

You can challenge the computer in single-player mode or grab a friend for hilarious local 2-player battles. Each round you win earns you coins, which you can use in the shop to unlock even more powerful and sillier weapons for your next showdown.
