Rooftop Duel

How to Play Rooftop Duel

Welcome to the wacky world of Rooftop Duel! The objective is simple: be the last one standing on the roof. Each round, you and your opponent will try to knock each other off the edge using an array of powerful and silly weapons, all while dealing with hilarious ragdoll physics.

The controls are easy to learn but tricky to master. First, tap your jump button to get airborne. The floaty jumps give you crucial time to aim or dodge. While in the air, press and hold the shoot button to aim your weapon, and then release it to fire. Choose your moment wisely!

Be careful! Every shot has a powerful recoil that will push you in the opposite direction. You can use this kickback to save yourself from falling or to quickly reposition, but an ill-timed shot can send you flying into the abyss. Win rounds to earn coins, which you can use in the shop to unlock even more ridiculous weapons for the next showdown. You can play solo against an AI or grab a friend for 2-player mode!
