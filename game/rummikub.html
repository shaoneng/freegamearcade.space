Rummikub

How to Play Rummikub

Welcome to the classic game of Rummikub! The goal is simple: be the first player to place all of your tiles from your rack onto the playing table. This version allows you to play against computer opponents, making it a perfect challenge for both new and experienced players.

To start, you'll place tiles in combinations called 'melds'. A meld can be either a 'group' (at least three tiles of the same number in different colors) or a 'run' (at least three tiles of the same color in a consecutive sequence, like 5-6-7). Your first meld must have a total value of at least 30 points.

Once you've made your initial meld, the real strategy begins! On your turn, you can add your tiles to existing melds on the table, or even rearrange the tiles on the table to create new opportunities for your own tiles. If you can't make a valid move, you must draw a new tile from the pouch. Plan your moves carefully, watch your opponents, and clear your rack to claim victory!
