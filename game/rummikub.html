Rummikub

How to Play Rummikub

Welcome to Rummikub, the classic tile-based game that's easy to learn but offers deep strategic fun! The goal is to be the first player to play all the tiles from your rack by forming sets on the table.

You can create two types of sets:

  • Groups: A set of three or four tiles with the same number, but each in a different color.
  • Runs: A set of three or more tiles with consecutive numbers, all of the same color (e.g., a red 5, 6, 7).

On your turn, you must play a valid set or draw a tile from the pool. After you've made your initial move, you can also add your tiles to existing sets already on the table, rearranging them to create new combinations. Plan your moves carefully, watch your opponents, and aim to clear your rack to claim victory!
