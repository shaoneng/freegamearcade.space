Rummikub

How to Play Rummikub

Welcome to Rummikub, the classic tile-based game of strategy and luck! The goal is simple: be the first player to get rid of all the tiles on your rack by creating clever combinations on the playing table.

You'll create two types of combinations, called 'melds'. A 'Group' is a set of three or four tiles of the same number in different colors. A 'Run' is a set of three or more consecutive numbers that are all the same color (e.g., a red 5, 6, and 7).

On your turn, you can either play tiles from your rack to form new melds or add to existing ones already on the table. If you're unable to make a valid move, you must draw a new tile from the pool. Plan your moves carefully to outsmart the computer opponent and empty your rack first!
