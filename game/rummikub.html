Rummikub

How to Play Rummikub

Welcome to Rummikub, the classic tile-based game that blends luck and strategy. The goal is simple: be the first player to play all the tiles from your rack. You can play against the computer or challenge friends in matches with 2, 3, or 4 players.

Tiles are numbered 1 through 13 in four different colors. To play your tiles, you must form 'melds' on the table. A meld can be a 'group' (three or four tiles of the same number but in different colors) or a 'run' (three or more consecutive numbers of the same color).

On your turn, you must either play one or more tiles from your rack or draw a new tile from the pool. To make your first play, the 'initial meld', you must lay down melds with a total value of at least 30 points. After that, you can add tiles to existing melds on the table or even rearrange them to create new melds with your own tiles. If you can't make a play, you must draw a tile and your turn ends.
