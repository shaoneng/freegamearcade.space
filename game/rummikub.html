Rummikub

How to Play Rummikub

Welcome to Rummikub, the classic tile-based game of strategy and luck! The goal is simple: be the first player to place all of your tiles from your rack onto the table.

You play tiles by forming them into 'sets'. A set can be a 'group' (three or four tiles of the same number but in different colors) or a 'run' (three or more consecutive numbers of the same color). Your first move, known as the 'initial meld', must consist of sets that total at least 30 points (the numbers on the tiles).

After your initial meld, you can add your tiles to existing sets on the table. You can also manipulate the sets on the table to create new opportunities for your own tiles. If you cannot make a valid move on your turn, you must draw a new tile from the pool. The first player to clear their rack wins the round. Plan your moves carefully and outsmart your opponents!
