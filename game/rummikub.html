Rummikub

How to Play Rummikub

Welcome to Rummikub, the classic tile-based game of strategy and luck! Your goal is to be the first player to get rid of all the tiles on your rack by playing them on the table in sets called 'melds'.

A 'meld' can be one of two types: a 'group' or a 'run'. A 'group' is a set of three or four tiles of the same number but in different colors. A 'run' is a set of three or more consecutive numbers, all in the same color (e.g., a red 4, 5, and 6).

To start, each player draws 14 tiles. Your first move, or 'initial meld', must consist of one or more melds from your rack that total at least 30 points. If you can't make this move, you must draw a new tile from the pool and end your turn. After your initial meld, you can add tiles to existing melds on the table or create new ones. If you cannot play any tile, you must draw one from the pool. The first player to clear their rack wins the game!
