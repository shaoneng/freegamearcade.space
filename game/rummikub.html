Rummikub

How to Play Rummikub

Welcome to Rummikub, the classic tile-matching game of strategy and luck! Your goal is simple: be the first player to play all the tiles from your rack. You can play against the computer or in multiplayer modes with 2, 3, or 4 players.

To start, you must make an "initial meld" of at least 30 points from your rack. A "meld" is a set of tiles placed on the table. There are two types of valid sets: a 'group' (three or four tiles of the same number but in different colors) and a 'run' (three or more consecutive numbers of the same color).

Once you've made your initial meld, you can add tiles to existing sets on the table during your turn. You can also manipulate the sets on the table by splitting them, adding to them, and rearranging them to use your own tiles. If you can't make a move, you must draw a new tile from the pool. Plan your moves carefully, watch your opponents, and clear your rack to win!
