Rummikub

How to Play Rummikub

Welcome to Rummikub, the classic tile-based game that's easy to learn but offers deep strategic gameplay! The main objective is simple: be the first player to get rid of all the tiles from your rack by forming them into valid sets on the table.

You can create two types of sets. The first is a 'group', which consists of three or four tiles of the same number but in different colors. The second is a 'run', which is made up of three or more consecutive numbers of the same color. The Joker tile is a wildcard and can represent any tile you need to complete a set!

On your turn, you can either play tiles from your rack to form new sets or add to existing sets already on the table. If you can't make a valid move, you must draw a new tile from the pool. Your first move, known as the 'initial meld', must have a total value of at least 30 points. After that, you're free to manipulate the tiles on the board to your advantage. You can choose to play against the computer or challenge 2 to 4 players online.

Think ahead, watch your opponents' moves, and find the perfect moment to play your tiles to win the game. Good luck!
