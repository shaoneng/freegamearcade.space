Rummikub

How to Play Rummikub

Welcome to Rummikub, the classic tile-based game of skill and strategy! The goal is simple: be the first player to place all the tiles from your rack onto the table. You can play against the computer or with 2, 3, or 4 players in exciting multiplayer matches.

To play, you must create combinations of tiles called 'melds'. There are two types of melds: 'groups' and 'runs'. A group consists of three or four tiles of the same number but in different colors. A run consists of three or more consecutive numbers of the same color.

On your turn, you can place your initial meld (which must total at least 30 points), add tiles to existing melds on the table, or manipulate the board to create new opportunities for your tiles. If you cannot make a move, you must draw a new tile from the pool. Plan your moves carefully, watch your opponents, and aim to clear your rack to claim victory!
