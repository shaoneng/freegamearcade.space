Rummikub

How to Play Rummikub

Welcome to Rummikub, the classic tile-matching game of skill and strategy! The goal is simple: be the first player to play all the tiles from your rack by forming valid sets on the table.

You start with a rack of numbered tiles in four different colors. To make a move, you must create "melds." A meld can be a "group" (three or four tiles of the same number but in different colors) or a "run" (three or more consecutive numbers all in the same color). Your very first move, called the "initial meld," must consist of one or more melds whose tiles add up to a total of at least 30 points.

After your initial meld is on the table, you can add tiles to existing melds in subsequent turnsâ€”even those played by your opponents! If you cannot make a valid move, you must draw a new tile from the pool. The first player to successfully empty their rack wins the game. You can choose to play against the computer or in multiplayer modes with 2, 3, or 4 players.
