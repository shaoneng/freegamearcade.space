Run

How to Play Run

The goal in Run is simple: guide your alien character through a series of cosmic tunnels without falling off into the void. Use your arrow keys to move left and right, and press the spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles.

The defining feature of Run is its unique gravity-shifting mechanic. Whenever you run onto a wall or the ceiling, gravity reorients itself, allowing you to stick to that surface. This is not just for showâ€”it's essential for survival!

You must use this gravity-flipping ability to navigate around increasingly large holes in the course. You can sprint sideways onto a wall to avoid a gap in the floor, or even jump mid-air to land on the ceiling. Chaining these flips together becomes crucial as the levels get faster and more complex.

Choose Adventure Mode to progress through levels sequentially, unlocking new, more challenging tunnels as you go. If you fall, you'll instantly respawn at the start of the current level, giving you another chance to master it. You can also select between normal and fast speeds from the menu for an even greater challenge.
