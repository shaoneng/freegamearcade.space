Run

How to Play Run

In Run, you take control of a small alien character sprinting through a series of tubular courses suspended in the vastness of space. The objective is simple: don't fall off! The path ahead is filled with missing tiles and tricky gaps that you must navigate to survive.

Use the Left and Right Arrow Keys to move your character side-to-side. Press the Spacebar to jump over smaller gaps. Time your jumps carefully, as the levels get progressively faster and the holes become wider.

The core mechanic of Run is its unique gravity system. Whenever you run onto a wall or the ceiling, the world rotates, and that surface becomes your new ground. This is essential for survival! Use this gravity-flipping ability to avoid large holes in the floor by simply running up a wall to find a safer path.

You can choose between Adventure Mode to progress through levels sequentially, or select a specific unlocked level from the main menu. For an added challenge, try the 'Fast' speed setting. If you fall, you'll instantly respawn at the beginning of the current level, so you can try again right away.
