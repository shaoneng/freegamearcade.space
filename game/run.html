Run

How to Play Run

Welcome to Run, the iconic endless runner that will test your reflexes and bend your mind! The goal is simple: guide your little alien character through a series of treacherous tunnels suspended in outer space. Use your arrow keys to move left and right to navigate the path.

The key to survival is mastering the unique gravity mechanic. When you run onto a wall or the ceiling, the entire world will rotate, making that surface your new floor. Use this ability to dodge the ever-increasing number of holes and gaps in your path. Press the space bar to jump over obstacles.

As you progress through the levels in Adventure Mode, the speed will increase and the courses will become more complex. If you fall off the path, you'll instantly restart at the beginning of the current level. Can you keep your footing and conquer all the tunnels?
