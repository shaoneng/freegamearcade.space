Run

How to Play Run

Your goal in Run is to guide a small alien safely through a series of 3D tunnels suspended in space. The path is full of holes, and falling off means you have to restart the current level. The game gets faster and the gaps get wider as you progress, demanding quick reflexes and precise timing.

The core mechanic is the ability to manipulate gravity. When you run onto a wall or the ceiling, the game's gravity will shift, allowing you to run on that surface. Use this unique ability to navigate around large gaps in the floor or to find a safer path through the tunnel.

Mastering the jump is crucial. A well-timed jump can clear a wide gap. You can also combine jumping with gravity flipsâ€”for instance, jumping towards a wall to land on it and continue running. The key is to think three-dimensionally and plan your route ahead.

In Adventure Mode, you'll tackle levels one by one, unlocking new, more complex tunnels as you succeed. You can also select the game speed before you start. Choose 'Normal' for a more casual experience or 'Fast' to truly test your limits.
