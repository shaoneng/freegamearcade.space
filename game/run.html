Run

How to Play Run

Your goal in Run is to guide your small alien character through a series of cosmic tunnels. The path is full of missing tiles, and your objective is to navigate these gaps by running and jumping to reach the end of each level.

Use the arrow keys to move left and right, and press the spacebar to jump. The defining feature of Run is its gravity-shifting mechanic. When you run onto a wall or the ceiling, the entire level's gravity will reorient, allowing you to run on that surface. This is essential for navigating complex sections and avoiding holes in the floor.

As you progress, the levels become faster and the gaps wider. You'll need to time your jumps carefully and chain wall-runs to survive. If you fall, you'll instantly restart the current level. Before you start, you can choose between 'Adventure Mode' to progress through levels sequentially or 'Infinite Mode' for an endless challenge. You can also select your speed to match your skill level.
