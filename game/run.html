Run

How to Play Run

Welcome to Run, the iconic endless runner with a gravity-bending twist! Your goal is simple: guide your alien character through a series of cosmic tunnels without falling off the edge. The levels become progressively faster and more challenging, demanding quick reflexes and precise timing.

Use the Left and Right Arrow Keys to move your character from side to side. Press the Spacebar to jump over gaps and obstacles in your path. Time your jumps carefully, as some gaps are wider than others and require an early leap to clear.

The signature feature of Run is its unique gravity mechanic. When you run onto a wall or the ceiling, the entire world will rotate, and that surface will become your new "floor." Master this ability to navigate complex pathways, avoid massive holes, and find safe routes. You can chain flips by jumping from a wall to the ceiling and back again to survive the trickiest sections.

Before you start, you can choose between Normal and Fast speed. If you're new to the game, we recommend starting with Normal to get a feel for the controls. If you fall, don't worry—you'll instantly restart at the beginning of the current level, ready for another attempt!
