Run

How to Play Run

Your goal in Run is to guide a small alien through a series of cosmic tunnels without falling off the edge. Use the arrow keys (Left and Right) to move side-to-side and the Spacebar or Up Arrow to jump over the gaps in the floor.

The most unique feature of Run is its gravity-defying physics. When you run onto a wall or the ceiling, the tunnel will rotate, and that surface becomes your new 'floor'. Use this mechanic strategically to find safe paths and avoid seemingly impossible gaps.

In Adventure Mode, you'll progress through increasingly difficult levels, each one faster and more complex than the last. If you fall, you simply restart the current level. You can also choose between normal and fast speeds from the main menu for an even greater challenge. Master the art of flipping gravity to survive!
