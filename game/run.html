Run

How to Play Run

Your primary objective in Run is to guide your alien character through a series of cosmic tunnels without falling into the void. Use the Left and Right arrow keys to move side-to-side along the path. To jump over gaps and missing tiles, press the Spacebar or the Up arrow key. Remember to time your jumps carefully, as the levels get progressively faster and more challenging.

The core mechanic of Run is its unique gravity-flipping feature. Whenever you run onto a wall or the ceiling, the game's gravity will reorient, making that surface your new "floor." This skill is crucial for survival, allowing you to bypass large holes or find safer paths. Master the art of chaining these flips together by jumping from walls to the ceiling and back again to navigate the trickiest sections.

In Adventure Mode, you'll progress through levels one by one, each unlocking after you complete the previous one. For an extra challenge, try Infinite Mode to see how long you can last.
