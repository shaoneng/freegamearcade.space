Run

How to Play Run

Your goal in Run is simple: navigate your alien character through a series of cosmic tunnels without falling off. Use the left and right arrow keys to move side-to-side and press the Spacebar to jump over the ever-increasing gaps in the floor.

The standout feature of Run is its gravity-defying physics. When you run onto a wall or the ceiling, the entire world reorients, making that surface your new "floor." Master this flipping mechanic to find safe paths and avoid seemingly impossible holes. You can even chain jumps from the floor to a wall and back again to clear wide obstacles.
