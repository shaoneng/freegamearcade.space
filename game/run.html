Run

How to Play Run

Your goal in Run is to guide a small alien safely through a series of cosmic tunnels. Use your arrow keys to move left and right, and press the spacebar (or up arrow) to jump over gaps in the path. The levels get faster and more challenging as you progress, so stay on your toes!

The unique feature of Run is its gravity-defying physics. When you run onto a wall or the ceiling, the entire world will rotate, and that surface will become your new "floor." This is the key to survival! Use this gravity-shifting ability to find new paths and avoid holes that would be impossible to jump over otherwise.

As the gaps become wider, you'll need to chain your jumps and wall-runs together. Don't be afraid to leap towards a wall mid-air to land on it and keep running. If you fall, you'll simply restart the current level, so you can try again immediately. Try out Adventure Mode to progress through levels sequentially, or test your skills on the fast speed setting for an even greater challenge.
