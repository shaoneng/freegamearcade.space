Run

How to Play Run

Your goal in Run is to guide a nimble alien through a series of treacherous tunnels floating in space. The controls are simple: use the left and right arrow keys to steer, and press the Space Bar to jump over the ever-increasing gaps in the path.

The signature mechanic of Run is gravity flipping. Whenever you run onto a side wall or the ceiling, gravity reorients, making that surface your new floor. You must master this technique to navigate around holes and find a safe route. Chaining flips together is essential for surviving the more complex and faster levels.

As you advance, the gaps become wider and appear more frequently. Time your jumps carefully to clear them. If you fall off the path, you'll instantly respawn at the start of that level, giving you another chance to conquer the course. You can choose your difficulty before starting, with 'Normal' and 'Fast' speed options available.
