Run

How to Play Run

In Run, you take control of a small alien sprinting through a hazardous 3D tunnel in space. Your objective is simple: run as far as you can without falling off the track. The levels become increasingly difficult, demanding quick reflexes and sharp timing to survive.

The game's standout feature is its gravity-shifting mechanic. When you run onto a side wall or the ceiling, the entire world rotates, and that surface becomes your new floor. Mastering this 'flip' is essential for navigating the course and avoiding the growing number of gaps that appear.

Use the left and right arrow keys to move your character sideways and press the spacebar to jump. Combine these movements to leap over holes or jump from the floor to a wall to change your path. If you fall, you'll instantly restart the current level, so stay focused!

Choose between Adventure Mode to progress through a series of levels or Infinite Mode for an endless challenge. You can also adjust the speed before you start, making the game more casual or intensely difficult depending on your preference.
