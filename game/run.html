Run

How to Play Run

Your goal in Run is to guide your alien character safely through a series of complex tunnels in space. The key is to avoid falling through the gaps in the floor, walls, and ceiling. Use the arrow keys to move left and right, and press the spacebar to jump over obstacles.

The most important mechanic is gravity-shifting. Whenever you run onto a wall or the ceiling, the gravity will reorient, allowing you to run on that surface. Use this unique ability to navigate around holes and find a clear path forward. You can chain flips together to cross wide gaps and tricky sections.

Choose Adventure Mode to progress through increasingly difficult levels, or test your endurance in Infinite Mode. The game gets faster and the gaps become more frequent as you advance. If you fall, you'll instantly restart at the beginning of the current level, so keep running and hone your reflexes!
