Run

How to Play Run

Welcome to Run, the classic gravity-defying platformer! Your goal is simple: guide your alien character through a series of cosmic tunnels without falling into the void. Use the arrow keys to move left and right, and press the spacebar to jump over gaps.

The core mechanic of Run is its unique gravity system. When you run onto a wall or the ceiling, the entire level will rotate, making that surface your new "floor." Master this ability to navigate around tricky gaps and find new paths through the level. You can even jump from a wall to the ceiling mid-air to chain flips and avoid wider holes.

As you progress through the levels in Adventure Mode, the course gets faster and more complex. If you fall, you'll instantly respawn at the beginning of the current level. Stay focused and time your jumps perfectly to conquer each stage and unlock the next!
