Run

How to Play Run

Your goal in Run is to guide a small alien through a series of cosmic tunnels. The path is full of missing tiles, so you'll need to be quick with your jumps to avoid falling into the void. If you fall, you restart the current level, so stay focused!

Use the arrow keys to move left and right, and press the spacebar (or up arrow) to jump. The most unique feature of Run is its gravity-shifting mechanic. When you run onto a wall or the ceiling, gravity will reorient, allowing you to run on that surface. This is essential for navigating complex sections of the tunnel and avoiding large gaps.

As you progress, the levels become faster and the holes in the path become wider. Timing your jumps and planning your wall-runs is key to success. You can choose 'Adventure Mode' to progress through levels sequentially, or select 'Infinite Mode' to test your endurance and see how long you can last.
