Run

How to Play Run

Your goal in Run is to guide your little alien character through a series of 3D tunnels in space. The path is full of holes, and falling off means you have to start the level over!

Use the Left and Right arrow keys to move your character from side to side. Press the Spacebar or Up Arrow key to jump over gaps in the floor.

The most unique feature of Run is the ability to flip gravity. Simply run onto any wall or ceiling, and it will become your new "floor." This is essential for navigating complex levels where the path is broken. You can chain jumps from the floor to the wall, to the ceiling, and back again to avoid falling.

As you progress through Adventure Mode, the levels become faster and the gaps get wider. You'll need to time your jumps perfectly and think on your feet to master the gravity-shifting parkour. Good luck!
