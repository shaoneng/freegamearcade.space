ShooterZ.io

How to Play ShooterZ.io

Begin your combat experience by either creating a new game room or joining an existing one. When creating a room, you can set a custom name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: Free-For-All (FFA) or Team. With 11 unique maps like Sanctum, FactoryX, and Ship, every match offers a new tactical challenge.

Once on the battlefield, your objective is to out-gun your opponents. Navigate the map, use cover strategically, and work with your team to achieve victory. You'll have access to a diverse arsenal, including the Deagle, MP5KA4, M87T shotgun, Blaser R93 sniper, and even an RPG. Switch between them to adapt to any combat situation.

Master the controls to dominate the arena. Use the WASD keys to move, and the Mouse to aim and shoot. Aim down sights with the Right Mouse Button. Sprint with Shift, crouch with C, and jump with the Spacebar. Reload your weapon with R and swap between your gear with the 1-9 keys. You can open the chat with T and view the menu with Tab.
