ShooterZ.io

How to Play ShooterZ.io

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of ShooterZ.io! To get started, you can either create your own game room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can set a name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: Free-For-All (FFA), where it's every soldier for themselves, or Team Deathmatch for strategic squad-based combat. Choose from over 10 distinct maps, each offering unique tactical advantages.

Once you're in the game, your objective is to eliminate as many opponents as possible using a wide array of weapons. Keep an eye on your surroundings, use cover effectively, and work with your team (in Team mode) to dominate the battlefield. Your performance, including kills, wins, and overall score, is tracked in the statistics section.

Master the controls to dominate the battlefield: Use the Left Mouse Button to shoot and the Right Mouse Button to aim down sights. Jump with the Space Bar, crouch with C, and hold Shift to run. Reload your weapon with R and switch between your arsenal using the 1-9 keys. You can open the chat with T (press Enter to send), pause with P, and view the in-game menu with Tab.
