ShooterZ.io

How to Play ShooterZ.io

Welcome to ShooterZ.io, a strategic first-person shooter that tests your skills against players from around the world in a fast-paced online arena. Your primary objective is to stay alert, utilize the map to your advantage, and dominate the battlefield.

Getting Started

You can either create your own game room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can set the name, the maximum number of players, and choose between Free-For-All (FFA) or Team-based game modes. With 11 unique maps like Sanctum, Yarimar, and FactoryX, every match offers a new tactical challenge.

Arsenal & Tactics

Equip yourself with a diverse range of weapons to suit your playstyle. From the stealthy Ballistic Knife and precise Blaser R93 sniper rifle to the explosive RPG, choosing the right tool for the job is key to victory. Keep an eye on your statistics to track your progress, including games played, wins, kills, and overall score.

Controls

  • Left Mouse Button: Shoot
  • Right Mouse Button: Aim
  • Space Bar: Jump
  • Hold Shift: Run
  • C: Crouch
  • R: Reload Weapon
  • 1-9 Keys: Change Weapon
  • T: Open Chat (Enter to send)
  • Tab: Open Menu
  • P: Pause Game
