ShooterZ.io

How to Play ShooterZ.io

Dive into the competitive arena of ShooterZ.io and prove your shooting prowess against players from around the world. To get started, you can either join an existing game room or create your own custom match. When creating a room, you can set a name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your game mode: Free-For-All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch.

Prepare for battle across 11 unique maps, including Sanctum, FactoryX, and Ship, each offering different tactical challenges. Your arsenal is packed with a variety of weapons, from the silent Ballistic Knife and versatile MP5KA4 to the powerful Blaser R93 sniper rifle and the explosive RPG.

Master the controls to dominate the battlefield: Use the Left Mouse Button to shoot and the Right Mouse Button to aim. Press R to reload your weapon and the 1-9 keys to switch between them. Move around the map and use the Space Bar to jump, hold Shift to run, and press C to crouch. You can open the in-game menu with Tab, pause with P, and communicate with your team using T to open chat.
