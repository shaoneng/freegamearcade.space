ShooterZ.io

How to Play ShooterZ.io

Welcome to ShooterZ.io, a strategic online shooter that pits you against players worldwide. To begin, you can either join an existing game room or create your own. When creating a room, you can set a custom name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: Free-For-All (FFA) for a chaotic solo battle or Team mode for coordinated tactical play.

Once on the battlefield, your objective is to eliminate opponents and stay alive. Use the environment for cover, keep an eye on the map for enemy positions, and work with your team if you're in Team mode. With 11 unique maps like Sanctum, BunkerX, and Ship, each match offers a new strategic challenge. Track your performance in the statistics section, which details your kills, wins, and overall score.

Master the controls to dominate the arena. Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot. Aim down sights with the right mouse button. Jump with the Space bar and hold Shift to run. Crouch with C for a smaller target profile. Reload your weapon with R and switch between your arsenal using the 1-9 number keys. Communicate with others by pressing T to open the chat. If you need a break, press Tab to open the menu or P to pause the game.
