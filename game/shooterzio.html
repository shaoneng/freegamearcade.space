ShooterZ.io

How to Play ShooterZ.io

Welcome to the intense battlefield of ShooterZ.io! Your goal is simple: outgun your opponents in a fast-paced, online first-person shooter. You can either host your own game or join an existing one to dive straight into the action.

Getting Started: To begin, you can create a new room. Give it a name, set the maximum number of players, and choose your preferred game mode: Free-For-All (FFA) where it's every soldier for themselves, or Team mode for cooperative combat. Alternatively, browse the list of existing games and join a match that's already in progress.

Master the Controls:

  • Movement: W, A, S, D keys
  • Jump: Space Bar
  • Run: Hold Shift
  • Crouch: C key
  • Shoot: Left Mouse Button
  • Aim Down Sights: Right Mouse Button
  • Reload: R key
  • Switch Weapon: 1-9 keys
  • Chat: T key to open, Enter to send
  • Menu/Scoreboard: Tab key
  • Pause: P key

Arsenal and Battlegrounds: Your survival depends on your skill and your gear. Arm yourself with a diverse range of weapons, from the precise Ballistic Knife and Deagle to the powerful MP5KA4, STW-25 rifle, and even an RPG. Battle across 11 unique maps, including Sanctum, FactoryX, and Ship, each offering different tactical challenges. Keep an eye on your stats to track your kills, wins, and overall score as you become a legend on the battlefield!
