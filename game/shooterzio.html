How to Play ShooterZ.io

Welcome to ShooterZ.io, a fast-paced online arena shooter where your strategic thinking and precise aiming will be put to the test. Before diving into the action, hereâ€™s what you need to know:

Game Modes & Rooms: You have the flexibility to either create your own custom room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can name it, set the maximum number of players, and choose between two exciting game modes: Free For All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch. This allows you to tailor your combat experience or jump straight into the fray with others.

Maps & Weapons: Prepare for diverse battles across 11 unique maps, including locations like Sanctum, Yarimar, BunkerX, and FactoryX. Each map offers distinct tactical opportunities. You'll have access to a wide array of weapons to suit your playstyle, from close-quarters options like the BallisticKnife and MP5KA4 to long-range firepower such as the Blaser R93 and the devastating RPG.

Controls: Master your movements and combat actions with these intuitive controls:

  • Left Mouse Button: Shoot
  • Right Mouse Button: Aim
  • R Key: Reload
  • C Key: Crouch
  • Space Bar: Jump
  • Hold Shift: Run
  • 1-9 Keys: Change Weapon
  • T Key: Open Chat (Enter to Send)
  • P Key: Pause Game
  • Tab Key: Open In-Game Menu

Keep an eye on your statistics section to track your progress, including games played, wins, kills, and score. Get ready to train your soldier, dominate the battlefield, and climb the ranks in ShooterZ.io!
