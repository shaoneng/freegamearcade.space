ShooterZ.io

How to Play ShooterZ.io

Welcome to the battlefield of ShooterZ.io! To begin, you can either create your own custom game room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can set a unique name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: Free-for-All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch. With over 11 distinct maps like Sanctum, FactoryX, and BunkerX, you can tailor the combat experience to your liking.

Mastering the controls is key to survival. Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot. Aim down sights for better accuracy with the right mouse button. Navigate the map using the standard WASD keys, jump with the Space bar, and crouch with C. Hold Shift to run and get into position quickly. To reload your weapon, press R. You can switch between your arsenal of weapons using the number keys (1-9).

Communicate with your team and other players by pressing T to open the chat, then Enter to send your message. If you need a break, press P to pause the game. You can always check the leaderboard and game menu by pressing Tab. Keep an eye on your stats, dominate the battlefield, and climb your way to the top!
