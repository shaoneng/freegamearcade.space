ShooterZ.io

How to Play ShooterZ.io

Welcome to the battlefield of ShooterZ.io! This is a fast-paced multiplayer shooter where you'll test your skills against players worldwide. To begin, you can either join an existing game room or create your own. When creating a room, you can set its name, the maximum number of players, and choose between Free-For-All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch modes. With 11 unique maps and a vast arsenal, every match offers a new strategic challenge.

Mastering the controls is crucial for survival. Use the W, A, S, D keys for movement, the Space Bar to jump, hold Shift to run, and press C to crouch. Aim your weapon using the Right Mouse Button and fire with the Left Mouse Button. Don't forget to press R to reload when you're out of ammo.

You can switch between your equipped weapons using the number keys 1-9. To communicate with your team or other players, press T to open the chat window and Enter to send your message. You can pause the game at any time by pressing P, or open the main menu by pressing Tab to check scores and settings.
