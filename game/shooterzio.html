ShooterZ.io

How to Play ShooterZ.io

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of ShooterZ.io! In this strategic multiplayer shooter, your goal is to outsmart and outgun opponents from around the world. To get started, you can either create your own custom game room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can set the name, maximum player count, and choose between 'Free-For-All' (FFA) or 'Team' game modes.

Master the battlefield by learning the essential controls:

  • Movement: Use W, A, S, D to move, Space Bar to jump, hold Shift to run, and press C to crouch.
  • Combat: Aim down your sights with the Right Mouse Button and fire with the Left Mouse Button. Press R to reload your weapon.
  • Weapons & Menu: Quickly switch between your arsenal using the number keys 1-9. Press Tab to open the in-game menu to check scores and settings.
  • Communication: Coordinate with your team by pressing T to open the chat window, typing your message, and hitting Enter to send.

With a variety of maps and a wide selection of weapons, from the Deagle to the RPG, every match offers a unique tactical experience. Keep an eye on your stats, adapt your strategy, and climb the leaderboards!
