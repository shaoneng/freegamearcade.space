ShooterZ.io

How to Play ShooterZ.io

Welcome to the intense battlefield of ShooterZ.io! Your objective is to out-shoot your opponents in a global online arena. You can start by either creating your own custom game room or joining an existing one to jump straight into the action. Choose between 'Free For All' (FFA), where it's every soldier for themselves, or 'Team' mode for cooperative combat.

Mastering the controls is key to victory. Use your mouse to aim and the Left Mouse Button to shoot. Aim down sights with the Right Mouse Button for better accuracy. Move your character with the standard WASD keys and jump with the Space Bar. You can run by holding Shift and crouch with the C key. Reload your weapon with R, and switch between your arsenal using the 1-9 keys. Communicate with your team using the chat (T to open, Enter to send).

Explore 11 diverse maps, from the tight corners of 'BunkerX' to the open spaces of 'Freedom'. Your performance is tracked in the statistics section, where you can see your kills, wins, and overall score. Now gear up, soldier, and dominate the arena!
