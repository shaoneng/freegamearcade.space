ShooterZ.io

How to Play ShooterZ.io

Welcome to the intense battlefield of ShooterZ.io! This strategic first-person shooter puts your skills to the test against players from all over the globe. Your objective is simple: outsmart and outgun your opponents to dominate the arena.

Before you dive in, you can either join an existing room or create your own. When creating a room, you can set a custom name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: Free-For-All (FFA) where it's every soldier for themselves, or Team mode for cooperative combat. With 11 diverse maps to choose from, including Sanctum, FactoryX, and BunkerX, no two matches are ever the same.

Mastering the controls is key to victory. Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot. Aim down sights with the right mouse button for better accuracy. Move your character with the standard WASD keys, jump with the Space bar, and hold Shift to run. You can crouch with C, reload your weapon with R, and switch between your arsenal using the 1-9 keys. Communicate with your team using the chat (T to open, Enter to send).

Choose from a wide array of weapons to suit your playstyle, including the Deagle, MP5KA4, and the powerful RPG. Your performance is tracked in the statistics section, where you can see your total games played, wins, kills, and overall score. Now, gear up and show them what you've got!
