ShooterZ.io

How to Play ShooterZ.io

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of ShooterZ.io! The objective is simple: eliminate your opponents and dominate the arena. Whether you're fighting solo in Free-for-All (FFA) or collaborating with others in Team mode, your strategic thinking and sharp shooting will be key to victory.

To begin, you can either create your own game room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can set a name, choose the maximum number of players, select a game mode (FFA or Team), and pick from 11 unique maps. Once in the game, keep an eye on your surroundings, use cover, and work with your team to secure a win.

Mastering the controls is essential for survival. Here's a full breakdown of the key commands:

  • Shoot: Left Mouse Button
  • Aim: Right Mouse Button
  • Jump: Space Bar
  • Run: Hold Shift
  • Crouch: C
  • Reload: R
  • Change Weapon: 1-9 Keys
  • Chat: T to open, Enter to send
  • Pause/Menu: P / Tab
