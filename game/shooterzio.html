How to Play ShooterZ.io

Welcome to the intense battlegrounds of ShooterZ.io! This strategic first-person shooter puts your skills to the test in a global online arena. You can jump into an existing game or create your own custom match to play with friends or other combatants from around the world.

To get started, you can either join an existing room from the server list or create your own. When creating a room, you have full control: give it a unique name, set the maximum number of players, and choose your game modeâ€”Free For All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch. With 11 different maps like Sanctum, FactoryX, and Ship, every match offers a new tactical challenge.

Mastering the controls is key to survival and victory. Use the Left Mouse Button to shoot and the Right Mouse Button to aim down your sights for better accuracy. Move your soldier with the standard WASD keys, jump with the Space bar, and crouch with C. Hold Shift to run and get into position quickly. Reload your weapon with R, and switch between your arsenal using the 1-9 keys. You can communicate with your team using the chat (press T to open, then Enter to send your message).
