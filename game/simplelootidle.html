Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle! In this addictive clicker RPG, your main goal is to battle monsters, collect powerful loot, and continuously grow stronger. Your character attacks enemies automatically, but you can join the fray by clicking the large red sword icon to deal extra damage and speed up your progress.

Keep an eye on the battle screen. Your health is the green bar, while the monster's is red. When a monster is defeated, it drops a piece of loot that automatically goes into your inventory on the right. Hover over new items to compare their stats with what you currently have equipped.

Equipping better gear is key to survival. Drag and drop stronger items from your backpack to the corresponding gear slots. Not all loot is worth keeping; click the "Sell" button and then click on unwanted items to turn them into valuable coins. Use these coins to purchase permanent upgrades from the red buttons on the left. A great starting upgrade is "Loot Chance" to get more items faster!

You'll advance to a new, more challenging stage after defeating every five monsters. These tougher enemies drop superior loot. If you are defeated, don't worry! Simply press "Start Over." You will return to Stage 1 but retain all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making each new run faster and more powerful.
