Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle, where your hero fights automatically, but you control their destiny! Your main goal is to defeat monsters, collect loot, and continuously upgrade your character. To give your hero a boost in battle, click the large red sword icon to deal extra damage and speed up the fight.

When a monster is defeated, it will drop a piece of gear that lands in your inventory on the right side of the screen. Hover over any item to see its stats. If a new item is better than what you currently have equipped, simply click to swap it out for an instant power boost.

Not all loot is worth keeping. You can sell weaker items by clicking the "Sell" button and then selecting the unwanted gear. This converts them into coins, which are essential for progress. Use your coins to purchase permanent upgrades from the red buttons on the left. A great starting upgrade is "Loot Chance," as it increases the frequency of item drops, helping you find better gear faster.

As you defeat monsters, you'll advance through stages. Every five victories moves you to a new stage with tougher enemies but also better rewards. If you are defeated in battle, don't worry! You'll simply restart at Stage 1, but you get to keep all of your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making each new run faster and more powerful than the last.
