Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle! In this addictive clicker RPG, your character automatically battles a series of monsters. Your role is to manage their gear, purchase upgrades, and click to deal extra damage, helping them overcome increasingly tough foes.

Your hero fights on their own, but you can speed things up by clicking the large red sword icon at the bottom of the screen. Each victory causes the monster to drop a piece of loot, which automatically goes into your backpack on the right. Hover over new items to compare their stats with what you currently have equipped.

To become stronger, drag and drop better armor and weapons from your backpack into the corresponding equipment slots. This will increase your damage, health, and defense. Not all loot is an upgrade; you can sell weaker items by clicking the "Sell" button and then clicking on the items you want to get rid of. This turns them into valuable coins.

Use your coins to purchase permanent upgrades using the red buttons on the left side of the screen. A great starting upgrade is "Loot Chance," as it increases the frequency of item drops, accelerating your progress. After defeating five monsters, you'll advance to the next stage, where you'll face tougher enemies that drop even better loot.

Don't worry if you're defeated! If your health bar runs out, simply click "Start Over." You'll return to Stage 1, but you will keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making your next climb through the stages much faster. The core loop is simple: fight, loot, equip, sell, upgrade, and repeat. Good luck, hero!
