Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle! Your adventure begins immediately as your hero automatically battles monsters. Keep an eye on your green health bar and the monster's red one. While your hero fights on their own, you can speed things up by clicking the large red sword icon to deal extra damage.

After defeating a monster, it will drop a piece of loot that appears in your inventory on the right. Hover over an item to see its stats. If it's better than what you're currently wearing, drag and drop it into the appropriate gear slot to equip it. Better gear means more damage and higher defense!

Not all loot is worth keeping. You can sell weaker or unwanted items by clicking the "Sell" button and then clicking the items you want to get rid of. This turns them into valuable coins. Use these coins to buy permanent upgrades from the red buttons on the left. A great starting upgrade is "Loot Chance" to get more items faster.

Every five monsters you defeat, you'll advance to the next stage, where tougher enemies await with even better loot. Don't worry if you're defeated! You'll simply restart at Stage 1, but you get to keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making your next climb much faster.

The core gameplay is a satisfying loop: fight, loot, equip, sell, and upgrade. Continue this cycle to become powerful enough to conquer the highest stages. Good luck, hero!
