Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle, a captivating clicker RPG where your goal is to battle monsters, collect powerful gear, and become unstoppable. Your character attacks automatically, making it easy to get started, but your strategic choices and clicks will determine your success.

To speed up battles, click the large red sword icon at the bottom of the screen. Each click delivers an extra attack, helping you defeat monsters faster. When an enemy is vanquished, it drops a piece of loot that automatically goes into your backpack on the right.

Hover over new items in your inventory to see their stats. If an item is better than what you're currently wearing, drag and drop it into the appropriate gear slot. Equipping better items increases your damage and defense, allowing you to take on tougher foes.

Not all loot is worth keeping. Sell weaker or unwanted items by clicking the "Sell" button and then clicking the items in your bag. This will convert them into coins, which you can use to purchase permanent upgrades from the red buttons on the left. A great starting upgrade is "Loot Chance" to get more gear drops.

After defeating five monsters, you'll advance to the next stage, where you'll face stronger enemies that drop even better loot. Don't worry if you're defeated; you'll restart at Stage 1 but keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making your next climb much easier.
