Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle! Your hero fights monsters automatically, allowing you to focus on strategy. You can speed up battles by clicking the large red sword icon for extra damage. As you defeat enemies, loot will drop into your inventory on the right side of the screen.

Managing your loot is key to getting stronger. Hover over new items in your backpack to compare their stats with your currently equipped gear. If an item is better, simply click to equip it. Weaker items can be sold for coins by clicking the "Sell" button and then selecting the junk you want to get rid of.

Use the coins you earn from selling loot to purchase permanent upgrades, found on the left side of the screen. A great starting upgrade is "Loot Chance," which increases the drop rate of items, helping you find better gear faster.

You'll advance to a new stage after every five monsters defeated. Each stage brings tougher foes but also better rewards. If a monster defeats you, don't worry! Simply press "Start Over." You'll return to Stage 1 but keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making each new run faster and more powerful.
