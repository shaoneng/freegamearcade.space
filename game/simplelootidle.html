Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle, an addictive RPG where you battle monsters, collect powerful gear, and become endlessly stronger. Your hero fights automatically, so you can sit back and watch the loot roll in. For a more active approach, click the large red sword icon to deal extra damage and defeat enemies faster.

When a monster is defeated, it drops a new piece of loot that automatically goes into your inventory on the right side of the screen. Hover your mouse over the new item to see its stats. If it's better than what you currently have equipped, drag and drop it into the corresponding slot to boost your power.

Not all loot is worth keeping. Weaker items will clutter your inventory. Click the "Sell" button, then click on the items you don't want to convert them into valuable coins. Use these coins to purchase permanent upgrades from the red buttons on the left. A great starting upgrade is "Loot Chance" to increase the frequency of item drops.

After defeating five monsters, you will advance to the next stage, where tougher enemies await with even better rewards. If you are defeated in battle, don't worry! Simply press "Start Over." You will return to Stage 1 but will keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, allowing you to progress even faster on your next run. Keep fighting, looting, and upgrading to see how far you can go!
