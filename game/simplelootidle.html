Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle! In this game, your character fights monsters automatically, allowing you to focus on strategy and upgrades. If you want to speed things up, click the large red sword icon to deal extra damage and defeat enemies faster.

Every monster you vanquish drops a piece of loot into your backpack on the right side of the screen. Hover your mouse over an item to check its stats. If it's better than your current gear, simply click and drag it to the correct equipment slot to power up your hero.

Not all loot is worth keeping. To clear out your inventory, click the "Sell" button, then click on the items you don't want to convert them into coins. Use these coins to buy permanent upgrades from the red buttons on the left. A great first choice is "Loot Chance" to get more items faster.

After defeating five monsters, you'll advance to the next stage, where tougher enemies await with better rewards. If you are defeated, don't worry! Just hit "Start Over." You'll return to Stage 1 but will keep all your purchased upgrades and equipped gear, making each new run quicker and more powerful.
