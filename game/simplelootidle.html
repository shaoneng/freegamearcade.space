Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle, an addictive RPG where you battle monsters, collect epic loot, and grow infinitely stronger. Your hero attacks monsters automatically, so you can sit back and watch the action. For a hands-on approach, click the large red sword icon to deal extra damage and defeat enemies faster.

After each victory, monsters drop new items that appear in your backpack on the right. Hover over an item to see its stats. If it's better than what you currently have equipped, drag and drop it into the corresponding gear slot to boost your power.

Not all loot is worth keeping. Click the "Sell" button, then click on any unwanted items in your inventory to turn them into valuable coins. Use these coins to purchase permanent upgrades using the red buttons on the left. A great first purchase is "Loot Chance" to increase the frequency of item drops.

You will advance to a new stage after defeating every five monsters. Each stage features tougher enemies but also rewards you with superior loot. Don't worry if you are defeated; you'll restart at Stage 1 but keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making your next climb to the top even quicker!
