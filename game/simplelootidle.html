Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Simple Loot Idle is a straightforward yet addictive idle RPG. Your hero fights monsters automatically, earning you loot and experience. You can jump into the action by clicking the large red sword icon to deal extra damage and speed up battles. The main goal is to defeat enemies, collect better gear, and push through progressively harder stages.

Get familiar with the screen layout. On the left, you'll find red buttons for purchasing permanent upgrades. The center of the screen shows the ongoing battle, with your health bar in green and the monster's in red. On the right are your equipment slots and backpack, where all your newly acquired loot appears.

When a monster is defeated, it drops an item directly into your backpack. Hover over the item to see its stats. If it's better than what you're currently wearing, simply drag and drop it into the appropriate slot. You can sell weaker or unwanted items by clicking the "Sell" button and then clicking on the gear you wish to discard. This converts them into valuable coins.

Use your coins to buy upgrades from the menu on the left. A great starting upgrade is "Loot Chance" to increase the frequency of drops. After defeating five monsters, you'll advance to the next stage, where tougher enemies await with even better rewards. If you are defeated, don't worry! Simply press "Start Over." You will return to Stage 1 but keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making your next climb much faster.
