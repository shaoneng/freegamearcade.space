Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle! In this game, your hero automatically fights monsters. While you can let the game play itself, you can speed things up by clicking the large red sword icon at the center of the screen to deal extra damage. Each defeated monster drops a piece of loot into your inventory.

Your gear and backpack are displayed on the right side of the screen. Hover over a new item in your backpack to check its stats. If it's better than your current equipment, simply click and drag it to the correct slot to equip it. Upgrading your gear is key to increasing your damage and surviving tougher fights.

Not every item you find will be an upgrade. To manage your inventory, click the "Sell" button, then click on the items you don't need to convert them into coins. Use these coins to purchase permanent upgrades, which are the red buttons on the left. The "Loot Chance" upgrade is a great first choice to help you find better gear more quickly.

After defeating five monsters, you'll advance to the next stage, where you'll face more powerful enemies that drop superior loot. If your health bar is depleted, you'll be defeated. Simply press "Start Over" to begin again from Stage 1, but you'll keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making your next run much easier.
