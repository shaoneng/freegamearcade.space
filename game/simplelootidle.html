Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle! Your hero fights monsters automatically. You can watch the battle unfold in the center of the screen, keeping an eye on your green health bar and the monster's red one. To speed things up, click the large red sword icon to deal extra damage.

Defeating a monster causes it to drop a piece of loot, which appears in your backpack on the right. Hover over an item to see its stats. If it's better than what you're currently wearing, drag it to the corresponding gear slot to equip it. Better gear means more damage and higher defense.

Not all loot is an upgrade. Click the 'Sell' button and then click on any items you don't need to convert them into coins. Use these coins to buy permanent upgrades from the red buttons on the left. A great starting upgrade is 'Loot Chance' to get more items faster.

You advance to a new stage after defeating five monsters. Each stage features stronger enemies but also offers better rewards. If you are defeated, don't worry! Simply press 'Start Over'. You'll return to Stage 1 but keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making your next run much easier.
