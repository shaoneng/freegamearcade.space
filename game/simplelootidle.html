How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle! Your hero automatically battles monsters, allowing you to focus on strategy and upgrades. If you want to speed up the fight, click the large red sword icon to deal extra damage. Keep an eye on your green health bar and the monster's red one to track the battle's progress.

After you defeat a monster, it drops a piece of loot that automatically goes into your backpack on the right side of the screen. Hover your mouse over new items to check their stats. If a new piece of gear is stronger than what you're currently wearing, simply click it to equip it and boost your power.

Your backpack will fill up quickly, but not every item is an upgrade. Click the "Sell" button, then select the items you don't need to convert them into coins. You can then spend these coins on the red upgrade buttons on the left, which permanently boost your stats. A good first purchase is "Loot Chance" to increase the frequency of item drops.

For every five monsters you defeat, you advance to the next stage. Higher stages present tougher enemies but also yield superior loot. If your hero falls in battle, simply press "Start Over." You'll return to Stage 1, but you'll keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making your next run faster and more powerful.
