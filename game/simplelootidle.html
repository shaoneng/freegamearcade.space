Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle! Your hero automatically attacks the monster in the center of the screen. While the combat is automated, you can speed things up by clicking the large red sword icon to deal extra damage.

When a monster is defeated, it drops a piece of loot that goes into your backpack on the right. Hover your mouse over the new item to check its stats. If it's better than what you currently have equipped, drag and drop it into the appropriate gear slot to increase your hero's power.

Not every item you find will be an upgrade. You can sell weaker or unwanted pieces to free up inventory space and earn coins. Simply click the "Sell" button, then click on the items you wish to discard. Use the coins you earn to purchase permanent upgrades using the red buttons on the left, such as "Loot Chance," to improve your future runs.

After defeating five monsters, you will advance to a new stage with tougher enemies but better loot drops. If your health bar empties, you'll be defeated. Press "Start Over" to begin again from Stage 1, but you'll keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making each new attempt easier and faster.
