Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Your hero battles monsters automatically in the center of the screen. While they fight on their own, you can speed things up by clicking the large red sword icon to deal extra damage. Keep an eye on your green health bar and the monster's red one to track the fight's progress.

When a monster is defeated, it drops a piece of loot that goes directly into your inventory on the right. Hover over an item to see its stats. If it's better than what you currently have equipped, simply drag and drop it into the appropriate gear slot to boost your power.

Not all loot is worth keeping. Click the "Sell" button and then click on any unwanted items in your backpack to turn them into coins. Use these coins to purchase permanent upgrades from the red buttons on the left. A great starting upgrade is "Loot Chance" to get more items faster.

You'll advance to a new stage after defeating every five monsters. Enemies get tougher with each stage, but they also reward you with better loot. If you are defeated, don't worry! Just hit "Start Over." You will return to Stage 1 but keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making your next run much easier.

The core gameplay loop is simple: battle, loot, equip, sell, and upgrade. Repeat this cycle to become progressively stronger and see how high you can climb!
