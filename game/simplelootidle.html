Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle! Your hero automatically fights monsters on screen. You can sit back and watch or actively participate by clicking the large red sword icon to deal extra damage and speed up battles. Keep an eye on your green health bar and the monster's red health bar to track the fight's progress.

When you defeat a monster, it drops a piece of loot that appears in your inventory on the right. Hover over an item to see its stats. If it's better than what you currently have equipped, simply click and drag it to the appropriate slot to upgrade your hero's power and defense.

Not all loot is worth keeping. To clear your inventory and earn coins, click the "Sell" button and then select the items you don't need. Use these coins to purchase permanent upgrades from the red buttons on the left. A great starting upgrade is "Loot Chance" to increase the frequency of item drops.

After defeating five monsters, you'll advance to the next stage, where tougher enemies await with even better rewards. If your health drops to zero, don't worry! You'll restart at Stage 1 but get to keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making each new run faster and more powerful. Keep fighting, looting, and upgrading to see how far you can climb!
