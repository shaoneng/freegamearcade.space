Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Dive into Simple Loot Idle, a free online clicker game where your hero automatically battles monsters to gather loot and grow stronger. While your character fights on their own, you can click the large red sword icon to deal extra damage and speed up the fight. Keep an eye on your green health bar and the monster's red one.

Each defeated monster drops new loot into your backpack on the right. Hover over items to see their stats. If an item is better than what you're wearing, drag and drop it into the appropriate gear slot to equip it. Better gear means more damage and tougher armor, helping you face stronger enemies.

Not all loot is useful. Sell weaker items by clicking the "Sell" button and then selecting the items you don't need. This turns them into coins, which you can spend on powerful upgrades using the red buttons on the left. A great starting upgrade is "Loot Chance" to get more items faster.

After every five monsters, you advance to a new stage with tougher foes and better rewards. If you're defeated, don't worry! Press "Start Over" to return to Stage 1, but you'll keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making each new run faster and easier. See how many stages you can conquer!
