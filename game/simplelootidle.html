Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle! Your hero fights monsters automatically, so you can sit back and watch the action unfold. The center of the screen shows the battle, with your health bar in green and the monster's in red. If you want to speed things up, click the large red sword icon to deal extra damage.

After defeating a monster, it will drop a piece of loot that automatically goes into your backpack on the right. Hover your mouse over the new item to see its stats. If it's better than your current gear, simply drag and drop it into the correct equipment slot to boost your power.

Not every item you find will be an upgrade. To get rid of junk, click the "Sell" button, then select the items you don't need to convert them into coins. You can spend these coins on powerful, permanent upgrades using the red buttons on the left. A good strategy is to invest in "Loot Chance" early to get more gear drops.

You'll advance to a new stage every five monsters you defeat. Each stage features tougher enemies but also rewards you with better loot. If you fall in battle, just hit "Start Over." You'll return to Stage 1, but you'll keep all your upgrades and equipped gear, making each new run faster and letting you push further than before!
