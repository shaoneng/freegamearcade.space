Simple Loot Idle

How to Play Simple Loot Idle

Welcome to Simple Loot Idle! Your hero automatically battles monsters on screen. Your health is the green bar, and the monster's is red. While combat is automatic, you can actively participate by clicking the large red sword icon to deal extra damage and defeat enemies faster.

Each defeated monster drops a piece of loot into your inventory on the right. Hover your mouse over an item to see its stats. If it's better than what you're currently wearing, simply click to equip it. Stronger gear means more damage and better defense, which is crucial for surviving tougher stages.

Not all loot is an upgrade. To clear your inventory and earn gold, click the "Sell" button and then select the items you don't need. Use this gold to purchase permanent upgrades using the red buttons on the left. A great starting upgrade is "Loot Chance" to get more items, helping you find better gear more quickly.

After defeating five monsters, you'll advance to the next stage, where you'll face stronger foes that drop superior loot. If your health bar is depleted, don't worry! Simply press "Start Over" to return to Stage 1. You'll keep all your equipped gear and purchased upgrades, making each new run faster and more powerful.
