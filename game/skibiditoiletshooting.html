Skibidi Toilet Shooting

How to Play Skibidi Toilet Shooting

Your critical mission in Skibidi Toilet Shooting is to defend your city from an invasion of relentless Skibidi Toilets. In this action-packed 3D shooter, you play as a lone soldier tasked with clearing the streets of these menacing toilet-headed creatures. They are numerous and aggressive, so you must act fast to survive.

Your goal in each of the ten challenging levels is to eliminate 25 Skibidi Toilets. Keep your distance and take them out from afar before they can charge and headbutt you. As you progress, you'll unlock more powerful weapons, starting with a handgun and moving up to shotguns and assault rifles. You can switch between your unlocked weapons by scrolling your mouse.

When the toilets become too crowded, use your limited supply of grenades to clear multiple enemies at once. Use them wisely, as you only have a few. At level two, you'll also receive a helmet, offering some protection against their attacks. Stay sharp, manage your arsenal, and cleanse the city to emerge victorious!
