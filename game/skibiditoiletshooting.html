How to Play Skibidi Toilet Shooting

Welcome to the urban battleground of Skibidi Toilet Shooting! Your primary objective is to defend the city by eliminating every Skibidi Toilet that appears. As a lone soldier in this 3D first-person shooter, you must survive relentless waves of toilet-headed enemies.

The Skibidi Toilets will charge at you as soon as they see you. To survive, you must move carefully and try to keep your distance. Aim and shoot them down before they get close enough to attack. You'll start with a basic handgun, but don't worryâ€”your arsenal will grow.

The game features 10 challenging levels. In each level, you must defeat a target number of 25 Skibidi Toilets to advance. As you complete levels, you will unlock more powerful weapons, from shotguns to an M4A1 assault rifle. You can switch between your unlocked weapons using the mouse scroll wheel.

When you're overwhelmed by a crowd of toilets, use your grenades! You have a limited supply, so throw them strategically to take out multiple enemies at once. Wait for the perfect moment to maximize your explosive impact and clear the streets.
