Skibidi Toilet Shooting

How to Play Skibidi Toilet Shooting

Your city is under invasion by an army of Skibidi Toilets, and you are the last line of defense! In this action-packed 3D shooter, your primary mission is to survive the onslaught and eliminate every toilet-headed enemy you see.

Navigate the city streets and use your first-person perspective to aim and shoot. The Skibidi Toilets will charge at you as soon as they spot you, so keeping your distance is the best strategy. Aim carefully and take them out before they get too close.

You start with a basic pistol, but as you clear levels, you'll unlock a powerful arsenal including shotguns and assault rifles. To pass each of the ten levels, you must destroy 25 Skibidi Toilets. Don't forget your grenades! When you're overwhelmed by a crowd, toss a grenade to clear them out, but use them wisely as they are limited.
