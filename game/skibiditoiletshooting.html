Skibidi Toilet Shooting

How to Play Skibidi Toilet Shooting

Your mission is to defend your city from an invasion of Skibidi Toilets. In this first-person shooter, you must eliminate all the toilet heads coming your way to survive and clear each level.

Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot. The key to survival is to keep your distance from the enemies, as they will rush you once they spot you. Eliminate them from afar before they can get close enough to attack.

You start with a basic pistol, but as you complete levels, you'll unlock more powerful weapons like shotguns and rifles. You can switch between your unlocked weapons using the mouse scroll wheel. You also have a limited number of grenades. Use them wisely when enemies are clustered together for maximum impact.

To advance to the next level, you must successfully take down 25 Skibidi Toilets. With ten challenging levels to conquer, your skills and arsenal will be put to the test.
