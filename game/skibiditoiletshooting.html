Skibidi Toilet Shooting

How to Play Skibidi Toilet Shooting

Your primary mission is to defend your city from a bizarre invasion of Skibidi Toilets. In this first-person shooter, you are a lone soldier tasked with eliminating all the toilet heads that are trying to take over. They are numerous and aggressive, so you must stay alert to survive.

The Skibidi Toilets will spot you and charge relentlessly. The best strategy is to keep your distance and take them out from afar with your firearm. As you defeat enemies and complete levels, you will unlock a powerful arsenal of new weapons, including shotguns and assault rifles. To switch between your acquired weapons, simply use the mouse wheel.

Each of the ten levels requires you to eliminate 25 Skibidi Toilets to advance. You also have a limited supply of grenades. These are perfect for crowd control when the enemies get too close. Use them wisely, waiting for the perfect moment to take out multiple toilets with a single explosion and conserve your limited supply.
