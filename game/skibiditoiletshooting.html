Skibidi Toilet Shooting

How to Play Skibidi Toilet Shooting

Welcome to Skibidi Toilet Shooting, a high-octane 3D first-person shooter! Your mission is to defend your city from an invasion of aggressive Skibidi Toilets. As a lone soldier, you must survive their attacks and clear them from your homeland.

The Skibidi Toilets are numerous and will charge at you on sight. A smart strategy is to keep your distance and eliminate them from afar with your firearm. As you defeat enemies and progress through the game, you'll unlock more powerful weapons to add to your arsenal, from shotguns to assault rifles.

When you find yourself surrounded by a crowd of toilets, use a grenade to take out multiple enemies at once. You have a limited number of grenades, so save them for critical moments to maximize their impact.

To complete a level, you must take down 25 Skibidi Toilets. There are ten challenging levels to conquer, each offering a unique environment and new weapons to master. Stay sharp, aim true, and reclaim your city!
