Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Welcome to Skribblio, the ultimate online drawing and guessing game! If you love word games and have a knack for drawing (or even if you don't!), you'll have a blast. The core of the game is simple: when it's your turn, you'll be given a word to draw. Do your best to illustrate it while other players try to guess what it is in the chat.

When you're not drawing, you're guessing! The faster you guess the correct word, the more points you get. Similarly, the quicker other players guess your drawing, the more points you'll rack up. A game room can host anywhere from 2 to 20 players, making for a lively and dynamic experience.

At the bottom of the screen, you'll find your toolbox. It lets you select different colors, change your brush size, use an eraser for mistakes, or clear the canvas. You can even use a drawing tablet for more precise control!

Skribblio can be played in over 28 languages, including English, German, French, and Italian. If a player is being disruptive, you can vote to kick them out. If you created the private room, you have full control. So gather your friends, or join a public game, and let the creative fun begin!
