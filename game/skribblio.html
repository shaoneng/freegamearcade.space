Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Skribblio is an exciting online multiplayer game of drawing and guessing, perfect for word game lovers and aspiring artists. The goal is simple: score the most points by drawing and guessing words correctly.

When it's your turn to draw, the game will give you a word. Use the toolbox at the bottom of the screen to select colors and brush sizes to illustrate that word. The other players will race to type the correct guess into the chat. The faster someone guesses, the more points you both earn!

When you're guessing, pay close attention to the drawing and type your answers as quickly as possible. The first player to get it right scores the most points for that round. A game room can have between 2 and 20 players. If a player is being disruptive, you can vote to kick them out to keep the game fun and fair.
