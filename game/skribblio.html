Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Scribble, scribble, scribble! ✍️ Skribblio is a fantastic online game where your drawing and guessing skills are put to the test. In each round, one player is given a word to draw, while the others race to guess what it is. The faster you guess correctly, and the more people guess your drawing, the more points you earn!

Join or create a room with 2 to 20 players. Skribblio is a social experience, so you might see players coming and going. The game is available in 28 languages, including English, German, French, and Italian, so you can play with people from all over the world. To get a game started quickly, invite your friends to join your room!

At the bottom of the screen, you'll find your toolbox with different colors, pen sizes, an eraser, and an undo button. For an enhanced experience, you can even connect a drawing tablet! If you encounter a disruptive player, you can vote to kick them from the room, ensuring a fun and fair game for everyone.
