Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Skribblio is an exciting multiplayer game where you test your drawing and guessing abilities. The goal is simple: score the most points by the end of the game! You can join a public room or create a private one to play with friends.

When it's your turn to draw, you'll be given a choice of three words. Pick one and start sketching it for the other players to guess. You have 80 seconds to create your masterpiece! Use the toolbox at the bottom of the screen to change colors, adjust your pen size, or use the eraser to fix mistakes.

When another player is drawing, it's your turn to guess! Watch the drawing take shape on the canvas and type your guesses into the chat box. The faster you guess the correct word, the more points you'll earn. Pay attention to the hints that appear at the top of the screen!

The player with the most points at the end of all rounds wins. Have fun, be creative, and see if you can top the leaderboard!
