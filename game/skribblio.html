Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Skribblio is an engaging online drawing and guessing game where creativity meets quick thinking.

The game unfolds in rounds: when it's your turn to draw, you'll be given a word to illustrate. Use the on-screen toolbox, complete with various colors, brush sizes, an eraser, and an undo button, to bring your drawing to life. The faster other players guess your word, the more points you earn.

When it's not your turn to draw, you become a guesser. Watch as other players draw and type your guesses into the chat. The first person to correctly guess the word, and the player who drew it, both earn points. Be quick with your guesses to maximize your score!

Skribblio rooms can accommodate 2 to 20 players. You can choose from 28 different languages, though English is the most common. If you create a room, you have the power to kick players. Otherwise, you can vote to kick players if their behavior is disruptive. Skribblio is a fun and interactive free browser game perfect for word game enthusiasts and aspiring artists alike.
