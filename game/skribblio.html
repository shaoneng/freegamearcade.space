Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Skribblio is a creative multiplayer drawing and guessing game, much like the classic Pictionary. When it's your turn, you'll choose one of three words and have 80 seconds to draw it on the canvas. The other players will try to guess the word by typing their answers in the chat. The faster they guess correctly, the more points they get, and you get points for every correct guess, too!

When it's not your turn to draw, put on your thinking cap! Watch the drawing unfold and type your guesses into the chat as quickly as possible. The player with the most points at the end of all rounds wins the game. You can create a private room to play with friends or join a public game with players from all over the world.

Use the toolbox at the bottom of the canvas to change your pen's color and size. Made a mistake? Don't worry, there's an eraser and an undo button to help you fix your masterpiece. If you encounter a player who isn't playing fair, you can vote to kick them from the room to keep the game fun for everyone.
