Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Skribblio is a fun and frantic online multiplayer game where you test your drawing and guessing skills. In each round, one player is chosen to draw a secret word, while the others race to guess what it is. Type your guesses into the chat â€“ the faster you guess correctly, the more points you'll score!

When it's your turn to draw, you'll have 80 seconds to bring the given word to life on the canvas. Use the toolbox at the bottom of the screen to select colors, change your brush size, use the eraser, or undo a mistake. Be creative and clear to help others guess your drawing quickly!

Join public rooms or create a private room to play with friends. A room can host between 2 and 20 players. If you encounter a player who isn't playing fair, you can vote to kick them from the game. If you created the room, you have the power to kick anyone without a vote.

Want to use a drawing tablet? Simply connect it to your computer and configure it to control your cursor. This can give you a major advantage in creating your masterpieces! The game supports over 25 languages, so you can play with people from all over the world.
