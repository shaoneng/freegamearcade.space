Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Skribblio is an exciting multiplayer drawing and guessing game. The rules are simple: when it's your turn, you choose a word from a few options and try to draw it. The other players in the room will try to guess what your drawing is by typing their answers in the chat. The goal is to be as fast as possible, whether you're drawing or guessing, to score the most points.

Each game consists of a few rounds, with every player getting a turn to draw. A turn lasts for 80 seconds. As the drawer, your points are based on how many players guess your word. As a guesser, you get more points for guessing the word faster than everyone else. Rooms can have between 2 and 20 players, and you can either join a public game or create a private room to invite your friends.

At the bottom of the canvas, you'll find a simple toolbox with different colors, pen sizes, an eraser, and an undo button to help you create your masterpiece. If you encounter a player who isn't playing fairly, you can click on their name to vote to kick them from the room. It's a fun, Pictionary-style experience you can enjoy right in your browser!
