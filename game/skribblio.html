Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Welcome to Skribblio, the addictive online game of drawing and guessing! The rules are simple: when it's your turn, the game will give you a secret word. Your mission is to draw that word for the other players in the room to guess. The faster they guess correctly, the more points everyone gets!

After your 80-second turn is up, it's your chance to be the guesser. Pay close attention to the drawing on the canvas and type your guesses into the chat box. The quicker you guess the correct word, the higher you'll climb on the scoreboard.

Use the handy toolbox at the bottom of the screen to bring your creations to life. You can change your pen's color and size, use an eraser for mistakes, or hit the undo button. For even more precision, you can connect a drawing tablet to your computer.

To start a game, simply enter your name, choose a language, and join a public room or create a private one to invite your friends. A game room can hold between 2 and 20 players. Have fun and let your creativity shine!
