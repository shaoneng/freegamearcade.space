Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Skribbl.io is a fun and creative multiplayer game where you test your drawing and guessing skills. The concept is simple: when it's your turn, you are given a word to draw, and the other players have to guess what it is. The faster you guess correctly, the more points you get!

Each game room can host between 2 and 20 players. Don't be surprised if you see players joining or leaving mid-game; the fun continues regardless. To start playing, simply choose your language and enter a room. For less common languages, you might have a short wait, or you can invite friends to join you for an instant game.

At the bottom of your screen, you'll find a toolbox with everything you need to create your masterpiece. You can change your pen's color and size, use an eraser for mistakes, or hit the undo button. Want to use a drawing tablet? Just connect it to your computer, set it up to control your cursor, and you're ready to show off your artistic talent.

Skribbl.io maintains a fair-play environment with a democratic voting system. If a player is disruptive, you can vote to kick them from the room. If you are the one who created the private room, you have the power to kick players without a vote. Please note there is no spectator mode, but don't be shy! Each turn is only 80 seconds long, giving you a quick and fun chance to participate.

Inspired by the classic board game Pictionary, Skribbl.io brings the beloved drawing and guessing experience to your browser. If you enjoy this game, you might also like other creative challenges in our Drawing Games collection!
