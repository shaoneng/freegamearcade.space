Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Skribblio is a fun and fast-paced multiplayer drawing and guessing game. The objective is simple: when it's your turn, you'll be given a word to draw. Do your best to illustrate it while the other players race to guess what it is. The faster you guess correctly, and the more people who guess your drawing, the more points you'll earn!

To start, simply enter your name and join a game, or create a private room to play with friends. Each game consists of a few rounds where every player gets a chance to draw. A round for drawing lasts 80 seconds, so you'll need to be quick with both your pen and your wit.

Use the toolbox at the bottom of the canvas to select different colors, change your brush size, or use the eraser to fix mistakes. If you're using a drawing tablet, you can connect it to your computer for a more precise and artistic experience.

Skribblio is all about having fun in a friendly environment. If you encounter a player who isn't playing fair, you can click on their name to vote to kick them from the room. A majority vote will remove them, ensuring the game stays enjoyable for everyone.
