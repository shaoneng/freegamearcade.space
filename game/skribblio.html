Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Skribblio is a fun and competitive online drawing and guessing game. The objective is simple: when it's your turn, you are given a word to draw. Do your best to illustrate it so the other players in the room can guess what it is. When it's someone else's turn, you need to be the fastest to type the correct word into the chat to score the most points.

You score points for both guessing correctly and for having others guess your drawing. The quicker the guess, the higher the points awarded. Each drawing round lasts for 80 seconds, so you need to be both a fast artist and a quick thinker to climb the leaderboard.

Join or create a room to get started. A room can host between 2 and 20 players. The game uses a simple toolbox at the bottom of the screen, allowing you to change your pen color and size, use an eraser, or clear your canvas. You can even connect a drawing tablet for more precise control!

Skribblio fosters a fair play environment. If a player is disruptive, you can vote to kick them from the room. If you are the room creator, you have the power to kick players without a vote. Choose your language, invite some friends, and get ready to scribble!
