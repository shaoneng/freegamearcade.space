Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Welcome to the hilarious world of Skribblio, the ultimate online drawing and guessing game! The rules are simple: when it's your turn, you'll be given a word to draw. Do your best to illustrate it for the other players in the room. When other players are drawing, it's your turn to guess! Type your guess into the chat box. The faster you guess the correct word, the more points you'll earn.

Each round is a race against the clock. As the artist, you have 80 seconds to bring your word to life using the simple drawing tools at the bottom of the screen. You can change colors, adjust your brush size, use an eraser, or clear the canvas entirely. As a guesser, keep your eyes on the drawing and the hints that appear at the top of the screen to figure out the word before anyone else.

You can create a private room to play with friends or join a public lobby with players from all over the world. Skribblio supports numerous languages, so you can test your skills with an international audience. The player with the most points at the end of all the rounds is crowned the winner!
