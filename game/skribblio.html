Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

The objective in Skribblio is simple and fun: draw the word you are given, and have other players guess it as quickly as possible. When it's not your turn to draw, you'll be on the other side, trying to guess what other players are scribbling. The faster you draw clearly and the faster you guess correctly, the more points you'll earn. The player with the most points at the end of all rounds wins!

All the tools you need are located at the bottom of the drawing canvas. You can select different colors, change your pen size, use an eraser for mistakes, or clear the entire canvas with the trash can icon. These simple controls make it easy for anyone to jump in and start drawing.

You can play in a public room or create a private room to play with your friends. A game room supports between 2 and 20 players. Skribblio is available in 28 languages, which you can select before joining a game. If you create a private room, simply share the link with your friends to invite them for a private match.

Skribblio features a community-driven voting system. If a player is not following the rules or is being disruptive, you can vote to kick them from the room. If a majority agrees, the player is removed, keeping the game fun and fair for everyone.
