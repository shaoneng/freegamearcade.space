Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Skribblio is a fun and competitive online drawing and guessing game. The rules are simple: when it's your turn, you'll be given a word to draw. Your goal is to illustrate that word as clearly as you can within the time limit. The other players in the room will try to guess the word by typing their answers in the chat.

When it's not your turn to draw, you'll be one of the guessers! Watch the canvas closely and type what you think the drawing represents. The faster you guess the correct word, the more points you'll earn. The artist also gets points based on how many people guess their drawing correctly.

Use the toolbox at the bottom of the drawing canvas to change colors, adjust your pen size, or use the eraser to fix mistakes. At the end of several rounds, the player with the highest score is crowned the winner! You can play with friends in a private room or join a public game with players from all over the world.
