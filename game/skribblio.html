Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Skribblio is a fun and frantic online drawing and guessing game, much like a digital version of Pictionary. The goal is simple: when it's your turn, you'll be given a word to draw. Do your best to illustrate it while other players in the room try to guess what it is. When it's someone else's turn to draw, you'll be the one guessing!

To play, simply enter a nickname and join a public game, or create a private room to play with friends. When it's your turn to draw, a selection of words will appear; choose one and start sketching. Use the toolbox at the bottom of the screen to change colors, adjust your pen size, or use the eraser to fix mistakes. When you are guessing, type your answers into the chat box. The faster you guess correctly, the more points you'll earn.

The game supports between 2 and 20 players per room and is available in 28 different languages. If you encounter a disruptive player, you can vote to kick them from the room, ensuring a fun experience for everyone. Get ready to put your drawing and deduction skills to the test!
