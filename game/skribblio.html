Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Welcome to Skribblio, the ultimate online drawing and guessing game! The rules are simple: when it's your turn, you'll be given a choice of three words. Pick one and do your best to draw it on the canvas for the other players to see.

When someone else is drawing, it's your turn to guess! Type your guesses into the chat box. The faster you guess the correct word, the more points you'll earn. The artist also earns points based on how many people guess their drawing correctly.

Each game consists of a few rounds, where every player gets a chance to draw. Use the toolbox at the bottom of the screen to change your pen's color and size, or to erase any mistakes. The player with the most points at the end of the game is crowned the winner!

You can create a private room to play with your friends or join a public game with players from all over the world. Just enter your name, choose a language, and you're ready to start scribbling!
