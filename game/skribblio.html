Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Skribblio is a creative and engaging online game that tests your drawing and guessing abilities. The core gameplay is simple: join a game room, and when it's your turn, you are given a word that you must draw for the other players. Your goal is to illustrate the word clearly enough for others to guess it.

When it's not your turn to draw, you'll be guessing! Watch the scribbles unfold and type your answers into the chat box. Speed is crucial in Skribblio. The faster you guess a word correctly, and the more people who guess your drawing, the more points you earn. The player with the most points at the end of the game wins!

You have a simple toolbox at the bottom of the screen to help you draw, including various colors, different brush sizes, an eraser, and an undo button. For a more precise drawing experience, you can even connect a drawing tablet to your computer.

Game rooms can host between 2 and 20 players. The game maintains a fair environment; if a player is disruptive, others can vote to kick them out of the room. If you create the room, you have the power to kick anyone without a vote. Choose from over 28 languages before you play and invite your friends for an instant game session!
