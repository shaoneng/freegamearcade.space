Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Skribblio is a fun and interactive online drawing and guessing game. The objective is simple: when it's your turn, you are given a word to draw. Do your best to illustrate it while other players try to guess what it is in the chat. When it's another player's turn to draw, you must guess their word as quickly as you can. The faster you or others guess correctly, the more points everyone earns!

To start, simply enter your name, choose your language, and join a public room or create a private one to invite your friends. A game room can hold between 2 and 20 players. The game is played in rounds, giving everyone a chance to draw and show off their artistic (or not-so-artistic) skills.

Use your mouse or a drawing tablet to sketch. At the bottom of the drawing canvas, you'll find a toolbox with different colors, pen sizes, an eraser, and a button to clear your canvas. Don't worry if you make a mistake; you have 80 seconds per turn to get your masterpiece just right.

The game has a voting system to maintain a fun environment. If a player is disruptive, you can click on their name to vote to kick them from the room. A majority vote is required to remove the player, ensuring a fair and enjoyable experience for everyone.
