Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Welcome to Skribblio, the addictive online drawing and guessing game! The rules are simple: when it's your turn, choose one of three words and do your best to draw it in 80 seconds. The other players in the room will try to guess your drawing by typing their answers into the chat.

When it's someone else's turn to draw, it's your time to guess! Pay close attention to the drawing and type what you think it is. The faster you guess the correct word, the more points you'll earn. The player with the most points at the end of all rounds wins the game!

Each game room can host between 2 and 20 players. Use the toolbox at the bottom of the canvas to change your pen's color and size, or use the eraser and undo buttons to fix any mistakes. Get creative and have fun bringing your words to life!
