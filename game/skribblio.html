Skribblio

How to Play Skribblio

Skribblio is a creative and engaging online game that combines drawing with word-guessing. The main objective is to draw a given word while other players try to guess it. When it's not your turn to draw, you'll be guessing what others are scribbling!

You score points by guessing the word correctly and by having others guess your drawing. The faster you are at both drawing clearly and guessing accurately, the more points you will accumulate. The game is played in rounds, with each player getting an 80-second turn to draw.

Game rooms can host between 2 and 20 players. Don't be surprised if players join or leave in the middle of a game. To play, you can use your mouse or even connect a drawing tablet for more precision. The drawing toolbox at the bottom of the screen provides options for pen color, size, an eraser, and an undo button.

While Skribblio is most popular in English, it supports 28 languages, including German, French, and Japanese. You can select your preferred language before joining a room. If you choose a less common language, you might have a short wait for others to join, or you can invite friends to play with you.

The game features a democratic moderation system. If a player is disruptive, you can vote to kick them from the room. If you are the creator of the room, you have the power to kick anyone without a vote. Inspired by the classic board game Pictionary, Skribblio brings the fun of drawing and guessing to your browser.
