Skribblio

About Skribblio & How to Play

Scribble, scribble, scribble! ✍️ Skribblio is a great online game for word game lovers with extensive drawing skills. The premise is simple: draw the word you are given, and let the other participants guess it as fast as possible to score points. When it's not your turn to draw, put on your thinking cap and guess what others are scribbling. The faster you are at both drawing and guessing, the more points you'll accumulate.

A Skribblio room can host a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 20 players. Players can join and leave rooms dynamically while a game is in progress. To get a game started quickly, invite your friends to play together!

Game Features & Tips

  • Controls: The toolbox at the bottom of the screen contains your pen, color palette, eraser, and an undo button for any drawing mishaps.
  • Tablet Support: Yes, you can use a drawing tablet! Simply connect it to your computer and configure it to control your cursor to bring your artistic talents to the game.
  • Languages: Play in your native tongue! Skribblio is available in 28 languages, including German, French, Italian, and Japanese. Choose your language before entering a room.
  • Moderation: Encounter a disruptive player? You can vote to kick them from the room. If you created the room, you have the power to kick anyone without a vote.
  • No Spectator Mode: Currently, there is no spectator mode. Don't be shy about participating—each drawing turn is only 80 seconds long. It becomes more fun and less intimidating after a couple of rounds!

Skribblio, created by ticedev, is heavily inspired by the classic board game Pictionary and is often considered its online successor. If you enjoy drawing games, you might also like to test your skills in other titles from our fantastic Drawing Games collection, all free to play in your browser.
