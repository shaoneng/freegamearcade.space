Slope Rolling

How to Play Slope Rolling

Your goal in Slope Rolling is simple: guide your ball down an endless, twisting slope for as long as possible. The further you go, the faster you'll travel, and the more challenging the course becomes!

Controls: Use the Left and Right arrow keys or the 'A' and 'D' keys to steer your ball. Your movements need to be precise to navigate the sharp turns and dodge the obstacles that appear in your path.

Gameplay: Stay focused on the track ahead to anticipate sudden drops, moving blocks, and narrow gaps. As you roll, be sure to collect the diamonds scattered along the way. These diamonds are your currency to unlock stylish new ball skins and helpful power-ups.

Power-ups: Once unlocked, you can activate special abilities like invincibility or a diamond magnet using the 'J', 'K', and 'L' keys. Use them strategically to extend your run and achieve a new high score!
