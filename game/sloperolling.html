Slope Rolling

How to Play Slope Rolling

The goal in Slope Rolling is to guide your ball down an endless, winding slope for as long as possible. The game gets progressively faster, and obstacles will appear more frequently, testing your reflexes and precision.

Use the Left and Right arrow keys or the 'A' and 'D' keys to steer your ball. Make steady, controlled movements, especially at high speeds, to avoid flying off the track. Try to stay near the center of the path and look ahead to anticipate upcoming turns and obstacles.

As you roll, be sure to collect the diamonds scattered on the slope. You can use these diamonds to unlock new skins for your ball. You can also grab special power-ups like invincibility, a diamond magnet, and a score booster. Use the 'J', 'K', and 'L' keys to activate these power-ups strategically to extend your run and achieve a new high score!
