Slope Rolling

How to Play Slope Rolling

Welcome to the fast-paced challenge of Slope Rolling! Your objective is simple: guide your ball down an endless, twisting slope and survive for as long as possible. The further you travel, the faster you'll go, putting your reflexes to the ultimate test.

Use the Left and Right arrow keys (or the A and D keys) to steer your ball. Your main goal is to stay on the track, dodge the red obstacles that appear, and avoid falling into the gaps. Try to stay centered on the path to give yourself the best chance to react to sudden turns.

As you roll, be sure to collect the diamonds scattered along the way. These can be used in the shop to unlock cool new ball skins and purchase powerful upgrades. You can also use special power-ups during your run with the J, K, and L keys to help you survive longer and achieve a new high score. Keep rolling and have fun!
