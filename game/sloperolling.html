Slope Rolling

How to Play Slope Rolling

Welcome to the thrilling challenge of Slope Rolling! The controls are simple to learn but difficult to master. Use the Left and Right arrow keys or the 'A' and 'D' keys on your keyboard to steer your ball left and right.

Your main objective is to guide the rolling ball down a never-ending, twisting track for as long as possible. The game starts at a manageable pace, but don't get too comfortable! The further you roll, the faster your ball will travel, and obstacles like red blocks and dangerous gaps will appear more frequently. Precision and foresight are key to survival.

As you navigate the slope, be sure to collect the diamonds scattered along the path. You can use these diamonds to unlock awesome new ball skins and purchase powerful items. Activate special power-ups like invincibility, magnet, and double income with the 'J', 'K', and 'L' keys to help you survive longer and set a new high score!
