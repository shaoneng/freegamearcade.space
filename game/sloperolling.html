Slope Rolling

How to Play Slope Rolling

In Slope Rolling, your goal is to guide a rolling ball down an endless, twisting track for as long as possible. The game starts at a manageable pace, allowing you to get comfortable with the controls, but be warned: the further you roll, the faster you go, and the more challenging the obstacles become!

Use the Left and Right arrow keys or the A and D keys to steer your ball. Your reflexes will be tested as you dodge blocks, leap across gaps, and navigate sharp turns. Try to stay centered on the slope to react quickly to sudden changes, and always look ahead to anticipate upcoming obstacles.

As you roll, make sure to collect the sparkling diamonds scattered along the path. These precious gems can be used to unlock exciting new ball skins and acquire powerful special power-ups like invincibility, a magnet to attract more diamonds, and double income boosts. Activate these power-ups wisely using the J, K, and L keys to extend your run and achieve an unbeatable high score!
