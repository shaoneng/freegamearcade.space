Slope Rolling

How to Play Slope Rolling

Your objective in Slope Rolling is to guide your ball down an endless, twisting track for as long as you can. Use the Left and Right arrow keys or the A and D keys on your keyboard to steer the ball. The controls are simple, but the challenge increases as you pick up speed.

As you progress, the track will become more difficult with sudden drops, sharp turns, and various obstacles blocking your path. To survive, you must react quickly. Try to keep your ball centered on the track to give yourself more time to react and look ahead to anticipate upcoming challenges. Avoid making sudden, sharp turns at high speeds to maintain control.

While rolling down the slope, be sure to collect the diamonds scattered on the path. You can use these diamonds in the game's shop to unlock new ball skins and purchase helpful power-ups like invincibility or a diamond magnet. Activate your power-ups with the J, K, and L keys to extend your run and achieve a new high score!
