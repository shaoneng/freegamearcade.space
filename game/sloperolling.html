Slope Rolling

How to Play Slope Rolling

Your objective in Slope Rolling is to guide your ball down a twisting, endless slope for as long as you can. Use the Left and Right arrow keys or the A and D keys to steer your ball and avoid falling off the edges.

As you progress, the ball will pick up speed, making the challenge more intense. You'll need to react quickly to dodge obstacles, navigate sharp turns, and jump over gaps in the track. Staying near the center of the path can help you react more effectively to sudden changes.

Keep an eye out for diamonds scattered along the slope. Collecting these allows you to unlock new skins for your ball and purchase special power-ups. You can activate these power-ups (using the J, K, and L keys) to gain advantages like invincibility or a diamond magnet, helping you to extend your run and achieve a new high score.
