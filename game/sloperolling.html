Slope Rolling

How to Play Slope Rolling

Your goal in Slope Rolling is to guide a ball down an endless, twisting track for as long as you possibly can. Use the Left and Right arrow keys or the A and D keys to steer your ball. Be precise with your movements to stay on the path and avoid falling off the edge into the abyss.

The game begins at a gentle pace to help you get accustomed to the controls, but the challenge quickly ramps up. As you travel further, your ball will accelerate, and obstacles like blocks and gaps will appear more frequently. You'll need sharp reflexes to navigate the sudden drops and tight turns. A good strategy is to try and keep your ball centered on the track, giving you more time to react to what's ahead.

As you race down the slope, collect the diamonds scattered along your path. These diamonds can be used in the shop to unlock a variety of cool new ball skins and powerful upgrades. You can also activate special power-ups like invincibility and a diamond magnet by pressing the J, K, and L keys. Use them wisely to extend your run and set a new high score!
