Slope Rolling

How to Play Slope Rolling

Get ready for a high-speed challenge in Slope Rolling! Your main objective is to control a rolling ball down an endless, twisting slope, surviving for as long as possible. The further you roll, the faster the ball gets, making it a true test of your reflexes.

Use the Left and Right arrow keys (or the A and D keys) to steer your ball. Your goal is to dodge a variety of obstacles, leap across treacherous gaps, and navigate sudden sharp turns. For the best control, try to stay near the center of the track and always look ahead to anticipate what's coming next.

As you speed down the slope, be sure to collect the diamonds scattered on the path. These diamonds are your key to unlocking awesome new ball skins and buying powerful upgrades like invincibility or a magnet to attract more diamonds. If you have a power-up ready, use the J, K, or L keys to activate it at the perfect moment to extend your run and rack up a massive high score!
