Slope Rolling

How to Play Slope Rolling

Your goal in Slope Rolling is to guide your ball down a challenging, never-ending slope for as long as you can. Use the Left and Right arrow keys (or the A and D keys on your keyboard) to steer your ball and avoid falling off the edges.

The track is filled with obstacles like blocks and sudden gaps that you must skillfully dodge. As you progress, the ball will pick up speed, making the challenge even more intense. Try to stay near the center of the track and look ahead to anticipate upcoming turns and obstacles, giving you more time to react.

While you're rolling, be sure to collect the diamonds scattered on the path. These diamonds can be used to unlock new skins for your ball and special power-ups. When you have a power-up like invincibility, a magnet, or double income, you can activate it using the J, K, or L keys to help extend your run and boost your score. Keep rolling and set a new high score!
