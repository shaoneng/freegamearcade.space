Slope Rolling

How to Play Slope Rolling

Your goal in Slope Rolling is simple yet challenging: guide your ball down a procedurally generated slope for as long as possible. The track is full of twists, turns, gaps, and obstacles. The further you roll, the faster your ball will move, putting your reflexes to the ultimate test.

Use the Left and Right arrow keys or the A and D keys on your keyboard to steer your ball. Make precise movements to stay on the path and dodge the red blocks that appear in your way. Avoid sudden, sharp turns at high speeds to maintain control and try to anticipate upcoming obstacles by looking ahead.

As you race down the slope, be sure to collect the diamonds scattered along the track. These diamonds are your currency to unlock new ball skins and purchase helpful power-ups. You can activate special abilities like invincibility, a diamond magnet, and a score multiplier using the J, K, and L keys. Use them strategically to extend your run and set a new high score!
