Slope Rolling

How to Play Slope Rolling

Your goal in Slope Rolling is to guide your ball down a perilous, never-ending track for as long as possible. The challenge ramps up as you progressâ€”the ball accelerates, and obstacles like blocks and gaps become more frequent. Test your reflexes and see how high you can score!

Master the simple controls to navigate the treacherous path. Use the Left and Right arrow keys or the A and D keys on your keyboard to steer your ball. Precise, steady movements are key, especially as you pick up speed. Try to anticipate upcoming turns and avoid sharp, sudden movements to maintain control.

As you race down the slope, be sure to collect the diamonds scattered along the way. These diamonds can be used to unlock awesome new ball skins and purchase special power-ups. Activate game-changing abilities like invincibility, a diamond magnet, or double income using the J, K, and L keys to extend your run and shatter your previous high scores.
