Slow Roads

How to Play Slow Roads

Slow Roads is not about winning races; it's about enjoying a peaceful and endless drive. The goal is to relax, soak in the procedurally generated scenery, and create your perfect road trip experience. You automatically drive forward, so your main focus is on steering and enjoying the view.

Use the on-screen interface to tailor the world to your mood. You can switch between different environments, including Earth, Mars, Venus, and the Moon. On Earth, you can change the season from a vibrant summer to a serene autumn or a stark winter. Each setting offers a completely different visual and atmospheric experience.

Further customize your drive by adjusting the complexity of the road, from a simple straight path to a challenging, winding route through gorgeous landscapes. You can also control the time of day, switching between a sunny afternoon and a starry night manually, or let it cycle automatically. Don't forget to turn on your headlights when it gets dark! Simply take control and enjoy the blissful journey.
