Slow Roads

How to Play Slow Roads

Welcome to Slow Roads, the ultimate chill driving experience! The goal is simple: drive, relax, and enjoy the scenery. There's no score, no race, and no destination. Just you, your car, and the endless open road. Use your keyboard to accelerate, brake, and steer as you cruise through procedurally generated landscapes.

The real magic of Slow Roads lies in its customization. You can tailor the entire environment to your liking. Start on Earth and cycle through Spring, Summer, Autumn, and Winter. For a truly otherworldly experience, switch your location to the surfaces of the Moon, Mars, or Venus, each with unique gravity and scenery.

You can also control the time of day. Switch between day and night manually, or set it to cycle automatically. Don't forget to turn on your headlights for night driving! For an extra challenge or a more scenic route, adjust the road's complexity from a simple straight path to a winding, challenging course through gorgeous terrain.

Before you hit the road, select from one of the available modern electric vehicles to find the one that best suits your driving style. Just sit back, tune into the chill music, and enjoy your peaceful journey.
