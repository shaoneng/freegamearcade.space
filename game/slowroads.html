Slow Roads

How to Play Slow Roads

Slow Roads is a minimalist driving game focused on relaxation and exploration. There are no timers, scores, or objectivesâ€”just you, your vehicle, and an endless, procedurally generated road. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to steer, accelerate, and brake. The goal is simply to enjoy the journey and the serene landscapes that unfold before you.

The real depth of Slow Roads lies in its customization. At any time during your drive, you can access the menu to tailor the experience. Change the environment from Earth to other celestial bodies like the Moon or Mars. Adjust the seasons, toggle between day and night, or set an automatic day/night cycle. You can even control the complexity of the road, from a simple straight path to winding, scenic routes.

Don't forget to explore the different electric vehicles available. When driving at night, remember to turn on your headlights for a more immersive experience. The game is designed to be a meditative escape, so feel free to experiment with all the settings to create your perfect, blissful road trip.
