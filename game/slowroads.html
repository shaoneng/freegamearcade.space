Slow Roads

How to Play Slow Roads

Welcome to Slow Roads, the ultimate zen driving simulator. Your goal is simple: drive, relax, and enjoy the procedurally generated scenery. There are no scores, no timers, and no objectives. Just an endless road for you to explore at your own pace.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to control your electric vehicle. Accelerate, brake, and steer to navigate the winding roads. The driving physics are designed to be smooth and satisfying, perfect for a casual journey.

The real magic of Slow Roads lies in its customization. Use the on-screen interface to change your environment completely. Switch between different seasons on Earth, or take your road trip to other worlds like Mars, Venus, or the Moon. You can also manually set the time of day or let it cycle automatically. Don't forget to adjust the road complexity to create everything from simple, straight paths to complex, scenic routes through the hills!
