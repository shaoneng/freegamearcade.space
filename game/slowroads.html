Slow Roads

How to Play Slow Roads

Slow Roads is less of a game and more of a zen-like experience. There are no objectives, scores, or timers. The goal is simply to drive, relax, and enjoy the procedurally generated scenery. Use your keyboard to steer the vehicle along the endless road, feeling the gentle hum of the engine and the chill music from the radio.

The true beauty of Slow Roads lies in its customization. You can tailor the entire environment to your mood. Change the season from a lush summer to a crisp autumn or a serene winter. You can even leave Earth behind and drive on the surfaces of Mars, Venus, or the Moon, each with unique landscapes and physics.

Further personalize your drive by switching between day and night, choosing from several electric vehicles, and adjusting the complexity of the road. A straight road offers a meditative drive, while a hard, winding road creates more dramatic and gorgeous landscapes. Just remember to turn on your headlights when driving at night!
