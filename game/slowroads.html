Slow Roads

How to Play Slow Roads

Slow Roads is less about winning and more about the journey. The goal is to simply drive and immerse yourself in the procedurally generated landscapes. There are no finish lines or opponents, just an endless road for you to enjoy at your own pace. Use your standard driving controls to accelerate, brake, and steer your vehicle.

The real magic of Slow Roads lies in its customization. Before you start or during your drive, you can access a menu to tailor the world to your liking. Switch between different environments like tranquil Earth seasons or otherworldly landscapes on Mars, Venus, and the Moon. You can also manually set the time of day or let it cycle automatically. Adjust the road complexity from a simple straight path to winding, scenic routes.

Settle in, pick your favorite electric vehicle, and start your drive. Remember to turn on your headlights for night driving to fully appreciate the atmosphere. Put on some music, relax, and enjoy the blissful road trip experience that Slow Roads offers.
