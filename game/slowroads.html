Slow Roads

How to Play Slow Roads

Slow Roads is designed to be a meditative and relaxing driving experience rather than a competitive race. The goal is to simply drive, explore, and soak in the serene, procedurally generated scenery. There's no finish line, just an endless road for you to enjoy at your own pace.

The true beauty of the game lies in its vast customization options. You can tailor the entire environment to match your mood. On Earth, you can switch between all four seasons. For a more sci-fi journey, you can change your location to the surfaces of Mars, Venus, or the Moon, each with its own unique atmosphere and physics.

Further customize your drive by adjusting the time of day, either manually or with an automatic cycle. If you choose to drive at night, remember to turn on your headlights for a more immersive experience. You can also select from different electric vehicles and even alter the complexity of the road, from a simple straight path to a challenging, winding course through stunning landscapes. Just sit back, relax, and enjoy the blissful journey.
