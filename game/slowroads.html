Slow Roads

How to Play Slow Roads

Slow Roads is a zen-like driving experience designed for relaxation, not racing. There are no finish lines or timers; the goal is simply to enjoy the journey. Take a break from reality and immerse yourself in a blissful, endless road trip through procedurally generated landscapes.

Control your electric vehicle using your keyboard. While the driving is straightforward, the real magic lies in the customization. Use the on-screen menu to tailor your experience. You can switch between different seasons on Earth, or take your drive to other worlds like Mars, Venus, and the Moon, each with unique physics and visuals. Adjust the time of day manually or let it cycle automatically, and don't forget to turn on your headlights at night!

Further personalize your trip by selecting from various levels of road complexity, from a simple straight path to a challenging, winding road that creates stunning scenic views. Choose your favorite electric car and find the perfect combination of settings to match your mood for the ultimate chill driving session.
