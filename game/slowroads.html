Slow Roads

How to Play Slow Roads

Welcome to Slow Roads, a game designed for relaxation and exploration. The primary goal is simply to drive and enjoy the serene, procedurally generated scenery. Use your standard driving controls (W/A/S/D or Arrow Keys) to accelerate, brake, and steer your electric vehicle along the endless road.

The true beauty of Slow Roads lies in its customization. Access the settings menu to tailor the experience to your mood. You can change the environment from familiar Earthly hills to the alien landscapes of Mars or the Moon. Adjust the season, switch between day and night, or set a dynamic day/night cycle.

Further refine your drive by altering the road's complexity, from a simple straight path to winding, challenging curves.
