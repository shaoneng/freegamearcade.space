How to Play Slow Roads

Welcome to the zen-like experience of Slow Roads. There's no score, no race, and no finish lineâ€”just you, your car, and an endless, procedurally generated road. The goal is simply to drive and relax.

Use your keyboard to steer, accelerate, and brake. The core of the game lies in its extensive customization options, accessible through the in-game menu. You can tailor your entire driving experience to fit your mood.

Start by choosing your environment. Drive through the four seasons on Earth, or take an otherworldly trip on the surfaces of Mars, Venus, or the Moon. You can also manually set the time of day or let it cycle automatically. Don't forget your headlights at night! Fine-tune the road's complexity, from a simple straight path to winding, challenging routes that create breathtaking landscapes. Finally, select your preferred electric vehicle and hit the open road for a blissful journey.
