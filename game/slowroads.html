Slow Roads

How to Play Slow Roads

Embark on a serene journey in Slow Roads, a game focused on relaxation rather than racing. The primary goal is to simply drive and immerse yourself in the beautiful, procedurally generated landscapes. There is no finish line; the road stretches on endlessly for a truly meditative experience.

Use your keyboard to control the vehicle. The WASD or Arrow Keys handle acceleration, braking, and steering. The experience is designed to be smooth and intuitive, allowing you to focus on the scenery.

The real magic of Slow Roads lies in its deep customization options, which you can adjust at any time:

  • Environment: Choose your home planet from Earth, Mars, Venus, or the Moon. On Earth, you can even switch between seasons to match your mood.
  • Time of Day: Manually set the time from a vibrant sunrise to a starry midnight, or enable an automatic cycle to watch the world change around you.
  • Road Complexity: Select from five levels, ranging from a simple, straight road to a challenging, winding path that generates more complex and gorgeous landscapes.
  • Vehicle: Pick your favorite from a selection of sleek, modern electric vehicles.

Experiment with all the settings to create your perfect, blissful road trip. The focus is on the journey, not the destination!
