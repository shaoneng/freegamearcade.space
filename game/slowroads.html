Slow Roads

How to Play Slow Roads

Welcome to Slow Roads, a driving game focused on relaxation and scenic exploration rather than racing. The goal is simply to drive and enjoy the journey through endlessly generated landscapes.

Use your keyboard to control the vehicle. The game offers extensive customization options directly on the screen. You can change the time of day, switch between seasons (altering the hills and scenery), and even choose your driving location—from a peaceful Earth-like environment to the surfaces of the Moon or Mars.

Adjust the road's complexity, from a simple straight path to a challenging, winding road with gorgeous vistas. There's no finish line, no points, and no pressure. Just a pure, blissful road trip simulation. Turn on your headlights at night, pick a vehicle, and let the open road soothe you.
