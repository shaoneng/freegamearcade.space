Slow Roads

How to Play Slow Roads

Slow Roads offers a tranquil and minimalist driving experience with no objectives other than to enjoy the journey. The core gameplay is simple: drive endlessly along procedurally generated scenic routes. While the controls are intuitive (typically using WASD or arrow keys for steering, accelerating, and braking), the real depth comes from customization.

Before you start, or at any time during your drive, you can tailor the world to your liking. Change the environment from terrestrial hills to the alien landscapes of Mars, Venus, or the Moon. Cycle through different seasons, or switch between a sunny day and a starry night. You can even set an automatic day/night cycle timer for a more immersive experience.

Further customize your drive by selecting from different electric vehicles and adjusting the complexity of the road itself, from simple straightaways to challenging, winding paths that create stunning vistas. The goal is to relax, listen to the calming music, and create your perfect, blissful road trip.
