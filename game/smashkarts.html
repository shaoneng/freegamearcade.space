Smash Karts

How to Play Smash Karts

Get ready for a wild ride in Smash Karts! The goal is simple: smash other players and be the last kart standing. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to steer your go-kart around the vibrant, chaotic battle arenas.

As you race, keep an eye out for floating gift boxes with question marks. Drive through them to pick up a random weapon or power-up. You might get anything from a powerful bazooka and homing missiles to slippery mines or even a devastating nuke!

Once you're armed, take aim at your opponents and fire away to smash them. Each smash earns you points (XP). The player with the most points at the end of the round wins. Stay on the move, dodge incoming fire, and use your weapons strategically to dominate the arena and become the ultimate kart champion!
