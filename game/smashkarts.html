How to Play Smash Karts

Welcome to the action-packed arena of Smash Karts! Your goal is not to finish first, but to survive and become the top smasher. Steer your go-kart through the vibrant, chaotic battlefield using your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD.

Keep an eye out for floating mystery boxes scattered across the map. Drive through them to pick up a random, powerful weaponâ€”from machine guns and missiles to devastating nukes! Once you're armed, take aim at your opponents and let loose to score points and climb the leaderboard.

The more players you eliminate, the more XP you'll earn. Use this XP to unlock new characters and karts. You can also join or create custom rooms to play with friends, choosing different game modes and rules. Stay alert, drive fast, and smash your way to victory!
