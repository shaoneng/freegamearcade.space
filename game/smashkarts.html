Smash Karts

How to Play Smash Karts

Welcome to the wild arenas of Smash Karts! Your goal is simple: smash as many opponents as you can before the time runs out. This isn't about crossing a finish line; it's about pure vehicular combat and becoming the king of the leaderboard.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to steer your kart around the dynamic, colorful maps. Drive with precision to dodge enemy attacks and position yourself for the perfect shot.

As you drive, keep an eye out for mystery gift boxes scattered across the arena. Drive through them to pick up a random weapon, ranging from missiles and guns to powerful nukes. Once you have a weapon, press the Spacebar to unleash it on an unsuspecting rival.

Rack up XP from your matches to level up and unlock new karts, characters, and hats. You can also create custom rooms to play with friends, choosing from different game modes, setting the round duration, and even customizing which weapons are available. Jump in, arm up, and start smashing!
