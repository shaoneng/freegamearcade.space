Smash Karts

How to Play Smash Karts

Welcome to the chaotic world of Smash Karts! This isn't your typical racing game; it's an all-out battle for survival. The main objective is to eliminate as many other players as possible within the time limit. The more karts you smash, the more points and XP you'll earn.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer your kart around the arena. Your kart accelerates automatically, so your focus is purely on maneuvering and combat. To unleash your weapon, press the Spacebar. Aim carefully to take out your rivals before they get you!

Keep a sharp eye out for floating mystery boxes scattered across the map. Drive through these boxes to pick up a random power-up or weapon. Your arsenal can include anything from powerful guns, homing missiles, and sneaky mines to the devastating nuke. Use these items strategically to gain an advantage and climb the leaderboard.

You can also join or create custom multiplayer rooms to tailor the experience. In these rooms, you can choose between different game modes, enable team play, set the round duration, and even customize which weapons are available in the match. Now, get in there and start smashing!
