Smash Karts

How to Play Smash Karts

Welcome to the exhilarating arena of Smash Karts! This isn't your typical racing game; it's an all-out vehicular battle. Your main objective is to eliminate as many other players as possible within the time limit to rack up points and climb the leaderboard.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys to steer your kart around the dynamic, colorful maps. Keep your eyes peeled for floating gift boxes scattered throughout the arena. Drive through these boxes to pick up a random, powerful weapon.

Once you're armed, it's time for mayhem! Aim at your opponents and press the Spacebar to unleash your weapon, whether it's a homing missile, a powerful gun, or a sneaky mine. Each successful smash earns you points. Stay agile, dodge incoming attacks, and keep grabbing new weapons to maintain your edge.

You can also customize your character and kart to stand out in the arena. Jump into a public match or create a custom room with friends to tweak settings like game modes, round duration, and available weapons. Now, get in there and start smashing!
