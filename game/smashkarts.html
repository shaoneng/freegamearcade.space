Smash Karts

How to Play Smash Karts

Jump into a fast-paced arena where the only goal is total kart domination! Use the Arrow Keys or WASD to steer your go-kart around the vibrant, chaotic battlegrounds.

This isn't a race; it's a fight for survival. Your objective is to eliminate as many opponents as possible before the time runs out. To arm yourself, drive through the floating mystery boxes marked with a '?'. These boxes will grant you a random, powerful weapon, ranging from machine guns and rockets to devastating mines and nukes.

Once you've acquired a weapon, press the Space Bar to unleash it on an unsuspecting rival. Aim true to rack up kills, earn XP, and climb to the top of the leaderboard. Stay on the move to avoid incoming fire, and remember that the player with the most smashes at the end of the round is crowned the winner!
