Smash Karts

How to Play Smash Karts

Welcome to the chaotic arenas of Smash Karts! Your goal isn't just to race, but to outlast and out-smash your opponents in a frantic multiplayer battle. Use your driving skills to navigate diverse and colorful maps while hunting down other players.

Control your kart using the standard keyboard controls (WASD or Arrow Keys) to steer, accelerate, and reverse. As you drive, keep an eye out for floating gift boxes scattered across the map. Drive through them to pick up a random weapon, from powerful missiles and machine guns to sneaky mines.

Once armed, aim and fire (using the Spacebar) at your opponents to score points and climb the leaderboard. The more players you eliminate, the more XP you earn, which helps you level up and unlock new karts and characters. Survive the mayhem, dominate the match, and become the king of the karts!
