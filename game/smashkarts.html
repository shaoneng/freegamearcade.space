Smash Karts

How to Play Smash Karts

Get ready for vehicular mayhem in Smash Karts! Your main goal isn't to race, but to survive and dominate. Use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your go-kart around the vibrant 3D battle arenas. The objective is simple: smash other players to earn points and climb the leaderboard before the round timer runs out.

Strategy is key to victory. As you drive, look for floating gift boxes scattered across the map. Driving through these will grant you a random power-up or weapon, from powerful missiles and machine guns to defensive shields and speed boosts. Aim carefully and use your items to eliminate opponents, but watch your back—everyone else is armed and dangerous too!

The more you play and the more opponents you defeat, the more XP you'll earn. This experience allows you to level up, unlocking a variety of new karts and characters to customize your look. You can also jump into custom multiplayer rooms to choose between different game modes, set match duration, and challenge your friends directly.
