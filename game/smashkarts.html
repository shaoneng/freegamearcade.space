Smash Karts

About Smash Karts

Imagine a 3D multiplayer go-kart bonanza where the track isn't just for racing, but for full-on vehicular mayhem! Welcome to Smash Karts, where elegance meets anarchy, and every turn could lead to either a victory parade or a spectacular kart-smashing spectacle.

As you hit the start button, you're zooming through a kaleidoscope of maps, from candyland nightmares to neon-lit cityscapes. But it's not just about who crosses the finish line first; it's about who remains un-smashed! You'll encounter a barrage of weaponry from your fellow kart jockeys—guns, missiles, mines, and yes, even nukes. Your mission? Stay alive, smash others, and rack up those sweet, sweet XP points. Just keep an eye out for those gift boxes; they're your lifeline for arming up with random weapons.

There are three game modes you can choose in custom multiplayer rooms. Apart from modes you can enable teams, set round duration between 3 to 20 mins, customize weapons and more. Dive into an colorful and chaotic maps, each with its own flair for destruction. These tracks are not just for racing; they're battlefields where every corner hides the potential for either glory or a hilarious wipeout.

Smash Karts was brought to life by TallTeam, who unleashed this game on March 25, 2020. So, strap in, rev up, and prepare for a no-holds-barred battle where the only rule is there are no rules. Welcome to Smash Karts, where every race is a riot!
