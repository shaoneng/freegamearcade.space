Snow Rider 3D

How to Play Snow Rider 3D

Welcome to the winter wonderland of Snow Rider 3D! The goal is simple: slide down the snowy mountain for as long as you can, avoiding all the obstacles in your path. To start your thrilling ride, simply click or tap on the sleigh.

Use the A/D keys or the Left/Right arrow keys to steer your sled from side to side. You'll need to be quick to dodge stationary obstacles and moving snowballs. When you see a low obstacle like a wooden block or a small snowdrift, press the W key or the Up arrow key to jump over it. Every obstacle you successfully pass adds to your score!

As you descend the mountain, keep an eye out for colorful gift boxes. Collect these gifts to save up and unlock a wide variety of new sleds in the shop, from simple plastic ones to Santa's legendary sleigh. While these new sleds look great, they are purely cosmetic and don't change the gameplay, so you can focus on mastering your skills!
