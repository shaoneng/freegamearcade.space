Snow Rider 3D

How to Play Snow Rider 3D

Welcome to the frosty slopes of Snow Rider 3D! Your goal is simple: survive as long as possible by guiding your sleigh down a treacherous, snow-covered mountain. The further you travel, the higher your score climbs.

Use your mouse or the left and right arrow keys to steer your sleigh and deftly avoid the many obstacles in your path. You'll encounter everything from static trees and rocks to moving snowballs that will test your reflexes.

Some barriers will require you to jump. Press the up arrow key or the spacebar to leap over low-lying obstacles and continue your thrilling descent.

While you're dodging hazards, don't forget to collect the gift boxes scattered across the course. These gifts are your currency to unlock an awesome collection of new sleds from the in-game shop, each with a unique look. The ultimate prize is Santa's Sleigh, but you'll need to be a skilled rider to collect enough gifts to afford it!
