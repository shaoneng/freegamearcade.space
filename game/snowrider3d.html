Snow Rider 3D

How to Play Snow Rider 3D

Welcome to the fast-paced slopes of Snow Rider 3D! Your objective is simple: guide your sleigh down an endless, obstacle-filled mountain and survive for as long as you can. Your score increases for every obstacle you successfully dodge.

Controls: Use the Left and Right arrow keys to steer your sleigh and navigate through the treacherous course. When you see a low-lying obstacle or a ramp, press the Up arrow key to jump over it. The controls are intuitive, but the challenge comes from the high speed and the increasing number of hazards.

Collectibles & Unlocks: While dodging snowballs and logs, be sure to collect the colorful gift boxes scattered along the way. These gifts are your currency to unlock a wide variety of awesome new sleds in the shop, including Santa's famous sleigh! While all sleds perform the same, you can pick your favorite design to slide in style.
