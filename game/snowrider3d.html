Snow Rider 3D

How to Play Snow Rider 3D

The objective in Snow Rider 3D is to slide down a snowy mountain for as long as possible, avoiding obstacles to achieve the highest score. Every obstacle you successfully dodge adds to your score, displayed on the left side of the screen.

Use the left and right arrow keys (or A and D keys) on your keyboard to steer your sled. This will help you navigate around dangers like snow masses, trees, and wooden posts that block your path.

When you see an obstacle that you can't steer around, press the up arrow key (or W key) to jump over it. Timing your jumps correctly is crucial for survival and extending your run!

As you slide, keep an eye out for gift boxes scattered on the path. Collect these gifts to unlock a wide array of new and exciting sleds in the game's shop, including the special Santa Sleigh!
