Snow Rider 3D

How to Play Snow Rider 3D

Your objective in Snow Rider 3D is to slide down a snowy mountain for as long as possible, avoiding obstacles and setting a new high score. The game is endless, so the challenge is to see how far your reflexes can take you!

Use your mouse or arrow keys (left and right) to steer your sled. You'll encounter stationary and moving obstacles, such as snowballs and wooden blocks. You must swerve to dodge them. Some obstacles require you to jump over them by pressing the up arrow key or spacebar. A single collision ends your run, so stay alert!

As you speed down the mountain, be sure to collect the colorful gift boxes scattered along the path. These gifts are your currency. Accumulate enough, and you can visit the in-game shop to unlock a wide variety of new sleds, including the legendary Santa Sleigh. While all sleds perform the same, they offer cool new designs to customize your ride.
