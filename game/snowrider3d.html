Snow Rider 3D

How to Play Snow Rider 3D

Your adventure begins on a vast, snow-covered mountain. To start your thrilling descent, simply click or tap on your sleigh. Once you're moving, your main objective is to slide as far as possible while avoiding the many obstacles that stand in your way. Quick reflexes are essential as you navigate the treacherous terrain at high speed.

Control your sleigh using your mouse or arrow keys to steer left and right. You'll encounter stationary obstacles like trees and moving hazards like giant snowballs. You must skillfully weave between them to stay in the game. For certain low obstacles, you'll need to jump at the right moment to clear them safely. Hitting any obstacle will end your run, so stay alert!

As you successfully dodge obstacles, your score will increase, displayed on the left side of the screen. Keep an eye out for colorful gift boxes scattered along the path. Collecting these gifts allows you to purchase new and exciting sleighs from the in-game shop, including Santa's famous ride! While the sleighs are visually different, they all handle the same, so you can pick your favorite style without affecting gameplay.
