Snow Rider 3D

How to Play Snow Rider 3D

Your objective in Snow Rider 3D is to guide your sled down a treacherous, snow-covered mountain for as long as possible. The further you go, the higher your score will be. The game is an endless runner, so the challenge is to survive and rack up points!

Getting started is simple: just click or tap on the sleigh to begin your descent. Use your arrow keys (or swipe on mobile) to steer left and right. You'll need quick reflexes to dodge a variety of obstacles, including static wooden barriers and moving snow masses. Timing is key, as you'll also need to jump over certain objects to avoid crashing.

While you're sliding and dodging, be sure to collect the colorful gift boxes scattered along the path. These gifts are your currency. Accumulate enough of them, and you can visit the in-game shop to unlock a wide variety of new and stylish sleds to customize your ride!
