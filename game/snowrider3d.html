Snow Rider 3D

How to Play Snow Rider 3D

The objective in Snow Rider 3D is to slide down an endless snowy mountain for as long as possible, avoiding obstacles to achieve the highest score. Your reflexes are your greatest asset in this fast-paced winter challenge!

Use the left and right arrow keys (or the A and D keys) to steer your sled. You'll need to make quick maneuvers to dodge trees, giant snowballs, wooden logs, and other hazards that appear in your path. Hitting any obstacle will end your run.

As you slide, keep an eye out for gift boxes scattered on the slope. Collecting these gifts is key to unlocking new sleds from the in-game shop. While all sleds perform the same, you can ride in style with cool designs, including Santa's famous sleigh which costs 200 gifts!
