Snow Rider 3D

How to Play Snow Rider 3D

Welcome to the snowy slopes of Snow Rider 3D! Your goal is to guide your sled down a treacherous mountain, avoiding countless obstacles to set a new high score. The controls are simple: use your arrow keys or mouse to steer left and right to dodge hazards.

As you hurtle down the mountain, you'll encounter a variety of challenges. You'll need to weave between massive snowballs, jump over wooden barriers, and navigate through tricky moving obstacles. Each obstacle you successfully pass adds to your score, displayed on the left of the screen.

Don't forget to collect the colorful gift boxes scattered along your path! These gifts are your currency to unlock a variety of awesome new sleds in the game's shop. While all sleds perform the same, you can ride in style with everything from a simple plastic slider to Santa's very own sleigh!
