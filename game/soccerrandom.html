Soccer Random

How to Play Soccer Random

Welcome to the unpredictable world of Soccer Random! The goal is simple: be the first player to score 5 goals. But how you get there is anything but ordinary. This is a physics-based game where your players jump and flip with a life of their own.

You control your entire team with just a single button. Press the button to make your players jump and kick. Timing is everything! Use the chaotic physics to your advantage to send the ball flying into your opponent's net. You can play against the computer in 1-player mode or challenge a friend in 2-player mode on the same device.

The real twist comes after every goal. The game will randomly change the playing field, the weather conditions, and even the type of ball you use! Adapt to icy fields, beach soccer, and different ball physics to stay ahead. The first to reach 5 goals wins the match!

Controls:

  • Player 1: Press the 'W' key.
  • Player 2: Press the 'UP ARROW' key.
  • Mobile: Tap the screen on your respective side to play.
