Soccer Random

How to Play Soccer Random

Welcome to the unpredictable pitch of Soccer Random! The goal is simple: be the first player to score 5 goals. However, the gameplay is anything but ordinary.

You control your entire team of ragdoll-like players with just one button. Press the button to make your players jump and kick simultaneously. The key is timing your jumps perfectly to hit the ball and send it flying into your opponent's net. Due to the wacky physics, your players will flip, flop, and flail in hilarious and unexpected ways.

To make things even more interesting, the game changes after every single goal! You might find yourself playing on an icy field, during a windy day, or with a ball that's not quite round. Adapt to the new conditions and keep scoring to win! You can play against the computer or challenge a friend in the exciting 2-player mode.
