Soccer Random

How to Play Soccer Random

Welcome to the wacky world of Soccer Random! The goal is simple: be the first player to score 5 goals. However, the gameplay is anything but predictable. You'll control a team of ragdoll-like players who jump and kick based on chaotic physics, leading to hilarious and unexpected moments.

The controls are incredibly easy to learn. Everything is done with just one button! Whether on a computer or mobile device, a single tap or keypress makes your players jump and kick simultaneously. Time your jumps perfectly to send the ball flying into your opponent's net.

For two-player mode on a computer:

  • Player 1: Press the 'W' key.
  • Player 2: Press the 'UP ARROW' key.

The "random" in the title is the key to the fun. After every goal, the game changes! You might find yourself playing on a snowy field, a sunny beach, with a heavier ball, or even a different shaped ball. Adapt to these new conditions to keep your winning streak alive!
