Soccer Random

How to Play Soccer Random

Welcome to the chaotic world of Soccer Random! The rules are simple, but the gameplay is anything but. Your main objective is to outscore your opponent by being the first to score 5 goals.

Control your entire team with just a single button! Press the button (or tap the screen) to make your ragdoll-like players jump, flip, and kick. Timing is everything, as you try to guide the ball into the opponent's net through sheer physics-based mayhem. The players' movements are unpredictable, leading to hilarious and surprising moments.

The real twist comes after every goal. The game will randomly change, transporting you to a new field, altering the weather conditions (like an icy pitch or a beach setting), and sometimes even changing the ball itself. Adapt to these new conditions and keep scoring to claim victory! You can play solo against the computer or challenge a friend in the two-player mode.
