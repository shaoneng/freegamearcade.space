Soccer Random

How to Play Soccer Random

Get ready for the most unpredictable soccer match of your life! In Soccer Random, your objective is to be the first to score 5 goals. But here's the catch: you control your entire team of floppy, physics-based players with just a single button.

Press the button at the right moment to make your players jump, flip, and kick the ball toward your opponent's goal. Every match is a hilarious display of ragdoll physics and unexpected bounces.

To keep things fresh, the game changes after every single goal. You might find yourself playing on an icy field, a sunny beach, or with a completely different ball. Adapt to the new environment and use the chaos to your advantage. You can challenge the computer or play against a friend in the exciting 2-player mode!
