How to Play Soccer Random Pro

Welcome to the chaotic field of Soccer Random Pro! The goal is simple: be the first to score five goals. However, getting the ball into the net is anything but simple. Embrace the unpredictable physics and get ready for some hilarious moments as players flip, flop, and fly across the screen.

The controls are designed for maximum fun with minimal complexity. Each player uses only one button to make their characters jump, flip, and kick. Whether you're playing against the computer or a friend, the objective remains the same: use the wild physics to your advantage!

Player 1 Controls: Press the 'W' key to make your player jump and kick.

Player 2 Controls: Press the 'Up Arrow' key to make your player jump and kick.
