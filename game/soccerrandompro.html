Soccer Random Pro

How to Play Soccer Random Pro

Welcome to the chaotic world of Soccer Random Pro! The objective is simple: be the first to score 5 goals. But how you get there is anything but ordinary. The controls are deceptively simple, with each player using just one button to jump and kick.

For Player 1, use the 'W' key. For Player 2, use the 'Up Arrow' key. Press your assigned button to make your ragdoll players jump, flip, and kick. Timing your press is crucial to hitting the ball, but the wacky physics will ensure every action is hilariously unpredictable. Embrace the randomness, laugh at the wild backflips and accidental own goals, and have a blast playing against the CPU or a friend!
