Soccer Random Pro

How to Play Soccer Random Pro

Welcome to the chaotic and hilarious world of Soccer Random Pro! The objective is simple: be the first to score 5 goals. However, the gameplay is anything but predictable.

The controls are deceptively easy. Each player uses just one button to control their entire team. Press your button to make your players jump, kick, and flip around the field. The key to victory is timing your jumps perfectly to connect with the ball.

Controls:

  • Player 1: Press the 'W' key.
  • Player 2: Press the 'Up Arrow' key.

Embrace the randomness! The game's wild physics engine means players will tumble and the ball will bounce in unexpected ways. This leads to funny moments, incredible fluke shots, and dramatic own goals. You can challenge the AI in 1-player mode or grab a friend for an exciting 2-player showdown on the same computer.
