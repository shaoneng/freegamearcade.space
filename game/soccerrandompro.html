Soccer Random Pro

How to Play Soccer Random Pro

Welcome to the unpredictable world of Soccer Random Pro! The goal is simple: be the first player to score 5 goals. The controls, however, lead to hilarious and chaotic results. This game is all about timing your single button press to make your players jump, kick, and flip towards the ball.

Controls:

  • Player 1: Press the 'W' key to make your player jump and kick.
  • Player 2: Press the 'UP ARROW' key to make your player jump and kick.

Embrace the wacky physics! Your players will flail and flip in unexpected ways, and the ball can bounce off anything. Sometimes an accidental backflip leads to the perfect goal, and other times it might be a comical own goal. Master the chaos to outscore your opponent and claim victory!
