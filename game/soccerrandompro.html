Soccer Random Pro

How to Play Soccer Random Pro

Get ready for a soccer match unlike any other! Soccer Random Pro is all about chaotic fun with incredibly simple controls. The goal is to score 5 goals against your opponent before they do.

The challenge comes from the game's wild, physics-based engine. Your players will jump, flip, and tumble randomly with each button press. The ball also behaves unpredictably, bouncing around the screen in surprising ways. Timing your jumps is key, but be prepared for hilarious own goals and lucky shots. Adapt to the randomness, embrace the chaos, and have a blast!

Controls:

  • Player 1: Press the 'W' key to make your players jump and kick.
  • Player 2: Press the Up Arrow key to make your players jump and kick.
