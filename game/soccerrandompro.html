Soccer Random Pro

How to Play Soccer Random Pro

Welcome to the chaotic field of Soccer Random Pro! The objective is simple: be the first player to score 5 goals. However, getting the ball into the net is anything but simple. This game relies on zany physics and one-button controls for maximum unpredictability and fun, whether you're playing against the computer or a friend.

The controls are incredibly easy to learn but difficult to master. Each player uses a single key to make their characters jump, flip, and kick. Use this one button to control all your players' actions!

Player 1: Press the 'W' key.

Player 2: Press the 'Up Arrow' key.

Time your jumps perfectly to hit the ball towards your opponent's goal. Don't be surprised if you score a hilarious own goal or win with a lucky backflip. Embrace the randomness and have a blast!
