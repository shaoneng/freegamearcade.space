How to Play Soccer Random Pro

Get ready for a soccer game unlike any other! In Soccer Random Pro, the goal is simple, but the gameplay is pure chaos. The first player or team to score 5 goals wins the match.

The controls are incredibly easy to learn but difficult to master. Each player is controlled by a single button, which makes them jump and kick simultaneously. The challenge comes from the wild physicsâ€”your players will flip, flop, and fly across the screen in the most unpredictable ways!

Controls for Player 1: Press the 'W' key to make your player jump and kick.

Controls for Player 2: Press the 'Up Arrow' key to make your player jump and kick.

Time your jumps perfectly to hit the ball into your opponent's goal. But be careful! The bouncy ball and clumsy players often lead to hilarious own goals. Embrace the randomness, laugh at the wacky moments, and strive to be the first to reach five points!
