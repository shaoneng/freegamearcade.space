Soldier Legend

How to Play Soldier Legend

Your mission in Soldier Legend is to defend your base from invading aliens. The game is a side-scrolling shooter where you must survive multiple waves of enemies in each level. Keep your trigger finger ready and eliminate every alien that appears on screen!

After successfully clearing a stage, you'll get a chance to upgrade your gear. Collect the coins dropped by defeated enemies and visit the armory between levels. Here, you can spend your earnings to prepare for the tougher challenges ahead.

The shop offers a variety of crucial upgrades. You can unlock and switch to more powerful weapons than your default pistol, purchase automated towers that provide extra firepower, and buy single-use power-ups. These power-ups can be activated mid-battle from the sidebar and recharge after each wave, giving you a strategic edge when you need it most.
