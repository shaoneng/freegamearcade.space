Soldier Legend

How to Play Soldier Legend

Your mission is simple but crucial: defend your base from an alien invasion! In this side-scrolling shooter, you are the last line of defense against extraterrestrial attackers who have crash-landed right in your backyard.

Each level challenges you with four waves of increasingly tough enemies. Your objective is to shoot them all before they overrun your position. Use your mouse on desktop or touch controls on mobile to aim and fire at the incoming swarm.

After successfully clearing a stage, you'll be rewarded with coins dropped by the defeated aliens. Make sure to scoop them up! These coins are your key to survival and upgrading your power.

Head to the armory between levels to spend your hard-earned coins. You can unlock a variety of more powerful weapons to replace your default pistol, purchase automated towers that help you defend the lane, and buy single-use power-ups for a critical advantage in battle.

During a fight, you can activate your purchased power-ups from the sidebar on the right. They provide a temporary boost and recharge between waves, so use them strategically to turn the tide of battle!
