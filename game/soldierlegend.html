Soldier Legend

How to Play Soldier Legend

Your mission in Soldier Legend is to defend your base from invading aliens. The game is a side-scrolling shooter where you must survive multiple waves of enemies in each level. Simply use your mouse or finger to aim and automatically shoot at the extraterrestrial threats before they overwhelm your position.

After successfully clearing a stage, you'll collect coins dropped by the defeated aliens. Head to the armory between levels to spend your hard-earned cash. You can unlock a variety of new weapons, purchase automated defense towers to help you in battle, and acquire powerful, single-use power-ups for when things get tough.

During a battle, you can activate your purchased power-ups from the sidebar on the right; these handy abilities recharge between waves. A good strategy is key: invest your coins wisely in the right upgrades to counter the increasingly difficult swarms of enemies. Lock, load, and show those aliens who's boss!
