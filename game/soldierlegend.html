Soldier Legend

How to Play Soldier Legend

Your mission in Soldier Legend is to defend your base from invading aliens. This action-packed game is a side-scrolling shooter where you must survive multiple waves of enemies in each level. Take aim and eliminate every alien that appears on screen. When enemies are defeated, they drop valuable coins—make sure to scoop them up!

After successfully clearing a stage, you'll get access to the shop. This is where you can spend your hard-earned coins to prepare for the next, more challenging assault. You can purchase a variety of upgrades, including more powerful guns to replace your default pistol, automated towers that help you defend the lane, and one-use power-ups for tight situations.

During battle, you can activate any purchased power-ups from the sidebar on the right. These abilities are single-use per wave but recharge between them, giving you a crucial edge when you're overwhelmed. Strategically upgrade your arsenal and defenses to hold the line and become the ultimate Soldier Legend!
