Soldier Legend

How to Play Soldier Legend

In Soldier Legend, your mission is to defend your base from an alien invasion. As the last line of defense, you must survive waves of enemies in this thrilling side-scrolling shooter. The game features rogue-lite elements, meaning each run offers new challenges and opportunities for upgrades.

The gameplay is simple yet addictive. Aim your weapon and shoot down all enemies in each wave. Once you've cleared all four waves in a level, you'll complete the stage. Defeated aliens drop valuable coins, so be sure to collect them all before moving on.

Between levels, you'll visit the armory where you can spend your hard-earned coins. Upgrade your arsenal by unlocking a variety of powerful weapons, from your starting pistol to more exotic firearms. You can also purchase automated towers that will help you defend your lane automatically.

Don't forget about power-ups! These are single-use items that can turn the tide of battle. Activate them from the sidebar on the right during a level for a temporary boost. They recharge between waves, so use them strategically to overcome the toughest swarms.
