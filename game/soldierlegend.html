Soldier Legend

How to Play Soldier Legend

Lock, load, and get ready to defend Earth! In Soldier Legend, aliens have crash-landed in the backyard, and you are the only trooper tough enough to stop their invasion. This action-packed, side-scrolling rogue-lite shooter will put your skills to the test.

Your mission is to survive waves of enemies attacking your base. Each level consists of four increasingly difficult waves. Defeat all the aliens, clear the stage, and collect the coins they drop. Between levels, head to the armory to spend your hard-earned coins on crucial upgrades to prepare for the next onslaught.

Upgrades and Arsenal

The shop is your key to survival. Here's what you can get:

  • Weapons: You'll start with a trusty pistol, but you can unlock a variety of wilder guns to increase your firepower.
  • Towers: Deploy these automatic towers on the lane to provide extra defensive support and shoot enemies for you.
  • Power-ups: These are powerful single-use items you can activate from the sidebar during a battle. They recharge between waves and can be upgraded in the shop for even greater effect.

Soldier Legend was created by QKY Games. If you enjoy defending lanes against endless hordes, check out a similar game like Monster Survivors for another thrilling challenge!
