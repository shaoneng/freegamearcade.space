Soldier Legend

How to Play Soldier Legend

Your mission in Soldier Legend is to defend your base from invading aliens. The game is a side-scrolling shooter where you'll face four waves of enemies in each level. Your goal is to eliminate all hostiles before they can overrun your position.

Use your controls to aim and shoot at the approaching aliens. As you defeat them, they will drop coins. Make sure to collect as many coins as you can, as they are crucial for strengthening your defenses for the next, more challenging swarm.

Between levels, you'll visit the armory. Here, you can spend your hard-earned coins to upgrade your equipment. Unlock more powerful weapons, purchase automatic defense towers to assist you in battle, and stock up on single-use power-ups to turn the tide when things get tough.

During a level, you can activate your purchased power-ups from the sidebar on the right. These abilities are incredibly useful for clearing out large groups of enemies but have a recharge time between waves, so use them strategically! Good luck, soldier!
