Soldier Legend

How to Play Soldier Legend

Welcome to Soldier Legend, where you are humanity's last hope against an alien invasion! The objective is simple: defend your base from incoming waves of extraterrestrial enemies. Each level consists of four increasingly difficult waves. Your goal is to eliminate every alien before they overrun your position.

As you defeat aliens, they will drop valuable coins. Be sure to collect these before the next wave begins. These coins are your key to survival, as they allow you to purchase crucial upgrades and equipment from the armory shop between stages.

In the shop, you can spend your coins to bolster your defenses. Upgrade from your default pistol to a variety of more powerful weapons. You can also purchase and deploy automated towers that will attack enemies for you, and buy single-use power-ups that can be activated mid-battle for a strategic advantage. Don't forget to upgrade your power-ups in the shop to increase their effectiveness!

The controls are straightforward for both desktop and mobile play. Aim your weapon and fire at the approaching aliens. Keep an eye on your power-ups on the side of the screen and use them wisely to clear tough swarms. Lock, load, and get ready to become a legend!
