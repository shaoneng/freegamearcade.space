Soldier Legend

How to Play Soldier Legend

Your primary mission in Soldier Legend is to defend your base from invading aliens who have crash-landed nearby. You are the last line of defense against a total takeover!

Each level challenges you with four waves of enemies. Your job is to shoot them all down. As you defeat aliens, they will drop coinsâ€”make sure to scoop them up! These coins are essential for upgrading your capabilities for the next fight.

After clearing a stage, you'll enter the armory. Here, you can spend your hard-earned coins to buy more powerful weapons, deploy automated defense towers to help you in battle, and acquire potent, single-use power-ups. Choose your upgrades wisely to prepare for the increasingly difficult swarms.

During battle, you can activate your purchased power-ups from the sidebar on the right. These recharge between waves, giving you a crucial edge when you need it most. Get ready to lock, load, and become the legend Earth needs!
