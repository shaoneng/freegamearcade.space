Soldier Legend

How to Play Soldier Legend

Step into the boots of an elite soldier in Soldier Legend, where you are Earth's last hope against an alien invasion. Your primary objective is to defend your base from incoming waves of extraterrestrial enemies. Each level consists of four intense waves that you must survive.

Use your controls to move your soldier and aim your weapon to shoot down all invaders. Once you successfully clear a stage, be sure to collect the coins dropped by your defeated foes. These coins are your key to survival.

Between levels, you'll enter the armory. Here, you can spend your hard-earned coins to upgrade your arsenal. Swap your starting pistol for more powerful guns, purchase and deploy automated defense towers to assist you, and stock up on single-use power-ups for a critical edge in the next battle. These power-ups can be activated mid-fight and will recharge between waves, giving you a recurring tactical advantage.
