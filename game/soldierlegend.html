Soldier Legend

How to Play Soldier Legend

Your mission in Soldier Legend is to defend your base from invading aliens. The game is a side-scrolling shooter where you'll face four waves of enemies in each level. Your primary objective is to eliminate all hostiles before they can overrun your position.

Control your soldier to shoot down the incoming aliens. As you defeat them, they will drop coins. Make sure to collect as many as you can, as these coins are crucial for your survival and for becoming more powerful.

Between stages, you can visit the armory to spend your hard-earned coins. Here, you can unlock a variety of new weapons, purchase automated towers that help defend your base, and buy single-use power-ups for a tactical advantage. Upgrading your gear is key to surviving the increasingly difficult swarms.

Power-ups can be activated mid-battle from the sidebar. They provide a temporary boost and recharge between waves, giving you a critical edge when you're in a tight spot. Good luck, soldier!
