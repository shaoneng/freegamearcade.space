Soldier Legend

How to Play Soldier Legend

Welcome to Soldier Legend, where you are the last line of defense against an alien invasion! Your primary mission is to protect your base from waves of incoming enemies in this action-packed, side-scrolling shooter.

Control your soldier to shoot down all the aliens across four waves in each level. As you defeat enemies, they will drop coins. Make sure to collect as much as you can. Surviving the onslaught and clearing the stage allows you to progress to the shop.

Between levels, use your hard-earned coins in the armory to power up. You can unlock a variety of powerful weapons, moving beyond your default pistol. You can also purchase and deploy automated towers that will help you defend your lane, and buy one-use power-ups to give you an edge in the next battle.

During a level, these powerful single-use items can be activated from the sidebar on the right. They provide a significant advantage but need to recharge between waves, so use them strategically! Ready your defenses, customize your loadout, and show those aliens who's boss!
