Solitaire

How to Play Solitaire

Solitaire is a classic single-player card game where the objective is to sort a full 52-card deck into four foundation piles. Each pile corresponds to a suit (Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs, Spades) and must be built in ascending order, starting with the Ace and ending with the King.

To begin, cards are dealt into seven tableau columns with the top card face-up. You can move face-up cards between these columns, but only onto a card of the opposite color and one rank higher (e.g., a red 7 can be placed on a black 8). Uncovering face-down cards by clearing the cards on top of them is key to revealing more moves.

If you run out of moves on the tableau, click the stock pile to draw new cards. The goal is to systematically move all cards to the four foundation piles at the top of the screen. The game is won when the entire deck is successfully sorted into the foundations and no cards remain on the tableau.
