Solitaire

How to Play Solitaire

The main goal in Solitaire is to move all 52 cards into the four "foundation" piles located at the top of the screen. Each foundation pile corresponds to one suit (Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs, Spades) and must be built in ascending order, starting from the Ace and ending with the King.

The game starts with seven card piles on the main playing area, known as the "tableau." You can move a face-up card onto another tableau pile if it's one rank lower and of the opposite color (for instance, placing a red 6 onto a black 7). Moving a card will reveal the face-down card beneath it. You can move entire sequences of cards together if they are properly ordered.

If you get stuck and have no available moves on the tableau, click the stock pile at the top to draw new cards. The game is won when you have successfully transferred all cards from the tableau and stock piles to the four foundations. You can use the "Undo" button to take back a move if you make a mistake.
