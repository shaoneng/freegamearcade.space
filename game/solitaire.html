Solitaire

How to Play Solitaire

The main objective in Solitaire is to move all 52 cards into four foundation piles. Each pile represents a suit (Hearts, Diamonds, Clubs, Spades) and must be built in ascending order, starting with the Ace and ending with the King.

To begin, cards are dealt into seven columns on the tableau. You can move face-up cards onto another column if the card you are moving is one rank lower and of the opposite color (e.g., a red 5 on a black 6). You can also move entire sequences of cards together.

If you uncover a face-down card, it will be flipped over. If a tableau column becomes empty, you can place a King (and only a King) in that space. Click on the stock pile in the top-left corner to draw new cards. The game is won when all cards have been successfully moved to the four foundation piles.
