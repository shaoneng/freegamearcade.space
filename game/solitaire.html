Solitaire

How to Play Solitaire

The main objective in Solitaire is to move all 52 cards into four "foundation" piles. Each pile represents a suit and must be built in ascending order, starting with the Ace and ending with the King.

To begin, cards are dealt into seven "tableau" columns on the main playing area. You can move face-up cards between these columns by placing them onto a card of the opposite color and one rank higher (for example, a red 6 can be placed on a black 7). Moving a card will reveal the face-down card beneath it, bringing it into play.

If you uncover an Ace, move it to one of the four foundation slots at the top. Continue building these foundation piles with the 2, 3, and so on for each suit. If you run out of moves on the tableau, click the stock pile at the top to draw new cards. The game is won when all cards are successfully sorted into the foundation piles.
