Solitaire

How to Play Solitaire

The goal of Solitaire is to build four "foundation" piles, one for each suit, in ascending order from Ace to King. You win the game when all 52 cards are moved to these foundation piles.

Cards are dealt into seven "tableau" columns. You can move cards between columns by placing them on a card of the next highest rank and the opposite color. For example, you can place a red 6 on a black 7. You can also move an entire sequence of cards (e.g., a red 6, black 5, and red 4) as a single unit. Moving a card may reveal a face-down card underneath, which you can then turn over.

If you run out of moves, click the stock pile in the top-left corner to draw a new card. You can play this card onto the tableau or a foundation pile. An empty tableau column can only be filled with a King.

Keep making moves and drawing from the stock pile until all cards are successfully sorted onto the foundation piles or no more moves are possible. Good luck!
