The goal in Solitaire is to sort all 52 cards into four foundation piles, one for each suit, in ascending order from Ace to King. Every card you clear is a small victory, offering a moment of calm in a busy day.

How to Play:

  • Start: Click "New Game" to shuffle and deal the cards into seven tableau piles.
  • Tableau Moves: Move face-up cards onto another pile if they are one rank lower and of the opposite color (e.g., a Red 8 on a Black 9).
  • Foundations: Move Aces to the foundation slots at the top. Build on them with cards of the same suit, from 2 up to King.
  • Stock Pile: Click the stock pile to draw one new card if you're out of moves. This version uses the easier Draw 1 rule.
  • Winning: You win the game when all 52 cards are correctly moved to the four foundation piles.

Tips for a Better Game:

  • Prioritize moves that uncover new, face-down cards.
  • Try to move Aces to the foundation as soon as they are available.
  • Avoid emptying a tableau pile unless you have a King ready to place in it.
  • Use the "Undo" button to reverse mistakes or try a different strategic path.
