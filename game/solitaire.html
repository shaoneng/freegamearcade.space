Solitaire

How to Play Solitaire

The goal of Solitaire is to create four foundation piles, one for each suit, in ascending order from Ace to King. You win when all 52 cards have been moved to these foundation piles.

To start, cards are dealt into seven columns, known as the tableau. You can move a card (or a sequence of sorted cards) onto another column if the top card of the destination column is one rank higher and of the opposite color. For example, you can place a red 6 on a black 7. Clicking on a face-down card will reveal it.

If you run out of moves on the tableau, click the stock pile in the top-left corner to draw new cards. When an Ace is uncovered, you can move it to one of the four foundation slots at the top. From there, you can add the 2 of that suit, then the 3, and so on, until you reach the King.

Keep making moves and drawing from the stock until you either complete all foundation piles and win, or you run out of possible moves.
