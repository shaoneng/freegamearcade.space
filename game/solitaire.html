Solitaire

How to Play Solitaire

The main goal in Solitaire is to move all 52 cards into the four "foundation" piles at the top of the screen. Each foundation pile must be built by suit, starting with the Ace and ending with the King.

To begin, you can move face-up cards between the seven columns on the main play area, known as the "tableau." A card can be placed on another if it is of the opposite color and one rank lower (for example, a red 9 on a black 10). Moving a card will reveal the face-down card beneath it.

If you run out of moves on the tableau, click the stock pile in the corner to draw a new card. You can play this card onto a tableau column or directly onto a foundation pile if it fits. Keep drawing cards and making moves until you've cleared the deck or can't make any more moves.

You win the game when all cards are successfully sorted into their respective foundation piles from Ace to King. If you get stuck, use the "Undo" button to try a different strategy!
