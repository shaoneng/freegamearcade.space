Solitaire Classic

How to Play Solitaire Classic

The main objective in Solitaire Classic is to move all 52 cards onto the four foundation piles located at the top of the game screen. Each foundation must be built up by suit, starting with the Ace and ending with the King (e.g., Ace of Spades, 2 of Spades, 3 of Spades, and so on).

To reveal cards and build your foundations, you must manipulate the cards in the seven columns of the tableau. You can move cards between columns by placing them on top of a card that is one rank higher and of the opposite color (e.g., a red 7 can be placed on a black 8). You can also draw cards from the stockpile when you run out of moves.

This version of Solitaire allows for customization! Click the "Theme" tab to change the design of the card backs and the game background. If you get stuck, use the "Hint" button for a suggestion or the "Undo" button to take back your last move. For a bigger advantage, you can use the Card Shuffle or Magic Wand boosters, but be aware that their uses are limited!
