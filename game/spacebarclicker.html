Spacebar Clicker

How to Play Spacebar Clicker

Welcome to Spacebar Clicker, the ultimate test of your clicking endurance! The objective is simple: click the giant spacebar on the screen or press the space key on your keyboard as many times as you can to earn points. Each press earns you one point.

As your score climbs, you can invest your points in powerful boosters like helper monkeys and boomer moms. These upgrades will automatically generate points for you, turning this into a rewarding idle game and skyrocketing your earnings even when you're not actively playing. The more points you accumulate, the more boosters will become available to purchase.

To maximize your scoring speed, combine different clicking methods! Use your mouse to click the on-screen button while simultaneously tapping the physical spacebar on your keyboard. This will double your earnings and help you unlock powerful upgrades much faster. Find a rhythm that works for you and watch the points roll in!
