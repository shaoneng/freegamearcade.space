Spacebar Clicker

How to Play Spacebar Clicker

Welcome to Spacebar Clicker! Your primary objective is incredibly simple: click the giant spacebar on your screen or press the spacebar on your keyboard to earn points. Every single press adds to your score, pushing you closer to becoming a clicking champion.

As you accumulate points, you'll unlock the ability to purchase powerful boosters. Invest your hard-earned points in upgrades like "booster monkeys" and "boomer moms" that will automatically click for you or multiply your earnings. These helpers turn the game into an idle powerhouse, generating points even when you're taking a break!

For maximum efficiency, try combining different inputs! Use your mouse to click the on-screen button while simultaneously tapping your physical spacebar. Experiment with different techniques to find the fastest way to rack up an astronomical score. The more you click, the more you can upgrade, and the faster your points will soar!
