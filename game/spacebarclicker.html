Spacebar Clicker

How to Play Spacebar Clicker

Welcome to Spacebar Clicker, the ultimate test of your clicking endurance! The objective is simple: click the giant spacebar on the screen or press the physical spacebar on your keyboard to earn points. Each press adds one point to your score.

As you accumulate points, you can invest them in powerful boosters that will click for you. Hire booster monkeys and boomer moms to automate your point generation. These helpers work tirelessly in the background, turning the game into an idle powerhouse and helping you earn points even when you're not actively clicking.

For maximum efficiency, try combining clicking methods! Use your mouse to rapidly click the on-screen button while simultaneously tapping your keyboard's spacebar with your other hand. This strategy will double your point-earning speed, allowing you to unlock all the boosters and achieve a legendary high score much faster.
