Spacebar Clicker

How to Play Spacebar Clicker

The goal in Spacebar Clicker is straightforward: click the giant spacebar on your screen as fast as you can! Each click earns you one point. You can use your mouse to click the on-screen button or press the actual spacebar on your keyboard.

For maximum points, you can combine methods! Try pressing your physical spacebar while simultaneously clicking the on-screen button with your mouse. This will double your clicking speed and help you earn points much faster.

As you accumulate points, you'll unlock the ability to purchase boosters. These automated helpers, like booster monkeys and boomer moms, will click for you, allowing you to earn points even when you're not actively clicking. Keep buying upgrades to increase your idle income and reach astronomical scores!
