Spacebar Clicker

How to Play Spacebar Clicker

The goal in Spacebar Clicker is simple: click the giant spacebar on the screen as fast as you can! Each click, either with your mouse on the screen or by pressing the physical spacebar on your keyboard, earns you one point.

As you accumulate points, you'll unlock the ability to purchase boosters and upgrades. These helpers, such as booster monkeys and boomer moms, will automatically generate points for you, turning the game into an idle powerhouse. Keep an eye on the right side of the screen for available upgrades as you reach new point milestones.

To maximize your earnings early on, try combining different clicking methods. You can click the on-screen bar with your mouse while simultaneously pressing the physical spacebar key. Experiment with different techniques to find the fastest way to rack up points and unlock all the powerful upgrades!
