Spacebar Clicker

How to Play Spacebar Clicker

The objective in Spacebar Clicker is simple: click the giant spacebar on the screen as fast as you can to earn points. You can use your mouse to click the on-screen button or press the actual spacebar key on your keyboard. Every single press rewards you with one point.

As your score grows, you'll unlock the ability to purchase automated helpers. Look out for boosters like 'booster monkeys' and 'boomer moms' appearing on the screen. Spend your hard-earned points on these upgrades to have them click for you, generating points automatically and turning the game into an idle powerhouse.

To maximize your initial earnings before you can afford the big upgrades, try different clicking techniques! For a speed boost, use both your keyboard's spacebar and your mouse to click simultaneously. This double-tapping method will help you rack up points twice as fast and unlock those powerful boosters sooner.
