Spend Elon Musk Money

How to Play Spend Elon Musk Money

Step into the shoes of a billionaire in this incredibly fun simulation game! The goal is simple: spend all of Elon Musk's vast fortune as quickly as you can. You start with over $241 billion in your virtual bank account.

To begin your shopping spree, browse through the list of extravagant items. You'll find everything from supercars and yachts to famous paintings and even sports franchises. Simply click on an item to purchase it, and watch the cost get deducted from your total balance at the top of the screen.

The faster you click, the faster you spend! Challenge yourself to see how quickly you can get your balance down to zero. Once you've spent all the money, or if you decide you've had enough of the billionaire lifestyle, you can click the 'Print Receipt' button to see a summary of all your purchases. Share it with your friends and compare your shopping lists!
