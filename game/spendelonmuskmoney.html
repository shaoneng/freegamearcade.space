Spend Elon Musk Money

How to Play Spend Elon Musk Money

Welcome to the ultimate shopping spree! In Spend Elon Musk Money, you've just inherited the tech mogul's entire fortune, and your only job is to spend it all before time runs out.

The game is incredibly simple. Your screen will be filled with a wide variety of items, from everyday purchases to extravagant luxuries like sports cars, private jets, and even entire companies. To buy an item, simply click on it. The cost of the item will be deducted from your total balance displayed at the top of the screen.

Your goal is to spend the entire fortune and bring your balance down to $0. Click as fast as you can to buy multiple items and watch the billions disappear. At the end of your shopping spree, you can print a receipt to see a full list of everything you've 'purchased'. Challenge your friends to see who can spend Elon's money the fastest!
