Spend Elon Musk Money

How to Play Spend Elon Musk Money

Welcome to the ultimate shopping spree! In Spend Elon Musk Money, you're given the monumental task of spending the entirety of Elon Musk's fortune. The gameplay is simple but addictively fun.

You'll start with a staggering balance of over $240 billion at the top of your screen. Below, you'll find a long list of items, ranging from everyday luxuries like a Netflix subscription to extravagant purchases like an F-16 fighter jet, a superyacht, or even a professional sports team.

To play, simply click the "Buy" button next to any item you desire. You can buy items multiple times to really rack up the spending! Watch your remaining balance decrease with every purchase. The objective is to get your balance down to zero.

Once you've either spent all the money or decided you've bought enough, you can click the "Print Receipt" button at the bottom of the page to see a full list of your purchases. Share it with your friends and see who can spend the fastest or who makes the wildest purchases!
