How to Play Spend Elon Musk Money

Welcome to the ultimate shopping spree! In Spend Elon Musk Money, you're given the mind-boggling net worth of the tech mogul, and your only objective is to spend every last dollar.

The gameplay is simple. At the top of the screen, you'll see your starting balanceâ€”a cool $241 billion or more. Below, you'll find a long list of items, ranging from everyday things like Big Macs to extravagant purchases like NFL teams, superyachts, and even fighter jets.

To start your shopping frenzy, simply click the 'Buy' button next to any item. You can purchase items multiple times. Each click adds the item to your cart and deducts its cost from your total balance. Watch the money counter decrease as you go on the biggest shopping spree of your life!

Challenge yourself to see how quickly you can get the balance to zero. Once you're done (or just exhausted from spending), you can click the 'Print Receipt' button at the bottom to see a full list of your extravagant purchases. Share it with your friends to see who has the wildest shopping list!
