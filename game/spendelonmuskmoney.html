How to Play Spend Elon Musk Money

Welcome to the ultimate shopping spree! In Spend Elon Musk Money, you're given the colossal fortune of the world's richest manâ€”over $241 billion! The objective is simple: spend every last dollar as fast as you can.

The game presents you with a long list of items, ranging from everyday luxuries like a Netflix subscription to extravagant purchases like an NBA team, a superyacht, or even a Falcon 9 rocket. Simply click the "Buy" button next to an item to purchase it. You can buy multiples of each item by clicking repeatedly.

Keep an eye on your remaining balance at the top of the screen. Your goal is to get this number down to zero. There's no time limit, so the challenge is in seeing just how difficult it is to spend such a vast amount of wealth.

Once you've spent all the money or simply can't click anymore, scroll to the bottom and hit the "Print Receipt" button. This will generate a detailed list of everything you bought. Share your receipt with friends and compare your billionaire shopping carts!
