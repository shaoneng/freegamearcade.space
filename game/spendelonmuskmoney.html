Spend Elon Musk Money

How to Play Spend Elon Musk Money

Step into the shoes of a billionaire in Spend Elon Musk Money! The game starts you off with the staggering net worth of Elon Musk, and a single, thrilling objective: spend it all. From the moment you begin, a massive bank balance is displayed at the top of your screen. Your goal is to make that number hit zero.

The gameplay is delightfully simple. Browse a vast catalog of items, ranging from everyday indulgences to outrageously expensive assets like sports teams, rockets, and luxury real estate. To make a purchase, simply click on the 'Buy' button next to an item. You can buy multiples of each item, so click away to see the money drain from your account. The faster you click, the quicker you'll spend.

Challenge yourself by starting the timer at the top of the screen before you begin your shopping spree. See just how long it takes you to burn through billions! Once you've spent all the money, or simply can't spend anymore, you can print a receipt of your extravagant purchases. Share your shopping list with friends and see who made the most outrageous buys!
