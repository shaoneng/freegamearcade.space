How to Play Spend Elon Musk Money

Welcome to the ultimate shopping spree! In Spend Elon Musk Money, you're given the keys to a vast fortune. The goal is simple: spend it all as fast as you can!

Start by looking at the massive bank balance at the top of the screen. Below, you'll find a wide array of items, from everyday goods to extravagant luxuries like sports cars, yachts, and even rockets. Simply click on an item to purchase it, and watch your remaining balance decrease.

The more you click, the more you buy. Try to purchase the most expensive items first to see the biggest drop in your funds. Can you spend over 241 billion dollars? Once you've had your fill of shopping (or if you miraculously manage to spend it all), you can click the "Print Receipt" button at the bottom to see a full list of your purchases. Share it with your friends and see who has the most outrageous shopping cart!
