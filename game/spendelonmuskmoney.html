Spend Elon Musk Money

How to Play Spend Elon Musk Money

Welcome to the ultimate shopping spree! In Spend Elon Musk Money, you're given the monumental task of spending the entirety of Elon's fortune. The game is simple: you start with over $200 billion in your virtual bank account.

Your goal is to buy items from the list to decrease your balance to zero. Click on any item you wish to purchase, from a Big Mac to a private jet or even an NBA team. The price of the item will be deducted from your total. Keep clicking and buying as fast as you can!

A timer at the top of the screen tracks how long it takes you to spend all the money. See if you can set a new record! When you're done (or just tired of being a billionaire), you can click the "Print Receipt" button to see a full list of your extravagant purchases. Share it with your friends and see who has the wildest shopping cart!
