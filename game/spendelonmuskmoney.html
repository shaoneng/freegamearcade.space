Spend Elon Musk Money

How to Play Spend Elon Musk Money

Welcome to the ultimate shopping spree! In Spend Elon Musk Money, you're given the challenge of spending the entirety of his massive fortune. The goal is simple: reduce the massive bank balance at the top of your screen to zero as quickly as you can.

To start spending, simply click on any of the luxurious items listed. From supercars and private jets to entire sports franchises and tech companies, each click adds an item to your virtual cart and subtracts its value from your total. The faster you click, the quicker you'll spend!

Keep an eye on the remaining balance. Can you actually manage to spend hundreds of billions of dollars? Once you've spent as much as you can (or want to), you can click the "Print Receipt" button to see a full list of your extravagant purchases. Share it with your friends and compare who had the wildest shopping spree!
