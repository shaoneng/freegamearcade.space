Spend Elon Musk Money

How to Play Spend Elon Musk Money

Step into the shoes of the world's richest man in this exciting spending simulation game! The goal is simple: you have been given Elon Musk's entire fortune, a whopping $241 billion, and you need to spend it all. But there's a catch - a timer is ticking, challenging you to see how quickly you can empty the bank account.

Getting started is easy. A massive list of items is available for purchase, ranging from everyday luxuries to extravagant acquisitions like superyachts, sports teams, and even space rockets. Simply click on the "Buy" button next to any item you desire. Each purchase will add the item to your cart and deduct its cost from your total balance, which is displayed at the top of the screen.

The controls are entirely mouse-based. Point and click your way through the shopping spree. The faster you click, the faster you'll spend. Try to get your balance down to $0. Once you've spent all the money or simply decide you're finished, you can click the "Print Receipt" button to see a full list of your purchases. Share your receipt with friends and see who made the most outrageous shopping cart!
