Spend Elon Musk Money

How to Play Spend Elon Musk Money

Welcome to the ultimate shopping spree! In Spend Elon Musk Money, you're given the tycoon's entire fortune with one simple goal: spend it all as fast as you can. The game is incredibly simple and satisfying to play.

Your total balance, a staggering sum of over $241 billion, is displayed at the top of the screen. Below it, you'll find a list of extravagant items, from luxury cars and mansions to entire sports teams and even space rockets. Simply click on any item to purchase it. With each click, the item's cost is deducted from your balance.

The challenge isn't just to spend the money, but to comprehend the sheer scale of such wealth. Can you buy an NBA team? A fighter jet? A cruise ship? Keep clicking and watch the numbers go down. When you've finally managed to hit $0 (or you've had enough of the billionaire life), you can print a receipt of your purchases to share with friends and compare your wild shopping lists!
