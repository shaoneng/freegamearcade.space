Spend Elon Musk Money

How to Play Spend Elon Musk Money

Step into the shoes of a billionaire! The game starts you off with the immense fortune of Elon Musk, displayed at the top of your screen. Your one and only goal is to spend every last dollar as quickly as you can.

Below your massive bank balance, you'll see a list of items ranging from everyday purchases to absurdly expensive assets like sports cars, luxury yachts, and even rocket companies. Simply click on an item to buy it. Each purchase will instantly deduct its cost from your total.

The challenge is to see how fast you can empty the account. Keep clicking and buying until your balance hits zero. Once you're done (or just want to see your shopping list), you can print a receipt to see a summary of your extravagant spending spree. Share it with your friends and see who can spend the money faster!
