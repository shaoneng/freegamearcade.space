Spend Elon Musk Money

How to Play Spend Elon Musk Money

Step into the shoes of a billionaire and take on the ultimate challenge: spending Elon Musk's entire fortune! The game starts you off with his astronomical net worth displayed at the top of the screen, and the clock is ticking.

Your goal is simple: buy everything you can to bring that massive number down to zero. Use your mouse to click on various items, from luxury goods like yachts and private jets to extravagant purchases like sports teams and tech companies. Each click deducts the item's cost from your total balance.

The faster you click, the quicker you'll spend. Try to empty the bank account completely. Once you've finished your shopping spree (or given up), you can print a final receipt to see a summary of your wild purchases. Share it with your friends and see who has the most outrageous taste!
