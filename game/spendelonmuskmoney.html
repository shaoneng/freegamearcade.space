Spend Elon Musk Money

How to Play Spend Elon Musk Money

What would you do if you woke up with Elon Musk's entire fortune in your bank account? This game lets you find out! The objective is hilariously simple: spend over $240 billion as quickly as you can.

Start the timer at the top of your screen and let the ultimate shopping spree begin. Click on a vast array of items, from luxury penthouses and supercars to private islands and even entire countries. The faster you click, the quicker you spend. Challenge yourself to get the balance down to $0 and see how long it takes.

If you get tired before the money runs out (it's harder than it looks!), don't forget to get your receipt. Click the "Print Receipt" button at the end to see a full list of your extravagant purchases. Share it with your friends and see who has the wildest billionaire shopping cart!
