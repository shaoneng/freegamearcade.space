Sprunked

How to Play Sprunked

Welcome to the DJ booth of Sprunked! Your goal is to create unique musical compositions by dressing up your crew of characters, known as Sprunks. To begin, simply use your mouse or trackpad to drag an outfit or accessory from the selection and drop it onto one of the seven main Sprunks waiting on stage.

Each outfit you apply will activate a unique sound loop. These sounds are categorized into Beats, Effects, Melodies, and Vocals. Experiment by combining different outfits on different Sprunks to discover funky new combos and build your track layer by layer. To remove a costume and its sound, just click on the Sprunk wearing it.

Feeling a bit spooky? Look for the black button in the bottom right corner of the screen. Clicking this will activate Horror Mode, transforming the game with a haunting atmosphere, eerie visuals, and chilling sounds. It's a perfect twist for thrill-seekers, but be warned, it might be a bit scary for younger players!
