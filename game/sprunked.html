Sprunked

How to Play Sprunked

Your goal in Sprunked is to become a DJ by creating unique musical compositions. You do this by dressing up a crew of characters called Sprunks. The controls are simple: use your mouse to drag an outfit or accessory from the bottom of the screen and drop it onto one of the seven Sprunks waiting on the stage.

Each time you add a costume to a Sprunk, they will start producing a sound loop. These sounds are divided into different categories: Beats, Effects, Melodies, and Vocals. Experiment by mixing and matching different costumes on different Sprunks to discover new musical combinations and create a funky track. To remove a costume and stop a sound, simply click on the dressed-up Sprunk.

For an extra layer of excitement (and spookiness), locate the black button in the bottom right corner of the screen. Clicking this button activates Horror Mode, which transforms the game's atmosphere with haunting visuals and eerie sounds. Use it with caution, but if you love a good thrill, it's the perfect way to add a chilling twist to your musical creations!
