Sprunked

How to Play Sprunked

Your goal in Sprunked is to become a DJ by creating unique musical compositions. You'll start with a crew of seven 'polo Sprunks' on your screen, each ready to produce a sound.

To create your mix, simply use your mouse to drag and drop different outfits and accessories onto the Sprunks. Each costume you apply will add a new sound layer to your track, categorized as Beats, Effects, Melodies, or Vocals. Experiment with different combinations to discover unique beats and funky combos!

To remove a costume and its associated sound, just click on the Sprunk wearing it. This allows you to easily swap out sounds and refine your masterpiece. In addition to the main costumes, there are seven bonus outfits that unlock even more special sounds.

Feeling brave? Look for the black button in the bottom right corner of the screen. Clicking this button activates Horror Mode, transforming the game with a spooky atmosphere, haunting visuals, and eerie music. It's a thrilling way to give your track a completely different vibe!
