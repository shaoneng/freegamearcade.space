Sprunked

How to Play Sprunked

Getting started in Sprunked is simple and fun! Your goal is to create unique music tracks by dressing up your crew of characters, known as Sprunks. Use your mouse or trackpad to drag a costume from the selection and drop it onto one of the seven polo-wearing Sprunks on screen. Each costume you add will trigger a unique sound loop.

Experiment with different combinations to see how the sounds mix together! The costumes are categorized to help you build your track: some add Beats, others add sound Effects, some contribute Melodies, and others provide Vocals. To stop a sound, simply click on the costumed Sprunk to remove its outfit.

For an extra thrill, look for the black button in the bottom right corner of the screen. Clicking this will activate Horror Mode, transforming the game with a spooky atmosphere, eerie visuals, and haunting sounds. It adds a whole new layer of excitement to your DJ session!
