Sprunked

How to Play Sprunked

The goal in Sprunked is to become a DJ by creating your own unique musical tracks. You do this by styling a crew of seven "Sprunks" with various costumes and accessories.

To play, simply use your mouse to drag an outfit or accessory from the selection and drop it onto one of the Sprunks on stage. Each character is tied to a specific sound set, and every costume you apply changes their unique musical loop, adding to your overall mix.

The costumes are divided into different categories that produce distinct sounds: Beats, Effects, Melodies, and Vocals. Experiment by combining different items on different Sprunks to discover new and exciting musical combos. To remove a costume and its sound, simply click on the character wearing it.

For an extra layer of excitement, look for the black button in the bottom right corner of the screen. Clicking this switch will activate Horror Mode, which transforms the game with a spooky atmosphere, haunting visuals, and eerie sounds. It adds a thrilling twist to your music creation, but be warned, it can be a bit scary!
