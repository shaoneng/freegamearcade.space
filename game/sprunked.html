Sprunked

How to Play Sprunked

Welcome to Sprunked, where you become the DJ! The goal is to create unique musical compositions by dressing up a crew of characters called Sprunks.

Controls: Use your mouse or trackpad to play. Simply click and drag an outfit or accessory from the selection and drop it onto one of the seven polo Sprunks on the stage. To remove a costume and its associated sound, just click on the character again.

Creating Your Mix: Each of the seven main Sprunks represents a different sound set. When you apply a costume, that Sprunk will start playing a unique sound loop. These sounds fall into four categories: Beats, Effects, Melodies, and Vocals. Experiment by combining different outfits on different Sprunks to build a layered, custom track. Don't forget to try the seven bonus costumes for even more special sound effects!

Horror Mode: Feeling adventurous? Look for the black button in the bottom right corner of the screen. Clicking this switch activates Horror Mode, transforming the game with a spooky atmosphere, eerie visuals, and haunting sounds. This mode can be intense, so be prepared for a thrill!
