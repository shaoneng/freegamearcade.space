Sprunked

How to Play Sprunked

Welcome to the creative world of Sprunked! Your goal is to become a DJ by creating unique musical compositions using a crew of characters called Sprunks.

Use your mouse or trackpad to get started. At the bottom of the screen, you'll find various costumes and accessories. Simply drag an outfit and drop it onto one of the seven polo Sprunks waiting on the stage. Each costume you apply adds a specific sound layer to your mix, categorized as Beats, Effects, Melodies, or Vocals.

Experiment by combining different outfits on different Sprunks to discover new beats and funky combos. If you want to remove a sound, just click on the costumed Sprunk to take off its outfit.
