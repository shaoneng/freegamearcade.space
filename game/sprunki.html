Sprunki

How to Play Sprunki

Welcome to the world of Sprunki, where you become the DJ of a very unusual band! The goal is to create your own unique musical mix. To play, simply use your mouse to drag and drop different clothing items and accessories onto the quirky characters on screen.

Each item you add, whether it's a hat, a shirt, or an accessory, corresponds to a specific sound. Experiment by combining different items to create layers of bass, melody, drums, and other sound effects. See what kind of beat you can compose by dressing up characters like Mr. Tree, Mr. Sun, and Lime.

For an extra thrill, find the special black hat and place it on any character. This will transform the game, shifting the cheerful theme to a darker, spookier atmosphere. The visuals will become eerie, and the music will take on a horror movie-like quality. Unleash your creativity and discover all the musical and visual combinations Sprunki has to offer!
