Sprunki

How to Play Sprunki

Step into the role of a DJ in Sprunki! The goal is to create your own unique musical mix by dressing up the characters on screen.

Simply use your mouse to drag and drop different clothing items and accessories from the bottom panel onto any of the Sprunki characters.

Each item you add—like a hat, glasses, or shirt—corresponds to a specific sound, such as a beat, bass line, melody, or vocal effect. Experiment with different combinations to build a complete song.

For a surprising twist, try placing the special black hat on any character. This will transform the game's happy vibe into a spooky, horror-themed experience with darker visuals and eerie music. See if you can create the ultimate creepy composition!
