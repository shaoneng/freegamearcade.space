Sprunki

How to Play Sprunki

Your goal in Sprunki is to create music by dressing up the quirky characters. Start by dragging and dropping different clothing items and accessories from the bottom panel onto any of the Sprunki band members on screen.

Each item you add—whether it's a hat, glasses, or a jacket—unlocks a unique musical sound like a beat, a bassline, a melody, or a voice effect. Experiment with different combinations to build a complete and layered song.

For a spooky surprise, find and place the special black hat on any character. This will instantly change the game's theme, introducing darker visuals and eerie sounds. Mix and match horror-themed items to create a truly haunting musical experience.

The game is controlled entirely with your mouse. Simply click, drag, and drop items to play. To remove a sound, just drag the item off the character and back to the selection panel.
