Sprunki

How to Play Sprunki

Step into the role of a DJ for the quirky band, the Sprunki! Your goal is to create unique musical compositions by dressing up the characters.

Simply use your mouse to drag and drop different clothing items and accessories from the menu at the bottom of the screen onto any of the characters, like Mr. Tree, Mr. Sun, Lime, or Garnold.

Each accessory you add contributes a distinct sound to the mix, such as a bass line, a percussion beat, a melody, or a voice effect. Experiment with different combinations across the characters to build a complete and layered song.

For a thrilling twist, find the black hat and place it on any character. This will transform the game, shifting the cheerful atmosphere to a dark and spooky one with eerie visuals and haunting sounds. Unleash your creativity and see what kind of musical masterpiece—or nightmare—you can compose!
