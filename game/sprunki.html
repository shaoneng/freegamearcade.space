Sprunki

How to Play Sprunki

Welcome to the creative world of Sprunki! The goal is to create your own unique music by dressing up the quirky characters of the Sprunki band. Simply use your mouse to drag and drop different clothes and accessories from the bottom of the screen onto any of the characters.

Each item you add, whether it's a hat, glasses, or a costume, will add a new musical layer to your composition. Experiment with different combinations of bass lines, drum beats, melodies, and sound effects to create a track that's uniquely yours.

Feeling a bit mischievous? Find the special black hat and place it on any character. This will unlock the game's hidden spooky mode, transforming the visuals and sounds into an eerie, horror-themed experience. See how many creepy combinations you can discover!
