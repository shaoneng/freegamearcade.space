Sprunki

How to Play Sprunki

Sprunki is a unique music game where your creativity sets the beat! To play, simply drag and drop various clothing items and accessories onto the quirky band members. Characters like Mr. Tree, Mr. Sun, Lime, and Garnold are ready for your styling.

Each costume or accessory you choose adds a different musical sound to your composition, whether it's a bassline, melody, or drum beat. Experiment with various combinations to create your own unique tunes and orchestrate your Sprunki band.

For a surprising twist, try putting the black hat on any Sprunki character. This special item will instantly shift the game's atmosphere to a darker, spookier theme, changing both the visuals and sounds. Unleash your inner DJ and explore both the joyful and eerie sides of the Sprunki world!
