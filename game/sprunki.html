Sprunki

How to Play Sprunki

Your goal in Sprunki is to become a DJ and create unique musical compositions. You'll do this by dressing up the quirky members of the Sprunki band. Simply use your mouse to drag and drop different clothes and accessories onto the characters.

Each item you add, whether it's a hat, glasses, or shirt, corresponds to a specific musical sound, like a bass line, a percussion beat, or a melody. Experiment by combining different items on various characters to build a layered and dynamic song. There are no wrong answers, so let your creativity run wild!

For a thrilling twist, find the special black hat and place it on any character. This will transform the game, shifting the cheerful atmosphere to a spooky, horror-themed experience. The visuals will get darker, the sounds will become eerie, and your cute characters will take on a creepy new look. See what chilling masterpieces you can create!
