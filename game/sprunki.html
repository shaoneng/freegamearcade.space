Sprunki

How to Play Sprunki

Your goal in Sprunki is to create unique music by dressing up a band of quirky characters. Simply use your mouse to drag and drop different clothing items and accessories onto each character. Every item you add contributes a unique sound to the mix, like a beat, a bassline, or a melody. Experiment with different combinations to compose your own custom song!

For a surprising twist, try placing the special black hat on any of the Sprunki characters. This will transform the game's happy vibe into a spooky, horror-themed experience, complete with darker visuals and eerie sounds. See how the music and the characters change as you explore this hidden mode!
