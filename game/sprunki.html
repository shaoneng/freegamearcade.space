Sprunki

How to Play Sprunki

Your goal is to become a DJ and create unique musical tracks by dressing up the quirky characters from the Sprunki band.

Use your mouse to drag and drop different clothing items and accessories from the menu at the bottom onto the characters on screen. Each item you add corresponds to a specific sound, such as a drum beat, a bassline, or a melody. As you layer more items, you'll build a complete and unique song.

To unlock the game's hidden twist, find the special black hat. Dragging this hat onto any character will transform the game into a horror-themed experience, altering the visuals to become darker and the sounds to become more eerie and spooky. Experiment with all the combinations to create your masterpiece!
