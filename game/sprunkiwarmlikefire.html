Sprunki Warm Like Fire

How to Play Sprunki Warm Like Fire

Welcome to the fiery musical world of Sprunki Warm Like Fire! Your goal is to become a DJ and create your own unique songs by styling the members of the Sprunki band.

The gameplay is simple and intuitive. On the screen, you'll see the band members. At the bottom, there is a selection of different outfits and accessories. Simply click and drag an accessory from the bottom and drop it onto one of the characters to dress them up.

Each piece of clothing you add corresponds to a specific sound—a beat, a melody, an effect, or a voice. As you add more items, the music builds and transforms. Experiment with different combinations to discover new rhythms and harmonies. To stop a sound, just drag the accessory off the character.

Warning: Be aware of the hat icon located in the bottom right corner of the screen. Dragging this item will trigger a hidden scary mode. If you prefer a lighthearted and fun musical session, it's best to avoid using this particular item.
