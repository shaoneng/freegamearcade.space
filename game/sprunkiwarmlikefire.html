Sprunki Warm Like Fire

How to Play Sprunki Warm Like Fire

Become a master music producer in this creative mashup game! Your goal is to dress up the quirky Sprunki characters to create a unique song. At the bottom of the screen, you'll find a variety of fiery accessories.

Simply click and drag an accessory icon and drop it onto one of the characters. Each piece of gear you add—from outfits to hats—triggers a new musical element, like a bass line, a melody, or a vocal effect. Mix and match different items on various characters to build your own awesome beat. To remove a sound, just drag the accessory off the character.

Warning: For a light and fun experience, avoid dragging the hat icon from the bottom right corner. This specific item triggers a hidden scary mode!
