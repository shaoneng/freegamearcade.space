Sprunki Warm Like Fire

How to Play Sprunki Warm Like Fire

Your goal in Sprunki Warm Like Fire is to become a DJ by creating your own unique musical mix. The game is a creative mashup, featuring the quirky characters from the Sprunki universe dressed in the fiery style of Incredibox's "Warm Like Fire" mode.

To create music, simply use your mouse to drag and drop different outfits and accessories from the bottom of the screen onto the characters. Each item you add will trigger a new sound, like a beat, a melody, or a voice effect. Experiment with different combinations to build a complete song!

You can style characters like Mr. Tree, Mr. Sun, Fun Bot, and others. Mix and match their accessories to find the perfect rhythm and control the entire musical composition through your fashion choices.

Warning: For a standard, fun musical experience, avoid dragging the hat icon located in the bottom right corner of the screen. Interacting with it will trigger a hidden scary mode!
