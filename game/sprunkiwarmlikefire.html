How to Play Sprunki Warm Like Fire

Welcome to the creative world of Sprunki Warm Like Fire! Your goal is to become a music producer by styling a band of quirky characters. It's simple and fun!

Create Your Mix: At the bottom of the screen, you'll see a selection of icons representing different accessories like hats, glasses, and more. Simply click and drag an icon onto one of the Sprunki characters on stage.

Layer the Sounds: Each accessory you add gives a character a unique sound, contributing a layer to the overall songâ€”be it a beat, a melody, a bassline, or a vocal effect. Experiment by adding and removing different accessories to see how the music changes.

Explore Combos: Try different combinations across all the characters to unlock special chorus effects and build a complete, fiery track. The power to create a unique hit is in your hands!

A Quick Warning: Be careful with the hat icon in the bottom right corner. Dragging it onto a character will trigger a secret scary mode. If you prefer a purely fun and lighthearted experience, it's best to avoid that one!
