Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Welcome to the arena! Your mission is simple: survive. Use your mouse to aim your weapon and the left mouse button to shoot at the incoming waves of enemies. Move around the playground using the WASD keys and press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles and dodge enemy attacks.

Your arsenal is varied and powerful. Switch between your different weapons by using the number keys (1, 2, 3, etc.) or by scrolling with your mouse wheel. Each weapon has its own strengths, from fast-firing pistols to devastating bazookas.

For crowd control, press the 'G' key to throw a grenade into a large group of foes. When you encounter the giant, menacing dolls, switch to your most powerful weapons like the bazooka or grenade launcher to take them down quickly. Stay mobile, keep shooting, and see how long you can last against the squid army!
