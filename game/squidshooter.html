Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Welcome to the arena! Your main objective in Squid Shooter is to survive for as long as possible against relentless waves of enemies. Use your movement keys to run and jump around the playground, dodging hordes of green-suited participants and pink-hooded guards. It's crucial to stay mobile to avoid getting cornered.

Aim your weapon with the mouse and click to fire. You have a vast arsenal at your disposal! Switch between your weapons using the mouse wheel or the number keys on your keyboard. For a group of enemies, press the 'G' key to toss a powerful grenade and clear a path. Be on the lookout for enemy projectiles like cash grenades and exploding coffinsâ€”dodge them at all costs!

As you progress, you'll encounter the giant, creepy dolls. These are tough mini-bosses that can soak up a lot of damage. When you see one, switch to your most powerful weapons, like the bazooka or grenade launcher, to take it down efficiently. Keep your reflexes sharp, your aim steady, and survive the chaos!
