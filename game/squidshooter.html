Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Your mission in Squid Shooter is to survive waves of enemies in a chaotic playground arena. Use your keyboard and mouse to control your character and blast away the opposition.

Move around the arena by running and jumping to dodge incoming enemies and their attacks. Hordes of green-suited participants and pink-hooded guards will charge at you, so stay mobile to avoid getting cornered. They'll even throw exploding cash grenades and coffin crates your way!

Aim with your mouse and shoot to eliminate your foes before they overwhelm you. You can switch between your weapons using the mouse wheel or the number keys on your keyboard. Your arsenal is packed with everything from pistols and SMGs to powerful bazookas. To clear out a large group of enemies quickly, press the 'G' key to throw a grenade. For tougher enemies like the giant dolls, use heavy-duty weapons like the grenade launcher or bazooka for maximum impact.
