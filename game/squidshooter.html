Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Your goal in Squid Shooter is to survive for as long as possible against relentless waves of enemies. You'll find yourself in a large playground arena. Use the space to your advantage! Run, jump, and dodge side-to-side to avoid getting cornered by the hordes of green-suited participants and pink-masked guards.

You are heavily armed. Aim your weapon with the mouse and fire to take down enemies before they overwhelm you. You can cycle through your arsenal, which includes pistols, SMGs, and even bazookas, using the mouse wheel or the number keys on your keyboard. For large groups of enemies, press the 'G' key to lob a powerful grenade and clear a path.

Different enemies require different tactics. While standard enemies go down with regular fire, the giant dolls are much tougher. Switch to a bazooka or grenade launcher to deal maximum damage to them. Be vigilant and dodge incoming projectiles like exploding cash and coffin crates to stay alive as the waves get progressively harder.
