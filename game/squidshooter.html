Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Welcome to Squid Shooter, where your goal is simple: survive! You're dropped into a chaotic playground arena and must fight off relentless waves of enemies inspired by the famous TV show. Use your agility to run, jump, and dodge incoming attacks from green-suited participants and pink-hooded guards.

Your survival depends on your firepower. Aim your mouse to shoot and take down enemies before they swarm you. You have a full arsenal at your disposalâ€”switch between weapons like pistols, SMGs, and rocket launchers using the mouse wheel or number keys (1, 2, 3, etc.). For crowd control, press the 'G' key to lob a powerful grenade into a group of foes.

Watch out for tougher opponents like the menacing giant dolls. These enemies can take a lot of damage, so it's best to use your most explosive weapons, like the bazooka or grenade launcher, to defeat them quickly. Stay mobile, keep shooting, and see how long you can last against the onslaught!
