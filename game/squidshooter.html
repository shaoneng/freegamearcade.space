Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Welcome to the arena! Your mission in Squid Shooter is simple: survive. You'll face relentless waves of enemies inspired by the hit show, including green-suited contestants, pink-hooded guards, and the infamous giant dolls. You must eliminate them all before they overwhelm you.

Navigate the playground arena by running and jumping to dodge incoming attacks and avoid being cornered by hordes of enemies. Keep moving to stay alive!

You're equipped with a powerful arsenal. Switch between your weapons using the number keys or the mouse wheel to find the right tool for the job. Use your standard firearms for smaller enemies, and save your heavy hitters like the bazooka for the tough giant dolls. To clear out a crowd quickly, toss a grenade by pressing the G key. Aim carefully, stay agile, and blast your way through the squid army!
