Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Your mission in Squid Shooter is simple: survive! You are dropped into an arena inspired by the show, but this time you're armed and dangerous. Waves of enemies, including green-suited contestants and pink-suited guards, will rush toward you. You must eliminate them before they overwhelm you.

Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the battlefield. Run and jump to dodge incoming attacks and avoid getting cornered. Keep an eye out for projectiles like cash grenades and exploding coffins thrown by your foes.

Your arsenal is your best friend. Use the mouse wheel or number keys (1, 2, 3, etc.) to switch between your weapons, from a standard pistol to powerful SMGs and explosive bazookas. For clearing out large groups of enemies, press the 'G' key to throw a grenade. When you encounter the giant, durable dolls, it's best to use high-impact weapons like the bazooka for maximum damage.
