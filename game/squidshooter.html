Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Your main objective in Squid Shooter is to survive waves of attacking enemies in a playground arena. Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to fire your weapon. Move around the map by running and jumping to avoid getting trapped by hordes of green-suited participants and pink-hooded guards.

Your arsenal is key to survival. Cycle through your different weapons using the mouse wheel or the number keys (1, 2, 3, etc.) on your keyboard. Each weapon is suited for different situations. For crowd control, press the 'G' key to throw a grenade into a large group of enemies.

Stay vigilant for powerful foes like the giant dolls. When they appear, switch to a high-damage weapon like the bazooka or grenade launcher for maximum effect. Keep moving to dodge incoming projectiles like cash grenades and exploding coffins. As you clear waves, the game gets harder, so keep your reflexes sharp and your aim true to survive!
