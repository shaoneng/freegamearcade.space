Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Welcome to the arena! Your main goal in Squid Shooter is to survive against relentless waves of enemies inspired by the hit show. You'll face hordes of green-uniformed participants, supportive pink-hooded guards, and even the giant, creepy dolls. Stay on the move to avoid getting cornered and taken down.

Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot. Run and jump around the playground to dodge enemy attacks, including explosive cash grenades and coffin crates. Keep an eye on your surroundings and never stop moving to stay alive.

You have a powerful arsenal at your disposal. Switch between your weapons using the mouse wheel or the number keys to adapt to any situation. Use pistols for precision, SMGs for rapid fire, and save the heavy hitters like the bazooka for tough enemies like the giant dolls. Don't forget your grenades! Press the 'G' key to toss a grenade into a crowd and clear a path in seconds. Good luck, shooter!
