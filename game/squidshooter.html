Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Your mission in Squid Shooter is to survive as long as possible against relentless waves of enemies. You'll find yourself in a playground arena, armed and ready to fight back against characters inspired by the popular TV series.

Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the arena. Run and jump to dodge incoming attacks and avoid being cornered by hordes of foes. Constant movement is the key to staying alive!

Aim with your mouse and click to shoot. Cycle through your impressive arsenal, from pistols to bazookas, using the mouse wheel or number keys. For effective crowd control, press the 'G' key to toss a powerful grenade into a group of enemies. When you face a giant doll, switch to a heavy weapon like a bazooka for maximum impact!
