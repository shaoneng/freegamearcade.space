Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Welcome to the arena! Your mission in Squid Shooter is simple: survive. You'll face relentless waves of enemies inspired by the popular TV series, including participants in green tracksuits and the formidable pink-suited guards. Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the chaotic playground.

Move around the map to dodge incoming attacks and avoid getting cornered. Aim with your mouse and click to fire your weapon. You have a vast arsenal at your disposal! Use the mouse wheel or the number keys (1, 2, 3...) to switch between pistols, SMGs, and even powerful rocket launchers. When things get crowded, press the 'G' key to toss a grenade and clear out a large group of foes.

Keep an eye out for the giant dolls. These enemies are tough and can absorb a lot of damage. It's best to use your heaviest weapons, like the bazooka or grenade launcher, to take them down quickly. Stay mobile, keep shooting, and see how long you can last against the squid army!
