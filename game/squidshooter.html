Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Your mission is to survive waves of enemies in a chaotic playground arena inspired by the hit show. You'll face hordes of green-uniformed participants, pink-hooded guards, and even the giant, menacing dolls. They won't stop coming, so your goal is to shoot them down before they overwhelm you.

Use the standard WASD keys to run and the mouse to aim and shoot. Jump over obstacles and dodge side-to-side to avoid getting cornered. Keep an eye out for enemy projectiles like cash grenades and exploding coffin crates.

You're armed with a powerful arsenal! Switch between your weapons using the mouse wheel or the number keys. Use pistols for precision shots and SMGs for crowd control. For large groups or the tough giant dolls, unleash the power of your bazooka or grenade launcher for maximum damage. Don't forget to press the 'G' key to lob a grenade into a dense pack of foes to clear a path.
