Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Welcome to the deadly playground! In Squid Shooter, your goal is to survive as long as possible against endless waves of enemies. You'll face hordes of green-tracksuited participants, their pink-suited guard allies, and even the giant, menacing dolls from the show. Stay alive by eliminating every foe before they overwhelm you.

Use the WASD keys to run around the arena and the Spacebar to jump over obstacles and dodge enemy attacks. Constant movement is key to survival, so run and jump to avoid getting cornered by the growing crowds of enemies.

Aim with your mouse and use the Left Mouse Button to shoot. Your arsenal is vast, ranging from pistols and SMGs to powerful bazookas. You can switch between your weapons using the Mouse Wheel or the number keys (1, 2, 3...). For crowd control, press the G key to lob a grenade into a group of enemies.

Keep an eye out for enemy projectiles! Foes will throw things like cash grenades and exploding coffin crates your way. Dodge these attacks to preserve your health. For tougher enemies like the giant dolls, switch to your most powerful weapons like the bazooka or grenade launcher to take them down quickly.
