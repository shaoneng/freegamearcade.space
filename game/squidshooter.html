Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Your mission in Squid Shooter is simple: survive! You are dropped into an arena inspired by the hit show, but this time you're armed. Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot. Navigate the playground by using the W, A, S, and D keys to run, and the Space Bar to jump. You'll need to constantly move, dodge, and jump over obstacles to avoid being overwhelmed by hordes of enemies.

You have a powerful arsenal at your disposal. Cycle through your weapons using the mouse wheel or the number keys (1, 2, 3, etc.) to select the right tool for the job, from pistols for precise shots to shotguns for close encounters. When enemies group up, press the 'G' key to toss a grenade and clear them out in a single blast. Keep an eye on your ammo and reload (R key) when you have a safe moment.

The enemies will come in waves, including the iconic green-suited participants and the formidable pink-hooded guards. Watch out for special attacks like exploding cash grenades and coffin crates. The biggest threat is the giant doll—when it appears, switch to your most powerful weapons like the bazooka or grenade launcher to take it down quickly before it overpowers you!
