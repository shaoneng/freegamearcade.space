Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

In Squid Shooter, your main goal is to survive waves of enemies by eliminating them before they overwhelm you. You'll find yourself in a sandy playground setting, armed to the teeth and ready for action.

Use your mouse to aim and shoot at the incoming hordes of green-uniformed participants, pink-hooded guards, and giant dolls. Dodge side to side and jump over obstacles to avoid enemy attacks and stay clear of explosive projectiles like cash grenades and coffin crates.

To switch weapons, use your mouse wheel or the number keys (1-9). Your arsenal includes pistols, SMGs, and even bazookas. Press 'G' to lob a grenade, which is excellent for clearing groups of enemies quickly. For tough foes like the giant dolls, a bazooka or grenade launcher will deal maximum damage. Keep moving, aim steadily, and react quickly to blast through the Squid army!
