Squid Shooter

How to Play Squid Shooter

Welcome to the arena! Your main goal in Squid Shooter is to survive for as long as possible against relentless waves of enemies inspired by the popular show. You'll face green-suited contestants, pink guards, and even the giant, creepy dolls.

Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot. Move around the playground with the WASD keys and press the Spacebar to jump over obstacles and dodge incoming attacks. Keep moving to avoid getting cornered by the hordes of enemies.

Your arsenal is key to survival. Switch between your weapons using the mouse wheel or the number keys (1, 2, 3, etc.) to adapt to different threats. For large groups of enemies or tough foes like the giant dolls, press the 'G' key to throw a powerful grenade. Aim carefully and manage your ammunition to stay alive as the rounds get progressively harder!
