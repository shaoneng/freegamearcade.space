StabFish io 2

How to Play StabFish io 2

Welcome to the frenzied waters of StabFish io 2! Your objective is to become the most formidable predator in the ocean. You begin as a small marine creature with a sharp tusk, navigating a vast multiplayer arena teeming with other players.

Use your mouse to guide your character through the water. Your creature will automatically follow your cursor's position. To grow larger and stronger, you must hunt and consume the food scattered throughout the map, as well as smaller players. The more you eat, the bigger you and your tusk will become.

Be vigilant and avoid larger opponents, as they can easily eliminate you. Use a click of your mouse to get a burst of speed. This boost is crucial for both chasing down prey and making a swift escape from danger. Dominate the food chain, climb the leaderboard, and become the king of the sea!
