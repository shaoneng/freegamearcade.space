StabFish io 2

How to Play StabFish io 2

Welcome to the frenzied underwater arena of StabFish io 2! Your goal is simple: become the biggest and most feared predator in the ocean. You start as a small fish with a dangerous tusk, but with a little hunting, you can grow into a true sea leviathan.

Navigating your creature is easy. Simply move your mouse to guide your fish through the water. To gain an advantage, you can use a speed boost. Press and hold the left mouse button to charge forward, which is perfect for closing the distance on smaller prey or making a quick getaway from a larger threat.

To grow, you must eat the food scattered throughout the ocean and, more importantly, impale and consume other players smaller than you. The more you eat, the bigger you get, and the higher your score climbs. But be warned: the ocean is full of bigger fish ready to make you their next meal. Keep an eye out for larger opponents and use your wits and speed to survive and thrive.
