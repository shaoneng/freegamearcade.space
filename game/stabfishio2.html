StabFish io 2

How to Play StabFish io 2

Your goal in StabFish io 2 is to become the largest and most feared predator in the ocean. You start as a small fish with a tusk and must eat, grow, and eliminate your competition to climb the leaderboard.

Navigate the treacherous waters by moving your mouse cursor; your fish will follow its direction. To get a burst of speed and charge at your enemies, press the left mouse button or the spacebar. Use this boost strategically to catch prey or escape from larger predators.

The core of the game is to eat and grow. Consume smaller, non-player fish and food scattered throughout the ocean to increase your size and the length of your tusk. As you get bigger, you can take on larger player-controlled opponents. A successful stab will eliminate them, leaving behind food for you to collect.
