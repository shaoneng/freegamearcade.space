StabFish io 2

How to Play StabFish io 2

Welcome to the frenetic underwater arena of StabFish.io 2! Your primary goal is to become the largest and most fearsome predator in the ocean. You start as a small fish with a dangerous tusk and must hunt smaller sea creatures and other players to grow in size and power.

Use your mouse to navigate your fish through the water; your character will automatically follow your cursor. To get a burst of speed and charge at your enemies, click the left mouse button. This is your main tool for both hunting prey and escaping from larger threats. Be strategic with your boosts, as they consume energy.

As you consume food and defeat opponents, your fish will grow, and your tusk will become longer and more formidable. The bigger you are, the more opponents you can take down. However, always be vigilant! Larger players can easily eliminate you. The key to survival is to eat, grow, and skillfully avoid any fish bigger than you until you can turn the tables on them.
