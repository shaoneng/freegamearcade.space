StabFish io 2

How to Play StabFish io 2

Welcome to the treacherous waters of StabFish io 2! Your mission is to become the most feared predator in the ocean. You start as a small fish with a sharp tusk, navigating a sea filled with food and other players.

The key to survival is to eat smaller fish and scattered food to grow larger and increase your score. As you grow, your tusk becomes more powerful, allowing you to take on bigger threats. Be strategic with your boosts to catch prey or escape from larger enemies. Avoid getting stabbed by players bigger than you, as it will mean game over. Keep eating, keep stabbing, and climb to the top of the food chain!

Controls:

  • On a Computer: Use your mouse to guide your fish. Click the left mouse button to boost your speed and charge at opponents.
  • On a Mobile Device: Drag your finger on the screen to move. Tap the screen to activate your boost.
