StabFish io 2

How to Play StabFish io 2

Welcome to the frenzied underwater arena of StabFish.io 2! Your primary objective is simple: survive and dominate. You begin your journey as a small fish with a tusk. To grow bigger and stronger, you must hunt and consume the smaller fish and food particles scattered throughout the ocean.

Controlling your fish is intuitive. Use your mouse to guide your character through the water. Your fish will automatically follow your cursor. To gain a burst of speed and impale your foes, simply click the left mouse button. This boost is crucial for both hunting down prey and making a swift escape from larger predators.

Strategy is key to becoming the mega-beast of the deep. Always be aware of your surroundings; larger fish with bigger tusks can eliminate you in an instant. Focus on eating smaller targets to climb the leaderboard. As you grow, you can take on more formidable opponents. Use your boost wisely, as it consumes energy. Can you rise to the top of the food chain and rule the sea?
