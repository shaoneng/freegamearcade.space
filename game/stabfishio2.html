How to Play StabFish io 2

Welcome to the intense underwater arena of StabFish.io 2! Your primary goal is to become the most feared predator in the sea by growing your fish and eliminating your rivals.

Controls:

  • On Desktop: Guide your fish by moving your mouse cursor. Click the left mouse button to boost your speed and charge at enemies.
  • On Mobile: Use the on-screen joystick to control your movement and tap the boost button to dash forward.

Start your marine adventure by hunting for food scattered across the map and consuming smaller, non-player fish. Each meal helps you grow and increases your score. As you get bigger, you can take on larger opponents. A successful stab on an enemy will eliminate them, allowing you to feast on their remains for a massive growth spurt.
