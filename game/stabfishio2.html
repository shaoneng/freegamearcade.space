StabFish io 2

How to Play StabFish io 2

Welcome to the treacherous waters of StabFish io 2! Your goal is to become the largest and most feared predator in the ocean. You control a marine creature with a deadly tusk, navigating a chaotic multiplayer arena.

Use your mouse to guide your creature through the deep. Hunt and eat smaller, non-player fish to gain points and grow your tusk. As you evolve, you can take on bigger challenges.

To attack, use your boost ability (typically a mouse click or spacebar) to dash forward and impale other players with your tusk. Be strategic! A successful stab will eliminate your opponent, but missing can leave you vulnerable. Always be wary of players larger than you, as their tusks are a significant threat. Survive, grow, and conquer the leaderboard!
