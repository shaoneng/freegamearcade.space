StabFish io 2

How to Play StabFish io 2

Welcome to the treacherous waters of StabFish io 2! Your journey begins as a small but ambitious sea creature with a sharp tusk. The primary objective is to grow into the most formidable predator in the ocean and dominate the leaderboard.

Use your mouse to guide your fish through the ocean. Your fish will automatically follow your cursor. To attack, simply point your tusk at smaller opponents and impale them. Consuming food and defeating other players will help you grow in size and evolve your tusk, making you a more effective hunter.

Be strategic with your movements. While hunting is key, survival is paramount. Avoid larger players at all costs, as their tusks are bigger and they can easily end your run. Use your speed boost (usually by clicking the left mouse button) to either chase down fleeing prey or make a quick escape from a dangerous predator. Keep an eye on the food chain and work your way to the top!
