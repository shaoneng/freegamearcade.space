How to Play StabFish io 2

Welcome to the frenzied underwater arena of StabFish.io 2! Your main goal is to become the largest and most powerful predator in the sea. You start as a small fish with a tusk and must hunt and consume smaller creatures to grow in size and evolve your weapon.

Use your mouse to navigate your fish through the water. Your fish will automatically follow your cursor's direction. To get a burst of speed and charge at your enemies, click the left mouse button. This boost is essential for both catching prey and escaping from larger predators, but be mindful as it consumes energy.

Survival is key. Focus on eating the glowing food particles and smaller fish to increase your score and grow. As you get bigger, you can take on more formidable opponents.
