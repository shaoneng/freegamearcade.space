Stack Fire Ball

How to Play Stack Fire Ball

Welcome to Stack Fire Ball! Your objective is to guide a powerful bouncing ball to the bottom of a spiraling tower of tiles. It's a game of rhythm, timing, and destruction.

The controls are simple: just tap and hold your mouse button (or your finger on a touch screen) to make the ball smash through the colorful tiles below. The longer you hold, the more platforms you'll break in a fiery combo. Release the button to make the ball stop and bounce gently.

Here's the catch: you must avoid the black tiles at all costs! If your ball hits a black section of the tower, it shatters, and your game is over. Wait for the tower to rotate to a safe position before you continue your descent. Patience and quick reflexes are key to reaching the finish line of each level.

For an alternative view, you can press the 'C' key on your keyboard to change the camera angle, giving you a different perspective on the action.
