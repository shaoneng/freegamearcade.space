Stack Fire Ball

How to Play Stack Fire Ball

Get ready for an addictive and satisfying experience with Stack Fire Ball! Your goal is to guide the fiery ball to the bottom of a colorful, twisting tower.

Tap and hold to make the ball smash through the colored tiles like a hot knife through butter. But be careful! Hitting the black tiles will end your run, so you'll need to release your tap to stop and wait for the right moment to continue.

  • Objective: Reach the bottom of the stack to complete the level.
  • Gameplay: Tap and hold to smash through colored platforms.
  • Avoid: Do not touch the black tiles.
  • Controls: Use your mouse or finger to tap and play. Press 'C' to change the camera view for a different perspective.

It's a perfect blend of fast-paced action and careful timing. Enjoy the modern graphics and see how many stages you can complete!
