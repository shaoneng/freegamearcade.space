Stack Fire Ball

How to Play Stack Fire Ball

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Stack Fire Ball! Your objective is simple but challenging: guide a bouncing ball to the bottom of a spiraling tower of platforms.

To play, simply tap and hold your mouse button (or your screen on mobile devices). This will make the ball drop and smash through the colorful sections of the platform stack below. The longer you hold, the faster you'll descend and build momentum.

However, you must be extremely careful to avoid the black sections of the platforms. If your ball touches a black tile, it's game over! Release your tap to make the ball pause and bounce, allowing you to time your descent perfectly and avoid the obstacles. Reach the bottom of the tower to complete the level and advance to the next challenge!
