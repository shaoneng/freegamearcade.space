How to Play Stack Fire Ball

The goal in Stack Fire Ball is to guide your bouncing ball to the bottom of a spiraling tower of tiles. The gameplay is deceptively simple but requires perfect timing and focus!

Controls: Simply tap and hold your mouse button (or your finger on a touch screen) to make the ball fire down and smash through the colorful platform sections. Release to make the ball stop.

Objective: Crash through the colored platforms to make your way down the tower. The ultimate rule is to avoid hitting the black sectionsâ€”touching a black tile will immediately end your run. Aim to reach the bottom of each tower to complete the level and progress to the next, more challenging stage.

For a different perspective during gameplay, you can press the 'C' key on your keyboard to change the camera view.
