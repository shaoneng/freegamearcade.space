Stack Fire Ball

How to Play Stack Fire Ball

The objective in Stack Fire Ball is simple yet addictive: guide your bouncing ball to the bottom of a massive, rotating tower of tiles to complete each level.

Click and hold your mouse button (or tap and hold on a touch screen) to make the ball smash through the colorful sections of the tower. As you hold, the ball will turn into a fiery projectile, demolishing everything in its path.

Be careful! Release the button when you see the black tiles. Hitting a black tile will shatter your ball and end the level, forcing you to restart. Time your smashes carefully to fall through the gaps and avoid the obstacles.

The key is to build momentum and crash through multiple platforms at once for a satisfying combo. Good luck, and have fun smashing your way to the finish line!
