Stack Fire Ball

How to Play Stack Fire Ball

Your objective in Stack Fire Ball is to guide a bouncing ball to the bottom of a massive, rotating tower of tiles. It's a game of rhythm, timing, and pure destructive fun!

To play, simply click and hold your mouse button (or tap and hold on a touch screen). This will make your ball plummet downwards, smashing through the colorful sections of the tower. Let go to make the ball stop and bounce.

The main challenge is to avoid the black tiles. These are indestructible, and hitting one will instantly end your run, forcing you to restart the level. You must time your drops to land only on the colored tiles.

If you smash through several colored platforms in a row without stopping, you'll activate fire mode! In this mode, your ball becomes a blazing comet, allowing you to smash through even the tough black tiles for a short period. For a different perspective, you can press the 'C' key to change the camera view.
