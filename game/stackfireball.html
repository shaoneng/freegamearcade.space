Stack Fire Ball

How to Play Stack Fire Ball

Welcome to Stack Fire Ball, an addictive and satisfying 3D arcade game! Your goal is to get the bouncing ball to the bottom of the spinning tower of platforms.

Controls: Simply tap and hold (or click and hold your mouse) to make the ball smash through the colorful tiles. Release to make the ball stop and bounce gently.

Objective: Crash through the colored sections of the stack to proceed downwards. Be careful to avoid the black tiles! Hitting a black tile will end your run instantly, forcing you to restart the level.

Pro-Tip: Build up momentum by smashing through several colored layers at once to activate a fiery power-up, allowing you to break through even the black tiles for a short period. Time your drops, avoid the obstacles, and reach the finish line!
