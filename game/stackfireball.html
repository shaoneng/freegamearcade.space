Stack Fire Ball

How to Play Stack Fire Ball

Welcome to the vibrant and fast-paced world of Stack Fire Ball! Your objective is to guide the bouncing ball down through a helical tower made of colorful tiles. The goal is to reach the bottom of the stack to complete each level.

The controls are incredibly simple. Just tap and hold your mouse button (or your screen if on a mobile device) to make the ball fire downwards, smashing through the colorful platforms in its path. Let go to make the ball stop and bounce.

Be strategic! While smashing through tiles is fun, you must avoid the black sections of the platform. Hitting a black tile will shatter your ball and end your run, forcing you to restart the level. Timing and attention are key to success.

For a different perspective, you can press the 'C' key on your keyboard to change the camera view, helping you find the best path down. Now, let the fire ball drop and see how many stages you can conquer!
