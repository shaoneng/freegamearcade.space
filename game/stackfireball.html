Stack Fire Ball

How to Play Stack Fire Ball

Guide the bouncing ball to the end of each stage by smashing through the tower of tiles. Your goal is to reach the bottom, but watch out for the dark tiles! Hitting them will end your game instantly.

This game combines relaxation with the need for sharp attention. With modern graphics, you'll love the satisfying feeling of knocking down colorful tiles as fast as you can. Tap to play and let the ball bounce its way through all the stages!

Controls:

  • Tap to Play: Tap and hold to make the ball smash through the tile platforms.
  • C Key: Press 'C' to change the camera view for a new perspective.
