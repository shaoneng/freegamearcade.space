Stack Fire Ball

How to Play Stack Fire Ball

Welcome to Stack Fire Ball, a game of precision, speed, and destruction! Your goal is simple: guide the fiery ball to the bottom of a massive, rotating tower of platforms.

To play, just tap and hold your mouse button or your screen. This will make the ball smash through the colorful platforms below it. Keep holding to build momentum and crash through multiple layers at once for a satisfying combo!

However, you must be careful! The tower is also filled with black platforms. If your ball touches a black platform, your run is over, and you'll have to start again. Release your hold to stop the ball and wait for the tower to rotate to a safe position before continuing your descent. Master the timing of when to smash and when to wait to reach the finish line of each level.
