Stack Fire Ball

How to Play Stack Fire Ball

Your objective in Stack Fire Ball is simple yet challenging: guide your bouncing ball to the bottom of a spiraling tower of platforms. You must smash through the colorful sections of the tower to descend.

The controls are incredibly straightforward. Simply tap and hold your mouse button (or your finger on a touch screen) to make the ball drop and shatter the platforms below. Be strategic! If you hit a black platform, your ball will shatter, and you'll have to start over.

Build up momentum by breaking several colored platforms in a row without stopping. This will activate a powerful fireball mode, allowing you to blast through any platform, including the black ones, for a short period. Reach the finish line at the bottom of the stack to complete the level and advance to the next challenge!
