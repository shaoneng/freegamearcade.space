Stack Fire Ball

How to Play Stack Fire Ball

Welcome to Stack Fire Ball! The goal is simple but addictive: guide your fiery ball to the bottom of a massive, rotating tower of platforms.

Tap and hold your screen or mouse button to make the ball smash through the colorful sections of the stack. Watch as the platforms shatter in a satisfying explosion of color! But be warned, your run will come to an abrupt end if you hit any of the black, forbidden tiles. Timing and patience are key.

Release your hold to let the ball bounce safely on the platforms while you wait for the right moment to continue your descent. For a different perspective, you can press the 'C' key to change the camera view. Can you conquer the tower and complete all the stages?
