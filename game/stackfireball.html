Stack Fire Ball

How to Play Stack Fire Ball

Welcome to Stack Fire Ball, a game that tests your timing and reflexes! The objective is simple: guide your bouncing ball to the very bottom of a rotating tower of platforms to complete each level.

To play, simply tap and hold your mouse button or screen. This will cause your ball to fire downwards, smashing through the colorful sections of the tower. The longer you hold, the more platforms you'll break.

Be warned! You must avoid the black tiles at all costs. If your ball touches a black tile while you're smashing, it will shatter, and you'll have to restart the level. Release your tap to make the ball stop and bounce safely until you find another opening.

For an extra boost, try smashing through multiple colored platforms in a row without stopping. This will build up a power meter and transform your ball into an invincible fireball, allowing you to blast through even the black tiles for a short period. For a different angle on the action, press the 'C' key to change the camera view.
