Stack Fire Ball

How to Play Stack Fire Ball

The objective in Stack Fire Ball is simple but challenging: guide your bouncing ball to the bottom of a spiraling tower of platforms. To do this, tap and hold to make the ball fire downwards, smashing through the colorful sections of the helix stack.

Your timing is everything! As the ball descends, you must navigate it through the gaps and avoid landing on the black tiles. Hitting a black tile will shatter your ball and end the level, forcing you to start over. The key is to find a rhythm and break through multiple platforms at once to build momentum.

For a different perspective on the action, you can press the 'C' key to change the camera view. Focus, time your drops perfectly, and watch the tower crumble beneath your fiery ball to complete each stage!
