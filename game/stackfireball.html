Stack Fire Ball

How to Play Stack Fire Ball

Your mission in Stack Fire Ball is simple yet challenging: guide your bouncing ball through a tall tower of rotating platforms to reach the finish line at the bottom.

Tap and hold your screen or mouse button to make the ball blaze downwards, smashing through the colorful sections of the platform. The longer you hold, the faster you'll build momentum for a fiery combo!

Be careful! You must release your tap to stop before the ball hits the black, forbidden sections of the platforms. A single touch on a black tile means game over, so timing your descent is key to victory.
