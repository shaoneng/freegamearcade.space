State.io

How to Play State.io

Your objective in State.io is simple yet challenging: achieve total world domination by conquering every territory on the map. You will face one or more opponents who are trying to do the same thing, so you'll need to be quick and strategic.

The controls are intuitive and entirely mouse-based. To send your units, first click on one of your territories (highlighted in your color). Then, click on an adjacent territory (either neutral or enemy-controlled) to launch an attack. Your units will automatically move to capture it.

Strategy is key to victory. The numbers on each territory represent the number of units. To capture a territory, you must send more units than are currently defending it. Be mindful that sending units will deplete the territory they came from, potentially leaving it vulnerable. You can also reinforce your own territories by sending units from one of your lands to another.

As you progress through the levels, the challenge increases with more opponents on the map. Use the chaos to your advantage, attacking enemies while they are busy fighting each other. Think fast, act decisively, and lead your state to victory!
