State.io

How to Play State.io

Your goal in State.io is simple yet challenging: conquer the entire map and eliminate your opponents. This is a game of pure strategy where quick thinking and decisive action are key to victory.

Use your mouse to command your units. Click on one of your territories (the numbers indicate your current troop strength) and then click on an adjacent territory to send half of your troops to capture it. You can select multiple territories you own to launch a larger, combined attack.

Your territories will automatically generate new troops over time. The larger the number on a territory, the more troops you have. A key strategy is to upgrade your territories by sending troops to them, which increases their troop generation rate and allows you to build up a stronger army for your next conquest.

As you advance through the levels, the challenge increases. You'll go from one-on-one duels to facing multiple opponents at once. Use the chaos of multi-way battles to your advantage, striking when your enemies are distracted or fighting each other.
