State.io

How to Play State.io

Your goal in State.io is simple yet challenging: achieve total world domination by conquering every state on the map. You must use strategic thinking to expand your territory while defending it from enemy attacks.

The controls are intuitive and entirely mouse-based. To send your units, first click on one of your own territories (highlighted in your color). Then, click on an adjacent neutral (gray) or enemy territory to launch an attack. Your units will automatically move to capture it.

Success depends on your tactics. Territories with higher numbers have more units. Capture strategic points to cut off your opponents and overwhelm them with numbers. In later levels, you'll face multiple enemies, so you might let them fight each other while you expand into undefended areas. Think fast, act decisively, and conquer the map!
