State.io

How to Play State.io

Welcome to the strategic world of State.io! Your goal is simple but challenging: conquer the entire map and eliminate your opponents. The game is controlled entirely with your mouse.

To begin your conquest, click on one of your territories (your starting color). This selects it. Then, click on an adjacent neutral (gray) or enemy territory to send half of your units from the selected territory to attack it. You can also drag from one of your territories to another to consolidate your forces or launch a coordinated attack.

The numbers on each territory represent the number of units stationed there. To capture a territory, you must send more units than it currently holds. Keep an eye on your opponent's moves and protect your borders while expanding. As you advance through the levels, you will face more than one opponent, demanding even greater tactical skill.
