State.io

How to Play State.io

Welcome to State.io, an intense real-time strategy game where your goal is total world domination! The objective is to capture every state on the map by strategically deploying your units. You'll start with a home base and must expand your territory before your opponents do the same.

The controls are incredibly simple. Use your mouse to command your armies. Click on one of your territories (the number indicates your unit count) and then drag your cursor to an adjacent territory to send half of your units to attack or reinforce it. To send all units, simply click your starting territory and then click the target territory.

Victory requires both aggressive offense and smart defense. Sending units from one of your territories to another you already own will upgrade its unit production. Keep an eye on your opponents' moves, protect your borders, and look for weaknesses to exploit. As you progress, you'll face more than one enemy at a time, making the challenge even greater!
