State.io

How to Play State.io

Welcome to State.io, a fast-paced game of strategy and conquest! Your primary goal is to take over the entire map by capturing all the territories. You'll start with a single state filled with units, and your mission is to expand your empire before your opponents do.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use your mouse to select one of your territories (your states). Then, click on an adjacent territory to send half of your units to attack it. You can send units to neutral states to capture them or to enemy states to conquer them. Sending units to your own states will reinforce them.

Success in State.io requires sharp tactical thinking. You must balance expanding your territory, defending your borders, and exploiting your opponents' weaknesses. As you advance through the levels, you'll face more enemies simultaneously, making the challenge even greater. Plan your moves carefully, overwhelm your foes, and achieve total domination!
