State.io

How to Play State.io

Welcome to State.io, a fast-paced and addictive real-time strategy game. Your goal is to achieve total domination by conquering all the states on the map.

The controls are intuitive and easy to master. Simply use your mouse to command your forces. To launch an attack, click on one of your territories (the numbers indicate your army's strength) and then click on an adjacent enemy or neutral territory to send your units to capture it.

Strategy is key to victory. You can select multiple of your territories to launch a combined, overwhelming assault on a single target. Keep an eye on the numbers – a larger army will defeat a smaller one. Your controlled states will passively generate more units over time, so plan your expansion while also defending your borders from enemy incursions. Conquer the map and declare victory!
