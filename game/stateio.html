State.io

How to Play State.io

Welcome to State.io, a game of tactics and world domination! Your primary goal is to conquer the entire map by strategically capturing all enemy and neutral territories. It's a fast-paced battle of wits where every move counts.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use your mouse to play. First, click on one of your territories to select it. Then, click on an adjacent territory (either neutral or an opponent's) to send half of your units from the selected base to attack and capture it.

Pay close attention to the numbers on each state; these represent your army's strength. You can only capture a territory if you send more units than it currently holds. A key strategy is to capture neutral territories first to increase your overall troop count before launching a full-scale assault on your opponents.

As you advance through the levels, the challenge intensifies. You will go from one-on-one battles to facing multiple adversaries at once. You must defend your borders, anticipate enemy movements, and seize opportunities to expand your empire and become the ultimate ruler!
