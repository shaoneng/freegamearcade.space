How to Play State.io

Welcome to the world of State.io, where strategy is everything! Your objective is to conquer the entire map by expanding your territory and eliminating all your opponents. The gameplay is intuitive and relies on quick thinking and decisive action.

To play, simply use your mouse. Click on one of your territories (your starting state) to select it. Once selected, click on an adjacent territory to send half of your units to capture it. To send all units from a territory, you can click and drag from your state to the target state. Keep an eye on the numbersâ€”a territory with more units will conquer one with fewer.

You must act fast to capture neutral states and strategically attack your opponents' territories. Remember to defend your own borders as well! As you advance through the levels, you'll face multiple enemies at once, making the challenge even more intense. Plan your moves, outsmart your rivals, and lead your state to total domination!
