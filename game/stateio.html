State.io

How to Play State.io

Welcome to State.io, a thrilling game of strategy and territorial conquest! Your primary goal is to take over the entire map by strategically commanding your army of dots.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use your mouse to select one of your territories (your starting color). Once selected, click on an adjacent neutral (gray) or enemy territory to send half of your units to attack and capture it.

To send units from multiple territories at once, you can click and drag across several of your states before selecting a target. A bigger territory generates more units over time, so capturing and holding larger states is key to building a powerful force. Be mindful of your own defenses, as your opponents will be trying to do the same to you!

Outmaneuver your rivals, cut off their expansion, and strategically overwhelm their forces to claim victory and dominate the world of State.io!
