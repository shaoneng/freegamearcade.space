Stick Fighter 3D

How to Play Stick Fighter 3D

Dive into the glowing world of Stick Fighter 3D, a fast-paced, 1v1 fighting game with a stunning neon aesthetic. You can challenge the computer in solo mode (1P) or battle it out with a friend on the same keyboard in the local 2-player mode (2P). Take control of your glowing stick figure—Player 1 is Orange, Player 2 is Blue—and use a combination of punches, kicks, and powerful special moves to claim victory!

The objective is simple: be the last stickman standing. Each player has a health bar, and the fight ends when one player's health is completely drained. The smooth animations and flashy design make every bout an eye-catching spectacle.

Master the Special Move: Every fighter has a devastating Special Move capable of an instant KO, regardless of the opponent's remaining health. To use it, you must wait for the special bar at the top of the screen to fully charge. Once it's full, it will flash, signaling that your ultimate attack is ready. Time it perfectly to unleash the move and win the round. Be cautious, as your opponent has the same ability. Keep an eye on their bar and stay ready to defend!
