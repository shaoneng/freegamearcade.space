Stick Fighter 3D

How to Play Stick Fighter 3D

The objective in Stick Fighter 3D is to win a one-on-one battle by depleting your opponent's health bar. You can choose to play solo against a computer opponent (1P mode) or grab a friend and battle it out on the same keyboard in local versus mode (2P mode).

Use your character's designated keys to move, punch, and kick. A combination of fluid movement and well-timed attacks is key to victory. The Orange Stickman is Player 1, and the Blue Stickman is Player 2.

Keep an eye on the special move bar at the top of the screen. As you fight, it will gradually fill up. Once full, a flash will indicate that your ultimate move is ready. Press the special attack button to perform an instant KO on your opponent, no matter how much health they have left! Be warned: your rival has the same power. If you see their bar is full, stay on defense to avoid a sudden defeat.
