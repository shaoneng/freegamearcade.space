Stick Fighter 3D

How to Play Stick Fighter 3D

Welcome to Stick Fighter 3D! This is an intense one-on-one fighting game with a vibrant neon glow. You can either challenge the computer in a solo battle (1P) or grab a friend and fight them on the same keyboard in the exciting two-player versus mode (2P). The goal is simple: use your arsenal of punches, kicks, and special moves to drain your opponent's health bar and win the round.

Choose your mode from the main menu to begin. Player 1 controls the Orange stickman, and Player 2 controls the Blue stickman. Use your designated movement keys to navigate the arena and your action keys to attack. The fluid animations make every punch and kick feel impactful as you duel for supremacy.

The key to victory lies in the powerful Special Move. Above your health bar, you'll see a special meter. As you fight, this bar will charge up. Once it's full, it will flash, signaling that your ultimate attack is ready. Press the special move button at the right moment to unleash an instant knockout, winning the round regardless of your opponent's remaining health!

Be strategic! Always keep an eye on your opponent's special meter as well. If you see their bar is full, play defensively and watch for their attack. A single well-timed special move can turn the tide of the entire battle, so stay alert and fight smart!
