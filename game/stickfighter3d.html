Stick Fighter 3D

How to Play Stick Fighter 3D

Welcome to the glowing arena of Stick Fighter 3D! This is a one-on-one fighting game where your goal is to knock out your opponent. You can choose to play solo against a computer-controlled fighter (1P mode) or challenge a friend on the same keyboard in the exciting two-player versus mode (2P mode). Use a combination of punches and kicks to drain your rival's health bar to zero and win the round.

Each fighter, the orange Player 1 and the blue Player 2, has a powerful Special Move that can turn the tide of battle. Watch the power bar at the top of the screen. As you fight, it will slowly fill up. When your bar is full, it will flash, signaling that your special attack is ready. Time it right to unleash a devastating move that can instantly knock out your opponent, regardless of their remaining health!

Strategy is key. Always keep an eye on your opponent's power bar as well. If you see their bar is full, be on your guard! A single mistake could lead to your instant defeat. Dodge, block, and wait for the perfect moment to strike. Master the flow of combat and the timing of your special moves to become the ultimate Stick Fighter champion.
