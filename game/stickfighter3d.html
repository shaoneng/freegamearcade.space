Stick Fighter 3D

How to Play Stick Fighter 3D

Welcome to the electrifying arena of Stick Fighter 3D! Start by choosing your mode: take on the CPU in 1P mode or grab a friend for a local showdown in 2P mode. The objective is to defeat your opponent by depleting their health bar using a combination of punches, kicks, and special moves.

The controls are set up for two players on one keyboard:

  • Player 1 (Orange): Use W, A, S, D to move, F to punch, G to kick, and R for your special attack.
  • Player 2 (Blue): Use the Arrow Keys to move, L to punch, K to kick, and P for your special attack.

Keep an eye on the special move bar at the top of the screen. As it fills up, you get closer to unleashing a devastating ultimate attack. Once the bar is full and flashes, press your special move button to perform an instant KO! This move can win you the round instantly, so time it perfectly. Be careful, as your opponent has the same ability. Watch their bar and stay on your guard!
