Stick Fighter 3D

How to Play Stick Fighter 3D

Step into a glowing arena in Stick Fighter 3D, a fast-paced 1v1 fighting game with a stunning neon aesthetic. Face off against the computer in solo mode or challenge a friend on the same keyboard in local 2P mode. With smooth animations and flashy combat, it's a stickman battle like you've never experienced before!

Take control of your glowing warrior—Player 1 in orange, Player 2 in blue—and use a combination of punches, kicks, and devastating special moves to deplete your opponent's health bar and win the round. The controls are intuitive, making it easy to jump right into the action.

Keep a close eye on the special move bar at the top of the screen. As you fight, it will charge up. Once it's full, a flash will signal that your ultimate attack is ready. Time it perfectly to unleash a special move that can instantly knock out your opponent, regardless of their remaining health! But be warned—your rival has the same ability. Stay vigilant, watch their bar, and be ready to dodge when they're powered up.

If you enjoy the head-to-head dueling action of Stick Fighter 3D, check out Drunken Duel for another chaotic and fun two-player fighting game experience.
