Stick Fighter 3D

How to Play Stick Fighter 3D

Dive into the glowing world of Stick Fighter 3D, a neon-style 1v1 fighting game. You can challenge the computer in solo mode or battle a friend on the same keyboard in local two-player mode. The objective is simple: punch, kick, and perform special moves to deplete your opponent's health bar and win the round!

Player 1 controls the Orange Stickman, and Player 2 pilots the Blue Stickman. The flashy design, smooth animations, and vibrant aesthetic make every fight an eye-catching spectacle. It's stickman combat like you've never seen it before!

Keep an eye on the special bar at the top of the screen. When it's fully charged, it will flash, signaling that your ultimate Special Move is ready. This powerful attack can instantly KO your opponent, no matter how much health they have left. Time it perfectly to snatch victory, but beware—your rival has the same ability. Stay vigilant, watch their bar, and be ready to dodge!

If you enjoyed the back-and-forth dueling of Stick Fighter 3D, you'll probably love Drunken Duel. It's another hilarious head-to-head fighting game, but with a tipsy, ragdoll twist.
