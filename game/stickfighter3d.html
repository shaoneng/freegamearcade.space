Stick Fighter 3D

How to Play Stick Fighter 3D

Welcome to the neon-drenched arena of Stick Fighter 3D! The goal is simple: defeat your opponent in a one-on-one duel. You can challenge the computer in solo mode (1P) or battle a friend on the same keyboard in versus mode (2P). Each fighter has a health bar, and the first to deplete their opponent's health wins the round.

Control your glowing stickman using your designated keys to move, jump, punch, and kick. Combine moves to create combos and keep your opponent on their toes. The key to victory lies in mastering both offense and defense.

Pay close attention to the special move bar at the top of the screen. As you fight, it will gradually fill up. Once your bar is full, it will flash, signaling that your powerful Special Move is ready. Press the special attack button at the right moment to unleash a devastating blow that results in an instant KO, regardless of your opponent's remaining health! Be careful, as your opponent can do the same. Keep an eye on their bar and stay ready to dodge when they're powered up.
