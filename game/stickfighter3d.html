Stick Fighter 3D

How to Play Stick Fighter 3D

Welcome to the glowing arena of Stick Fighter 3D! This is a fast-paced, 1-on-1 fighting game where you can battle against the computer (1P mode) or challenge a friend on the same keyboard (2P mode). The goal is simple: deplete your opponent's health bar by landing punches, kicks, and devastating special moves before they do the same to you.

Each fighter has access to a game-changing Special Move. Keep an eye on the power bar at the top of the screen. Once it's fully charged, it will flash, signaling that your special is ready. Time it right to unleash an instant knockout, regardless of your opponent's remaining health! Be cautious, as your opponent can do the same. Stay vigilant, defend against their special attacks, and seize your moment to claim victory in the neon duel!
