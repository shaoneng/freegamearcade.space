Stick Fighter 3D

How to Play Stick Fighter 3D

Welcome to the neon-drenched arena of Stick Fighter 3D! This is a classic 1v1 fighting game where you can go head-to-head against the computer (1P mode) or challenge a friend on the same keyboard for some local multiplayer action (2P mode).

Your objective is simple: use a combination of punches and kicks to deplete your opponent's health bar and win the round. Master your moves and outmaneuver your rival to dominate the fight.

The ultimate weapon in your arsenal is the Special Move. Keep an eye on the power bar at the top of the screen. Once it's fully charged, it will flash, signaling that your special is ready. Time it perfectly to unleash an attack that will instantly knock out your opponent, regardless of their remaining health. Be careful, though—your opponent can do the same to you! Stay vigilant and watch their power bar to avoid a sudden defeat.
