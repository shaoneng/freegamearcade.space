Stick Fighter 3D

How to Play Stick Fighter 3D

Step into a neon-lit arena with Stick Fighter 3D, a thrilling 1v1 fighting game where glowing stick figures battle it out. You can challenge the computer in solo mode (1P) or fight a friend on the same keyboard in local versus mode (2P). The goal is simple: punch, kick, and use your combat skills to drain your opponent's health bar and win the round!

Player 1 controls the Orange Stickman, and Player 2 controls the Blue Stickman. The smooth animations and flashy, glowing aesthetic make every fight a visual spectacle you won't want to miss.

The Ultimate Special Move

Every fighter has a devastating Special Move capable of an instant KO, regardless of the opponent's health. To use it, you must wait for the power bar at the top of the screen to fully charge. When your bar is full, it will flash—that's your cue! Press the special move button at the right moment to claim victory. But be warned: your opponent has the same ability. Keep a close eye on their power bar; if it's full, stay on your guard, as one false move could spell your defeat.

If you enjoy the fast-paced dueling action of Stick Fighter 3D, check out Drunken Duel. It's another fun head-to-head fighting game, but with the hilarious twist of drunk ragdoll physics!
