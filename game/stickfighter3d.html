Stick Fighter 3D

How to Play Stick Fighter 3D

Stick Fighter 3D is an exhilarating 1v1 fighting game featuring glowing stick figures. You can choose to play in Solo mode against a challenging CPU opponent or engage in thrilling Versus mode with a friend on the same keyboard.

The objective is simple: deplete your opponent's health bar before they deplete yours. Utilize a combination of punches and kicks to land blows and wear down your rival.

Each fighter possesses a devastating Special Move. A bar above your character indicates its charge level. Once this bar is fully charged and flashes, you can unleash a powerful, instant-KO attack. Be mindful, however, as your opponent also has this ability; watch their bar carefully and be prepared to defend! Strategize your moves, time your specials, and dominate the arena to become the ultimate Stick Fighter 3D champion.
