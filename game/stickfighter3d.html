Stick Fighter 3D

How to Play Stick Fighter 3D

Welcome to the neon-lit world of Stick Fighter 3D! The goal is simple: fight your opponent in a one-on-one duel and be the last one standing. Deplete their health bar using a combination of punches, kicks, and special attacks to win the round. You can dive into the action solo against a computer-controlled opponent (1P mode) or challenge a friend on the same keyboard for some local multiplayer fun (2P mode).

Player 1 controls the Orange Stickman, and Player 2 controls the Blue Stickman. Use your designated keys to move, jump, punch, and kick. The animations are smooth and responsive, allowing for fast-paced combat. Combine your moves to create combos and keep your opponent off-balance.

The ultimate game-changer is the Special Move. Each player has a special bar at the top of the screen that charges during combat. Once your bar is full, it will flash, signaling that your powerful special attack is ready. Landing this move results in an instant KO, regardless of your opponent's remaining health! Be strategic—use your special move at the perfect moment, and be vigilant when your opponent's bar is full, as you could be the one getting knocked out.
