Stick Fighter 3D

How to Play Stick Fighter 3D

Welcome to the electrifying arena of Stick Fighter 3D! This is a fast-paced, one-on-one fighting game where your goal is to knock out your opponent. You can choose to battle against the computer in 1P mode or challenge a friend on the same keyboard in 2P mode. Each player controls a glowing stickman and has a health bar at the top of the screen. The first fighter to deplete their opponent's health wins the round!

Control your stickman's movements to dodge attacks and get into position. Use your assigned keys to throw punches and kicks, stringing them together into combos to overwhelm your rival. Player 1 controls the Orange Stickman, while Player 2 controls the Blue Stickman. Master your fighter's basic moves to gain the upper hand in the duel.

The key to victory lies in the powerful Special Move. Below your health, you'll see a special power bar that charges up as you fight. Once it's full, it will flash, signaling that your ultimate attack is ready. Press the special move button at the right moment to unleash an instant KO, winning the round regardless of your opponent's remaining health. But be careful! Your opponent is charging their special move too. Keep an eye on their bar and be ready to defend when they are about to strike!
