Stickman Destruction

How to Play Stickman Destruction

Welcome to Stickman Destruction, a game where causing mayhem is the main objective! The goal is simple: inflict as much damage as possible on the stickman character by breaking his bones in spectacular crashes.

To begin, select a vehicle and a map. On the game screen, you will see a "Start" button. Click and hold this button to build up power and speed for your launch. The longer you hold it, the more powerful the launch will be.

Once you've reached your desired speed, release the button to send your stickman flying! Strategically aim for obstacles, ramps, and hazards within the level to maximize the number of broken bones and earn points. The more creative and devastating the crash, the higher your score!

Use the points you earn to unlock over 22 different maps and a variety of vehicles. Each new map and vehicle offers unique opportunities for destruction, allowing you to create even more dangerous and high-scoring scenarios.
