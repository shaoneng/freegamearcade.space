Stickman Destruction

How to Play Stickman Destruction

Welcome to the controlled chaos of Stickman Destruction! Your primary goal is to inflict as much damage as possible on the stickman character by launching him into various obstacles. The more bones you break, the higher your score!

To begin, select a vehicle and a map. On the play screen, click and hold the "Start" button to build up power and speed. When you've reached your desired power level, release the button to launch your stickman and watch the destruction unfold.

Every collision and broken bone earns you points. Use these points to unlock a wide array of new vehicles and over 22 different maps, each presenting more dangerous and creative opportunities for mayhem. Experiment with different setups to maximize your score and become the ultimate master of destruction.
