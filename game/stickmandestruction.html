Stickman Destruction

How to Play Stickman Destruction

Welcome to the chaotic world of Stickman Destruction! Your main goal is to cause as much damage as possible to the stickman character. The more bones you break and the more spectacular the crash, the higher your score will be.

To begin the destruction, click and hold the "Start" button on the screen. This builds up power and speed for your launch. Watch the power meter, and when you're ready, release the button to send your stickman flying into the level's obstacles.

Use the points you earn from each round to unlock new content. There are 22 different maps and various vehicles to choose from, each offering unique ways to inflict damage. Experiment with different combinations of maps and vehicles to find the most effective ways to maximize your score.

You can also challenge other players! Stickman Destruction features a multiplayer mode where you can create your own room or join an existing one to compete against others in a festival of ragdoll mayhem.
