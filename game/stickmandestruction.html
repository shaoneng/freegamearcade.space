Stickman Destruction

How to Play Stickman Destruction

The main goal in Stickman Destruction is to cause as much damage as possible to the stickman character. You earn points for every bone broken and every collision, so aim for maximum impact!

To begin a level, select a vehicle and a starting position. Press and hold the 'Start' button to build up power for your launch. The longer you hold, the faster your vehicle will launch. Release the button at the right moment to send your stickman flying into the various obstacles on the map.

Use the points you earn from each crash to unlock new vehicles and over 22 different maps. Each map offers unique and more dangerous obstacles. Experiment with different vehicles, launch speeds, and map layouts to maximize your score and create spectacular, bone-crunching crashes!
