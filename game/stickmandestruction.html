Stickman Destruction

How to Play Stickman Destruction

The goal of Stickman Destruction is to cause as much damage as you can to the ragdoll stickman. The more bones you break and the more chaos you create by hitting obstacles, the higher your score will be.

To begin, select a vehicle and a map. Click and hold the "Start" button to build up power and speed for your launch. When you're ready, release the button to send your stickman flying into the level's various hazards.

Use the points you earn from your destructive crashes to unlock over 20 different maps and a variety of vehicles. Each new map and vehicle offers unique opportunities for mayhem, so experiment to find the most effective combinations for maximum points!

For an extra challenge, you can also join or create a room to compete against other players in multiplayer mode and see who is the ultimate destruction master.
