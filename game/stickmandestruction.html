Stickman Destruction

How to Play Stickman Destruction

The goal in Stickman Destruction is to cause as much damage as you can to the stickman to earn points. More broken bones and brutal collisions mean a higher score!

To begin, select a vehicle and a starting pose for your stickman. Once in the game, press and hold the 'Start' button to build up power for your launch. The longer you hold it, the more forceful the launch will be.

Release the 'Start' button to dismount and send your stickman flying into the course's various obstacles. Watch as the ragdoll physics take over and the points start to accumulate.

Use the points you earn from your destructive runs to unlock 22 different maps and a variety of vehicles, each providing a unique way to cause mayhem. Strategically choose your map and vehicle to maximize your score!
