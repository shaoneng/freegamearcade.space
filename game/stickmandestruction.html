Stickman Destruction

How to Play Stickman Destruction

Get ready for some physics-based mayhem! The goal in Stickman Destruction is to cause as much damage to the stickman character as possible. The more bones you break and the more chaos you create, the higher your score will be.

To begin, select a vehicle and a level. Each of the 22 maps offers a unique layout with different obstacles designed for maximum destruction. Once you're ready, press and hold the 'Start' button on the screen to build power for your launch. The longer you hold it, the faster you'll go.

Release the button to send your stickman flying! Strategically aim for walls, spikes, and other hazards to maximize your score. Points are awarded for every bone broken. Use your earnings to unlock new vehicles and even more dangerous maps to take the destruction to the next level.
