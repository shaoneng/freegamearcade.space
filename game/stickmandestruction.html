Stickman Destruction

How to Play Stickman Destruction

The goal in Stickman Destruction is to cause as much damage as possible to the stickman character. You'll earn points for every bone you break and every spectacular crash you create.

To start a level, select a vehicle and a map. Press and hold the 'Start' button to build up power for your launch. Release the button at the right moment to send your stickman and vehicle flying into the level's obstacles.

The more points you earn, the more you can unlock! Use your points to gain access to 22 different maps, each with unique layouts and hazards, as well as new vehicles that cause different types of impact. Experiment with different combinations to maximize your score.

Get creative! The key to a high score is strategically calculating the best angle and speed to hit the most obstacles. Have fun creating chaotically beautiful destruction!
