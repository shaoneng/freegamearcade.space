Stickman Destruction

How to Play Stickman Destruction

The goal in Stickman Destruction is to cause as much damage as possible to the stickman character to earn points. It's a game of creative, physics-based chaos!

To begin, select a vehicle and one of the available maps. Once the level starts, press and hold the "Start" button on the screen. The longer you hold it, the more power you will build up for the launch.

When you're ready, release the button to send your stickman flying into the level's obstacles. Every bone you break and collision you cause will rack up points. Use these points to unlock over 20 different maps and new vehicles, creating even more spectacular ways to cause destruction.
