Stickman Destruction

How to Play Stickman Destruction

Welcome to the chaotic world of Stickman Destruction! This game is all about causing as much damage as possible to a ragdoll stickman to get the highest score. The core gameplay is simple, but mastering the art of destruction requires creativity and strategy.

To begin, select a vehicle and a map. Press and hold the "Start" button to build up power and speed. When you've reached your desired power level, release the button to launch your stickman into the level. Your objective is to crash into obstacles, break bones, and create as much mayhem as you can to rack up points.

The more damage you cause, the more points you'll earn. These points are crucial for unlocking new content, including 22 different maps with unique hazards and a variety of vehicles with different destructive capabilities. Experiment with different combinations to maximize your score. You can even join or create rooms to play with others in multiplayer mode!
