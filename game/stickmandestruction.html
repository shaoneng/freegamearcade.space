Stickman Destruction

How to Play Stickman Destruction

Welcome to the chaotic world of Stickman Destruction! Your objective is to cause as much damage as possible to the stickman character. The more bones you break and the more spectacular the crash, the higher your score will be.

To begin the destruction, select a vehicle and a map. On the game screen, press and hold the "Start" button. This will build up power for your launch. Watch the power meter, and when you've reached your desired speed, release the button to send your stickman flying into the obstacles.

Use your creativity and strategic thinking to maximize the damage. Experiment with different launch powers and angles. The points you earn can be used to unlock a variety of new vehicles and over 20 unique maps, each with its own set of dangerous props and layouts.

You can even take the mayhem online! Create or join a room to compete against other players in multiplayer mode to see who is the ultimate master of destruction.
