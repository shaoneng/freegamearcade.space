Stickman Destruction

How to Play Stickman Destruction

Welcome to the chaotic world of Stickman Destruction! Your main objective is to cause as much damage to the stickman as possible to earn points.

To begin, select a vehicle and a level. On the game screen, press and hold the "Start" button to build up power and speed. Release the button to launch your stickman into the level's obstacles.

The more bones you break and the more damage you inflict, the higher your score will be. Use your points to unlock new vehicles and over 20 different maps, each with unique and dangerous environments. Experiment with different setups to maximize your score and create spectacular crashes!
