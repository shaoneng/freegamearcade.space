Stickman Destruction

How to Play Stickman Destruction

Getting started with Stickman Destruction is easy!

First, click and hold the 'Start' button on the playing screen. As you hold, your launch power will build up. Release the button at the desired speed to send your Stickman flying.

The main objective is to cause as much damage as possible to the Stickman by strategically utilizing the environment. Aim to break bones and create spectacular impacts to earn points.

Use your earned points to unlock new content! Explore 22 different maps, each with unique obstacle alternatives. You can also acquire various vehicles, each with distinct characteristics to enhance the destruction.

For even more fun, Stickman Destruction offers a multiplayer experience. Create your own room or join an existing one to compete with friends and other players, seeing who can achieve the most catastrophic destruction!
