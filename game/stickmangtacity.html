Stickman GTA City

How to Play Stickman GTA City

Welcome to the wild streets of Stickman City! Your goal is to thrive in this open-world sandbox. You can choose to follow the storyline by completing missions assigned by your Mafia Boss, or you can simply explore the vast city, creating your own chaotic adventures.

Use your controls to move your stickman, steal cars, and interact with the world. You'll need to dodge cops, engage in shootouts, and sometimes run over a few pedestrians to get where you're going. Master the art of driving and shooting to survive and dominate the city.

For an extra edge, use your in-game phone to activate cheats. You can instantly spawn tanks, helicopters, or even a jetpack to cause maximum mayhem. You can also visit the shop to change your Stickman's skin, with each avatar offering different stats for power, health, and attack.

Mature Content Warning: Stickman GTA City features frequent violence and crime-focused missions. It is recommended for mature audiences. Player discretion is advised.
