Stickman GTA City

How to Play Stickman GTA City

Welcome to the sprawling metropolis of Stickman GTA City! Jump into this action-packed open world and play by your own rules. Use your keyboard and mouse to navigate the city, interact with objects, and engage in high-octane shootouts. The primary goal is to thrive, whether by following the main story or by creating your own brand of chaos.

Your journey will involve taking on missions from your Mafia boss, but the city is your playground. Feel free to "borrow" any car you see on the street to get around faster. Be warned, the police won't take kindly to your activities! Evade them in thrilling chases and don't be afraid to use heavy machinery like tanks and helicopters to assert your dominance.

For an extra edge, use your in-game phone to activate cheats. Instantly spawn powerful vehicles, restock your ammo, or even get a jetpack to explore the city from above. You can also visit the shop to customize your stickman's appearance, with different skins offering unique stats for health and power.

Controls:

  • WASD: Move your character
  • Mouse: Aim and look around
  • Left Mouse Button: Shoot / Attack
  • Right Mouse Button: Aim
  • F Key: Enter / Exit vehicles
  • Spacebar: Jump
  • Shift: Sprint

⚠️ Mature Content Warning: This game contains violence and crime-related themes and is recommended for mature players. Player discretion is advised.
