Stickman GTA City

How to Play Stickman GTA City

Welcome to the sprawling, chaotic world of Stickman GTA City! Your goal is to navigate this open-world city, taking on missions, evading law enforcement, and rising through the criminal ranks. Whether you follow the story or create your own path of mayhem, the choice is yours.

Use your keyboard to control your stickman's movement and drive vehicles. Your mouse is used for aiming and firing your weapons or performing melee attacks. Interact with the world, steal cars, and engage in high-octane shootouts.

Take on missions from your Mafia boss to earn cash and respect, or simply explore the vast 3D city at your own pace. Be prepared to dodge the cops, as your criminal activities won't go unnoticed! From high-speed chases to intense gunfights, every moment is packed with action.

Feeling a little mischievous? Use your in-game phone to activate cheats! Instantly spawn powerful vehicles like tanks, helicopters, or even a jetpack. You can also visit the shop to customize your stickman's skin, with each avatar offering unique stats for health, power, and attack.

⚠️ Mature Content Warning: Stickman GTA City is recommended for mature players due to frequent violence and crime-focused gameplay. Player discretion is advised.
