Stickman GTA City

How to Play Stickman GTA City

Welcome to the chaotic streets of Stickman GTA City! Your goal is to navigate this vast open world, completing missions for your Mafia Boss or simply causing mayhem on your own terms. Use your movement controls to explore the city, interact with objects, and run from the law. You can steal any car you see to get around faster, but be prepared for a police chase!

Combat is a key part of the experience. Use your attack controls to shoot and fight enemies that stand in your way. The city is your playground, filled with powerful vehicles to command. Feeling destructive? Find and pilot a tank or a helicopter to wreak havoc from the ground or the sky!

For an extra edge, use your in-game phone to activate cheats. You can spawn vehicles like jetpacks, get full ammo, or unlock new weapons instantly. Don't forget to visit the shop to customize your stickman's appearance. Each skin comes with unique stats for power, health, and attack, allowing you to tailor your playstyle.
