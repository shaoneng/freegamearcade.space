How to Play Stickman GTA City

Welcome to the chaotic streets of Stickman GTA City! Your goal is to rise through the criminal underworld in this open-world action game. Use the standard keyboard controls to move your stickman around the city and a mouse to aim and shoot or attack.

You can choose your own path: follow the storyline by completing missions assigned by your Mafia Boss, or simply explore the vast 3D city at your own pace. Feel free to 'borrow' any car you see on the street to get around faster, but be prepared to outrun the police!
