Stickman GTA City

About & How to Play Stickman GTA City

Tired of searching for a free GTA game to play in your browser, only to be disappointed? Your search ends here! Get ready to relive the glory days of cruising through cities like in San Andreas or Vice City with an online game that captures the essence of open-world chaos.

Stickman GTA City is a 3D action-adventure game you can play directly in your browser, absolutely free. You'll be stealing cars, dodging cops, and probably running over a few stick pedestrians. Want to cause more mayhem? Tanks and helicopters are at your disposal!

Gameplay & Controls

Whether you're following orders from your Mafia Boss or just exploring the vast open world, Stickman GTA City lets you play it your way. Master the controls to own the streets:

  • Movement: Use standard keyboard controls (WASD/Arrow Keys) to move and drive.
  • Shooting / Attacking: Use your mouse to aim and the left-click to fire or attack.
  • Interaction: Follow on-screen prompts to enter vehicles, start missions, or interact with objects.

Tips, Tricks, and Cheats

Sometimes you just want to fly around in a jetpack without earning it. Use your in-game phone to experiment with cheats! You can spawn tanks, helicopters, and jetpacks, or fill your ammo and unlock new weapons without typing in rusty cheat codes. You can also visit the shop to change your Stickman's skin—each avatar comes with different power, health, and attack stats, allowing you to be the Usain Bolt or the Hulk of stickmen.

⚠️ Mature Content Warning

Stickman GTA City is recommended for mature players due to frequent violence, crime-focused missions, and intense action sequences. Player discretion is advised.

Developed by FreezeNova, Stickman GTA City is the love child of chaos and simplicity. It’s where you can enjoy the thrill of GTA without the guilt of actual crime. Dive in, have a laugh, and see if you can become the ultimate crime lord. If you enjoy it, don't forget to check out our other free GTA games for more action-packed fun!
