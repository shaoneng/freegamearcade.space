Stickman GTA City

How to Play Stickman GTA City

Welcome to the chaotic streets of Stickman GTA City! Your goal is to navigate this vast open world, complete missions, and cause as much mayhem as you like. Use standard keyboard controls for movement and your mouse to aim and attack.

You can follow the storyline by taking on missions from your Mafia Boss or go rogue and explore the city at your own pace. Steal cars, motorcycles, helicopters, and even military tanks to get around. Be prepared to dodge the cops as your crime spree intensifies!

For an extra edge, use your in-game phone to activate cheats. You can spawn vehicles, get full ammo, or unlock new weapons instantly. Don't forget to visit the shop to change your Stickman's skin—each avatar has unique stats for power, health, and attack, allowing you to tailor your gameplay style.

⚠ Mature Content Warning: Stickman GTA City contains frequent violence and crime-focused themes, making it suitable for mature players. Player discretion is advised.
