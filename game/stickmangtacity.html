How to Play Stickman GTA City

Welcome to the lawless streets of Stickman GTA City! Your goal is to navigate the open world, complete missions, or simply cause as much chaos as you desire. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to move your stickman around the city. When you're ready to cause some trouble, use your mouse to aim and the left-click button to shoot or attack.

To get around faster, approach any vehicle and press the designated key (usually 'F' or 'Enter') to steal it. You can drive cars, fly helicopters, and even command tanks! Keep an eye on your mini-map for mission objectives, police, and points of interest.
