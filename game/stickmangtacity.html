Stickman GTA City

How to Play Stickman GTA City

Movement: Use the W, A, S, D keys or Arrow keys to move your Stickman character around the open world.

Shooting / Attacking: Use your mouse to aim and click to shoot or perform melee attacks. Engage in intense action sequences and take down enemies.

Explore and Complete Missions: Dive into a vast 3D city, complete crime-focused missions for your Mafia Boss, or simply explore the environment. Steal cars, evade the police, and create your own chaotic adventures.

Cheats & Customization: Use the in-game phone to activate exciting cheats, such as spawning tanks, helicopters, or even jetpacks. You can also replenish ammo or unlock new weapons instantly. Visit the shop to change your Stickman's skin, with each avatar offering unique power, health, and attack stats to suit your playstyle.

Mature Content Warning: Stickman GTA City contains frequent violence, crime-focused missions, and intense action sequences. Player discretion is advised.
