Stickman GTA City

How to Play Stickman GTA City

Your long search for a proper, free-to-play GTA-style browser game is finally over! Introducing Stickman GTA City, a 3D action-adventure game that brings the chaotic fun of San Andreas right to your screen, no downloads required.

Get ready to steal cars, dodge the police, and maybe accidentally run over a few stick-figure pedestrians. Whether you're taking orders from your Mafia boss, causing mayhem with tanks and helicopters, or just exploring the vast open world, Stickman GTA City lets you play your way.

⚠️ Mature Content Warning

Stickman GTA City is recommended for mature players due to frequent violence, crime-focused missions, and intense action sequences. These elements may be unsuitable for younger audiences. Player discretion is advised.

Tips and Tricks

  • Cheats Activated: Feeling impatient? Use your in-game phone to activate cheats! Spawn tanks, helicopters, or even a jetpack without having to remember old-school codes. You can also refill ammo or unlock weapons instantly.
  • Customize Your Stickman: Head to the shop to change your character's skin. Each avatar comes with unique power, health, and attack stats, so you can tailor your stickman to your playstyle.
  • Find Your Own Path: There's no single right way to play. Embrace the open world, create your own story, and see what kind of crime lord you can become.

Developed by FreezeNova, Stickman GTA City is the perfect blend of chaos and simplicity. It's a game that doesn't take itself too seriously, and neither should you. Dive in, have a laugh, and enjoy the thrill of GTA without the real-world consequences. If you have a blast, be sure to check out our other free GTA games for more action!
