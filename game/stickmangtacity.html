Stickman GTA City

How to Play Stickman GTA City

Welcome to the lawless streets of Stickman GTA City! Your goal is to navigate this sprawling 3D open world, taking on the role of a stickman with big criminal ambitions. You can choose to follow the storyline by completing missions assigned by your Mafia boss, or you can forge your own path by simply exploring the city, causing chaos, and seeing what trouble you can get into.

Mastering the controls is key to survival. Use your keyboard to control your stickman's movement and to drive the various vehicles you'll inevitably "borrow." Your mouse is essential for aiming and firing your weapons or engaging in hand-to-hand combat. Keep an eye on your surroundings, as police will respond to your criminal activities.

The city is your playground! Hijack cars, motorcycles, and even military vehicles like tanks and helicopters to get around or to wreak havoc. The world is filled with opportunities for mayhem, from epic police chases to exploring hidden corners of the map. Don't be afraid to experiment and see what you can discover.

For an extra edge, visit the shop to change your stickman's skin, with each avatar offering different stats for power, health, and attack. Feeling a bit mischievous? Use your in-game phone to activate cheats! You can instantly spawn powerful vehicles like tanks and jetpacks, refill your ammo, or unlock new weapons without the hassle of traditional cheat codes.

Mature Content Warning: Stickman GTA City contains frequent violence and crime-focused themes, making it suitable for mature players. Player discretion is advised.
