Stickman GTA City

How to Play Stickman GTA City

Welcome to the wild streets of Stickman GTA City! Your goal is to thrive in this open-world city by completing missions, exploring, or simply causing chaos. Use your keyboard's WASD or arrow keys for movement and the mouse to aim and shoot or attack.

To get around the city faster, approach any vehicle and press the designated key (usually F) to steal it. You can follow the on-screen map to find missions given by your Mafia Boss or just drive around and explore the vast 3D environment at your own pace.

Feeling a bit mischievous? Use the in-game phone to activate cheats! You can spawn awesome vehicles like tanks and helicopters, or even get a jetpack for a bird's-eye view of the city. Don't forget to visit the shop to change your stickman's skin—each one comes with unique stats to help you in your criminal career. Whether you follow the rules or make your own, the city is yours to conquer!
