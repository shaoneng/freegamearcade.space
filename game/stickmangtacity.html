Stickman GTA City

About Stickman GTA City

Have you been searching for a free GTA-style game to play in your browser, only to be disappointed? Your search ends here! We've brought you an online game that captures the essence of classics like San Andreas and Vice City.

Stickman GTA City is a 3D action-adventure game you can play directly on your browser for free. You'll be stealing cars, dodging cops, and accidentally running over some stick pedestrians. With tanks and helicopters available, it's a world of chaotic fun!

Whether you're following orders from your Mafia Boss or just exploring the vast open world, Stickman GTA City lets you play it your way. Go out there, find your own path, and have fun!

Mature Content Warning

Stickman GTA City is recommended for mature players due to frequent violence, crime-focused missions, and intense action sequences. These elements may be unsuitable for younger audiences. Player discretion is advised.

Gameplay & Features

  • Open-World Freedom: Don't feel like doing missions? No problem. Grab a car, explore the city, or cause some mayhem. Maybe you can share your story in the comments below!
  • In-Game Cheats: Sometimes you just want to fly a jetpack. Use your in-game phone to spawn tanks, helicopters, and jetpacks, or get infinite ammo and new weapons without remembering old-school cheat codes.
  • Character Customization: Head to the shop to change your Stickman's skin. Each avatar comes with different power, health, and attack stats, letting you choose between being the Usain Bolt or the Hulk of stickmen.

Stickman GTA City is developed by FreezeNova. It's the perfect mix of chaos and simplicity, where you can enjoy the thrill of GTA without the real-world consequences. If you enjoy this game, be sure to check out our other free GTA games for more action-packed fun!
