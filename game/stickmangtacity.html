Stickman GTA City

About Stickman GTA City

If you've been searching for a free GTA game to play in your browser, your search ends here. Stickman GTA City is a 3D action-adventure game that captures the chaotic essence of classics like San Andreas and Vice City, all with a stick-figure twist.

Jump into a vast open world where you can play it your way. Follow missions from your Mafia Boss or simply go on a rampage. Steal cars, dodge the police, and maybe cause a little accidental mayhem. The city is your playground, and it's filled with everything from sports cars to military tanks and helicopters!

Mature Content Warning

Stickman GTA City is recommended for mature players due to frequent violence, crime-focused missions, and intense action sequences. These elements may be unsuitable for younger audiences. Player discretion is advised.

Unleash the Chaos

There's no single way to play. Explore, experiment, and find your own path to becoming a crime lord. Use your in-game phone to activate cheats—spawn tanks, helicopters, or a jetpack instantly. You can also refill your ammo or unlock new weapons without having to remember any cheat codes.

Head to the shop to customize your Stickman's skin. Each avatar has unique power, health, and attack stats, letting you choose between being a speedy rogue or a powerful tank.

Stickman GTA City is developed by FreezeNova. It's the perfect game to enjoy the thrill of GTA with a humorous, less serious vibe. Dive in, have a laugh, and see if you have what it takes to rule the stick-figure streets.
