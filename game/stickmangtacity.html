Stickman GTA City

How to Play Stickman GTA City

Welcome to the wild streets of Stickman GTA City! Your goal is to navigate the open world, complete missions, and cause a little chaos along the way. Use your keyboard and mouse to control your stickman character. Move around the city on foot, or approach any vehicle to hijack it for a faster ride.

The city is yours to explore, but you can also follow mission objectives given by your Mafia Boss to earn rewards and progress the story. Engage in shootouts with rival gangs and evade the police who will chase you down if your crime level gets too high. Use your in-game phone to activate cheats, allowing you to instantly spawn powerful vehicles like tanks and helicopters, or even equip a jetpack for aerial freedom!

Don't forget to visit the shop to customize your stickman's appearance. Different skins offer unique stats for power, health, and attack, letting you tailor your playstyle. Whether you're a mission-focused gangster or a free-roaming agent of chaos, the city is your playground.

⚠️ Mature Content Warning: Stickman GTA City features frequent violence and crime-related themes. It is recommended for mature players. Player discretion is advised.
