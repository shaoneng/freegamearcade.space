Stickman GTA City

About Stickman GTA City

Have you been searching for a free GTA game to play in your browser, only to find disappointing imitations? The search is over! We present Stickman GTA City, a 3D action-adventure game that brings the chaotic spirit of classics like San Andreas and Vice City directly to your screen, for free.

Get ready to dive into a vast open world where you call the shots. Steal cars, outrun the police, and maybe accidentally run over a few stick-figure pedestrians. From powerful tanks to soaring helicopters, the city is your playground. Whether you're taking on missions from your Mafia boss or just causing mayhem, Stickman GTA City lets you play your way. Have fun!

⚠️ Mature Content Warning: Stickman GTA City is recommended for mature players due to frequent violence, crime-focused missions, and intense action sequences. These elements may be unsuitable for younger audiences. Player discretion is advised.

Let's face it; sometimes you just want to fly around in a jetpack without all that pesky "earning it" stuff. Experiment with cheats using your in-game phone to make the game even more exciting. You can spawn tanks, helicopters and even jetpacks, fill your ammo or unlock new weapons instantly.

You can also change your Stickman's skin in the shop. Each avatar comes with different power, health and attack stats, so you can decide if you want to be the Usain Bolt or the Hulk of stickmen.

In the end, Stickman GTA City is the love child of chaos and simplicity, wrapped in a stick-figure blanket. It's not taking itself seriously, and neither should you. It's where you can enjoy the thrill of GTA without the guilt of actual crime. If you enjoy Stickman GTA City, don't forget to check out our other free GTA games for more action-packed fun! Play now and embrace the chaos!

Stickman GTA City is developed by FreezeNova.
