Stickman GTA City

How to Play Stickman GTA City

Welcome to the chaotic, open world of Stickman GTA City! Your goal is to navigate the city as a stickman, either by following the story and completing missions for your Mafia Boss or by creating your own brand of mayhem. Explore the vast 3D environment, interact with the world, and live the life of a stick-figure crime lord.

Use the standard movement controls to walk and run around the city. To get around faster, you can approach any vehicle—from sports cars to tanks—and hijack it for a joyride. Be warned, illegal activities like stealing cars and causing trouble will attract the attention of the police, so be ready to make a quick getaway!

Feeling mischievous? Use your in-game phone to access a variety of cheats. You can instantly spawn powerful vehicles like helicopters and jetpacks, refill your ammunition, or unlock new weapons without the grind. You can also visit the shop to change your stickman's skin, which not only changes your appearance but also modifies your power, health, and attack stats.

Mature Content Warning: Stickman GTA City is recommended for mature players due to frequent violence, crime-focused missions, and intense action sequences. These elements may be unsuitable for younger audiences. Player discretion is advised.
