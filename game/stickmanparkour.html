Stickman Parkour

How to Play Stickman Parkour

Embark on an epic adventure with Stickman Parkour! Your goal is to guide the agile stickman through 30 challenging levels, helping him find his way to freedom. Each level is a unique puzzle filled with obstacles and a single golden key waiting to be found.

The controls are simple and intuitive:

  • For PC/Computer: Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys to move left and right. Press the Up Arrow or Spacebar to jump.
  • For Mobile/Tablet: Use the on-screen touch controls to navigate your stickman.

In each stage, you must find the hidden key. Once you've collected the key, make your way to the locked chest to open the exit gate. Master the art of parkour by swinging on ropes, dodging sharp spikes, swimming through water, and perfectly timing your jumps to overcome all challenges. Can you complete all 30 levels and secure Stickman's escape?
