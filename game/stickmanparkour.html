Stickman Parkour

How to Play Stickman Parkour

Welcome to the exciting world of Stickman Parkour! Your goal is to guide the agile Stickman through 30 challenging levels. In each level, you must find and collect the golden key, which unlocks the gate to the next stage. Navigate the environment, overcome obstacles, and help Stickman find his way out.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use the arrow keys or WASD on your computer to move left, right, and jump. If you're playing on a mobile device, use the on-screen touch controls to guide Stickman's movements. Master the timing of your jumps to clear gaps and reach new platforms.

Be careful! The levels are filled with hazards like sharp spikes and deep water. You'll need to use your parkour skills to swing on ropes, leap over dangers, and even swim to safety. Collect all the keys, open every gate, and complete this epic adventure!
