Stickman Parkour

How to Play Stickman Parkour

Your goal in Stickman Parkour is to guide your athletic stick figure through 30 unique levels. In each level, you must find and collect the golden key. This key will unlock the gate to the next stage of your adventure, bringing you one step closer to finding the way out.

Use your keyboard controls (arrow keys or WASD) or on-screen mobile controls to run, jump, and climb. Navigate a world filled with tricky platforms, sharp spikes, and other hazards. You'll need to time your jumps perfectly, swing on ropes like a pro, and even swim through water sections to overcome the challenges that stand in your way.

Master the simple controls and think quickly to solve each level's puzzle. Help the stickman find his way out by collecting all the keys and conquering every obstacle in this epic parkour journey!
