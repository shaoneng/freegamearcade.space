Stickman Parkour

How to Play Stickman Parkour

Your goal in Stickman Parkour is to guide your athletic stick figure through a series of challenging levels. The main objective in each stage is to find and collect the golden key. This key is your ticket to unlocking the gate and advancing to the next exciting challenge.

Navigate the world using your keyboard or touch controls. Make your stickman run, jump, and climb to overcome obstacles. You'll need precise timing to leap over deadly spikes, swing across gaps on ropes, and even swim through watery passages. Keep an eye on your surroundings to avoid traps and find the safest path forward.

With 30 levels to master, the difficulty will increase as you progress. Each level presents a unique layout and a new set of puzzles to solve. Collect the key, open the gate, and prove you have what it takes to complete this epic parkour adventure!
