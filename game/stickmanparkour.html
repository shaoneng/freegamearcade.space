Stickman Parkour

How to Play Stickman Parkour

Your goal in Stickman Parkour is to guide your athletic stick figure through a series of challenging levels. The main objective is to collect all the golden keys scattered throughout each stage to unlock the exit gate and advance.

Use your keyboard controls (arrow keys or WASD) or on-screen mobile controls to run, jump, and climb. You'll need to master your timing to leap over gaps, dodge deadly spikes, and swing on ropes like a true parkour expert. Some levels even require you to swim through water to reach new areas.

With 30 unique levels to conquer, the difficulty will increase as you progress. Stay alert, plan your moves carefully, and help Stickman find his way out. The game is easy to pick up but offers a satisfying challenge for those who aim to master all 30 levels.
