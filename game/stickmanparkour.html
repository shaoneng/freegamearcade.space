Stickman Parkour

How to Play Stickman Parkour

Your mission in Stickman Parkour is to navigate our nimble hero through 30 levels packed with action and obstacles. The main goal on each stage is to collect the golden key, which unlocks the gate to the next level. Prepare to run, jump, and climb your way to victory!

For PC Players: Use the Arrow Keys or W, A, D keys for movement. The 'A' key moves you left, 'D' moves you right, and the 'W' or Up Arrow key makes you jump. Precision and timing are essential to dodge sharp spikes and leap across wide gaps.

For Mobile Players: The game features intuitive on-screen controls. Use the directional arrows on the left side of the screen to move, and tap the jump button on the right to leap into the air.

Throughout your adventure, you'll encounter special challenges like swinging on ropes and swimming through water. Master these skills to conquer every level and help Stickman complete his epic parkour quest!
