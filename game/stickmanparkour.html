Stickman Parkour

How to Play Stickman Parkour

Welcome to the agile world of Stickman Parkour! Your main goal is to guide the stickman through 30 increasingly difficult levels. In each stage, you must navigate a treacherous course to find and collect golden keys. These keys are essential for unlocking the ancient chests and gates that block your path, allowing you to proceed to the next challenge.

As you run, jump, and climb, you'll need sharp reflexes to survive. The levels are littered with dangerous obstacles, including sharp spikes, deep water, and tricky platforms. You'll also get to swing on ropes like a true parkour master. The mechanics are simple to pick up, but mastering the timing and precision required to complete all the levels will be a true test of your skills.

The controls are intuitive for any platformer fan. On a computer, use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys to move your stickman left and right, and to jump. On mobile devices, simply use the on-screen touch controls to navigate the course. Help the stickman find his way out and complete his epic adventure!
