Stunt Rider

How to Play Stunt Rider

Welcome to Stunt Rider, the ultimate 3D driving sandbox! The main goal is to drive your high-performance car through various landscapes, using massive ramps to perform incredible aerial stunts. Push the pedal to the metal and explore everything the world has to offer.

Use your standard driving controls to accelerate, brake, and steer. When you launch off a ramp, maneuver your car in mid-air to execute amazing flips and barrel rolls. The crazier the stunt, the more points you'll earn! Also, look for 'F' icons scattered around the map to collect bonus points.

Choose from four distinct playgrounds: the city center, a rural road, an industrial zone, or a snowy area. Each map is packed with ramps, tunnels, and obstacles. Don't be afraid to crash and cause some chaos! If your car gets too damaged, simply press the 'F' key to instantly repair it and get back to the action without missing a beat.
