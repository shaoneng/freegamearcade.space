Stunt Rider

How to Play Stunt Rider

Get behind the wheel in Stunt Rider, a 3D driving game focused on freedom and spectacular stunts. Your main goal is to explore four diverse maps—City, Rural, Industrial, and Snowy—and use the massive ramps to launch your car into the air.

Perform incredible aerial maneuvers like backflips and barrel rolls to earn points. While in the air, use your directional controls to spin and flip your vehicle. You can also earn points by drifting and by collecting the floating "F" icons scattered throughout each level.

Don't worry about crashing or damaging your car. Part of the fun is seeing what happens! If your vehicle gets wrecked, you can instantly restore it to perfect condition by pressing the F key. Now, hit the gas and unleash your inner stunt driver!
