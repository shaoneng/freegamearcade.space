Stunt Rider

How to Play Stunt Rider

Welcome to Stunt Rider, the ultimate 3D driving sandbox! The goal isn't to race, but to explore and perform the most incredible stunts imaginable. Put the pedal to the metal and speed towards massive ramps scattered across four distinct, open-world maps: the bustling city center, a quiet rural road, a gritty industrial zone, and a slippery snowy area.

Use your keyboard to control your vehicle. Once you launch into the air off a ramp, maneuver your car left or right to execute breathtaking barrel rolls and full backflips. The crazier the stunt, the more points you'll earn! Also, keep an eye out for floating 'F' icons on the road to collect bonus points.

Don't be afraid to push the limits and crash! In Stunt Rider, wrecking your vehicle is part of the fun. No matter how much damage you take, you can instantly restore your car to perfect condition by simply pressing the 'F' key. Now get out there, explore, and become the ultimate stunt driver!
