Stunt Rider

How to Play Stunt Rider

Welcome to the high-flying world of Stunt Rider! The main objective is to drive powerful cars across four expansive maps and perform breathtaking stunts. Explore the city, countryside, industrial zone, or snowy mountains, each filled with ramps, tunnels, and obstacles designed for ultimate driving freedom.

Use your driving skills to hit the ramps at top speed. While airborne, maneuver your car to perform incredible backflips, barrel rolls, and somersaults to rack up points. You can also earn points by executing perfect drifts on the ground. Keep an eye out for 'F' point collectibles scattered throughout the maps for an extra score boost.

Don't be afraid to push your car to its limits! In Stunt Rider, you can crash and smash your vehicle as much as you like. If your car gets too damaged, simply press the 'F' key to instantly repair it and get back to the action. Embrace the chaos and see what spectacular stunts you can pull off!
