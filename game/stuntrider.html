Stunt Rider

How to Play Stunt Rider

Welcome to Stunt Rider, the ultimate 3D driving sandbox! Your main objective is to have fun by performing incredible stunts. Take control of your car and speed towards massive, sky-high ramps scattered across four distinct maps: the city, rural roads, an industrial park, and a snowy wonderland.

Use your driving skills to launch your vehicle into the air. While airborne, maneuver your car left or right to perform breathtaking barrel rolls and complete backflips. Combine different moves to see what crazy stunts you can pull off. The more spectacular the jump, the more points you'll earn!

Keep an eye out for "F" points on the road. Collecting these will boost your score. Don't be afraid to push the limits and get a little reckless. In Stunt Rider, crashing is part of the fun. If your car gets too banged up, simply press the 'F' key to instantly repair it and get back to the action.
