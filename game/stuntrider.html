Stunt Rider

How to Play Stunt Rider

Your goal in Stunt Rider is to drive powerful cars across vast, open-world maps and perform the most incredible stunts imaginable. Push the pedal to the metal and speed towards massive ramps, tunnels, and destructible objects. The objective is pure freedom—enjoy the thrill of high-speed driving and see what crazy maneuvers you can pull off.

Earn points by executing spectacular jumps, long drifts, and mid-air flips. While exploring, keep an eye out for floating 'F' icons on the road; collecting them will grant you bonus points. To perform aerial stunts like backflips and barrel rolls, drive onto a ramp at maximum speed. Once airborne, use the controls to maneuver your car left or right to start a roll or flip.

Don't be afraid to push your car to its limits! Scratches and crashes are part of the fun. If your vehicle gets too damaged, you can instantly repair it back to pristine condition by pressing the 'F' key. With four unique environments to explore—the city, rural roads, an industrial zone, and a snowy area—the possibilities for chaotic fun are endless. Challenge yourself to master every ramp and obstacle without a scratch, or simply unleash chaos and enjoy the ride!
