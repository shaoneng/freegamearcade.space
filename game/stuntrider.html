Stunt Rider

How to Play Stunt Rider

Welcome to the high-octane world of Stunt Rider! The objective is simple: drive awesome cars and perform the most insane stunts possible. Use your keyboard to accelerate, brake, and steer your vehicle across four distinct, sprawling maps: the city, a rural road, an industrial zone, and a snowy landscape.

Each map is a playground filled with sky-high ramps, loops, tunnels, and destructible obstacles. Launch your car off ramps and use your steering controls while airborne to perform breathtaking flips, barrel rolls, and somersaults. The crazier the stunt, the more points you earn!

Keep an eye out for "F" point collectibles scattered throughout the maps to boost your score. Don't be afraid to crash and cause some chaos—your car can take a beating! If it gets too damaged, simply press the 'F' key to instantly repair it and get back to the action. Your only limit is your imagination, so hit the gas and see what incredible stunts you can pull off!
