Stunt Rider

How to Play Stunt Rider

Welcome to Stunt Rider! The goal is simple: drive awesome cars and perform incredible stunts. Use the arrow keys or WASD to accelerate, brake, and steer your vehicle. Explore four different maps, including a city, rural roads, an industrial area, and a snowy landscape, each filled with unique opportunities for mayhem.

The world is your playground! Use the numerous ramps scattered across each map to launch your car into the air. While airborne, use your steering controls to perform amazing barrel rolls and flips. The more spectacular your jump or drift, the more points you'll earn. Also, be sure to collect the floating 'F' icons to boost your score.

Don't be afraid to crash and cause some destruction! If your car gets too banged up, you can instantly fix it. Simply press the 'F' key to repair your vehicle and get right back to the action without missing a beat.
