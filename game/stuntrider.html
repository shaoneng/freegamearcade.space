Stunt Rider

How to Play Stunt Rider

Your goal in Stunt Rider is to drive high-performance cars across vast, open-world maps and perform the most insane stunts imaginable. Explore four different playgrounds—the city, a rural road, an industrial area, and a snowy landscape—each filled with massive ramps, tunnels, and obstacles designed for spectacular action.

Use your keyboard to control the car. Accelerate towards ramps at top speed to launch into the air. While airborne, maneuver your vehicle left or right to execute breathtaking barrel rolls and somersaults. The more daring the stunt, the more points you'll earn.

Keep an eye out for 'F' point icons scattered throughout the maps to boost your score. Don't worry about damaging your ride; this game encourages crashing! If your car gets too banged up, simply press the 'F' key to instantly repair it and get back to the action. Now, get out there and defy gravity!
