Stunt Rider

How to Play Stunt Rider

Welcome to the high-flying world of Stunt Rider! Your main goal is to drive your car at top speed and launch off massive ramps to perform incredible aerial stunts. Explore four unique playgrounds: the city center, a rural road, an industrial park, and a snowy mountain area. Each map is filled with ramps, tunnels, and obstacles designed for maximum fun.

As you drive, keep an eye out for "F" points scattered on the road. Collecting these will boost your score. You also earn points for executing spectacular jumps and long drifts. The crazier the stunt, the higher your score!

To perform tricks like barrel rolls and somersaults, hit a ramp at full speed. While your car is airborne, use the directional controls (left/right) to maneuver and rotate your vehicle. Experiment with different combinations to see what amazing stunts you can pull off.

Don't be afraid to crash! This game encourages you to push the limits. If your car gets damaged, you can instantly repair it back to pristine condition by pressing the 'F' key. Now get out there and defy gravity!
