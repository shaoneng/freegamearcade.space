Stunt Rider

How to Play Stunt Rider

Welcome to the high-octane world of Stunt Rider! Your objective is to drive a powerful car through vast, open-world environments and perform the most spectacular stunts imaginable. The goal isn't to race against others, but to push the limits of your car and your skills by executing daring jumps, drifts, and mid-air flips.

Explore four distinct playgrounds: the bustling city center, a quiet rural road, a gritty industrial zone, and a treacherous snowy area. Each map is filled with massive ramps, loops, tunnels, and other obstacles designed for maximum stunt potential. Put the pedal to the metal and launch your car into the sky!

Earn points by performing stylish maneuvers. You'll be rewarded for impressive jumps and long, controlled drifts. Keep an eye out for glowing "F" points scattered across the map to get an instant score boost. While in the air, use the controls to maneuver your car, performing barrel rolls and somersaults for even more points.

Don't be afraid to get a little reckless! In Stunt Rider, crashing is part of the fun. If your car gets too banged up, you can instantly repair it to pristine condition by pressing the 'F' key. This allows you to experiment with the craziest stunts without any consequences. Now get out there and become the ultimate Stunt Rider!
