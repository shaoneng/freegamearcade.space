Stunt Rider

How to Play Stunt Rider

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of Stunt Rider! Your objective is to drive high-performance cars across vast, open-world maps filled with ramps, loops, and obstacles. The goal isn't just to drive, but to perform the most spectacular stunts imaginable, from soaring backflips to dizzying barrel rolls.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to control your vehicle. Hit the gas and accelerate towards the massive ramps scattered throughout each environment. While airborne, use your steering controls to maneuver your car, performing flips and rolls to earn points. If your car gets too banged up from all the action, simply press the F key to instantly repair it and get back to the fun.

Explore four unique playgrounds: the bustling city center, quiet rural roads, a rugged industrial zone, and a slippery snowy area. As you drive, keep an eye out for floating "F" icons. Collecting these will grant you bonus points. Challenge yourself to chain together drifts and jumps to maximize your score, or simply enjoy the freedom of causing chaos without consequences. It's all about having fun and pushing the limits of what your car can do!
