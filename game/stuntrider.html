How to Play Stunt Rider

Welcome to Stunt Rider, the ultimate 3D driving sandbox where the goal is pure, high-octane fun! Your objective is to explore vast, open-world maps, hit massive ramps at top speed, and perform the most insane aerial stunts imaginable.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to accelerate, brake, and steer your vehicle. Drive towards the giant ramps scattered across each map to launch your car into the air. While airborne, use the left and right keys to execute incredible barrel rolls and backflips. The crazier the stunt, the more points you'll earn!

Explore four unique environments: the city center, a rural road, an industrial zone, and a snowy area. Each map is filled with opportunities for spectacular jumps, drifts, and crashes. Keep an eye out for 'F' point icons on the road to boost your score.

Don't be afraid to push your car to its limits! If you crash or damage your vehicle, simply press the 'F' key to instantly repair it to pristine condition. This allows you to experiment with wild maneuvers without any penalty. Now, get in the driver's seat and show off your skills!
