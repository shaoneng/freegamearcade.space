Stunt Rider

How to Play Stunt Rider

Welcome to Stunt Rider! Your goal is to drive high-performance cars and perform the most incredible stunts imaginable. Explore four unique open-world maps: the bustling city center, a quiet rural road, a rugged industrial zone, and a slippery snowy area. Each map is filled with sky-high ramps, tunnels, and obstacles designed for maximum fun.

The core of the game is about aerial acrobatics. Drive at full speed towards a ramp to launch your car into the air. While airborne, use the left and right controls to spin your vehicle, performing amazing barrel rolls and backflips. The crazier the stunt, the more points you'll earn. Also, be sure to collect the 'F' point icons scattered around the maps for an extra score boost.

Don't be afraid to get a little reckless! Crashing and damaging your car is part of the experience. If your ride gets too banged up, you can instantly repair it to pristine condition by pressing the 'F' key. This lets you experiment with wild jumps and landings without any consequences. Challenge yourself to complete clean runs or just let loose and cause some mayhem!
