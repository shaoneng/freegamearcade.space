Stunt Rider

How to Play Stunt Rider

Welcome to Stunt Rider! Your goal is to drive high-performance cars across vast, open-world maps and perform breathtaking stunts. Push your driving skills to the limit by launching off massive ramps, drifting around corners, and exploring four unique environments: the city, rural roads, an industrial park, and a snowy landscape.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to accelerate, brake, and steer. When you hit a ramp and go airborne, use the left and right keys to maneuver your car, performing incredible barrel rolls and backflips. The crazier the stunt, the more points you'll earn!

Keep an eye out for 'F' point collectibles scattered across the map to boost your score. Don't be afraid to get a little reckless! Crashing is part of the fun, and if your car gets too damaged, simply press the 'F' key to instantly repair it and get back to the action.
