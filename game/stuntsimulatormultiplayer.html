Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

How to Play Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

Welcome to the high-flying world of Stunt Simulator Multiplayer! Your main goal is to perform the most spectacular aerial stunts with your car to earn points. Start by either joining an existing room with other players or creating your own private room. You can even choose a night mode for a different driving challengeâ€”just remember to press the 'F' key to turn on your headlights!

Once you're in, select one of the three unique maps: the futuristic Hazelgreen, the nature-filled Silverstone, or the expansive Ironbark. Each map is filled with massive ramps, rolling hills, and unique obstacles designed for maximum airtime. Put the pedal to the metal, hit a ramp at full speed, and launch your car into the sky.

While airborne, perform flips and other acrobatic maneuvers to rack up points. The crazier the stunt, the more points you'll earn! Use these points to unlock new vehicles from a selection of ten cars. You start with three, but can work your way up to unique rides like the Concept Car or the massive 6-wheeled truck. Master the maps, perfect your stunts, and become the top driver in the lobby!
