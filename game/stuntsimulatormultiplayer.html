Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

How to Play Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

Welcome to the high-flying world of Stunt Simulator Multiplayer! Your goal is simple: become the ultimate stunt driver. Get behind the wheel, hit the gas, and launch your car off massive ramps and over hilly terrain. The crazier the stunts and flyovers you perform, the more points you'll earn.

You can compete against other players from around the world by joining an open room, or create your own private room to play with friends. Choose from three unique maps, each with a different theme. Explore the futuristic ramps of Hazelgreen, the natural mountain roads of Silverstone, or the vast industrial playground of Ironbark. You can even create or join a night mode room for an extra challenge!

Start with a choice of three vehicles: the Compact Car, Coupe, or Pickup. As you rack up points from your amazing stunts, you can use them to unlock seven more vehicles, including a sleek Concept Car and an outrageous 6-wheeled monster. Each car offers a unique driving experience.

Use your keyboard to control your vehicle. Typically you will use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive. Don't forget to press the F key to turn on your headlights during night drives. Now, get out there and defy gravity!
