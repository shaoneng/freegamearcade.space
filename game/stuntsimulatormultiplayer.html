Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

How to Play Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

Your goal in Stunt Simulator Multiplayer is to become the ultimate stunt driver! Hit the gas and race across vast, open-world maps filled with ramps, hills, and obstacles. The main objective is to launch your car into the air and perform the most incredible flips and flyovers to earn points. These points are your key to unlocking new and better vehicles.

This game is all about shared chaos! You can either join an existing room with other players or create your own private room to play with friends. Choose from three distinct maps: the futuristic "Hazelgreen," the nature-filled "Silverstone," or the industrial "Ironbark." Each map offers unique opportunities for massive airtime and crazy stunts. You start with access to a few basic cars, but as you rack up points from your stunts, you can unlock a total of ten unique vehicles. From sleek concept cars to a wild 6-wheeled beast, each car offers a different driving experience. Keep performing those death-defying stunts to earn enough points and collect them all!
