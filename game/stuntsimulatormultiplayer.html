Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

How to Play Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

Welcome to the high-flying world of Stunt Simulator Multiplayer! Your goal is to become the ultimate stunt driver by performing incredible acrobatic maneuvers. Start by either joining an existing game room or creating your own private session to play with friends. You can even choose a night mode for an extra challenge, but don't forget to turn on your headlights by pressing the F key!

Once you're in a game, hit the gas and explore one of the three unique maps: the futuristic Hazelgreen, the natural Silverstone, or the industrial Ironbark. Each map is packed with massive ramps, hills, and obstacles. Use these features to launch your car into the air at top speed.

While airborne, perform flips, spins, and other crazy stunts to rack up points. The more daring the maneuver, the higher your score! Use the points you earn to unlock a total of ten different vehicles, each with its own unique handling and style. Start with the basics and work your way up to the wild 6-wheeled car!
