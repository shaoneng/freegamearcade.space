Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

How to Play Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

Welcome to the high-flying world of Stunt Simulator Multiplayer! Your objective is to drive powerful cars and perform the most daring stunts imaginable across vast, detailed maps. Join a public room with other players or create a private lobby to practice your skills. The goal is to earn points by launching your car off ramps, hills, and platforms to execute incredible mid-air flips and tricks.

As you successfully land stunts, you'll accumulate points. These points are your key to unlocking a wider variety of vehicles. Start with a basic compact car and work your way up to unlocking all ten vehicles, including a powerful Coupe Car, a rugged Pickup, and even a wild 6-wheeled concept car! The more spectacular your stunts, the faster you'll build your dream garage.

Choose from three unique maps, each offering a different experience. Explore the futuristic ramps of Hazelgreen, navigate the natural, forested terrain of Silverstone, or go wild in the massive industrial shipping area of Ironbark. For an extra challenge, try the night mode and light up the darkness with your headlights (press the 'F' key). Put the pedal to the metal and see what incredible aerial feats you can achieve!
