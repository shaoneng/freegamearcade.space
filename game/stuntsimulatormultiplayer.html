Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

How to Play Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

Welcome to the high-flying world of Stunt Simulator Multiplayer! Your primary goal is to join a lobby with other players, or create your own private room, and perform the most daring aerial stunts imaginable. Choose from three expansive maps, each offering a unique environment for your automotive acrobatics.

Put the pedal to the metal and use the various ramps, hills, and platforms scattered across each map to launch your vehicle into the air. While airborne, execute flips and other crazy maneuvers to rack up points. The more impressive the stunt, the more points you'll earn!

Use your hard-earned points to expand your garage. You start with three vehicles, but you can unlock a total of ten unique cars, including a high-tech Concept Car and a massive 6-wheeled truck. Each car offers a different driving experience, so try to collect them all.

Explore the futuristic cityscape of Hazelgreen, the natural mountain roads of Silverstone, or the industrial port of Ironbark. For an extra challenge, join or create a room with 'Night' mode enabled and light up the darkness with your headlights by pressing the F key.
