Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

How to Play Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

The goal in Stunt Simulator Multiplayer is to become the ultimate stunt driver by performing incredible flyovers and acrobatic feats. Join an existing room or create your own private lobby to play with friends. Once you're in, select one of the unique maps filled with massive ramps, rolling hills, and other obstacles designed for launching your car into the air.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to control your vehicle. Press the gas pedal and build up speed as you approach a jump. Once airborne, try to perform flips and other tricks to rack up points. The crazier the stunt, the more points you'll earn! These points are your key to unlocking new vehicles.

The game features three distinct maps—the futuristic Hazelgreen, the nature-filled Silverstone, and the industrial Ironbark—each offering a different stunting experience. You start with a few basic cars, but as you earn points, you can unlock up to ten unique vehicles, including a Concept Car and even a massive 6-wheeled truck. For an extra challenge, try the night mode and remember to turn on your headlights with the 'F' key!
