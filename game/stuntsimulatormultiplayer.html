Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

How to Play Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

Welcome to the ultimate driving playground! To begin, you can either create a private room to challenge your friends or join a public room to test your skills against other players from around the world. You can also choose a night setting for a different vibe!

The main goal is to rack up points by performing jaw-dropping stunts. Use the WASD or Arrow keys to accelerate, brake, and steer your vehicle. Drive at full speed towards the many ramps, hills, and platforms to launch your car into the air.

While airborne, maneuver your car to pull off incredible flips and spins. The more daring and complex your stunts, the more points you will earn. Use these points to unlock ten unique cars, including a crazy 6-wheeled vehicle and a sleek concept car. You start with three cars, so get stunting to expand your garage!

Explore three diverse maps: the futuristic city of Hazelgreen, the rugged, nature-filled Silverstone, and the industrial port of Ironbark. Each offers a unique environment for your acrobatic driving. If you're driving at night, press the 'F' key to switch on your headlights and light up the track.
