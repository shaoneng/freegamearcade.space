Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

How to Play Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

Welcome to the adrenaline-fueled world of Stunt Simulator Multiplayer! Your goal is to become the ultimate stunt driver by performing incredible aerial maneuvers and high-speed tricks. Compete against other players online or set up your own private room to practice your skills.

Get behind the wheel and explore one of three unique maps filled with massive ramps, loops, and challenging terrain. Use these features to launch your car into the air and perform flips, spins, and other crazy stunts. The more spectacular your moves, the more points you'll earn. Use these points to unlock a diverse lineup of 10 different vehicles, each with its own unique handling.

Choose from three distinct maps: the futuristic Hazelgreen, the nature-filled Silverstone, or the industrial Ironbark. You can also switch between day and night modes for a different driving experience. Don't forget to turn on your headlights by pressing the 'F' key during night drives!

Controls:

  • WASD / Arrow Keys: Drive your car
  • Spacebar: Use the handbrake
  • F: Toggle headlights
  • C: Change camera view
  • R: Reset car
