Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

How to Play Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

Your goal in Stunt Simulator Multiplayer is to perform the most epic acrobatic stunts with your car to earn points and unlock new vehicles. Compete against other players in real-time across vast, open-world maps designed for chaos and creativity.

You can create a private room to play with friends or jump into an existing public room. The game features three distinct maps to explore: the futuristic Hazelgreen, the natural and rugged Silverstone, and the vast industrial port of Ironbark. Each map is filled with jumping ramps, hilly roads, and unique obstacles to test your driving skills.

Use the WASD or arrow keys to drive your car. Hit the gas and launch yourself into the air to perform flips and other stunts, which will earn you points. The more daring your maneuver, the higher your score! Use the points you earn to unlock new cars; you start with three vehicles, but there are ten in total to collect, including a wild 6-wheeled car.

For a different challenge, try the night mode by creating or joining a room with the 'Night' option enabled. Don't forget to press the 'F' key to turn on your headlights and light up the darkness!
