Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

How to Play Stunt Simulator Multiplayer

Your goal in Stunt Simulator Multiplayer is to become the ultimate stunt driver by performing incredible aerial maneuvers and earning points. Compete against other players in real-time or create your own private room to practice your skills.

Get behind the wheel of your chosen vehicle and hit the gas! Use the numerous ramps, hills, and platforms scattered across each map to launch your car into the air. While airborne, perform flips and other crazy tricks to score points. The more daring the stunt, the higher your score.

The points you earn can be used to unlock a fleet of ten unique cars, each with its own handling and style. Start with a basic compact car and work your way up to a wild 6-wheeled monster or a sleek concept car.
