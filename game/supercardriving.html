Super Car Driving

How to Play Super Car Driving

Welcome to the driver's seat of Super Car Driving! Your main goal is to master three distinct game modes. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to accelerate, brake, and steer your high-performance vehicle.

Stunt Mode: Unleash your inner daredevil across 20 thrilling stunt courses. Navigate wild ramps, intricate loops, and challenging obstacles designed to test the limits of your car's performance and your driving ability.

Parking Mode: Put your precision to the test in 22 unique parking challenges. Follow the red arrows on the map to find your designated spot. Carefully maneuver your supercar through tight spaces in airports, city streets, and mall parking lots to prove you're a parking pro.

Free Roam Mode: If you prefer to explore, jump into the open-world city. Drive around freely, discover the roads, or take on fun side missions like racing through speed cameras or becoming a taxi driver to earn rewards. The choice is yours!
