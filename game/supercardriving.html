Super Car Driving

How to Play Super Car Driving

Take control of your supercar using your keyboard. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to accelerate, brake, and steer your vehicle. Use the Spacebar for the handbrake to execute sharp drifts and turns. Look for on-screen prompts for any other special actions or to interact with mission objectives.

Super Car Driving offers three distinct modes to enjoy. In Stunt Mode, navigate your car through surreal landscapes filled with ramps, loops, and obstacles to complete 20 thrilling challenges. In Parking Mode, test your precision by carefully maneuvering your car into designated spots across 22 increasingly difficult levels—just follow the red arrows! Finally, in Free Roam Mode, explore an open-world city at your own pace, take on taxi missions, or race through speed cameras for rewards.

Your goal varies depending on the mode you choose. Whether it's landing a perfect jump, parking without a scratch, or earning cash in the city, the objective is to master your vehicle and conquer every challenge presented. Have fun and enjoy the drive!
