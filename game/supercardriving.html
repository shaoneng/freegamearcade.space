Super Car Driving

How to Play Super Car Driving

Super Car Driving offers an immersive experience across three exciting modes. To start, simply launch the game and navigate the main menu to select your desired mode.

In **Stunt Challenges**, use your driving skills to conquer 20 unique courses filled with ramps, loops, and obstacles. Focus on precision and speed to complete each challenge.

For **Parking Levels**, guide your supercar through 22 challenging scenarios. Follow the red arrows to find your designated parking spot, showcasing your control and accuracy in various environments like airports and city streets.

In **Free Roam** mode, explore a large open-world city at your leisure. Drive freely, or take on optional side missions such as racing through speed cameras or working as a taxi driver to earn in-game rewards.

The game typically uses standard driving controls: Use **WASD** or **Arrow Keys** to steer and accelerate/brake. Use the **Mouse** to navigate menus and select options. Experiment with controls once in-game for any specific keybindings.
