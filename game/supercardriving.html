Super Car Driving

How to Play Super Car Driving

Welcome to the driver's seat of Super Car Driving! Your objective is to explore and conquer the three distinct game modes using a selection of high-performance vehicles.

Controls: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer, accelerate, and brake your supercar. Press the Space Bar to use the handbrake for sharp turns and drifts.

Game Modes:

Stunt Mode: Unleash your inner daredevil across 20 exhilarating stunt challenges. Navigate surreal landscapes filled with massive ramps, loops, and gravity-defying twists to test the limits of your car and your skill.

Parking Mode: Prove your precision in 22 challenging parking levels. Carefully maneuver your agile supercar through tricky obstacles in various settings like airports and city lots. Follow the red arrows to find the designated parking spot and earn coins.

Free Roam Mode: Enjoy the freedom of an open-world city. Drive at your leisure, or take on missions like racing through speed cameras or becoming a taxi driver to earn rewards.
