Super Car Driving

How to Play Super Car Driving

Super Car Driving offers a diverse driving experience with three unique game modes. Get ready to test your skills behind the wheel of high-performance sports cars like Ferrari and Lamborghini. The controls are simple and intuitive:

  • Arrow Keys or WASD: Steer, accelerate, and brake.
  • Spacebar: Use the handbrake for sharp turns and drifts.
  • C Key: Change the camera view.

Game Modes

Stunt Mode: Unleash your inner daredevil across 20 exhilarating stunt challenges. Navigate surreal landscapes filled with massive ramps, mind-bending loops, and gravity-defying twists that will push your car to its absolute limit.

Parking Mode: Test your precision and control in 22 intense parking levels. Maneuver agile supercars into designated spots by following the red arrows. The challenges take place in various locations like airports and city lots, with all levels unlocked from the start.

Free Roam Mode: Explore a vast, open-world city at your own pace. Drive freely, discover the roads, or take on missions for rewards. You can race through speed cameras or even work as a taxi driver, picking up passengers for cash.
