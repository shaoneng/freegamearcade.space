Super Car Driving

How to Play Super Car Driving

Welcome to Super Car Driving! This game offers three distinct modes: Stunt, Parking, and Free Roam. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your car, and the Spacebar for the handbrake. Use the 'C' key to change your camera view for the best perspective.

In Stunt Mode, your goal is to conquer 20 challenging tracks filled with insane ramps, loops, and obstacles. Navigate these surreal landscapes and push your supercar to its absolute limits to complete each exhilarating challenge.

Test your precision in Parking Mode. Follow the red arrows to locate the designated parking spot across 22 different levels, set in locations like airports and city streets. Carefully maneuver your agile vehicle into the spot without crashing. All levels are unlocked, so you can tackle any challenge you like.

For a more relaxed experience, head into Free Roam. Drive around the open-world city, explore the streets, or take on optional missions like speed camera challenges or taxi driving to earn in-game rewards. The choice is yours!
