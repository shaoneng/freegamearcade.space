Super Car Driving

How to Play Super Car Driving

Welcome to Super Car Driving! Your goal is to master driving a variety of high-performance supercars across three unique and exciting game modes. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to control acceleration, braking, and steering. Choose the mode that best suits your driving style!

In Stunt Mode, your objective is to conquer 20 thrilling challenges. Navigate your supercar through surreal landscapes filled with mind-bending ramps, intricate loops, and gravity-defying twists. Push your driving skills and your car to the absolute limit to complete each unique stunt course.

Test your precision and control in Parking Mode. This mode features 22 intense levels where you must maneuver your agile car into designated parking spots without crashing. Follow the red arrows on the ground to find your target. The levels feature various locations like airports and city lots, each with tricky new obstacles.

For ultimate freedom, jump into Free Roam Mode. Drive at your leisure through a large, open-world city with light traffic. You can simply cruise and explore, or take on optional missions like racing through speed cameras or working as a taxi driver to earn in-game rewards and coins.
