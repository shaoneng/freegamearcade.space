Super Car Driving

How to Play Super Car Driving

Master the streets and challenges in Super Car Driving by familiarizing yourself with the controls and game modes. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to steer, accelerate, and brake your vehicle. Use the Spacebar for the handbrake to execute sharp turns and drifts.

The game features three distinct modes to choose from:

Stunt Mode: Unleash your inner daredevil across 20 exhilarating stunt challenges. Navigate surreal landscapes filled with mind-bending ramps, intricate loops, and gravity-defying twists to test the limits of your supercar.

Parking Mode: Test your precision and control in 22 intense parking levels. Follow the red arrows to locate the designated parking spot across various maps, including airports and city lots. The challenges increase as you navigate tricky obstacles with agile, high-performance cars.

Free Roam Mode: Explore an open-world city at your own pace. Drive freely, discover the roads, or take on optional missions like racing through speed cameras or working as a taxi driver to earn in-game coins.
