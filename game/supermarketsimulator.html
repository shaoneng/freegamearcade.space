Supermarket Simulator

How to Play Supermarket Simulator

Welcome to Supermarket Simulator, where you're the boss! Your journey begins with a starting capital of 10,000 coins. The main goal is to transform your initial small shop into a sprawling, successful supermarket. You are in complete control of every decision, from the products you sell to the staff you hire.

The core of the game revolves around keeping your shelves stocked and your customers happy. You'll need to place orders for new goods, unpack boxes, and arrange products on the shelves. Pay attention to what your customers are buying to optimize your inventory and maximize profits. A well-stocked store is a profitable store!

As your business grows, you'll need to manage more than just inventory. Update price tags to stay competitive, expand your store's layout, and even hire employees to help with tasks like cashiering and stocking. Making smart business decisions is key to building your retail empire and becoming the ultimate supermarket tycoon. Dive in and start building the store of your dreams!
