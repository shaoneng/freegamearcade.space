How to Play Supermarket Simulator

Welcome to your very own supermarket! Your goal is to manage and grow your store into a bustling retail giant. You begin with a starting budget to purchase your initial inventory and set up shop.

Use your computer in the office to order new products. Keep an eye on your stock levels â€“ empty shelves mean unhappy customers and lost sales! Once the delivery arrives, you'll need to unbox the items and place them on the shelves for customers to buy.

Don't forget to set the prices for your goods. You can adjust them at any time to maximize your profits or attract more shoppers. When customers are ready to check out, head to the cash register to scan their items and take their payment.

As your store grows, you can invest your profits in expanding your store's size, buying more shelves, and unlocking new types of products to sell. You can also hire employees like cashiers and restockers to help manage the workload, freeing you up to focus on the bigger picture. Good luck, manager!
