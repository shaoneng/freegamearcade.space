Supermarket Simulator

How to Play Supermarket Simulator

Welcome to your very own store! In Supermarket Simulator, you're the boss. Your goal is to build and manage a successful supermarket. You start with a generous amount of cash to get your business off the ground.

Your first tasks involve setting up the shop. Use your mouse to navigate the store, order your first batch of products from the computer, and place them on the shelves for customers to buy. Don't forget to open your store and set the prices for your goods!

Once the doors are open, it's time to run the checkout. Man the cash register to scan items, take payment, and give change to your customers. As you earn more profit, you can order a wider variety of goods, from snacks and drinks to everyday essentials, to attract more shoppers.

Managing a growing store is a lot of work. You'll need to keep the shelves stocked, the floors clean, and the customers happy. Eventually, you can hire cashiers and other staff to help you run the business, freeing you up to focus on expanding your store and becoming the ultimate supermarket tycoon!
