How to Play Supermarket Simulator

Welcome to Supermarket Simulator! Your goal is to build and manage a thriving supermarket from the ground up. You start with an empty shop and a budget to get things going. Your first task is to purchase shelving and stock it with your initial products.

Once your store is open, you'll take on every role. Use your mouse and keyboard to move around, interact with objects, and serve customers. You'll need to manually stock shelves with goods from the storage room, operate the cash register to check out customers, and keep the store clean and organized.

As you earn money, you can expand your business. Order new types of products to attract more customers, purchase additional licenses to sell more goods, and eventually hire employees like cashiers and restockers to automate tasks. Managing your finances, product prices, and inventory is key to success.
