How to Play Supermarket Simulator

Welcome to your very own store! In Supermarket Simulator, you're the boss. Your main goal is to transform a small, empty shop into a bustling, profitable supermarket. You'll start with a budget to get things rolling, but every decision from here on out is yours.

Your daily tasks involve managing every aspect of the store. Use the computer to order your first batch of products. Once they arrive, unbox them and carefully place them on the shelves for your customers to see. Make sure to set fair prices to attract buyers while still making a profit.

When customers are ready to check out, head to the cash register. Scan their items, take their payment (cash or card), and give them the correct change. A happy customer is a returning customer! As you earn more money, you can expand your store, purchase licenses for new types of goods, and add more shelves and refrigerators.
