Supermarket Simulator

How to Play Supermarket Simulator

Welcome to your very own store! In Supermarket Simulator, you take on the role of an aspiring entrepreneur aiming to build a retail empire. You'll start with a modest shop and a healthy budget, but it's up to you to turn it into the go-to supermarket in town.

Your daily tasks involve managing every aspect of the business. Use the computer in your office to order new products, from snacks and drinks to fresh produce. Once the goods arrive, you'll need to unbox them and carefully stock the shelves. Don't forget to set competitive prices to attract customers and maximize your profit.

When customers come in, head to the checkout counter to ring up their purchases and handle the cash. As you earn more money, you can invest it back into your business. Purchase licenses for new types of products, expand the size of your store, and eventually hire staff like cashiers and restockers to help you manage the growing workload. Keep your customers happy and your shelves full to become the ultimate supermarket tycoon!
