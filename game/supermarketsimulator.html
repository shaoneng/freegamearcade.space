Supermarket Simulator

How to Play Supermarket Simulator

Welcome to your very own store! In Supermarket Simulator, your goal is to build and manage a thriving retail business. You begin with a generous starting capital of 10,000 coins to kickstart your dream supermarket. The first step is to order your initial stock. Use your in-game computer to purchase a variety of products that you think will sell well.

Once your delivery arrives, it's time to get to work. Unbox the goods and strategically place them on the shelves for your customers to find. Don't forget to set your prices! After stocking up, open your store and get ready for your first customers. You'll need to head to the checkout counter to ring up their purchases and handle the cash.

As your profits grow, you can reinvest back into your business. Purchase store licenses to expand your floor space, buy more shelving and refrigerators, and unlock a wider range of products to sell. To keep up with demand, you'll eventually need to hire staff, including cashiers to handle transactions and restockers to keep the shelves full. Manage every detail to turn your small shop into the biggest supermarket in town!
