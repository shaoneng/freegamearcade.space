Supermarket Simulator

How to Play Supermarket Simulator

Welcome to Supermarket Simulator, where you get to build and manage your very own store! You'll begin your entrepreneurial journey with a generous starting capital of 10,000 coins. Your first task is to purchase and stock your shelves with a variety of products. Use your computer to order goods, from snacks and drinks to everyday essentials.

Once your store is stocked, it's time to open for business! Customers will start coming in to shop. You'll need to man the cash register, scanning items and taking payments. As you earn money, you can reinvest it back into your business. Buy more shelves, expand your store's layout, and unlock new types of products to sell. Keep an eye on your stock levels and reorder items before they run out.

As your supermarket grows, you won't have to do everything yourself. You can hire employees like cashiers and stockers to help manage the daily operations. Your goal is to turn your small shop into a bustling, profitable supermarket. Every decision is yours, from product pricing to store design. Jump in and start building your retail empire today!
