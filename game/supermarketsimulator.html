How to Play Supermarket Simulator

Welcome to Supermarket Simulator, where you're the boss! You'll begin your retail journey with a generous starting fund of 10,000 coins. Your first task is to set up your store by purchasing your initial stock. Buy various goods like snacks, drinks, and other essentials to fill your shelves.

Once your store is stocked, it's time to open for business. Customers will come in to purchase items. You'll need to manage the checkout, scan items, and handle payments. The goal is to transform your humble corner shop into a massive, thriving supermarket. Every decision, from store layout to product selection, is in your hands!
