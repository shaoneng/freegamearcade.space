Supermarket Simulator

How to Play Supermarket Simulator

Welcome to your new career as a store manager in Supermarket Simulator! You'll start with a hefty 10,000 coins to kickstart your business. Your goal is to transform a small, empty shop into a bustling retail empire. It's your chance to build, manage, and have fun all at once.

From the very beginning, every decision is yours. You'll be the one ordering new products, from candy bars to essential groceries, and making sure the shelves are always stocked. Use the in-game interface to purchase new items and place them strategically around your store.

A successful supermarket needs happy customers, which means fair prices and efficient service. You'll be responsible for setting and updating price tags on all your goods. As the store gets busier, you can even hire employees to help you run the cash register and keep things running smoothly. Use your earnings to expand your store, buy new equipment, and offer a wider variety of products. Dive in and show everyone you have what it takes to run the best supermarket in town!
