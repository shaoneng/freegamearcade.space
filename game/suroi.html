How to Play Suroi

Welcome to the unforgiving world of Suroi, a 2D battle royale where only the strongest survive. Your goal is to be the last player standing in a hostile, post-apocalyptic arena.

You begin with nothing but your determination. Immediately start exploring the map to find loot. Search buildings and chests for weapons like MP40s, protective gear like helmets and vests, and crucial healing items such as medkits and cola. Everything you find can give you an edge over your opponents.

Stay vigilant! The arena is swarming with other players who are just as desperate to survive as you are. Use your gathered gear to eliminate them. At the same time, keep an eye on the map to avoid the toxic orange gas that continuously shrinks the play area, forcing players closer together for intense showdowns.
