Suroi

How to Play Suroi

Welcome to the world of Suroi, a fast-paced 2D battle royale where survival is everything. You'll start with nothing, so your first priority is to explore the map and find loot. Search buildings and break open chests to discover weapons, armor, healing items, and grenades.

Use the WASD keys to move around the arena and your mouse to aim and shoot. Keep an eye out for other players—they are all trying to eliminate you to be the last one standing. Equip vests and helmets for better protection, and use different weapons like MP40s to gain a tactical advantage.

A dangerous orange gas will slowly close in, shrinking the playable area. Make sure to stay inside the safe zone to avoid taking damage. Use medkits and cola to restore your health, especially during intense firefights or when you're caught in the gas.

Whether you're playing solo or reviving your partner in duo mode, the goal remains the same: be the last survivor. Good luck!
