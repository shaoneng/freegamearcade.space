Suroi

How to Play Suroi

Welcome to the desolate battleground of Suroi! Your primary mission is to be the last person standing in this intense 2D multiplayer survival game. You start with nothing, so your first priority is to explore the map and scavenge for supplies. Look for weapons, ammunition, protective gear like helmets and vests, and crucial healing items such as medkits and cola.

As you gather loot, you'll encounter other players who are trying to do the same. You must decide whether to engage in combat or avoid confrontation. Use your weapons to eliminate opponents and claim their gear. Keep a close eye on the map to avoid the toxic orange gas that continuously shrinks the play area, forcing players closer together for a final showdown.

You can play in Solo mode, where you're all on your own, or team up with a friend in Duo mode, where you can revive a fallen teammate. Manage your inventory wisely, use healing items strategically, and always be aware of your surroundings to dominate the field and claim victory.
