Suroi

How to Play Suroi

Welcome to the post-apocalyptic world of Suroi! Your objective is simple: be the last player standing. You start with nothing but your determination. Immediately start exploring the 2D map to find loot, including weapons, ammo, armor, and essential healing items like cola and medkits.

Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot or use items. The WASD keys control your movement across the battlefield. As the match progresses, a toxic orange gas will close in, shrinking the safe zone and forcing confrontations. Keep an eye on the map to stay out of the gas and hunt down your opponents.

Suroi offers different modes. In solo mode, you're on your own, so prioritize finding good gear and healing items. In duo mode, you can work with a teammate and even revive them if they fall. Communication and strategy are key to winning as a pair. Manage your inventory wisely, save powerful items like Medikits for critical moments, and always be ready for a fight!
