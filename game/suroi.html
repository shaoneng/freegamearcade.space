Suroi

How to Play Suroi

Welcome to the hostile world of Suroi! Your goal is simple: be the last player standing. This 2D battle royale drops you into a post-apocalyptic arena with nothing but your determination. You must explore the map, scavenge for resources, and eliminate every opponent you encounter to claim victory.

Start by searching buildings and breaking open chests to find loot. You'll need weapons like the MP40, protective gear like helmets and vests, and healing items to survive firefights. Keep an eye on the map to avoid the encroaching toxic orange gas, which shrinks the playable area over time and will damage you if you're caught in it.

Suroi offers different ways to play. In Solo mode, you're completely on your own, so strategy and resource management are key. In Duo mode, you can team up with a friend or a random player. If your teammate is knocked down, you can revive them, adding a cooperative layer to the survival experience.

Conserve your powerful healing items, like Medikits, for critical moments when your health is low. Always be aware of your surroundings, listen for footsteps, and use cover to your advantage. Good luck, and may you be the ultimate survivor!
