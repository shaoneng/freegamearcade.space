Suroi

How to Play Suroi

Welcome to the post-apocalyptic 2D world of Suroi. You start with nothing but the will to survive. Your first mission is to explore the arena and scavenge for anything that can help you. Grab consumables like cola for your backpack, and equip vests and helmets for crucial protection. Finding a powerful weapon like an MP40 or a handful of grenades means you're on the right track to survival.

Suroi.io is an online multiplayer survival game where the goal is simple: be the last one standing. Inspired by classics like Surviv.io, you must gather loot from chests, find weapons, stock up on medkits, and survive in a hostile environment. Keep an eye on the shrinking map and avoid the toxic orange gas. Collect your gear, eliminate your opponents, and climb your way to the top of the leaderboard.

The game offers different modes with unique mechanics. In Duo mode, you can work with a teammate and revive each other, adding a strategic layer to the gameplay. In Solo mode, you're all on your own, making healing items absolutely essential. Always save your Medikits for critical moments when your health is low. Master the art of looting and fighting to dominate the field!

Suroi.io was created by Hasanger Games. If you enjoy fast-paced, browser-based battle royales like Surviv.io and ZombsRoyale.io, you'll feel right at home in Suroi.
