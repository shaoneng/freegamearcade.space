Suroi

How to Play Suroi

Welcome to the intense, 2D world of Suroi, a battle royale where survival is everything. Your objective is to be the last player or team standing. You'll start with nothing, so your first priority is to scavenge for loot. Explore buildings and open chests to find weapons, armor, healing items, and ammo. Keep an eye out for enemies and the encroaching toxic orange gas that shrinks the play area over time.

Use the WASD keys to move your character around the map. Aim with your mouse and click the left mouse button to shoot or use your melee weapon. Use the F key to pick up items, open doors, and interact with objects. You can switch between your equipped weapons using the number keys (1, 2) or the mouse wheel.

Manage your inventory effectively. Healing items like medkits and cola are crucial for survival, especially in tough fights. In duo mode, you can work with a teammate and revive them if they get knocked down. In solo mode, you're on your own, so play smart and choose your battles wisely. Dominate the field, climb the leaderboard, and become the ultimate survivor!
