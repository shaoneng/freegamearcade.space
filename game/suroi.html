Suroi

How to Play Suroi

Welcome to the post-apocalyptic arena of Suroi! Your primary goal is to be the last player standing. You'll start with nothing, so your first task is to explore the map and scavenge for loot. Search buildings and crates for anything that can help you survive: weapons, ammo, protective gear, and healing items.

As you explore, you'll find essential gear like vests and helmets for protection, and a variety of weapons from pistols to powerful rifles. Keep an eye out for healing items like cola and medkits to restore your health after a fight. A crucial element of survival is to always be aware of the toxic orange gas that slowly closes in on the map, forcing players into smaller and smaller areas.

You can choose to play solo, where you are completely on your own, or team up with a friend in duo mode. In duo mode, you have the advantage of being able to revive your teammate if they are knocked down. Work together to gather loot, cover each other's backs, and outlast the other teams. In either mode, the strategy is the same: gear up, stay inside the safe zone, and eliminate every opponent you encounter to claim victory!
