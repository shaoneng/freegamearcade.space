Suroi

How to Play Suroi

Welcome to the desolate world of Suroi! Your primary goal is simple: be the last survivor. You'll start with nothing but your determination. Roam the 2D map to scavenge for essential items. Look for backpacks to carry more loot, vests and helmets for protection, and a variety of weapons like MP40s and grenades to gain an edge over your opponents.

As you explore, always be aware of your surroundings. Other players are just as desperate to survive as you are. Keep an eye on the map to avoid the dangerous orange gas that gradually closes in, forcing players into smaller and smaller areas. Collect healing items like cola and medkits to restore your health after a fight.

Suroi offers different modes to test your skills. In Duo mode, you can team up with a friend and revive each other when knocked down. In Solo mode, you're completely on your own, so managing your health and resources is crucial. A key tip is to save powerful items like Medkits for critical moments when you're low on health. Now get out there and claim your spot on the leaderboard!
