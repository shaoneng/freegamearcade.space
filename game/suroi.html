How to Play Suroi

Welcome to the desolate, 2D world of Suroi. Your objective is simple: be the last player standing. You'll start with nothing, so your first priority is to explore the map and find loot. Open chests and search buildings to gather weapons, ammunition, armor (vests and helmets), and crucial healing items like medkits and cola.

Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot or use items. Navigate the arena using the standard WASD keys. Keep an eye out for other playersâ€”they are your primary threat. Your goal is to eliminate them before they eliminate you. Stay mobile and use cover to your advantage during firefights.

A toxic orange gas will periodically shrink the play area, forcing players closer together. Avoid this gas at all costs, as it will drain your health. In Duo mode, you can revive a fallen teammate, but in Solo mode, you're on your own. Conserve your best healing items, like Medkits, for critical moments when your health is low. Gear up, stay alert, and fight your way to the top of the leaderboard!
