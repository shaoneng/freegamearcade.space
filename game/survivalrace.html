Survival Race

How to Play Survival Race

Welcome to the ultimate automotive battle royale! The goal in Survival Race is simple but challenging: be the last car remaining on the platform to win.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to accelerate, brake, and steer your vehicle. Drive at full speed across the arena, strategically crashing into other cars to push them over the edge. Timing and precision are key to knocking out your opponents while staying safe yourself.

Keep an eye out for opportunities to perform stunts, navigate tricky loops, and conquer steep slopes. Not only do these maneuvers add to the fun, but you can also collect coins scattered across the platform to increase your score. Stay alert, drive aggressively, and outlast the competition to become the champion of the Survival Race!
