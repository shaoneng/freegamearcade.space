Survival Race

How to Play Survival Race

Welcome to the ultimate vehicular battle royale! In Survival Race, your primary objective is to be the last car driving. The arena is a high-flying platform, and the only way to win is to ensure you're the sole survivor at the end of the round.

Use your driving skills to navigate the platform at high speeds. The key to victory is to strategically crash into your opponents, sending them flying off the edge. Be aggressive, but also be careful not to get pushed out yourself! Use the environment to your advantage and master the art of the tactical shunt.

As you battle for supremacy, don't forget to perform awesome stunts and collect the coins scattered across the arena. These will help you achieve a higher score and prove your dominance. It's a fast-paced, action-packed game where only the best driver survives.
