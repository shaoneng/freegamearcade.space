Survival Race

How to Play Survival Race

The goal in Survival Race is straightforward: be the last car standing! You're in a high-flying arena with other vehicles, and your mission is to knock them all off the edge before they do the same to you.

Use your keyboard controls to steer your vehicle across the platform. Gain speed, line up your targets, and crash into other cars with force to send them flying out of the arena. Be careful to manage your own position, as one wrong move could send you plummeting.

As you drive, look out for opportunities to perform stunts and collect coins scattered around the arena. These can help boost your score and give you an edge in this chaotic battle royale on wheels.
