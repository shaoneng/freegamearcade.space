Survival Race

How to Play Survival Race

Welcome to the ultimate automotive battle royale! In Survival Race, your one and only goal is to be the last vehicle remaining in the arena. You'll be driving on a dangerous platform high in the sky, so be careful not to fall off!

Use your driving skills to steer your car at high speeds across the platform. The main objective is to ram into your opponents with force, pushing them over the edge to eliminate them from the competition. Be aggressive and tactical, but always watch your back so you don't get knocked off yourself!

As you battle for supremacy, you can also perform cool stunts and collect coins that are scattered throughout the arena. These actions will help you achieve the highest score. Master the chaos, outmaneuver your rivals, and survive the race to claim victory!
