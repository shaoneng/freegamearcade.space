Survival Race

How to Play Survival Race

Welcome to the high-stakes arena of Survival Race! Your objective is straightforward: survive. You'll be driving a car on a platform high in the sky, and the only way to win is to be the last one remaining.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD keys to steer your vehicle. Accelerate to full speed and use your momentum to crash into other cars. The goal is to push them off the platform while avoiding being knocked off yourself.

As you battle it out, keep an eye out for coins scattered across the arena. Collecting them will boost your score. Be mindful of gaps and holes in the platform – falling through means instant elimination! Master the art of drifting and aggressive driving to dominate the competition and become the ultimate survival racing champion.
