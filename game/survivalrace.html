Survival Race

How to Play Survival Race

The objective in Survival Race is simple but challenging: be the last car standing in the arena. This is a battle royale on wheels where survival is everything.

Use your driving skills to navigate the platform at high speeds. Your primary weapon is your vehicle itself—ram into other cars with force to knock them off the edge and out of the game.

While you fight for survival, keep an eye out for opportunities to perform epic stunts and roll over holes to your advantage. Don't forget to collect the coins scattered across the arena to boost your score. Stay alert, drive aggressively, and outlast all your opponents to claim victory!
