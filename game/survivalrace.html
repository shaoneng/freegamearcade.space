Survival Race

How to Play Survival Race

Welcome to the ultimate automotive battle royale! The goal in Survival Race is simple but challenging: be the last car driving on the platform. Use your driving skills to outmaneuver your opponents in a high-stakes arena suspended in the sky.

Control your car and accelerate to full speed. Your primary objective is to crash into other cars, knocking them off the edge of the platform. The last player remaining in the arena wins the round. Be careful not to fall off yourself!

While fighting for survival, you can also perform epic stunts and collect coins scattered across the arena. Master the controls, use your momentum wisely, and become the undisputed champion of the Survival Race!
