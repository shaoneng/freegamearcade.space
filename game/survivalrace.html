Survival Race

How to Play Survival Race

Welcome to Survival Race, the ultimate test of vehicular combat and endurance! The goal is simple but challenging: be the last car remaining in the arena. You're pitted against other drivers on a suspended platform where one wrong move could send you plummeting. It's a true battle royale on wheels where only the strongest survivor claims victory.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to control your car. Steer with precision, accelerate to build momentum, and use your speed to ram into other vehicles. A well-timed hit can knock an opponent right off the edge of the platform. Be aggressive, but also be mindful of your own position to avoid getting pushed out yourself.

The arena is more than just a flat surface. It's filled with ramps, loops, and holes that you can use to your advantage. Perform incredible stunts to gain an edge or tactically roll over holes to dodge attacks. As you drive, be sure to collect coins scattered across the platform to increase your score and prove your dominance in this high-octane race for survival.
