Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner! Your mission is to guide a skilled ninja through challenging levels filled with enemies. The controls are simple and intuitive: just click and drag your mouse (or use your finger on a touch screen) to control your sword's movement on the screen.

As you automatically run forward, use your blade to slice through waves of enemies, deflect incoming shurikens, and even cut bullets in half. Be prepared for intense action, as you'll also need to skillfully maneuver your sword to dodge massive projectiles that can't be sliced. Timing is everything!

Each successful slice and dodge brings you closer to completing the level. Enjoy the epic slow-motion effects that trigger during critical moments, making your ninja skills feel even more powerful. Collect coins along the way to unlock a wide variety of cool swords and trail effects in the shop to customize your gameplay!
