Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Grab your katana and get ready to slice your way through waves of enemies! Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner is an action runner game where you become a blade-wielding ninja on a high platform filled with enemies. The gameplay is simple yet addictive: use your mouse or finger to slice through anything that comes your way.

Your objective is to cut down enemy warriors, deflect flying shurikens, and even split bullets in mid-air. Master the timing to survive and enjoy the satisfying slow-motion effects that trigger on perfect hits, making you feel like a true action hero. It’s a perfect mix of the slicing fun from games like Fruit Ninja and the non-stop action of endless runners.

Unlock & Customize

One of the coolest features is the ability to customize your gear. Earn coins by playing and spend them in the shop to unlock:

  • Unique Swords: From classic katanas to more unconventional blades, collect dozens of different weapons to find your favorite.
  • Awesome Trails: Add visual flair to your attacks with special trail effects. These don't change gameplay, but they make every slice look incredibly stylish, especially during slow-mo sequences.

Developed by PlayGama, this game is built for quick and engaging fun. If you're craving more stealthy action, give Blocky Parkour Ninja a try for a different kind of ninja challenge!
