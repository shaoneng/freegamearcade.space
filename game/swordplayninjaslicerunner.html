Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Your mission in Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner is to guide your ninja through a series of challenging levels, slicing everything that stands in your way. You'll automatically run forward, so your focus is purely on offense and defense. Use your blade to cut down enemies, deflect incoming shurikens and bullets, and dodge unblockable projectiles.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use your mouse to control the sword. Click and hold the left mouse button, then drag it across the screen to slice. The path of your mouse cursor is the path of your sword. On mobile or touch devices, simply use your finger. Aim carefully to hit multiple targets in a single fluid motion.

Successfully clearing levels will earn you coins. Use these coins in the shop to unlock new swords and awesome trail effects to customize your ninja. Each new weapon and trail adds a fresh visual flair to your slicing action, making every victory even more satisfying!
