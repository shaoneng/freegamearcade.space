Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Grab your katana and dive into the action of Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner! In this thrilling action runner, you become a master ninja on a high-speed platform, slicing your way through hordes of enemies. Use your finger to control your blade and cut down everything in your path, from flying shurikens to bullets, all while dodging massive projectiles.

The game features a vibrant 3D world that shifts with each level, complete with satisfying slow-motion effects that make every slice feel epic. It combines the addictive slicing mechanics of games like Fruit Ninja with the non-stop excitement of an endless runner.

Customize Your Blade

One of the best features is the vast collection of sword skins and trail effects available in the shop. You can unlock random swords with coins or earn specific ones by leveling up. From classic katanas to more unique weapons, there's a blade for every style.

Leave a Stylish Trail

Add extra flair to your attacks with unlockable trail effects. These visual enhancements make every swing more exciting, especially during those cool slow-mo moments. While they don't change gameplay, they add a stylish, VR-like feel to your ninja skills. Mix and match swords and trails to create your ultimate ninja aesthetic!

Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner was developed by PlayGama.

If you enjoy this kind of stealthy swordplay, be sure to check out Blocky Parkour Ninja. It offers a 3D ninja platforming experience where you sneak through enemy bases and dodge traps with precision.
