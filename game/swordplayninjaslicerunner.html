Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Step into the shoes of a blade-wielding ninja in Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner! This action-packed runner game challenges you to slice your way through hordes of enemies on a high platform. With simple finger movements, you'll deflect flying shurikens, slice through bullets, and shatter enemy shields, all while dodging massive projectiles.

The game features a vibrant 3D world that changes with each level, combining the satisfying slicing mechanics of Fruit Ninja with the thrilling pace of an endless runner. Master the sword fights and enjoy cinematic slow-motion effects that make every slice feel epic.

Customize Your Arsenal

One of the highlights of Sword Play is the extensive collection of cosmetic upgrades. Visit the shop to unlock a wide range of sword skins and trail effects to personalize your ninja experience:

  • Swords: Unlock unique weapons, from classic katanas to more unconventional blades. You can get random swords using coins or earn specific ones by leveling up and completing challenges.
  • Trails: Add a visual flair to every swing! These effects leave a stylish trail behind your blade, making your attacks even more spectacular, especially during slow-motion sequences. They don't change gameplay, but they definitely boost the cool factor.

Developed by PlayGama, this game is perfect for quick, action-filled fun. If you're looking for more ninja adventures, try Blocky Parkour Ninja for a dose of 3D stealth and platforming action.
