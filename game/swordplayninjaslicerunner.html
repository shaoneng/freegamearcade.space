Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Embark on your ninja journey in Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner! The controls are simple and intuitive: just use your mouse or finger to move your sword across the screen.

Your primary goal is to slice and dice everything that comes your way. This includes enemy projectiles like flying shurikens and bullets, as well as enemies holding shields. A well-timed slice will destroy them and clear your path. Watch out for massive bullets and other obstacles that you'll need to dodge to survive.

As you progress, you'll experience awesome slow-motion effects that make every slice feel incredibly satisfying. Complete levels and collect coins to spend in the shop, where you can unlock a vast arsenal of cool swords and stylish trail effects to personalize your gameplay. Master the blade and see how far you can run!
