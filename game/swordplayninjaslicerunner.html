Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Your primary goal is to guide your ninja through each level by controlling their sword. The ninja runs forward automatically on a high platform filled with enemies and obstacles.

Use your mouse or finger to control the blade. Click and drag your mouse (or swipe your finger on a touch screen) across the screen to slice through enemies, deflect incoming bullets, and destroy flying shurikens. Your precision and timing are key to survival.

The game features satisfying slow-motion effects during key moments, giving you a better chance to react and perform stylish takedowns. Successfully clearing levels will earn you coins.

Use the coins you collect in the in-game shop to unlock a wide variety of unique sword skins and cool trail effects. These customizations don't change the gameplay, but they make every slice more visually exciting!
