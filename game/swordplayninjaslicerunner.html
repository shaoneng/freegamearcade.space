Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Your goal in Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner is to survive as long as possible by slicing through enemies and dodging obstacles. You automatically run forward on a high platform.

Use your mouse or finger to draw a line across the screen. This will swing your katana, slicing anything in its path. Cut down waves of enemies, deflect incoming shurikens, and even slice bullets in half before they reach you.

Be mindful of enemies with shields, which may require a more strategic slice to defeat. You'll also need to dodge massive bullets and other hazards. Move your character left or right by dragging your slice to avoid these threats.

Look out for the epic slow-motion sequences! These moments give you extra time to react and perform spectacular slices. Master your timing to become the ultimate ninja warrior.
