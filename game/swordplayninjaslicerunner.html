Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Take on the role of a skilled ninja in this fast-paced action runner. The controls are simple: use your mouse or finger to move your sword across the screen.

Your goal is to slice through any enemies or projectiles that stand in your way. Cut down enemy ninjas, deflect flying shurikens, and even split bullets in half with a well-timed swipe. Be careful to dodge massive obstacles that cannot be sliced!

As you progress through the levels, you'll earn coins. Use these coins in the shop to unlock new, stylish swords and awesome visual trail effects to customize your ninja experience. Master the blade, complete the levels, and become the ultimate slice master!
