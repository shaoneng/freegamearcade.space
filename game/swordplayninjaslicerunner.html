Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner! Your character runs forward automatically, and your mission is to control the sword to eliminate all threats in your path.

Use your mouse (on desktop) or your finger (on mobile) to click and drag across the screen. This action swings your katana, allowing you to slice enemies, deflect incoming shurikens, and even cut bullets right out of the air. Some larger projectiles cannot be sliced and must be dodged.

Timing is everything! Successful slices trigger epic slow-motion effects, giving you a satisfying sense of power. As you progress, you'll earn coins which can be used in the in-game shop to unlock a wide variety of unique sword skins and stylish trail effects to personalize your ninja warrior.
