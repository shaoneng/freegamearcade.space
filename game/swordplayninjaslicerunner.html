Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Grab your katana and get ready to slice your way through waves of enemies! Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner is an action runner game where you become a blade-wielding ninja on a high platform filled with enemies. Your goal is to slice flying shurikens, deflect bullets, and shatter shields, all with the simple swipe of your finger.

The game combines the satisfying slicing action of Fruit Ninja with the exciting, fast-paced challenge of an endless runner. Dodge massive bullets and enjoy the epic slow-motion effects that trigger on your best moves. Each level brings a new, colorful 3D world to conquer.

Unlock Swords and Trails

One of the most engaging parts of the game is the vast collection of sword skins and trail effects you can unlock in the shop. You can get random swords with 700 coins or earn specific ones by leveling up and completing challenges. Find everything from classic katanas to more unique and quirky weapons to wield.

You can also customize your attacks with trail effects, which leave a visual mark with every swing. These effects make every attack more exciting, especially during slow-mo moments. While they don't change gameplay, they add a stylish, VR-like flair to your ninja's moves.

Tip: Try combining different swords and trails to create a personalized ninja vibe that matches your playstyle!
