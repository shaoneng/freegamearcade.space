Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Welcome to the world of Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner! Your mission is to guide a skilled ninja through a series of challenging platforms, slicing everything that stands in your way. Use your mouse or finger to control your katana's path across the screen.

As you run forward automatically, enemies and projectiles like shurikens and bullets will fly towards you. Swipe your blade to cut them down before they can hit you. Time your slices perfectly to trigger epic slow-motion effects, giving you a stylish advantage. You'll also need to dodge massive obstacles that cannot be sliced, so stay alert!

Earn coins by completing levels and use them in the shop to unlock a variety of unique swords and cool trail effects. These customizations don't change the gameplay, but they make every slice feel more personal and awesome. Master the art of the blade and see how far you can run!
