Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Grab your katana and prepare to slice through endless waves of enemies! In this action-packed runner, you control a powerful ninja on a high platform, facing a barrage of foes. Use your finger to move your character and automatically slice through anything in your path, from flying shurikens and bullets to enemy shields. You'll also need to dodge massive projectiles to survive.

Each level presents a new, colorful 3D world to conquer. Experience the thrill of intense sword fights and satisfying slow-motion effects that highlight your best moves. The game masterfully combines the slicing fun of Fruit Ninja with the addictive challenge of an endless runner.

Customize Your Arsenal

One of the most exciting features is the ability to customize your ninja's gear. Head to the in-game shop to unlock a wide variety of sword skins and trail effects. You can spend 700 coins for a random sword or earn specific blades by leveling up and completing challenges. Equip everything from classic katanas to more unconventional weapons to define your style.

Enhance your attacks with spectacular trail effects that leave a visual mark with every swing. While they don't change the gameplay, they add a stylish, VR-like flair to your combat. Try combining different swords and trails to create the ultimate ninja aesthetic!

Looking for More Action?

If you enjoy the thrill of being a ninja, be sure to check out Blocky Parkour Ninja. It offers a different kind of challenge, focusing on stealth and precision platforming as you sneak through enemy bases and dodge deadly traps.
