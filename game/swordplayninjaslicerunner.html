Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Grab your katana and get ready to slice your way through waves of enemies! Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner is an action runner game where you become a blade-wielding ninja on a high platform filled with enemies. Using just your finger, you'll slice flying shurikens, deflect bullets, and shatter shields while dodging massive obstacles.

The game immerses you in a simple yet colorful 3D world that changes with each level. Enjoy satisfying slow-motion effects that highlight your epic moves, blending the slicing fun of Fruit Ninja with the constant thrill of an endless runner.

Customize Your Style

A key feature is the wide range of sword skins and trail effects you can unlock in the shop to make your slicing feel even more rewarding:

  • Swords: Unlock random swords with coins or collect specific ones by leveling up. Customize your arsenal with weapons ranging from classic katanas to quirky alternatives.
  • Trails: Leave a mark with every swing! Unlock visual trails like fire or light to add extra flair to your attacks, making every hit more stylish, especially during slow-mo moments.

Tip: Trails don’t change gameplay, but they make every hit more spectacular. Try combining different swords and trails to match your ninja vibe!
