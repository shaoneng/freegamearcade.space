Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Grab your katana and get ready to slice your way through waves of enemies! Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner is an action runner game where you become a blade-wielding ninja on a high platform filled with enemies. You'll be slicing flying shurikens, bullets, and shields, or dodging massive projectiles with just a move of your finger.

The game throws you into a simple but colorful 3D world that changes with each level. Complete sword fights, enjoy the slicing action, and appreciate the satisfying slow-motion effects. It’s a perfect mix of Fruit Ninja’s slicing satisfaction and the thrilling fun of endless runners.

A key part of the fun is customization! You can unlock a wide range of sword skins and trail effects in the shop. Use coins to unlock random swords or earn specific ones by leveling up and completing challenges. Change up your blade with unique weapons, from classic katanas to quirky alternatives, and leave a stylish trail with every swing.

Tip: Trails don’t change gameplay, but they make every hit more stylish and add to the immersive experience. Try combining different swords and trails to match your unique ninja vibe!
