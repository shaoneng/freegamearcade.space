Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner! Your objective is simple but thrilling: become a master ninja and slice your way through every obstacle and enemy.

Use your mouse or finger to control your katana. Drag across the screen to slice through enemies, flying shurikens, and even bullets. Time your slashes carefully to take down everything in your path.

As you run along the high platforms, you'll encounter various challenges. Dodge massive projectiles and deflect attacks with precision. Enjoy the satisfying slow-motion effects that trigger on perfect hits, making you feel like a true blade master.

Collect coins as you play to unlock a wide array of new swords and cool trail effects in the shop. These customizations don't just look great—they make every slice feel unique and powerful. Complete levels and challenges to earn even more rewards!
