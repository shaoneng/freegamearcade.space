Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Welcome to the world of Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner! Your mission is simple yet challenging: guide your ninja through a series of dynamic levels, slicing everything that stands in your way. Use your mouse or finger to control your katana's path across the screen. Your movement is automatic, so your focus is purely on offense and defense.

As you run, enemies will appear, and projectiles like shurikens and bullets will fly toward you. A swift slice will destroy them, protecting you from harm. Time your slashes perfectly to enjoy satisfying slow-motion effects that highlight your ninja skills. Dodge massive obstacles that cannot be cut and reach the end of each stage to progress.

Earn coins by defeating enemies and completing levels. Use these coins in the shop to unlock a wide variety of cool new swords and dazzling trail effects. While these upgrades are cosmetic, they add a fantastic personal flair to your slicing action, making every victory even more stylish!
