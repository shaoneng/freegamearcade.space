Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Your goal is to guide your ninja's katana using your mouse or finger. As you automatically advance along the platform, enemies and various projectiles will fly towards you. Your mission is to slice them before they hit you.

Click and drag your mouse (or swipe your finger on a touch screen) to control the blade. Swiftly cut through flying shurikens, bullets, and enemy shields. Timing is key to deflecting attacks and eliminating foes. The game features satisfying slow-motion effects during intense moments, giving you a better chance to react.

Watch out for massive bullets and other large obstacles that cannot be sliced. You must skillfully move your sword away to dodge them. Surviving each level earns you coins, which can be spent in the shop to unlock a wide variety of cool swords and stylish trail effects to personalize your ninja experience.
