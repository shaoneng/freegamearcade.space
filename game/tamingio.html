Taming io

How to Play Taming io

Welcome to the wild world of Taming.io! Your goal is to survive, thrive, and become the most powerful tamer on the server. You'll start with nothing but a single pet by your side. Use your mouse to move around the map and interact with the environment.

Begin by gathering essential resources. Click on trees to get wood and rocks to mine stone. These resources are crucial for crafting tools, weapons, and building structures. As you gather, you'll gain experience points (XP) and level up. Each time you level up, you can choose a new skill or item to help you on your journey.

One of the core mechanics is taming wild animals. Approach an animal and attack it until its health is low, then you can tame it to join your team. Build a powerful squad of pets to fight alongside you. You can also build windmills to generate gold, which is used for purchasing special items and upgrades. Team up with friends to form alliances, share resources, and conquer the wilderness together!
