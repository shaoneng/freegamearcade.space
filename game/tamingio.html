Taming io

How to Play Taming io

Welcome to the wild world of Taming.io! Your goal is to survive, thrive, and become the most powerful tamer. Use your mouse to move your character and interact with the environment. Left-click to attack or gather resources.

Start by gathering essential resources. Hit trees to collect wood, strike rocks for stone and gold, and pick fruits for food. These materials are the foundation for your survival, allowing you to craft tools, weapons, and build protective structures.

You begin with a loyal pet. As you explore, you can tame other wild animals to join your squad. Your pets will fight alongside you, helping you defeat hostile creatures and other players. Building a strong team of pets is key to dominating the arena.

As you gather, craft, and fight, you will gain experience and level up. Each time you level up, you can choose a new skill or item to upgrade your character. Build windmills to generate a steady stream of gold, which you can use for powerful upgrades and to fortify your base. Team up with friends to increase your chances of survival and climb to the top of the leaderboard!
