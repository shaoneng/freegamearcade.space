How to Play Taming io

Welcome to the survival adventure of Taming.io! You'll start in the wilderness with just one loyal pet. Use the WASD keys to move and your mouse to look around. The Left Mouse Button is your primary action keyâ€”use it to attack, harvest resources like wood from trees and stone from rocks, and interact with objects.

Your main goal is to survive and become stronger. Use the resources you've gathered to craft tools, weapons, and structures. Building a windmill is essential, as it will generate gold for you over time. This gold is crucial for purchasing upgrades and powerful items to aid your survival against hostile creatures and other players.

You don't have to face the world alone! You can tame wild animals you encounter to build your own personal army. Each animal has unique skills. For even greater power, form alliances with friends and other players to expand your team, dominate the map, and climb your way to the top of the leaderboard as the ultimate tamer.
