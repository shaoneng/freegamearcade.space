Taming io

How to Play Taming io

Welcome to the wild world of Taming.io! Your adventure begins in a vast jungle with only a single magical pet for company. To survive and dominate, you must act quickly. Start by harvesting resources: hit trees for wood and break rocks for stone. Use these materials to craft essential tools, powerful weapons, and structures for defense.

As you explore, you'll encounter various wild animals. Weaken them in battle and then tame them to join your squad! The more pets you have, the stronger you become. You can also team up with other players to form alliances, making it easier to survive against tough opponents and bosses.

Leveling up is key to success. With each new level, you'll be able to unlock and choose from a variety of new items, including better weapons, useful buildings like windmills for generating gold, and more. Choose your upgrades wisely to suit your playstyle and climb the leaderboard to become the number one tamer!
