Taming io

How to Play Taming io

Welcome to the wild! Your adventure in Taming.io begins with just you and your loyal pet. Use your mouse to move and click to attack or gather resources. Start by hitting trees for wood and rocks for stone. These are the basic materials you'll need to craft your first tools and build a shelter to protect you from other players and hostile creatures.

A key part of the game is taming wild animals. Approach an animal and attack it until its health is low, then you can tame it to join your squad. Each pet has unique abilities, so build a diverse team! You can also build structures like windmills to generate gold, which is essential for purchasing upgrades and powerful items.

As you gather, build, and defeat enemies, you'll gain experience and level up. With each level, you'll get to choose from a selection of upgrades, including new weapons, tools, or food. Team up with friends to form a powerful alliance, conquer the map, and climb the global leaderboard to prove you are the ultimate tamer!
