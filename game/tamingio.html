Taming io

How to Play Taming io

Welcome to the wild world of Taming io! Your adventure begins with just you and your first magical pet. Use your mouse to navigate the world and the left-click button to attack or gather resources. Start immediately by hitting trees for wood and rocks for stone.

As you gather resources, you can craft tools, weapons, and build structures to protect yourself. A key objective is to build a windmill, which will generate gold for you over time. Gold is crucial for purchasing upgrades and other essential items.

Don't face the dangers alone! You can tame wild animals you encounter by approaching them and attacking until their health bar is depleted. Once tamed, they will join your squad and fight alongside you. You can also team up with other players to form powerful alliances and increase your chances of survival.

Defeating enemies and gathering resources will earn you experience points. As you level up, you can choose new upgrades and abilities to become stronger. Keep an eye on your health and hunger, and strive to climb the leaderboard to become the ultimate tamer!
