Taming io

How to Play Taming io

Welcome to the wilderness of Taming.io! Your goal is to survive, thrive, and become the most powerful tamer. You start with only your character and a single pet. Immediately begin gathering resources by hitting trees for wood and rocks for stone.

Use your collected resources to craft tools, weapons, and essential buildings. A key structure is the windmill, which will automatically generate gold for you. Gold is crucial for purchasing upgrades and items that will help you survive longer and defeat enemies.

You're not alone in this world! Tame wild animals you encounter to build a personal squad. Each animal has unique abilities. You can also team up with other players and their pets to form a powerful alliance, making it easier to explore and conquer the map.

As you perform actions and defeat opponents, you will gain experience and level up. Each level-up allows you to choose from a selection of new items like powerful weapons, better tools, or food for healing. Choose your upgrades wisely to suit your playstyle and dominate the leaderboard!
