Taming io

How to Play Taming io

Welcome to the wilds of Taming.io! Your journey begins with just you and your first pet. Use the WASD keys to move around the map and your mouse to interact with the environment. Left-click to gather resources like wood from trees and stone from rocks. These materials are crucial for crafting tools, weapons, and building your base.

As you explore, you'll encounter wild animals. Approach them and press the action key to tame them. Tamed animals will follow you and help you fight against other creatures and players, forming your own survival squad. The more you have, the stronger you become!

Defeating enemies and gathering resources will grant you experience points. As you level up, you'll be able to choose from a selection of new items, including powerful weapons and useful structures. Build windmills to generate gold, which can be used for upgrades. Team up with friends, expand your territory, and climb the leaderboard to prove you are the ultimate tamer!
