Taming io

How to Play Taming io

Welcome to the wild world of Taming.io! You begin your adventure in the jungle with a single pet. Your immediate goal is survival. Use your mouse to move and click to interact. Start by hitting trees and rocks to gather wood and stone. These resources are crucial for crafting your first tools and weapons.

A key feature of the game is taming wild animals. Approach different creatures and tame them to have them join your side, fighting and gathering for you. You can build a powerful squad of pets! For even greater strength, form alliances with other players and combine your animal teams to take on powerful bosses and other players.

As you gather resources, defeat enemies, and survive, you will gain experience and level up. With each new level, you'll be able to choose from a selection of upgrades, including better weapons, more effective tools, or structures like windmills to generate gold. Choose your upgrades wisely to suit your playstyle.

The ultimate goal is to become the top player on the server's leaderboard. Build a secure base, assemble an unstoppable team of pets, and outlast all your rivals to prove you are the ultimate tamer!
