Taming io

How to Play Taming io

Your journey in Taming.io begins in a vast wilderness with just one magical pet by your side. Your immediate goal is to survive. Use your mouse to move and click to interact with the environment. Start by gathering essential resources: hit trees for wood, break rocks for stone, and collect fruits for food. These materials are crucial for crafting your first tools and weapons.

Once you have some resources, you can craft items to help you progress. Build a windmill to start generating gold, which is vital for upgrades and other purchases. Craft weapons to defend yourself against hostile creatures and other players. Building structures like walls and spikes can create a safe base for you and your pets.

The key to success is building your animal army. Approach wild animals and engage them in battle. Once you defeat them, you'll have the chance to tame them, adding them to your team. Each animal has unique abilities. You can also team up with other players to form powerful alliances, combining your pet squads to dominate the server and climb the leaderboard.

As you gather, craft, and battle, you'll gain experience and level up. With each new level, you'll be able to unlock new items, more powerful weapons, and new buildings. Choose your upgrades wisely to suit your playstyle and become the ultimate tamer in the world of Taming.io!
