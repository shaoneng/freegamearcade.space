Taming io

How to Play Taming io

Welcome to the wild world of Taming.io! Your adventure begins with just you and a single loyal pet. Use the WASD keys to move around the map and the mouse to interact with the environment. Your primary goal is to survive, which means gathering resources immediately. Click on trees to get wood, rocks for stone, and bushes for fruit to keep your health up.

As you collect resources, you can start crafting essential items. Access your inventory and crafting menu to create tools like axes and pickaxes, which will help you gather resources faster. You can also craft weapons to defend yourself against hostile players and animals. A key early objective is to build a windmill, which will generate gold for you over time, allowing you to purchase powerful upgrades.

The most unique feature of Taming.io is the ability to tame wild animals. Approach an animal and attack it until its health is low, then stop attacking. If successful, it will join you as a pet! You can build a squad of loyal animal companions who will follow you and automatically fight for you. Team up with friends and combine your pet armies to become an unstoppable force.

Everything you do, from gathering to fighting, earns you experience points (XP). As you level up, you'll be presented with choices for new items and abilities. Choose wisely to suit your playstyle, whether you want to be a master builder, a fierce warrior, or the ultimate animal tamer. Keep an eye on the leaderboard and strive to become the top player on the server!
