Tanks of the Galaxy

How to Play Tanks of the Galaxy

Your mission is to command a powerful tank through a series of hostile galactic arenas. Use your controls to navigate the maze-like levels, hunting down enemy tanks and other obstacles.

Aim and fire your cannon to destroy enemy units and explosive barrels. When defeated, they will drop valuable coins. Be sure to drive over the coins to collect them as your reward.

Between levels, visit the Hangar (the in-game shop) to spend your hard-earned coins. You can purchase powerful new weapons and essential upgrades to enhance your tank's combat capabilities and survivability.

Successfully clear all enemies in a level to unlock the next one. With 25 unique stages to conquer, your ultimate goal is to push deeper into space and prove you're the best tank commander in the galaxy!
