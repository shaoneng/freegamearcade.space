Tanks of the Galaxy

How to Play Tanks of the Galaxy

Your mission in Tanks of the Galaxy is to conquer 25 unique levels by destroying all enemy forces. Use your controls to navigate your tank through each maze-like arena, hunting down hostile tanks and explosive barrels.

When you destroy an enemy or a barrel, they will drop valuable coins. Make sure to drive over these coins to collect them. Your survival and success depend on gathering as much loot as possible!

Between levels, you'll visit the Hangar. Here, you can spend the coins you've collected to purchase powerful new weapons and essential upgrades for your tank. A better-equipped tank will give you the edge you need to clear the increasingly difficult later stages. Choose your upgrades wisely and dominate the galaxy!
