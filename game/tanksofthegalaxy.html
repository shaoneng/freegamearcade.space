Tanks of the Galaxy

How to Play Tanks of the Galaxy

Welcome, Commander! Your mission in Tanks of the Galaxy is to navigate your tank through a series of challenging, maze-like levels. Use your controls to move your tank around the arena and aim your cannon. Your primary objective is to destroy all enemy tanks and any explosive barrels you find in your path.

As you defeat enemies and destroy barrels, they will drop valuable coins. Make sure to collect them! Between levels, you can visit the Hangar, which acts as the game's shop. Spend your hard-earned coins to purchase powerful new weapons and crucial upgrades for your tank, making you a more formidable force on the battlefield.

The game features 25 unique levels, each more challenging than the last. To advance, you must clear the current level of all hostile forces. Strategize your attacks, upgrade wisely, and push deeper into enemy territory to prove your mettle as the galaxy's finest tank commander!
