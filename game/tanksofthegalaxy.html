Tanks of the Galaxy

How to Play Tanks of the Galaxy

Your mission in Tanks of the Galaxy is to command a lone tank and conquer 25 unique levels filled with hostile forces. The objective is to clear each arena of enemy tanks to advance to the next stage.

Use your keyboard's WASD or Arrow Keys to steer your tank through the maze-like environments. Aim your cannon with the Mouse and click the Left Mouse Button to fire.

As you destroy enemy tanks and environmental objects like barrels, they will drop valuable coins. Maneuver your tank to collect these coins before they disappear. Your survival and success depend on it!

Between levels, visit the Hangar to spend your hard-earned coins. You can purchase a variety of powerful new weapons and essential upgrades to enhance your tank's firepower and durability. Choose your upgrades wisely to overcome increasingly difficult challenges.
