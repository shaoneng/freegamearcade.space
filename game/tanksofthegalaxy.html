Tanks of the Galaxy

How to Play Tanks of the Galaxy

Your main objective in Tanks of the Galaxy is to navigate each level, destroying all enemy tanks and obstacles. Control your tank's movement through the maze-like arenas and aim your cannon to eliminate threats. Keep an eye on your health and maneuver strategically to avoid incoming fire.

As you destroy enemy tanks and barrels, they will drop coins. Make sure to drive over these coins to collect them. After successfully clearing a level, you can visit the Hangar. In the Hangar, you can spend your hard-earned coins to buy more powerful weapons and crucial upgrades, making your tank more formidable for the challenges ahead.

The game features 25 unique levels, each more challenging than the last. By clearing a level, you unlock the next one. Continue your campaign, upgrade your arsenal, and conquer the entire galaxy one level at a time!
