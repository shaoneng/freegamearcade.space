Tanks of the Galaxy

How to Play Tanks of the Galaxy

Your mission in Tanks of the Galaxy is to navigate through a series of challenging levels, destroying all enemy forces in your path.

Use your controls to steer your tank through each maze-like arena. Aim your cannon and fire to eliminate hostile tanks and explosive barrels.

When you destroy enemies and barrels, they will drop valuable coins. Make sure to collect them! After each level, you can visit the Hangar to spend your coins on powerful new weapons and essential upgrades for your tank.

Clearing a level will unlock the next one, with 25 unique stages to conquer. Keep upgrading your arsenal to face increasingly difficult challenges as you venture deeper into the galaxy.
