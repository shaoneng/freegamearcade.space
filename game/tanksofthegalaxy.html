Tanks of the Galaxy

How to Play Tanks of the Galaxy

Welcome to the battlefield, Commander! Your mission in Tanks of the Galaxy is to navigate treacherous cosmic arenas and prove you're the best tank operator in the galaxy.

Movement: Use the WASD keys or Arrow keys on your keyboard to steer your tank through the level.

Combat: Aim with your mouse and click the left mouse button to fire your cannon. Your goal is to destroy all enemy tanks and destructible barrels in the area.

Upgrades: When you destroy enemies, they drop valuable coins. Be sure to collect them! Between levels, you can visit the Hangar to spend your coins on powerful new weapons and essential upgrades to enhance your tank's performance.

Progression: Clear all enemies on a level to unlock the next one. With 25 unique levels to conquer, the challenge will only get tougher. Good luck!
