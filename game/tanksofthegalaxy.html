Tanks of the Galaxy

How to Play Tanks of the Galaxy

Your mission in Tanks of the Galaxy is to navigate your tank through a series of hostile, maze-like levels. Use your controls to move around the arena and aim your cannon at enemy tanks and explosive barrels.

Destroying enemies and barrels will cause them to drop valuable coins. Maneuver your tank to collect these coins before they disappear. Your goal is to clear each level of all hostile forces to advance to the next challenge.

Between levels, you can visit the Hangar. This is your command center for upgrades. Spend the coins you've collected to purchase more powerful weapons and improve your tank's performance. Strategic upgrades are key to conquering the 25 unique levels and becoming a true legend of the galaxy!
