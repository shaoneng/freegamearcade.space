Tanks of the Galaxy

How to Play Tanks of the Galaxy

Welcome, Commander! Your mission in Tanks of the Galaxy is to navigate a series of challenging levels, destroying all enemy forces in your path. Use your controls to steer your tank through the maze-like arenas, hunting down hostile tanks and blasting them to bits.

When you destroy an enemy tank or barrel, it will drop valuable coins. Make sure to drive over and collect them! These coins are your currency for survival and domination. Between levels, you can visit the Hangar (the in-game shop) to spend your hard-earned coins on crucial upgrades and devastating new weapons.

Your goal is to clear each of the 25 unique levels to progress deeper into enemy territory. Each new level presents a tougher challenge, so keep upgrading your tank to stand a chance. Good luck, and may your aim be true!
