Tanks of the Galaxy

How to Play Tanks of the Galaxy

Your mission in Tanks of the Galaxy is to clear all 25 levels by destroying all enemy forces. It's a top-down shooter where strategy and firepower are key.

Controls: Use your mouse or keyboard (W/A/S/D or Arrow Keys) to steer your tank through each maze-like arena. Aim with your mouse and click the left mouse button to fire your main cannon.

Gameplay: Blast enemy tanks and explosive barrels. When destroyed, they will drop coins. Drive over the coins to collect them. Be careful to avoid enemy fire!

Upgrades: Between levels, you'll visit the Hangar. Spend the coins you've collected to buy powerful new weapons and upgrade your tank's capabilities. A stronger tank will be essential for conquering later levels.
