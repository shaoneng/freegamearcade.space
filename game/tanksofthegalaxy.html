Tanks of the Galaxy

How to Play Tanks of the Galaxy

Welcome to the battlefield, Commander! Your mission in Tanks of the Galaxy is to navigate a series of hostile arenas and eliminate all opposition.

Controls: Use your mouse or keyboard (check in-game for specifics) to steer your tank through each maze-like level. Aim your cannon and fire to destroy enemy tanks and explosive barrels.

Collect & Upgrade: Every enemy or barrel you destroy will drop coins. Drive over these coins to collect them. After completing a level, you can visit the Hangar to spend your hard-earned coins on powerful new weapons and vital performance upgrades for your tank.

Progression: Clear all enemies from a level to unlock the next. With 25 unique levels to conquer, you'll need to continuously upgrade your arsenal to survive the escalating challenge. Good luck!
