Tanks of the Galaxy

How to Play Tanks of the Galaxy

Your objective in Tanks of the Galaxy is to navigate your tank through a series of challenging levels, destroying all enemy forces you encounter. Use your controls to move around the maze-like arenas and aim your cannon at hostile tanks and explosive barrels.

When you destroy enemies or barrels, they will drop valuable coins. Maneuver your tank to collect these coins before they disappear. The more coins you gather, the more powerful you can become!

Between levels, you'll visit the Hangar. This is your shop where you can spend your hard-earned coins on crucial upgrades. Purchase new, more powerful weapons and improve your tank's stats to stand a chance against tougher opponents in later stages. Clear all 25 levels to prove you are the ultimate tank commander in the galaxy!
