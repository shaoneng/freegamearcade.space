Tanks of the Galaxy

How to Play Tanks of the Galaxy

Your mission in Tanks of the Galaxy is to command a powerful tank through a series of challenging top-down arenas. Use your keyboard to navigate the maze-like levels and your mouse to aim and fire your cannon. Your primary objective is to destroy all enemy tanks and explosive barrels in your path.

As you obliterate your foes, they will drop valuable coins. Maneuver your tank to collect these coins before they disappear. The more you collect, the more powerful you can become!

Between levels, visit the Hangar to spend your hard-earned coins. You can purchase a variety of powerful new weapons and crucial upgrades for your tank. Strategically choose your upgrades to overcome the increasingly difficult challenges across 25 unique levels. Clear each level to unlock the next and prove you're the ultimate tank commander in the galaxy!
