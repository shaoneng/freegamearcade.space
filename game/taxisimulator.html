Taxi Simulator

How to Play Taxi Simulator

Welcome to the driver's seat! Your main goal in Taxi Simulator is to work as a cab driver in a busy city. You'll need to pick up passengers and drop them off at their destinations as quickly and safely as possible.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to control your taxi. Accelerate, brake, and steer your way through the city traffic. Keep an eye on the mini-map located in the top-right corner of your screen. The red circle marks the location of your next passenger, and the green circle shows you where you need to drop them off.

Once you reach the drop-off point, the challenge isn't over. You must carefully maneuver your taxi into the designated parking space to successfully complete the level and earn your fare. Drive carefully, follow the map, and prove you're the best taxi driver in town!
