Taxi Simulator

How to Play Taxi Simulator

Your mission is to become the most reliable taxi driver in the city. Each level presents a new challenge where you must pick up a customer and transport them to their destination safely and quickly.

Keep a close eye on the mini-map located in the top-right corner of your screen. The red circle indicates your customer's pickup location, while the green circle marks their destination. Navigate through the city's traffic to reach these points.

It's not just about speed! Once you reach the pickup or drop-off point, you must park your taxi precisely within the designated area. Proper parking is key to completing the mission and moving on to the next level.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to accelerate, brake, and steer your cab. Master the controls to weave through traffic and execute perfect parking maneuvers.
