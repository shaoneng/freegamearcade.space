How to Play TB World

Welcome to TB World, the ultimate sandbox game where you can bring your stories to life! The goal is to create, decorate, and play in your own custom world. With every item and character feature completely unlocked from the start, your imagination is the only rule.

The controls are simple and intuitive, designed for easy creation on any device.

  • Computer: Use your mouse to click, drag, and drop characters and objects anywhere you like on the screen.
  • Mobile/Tablet: Tap and hold an item to pick it up, then drag it to your desired location and release your finger to place it.
