TB World

How to Play TB World

Getting started in TB World is as easy as using your imagination! In this creative sandbox game, your main goal is to design spaces, dress up characters, and create your own unique stories. All items and features are unlocked from the start, so there are no limits to what you can build.

The controls are simple and intuitive:

For Computer Players: Use your mouse to click on any character or object. Hold the mouse button down to drag it around the screen, and release the button to place it in its new spot.

For Mobile and Tablet Players: Tap on an item and hold your finger on the screen to pick it up. Drag it to where you want it to go, and lift your finger to place it down. It's that simple to start decorating and playing!
