TB World

How to Play TB World

Welcome to the boundless world of TB World! Getting started is simple, as all items and features are unlocked from the very beginning. Your main goal is to use your creativity to build, decorate, and tell your own stories.

Controls:

  • On a Computer: Use your mouse to click, drag, and drop characters and items anywhere you like. Interact with different objects by clicking on them.
  • On a Mobile Device: Simply tap and hold an item to pick it up, then drag it to your desired location and release your finger to place it.

Explore different locations, customize your characters' outfits, and design the perfect house. There are no rules or goals, just endless opportunities for imaginative play!
