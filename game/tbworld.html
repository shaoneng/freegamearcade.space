TB World

How to Play TB World

Welcome to TB World, where your creativity is the only limit! This special fan-made version of Toca Boca World gives you instant access to every item and feature right from the start. Everything is unlocked, so you can dive straight into decorating houses, creating unique characters, and bringing your own stories to life without any restrictions.

The goal is pure, open-ended fun. Design your dream home, dress up your characters in countless outfits, and create imaginative scenarios. Think of it as a digital dollhouse with an infinite collection of furniture and accessories. You make the rules and tell the stories!

Controls:

  • Computer: Use your mouse to click, drag, and drop characters and objects around the world.
  • Mobile: Tap and hold to pick something up, then release your finger to place it where you want.

If you love the freedom of TB World, you might also enjoy other creative games like PopTropica, which offers a mix of character creation and puzzle-solving adventures.
