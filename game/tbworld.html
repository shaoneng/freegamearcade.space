How to Play TB World

Welcome to TB World, where your imagination is the only limit! Getting started is simple and intuitive, allowing you to jump straight into the fun of creating and decorating.

On a Computer: Use your mouse to interact with the world. Click and hold on any character or object to pick it up, then drag it to your desired location. Release the mouse button to place it down.

On a Mobile Device: The controls are just as easy. Tap and hold on an item or character to select it. Drag your finger across the screen to move it, and lift your finger to place it wherever you like. Explore all the unlocked items and start building your dream scenes!
