TB World

How to Play TB World

TB World offers intuitive controls for both desktop and mobile players, ensuring a smooth creative experience.

On Computer: Use your mouse to easily drag and drop objects around the screen. Click on an item to select it, then hold and move your mouse to place it wherever you desire.

On Mobile Devices: Simply tap and hold on an item to pick it up. Drag your finger to the desired location, then release to place it. This allows for effortless design and interaction on tablets and smartphones.
