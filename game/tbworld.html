TB World

How to Play TB World

In TB World, your creativity has no limits! This free online kids game takes the core fun of Toca Boca World and gives you instant access to every item and feature—everything is unlocked! No more waiting for special unlocks or saving up in-game currency. Instead, you can dive right in and start decorating the Toca Boca World with any item you desire.

You set the rules here. If you love making up stories, decorating houses, and customizing unique Toca Boca characters, then TB World is the game for you. It's like playing house on your tablet or computer with an infinite amount of dolls and toy furniture.

The game is mobile compatible, so you can play whether you're on a computer or a tablet. Prepare to create your own imaginative world and share it with friends!

Computer: Use your mouse to drag and drop objects around the screen.

Mobile: Tap and hold to pick something up, then release to place it.

Originally, TB World was inspired by the popular Toca Boca World series. This is a special fan-made version where everything is unlocked from the start, letting you test out all sorts of possibilities. If you enjoy TB World, you might also like Poptropica, another great online kids' game where you can create characters and go on platformer and puzzle adventures.
