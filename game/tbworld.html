TB World

How to Play TB World

In TB World, your creativity has no limits! This free online kids game is inspired by the fun of Toca Boca World but gives you instant access to every single item and feature right from the start. Everything is unlocked! No more waiting or saving up in-game currency. You can dive right in and start decorating, creating stories, and customizing unique characters.

The controls are super simple and designed for creative fun. On a computer, just use your mouse to drag and drop objects and characters anywhere you want. On a mobile device or tablet, tap and hold to pick something up, then release your finger to place it.

This is a fan-made version that lets you test out all sorts of possibilities without any restrictions. Design your dream house, dress up your characters in cool outfits, and create your own imaginative world. If you enjoy creative freedom, you'll love this game. You might also like PopTropica, another great online game where you create characters and go on puzzle adventures!
