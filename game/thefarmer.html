The Farmer

How to Play The Farmer

Welcome to your own virtual farm! The Farmer is a 3D simulation game where you'll experience the full cycle of farming. Your main goal is to successfully cultivate your land, grow crops, and sell them for a profit. Begin by selecting one of four tractorsâ€”start with a basic model and save up your earnings to unlock the powerful blue tractor later on.

Each level presents a new task. Your first job is usually to prepare the soil. You'll need to attach the cultivator to your tractor and drive to the designated field. Follow the on-screen arrows to guide you. Once there, carefully drive over the entire area until the progress bar in the top-right corner is full.

After cultivating, it's time to plant. Head back and swap your tool for the seed drill. Return to the field and sow your seeds, making sure to cover the entire plot for the best possible yield. Later levels will introduce other tasks like watering and using pesticides, all performed with your trusty tractor.

As you complete the 15 levels, you'll eventually get to harvest your crops. The final step is to transport your harvest to the city and sell it, earning the rewards for all your hard work. Enjoy the satisfaction of a job well done in The Farmer!
