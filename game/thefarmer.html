The Farmer

How to Play The Farmer

Welcome to your very own virtual farm! Your journey in The Farmer begins by selecting one of four powerful tractors to work your land. The better tractors can be unlocked as you earn money from completing jobs.

Each level presents a new farming task. Start by attaching the required tool to your tractor, such as a cultivator or a seed drill. Follow the on-screen arrows to navigate to your field. Once you arrive, your objective is to complete the taskâ€”like plowing the entire field or sowing all your seeds. Keep an eye on the progress bar in the top-right corner to see how much of the job is left.

As you progress through the game's 15 levels, you'll engage in the full cycle of farming. After cultivating and sowing, you'll tend to your crops and, finally, harvest them. The ultimate goal is to transport your harvest to the city, sell your crops, and earn the rewards for your hard work!
