The Farmer

How to Play The Farmer

Welcome to The Farmer, a realistic 3D farming simulator where you can experience the entire process of cultivating a field. Your goal is to manage your farm, from tilling the soil to selling your final harvest. Get ready to jump into your tractor and embrace the life of a modern farmer!

Begin your journey by selecting a tractor. You have four different models to choose from, with the final one being unlockable as you earn money by completing tasks. Once you've chosen your vehicle, you're ready to head to your field and start the first level.

Each stage of farming requires a specific tool. You'll start by attaching a cultivator to your tractor. Follow the on-screen arrow signs to navigate to the designated area. Once there, drive your tractor across the entire field until the progress bar in the top right corner is full. You'll follow a similar process for sowing, where you'll attach a seed drill and carefully plant seeds across the cultivated land.

The game features 15 different levels, each presenting a new farming operation. After successfully growing and harvesting your crops, your final task is to transport them to the city to sell them. Enjoy the satisfaction of being rewarded for all your hard work on the farm!
