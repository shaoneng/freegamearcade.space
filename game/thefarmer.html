The Farmer

How to Play The Farmer

Welcome to The Farmer, a realistic 3D farming simulation where you manage every aspect of your own farm. Your goal is to successfully cultivate, grow, and harvest crops across 15 engaging levels.

Your journey begins by selecting a tractor from the garage. While most are available from the start, you can use the money you earn from completing tasks to unlock the best tractor for the job. Once you've chosen your vehicle, it's time to head out to the fields and get to work.

Each level will present a specific task. Start by attaching the correct tool to your tractor, such as the cultivator or the seed drill. Follow the on-screen arrow signs to navigate to the designated area on your land. When you arrive, complete the operation, whether it's plowing the entire field or carefully sowing seeds. Keep an eye on the progress bar in the top-right corner to see how much of the task is left to complete.

As you progress, you'll undertake a variety of farming activities, including watering your plants and applying pesticides. The ultimate goal is to see your hard work pay off. Harvest your mature crops, load them up, and drive to the city to sell them for a profit! Enjoy the rewarding experience of building a successful farm from the ground up.
