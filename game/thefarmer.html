The Farmer

How to Play The Farmer

Begin your farming journey by selecting a tractor. You start with a basic model, but you can unlock more powerful ones by earning money from completing tasks. Once you've chosen your vehicle, it's time to get to work on your land!

Your first task is typically to cultivate the field. Attach the cultivator tool to your tractor and follow the on-screen arrow signs to the designated area. Drive your tractor carefully to ensure the entire plot of land is tilled. A progress bar in the upper right corner will show you how much you have completed.

After preparing the soil, you'll need to sow the seeds. Switch your attachment to the seed drill, head back to the field, and plant the seeds evenly across the tilled ground. The game will guide you through all necessary operations, including watering and pesticide application, to ensure a healthy crop.

Progress through 15 different levels, each offering unique farming challenges. The ultimate goal is to harvest your crops, transport them to the city, and sell them for a profit. Enjoy the satisfying reward for all your hard work in this immersive farming simulation!
