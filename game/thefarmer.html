The Farmer

How to Play The Farmer

Welcome to the rewarding world of virtual agriculture! Your journey in The Farmer begins by selecting your starting vehicle from a choice of four powerful tractors. As you complete jobs and earn money, you can even unlock a top-tier tractor to make your work more efficient.

Each level presents a specific farming task. You'll start by attaching the correct tool to your tractor, such as a cultivator for plowing the field or a seed drill for planting. Drive your tractor and the attached equipment to the designated area on your land to begin the job.

To complete your tasks, follow the arrow signs on the map. As you work on the field—whether you're plowing, sowing, or using pesticides—keep an eye on the progress bar in the top-right corner of the screen. Your goal is to reach 100% completion for each step of the process.

Progress through 15 different levels, mastering every aspect of farming. The final challenge involves harvesting your successful crops, transporting them to the city, and selling them to be rewarded for all your hard work. Get behind the wheel and start your farming adventure today!
