The Farmer

How to Play The Farmer

Welcome to your very own virtual farm! In The Farmer, you'll experience the full cycle of modern farming from the driver's seat of your tractor. Your main goal is to successfully complete various farming tasks across 15 challenging levels, from preparing the soil to selling your final harvest.

Begin by selecting one of the available tractors. As you complete jobs and earn money, you can unlock more powerful vehicles. Each level will present a new task. Start by attaching the correct tool to your tractor, such as the cultivator. Follow the on-screen arrows to navigate to your field.

Once in the field, drive your tractor methodically to cover the entire area. You can track your progress with the percentage bar in the top right corner. After cultivating, you'll switch to the seed drill to plant your crops. Later levels will introduce tasks like watering and applying pesticides. Complete all the steps to harvest your crops, take them to the city, and earn your reward!
