The Smurfs Cooking

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

Step into the kitchen with your favorite blue friends! The annual Smurf Food Festival is on, but Chef Smurf needs your help. He's forgotten his recipes, and it's up to you to save the day.

Your goal is to serve hungry Smurfs all the delicious treats they ask for. Get ready to whip up muffins, cupcakes, juice, and more. Pay close attention to their orders and work quickly to assemble the snacks before the timer runs out.

The faster you are, the happier your Smurf customers will be. This sweet and charming cooking challenge is perfect for fans of all ages and is an officially licensed game, ensuring an authentic Smurf experience!
