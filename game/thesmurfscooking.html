The Smurfs Cooking

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

Welcome to the Smurf Food Festival! Your goal is to help Chef Smurf serve all the hungry Smurfs who come to his food stall. Pay close attention to the order bubbles that appear above each Smurf's head.

Use your mouse or tap the screen to select the correct ingredients to assemble the order. You'll be making everything from cupcakes and muffins to juices with whipped cream. Work fast, as each Smurf has a patience meter!

Once an item is prepared, click or tap on it to serve it to the correct Smurf. The faster you serve, the higher your score will be. Keep the food flowing and don't let the timer run out. Good luck, and have a smurfy time!
