The Smurfs Cooking

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

Welcome to the Smurf Food Festival! Your goal in The Smurfs Cooking is to help Chef Smurf by serving all the hungry Smurfs who visit your food stall. Pay close attention to the orders that appear in the speech bubbles above each Smurf.

Use your mouse or tap on the screen to assemble the requested items. You'll be making delicious cupcakes, muffins, juices, and other tasty snacks. Click on the correct ingredients in the right order to prepare the treat.

Once an order is complete, serve it to the correct Smurf. Be quick! The Smurfs are hungry, and you're working against the clock. Serve as many customers as you can before time runs out to make the festival a huge success!
