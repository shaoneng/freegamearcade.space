The Smurfs Cooking

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

Welcome to the Smurf Food Festival! In The Smurfs Cooking, your goal is to help a stressed-out Chef Smurf serve a variety of delicious treats to all the hungry Smurfs.

Pay close attention to the orders appearing above each Smurf's head. You'll need to quickly assemble muffins, cupcakes with whipped cream, and pour juice to match their requests. Click or tap on the ingredients in the correct order to prepare the food.

Work fast! A timer is ticking down, and the Smurfs will get impatient if you take too long. Keep the line moving and serve as many happy customers as you can to get a high score. It's a sweet, fast-paced challenge perfect for fans of The Smurfs and cooking games alike!
