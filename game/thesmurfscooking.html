The Smurfs Cooking

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

Welcome to the Smurf Food Festival! Your mission in The Smurfs Cooking is to assist Chef Smurf by managing the food stall and serving all the hungry Smurfs who come to visit. Get ready for a fast-paced, fun-filled cooking challenge!

As Smurfs approach your counter, they will show you their desired order in a speech bubble. Pay close attention to exactly what they want, from the type of muffin or cupcake to the specific toppings and drinks.

To prepare an order, simply click or tap on the required items in the correct sequence. You might need to bake a cupcake, add some whipped cream, and pour a glass of juice. Accuracy and speed are key to keeping your customers happy.

Each level has a timer or a goal to reach. You must serve the Smurfs quickly before their patience runs out. The faster you serve, the more points you'll earn. Keep the food flowing, make your customers smurftastically happy, and prove you're the best chef in the village!
