The Smurfs Cooking

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

Welcome to the Smurf Village kitchen! Your goal in The Smurfs Cooking is to help Chef Smurf prepare delicious snacks for the annual Smurf Food Festival. Time is ticking, and the Smurfs are hungry, so you'll need to be quick!

Use your mouse or touchscreen to interact with the game. When a Smurf approaches the counter, they will show you their order in a speech bubble. Click or tap on the ingredients on your counter to assemble the treat exactly as requested.

Combine items like muffins, whipped cream, and sprinkles to create perfect cupcakes and other snacks. Don't forget the drinks! Serve the correct order to the Smurf before their patience runs out. The faster you serve, the happier they'll be, and the more points you'll earn. Keep an eye on the timer and serve as many Smurfs as you can to make the festival a huge success!
