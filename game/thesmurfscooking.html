The Smurfs Cooking

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

Welcome to the Smurf Food Festival! Your mission is to assist Chef Smurf in his busy kitchen. The gameplay is simple and fun, perfect for all ages.

When a Smurf customer arrives, they will show you their order in a speech bubble. Pay close attention to the items they want, which could be anything from cupcakes and muffins to fresh juice.

Click or tap on the ingredients and machines in the correct sequence to prepare the order. For example, you might need to grab a muffin, add some whipped cream, and serve it on a plate. Be quick! A timer is ticking, and the Smurfs are hungry.

Serve customers correctly and quickly to earn points and advance through levels. The faster you are, the happier the Smurfs will be. Good luck, and have a smurfy time!
