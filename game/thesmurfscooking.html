The Smurfs Cooking

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

Your mission in The Smurfs Cooking is to help Chef Smurf manage his food stall at the bustling Smurf Food Festival. It's a race against the clock to keep your fellow Smurfs happy and well-fed!

As hungry Smurfs approach your counter, they will show you their order in a speech bubble. Pay close attention to what they want, whether it's a delicious cupcake, a muffin, a glass of juice, or a combination of treats.

Using your mouse or touchscreen, click or tap on the ingredients in the correct sequence to prepare the order. Once the item is ready, serve it to the waiting Smurf. Work quickly and accurately to earn points and keep the line moving. If you take too long, the Smurfs will get impatient and leave!
