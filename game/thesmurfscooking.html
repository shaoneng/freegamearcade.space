The Smurfs Cooking

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

Welcome to the Smurf Food Festival! Your mission in The Smurfs Cooking is to help Chef Smurf manage his busy food stall. Hungry Smurfs will approach the counter with their orders, and it's your job to prepare and serve them as quickly as possible.

Pay close attention to the order bubbles above each Smurf's head. You'll need to assemble the correct treats, which might include cupcakes, muffins, or juice. Click or tap on the ingredients in the right sequence to prepare the item. Once it's ready, drag it to the waiting Smurf to complete their order and earn points.

The clock is ticking! You need to serve a certain number of customers to pass each level. Be efficient and avoid making mistakes, as angry customers will leave, and your time is limited. Keep the food flowing and the Smurfs happy to become the star of the festival!
