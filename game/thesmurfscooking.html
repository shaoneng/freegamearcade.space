The Smurfs Cooking

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

Your mission in The Smurfs Cooking is to help Chef Smurf run his food stall at the annual Smurf Food Festival. Hungry Smurfs will approach the counter with their orders. Your goal is to prepare and serve them the correct items as quickly as possible.

Pay close attention to each Smurf's request. You'll need to assemble treats like muffins, cupcakes with whipped cream, and delicious juices. Click or tap on the ingredients in the correct order to prepare the food.

Once the order is ready, serve it to the waiting Smurf. Work fast to keep your customers happy and earn points. Don't let the timer run out or let the Smurfs get too impatient! The faster you serve, the higher your score will be.
