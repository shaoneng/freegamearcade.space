The Smurfs Cooking

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

Welcome to the Smurf Food Festival! Your mission is to assist Chef Smurf in his kitchen as he prepares treats for all the other Smurfs. Your goal is to serve customers quickly and accurately to keep them happy and score points.

Pay close attention to the order bubbles that appear above each Smurf's head. You'll need to prepare a variety of items, from delicious muffins and cupcakes to refreshing juice. To create an item, simply click or tap on the ingredients on your counter in the correct sequence.

Once the order is complete, drag it to the waiting Smurf. Work as fast as you can, because you're on the clock! The faster you serve, the higher your score will be. Keep the food flowing and make this the best Smurf Food Festival ever!
