The Smurfs Cooking

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

Your mission in The Smurfs Cooking is to assist Chef Smurf during the bustling annual Smurf Food Festival. Hungry Smurfs will approach your counter with specific orders for delicious snacks like cupcakes, muffins, and juice.

Pay close attention to each Smurf's order bubble. You'll need to assemble their requested treat perfectly. Use your mouse or touch screen to tap on the correct ingredients in the right sequence. This might involve selecting a muffin base, adding a swirl of whipped cream, and pouring a refreshing juice.

Once the order is complete, serve it to the waiting Smurf to earn points and keep them happy. But be quick! Each Smurf has a patience meter, and if you take too long, they'll leave unhappy. Manage your time effectively to serve as many customers as possible before the day's timer runs out.
