The Smurfs Cooking

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

Welcome to Chef Smurf's kitchen! In The Smurfs Cooking, your goal is to help prepare delicious treats for the hungry Smurfs attending the annual food festival. The controls are simple and intuitive, designed for fun and fast-paced action.

Pay close attention to the orders that appear above each Smurf's head. Click or tap on the correct ingredients—like muffins, cupcakes, whipped cream, and juice—in the right sequence to assemble their snack. Once an order is complete, serve it to the waiting Smurf to earn points.

Be quick! A timer is ticking down, and the Smurfs will get impatient if you take too long. Manage your time effectively, prepare orders accurately, and keep the food flowing to make the festival a smurftastic success!
