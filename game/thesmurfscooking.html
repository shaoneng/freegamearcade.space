The Smurfs Cooking

How to Play The Smurfs Cooking

Welcome to the Smurf Food Festival! Your mission is to help Chef Smurf serve all the hungry Smurfs who visit his food stall. It's a race against the clock, so you'll need to be fast and accurate to keep everyone happy!

Pay close attention to the Smurfs lining up at the counter. Each one will have a speech bubble above their head showing the exact order they want. This could be a cupcake with a specific topping, a muffin, or a refreshing juice.

To prepare an order, simply click or tap on the required food items on your counter. For items like cupcakes, you'll need to first select the base and then add the correct whipped cream or topping as requested by the customer.

Once the order is complete, click and drag the finished treat to the Smurf who ordered it. Serve them quickly to earn more points and keep the line moving. Don't let the Smurfs wait too long, or they'll get impatient and leave!
