Thief Puzzle

How to Play Thief Puzzle

Your goal in Thief Puzzle is simple: steal the target item in each level to win! You play as a clever thief with an incredibly long and stretchy arm.

To play, just click (or tap on mobile) and drag your hand to the item you want to steal. Your arm will stretch around obstacles and towards the prize. Let go to snatch the item.

Each level is filled with traps, puzzles, and characters that can stop you. If your arm touches an obstacle like a laser, a guard, or a buzzing bee, you'll fail the level. Plan your path carefully to avoid getting caught and enjoy the funny animations, whether you win or lose!
