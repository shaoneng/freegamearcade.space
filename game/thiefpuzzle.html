Thief Puzzle

How to Play Thief Puzzle

Your mission in Thief Puzzle is simple: steal the target item in each level! You play as a clever thief whose only superpower is an incredibly long and stretchy arm. To play, simply click (or tap) and drag your hand from the thief towards the object you want to grab.

Be careful! Each level is filled with tricky obstacles, traps, and characters that will get in your way. If your arm touches anything it shouldn't, you'll fail the level and see a funny failure animation. You'll need to carefully navigate around things like lasers, TNT barrels, angry dogs, and more.

Success requires a mix of clever thinking and precise control. Analyze the scene, plan your route, and execute the perfect heist. With hundreds of unique and hilarious scenarios to solve, the fun never stops!
