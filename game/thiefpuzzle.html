Thief Puzzle

How to Play Thief Puzzle

Welcome to Thief Puzzle, a hilarious and clever puzzle game where you step into the shoes of a sneaky thief with an incredible superpower: a super-stretchy arm! Your mission on every level is simple—stretch out your hand and grab the target item, whether it's a bag of groceries or a priceless diamond ring.

Getting the prize is trickier than it looks. You must carefully navigate your elastic arm around a variety of obstacles. Dodge cunning traps, avoid angry characters, and be careful not to touch dangerous objects like TNT barrels or fire hydrants. One wrong move can trigger a chaotic and funny chain reaction, ending your heist in a hilarious fail.

Each of the 400 levels is a unique, self-contained story presented like a mini-cartoon. Whether you succeed or fail, the game rewards you with funny stickman animations that bring each scenario to life. From snatching a suitcase in front of the Eiffel Tower to swiping a treasure chest from a pirate, the game is packed with silly situations that will keep you entertained.

The joy of Thief Puzzle comes from experimentation. The game encourages you to try different paths, and even failure is part of the fun, often resulting in a slapstick scene that will make you laugh. If you love games where creativity and humor are key, and every choice leads to a surprising outcome, you'll find hours of fun in Thief Puzzle.
