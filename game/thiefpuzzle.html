Thief Puzzle

How to Play Thief Puzzle

Your mission in Thief Puzzle is simple: play as a master thief and use your incredibly elastic, stretchy arm to steal the target item in each level.

To play, click (or tap) and drag your hand to extend your arm towards the prize. Your goal is to navigate around obstacles and grab the item without touching anything dangerous.

Be careful! Each level is filled with traps, guards, and other hazards. If your arm touches an obstacle, you'll trigger a funny fail animation and have to restart the level. Think creatively to find the safest path for your hand to complete the heist!
