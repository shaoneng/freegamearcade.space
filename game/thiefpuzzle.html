Thief Puzzle

How to Play Thief Puzzle

Welcome to Thief Puzzle, where you become a master thief with a very special talent: an incredibly stretchy arm! In this clever and funny puzzle game, your goal is simple: stretch your arm to grab the target item in each level. But getting there is where the fun begins.

Each level presents a unique cartoon scene filled with traps, obstacles, and watchful characters. You'll need to think carefully and strategically guide your elastic arm to avoid setting off hilarious chain reactions. Touch the wrong thing, and you'll be treated to a funny failure animation. From snatching a pirate's treasure to swiping an engagement ring mid-proposal, the scenarios are endless and always entertaining.

With 400 levels to solve, the challenges become progressively trickier and more creative. One moment you're dodging a simple trap, the next you're navigating complex scenes with lasers, guards, and TNT barrels. The game is all about trial and error, so don't be afraid to experiment and enjoy the comical outcomes!

Not at all! Thief Puzzle is designed for everyone. While some levels can be tricky, the fun is in experimenting and finding the solution. You'll learn the ropes as you play.
