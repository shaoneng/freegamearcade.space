Thief Puzzle

How to Play Thief Puzzle

Welcome to Thief Puzzle, where your goal is to become the ultimate master thief! In each level, you're presented with a unique scenario where you need to steal a specific item.

To play, use your mouse (on desktop) or your finger (on mobile) to click and drag the thief's incredible stretchy arm. Guide his hand through the level to reach the target object, whether it's a bag of groceries, a precious diamond, or something completely unexpected.

The challenge lies in navigating the obstacles. You must carefully maneuver the arm around traps, lasers, sleeping guards, angry dogs, and other hazards. If your arm touches any of these obstacles, you'll fail the level and be treated to a funny animation. Think carefully about the path your arm will take before you act!

Successfully grab the item and retract your arm without getting caught to complete the level and move on to the next hilarious heist. With hundreds of clever puzzles, each attempt is a mix of strategy, timing, and a bit of mischievous fun.
