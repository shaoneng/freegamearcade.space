Thief Puzzle

How to Play Thief Puzzle

Thief Puzzle is an engaging puzzle game where your goal is simple: grab the target item in each level. You control a clever thief with a remarkably stretchy arm. Use your mouse or finger (on mobile) to drag and extend his arm to reach the desired object.

Be careful though! Each of the 400 levels is filled with various traps, angry characters, obstacles, and unpredictable elements like TNT barrels. Touching the wrong thing or getting caught will result in a funny, cartoon-style failure animation. Observe the environment carefully, plan your stretch, and avoid triggering chain reactions to successfully complete your heist. Experiment with different approaches; the game often rewards clever thinking and trial-and-error.
