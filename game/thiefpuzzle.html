Thief Puzzle

How to Play Thief Puzzle

Your mission in Thief Puzzle is to become the sneakiest thief around by solving clever puzzles. In each level, your goal is to grab a specific item without getting caught.

Controls:
- Use your mouse to click and drag the thief's hand.
- Stretch his elastic arm around obstacles to reach the target item.
- Release the mouse button to snatch the prize and complete the level.

Gameplay:
Think before you grab! Each stage is filled with traps, angry characters, and other objects that can foil your plan. If your arm touches anything it shouldn't, you'll trigger a funny fail animation and have to try again. You'll need to be clever to navigate past lasers, avoid waking up guards, and figure out the safest path to victory. With hundreds of unique scenarios, every level presents a new and hilarious challenge to solve.
