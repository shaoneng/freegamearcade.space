Thief Puzzle

How to Play Thief Puzzle

Your goal in Thief Puzzle is to help a clever thief steal a specific item in each level. You'll use his one special power: an incredibly long and stretchy arm! To play, simply click (or tap on mobile) and hold your mouse button, then drag to extend the thief's arm towards the prize.

Be careful! Each level is filled with obstacles, traps, and characters that will stop you. You must navigate your arm around these dangers. If your arm touches anything it shouldn't, like a laser, a guard, or an explosive barrel, you'll fail the level and be treated to a funny animation.

Success requires both creativity and precision. Look at the entire scene to figure out the safest path for your arm. Sometimes you'll need to be sneaky, and other times you'll need to be quick. With hundreds of levels, the puzzles get trickier, so think before you stretch!
