Thief Puzzle

How to Play Thief Puzzle

Your goal in every level of Thief Puzzle is to steal a specific item using your character's incredibly long, stretchy arm. You are a clever thief with the superpower to reach almost anything, but you'll need to be smart to succeed.

To play, simply click (or tap) and drag your mouse to stretch the thief's arm towards the target object. Aim carefully to grab the prize without touching any obstacles, setting off traps, or alerting any guards.

Each level is a unique puzzle filled with funny stickman characters, lasers, TNT barrels, and all sorts of other hazards. If your arm touches anything it shouldn't, you'll trigger a hilarious failure animation and have to restart the level. Think creatively to find the safest path for your arm to complete the heist!

With 400 different levels, each presenting a new comical scenario and challenge, you'll have endless puzzles to solve and funny situations to enjoy. Whether you win or fail, a funny animation awaits!
