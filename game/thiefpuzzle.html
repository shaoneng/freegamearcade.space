Thief Puzzle

How to Play Thief Puzzle

Your objective in Thief Puzzle is simple: use your character's incredibly long and stretchy arm to steal a specific item in each level.

To play, just click (or tap on mobile) and drag your hand towards the target item. Carefully guide your arm around obstacles, traps, and other characters. If your arm touches anything it shouldn't, you'll trigger a funny fail animation and have to restart the level.

Think strategically about the path you take. Some objects can be helpful, while others will instantly cause you to fail. Experiment with different routes to solve the puzzle and complete the heist successfully. With hundreds of unique scenarios, each level presents a new and clever challenge to overcome.
