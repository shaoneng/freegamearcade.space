Thief Puzzle

How to Play Thief Puzzle

Your goal in Thief Puzzle is simple: use your incredibly stretchy arm to steal the target item in each level. To play, just click and hold your mouse button (or tap and hold on a touch screen), then drag to extend the thief's arm towards the prize.

Be strategic! You must navigate around a variety of obstacles and traps. These can include anything from angry guards and sleeping dogs to lasers and explosive TNT barrels. If your arm touches any of these hazards, you'll trigger a hilarious fail animation and have to restart the level.

Think carefully about the path your arm will take. Sometimes you need to be quick, and other times you need a clever, indirect route to succeed. With hundreds of levels, each with its own unique challenge, you'll need all your wits to pull off the perfect heist!
