Tic Tac Toe

How to Play Tic Tac Toe

Welcome to the classic game of Tic Tac Toe! Your objective is to be the first player to form a line of three of your symbols on the 3x3 grid. You can win by creating a line horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Players take turns placing their mark—either 'X' or 'O'—into an empty square on the board. The game offers two exciting modes: challenge a friend in a local 2-player match or test your strategic thinking against the computer's AI.

Success in Tic Tac Toe requires both offense and defense. While you focus on creating your own winning line, you must also pay close attention to your opponent's moves to block them from getting three in a row. Plan ahead, anticipate your opponent, and claim your victory!
