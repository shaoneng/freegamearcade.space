Tic Tac Toe

How to Play Tic Tac Toe

Welcome to the classic game of Tic Tac Toe! The objective is simple: be the first player to form a line of three of your symbols (either 'X' or 'O') on the 3x3 grid. This winning line can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.

The game is played in turns. On your turn, simply click or tap on an empty square to place your mark. You can choose to play against a friend in 2-player mode or challenge the computer (AI) for a single-player experience.

Winning requires both offense and defense. While you try to create your own line of three, you must also be vigilant to block your opponent from doing the same. Think ahead and anticipate your opponent's moves to secure your victory in this timeless game of strategy!
