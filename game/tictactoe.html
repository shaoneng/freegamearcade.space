Tic Tac Toe

How to Play Tic Tac Toe

Welcome to the classic game of Tic Tac Toe! The objective is simple: be the first player to get three of your marks in a row on the 3x3 grid.

Players take turns placing their symbol, either an 'X' or an 'O', into one of the empty squares on the board. You can choose to play against a friend in 2-player mode or test your skills against the computer's AI.

A win is achieved by lining up three of your marks consecutively. This can be done horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. The first player to achieve this wins the round!

Strategy is key. You'll need to think ahead to create your own winning opportunities while simultaneously blocking your opponent from doing the same. Plan your moves carefully to outsmart your rival and claim victory!
