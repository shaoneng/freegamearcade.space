Tic Tac Toe

How to Play Tic Tac Toe

Welcome to the classic game of Tic Tac Toe! Your goal is simple: be the first player to place three of your marks (either 'X' or 'O') in a continuous line. This can be a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row.

The game is played on a 3x3 grid. You and your opponent will take turns placing your mark in an empty square. Simply click or tap on the square where you wish to place your symbol.

Choose to challenge a friend in 2-player mode or test your wits against the computer AI. To win, you'll need to think ahead. Plan your moves to create opportunities for a three-in-a-row victory while simultaneously blocking your opponent from doing the same. If all nine squares are filled and no one has won, the game is a draw!
