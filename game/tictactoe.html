Tic Tac Toe

How to Play Tic Tac Toe

Welcome to the classic game of Tic Tac Toe! This simple yet strategic game is played on a 3x3 grid. One player uses the 'X' symbol, and the other uses the 'O' symbol.

Players take turns placing their mark in an empty square on the grid. The objective is to be the first player to form a line of three of your own marks. This winning line can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.

You have two ways to play: challenge a friend in the 2-player mode, or test your skills against the computer's AI. To win, you must not only plan your own moves to create a three-in-a-row but also block your opponent from doing the same. Think ahead and secure your victory!
