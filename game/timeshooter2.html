Time Shooter 2

How to Play Time Shooter 2

Welcome to Time Shooter 2, where you have the ultimate power: time moves only when you move! Stand still to pause the action, giving you precious moments to assess the situation, plan your next move, and line up the perfect shot. This unique mechanic turns every firefight into a strategic puzzle.

Use the WASD keys to move and your mouse to look around and aim. A left-click will fire your weapon or throw an object you're holding. Use the right mouse button to pick up weapons from fallen enemies or grab throwable items from the environment. Remember, every step you take advances time, so move wisely!

Start with a basic pistol, but be ready to upgrade. Disarm your opponents and arm yourself with more powerful weapons like rifles.
