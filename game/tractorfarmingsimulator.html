Tractor Farming Simulator

How to Play Tractor Farming Simulator

Welcome to your very own virtual farm! In Tractor Farming Simulator, your goal is to successfully complete a series of agricultural tasks across 19 distinct levels. Each level presents a new challenge, from preparing the soil to harvesting the crops.

Your journey begins in the driver's seat of your powerful tractor. For each level, you must first attach the required piece of farming machinery to your vehicle. Once you're geared up, the game will guide you with green arrows, leading you to the specific plot of land that needs your attention.

Follow the instructions carefully to perform essential farming duties such as cultivating the fields, sowing seeds, and watering the land. Master the controls of your tractor and its attachments to efficiently complete each task and progress to the next level. Get ready to roll up your sleeves and enjoy the rewarding life of a farmer!
