Tractor Farming Simulator

How to Play Tractor Farming Simulator

Welcome to your virtual farm! In Tractor Farming Simulator, your goal is to complete a series of agricultural tasks across 19 distinct levels. Each level presents a new challenge, from preparing the soil to harvesting your crops.

Your first step in every level is to hop into your powerful tractor. From there, you'll need to attach the specific piece of farming machinery required for the current task, such as a cultivator or a seeder. Once your equipment is hitched, simply follow the green arrows on the screen. These will guide you to the designated area on your land where the work needs to be done.

Follow the on-screen instructions to operate your machinery correctly. You'll be cultivating the field, sowing seeds, and watering the land to help your farm thrive. Complete each task successfully to advance to the next level and prove your skills as the ultimate virtual farmer!
