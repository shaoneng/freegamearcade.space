Tractor Farming Simulator

How to Play Tractor Farming Simulator

Welcome to Tractor Farming Simulator, a detailed agricultural game where you step into the boots of a modern farmer. Your main responsibility is to manage all aspects of farm work, from preparing the soil to bringing in the harvest across 19 challenging levels.

In each level, your first task is to get into your tractor and attach the required farming machine for the job at hand. Once you're ready, the game will guide you with green arrows, directing you to the specific plot of land that needs your attention.

Follow the instructions to complete essential farming duties step-by-step, such as cultivating the field, sowing the seeds, and watering the crops. Master the controls of your powerful tractor and its attachments to become the ultimate virtual farmer in this realistic simulation!
