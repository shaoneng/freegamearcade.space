Tractor Farming Simulator

How to Play Tractor Farming Simulator

Welcome to your very own virtual farm! In Tractor Farming Simulator, your goal is to successfully manage all the agricultural work required to run a thriving farm. Each level presents a new challenge, from preparing the soil to harvesting your crops.

To begin, you'll need to hop into your tractor. For each of the 19 levels, you will be assigned a specific task that requires a particular piece of farming equipment. Drive your tractor to the correct machine and attach it to your vehicle to get started.

Once your equipment is attached, the game will guide you to the designated area on your land using green arrows on the ground. Follow these arrows to find the field where you need to perform your task, whether it's cultivating the land, sowing seeds, or watering your growing plants. Complete each step-by-step task to advance to the next level and prove your farming skills!
