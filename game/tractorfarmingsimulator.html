Tractor Farming Simulator

How to Play Tractor Farming Simulator

Welcome to your own virtual farm! In Tractor Farming Simulator, your goal is to complete a series of agricultural tasks to become a master farmer. The game is divided into 19 levels, each with a specific objective to accomplish.

Each level begins with you needing to get into your tractor. Once you're in the driver's seat, you must attach the correct piece of farming equipment for the job, such as a plow or a seeder.

Don't worry about getting lost! The game will guide you with green arrows, showing you exactly where you need to go. Follow these arrows to the designated field and begin your task, whether it's cultivating the soil, sowing seeds, or watering your land. Complete the task to pass the level and move on to the next challenge.
