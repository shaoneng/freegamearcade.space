Traffic Jam 3D

How to Play Traffic Jam 3D

The goal in Traffic Jam 3D is to drive as far as you can on a busy highway, skillfully overtaking other vehicles without crashing. The faster you go and the closer you get to other cars without hitting them, the more points you'll score.

Control your vehicle using your keyboard. Use the W/Up Arrow key to accelerate, the S/Down Arrow key to brake, and the A/D or Left/Right Arrow keys to steer left and right. Stay alert and keep your eyes on the road to anticipate traffic patterns.

The game offers four distinct modes to test your skills: Career, Infinite, Time Against, and Free mode. Each one provides a unique challenge, from completing specific objectives to simply surviving the endless onslaught of traffic.

Before you hit the road, you can customize your driving scenario. Choose from different maps like Highway, Desert, or City, select the time of day (morning, dusk, or night), and decide between one-way or two-way traffic for a new experience every time you play.
