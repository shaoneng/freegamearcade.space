Traffic Jam 3D

How to Play Traffic Jam 3D

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Traffic Jam 3D! Your primary objective is to drive as far as possible on a busy highway without crashing into other vehicles. The longer you drive and the more near-misses you have, the higher your score will be.

Before you hit the road, you can customize your driving experience. The game features four distinct modes to test your skills. You can also select your preferred environmentâ€”choose between the open Highway, the arid Desert, or the bustling City. Further tailor your game by setting the time of day (morning, evening, or night) and the traffic flow (one-way or two-way).

Controlling your car is simple. On a desktop, use the arrow keys or WASD keys to steer, accelerate, and brake. If you're playing on a mobile device, intuitive on-screen controls will appear, allowing you to navigate the traffic with ease. Keep your eyes on the road, anticipate the movement of other cars, and become a master of the highway!
