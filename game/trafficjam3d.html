Traffic Jam 3D

How to Play Traffic Jam 3D

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Traffic Jam 3D! Your main objective is to drive your car on a busy highway, overtaking other vehicles at high speed without causing a crash. Master the art of weaving through traffic to prove your driving prowess and achieve high scores.

On a desktop or laptop, use the arrow keys or WASD to steer your vehicle left and right. On mobile devices, the game uses intuitive touch controls to guide your car. The simple controls allow you to focus on the thrill of the drive and the challenge of avoiding the other cars on the road.

The game features four distinct modes to keep you engaged: Career, Infinite, Time Against, and Free. You can also customize your driving environment by choosing from maps like Highway, Desert, or City. Select the time of day (morning, dusk, or night), and decide if you want to brave one-way or two-way traffic. Choose your favorite setup and hit the road!
