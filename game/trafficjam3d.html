Traffic Jam 3D

How to Play Traffic Jam 3D

The objective in Traffic Jam 3D is simple: drive your vehicle through heavy highway traffic and go as far as possible without crashing. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to steer your car, accelerate, and brake. The key is to skillfully overtake other vehicles at high speed, weaving through lanes to avoid collisions.

Before you start, you can customize your experience. Choose from four different game modes to challenge yourself. You can also select your preferred driving environment—Highway, Desert, or City—and set the time of day to morning, dusk, or night. For an extra challenge, you can even decide if the traffic flows one-way or two-ways. Jump in, customize your ride, and see how you rank on the endless road!
