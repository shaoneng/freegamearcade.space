Traffic Jam 3D

How to Play Traffic Jam 3D

Welcome to the fast-paced highways of Traffic Jam 3D! Your primary objective is to drive as far as possible, skillfully overtaking other vehicles without crashing. Before you start, you have several options to customize your driving experience.

First, choose from four distinct game modes to find your favorite challenge. Next, set your scene by selecting a map—race through a bustling Highway, a vast Desert, or a detailed City. You can also decide the time of day, whether you want to drive in the bright morning, the beautiful dusk, or the challenging night.

Finally, decide if you'll navigate a one-way road or face the added difficulty of two-way traffic. Once you're on the road, use your keyboard or on-screen controls to steer your vehicle, accelerate, and brake. Keep a sharp eye on the traffic, anticipate other drivers' movements, and weave through the lanes to keep going. The longer you drive without crashing, the higher your score will be!
