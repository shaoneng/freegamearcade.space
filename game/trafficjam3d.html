Traffic Jam 3D

How to Play Traffic Jam 3D

Welcome to the fast-paced world of Traffic Jam 3D! Your primary objective is to drive as far as possible on a busy highway, skillfully overtaking other vehicles without causing a collision. Keep a sharp eye on the road and react quickly to avoid accidents, as one wrong move can end your run.

The game offers you four distinct modes to test your driving prowess. Each mode presents a different challenge, so be sure to try them all to find your favorite. Whether you prefer a straightforward endless drive or a more objective-based challenge, there's a mode for every type of driver.

Before you hit the road, you can customize your driving environment. Choose your preferred map from Highway, Desert, or City. Set the time of day to morning, dusk, or night for a different atmosphere. You can even decide if you want to navigate one-way or two-way traffic, adding another layer of difficulty. The choice is yours!

Controls: Use the W/Up Arrow key to accelerate, the S/Down Arrow key to brake, and the A/Left Arrow and D/Right Arrow keys to steer your vehicle left and right.
