Traffic Jam 3D

How to Play Traffic Jam 3D

Your goal in Traffic Jam 3D is to drive as far as possible on a busy highway without crashing. Use your keyboard controls to steer your vehicle left and right, accelerating to overtake slower cars and braking to avoid collisions. Keep your eyes on the road and anticipate the movements of other drivers to maintain your speed and rack up a high score.

Before you start, you can customize your driving experience. Choose from four different game modes to challenge your skills.
