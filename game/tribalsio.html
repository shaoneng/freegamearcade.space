Tribals io

How to Play Tribals io

Welcome to the world of Tribals io, a multiplayer survival game where you begin your journey as a caveman stranded on a vast island. Your primary objective is to survive the harsh environment. You start with nothing but a simple stone. Begin by exploring the island to find and gather essential resources like wood, leaves, and stone. These materials are the foundation for everything you will create to ensure your survival.

Crafting is key to your success. Press the 'Tab' key to open your main interface, which is split into two sections. On the left is your inventory, showing all the resources you've collected. On the right is the crafting menu. Here, you can browse through categories like 'Beginning,' 'Weapons,' and 'Structure' to see what you can create. To craft an item, simply click on it, and the game will show you the required resources. If you have what you need, you can craft it instantly.

The island's weather is unpredictable, and the nights are cold and dangerous. Building a shelter is crucial for protection against the elements and for resting safely. To start building, you first need to craft a 'Building Plan'—which is easily made from a single leaf. Once you have your plan, you can start constructing your very own shack to stay warm, dry, and safe from threats.

You are not alone on this island. Dangers lurk around every corner, from aggressive bears to other players who might not be friendly. Craft weapons like spears and tools like axes to defend yourself and hunt for food. While you can go it alone, teaming up with other players can significantly improve your chances of survival. Work together to build a stronger base, gather resources faster, and fend off common enemies. How many days can you endure?
