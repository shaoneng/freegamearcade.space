Tribals io

How to Play Tribals io

Your adventure in Tribals io begins as a caveman stranded on a deserted island. The primary objective is to survive for as long as possible. Start by exploring your surroundings to gather basic resources such as wood, stone, and leaves. These initial materials are the foundation for everything you will build and create.

To craft items, press the 'Tab' key to open your inventory and the crafting menu. On the left, you'll see all the resources you've collected. On the right is the crafting menu, organized into categories like 'Weapons' and 'Structure'. Select an item to see what resources are required, then click to craft it if you have the necessary materials.

The island is full of dangers. You'll need to defend yourself from aggressive wildlife like bears and from other players who may not be friendly. The weather is also a threat; cold nights can harm you, so building a shelter is crucial. Craft weapons for protection and tools like axes and pickaxes to gather resources more efficiently. A simple shack will provide shelter from the elements and a safe place to rest.

Team up with other players to form a tribe. Working together can make gathering, building, and defending your base much easier. Communicate and coordinate to dominate the island and ensure your collective survival against all odds.
