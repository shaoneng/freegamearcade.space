Tribals io

How to Play Tribals io

Welcome to Tribals io, a multiplayer survival adventure where you awaken on a mysterious island as a caveman. Your journey begins with nothing but a single stone. Your primary goal is to survive by exploring the island, gathering resources, and crafting everything you need to endure the dangers that await.

To get started, move around using the W, A, S, D keys and use your mouse to interact with the environment. Gather essential materials like wood, stone, and leaves from your surroundings. Press the Tab key to open your inventory and the crafting menu. Here, you can see what you've collected and browse different categories like Weapons, Structures, and Tools to craft new items.

Survival is not just about crafting; it's also about defense. The island is home to threats like aggressive bears and other players who might not be friendly. Crafting weapons will help you fend off attacks, and building a shelter is crucial for staying warm and safe from the elements.

Team up with friends to increase your chances of survival! Working together makes gathering resources and defending your base much easier. Can you build a thriving tribal community, or will you fall victim to the island's unforgiving nature? Dive in and see how many days you can survive!
