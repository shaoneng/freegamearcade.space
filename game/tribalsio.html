Tribals io

How to Play Tribals io

Welcome to the wild island of Tribals.io! You begin your journey as a primitive human with nothing but a single stone. Your primary objective is to survive. Start by exploring your surroundings to gather basic resources like wood, stone, and leaves. These are the building blocks for everything you'll need to stay alive.

To craft items, press the `Tab` key. This opens two windows: your inventory on the left, showing what you've collected, and the crafting menu on the right. The crafting menu is organized into categories like Weapons, Structures, and Tools. Select an item to see the required resources, then click to craft it if you have the necessary materials.

The island is full of dangers. You'll face threats from wild animals like bears and potentially hostile players. The weather is also a major factor; rain can make you sick, and the cold nights can be deadly. It's crucial to craft weapons for defense and build a shelter to protect yourself from the elements and get some sleep. Start by crafting a building plan to begin construction on your first base.

While you can play solo, teaming up with other players can significantly boost your chances of survival. Work together to gather resources faster, build a larger base, and defend against common threats. The world of Tribals.io is harsh, but with teamwork, you can thrive!
