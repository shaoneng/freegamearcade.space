Tribals io

How to Play Tribals io

Your adventure in Tribals io begins as you wake up on a deserted island with only a stone. Your primary goal is to survive. Start by exploring your surroundings to gather essential resources like wood, stone, and leaves. These are the building blocks for everything you'll need to endure the island's challenges.

Crafting is key to your survival. Press the 'Tab' key to open your inventory and the crafting menu. On the right, you'll see various categories like "Beginning," "Weapons," and "Structure." Click a category to see what you can create, and select an item to view the required resources. Gather what's needed and craft tools, weapons, and more to improve your chances.

The island is full of dangers. You'll face threats from wild animals like bears and even other players. The weather is also a constant challenge, with cold nights and sudden rainstorms that can make you sick. To protect yourself, it's crucial to build a shelter.
