Tribals io

About Tribals io

Tribals.io is a multiplayer survival game where you wake up on an island as a caveman. You have nothing in your pockets but a stone. Explore the island to gather essential resources to use for your benefit. Gather all the effective materials to create something more significant and valuable. This crafting game will remind you of famous video games like Minecraft, Rust, and Raft!

You will come across many threats on the island, from wild bears to other cavemen. You have to protect yourself from them. The weather also poses a challenge, with severe temperature differences between day and night. Craft weapons and tools to protect yourself in any situation. Press the Tab key to open your inventory and the crafting menu. There, you can see the resources needed to craft items across various categories like Weapons, Structures, and more.

Building a shelter is a must to protect yourself from rain, get good sleep, and stay safe. To begin, you'll first need to craft a building plan, which is as simple as picking up a leaf. Enhance your gaming experience by teaming up with other players. Let's see how many days you can survive on this island!

Tribals.io was developed by ONRUSH Studio. If you enjoy this survival experience, you may also like other survival games like EvoWorld.io (FlyOrDie).
