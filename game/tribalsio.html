Tribals io

How to Play Tribals io

Welcome to Tribals io, a multiplayer survival game where your main goal is to endure the challenges of a wild island. You begin as a caveman with nothing but a stone. Your survival depends on your ability to explore, gather resources, and craft everything you need to live another day.

Move around the island to find and collect essential materials like wood, stone, and leaves. Press the 'Tab' key to open your inventory and the crafting menu. Here, you can see what you've collected and what you can create. Browse categories like Weapons, Structures, and Tools to see the required resources and craft items to help you progress.

The island is full of dangers, including hostile animals like bears and even other players. The weather is also a threat; cold nights and rain can make you sick. To protect yourself, it's crucial to build a shelter. Start by crafting a building plan, which you can create simply by picking up a leaf. A solid base will be your safe haven against the elements and enemies.

You don't have to face the island alone! Enhance your chances of survival by teaming up with other players. Working together can make gathering resources, building a base, and fending off threats much more manageable. Good luck, and see how long you can survive!
