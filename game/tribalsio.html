Tribals io

How to Play Tribals io

Welcome to the challenging world of Tribals io! Your goal is to survive on a wild island where you begin with nothing but a single stone. Start by exploring your surroundings to find and gather essential resources like wood, leaves, and more rocks. These raw materials are the foundation for everything you'll need to survive.

To craft items, press the Tab key. This will open your inventory on the left and the crafting menu on the right. Browse through categories like Weapons, Tools, and Structures. Select an item to see what resources are required, then craft it if you have the necessary materials. Your first priorities should be creating basic tools to gather resources more efficiently and a weapon for defense.

The island is full of dangers. You'll need to protect yourself from aggressive wildlife like bears and from other hostile players. The dynamic weather system also poses a threat; rain can make you sick and nights are dangerously cold. Building a shelter is essential for survival. Craft a building plan and construct a simple base to stay warm, dry, and safe from threats.

Master the controls to navigate this world: use WASD to move, the mouse to look, and the left mouse button to attack or use an item. Press E to pick up items. Good luck, and see how many days you can last!
