Tribals io

How to Play Tribals io

Welcome to the challenging world of Tribals io, a multiplayer survival adventure! You start as a lone caveman on a vast island with nothing but a single stone. Your primary goal is simple: survive as long as you can.

Explore the island to find and gather essential resources like wood, stone, and leaves. These materials are the key to your survival. To craft items, press the 'Tab' key. This opens your inventory on the left and the crafting menu on the right. Browse through categories like Weapons, Structures, and Tools to see what you can create.

The island is full of dangers. You'll need to defend yourself from hostile animals like bears and even other players. A simple leaf is all you need to create your first building plan!

You can choose to face the challenges alone or team up with other players to increase your chances of survival. Work together to build a base, hunt, and defend against common threats. See how long you can thrive in this primitive world!
