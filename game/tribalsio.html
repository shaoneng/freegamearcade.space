Tribals io

How to Play Tribals io

Welcome to the wild world of Tribals.io! Your primary goal is to survive for as long as possible on a dangerous island. You begin as a caveman with only a single stone. You'll need to explore your surroundings to gather resources, craft tools and weapons, and build a shelter to protect yourself from threats.

Use your mouse and keyboard (WASD for movement) to navigate the island. Collect essential resources like wood, stone, leaves, and berries. To craft items, press the Tab key. This opens your inventory on the left and the crafting menu on the right. Select a category like 'Weapons' or 'Structure', choose the item you want to make, and if you have the required resources, you can craft it instantly.

The island is not empty. You'll face threats from aggressive animals like bears and from other players who might see you as a target. The weather is also a major factor; rain can make you sick, and the cold nights are dangerous without a fire or shelter. Build a base to stay safe, craft weapons like a spear to defend yourself, and always be aware of your health, hunger, and temperature.

Surviving alone is tough. You can team up with other players to form a tribe. Working together makes it easier to gather resources, build a larger base, and defend against common enemies. Communication and cooperation are key to dominating the island.
