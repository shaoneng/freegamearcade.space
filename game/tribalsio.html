How to Play Tribals io

Welcome to the challenging world of Tribals io! Your goal is to survive for as long as possible on a hostile island. You'll start with nothing but a single stone.

Begin by exploring the island to gather essential resources like wood, stone, and leaves. These materials are crucial for your survival. To craft items, press the 'Tab' key. This will open your inventory on the left and the crafting menu on the right. Here, you can create tools, weapons, and building plans.

Survival isn't just about crafting; you must also protect yourself from threats. The island is home to dangerous animals like bears, and other players might not be friendly.
