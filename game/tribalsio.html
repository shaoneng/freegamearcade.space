Tribals io

How to Play Tribals io

Welcome to the wild island of Tribals.io! Your objective is to survive for as long as possible. You begin as a caveman with only a single stone. Your first task is to explore the island and gather basic resources like wood, leaves, and more stone to begin your journey.

To craft items, press the `Tab` key. This will open two windows: your inventory on the left and the crafting menu on the right. In the crafting menu, you can browse categories like Weapons, Structures, and Tools. Click on an item to see the required resources, and if you have them, click to craft it. Start by making simple tools like a stone axe or pickaxe to gather resources more efficiently.

Survival is not just about gathering. You must defend yourself from threats, which include aggressive wildlife like bears and other players who might be hostile. The weather is also a danger; rain can make you sick, and the cold nights can harm you. Craft weapons for defense and warm clothes for the cold.

Building a shelter is crucial for long-term survival. It provides protection from the elements and a safe place to sleep and recover. Before you can build, you must first craft a building plan, which is easily made from a single leaf. From there, you can construct walls, floors, and a roof to create your own base.
