Tribals io

How to Play Tribals io

Welcome to the challenging world of Tribals io! You begin your journey as a caveman stranded on a mysterious island with nothing but a stone. Your primary goal is survival. Start by exploring your surroundings to gather essential resources like wood, stone, and leaves. These are the building blocks for everything you will create.

Crafting is key to your success. Press the 'Tab' key to open your inventory and the crafting menu. On the left, you'll see all the resources you've collected. On the right is the crafting menu, organized into categories like Weapons, Structures, and Workbench. Select an item you wish to craft to see the required materials, then click to create it. Start by crafting basic tools to help you gather resources more efficiently.

The island is full of dangers. You'll face threats from wild animals like bears and potentially hostile players. The weather is also a formidable foe; the temperature drops significantly at night, and rain can make you sick. To survive these elements, you must build a shelter. Craft a Building Plan first (which only requires a leaf), and then construct a shack to stay safe, warm, and dry.

You don't have to face the wilderness alone! Tribals io is a multiplayer game, so consider teaming up with other players. Working together can make gathering, building, and defending your base much easier. How long can you and your tribe survive?
