Tribals io

How to Play Tribals io

Your journey in Tribals io begins on a deserted island as a caveman with only a stone. The primary goal is survival. Explore your surroundings to gather essential resources like wood, stone, and leaves. These materials are the key to your progression and survival against the island's many dangers.

Crafting is at the heart of the game. Press the 'Tab' key to open your inventory and the crafting menu. Here, you can see all the items you can create, from basic tools to powerful weapons and structures. Select an item to view the required resources, gather what you need, and craft it to enhance your capabilities.

The island is not friendly. You'll face threats from wild animals like bears, other hostile players, and even the weather. The temperature drops significantly at night, and rain can make you sick. To protect yourself, craft weapons and build a shelter. Your first step to building is crafting a "Building Plan," which you can create simply by picking up a leaf. Good luck, and see how long you can survive!
