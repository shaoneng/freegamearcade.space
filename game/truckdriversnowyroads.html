Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

How to Play Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

Get behind the wheel and prepare for an icy adventure! The controls are simple and support two players for split-screen action.

Player 1: Use the Arrow Keys to accelerate, brake, and steer your truck.

Player 2: Use the W, A, S, D keys to control your vehicle.

The game offers three exciting modes:

Free Drive: The perfect mode to relax and get a feel for the trucks. Cruise through the expansive snowy landscapes, discover hidden paths, or just enjoy the ride with a friend. You can even customize the in-game radio with your favorite tunes!

Missions: Take on 10 challenging delivery missions. Your goal is to transport cargo safely to its destination. Drive carefully on the slippery roadsâ€”slow and steady wins the race here. Successful deliveries earn you coins to upgrade your garage.

Racing: Put your skills to the test in 10 adrenaline-fueled races. Compete against the AI or challenge a friend in split-screen mode to see who is the fastest driver on the ice.

Earn coins by completing missions and winning races to unlock a fleet of 12 unique off-road trucks, each with its own performance characteristics.
