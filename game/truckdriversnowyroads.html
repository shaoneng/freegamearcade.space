Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

How to Play Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

Welcome to the winter wilderness! Your goal in Truck Driver: Snowy Roads is to master the icy terrain, complete challenges, and unlock a full garage of powerful off-road trucks. The game can be played solo or with a friend in split-screen mode.

Player 1 Controls: Use the Arrow Keys (Up, Down, Left, Right) to steer, accelerate, and brake your truck.

Player 2 Controls (Split-screen): Use the W, A, S, D keys to control the second vehicle.

Choose from three distinct game modes:

  • Free Drive: Explore the vast, snowy map at your own pace, alone or with a friend. It's the perfect mode to practice your driving or just enjoy the scenery.
  • Missions: Take on 10 challenging delivery tasks. Careful driving is criticalâ€”one wrong move on the slippery roads could mean losing your cargo!
  • Racing: Compete against the AI or a friend across 10 thrilling, icy tracks. Put your skills to the test and aim for the finish line.

Earn coins by successfully completing missions and winning races. Use your earnings to unlock all 12 unique trucks in the garage, each with its own power and handling characteristics!
