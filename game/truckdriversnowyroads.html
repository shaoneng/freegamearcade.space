Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

How to Play Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

Welcome to the icy, challenging world of Truck Driver: Snowy Roads! Your main goal is to master driving powerful trucks through treacherous snow-covered environments. The game offers three distinct modes to test your skills, playable alone or with a friend in split-screen.

Game Modes:

  • Free Drive: The perfect mode to relax and explore. Cruise through the vast winter landscapes, discover hidden paths, or practice your driving with a friend.
  • Missions: Take on 10 nail-biting delivery challenges. Your objective is to transport your cargo safely to its destination. Drive carefully on the slippery roadsâ€”slow and steady is the key to success!
  • Racing: Get your adrenaline pumping by competing on 10 different icy tracks. Race against the computer or challenge a friend to see who is the fastest driver on the mountain.

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use the Arrow Keys to drive, accelerate, and brake.
  • Player 2 (Split-Screen): Use the W, A, S, D Keys to control your truck.

Earn coins by completing missions and winning races to unlock a garage full of 12 unique off-road trucks, each with its own handling and power. Good luck, driver!
