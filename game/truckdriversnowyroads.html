Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

How to Play Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

Welcome to the icy challenge of Truck Driver: Snowy Roads! Your goal is to master the art of driving heavy trucks on slippery, snow-covered terrain. The game supports both single-player and two-player split-screen modes. The controls are simple:

  • Player 1: Use the Arrow Keys to drive your truck.
  • Player 2: Use the WASD Keys to drive your truck in split-screen mode.

The game offers three distinct modes to enjoy. In Free Drive Mode, you can explore the vast winter landscapes at your own pace, test out different trucks, or just relax with a friend. In Mission Mode, you'll take on 10 nail-biting delivery challenges where you must transport cargo safely to its destination. Be gentle with the controls, as one wrong move can mean failure! Finally, Racing Mode lets you get your adrenaline pumping as you compete against AI or a friend across 10 thrilling, icy tracks.

As you complete missions and win races, you'll earn coins. Use these coins in the garage to unlock up to 12 unique off-road trucks, each with different performance characteristics. Find your favorite, customize the radio with your own tunes, and conquer the snowy roads!
