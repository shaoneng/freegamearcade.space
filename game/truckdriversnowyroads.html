Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

How to Play Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

Get behind the wheel and prepare for an epic winter adventure in Truck Driver: Snowy Roads! The game offers several ways to play, catering to every type of driver. You can use your keyboard to control your truck, with support for two players on a split-screen.

Choose Your Adventure

The game features three distinct modes:

  • Free Drive: Want to just cruise? Select this mode to explore the vast, snow-covered map at your own pace. It's perfect for practicing your driving, finding hidden shortcuts, or enjoying a relaxing drive with a friend.
  • Missions: Ready for a challenge? Take on 10 nail-biting delivery missions. Your goal is to transport cargo safely to the finish line. The roads are slippery, so slow and steady wins the race. Protect your cargo to succeed!
  • Racing: Feel the need for speed? Jump into the racing mode and compete on 10 different icy tracks. You can race against an AI opponent or go head-to-head with a friend in split-screen action.

Unlock and Customize

As you complete missions and win races, you'll earn coins. Use these coins in the garage to unlock a fleet of 12 unique off-road trucks, each with different handling and power. Find the truck that best suits your style. Don't forget to use the in-game radio to pick the perfect soundtrack for your drive!
