Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

How to Play Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

Welcome to the ultimate off-road challenge! In Truck Driver: Snowy Roads, your goal is to master driving powerful trucks through treacherous, snow-covered environments. Start by choosing one of the three exciting game modes available from the main menu.

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use the Arrow Keys to accelerate, brake, and steer your truck.
  • Player 2 (Split-Screen Mode): Use the W, A, S, D keys to control your vehicle.

Game Modes Explained:

  • Missions: Take on 10 challenging delivery missions. The key is to drive with care and precision to keep your cargo safe on the slippery roads. One wrong move could mean failure!
  • Racing: Get your adrenaline pumping by competing on 10 different icy tracks. You can race against an AI opponent or challenge a friend in split-screen mode.
  • Free Drive: Want to just relax? Select Free Drive to explore the open world, test out different trucks, and find hidden paths without any pressure. It's the perfect mode for practicing or a casual drive.

Earn coins by successfully completing missions and winning races. Use these coins to unlock a fleet of 12 unique trucks, each with its own handling and power. Good luck on the snowy roads!
