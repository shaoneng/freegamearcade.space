Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

How to Play Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

Get behind the wheel and conquer the frozen wilderness! Use your driving skills to navigate the challenging terrain. The controls are simple and can be shared for two-player action.

Player 1: Use the W, A, S, D keys to drive your truck.

Player 2: Use the Arrow Keys to drive your truck.

The game offers three exciting modes to choose from:

Free Drive: Explore the vast, snowy map at your own pace. Discover hidden paths, test out different trucks, or just enjoy a relaxing drive with a friend in split-screen mode. You can even customize the in-game radio with your favorite tunes!

Missions: Take on 10 challenging delivery missions. Your goal is to transport cargo safely to its destination. The roads are slippery, so drive carefullyâ€”slow and steady wins the race. Successful deliveries earn you coins.

Racing: Compete against the AI or another player on 10 icy race tracks. Put the pedal to the metal and prove you're the fastest driver on the mountain!

Earn coins by completing missions and winning races to unlock all 12 powerful off-road trucks in the garage. Each truck offers a unique driving experience. Start your engine and begin your snowy adventure!
