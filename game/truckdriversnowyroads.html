Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

How to Play Truck Driver: Snowy Roads

Welcome to the winter wonderland of Truck Driver: Snowy Roads! Your main goal is to master the art of driving powerful trucks on icy, challenging terrains. The game offers three distinct modes to explore: Missions, Racing, and Free Drive. Whether you're playing solo or with a friend in split-screen mode, there's an adventure waiting for you.

Controls for Player 1: Use the Arrow Keys to drive your truck. Press C to change the camera view and R to reset your truck if you get stuck. Use the Spacebar for the handbrake.

Controls for Player 2 (Split-Screen): Use the W, A, S, D keys to drive. Press K to change the camera view and L for the handbrake.

In Missions Mode, your objective is to deliver cargo safely across 10 challenging levels. Drive carefully, as one wrong move on the slippery roads can mean losing your precious load! In Racing Mode, compete against AI or a friend on 10 exhilarating tracks. If you just want to relax, jump into Free Drive Mode to explore the map, find hidden paths, and enjoy the scenery at your own pace. You can even customize your journey by changing the radio station to listen to your favorite tunes!

As you complete missions and win races, you'll earn coins. Use these coins in the garage to unlock and purchase up to 12 different off-road trucks, each with unique performance and handling characteristics. Find the perfect rig for your driving style and conquer the snowy roads!
