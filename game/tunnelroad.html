Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Tunnel Road is a 3D avoidance game where you navigate a sci-fi, spinning tunnel full of twists, turns, and sudden obstacles. It's a pure test of your reflexes wrapped up in a slick, modern package. The goal is simple: survive for as long as you can.

The game starts at a manageable pace, but the tempo quickly picks up, increasing the pressure. Your ball speeds forward automatically, and you must steer left or right to dodge everything in your path. Hit just one obstacle, and you're sent back to level 1, unless you use one of your limited revivals to keep your streak going.

Game Modes

1-Player Mode: Choose this mode to challenge yourself and see how many levels you can conquer. The speed increases gradually, pushing your reaction time to its limits. There are no power-ups or brakesâ€”it's just you against the endless tunnel.

2-Player Mode: Bring a friend for a competitive race! The screen splits vertically, and both players race side-by-side in parallel tunnels. The first one to crash loses the round. Player 1 uses the 'A' and 'D' keys to steer. Can you outlast your opponent?

Tips and Similar Games

Developed by gamevgames, Tunnel Road offers a modern polish on the classic endless runner genre. If Tunnel Road gets your adrenaline pumping, try Extreme Run 3D. It offers a similar high-speed challenge but with even more intense visuals and dynamic obstacles like spikes and wrecking balls.
