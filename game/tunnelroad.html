Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

The goal in Tunnel Road is simple: survive for as long as you can. You'll automatically speed forward through a twisting, 3D tunnel filled with randomized blockades. Your only job is to steer left and right to dodge everything in your path. The game gets progressively faster, demanding quick reflexes to stay in the race.

Start by choosing between 1-Player and 2-Player mode from the main menu. In 1-Player mode, you challenge yourself to beat your own high score and progress through levels. If you hit an obstacle, your run ends, and you must restart from level 1, unless you use a revival.

For a competitive twist, grab a friend for 2-Player mode! The screen splits, and you both race side-by-side in parallel tunnels. The first player to crash loses the round. Player 1 controls their ball using the 'A' and 'D' keys to move left and right, while Player 2 uses the Left and Right Arrow keys.
