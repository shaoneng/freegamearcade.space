Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Welcome to the twisting, high-speed challenge of Tunnel Road! The objective is simple: steer your ball through a chaotic, spinning tunnel and survive for as long as possible. The tunnel is filled with sudden blockades and gaps, requiring sharp reflexes to navigate successfully. The further you go, the faster you travel, pushing your skills to the limit.

Single-Player Mode: Your goal is to complete as many levels as possible in one run. Use the A and D keys or the left and right arrow keys to steer your ball. Each time you successfully navigate a section, you advance to the next level. Be careful! Hitting just one obstacle will send you back to Level 1, forcing you to start your streak over.

Two-Player Mode: Challenge a friend in an exciting split-screen race! The screen divides, with Player 1 on the left and Player 2 on the right. Both players race through the same tunnel simultaneously. The first player to crash loses the round, and the other is declared the winner. Player 1 uses the A (left) and D (right) keys, while Player 2 uses the Left and Right arrow keys.
