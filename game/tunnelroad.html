Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Tunnel Road is a 3D avoid game where you navigate a sci-fi, spinning tunnel full of twists, turns, and sudden obstacles. The goal is simple: survive as long as you can. The game starts off manageable, but the speed and pressure increase as you progress.

Controls & Objective: Your ball speeds forward automatically. You must steer left or right to dodge everything in your path. Each completed run advances you to a new level. Hitting just one obstacle sends you back to level 1, unless you choose to use a limited revival to keep your streak going.

2-Player Mode: Challenge a friend in a split-screen race! In this mode, both players race through the same tunnel side by side on their half of the screen. The goal is to survive longer than your opponent. If one player crashes, the other instantly wins the round. Player 1 uses 'A' (left) and 'D' (right) to steer.

There are no power-ups, no brakes, and no slow-motion. It's a pure test of your reflexes wrapped up in a slick, modern package. Let's see how long you can last in the wormhole!
