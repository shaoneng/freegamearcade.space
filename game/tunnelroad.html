Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Welcome to the ultimate reflex challenge! In Tunnel Road, your objective is to steer your ball through a dizzying, high-speed tunnel, avoiding obstacles for as long as you can. The game speeds up as you progress, demanding quicker reactions to survive.

1-Player Mode:

  • Your ball moves forward automatically.
  • Use the A/D keys or the Left/Right Arrow keys to steer and dodge the blockades.
  • Complete a run to advance to the next level.
  • If you crash, you must restart from Level 1, so stay sharp!

2-Player Mode:

  • Compete against a friend on a split-screen. The first player to crash loses the round.
  • Player 1 (Left Side): Uses the A and D keys.
  • Player 2 (Right Side): Uses the Left and Right Arrow keys.
