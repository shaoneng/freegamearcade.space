Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Get ready for a pure test of your reflexes! In Tunnel Road, your goal is to survive as long as possible inside a constantly spinning and twisting tunnel. Your ball moves forward automatically, and you must steer left and right to dodge the blockades that appear in your path. The game gets progressively faster, demanding quick reactions. If you hit an obstacle, your run is over, and you start again from level 1. It's a simple, addictive challenge that will keep you on the edge of your seat.

Bring a friend for even more excitement in 2-Player Mode! The screen splits vertically, and you'll each race side-by-side in your own tunnel. The first player to crash loses the round, giving the other an instant victory. The controls are simple: Player 1 (left side) uses the 'A' and 'D' keys to move, while Player 2 (right side) uses the Left and Right Arrow keys. Challenge your friends and see who has the superior reflexes!
