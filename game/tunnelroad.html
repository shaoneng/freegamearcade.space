Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Your goal in Tunnel Road is simple: survive for as long as you can. You control a ball speeding through a constantly spinning and twisting tunnel. You must steer left and right to dodge the blockades that appear in your path. The longer you survive, the faster you go, making the challenge progressively harder. If you hit an obstacle, your run is over, and you start again from level 1.

1-Player Mode: To play solo, select 1-Player Mode from the main menu. Use the 'A' and 'D' keys or the left and right arrow keys to steer your ball and avoid crashing.

2-Player Mode: For a competitive challenge, choose 2-Player Mode. The screen splits vertically, and you'll race alongside a friend in parallel tunnels. The first player to crash loses the round!

  • Player 1 (Left Side): Uses 'A' to move left and 'D' to move right.
  • Player 2 (Right Side): Uses the Left and Right Arrow Keys to move.
