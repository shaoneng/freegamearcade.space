Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Tunnel Road is a thrilling 3D avoidance game that drops you into a mesmerizing, spinning sci-fi tunnel. Your mission is to survive as long as possible by navigating twists, turns, and sudden obstacles. While the game starts at a manageable pace, the tempo quickly increases, pushing your reflexes to the limit.

The core gameplay is a pure test of skill. Your ball accelerates forward automatically, and you must steer left or right to dodge everything in your path. There are no power-ups, brakes, or slow-motion abilitiesâ€”it's just you against the ever-changing tunnel. Successfully completing a run advances you to the next level, but a single collision sends you back to the start. You do have a limited number of revivals, which can be used to continue your streak.

Game Modes

You can enjoy Tunnel Road in two exciting modes:

  • 1-Player Mode: A solo challenge against the tunnel itself. See how many levels you can clear and how high you can score.
  • 2-Player Mode: Challenge a friend in a split-screen race. Both players navigate the same tunnel side-by-side on their half of the screen. The first player to crash loses the round, making for an intense head-to-head battle. Player 1 uses 'A' and 'D' to move left and right.

If you enjoy the high-speed challenge of Tunnel Road, you should also try Extreme Run 3D. It offers a similar reflex-based experience but cranks up the intensity with even faster curves and more dynamic obstacles.
