Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Welcome to the electrifying challenge of Tunnel Road! Your goal is simple but demanding: survive for as long as possible inside a high-speed, twisting tunnel packed with obstacles.

In 1-Player Mode, you control a ball that automatically speeds forward. Your job is to steer left and right to dodge the blockades that appear in your path. The game gets progressively faster, pushing your reflexes to the absolute limit. One collision sends you back to the start, so stay focused!

For a competitive thrill, grab a friend and dive into 2-Player Mode. The screen splits vertically, and each player races through a parallel tunnel. The objective is to outlast your opponent. The first player to crash loses the round, crowning the other as the winner. It's a true test of skill and nerves!

Controls:

  • Player 1 (Left Side): Use 'A' to move left and 'D' to move right.
  • Player 2 (Right Side): Use the Left and Right Arrow keys to move.
