Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Welcome to the ultimate test of reflexes! In Tunnel Road, your objective is to navigate a rapidly moving ball through a twisting, obstacle-filled tunnel. Your goal is survival: stay alive for as long as you can by dodging the blockades that appear in your path. The game gets progressively faster, demanding quick reactions and sharp focus. If you crash, you'll be sent back to level 1, so every move counts!

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use the 'A' and 'D' keys or the left and right arrow keys to steer your ball. There are no brakes and no power-upsâ€”it's a pure challenge of skill and endurance.

For an extra layer of excitement, try the 2-Player mode. The screen splits vertically, and you'll race side-by-side against a friend in the same tunnel. The first player to crash loses the round. Player 1 uses the 'A' and 'D' keys to control the ball on the left, while Player 2 uses the left and right arrow keys for the ball on the right. May the best player win!
