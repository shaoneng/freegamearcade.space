Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Welcome to Tunnel Road, a pure test of reflexes and endurance! Your goal is to navigate your ball through a mesmerizing, spinning tunnel for as long as possible. The game features two exciting modes to choose from.

1-Player Mode: Your objective is survival. Use the 'A' and 'D' keys or the Left and Right Arrow keys to steer your ball and dodge the sudden blockades that appear in your path. The tunnel's speed gradually increases, demanding quicker reactions. If you crash, you must restart from Level 1, but you have the option to use a revival to keep your level progress.

2-Player Mode: Challenge a friend in a head-to-head battle! The screen splits, with each player controlling their own ball in a parallel tunnel. The first player to hit an obstacle loses, making the other player the winner of the round. Player 1 (left side) uses the 'A' and 'D' keys, while Player 2 (right side) uses the Left and Right Arrow keys. May the best survivor win!
