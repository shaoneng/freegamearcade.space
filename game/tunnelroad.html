Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Welcome to Tunnel Road, a high-speed 3D avoidance game that sends you hurtling through a twisting, sci-fi tunnel. The objective is straightforward: survive for as long as possible. You'll need sharp reflexes to dodge the sudden blockades and gaps that appear in your path. The game's speed gradually increases, pushing your skills to the limit. Make one mistake, and you're back to the start!

1-Player Mode: Navigate the tunnel solo and try to beat your own high score. As you successfully complete runs, you'll advance to new levels. Steer your ball left and right to avoid crashing. There are no power-ups or brakesâ€”just pure skill.

2-Player Mode: Challenge a friend in an exciting split-screen duel. You'll both race through parallel tunnels, and the last player standing wins the round. The controls are simple:

  • Player 1 (Left Screen): Use 'A' and 'D' to move left and right.
  • Player 2 (Right Screen): Use the Left and Right Arrow keys.

Developed by gamevgames, Tunnel Road offers a polished and modern take on classic endless runners. If you enjoy the adrenaline rush, try Extreme Run 3D for another intense reflex challenge.
