Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Welcome to the ultimate reflex challenge! In Tunnel Road, your goal is to guide your ball through a futuristic, spinning tunnel for as long as possible. The tunnel is filled with twists, turns, and sudden blockades that you must avoid. The game starts at a manageable pace but gets progressively faster, pushing your reaction time to the limit. If you hit an obstacle, you must restart from the beginning, so stay focused!

Single Player Mode: Use the A/D keys or the Left/Right Arrow keys to steer your ball left and right. Your only goal is to survive for as long as you can.

Two Player Mode: Challenge a friend in a head-to-head race! The screen splits vertically. Player 1 (left side) uses the A and D keys. Player 2 (right side) uses the Left and Right Arrow keys. The first player to crash loses the round. May the best survivor win!
