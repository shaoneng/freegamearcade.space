Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Welcome to the dizzying depths of Tunnel Road! The objective is straightforward: guide your ball through a perpetually twisting and spinning tunnel, avoiding all obstacles in your path. The game starts at a manageable pace but quickly accelerates, demanding sharp reflexes and intense focus. If you collide with an obstacle, your run ends, and you'll have to start over from the beginning unless you use a revival.

1-Player Mode: Your goal is to survive as long as possible and set a new high score. Use the 'A' and 'D' keys or the left and right arrow keys to steer your ball and dodge the blockades.

2-Player Mode: Challenge a friend in a head-to-head race! The screen splits vertically, and each player controls their own ball in a parallel tunnel. The goal is to outlast your opponent. The first player to crash loses the round! Player 1 uses the 'A' (left) and 'D' (right) keys.
