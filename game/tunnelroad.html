Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Dive into the thrilling 3D world of Tunnel Road, an addictive endless runner where quick reflexes are key! Your objective is to navigate a spinning, sci-fi tunnel and dodge all upcoming obstacles for as long as possible.

Gameplay:

  • Control your ball by steering left or right to avoid collisions.
  • The game's speed constantly increases, pushing your reaction limits.
  • Hitting an obstacle will send you back to Level 1, unless you use a limited revival.

Game Modes:

  • 1-Player Mode: Challenge yourself to achieve the highest score and progress through levels solo.
  • 2-Player Mode: Compete with a friend on a split-screen! Each player controls their own ball in parallel tunnels. The first player to crash loses the round. (Player 1 controls with 'A' for left and 'D' for right).

Stay sharp, anticipate the twists and turns, and master the Tunnel Road!
