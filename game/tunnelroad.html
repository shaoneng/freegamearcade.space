Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Your mission in Tunnel Road is simple: survive for as long as possible! You're racing down a high-speed, spinning tunnel filled with unpredictable obstacles. Your ball moves forward automatically, and it's your job to steer left and right to dodge everything in your path. The game gets progressively faster, demanding quicker reflexes the further you go. One wrong move, and your run is over!

Game Modes & Controls

1-Player Mode: Challenge yourself to get the highest score. Use the 'A' and 'D' keys or the Left and Right Arrow keys to steer your ball through the tunnel.

2-Player Mode: Compete against a friend on a split-screen! The goal is to be the last one standing. Player 1 (left side) uses the 'A' and 'D' keys. Player 2 (right side) uses the Left and Right Arrow keys. The first player to crash loses the round!
