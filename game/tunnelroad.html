Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Tunnel Road is a thrilling 3D avoidance game where you steer a ball through a constantly spinning, sci-fi tunnel. Your goal is simple: survive for as long as possible by dodging a relentless barrage of obstacles that appear with little warning.

Start by choosing between 1-Player or 2-Player mode. Once in the game, your ball accelerates forward automatically. You only need to control its side-to-side movement. The challenge lies in reacting to the twisting tunnel and the randomized blockades. As you progress, the speed increases, pushing your reflexes to their limit.

If you hit an obstacle, you have to restart from level 1. However, you can choose to use one of your limited revivals (by watching an ad) to keep your current progress and continue your run. There are no power-ups or brakes—it's a pure test of skill and endurance.

For a competitive experience, grab a friend for 2-Player mode. The screen splits vertically, with each player controlling a ball on their side. The first one to crash loses the round, making for intense, head-to-head action. Player 1 uses 'A' (left) and 'D' (right), while Player 2 uses the left and right arrow keys.
