Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Your objective in Tunnel Road is to survive for as long as you can inside a fast-paced, twisting 3D tunnel. You control a ball that is constantly moving forward, and you must steer left and right to dodge the colorful blockades and gaps that appear in your path. The game gets progressively faster, putting your reflexes to the ultimate test.

1-Player Mode: Use the 'A' and 'D' keys or the left and right arrow keys to steer your ball. Your goal is to complete each level without crashing. If you hit an obstacle, your run ends, and you must restart from level 1. Keep your focus sharp as the tunnel spins and the speed picks up!

2-Player Mode: Challenge a friend in a head-to-head competition! The screen splits vertically, with each player controlling their own ball in a parallel tunnel. Player 1 uses 'A' and 'D' keys to move, while Player 2 uses the left and right arrow keys. The first player to crash loses the round, making the other player the winner. It's a thrilling race to see who has the superior reflexes!
