Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Tunnel Road is a thrilling 3D avoidance game that tests your reflexes. Your mission is simple: navigate your ball through a futuristic, spinning tunnel filled with twists, turns, and unexpected obstacles. The longer you survive, the faster it gets, pushing your skills to the limit.

The game is all about precision and timing. Steer left or right to dodge the blockades in your path. Each successful run advances you to the next level. However, a single collision sends you back to the start. You have a limited number of revivals to continue your streak if you make a mistake, so use them wisely!

1-Player Mode

In single-player mode, it's just you against the ever-accelerating tunnel. Focus on your reflexes and aim for the highest level possible. There are no power-ups or brakes—just pure, unadulterated skill and the tunnel ahead.

2-Player Mode

Challenge a friend in the exciting 2-player mode! The screen splits vertically, with each player controlling their own ball in a parallel tunnel. The goal is to outlast your opponent. The first player to crash loses the round. Player 1 uses the 'A' and 'D' keys to move left and right.

Tunnel Road, developed by gamevgames, modernizes the classic endless runner with slick graphics and smooth controls. If you enjoy this high-speed challenge, be sure to try Extreme Run 3D for an even more intense reflex test with dynamic obstacles.
