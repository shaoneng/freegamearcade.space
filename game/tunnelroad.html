Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Your objective in Tunnel Road is to navigate your ball through a constantly twisting and turning tunnel for as long as you can. The tunnel is filled with obstacles, and hitting just one will end your run, forcing you to restart from level 1. Keep your focus sharp, as the speed gradually increases, making the challenge more intense with every second you survive.

In 1-Player Mode, you control your ball using the 'A' and 'D' keys or the left and right arrow keys. Steer left and right to dodge the blockades that appear in your path. It's a pure test of your reflexesâ€”no power-ups, no brakes, just you against the ever-changing tunnel.

For a competitive challenge, select 2-Player Mode from the main menu. The screen splits vertically, and you and a friend race side-by-side in parallel tunnels. The first player to crash loses the round. Player 1 uses the 'A' and 'D' keys, while Player 2 uses the Left and Right arrow keys. See who can outlast the other in this thrilling head-to-head race!
