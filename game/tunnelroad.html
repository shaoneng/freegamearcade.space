Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Welcome to Tunnel Road, a high-speed test of your reflexes! The goal is simple: guide your ball through a twisting, spinning tunnel for as long as you can without crashing. The tunnel is filled with unpredictable obstacles, so you'll need to stay sharp to survive.

Single Player Mode: Use the 'A' and 'D' keys or the Left and Right Arrow keys to steer your ball. Navigate through the gaps and avoid hitting any of the red blockades. The game gets progressively faster, pushing your reaction time to the limit. If you crash, you'll have to start over from level 1, so every move counts!

Two Player Mode: Challenge a friend in the split-screen 2-player mode! The screen divides, and each player controls their own ball in a parallel tunnel. The objective is to outlast your opponent. The first player to crash loses the round. Player 1 uses the 'A' and 'D' keys to control the ball on the left.
