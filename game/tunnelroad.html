Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

Tunnel Road is a thrilling 3D avoidance game where you navigate a vibrant, spinning sci-fi tunnel. The objective is to survive for as long as possible by dodging twists, turns, and sudden obstacles. Inspired by classic endless runners, this game offers a modern, polished experience with randomized levels and competitive 2-player support.

To begin, choose between 1-Player or 2-Player mode from the main menu. Once in the game, your ball automatically speeds forward. Your only job is to steer left or right to avoid everything in your path. The game's speed gradually increases, pushing your reflexes to the limit. There are no power-ups, brakes, or slow-motion—just a pure test of skill.

1-Player Mode: Your goal is to survive and progress through as many levels as you can. Each successful run advances you to the next level. However, a single collision with an obstacle will send you back to level 1, unless you choose to use one of your limited revivals.

2-Player Mode: Challenge a friend in a split-screen showdown! Both players race side-by-side in parallel tunnels. The first one to crash loses the round, and the other player wins. The screen is split vertically: Player 1 uses 'A' (left) and 'D' (right) to control the ball on the left side, while Player 2 controls the ball on the right.

Developed by gamevgames, Tunnel Road runs smoothly directly in your browser. If you enjoy this type of high-speed reflex challenge, try Extreme Run 3D for an even more intense experience with a greater variety of dynamic obstacles.
