Tunnel Road

How to Play Tunnel Road

The goal in Tunnel Road is to guide your ball through a dizzying, high-speed tunnel for as long as possible, dodging every obstacle in your path. The game is a pure test of reflexes and can be played solo or with a friend.

In 1-Player Mode, you control a single ball speeding through the tunnel. Your objective is to survive and progress through increasingly difficult levels. The speed picks up as you go, demanding more and more focus. If you hit an obstacle, you have to start over from level 1, so every move counts!

For a competitive challenge, select 2-Player Mode from the main menu. The screen will split vertically, and you and a friend will race side-by-side. The last player standing wins the round. Player 1 uses the 'A' key to move left and the 'D' key to move right. First one to crash loses!
