Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

In Ultimate Flying Car, you have two thrilling game modes to choose from: Free Ride and Racing.

In **Free Ride** mode, you can freely explore a vast city map, complete with long roads, palm trees, and stunt areas. Enjoy soaring through the sky, performing incredible aerial maneuvers with your chosen flying car.

For a competitive experience, select **Racing** mode. Here, you'll compete against three other drivers across two laps of the city. Drive at high speeds and try to outpace your opponents. Be careful to stay on the road, as going off will cause you to respawn after 3 seconds.

Both modes can be enjoyed solo or with a friend using the **2 Player** option. Start with default cars inspired by BMW, and unlock new, more stylish vehicles, including a coveted purple Ferrari, by collecting diamonds and winning races.
