Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Welcome to the future of driving! In Ultimate Flying Car, your goal is to master vehicles that can both race on the streets and soar through the skies. Start by choosing your preferred game mode from the main menu.

Racing Mode: Compete against three other drivers in a thrilling two-lap race across a vast city map. Use your speed and flying ability to gain an edge, but be careful! Driving off the road will respawn you, costing precious time. Finish first to earn coins and unlock new cars.

Free Ride Mode: If you prefer a more relaxed experience, select Free Ride. This mode lets you freely explore the entire map, discover stunt ramps, and enjoy the sensation of flying without any pressure. It's the perfect way to practice your skills or just have fun.

2-Player Mode: You can enjoy both Racing and Free Ride with a friend! Challenge them to a head-to-head race or simply explore the city together. Collect diamonds scattered around the map in either mode to save up for the best cars in the garage.
