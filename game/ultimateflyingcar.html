Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Welcome to the future of driving! In Ultimate Flying Car, you can take control of powerful sports cars that can also sprout wings and take to the skies. Your objective is to master both driving and flying to conquer the game's two distinct modes: Free Ride and Racing. You can even bring a friend along for the ride in the 2-player mode!

Player 1 Controls:

  • Drive: W, A, S, D
  • Nitro: Left Shift
  • Fly/Drive Mode: F
  • Reset Car: R

Player 2 Controls:

  • Drive: Arrow Keys
  • Nitro: K
  • Fly/Drive Mode: J
  • Reset Car: L

In Free Ride mode, you have the freedom to explore a sprawling city filled with long roads, stunt ramps, and beautiful scenery. Collect diamonds scattered across the map to earn currency. In Racing mode, compete against three other drivers in an intense two-lap race. Use your nitro and flying abilities strategically to gain an edge. Winning races and collecting diamonds will allow you to unlock new, faster, and more stylish flying cars!
