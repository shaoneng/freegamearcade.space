Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Welcome to Ultimate Flying Car, where the dream of flying cars becomes a reality! In this game, you get behind the wheel of powerful sports cars equipped with wings, allowing you to seamlessly transition from driving on the streets to soaring through the skies.

You can choose between two exciting game modes. In Free Ride, you can explore a vast and detailed city at your own pace. Discover stunt ramps, long highways, and beautiful scenery without any pressure. In Racing mode, you'll compete against three other drivers in intense 2-lap races. Push your skills to the limit to finish in first place! Both modes can be enjoyed solo or with a friend in the 2-Player option.

Your goal is either to dominate the racetrack or to master the art of flight. While driving, look for opportunities to take off and explore the city from above. As you play, collect diamonds scattered around the map and earn coins by winning races. Use these to unlock a fleet of six unique flying cars, each with its own style and performance.

Controls:

Player 1:
  • Move: W, A, S, D keys
  • Nitro / Fly: Left Shift
  • Look Back: T key
  • Respawn: R key
Player 2:
  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Nitro / Fly: N key
  • Look Back: L key
  • Respawn: U key
