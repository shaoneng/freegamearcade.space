Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Welcome to a world where cars have wings! In Ultimate Flying Car, you take control of futuristic sports cars that can seamlessly switch between driving on the road and soaring through the air. Experience the ultimate freedom in a vast, open-world city.

The game offers two exciting modes to choose from. In Free Ride mode, explore the sprawling map at your own pace. Discover hidden stunt ramps, practice your flying skills, and enjoy the stunning scenery. If you're looking for competition, jump into Racing mode. Compete against three other drivers in a thrilling two-lap race to prove you're the fastest on the ground and in the air. You can also challenge a friend in the 2 Player option for either mode!

As you explore and win races, collect diamonds and coins. Use this currency to unlock an impressive garage of new, faster, and more stylish flying cars. The controls are intuitive:

  • Player 1: Use WASD to drive, Left Shift for nitro, and F to activate flight. Press R to reset your car.
  • Player 2: Use the Arrow Keys to drive, K for nitro, and J to activate flight. Press O to reset your car.
