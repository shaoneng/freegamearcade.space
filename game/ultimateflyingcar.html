Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Ever dreamed of a car that could soar through the skies? In Ultimate Flying Car, that dream becomes a reality! This 3D driving and flying game puts you behind the wheel of incredible winged sports cars.

Choose Your Adventure

Ultimate Flying Car offers two exciting game modes, playable solo or with a friend in 2-player mode:

  • Free Ride: Explore a sprawling city map at your own pace. Discover long roads, stunt areas, funfairs, and take in the spectacular views under a red sky. Perfect for practicing your flying, performing stunts, and just enjoying the freedom.
  • Racing: Compete against three other drivers in a thrilling 2-lap race across the same vast map. Speed is key, but so is control! Stay on the road to avoid being respawned, and push your flying car to its limits to finish first.

Unlock Your Fleet

You start with two awesome flying cars inspired by famous sports models, but there are six in total to collect. Earn coins by winning races and find hidden diamonds in Free Ride mode to unlock new, more stylish, and faster vehicles. Work your way up to the ultimate purple supercar for the most exhilarating experience!
