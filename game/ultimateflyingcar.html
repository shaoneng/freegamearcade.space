Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Welcome to the future of driving! In Ultimate Flying Car, your goal is to master sports cars that can also take flight. Start by picking your favorite winged vehicle from the garage. The game offers two exciting modes: Free Ride and Racing, both available for solo play or with a friend in 2-player mode.

In Free Ride mode, you have the freedom to explore a vast, open-world city. There are no rules or objectives—simply drive on the streets, soar through the sky, and perform incredible stunts using the various ramps and unique structures scattered across the map. This is the perfect mode to hone your flying skills and enjoy the amazing views.

If you crave competition, jump into Racing mode. You'll race against three other drivers in a thrilling 2-lap competition across the city. Use your driving speed and flying ability to gain an advantage, master the turns, and aim for first place. Winning races and collecting diamonds will earn you currency to unlock even faster and more stylish cars for your collection.
