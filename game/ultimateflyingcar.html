Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Welcome to the future of driving! In Ultimate Flying Car, you get to pilot incredible sports cars that can also take to the skies. Your main goal is to have fun, whether you're exploring a vast city or competing in high-octane races.

The game offers two exciting modes. In Free Ride mode, you have the freedom to roam a huge map filled with stunt ramps, long roads, and beautiful scenery. Practice your flying, perform epic stunts, or simply cruise around and enjoy the view. In Racing mode, you'll go head-to-head against three other drivers in a thrilling two-lap race. Use your speed and flying ability to gain an edge and finish first!

You can enjoy both modes solo or challenge a friend in the 2 Player option. As you play, collect diamonds scattered around the map and win races to earn coins. Use these to unlock a fleet of six unique flying cars, each with its own style and performance.

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use WASD to drive, Left Shift for Nitro, F to activate/deactivate flight, and R to reset your car.
  • Player 2: Use the Arrow Keys to drive, K for Nitro, L to activate/deactivate flight, and O to reset your car.
