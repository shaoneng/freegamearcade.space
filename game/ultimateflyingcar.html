Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Welcome to the future of driving! In Ultimate Flying Car, the sky is no longer the limit. Your goal is to pilot incredible sports cars equipped with wings. Begin by choosing one of two game modes: the relaxing Free Ride or the competitive Racing mode. You can enjoy the experience solo or challenge a friend in the 2-player option.

In Free Ride mode, you have the freedom to explore a vast and vibrant city. The map is filled with long roads, stunt ramps, and beautiful scenery. Practice your flying, perform jaw-dropping stunts, or simply cruise through the streets and skies at your own pace. There are no objectives, just pure driving and flying fun.

If you crave competition, select Racing mode. You'll compete against three other drivers on the same expansive map for a two-lap race. Use your speed and flying ability to gain an edge, but be careful not to stray too far off the course, or you'll be respawned. The first one to cross the finish line wins!

As you play, you'll earn diamonds and coins. Use this currency to unlock a garage of six unique flying cars, each with its own style. Start with two great vehicles and work your way up to the ultimate high-performance machine. Master both the road and the sky to become the ultimate flying car champion!
