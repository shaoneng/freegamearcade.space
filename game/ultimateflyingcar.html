Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Welcome to the future of driving in Ultimate Flying Car! Your main goal is to master both driving on the ground and soaring through the skies. The game offers two exciting modes to explore: Free Ride and Racing. You can enjoy these modes solo or challenge a friend in the 2-player option.

In Free Ride mode, you have complete freedom to explore a massive city filled with long roads, stunt ramps, and beautiful scenery. Practice your flying skills, perform incredible stunts, or simply cruise around and enjoy the view. In Racing mode, you'll compete against three other drivers in a thrilling two-lap race across the same city map. Stay on the road and use your nitro wisely to finish first!

As you play, collect diamonds scattered throughout the city and earn coins by winning races. Use this currency to unlock a total of six stylish and powerful flying cars, each with unique performance. Start with two great cars and work your way up to the ultimate vehicle!

Controls:

Player 1:

  • Move: W, A, S, D
  • Nitro: Left Shift
  • Look Back: T
  • Fix Car Position: R
  • Handbrake: Space Bar

Player 2:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Nitro: M
  • Look Back: L
  • Fix Car Position: U
  • Handbrake: K
