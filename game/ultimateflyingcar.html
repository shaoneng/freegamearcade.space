Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Get ready for a stunning driving and flying experience where the sky is literally the limit! In Ultimate Flying Car, you can choose from a garage of stylish sports cars, each equipped with wings for soaring through the city.

The game offers two exciting modes. In Free Ride mode, you can explore a vast city map filled with long roads, stunt areas, and beautiful scenery. Practice your flying, perform amazing aerial stunts, or just enjoy a relaxing drive. In Racing mode, you'll compete against three other drivers in a thrilling two-lap race across the same expansive map. Stay on the road, use your nitro wisely, and aim for first place to earn coins and diamonds.

You can enjoy both modes solo or challenge a friend in the 2 Player option. Use the diamonds and coins you collect from races and exploration to unlock six unique flying cars, including a high-end purple sports car that's the fastest of them all!

Controls - Player 1:

  • Drive: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Nitro/Boost: Left Shift
  • Handbrake: Spacebar

Controls - Player 2:

  • Drive: Arrow Keys
  • Nitro/Boost: N
  • Handbrake: M
