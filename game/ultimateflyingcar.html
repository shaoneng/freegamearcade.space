Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Welcome to the future of driving! In Ultimate Flying Car, you get behind the wheel of a high-tech sports car that can also take to the skies. Your first choice is to decide which game mode you want to play: Free Ride or Racing. You can also team up with a friend for an exciting 2-player experience in either mode.

In Free Ride mode, you have the freedom to explore a vast, open-world city. Practice your flying, perform incredible stunts using ramps and other structures, or simply cruise the streets and enjoy the stunning scenery. There are no rules or objectives, just pure, unadulterated fun.

If you're feeling competitive, jump into Racing mode. Here, you'll go head-to-head against three other drivers in a thrilling two-lap race across the city. Use your speed, driving skills, and flying ability to outmaneuver your opponents and claim first place. Winning races earns you coins to unlock new vehicles.

As you play, keep an eye out for diamonds scattered around the map. Collecting diamonds and winning races provides you with the currency needed to unlock a fleet of six unique flying cars, each with its own style. Start with the initial vehicles and work your way up to the ultimate ride!
