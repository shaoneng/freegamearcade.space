Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Welcome to the future of driving! In Ultimate Flying Car, your goal is to master both racing on the ground and soaring through the sky. Start by choosing your preferred game mode and selecting one of the awesome winged sports cars.

The game offers two exciting modes. In Free Ride, you can explore a massive, open-world city at your leisure. Discover stunt ramps, fly between skyscrapers, and enjoy total freedom. In Racing mode, you'll compete against three other drivers in thrilling 2-lap races. Use your speed and flying ability to gain an edge and finish first!

You can enjoy both modes solo or challenge a friend by selecting the 2-Player option. As you race and explore, collect diamonds and earn coins to unlock a fleet of faster and more stylish flying cars. Work your way up to the ultimate supercar and dominate the streets and the skies!
