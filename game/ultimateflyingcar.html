Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Welcome to the future of driving in Ultimate Flying Car! Your main goal is to pilot incredible sports cars that can also take flight. The game offers two exciting modes to choose from, both available for solo play or with a friend in 2-player mode.

In Free Ride mode, you have the freedom to explore a vast and detailed city. Practice your flying, discover stunt ramps, and simply enjoy the breathtaking views without any pressure. It's the perfect way to get a feel for your car's unique handling in the air and on the ground.

If you're looking for a challenge, jump into Racing mode. Here, you'll compete against three other drivers in a thrilling 2-lap race across the same expansive map. Push your car to its limits, but be careful! Driving off the road will cause you to respawn, costing you precious time. Win races and collect diamonds scattered throughout the world to unlock faster and more stylish flying cars.

Whether you're casually soaring through the skies or battling for first place, the choice is yours. Select your car, pick your mode, and get ready for an unforgettable driving experience!
