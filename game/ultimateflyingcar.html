Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Welcome to Ultimate Flying Car! Your objective is to either race against opponents or freely explore a vast city, both on the ground and in the air. Begin by selecting your game mode: Racing or Free Ride. You can also choose between 1-Player or 2-Player mode to play solo or challenge a friend.

Use your keyboard to control your vehicle. Typically, the Arrow Keys or WASD keys are used for steering, accelerating, and braking. To take flight, engage the flight mode (often with the Shift key) to deploy your wings and soar through the sky.

In Racing Mode, your goal is to complete two laps and finish ahead of three other drivers. Stick to the track to maintain your speed. If you go off-road, you'll be respawned back on the track after a few seconds.

In Free Ride Mode, there are no rules! Explore the expansive map, discover stunt ramps, collect diamonds scattered around the city, and enjoy the stunning views. Use the diamonds and coins won from races to unlock faster and more stylish flying cars in the garage.
