Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Welcome to Ultimate Flying Car, where the dreams of your childhood come true! In this game, you get to drive and fly high-tech sports cars through a sprawling city.

Begin by choosing your game mode. Select Free Ride to explore the vast map at your own pace, discovering stunt ramps, scenic views, and funfair attractions. Or, if you're feeling competitive, choose Racing mode to go head-to-head against three other drivers in an intense 2-lap race to the finish line.

Both modes can be enjoyed solo or with a friend in the 2-player option. As you play, collect diamonds scattered throughout the city and win races to earn coins. Use this currency to unlock a fleet of six unique flying cars, each more stylish and powerful than the last. Master both driving on the streets and soaring through the sky to become the ultimate champion!
