Ultimate Flying Car

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car

Welcome to the future of driving! In Ultimate Flying Car, you get behind the wheel of incredible sports cars that can also take flight. Your first step is to choose your game mode and select a vehicle from your garage. You can start with two unlocked cars and earn more as you play.

The game features two primary modes: Free Ride and Racing. In Free Ride mode, you have the freedom to explore a vast city map filled with long roads, stunt ramps, and beautiful scenery. Practice your flying skills, perform incredible aerial stunts, or simply enjoy a relaxing drive through the streets and skies.

If you crave competition, jump into the Racing mode. You'll compete against three other drivers on the same expansive map. The goal is to complete two laps and finish in first place. Stay on the road, use your flying ability strategically, and outmaneuver your opponents to claim victory. Both modes can be enjoyed solo or with a friend in the 2-player option for double the fun!

As you race and explore, collect diamonds scattered throughout the map. Winning races will also earn you coins. Use this currency to unlock a fleet of six stylish and powerful flying cars, including the ultimate purple supercar. Master both driving and flying to become the undisputed champion of the skies.
