Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Welcome to the high-flying world of Ultimate Flying Car 2! Your goal is to master both driving on the ground and soaring through the air. The game offers two exciting modes to choose from: Free Ride and Racing.

In Free Ride mode, you can explore the sprawling city at your own pace. Discover hidden ramps, perform epic stunts, and even engage in a game of car football. Use your keyboard to control the car's movement, acceleration, and braking. A specific key will activate the flight mode, allowing you to take off and navigate the skies.

Racing mode pits you against other competitors in a fast-paced race to the finish line. Use your driving and flying skills to gain an advantage and be the first to cross the finish.

The game also features a fantastic Split-Screen Mode for two players. Grab a friend and either explore the city together in Free Ride or go head-to-head in a competitive race on the same computer!
