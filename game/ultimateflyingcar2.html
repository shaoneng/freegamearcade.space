Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Get ready to defy gravity in Ultimate Flying Car 2! This game seamlessly blends high-octane street racing with thrilling aerial flight. Choose your advanced vehicle and get ready to explore, race, and perform incredible stunts.

The game features two main modes to enjoy. In Free Ride, you can explore a sprawling city at your own pace, discover hidden ramps, and even play car football. For a more competitive thrill, jump into Racing Mode to compete against other drivers in action-packed races where speed and skill are key. Best of all, you can enjoy both modes solo or with a friend using the split-screen feature!

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: WASD keys
  • NOS / Fly: Left Shift
  • Reset Car: R key
  • Look Back: T key

Player 2 Controls:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • NOS / Fly: N key
  • Reset Car: L key
  • Look Back: P key
