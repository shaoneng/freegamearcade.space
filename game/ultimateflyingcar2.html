How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Get behind the wheel and take to the skies in Ultimate Flying Car 2! Your primary goal is to drive your advanced vehicle through cityscapes, but with a twistâ€”your car can also fly. Use your keyboard to control the car's movement on the ground and to engage the flight mode to soar through the air.

The game offers two distinct modes for endless fun. In Free Ride Mode, you have the freedom to explore the vast map, discover secret ramps, practice your stunts, and master the unique physics of your flying car. If you crave competition, jump into Racing Mode to go head-to-head against other racers in high-speed, action-packed events.

One of the best features is the split-screen option. You can challenge a friend right on the same screen in either Free Ride or Racing mode. Buckle up, hit the gas, and decide whether you want to rule the streets or the skies!
