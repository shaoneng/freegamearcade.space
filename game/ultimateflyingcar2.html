Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Get ready to experience the ultimate fusion of high-speed racing and airborne adventure! Ultimate Flying Car 2 puts you in the driver's seat of futuristic vehicles that can seamlessly switch between driving on the ground and soaring through the skies.

The game offers two primary modes for endless fun. In Free Ride Mode, you have the freedom to explore a sprawling city, practice your flying skills, discover hidden ramps, and perform jaw-dropping stunts. If you're feeling competitive, jump into Racing Mode to go head-to-head against other drivers in thrilling races. You can also enjoy the game with a friend using the split-screen feature in both modes!

Controls for Player 1:

  • Move: W, A, S, D Keys
  • NOS/Boost: Left Shift
  • Switch to Flying Mode: F Key
  • Look Back: T Key
  • Reset Car: R Key

Controls for Player 2:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • NOS/Boost: M Key
  • Switch to Flying Mode: K Key
  • Look Back: L Key
  • Reset Car: O Key
