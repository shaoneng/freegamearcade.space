Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Get ready to take control of a high-tech vehicle that's part race car, part airplane! In Ultimate Flying Car 2, you have the freedom to drive on the ground or soar through the skies. The game offers two exciting modes to choose from:

Free Ride Mode: Explore vast, detailed city maps at your own pace. Discover hidden ramps, practice your flying skills, perform incredible stunts, or even play a round of car football. This mode is all about exploration and having fun without limits.

Racing Mode: Put your skills to the test against other racers in adrenaline-pumping competitions. Speed through checkpoints on the ground and in the air to prove you're the fastest.

Two-Player Fun: The game shines with its split-screen feature. Grab a friend and enjoy either Free Ride or Racing mode together on the same computer. Collaborate to find secrets or compete to see who is the ultimate flying car champion!
