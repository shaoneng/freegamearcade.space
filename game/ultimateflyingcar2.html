Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Get ready for a unique driving experience where your car can both race on the ground and soar through the air. The goal in Ultimate Flying Car 2 depends on the mode you choose, but it always involves high-octane action and exploration.

The game features two main modes. In Free Ride, you can explore the vast city at your own pace, perform incredible stunts, find ramps, and even play car football. In Racing Mode, you'll compete against other drivers in a thrilling race to the finish line. Both modes can be played solo or with a friend using the split-screen feature.

The game supports both single-player and two-player split-screen action. For Player 1, use the arrow keys to drive and specific keys for nitro and flight. For Player 2, the WASD keys are typically used for movement. Check the in-game instructions for the exact key layout for flying, boosting, and other special actions. Grab a friend and see who is the ultimate flying car champion!
