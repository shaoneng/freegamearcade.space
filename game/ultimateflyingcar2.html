Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Welcome to the driver's seat of the future! In Ultimate Flying Car 2, you're not just a racer; you're a pilot. The goal is to master both driving on the ground and soaring through the air.

Choose your preferred game mode to get started. In Free Ride mode, you can explore the vast, detailed city at your own pace. Practice your flying, perform incredible stunts using ramps, and discover hidden secrets without any pressure.

For a competitive thrill, select Racing Mode. Here, you'll compete against other cars in high-stakes races where speed and precision are key.

One of the standout features is the split-screen mode, allowing you to challenge a friend on the same computer. You can either explore together in Free Ride or go head-to-head in a race. Buckle up and take to the skies!
