Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Welcome to the future of racing! Ultimate Flying Car 2 combines high-speed driving on the ground with thrilling flight through the skies. Choose between two exciting modes: **Free Ride**, where you can explore a vast city, perform stunts, and discover secrets, or **Racing Mode**, where you compete against other drivers to prove your skills.

This game features an awesome split-screen mode, allowing you to challenge a friend on the same computer. Master the controls below to dominate the road and the sky!

Player 1 Controls:

  • Drive/Steer: W, A, S, D keys
  • Nitro Boost: Left Shift
  • Activate/Deactivate Flight Mode: F key
  • Reset Car: R key
  • Change Camera: C key

Player 2 Controls:

  • Drive/Steer: Arrow Keys
  • Nitro Boost: K key or Right Shift
  • Activate/Deactivate Flight Mode: G key
  • Reset Car: O key
  • Change Camera: L key
