Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Welcome to the future of racing! In Ultimate Flying Car 2, you have the freedom to drive on the ground or take to the skies. The game offers two main modes to enjoy, either solo or with a friend.

Free Ride Mode: This mode is your personal playground. Drive or fly through a massive city, discovering ramps, pulling off incredible stunts, and exploring every corner. You can even engage in a game of car football! This mode is perfect for practicing your flying skills or just having fun without the pressure of a race.

Racing Mode: Ready for a challenge? Compete against other racers on thrilling tracks that blend ground and air sections. The first to cross the finish line wins. Use your boost and flying ability strategically to gain an edge.

2-Player Split-Screen: Grab a friend and enjoy both Free Ride and Racing modes on the same screen! Compete head-to-head or explore the city together for double the fun.
