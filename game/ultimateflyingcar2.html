Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Get ready to take control of a high-tech vehicle that can both race on the ground and soar through the sky! The goal in Ultimate Flying Car 2 is to explore, race, and perform stunts in a massive open world. You can play solo or challenge a friend in the exciting two-player split-screen mode.

Controls:

Player 1:

  • Move: W, A, S, D keys
  • Nitro/Fly: Left Shift
  • Reset Car: R key
  • Change Camera: C key

Player 2:

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Nitro/Fly: M key
  • Reset Car: U key
  • Change Camera: K key

Choose between two main game modes. In Free Ride, you and a friend can explore the city, discover ramps, and practice your flying skills without any pressure. In Racing Mode, compete against other cars in thrilling races to prove you're the fastest on the ground and in the air!
