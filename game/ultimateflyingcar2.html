Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Welcome to the high-octane world of Ultimate Flying Car 2! Your goal is to race, explore, and perform stunts in a futuristic city where cars can fly. Choose your mode and get ready for an adrenaline-fueled adventure, either solo or with a friend in split-screen mode.

Player 1 Controls: Use the W, A, S, D keys to drive your car. Press Shift for a nitro boost and T to look back. If you crash, use the R key to reset your car's position. To transform into a flying car and take to the skies, press the F key.

Player 2 Controls: Use the Arrow Keys to drive. Press K for a nitro boost and L to look back. Reset your car with the U key. To activate flight mode, press the H key.

Game Modes: The game features two exciting modes. In Free Ride, you can freely explore the vast city, discover ramps, and practice your flying and driving skills. In Racing Mode, you'll compete against other cars in thrilling races to see who is the ultimate champion. Both modes are available for single-player and two-player split-screen.
