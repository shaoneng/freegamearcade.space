Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Get behind the wheel—and wings—of a high-tech vehicle in Ultimate Flying Car 2! The game offers two primary modes to enjoy: Free Ride and Racing. Select your preferred mode from the main menu to begin your adventure. The specific controls for driving, flying, and using nitro will be displayed on your screen.

In Free Ride mode, you have an entire city as your playground. Drive on the streets, or press the flight button to soar through the skyscrapers. This mode is perfect for practicing your flying skills, discovering hidden ramps, and performing epic stunts without any pressure.

If you're looking for a challenge, jump into Racing mode. Compete against other cars on dynamic tracks that will test both your driving and flying abilities. Use your nitro boosts strategically to gain an edge and be the first to cross the finish line.

One of the best features of Ultimate Flying Car 2 is its split-screen 2-player mode. Grab a friend and you can enjoy both Free Ride and Racing together on the same computer! Compete head-to-head or explore the city as a team for double the fun.
