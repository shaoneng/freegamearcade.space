Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Welcome to the driver's seat of the future! In Ultimate Flying Car 2, your goal is to master both high-speed driving on the ground and exhilarating flight through the air. You can explore a vast city, perform stunts, or compete in intense races.

Player 1 Controls:

  • Move/Steer: W, A, S, D keys
  • Nitro/NOS: SHIFT key
  • Look Back: T key
  • Reset Car Position: R key
  • Switch Camera: C key

Player 2 Controls (Split-Screen):

  • Move/Steer: Arrow Keys
  • Nitro/NOS: M key
  • Look Back: P key
  • Reset Car Position: O key
  • Switch Camera: K key

Choose between two exciting game modes. In Free Ride, you and a friend can explore the sprawling city at your own pace, discover secrets, and practice your flying skills. In Racing Mode, you'll compete against others in thrilling four-player competitions where speed and skill are key to victory. Both modes are available for single-player or two-player split-screen action!
