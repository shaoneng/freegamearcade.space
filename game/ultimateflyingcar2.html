Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Ultimate Flying Car 2 offers both single-player and thrilling 2-player split-screen modes. Get ready to drive, fly, and perform incredible stunts across detailed city maps!

Single Player / Player 1 Controls:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Nitro: Shift
  • Handbrake: Space
  • Toggle Flight Mode: F
  • Change Camera: C

Player 2 Controls (Split-Screen Mode):

  • Move: Arrow Keys
  • Nitro: K
  • Handbrake: L
  • Toggle Flight Mode: I
  • Change Camera: O

Choose between two exciting game modes: Free Ride allows you to explore the open world, perform gravity-defying stunts, smash obstacles, or even play car football. In Racing Mode, you'll compete in intense four-player races where precision and speed are key. Team up with a friend or go head-to-head on the same screen for double the fun!
