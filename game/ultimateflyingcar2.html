Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Get ready for a high-flying racing experience in Ultimate Flying Car 2! The goal is to master driving on the ground and soaring through the air. You can choose between two exciting game modes: a relaxed Free Ride to explore the city and practice your skills, or a competitive Racing Mode to challenge other drivers.

This game is perfect for solo play or for challenging a friend with its split-screen two-player mode. The controls are split for cooperative or competitive fun on one keyboard.

Player 1 Controls:
Move: W, A, S, D
Nitro / Fly: Left Shift
Look Back: T
Reset Car: R
Change Camera: C

Player 2 Controls:
Move: Arrow Keys
Nitro / Fly: M
Look Back: L
Reset Car: U
Change Camera: K
