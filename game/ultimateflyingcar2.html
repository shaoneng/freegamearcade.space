Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Welcome to the high-octane world of Ultimate Flying Car 2! Your objective is to master both ground-based racing and gravity-defying flight. Choose your mode and get ready for an adventure. You can play solo or challenge a friend on the same screen with the fantastic split-screen feature.

Game Modes:

- Free Ride: Explore a massive open-world city without any rules. Practice your stunts, discover secret locations, and perfect your flying skills. This is your personal playground!

- Racing: Compete against AI or a friend in thrilling races across dynamic tracks. Use your nitro boosts and flying ability to gain the ultimate edge and claim victory!

Controls (Player 1): Use W, A, S, D to drive. Press Left SHIFT for a nitro boost and the SPACEBAR for the handbrake. You can look behind you with T and reset your car's position with R.

Controls (Player 2): Use the ARROW KEYS to drive. Press M for nitro and K for the handbrake. Look back with L and reset your car with U.
