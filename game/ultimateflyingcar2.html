Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Welcome to the high-flying world of Ultimate Flying Car 2! Your goal is to master both driving on the ground and soaring through the sky. The game offers two distinct modes for you to enjoy.

In Free Ride mode, you have the freedom to explore the massive city at your own pace. Practice your stunts, find hidden ramps, and get comfortable with switching between driving and flying. This is the perfect place to hone your skills.

For more competitive action, jump into Racing mode. Here, you'll compete against other cars in thrilling races to the finish line. Use your speed, flying ability, and nitro boosts strategically to gain an edge and claim victory.

The game also features an exciting split-screen mode for two players. Challenge a friend on the same computer in either Free Ride or Racing mode. Controls are set up for two players: Player 1 uses WASD to drive, Shift for NOS/Fly, and R to reset. Player 2 uses the Arrow Keys to drive, M for NOS/Fly, and O to reset. Buckle up and have fun!
