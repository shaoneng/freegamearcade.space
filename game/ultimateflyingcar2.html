Ultimate Flying Car 2

How to Play Ultimate Flying Car 2

Welcome to the future of racing! In Ultimate Flying Car 2, you can seamlessly switch between driving on city streets and soaring through the sky. Choose between two exciting modes: "Free Ride" to explore the vast, detailed map at your leisure, or "Racing" to compete against other drivers. The game also features a fantastic split-screen mode, allowing you to challenge a friend on the same computer.

Player 1 Controls:

- Move/Steer: WASD Keys
- Nitro / Fly: Left-Shift
- Handbrake: Spacebar
- Look Back: T Key
- Reset Car Position: R Key

Player 2 Controls:

- Move/Steer: Arrow Keys
- Nitro / Fly: Right-Shift
- Handbrake: K Key
- Look Back: P Key
- Reset Car Position: O Key
