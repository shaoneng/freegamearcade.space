Ultimate Moto

How to Play Ultimate Moto

Get ready for a pure arcade experience in Ultimate Moto! The goal is to guide your neon bike to the finish line on each level without tipping over and crashing.

Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your motorcycle. Press the forward key to accelerate and use the up/down keys to balance your bike while in the air to land safely.

As you speed through the glowing tracks, make sure to collect as many diamonds as you can. These gems are your key to unlocking cooler, faster bikes and upgrading your engine for better performance. For a stylish bonus, try to perform a mid-air flip to earn extra diamonds!

Each level presents a new challenge with trickier ramps and longer jumps. If you crash, don't worry! You can restart the level instantly and try again. Good luck!
