Ultimate Moto

How to Play Ultimate Moto

Get right into the action in Ultimate Moto! Your main objective is to guide your motorcycle through a series of challenging, neon-lit levels and cross the finish line without tipping over and crashing.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your bike. The up/down arrows (or W/S) control your acceleration and braking, while the left/right arrows (or A/D) are used to lean back and forth, helping you maintain balance during jumps and on steep slopes.

As you speed through each track, make sure to collect as many diamonds as you can. These gems are your currency for unlocking new, cooler bikes and upgrading your engine for better speed and power. For an extra bonus, try to perform a full flip while airborne to earn three extra diamonds! If you do happen to crash, don't worry—you can instantly restart the level and try again.
