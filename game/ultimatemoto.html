Ultimate Moto

How to Play Ultimate Moto

Welcome to the neon-drenched tracks of Ultimate Moto! Your goal is to navigate your motorcycle through each level and reach the finish line without crashing.

Use either the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your bike. Press the up arrow or 'W' to accelerate, and the down arrow or 'S' to brake or reverse. Use the left and right arrow keys (or 'A' and 'D') to lean back and forward, which is crucial for balancing your bike on hills and landing jumps safely.

Along each course, collect as many diamonds as you can. These are your in-game currency, which you can use in the menu to purchase new, faster bikes and upgrade your engine.
