Ultimate Moto

How to Play Ultimate Moto

Your main objective in Ultimate Moto is to navigate your rider through a series of glowing, neon-lit tracks and safely reach the finish line. Each level introduces new challenges, including steep hills, long jumps, and tricky loops that will test your balance and control.

The controls are straightforward. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to maneuver your bike. Press the 'W' or Up Arrow key to accelerate forward, and the 'S' or Down Arrow key to brake or move in reverse. Use the 'A'/Left Arrow and 'D'/Right Arrow keys to lean your rider, which is essential for maintaining balance during jumps and on uneven surfaces.

As you race, collect the sparkling diamonds scattered throughout each course. These gems are your currency for upgrading your ride.
