Ultimate Moto

How to Play Ultimate Moto

Your main objective in Ultimate Moto is to navigate your bike through each neon-lit level and reach the finish line without crashing. Each stage presents a new set of obstacles, including steep hills, long jumps, and tricky loops.

The controls are straightforward and easy to master. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to accelerate, brake, and balance your bike while in the air.

As you race through the tracks, be sure to collect as many diamonds as you can. These gems are your currency for unlocking new, cooler bikes and upgrading your engine for better performance. You can even earn bonus diamonds by successfully landing a flip in the air!

Don't worry if you crash! You can instantly restart the level and try again. Master the physics, time your jumps, and conquer every course.
