Ultimate Moto

How to Play Ultimate Moto

Your mission in Ultimate Moto is to navigate your neon bike through a series of glowing tracks filled with loops, ramps, and obstacles. The primary goal is to reach the finish line on each level without crashing your bike.

Use the WASD or arrow keys to control your bike's movement and balance. As you race through the courses, collect as many sparkly diamonds as you can. These gems are your currency for unlocking new bikes and upgrading your engine for better performance.

Show off your skills by performing flips in the air to earn bonus diamonds. Each successful flip adds three extra gems to your total. Don't worry if you crash; you can instantly restart the level and try again to conquer the challenging terrain.
