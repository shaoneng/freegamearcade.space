Ultimate Moto

How to Play Ultimate Moto

Welcome to the glowing tracks of Ultimate Moto! The goal is simple: ride your bike to the finish line on each level without tipping over and crashing.

Use the Arrow Keys or WASD to control your motorcycle. The up/down keys (or W/S) control your acceleration and braking, while the left/right keys (or A/D) are used to lean back and forth. Mastering your balance is key to landing big jumps and navigating tricky loops.

As you race through each level, collect the sparkling diamonds scattered along the track. These gems are your currency to buy new, cooler bikes and upgrade your engine for better speed and handling.
