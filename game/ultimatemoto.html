Ultimate Moto

How to Play Ultimate Moto

Welcome to the neon-lit tracks of Ultimate Moto! Your primary goal is to navigate your motorcycle through each level's challenging obstacle course and reach the finish line without crashing. The game is all about balance, speed, and precision.

The controls are refreshingly simple. Use the WASD keys or the Arrow Keys on your keyboard to control your bike. Press the up arrow or 'W' to accelerate, and the down arrow or 'S' to brake or reverse. Use the left and right arrows (or 'A' and 'D') to balance your bike while in the air to ensure a safe landing.

As you race through the levels, be sure to collect the glowing diamonds scattered along the track. These gems are your key to unlocking cooler bikes and more powerful engine upgrades in the shop.
