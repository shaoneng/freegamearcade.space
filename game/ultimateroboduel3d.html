Ultimate Robo Duel 3D

How to Play Ultimate Robo Duel 3D

Welcome to the high-tech wrestling arena of Ultimate Robo Duel 3D! Your objective is to defeat your robot opponent in a one-on-one battle. Keep a close eye on your health bar at the top of the screen; drain your opponent's health to zero to win the match!

You can take on the challenge in two exciting modes. In 'Campaign' mode, you'll fight against computer-controlled opponents, progressing through increasingly difficult levels. Or, grab a friend and battle it out in '2 Player' mode for some local multiplayer action.

Unleash a variety of attacks to take down your rival. Use punches and kicks to deal damage, and fire projectiles for ranged attacks. Don't forget to use your 'Special Hit' for a massive burst of damage when the time is right. Master your robot's moveset to dominate the arena.

As you win fights, you'll earn money. Use this currency in the 'Robots' section to unlock new and more powerful mechanical warriors. Each robot has unique stats for battery power, bullet force, and special attack warm-up time, so choose wisely to fit your fighting style!
