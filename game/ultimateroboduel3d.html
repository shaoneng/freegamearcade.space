Ultimate Robo Duel 3D

How to Play Ultimate Robo Duel 3D

Welcome to the electrifying world of Ultimate Robo Duel 3D! Your mission is to enter the wrestling arena and prove your mettle in intense robot-on-robot combat. You can challenge the computer in Story Mode, fighting your way through progressively tougher opponents, or you can go head-to-head with a friend in the thrilling 2-Player mode.

In the arena, your goal is to defeat your opponent by depleting their health bar to zero. Use a combination of punches and kicks to deal damage. For a more powerful assault, fire your robot's built-in bullets or unleash a devastating "Special Hit" to inflict massive damage. Keep a close eye on both your health bar and your opponent's to strategize your attack and defense.

As you win matches, you'll earn money that you can use to unlock a variety of new and powerful robot fighters. Each robot has unique statistics for battery power, bullet force, and special attack warm-up times, so choose wisely to find the machine that best suits your fighting style!

Controls:

  • Player 1: Use W, A, S, D to move. F for punch, G for kick, and H for special attack.
  • Player 2: Use Arrow Keys to move. U for punch, J for kick, and K for special attack.
