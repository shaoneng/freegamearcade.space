Unpacking

How to Play Unpacking

Welcome to your new home! Unpacking is a relaxing and satisfying puzzle game where your goal is to tidy up by finding the perfect place for every item. Start by clicking on the boxes to open them and reveal the items inside.

Once an item is out, simply click and drag it to where you think it belongs in the room. Place books on the bookshelf, clothes in the closet, and dishes in the kitchen cabinets. There are no timers or scores, so you can take your time and enjoy the process of creating a neat and organized living space.

Some items have specific places they must go. If an item is placed incorrectly, it will be highlighted with a red outline. Keep rearranging until every object has found its proper home. When all items in a room are correctly placed, you'll complete the level and move on to the next chapter of your life!
