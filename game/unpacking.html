Unpacking

How to Play Unpacking

Welcome to your new home! Your task in Unpacking is to take items out of moving boxes and find a suitable place for them in each room. It's a calm and meditative puzzle game that's all about organization and decoration.

Simply click on a box to open it and reveal an item. Click and drag the item to move it around the room. Find the correct spot for each itemâ€”dishes go in the kitchen cupboards, books on the shelves, clothes in the closet, and so on.

A level is complete once you have successfully unpacked all the boxes and placed every single item in an appropriate location. Items that are not in the right place will be highlighted with a red outline, guiding you to find a better home for them. Tidy up room by room and watch a life story unfold through the possessions you organize!
