Unpacking

How to Play Unpacking

Welcome to your new home! In Unpacking, your goal is to transform a cluttered space into a cozy, organized room. The gameplay is simple and intuitive, making it a perfect relaxing puzzle.

Start by clicking on the cardboard boxes to open them and reveal the items inside. Each item needs to find its proper place in the room. Click and drag items from the boxes to shelves, tables, closets, and drawers.

Pay attention to the outlines! When you hover an item over a correct spot, it will flash. An incorrect placement will be highlighted in red. You must find the right home for every single object to successfully complete the level and move on to the next chapter of your new life.
