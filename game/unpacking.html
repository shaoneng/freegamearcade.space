Unpacking

How to Play Unpacking

Welcome to your new home! Your task in Unpacking is to create a tidy and organized living space by finding the right place for every item. The game is a relaxing puzzle that combines home decoration with satisfying organization.

To begin, simply click or tap on a cardboard box to open it and reveal the items inside. One by one, take each item out of the box. Use your mouse to click, drag, and drop each object into a suitable location within the room. Think about where things like books, kitchen utensils, or clothes would normally go.

The puzzle lies in figuring out the correct placement for everything. Not every spot will work! Keep arranging items until you find their designated home. Once all items from the boxes are correctly placed, the level is complete, and you can move on to the next room and the next chapter of your life's story.
