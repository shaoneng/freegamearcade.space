Unpacking

How to Play Unpacking

Welcome to your new home! Unpacking is a relaxing puzzle game where your goal is to turn a cluttered space into a tidy, organized room. The gameplay is simple and intuitive, making it easy for anyone to pick up and play.

Start by clicking on the cardboard boxes scattered around the room. Each click will reveal a new item that needs to be placed. Simply click and drag an item from the box and drop it where you think it belongs. Place books on the bookshelf, dishes in the kitchen cabinets, and clothes in the closet.

The challenge comes from finding the correct spot for every single object. Some items have very specific places they need to be. Once all items from the boxes are placed correctly, the room is complete, and you can move on to the next stage of your life. Unpack everything and watch a story unfold through the items you own!
