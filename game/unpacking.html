Unpacking

How to Play Unpacking

Welcome to your new home! Your task in Unpacking is to transform a cluttered space into a tidy, organized living area. The gameplay is simple and intuitive: click on the cardboard boxes to open them and reveal the items inside. One by one, you'll need to find the perfect spot for each object.

Drag and drop items from the boxes to shelves, desks, closets, and counters using your mouse. The goal is to place everything in a logical and appropriate location. While some items might fit in several places, there's a correct spot for each one. Once all items in a room are placed correctly, you'll be able to move on to the next, uncovering more of the story as you go.

There's no rush and no score, so take your time and enjoy the calming process of creating order from chaos. Simply use your mouse to click, drag, and drop each possession until the room feels just right!
