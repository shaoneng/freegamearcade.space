Unpacking

How to Play Unpacking

Welcome to your new home! Your task in Unpacking is to open the moving boxes and organize each room, creating a tidy and lived-in space. It's a relaxing puzzle that's all about finding the right spot for every possession.

To begin, simply click on a cardboard box to open it up. Click again on the items inside to take them out one by one. You'll unpack everything from books and clothes to kitchen utensils and personal keepsakes.

Once an item is in your hand, use your mouse to drag it to its proper place in the room. Books go on the bookshelf, dishes go in the kitchen cupboards, and clothes go in the closet. Think logically about where each item belongs.

Continue unpacking and placing items until all the boxes are empty and everything is neatly arranged. The level is complete once every item has found its correct home. Enjoy the satisfying process of turning chaos into a cozy, organized home!
