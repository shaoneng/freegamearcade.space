Unpacking

How to Play Unpacking

Welcome to your new home! In Unpacking, a relaxing and satisfying organization puzzle game, your task is to turn chaos into order. Open up the boxes and find the perfect spot for every item you pull out, transforming empty rooms into cozy living spaces.

The gameplay is simple and intuitive. Click on a box to open it, then drag and drop items into their designated places around the room. From books on the shelf to dishes in the cupboard, everything has its place. It’s a meditative experience that rewards neatness and attention to detail.

As you progress, you'll move through different stages of life, unpacking memories and creating a home. Each room presents a new puzzle, challenging you to think about where each possession belongs. Can you tidy up every room and create a perfectly organized home?
