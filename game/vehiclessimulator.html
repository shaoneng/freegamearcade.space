Vehicles Simulator

How to Play Vehicles Simulator

Getting started in Vehicles Simulator is all about freedom and choice. Your first step is to select one of the three available maps. If you're in the mood for off-roading, pick the map with sandy beaches and hills. For a more urban experience, choose one of the two city maps.

Next, pick your ride! You have access to seven unique vehicles right from the start. Whether you want to cruise in a sleek sports car, haul cargo with a semi-truck (press 'T' to attach the trailer), navigate the city in a bus, or cause chaos in a military tank, the choice is yours.

For an enhanced experience, don't forget to check out the 'Settings' menu on the right side of the screen. Here, you can fine-tune your vehicle's performance by adjusting the engine, transmission, steering, and even activating driving assists. This allows you to tailor the handling to your exact preference.

Once you're set up, just hit the road! There are no rules or missions to follow. Explore the maps, test your vehicle's limits, perform stunts, and enjoy the pure pleasure of driving.
