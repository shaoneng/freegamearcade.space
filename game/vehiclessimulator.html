Vehicles Simulator

How to Play Vehicles Simulator

Welcome to the ultimate driving sandbox! In Vehicles Simulator, your goal is pure freedom and enjoyment. Start by selecting one of three distinct maps: a hilly seaside with a sandy beach, a sprawling city, or an open area with various buildings. Once you've chosen your playground, pick your ride from seven awesome options, including a city bus, a sleek sports car, a massive monster truck, and even a military tank!

For a truly custom experience, head over to the "Settings" section on the right side of your screen. Here, you can fine-tune your vehicle's performance by adjusting the engine, transmission, steering, and more. You can also activate driving assists to make handling smoother, doubling your driving pleasure.

Ready for something truly unique? Take control of the military 8x8 vehicle or a powerful tank and cause some mayhem! If you prefer heavy-duty hauling, select the truck, drive up to the trailer, and press the 'T' key to attach it. With no rules or restrictions, you're free to explore, experiment, and drive however you want!
