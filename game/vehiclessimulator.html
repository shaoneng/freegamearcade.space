Vehicles Simulator

How to Play Vehicles Simulator

Welcome to the ultimate driving sandbox! In Vehicles Simulator, there are no missions or restrictions, just pure driving enjoyment. Your first step is to choose your ride from an impressive lineup of seven different vehicles. Will you pick a sleek sports car, a massive city bus, a powerful monster truck, or even a military tank? After selecting your vehicle, pick one of the three unique maps to start your adventure.

Once you're in the game, use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your vehicle. Feel free to explore every corner of the map, whether it's cruising through city streets, climbing sandy dunes by the sea, or navigating an open area with various structures. If you've chosen the truck with a trailer, drive up to the trailer and press the 'T' key to attach it.

For an even more personalized experience, dive into the vehicle settings. Click on the "Settings" button on the right side of your screen to access a wide range of customization options. You can tweak the engine, transmission, steering, and even enable driving assists. Fine-tune your vehicle to match your driving style and maximize the fun!
