Vehicles Simulator

How to Play Vehicles Simulator

Welcome to the driver's seat of Vehicles Simulator! Getting started is simple. First, choose one of the three available maps that best suits your driving style—whether you prefer cruising city streets, tackling sandy dunes by the sea, or exploring an area filled with buildings. Once you've picked your playground, select your dream ride from a lineup of seven awesome vehicles, including a city bus, sports car, monster truck, and even a tank!

Use the standard WASD or Arrow Keys to steer your vehicle. The Spacebar acts as the handbrake for sharp stops and drifts. To get a better view of your surroundings, press the C key to cycle through different camera angles. There are no rules or missions—just pure, unadulterated driving freedom. Explore every corner of the map at your own pace.

Take your experience to the next level by customizing your vehicle. Click on the "Settings" section on the right side of the screen to fine-tune your car's performance. You can adjust the engine, transmission, steering, and even activate driving assists to match your preferences. These adjustments can completely change how your vehicle handles, adding a new layer of fun to the game.

Ready for some heavy-duty action? Hop into the driver's seat of the military 8x8 or the tank for a truly unique experience. If you choose the truck, you can even haul a trailer. Simply drive the truck close to the trailer and press the T key to attach it. Now you're ready to hit the road with your massive rig!
