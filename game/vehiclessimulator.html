Vehicles Simulator

How to Play Vehicles Simulator

Welcome to Vehicles Simulator! Getting started is easy. First, select your preferred vehicle from a diverse lineup that includes everything from a city bus and sports car to a massive tank and a rugged monster truck.

Once you've chosen your ride, pick one of the three available maps to explore. Whether you prefer cruising through city streets, tackling sandy dunes by the sea, or finding open spaces for stunts, there's a map for you. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your vehicle.

For a truly personalized experience, dive into the "Settings" menu on the right side of the screen. Here, you can fine-tune your vehicle's performance by adjusting the engine, transmission, steering, and even activate driving assists to match your playstyle.

Want to try something unique? Hop into the truck, drive up to the detached trailer, and press the 'T' key to attach it for a hauling challenge. Or, cause some chaos by driving the tank or 8x8 military vehicle through the city! With no rules or objectives, you're free to explore and enjoy the drive however you want.
