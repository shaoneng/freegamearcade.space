Vehicles Simulator

How to Play Vehicles Simulator

Welcome to the ultimate driving sandbox! In Vehicles Simulator, your goal is to simply have fun. Begin by selecting your desired map to drive in. You can choose between cruising over sandy hills by the sea, navigating busy city streets, or exploring an area filled with various buildings and obstacles. There are no rules or missions, giving you complete freedom.

Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your vehicle. Press the Spacebar for the handbrake to make sharp turns or quick stops. You can switch between seven incredible vehicles at any time, including a city bus, a sleek sports car, a powerful truck, a monster truck, an 8x8 military vehicle, and even a tank! Each one offers a completely different driving experience.

To fine-tune your ride, click on the "Settings" button on the right side of the screen. Here, you can adjust the engine, transmission, steering, and even activate driving assists to match your playstyle. If you choose the truck, you can find and attach a trailer by driving near it and pressing the T key. Experiment with all the vehicles and settings to maximize your driving enjoyment!
