Vehicles Simulator

How to Play Vehicles Simulator

Vehicles Simulator offers an open-world driving experience. Begin by selecting one of three distinct maps: a scenic beach area, a detailed city environment, or an expansive area with various buildings. Each map provides unique challenges and opportunities for exploration.

Next, choose your preferred vehicle from a diverse garage of seven options. You can drive anything from a nimble sedan, a sporty car, a sturdy city bus, or a powerful monster truck, to heavy-duty vehicles like a truck, a tank, or a military 8x8. Experiment with different vehicles to see how they handle across the varied terrain.

Once you're in the driver's seat, use the standard driving controls (usually WASD or arrow keys) to navigate. If you choose the truck, you can attach a trailer by driving near it and pressing the 'T' key. Don't forget to explore the "Settings" section on the right side of the screen. Here, you can fine-tune your driving experience by adjusting the engine, transmission, and steering, or even activate various driving assists to match your play style. Enjoy unrestricted driving freedom!
