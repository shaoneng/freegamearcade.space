How to Play Vehicles Simulator

Welcome to the ultimate driving sandbox! Getting started in Vehicles Simulator is easy. First, choose from one of the three available maps to set your scene. Whether you prefer driving through city streets, a sandy beach, or an open area with buildings, the choice is yours.

Next, select your ride! You have access to seven awesome vehicles, from a standard sedan and sports car to a city bus, monster truck, and even a military 8x8 and a tank. Use the on-screen arrows or menu to cycle through your options.

Once you're in the game, use the WASD or Arrow Keys to drive. For an even more personalized experience, click on the "Settings" menu on the right side of the screen. Here you can adjust your vehicle's engine, transmission, steering, and activate driving assists. There are no rules or missions—just pure driving freedom. Explore, drift, and enjoy the open world!
