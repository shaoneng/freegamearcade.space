Vehicles Simulator

How to Play Vehicles Simulator

Welcome to Vehicles Simulator, where the open road is your playground! There are no missions, timers, or restrictions—just pure driving freedom. Your goal is to explore the vast maps, test out different vehicles, and enjoy a realistic driving experience at your own pace.

To begin, select your desired map. You can choose from a coastal area with sandy hills perfect for off-roading, a dense city environment, or an open area full of buildings for stunting. After picking a map, choose your vehicle. You have access to seven awesome options: a city bus, sedan, sports car, truck, a powerful tank, a military 8x8, and a monster truck.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to accelerate, brake, and steer. The best part of the game is the customization! Click the "Settings" button on the right side of the screen to adjust your vehicle’s engine, transmission, steering, and even activate driving assists to tailor the handling to your personal preference. If you're driving the truck, you can approach a trailer and press the 'T' key to attach it.
