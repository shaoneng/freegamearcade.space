How to Play Vehicles Simulator

Welcome to Vehicles Simulator, where the road is yours to command! Getting started is simple. First, select one of the three available maps to begin your driving adventure. Choose between a coastal area with sandy hills, a detailed city environment, or a wide-open space filled with buildings for you to explore.

Next, pick your ride! You have an incredible selection of seven vehicles: a city bus, a classic sedan, a speedy sports car, a heavy-duty truck, a powerful tank, a rugged military 8x8, and a massive monster truck. There are no rules or objectivesâ€”just pure driving freedom to do whatever you want!

For an even more personalized experience, click the "Settings" button on the right side of the screen. Here, you can fine-tune your vehicle's performance by adjusting the engine, transmission, and steering. You can also toggle driving assists to match your playstyle. If you choose the truck, you can even attach a trailer by driving near it and pressing the 'T' key. Get behind the wheel and enjoy the ride!
