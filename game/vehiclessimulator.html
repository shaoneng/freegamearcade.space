How to Play Vehicles Simulator

Welcome to Vehicles Simulator! Getting started is simple. First, select one of the three available maps to begin your driving adventure. Choose between a sprawling city, a sandy beach area, or a zone filled with buildings perfect for stunts.

Once you're in the game, you can switch between seven unique vehicles at any time. Your options range from a standard sedan and a sports car to a city bus, a monster truck, a heavy-duty truck with a trailer, a military 8x8, and even a tank! Drive freely, explore the environment, and perform stunts. There are no missions or restrictionsâ€”just pure driving enjoyment.

To truly make the game your own, click on the "Settings" menu on the right side of the screen. Here you can customize your vehicle's performance by adjusting the engine, transmission, steering, and even activating driving assists. For the large truck, you can attach its trailer by driving close to it and pressing the 'T' key.
