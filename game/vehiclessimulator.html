Vehicles Simulator

How to Play Vehicles Simulator

Welcome to Vehicles Simulator, a world where you have the ultimate freedom to drive! To begin, select your desired map. Choose between rolling hills by the sea, busy city streets, or a wide-open area with buildings for stunting. After picking your playground, choose from a fantastic lineup of seven unique vehicles. Your options range from a standard sedan and a sports car to a massive city bus, a monster truck, a heavy-duty truck, and even a military 8x8 and a tank!

Once you're in the game, the world is your oyster. There are no missions or restrictions, just pure driving enjoyment. You can customize your vehicle's performance by clicking on the "Settings" section on the right. Here, you can adjust the engine power, transmission, steering, and even activate driving assists to tailor the experience to your liking. This level of control enhances the fun and allows you to create the perfect ride.

Want to try something wild? Hop into the tank or the military 8x8 and see what it's like to command such a massive machine through the city. If you choose the truck, you can even attach a trailer. Simply drive up to it and press the 'T' key to connect it. Get ready to explore, perform stunts, and enjoy the open road!
