Vehicles Simulator

How to Play Vehicles Simulator

The goal in Vehicles Simulator is pure driving freedom. There are no missions, timers, or restrictionsâ€”just open worlds to explore. Start by selecting your favorite vehicle from a diverse lineup that includes a city bus, sedan, sports car, truck, tank, military 8x8, and a monster truck.

Once you've chosen your ride, pick one of the three available maps. You can cruise through vast city streets, tackle sandy hills by the sea, or test your vehicle's performance in a special area filled with ramps and buildings. You are free to drive wherever you want and discover what each map has to offer.

For a personalized experience, customize your vehicle's performance by clicking the "Settings" section on the right side of the screen. Here, you can adjust the engine, transmission, steering, and even activate driving assists. Use your keyboard to control the vehicle. If you're driving the truck with a trailer, simply drive near the trailer and press the 'T' key to attach it.
