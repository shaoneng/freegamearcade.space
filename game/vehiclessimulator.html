Vehicles Simulator

How to Play Vehicles Simulator

Welcome to the driver's seat! Getting started is simple. First, select one of the three available maps to begin your adventure. Choose between a hilly seaside landscape, a sprawling city, or an open area filled with buildings perfect for stunting. You have complete freedom to drive wherever and however you want—no rules, no missions, just pure driving enjoyment.

Once you're in the game, you can switch between seven awesome vehicles at any time. The lineup includes a city bus, a sedan, a sports car, a truck, a monster truck, a military 8x8, and even a tank! For an enhanced experience, click the "Settings" button on the right to customize your vehicle's engine, transmission, steering, and driving assists. Fine-tuning your ride makes the experience even more fun.

Ready for some heavy machinery? Take control of the tank or the military 8x8 for some serious fun. You can even haul cargo with the truck! Simply drive the truck close to the trailer and press the 'T' key to attach it. The open world is your playground, so get creative and see what you can do!
