Vehicles Simulator

How to Play Vehicles Simulator

Welcome to Vehicles Simulator! This game gives you complete freedom to get behind the wheel of seven incredible vehicles. Choose your favorite car and explore one of three unique maps at your own pace. There are no rules, timers, or missionsâ€”just pure, unadulterated driving enjoyment.

Begin by selecting your ride from an impressive lineup that includes a city bus, a sleek sports car, a rugged truck, a powerful monster truck, and even a military 8x8 and a tank! After picking your vehicle, choose your environment. You can cruise through a bustling city, tackle sandy hills by the sea, or explore a vast area filled with buildings and ramps for stunts.

Use the standard WASD or Arrow Keys to drive your vehicle. For special actions, such as attaching a trailer to your truck, approach the trailer and press the 'T' key. Don't forget to dive into the "Settings" menu! Here, you can fine-tune your vehicle's performance by adjusting the engine, transmission, steering, and even activate driving assists to tailor the experience perfectly to your driving style.
