Vex 3

How to Play Vex 3

Your primary goal in Vex 3 is to navigate your stickman character through a series of hazardous levels, known as "Acts", and reach the green portal at the very end. This fast-paced platformer demands precision, timing, and quick reflexes to overcome its many challenges.

Control your stickman using the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard. Use the up arrow or 'W' to jump, and the down arrow or 'S' to slide or crouch under obstacles. Mastering these basic movements is crucial for navigating the intricate and dangerous environments.

The world of Vex 3 is filled with deadly traps. Pay close attention to the color scheme: red elements like spikes, saw blades, and smashing blocks are dangerous and will send you back to your last checkpoint. Black areas are generally safe. Use walls to jump off of and ropes to slide down to traverse the complex levels.

Don't worry about starting over completely if you get caught. Red flags scattered throughout each act serve as checkpoints. When you touch one, it becomes your new respawn point. Prepare for a challenge, as the game features 10 standard acts, 9 even harder challenge acts, and a "Hard Mode" for true masters.
