Vex 3

How to Play Vex 3

Welcome to the challenging world of Vex 3! Your goal in this fast-paced stickman platformer is to guide your character safely through a series of obstacle-filled levels, known as "Acts". The ultimate objective is to reach the green portal at the end of each Act to progress.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to run, jump, and slide. Navigate through deadly environments filled with hazards like spinning saw blades, sharp spikes, and falling blocks. Timing and precision are crucial to survive. You'll need to jump over gaps, slide under obstacles, and climb walls to make your way through each course.

Don't worry if you get caught in a trap! The game features helpful checkpoints marked by red flags. When you die, you will respawn at the last checkpoint you activated. This allows you to tackle difficult sections repeatedly without starting the entire level over. Keep an eye on your time and aim for trophies by completing acts as quickly as possible!
