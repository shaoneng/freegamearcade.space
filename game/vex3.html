Vex 3

How to Play Vex 3

Your objective in Vex 3 is to guide your stickman character through a series of challenging levels, known as "Acts." Each Act is filled with dangerous traps like spikes, saw blades, and crumbling blocks. Navigate this perilous environment to reach the green portal at the end to complete the level.

Use the Arrow keys or WASD keys on your computer to control your stickman. Press the Up Arrow or 'W' key to jump. You can perform a wall jump by jumping towards a wall and then pressing the jump key again to scale vertical surfaces. To slide under tight spaces, press the Down Arrow or 'S' key while running.

Throughout each level, you will find red flags that act as checkpoints. When you touch one, it becomes your new respawn point if you get caught in a trap.
