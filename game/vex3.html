Vex 3

How to Play Vex 3

Your primary goal in Vex 3 is to guide your stickman character to the green portal at the end of each stage, known as an 'Act'. The path is filled with a variety of dangerous obstacles designed to test your timing and agility.

Navigate treacherous environments filled with spikes, spinning saw blades, and falling blocks. Use your platforming skills to run, jump, slide, and climb to avoid these hazards. Red areas signal danger, while black zones are typically safe. If you get caught in a trap, you'll respawn at the last red checkpoint flag you touched.

The game features ten standard acts to complete. As you master them, you can take on nine additional 'Challenge Acts' for an even tougher test. For the ultimate trial, earning a Gold Trophy on an act unlocks its 'Hard Mode', which introduces even more perilous obstacles.
