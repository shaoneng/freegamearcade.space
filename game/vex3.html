Vex 3

How to Play Vex 3

Welcome to the intense world of Vex 3! Your goal is simple but challenging: guide your stickman character to the green portal at the end of each level, known as an "Act".

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or the WASD keys to move left, right, jump, and slide. Navigate through a landscape filled with deadly traps like spinning saw blades, sharp spikes, and falling blocks. Anything colored red is dangerous, so be careful!

Along the way, you'll find red flags that act as checkpoints. If you get caught in a trap, you'll respawn at the last flag you touched, saving your progress. Master your timing and reflexes to complete all ten standard Acts and take on the nine extra-difficult challenge stages for the ultimate test of skill.
