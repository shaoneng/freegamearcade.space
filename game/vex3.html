Vex 3

How to Play Vex 3

Your primary objective in Vex 3 is to guide your stickman character through a series of challenging levels, known as 'Acts'. To successfully complete an Act, you must navigate a dangerous environment and reach the green portal at the very end.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys (or WASD) to run, jump, and slide. On mobile devices, use the on-screen controls. Mastering the timing of your movements is crucial for overcoming the game's many obstacles.

The levels are filled with deadly traps highlighted in red, such as spikes, circular saws, and falling blocks. Avoid these at all costs! If you touch a trap, your character will be sent back to the last red checkpoint flag you activated.

As you progress, you'll need to perform more advanced maneuvers like wall-jumping, swimming, and using zip lines to advance. Strive to complete each Act as quickly as possible to earn trophies and unlock the even more difficult 'Hard Mode' for the ultimate test of your platforming skills.
