Vex 3

How to Play Vex 3

Your primary goal in Vex 3 is to guide your stickman character through a series of challenging levels, known as 'Acts'. Each act is filled with dangerous obstacles, and your objective is to navigate past them to reach the green portal at the very end.

Use the arrow keys or WASD keys on your keyboard to control your character. The Up arrow key or 'W' allows you to jump, while the Left and Right arrow keys or 'A' and 'D' move you horizontally. Press the Down arrow key or 'S' to slide or crouch under obstacles. You can also swim, climb walls, and hang from poles to traverse the environment.

The world of Vex is perilous. You'll encounter deadly spikes, spinning saw blades, falling blocks, and other traps designed to stop you. If you get hit, you'll respawn at the last red checkpoint flag you touched. Timing, precision, and quick reflexes are essential to survive and conquer each act. Pay attention to the red-colored traps, which signal danger, and aim for fast completion times to earn trophies!
