Vex 3

How to Play Vex 3

In Vex 3, your primary goal is to guide your stickman character through a series of challenging levels, known as "Acts." The objective in each Act is to reach the green portal at the end, overcoming a maze of deadly traps and obstacles.

Control your character using the Arrow Keys or WASD keys on your keyboard. Use the up arrow or 'W' to jump, and the left/right arrows or 'A'/'D' to move horizontally. You can also slide under tight spaces by pressing the down arrow or 'S' while running, and climb walls by repeatedly jumping against them.

The world of Vex is dangerous. Watch out for red-colored zones, which indicate hazards like spikes, saw blades, and smashing blocks. If you get caught in a trap, you'll respawn at the last checkpoint you touched, marked by a red flag. Don't be discouraged if you die multiple times; the game is designed to test your persistence!

As you progress, aim to complete levels quickly to earn trophies. Unlocking all trophies for an act will grant you access to its "Hard Mode," offering an even greater challenge for true Vex masters. Good luck!
