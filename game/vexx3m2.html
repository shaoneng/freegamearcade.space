Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

Your goal in Vex X3M 2 is to guide your stickman on a motorcycle through a series of treacherous levels as quickly as possible. The game combines the parkour elements of the Vex series with the bike stunt action of Moto X3M. Navigate through a gauntlet of deadly traps, including spikes, saws, explosives, and moving platforms, to reach the finish line.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your motorcycle. The Up/W key accelerates, Down/S brakes or reverses, and the Left/A and Right/D keys are used to lean back and forward. Leaning is crucial for maintaining balance in the air and performing flips. A stable landing is key to keeping your speed and momentum.

While speed is important for a good score, it's wise to approach each level with caution on your first attempt. Take time to observe the patterns of moving traps and plan your route. Make use of new mechanics like kite gliders and teleportation portals to overcome massive gaps and complex obstacles.
