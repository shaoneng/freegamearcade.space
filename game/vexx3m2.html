Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

Vex X3M 2 blends the fast-paced parkour of the Vex series with the thrilling stunts of Moto X3M. Your goal is to navigate your character on a motorcycle through a series of treacherous levels filled with traps like spikes, saws, and explosives. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to accelerate, brake, and balance your bike as you race towards the finish line.

Success in this game is about more than just speed. Take time on your first run to observe the patterns of moving traps and the layout of the course. Rushing blindly will likely lead to many restarts. Each level is dotted with checkpoint flags, so if you crash, you can quickly get back into the action without starting over completely.

This installment introduces exciting new mechanics like kite gliders and teleportation portals. Spend a moment getting used to how these tools work, as mastering them is key to overcoming some of the game's biggest challenges. The faster you complete each level, the higher your score and star rating will be, so once you've learned the layout, hit the gas and perform epic flips to prove your skills!
