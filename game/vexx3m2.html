Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

Welcome to the high-octane world of Vex X3M 2! The goal is to guide your stickman on his motorcycle through a series of increasingly difficult levels, reaching the finish line as quickly as possible. Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your bike: press the Up Arrow or 'W' to accelerate, the Down Arrow or 'S' to brake, and the Left/Right Arrows or 'A'/'D' to lean back and forth. Leaning is crucial for landing jumps and performing flips!

Each level is a gauntlet of deadly traps, from spinning saws and sharp spikes to explosive mines and crumbling platforms. You'll need sharp reflexes to dodge these obstacles. A single mistake will send you back to the last checkpoint you passed, so keep an eye out for those checkpoint flags!

This sequel introduces exciting new mechanics like kite gliding and teleportation portals. Learn to master these tools to cross huge gaps and navigate complex sections of the track. While it's tempting to speed through, it's often wise to observe the pattern of moving traps before you proceed. The faster you complete an act, the higher your score and star rating will be, so find the perfect balance between speed and caution.
