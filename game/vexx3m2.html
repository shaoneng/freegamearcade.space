Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

Your goal in Vex X3M 2 is to race your motorcycle through a series of treacherous levels as quickly as possible. Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to accelerate, brake, and balance your bike. Navigate through a gauntlet of familiar Vex traps, including deadly spikes, spinning saws, and explosives. Your objective is to reach the finish line in one piece, hitting checkpoints along the way to save your progress.

This game introduces exciting new mechanics to master. Look for special pads that launch you into the air with a kite glider, allowing you to soar over massive gaps. You'll also encounter teleportation portals that instantly transport you to different parts of the level. Learning how to control these new tools is key to overcoming the toughest challenges and finding the fastest routes.

Rushing through a level on your first attempt is a recipe for disaster. Take your time to observe the patterns of moving traps and platforms. Timing is everything. Don't be afraid to fail; restarting from a checkpoint is quick and lets you apply what you've learned. The faster you complete a level, the higher your score and star rating will be, so practice your runs to achieve mastery!
