Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

The goal in Vex X3M 2 is to race your motorcycle through a series of obstacle-packed levels as fast as you can. It's a high-speed fusion of parkour and bike stunts, where precision and timing are everything.

Use your keyboard to control your bike's movement. You'll need to accelerate, brake, and balance your bike to jump over massive gaps, navigate moving platforms, and avoid deadly traps like spikes and explosives. One wrong move will send you back to the last checkpoint flag you passed.

Don't just speed through on your first try. Take a moment to observe the patterns of moving traps and plan your route. Make use of special mechanics like kite gliders and teleporters, but be sure to practice with them first as they can be tricky to control. The faster you finish a level, the better your star rating will be, so once you've mastered a track, go for speed!
