Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

Vex X3M 2 combines the thrilling parkour of Vex with the high-octane stunts of Moto X3M. Your mission is to navigate your stickman on a turbocharged motorcycle through a series of increasingly difficult levels. Each track is packed with deadly obstacles like spinning saws, sharp spikes, explosives, and shifting platforms. The goal is to reach the finish line as quickly as possible, using checkpoints along the way to save your progress.

Control your bike using the arrow keys or WASD to accelerate, brake, and lean forward or backward. Mastering your balance is crucial for landing jumps and performing flips. This sequel introduces exciting new mechanics to help you traverse the treacherous courses. Use kite gliders to soar across massive gaps and step through teleportation portals to instantly transport across the map. Learning to use these tools effectively is key to mastering the game.

This is a game of skill and precision. Rushing through a level on your first try is a recipe for disaster. Take your time to observe the patterns of moving traps and plan your route. Restart quickly from your last checkpoint and apply what you've learned to overcome the challenge. The faster you complete each level, the higher your star rating will be!
