Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

The goal in Vex X3M 2 is to race your stickman on a motorcycle to the end of each level as fast as possible. Use your arrow keys or WASD to control your bike: accelerate, brake, and lean forward or backward to maintain balance. The objective is to navigate a series of dangerous obstacles, from sharp spikes and saws to explosive devices and moving platforms.

This is a game of skill and precision. Rushing through a level on your first attempt is a recipe for disaster. Take your time to observe the patterns of moving traps and plan your route. Checkpoints, marked by flags, will save your progress, so you won't have to start from the very beginning after a crash. Learning the layout of each stage is key to achieving a fast time.

Vex X3M 2 introduces exciting new mechanics to master. You'll encounter special pads that launch you into the air with a kite glider, allowing you to soar over massive gaps. You'll also find teleportation portals that instantly transport you to another part of the track. Get comfortable with these tools, as they are essential for completing the more complex levels and finding the quickest path to victory.
