Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

Welcome to Vex X3M 2, where the slick parkour madness of Vex meets the extreme bike stunts of Moto X3M. If you thought dodging spikes on foot was intense, wait until you try it on a motorcycle! This parkour racing game combines tight controls and tricky traps with a turbocharged bike, creating a wild, action-packed ride.

Race through obstacle-packed tracks, soaring over gaps and flipping through moving platforms. Master new mechanics like kite gliding and teleportation portals to conquer each level. But be warned—this game requires serious skill. One wrong move sends you back to the last checkpoint flag.

For a better score, try to learn the levels first. In parkour games like this, it's a bad idea to speed through on your first try. Take a moment to study the traps and their timing; most follow predictable patterns. Getting the rhythm down makes dodging much easier. When you do crash, restart quickly and try again. The faster you finish, the better your star rating!

Created by Azerion Casual Games, Vex X3M 2 is the perfect hybrid for players who love action platformers and stunt challenges. If you enjoy flipping over danger, check out similar titles like Moto X3M Online and Vex 7!
