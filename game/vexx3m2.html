Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

Vex X3M 2 combines the thrilling parkour of the Vex series with the extreme bike stunts of Moto X3M. Your goal is to navigate your stickman on a motorcycle through a series of obstacle-packed levels as quickly as possible. Use your arrow keys or WASD to control your bike's movement, acceleration, and balance.

Each track is filled with deadly traps, including spikes, falling platforms, explosives, and moving saws. Timing is everything! Take a moment to observe the patterns of moving obstacles before you speed through. One wrong move will send you back to the last checkpoint flag you passed.

This sequel introduces exciting new mechanics like kite gliding and teleportation portals. Learn to use these tools to soar across huge gaps and navigate complex sections of the course.
