Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

Your goal in Vex X3M 2 is to guide your stickman rider through a series of dangerous levels as quickly as possible. Use the arrow keys or WASD to accelerate, brake, and balance your bike. Navigate through a gauntlet of deadly traps, including spikes, falling platforms, and explosive devices.

This isn't a game you can just speed through on your first attempt. Take a moment to observe the patterns of moving obstacles and traps before you proceed. A little patience will save you from countless restarts. Timing is everything, so pay close attention to the rhythm of the level to dodge traps successfully.

Look out for special mechanics that can help you traverse the course. Use kite gliders to soar over massive gaps and step into teleportation portals to instantly transport across the map. Mastering these tools is key to setting record times and overcoming the toughest challenges.

Don't worry if you crash! The game features frequent checkpoint flags. When you hit a trap, you'll restart from the last checkpoint you passed, allowing you to quickly get back into the action. The faster you finish, the better your star rating will be, so keep trying for that perfect run!
