Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

The objective in Vex X3M 2 is to guide your stickman on a motorcycle through a series of challenging obstacle courses. Use your keyboard controls to accelerate, brake, and balance your bike as you navigate through deadly traps like spikes, explosives, and rolling platforms.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your motorcycle. Press **Up Arrow / W** to accelerate forward and **Down Arrow / S** to brake or reverse. Use the **Left Arrow / A** and **Right Arrow / D** keys to lean your rider back and forward. This is crucial for landing flips and maintaining balance after big jumps.

Success in this game requires a mix of speed and caution. On your first attempt, take a moment to study the patterns of moving traps and falling platforms. Checkpoints are marked with flags; make sure to touch them to save your progress, so you don't have to restart the entire level if you crash.

Master the new mechanics like kite gliding and teleportation portals. These can help you cross huge gaps and bypass tricky sections, but they require practice to control effectively. Perform flips and stunts to shave seconds off your time and achieve the best possible score!
