Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

Vex X3M 2 is an action-packed fusion of parkour and motorcycle stunts. Your goal is to guide your rider through a series of challenging levels filled with deadly traps, massive gaps, and dynamic obstacles. Control your motorcycle's acceleration, braking, and balance to navigate the treacherous terrain.

Speed is essential, but so is strategy! On your first attempt, take a moment to observe the patterns of moving saws, falling platforms, and other hazards. Timing your movements is key to survival. Use the new mechanics like kite gliding to soar over large chasms and teleportation portals to instantly warp across the map. These tools are powerful but require practice to master.

Don't be discouraged by crashes! Checkpoints are scattered throughout each level, allowing you to quickly restart without losing too much progress. The faster you complete a level, the higher your star rating will be. Master the course, beat the clock, and unlock new cosmetic items to show off your skills!
