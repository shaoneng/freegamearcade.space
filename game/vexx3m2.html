How to Play Vex X3M 2

Vex X3M 2 takes the classic Vex stickman and puts him on a turbocharged motorcycle! Your goal is to navigate through a series of treacherous levels packed with deadly traps. Use your arrow keys or WASD to control your bike's acceleration, braking, and balance as you race toward the finish line.

This is a game of skill and timing, not just speed. Rushing through a level on your first attempt is a recipe for disaster. Take a moment to observe the patterns of moving saws, falling platforms, and shooting cannons. Checkpoint flags will save your progress, so don't be afraid to take risks, but always be prepared to learn from your crashes.

Mastering the new mechanics is key to success. You'll encounter kite gliders to soar across huge gaps and teleportation portals that will warp you across the map. Spend some time getting used to how these tools workâ€”they can be tricky at first but are essential for clearing the toughest stages.

Your ultimate objective is to finish each level as quickly as possible. The faster you are, the better your star rating will be. So, once you've learned the layout, hit the gas, perform awesome flips, and speed your way to victory!
