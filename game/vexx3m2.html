How to Play Vex X3M 2

Welcome to the high-octane world of Vex X3M 2! The goal is to navigate your stickman on a motorcycle through treacherous levels packed with deadly traps. Use your arrow keys or on-screen controls to accelerate, brake, and balance your bike. Your objective is to reach the final checkpoint in each stage as quickly as possible.

This isn't just a race; it's a test of skill and precision. You'll need to perform flips and stunts to navigate moving platforms, dodge spikes, and survive explosions. The game introduces new mechanics like kite gliders to soar over huge gaps and teleportation portals that add a layer of strategy. Mastering these tools is crucial for overcoming the most difficult challenges.

Don't rush through the levels on your first try! Take time to observe the patterns of moving traps and plan your route. Checkpoint flags will save your progress, so don't be afraid to fail. A quick restart is all it takes to get back in the action. The faster you finish, the better your score, so once you've learned the layout, hit the gas and aim for a perfect run!
