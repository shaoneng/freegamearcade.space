How to Play Vex X3M 2

Welcome to the ultimate test of speed and skill! In Vex X3M 2, you're not just runningâ€”you're riding a powerful motorcycle through a gauntlet of deadly traps. The goal is to reach the end of each level as fast as you can. Use your keyboard controls to accelerate, brake, and balance your bike to perform flips and navigate the treacherous terrain.

Each level introduces new challenges, from classic Vex obstacles like spikes and saws to dynamic elements like falling platforms and moving walls. This sequel adds exciting new mechanics like kite gliders and teleportation portals, which you'll need to master to clear huge gaps and complex sections. Don't rush on your first attempt; take time to learn the layout and the timing of the traps to avoid constant restarts.

Checkpoints are your best friend! Hitting a checkpoint flag saves your progress, so you won't have to start from the beginning after a crash. While crashing is part of the game, restarting quickly is key to achieving a high score. The faster you complete a level, the better your star rating will be. So, buckle up, stay sharp, and get ready for a wild ride!
