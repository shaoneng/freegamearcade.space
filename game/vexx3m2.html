How to Play Vex X3M 2

Welcome to the high-speed chaos of Vex X3M 2! In this game, you control a stickman on a powerful motorcycle, racing through deadly obstacle courses. The goal is to reach the finish line as quickly as possible, navigating everything from spikes and saws to explosives and moving platforms. It's a thrilling mashup of classic Vex parkour and Moto X3M bike stunts.

Use your keyboard's arrow keys or WASD to control your bike. The up/W key accelerates, the down/S key brakes, and the left/A and right/D keys are used to lean your bike forward and backward. Balancing is crucial for landing jumps and performing flips without crashing!

Patience is key! Don't rush through a level on your first attempt. Take time to observe the patterns of moving traps and platforms. Look for checkpoint flags, as they will save your progress. Master the new mechanics like kite gliders and teleportation portals to cross huge gaps and bypass tricky sections.
