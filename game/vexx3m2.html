How to Play Vex X3M 2

Welcome to the high-octane world of Vex X3M 2! In this game, you take control of the iconic Vex stickman, but this time, he's on a powerful motorcycle. Your goal is to race through a series of increasingly difficult levels packed with deadly traps, massive jumps, and mind-bending obstacles. Use your skill and reflexes to navigate each course and reach the finish line as quickly as possible.

The controls are intuitive and responsive. Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your bike: press the Up/W key to accelerate, the Down/S key to brake, and the Left/A and Right/D keys to lean back and forward. Balancing your bike is crucial for landing jumps perfectly and navigating tricky inclines and declines.

Each level introduces new challenges, from classic spikes and saws to dynamic elements like teleportation portals and kite gliders. Don't be discouraged if you crash; checkpoints are scattered throughout each stage, allowing you to restart without losing all your progress. For the best score, try to memorize trap patterns and find the fastest route, but on your first attempt, it's wise to proceed with caution to learn the layout.
