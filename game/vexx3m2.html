Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

Vex X3M 2 blends the slick parkour madness of Vex with the extreme bike stunts of Moto X3M. Prepare to take on familiar Vex challenges—spikes, explosives, and rolling platforms—but this time, on a turbocharged motorcycle. This parkour racing game introduces new mechanics like kite gliding and teleportation portals, turning each level into a wild, action-packed ride.

This is a skill game that rewards precision and patience. It's best to avoid speeding through a level on your first attempt. Instead, take a moment to study the layout of traps and obstacles. Pay close attention to the rhythm of moving parts, much like in Geometry Dash, as they often follow predictable patterns. Mastering new tools like gliders and teleporters is key, even if they're tricky at first.

Don't be afraid to take risks, but be ready for the unexpected. Platforms will fall, walls will move, and one wrong move will send you back to the last checkpoint flag. If you crash, don't worry! Quickly restart and try again. The faster you finish, the better your score and star rating will be. As you improve, don't forget to check out unlockable skins to show off your progress and mastery of the course.

Created by Azerion Casual Games, Vex X3M 2 is a fan-favorite hybrid perfect for players who love action platformers and stunt challenges. If you enjoy flipping over danger and racing against the clock, try similar games like Moto X3M Online for classic bike action or Vex 7 for the latest in parkour challenges.
