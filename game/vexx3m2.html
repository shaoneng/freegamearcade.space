Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

Vex X3M 2 blends the high-speed parkour of Vex with the intense bike stunts of Moto X3M. Your mission is to navigate your motorcycle through treacherous tracks packed with spikes, saws, explosives, and moving platforms. This game demands skill, but mastering the controls and conquering the courses is incredibly satisfying.

Don't try to speed through a level on your first attempt. It's a skill game that rewards patience. Take a moment to observe the layout of the traps and obstacles. Understanding the patterns will save you from countless restarts and help you plan your route.

Each level introduces new mechanics like kite gliding and teleportation portals. Spend some time getting used to these tools, as they are key to overcoming certain challenges. Much like in rhythm games, moving traps follow predictable patterns. Learn their timing to dodge them effectively and maintain your momentum.

You will crash, but don't worry! Checkpoint flags are scattered throughout the levels, so you can quickly restart without losing too much progress. The faster you complete a course, the higher your score and star rating will be. So, once you've mastered a section, don't be afraid to hit the gas!
