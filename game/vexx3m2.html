Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

Your goal in Vex X3M 2 is to navigate your motorcycle through a series of obstacle-filled levels as quickly as possible. This game blends the fast-paced parkour of the Vex series with the thrilling stunts of Moto X3M. You'll need to jump over gaps, dodge deadly traps like spikes and saws, and perform flips to stay in control.

Use your arrow keys or WASD to accelerate, brake, and balance your bike. Timing is everything. Pay close attention to the patterns of moving platforms and traps. This sequel introduces new mechanics like kite gliders and teleportation portals. Take a moment to learn how they work, as mastering them is essential for getting through the tougher stages.

Don't rush through a level on your first attempt. It's better to observe the layout and learn the timing of the obstacles. Checkpoint flags will save your progress, so don't be afraid to take risks. The faster you complete each act, the better your star rating will be. Aim for a perfect run to unlock new skins and show off your skills!
