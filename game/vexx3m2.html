How to Play Vex X3M 2

Welcome to the high-octane world of Vex X3M 2! The goal is to guide your character on a motorcycle through a series of challenging obstacle courses and reach the finish line as fast as possible. This game blends the precise platforming of the Vex series with the thrilling stunts of Moto X3M.

Use your keyboard controls (typically WASD or Arrow Keys) to accelerate, brake, and balance your bike. You'll need to navigate past deadly traps like spinning saws, spikes, explosives, and falling platforms. Timing is crucial! Watch the patterns of moving obstacles before you make your move. Hitting a trap will send you back to the last checkpoint flag you passed.

Look out for new and exciting mechanics! Some levels feature kite gliders to soar across huge gaps or teleportation portals that will instantly warp you to another part of the course. Don't be afraid to perform flips and stunts in the air for a better score, but always aim for a smooth landing.
