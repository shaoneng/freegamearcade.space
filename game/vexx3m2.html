How to Play Vex X3M 2

Welcome to the high-speed chaos of Vex X3M 2! The objective is to guide your stickman on a motorcycle through a series of intense obstacle courses. Navigate through deadly traps like spikes, saws, explosives, and moving platforms to reach the finish line as quickly as possible.

Use your controls to accelerate, brake, and flip your bike to maintain balance and avoid crashing. This game is a hybrid of the Vex and Moto X3M series, so expect a blend of slick parkour and extreme bike stunts. One wrong move will send you back to the last checkpoint flag you passed, so timing and precision are crucial.

Pay close attention to new mechanics introduced in the levels, such as kite gliders for soaring across large gaps and teleportation portals for instant travel.
