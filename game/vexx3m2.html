Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

Your goal in Vex X3M 2 is to race your motorcycle through a series of increasingly difficult obstacle courses. You'll need to dodge deadly traps like spinning saws, spikes, explosives, and collapsing platforms to reach the end of each act.

Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your bike. The Up Arrow or 'W' key accelerates you forward, while the Down Arrow or 'S' key applies the brakes or reverses. Use the Left and Right Arrow keys (or 'A' and 'D') to lean your character, which is essential for balancing your bike during jumps, performing flips, and landing safely.

Patience is key! Don't try to speed through a level on your first try. Take a moment to study the layout and the timing of moving obstacles. Checkpoint flags will save your progress, so you can restart from your last flag if you crash. Keep an eye out for special game mechanics like gliders and teleporters, as they are often required to clear massive gaps or complex sections of the track.
