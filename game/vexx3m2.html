Vex X3M 2

How to Play Vex X3M 2

Get ready for Vex X3M 2, the insane hybrid where the parkour action of Vex collides with the high-octane bike stunts of Moto X3M! If you thought dodging saws and spikes on foot was a challenge, try doing it on a turbocharged motorcycle. This game amps up the classic Vex formula with deadly traps, explosive hazards, and brand-new mechanics like kite gliding and teleportation portals, turning every level into an extreme test of skill.

Your goal is to race your bike through a gauntlet of obstacle-filled tracks. You'll need to soar over massive gaps, perform insane flips, and navigate through moving traps and collapsing platforms. Be warned: this game demands precision and quick reflexes. One wrong move will send you flying back to the last checkpoint flag you passed. But don't be afraid to take risks—speed and daring are rewarded!

A smart player is a patient player. On your first attempt at a level, take your time to observe the layout of the traps and the timing of moving obstacles. Just like in games like Geometry Dash, most traps follow a predictable pattern. Once you learn the rhythm, dodging them becomes much more manageable. Get comfortable with special tools like gliders and teleporters; mastering them is key to overcoming the toughest challenges.

Crashing is part of the fun! When you wipe out, just quickly restart from the last checkpoint and give it another shot. The faster you can complete a level, the higher your score and star rating will be. And don't forget to check out the unlockable skins to show off your hard-earned progress and mastery of the courses.
