Voxiom

How to Play Voxiom

Welcome to the blocky battlegrounds of Voxiom! This game combines first-person shooting with sandbox crafting, where your main goal is to survive. You will start with basic gear: a Shovel for digging and building, a Combat Assault Rifle, and a Strike Pistol for defense. Gather resources from the environment to craft better items and build protective structures.

Use the standard WASD keys to move, Spacebar to jump, and your mouse to aim and shoot. Press 'E' to open your inventory and interact with objects like workbenches. Workbenches are crucial for crafting more advanced weapons and tools, giving you a significant advantage in combat. Strategic building can provide essential cover or a high vantage point against your enemies.

Your inventory space is limited, so manage it carefully. You have 5 ammo slots, 5 backpack slots, and 5 hotbar slots for quick access to items. Prioritize essentials like powerful weapons, ammunition, and healing supplies. A well-organized inventory is key to reacting quickly in a firefight and achieving victory.

Voxiom offers several exciting game modes. In Battle Royale, fight to be the last player standing as a toxic fog closes in. Capture the Gems is a team-based objective mode. Survival tests your long-term endurance, while Free for All is a pure deathmatch. Choose your mode and prove your skills!
