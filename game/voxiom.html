Voxiom

How to Play Voxiom

Welcome to the dynamic world of Voxiom, where voxel-based creativity meets intense first-person shooter action! Your primary goal is survival. You'll need to out-build, out-craft, and out-shoot players from all over the world.

You begin each match with a basic set of equipment: a Shovel for digging and breaking blocks, a Combat Assault Rifle, and a Strike Pistol. Use your shovel to gather resources like wood and stone from the environment. These resources are crucial for crafting more advanced items and building defensive structures.

Find a workbench on the map to craft superior weapons, tools, and other essential gear. A better arsenal gives you a significant advantage in combat. However, your inventory space is limited, with 5 ammo slots, 5 backpack slots, and 5 hotbar slots. Managing this space effectively is key to victory, so choose your items wisely and prioritize what you need most for the situation.

Voxiom features several exciting game modes to test your skills:

  • Capture the Gems: Work with your team to capture and hold gems to score points.
  • Battle Royale: A classic last-man-standing mode where you fight to survive in a shrinking toxic fog.
  • Survival: A sandbox mode focused on building, crafting, and surviving against the environment and other players.
  • Free for All: Pure, chaotic combat where it's every player for themselves.

Jump in, adapt to the battlefield, and prove you have what it takes to dominate in Voxiom!
