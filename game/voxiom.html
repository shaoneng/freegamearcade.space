Voxiom

How to Play Voxiom

Welcome to the world of Voxiom, a dynamic game that combines the creative freedom of sandbox games with the fast-paced action of a first-person shooter. Your goal is to survive and thrive by building, crafting, and fighting against other players online.

You'll start each match with basic equipment: a Shovel for digging and building, a Combat Assault Rifle, and a Strike Pistol. Use the shovel to mine blocks and build defenses. To gain an edge, find workbenches scattered across the map to craft more advanced and powerful weapons.

Inventory management is crucial for survival. You have a limited number of slots: 5 for ammo, 5 for your backpack, and 5 for your hotbar. Choose your items wisely, as you can't carry everything. A well-organized inventory with essential items is key to victory.

Voxiom features several exciting game modes to test your skills, including Capture the Gems, Battle Royale, Survival, and Free for All. Each mode offers a unique challenge, but the core objective remains the same: be the last one standing. Defend yourself, outsmart your enemies, and conquer the blocky battlefield!
