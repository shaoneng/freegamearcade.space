Voxiom

How to Play Voxiom

Welcome to the voxel world of Voxiom! The goal is to survive and dominate through a mix of building, crafting, and shooting. Use the standard WASD keys to move around the map, Spacebar to jump, and the mouse to aim and shoot. You can switch between your equipped items using the number keys.

Just like in a sandbox game, you can interact with the world. Use your shovel to dig blocks and gather resources. These resources can be used at a workbench to craft more advanced weapons and tools. You can also place blocks to build defenses, create cover, or reach strategic high ground. Be creative to outsmart your opponents!

You start with a basic Shovel, a Combat Assault Rifle, and a Strike Pistol. To win, you'll need better gear. Pay close attention to your inventory, which is limited to 5 ammo slots, 5 backpack slots, and 5 hotbar slots. Managing your inventory wisely is crucial for victory, so make sure you're only carrying the most essential items for your strategy.

Voxiom offers several exciting game modes to test your skills. Jump into a classic Battle Royale where the last player standing wins, team up in Capture the Gems, see how long you can last in Survival, or go for pure action in Free for All. Each mode offers a unique challenge, but the core objective remains the same: survive and eliminate the competition.
