Voxiom

How to Play Voxiom

Welcome to Voxiom, a dynamic multiplayer game that combines sandbox crafting with intense first-person shooter action. Your main objective is to survive and dominate by building, crafting, and fighting against players from around the world.

You begin each match with basic tools: a Shovel for digging and building, a Combat Assault Rifle, and a Strike Pistol. To gain an edge, you must use workbenches scattered across the map to craft more advanced weapons and items. Scavenging for resources is key to your success.

Inventory management is crucial for victory. You have a limited number of slots: 5 for ammo, 5 for your backpack, and 5 for your hotbar. Choose wisely what you carry, prioritizing essential items that will help you in combat and survival situations.

Voxiom offers several exciting game modes to keep the action fresh. Choose from Capture the Gems, Battle Royale, Survival, or a classic Free for All. Each mode presents unique challenges, but the core goal remains the same: learn to defend yourself and outlast your opponents in this hostile open world.
