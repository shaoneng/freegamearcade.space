Voxiom

How to Play Voxiom

Welcome to Voxiom, an exciting fusion of sandbox creativity and intense first-person shooter action! In this multiplayer world, you'll need to think on your feet, combining strategic building and crafting with sharp shooting skills to outlast your opponents.

You start each match with basic gear: a Shovel for digging and building, a Combat Assault Rifle, and a Strike Pistol. Use your shovel to mine blocks and build defenses. Gather resources to craft more advanced weapons and items at workbenches to gain a tactical advantage in battle.

Inventory management is key to victory. You have a limited number of slots for your hotbar, backpack, and ammo. Choose your loadout carefully, prioritizing the most essential items for your strategy, as you can't carry everything you find.

Voxiom offers several game modes to test your abilities, including the classic Battle Royale, strategic Capture the Gems, challenging Survival, and chaotic Free for All. No matter which mode you choose, your primary goal is to fight, build, and survive against players from all over the globe.
