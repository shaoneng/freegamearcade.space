Voxiom

How to Play Voxiom

Welcome to the blocky battlegrounds of Voxiom! This game is a unique fusion of a creative sandbox and a fast-paced first-person shooter. Your primary goal is to survive, whether you're building a fortress, crafting weapons, or engaging in intense firefights with players from around the world.

You'll start each match with a basic loadout: a Shovel for digging and building, a Combat Assault Rifle, and a Strike Pistol. To get ahead, you must explore the map, gather resources, and use workbenches to craft more advanced weapons and items. Strategic crafting is key to gaining a significant power advantage over your opponents.

Inventory management is crucial for victory. You have a limited number of slots: 5 for ammo, 5 for your backpack, and 5 for your hotbar. Choose your items wisely, as you can't carry everything. Prioritize essential gear to ensure you're prepared for any situation.

Voxiom features several exciting game modes to test your skills: Capture the Gems, Battle Royale, Survival, and Free for All. While each mode has a different objective, the core challenge remains the same: stay alive against all odds in a hostile, open-world environment.

Basic Controls:

  • W, A, S, D: Move
  • Spacebar: Jump
  • Left Shift: Sprint
  • C: Crouch
  • Mouse: Look / Aim
  • Left Mouse Button: Shoot / Use Item
  • Right Mouse Button / F: Aim Down Sights
  • E: Interact
  • Tab: Open Inventory
  • R: Reload
