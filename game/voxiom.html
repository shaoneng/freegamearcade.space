Voxiom

How to Play Voxiom

To begin your Voxiom adventure, you'll be equipped with a basic Shovel, a Combat Assault Rifle, and a Strike Pistol. Your primary goal is to survive in a dynamic, pixelated open world that blends crafting with intense shooting action.

Explore your surroundings to gather resources, which you can then use at workbenches to craft more advanced weapons and essential tools. Resource management is key; pay close attention to your limited inventory, consisting of 5 ammo slots, 5 backpack slots, and 5 hotbar slots. Prioritizing the most critical items is crucial for success.

Voxiom offers four distinct game modes to keep the gameplay fresh: Capture the Gems, Battle Royale, Survival, and Free for All. Each mode presents unique objectives and challenges, forcing you to adapt your strategy. Additionally, a Custom Game mode becomes available once you reach level 60, offering even more personalized experiences.

Be prepared for constant threats from other online players worldwide. Whether you choose to collaborate with allies or engage in solo combat, mastering both your building and shooting skills is essential to defend yourself and emerge victorious in this fast-paced sandbox shooter.
