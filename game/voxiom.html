Voxiom

How to Play Voxiom

Welcome to the blocky battlegrounds of Voxiom! Your main objective is to survive and dominate in this multiplayer sandbox shooter. You'll start with basic gear: a Shovel, a Combat Assault Rifle, and a Strike Pistol. Use these to defend yourself and gather resources.

The world is your canvas. Break blocks to collect materials and use workbenches to craft more powerful weapons and tools. Strategic building is key to creating cover and getting a tactical advantage over other players from around the world.

Pay close attention to your inventory. You have limited slots (5 for ammo, 5 for your backpack, and 5 for your hotbar), so choose your equipment wisely. Carrying the most essential items can be the difference between victory and defeat.

Voxiom offers several game modes to test your skills, including Capture the Gems, Battle Royale, Survival, and Free for All. Each mode presents a unique challenge, but the core principle remains the same: fight, build, and survive!
