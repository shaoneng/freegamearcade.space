Voxiom

How to Play Voxiom

Welcome to the blocky battlegrounds of Voxiom! This game is a unique blend of sandbox building and first-person shooting. Your main goal across all game modes is simple: survive. You'll start each match with basic gear, including a shovel for digging and building, a Combat Assault Rifle, and a Strike Pistol.

To gain an edge, you must gather resources and use workbenches to craft more powerful weapons and tools. Be mindful of your inventory! You have limited space with 5 hotbar slots, 5 backpack slots, and 5 ammo slots. Strategic inventory management is crucial for success, so prioritize carrying the most essential items for your survival.

Voxiom offers several exciting game modes to test your skills. Jump into Battle Royale to be the last player standing, team up in Capture the Gems, fight for your life in Survival, or cause chaos in a classic Free for All. Each mode presents a different challenge, but all require quick reflexes, smart building, and sharp shooting.

Controls are standard for FPS games: use the W, A, S, D keys to move, Spacebar to jump, Shift to sprint, and C to crouch. Use your mouse to aim and the left mouse button to shoot or use an item. The right mouse button is for aiming down sights. Press E to interact with objects and R to reload your weapon.
