War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the notebook battlefield! Choose between a single-player (1 P) mission or a two-player (2 P) duel directly from the title screen. In two-player mode, you can battle a friend on the same keyboard, with one player controlling the Purple Tank and the other controlling the Green Tank.

In single-player mode, your goal is to navigate a challenging obstacle course divided into three zones. Each zone is blocked by red laser doors. To progress, you must find and destroy all the high-value targets in the current area, which include enemy tanks, turrets, and soldiers. Keep an eye on your green health bar at the top left; if it runs out, your tank is destroyed, and you'll have to start over.

Control your tank's movement and aim your turret before firing, as your shots only travel in the direction you are facing. Use the environment to your advantage! Some walls are thin enough to shoot through, while others will ricochet your shots, creating tactical opportunities. Be sure to roll over the colored icons scattered across the map to grab instant power-ups and give yourself an edge.
