War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the hand-drawn battlefield of War of Tanks Paper Note! The game can be enjoyed solo or with a friend in two-player mode, selected from the main menu. Player 1 controls the Purple Tank, and Player 2 commands the Green Tank.

In single-player mode, your mission is to navigate three distinct zones on the notebook paper. Each zone is blocked by red laser gates. To disable these gates and advance, you must find and destroy all the designated targets within the area, which include enemy tanks, turrets, and infantry clusters. Keep an eye on your green health bar in the top-left corner; if it depletes, your tank is destroyed, and the game is over.

Maneuver your tank through the maze-like levels, avoiding obstacles and enemy fire. Your tank can only shoot in the direction it's currently facing, so position yourself carefully before firing. Some thin walls can be shot through, while others will ricochet your shots, adding a strategic puzzle element to the combat.

Throughout the battlefield, you'll find colored icons. Drive over these to instantly collect power-ups that enhance your tank's abilities. In two-player mode, these power-ups appear more frequently, creating a competitive race to grab them before your opponent does!
