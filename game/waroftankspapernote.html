War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Dive into a battlefield sketched on notebook paper in War of Tanks Paper Note! This charming and intense arcade shooter brings doodle warfare to life. You can take on the challenge solo or battle a friend in two-player mode right on the same keyboard.

Single-Player Mission: Your goal is to navigate three distinct zones, each protected by red laser gates. To disable these gates and advance, you must find and destroy five high-value targets in each zone. Keep an eye on your green health bar at the top left—if it runs out, your tank is toast!

Controls & Tactics: Your tank can only fire in the direction it's facing, so positioning is key. Line up your shots carefully to take out enemy doodles, turrets, and soldiers. Use the environment to your advantage; some paper walls can be shot through, while others will ricochet your projectiles, creating tricky puzzle-like scenarios. Clear all fifteen targets to win!

Two-Player Mode: Grab a friend and go head-to-head! Player 1 controls the Purple Tank, and Player 2 controls the Green Tank. Keep an eye out for colored power-up icons that spawn on the map—they grant instant upgrades and can give you the edge in battle.
