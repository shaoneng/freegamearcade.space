War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Dive into a battlefield sketched on notebook paper in War of Tanks Paper Note! This free online game features a charming hand-drawn aesthetic where you guide a tiny tank through mazes of spinning barriers, laser grids, and enemy doodles. The game supports both single-player and a two-player mode on the same keyboard, with Player 1 controlling the purple tank and Player 2 commanding the green one.

In single-player, your objective is to clear three distinct zones gated by red laser doors. To progress, you must destroy all marked targets in an area, which can be enemy tanks, turrets, or soldiers. Monitor your green health bar—if it depletes, your tank is destroyed, and you'll have to start over.

Each zone has five high-value targets. You must strategically navigate the doodle-filled map, timing your movements to avoid rotating cannons and other hazards. Your tank fires only in the direction it's facing, so aim carefully before shooting. Some paper walls are thin enough to shoot through, while others will ricochet your shots, turning the level into a tactical puzzle. Clear all fifteen targets to win!

Keep an eye out for colored icons scattered across the battlefield. Rolling over these grants you instant power-ups, giving you a crucial edge. In two-player mode, these bonuses spawn more frequently, so be quick to grab them before your opponent does.
