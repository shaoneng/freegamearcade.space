War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Grab your favorite doodle pen and dive straight into War of Tanks Paper Note! This free online tank game brings the battlefield to a sheet of squared notebook paper. Guide your hand-colored tank through mazes of spinning barriers, pencil-red laser grids, and enemy doodles. You can even battle a friend on the same keyboard in two-player mode!

In single-player, your mission is to clear an obstacle course divided into three zones. Each zone is protected by glowing red laser doors. To progress, you must destroy all the marked targets—enemy tanks, turrets, or soldiers—to deactivate the lasers. Keep an eye on your green health bar; if it runs out, your tank is crumpled, and the game is over.

Each of the three zones contains five high-value targets. Hunt them down while skillfully navigating past rotating cannons and barriers. Remember, you can only shoot in the direction your tank is facing, so aim carefully. Some paper walls are thin enough to shoot through, while others will ricochet your shots, adding a puzzle element to the combat. Destroy all fifteen targets to win!

Look for colored icons scattered across the map. Rolling over them grants you instant upgrades. In two-player mode, these power-ups appear more frequently, creating a frantic race to see who can grab them first and gain the upper hand.
