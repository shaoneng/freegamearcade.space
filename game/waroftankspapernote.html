War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the paper battlefield! In War of Tanks Paper Note, your goal is to guide your doodle tank through a maze drawn on squared notebook paper. Navigate past obstacles, destroy enemy doodles, and complete your mission without getting erased from the page.

Choose between single-player (1P) or two-player (2P) mode from the title screen. In two-player mode, you and a friend can battle it out on the same keyboard, competing for power-ups and battlefield dominance.

In single-player mode, your mission is to destroy 15 high-value targets spread across three distinct zones. Each zone is blocked by red laser gates. To disable the lasers and advance, you must destroy all five targets within the current zone. Keep an eye on your green health bar at the top-left; if it runs out, your tank is destroyed, and the mission ends.

Your tank fires projectiles in the direction it is currently facing, so you must line up your turret before you shoot. Use the environment to your advantage! Some walls are thin enough to be shot through, while others will ricochet your shots, creating tricky puzzle-like scenarios.

As you explore the map, roll over colored icons to pick up instant power-ups and upgrades. These bonuses are crucial for survival and give you an edge over your enemies. In two-player mode, they spawn more frequently, so be quick to grab them!
