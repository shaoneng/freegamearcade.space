War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the hand-drawn battlefield of War of Tanks Paper Note! Choose your mode from the title screen: battle through a challenging obstacle course in single-player (1P) or go head-to-head against a friend on the same keyboard in two-player mode (2P). Player 1 controls the Purple Tank, and Player 2 commands the Green Tank.

In single-player, your mission is to clear three distinct zones gated by red laser doors. To progress, you must destroy all five high-value targets in each zone—these can be enemy tanks, turrets, or groups of soldiers. Keep an eye on your green health bar at the top left; if it runs out, your tank is scrapped, and you'll have to start over. Successfully destroy all fifteen targets to win!

Navigate the maze of spinning barriers and time your movements carefully to avoid enemy fire. Your tank can only shoot in the direction it's facing, so you'll need to position yourself perfectly before firing. Experiment with your shots—some walls can be shot through, while others will ricochet your projectiles, creating tactical opportunities to hit enemies around corners.

Be on the lookout for colored icons scattered across the map. Drive over them to collect instant power-ups that can give you a crucial advantage. In two-player mode, these power-ups appear more frequently, leading to a frantic race to grab them before your opponent does!
