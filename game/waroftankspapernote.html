War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Grab your favorite doodle pen and dive straight into War of Tanks Paper Note! This free online tank game brings intense arcade action to life on squared notebook paper. Guide your hand-drawn tank through mazes, dodge laser grids, and blast enemy doodles. You can tackle the mission solo or challenge a friend in two-player mode right from the title screen.

In single-player mode (1P), your mission is to clear an obstacle course divided into three zones. Each zone is locked by red laser doors. To progress, you must destroy all marked targets within an area, which can be enemy tanks, turrets, or soldier clusters. Keep an eye on your green health bar at the top left; if it runs out, your tank crumples, and the game is over.

Navigate your tank and line up your shots carefully, as projectiles only travel in the direction you're facing. Some paper-thin walls can be shot through, while others will ricochet your shots, creating tricky puzzle-like scenarios. Clearing all fifteen high-value targets across the three zones wins you the game!

Look for colored icons scattered across the battlefield. Rolling over them grants you instant power-ups. These bonuses appear more frequently in two-player mode, adding a competitive rush to grab them before your opponent does.

War of Tanks Paper Note is a creative and fun title developed by NeplingameStudio.
