War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the hand-drawn battlefield! On the title screen, choose between single-player (1P) or two-player (2P) mode. In single-player, your mission is to navigate a challenging obstacle course divided into three zones. To advance, you must destroy all marked targets—enemy tanks, turrets, or foot soldiers—in each area. Eliminating these targets shuts down the red laser gates, allowing you to proceed to the next zone.

Maneuver your tank through the squared paper mazes, but be strategic! Your tank can only fire in the direction it's currently facing, so you'll need to line up your shots perfectly. Some paper walls are thin enough to shoot through, while others will ricochet your projectiles, creating tricky puzzle-like scenarios. Keep an eye on your green health bar at the top left; if it runs out, your tank is destroyed, and the game is over.

In two-player mode, you and a friend can battle it out on the same keyboard, with Player 1 controlling the Purple Tank and Player 2 controlling the Green Tank. Throughout the battlefield in both modes, you'll find colored icons. Drive over them to receive instant power-ups and gain an advantage over your enemies or your friend. Clear all fifteen targets across the three zones to win!
