War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the hand-drawn battlefield! Begin by choosing your game mode on the title screen: 1P for a solo mission or 2P to team up with a friend on the same keyboard. Player 1 controls the Purple Tank, and Player 2 commands the Green Tank. Navigate your doodle tank through a maze sketched on squared notebook paper.

In single-player mode, your objective is to clear an obstacle course divided into three zones. Each zone is blocked by red laser doors. To disable these lasers and advance, you must find and destroy all the marked targets within the area, which can be enemy tanks, turrets, or groups of soldiers. Keep an eye on your green health bar in the top-left corner; if it runs out, your tank is destroyed, and the game is over.

Each of the three zones contains five high-value targets. You must skillfully maneuver around spinning barriers and rotating cannons to eliminate them. Remember, your tank can only shoot in the direction it's currently facing, so position yourself carefully before firing. Some paper walls are thin enough to shoot through, while others will ricochet your shots, adding a puzzle element to the combat. Destroy all fifteen targets to win the game!

Look for colored icons scattered across the map. Driving over these will grant your tank an instant power-up or bonus. In two-player mode, these icons appear more frequently, creating a fun race between you and your friend to grab them first and gain a tactical advantage.
