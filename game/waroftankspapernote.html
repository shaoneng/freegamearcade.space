How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the notebook battlefield! In War of Tanks Paper Note, you control a hand-drawn tank in a world sketched on squared paper. You can choose to play solo (Purple Tank) or challenge a friend in two-player mode on the same keyboard (Green Tank).

In single-player mode, your mission is to navigate an obstacle course divided into three zones. Each zone is blocked by a red laser gate. To progress, you must find and destroy all five high-value targets within that zoneâ€”these can be enemy tanks, turrets, or groups of doodle soldiers. Keep an eye on your green health bar; if it runs out, your tank is destroyed!

Maneuver your tank carefully through spinning barriers and other hazards. Your cannon only fires in the direction your tank is facing, so strategic positioning is key. Some paper-thin walls can be shot through, while others will ricochet your shots, adding a puzzle element to the combat. Clear all fifteen targets across the three zones to win.

Throughout the battlefield, you'll find colored icons. Drive over them to get instant power-ups and upgrades for your tank. In two-player mode, these bonuses appear more frequently, creating a frantic race to grab them before your opponent does!
