War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the doodled battlefield! Choose to play solo (1P) or challenge a friend on the same keyboard (2P) from the title screen. Player 1 pilots the Purple Tank, and Player 2 controls the Green Tank. Your goal is to navigate your hand-drawn tank through a maze-like level on a sheet of paper.

In single-player mode, the mission is to clear three zones. To advance, you must destroy all the designated targets in your current area, which can include enemy tanks, turrets, and soldier doodles. Keep an eye on your green health bar in the top-left corner; if it depletes, your tank is scrapped, and the game ends.

Maneuver carefully around spinning barriers and red laser grids. Your tank can only fire in the direction it's facing, so aim carefully before shooting. Some paper walls are thin enough to shoot through, while others will ricochet your shots, creating a fun puzzle-like challenge. Don't forget to grab the colored power-up icons scattered across the map for an instant advantage!
