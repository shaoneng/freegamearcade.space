War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Grab your favorite doodle pen and dive straight into War of Tanks Paper Note. It's a free online tank game with a unique aesthetic, looking like it was drawn during a boring math class. Every element, from your tank to the laser fences and turrets, is sketched on squared notebook paper, creating a battlefield that feels both nostalgic and brand-new. You can guide your tank through these hand-drawn mazes alone or team up with a friend on the same keyboard for intense two-player action.

In single-player mode (1P), you'll navigate an obstacle course split into three zones, each blocked by glowing red laser doors. To progress, you must destroy all marked targets within a zone, which can include enemy tanks, turrets, or clusters of foot soldiers. Successfully clearing the targets deactivates the lasers, unlocking the next area. Keep an eye on your green health bar at the top left; if it runs out, your tank gets crumpled, and the game is over.

Each of the three zones contains five high-value targets. You can destroy them in any order while weaving through spinning barriers and timing your movements past rotating cannons. Your tank's projectiles travel only in the direction it's facing, so aim carefully before you shoot. Some paper walls are thin enough to shoot through, while others will ricochet your shots, adding a puzzle element to the combat. Clear all fifteen targets to win the game and claim victory!

Keep an eye out for colored icons scattered across the battlefield. Rolling over them grants you an instant upgrade or bonus. These power-ups appear more frequently in two-player mode, so be quick to grab them before your opponent does!

War of Tanks Paper Note is created by NeplingameStudio.
