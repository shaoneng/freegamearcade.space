War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the hand-drawn battlefield of War of Tanks Paper Note! Choose between single-player (1P) or two-player (2P) mode from the title screen. In single-player, your mission is to navigate three distinct zones, each protected by red laser doors. To progress, you must destroy all the marked targets in an area, which include enemy tanks, turrets, and groups of soldiers.

Player 1 (Purple Tank): Use the WASD keys to move your tank around the paper maze. To fire, press the G key. Your tank can only shoot in the direction it's currently facing, so make sure to align your turret with your target before letting a shot fly. Keep an eye on your green health bar in the top-left corner; if it runs out, your tank is destroyed!

Player 2 (Green Tank): In two-player mode, use the Arrow Keys to move your tank. Press the L key to fire your cannon. The goal is to outmaneuver and outgun your opponent in a head-to-head doodle duel.

Throughout the battlefield, you'll find colored icons. Drive over them to instantly receive a power-up or bonus. In two-player mode, these spawn more frequently, so be quick to grab them! Pay attention to the environment—some thin walls can be shot through, while others will cause your shots to ricochet, adding a strategic puzzle element to the combat.
