War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the hand-drawn battlefield of War of Tanks Paper Note! This game transforms a simple piece of notebook paper into an intense arena for tank combat. Your goal is to navigate your tank, destroy targets, and survive the doodle onslaught.

Game Modes: Choose between single-player (1 P) or two-player (2 P) mode from the title screen. In two-player mode, you and a friend can battle it out on the same keyboard, with Player 1 controlling the Purple Tank and Player 2 controlling the Green Tank.

Single-Player Objective: Your mission is to clear an obstacle course divided into three zones. Each zone is blocked by red laser gates. To deactivate these gates, you must destroy all the designated targets within that area—these can be enemy tanks, turrets, or groups of soldiers. In total, there are 15 high-value targets to eliminate across all three zones. Your health is displayed by the green bar at the top left; if it runs out, your tank is destroyed!

Controls & Combat: Your tank moves and fires in the direction it's facing, so you'll need to position yourself carefully before shooting. Be mindful of the environment; some walls are thin enough to shoot through, while others will ricochet your shots, creating tactical opportunities.

Power-ups: Keep an eye out for colored icons scattered across the map. Drive over them to instantly receive a temporary upgrade or bonus, giving you a crucial advantage in the fight.
