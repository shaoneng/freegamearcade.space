War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the hand-drawn battlefield! Begin by choosing your mode on the title screen: 1 Player for a solo mission or 2 Player to challenge a friend on the same keyboard. Player 1 controls the Purple Tank, and Player 2 takes the helm of the Green Tank.

In single-player mode, your objective is to navigate an obstacle course divided into three zones. Each zone is protected by red laser doors. To advance, you must destroy all the high-value targets in the current areaâ€”these can be enemy tanks, turrets, or groups of soldiers. In total, there are 15 targets to eliminate across the three zones.

Maneuver your tank through the maze-like levels, avoiding spinning barriers and timing your movements past enemy fire. Your tank shoots in the direction it's facing, so you must align your turret before firing. Keep a close watch on your green health bar at the top of the screen; if it runs out, your tank is destroyed, and the game ends.

Look for colored icons scattered across the map. Rolling over these will grant you an instant power-up, giving you an advantage. In two-player mode, these bonuses appear more frequently, creating a frantic race to see who can grab them first. Clear all targets to complete your mission and prove you're the master of the paper battlefield!
