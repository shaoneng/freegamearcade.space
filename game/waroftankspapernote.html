How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the hand-drawn battlefield! The objective in War of Tanks Paper Note is to navigate your tank through a series of challenging levels, destroying all designated targets. You can choose to play solo in the 1-player mode or challenge a friend in the 2-player mode, both on the same keyboard.

In single-player mode, your mission is to advance through three distinct zones, each protected by red laser gates. To deactivate these lasers and proceed, you must find and destroy all high-value targets, which can be enemy tanks, turrets, or groups of soldiers. Keep a close watch on your green health bar at the top-left; if it empties, your tank is destroyed, and you'll have to start over.

Control your tank's movement and aim carefully, as your cannon only fires in the direction your tank is currently facing. Skillfully maneuver past rotating cannons and other scribbled obstacles to line up the perfect shot.

Be sure to roll over the colored icons scattered across the battlefield. These provide instant power-ups and upgrades for your tank, giving you a crucial advantage. In two-player mode, these bonuses spawn more frequently, so grab them quickly before your opponent does!
