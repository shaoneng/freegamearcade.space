War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the hand-drawn battlefield! This game can be played solo or with a friend on the same keyboard. Choose between 1P (single-player) or 2P (two-player) on the title screen. Player 1 controls the Purple Tank, and Player 2 controls the Green Tank.

In single-player mode, your goal is to navigate a challenging obstacle course divided into three zones. Each zone is protected by red laser doors. To progress, you must destroy all the marked targets within a zone, which include enemy tanks, turrets, or soldiers. Keep an eye on your green health bar in the top-left corner; if it runs out, your tank is destroyed, and you'll have to start over!

Mastering movement and aiming is key. Your tank's cannon only fires in the direction it's facing, so you must carefully position yourself before shooting. Maneuver through spinning barriers and time your movements to avoid rotating cannons. Some paper walls are thin enough to be shot through, while others will ricochet your shots, creating tricky puzzle-like scenarios.

Look out for colored icons scattered across the map. Rolling over these will grant you instant power-ups and upgrades. In two-player mode, these bonuses appear more frequently, leading to a frantic race to see who can grab them first and gain the upper hand.
