War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Grab your favorite doodle pen and dive straight into War of Tanks Paper Note. It's a free online tank game that looks like it was drawn on squared notebook paper. The action is intense as you guide a tiny hand-colored tank through mazes of spinning barriers, pencil-red laser grids, and enemy doodles. Best of all, you can challenge a friend and play on the same keyboard!

Choose between 1-player and 2-player modes on the title screen. Player 1 controls the Purple Tank, while Player 2 handles the Green Tank. In single-player mode, your mission is to navigate an obstacle course divided into three zones, each protected by glowing red laser doors.

To advance, you must destroy the marked targets—enemy tanks, turrets, or groups of soldiers—to disable the lasers and unlock the next area. Keep a close watch on your green health bar at the top left; if it depletes, your tank gets crunched into paper scraps, and the run ends. Each zone contains five high-value targets. Clear all fifteen targets across the three zones to claim victory.

Maneuver carefully, as projectiles only travel in the direction your tank is facing. Some paper walls are thin enough to fire through, while others bounce shots back, turning the battlefield into a ricochet puzzle. Be sure to roll over colored icons to gain instant upgrades, which are especially valuable in the competitive two-player mode.
