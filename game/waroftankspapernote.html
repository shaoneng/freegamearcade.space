War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the doodled battlefield of War of Tanks Paper Note! This game brings tank combat to life on squared notebook paper. You can dive into the action solo or challenge a friend in the two-player mode right from the start screen. Player 1 controls the Purple Tank, and Player 2 commands the Green Tank, both on the same keyboard.

In single-player mode, your mission is to navigate an obstacle course divided into three zones. Each zone is protected by red laser gates. To deactivate these lasers and advance, you must find and destroy all the designated targets within that area, which could be enemy tanks, turrets, or groups of foot soldiers. Keep an eye on your green health bar at the top left; if it runs out, your tank is scrapped, and you'll have to start over!

Master the controls to survive. Guide your tank through the maze, but remember that you can only shoot in the direction your tank is currently facing. Line up your shots carefully before firing! Some paper walls are thin enough to shoot through, while others will ricochet your projectiles, adding a puzzle-like element to the combat.

Be on the lookout for colored icons scattered across the map. Rolling over these will grant you instant power-ups, giving you a crucial advantage. In two-player mode, these bonuses appear more frequently, sparking a frantic race to grab them before your opponent does. Collect them to gain the upper hand and dominate the paper battlefield!
