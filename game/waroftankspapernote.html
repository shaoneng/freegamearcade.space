War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the paper battlefield of War of Tanks Paper Note! This game transforms your screen into a squared notebook page where hand-drawn tank battles come to life. You can choose to embark on a solo mission (1P) or battle a friend on the same keyboard in two-player mode (2P).

Single-Player Mode: Your mission is to navigate a large map broken into three distinct zones. To progress, you must destroy all fifteen high-value targetsâ€”these can be enemy tanks, turrets, or soldier clusters. Each zone is protected by red laser gates that deactivate once you've cleared the required targets in the area. Keep an eye on your green health bar; if it runs out, your tank is scrapped, and you'll have to start over!

Controls & Combat: Your tank moves and shoots in the direction it's facing, so positioning is key! Line up your shots carefully to take out enemies and targets. The battlefield is a maze of obstacles. Some paper-thin walls can be shot through, while others will ricochet your projectiles, creating tricky puzzle-like scenarios. Use the environment to your advantage!

Power-Ups: Throughout the map, you'll find colored icons. Drive over them to instantly receive a powerful upgrade for your tank. In two-player mode, these power-ups spawn more frequently, creating a frantic race to see who can grab them first and gain the upper hand.
