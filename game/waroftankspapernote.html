War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Welcome to the hand-drawn battlefield of War of Tanks Paper Note! Your mission is to command a doodle tank through a maze-like arena sketched on notebook paper. You can choose to tackle the challenge solo in 1-player mode or battle against a friend in 2-player mode directly from the title screen.

In single-player mode, the objective is to clear fifteen high-value targets spread across three distinct zones. Each zone is protected by red laser gates. To disable these gates and advance, you must destroy all marked targets within the current area, which can include enemy tanks, turrets, or even clusters of hand-drawn soldiers. Keep an eye on your green health bar at the top leftâ€”if it runs out, your tank is crumpled, and the game is over!

Maneuver your tank through the obstacles and line up your shots carefully. Your cannon only fires in the direction your tank is currently facing. Use the environment to your advantage! Some thin walls can be shot through, while others will cause your shots to ricochet, creating strategic opportunities to hit targets around corners.

Throughout the map, you'll find colored icons. Drive over these to instantly receive a power-up or bonus for your tank. In the fast-paced two-player mode, these power-ups appear more frequently, so be quick to grab them before your opponent does to gain a crucial advantage.
