War of Tanks Paper Note

How to Play War of Tanks Paper Note

Grab your favorite doodle pen and dive straight into War of Tanks Paper Note! It’s a free online tank game that looks like it was sketched on notebook paper, but the action is as intense as any arcade shooter. Guide your tiny hand-colored tank through mazes of spinning barriers, pencil-red laser grids, and enemy doodles that want to erase you from the page. This charming paper-craft style makes the battlefield feel both nostalgic and brand-new.

Choose between single-player or two-player co-op on the same keyboard. The Purple Tank is Player 1, and the Green Tank is Player 2. In single-player, you'll face an obstacle course divided into three zones, each blocked by glowing red laser doors. To progress, you must destroy marked targets—tanks, turrets, or soldier clusters—to deactivate the lasers. Keep an eye on your green health bar; if it empties, your tank gets crumpled up, and the run is over.

Each zone has five high-value targets. Hunt them down while weaving through barriers and timing your moves past rotating cannons. Your tank can only fire in the direction it's facing, so line up your shots carefully. Some paper walls are thin enough to shoot through, while others will ricochet your shots back, adding a puzzle element to the combat. Clear all fifteen targets to conquer the paper battlefield!

Look for colored icons scattered across the map. Rolling over them grants you an instant upgrade. In two-player mode, these bonuses appear more frequently, so be quick to snag them before your friend does. War of Tanks Paper Note is created by NeplingameStudio.
