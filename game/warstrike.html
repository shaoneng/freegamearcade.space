WarStrike

How to Play WarStrike

WarStrike offers a premium first-person shooter experience directly in your browser. You can choose between two exciting game modes: a thrilling Campaign or fast-paced Multiplayer battles.

Campaign Mode: You play as an elite MI-6 agent on a mission to stop TAL BRODY, an international terrorist threatening the world with a deadly virus. Progress through 24 chapters, each with unique objectives like clearing zones, planting bombs, or completing sniper missions. As you complete missions, you'll level up, improve your skills, and unlock new weapons to aid in your quest.

Multiplayer Mode: If you prefer competitive action, jump into Multiplayer. Team up with other players in classic Team Deathmatch. The first team to score 20 kills wins the round. Coordinate with your allies and utilize different maps like Dockyard, Pool Party, and Farm House to gain a tactical advantage.

Controls & Upgrades: Use standard FPS controls (WASD to move, Mouse to aim and shoot). In both modes, you will earn cash and gold. Use this currency to unlock a powerful arsenal of weapons like the UMP and HMG, and upgrade them with parts like scopes, silencers, and grips to match your playstyle.
