WarStrike

How to Play WarStrike

WarStrike offers a console-level first-person shooter experience directly in your browser, with both a deep single-player campaign and fast-paced multiplayer action. Your goal is to complete missions, defeat enemies, and upgrade your gear to become the ultimate soldier.

Controls:

  • WASD: Move your character
  • Mouse: Aim and look around
  • Left Mouse Button: Shoot
  • Right Mouse Button: Aim down sights
  • R: Reload your weapon
  • Spacebar: Jump
  • Shift: Sprint

Campaign Mode: You play as an elite MI-6 agent on a mission to stop the international terrorist, Tal Brody. His plan involves a deadly virus that can turn people into zombies. Progress through 24 chapters, completing objectives like clearing zones, planting bombs, and executing sniper missions to unravel the story and save the world.

Multiplayer Mode: Jump into quick and intense team deathmatches. Choose from maps like Dockyard, Pool Party, or Crash Site. Team up with other players online and be the first team to reach 20 kills to claim victory. Communication and strategy are key to dominating the opposition.

Upgrades and Arsenal: In both game modes, you'll earn cash and gold. Use this currency to unlock a powerful arsenal of weapons, including snipers, SMGs, and HMGs. You can also upgrade your weapons with attachments like scopes, silencers, and grips, or customize their look with various skins to enhance your combat effectiveness.
