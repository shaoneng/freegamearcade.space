WarStrike

How to Play WarStrike

WarStrike offers two distinct and thrilling game modes: a deep single-player Campaign and fast-paced Multiplayer battles. Choose your preferred mode from the main menu to begin.

In Campaign Mode, you take on the role of an elite MI-6 Special Soldier. Your mission is to hunt down Tal Brody, an international terrorist threatening the world with a deadly virus. You will progress through 24 chapters, each with unique objectives like clearing zones, planting bombs, or executing sniper missions. As you complete missions and level up, you'll improve your soldier's skills and earn cash and gold.

In Multiplayer Mode, you can jump straight into the action. Pick from maps like Dockyard, Pool Party, or Farm House and join a team deathmatch. The goal is simple: work with your teammates to be the first team to score 20 kills. Communication and strategy are vital to dominating the opposition.

Use the cash and gold earned in either mode to build your ultimate arsenal. Unlock powerful weapons like the Sniper PXed, UMP, and HMG. You can also upgrade them with attachments like scopes, silencers, and grips, or customize their look with various skins to create a loadout that suits your playstyle.

Please Note: This game contains mature themes, including intense action and violence, and may not be suitable for all players.
