WarStrike

How to Play WarStrike

WarStrike offers a console-level first-person shooter experience directly in your browser, featuring two distinct game modes: a story-driven Campaign and fast-paced Multiplayer battles.

Campaign Mode: You step into the shoes of an elite MI-6 Special Soldier on a critical mission to stop the international terrorist, Tal Brody. Your objective is to prevent him from unleashing a deadly virus. Navigate through 24 chapters, completing diverse missions that include clearing zones, planting bombs, and executing precise sniper shots. As you progress and level up, you'll enhance your soldier's skills and unlock a powerful arsenal of weapons and attachments to tackle more intense challenges.

Multiplayer Mode: If you prefer quick action, jump into the Multiplayer Mode. Choose from various maps like Dockyard, Pool Party, or Farm House. Team up with other players in a classic team deathmatch format where the first team to score 20 kills wins. Communication and strategy are key to dominating the competition.

Upgrades and Customization: In both modes, you'll earn cash and gold. Use this currency to unlock and upgrade a wide range of weapons, from the UMP to the HMG. Further customize your loadout with attachments like scopes, silencers, and grips, or change your weapon's look with unique skins.

Mature Content Advisory: Please be aware that WarStrike contains intense action and violence that may not be suitable for all players. Player discretion is advised.
