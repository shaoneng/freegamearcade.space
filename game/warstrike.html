WarStrike

How to Play WarStrike

Welcome to WarStrike, a high-octane first-person shooter you can play right in your browser. The game offers two main modes: a deep Campaign and fast-paced Multiplayer battles.

Campaign Mode: You are an elite MI-6 operative tasked with a world-saving mission. Your objective is to track down and capture the international terrorist, Tal Brody, who threatens to unleash a deadly virus. Navigate through 24 chapters, each with unique objectives like clearing zones, planting bombs, and executing precise sniper shots. As you progress, you'll earn cash and gold to level up your soldier's skills and unlock a powerful arsenal of weapons and upgrades.

Multiplayer Mode: If you prefer quick action, jump into a team deathmatch. Choose from various maps like Dockyard or Pool Party, team up with other players, and be the first team to score 20 kills. Success in multiplayer also rewards you with cash and gold for customizing your gear.

Controls: Use your mouse to aim and shoot. Standard FPS keyboard controls (WASD) are used for movement. Follow the on-screen prompts for specific actions like planting bombs or interacting with objects.
