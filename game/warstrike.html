WarStrike

How to Play WarStrike

WarStrike offers a console-like first-person shooter experience directly in your browser. You can choose between an immersive single-player campaign or fast-paced multiplayer battles.

Campaign Mode: As an elite MI-6 agent, your mission is to hunt down Tal Brody, an international terrorist threatening the world with a deadly virus. Navigate through 24 intense chapters with varied objectives, including clearing zones, defusing bombs, and engaging in sniper missions. Follow the story and bring down the ultimate threat.

Multiplayer Mode: For instant action, select Multiplayer and choose from maps like Dockyard, Pool Party, or Crash Site. Join a team and compete in a classic team deathmatch. The first team to score over 20 kills wins the round. Collaborate with your teammates and dominate the leaderboard!

Weapons & Upgrades: Whether playing the campaign or multiplayer, you'll earn cash and gold. Use your earnings to unlock and upgrade a wide array of weapons like the Sniper PXed, UMP, and HMG. Further enhance your loadout with attachments like scopes, silencers, and grips, or customize your weapon's appearance with unique skins.

Mature Content Advisory: This game contains themes that may not be suitable for all players, including intense action and violence. Player discretion is advised.
