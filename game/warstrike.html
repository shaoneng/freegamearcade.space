WarStrike

How to Play WarStrike

WarStrike offers two main ways to play: a story-driven Campaign Mode and a competitive Multiplayer Mode. In both modes, your goal is to eliminate enemies, complete objectives, and earn rewards to upgrade your soldier and arsenal. The game plays like a classic first-person shooter, using your mouse to aim and shoot, and keyboard to move.

In Campaign Mode, you step into the shoes of an MI-6 Special Soldier. Your mission is to track down the international terrorist, Tal Brody, and prevent him from unleashing a deadly virus. Progress through 24 chapters, each with unique objectives like clearing zones, planting bombs, or completing sniper missions. As you complete missions and level up, you'll enhance your soldier's skills and unlock new weapons.

For quick action, choose Multiplayer Mode. Join a team and fight in intense deathmatches on various maps, including Dockyard, Pool Party, and Farm House. The objective is to work with your teammates to reach 20 kills before the opposing team does. Communication and strategy are key to dominating the competition.

Throughout the game, you'll earn cash and gold. Use this currency in the armory to unlock a wide range of weapons like the Sniper PXed and UMP. You can also upgrade your gear with attachments like scopes and silencers, or customize your look with different weapon skins to create the ultimate soldier.
