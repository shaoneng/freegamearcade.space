WarStrike

How to Play WarStrike

WarStrike offers a high-octane, first-person shooter experience with two distinct game modes: a story-driven Campaign and fast-paced Multiplayer. Whether you prefer a deep narrative or quick action, there's a mode for you.

In Campaign Mode, you step into the boots of an elite MI-6 special soldier. Your mission is to track down and neutralize TAL BRODY, an international terrorist threatening the world with a deadly virus. Progress through 24 chapters, completing varied objectives such as clearing hostile zones, strategically placing bombs, and executing precise sniper shots. As you advance, you'll level up, enhance your soldier's abilities, and unlock new weapons for the fight ahead.

For instant action, jump into Multiplayer Mode. Choose from maps like Dockyard, Pool Party, and Farm House to engage in thrilling team deathmatches. The goal is simple: team up with other players and be the first to reach 20 kills. Communication and strategy are key to dominating the battlefield and securing victory for your team.

In both modes, you'll earn cash and gold. Use this currency to build your arsenal by unlocking powerful weapons like the Sniper PXed, UMP, and HMG. You can also upgrade your firearms with attachments such as scopes, silencers, and grips to improve their performance, or customize their look with various skins.

Mature Content Advisory

This game features intense action and violence and may not be suitable for all ages. Player discretion is advised.
