WarStrike

How to Play WarStrike

WarStrike offers two distinct game modes: a story-driven Campaign and fast-paced Multiplayer battles. Choose your preferred mode from the main menu to begin.

In Campaign Mode, you step into the boots of an elite MI-6 soldier on a critical mission. Your goal is to track down and capture the international terrorist, Tal Brody, before he unleashes a deadly virus. Navigate through 24 chapters, completing varied objectives like clearing zones of enemies, planting bombs, and executing precise sniper shots. As you progress and level up, you'll enhance your soldier's abilities and unlock new equipment.

If you prefer competitive action, jump into Multiplayer Mode. Here, you'll join a team and face off against other players in a team deathmatch. The objective is simple: work with your teammates to be the first team to reach 20 kills. Communication and strategy are key to dominating maps like Dockyard, Pool Party, and Farm House.

In both modes, you will earn cash and gold that can be used to unlock a powerful arsenal of weapons, including the Sniper PXed, UMP, and HMG. You can also upgrade your firearms with attachments like scopes, silencers, and grips to gain a tactical advantage.
