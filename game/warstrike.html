WarStrike

How to Play WarStrike

WarStrike offers two exciting modes: a story-driven Campaign and fast-paced Multiplayer battles. Your goal is to complete missions, eliminate enemies, and become a legendary soldier.

Campaign Mode: You are an elite MI-6 soldier tasked with stopping the international terrorist, Tal Brody, from unleashing a deadly virus. Follow the story through 24 chapters, each with unique objectives like clearing zones, planting bombs, or completing sniper missions. As you progress, you'll level up your skills and earn currency.

Multiplayer Mode: Jump into team deathmatches on various maps. The objective is simple: work with your team to be the first to reach 20 kills. Communication and strategy are key to dominating the opposing team.

In both modes, you'll earn cash and gold. Use this currency in the armory to unlock powerful new weapons like the Sniper PXed and UMP, and upgrade them with scopes, silencers, and custom skins to enhance your performance in battle.
