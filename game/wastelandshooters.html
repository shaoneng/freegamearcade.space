Wasteland Shooters

How to Play Wasteland Shooters

Welcome to the lawless expanse of Wasteland Shooters! Your mission is to survive and dominate in this intense multiplayer shooter. Use your tactical skills and sharp reflexes to navigate the harsh environments, find strategic hiding spots, and eliminate your opponents to climb the leaderboard.

To jump into the action, you can either create your own private game room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can set a custom name, define the maximum number of players, and choose between Free-For-All (FFA) or Team-based combat. Battle across four unique maps: Dusty, RedWaste, Wasteland, and WasteTwon, each offering different tactical opportunities. Arm yourself with a wide range of weapons, from the silent Ballistic Knife to the explosive RPG, and prove your skills are unmatched.

Master the controls to become a pro: use WASD or the arrow keys to move, the left mouse button to fire your weapon, R to reload, the spacebar to jump, and the arrow keys to switch between your weapons.
