Wasteland Shooters

How to Play Wasteland Shooters

In Wasteland Shooters, your mission is to eliminate opponents and prove your combat skills in a post-apocalyptic environment. This multiplayer FPS allows you to forge your own path in the world of shooters.

To start playing, you can either create your own room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you have the flexibility to customize its name, set the maximum number of players, and choose between 'Free For All' (FFA) or 'Team' game modes.

Once in a game, navigate the four distinct maps – Dusty, RedWaste, Wasteland, and WasteTwon – and utilize their layouts for tactical advantage. Equip yourself with a variety of powerful weapons, from the Ballistic Knife and MP5KA4 to the STW-25, M87T, Blaser R93, RPG, and M79, to dominate your foes.

Keep an eye on key display elements: the kill feed on the top right, your currently equipped weapons in the center, and your health and ammo levels at the bottom right. The time and leading player are also displayed in the center, with chat on the center left. Your overall statistics, including games played, wins, kills, and score, are tracked in the dedicated statistics section.

Controls:

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your character
  • Left Mouse Button: Fire weapon
  • R: Reload
  • Space: Jump
  • Arrow Keys: Switch weapons
