How to Play Wasteland Shooters

Welcome to the desolate battlegrounds of Wasteland Shooters! To begin your journey, you'll first need to either create your own game room or join an existing one. When creating a room, you can set a name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: a chaotic Free-for-All (FFA) or strategic Team deathmatch.

Once you're in the game, your mission is to eliminate opponents across four distinct maps. Use the environment to your advantageâ€”find cover, hunt down your enemies, and attack with precision. Keep an eye on the on-screen display for your health, ammo, and the kill feed to stay ahead of the competition.

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use the WASD or Arrow Keys to move your character. Aim with your mouse and press the Left Mouse Button to fire your weapon. Press the R key to reload, and the Spacebar to jump. You can cycle through your arsenal using the Arrow Keys.
