Wasteland Shooters

How to Play Wasteland Shooters

Welcome to the rugged battlegrounds of Wasteland Shooters! Your journey begins by either creating your own game room or joining an existing one. When creating a room, you can set a name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: a chaotic Free-For-All (FFA) or a strategic Team deathmatch.

Once in the game, your mission is simple: eliminate your opponents and climb the leaderboard. You'll battle across four distinct maps: Dusty, RedWaste, Wasteland, and WasteTwon. Use the environment to your advantage by finding cover, setting up ambushes, and hunting down your foes. Keep an eye on the clock and the leading player's score to stay on top of the action.

Controls:
- Move: Use the WASD or Arrow Keys.
- Jump: Press the Spacebar.
- Fire: Click the Left Mouse Button.
- Reload: Press the R key.
- Switch Weapons: Use the Arrow Keys.

Stay vigilant and manage your resources. Your health and ammo are displayed on the bottom right of the screen. A kill feed at the top right shows recent eliminations, and you can communicate with other players using the chat on the left. Master the controls, learn the maps, and prove you're the ultimate Wasteland Shooter!
