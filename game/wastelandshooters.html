Wasteland Shooters

How to Play Wasteland Shooters

Welcome to the intense, action-packed battlegrounds of Wasteland Shooters! Your journey begins by either creating your own game room or joining one hosted by other players. When creating a room, you can give it a unique name, set the maximum player count, and choose between a chaotic Free-for-All (FFA) or a strategic Team-based match.

Master the simple controls to outmaneuver your opponents:

  • Move: Use WASD or the Arrow Keys to navigate the map.
  • Fire Weapon: Click the Left Mouse Button to shoot.
  • Reload: Press the R key to reload your weapon.
  • Jump: Use the Spacebar to jump over obstacles.
  • Switch Weapons: Use the Arrow Keys to cycle through your arsenal.

Your objective is to eliminate as many enemies as possible across four distinct maps: Dusty, RedWaste, Wasteland, and WasteTwon. Each map provides unique opportunities for hiding, hunting, and attacking. Utilize a wide range of weapons from the Deagle to the RPG to climb the leaderboard and prove your skills are the best in the wasteland.
