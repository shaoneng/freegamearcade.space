Wasteland Shooters

How to Play Wasteland Shooters

Welcome to the desolate battlegrounds of Wasteland Shooters! Your mission is to outgun and outmaneuver opponents in fast-paced multiplayer combat. To begin, you can either create your own game room—setting the name, max players, and game mode (Free-for-All or Team Deathmatch)—or jump straight into the action by joining an existing room.

Once you're in, use the environment to your advantage across four distinct maps: Dusty, RedWaste, Wasteland, and WasteTwon. Each map offers unique opportunities to hide, hunt, and ambush your enemies. Cycle through a powerful arsenal of weapons to find the perfect tool for every situation.

Master the controls to dominate the arena:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Fire: Left Mouse Button
  • Reload: R Key
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Switch Weapons: Arrow Keys

Keep an eye on your on-screen display for crucial information like your health, ammo, the kill feed, and the current match leader. Work with your team or fight for yourself to climb the leaderboard and prove you are the ultimate Wasteland Shooter!
