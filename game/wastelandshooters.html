Wasteland Shooters

How to Play Wasteland Shooters

Welcome to the gritty, action-packed world of Wasteland Shooters! Your goal is to outsmart and outgun your opponents in fast-paced multiplayer battles. To begin, you can either create your own private room—setting the name, player limit, and game mode (Free-for-All or Team)—or jump straight into the action by joining an existing room from the server list.

Once in the game, your mission is to eliminate enemies across four unique and hazardous maps. Use the environment to your advantage by finding cover, setting up ambushes, and hunting down your foes. Keep an eye on your health and ammo, and work with your team (in Team mode) to dominate the match. The player with the most kills or the last team standing wins!

Controls:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Fire: Left Mouse Button
  • Reload: R Key
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Switch Weapons: Arrow Keys
