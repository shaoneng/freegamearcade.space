Wasteland Shooters

How to Play Wasteland Shooters

Welcome to the unforgiving world of Wasteland Shooters! Your journey begins by either creating a new game room or joining an existing one. When creating a room, you can set a custom name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: a chaotic Free-for-All (FFA) or a strategic Team-based battle.

Once in the game, your mission is to outwit and outgun your opponents across four distinct maps: Dusty, RedWaste, Wasteland, and WasteTwon. Use the environment to your advantage—find cover, set up ambushes, and hunt down your enemies. Master a wide array of weapons, from the silent Ballistic Knife to the explosive RPG, to climb the leaderboards and prove your skill.

Keep an eye on the on-screen display for crucial information like the kill feed, your health and ammo, and the current match leader. Communicate with your team using the chat and track your long-term progress in the statistics section.

Controls:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Fire: Left Mouse Button
  • Reload: R Key
  • Jump: Spacebar
