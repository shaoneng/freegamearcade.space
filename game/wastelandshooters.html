Wasteland Shooters

How to Play Wasteland Shooters

Dive into the action by first creating a game room or joining an existing one. When you create a room, you can give it a unique name, set the maximum number of players, and choose your game mode: a chaotic Free-For-All (FFA) or strategic Team Deathmatch. With four distinct maps—Dusty, RedWaste, Wasteland, and WasteTwon—each battle offers a new tactical challenge.

Master the controls to dominate the arena. Use the WASD keys or Arrow Keys to move around and the mouse to aim and look. Fire your weapon with the Left Mouse Button and press the R key to reload when you're running low on ammo. Use the Spacebar to jump over obstacles or dodge enemy fire. You can switch between your weapons using the Number Keys.

Your objective is simple: eliminate more opponents than anyone else to prove you're the ultimate survivor in the wasteland. Use the environment for cover, track your kills on the feed, and climb to the top of the leaderboard!
