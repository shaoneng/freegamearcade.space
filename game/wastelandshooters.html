Wasteland Shooters

How to Play Wasteland Shooters

Welcome to the desolate battlegrounds of Wasteland Shooters! Your mission is to prove your combat prowess and become the most feared warrior in this post-apocalyptic world. Outsmart, outmaneuver, and outgun your opponents to climb to the top of the leaderboard.

To begin, you can either create your own game room or join one hosted by another player. When creating a room, you can set a custom name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: Free-For-All (FFA) or Team Deathmatch. Battle it out across four distinct maps: Dusty, RedWaste, Wasteland, and WasteTwon, each offering unique tactical opportunities for hiding, hunting, and attacking.

Controls:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Fire: Left Mouse Button
  • Reload: R key
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Switch Weapons: Arrow keys

Keep an eye on your health and ammo, watch the kill feed to track the action, and use the in-game chat to coordinate with your team or taunt your rivals. With a wide range of weapons at your disposal, from the stealthy Ballistic Knife to the explosive RPG, your tactical choices will define your path to victory.
