Wasteland Shooters

How to Play Wasteland Shooters

Welcome to the desolate battlegrounds of Wasteland Shooters! To begin your journey, you can either create your own custom game room or join one hosted by another player. When creating a room, you can set a unique name, choose the maximum number of players, and select your preferred game mode: a chaotic Free-For-All (FFA) or a strategic Team-based match.

Once you're in the game, your mission is simple: eliminate your opponents and climb the leaderboard. Navigate through four distinct maps, using the environment to hide, hunt, and ambush your foes. Keep an eye on your health and ammo, and work with your team (if applicable) to secure victory before time runs out.

Master the controls to dominate the arena:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Fire: Left Mouse Button
  • Reload: R Key
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Switch Weapons: Arrow Keys
