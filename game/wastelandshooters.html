Wasteland Shooters

How to Play Wasteland Shooters

Welcome to the desolate battlegrounds of Wasteland Shooters! Your goal is to outsmart and outgun your opponents in fast-paced multiplayer combat. To begin, you can either create your own game room—setting a name, player limit, and game mode (Free-for-All or Team)—or jump straight into an existing room created by other players.

Once you're in the game, use your tactical skills to navigate across four unique maps: Dusty, RedWaste, Wasteland, and WasteTwon. Find cover, hunt down your foes, and use your diverse arsenal to claim victory. Keep an eye on your health, ammo, and the kill feed to stay on top of the action.

Controls:

  • Move: WASD or Arrow Keys
  • Fire: Left Mouse Button
  • Reload: R Key
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Switch Weapons: Arrow Keys
