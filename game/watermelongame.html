Watermelon Game

How to Play Watermelon Game

Welcome to the deliciously addictive world of the Watermelon Game! The rules are simple, but the strategy is deep. Your goal is to create the largest fruit possible—the glorious watermelon—without letting your container overflow.

You will be given a sequence of fruits to drop into the container. Use your mouse or finger to decide where to drop the current fruit. When two identical fruits touch, they will merge and evolve into the next bigger fruit in the cycle. For example, two blueberries will merge into a strawberry, two strawberries into a grape, and so on.

Plan your drops carefully! Think ahead to position fruits for future merges and create satisfying chain reactions. Keep an eye on the space you have left. If the fruits stack up and cross the line at the top of the container, it's game over. Try to beat your high score with each attempt!
