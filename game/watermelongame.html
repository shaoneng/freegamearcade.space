Watermelon Game

How to Play Watermelon Game

The objective of the Watermelon Game is to score as high as possible by merging fruits. You start by dropping small fruits into a container. When two identical fruits touch, they merge into a single, larger fruit.

Use your mouse to control where the fruit drops. The cycle of fruits progresses from small to large, starting with blueberries and evolving into strawberries, grapes, peaches, and more, all the way up to the giant watermelon. Planning your drops is key to creating chain reactions and making larger fruits efficiently.

Be strategic with your placement! The container has limited space. If the fruits stack up and cross the line at the top, the game is over. A good tip is to try and keep your larger fruits at the bottom to create a stable base and prevent the container from overflowing too quickly.
