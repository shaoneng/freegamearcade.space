Watermelon Game

How to Play Watermelon Game

The objective of the Watermelon Game is to score as many points as possible by merging fruits. You will drop various fruits, one at a time, into a container. Use your mouse or finger to position the fruit before releasing it.

When two identical fruits touch, they combine to form a single, larger fruit. The key is to learn the fruit evolution cycle, starting from small berries and progressing to larger fruits like oranges, coconuts, and pineapples.

Your ultimate goal is to create the largest fruit of all â€“ the giant watermelon! This requires careful planning and a chain of successful merges. The game ends if the fruits pile up and cross the line at the top of the container, so manage your space wisely.

A pro tip for a high score is to try and keep your largest fruits at the bottom of the container. This prevents smaller fruits from getting trapped underneath and makes it easier to set up chain-reaction merges.
