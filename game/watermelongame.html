Watermelon Game

How to Play Watermelon Game

The goal of Watermelon Game is simple yet captivating: combine smaller fruits to create larger ones, with the ultimate prize being the giant watermelon. Each round, you'll drop a fruit from the top of the screen into the container below.

When two identical fruits touch, they will merge into the next biggest fruit in the cycle. For example, two blueberries will combine to become a strawberry, two strawberries become a grape, and so on. This creates a chain of evolution you must manage.

Strategy is key! Plan where you drop each fruit to set up future merges and create satisfying chain reactions. Be mindful of the limited space in the container. If the fruits stack up and breach the line at the top, it's game over. Aim for a high score by creating as many large fruits as you can before the container overflows!
