Watermelon Game

How to Play Watermelon Game

Welcome to the deliciously addictive world of the Watermelon Game! The goal is simple yet challenging: create the largest fruit possible—the glorious watermelon—without overflowing your container.

Use your mouse or finger to control where the next fruit will be dropped into the box. Click or tap to release the fruit.

When two identical fruits touch, they merge into the next biggest fruit in the sequence. For example, two blueberries will combine to form a strawberry, two strawberries become a grape, and so on, all the way up to the watermelon.

Plan your drops carefully! Think ahead to position fruits for easy merges and chain reactions. Keep an eye on the physics—fruits will roll and stack, so a poorly placed fruit could ruin your setup. If the fruits stack up and cross the line at the top of the container, the game is over. Try to beat your own high score with each attempt!
