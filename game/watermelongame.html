Watermelon Game

How to Play Watermelon Game

Welcome to the deliciously addictive challenge of the Watermelon Game! The objective is simple: drop various fruits into a container and try to score as high as possible without letting them overflow.

When two identical fruits touch, they merge into a single, larger fruit. Your goal is to continue this merging process, evolving from tiny blueberries all the way up to the grand prize â€“ the giant watermelon. Use your mouse to decide where to drop the next fruit that appears at the top of the screen.

Strategy is key! Plan your drops carefully to set up chain reactions. Try to keep smaller fruits on top and group larger fruits towards the bottom to make merging easier. Be careful, though! If the fruits pile up and cross the line at the top of the container, your game will end.

There are no time limits, so take your time, plan your moves, and see if you have what it takes to master the art of fruit merging and create the ultimate watermelon!
