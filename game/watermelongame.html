Watermelon Game

How to Play Watermelon Game

The goal of the Watermelon Game is simple yet captivating: merge identical fruits to create larger ones, all within a confined container. Your ultimate aim is to create the giant watermelon!

Use your mouse to control the position where you drop the next fruit from the top. Click to release the fruit into the container below. You can see which fruit is coming next, so you can plan your moves ahead of time.

When two identical fruits touch, they merge into the next fruit in the evolutionary cycleâ€”for example, two blueberries become a strawberry. This can trigger satisfying chain reactions, clearing space and boosting your score.

Be strategic with your placements! If the fruits pile up and cross the line at the top of the container, the game is over. Plan your drops carefully to keep the pile low and create space for larger fruits.
