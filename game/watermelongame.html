Watermelon Game

How to Play Watermelon Game

The goal of Watermelon Game is simple: merge fruits to create bigger ones without letting the bowl overflow. You start by dropping tiny blueberries into a container. When two identical fruits touch, they combine to form the next larger fruit in the sequence.

Continue merging fruits like strawberries, peaches, plums, and lemons, working your way up to the ultimate goal: the giant watermelon. Be strategic with your drops! Overfilling the container will end your game.

For best results, try to keep your larger fruits towards the bottom and center of the bowl to allow smaller fruits to merge above them and create chain reactions. Planning your drops and maintaining some symmetry in the bowl are key to maximizing your score and reaching the watermelon!
