Watermelon Game

How to Play Watermelon Game

The goal of Watermelon Game is to get the highest score possible by merging fruits. You'll drop different fruits into a container one at a time. Use your mouse to aim and click to release the fruit.

When two identical fruits touch, they will combine to create a new, larger fruit. The cycle continues, with smaller fruits merging into bigger ones, all leading up to the ultimate goal: the giant watermelon.

Strategy is key! Plan your drops carefully to set up chain reactions and clear space. If the fruits stack up and cross the line at the top of the container, the game is over. Try to keep your larger fruits at the bottom to avoid blocking smaller merges.
