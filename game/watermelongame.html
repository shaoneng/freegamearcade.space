Watermelon Game

How to Play Watermelon Game

The goal of the Watermelon Game is simple yet challenging: create the largest fruit possible without letting the container overflow. You start by dropping small fruits, like blueberries, from the top of the screen. Use your mouse or finger to decide where each fruit will land.

When two identical fruits touch, they merge into a single, larger fruit. For example, two blueberries combine to become a strawberry, two strawberries become a grape, and so on. The progression continues through various fruits, with the ultimate goal being to merge two of the second-largest fruits to create the final, giant watermelon!

Strategy is key to achieving a high score. You must carefully plan your drops to set up future merges and chain reactions. Be careful not to fill the container too quickly! If your pile of fruits stacks up and crosses the line at the top, your game is over. Try to keep larger fruits at the bottom to create a stable base and prevent smaller fruits from getting trapped.
