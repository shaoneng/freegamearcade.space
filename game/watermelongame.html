Watermelon Game

How to Play Watermelon Game

The objective of the Watermelon Game is simple yet captivating: merge fruits to create bigger ones, with the ultimate goal of forming a giant watermelon. You'll drop various fruits into a container, and when two identical fruits touch, they combine into the next fruit in the sequence.

Use your mouse to control where each fruit is dropped into the container. Plan your drops carefully! The physics of the game means fruits will roll and settle, so think ahead to set up future merges. The game ends if your fruits stack up and cross the line at the top of the container, so managing your space is crucial.

A key strategy is to keep your larger fruits at the bottom and smaller fruits at the top, preventing smaller fruits from getting trapped. Try to create chain reactions by arranging your merges thoughtfully. With no time limits, you can take your time to plan the perfect drop and work your way towards that satisfying final watermelon!
