Watermelon Game

How to Play Watermelon Game

The goal in Watermelon Game is simple yet captivating: create the largest fruit possible, the giant watermelon, without letting your container overflow. You start by dropping small fruits, like blueberries, into a glass bowl.

When two identical fruits touch, they merge into the next biggest fruit in the sequence (e.g., two blueberries become a strawberry). You must strategically drop each new fruit to set up merges and chain reactions. Plan your placements carefully to manage space effectively.

The game ends if the fruits stack up and cross the line at the top of the container. There are no time limits, so take your time, plan your moves, and aim for that satisfying final merge to create the ultimate watermelon!
