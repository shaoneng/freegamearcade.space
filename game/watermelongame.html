Watermelon Game

How to Play Watermelon Game

Welcome to the deliciously addictive world of the Watermelon Game! The objective is simple: drop fruits into a container and merge them to create bigger fruits. Your ultimate goal is to create the largest fruit of all—the mighty watermelon.

Use your mouse or finger to control where each fruit is dropped. When two identical fruits touch, they'll instantly merge into the next fruit in the evolution cycle (for example, two cherries become a strawberry). Planning your drops is crucial. Try to group similar fruits together to set up chain reactions and efficiently clear space.

The challenge lies in managing the limited space within the container. If your fruits stack up and cross the red line at the top, the game ends. A good strategy is to keep larger, harder-to-merge fruits at the bottom and sides, leaving room in the middle for smaller, more frequent merges. Good luck, and have fun merging!
