Watermelon Suika

How to Play Watermelon Suika

The objective of Watermelon Suika is to combine identical fruits to create larger ones, with the ultimate goal of forming a giant watermelon. You'll be given a sequence of fruits to drop into a container, starting with small ones like berries.

Use your mouse or finger to control the position of the fruit at the top of the screen. Move it left or right to aim, and then release the mouse button or lift your finger to drop the fruit into the container below. The game uses realistic physics, so fruits will roll and settle.

When two of the same fruits touch, they will merge and evolve into the next larger fruit in the cycle. Plan your drops strategically to set up chain reactions and make the most of the limited space. The game ends if the fruits pile up and cross the line at the top of the box. Think ahead and aim for the biggest merges to get the highest score!
