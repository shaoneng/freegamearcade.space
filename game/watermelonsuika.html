Watermelon Suika

How to Play Watermelon Suika

The goal in Watermelon Suika is to combine identical fruits to create bigger fruits, with the ultimate aim of making a giant watermelon. A sequence of fruits will appear at the top of the screen, and your task is to drop them into the container below.

Use your mouse to aim where you want the fruit to land. On a mobile device, simply drag your finger to position the fruit. When you are ready, click the mouse button or lift your finger to release the fruit. When two of the same fruits touch inside the container, they merge into the next largest fruit in the evolution cycle.

Be strategic with your placements! The game is over if the fruits pile up and cross the line at the top of the container. Think ahead to set up clever combinations and chain reactions to score points and create the legendary watermelon without overflowing the box.
