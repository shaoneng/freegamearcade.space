Watermelon Suika

How to Play Watermelon Suika

Welcome to the addictive world of Watermelon Suika! The objective is simple: drop fruits into a container and merge them to create larger fruits, with the ultimate goal of creating a massive watermelon. Be careful, though! If your fruits pile up and cross the line at the top of the box, the game is over.

Use your controls to position the fruit at the top of the screen, then release it into the container. When two identical fruits touch, they'll combine to form the next fruit in the evolutionary cycle. Plan your drops carefully to set up chain reactions and manage your space efficiently.

The controls are intuitive and easy to learn. On a computer, use your mouse to aim and click to drop. For mobile players, simply drag your finger to position the fruit and release to let it fall. Master these simple controls to become a fruit-merging champion!
