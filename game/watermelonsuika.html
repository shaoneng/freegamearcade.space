Watermelon Suika

How to Play Watermelon Suika

Welcome to Watermelon Suika, the delightful fruit-merging puzzle game! The goal is simple but addictive: combine fruits to create bigger ones, all the way up to the giant watermelon, without letting the container overflow.

Use your mouse or finger to move the current fruit left and right along the top of the box. Once you've chosen the perfect spot, release the mouse button or lift your finger to drop the fruit. When two identical fruits touch, they will merge and evolve into the next largest fruit in the sequence.

Your strategy is key! Plan your drops carefully to set up chain reactions and make the most of the limited space. If the fruits pile up and cross the line at the top of the box, the game is over. Explore different modes like Adventure and Puzzle for unique challenges that test your logic and physics skills!
