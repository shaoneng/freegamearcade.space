Watermelon Suika

How to Play Watermelon Suika

The goal of Watermelon Suika is to merge fruits to create the largest fruit possible, the giant watermelon, without overflowing the container.

Use your mouse (on desktop) or finger (on mobile) to aim where you want to drop the current fruit shown at the top. Click or release your finger to let the fruit fall into the play area.

When two identical fruits touch, they will merge into a single, larger fruit. This creates more space and progresses you toward the next fruit in the evolution cycle. The key is to strategically place fruits to set up combos and efficient merges.

Your game ends if the fruits stack up and cross the line at the top of the container. Plan your drops carefully, use the physics to your advantage, and aim for that delicious watermelon to get the highest score!
