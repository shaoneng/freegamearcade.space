Watermelon Suika

How to Play Watermelon Suika

Welcome to Watermelon Suika! The objective is to merge identical fruits to create larger ones, with the ultimate goal of forming a giant watermelon. A fruit will appear at the top of the screen, which you control.

Use your mouse to move the fruit left and right, and click to drop it into the container below. On a mobile device, simply drag your finger to aim and release to drop.

When two of the same fruits touch, they will combine into the next biggest fruit in the cycle. Be strategic with your placements to create chain reactions and manage your space effectively. If the fruits pile up and cross the line at the top, the game is over. See how many watermelons you can make!
