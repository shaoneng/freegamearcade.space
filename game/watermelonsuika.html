Watermelon Suika

How to Play Watermelon Suika

Welcome to the addictively fun world of Watermelon Suika! The objective is simple: create the largest fruit possible—the glorious watermelon—without overflowing the container.

You'll start by dropping small fruits from the top. Use your mouse to aim where you want the fruit to fall, and click to release it. On mobile devices, simply drag your finger to position the fruit and release to drop.

When two identical fruits touch, they will merge into a single, larger fruit. The cycle continues, from small berries to cherries, oranges, and beyond, all the way up to the coveted watermelon. Plan your drops carefully to set up chain reactions and make the most of the limited space. If the fruits pile up and cross the line at the top, the game is over!

Beyond the classic mode, Watermelon Suika offers various puzzle levels that add unique twists, such as physics challenges and logic problems, to keep the gameplay fresh and exciting.
