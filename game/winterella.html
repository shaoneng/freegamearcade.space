Winterella

How to Play Winterella

Welcome to the magical world of Winterella! Your mission is to help three beautiful ice queens get ready for the spectacular Winter Ball. Each queen has a unique style, and they're counting on your fashion expertise to look their absolute best.

Playing is simple and intuitive. Use your mouse on a desktop or your finger on a mobile device to interact with the game. Select one of the three queens to begin her makeover journey.

Follow the on-screen instructions to prepare each queen's face for makeup. Then, unleash your creativity by choosing from a wide array of icy and cool-toned cosmetics. Apply eyeshadow, mascara, blush, and lipstick to craft a stunning look that perfectly captures the spirit of winter. Once you're satisfied with your creation, move on to the next queen and repeat the process until all three are ready to dazzle at the ball!
