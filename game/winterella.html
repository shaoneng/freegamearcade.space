How to Play Winterella

Welcome to Winterella, where your fashion and makeup skills are needed for the most anticipated event of the seasonâ€”the Winter Ball! Your mission is to help three beautiful and diverse ice queens get ready for their big night. Each girl has a unique style, and it's up to you to make them look absolutely breathtaking.

The gameplay is simple and intuitive. Start by choosing one of the three queens. You will then be guided through a series of beauty steps. First, prepare their skin by cleansing and prepping their faces. Once the canvas is ready, dive into the makeup! Select from a variety of icy and winter-themed cosmetics, including eyeshadow, lipstick, and blush, to create the perfect look.

All actions are controlled with your mouse. Simply click or tap on the cosmetic items and then on the queen's face to apply them. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete each stage of the makeover. Once you're finished with one queen, you can move on to the next until all three are ready to dazzle at the ball!
