Winterella

How to Play Winterella

Welcome to the enchanting world of Winterella! Your mission is to prepare three beautiful ice queens for the grand Winter Ball. Each queen is waiting for your unique styling touch to look her absolute best for this special occasion.

Use your mouse on a desktop or your finger on a mobile device to interact with the game. Select from a wide range of cosmetics, including eyeshadow, lipstick, blush, and more. Follow the on-screen prompts to cleanse and prepare each queen's face before applying the makeup.

Experiment with different icy and cool-toned colors to match the winter theme. Click or tap on the makeup items and then apply them to the queen's face. Once you are satisfied with your creation for one queen, you can move on to the next. The goal is to make all three queens look breathtakingly beautiful for the ball!
