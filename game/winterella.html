Winterella

How to Play Winterella

Your mission in Winterella is to become the ultimate stylist for three stunning ice queens attending the Winter Ball. The game is controlled entirely with your mouse or by tapping on your mobile device.

First, select one of the three queens to begin her makeover. You will be guided through a series of beauty steps, starting with preparing her skin for makeup.

Follow the on-screen prompts to select and apply various cosmetics. Choose from a palette of frosty, winter-themed colors for eyeshadow, lipstick, blush, and more. Simply click or tap on the desired cosmetic and then on the queen's face to apply it. Experiment with different combinations to create a look that is both beautiful and fitting for a winter queen!

Once you are satisfied with your creation for one queen, you can move on to the next. The goal is to make all three women look breathtakingly beautiful for their special evening.
