Winterella

How to Play Winterella

Your mission in Winterella is to become the royal stylist for three enchanting ice queens attending the annual Winter Ball. Each queen has a unique style, and it's your job to create the perfect frosty makeup look to match their beauty and the wintery theme.

The game guides you through each step of the makeover process. Start by preparing each queen's face for makeup. Then, explore the wide array of icy cosmetics, from shimmering eyeshadows to cool-toned lipsticks and blushes. Select the colors and styles that you think will make them the belles of the ball.

Playing is simple! Just use your mouse to click or your finger to tap on the makeup items and apply them to the queens. Follow the on-screen prompts to move from one step to the next and complete all three stunning transformations.
