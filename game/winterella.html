Winterella

How to Play Winterella

In Winterella, your goal is to get three beautiful ice queens ready for the grand Winter Ball. You are their personal makeup artist, and they are counting on you to make them look stunning and perfectly suited for the chilly, magical evening!

The game features three distinct models with unique beauty. Use your mouse to interact with the on-screen cosmetics. Start by preparing their faces for makeup, then dive into the fun part: applying a full range of icy cosmetics.

Select from a variety of ice-themed colors and accessories. Choose eyeshadows, lipsticks, and blushes that fit the winter theme. Experiment with different combinations to create the most beautiful and breathtaking looks for each queen. Simply click or tap on the items you want to use and apply them to the models to bring your vision to life.

The game is fully compatible with both desktop computers (using a mouse) and mobile devices (using your touchscreen). Enjoy this cozy and creative experience and show off your styling skills!
