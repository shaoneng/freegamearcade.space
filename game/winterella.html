Winterella

How to Play Winterella

In Winterella, your mission is to prepare three unique winter queens for the glamorous Winter Ball. Your creativity and sense of style will bring their icy beauty to life!

Begin by selecting one of the three beautiful girls: a brunette from Brazil, an Afro queen with dreadlocks, or a blonde from the cold lands. Each queen has her own unique ethnic beauty, providing a fresh canvas for your artistry.

Use your mouse or touchscreen to navigate the makeup options. Click on different categories like eyeshadow, lipstick, blush, and accessories. Choose from a palette of cool, winter-themed colors to create a stunning, frosty look that will make each girl the belle of the ball. Once you're satisfied with one queen, move on to the next until all three are ready to make their grand entrance!
