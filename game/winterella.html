Winterella

How to Play Winterella

Your mission in Winterella is to prepare three stunning ice queens for the grand Winter Ball. The game is a simple and intuitive point-and-click (or tap on mobile) experience.

Start by selecting one of the three queens. Each has a unique ethnic beauty. Your first step is to prepare her face for makeup, ensuring a flawless canvas. Follow the on-screen prompts to cleanse and prep her skin.

Next, dive into the makeup kit! You'll find a beautiful selection of frosty, winter-themed cosmetics. Choose from various eyeshadows, eyeliners, lipsticks, and blushes in cool, icy tones. Experiment with different combinations to create the perfect breathtaking look that suits the winter theme. Once you are satisfied with one queen, move on to the next until all three are ready to dazzle at the ball!
