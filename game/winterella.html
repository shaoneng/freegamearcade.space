Winterella

How to Play Winterella

Your mission in Winterella is to prepare three beautiful ice queens for the grand Winter Ball. Each girl needs a unique and stunning makeup look that fits the frosty, enchanting theme.

Using your mouse (on desktop) or finger (on mobile), select from a wide array of ice-themed cosmetics. Follow the on-screen guides to cleanse their faces before applying foundation, eyeshadow, lipstick, and other glamorous accessories. Simply click or tap on the makeup items you wish to use and apply them to the girls.

You will style each of the three diverse and beautiful queens in turn. Experiment with different cool-toned colors and shimmering styles to create the most breathtaking winter looks and make them the undeniable stars of the ball!
