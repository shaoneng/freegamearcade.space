Wood Block Jam

How to Play Wood Block Jam

The goal in Wood Block Jam is to clear the board by moving each colored block to its matching exit. It's a game of strategy and foresight, where every move counts.

Each block is marked with an arrow indicating the only direction it can move. To move a block, simply click (or tap) and swipe it in its designated direction. Plan your moves carefully, as one block can obstruct the path of another.

As you progress, you'll encounter various challenges and new mechanics. Some paths may be blocked, tiles might be frozen, and special corner tiles will require you to think outside the box. The key is to find the right sequence of moves to free up space and clear a path for every block.
