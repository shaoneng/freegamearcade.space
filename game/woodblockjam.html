Wood Block Jam

How to Play Wood Block Jam

Welcome to Wood Block Jam, a delightful and challenging puzzle game that will test your strategic thinking. The objective is simple: clear the board by moving all the colored wooden blocks to their matching exit points.

Each block can only move in the direction indicated by the arrow on it. To move a block, simply click (or tap) and swipe in that direction. Plan your moves carefully, as the blocks can obstruct each other, creating a traffic jam on the wooden grid.

As you progress, you'll encounter new mechanics like frozen tiles and corner pieces, adding more layers to the puzzle. Think ahead, find the right sequence of moves, and slide your way to victory through hundreds of brain-teasing levels!
