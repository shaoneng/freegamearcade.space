How to Play Wood Block Jam

The objective in Wood Block Jam is to strategically clear the board by guiding each colored block to its matching exit. Itâ€™s a game that blends logic and spatial awareness, where every move you make can either solve the puzzle or block your path entirely.

Controls are designed to be smooth and simple. If you're playing on a PC or Chromebook, use your mouse to click and drag a block in the direction you want it to move. On mobile devices, simply tap and swipe the block with your finger.

Pay close attention to the direction each block faces, as it can only move that way. You must think ahead, planning your moves to create space and clear paths for other blocks. As you advance through the levels, you'll encounter new challenges like frozen blocks and special tiles that introduce fresh strategic layers to this addictive brain-teaser.
