WormsZone.io

How to Play WormsZone.io

Your primary objective in WormsZone.io is to grow your worm into the largest in the arena. Navigate the vibrant battlefield and consume all the food you can find—from snacks to giant burgers—to increase your length and score.

To eliminate rivals, you must cleverly maneuver your worm to make other players' heads crash into your body. When they're defeated, they'll turn into a feast of food for you to collect. Be strategic and use your growing size to trap smaller worms, but stay vigilant to avoid crashing yourself!

Control your worm's movement simply by guiding it with your mouse. For a burst of speed to chase down food or escape a dangerous situation, click the left or right mouse button. Keep an eye out for special potions scattered across the map; these can give you temporary advantages like increased speed, a wider field of view, or bonus points for the food you eat.

Express your personality by visiting the Worm Wardrobe! As you play and complete missions, you'll earn coins that you can use to unlock a huge variety of cool skins, colors, and faces for your worm, making you stand out in the crowded arena.
