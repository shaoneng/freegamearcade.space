How to Play WormsZone.io

Welcome to the competitive and colorful arena of WormsZone.io! The main objective is to grow your worm into the largest one in the game by eating food and defeating other players.

Navigate the vast playing field with your mouse, guiding your worm towards snacks and power-ups. The more you eat, the bigger you get, which makes it easier to trap rivals. To defeat another worm, you must make its head collide with your worm's body. Once defeated, they will transform into a pile of nutritious food that you can consume for a massive growth boost.

Be careful not to run into other worms yourself, or it's game over! Use the left or right mouse button to activate a speed boost, which is perfect for cutting off an opponent or making a quick escape. Keep in mind that boosting will gradually decrease your size, so use it strategically. Look for potions and special items on the map to gain temporary advantages like better vision, increased speed, or a higher food magnet range.
