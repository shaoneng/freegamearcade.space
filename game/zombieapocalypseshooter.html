How to Play Zombie Apocalypse Shooter

Welcome to the front lines of the apocalypse! In Zombie Apocalypse Shooter, your objective is to survive against waves of the undead and complete a series of challenging missions. As a first-person shooter, the controls are intuitive and easy to master.

  • Movement: Use the WASD keys to walk around the map.
  • Aim and Shoot: Use your mouse to aim your weapon and click the left mouse button to fire.
  • Reload: Press the R key to reload your weapon when you run out of ammunition.
  • Jump: Use the Spacebar to jump over obstacles and gain a tactical advantage.
