Zombie Apocalypse Shooter

How to Play Zombie Apocalypse Shooter

In Zombie Apocalypse Shooter, you are a lone survivor facing a relentless horde of the undead. This first-person shooter (FPS) challenges you to stay alive by eliminating every zombie in your path and successfully completing a series of dangerous missions.

Use your mouse to look around and aim your weapon. Press the left mouse button to fire. Use the standard WASD keys on your keyboard to move forward, left, backward, and right. Keep moving to avoid getting cornered by the zombies.

Your goal is to complete each mission objective, which typically involves clearing an area of all zombies. Conserve your ammunition, aim for headshots for maximum efficiency, and always be aware of your surroundings. The fate of humanity rests on your shoulders!
