Julie Beauty Salon

How to Play Julie Beauty Salon

Unleash your inner stylist and give your clients the ultimate makeover! In Julie Beauty Salon, you take charge of your very own salon, helping customers look their absolute best with your creative touch.

Core Gameplay:

  • Follow Instructions: The game guides you through each step. Simply click or tap on the highlighted tools and areas to perform actions.
  • Hair Care: Start by treating your client's hair. You'll wash, condition, rinse, and dry it to get it ready for styling.
  • Facial Spa: Apply various face masks, creams, and treatments to give your client a fresh, glowing complexion.
  • Makeup Artistry: Dive into the makeup kit! Apply foundation, blush, eyeshadow, mascara, and lipstick to create a stunning look.
  • Dress Up: No makeover is complete without the perfect outfit. Browse through a wardrobe of stylish clothes and accessories to finish the look.
  • Client Satisfaction: Your goal is to complete all the steps to make your client happy. A happy client means a successful salon!

Controls:

  • Mouse/Touch: Use your mouse cursor or finger to click/tap on the items and characters on the screen.

This game is perfect for those who love fashion, creativity, and makeover games. It's a relaxing and fun experience suitable for all ages. Get ready to style!
