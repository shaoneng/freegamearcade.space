Kick the Pirate

How to Play Kick the Pirate

Ahoy matey! In Kick the Pirate, your job is simple: turn this grumpy pirate into your personal punching bag. It's the perfect stress-busting clicker game to blow off some steam after a long day.

Core Gameplay:

  • Start by clicking or tapping the pirate as fast (or as furiously) as you like.
  • Each hit earns you coins, which you'll use to buy new, wacky weapons.
  • Deplete the pirate's health bar to complete the level and earn even more rewards.
  • As you progress, you can unlock more powerful tools of destruction.
  • If you'd prefer less graphic visuals, use the drop icon in the corner to toggle off the blood splatter.
  • Toggle settings like sound in the top right corner.

Weapons to Unlock:

There are tons of wacky weapons to unlock, each more satisfying than the last. You can switch between weapons using the buttons at the top of the screen. Some examples include:

  • Cannonball: Send him flying with a satisfying boom.
  • Flintlock Pistol: A classic choice for any pirate encounter.
  • Battle Axe: For when you need some heavy-hitting action.
  • Ship Anchor: Drop it on him for maximum impact.
  • TNT Barrel: Explosive fun guaranteed.

Kick the Pirate was developed by FreezeNova. While it uses cartoon-style visuals, the core gameplay revolves around humorous violence. Recommended for players 10 and up, with parental guidance if needed.
