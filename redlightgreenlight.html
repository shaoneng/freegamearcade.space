Red Light Green Light

How to Play Red Light Green Light

Step into the iconic and nerve-wracking world of the hit TV series Squid Game. In this online version of Red Light, Green Light, you are a numbered contestant dropped into a deadly playground where movement can mean either survival or death. Nothing in between.

The Rules of Survival:

  • The goal is to run from the starting line to the finish line at the far end of the field.
  • When the giant doll has its back turned and says "Green Light," you are safe to move towards the finish line.
  • When the doll suddenly turns to face you and says "Red Light," you must freeze immediately. Do not move a muscle!
  • If the doll sees you moving during a "Red Light" phase, you are eliminated from the game.

Controls:

The controls are simple and intuitive. Use your mouse or keyboard to move your character forward. Release the button or key to stop instantly. Timing and reflexes are everything!

This fan-made experience is designed for quick, casual fun, capturing the tension of the show with a clean design and responsive movement. Hit the play button and see if you have what it takes to survive!
