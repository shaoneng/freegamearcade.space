Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

How to Play Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner

Grab your katana and get ready to slice your way through waves of enemies! Sword Play Ninja Slice Runner is an action runner game where you become a blade-wielding ninja on a high platform filled with enemies. It's a fun mix of Fruit Ninja’s slicing satisfaction and the thrill of endless runners.

Core Gameplay:

  • Use your finger or mouse to control your sword and slice anything that comes your way.
  • Cut down flying shurikens, bullets, shields, and other enemy projectiles.
  • Dodge massive bullets and other obstacles to survive.
  • Enjoy satisfying slow-motion effects that trigger during epic slicing moments.
  • Complete each level by clearing the platform of all enemies in a simple but colorful 3D world.

Unlockable Swords & Trails

One of the coolest parts of the game is the wide range of sword skins and trail effects you can unlock in the shop. They make your slicing feel even more fun and rewarding!

Swords:

  • Unlock random swords with 700 coins or collect specific ones by leveling up or completing challenges.
  • Change up your blade with unique weapons, from classic katanas to other quirky alternatives.

Trails:

  • Leave a stylish trail when you swing your sword. These visual effects make every attack more exciting.
  • Unlock different trail effects in the shop to add extra flair to every slash.

Tip: Trails don’t change gameplay, but they make every hit more stylish, creating a VR-like experience. Try combining different swords and trails to match your ninja vibe!

This game was developed by PlayGama. If you're craving more stealthy swordplay, check out Blocky Parkour Ninja for a 3D platforming experience.
